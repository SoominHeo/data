S. Korea beats Oman 2-0 in Olympic football qualifier
On Sept. 21, South Korea beat Oman 2-0 at the Changwon Football Center, around 400 kilometers southeast of Seoul. It was the start of the final round of the Asian football qualification for Korea’s 2012 London Olympics bid.
 Midfielder Yoon Bitgaram scored on a free kick in the 23rd minute, while Kim Bo-kyung added the second goal in the second half of the game.
 According to Yonhap News, following Yoon’s opening goal, South Korea picked up its finesse and led the day’s game. South Korea is in the same group as Oman, Qatar and Saudi Arabia in Group A. Its next match is against Qatar on Nov. 23.
