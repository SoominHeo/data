Busan film festival’s venue unveiled
The permanent venue of the annual Busan International Film Festival (BIFF), one of Asia’s largest film festivals, was unveiled to the public on Sept. 29, Yonhap News reported.
Busan Cinema Center has grabbed people’s attention for its Guinness World Record-sized roof. 
It took three years and 167.8 billion won to complete the center, which is consisted of a multi-purpose performance hall, a medium-sized theater, two small theaters, BIFF organizing committee’s office building and a 4,000-seat outdoor theater, where opening and closing ceremonies of the festival will be held on a yearly basis, said the report.
