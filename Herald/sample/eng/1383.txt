Passenger trains collide near Daegu Station
A KTX bullet train collided with a Mugunghwa train on Aug. 31, causing service disruptions between Seoul and Busan late into the night.
 The two trains were both heading for Seoul and collided with each other in the morning near Daegu Station. Eight passenger cars of the KTX train and the engine car of the Mugunghwa train were derailed.
 No casualties or serious injuries were reported from the crash, and Seoul-Busan rail services were resumed later that night.
