Fun Keywords of the Week
Desert hotel in China 중국 사막호텔
 　The flower-like structure is a steel-made hotel in a Chinese desert. This hotel is located 800 km from the capital city of Beijing. What is surprising about the structure is that it has not used water or concrete for construction and that it produces electricity and water of its own.
Artificial muscle 터미네이터 인공 근육
 　Artificial muscles developed by a U.S. government office are said to be 1,000 times stronger than those of humans. This means a robot carrying these muscles will be able to throw an object that is 50 times heavier than itself, said the U.S. developers of the muscles.
