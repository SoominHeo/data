Fun Keywords of the Week
Nose-typing champion (코 타이핑 세계 기록)
The world’s fastest nose typer, Khushid Hussein, attracted a lot of Internet attention recently. The Indian man correctly entered a 103-character sentence in 47.44 seconds, setting a new Guinness World Record. The previous record was 1 minute and 33 seconds.
Child intruder (꼬마 침입자)
Brazilian football star Neymar was caught on camera posing with an unidentified child after a recent match between South Africa and Brazil. The young South African boy, who entered the ground after the final whistle of the game, was stopped by security guards, but Neymar and his Brazilian teammates took him from the guards to give the boy some great moments.
