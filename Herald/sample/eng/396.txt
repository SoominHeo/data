Seoul’s guest houses welcome ‘hallyu’ fans
Guest houses located in the Metropolitan Seoul area that resemble Western bed and breakfasts are enjoying an increased popularity with young Asian tourists, says Yonhap News.
 According to the report, these guest houses stole travels’ heart by offering reasonable lodging price at around 20,000 won ($17.80) a night per person. 
 The majority of guests came from other Asian countries such as Japan, Thailand and China seeking to get a closer look at K-pop idols’ live performances and to enjoy shopping in Myeong-dong, Dongdaemun and Cheongdam-dong. 
 More than 20 guest houses are operating in the Myeong-dong and Namsan-dong districts.
