Four S. Korean female CEOs chosen as Asia’s 50 power businesswomen
Four South Korean business leaders made the Forbes Asia list of top 50 businesswomen in the magazine’s March 12 issue.
 Those four were Romi Han of Haan Corp., Hyun Jeong-eun of Hyundai Group, Kim Sung-joo of Sungjoo Group and MCM Holdings AG. and Lee Mi-kyung of CJ Entertainment & Media. The companies they represent all have at least $100 million in annual revenue, reports Yonhap News.
 China had the highest number with 21 businesswomen on the list, followed by India with eight and Singapore with five. South Korea and Indonesia both shared four spots, followed by Japan with three, Australia with two and one for each in the Philippines, Thailand, and Vietnam.
