Korea’s ballet company plays ‘Swan Lake’ in South Africa
South Korea‘s Universal Ballet performed Tchaikovsky’s “Swan Lake” in Johannesburg on March 16-18 to commemorate the 20th anniversary of diplomatic relations between South Korea and South Africa.
 The show was sponsored by South Korean Embassy in Johannesburg, the Korea Foundation and Joburg Theater. The Mariinsky Theater version of “Swan Lake,” was put on at Joburg Theater in the South African city. It has been performed in 12 countries, including the United States, Canada, Germany and Italy. 
 Julia Moon, general director of Universal Ballet, hopes the performance will help introduce the high standard of Korean ballet to the world, said Yonhap News.
