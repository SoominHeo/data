Ronaldo sets Spanish record for goals
Famous footballer Cristiano Ronaldo recently became the fastest player to reach 100 goals in the Spanish league during a match between Real Madrid and Real Sociedad on March 24. 
The record was previously held by Ferenc Puskas, who scored 100 goals in 105 matches in the 1960s. Ronaldo was able to score 101 goals in only 92 games.
