Korean medical care highly favored by tourists
Foreign tourists from the U.S., European countries and especially Japan are rushing to Korea seeking medical treatment.
 Japanese tourists who enjoy Korean dramas and music gather information on Korean medical treatment through various culture magazines and booklets, according to news reports. 
Some clinics in the Seoul metropolitan region even have professional interpreters to assist with medical care for Japanese tourists.
 Major medical tourism packages in the county include skin care, cosmetic surgery, acupuncture and diet programs.
 Acupuncture can help fight aging, wrinkles and deteriorating skin tones, according to oriental medicine experts.
 Most of the foreign tourists seeking Korean medical care are from Japan and China, but the number of those from European countries including Russia and the U.S. are steadily increasing, according to news reports.
