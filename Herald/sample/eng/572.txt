Asian second languages, Thai students’ favorite
Chinese, Japanese and Korean are the three most po second foreign languages for Thai students, a local survey showed.
 The survey by the Office of Basic Education Commission (OBEC) revealed that some 300,000 students would choose to study Chinese as their second foreign language, with 34,000 selecting Japanese, followed by 12,000 students opting for Korean. 
 “French was the most popular second foreign language in Thailand in the past but the number of students studying Asian languages rose alongside Asia’s economic growth,” noted Usanee Watanapan, deputy director of OBEC.
