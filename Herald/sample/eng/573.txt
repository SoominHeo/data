Korea hosts ‘Green Growth Summit’
Government officials and experts around the world will gather in Seoul early May to discuss ways to promote green development.
 In this year’s second Global Green Growth Summit, scheduled for May 11 to 12, dignitaries will seek to strengthen international cooperation in the area of green growth with the help of environmentally friendly industries.
 Key participants include UNEP executive director Achim Steiner, OECD director of the environment Simon Upton, GGGI chairman Han Seung-soo, Danish Crown Prince Frederik, Kiribati’s President Anote Tong and Masayoshi Son, the chairman and chief executive of Japan’s Softbank Corp, according to the event’s state-run organizer, the Global Green Growth Institute.
