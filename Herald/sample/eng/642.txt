Korea’s fifth int‘l boat show opens
The fifth Korea International Boat Show opened in Hwaseong and Ansan in Gyeonggi Province on May 30, KBS reported. 
 The international boat show, in which 340 companies from 20 countries are participating, lasts until June 3 and all tickets are sold out.
 At the event, boats, yachts and marine leisure equipment made by top importers such as Italy, France and Germany will go on display, according to the report.
The Korea Match Cup 2012, one of three top yacht matches, was held on the same day with 12 international yacht teams competing for the top spot.
