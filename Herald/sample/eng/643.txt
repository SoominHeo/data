Judges of Superstar K4 announced
South Korea’s veteran singer, Lee Seung-cheol and queen of hip hop, Yoon Mi-rae, will come back as judges of the fourth season of Superstar K, the show’s production team said.
 Lee and Yoon were judges in Superstar K3, and the two will join singer PSY for the fourth season.
 Yoon, well known for her divine rapping and vocal skills, won the public’s heart in the last season with her empathetic judging style. 
 The fourth season of Superstar K will begin airing in August 2012.
