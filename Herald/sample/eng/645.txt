New Chromebook unveiled
New Chromebook unveiled.wmaSamsung Electronics released the next-generation Chromebook laptop and their first desktop computer for Chrome, called Chromebox, on May 29.
 The latest releases by Google Inc. and Samsung provides an opportunity for consumers to convert to a new computer operating system (OS) revolving around the popular Chrome Web browser. The first Chrome OS-based devices were released a year ago, representing a new entirely cloud-based approach to personal computing. 
The price for Chromebook models that only connect through Wi-Fi is $449, while those that connect on a 3G network are $549. The Chromebox is priced at $329.
