LinkedIn, eHarmony hacked
Social networking site LinkedIn and online dating service eHarmony have become recent targets for a successful password hacking attack. 　
 The attack resulted in 1.5 million eHarmony passwords and 6.5 million LinkedIn passwords to become stolen. It is believed that the same hacker stole both sets of user information and published them on the website insidepro.com under the username “dwdm.” 
According to Reuters, “the breaches are the latest in a string of high-profile attacks around the world that have put the personal information of millions at risk.”
