Korea’s FIFA ranking drops to 35th place
South Korea’s FIFA world ranking has slipped four notches to 35th, a report by the world’s football governing body said on June 7.
 The nation maintained its third-highest spot in the Asia and Oceania region. The region’s top soccer team, Japan, came at 23rd and Australia was next in line at 24th place, Yonhap News said.
 Spain topped the list for the second time in a row, trailed by Uruguay and Germany.
