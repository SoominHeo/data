K-pop stars to perform at A-Nation festival
Four K-pop groups -- Big Bang, TVXQ, Super Junior and U-Kiss -- will perform at the A-Nation festival, the biggest music festival in Japan. The festival will take place from Aug. 18 to 26.
 According to a KBS global report, TVXQ will make an appearance on the Osaka show on Aug.19 and later in Tokyo on Aug. 25. Big Bang is forecast to perform their show in Tokyo on Aug. 26.
 A-Nation is one of Japan’s top summer festivals which has been running for 10 years.
