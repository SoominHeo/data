Over 20,000 journalists to gather in London for report
About 21,000 journalists and photographers are expected to swarm into London for the Olympic Games, according to AFP news.
 Britain’s official Olympic broadcaster, the BBC will offer 2,500 hours of live coverage, and U.S. broadcaster NBC is planning to air more than 200 hours of 3-D footage, said the news.
 In the interviews with AFP, BBC officials said that the London Olympics would be the first truly "Digital Olympics" with the national broadcaster offering viewers the most comprehensive coverage of an Olympic Games ever.
