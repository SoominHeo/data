Girls’ Generation, foreigners’ favorite K-pop group
Korean girl group Girls’ Generation was the most popular K-pop group among foreigners participating in the “2012 K-pop Cover Dance Festival” contest, the festival’s committee said.
 A total of 1,350 teams from some 50 countries participated in the contest by submitting their videos of dance performances. Of the videos, Girls’ Generation’s choreography was most frequently emulated with 155 entries. 
According to the committee, Girls’ Generation was most popular among Asian and European fans. 
The final round of the K-pop dance festival will be held on Sept. 21 in Gyeongju, Korea.
