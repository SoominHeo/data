Korea’s delegation for London Olympics formed
Korea’s delegation for the Summer Olympic Games in London pledged to make their best efforts at a ceremony in Seoul.
 The delegation consists of 245 athletes, and 129 sports officials were there at the ceremony getting ready for the upcoming Olympics which begins on July 27.
 The 2012 delegation includes the smallest number of athletes for a Summer Olympics since the 1984 Los Angeles Games, when 210 athletes represented South Korea. 
The South Korean delegation hopes to win 10 gold medals.
