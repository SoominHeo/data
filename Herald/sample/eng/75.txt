N. Korea to farm Russia‘s Far East
On Sept. 1, Yonhap News referred to a report made by Russia’s state-funded radio station that North Korea is poised to develop farmland in Russia‘s Far East region. 
 The Voice of Russia said that a North Korean agricultural delegation had a meeting with his Russian counterpart to figure out in earnest how to cultivate the empty land in Russia’s Far East Amur region.
 According to Russia’s international radio broadcasting service, there is vacant lot of 200,000 hectares in the Amur region.
