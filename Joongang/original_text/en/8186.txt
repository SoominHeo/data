Creditors of Dong-Ah Group are now working out a plan to reschedule 1.8 trillon won of Dong-Ah Construction and Industry debt.

 On April 8, in a meeting of 46 creditors, including the main creditor Seoul Bank, they agreed to convert 1.1 trillion of Dong-Ah‘s debt into equity and the remaining 700 billion won will be covered 

 Creditors are considering ways to deal with Korea Express Company and how to convert the payment guarantee into self-debt and then to issues new share to be purchased 

 “The creditors’ principle on handling Korea Express is to sell the company by the end of September,” said a source from the creditors.
