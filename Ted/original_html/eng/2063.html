Arthur Benjamin

A performance of "Mathemagic"

Posted Dec 2007 Rated Jaw-dropping, Fascinating

0:11 Good morning, ladies and gentlemen. My name is Art Benjamin, and I am a "mathemagician." What that means is, I combine my loves of math and magic to do something I call "mathemagics."
