Rainn Wilson

Ideas worth dating

Posted Oct 2016 Rated Funny, Ingenious

0:12 (Music)

0:17 Rainn Wilson: It takes its toll, being alone. I'm a little bit lost, and it's finally time to make a real connection. Who am I?

0:27 (Drums)

0:29 I'm a single white male, 45 years of age. I love animals. Gainfully employed. I'm a people person. I keep fit. Who am I looking for? I'm looking for my idea mate. Are you that idea that matches with who I really am?

0:51 (Video) Ron Finley: How would you feel if you had no access to healthy food? Gardening is the most therapeutic and defiant act you can do.

1:39 RW: Well, I'm a little nearsighted, but yeah.

In fact, even the sensory information that your eyes are receiving, your ears are receiving, is completely meaningless because it could mean anything. That tree could be a large object far away or a small object up close, and your brain has no way of knowing.

2:39 You're not making the face. You're making the "I'm not good at life" face.
