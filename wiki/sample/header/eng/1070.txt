HONNE are a British electronic soul duo formed in 2014 in Bow, London, consisting of James Hatcher  and Andy Clutterbuck  who both write, record and produce the music.
The band released their first two EPs, Warm on a Cold Night and All in the Value, on Super Recordings in 2014.
In 2015 they released their third EP, Coastal Love, on their own imprint Tatemae Recordings.
Their debut studio album Warm on a Cold Night was released on 22 July 2016 through Tatemae Recordings.
