The NBA 25th Anniversary Team was announced on 11 December 1971 to celebrate the 25-year existence of BAA/NBA.
This team was meant to be an All-NBA Team for that period.
Up until that time, All-NBA Teams only consisted of 2 teams , so this Team also only consists of 2 teams/10 players .
The list excluded players who were still playing in the NBA, such as Wilt Chamberlain and Jerry West.
