NKOTBSB was a supergroup consisting of the members of American boy bands New Kids on the Block and Backstreet Boys.
Howie Dorough of the Backstreet Boys came up with the name, which is a combination of established initialisms of each groups' names, NKOTB and BSB.
Together they have released one compilation album, the eponymous NKOTBSB , one single, and toured in North America, Europe, Australia, and Asia in 2011 and 2012.
