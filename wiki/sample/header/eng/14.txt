The oscar  is a species of fish from the cichlid family known under a variety of common names, including tiger oscar, velvet cichlid, and marble cichlid.
In tropical South America, where the species naturally resides, A. ocellatus specimens are often found for sale as a food fish in the local markets.
The fish has been introduced to other areas, including China, Australia, and the United States.
It is considered a popular aquarium fish in Europe and the U.S.
