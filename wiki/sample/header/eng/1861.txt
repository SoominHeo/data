Gal Gadot-Varsano  is an Israeli actress and model.
As an actress, Gadot is known for her role as Gisele Yashar in The Fast and the Furious film series.
In 2016, Gadot began playing the role of Wonder Woman in the DC Extended Universe, starting with Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice and continuing as the lead in Wonder Woman .
In past years, she has been ranked as one of the highest earning models in Israel, behind Bar Refaeli.
She is the face of Gucci's Bamboo perfume.
