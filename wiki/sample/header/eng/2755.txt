The long-tongued nectar bat , also known as the northern blossom bat, honey nectar bat, least blossom-bat, dagger-toothed long-nosed fruit bat, and lesser long-tongued fruit bat, is a species of megabat.
M. minimus is one of the smallest species in the family Pteropodidae, with an average length of 60–85 mm.
It has a reddish-brown colouring with relatively long hair compared to the other species.
The hair on the abdomen is a lighter colour, and a dark brown stripe runs bilaterally down the top of the head and back.
