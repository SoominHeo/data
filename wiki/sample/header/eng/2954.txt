Megumi Nakajima  is a Filipino-Japanese voice actress and singer, currently represented by the Stardust Promotion talent agency.
She was born to a Japanese father and a Filipina mother.
In 2003, she participated in the "Self Stardust Promotion" contest and passed, thereby affiliating her to the agency.
In 2007, she auditioned to the Victor Entertainment sanctioned "Victor Vocal &amp; Voice Audition" and was chosen to voice Ranka Lee in Macross Frontier.
Her voice was also sampled to create the Vocaloid named Megpoid
