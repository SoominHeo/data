The Tōbu Utsunomiya Line  is a 24.3-kilometre railway line in Tochigi Prefecture, Japan, owned and operated by the private railway operator Tobu Railway.
It connects Shin-Tochigi Station in Tochigi with Tobu Utsunomiya Station in Utsunomiya.
Shin-Tochigi Station offers connections to the Tōbu Nikkō Line and the Tobu Main Line network.
