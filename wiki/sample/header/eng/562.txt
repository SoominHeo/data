The Type 216 is a submarine design concept announced by Howaldtswerke-Deutsche Werft based on the Type 212/214.
It is a larger design targeted to meet the needs of the Australian Collins-class submarine replacement project, also known as Sea 1000, and the needs of other countries possibly including India and Canada.
The design is double hulled with two decks, includes a fuel cell, Permasyn motor, and lithium-ion batteries.
