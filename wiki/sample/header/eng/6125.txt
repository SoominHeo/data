Vahdat  is a city in western Tajikistan, just 10Â km east of Dushanbe.
In the last century the city has been renamed several times.
The city was known as Yangi-Bazar until 1936, when it was renamed Ordzhonikidzeabad in honor of Georgian communist Grigoriy Ordzhonikidze.
In 1992 after Tajikistan became independent the city was renamed Kofarnihon  and in 2003 it was renamed Vahdat.
