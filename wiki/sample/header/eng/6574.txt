Boaz Solossa  is an Indonesian professional footballer that currently plays for Persipura Jayapura in the Liga Super Indonesia.
He is known by his high leveled dribbling technique and his crossing accuracy or short-passing with his left-foot.
In 2011, Boaz got an offer to play at Dutch club VVV-Venlo, but because of family reasons he chose to keep playing in Persipura Jayapura.
