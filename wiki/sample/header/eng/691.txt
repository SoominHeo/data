July 4 is the 185th day of the year  in the Gregorian calendar.
There are 180 days remaining until the end of the year.
This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Monday, Wednesday or Saturday  than on Thursday or Friday , and slightly less likely to occur on a Tuesday or Sunday .
The Aphelion, the point in the year when the Earth is farthest from the Sun, occurs around this date.
