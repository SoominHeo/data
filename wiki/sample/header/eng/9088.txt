Ahn Nae-sang  is a South Korean actor.
He began his career on the stage, and in 1994 made his film debut in the Bong Joon-ho short film Baeksekin , followed by Jang Sun-woo's Bad Movie in 1997.
He has since starred in numerous films, with supporting roles in Lee Chang-dong's Oasis and Poetry, and a leading role in Hoichori .
He also appears in television series, notably Conspiracy in the Court, First Wives' Club, Three Brothers, and High Kick: Revenge of the Short Legged.
