Edward August Hennig  was an American gymnast who competed in the 1904 Summer Olympics.
He died in Summit County, Ohio.
Hennig won two gold medals, one of them at club swinging.
In the horizontal bar event he and his countryman Anton Heida had the same score and the gold medal was shared between them.
He also competed in the pommel horse event without winning a medal.
