<p><b>Shueili Township</b> (<a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">水里鄉</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">Hanyu Pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Shuǐlǐ Xiāng</span></i>; <a href="/wiki/Tongyong_Pinyin" title="Tongyong Pinyin">Tongyong Pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn" xml:lang="zh-Latn">Shuěilǐ Siang</span></i>) or <b>Shuili Township</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Township_(Taiwan)" title="Township (Taiwan)">rural township</a> in <a href="/wiki/Nantou_County" title="Nantou County">Nantou County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Taiwan" title="Taiwan">Taiwan</a>.</p>
