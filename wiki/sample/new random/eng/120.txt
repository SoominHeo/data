<p><b>Blagoveshchensk</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Благове́щенск</span>; <span class="Unicode"><a href="/wiki/Bashkir_language" title="Bashkir language">Bashkir</a>: <span lang="ba" xml:lang="ba">Благовещен</span></span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">town</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Republic_of_Bashkortostan" title="Republic of Bashkortostan">Republic of Bashkortostan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>, located 42 kilometers (26 mi) north of <a href="/wiki/Ufa" title="Ufa">Ufa</a> on the right bank of the <a href="/wiki/Belaya_River_(Kama)" title="Belaya River (Kama)">Belaya River</a>. Population: <span class="nowrap">34,239 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2010)" title="Russian Census (2010)">2010 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2010Census_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-2010Census-4">[4]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">32,989 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2002)" title="Russian Census (2002)">2002 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PopCensus_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-PopCensus-7">[7]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">27,705 (<a href="/wiki/Soviet_Census_(1989)" title="Soviet Census (1989)">1989 Census</a>)</span>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census1989_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-Census1989-8">[8]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Blagoveshchensk,_Republic_of_Bashkortostan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It was founded in 1756 as a settlement serving the copper melting works and was granted town status in 1941.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gr_6-2"><a href="#cite_note-gr-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Administrative_and_municipal_status">Administrative and municipal status</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Blagoveshchensk,_Republic_of_Bashkortostan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Administrative and municipal status">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Within the <a href="/wiki/Subdivisions_of_Russia#Administrative_divisions" title="Subdivisions of Russia">framework of administrative divisions</a>, Blagoveshchensk serves as the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_center" title="Administrative center">administrative center</a> of <a href="/wiki/Blagoveshchensky_District,_Republic_of_Bashkortostan" title="Blagoveshchensky District, Republic of Bashkortostan">Blagoveshchensky District</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-OKATO_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-OKATO-2">[2]</a></sup> even though it is not a part of it.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RBashkortostan_adm_1-4"><a href="#cite_note-RBashkortostan_adm-1">[1]</a></sup> As an administrative division, it is incorporated separately as the <b><a href="/wiki/City_of_federal_subject_significance" title="City of federal subject significance">town of republic significance</a> of Blagoveshchensk</b>—an administrative unit with the status equal to that of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_the_Republic_of_Bashkortostan" title="Administrative divisions of the Republic of Bashkortostan">districts</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RBashkortostan_adm_1-5"><a href="#cite_note-RBashkortostan_adm-1">[1]</a></sup> As a <a href="/wiki/Subdivisions_of_Russia#Municipal_divisions" title="Subdivisions of Russia">municipal division</a>, the town of republic significance of Blagoveshchensk is incorporated within Blagoveshchensky Municipal District as <b>Blagoveshchensk Urban Settlement</b>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RBashkortostan_mun_3-4"><a href="#cite_note-RBashkortostan_mun-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
