<p>The <b>Lesotho national football team</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Afrikaans_language" title="Afrikaans language">Afrikaans</a>: <span lang="af" xml:lang="af"><i>Lesothaanse nasionale sokkerspan</i></span>), nicknamed the <i>Likuena (Crocodiles)</i>, is the national team of <a href="/wiki/Lesotho" title="Lesotho">Lesotho</a> and is controlled by the <a href="/wiki/Lesotho_Football_Association" title="Lesotho Football Association">Lesotho Football Association</a>. The country has never qualified for the final stages of the <a href="/wiki/FIFA_World_Cup" title="FIFA World Cup">World Cup</a> or the <a href="/wiki/Africa_Cup_of_Nations" title="Africa Cup of Nations">Africa Cup of Nations</a>.</p>
