<p><b>Li Yuan-tsu</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">李元簇</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Lǐ Yuáncù</span></i>; born September 24, 1923 in <a href="/wiki/Pingjiang_County" title="Pingjiang County">Pingjiang</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hunan" title="Hunan">Hunan</a>), is a <a href="/wiki/Kuomintang" title="Kuomintang">Kuomintang</a> politician who served under <a href="/wiki/Lee_Teng-hui" title="Lee Teng-hui">Lee Teng-hui</a> as the eighth <a href="/wiki/Vice_President_of_the_Republic_of_China" title="Vice President of the Republic of China">Vice President of the Republic of China</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Li is of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hakka" title="Hakka">Hakka</a> ancestry.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
