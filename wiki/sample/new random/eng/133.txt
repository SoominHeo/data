<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Minori_Aoi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Debut_and_early_adult_videos">Debut and early adult videos</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Minori_Aoi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Debut and early adult videos">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Tokyo" title="Tokyo">Tokyo</a> on March 28, 1980, Minori Aoi was active in tennis during junior high school and high school.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Minori_Aoi_JMate_Interview_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Minori_Aoi_JMate_Interview-2">[2]</a></sup> She made her AV debut in the February 1999 release for the <a href="/wiki/Media_Station_(company)" title="Media Station (company)">Media Station Cosmos Plan</a> label, <i>Pure Heart</i> (純心).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-debut_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-debut-3">[3]</a></sup> Her first video was a difficult experience for her. She recalls, "I really hated it at the time. I think I also cried during then… it's the worst one I've been in. It's definitely not something I could recommend to my fans."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Minori_Aoi_JMate_Interview_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-Minori_Aoi_JMate_Interview-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>In November of her debut year, Aoi starred in <i>Dreaming Sailor</i>, a campus drama.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Dreaming_Sailor_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Dreaming_Sailor-4">[4]</a></sup> <i>I Love Big Needle</i> from January 2000 had Aoi playing the role of a nurse who is subject to sexual abuse from both the doctors and her patients.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> In <i>Angel</i>, released later the same month, Aoi acted as an angel who comes to earth to satisfy mortal desires.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> <i>Fetish Virgin IV</i> from March 2000, subtitled <i>Costume Play Mania</i>, featured Aoi in the popular <i><a href="/wiki/Cosplay" title="Cosplay">cosplay</a></i> genre.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Comeback">Comeback</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Minori_Aoi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Comeback">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Aoi quickly became a popular star of adult video, image video, photo books, and magazine appearances. However, even after her success, she retained her ambivalent attitude towards the AV profession, saying "I still can't get used to shooting in front of a camera though. There are some that enjoy it or get turned on by it, but I still get nervous even after fifteen tapes. There are times when I ponder about running out."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Minori_Aoi_JMate_Interview_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-Minori_Aoi_JMate_Interview-2">[2]</a></sup> She retired from the AV industry briefly in 2000 but returned in March 2001 in the video <i>Minori Aoi - Underground Job</i>. The AV had Aoi playing the role of a room service maid.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> Her second come-back video, <i>Confinement</i> (April 2001), was in the bondage genre.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> Up to this point in her career, Aoi had appeared mainly with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cosmos_Plan" title="Cosmos Plan">Cosmos Plan</a> label. In June 2001, she released her first video under the <a href="/wiki/Try-Heart_Corporation" title="Try-Heart Corporation">Try-Heart Corporation</a>'s SexiA label. Entitled <i>I Want To Fellatio</i>, the video focused on Aoi's oral sex techniques.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> The DVD version of this film included an instructional "How to Have an Orgasm" demonstration given by Aoi in a cheerleader costume.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_career">Later career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Minori_Aoi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Later career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Aoi was featured in the fashion magazine <i>An An</i> in 2003, and in November of that year <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cosmos_Plan" title="Cosmos Plan">Cosmos Plan</a> re-issued her work for the company in three volumes, and collected into one box set.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2012, when DMM held a poll of its customers to choose the 100 all-time best AV actresses to celebrate the 30th anniversary of adult videos in Japan, Aoi finished in 81st place in the overall balloting.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
