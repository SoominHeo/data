<p><b>Yi Seo-woo</b> (March 1, 1633 – October 14, 1709) was a <a href="/wiki/Korea" title="Korea">Korean</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Joseon_Dynasty" title="Joseon Dynasty">Joseon Dynasty</a> politician, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Neo-Confucian" title="Neo-Confucian">Neo-Confucian</a> scholar, and early <a href="/wiki/Silhak" title="Silhak">silhak</a> writer. He was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Southerners_(Korean_political_faction)" title="Southerners (Korean political faction)">Southerners</a> <a href="/wiki/Political_factions_in_Joseon_Dynasty" title="Political factions in Joseon Dynasty">political faction</a>.</p>
