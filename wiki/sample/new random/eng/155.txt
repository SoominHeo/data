<p><b>Risako Itō</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">伊藤 梨沙子</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Itō Risako</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born February 20, 1996 in <a href="/wiki/Tokyo" title="Tokyo">Tokyo</a>)</span><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-profile_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-profile-1">[1]</a></sup> is a Japanese actress, fashion model, and gravure model.</p>
<p>Itō was once represented with Ever Green Entertainment.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Risako_It%C5%8D&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>By the time she was in fourth grade, Itō started working as magazine model.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> In 2005 she joined Ever Green Entertainment.</li>
<li>Itō made regular appearances in "Oha Girl" of <i><a href="/wiki/Oha_Suta" title="Oha Suta">Oha Suta</a></i> along with <a href="/wiki/Mayu_Matsuoka" title="Mayu Matsuoka">Mayu Matsuoka</a> and Hidemi Hikita from the 7 April 2008 until the 26 March 2010.</li>
<li>In November 2011 she was chosen as the Miss Weekly Young Jump<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[a]</a></sup> of Gravure Japan co-hosted by <i><a href="/wiki/Weekly_Young_Jump" title="Weekly Young Jump">Weekly Young Jump</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Weekly_Playboy" title="Weekly Playboy">Weekly Playboy</a></i>.</li>
<li>Itō left Ever Green Entertainment in April 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[4]</a></sup></li>
<li>In June 2016 she later joined the talent agency Cube.</li>
</ul>
