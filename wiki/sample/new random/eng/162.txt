<p><br/>
<b>Korea National Oil Corporation (KNOC)</b> is the national oil and gas company of <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a> and one of the most important industrial companies in the country. The company operates <a href="/wiki/Oil" title="Oil">oil</a> and <a href="/wiki/Natural_gas" title="Natural gas">gas</a> fields in <a href="/wiki/Vietnam" title="Vietnam">Vietnam</a>, <a href="/wiki/Libya" title="Libya">Libya</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peru" title="Peru">Peru</a>, <a href="/wiki/Indonesia" title="Indonesia">Indonesia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nigeria" title="Nigeria">Nigeria</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yemen" title="Yemen">Yemen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kazakhstan" title="Kazakhstan">Kazakhstan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a> and <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>.</p>
<p>The company has <a href="/wiki/Oil" title="Oil">oil</a> reserves of around 600 million barrels (95,000,000 m<sup>3</sup>) <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Harvest_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Harvest-3">[3]</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Natural_gas" title="Natural gas">gas</a> reserves of 10 billion m<sup>3</sup>.</p>
<p>On 26 September 2008 KNOC signed a deal with the <a href="/wiki/Kurdistan_Regional_Government" title="Kurdistan Regional Government">Kurdistan Regional Government</a> where KNOC was granted rights to explore and drill on several sites in the Iraqi Kurdistan in exchange for valuable support on a range of infrastructure projects.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2009, the company purchased <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Harvest_Energy_Trust" title="Harvest Energy Trust">Harvest Energy Trust</a>, along with its subsidiary <a href="/wiki/North_Atlantic_Refining" title="North Atlantic Refining">North Atlantic Refining</a> and its <a href="/wiki/Come_By_Chance_Refinery" title="Come By Chance Refinery">refinery</a> in <a href="/wiki/Come_By_Chance,_Newfoundland_and_Labrador" title="Come By Chance, Newfoundland and Labrador">Come By Chance, Newfoundland and Labrador</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Harvest_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-Harvest-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 2 July 2010 KNOC confirmed preliminary discussions regarding a possible cash offer for the entire issued and to-be-issued share capital of <a href="/wiki/Dana_Petroleum" title="Dana Petroleum">Dana Petroleum</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-upstream020710_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-upstream020710-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
