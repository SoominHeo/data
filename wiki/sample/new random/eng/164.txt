<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Padam_Padam..._The_Sound_of_His_and_Her_Heartbeats&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A convict named Yang Kang-chil (<a href="/wiki/Jung_Woo-sung" title="Jung Woo-sung">Jung Woo-sung</a>) serves 16 years in prison for a murder he didn't commit. His innocent nature is protected by his best friend and "guardian angel" Gook-soo (<a href="/wiki/Kim_Bum" title="Kim Bum">Kim Bum</a>). Prior to his release he experiences a near-death event and only survives thanks to a miracle. Gook-soo explains that Kang-chil will have three near-death miracles and he will only survive them if he learns from the experience.</p>
<p>After his release, Kang-chil becomes a carpenter and falls in love with Jang Ji-na (<a href="/wiki/Han_Ji-min" title="Han Ji-min">Han Ji-min</a>) a veterinarian described as a "selfish woman." Despite her selfishness, Ji-na is irrevocably drawn to Kang-chil and falls deeply in love with him. However, things take a turn for the worse when it is revealed that Ji-na's uncle was the victim in Kang-chil's murder case. In order to prove his innocence Kang-chil desperately seeks evidence that will bring the real murderer, Park Chan-gul (<a href="/wiki/Jun-seong_Kim" title="Jun-seong Kim">Jun-seong Kim</a>), to justice and also save his relationship with Ji-na.</p>
