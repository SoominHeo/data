<p><b>Ryan McLaughlin</b> (born 30 September 1994) is a Northern Irish <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays for <a href="/wiki/Oldham_Athletic_A.F.C." title="Oldham Athletic A.F.C.">Oldham Athletic</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">defender</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Northern_Ireland_national_football_team" title="Northern Ireland national football team">Northern Ireland national football team</a>. He is the younger brother of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fleetwood_Town" title="Fleetwood Town">Fleetwood Town</a> right back, <a href="/wiki/Conor_McLaughlin" title="Conor McLaughlin">Conor McLaughlin</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryan_McLaughlin&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Liverpool">Liverpool</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryan_McLaughlin&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Liverpool">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Liverpool signed 16-year-old Ryan McLaughlin in the summer of 2011 from <a href="/wiki/Glenavon_F.C." title="Glenavon F.C.">Glenavon</a> on a three-year deal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 9 January 2014, McLaughlin was signed by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_League_Championship" title="Football League Championship">Football League Championship</a> side <a href="/wiki/Barnsley_F.C." title="Barnsley F.C.">Barnsley</a> on a 28-day loan deal and was given squad number 27.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> He made his club and senior career debut on 18 January against <a href="/wiki/Blackpool_F.C." title="Blackpool F.C.">Blackpool</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_League_Championship" title="Football League Championship">Championship</a>. On 29 January 2014 it was announced that McLaughlin sustained an injury that will keep him out for four weeks.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Two days later his loan was extended until the end of the season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>The only senior appearance McLaughlin made in the 2014–15 season was in an international friendly for <a href="/wiki/Northern_Ireland_national_football_team" title="Northern Ireland national football team">Northern Ireland</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aberdeen_loan_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-aberdeen_loan-6">[6]</a></sup> In August 2015, he was loaned to <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Premiership" title="Scottish Premiership">Scottish Premiership</a> club <a href="/wiki/Aberdeen_F.C." title="Aberdeen F.C.">Aberdeen</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aberdeen_loan_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-aberdeen_loan-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 10 June 2016, he was released from the club.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Oldham_Athletic">Oldham Athletic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryan_McLaughlin&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Oldham Athletic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 8 August 2016, McLaughlin signed a one-year deal with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_League_One" title="Football League One">League One</a> side <a href="/wiki/Oldham_Athletic_A.F.C." title="Oldham Athletic A.F.C.">Oldham Athletic</a>, with the option of a further year.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryan_McLaughlin&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>McLaughlin has represented Northern Ireland at Under-16, Under-17 and currently at Under-19 and Under-21 level. He was called up to the <a href="/wiki/Northern_Ireland_national_football_team" title="Northern Ireland national football team">Northern Ireland senior squad</a> for a friendly against <a href="/wiki/Finland_national_football_team" title="Finland national football team">Finland</a> on 15 August 2012 but was forced to withdraw due to a hip injury.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 19 May 2014, he was once again called up to the senior squad for two friendly matches in South America.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> On 31 May 2014, he made his National Team debut as a substitute against <a href="/wiki/Uruguay_national_football_team" title="Uruguay national football team">Uruguay</a>. McLaughlin became the first Liverpool player to appear for Northern Ireland since <a href="/wiki/Elisha_Scott" title="Elisha Scott">Elisha Scott</a> 78 years previously.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
