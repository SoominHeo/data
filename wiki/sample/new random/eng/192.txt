<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Anyang-dong</b> (안양동, 安養洞) is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Neighborhood" title="Neighborhood">neighborhood</a> of <a href="/wiki/Manan-gu" title="Manan-gu">Manan district</a> in the city of <a href="/wiki/Anyang,_Gyeonggi" title="Anyang, Gyeonggi">Anyang</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gyeonggi" title="Gyeonggi">Gyeonggi</a> Province, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. It is officially divided into Anyang-1-dong, Anyang-2-dong, Anyang-3-dong, Anyang-4-dong, Anyang-5-dong, Anyang-6-dong, Anyang-7-dong, Anyang-8-dong and Anyang-9-dong.</p>
