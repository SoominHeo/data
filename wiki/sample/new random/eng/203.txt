<p><b>Malminkartano railway station</b> (<a href="/wiki/Finnish_language" title="Finnish language">Finnish</a>: <span lang="fi" xml:lang="fi"><i>Malminkartanon rautatieasema</i></span>, <a href="/wiki/Swedish_language" title="Swedish language">Swedish</a>: <span lang="sv" xml:lang="sv"><i>Malmgårds järnvägsstation</i></span>) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Railway_station" title="Railway station">railway station</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Helsinki_commuter_rail" title="Helsinki commuter rail">Helsinki commuter rail</a> network located in northern <a href="/wiki/Helsinki" title="Helsinki">Helsinki</a>, <a href="/wiki/Finland" title="Finland">Finland</a>. It is located approximately eleven kilometres to the north of <a href="/wiki/Helsinki_Central_railway_station" title="Helsinki Central railway station">Helsinki Central railway station</a>.</p>
<p>The station is served by circular lines I and P, between the stations of <a href="/wiki/Kannelm%C3%A4ki_railway_station" title="Kannelmäki railway station">Kannelmäki</a> and <a href="/wiki/Myyrm%C3%A4ki_railway_station" title="Myyrmäki railway station">Myyrmäki</a>.</p>
<p>The station has two platforms, one for southbound and one for northbound trains, and is quite unique in that the majority of the station is situated inside a tunnel with trains stopping 220 metres (720 ft) inside it.</p>
