<p><b>Gokiso Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">御器所駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Gokiso-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is an underground <a href="/wiki/Metro_station" title="Metro station">metro station</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Sh%C5%8Dwa-ku,_Nagoya" title="Shōwa-ku, Nagoya">Shōwa-ku</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nagoya" title="Nagoya">Nagoya</a>, <a href="/wiki/Aichi_Prefecture" title="Aichi Prefecture">Aichi Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a> operated by the <a href="/wiki/Nagoya_Municipal_Subway" title="Nagoya Municipal Subway">Nagoya Municipal Subway</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Tsurumai_Line" title="Tsurumai Line">Tsurumai Line</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nagoya_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-nagoya-1">[1]</a></sup> The station is an <a href="/wiki/Interchange_station" title="Interchange station">interchange station</a> between the Tsurumai Line and the Sakura-dōri Line and is located 11.9 rail kilometers from the terminus of the Tsurumai Line at <a href="/wiki/Kami-Otai_Station" title="Kami-Otai Station">Kami-Otai Station</a> and 8.4 rail kilometers from the terminus of the Sakura-dōri Line at <a href="/wiki/Nakamura_Kuyakusho_Station" title="Nakamura Kuyakusho Station">Nakamura Kuyakusho Station</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gokiso_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Gokiso Station was opened on 18 March 1977.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-history_of_subways_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-history_of_subways-2">[2]</a></sup> The Sakura-dōri Line began operations to this station from 30 March 1994. <a href="/wiki/Platform_screen_doors" title="Platform screen doors">Platform screen doors</a> were installed on the Sakura-dōri Line platform from 28 May 2011.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Layout">Layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gokiso_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Gokiso Station has two underground opposed <a href="/wiki/Side_platform" title="Side platform">side platforms</a> for the Tsrumai Line and one underground <a href="/wiki/Island_platform" title="Island platform">island platform</a> for the Sakura-dōri Line.</p>
