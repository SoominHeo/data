<p><b>Imishli</b> (İmişli) is a <a href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_Azerbaijan" title="Administrative divisions of Azerbaijan">rayon</a> of <a href="/wiki/Azerbaijan" title="Azerbaijan">Azerbaijan</a>. Imishli and neighbouring <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sabirabad_Rayon" title="Sabirabad Rayon">Sabirabad</a> <a href="/wiki/Rayon" title="Rayon">rayon</a> share <a href="/wiki/Lake_Sarysu" title="Lake Sarysu">Sarysu</a>, the largest lake in Azerbaijan.</p>
<p>In August 1930, <a href="/wiki/Qaradonlu" title="Qaradonlu">Qaradonlu</a> was organised on Mil-Mugan historical fields as a region. Earlier, during the <a href="/wiki/Tsardom_of_Russia" title="Tsardom of Russia">Tsardom of Russia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Qaradonlu" title="Qaradonlu">Qaradonlu</a> belonged to the <a href="/wiki/Javad_Khan" title="Javad Khan">Javad Khan</a> administration. The <a href="/wiki/Qaradonlu" title="Qaradonlu">Qaradonlu</a> region is situated in a favourable area near the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Aras_River" title="Aras River">Aras River</a> providing water resources, productive land and caravan roads. As early as the <a href="/wiki/October_Revolution" title="October Revolution">October Revolution</a>, land-reclamation and irrigation work were made based on the intense irrigated plant-growing. In 1906, the first School of <a href="/wiki/Qaradonlu" title="Qaradonlu">Qaradonlu</a> region was established, with many living-buildings, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Caravanserais" title="Caravanserais">caravanserais</a>, mills and shops in the area. In 1933, <a href="/wiki/Qaradonlu" title="Qaradonlu">Qaradonlu</a> Machine-Tractor Plant was built in the <a href="/wiki/Qaradonlu" title="Qaradonlu">Qaradonlu</a> historical region. As a result of the construction of the railway between <a href="/wiki/%C6%8Fl%C9%99t" title="Ələt">Ələt</a> and <a href="/wiki/Yerevan" title="Yerevan">Yerevan</a>, people moved from the center of region to suburbs of the railway stations. The population increase motivated two-story brick buildings to be built among the <a href="/wiki/Qaradonlu" title="Qaradonlu">Qaradonlu</a> and Imishli villages, so the capital of the region was moved to here and the region was considered <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Imishli_Rayon" title="Imishli Rayon">Imishli Rayon</a>. Imishli village was rapidly expanded, much like the capital of the region. Government houses were constructed, completely transforming the overall appearance of the village. In 1944, Imishli was awarded status of town-type settlement, which progressed to city status in the 1960s. In 1959, on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Aras_River" title="Aras River">Aras River</a> in the <a href="/wiki/B%C9%99hramt%C9%99p%C9%99" title="Bəhramtəpə">Bəhramtəpə</a> area, water storage was used - which was unique to that time period. The Bash Mugam and Azizbaiov irrigation canals provided water not only to the land area of Imishli, but also to land areas of <a href="/wiki/Bilasuvar_District" title="Bilasuvar District">Bilasuvar District</a> and <a href="/wiki/Saatly_District" title="Saatly District">Saatly District</a>.</p>
