<p><i><b>Barefooted Friends</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko">맨발의 친구들</span>) was a <a href="/wiki/South_Korean" title="South Korean">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Reality_television" title="Reality television">reality</a>-<a href="/wiki/Variety_show" title="Variety show">variety show</a>; a part of <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Broadcasting_System" title="Seoul Broadcasting System">SBS</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Good_Sunday" title="Good Sunday">Good Sunday</a></i> lineup, along with <i><a href="/wiki/Running_Man_(TV_series)" title="Running Man (TV series)">Running Man</a></i>. It was first aired on April 21, 2013. The show is an "real outdoor hardship variety"; a spin on typical outdoor variety shows.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-manchin_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-manchin-1">[1]</a></sup> The members experience "real happiness" with others through challenges.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-manchin_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-manchin-1">[1]</a></sup> The words, <i>Barefooted</i> is supposed to mean essence, sincerity, and real hardships, where as <i>Friends</i> not only represents the cast, but the people that are met as well.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-concept_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-concept-2">[2]</a></sup> It has garnered attention as being the come-back program for <a href="/wiki/Kang_Ho-dong" title="Kang Ho-dong">Kang Ho-dong</a>, the main MC of the program, after leaving <i>Good Sunday'</i>s <i><a href="/wiki/X-Man_(TV_series)" title="X-Man (TV series)">X-man</a></i> in April 2007.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> After a seven-month run, the program was cancelled due to low ratings and to make room for <i><a href="/wiki/K-pop_Star_3" title="K-pop Star 3">K-pop Star 3</a></i>, with the final episode airing on November 17, 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Format">Format</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Barefooted_Friends&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Format">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Episode_1-6">Episode 1-6</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Barefooted_Friends&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Episode 1-6">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>A new life experience meeting new people in a new place, and exploring the cultural heritage and natural environment unseen elsewhere, the members search for real beauty and happiness on foreign soil. The members travel to foreign countries to experience "real happiness" with locals. Unlike typical vacations, they live like locals and experience what a day in their life is like. Members must provide for themselves as no support from staff is given. The members experience the essence of culture, food, music, nature, and lifestyle while surviving on their own in the foreign country. This program is a differentiated real variety program providing affection and enlightenment to viewers.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-concept_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-concept-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Episode_7-31">Episode 7-31</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Barefooted_Friends&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Episode 7-31">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The members take part in challenges based on different themes (diving, song writing, cooking, etc.).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Cast">Cast</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Barefooted_Friends&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Cast">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The original members of <i>Barefooted Friends</i> are <a href="/wiki/Kang_Ho-dong" title="Kang Ho-dong">Kang Ho-dong</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yoon_Jong-shin" title="Yoon Jong-shin">Yoon Jong-shin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yoo_Se-yoon" title="Yoo Se-yoon">Yoo Se-yoon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kim_Bum-soo" title="Kim Bum-soo">Kim Bum-soo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kim_Hyun-joong" title="Kim Hyun-joong">Kim Hyun-joong</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yoon_Shi-yoon" title="Yoon Shi-yoon">Yoon Si-yoon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Eunhyuk" title="Eunhyuk">Eunhyuk</a>, <a href="/wiki/Uee" title="Uee">Uee</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> Unlike the other members, this is the first time Kim Bum-soo, Kim Hyun-joong, Yoon Si-yoon, and Uee are participating in a variety show as regulars, and the synergy effect between the experienced variety seniors and rookies are anticipated.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> As of June 9, 2013, Yoo Se-yoon has left the program due to his <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/DUI" title="DUI">DUI</a> case, and will not appear from episode 10.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Eun_Ji-won" title="Eun Ji-won">Eun Ji-won</a> has replaced him. On June 11, 2013, Kim Bum-soo suffered an injury to his knee during the recording of the Diving Special and was transported to a hospital. He has since left the program.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> Eunhyuk has not appeared on the program since the diving special, and is presumed to have left the program. On October 31, 2013, it was announced that Kim Hyun-joong has completed his final filming of the program and will be leaving due to schedule conflicts, airing November 3.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
