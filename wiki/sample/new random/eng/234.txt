<p><b>Daegu Metro Line 2</b> is the second <a href="/wiki/Rapid_transit" title="Rapid transit">rapid transit</a> line in the <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> city of <a href="/wiki/Daegu" title="Daegu">Daegu</a>. It is operated by the <a href="/wiki/Daegu_Metropolitan_Transit_Corporation" title="Daegu Metropolitan Transit Corporation">Daegu Metropolitan Transit Corporation</a>.</p>
<p>The line first began running from Munyang to Sawol on October 18, 2005, a distance of 29 km. The line had been scheduled to open some years earlier, but fallout from the IMF crisis of the late 1990s caused delays in construction.</p>
<p>On September 19, 2012, the extension from Sawol to Yeongnam University opened.</p>
