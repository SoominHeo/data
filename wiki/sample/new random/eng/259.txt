<p><b>Gal Sone</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">ギャル曽根</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Gyaru Sone</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born <b>Natsuko Sone</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">曽根 菜津子</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Sone Natsuko</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> on December 4, 1985, in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Maizuru,_Kyoto" title="Maizuru, Kyoto">Maizuru</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kyoto_Prefecture" title="Kyoto Prefecture">Kyoto</a>)</span> is a female <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> <a href="/wiki/Competitive_eating" title="Competitive eating">competitive eater</a> and singer. Her popularity has helped produce a resurgence in Japanese competitive eating, or <i>ōgui</i>, after the outcry following the death of a Japanese schoolboy imitating an eating stunt in 2002<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> resulted in a three-year hiatus for major Japanese eating contests.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Contest_history">Contest history</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gal_Sone&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Contest history">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/TV_Tokyo" title="TV Tokyo">TV Tokyo</a>'s <a class="external text" href="http://www.tv-tokyo.co.jp/oogui2005" rel="nofollow">"Gluttonous King"</a> contest held in the fall of 2005, open to both men and women, Sone qualified for the finals and beat out Miyuki Iwata, then considered the leading Japanese female competitive eater after the legendary (and currently inactive) <a href="/wiki/Takako_Akasaka" title="Takako Akasaka">Takako Akasaka</a>, for <a class="external text" href="https://translate.google.com/translate?u=http%3A%2F%2Fplaza.rakuten.co.jp%2Fspillane%2Fdiary%2F200511100000%2F&amp;langpair=ja%7Cen&amp;hl=en&amp;ie=UTF8" rel="nofollow">sixth place</a> by just 6 grams of bread. TV Tokyo followed up its mixed gender contest with a <a class="external text" href="http://www.tv-tokyo.co.jp/oogui2006/" rel="nofollow">women's only contest</a> in the spring of 2006 in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Okinawa" title="Okinawa">Okinawa</a>. Sone easily won the event and had a comfortable enough lead in the final contest that she could afford to fix her makeup while the contest clock was running. Gal Sone improved to 3rd from 6th in the fall 2006 <a class="external text" href="http://www.tv-tokyo.co.jp/oogui2006_2/" rel="nofollow">"Gluttonous King"</a> contest, held in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hokkaid%C5%8D" title="Hokkaidō">Hokkaidō</a> and retained her crown in the spring 2007 <a class="external text" href="http://www.tv-tokyo.co.jp/oogui2007_4/" rel="nofollow">"Gluttonous Queen"</a> contest in <a href="/wiki/Hawaii" title="Hawaii">Hawaii</a>.</p>
<p>In addition to her "Gluttonous King/Queen" competitions, Sone has made numerous other television appearances, most notably a weekly Wednesday morning contest with <b>"The Giant" Nobuyuki Shirota</b>, runner-up in <a href="/wiki/Nathan%27s_Hot_Dog_Eating_Contest" title="Nathan's Hot Dog Eating Contest">Nathan's Hot Dog Eating Contest</a> in 2004. On July 11, 2006, Sone ate 6 kg of curry in 23 minutes (Shirota required 14 minutes) In another telecast, the "Gal" consumed 40,000 calories in several restaurants. In a recent program, Gal Sone attempted in a single meal to exceed the 15,000 calories that it is claimed that <a href="/wiki/Elvis_Presley" title="Elvis Presley">Elvis Presley</a> consumed every day near the end of his life.</p>
<p>When an <a href="/wiki/X-ray" title="X-ray">X-ray</a> was performed on her stomach, it appeared to be normal size, but its connection to the small intestine was larger than normal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-singtao_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-singtao-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Gal Sone has appeared in a commercial for Aoki's Pizza, who created a <a class="external text" href="http://www.aokispizza.co.jp/page/04/03lovemayo.html" rel="nofollow">mayonnaise pizza</a> in her honor. Mayonnaise sauce is her favourite sauce.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-singtao_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-singtao-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Music_career">Music career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gal_Sone&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Music career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sone appeared in two <a href="/wiki/Karaoke" title="Karaoke">karaoke</a> television programs in late 2006. On one show, she ate sushi quickly during an instrumental section of her song. On April 24, 2007, it was announced that Sone would form a singing group directed by <a href="/wiki/Tsunku" title="Tsunku">Tsunku</a> named <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Gyaruru&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Gyaruru (page does not exist)">Gyaruru</a> with <a href="/wiki/Nozomi_Tsuji" title="Nozomi Tsuji">Nozomi Tsuji</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ami_Tokito" title="Ami Tokito">Ami Tokito</a>. On May 14, 2007, after Tsuji's resignation from the band, <a href="/wiki/Asami_Abe" title="Asami Abe">Asami Abe</a> joined in her place. <a class="external text" href="http://www.tnx.cc/release/gal/20070620.htm" rel="nofollow">Their first single</a>, "<a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Boom_Boom_Meccha_Maccho!&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Boom Boom Meccha Maccho! (page does not exist)">Boom Boom Meccha Maccho!</a>" <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">Boom Boom めっちゃマッチョ!</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>boom boom very macho!</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>, was released on June 20, 2007 on Japanese independent label <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=TN-mix&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="TN-mix (page does not exist)">TN-mix</a> through <a href="/wiki/Pony_Canyon" title="Pony Canyon">Pony Canyon</a>. Sone's nickname in Gyaruru is Sonene.</p>
