<p><b>Arco di Travertino</b> is a station on the Rome Metro. It is on <a href="/wiki/Line_A_(Rome_Metro)" title="Line A (Rome Metro)">Line A</a> and is located in Rome's 9th district, between Colli Albani and Porta Furba stations.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
