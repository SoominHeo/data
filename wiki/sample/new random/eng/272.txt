<p><b>Henrique Adriano Buss</b> (born 14 October 1986), known as <b>Henrique</b> (<small>Brazilian Portuguese: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Portuguese" title="Help:IPA for Portuguese">[ẽˈʁiki]</a></span>), is a Brazilian professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays for <a href="/wiki/Fluminense_FC" title="Fluminense FC">Fluminense</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">defender</a>. He has played for the <a href="/wiki/Brazil_national_football_team" title="Brazil national football team">Brazil national team</a>, making his debut in 2008<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> and playing at the <a href="/wiki/2014_FIFA_World_Cup" title="2014 FIFA World Cup">2014 FIFA World Cup</a>.</p>
<p>His main asset is his versatility, as he can play as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)#Centre-back" title="Defender (association football)">central defender</a>, <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)#Full-back" title="Defender (association football)">right back</a> or <a href="/wiki/Midfielder#Defensive_midfielder" title="Midfielder">defensive midfielder</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Henrique_(footballer,_born_1986)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Coritiba_and_Palmeiras">Coritiba and Palmeiras</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Henrique_(footballer,_born_1986)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Coritiba and Palmeiras">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Henrique started his career at <a href="/wiki/Coritiba_Foot_Ball_Club" title="Coritiba Foot Ball Club">Coritiba</a> in his home region. In 2008, he was bought by Palmeiras in a deal partially financed by <a href="/wiki/Traffic_Group" title="Traffic Group">Traffic Group</a>. Part of the later transfer fee of Henrique from Barcelona was credited to <a href="/wiki/Desportivo_Brasil" title="Desportivo Brasil">Desportivo Brasil</a>, the subsidiary of Traffic<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> and re-distributed by Desportivo to Palmeiras.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Barcelona">Barcelona</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Henrique_(footballer,_born_1986)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Barcelona">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Dutch giants <a href="/wiki/AFC_Ajax" title="AFC Ajax">Ajax</a> were said to have tabled a bid for Henrique in June 2008,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ajax_.27offer_eight_million_euros.27_for_Henrique_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-Ajax_.27offer_eight_million_euros.27_for_Henrique-6">[6]</a></sup> which was soon followed by a report saying he had agreed terms with <a href="/wiki/FC_Barcelona" title="FC Barcelona">Barcelona</a>. In the end, Barcelona had secured Henrique's services from Palmeiras for €8 million, with an extra €2 million in bonuses. He signed a five-year deal and was to be immediately loaned out to German <a href="/wiki/Bundesliga" title="Bundesliga">Bundesliga</a> side <a href="/wiki/Bayer_04_Leverkusen" title="Bayer 04 Leverkusen">Bayer Leverkusen</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Henrique_arrives_today_in_Barcelona_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-Henrique_arrives_today_in_Barcelona-7">[7]</a></sup> The transfer to Barça was officially completed a day later.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Henrique_goes_from_Bar.C3.A7a_to_Bayer_Leverkusen_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-Henrique_goes_from_Bar.C3.A7a_to_Bayer_Leverkusen-8">[8]</a></sup> Henrique made 27 league appearances in his season with Leverkusen and reached the final of the <a href="/wiki/2008%E2%80%9309_DFB-Pokal" title="2008–09 DFB-Pokal">2008–09 DFB-Pokal</a>. He returned to Barcelona in June 2009, being given the number 23 shirt upon his return. On 31 August 2009, he was again loaned out, this time to <a href="/wiki/Racing_de_Santander" title="Racing de Santander">Racing de Santander</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> The loan was renewed for a second year on 26 July 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p>Barcelona wrote-down €1.757 million<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> for the residual contract value of Henrique for the <a href="/wiki/2010%E2%80%9311_FC_Barcelona_season" title="2010–11 FC Barcelona season">2010–11 season</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Palmeiras">Palmeiras</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Henrique_(footballer,_born_1986)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Palmeiras">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 15 July 2011, Henrique returned to Brazil on another loan deal from Barcelona.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup> During the <a href="/wiki/2012_Copa_do_Brasil" title="2012 Copa do Brasil">2012 Copa do Brasil</a> campaign, coach <a href="/wiki/Luiz_Felipe_Scolari" title="Luiz Felipe Scolari">Luiz Felipe Scolari</a> started playing Henrique as a holding midfielder and the player thrived in the new role, with Palmeiras eventually winning the competition.</p>
<p>On 2 June 2012, Barcelona cancelled Henrique's contract, prompting his release from the club.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup> He then joined Palmeiras on a free transfer after a successful loan spell in 2011–12.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup> Palmeiras, however, remained the holder of 80% of Henrique's economic rights, with 20% of any future transfer fee to be paid to unknown parties.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup> <span class="citation-needed-content" style="padding-left:0.1em; padding-right:0.1em; color:#595959; border:1px solid #DDD;">Henrique also became second captain of the squad, behind dead-ball specialist <a href="/wiki/Marcos_Assun%C3%A7%C3%A3o" title="Marcos Assunção">Marcos Assunção</a> in the pecking order.</span><sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="margin-left:0.1em; white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (January 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Napoli">Napoli</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Henrique_(footballer,_born_1986)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Napoli">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 30 January 2014, Henrique officially signed with Italian <a href="/wiki/Serie_A" title="Serie A">Serie A</a> club <a href="/wiki/S.S.C._Napoli" title="S.S.C. Napoli">Napoli</a>. The deal was completed for <span class="citation-needed-content" style="padding-left:0.1em; padding-right:0.1em; color:#595959; border:1px solid #DDD;">€4 million</span><sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="margin-left:0.1em; white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (January 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> with the player agreeing to a three-and-a-half-year contract.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup> He played all 90 minutes of their 3–1 victory over <a href="/wiki/ACF_Fiorentina" title="ACF Fiorentina">Fiorentina</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2014_Coppa_Italia_Final" title="2014 Coppa Italia Final">2014 Coppa Italia Final</a> on 3 May.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Fluminense">Fluminense</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Henrique_(footballer,_born_1986)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Fluminense">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 11 January 2016, Henrique joined <a href="/wiki/Fluminense_FC" title="Fluminense FC">Fluminense</a> on a three-year contract.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Henrique_(footballer,_born_1986)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On 21 March 2008, Henrique received his first call up to the <a href="/wiki/Brazil_national_football_team" title="Brazil national football team">Brazil national team</a> after centre back <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Juan_Silveira_dos_Santos" title="Juan Silveira dos Santos">Juan</a> was cut from the squad due to an injury to his ankle.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Dunga_convoca_Henrique.2C_do_Palmeiras.2C_para_o_lugar_de_Juan_20-0"><a href="#cite_note-Dunga_convoca_Henrique.2C_do_Palmeiras.2C_para_o_lugar_de_Juan-20">[20]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 16 April 2013, Henrique was called up by <a href="/wiki/Luiz_Felipe_Scolari" title="Luiz Felipe Scolari">Luiz Felipe Scolari</a>, his former coach at Palmeiras, for a friendly against <a href="/wiki/Chile_national_football_team" title="Chile national football team">Chile</a> on 24 April at the <a href="/wiki/Mineir%C3%A3o" title="Mineirão">Mineirão</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-21"><a href="#cite_note-21">[21]</a></sup> But on 19 April, as Palmeiras would play against <a href="/wiki/Club_Tijuana" title="Club Tijuana">Tijuana</a> the next day, Henrique was cut from the list, and <a href="/wiki/Rodrigo_Moledo" title="Rodrigo Moledo">Rodrigo Moledo</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sport_Club_Internacional" title="Sport Club Internacional">Internacional</a> was called up in his place.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-22"><a href="#cite_note-22">[22]</a></sup> On the same day, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Conmebol" title="Conmebol">Conmebol</a> had moved the Palmeiras game and Henrique was recalled, but Moledo was nonetheless kept on the list.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gazetaesportiva.net_1_23-0"><a href="#cite_note-gazetaesportiva.net_1-23">[23]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 7 May 2014, Henrique was selected as part of the <a href="/wiki/2014_FIFA_World_Cup_squads#Brazil" title="2014 FIFA World Cup squads">Brazil squad</a> for the upcoming <a href="/wiki/2014_FIFA_World_Cup" title="2014 FIFA World Cup">World Cup</a>, to be played on home soil.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-24"><a href="#cite_note-24">[24]</a></sup> His sole appearance at the tournament was the final two minutes of the quarter-final victory over <a href="/wiki/Colombia_national_football_team" title="Colombia national football team">Colombia</a>, replacing the injured forward <a href="/wiki/Neymar" title="Neymar">Neymar</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-25"><a href="#cite_note-25">[25]</a></sup> as Brazil eventually finished fourth.</p>
