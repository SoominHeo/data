<p><b>Oubritenga</b> is one of the 45 <a href="/wiki/Provinces_of_Burkina_Faso" title="Provinces of Burkina Faso">provinces</a> of <a href="/wiki/Burkina_Faso" title="Burkina Faso">Burkina Faso</a>, located in its <a href="/wiki/Plateau-Central_Region" title="Plateau-Central Region">Plateau-Central Region</a>.</p>
<p>Its capital is <a href="/wiki/Ziniar%C3%A9" title="Ziniaré">Ziniaré</a>.</p>
