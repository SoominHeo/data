<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Aurelios Zopyros</h1>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Aurelios Zopyros</b> of Athens is the last reported athlete at the <a href="/wiki/Ancient_Olympic_Games" title="Ancient Olympic Games">Ancient Olympic Games</a> before the banning by <a href="/wiki/Theodosius_I" title="Theodosius I">Theodosius I</a> in AD393. He was victor in "junior boxing" in 385. His brother Eukarpidês was also an Olympic <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pankratiast" title="Pankratiast">pankratiast</a> winner in 381.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
