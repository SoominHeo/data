<p><b>Lu'an</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%85%AD" title="wikt:六">六</a><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%AE%89" title="wikt:安">安</a></span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Lù'ān</span></i>), is a <a href="/wiki/Prefecture-level_city" title="Prefecture-level city">prefecture-level city</a> in western <a href="/wiki/Anhui" title="Anhui">Anhui</a> province, People's Republic of China, bordering <a href="/wiki/Henan" title="Henan">Henan</a> to the northwest and <a href="/wiki/Hubei" title="Hubei">Hubei</a> to the southwest. At the 2010 census, it had a total population of 5,612,590, whom 1,644,344 resided in the built-up area made of 2 urban districts. Neighbouring prefecture-level cities are the provincial capital of <a href="/wiki/Hefei" title="Hefei">Hefei</a> to the east, <a href="/wiki/Anqing" title="Anqing">Anqing</a> to the south, <a href="/wiki/Huanggang" title="Huanggang">Huanggang</a> (Hubei) and <a href="/wiki/Xinyang" title="Xinyang">Xinyang</a> (Henan) to the west, and <a href="/wiki/Huainan" title="Huainan">Huainan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fuyang" title="Fuyang">Fuyang</a> to the north. Although the character "<a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%85%AD" title="wikt:六">六</a>" (literally: "six") is normally pronounced "Liù", in this case it changes to "Lù" on account of the local dialect.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography_and_climate">Geography and climate</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lu%27an&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography and climate">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Lu'an is marked by the southern fringes of the <a href="/wiki/North_China_Plain" title="North China Plain">North China Plain</a> in its north and the northern part of the <a href="/wiki/Dabie_Mountains" title="Dabie Mountains">Dabie Mountains</a> in its south. Its administrative area spans 31°01′−32°40′ N latitude and 115°20′−117°14′ E longitude.</p>
<p>Lu'an has a <a href="/wiki/Monsoon" title="Monsoon">monsoon</a>-influenced, <a href="/wiki/Humid_subtropical_climate" title="Humid subtropical climate">humid subtropical climate</a> (<a href="/wiki/K%C3%B6ppen_climate_classification" title="Köppen climate classification">Köppen</a> <i>Cfa</i>), with four distinct seasons. Winters are cold and damp, with average low temperatures in January dipping just below freezing; the January 24-hour average temperature is 2.6 °C (36.7 °F). Summers are typically hot and humid, with a July average of 27.8 °C (82.0 °F). The annual mean is 15.68 °C (60.2 °F), while annual precipitation averages just above 1,100 millimetres (43 in), a majority of which occurs from May to August. Annual sunshine duration is 2,000 to 2,300 hours.</p>
