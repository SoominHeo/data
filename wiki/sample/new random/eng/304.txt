<p>The <b>1961 Copa de Campeones de América</b> was the second season of the competition, <a href="/wiki/South_America" title="South America">South America</a>'s prized <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> tournament. Nine teams entered, two more than the previous season, with <a href="/wiki/Venezuela" title="Venezuela">Venezuela</a> not sending a representative. In order to further enhance the competition, <a href="/wiki/CONMEBOL" title="CONMEBOL">CONMEBOL</a> maintained the criteria of having regional clashes in order to take advantage of the cross-border rivalries</p>
<p>Although <a href="/wiki/Pe%C3%B1arol" title="Peñarol">Peñarol</a> also won their national league, this became the first and, so far, only time the defending champions did not qualify automatically to the next edition. Nevertheless, the <i>Manyas</i> successfully defended their title by defeating <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Palmeiras" title="Palmeiras">Palmeiras</a> in the final.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Format">Format</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1961_Copa_Libertadores&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Format">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Each match-up was a two-team group stage. Because of the increase in participants, a preliminary group was implemented in the competition, with the rest following the previous season's format. Wins were awarded two points, 1 point for a draw, and no points for a loss. The team with the most points after a home and away game advanced to the next stage.</p>
<p>If the teams still remained tied, goal difference will become a factor. A <a href="/wiki/One-game_playoff" title="One-game playoff">one-game playoff</a> would be implemented in case the teams are still tied. A draw of lots was to become the last solution to breaking a tie. Controversially, Independiente Santa Fe and Jorge Wilstermann tied on points which would have required a playoff; CONMEBOL, however, scraped the idea and decided that the last semifinal slot was to be given by a draw of lots which the Colombians won.</p>
