<p><b>Roppongi Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">六本木駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Roppongi-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a subway station in <a href="/wiki/Minato,_Tokyo" title="Minato, Tokyo">Minato, Tokyo</a>, Japan, operated jointly by <a href="/wiki/Tokyo_Metro" title="Tokyo Metro">Tokyo Metro</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Toei_Subway" title="Toei Subway">Toei Subway</a>.</p>

<p>The Toei Oedo Line platform 1 is 42 m underground, making this station the deepest of the Tokyo subway stations. (The Toei Oedo Line platform 2 is 32 m underground.)</p>
