<p><b>Santa Lucía</b> is a city in the state of <a href="/wiki/Miranda_(state)" title="Miranda (state)">Miranda</a>, <a href="/wiki/Venezuela" title="Venezuela">Venezuela</a>. It is the capital of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Paz_Castillo_Municipality,_Miranda" title="Paz Castillo Municipality, Miranda">Paz Castillo Municipality</a>.</p>
