<p>The <b>Seohaean Expressway</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: 서해안 고속도로; Seohaean Gosok Doro), meaning "West Coast Expressway", is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Freeway" title="Freeway">freeway</a> in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>, connecting <a href="/wiki/Mokpo" title="Mokpo">Mokpo</a> to <a href="/wiki/Gunsan" title="Gunsan">Gunsan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dangjin" title="Dangjin">Dangjin</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a>.</p>
<p>It is numbered 15. The entire length from Seoul to Mokpo is 345 km and the posted speed limit is 110 km/h, enforced primarily by speed cameras. It is connected of <a href="/wiki/Seohae_Bridge" title="Seohae Bridge">Seohae Grand Bridge</a> in Pyeongtaek to Dangjin.</p>
<p>The Branch Expressways of Seohaean Expressway are <a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Korea_Expressway_No.151.svg"><img alt="Korea Expressway No.151.svg" data-file-height="500" data-file-width="500" height="20" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/89/Korea_Expressway_No.151.svg/20px-Korea_Expressway_No.151.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/89/Korea_Expressway_No.151.svg/30px-Korea_Expressway_No.151.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/89/Korea_Expressway_No.151.svg/40px-Korea_Expressway_No.151.svg.png 2x" width="20"/></a> <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Seocheon-Gongju_Expressway&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Seocheon-Gongju Expressway (page does not exist)">Seocheon-Gongju Expressway</a> and <a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Korea_Expressway_No.153.svg"><img alt="Korea Expressway No.153.svg" data-file-height="500" data-file-width="500" height="20" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5e/Korea_Expressway_No.153.svg/20px-Korea_Expressway_No.153.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5e/Korea_Expressway_No.153.svg/30px-Korea_Expressway_No.153.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5e/Korea_Expressway_No.153.svg/40px-Korea_Expressway_No.153.svg.png 2x" width="20"/></a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pyeongtaek-Siheung_Expressway" title="Pyeongtaek-Siheung Expressway">Pyeongtaek-Siheung Expressway</a>.</p>
<p>Typical facilities of this expressway is <a href="/wiki/Seohae_Bridge" title="Seohae Bridge">Seohae Bridge</a>(<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: 서해대교; Seohae Daegyo) which the total length of 7.3km linking the <a href="/wiki/Pyeongtaek" title="Pyeongtaek">Pyeongtaek</a>(<a href="/wiki/Gyeonggi_Province" title="Gyeonggi Province">Gyeonggi Province</a>) and <a href="/wiki/Dangjin" title="Dangjin">Dangjin</a>(<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chungnam_Province" title="Chungnam Province">Chungnam Province</a>). The bridge is the third long bridge in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Republic_of_Korea" title="Republic of Korea">Republic of Korea</a>.</p>
<p>The highway is a major highway linking the Seohaean Region(서해안권) for <b>the 1st North-South axis</b>(남북 제1축), the road through the <a href="/wiki/South_Jeolla_Province" title="South Jeolla Province">South Jeolla Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/North_Jeolla_Province" title="North Jeolla Province">North Jeolla Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Chungcheong_Province" title="South Chungcheong Province">South Chungcheong Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gyeonggi_Province" title="Gyeonggi Province">Gyeonggi Province</a>.</p>
