<p><b>Van Darkholme</b> (born October 24, 1972) is an American <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Performance_artist" title="Performance artist">performance artist</a>, <a href="/wiki/Film_director" title="Film director">director</a>, as well as a photographer.</p>
<p>Darkholme is among the few Asian American men working in Western gay porn as a director and actor, and is of <a href="/wiki/Vietnamese_people" title="Vietnamese people">Vietnamese</a> descent.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Much of his work as a director and actor contains <a href="/wiki/Bondage_(BDSM)" title="Bondage (BDSM)">bondage</a> and particularly <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Shibari" title="Shibari">shibari</a>, the Japanese art of rope bondage, a specialty within <a href="/wiki/BDSM" title="BDSM">BDSM</a> cultures. Van Darkholme is a screen name; his birth name has been kept private. His Japanese fans call him "Van Darkholme TDN".</p>
<p>Throughout the 1990s, Darkholme was a fashion and photographic <a href="/wiki/Model_(person)" title="Model (person)">model</a>. It is notable that Van was filming non-pornographic movies at first, some of his appearances include minor characters in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hong_Kong_action_movies" title="Hong Kong action movies">Hong Kong action movies</a> <i><a href="/wiki/The_Adventurers_(1995_film)" title="The Adventurers (1995 film)">The Adventurers</a></i> (with <a href="/wiki/Andy_Lau" title="Andy Lau">Andy Lau</a>) and <i><a href="/wiki/Who_Am_I%3F_(1998_film)" title="Who Am I? (1998 film)">Who Am I?</a></i> (with <a href="/wiki/Jackie_Chan" title="Jackie Chan">Jackie Chan</a>). Darkholme identifies as gay.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
