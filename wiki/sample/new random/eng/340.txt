<p><b>Ripabottoni</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Campobasso" title="Province of Campobasso">Province of Campobasso</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Molise" title="Molise">Molise</a>, located about 20 kilometres (12 mi) northeast of <a href="/wiki/Campobasso" title="Campobasso">Campobasso</a>. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 644 and an area of 31.9 square kilometres (12.3 sq mi).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-istat_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-istat-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Ripabottoni borders the following municipalities: <a href="/wiki/Bonefro" title="Bonefro">Bonefro</a>, <a href="/wiki/Campolieto" title="Campolieto">Campolieto</a>, <a href="/wiki/Casacalenda" title="Casacalenda">Casacalenda</a>, <a href="/wiki/Monacilioni" title="Monacilioni">Monacilioni</a>, <a href="/wiki/Morrone_del_Sannio" title="Morrone del Sannio">Morrone del Sannio</a>, <a href="/wiki/Provvidenti" title="Provvidenti">Provvidenti</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sant%27Elia_a_Pianisi" title="Sant'Elia a Pianisi">Sant'Elia a Pianisi</a>.</p>
