<p><b>Andrés Velencoso Segura</b> (born 11 March 1978) is a Spanish model. He is best known for ad campaigns such as <a href="/wiki/Chanel" title="Chanel">Chanel Allure Homme</a>, Sport fragrance and <a href="/wiki/Louis_Vuitton" title="Louis Vuitton">Louis Vuitton</a> campaign with <a href="/wiki/Jennifer_Lopez" title="Jennifer Lopez">J-Lo</a> in 2003.</p>
<p>In June 2009, he was ranked #6 in MODELS.com's "<i>Top Icons Men</i>", alongside male models <a href="/wiki/Mathias_Lauridsen" title="Mathias Lauridsen">Mathias Lauridsen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tyson_Ballou" title="Tyson Ballou">Tyson Ballou</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mark_Vanderloo" title="Mark Vanderloo">Mark Vanderloo</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and currently he ranks No.16 on Top 25 list "<i>The Money Guys</i>" following his compatriots <a href="/wiki/Oriol_Elcacho" title="Oriol Elcacho">Oriol Elcacho</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jon_Kortajarena" title="Jon Kortajarena">Jon Kortajarena</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Andr%C3%A9s_Velencoso&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Andrés Velencoso was born in <a href="/wiki/Tossa_de_Mar" title="Tossa de Mar">Tossa de Mar</a>, Catalonia, Spain.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-CoverMen_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-CoverMen-3">[3]</a></sup> His father is a restaurant owner and his mother, a homemaker, died in 2002. He has two younger sisters named Silvia and Sonia.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-CoverMen_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-CoverMen-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Andr%C3%A9s_Velencoso&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Velencoso started his modelling career after he finished high school, signing a contract with Group Model Management, a Spanish <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Modelling_agency" title="Modelling agency">modelling agency</a>. But the beginning of his career was filled with rejections, a buzzed haircut and a muscular body did not seem to be the ideal image that the European market demands. When Velencoso went to <a href="/wiki/Milan" title="Milan">Milan</a>, almost every agent thought that he was too big and goofy. His fortunes changed when Natalie Kates, an agent from New York City-based Q Model Management opened the door for him in 2001, despite his inability to speak English. Kates started him off fresh with four new polariods in his book and sent him to meet people. One of the people was photographer Matt Albiani, who loved Andres for an editorial that he was working on. The next day Andres found himself on the set of a shoot in <a href="/wiki/East_Hampton_(village),_New_York" title="East Hampton (village), New York">East Hampton, New York</a>. After just a few days in the city, he caught the eyes of some of the industry's most famous photographers, including <a href="/wiki/Michael_Thompson_(photographer)" title="Michael Thompson (photographer)">Michael Thompson</a> and Francois Nars.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> In 2002 he booked the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Banana_Republic_(clothing_retailer)" title="Banana Republic (clothing retailer)">Banana Republic</a> spring ad campaign and a year later stars in the <a href="/wiki/Louis_Vuitton" title="Louis Vuitton">Louis Vuitton</a> ad campaign, alongside <a href="/wiki/Jennifer_Lopez" title="Jennifer Lopez">Jennifer Lopez</a>. He signed a fragrance contract with <i><a href="/wiki/Chanel" title="Chanel">Chanel Allure Homme Sport</a></i> and became the face of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Loewe,_S.A." title="Loewe, S.A.">Loewe</a> in 2004. Landed his first magazine cover for <i><a href="/wiki/Arena_Homme_%2B" title="Arena Homme +">Arena Homme +</a></i> for the winter 2005 edition and appeared in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jean_Paul_Gaultier" title="Jean Paul Gaultier">Jean Paul Gaultier</a> ad campaign, with <a href="/wiki/Gisele_B%C3%BCndchen" title="Gisele Bündchen">Gisele Bündchen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Diana_Dondoe" title="Diana Dondoe">Diana Dondoe</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NYmag_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-NYmag-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>Velencoso has been on the covers of many top fashion magazines including <i><a href="/wiki/L%27Officiel_Hommes" title="L'Officiel Hommes">L'Officiel Hommes</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Hercules" title="Hercules">Hercules</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Arena_Homme_%2B" title="Arena Homme +">Arena Homme +</a></i>, and international editions of <i><a href="/wiki/Elle_(magazine)" title="Elle (magazine)">Elle</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Vanity_Fair_(magazine)" title="Vanity Fair (magazine)">Vanity Fair</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/L%27Officiel_Hommes" title="L'Officiel Hommes">L'Officiel Hommes</a></i>. Since his debut, he has been the face of many advertising campaigns including <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Banana_Republic_(clothing_retailer)" title="Banana Republic (clothing retailer)">Banana Republic</a>, <a href="/wiki/Louis_Vuitton" title="Louis Vuitton">Louis Vuitton</a>, <a href="/wiki/H%26M" title="H&amp;M">H&amp;M</a>, <a href="/wiki/Trussardi" title="Trussardi">Trussardi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chanel" title="Chanel">Chanel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ermenegildo_Zegna" title="Ermenegildo Zegna">Ermenegildo Zegna</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Loewe,_S.A." title="Loewe, S.A.">Loewe</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jean_Paul_Gaultier" title="Jean Paul Gaultier">Jean Paul Gaultier</a>, <a href="/wiki/Elie_Tahari" title="Elie Tahari">Elie Tahari</a> and <a href="/wiki/Etro" title="Etro">Etro</a>. Apart from Thompson and Nars, he has worked with acclaimed photographers such as <a href="/wiki/Terry_Richardson" title="Terry Richardson">Terry Richardson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Karl_Lagerfeld" title="Karl Lagerfeld">Karl Lagerfeld</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Inez_van_Lamsweerde" title="Inez van Lamsweerde">Inez van Lamsweerde</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vinoodh_Matadin" title="Vinoodh Matadin">Vinoodh Matadin</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NYmag_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-NYmag-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>He is signed with <a href="/wiki/IMG_Models" title="IMG Models">IMG Models</a> in New York City, <a href="/wiki/Marilyn_Agency" title="Marilyn Agency">MGM</a> in Paris, 2pm Model Management in <a href="/wiki/Copenhagen" title="Copenhagen">Copenhagen</a>, I LOVE Models in <a href="/wiki/Milan" title="Milan">Milan</a> and Donna Models in Tokyo.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> He was featured in <a href="/wiki/Kylie_Minogue" title="Kylie Minogue">Kylie Minogue</a>'s new frangrance 'Inverse' was in the official print campaign and TV ad.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> Velencoso received the 'Tossenc D'Honor' award in his home town Tossa de Mar on 14 May 2010 in <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Girona" title="Province of Girona">Girona</a>, Spain.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Andr%C3%A9s_Velencoso&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Velencoso began dating Australian pop singer <a href="/wiki/Kylie_Minogue" title="Kylie Minogue">Kylie Minogue</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> in July 2008.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> He bears his mother's name <i>Lucia</i> tattooed on the left side of his chest.In April 2009, it was reported he got inked with the singer's name <i>Kylie</i> just below his hip, a sign of his devotion to her.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> The couple broke up in October 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
<p>English is his fourth language, after Spanish, French and Italian.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup></p>
