<p>The <b>men's 200 metre freestyle</b> was a sprint <a href="/wiki/Swimming_(sport)" title="Swimming (sport)">swimming</a> event in the <a href="/wiki/Swimming_at_the_1900_Summer_Olympics" title="Swimming at the 1900 Summer Olympics">Swimming at the 1900 Summer Olympics</a> program in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>. It was the shortest of the three freestyle events. It was held on 11 August and 12 August 1900. 26 swimmers from 10 nations competed.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SportsRef_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-SportsRef-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
