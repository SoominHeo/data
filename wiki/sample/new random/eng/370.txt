<p><b>MBC Game</b> (MBC 게임, 엠비씨 게임) was a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Specialty_channel" title="Specialty channel">specialty television channel</a> owned by <a href="/wiki/MBC_Plus_Media" title="MBC Plus Media">MBC Plus Media</a>. The channel primarily broadcast programming related to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Video_games" title="Video games">video games</a>, but it, along with its competitor <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ongamenet" title="Ongamenet">Ongamenet</a>, was well known for its extensive coverage of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Competitive_video_gaming" title="Competitive video gaming">competitive video gaming</a></p>
<p>The channel was discontinued on January 31, 2012, and replaced by a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Music_channel" title="Music channel">music channel</a>, <a href="/wiki/MBC_Music" title="MBC Music">MBC Music</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
