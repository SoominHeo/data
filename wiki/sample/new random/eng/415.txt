<p><b>.mp</b> is the <a href="/wiki/Internet" title="Internet">Internet</a> <a href="/wiki/Country_code" title="Country code">country code</a> <a href="/wiki/Top-level_domain" title="Top-level domain">top-level domain</a> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/CcTLD" title="CcTLD">ccTLD</a>) for <a href="/wiki/Northern_Mariana_Islands" title="Northern Mariana Islands">Northern Mariana Islands</a>. There are a few sites related to the Northern Mariana Islands in this domain (such as governmental sites under <b>.gov.mp</b> and a few sites in <b>.org.mp</b> and <b>.co.mp</b>). The get.mp site allows users to register and manage .mp domains. The .mp name comes from <a href="/wiki/ISO_3166" title="ISO 3166">ISO 3166</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> which specifies MP as the two letter designation for the <a href="/wiki/Northern_Mariana_Islands" title="Northern Mariana Islands">Northern Mariana Islands</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Official_registry_site">Official registry site</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=.mp&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Official registry site">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Domain name registry services for .mp domains are operated by Saipan DataCom, a local ISP.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-iana-db-mp_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-iana-db-mp-3">[3]</a></sup> Domains with four or more characters may be purchased for $20. Premium domains under four characters range in price, with two-character domains running $5,000 for a three-year registration. There are some sites related to the Northern Mariana Islands under third-level domains <b>.gov.mp</b> and <b>.co.mp</b>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Whois_services">Whois services</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=.mp&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Whois services">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The MP Domain does not have public whois services available. Saipan DataCom will provide whois data in cases of potential trademark infringement.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Although ICANN documents specify whois.nic.mp<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-iana-db-mp_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-iana-db-mp-3">[3]</a></sup> as of April 2016 this host does not resolve.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Chi.mp">Chi.mp</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=.mp&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Chi.mp">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Saipan_DataCom&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Saipan DataCom (page does not exist)">Saipan DataCom</a> formerly operated chi.mp, a content hub and identity management platform providing .mp domains and websites. Old free .mp registrations provided earlier by chi.mp were terminated without a notice and the chi.mp web site has been defunct since at least May 2013<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (October 2013)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup>. As of October 2015 get.mp is offering chi.mp for sale for $20,000.00.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
