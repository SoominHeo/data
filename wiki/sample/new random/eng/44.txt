<p><b>Musahi-Koganei Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">武蔵小金井駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Musashi-Koganei-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a railway station on the <a href="/wiki/Ch%C5%AB%C5%8D_Main_Line" title="Chūō Main Line">Chūō Main Line</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Koganei,_Tokyo" title="Koganei, Tokyo">Koganei, Tokyo</a>, Japan, operated by <a href="/wiki/East_Japan_Railway_Company" title="East Japan Railway Company">East Japan Railway Company</a> (JR East).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lines">Lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Musashi-Koganei_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Musashi-Koganei Station is served by the <a href="/wiki/Ch%C5%AB%C5%8D_Line_(Rapid)" title="Chūō Line (Rapid)">Chūō Line (Rapid)</a> Service. It is 29.1 kilometers from the nominal starting point of the line at <a href="/wiki/Tokyo_Station" title="Tokyo Station">Tokyo</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_layout">Station layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Musashi-Koganei_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Station layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The station has two elevated <a href="/wiki/Island_platform" title="Island platform">island platforms</a> serving four tracks, with the station building located underneath. The station has a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Midori_no_Madoguchi" title="Midori no Madoguchi">Midori no Madoguchi</a> staffed ticket office.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Musashi-Koganei_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The station opened on 15 January 1926.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-kawashima2010_Vol.2_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-kawashima2010_Vol.2-1">[1]</a></sup> The station became part of the JR East network after the privatization of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/JNR" title="JNR">JNR</a> on April 1, 1987.</p>
