<p><b>Koro Bessho</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">別所 浩郎</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Bessho Kōrō</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born 5 February 1953)</span> is a diplomat of <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a> and has served as the Japan <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Permanent_Representatives_to_the_United_Nations" title="Permanent Representatives to the United Nations">Permanent Representative to the United Nations</a> since 10 June 2016. He served as the <a href="/wiki/President_of_the_United_Nations_Security_Council" title="President of the United Nations Security Council">President</a> of the <a href="/wiki/United_Nations_Security_Council" title="United Nations Security Council">United Nations Security Council</a> for June 2016.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Koro_Bessho&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>His hometown is <a href="/wiki/Hy%C5%8Dgo_Prefecture" title="Hyōgo Prefecture">Hyōgo Prefecture</a> and stayed in <a href="/wiki/New_Zealand" title="New Zealand">New Zealand</a> when he was an elementary and junior high school student.</p>
<p>He worked at the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Foreign_Affairs_(Japan)" title="Ministry of Foreign Affairs (Japan)">Ministry of Foreign Affairs</a> after his graduating from <a href="/wiki/Nada_High_School" title="Nada High School">Nada High School</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Kobe" title="Kobe">Kobe</a> and the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Tokyo" title="University of Tokyo">University of Tokyo</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Bachelor_of_Laws" title="Bachelor of Laws">Bachelor of Laws</a> in 1975.</p>
<p>He had served as the Executive Secretary to the <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_Japan" title="Prime Minister of Japan">Prime Minister</a> <a href="/wiki/Junichiro_Koizumi" title="Junichiro Koizumi">Junichiro Koizumi</a> since April 2001.</p>
<p>His hobby is <a href="/wiki/Noh" title="Noh">Noh</a>-<a href="/wiki/Ky%C5%8Dgen" title="Kyōgen">Kyōgen</a>.</p>
