<p><b><a href="/wiki/Afghanistan" title="Afghanistan">Afghanistan</a></b> first competed at the <a href="/wiki/Summer_Olympic_Games" title="Summer Olympic Games">Summer Olympic Games</a> at the <b><a href="/wiki/1936_Summer_Olympics" title="1936 Summer Olympics">1936 Summer Olympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Berlin" title="Berlin">Berlin</a>. In all they sent 19 competitors, but only 13 competed.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
