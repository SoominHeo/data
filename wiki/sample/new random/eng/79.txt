<p><b>Ismaël Abdul Rahman Roch Traoré</b> (born 18 August 1986 in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">French</a>-born Ivorian professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays for French club <a href="/wiki/Angers_SCO" title="Angers SCO">Angers</a> in <a href="/wiki/Ligue_1" title="Ligue 1">Ligue 1</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
