<p>The <b>Haengju Bridge</b> crosses the <a href="/wiki/Han_River_(Korea)" title="Han River (Korea)">Han River</a> in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a> and connects the city of <a href="/wiki/Goyang" title="Goyang">Goyang</a> and the district of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gangseo-gu,_Seoul" title="Gangseo-gu, Seoul">Gangseo-gu</a>. The first <a href="/wiki/Bridge" title="Bridge">bridge</a> was completed in 1978, but the bridge was very small and increasing developments led to the construction of a new bridge. The 2nd bridge was finished in 1995. On December 16, 2000, the third bridge was completed. Now the original bridge is only used as an emergency road.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
