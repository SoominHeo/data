<p><b>Nabeul Governorate</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tunisian_Arabic_language" title="Tunisian Arabic language">Tunisian Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="aeb" xml:lang="aeb">ولاية نابل</span>‎ <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Tunisian Arabic transliteration">Wilāyat Nābil</span></i> <small><a href="/wiki/Tunisian_Arabic" title="Tunisian Arabic">Tunisian</a> pronunciation:</small> <span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Arabic" title="Help:IPA for Arabic">[ˈnɛːbɪl]</a></span>) is one of the 24 <a href="/wiki/Governorates_of_Tunisia" title="Governorates of Tunisia">governorates of Tunisia</a>. It is situated in north-eastern Tunisia. It covers an area of 2,788 km² and has a population of 787,920 (2014 census).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The capital is <a href="/wiki/Nabeul" title="Nabeul">Nabeul</a>.</p>
<p>Nabeul is surrounded by the <a href="/wiki/Mediterranean_Sea" title="Mediterranean Sea">Mediterranean Sea</a>, expect the south west side where it is delimited by the three governorates <a href="/wiki/Zaghouan_Governorate" title="Zaghouan Governorate">Zaghouan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sousse_Governorate" title="Sousse Governorate">Sousse</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ben_Arous_Governorate" title="Ben Arous Governorate">Ben Arous</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nabeul_Governorate&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Nabeul" title="Nabeul">Nabeul</a>, the main city of the governorate, was founded in the 5th century BC by the <a href="/wiki/Greeks" title="Greeks">Greeks</a> of <a href="/wiki/Cyrene,_Libya" title="Cyrene, Libya">Cyrene</a>, serving as a trade port.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> Its name is an Arabised form of the <a href="/wiki/Greek_language" title="Greek language">Greek</a> <i>Neapolis</i> 'new city' (an <a href="/wiki/Etymology" title="Etymology">etymology</a> it shares with <a href="/wiki/Naples" title="Naples">Naples</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neapoli,_Thessaloniki" title="Neapoli, Thessaloniki">Neapoli</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Nablus" title="Nablus">Nablus</a>).<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
