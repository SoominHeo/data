<p><br/>
<b>1668 (<a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MDCLXVIII</a>)</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap year</a> <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Sunday" title="Leap year starting on Sunday">starting on Sunday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> AG) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Wednesday" title="Leap year starting on Wednesday">leap year starting on Wednesday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> ED) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>, the 1668th year of the <a href="/wiki/Common_Era" title="Common Era">Common Era</a> (CE) and <i><a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a></i> (AD) designations, the 668th year of the <a href="/wiki/2nd_millennium" title="2nd millennium">2nd millennium</a>, the 68th year of the <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th century</a>, and the 9th year of the <a href="/wiki/1660s" title="1660s">1660s</a> decade. As of the start of 1668, the Gregorian calendar was 10 days ahead of the Julian calendar, which remained in localized use until 1923.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1668&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93June">January–June</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1668&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–June">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>January – The <a href="/wiki/Triple_Alliance_(1668)" title="Triple Alliance (1668)">Triple Alliance of 1668</a> is formed between <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Sweden</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Dutch_Republic" title="Dutch Republic">United Provinces</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_13" title="February 13">February 13</a> – In <a href="/wiki/Lisbon" title="Lisbon">Lisbon</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Lisbon_(1668)" title="Treaty of Lisbon (1668)">peace treaty</a> is established between <a href="/wiki/Afonso_VI_of_Portugal" title="Afonso VI of Portugal">Afonso VI of Portugal</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Carlos_II_of_Spain" title="Carlos II of Spain">Carlos II of Spain</a>, by mediation of <a href="/wiki/Charles_II_of_England" title="Charles II of England">Charles II of England</a>, where the legitimacy of the Portuguese monarch is recognized. Portugal yields <a href="/wiki/Ceuta" title="Ceuta">Ceuta</a> to Spain.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_2" title="May 2">May 2</a> – The first <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Aix-la-Chapelle_(1668)" title="Treaty of Aix-la-Chapelle (1668)">Treaty of Aix-la-Chapelle</a> ends the <a href="/wiki/War_of_Devolution" title="War of Devolution">War of Devolution</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="July.E2.80.93December">July–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1668&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: July–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_7" title="July 7">July 7</a> – Bishop <a href="/wiki/Isaac_Barrow_(bishop)" title="Isaac Barrow (bishop)">Isaac Barrow</a> founds the <a href="/wiki/Bishop_Barrow_Trust" title="Bishop Barrow Trust">Bishop Barrow Trust</a> to establish a university on the <a href="/wiki/Isle_of_Man" title="Isle of Man">Isle of Man</a> (<a href="/wiki/King_William%27s_College" title="King William's College">King William's College</a>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li>Mid-July – <a href="/wiki/Henry_Morgan" title="Henry Morgan">Henry Morgan</a> sacks Portobello and <a href="/wiki/Panama_City" title="Panama City">Panama City</a>; the lack of booty from the latter attack leads to the city being burned.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_9" title="September 9">September 9</a> – <a href="/wiki/Moli%C3%A8re" title="Molière">Molière</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Comedy" title="Comedy">comedy</a>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/L%27Avare" title="L'Avare">L'Avare</a></i>, is first performed.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Date_unknown">Date unknown</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1668&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Date unknown">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/British_East_India_Company" title="British East India Company">British East India Company</a> takes over <a href="/wiki/Mumbai" title="Mumbai">Bombay</a>.</li>
<li>The first National Bank in Europe (the <i>Riksbank</i>) is founded in <a href="/wiki/Stockholm" title="Stockholm">Stockholm</a>, Sweden.</li>
<li>Emperor <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yohannes_I_of_Ethiopia" title="Yohannes I of Ethiopia">Yohannes I of Ethiopia</a> convenes a church council in <a href="/wiki/Gondar" title="Gondar">Gondar</a>, which decides to expel all <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic" title="Roman Catholic">Roman Catholics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Ethiopia" title="Ethiopia">Ethiopia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Isaac_Newton" title="Isaac Newton">Isaac Newton</a> builds the first <a href="/wiki/Reflecting_telescope" title="Reflecting telescope">reflecting telescope</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-books.google.com_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-books.google.com-2">[2]</a></sup> (<a href="/wiki/Newton%27s_reflector" title="Newton's reflector">Newton's reflector</a>).</li>
</ul>
