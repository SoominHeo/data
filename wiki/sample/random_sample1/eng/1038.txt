<p><b>GhostBSD</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Unix-like" title="Unix-like">Unix-like</a> <a href="/wiki/Operating_system" title="Operating system">operating system</a> based on <a href="/wiki/FreeBSD" title="FreeBSD">FreeBSD</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> with <a href="/wiki/MATE_(software)" title="MATE (software)">MATE</a> as its default <a href="/wiki/Desktop_environment" title="Desktop environment">desktop environment</a> (GNOME was the previous desktop environment). It aims to be easy to install, ready-to-use and easy to use. The project goal is to combine security, privacy, stability, usability, openness, freedom and to be free of charge.</p>
