<p><i><b>Gift from SG Wannabe</b></i> is the sixth Korean studio album by <a href="/wiki/SG_Wannabe" title="SG Wannabe">SG Wannabe</a>. The album has so far sold 100,500 copies.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notable_tracks">Notable tracks</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gift_from_SG_Wannabe&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Notable tracks">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id=".22I_Love_You.22">"I Love You"</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gift_from_SG_Wannabe&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title='Edit section: "I Love You"'>edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>"I Love You" was the title song for this album. <a href="/wiki/Yurisangja" title="Yurisangja">Yurisangja</a>'s Park Seung-hwa participated in the chorus of "I Love You".</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id=".22Cry_Baby.22">"Cry Baby"</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gift_from_SG_Wannabe&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title='Edit section: "Cry Baby"'>edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
