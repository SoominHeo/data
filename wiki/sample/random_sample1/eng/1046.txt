<p><b>HD 109749 b</b> is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Extrasolar_planet" title="Extrasolar planet">extrasolar planet</a> that orbits extremely close to the <a href="/wiki/Star" title="Star">star</a> <a href="/wiki/HD_109749" title="HD 109749">HD 109749</a>, taking only 5.24 days to orbit at the distance of 0.063 AU.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Fischer2006_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-Fischer2006-1">[1]</a></sup> This planet was discovered on August 22, 2005 - the same day as the discovery of <a href="/wiki/Gliese_581_b" title="Gliese 581 b">Gliese 581 b</a>.</p>
