<p><b>HD 11964 c</b> is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Extrasolar_planet" title="Extrasolar planet">extrasolar planet</a> approximately 107 <a href="/wiki/Light-year" title="Light-year">light-years</a> away in the <a href="/wiki/Constellation" title="Constellation">constellation</a> of <a href="/wiki/Cetus" title="Cetus">Cetus</a>. The planet was discovered in a close-orbit around the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yellow_subgiant" title="Yellow subgiant">yellow subgiant</a> <a href="/wiki/Star" title="Star">star</a> <a href="/wiki/HD_11964" title="HD 11964">HD 11964</a>. The planet has a <a href="/wiki/Minimum_mass" title="Minimum mass">minimum mass</a> 35 times the mass of <a href="/wiki/Earth" title="Earth">Earth</a> and is located in a mildly eccentric orbit which takes almost 38 <a href="/wiki/Day" title="Day">days</a> to complete. HD 11964 c was a possible planet discovered on the same day as <a href="/wiki/HD_11964_b" title="HD 11964 b">HD 11964 b</a> in 2005. HD 11964 c was first proposed in a paper published in 2007,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> and finally confirmed with new data presented in a review of multi-planet systems which appeared on the <a href="/wiki/ArXiv" title="ArXiv">arXiv</a> <a href="/wiki/Preprint" title="Preprint">preprint</a> website in 2008.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Wright2009_1-7"><a href="#cite_note-Wright2009-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Some sources have used the designation "HD 11964 b" for this planet,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> however in their review of the properties of multi-planet extrasolar <a href="/wiki/Planetary_system" title="Planetary system">planetary systems</a>, the discovery team has stated that the correct designation for this planet is HD 11964 c and the reversed system was due to confusion related to private communications between various groups of astronomers.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Wright2009_1-8"><a href="#cite_note-Wright2009-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
