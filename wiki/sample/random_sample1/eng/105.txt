<p><br/>
<b>1707 (<a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MDCCVII</a>)</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year" title="Common year">common year</a> <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Saturday" title="Common year starting on Saturday">starting on Saturday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> B) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Wednesday" title="Common year starting on Wednesday">common year starting on Wednesday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> E) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>, the 1707th year of the <a href="/wiki/Common_Era" title="Common Era">Common Era</a> (CE) and <i><a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a></i> (AD) designations, the 707th year of the <a href="/wiki/2nd_millennium" title="2nd millennium">2nd millennium</a>, the 7th year of the <a href="/wiki/18th_century" title="18th century">18th century</a>, and the 8th year of the <a href="/wiki/1700s_(decade)" title="1700s (decade)">1700s</a> decade. As of the start of 1707, the Gregorian calendar was 11 days ahead of the Julian calendar, which remained in localized use until 1923. In the <a href="/wiki/Swedish_calendar" title="Swedish calendar">Swedish calendar</a> it was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Tuesday" title="Common year starting on Tuesday">common year starting on Tuesday</a>, one day ahead of the Julian and ten days behind the Gregorian calendar.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93June">January–June</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1707&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–June">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_1" title="January 1">January 1</a> – <a href="/wiki/John_V_of_Portugal" title="John V of Portugal">John V</a> is crowned <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/King_of_Portugal_and_the_Algarves" title="King of Portugal and the Algarves">King of Portugal and the Algarves</a> in <a href="/wiki/Lisbon" title="Lisbon">Lisbon</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_16" title="January 16">January 16</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Acts_of_Union_1707" title="Acts of Union 1707">Treaty (or Act) of Union</a> of the two Kingdoms of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Scotland" title="Kingdom of Scotland">Scotland</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a> is ratified by the <a href="/wiki/Parliament_of_Scotland" title="Parliament of Scotland">Parliament of Scotland</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_3" title="March 3">March 3</a> – Death of the <a href="/wiki/Mughal_Empire" title="Mughal Empire">Emperor</a> <a href="/wiki/Aurangzeb" title="Aurangzeb">Aurangzeb</a> in Delhi.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_19" title="March 19">March 19</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Acts_of_Union_1707" title="Acts of Union 1707">Act of Union</a> with Scotland is ratified by the <a href="/wiki/Parliament_of_England" title="Parliament of England">Parliament of England</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_25" title="April 25">April 25</a> (April 14 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Old_Style" title="Old Style">Old Style</a>) – At the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Almansa" title="Battle of Almansa">Battle of Almansa</a> (Spain) in the <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Spanish_Succession" title="War of the Spanish Succession">War of the Spanish Succession</a>, the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Bourbon" title="House of Bourbon">Bourbon</a> army of <a href="/wiki/Enlightenment_in_Spain" title="Enlightenment in Spain">Spain</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_France" title="Kingdom of France">France</a> (with Irish mercenaries) under the French-born Englishman <a href="/wiki/James_FitzJames,_1st_Duke_of_Berwick" title="James FitzJames, 1st Duke of Berwick">James FitzJames, 1st Duke of Berwick</a>, soundly defeats the allied forces of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Portugal" title="Kingdom of Portugal">Portugal</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Dutch_Republic" title="Dutch Republic">Dutch Republic</a> led by the French-born <a href="/wiki/Huguenot" title="Huguenot">Huguenot</a> in English service <a href="/wiki/Henri_de_Massue,_Earl_of_Galway" title="Henri de Massue, Earl of Galway">Henri de Massue, Earl of Galway</a>. Following this, <a href="/wiki/Philip_V_of_Spain" title="Philip V of Spain">Philip V of Spain</a> promulgates the first <a href="/wiki/Nueva_Planta_decrees" title="Nueva Planta decrees">Nueva Planta decrees</a>, bringing the Kingdoms of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Valencia" title="Kingdom of Valencia">Valencia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Aragon" title="Kingdom of Aragon">Aragon</a> under the laws of the <a href="/wiki/Crown_of_Castile" title="Crown of Castile">Crown of Castile</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_1" title="May 1">May 1</a> – The new sovereign state of Great Britain comes into being as a result of the <a href="/wiki/Acts_of_Union_1707" title="Acts of Union 1707">Acts of Union</a> which combine the Kingdoms of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Scotland" title="Kingdom of Scotland">Scotland</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a> into a single united <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Great_Britain" title="Kingdom of Great Britain">Kingdom of Great Britain</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> and merge the Parliaments of <a href="/wiki/Parliament_of_England" title="Parliament of England">England</a> and <a href="/wiki/Parliament_of_Scotland" title="Parliament of Scotland">Scotland</a> to form the <a href="/wiki/Parliament_of_Great_Britain" title="Parliament of Great Britain">Parliament of Great Britain</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Cassell.27s_Chronology291_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Cassell.27s_Chronology291-3">[3]</a></sup></li>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="July.E2.80.93December">July–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1707&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: July–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_29" title="July 29">July 29</a>–<a href="/wiki/August_21" title="August 21">August 21</a> – <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Spanish_Succession" title="War of the Spanish Succession">War of the Spanish Succession</a>: <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Toulon_(1707)" title="Battle of Toulon (1707)">Battle of Toulon</a> – Allies are obliged to withdraw, but the French fleet is effectively put out of action.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/October_22" title="October 22">October 22</a> – <a href="/wiki/Scilly_naval_disaster_of_1707" title="Scilly naval disaster of 1707">Scilly naval disaster</a>: four <a href="/wiki/Royal_Navy" title="Royal Navy">Royal Navy</a> ships run aground in the <a href="/wiki/Isles_of_Scilly" title="Isles of Scilly">Isles of Scilly</a> because of faulty <a href="/wiki/Navigation" title="Navigation">navigation</a>. Admiral Sir <a href="/wiki/Cloudesley_Shovell" title="Cloudesley Shovell">Cloudesley Shovell</a> and at least 1450 sailors all drown.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/October_23" title="October 23">October 23</a> – The Parliament of the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Great_Britain" title="Kingdom of Great Britain">Kingdom of Great Britain</a> first meets in London.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/October_28" title="October 28">October 28</a> – <a href="/wiki/1707_H%C5%8Dei_earthquake" title="1707 Hōei earthquake">Hōei earthquake</a>, the most powerful in Japan until <a href="/wiki/2011" title="2011">2011</a>, with an estimated <a href="/wiki/Richter_magnitude_scale" title="Richter magnitude scale">local magnitude</a> of 8.6.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/December_16" title="December 16">December 16</a> – The <a href="/wiki/H%C5%8Dei_eruption_of_Mount_Fuji" title="Hōei eruption of Mount Fuji">last recorded eruption of Mount Fuji</a> begins in Japan.</li>
</ul>
