<p><b>HD 2638 b</b> is a planet of the star <a href="/wiki/HD_2638" title="HD 2638">HD 2638</a>. It is a typical "<a href="/wiki/Hot_Jupiter" title="Hot Jupiter">hot Jupiter</a>", a planet that orbits its parent star in a very tight "torch orbit". Distance to the star is less than 1/20th <a href="/wiki/Earth" title="Earth">Earth's</a> distance from the Sun. One orbital revolution lasts only about three and half <a href="/wiki/Day" title="Day">days</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Moutou2005_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Moutou2005-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
