<p><b>HD 76700 b</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Exoplanet" title="Exoplanet">exoplanet</a> discovered orbiting the star in 2002. The planet is a <a href="/wiki/Hot_Jupiter" title="Hot Jupiter">hot Jupiter</a> with a mass at least 0.233 times that of <a href="/wiki/Jupiter" title="Jupiter">Jupiter</a>. It orbits very close to its parent star and completes one orbit in less than four <a href="/wiki/Day" title="Day">days</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Tinney2003_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Tinney2003-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Butler2006_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Butler2006-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
