<p><b>HD 81040 b</b> is a massive, <a href="/wiki/Gas_giant" title="Gas giant">gas giant</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Extrasolar_planet" title="Extrasolar planet">planet</a> that orbits the <a href="/wiki/Star" title="Star">star</a> <a href="/wiki/HD_81040" title="HD 81040">HD 81040</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Orbital_period" title="Orbital period">period</a> is just over 1000 <a href="/wiki/Day" title="Day">days</a>. It has <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Semimajor_axis" title="Semimajor axis">semimajor axis</a> of 1.94 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Astronomical_Unit" title="Astronomical Unit">AU</a>. However, the orbit is extremely <a href="/wiki/Orbital_eccentricity" title="Orbital eccentricity">eccentric</a>, little over 0.5 in value.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Sozzetti2006_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-Sozzetti2006-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
