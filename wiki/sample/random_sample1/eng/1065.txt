<p>"<b>HERO / Sweet Jewel</b>" is the 2nd single by the Japanese girl <a href="/wiki/Japanese_idol" title="Japanese idol">idol</a> group <a href="/wiki/Fairies_(band)" title="Fairies (band)">Fairies</a>, released in Japan on December 21, 2011 on the label <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sonic_Groove" title="Sonic Groove">Sonic Groove</a> (a subsidiary of <a href="/wiki/Avex_Group" title="Avex Group">Avex Group</a>).</p>
<p>It is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Double_A-side" title="Double A-side">double-A-side</a> single.</p>
<p>The physical CD single debuted at number 9 in the <a href="/wiki/Oricon" title="Oricon">Oricon</a> weekly singles chart.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Oricon_Japanese_Singles_Weekly_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Oricon_Japanese_Singles_Weekly-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
