<p><b>HIP 14810 d</b> is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Extrasolar_planet" title="Extrasolar planet">extrasolar planet</a> approximately 170 <a href="/wiki/Light-year" title="Light-year">light-years</a> away in the <a href="/wiki/Constellation" title="Constellation">constellation</a> of <a href="/wiki/Aries_(constellation)" title="Aries (constellation)">Aries</a>. This planet has mass at least 0.57 times that of <a href="/wiki/Jupiter" title="Jupiter">Jupiter</a> and orbits at 1.89 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Astronomical_Unit" title="Astronomical Unit">AU</a> in an eccentric orbit.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Wright2009_2-8"><a href="#cite_note-Wright2009-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
