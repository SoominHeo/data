<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="170">170</span></h3>
<div class="hatnote relarticle mainarticle selfreference noprint" role="note">This section is <a href="/wiki/Transclusion" title="Transclusion">transcluded</a> from <a href="/wiki/170" title="170">170</a>. <span class="plainlinks" style="font-style: normal"><small>(<a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=170&amp;action=edit">edit</a> | <a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=170&amp;action=history">history</a>)</small></span></div>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=170&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-1" title="170">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Roman_Empire">Roman Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=170&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-2" title="170">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Suebian" title="Suebian">Suebian</a> tribes of the <a href="/wiki/Marcomanni" title="Marcomanni">Marcomanni</a> cross the <a href="/wiki/Danube" title="Danube">Danube</a> and invade northern <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Roman_army" title="Roman army">Roman army</a> (20,000 men) is destroyed near <a href="/wiki/Carnuntum" title="Carnuntum">Carnuntum</a> in <a href="/wiki/Pannonia" title="Pannonia">Pannonia</a>.</li>
<li>The Marcomanni plunder Opitergium (modern <a href="/wiki/Oderzo" title="Oderzo">Oderzo</a>) and besiege <a href="/wiki/Aquileia" title="Aquileia">Aquileia</a>. This is the first time hostile forces entered Italy since <a href="/wiki/101_BC" title="101 BC">101 BC</a>.</li>
<li>Emperor <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Aurelius" title="Marcus Aurelius">Marcus Aurelius</a> writes in <a href="/wiki/Sirmium" title="Sirmium">Sirmium</a> (<a href="/wiki/Pannonia" title="Pannonia">Pannonia</a>) his first of 12 books of the <i><a href="/wiki/Meditations" title="Meditations">Meditations</a></i> in <a href="/wiki/Koine_Greek" title="Koine Greek">Koine Greek</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Costoboci" title="Costoboci">Costoboci</a> cross the Danube (<a href="/wiki/Dacia" title="Dacia">Dacia</a>) and ravage <a href="/wiki/Thrace" title="Thrace">Thrace</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Balkans" title="Balkans">Balkan Peninsula</a>. They reached <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Eleusina" title="Eleusina">Eleusina</a>, near <a href="/wiki/Athens" title="Athens">Athens</a>, and destroyed the temple of the <a href="/wiki/Eleusinian_Mysteries" title="Eleusinian Mysteries">Eleusinian Mysteries</a>.</li>
<li>Marcus Aurelius orders humane treatment for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Christians" title="Christians">Christians</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slavery" title="Slavery">slaves</a> throughout the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Empire" title="Roman Empire">Roman Empire</a>.</li>
<li>An <a href="/wiki/Equestrian_Statue_of_Marcus_Aurelius" title="Equestrian Statue of Marcus Aurelius">Equestrian Statue of Marcus Aurelius</a> is erected in <a href="/wiki/Ancient_Rome" title="Ancient Rome">Rome</a>.</li>
</ul>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place_2">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=171&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-1" title="171">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Roman_Empire_2">Roman Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=171&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-2" title="171">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li>Emperor <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Aurelius" title="Marcus Aurelius">Marcus Aurelius</a> forms a new military command, the <i>praetentura Italiae et Alpium</i>. <a href="/wiki/Aquileia" title="Aquileia">Aquileia</a> is relieved, and the <a href="/wiki/Marcomanni" title="Marcomanni">Marcomanni</a> are evicted from Roman territory.</li>
<li>Marcus Aurelius signs a peace treaty with the <a href="/wiki/Quadi" title="Quadi">Quadi</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Sarmatians" title="Sarmatians">Sarmatian</a> <a href="/wiki/Iazyges" title="Iazyges">Iazyges</a>. The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Germanic_tribes" title="Germanic tribes">Germanic tribes</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Hasdingi" title="Hasdingi">Hasdingi</a> (<a href="/wiki/Vandals" title="Vandals">Vandals</a>) and the <a href="/wiki/Lacringi" title="Lacringi">Lacringi</a> become Roman allies.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Armenia" title="Armenia">Armenia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mesopotamia" title="Mesopotamia">Mesopotamia</a> become <a href="/wiki/Protectorate" title="Protectorate">protectorates</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Empire" title="Roman Empire">Roman Empire</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Costoboci" title="Costoboci">Costoboci</a> cross the Danube (<a href="/wiki/Dacia" title="Dacia">Dacia</a>) and ravage <a href="/wiki/Thrace" title="Thrace">Thrace</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Balkans" title="Balkans">Balkan Peninsula</a>. They reach <a href="/wiki/Eleusis" title="Eleusis">Eleusis</a>, near <a href="/wiki/Athens" title="Athens">Athens</a>, and destroy the temple of the <a href="/wiki/Eleusinian_Mysteries" title="Eleusinian Mysteries">Eleusinian Mysteries</a>.</li>
<li>In May or June, the <a href="/wiki/Orator" title="Orator">orator</a> <a href="/wiki/Aelius_Aristides" title="Aelius Aristides">Aelius Aristides</a> delivers a public speech in <a href="/wiki/Smyrna" title="Smyrna">Smyrna</a>, lamenting the damage recently inflicted to the sacred site of <a href="/wiki/Eleusis" title="Eleusis">Eleusis</a>.</li>
</ul>

<li>Emperor <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Aurelius" title="Marcus Aurelius">Marcus Aurelius</a> crosses the <a href="/wiki/Danube" title="Danube">Danube</a> with an expeditionary force, he subdues the <a href="/wiki/Marcomanni" title="Marcomanni">Marcomanni</a> and their allies. <h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Roman_Empire_8">Roman Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=177&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-2" title="177">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Commodus" title="Commodus">Lucius Aurelius Commodus Caesar</a> (age 15) and <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Peducaeus_Plautius_Quintillus" title="Marcus Peducaeus Plautius Quintillus">Marcus Peducaeus Plautius Quintillus</a> become <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Roman_Consul" title="Roman Consul">Roman Consuls</a>.</li>
<li>Commodus is given the title <i><a href="/wiki/Augustus_(honorific)" title="Augustus (honorific)">Augustus</a></i> and is made co-emperor with the same status as his father Marcus Aurelius.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Aurelius" title="Marcus Aurelius">Marcus Aurelius</a> begins a systematic persecution of <a href="/wiki/Christianity" title="Christianity">Christians</a> at <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a>, the followers take refuge in the <a href="/wiki/Catacombs_of_Rome" title="Catacombs of Rome">catacombs</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Church_(building)" title="Church (building)">Churches</a> in southern <a href="/wiki/Gaul" title="Gaul">Gaul</a> are destroyed after a crowd accuses the local Christians of practicing <a href="/wiki/Cannibalism" title="Cannibalism">cannibalism</a> and <a href="/wiki/Incest" title="Incest">incest</a>.</li>
<li>Forty-seven Christians are martyred in <a href="/wiki/Lyon" title="Lyon">Lyon</a> (<a href="/wiki/Saint" title="Saint">Saint</a> <a href="/wiki/Blandina" title="Blandina">Blandina</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pothinus" title="Pothinus">Pothinus</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bishop_of_Lyon" title="Bishop of Lyon">bishop of Lyon</a>, are among them).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Marcomannic_Wars" title="Marcomannic Wars">Second Marcomannic War</a>: Marcus Aurelius and Commodus begin war against the <a href="/wiki/Quadi" title="Quadi">Quadi</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Marcomanni" title="Marcomanni">Marcomanni</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia_4">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=177&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-3" title="177">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li>Chinese troops suffer a crushing defeat against a confederacy of <a href="/wiki/Central_Asia" title="Central Asia">Central Asian</a> tribes led by the <a href="/wiki/Xianbei" title="Xianbei">Xianbei</a> (see <a href="/wiki/Five_Barbarians" title="Five Barbarians">Wu Hu</a>).</li>
</ul>

<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Roman_Empire_9">Roman Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=178&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-2" title="178">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bruttia_Crispina" title="Bruttia Crispina">Bruttia Crispina</a> marries <a href="/wiki/Commodus" title="Commodus">Commodus</a> and receive the title of <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Augusta_(honorific)" title="Augusta (honorific)">Augusta</a></i>.</li>
<li>Emperor <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Aurelius" title="Marcus Aurelius">Marcus Aurelius</a> and his son Commodus arrive at <a href="/wiki/Carnuntum" title="Carnuntum">Carnuntum</a> in <a href="/wiki/Pannonia" title="Pannonia">Pannonia</a>, and travel to the <a href="/wiki/Danube" title="Danube">Danube</a> to fight against the <a href="/wiki/Marcomanni" title="Marcomanni">Marcomanni</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia_5">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=178&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-3" title="178">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li>Last (7th) year of <i>Xiping</i> era and start of <i>Guanghe</i> era of the Chinese <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Han_Dynasty" title="Han Dynasty">Han Dynasty</a>.</li>
<li>In India, the decline of the <a href="/wiki/Kushan_Empire" title="Kushan Empire">Kushan Empire</a> begins. The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sassanides" title="Sassanides">Sassanides</a> take over <a href="/wiki/Central_Asia" title="Central Asia">Central Asia</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Religion_3">Religion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=178&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-4" title="178">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Montanism" title="Montanism">Montanist</a> <a href="/wiki/Heresy" title="Heresy">heresy</a> is condemned for the first time.</li>
</ul>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place_10">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=179&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-1" title="179">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Roman_empire_10">Roman empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=179&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-2" title="179">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Castra" title="Castra">Roman fort</a> Castra Regina ("fortress by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Regen_river" title="Regen river">Regen river</a>") is built at <a href="/wiki/Regensburg" title="Regensburg">Regensburg</a>, on the right bank of the <a href="/wiki/Danube" title="Danube">Danube</a> in <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Roman_legionary" title="Roman legionary">Roman legionaries</a> of <a href="/wiki/Legio_II_Adiutrix" title="Legio II Adiutrix">Legio II <i>Adiutrix</i></a> <i>engrave on the rock of the <a href="/wiki/Tren%C4%8D%C3%ADn" title="Trenčín">Trenčín</a> Castle (<a href="/wiki/Slovakia" title="Slovakia">Slovakia</a>) the name of the town</i> Laugaritio<i>, marking the northernmost point of Roman presence in that part of <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>.</i></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Aurelius" title="Marcus Aurelius">Marcus Aurelius</a> drives the <a href="/wiki/Marcomanni" title="Marcomanni">Marcomanni</a> over the Danube and reinforces the border. To repopulate and rebuild a devastated <a href="/wiki/Pannonia" title="Pannonia">Pannonia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ancient_Rome" title="Ancient Rome">Rome</a> allows the first German colonists to enter territory controlled by the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Empire" title="Roman Empire">Roman Empire</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia_6">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=179&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-3" title="179">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Abgar_IX_of_Osroene" title="Abgar IX of Osroene">Abgar IX the Great</a> becomes King of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Edessa,_Mesopotamia" title="Edessa, Mesopotamia">Edessa</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gogukcheon_of_Goguryeo" title="Gogukcheon of Goguryeo">Gogukcheon</a> succeeds his father Shindae as King of <a href="/wiki/Goguryeo" title="Goguryeo">Goguryeo</a>.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Han_Dynasty" title="Han Dynasty">Han Dynasty</a> <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>: The full title of the <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nine_Chapters_on_the_Mathematical_Art" title="Nine Chapters on the Mathematical Art">Nine Chapters on the Mathematical Art</a></i> appears on two bronze standard measures dated to this year, yet there is speculation that the same book existed beforehand only under different titles. In the 3rd century, <a href="/wiki/Liu_Hui" title="Liu Hui">Liu Hui</a> would provide commentary on this important early Chinese mathematical treatise.</li>
</ul>
