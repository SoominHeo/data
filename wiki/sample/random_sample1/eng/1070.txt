<p><b>HONNE</b> are a British <a href="/wiki/Electronic_music" title="Electronic music">electronic</a> <a href="/wiki/Soul_music" title="Soul music">soul</a> duo formed in 2014 in <a href="/wiki/Bow,_London" title="Bow, London">Bow</a>, <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, consisting of James Hatcher (producer) and Andy Clutterbuck (singer/producer) who both write, record and produce the music.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The band released their first two EPs, <i>Warm on a Cold Night</i> and <i>All in the Value</i>, on Super Recordings in 2014. In 2015 they released their third EP, <i>Coastal Love</i>, on their own imprint Tatemae Recordings. Their debut studio album <i>Warm on a Cold Night</i> was released on 22 July 2016 through Tatemae Recordings.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Music_career">Music career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=HONNE&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Music career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2014:_Early_career_and_first_EPs">2014: Early career and first EPs</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=HONNE&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 2014: Early career and first EPs">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Clutterbuck and Hatcher met while at university and started making music together under the name of HONNE – a Japanese word (本音) meaning "real intention" – in late 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> HONNE's musical style has been described as "futuristic soul destined to re-invent babymaking music" by <i>The Telegraph</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The band released their debut single "Warm on a Cold Night" in September 2014 via Super Recordings, the label that launched <a href="/wiki/AlunaGeorge" title="AlunaGeorge">AlunaGeorge</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bondax" title="Bondax">Bondax</a>. They followed up with their second EP <i>All in the Value</i>, also released on Super Recordings, in November 2014, and made their live debut at the Sebright Arms on the same day.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2015:_Coastal_Love_EP_and_live_performances">2015: <i>Coastal Love</i> EP and live performances</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=HONNE&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 2015: Coastal Love EP and live performances">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>HONNE started 2015 with a sold out show at Electrowerkz in London in February<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> before announcing the release of their EP ‘Coastal Love’ on their own imprint Tatemae Recordings. Following the release of their <i>Coastal Love</i> EP on 1 May, the band played a sold out headline gig at The Laundry in London on 13 May<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> and performed on the <a href="/wiki/BBC_Introducing" title="BBC Introducing">BBC Introducing</a> stage at <a href="/wiki/BBC" title="BBC">BBC</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Radio_1%27s_Big_Weekend" title="Radio 1's Big Weekend">Radio 1's Big Weekend</a> 2015 in Norwich.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> From early to mid-2015, the band performed at various festivals in the UK and across Europe, including <a href="/wiki/The_Great_Escape_Festival" title="The Great Escape Festival">The Great Escape Festival</a> 2015, Field Day, Calvi on the Rocks, Latitude and supported <a href="/wiki/Kwabs" title="Kwabs">Kwabs</a> on his UK and European tour.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2016:_Gone_Are_the_Days_and_Warm_on_a_Cold_Night">2016: <i>Gone Are the Days</i> and <i>Warm on a Cold Night</i></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=HONNE&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2016: Gone Are the Days and Warm on a Cold Night">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Released early in 2016, <i>Gone Are the Days</i> features the song of the same name from their <i>Coastal Love</i> EP as well as six new tracks, including a remix of the title song. HONNE followed this up with their first full-length album, <i>Warm on a Cold Night</i>, on 22 July 2016.</p>
<p>The album cover features Japanese characters for aesthetic reasons, and features an image taken on the night of one of their London shows.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
