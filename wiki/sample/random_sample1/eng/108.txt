<p><br/>
<b>1722 (<a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MDCCXXII</a>)</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year" title="Common year">common year</a> <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Thursday" title="Common year starting on Thursday">starting on Thursday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> D) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Monday" title="Common year starting on Monday">common year starting on Monday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> G) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>, the 1722nd year of the <a href="/wiki/Common_Era" title="Common Era">Common Era</a> (CE) and <i><a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a></i> (AD) designations, the 722nd year of the <a href="/wiki/2nd_millennium" title="2nd millennium">2nd millennium</a>, the 22nd year of the <a href="/wiki/18th_century" title="18th century">18th century</a>, and the 3rd year of the <a href="/wiki/1720s" title="1720s">1720s</a> decade. As of the start of 1722, the Gregorian calendar was 11 days ahead of the Julian calendar, which remained in localized use until 1923.</p>

Safavid <a href="/wiki/Sultan_Husayn" title="Sultan Husayn">Sultan Husayn</a> abdicates and acknowledges <a href="/wiki/Mahmud_Hotak" title="Mahmud Hotak">Mahmud Hotak</a> as the new Shah of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Persia" title="Persia">Persia</a>.</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/Brown_Bess" title="Brown Bess">Brown Bess</a> <a href="/wiki/Musket" title="Musket">Musket</a> enters into service of the <a href="/wiki/British_Army" title="British Army">British Army</a></li>
