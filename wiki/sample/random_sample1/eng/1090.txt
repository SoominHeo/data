<p><i><b>Hyde</b></i> is the first <a href="/wiki/Extended_play" title="Extended play">mini-album</a> by the South Korean boy band <a href="/wiki/VIXX" title="VIXX">VIXX</a>. It was released on May 20, 2013 under <a href="/wiki/Jellyfish_Entertainment" title="Jellyfish Entertainment">Jellyfish Entertainment</a>, and features the single of the same name. The album was re-released as <i>Jekyll</i> on July 31, 2013 with the single "G.R.8.U".</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Singles">Singles</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hyde_(EP)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Singles">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id=".22Hyde.22">"Hyde"</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hyde_(EP)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title='Edit section: "Hyde"'>edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>VIXX promoted <i>Hyde</i> with the song of the same name. The song's lyrics were written by songwriter <a href="/wiki/Kim_Eana" title="Kim Eana">Kim Eana</a>, and the rap portion was written by Ravi. The music was composed by <a href="/wiki/Jellyfish_Entertainment" title="Jellyfish Entertainment">Jellyfish Entertainment</a> CEO Hwang Se Jun and Swedish production team D30 (Caesar &amp; Loui and Olof Lindskog).<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> The song's music video was directed by Hong Won-ki of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ZanyBros" title="ZanyBros">ZanyBros</a>, who also directed their previous music videos, "Super Hero" and "Rock Ur Body".<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<p>Promotion began on May 23 on <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/M!_Countdown" title="M! Countdown">M! Countdown</a></i>, and wrapped up with a goodbye stage on <i><a href="/wiki/Inkigayo" title="Inkigayo">Inkigayo</a></i> on June 30.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> The song peaked at number 35 on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gaon_Singles_Chart" title="Gaon Singles Chart">Gaon Singles Chart</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id=".22G.R.8.U.22">"G.R.8.U"</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hyde_(EP)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title='Edit section: "G.R.8.U"'>edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The lead single from <i>Jekyll</i>, "G.R.8.U" (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">대.다.나.다.너</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Dae.da.na.da.neo</span></i>), was written by <a href="/wiki/Kim_Eana" title="Kim Eana">Kim Eana</a> and produced by <a href="/wiki/Hyuk_Shin" title="Hyuk Shin">Hyuk Shin</a>, DK, <a href="/wiki/Ross_Lara" title="Ross Lara">Ross Lara</a> and <a href="/wiki/Todd_Wright_(musician)" title="Todd Wright (musician)">Todd Wright</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> The song's music video was directed by Hong Won-ki of ZanyBros.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> Promotion began on August 1, and wrapped up with a goodbye stage on <i>Inkigayo</i> on September 8.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> The song peaked at number 14 on Gaon Singles Chart.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
