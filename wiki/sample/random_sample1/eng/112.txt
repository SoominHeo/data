<p><br/>
<b>1748 (<a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MDCCXLVIII</a>)</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap year</a> <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Monday" title="Leap year starting on Monday">starting on Monday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> GF) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Friday" title="Leap year starting on Friday">leap year starting on Friday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> CB) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>, the 1748th year of the <a href="/wiki/Common_Era" title="Common Era">Common Era</a> (CE) and <i><a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a></i> (AD) designations, the 748th year of the <a href="/wiki/2nd_millennium" title="2nd millennium">2nd millennium</a>, the 48th year of the <a href="/wiki/18th_century" title="18th century">18th century</a>, and the 9th year of the <a href="/wiki/1740s" title="1740s">1740s</a> decade. As of the start of 1748, the Gregorian calendar was 11 days ahead of the Julian calendar, which remained in localized use until 1923.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93June">January–June</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1748&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–June">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_12" title="January 12">January 12</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ahmad_Shah_Durrani" title="Ahmad Shah Durrani">Ahmad Shah Durrani</a> captures <a href="/wiki/Lahore" title="Lahore">Lahore</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_11" title="March 11">March 11</a> – In battle near Manupur (15 km northwest of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sirhind" title="Sirhind">Sirhind</a>), Mughal forces under Prince <a href="/wiki/Ahmad_Shah_Bahadur" title="Ahmad Shah Bahadur">Ahmad Shah Bahadur</a> are victorious against <a href="/wiki/Ahmad_Shah_Durrani" title="Ahmad Shah Durrani">Ahmad Shah Durrani</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_28" title="March 28">March 28</a> – A fire in the <a href="/wiki/City_of_London" title="City of London">City of London</a> causes over a million pounds worth of damage.</li>
<li>April – <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Maastricht_(1748)" title="Siege of Maastricht (1748)">Maastricht is conquered</a> by <a href="/wiki/Maurice_de_Saxe" title="Maurice de Saxe">Maurice de Saxe</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_24" title="April 24">April 24</a> – <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Austrian_Succession" title="War of the Austrian Succession">War of the Austrian Succession</a>: A congress assembles at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Aix-la-Chapelle" title="Aix-la-Chapelle">Aix-la-Chapelle</a> (<a href="/wiki/Aachen" title="Aachen">Aachen</a>) with the intent to conclude the war.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="July.E2.80.93December">July–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1748&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: July–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>August – The <a href="/wiki/Nymphalis_antiopa" title="Nymphalis antiopa">Camberwell Beauty</a> butterfly is named after specimens found at Camberwell in London.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_24" title="September 24">September 24</a> – <a href="/wiki/Shahrukh_Afshar" title="Shahrukh Afshar">Shah Rukh</a> becomes ruler of <a href="/wiki/Greater_Khorasan" title="Greater Khorasan">Greater Khorasan</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/October_18" title="October 18">October 18</a> – <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Austrian_Succession" title="War of the Austrian Succession">War of the Austrian Succession</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Aix-la-Chapelle_(1748)" title="Treaty of Aix-la-Chapelle (1748)">Treaty of Aix-la-Chapelle</a> is signed to end the war. Great Britain obtains <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Madras" title="Madras">Madras</a>, in India, from France, in exchange for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fortress" title="Fortress">fortress</a> of <a href="/wiki/Louisbourg" title="Louisbourg">Louisbourg</a> in Canada.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Date_unknown">Date unknown</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1748&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Date unknown">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Leonhard_Euler" title="Leonhard Euler">Leonhard Euler</a> publishes <i><a href="/wiki/Introductio_in_analysin_infinitorum" title="Introductio in analysin infinitorum">Introductio in analysin infinitorum</a></i>, an introduction to pure analytical mathematics, in Berlin.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Montesquieu" title="Montesquieu">Montesquieu</a> publishes <i><a href="/wiki/The_Spirit_of_the_Laws" title="The Spirit of the Laws">De l'esprit des lois</a></i>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Friedrich_Gottlieb_Klopstock" title="Friedrich Gottlieb Klopstock">Friedrich Gottlieb Klopstock</a> publishes the first three <a href="/wiki/Canto" title="Canto">cantos</a> of his <a href="/wiki/Epic_poetry" title="Epic poetry">epic poem</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Der_Messias_(Klopstock)" title="Der Messias (Klopstock)">Der Messias</a></i> in <a href="/wiki/Hexameter" title="Hexameter">hexameters</a> (anonymously) in <i><a href="/wiki/Bremer_Beitr%C3%A4ge" title="Bremer Beiträge">Bremer Beiträge</a></i> (<a href="/wiki/Leipzig" title="Leipzig">Leipzig</a>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Adam_Smith" title="Adam Smith">Adam Smith</a> begins to deliver public lectures in <a href="/wiki/Edinburgh" title="Edinburgh">Edinburgh</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Royal_Swedish_Academy_of_Sciences" title="Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences">Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences</a> makes <a href="/wiki/Eva_Ekeblad" title="Eva Ekeblad">Eva Ekeblad</a> its first female member.</li>
<li>Construction of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sveaborg" title="Sveaborg">Sveaborg</a> fortification begins near <a href="/wiki/Helsinki" title="Helsinki">Helsinki</a>.</li>
<li>The ruins of <a href="/wiki/Pompeii" title="Pompeii">Pompeii</a> are rediscovered.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Louis_XV_of_France" title="Louis XV of France">Louis XV of France</a> breaks his promise to eliminate the <a href="/wiki/Income_tax" title="Income tax">income tax</a> after the <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Aix-la-Chapelle_(1748)" title="Treaty of Aix-la-Chapelle (1748)">Treaty of Aix-la-Chapelle</a> ends the war. The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Parlement_of_Paris" title="Parlement of Paris">Parlement of Paris</a> protests so he reduces the tax to 5%.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
