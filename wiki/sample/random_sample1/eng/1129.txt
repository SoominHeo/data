<p><b>ITV</b> or <b>iTV</b> may refer to:</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="ITV">ITV</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=ITV&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: ITV">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>Independent Television, a British television network:
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/ITV_(TV_network)" title="ITV (TV network)">ITV (TV network)</a>, a television network covering the United Kingdom, the Isle of Man and the Channel Islands</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/ITV_(TV_channel)" title="ITV (TV channel)">ITV (TV channel)</a>, a brand name used by ITV plc for twelve franchises of the ITV Network covering England, Southern Scotland, Wales, the Isle of Man and the Channel Islands</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/ITV_Digital" title="ITV Digital">ITV Digital</a>, a UK digital terrestrial TV broadcaster, which opened in 1998 as ONdigital and closed in 2002</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/ITV_plc" title="ITV plc">ITV plc</a>, a British company which owns twelve of the fifteen ITV Network franchises</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/ITV_Studios" title="ITV Studios">ITV Studios</a>, a production company owned by ITV plc</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Itv.com" title="Itv.com">itv.com</a>, the main website of ITV plc</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/ITV_Parapentes" title="ITV Parapentes">ITV Parapentes</a>, a defunct French aircraft manufacturer</li>
<li>ITV Independent Television Tanzania, a Tanzanian television station and member of the <a href="/wiki/Commonwealth_Broadcasting_Association" title="Commonwealth Broadcasting Association">Commonwealth Broadcasting Association</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/CITV-DT" title="CITV-DT">CITV-DT</a>, a television station in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/%C4%B0ctimai_Television" title="İctimai Television">İctimai Television</a>, an Azerbaijani television station</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Infotainment_Television" title="Infotainment Television">Infotainment Television</a>, a New-Delhi based news production house</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Internacia_Televido" title="Internacia Televido">Internacia Televido</a>, the first Esperanto-language TV</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/I-Television" title="I-Television">i-Television</a>, a television station in Ehime, Japan</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Information_Television_Network" title="Information Television Network">Information Television Network</a>, an American television production company</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="iTV_2">iTV</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=ITV&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: iTV">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/ITV_(Thailand)" title="ITV (Thailand)">iTV (Thailand)</a>, a Thai television station, renamed in 2007 as TITV, now defunct and replaced by Thai Public Broadcasting Service</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/ITV_Cable_16" title="ITV Cable 16">iTV Cable 16</a>, an educational cable television channel in San Diego, California</li>
<li>iTV, a Vietnamese Interactive Music Channel, owned by <a href="/wiki/Vietnam_Multimedia_Corporation" title="Vietnam Multimedia Corporation">Vietnam Multimedia Corporation</a></li>
<li>iTV, the pre-release codename for the <a href="/wiki/Apple_TV" title="Apple TV">Apple TV</a> Home media product</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/I-Television" title="I-Television">i-Television</a>, a television station in Ehime Prefecture, Japan</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Interactive_television" title="Interactive television">Interactive television</a> (iTV), television which allows the exchange of information between the sender and the receiver</li>
<li>i: Independent Television, now called <a href="/wiki/Ion_Television" title="Ion Television">Ion Television</a>, an American television network</li>
</ul>
