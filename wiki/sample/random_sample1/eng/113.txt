<p><br/>
<b>1751 (<a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MDCCLI</a>)</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year" title="Common year">common year</a> <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Friday" title="Common year starting on Friday">starting on Friday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> C) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Tuesday" title="Common year starting on Tuesday">common year starting on Tuesday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> F) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>, the 1751st year of the <a href="/wiki/Common_Era" title="Common Era">Common Era</a> (CE) and <i><a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a></i> (AD) designations, the 751st year of the <a href="/wiki/2nd_millennium" title="2nd millennium">2nd millennium</a>, the 51st year of the <a href="/wiki/18th_century" title="18th century">18th century</a>, and the 2nd year of the <a href="/wiki/1750s" title="1750s">1750s</a> decade. As of the start of 1751, the Gregorian calendar was 11 days ahead of the Julian calendar, which remained in localized use until 1923. In Britain and its colonies, 1751 only had 282 days due to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Calendar_Act_of_1750" title="Calendar Act of 1750">Calendar Act of 1750</a>.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93June">January–June</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1751&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–June">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_25" title="March 25">March 25</a>
<ul>
<li>For the last time, <a href="/wiki/New_Year%27s_Day" title="New Year's Day">New Year's Day</a> is legally on March 25 in <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Wales</a>.</li>
<li>Upon his death, <a href="/wiki/Frederick_I_of_Sweden" title="Frederick I of Sweden">Frederick I of Sweden</a> is succeeded as king by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Adolf_Frederick_of_Sweden" title="Adolf Frederick of Sweden">Adolf Frederick</a>.</li>
</ul>
</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/May_11" title="May 11">May 11</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Pennsylvania" title="Pennsylvania">Pennsylvania</a> <a href="/wiki/Legislature" title="Legislature">legislature</a> grants a charter to <a href="/wiki/Benjamin_Franklin" title="Benjamin Franklin">Benjamin Franklin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Bond_(physician)" title="Thomas Bond (physician)">Dr. Thomas Bond</a> for the establishment in <a href="/wiki/Philadelphia" title="Philadelphia">Philadelphia</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Pennsylvania_Hospital" title="Pennsylvania Hospital">first hospital</a> in the Colonies.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="July.E2.80.93December">July–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1751&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: July–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_28" title="July 28">July 28</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Kartli_(1484%E2%80%931762)" title="Kingdom of Kartli (1484–1762)">Kingdom of Kartli</a> defeats a large army of <a href="/wiki/Tabriz_Khanate" title="Tabriz Khanate">Tabriz Khanate</a> under <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Erekle_II" title="Erekle II">Erekle II</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Kirkhbulakh" title="Battle of Kirkhbulakh">Battle of Kirkhbulakh</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_31" title="July 31">July 31</a> – Fire destroys 1,000 houses in <a href="/wiki/Stockholm" title="Stockholm">Stockholm</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_13" title="August 13">August 13</a> – <a href="/wiki/The_Academy_and_College_of_Philadelphia" title="The Academy and College of Philadelphia">The Academy and College of Philadelphia</a>, predecessor to the private <a href="/wiki/University_of_Pennsylvania" title="University of Pennsylvania">University of Pennsylvania</a>, opens its doors, with <a href="/wiki/Benjamin_Franklin" title="Benjamin Franklin">Benjamin Franklin</a> as president.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_13" title="September 13">September 13</a> – <a href="/wiki/Kalv%C3%A1ria_Bansk%C3%A1_%C5%A0tiavnica" title="Kalvária Banská Štiavnica">Kalvária Banská Štiavnica</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Hungary" title="Kingdom of Hungary">Kingdom of Hungary</a> is completed.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/October_27" title="October 27">October 27</a> – The <a href="/wiki/H%C5%8Dreki" title="Hōreki">Hōreki</a> period begins in <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/December_3" title="December 3">December 3</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Arnee" title="Battle of Arnee">Battle of Arnee</a> in <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Second_Carnatic_War" title="Second Carnatic War">Second Carnatic War</a>): A <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/British_East_India_Company" title="British East India Company">British East India Company</a>-led force under <a href="/wiki/Robert_Clive" title="Robert Clive">Robert Clive</a> defeats and routs a much larger Franco-Indian army under the command of Raza Sahib at <a href="/wiki/Arani,_Chennai" title="Arani, Chennai">Arni</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Date_unknown">Date unknown</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1751&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Date unknown">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Glasgow" title="University of Glasgow">University of Glasgow</a>
<ul>

</ul>
</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/Maria_Theresa_thaler" title="Maria Theresa thaler">Maria Theresa thaler</a> minted; it becomes an international currency.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
