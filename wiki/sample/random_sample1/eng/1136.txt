<p>"<b>In My Head</b>" is a song by the South Korean <a href="/wiki/Rock_music" title="Rock music">rock</a> band <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/CN_Blue" title="CN Blue">CN Blue</a>. It is their major debut single in <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a> under <a href="/wiki/Warner_Music_Group" title="Warner Music Group">Warner Music Japan</a> and fourth single overall. The song was written by Kenji Tamai and Yoshifumi Kanamaru and composed by <a href="/wiki/Jung_Yong-hwa" title="Jung Yong-hwa">Jung Yong-hwa</a>. It was released on October 19, 2011 in 3 different editions: CD+DVD,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-limited_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-limited-1">[1]</a></sup> Regular edition<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-release_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-release-2">[2]</a></sup> and Lawson store limited edition.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lawson_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-lawson-3">[3]</a></sup> A Korean version of the song was released on the group's third <a href="/wiki/Extended_play" title="Extended play">mini-album</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Ear_Fun" title="Ear Fun">Ear Fun</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Composition">Composition</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=In_My_Head_(CNBLUE_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Composition">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"In My Head" was written by Kenji Tamai and Yoshifumi Kanamaru and composed by <a href="/wiki/Jung_Yong-hwa" title="Jung Yong-hwa">Jung Yong-hwa</a>. "Mr. KIA (Know It All)" was written and composed by Jung Yong Hwa and Ryo. "Rain of Blessing" was written by Kenji Tamai, Kaori Fukano and Keisuke and composed by <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jong-hyun" title="Lee Jong-hyun">Lee Jong-hyun</a> and Ryo.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-release_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-release-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Promotions">Promotions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=In_My_Head_(CNBLUE_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Promotions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>To promote the single, the band performed in the TV shows <i>Music Japan</i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Music_Fair" title="Music Fair">Music Fair</a></i> on October 22. They promoted the song in South Korea in a special Korean version of the song in <a href="/wiki/Korean_Broadcasting_System" title="Korean Broadcasting System">KBS</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Music_Bank_(TV_series)" title="Music Bank (TV series)">Music Bank</a> Year-End special</i> and <a href="/wiki/Munhwa_Broadcasting_Corporation" title="Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation">MBC</a>'s <i>Gayo Daejun</i>. The song was chosen to be the ending theme song of <i><a href="/wiki/Supernatural_(U.S._TV_series)#Supernatural:_The_Animation" title="Supernatural (U.S. TV series)">Supernatural: The Animation</a></i>, an animated version of the TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Supernatural_(U.S._TV_series)" title="Supernatural (U.S. TV series)">Supernatural</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Music_video">Music video</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=In_My_Head_(CNBLUE_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Music video">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A 30-seconds version of the music video was released in September 22, 2011<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> and the full music video released on September 26, 2011 on the music channel <i>Space Shower TV</i>. Warner Music Japan uploaded the video on <a href="/wiki/YouTube" title="YouTube">YouTube</a> on October 31st.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-musicvideo_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-musicvideo-6">[6]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Chart_performance">Chart performance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=In_My_Head_(CNBLUE_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Chart performance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The single debuted at number 3 in <a href="/wiki/Oricon" title="Oricon">Oricon</a>'s Daily chart and climbed to number 2 two days after. It debuted at number 4 in Oricon's Weekly chart with 71,200 copies sold on the first week, only behind to <a href="/wiki/Kara_(South_Korean_band)" title="Kara (South Korean band)">Kara</a>'s "<a href="/wiki/Winter_Magic_(song)" title="Winter Magic (song)">Winter Magic</a>", <a href="/wiki/Bump_of_Chicken" title="Bump of Chicken">Bump of Chicken</a>'s "Zero" and <a href="/wiki/NMB48" title="NMB48">NMB48</a>'s "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Oh_My_God!_(NMB48_song)" title="Oh My God! (NMB48 song)">Oh My God!</a>".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> The single peaked number 96 in 2011's Oricon Yearly chart. In the end of October, the single got certified Gold by <i><a href="/wiki/Recording_Industry_Association_of_Japan" title="Recording Industry Association of Japan">RIAJ</a></i> for sold 100,000 copies of the physical single.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-riajgold_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-riajgold-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
