<p><i><b>Infinite Visions</b></i> is a live DVD by <a href="/wiki/Finland" title="Finland">Finnish</a> <a href="/wiki/Power_metal" title="Power metal">power metal</a> band <a href="/wiki/Stratovarius" title="Stratovarius">Stratovarius</a>. The album isn't technically a live album, but more just a collection of home videos by the band from 1988 to 2000. It chronicles the change of the band through those 12 years, from <i><a href="/wiki/Fright_Night_(album)" title="Fright Night (album)">Fright Night</a></i> up to <i><a href="/wiki/Infinite_(Stratovarius_album)" title="Infinite (Stratovarius album)">Infinite</a></i>. The DVD contains live footage from shows and tours, backstage with the band, the recording of <a href="/wiki/Episode" title="Episode">Episode</a> and Infinite and just some random messing around. This DVD also contains the 30 second clip of <a href="/wiki/Sonata_Arctica" title="Sonata Arctica">Sonata Arctica</a> playing Black Diamond live. There is over 2 hours of home footage from the band, along with 6 music videos at the end.</p>
