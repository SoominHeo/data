<p>"<b>It's My Life / Your Heaven</b>" is the eighteenth single and third double A-side single by Japanese pop singer-songwriter <a href="/wiki/Yui_(singer)" title="Yui (singer)">Yui</a>. The single was released on January 26, 2011.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background_and_writing">Background and writing</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=It%27s_My_Life/Your_Heaven&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background and writing">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>When asked about the first A-side single, "It's My Life", Yui responded with that song was created with the feeling in supporting people during hardships.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ucan_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-ucan-1">[1]</a></sup> She also connected this with the early part of her life, when she decided to quit school and to undertake the path of music. Furthermore, in reflecting the song, Yui also talked about the fun part about being a musician, such as receiving goodwill messages or meeting a variety of people.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ucan_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-ucan-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Promotion_and_tie-ins">Promotion and tie-ins</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=It%27s_My_Life/Your_Heaven&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Promotion and tie-ins">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The A-side, "Your Heaven", was tied to Sony Walkman's "PlayYou" commercials, and other A-side was used as Japanese company, U-Can's campaign song for the 2011 year.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> In addition to the "Your Heaven" music video provided in the CD+DVD version of the single, a short movie was also included showing Yui's visit to Sweden for the shooting of the music video.</p>
