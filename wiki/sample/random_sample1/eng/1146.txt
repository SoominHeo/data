<p><b>Jorge Daniel "J. D." Pardo</b> (born September 7, 1980) is an <a href="/wiki/Americans" title="Americans">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Actor" title="Actor">actor</a> born in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Panorama_City" title="Panorama City">Panorama City</a>, <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>, to an <a href="/wiki/Argentines" title="Argentines">Argentine</a> father and a mother from <a href="/wiki/El_Salvador#Demographics" title="El Salvador">El Salvador</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=J._D._Pardo&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>JD Pardo's most recent role was playing "Nate"/Jason in the <a href="/wiki/NBC" title="NBC">NBC</a> sci-fi series, <i><a href="/wiki/Revolution_(TV_series)" title="Revolution (TV series)">Revolution</a></i>, co-starring <a href="/wiki/Billy_Burke_(actor)" title="Billy Burke (actor)">Billy Burke</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Previously he was best known for his role as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gwen_Araujo" title="Gwen Araujo">Edward Araujo, Jr./Gwen Amber Rose Araujo</a> in the Lifetime Network Movie called <i><a href="/wiki/A_Girl_Like_Me:_The_Gwen_Araujo_Story" title="A Girl Like Me: The Gwen Araujo Story">A Girl Like Me: The Gwen Araujo Story</a></i>. Previous roles were on FOX's <i><a href="/wiki/Drive_(2007_TV_series)" title="Drive (2007 TV series)">Drive</a></i> and The CW's <i><a href="/wiki/Hidden_Palms" title="Hidden Palms">Hidden Palms</a></i>. Both series were cancelled after their first seasons. Pardo played Young Santiago in the film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Burning_Plain" title="The Burning Plain">The Burning Plain</a></i> (2008) starring opposite <a href="/wiki/Jennifer_Lawrence" title="Jennifer Lawrence">Jennifer Lawrence</a> with <a href="/wiki/Charlize_Theron" title="Charlize Theron">Charlize Theron</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kim_Basinger" title="Kim Basinger">Kim Basinger</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> JD played the half-vampire Nahuel in the second half of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Twilight_Saga:_Breaking_Dawn_%E2%80%93_Part_2" title="The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn – Part 2">Breaking Dawn</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> and a member of a drug cartel in <i><a href="/wiki/Snitch_(film)" title="Snitch (film)">Snitch</a></i>. Pardo also starred in <a href="/wiki/The_CW" title="The CW">The CW</a> TV series <i><a href="/wiki/The_Messengers_(TV_series)" title="The Messengers (TV series)">The Messengers</a></i> that aired during the 2014-2015 season.</p>
