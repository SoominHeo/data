<p>"<b>Justice to Believe/Aoi Iro</b>" is the 14th single from Japanese <a href="/wiki/Voice_acting_in_Japan" title="Voice acting in Japan">voice actress</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Singer" title="Singer">singer</a> <a href="/wiki/Nana_Mizuki" title="Nana Mizuki">Nana Mizuki</a>.</p>
<p>The single was ranked #4 on the Japanese <a href="/wiki/Oricon" title="Oricon">Oricon</a> charts.</p>
