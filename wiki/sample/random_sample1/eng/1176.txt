<p><b>K'Pop</b> (케이팝, also known as K-population) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korean" title="South Korean">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Dance_music" title="Dance music">dance music</a> group. They disbanded in 2004 after their third album, <i>Memories</i>. Rumors later surfaced that they disbanded due to gay romance that led to cheating and assault.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Members">Members</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=K%27Pop_(band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Members">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><b>Joomin</b> (주민) (born March 1, 1981) is the leader of the band and the main rapper, though sometimes he does get singing parts. His hobbies are computer games, dancing and skiing. His favourite artists are H.O.T., Seo Taeji, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/N%27Sync" title="N'Sync">N'Sync</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/P._Diddy" title="P. Diddy">P. Diddy</a>. He has one younger brother.</li>
<li><b>Donghwa</b> (동화) (born August 25, 1981) is a rapper. His real name is Kim Jongsuk. He has one older brother and likes playing basketball. His favourite artists are H.O.T and Seo Tae Ji.</li>
<li><b>Youngwon</b> (영원) (born September 5, 1982) is the lead singer of K'pop, although has been getting fewer parts to sing. His favourite artists are <a href="/wiki/Brian_McKnight" title="Brian McKnight">Brian McKnight</a> and <a href="/wiki/R._Kelly" title="R. Kelly">R. Kelly</a> and he has one younger brother. Young Won's real name is Lee Hyungu.</li>
<li><b>Yoobin</b> (유빈) (born January 5, 1983) is a rapper and singer. His real name is Lee Yinho. His hobbies are golfing, swimming and bowling. He has one older sister and a dog and his favorite artists are Brian McKnight and <a href="/wiki/Whitney_Houston" title="Whitney Houston">Whitney Houston</a>.</li>
<li><b>Woohyun</b> (우현) (born February 2, 1983) is a singer. His hobby is dancing, and his favourite artists are <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seo_Tai_Ji" title="Seo Tai Ji">Seo Tai Ji</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/H.O.T" title="H.O.T">H.O.T</a>. He has one younger sister.</li>
</ul>
