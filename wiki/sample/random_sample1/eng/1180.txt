<p>The <b>2017 K3 League Basic</b> is the first season of the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=K3_League_Basic&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="K3 League Basic (page does not exist)">K3 League Basic</a>. The top 12 teams from <b>2016 K3 League</b> remained in the league renamed <b>2017 K3 League Advanced</b>, and the remaining 8 teams were relegated to this new league. Yeonggwang FC withdrew this season, and two new teams Busan FC and Pyeongtaek G-SMATT FC joined it.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Competition_format">Competition format</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_K3_League_Basic&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Competition format">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The 2017 season runs from March through to October. The 9 teams compete in a single division. Each team plays each other home and away for a total of 16 games. After the regular season, the top team and the winner of the playoff between 2nd-4th teams will qualify for the 2018 K3 League Advanced.</p>
