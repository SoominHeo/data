<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>KPC</b> is an abbreviation for</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Parsec#Parsecs_and_kiloparsecs" title="Parsec">kiloparsec</a> (kpc), a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Unit_of_measurement" title="Unit of measurement">unit of measurement</a> used in <a href="/wiki/Astronomy" title="Astronomy">astronomy</a></li>
<li>1000 pieces - Thousand pieces (units).</li>
<li>Klebsiella pneumoniae carbapenemase (KPC)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>KPC</b> is an acronym for</p>
<ul>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kaltim_Prima_Coal&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kaltim Prima Coal (page does not exist)">Kaltim Prima Coal</a>, A <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Thermal_coal" title="Thermal coal">thermal coal</a> mining in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kalimantan_Timur" title="Kalimantan Timur">Kalimantan Timur</a>, <a href="/wiki/Indonesia" title="Indonesia">Indonesia</a>.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kampala_Pentecostal_Church" title="Kampala Pentecostal Church">Kampala Pentecostal Church</a>, an English speaking church downtown Kampala, Uganda</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kenai_Peninsula_College" title="Kenai Peninsula College">Kenai Peninsula College</a>, a college with three campuses on the Kenai Peninsula in Alaska</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kenya_Pipeline_Company" title="Kenya Pipeline Company">Kenya Pipeline Company</a>, a state corporation of Kenya</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Key_Product_Characteristic&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Key Product Characteristic (page does not exist)">Key Product Characteristic</a>, A product characteristic for which reasonably anticipated variation could significantly affect the product’s safety or compliance with government standards or regulations, or is likely to significantly affect customer satisfaction with a product</li>
<li>Kiribati Protestant Church, the former name of the <a href="/wiki/Kiribati_Uniting_Church" title="Kiribati Uniting Church">Kiribati Uniting Church</a>, the second-largest religious group in Kiribati</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Beta-lactamase#KPC_.28K._pneumoniae_carbapenemase.29_.28Class_A.29" title="Beta-lactamase">Klebsiella pneumoniae Carbapenemase</a>, the gene for an enzyme produced by some highly antibiotic-resistant bacteria</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kosovo_Protection_Corps" title="Kosovo Protection Corps">Kosovo Protection Corps</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kuwait_Petroleum_Corporation" title="Kuwait Petroleum Corporation">Kuwait Petroleum Corporation</a>, an umbrella company which takes control of all petroleum and oil-related aspects of Kuwait</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kunzang_Palyul_Choling" title="Kunzang Palyul Choling">Kunzang Palyul Choling</a>, a Tibetan Buddhist organization headed by Jetsunma Ahkon Lhamo</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kids_Prefer_Cheese&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kids Prefer Cheese (page does not exist)">Kids Prefer Cheese</a>, An economics blog by <a href="/wiki/Michael_Munger" title="Michael Munger">Michael Munger</a> and Kevin Grier.</li>
</ul>
