<p><i><b>Kis-My-Journey</b></i> is the third <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Studio_album" title="Studio album">studio album</a> by Japanese boy band <a href="/wiki/Kis-My-Ft2" title="Kis-My-Ft2">Kis-My-Ft2</a>. It was released July 7, 2014, under the record label <a href="/wiki/Avex_Trax" title="Avex Trax">Avex Trax</a>. It was released in four different editions:</p>
<ul>
<li>First Edition A（AVCD-93000/B）</li>
<li>First Edition B（AVCD-93001/B）</li>
<li>Regular Edition（AVCD-93002）</li>
<li>Kis-My Shop Edition（AVC1-93003<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<p>The First Edition A packaging came in a pink pouch with Original Sticker Set A, while the First Edition B packaging came in a blue pouch with Original Sticker Set B.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-KMJ_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-KMJ-2">[2]</a></sup> The First Edition A, First Edition B, and first press run of Regular Edition packages（AVCD-93002X）each came with a prize entry serial code.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-KMJ_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-KMJ-2">[2]</a></sup> Each package comes with one of two deluxe 20-page photo booklet, with first editions A and B both including one and regular and Kis-My Shop editions having another.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-KMJ_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-KMJ-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Three days after release, Kis-My-Ft2 started on their first 4-dome tour titled 2014 Concert Tour Kis-My-Journey.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Commercial_performance">Commercial performance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kis-My-Journey&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Commercial performance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
