<p>The <b>2017 K League Classic</b> will be the 35th season of the top division of professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a> since its establishment in 1983 as <a href="/wiki/K_League" title="K League">K League</a> and the fifth season in its current name, the <a href="/wiki/K_League_Classic" title="K League Classic">K League Classic</a>.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/FC_Seoul" title="FC Seoul">FC Seoul</a> are the defending champions.</p>
