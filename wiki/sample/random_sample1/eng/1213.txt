<p><b>LATAM Cargo Chile</b>, formerly <i>LAN Cargo S.A.</i>, is a <a href="/wiki/Cargo_airline" title="Cargo airline">cargo airline</a> based in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Santiago,_Chile" title="Santiago, Chile">Santiago</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chile" title="Chile">Chile</a> and the freight subsidiary of the <a href="/wiki/LATAM_Airlines_Group" title="LATAM Airlines Group">LATAM Airlines Group</a>. It is operating cargo flights within <a href="/wiki/South_America" title="South America">South America</a>, to <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a> and <a href="/wiki/North_America" title="North America">North America</a> from its hubs at <a href="/wiki/Miami_International_Airport" title="Miami International Airport">Miami International Airport</a> and <a href="/wiki/Comodoro_Arturo_Merino_Ben%C3%ADtez_International_Airport" title="Comodoro Arturo Merino Benítez International Airport">Santiago International Airport</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FI_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-FI-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>It is a sister company of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/LATAM_Cargo" title="LATAM Cargo">LATAM Cargo México</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ABSA_Cargo_Airline" title="ABSA Cargo Airline">LATAM Cargo Brasil</a> and <a href="/wiki/LATAM_Cargo_Colombia" title="LATAM Cargo Colombia">LATAM Cargo Colombia</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=LATAM_Cargo_Chile&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The airline is based on the original cargo divisions of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/LAN_Chile" title="LAN Chile">LAN Chile</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ladeco" title="Ladeco">Ladeco</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Fast_Air&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Fast Air (page does not exist)">Fast Air</a>. It also works closely with the other cargo airlines in the group, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ABSA_-_Aerolinhas_Brasileiras" title="ABSA - Aerolinhas Brasileiras">ABSA - Aerolinhas Brasileiras</a>, <a href="/wiki/Florida_West_International_Airways" title="Florida West International Airways">Florida West International Airways</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/MasAir" title="MasAir">MasAir</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FI_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-FI-1">[1]</a></sup> LAN Cargo has been rebranded to LATAM Cargo Chile as part of the merger of its parent <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/LAN_Chile" title="LAN Chile">LAN Chile</a> with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/TAM_Airlines" title="TAM Airlines">TAM Airlines</a> to form <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/LATAM_Airlines" title="LATAM Airlines">LATAM Airlines</a> during 2016.</p>
