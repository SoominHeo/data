<p><br/>
<b>1810 (<a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MDCCCX</a>)</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year" title="Common year">common year</a> <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Monday" title="Common year starting on Monday">starting on Monday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> G) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Saturday" title="Common year starting on Saturday">common year starting on Saturday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> B) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>, the 1810th year of the <a href="/wiki/Common_Era" title="Common Era">Common Era</a> (CE) and <i><a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a></i> (AD) designations, the 810th year of the <a href="/wiki/2nd_millennium" title="2nd millennium">2nd millennium</a>, the 10th year of the <a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">19th century</a>, and the 1st year of the <a href="/wiki/1810s" title="1810s">1810s</a> decade. As of the start of 1810, the Gregorian calendar was 12 days ahead of the Julian calendar, which remained in localized use until 1923.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93March">January–March</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1810&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–March">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_4" title="January 4">January 4</a> – Australian <a href="/wiki/Seal_hunting" title="Seal hunting">seal hunter</a> <a href="/wiki/Frederick_Hasselborough" title="Frederick Hasselborough">Frederick Hasselborough</a> discovers <a href="/wiki/Campbell_Island,_New_Zealand" title="Campbell Island, New Zealand">Campbell Island</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Subantarctic" title="Subantarctic">Subantarctic</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_12" title="January 12">January 12</a> – The marriage of <a href="/wiki/Napoleon" title="Napoleon">Napoleon</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jos%C3%A9phine_de_Beauharnais" title="Joséphine de Beauharnais">Joséphine</a> is annulled.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_20" title="February 20">February 20</a> – <a href="/wiki/County_of_Tyrol" title="County of Tyrol">Tyrolean</a> rebel leader <a href="/wiki/Andreas_Hofer" title="Andreas Hofer">Andreas Hofer</a> is executed.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_4" title="March 4">March 4</a> – <a href="/wiki/Peninsular_War" title="Peninsular War">Peninsular War</a>: The French Army, under the command of <a href="/wiki/Andr%C3%A9_Mass%C3%A9na" title="André Masséna">André Masséna</a>, retreats from Portugal.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_11" title="March 11">March 11</a> – <a href="/wiki/Napoleon" title="Napoleon">Napoleon</a> marries <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Marie-Louise_of_Austria" title="Marie-Louise of Austria">Marie-Louise of Austria</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="April.E2.80.93June">April–June</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1810&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: April–June">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_19" title="April 19">April 19</a> – <a href="/wiki/Venezuela" title="Venezuela">Venezuela</a> achieves home rule: <a href="/wiki/Vicente_Empar%C3%A1n" title="Vicente Emparán">Vicente Emparán</a>, <a href="/wiki/Governor" title="Governor">Governor</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Captaincy_General_of_Venezuela" title="Captaincy General of Venezuela">Captaincy General of Venezuela</a>, is removed by the people of <a href="/wiki/Caracas" title="Caracas">Caracas</a> and a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Military_junta" title="Military junta">junta</a> is installed. Venezuela is the first South American state to proclaim independence from Spain.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_27" title="April 27">April 27</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ludwig_van_Beethoven" title="Ludwig van Beethoven">Beethoven</a> composes his famous piano piece, <i><a href="/wiki/F%C3%BCr_Elise" title="Für Elise">Für Elise</a></i>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_1" title="May 1">May 1</a> – <a href="/wiki/Macon%27s_Bill_Number_2" title="Macon's Bill Number 2">Macon's Bill Number 2</a> becomes law in the United States, intending to motivate <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom_of_Great_Britain_and_Ireland" title="United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland">Britain</a> and France to stop seizing American vessels during the <a href="/wiki/Napoleonic_Wars" title="Napoleonic Wars">Napoleonic Wars</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_3" title="May 3">May 3</a> – <a href="/wiki/Lord_Byron" title="Lord Byron">Lord Byron</a> swims across the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hellespont" title="Hellespont">Hellespont</a> in <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Pocket_On_This_Day_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Pocket_On_This_Day-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_10" title="May 10">May 10</a> – Rev. <a href="/wiki/Henry_Duncan_(minister)" title="Henry Duncan (minister)">Henry Duncan</a> opens the world's first commercial <a href="/wiki/Savings_bank" title="Savings bank">savings bank</a> in <a href="/wiki/Ruthwell" title="Ruthwell">Ruthwell</a>, Scotland.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_18" title="May 18">May 18</a>–<a href="/wiki/May_25" title="May 25">May 25</a> – <a href="/wiki/May_Revolution" title="May Revolution">May Revolution</a>: Armed citizens of <a href="/wiki/Buenos_Aires" title="Buenos Aires">Buenos Aires</a> expel the <a href="/wiki/Viceroy" title="Viceroy">Viceroy</a> and establish a provincial government for <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentina</a> (the <i><a href="/wiki/Primera_Junta" title="Primera Junta">Primera Junta</a></i>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_4" title="June 4">June 4</a> – <a href="/wiki/The_Society_in_Dedham_for_Apprehending_Horse_Thieves" title="The Society in Dedham for Apprehending Horse Thieves">The Society in Dedham for Apprehending Horse Thieves</a> is founded in <a href="/wiki/Dedham,_Massachusetts" title="Dedham, Massachusetts">Dedham, Massachusetts</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_23" title="June 23">June 23</a> – <a href="/wiki/John_Jacob_Astor" title="John Jacob Astor">John Jacob Astor</a> forms the <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Fur_Company" title="Pacific Fur Company">Pacific Fur Company</a>.</li>
<li>June – <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Appert" title="Nicolas Appert">Nicolas Appert</a> publishes <i>L'art de conserver pendant plusieurs années toutes les substances animales ou végétales</i>, the first description of modern food preservation using airtight containers.</li>
<li>April through Summer – <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Hawaii" title="Kingdom of Hawaii">Kingdom of Hawaii</a> unified.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="July.E2.80.93September">July–September</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1810&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: July–September">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_9" title="July 9">July 9</a> – <a href="/wiki/Napoleon" title="Napoleon">Napoleon</a> annexes the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Holland" title="Kingdom of Holland">Kingdom of Holland</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_11" title="July 11">July 11</a> – <a href="/wiki/Frederick_Hasselborough" title="Frederick Hasselborough">Frederick Hasselborough</a> discovers <a href="/wiki/Macquarie_Island" title="Macquarie Island">Macquarie Island</a> in the subantarctic.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_20" title="July 20">July 20</a> – <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Patria_Boba" title="Patria Boba">Patria Boba</a></i>: A junta of seven patriots led by <a href="/wiki/Jos%C3%A9_Acevedo_y_G%C3%B3mez" title="José Acevedo y Gómez">José Acevedo y Gómez</a> assemble in <a href="/wiki/Bogot%C3%A1" title="Bogotá">Bogotá</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Viceroyalty_of_New_Granada" title="Viceroyalty of New Granada">Viceroyalty of New Granada</a> (modern-day <a href="/wiki/Colombia" title="Colombia">Colombia</a>) to declare its independence from the <a href="/wiki/Spanish_Empire" title="Spanish Empire">Spanish Empire</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_2" title="August 2">August 2</a>– 200 citizens are slaughtered in the Royal barracks and the surrounding streets of <a href="/wiki/Quito" title="Quito">Quito</a>, Ecuador by royalist troops.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_6" title="August 6">August 6</a> – The city of <a href="/wiki/Santa_Cruz_de_Mompox" title="Santa Cruz de Mompox">Santa Cruz de Mompox</a>, in modern-day <a href="/wiki/Colombia" title="Colombia">Colombia</a>, declares independence from the <a href="/wiki/Spanish_Empire" title="Spanish Empire">Spanish Empire</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_20" title="August 20">August 20</a>–<a href="/wiki/August_27" title="August 27">27</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Grand_Port" title="Battle of Grand Port">Battle of Grand Port</a>: The French force the British <a href="/wiki/Royal_Navy" title="Royal Navy">Royal Navy</a> fleet attempting to blockade a harbour on <a href="/wiki/Isle_de_France_(Mauritius)" title="Isle de France (Mauritius)">Isle de France (Mauritius)</a> to surrender.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_21" title="August 21">August 21</a> – <a href="/wiki/Charles_XIV_John_of_Sweden" title="Charles XIV John of Sweden">Jean Baptiste Bernadotte</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Marshal_of_France" title="Marshal of France">Marshal of France</a>, is elected <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Crown_Prince" title="Crown Prince">Crown Prince</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Sweden</a> by the Swedish <a href="/wiki/Riksdag_of_the_Estates" title="Riksdag of the Estates">Riksdag of the Estates</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_8" title="September 8">September 8</a> – The <i><a href="/wiki/Tonquin_(1807)" title="Tonquin (1807)">Tonquin</a></i> sets sail from <a href="/wiki/New_York_Harbor" title="New York Harbor">New York Harbor</a> with 33 employees of <a href="/wiki/John_Jacob_Astor" title="John Jacob Astor">John Jacob Astor</a>'s newly created <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Fur_Company" title="Pacific Fur Company">Pacific Fur Company</a> on board. After a 6-month journey around the tip of South America, the ship arrives at the mouth of the <a href="/wiki/Columbia_River" title="Columbia River">Columbia River</a> and Astor's men establish the fur-trading town of <a href="/wiki/Astoria,_Oregon" title="Astoria, Oregon">Astoria</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_16" title="September 16">September 16</a> – <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grito_de_Dolores" title="Grito de Dolores">Grito de Dolores</a></i>: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Miguel_Hidalgo" title="Miguel Hidalgo">Miguel Hidalgo</a>, a Catholic priest from <a href="/wiki/Guanajuato" title="Guanajuato">Guanajuato</a>, incites the revolt that becomes the <a href="/wiki/Mexican_War_of_Independence" title="Mexican War of Independence">Mexican War of Independence</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_18" title="September 18">September 18</a> – <a href="/wiki/Chile" title="Chile">Chile</a> forms its <a href="/wiki/Government_Junta_of_Chile_(1810)" title="Government Junta of Chile (1810)">First National Junta</a>, which is the country's first step towards <a href="/wiki/Chilean_War_of_Independence" title="Chilean War of Independence">its independence</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_23" title="September 23">September 23</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_West_Florida" title="Republic of West Florida">Republic of West Florida</a> declares independence from Spain.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_26" title="September 26">September 26</a> – A new <a href="/wiki/Swedish_Act_of_Succession" title="Swedish Act of Succession">Act of Succession</a> is adopted by the <a href="/wiki/Riksdag_of_the_Estates" title="Riksdag of the Estates">Riksdag of the Estates</a> and <a href="/wiki/Charles_XIV_John_of_Sweden" title="Charles XIV John of Sweden">Jean Baptiste Bernadotte</a> becomes heir to the Swedish throne.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="October.E2.80.93December">October–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1810&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: October–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>October – King <a href="/wiki/George_III_of_the_United_Kingdom" title="George III of the United Kingdom">George III of the United Kingdom</a> is recognized as insane.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/October_12" title="October 12">October 12</a> – First <a href="/wiki/Oktoberfest" title="Oktoberfest">Oktoberfest</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Bavaria" title="Bavaria">Bavarian</a> royalty invites the citizens of <a href="/wiki/Munich" title="Munich">Munich</a> to join the celebration of the marriage of <a href="/wiki/Ludwig_I_of_Bavaria" title="Ludwig I of Bavaria">Crown Prince Ludwig of Bavaria</a> to Princess <a href="/wiki/Therese_of_Saxe-Hildburghausen" title="Therese of Saxe-Hildburghausen">Therese of Saxe-Hildburghausen</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/October_27" title="October 27">October 27</a> – The United States annexes the <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_West_Florida" title="Republic of West Florida">Republic of West Florida</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_17" title="November 17">November 17</a> – <a href="/wiki/Anglo-Swedish_War_(1810%E2%80%9312)" title="Anglo-Swedish War (1810–12)">Anglo-Swedish War (1810–12)</a>: Sweden declares war on the United Kingdom.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_23" title="November 23">November 23</a> – English actress <a href="/wiki/Sarah_Booth" title="Sarah Booth">Sarah Booth</a> debuts at the <a href="/wiki/Royal_Opera_House" title="Royal Opera House">Theatre Royal, Covent Garden</a> in London.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_29" title="November 29">November 29</a>–<a href="/wiki/December_3" title="December 3">December 3</a> – <a href="/wiki/Invasion_of_Isle_de_France" title="Invasion of Isle de France">Invasion of Isle de France</a>: British forces oblige the French to surrender <a href="/wiki/Isle_de_France_(Mauritius)" title="Isle de France (Mauritius)">Isle de France (Mauritius)</a>.</li>
</ul>

<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Amadou_Lobbo" title="Amadou Lobbo">Amadou Lobbo</a> initiates <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Amadu%27s_Jihad" title="Amadu's Jihad">his jihad</a> in present-day <a href="/wiki/Mali" title="Mali">Mali</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ching_Shih" title="Ching Shih">Ching Shih</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cheung_Po_Tsai" title="Cheung Po Tsai">Cheung Po Tsai</a> surrender their pirate fleet to the Chinese government.</li>
<li>The first <a href="/wiki/Steamboat" title="Steamboat">steamboat</a> sails on the <a href="/wiki/Ohio_River" title="Ohio River">Ohio River</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Johann_Wolfgang_von_Goethe" title="Johann Wolfgang von Goethe">Johann Wolfgang von Goethe</a> publishes his <i><a href="/wiki/Theory_of_Colours" title="Theory of Colours">Theory of Colours</a></i>.</li>
<li>General Union of Spinners organizes <a href="/wiki/Strike_action" title="Strike action">strike action</a> to raise wages in the smaller UK <a href="/wiki/Cotton" title="Cotton">cotton</a> centres to the <a href="/wiki/Manchester" title="Manchester">Manchester</a> level.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Saint_Petersburg" title="Saint Petersburg">Saint Petersburg</a> <a href="/wiki/Military_Engineering-Technical_University" title="Military Engineering-Technical University">main military engineering school</a> becomes the first engineering <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Higher_learning_institution" title="Higher learning institution">higher learning institution</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Russian_Empire" title="Russian Empire">Russian Empire</a>, after addition of officers' classes and application of a five-year term of teaching.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Friedrich_Krupp" title="Friedrich Krupp">Friedrich Krupp</a> establishes a steel foundry in <a href="/wiki/Essen" title="Essen">Essen</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Rocky_Point_Manor" title="Rocky Point Manor">Rocky Point Manor</a> is built in <a href="/wiki/Harrodsburg,_Kentucky" title="Harrodsburg, Kentucky">Harrodsburg, Kentucky</a>.</li>
<li>Moose become extinct in the <a href="/wiki/Caucasus" title="Caucasus">Caucasus</a>.</li>
<li>18,000 <a href="/wiki/Angola" title="Angola">Angolans</a> are sold at <a href="/wiki/Rio_de_Janeiro" title="Rio de Janeiro">Rio de Janeiro</a>, Brazil.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Palm_oil" title="Palm oil">Palm oil</a> sales from West Africa to Britain reach 1,000 tons.</li>
<li>4,500 chests of <a href="/wiki/Opium" title="Opium">opium</a> are sold in China.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sake_Dean_Mahomed" title="Sake Dean Mahomed">Sake Dean Mahomed</a> opens the <i><a href="/wiki/Hindoostanee_Coffee_House" title="Hindoostanee Coffee House">Hindoostanee Coffee House</a></i>, the first <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Indian_restaurant" title="Indian restaurant">Indian restaurant</a> in London.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
