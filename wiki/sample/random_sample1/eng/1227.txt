<p><b>LS Group</b> is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Chaebol" title="Chaebol">chaebol</a></i> (conglomerate). It is composed of LS Corp. (<i>see below</i>), Gaon Cable, E1 (LPG), YESCO (LNG) and LS Asset Management (formerly Delta Asset). "LS Corp." is a holding company, which comprises <a href="/wiki/LS_Cable_%26_System" title="LS Cable &amp; System">LS Cable &amp; System</a> (power &amp; communications cables), LS Industrial Systems (electrical equipment &amp; automation systems), LS-Nikko Copper (copper smelter &amp; refiner) and LS Mtron (machinery &amp; components).</p>
<p>LS Group was spun off from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/LG_Group" title="LG Group">LG Group</a> in 2003. LS Group and its subsidiaries have approximately (USD)$25.2 billion in revenue worldwide with about 100 subsidiaries in 25 countries. The company is controlled by the Koo family, the founding family of LG Group.</p>
<p>LS means "Leading Solution".</p>
