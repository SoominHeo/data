<p><i><b>Luna Sea 3D in Los Angeles</b></i> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Live_album" title="Live album">live album</a>, <a href="/wiki/3D_film" title="3D film">3D</a> <a href="/wiki/Concert_film" title="Concert film">concert film</a> and <a href="/wiki/Home_video" title="Home video">home video</a> by Japanese rock band <a href="/wiki/Luna_Sea" title="Luna Sea">Luna Sea</a>. It was filmed in 3-D at the <a href="/wiki/Hollywood_Palladium" title="Hollywood Palladium">Hollywood Palladium</a> in the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> on December 4, 2010, as part of their <a href="/wiki/20th_Anniversary_World_Tour_Reboot" title="20th Anniversary World Tour Reboot">20th Anniversary World Tour Reboot -to the New Moon-</a>.</p>
<p>The album was released on June 1, 2011, while the video began showing in theaters nationwide on June 4.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gravity_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-gravity-1">[1]</a></sup> The album reached number 15 on the <a href="/wiki/Oricon" title="Oricon">Oricon</a> chart.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Oricon_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Oricon-2">[2]</a></sup> The film was later released on <a href="/wiki/DVD" title="DVD">DVD</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Blu-ray_Disc" title="Blu-ray Disc">Blu-ray</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Blu-ray_Disc#Blu-ray_3D" title="Blu-ray Disc">3D Blu-ray</a> on February 22, 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3D_homevideo_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-3D_homevideo-3">[3]</a></sup> The Blu-ray version reached number 48 on Oricon's Blu-ray chart,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Oriconblu_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Oriconblu-4">[4]</a></sup> while the DVD version peaked at number 67.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Oricondvd_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Oricondvd-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
