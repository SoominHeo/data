<p><i><b>Legend of 2PM</b></i> is the second Japanese studio album (fourth album overall) by <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> boy band <a href="/wiki/2PM" title="2PM">2PM</a>. It was released in February 13, 2013 in three editions:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-official_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-official-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li>Regular edition: CD <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-regular_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-regular-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li>Limited Edition A: CD + DVD <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-typea_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-typea-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li>Limited Edition B: CD + CD <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-typeb_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-typeb-4">[4]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<p>There are 9 new tracks in this second Japanese studio album. Tracks from both <a href="/wiki/Beautiful_(2PM_song)" title="Beautiful (2PM song)">Beautiful</a> and <a href="/wiki/Masquerade_(2PM_song)" title="Masquerade (2PM song)">Masquerade</a> are also included in this album. "This Is Love" and "So Bad" were released digitally in <a href="/wiki/ITunes_Store" title="ITunes Store">iTunes Store</a> on February 18–19, 2013, as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Promotional_single" title="Promotional single">promotional single</a> from the album.</p>
