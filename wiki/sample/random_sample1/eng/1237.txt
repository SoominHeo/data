<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lovendor&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The band was formed in 2012 around <a href="/wiki/Morning_Musume" title="Morning Musume">Morning Musume</a> member <a href="/wiki/Reina_Tanaka" title="Reina Tanaka">Reina Tanaka</a>. Originally there were four members: Reina Tanaka (vocals), Marina Okada (vocals), Yuki Uozumi (guitar), Marin Miyazawa (guitar).</p>
<p>In July 2016 it was announced that Yuki Uozumi was to leave the band on September 16.</p>
