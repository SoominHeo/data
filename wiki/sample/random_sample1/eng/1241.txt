<p><i><b>Love Travels At Illegal Speeds</b></i> is the sixth solo <a href="/wiki/Album" title="Album">album</a> by <a href="/wiki/Graham_Coxon" title="Graham Coxon">Graham Coxon</a>. It was released 13 March 2006 in the United Kingdom, the day after Coxon's 37th birthday. The album was produced by <a href="/wiki/Stephen_Street" title="Stephen Street">Stephen Street</a> who also produced Coxon's previous album, <i><a href="/wiki/Happiness_in_Magazines" title="Happiness in Magazines">Happiness in Magazines</a></i>.</p>
