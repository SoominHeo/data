<p><i><b>Here &amp; There – S.E.S. Singles Collection</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Compilation_album" title="Compilation album">compilation album</a> in <a href="/wiki/Japanese_language" title="Japanese language">Japanese</a> released in 2001 by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/S.E.S._(group)" title="S.E.S. (group)">S.E.S.</a> under the <a href="/wiki/Record_label" title="Record label">record label</a> VAP.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Single">Single</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Here_%26_There_%E2%80%93_S.E.S._Singles_Collection&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Single">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Lovin’ You" was the first and only promotional single from the album. Released August 2, 2000, it was S.E.S.'s seventh and last full Japanese single. It has sold approximately 3,000 copies. It was accompanied by a <a href="/wiki/Extended_play" title="Extended play">single EP</a>, containing remixes and instrumentals.</p>
