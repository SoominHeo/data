<p>"<b>Mind Games</b>" is the 28th single by Zard<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and released 7 April 1999 under B-Gram Records label.The single debuted at #1 rank first week. It charted for 6 weeks and sold over 167,000 copies.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> This is last time Zard single reach #1 rank first week in Oricon rankings.</p>
