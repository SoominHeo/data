<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">NBA 25th Anniversary Team</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <b>NBA 25th Anniversary Team</b> was announced on 11 December 1971 to celebrate the 25-year existence of <a href="/wiki/Basketball_Association_of_America" title="Basketball Association of America">BAA</a>/<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/NBA" title="NBA">NBA</a>. This team was meant to be an <a href="/wiki/All-NBA_Team" title="All-NBA Team">All-NBA Team</a> for that period. Up until that time, All-NBA Teams only consisted of 2 teams (First and Second), so this Team also only consists of 2 teams/10 players (4 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Forward_(basketball)" title="Forward (basketball)">Forwards</a>, 2 <a href="/wiki/Center_(basketball)" title="Center (basketball)">Centers</a>, and 4 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Guard_(basketball)" title="Guard (basketball)">Guards</a>). The list excluded players who were still playing in the NBA, such as <a href="/wiki/Wilt_Chamberlain" title="Wilt Chamberlain">Wilt Chamberlain</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jerry_West" title="Jerry West">Jerry West</a>.</p>
<p></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Selection_process">Selection process</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=NBA_25th_Anniversary_Team&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Selection process">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A panel selected 25 nominees from all eligible players, made up from 10 Forwards, 10 Guards, and 5 Centers. Then every NBA player that had been named All NBA 1st Team up to that time gave his vote. The four top voted forwards, two top voted centers, and four top voted guards made the team.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Eligibility_requirements">Eligibility requirements</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=NBA_25th_Anniversary_Team&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Eligibility requirements">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>To be eligible as a nominee, a player must (by 1971):</p>
<ol>
<li>have completed his career, and</li>
<li>have been named All-NBA Team at least once.</li>
</ol>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="NBA_25th_Anniversary_Team">NBA 25th Anniversary Team</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=NBA_25th_Anniversary_Team&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: NBA 25th Anniversary Team">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>List of 25th Anniversary Team players, sorted by position and vote received.</p>
<ul>
<li>F <a href="/wiki/Bob_Pettit" title="Bob Pettit">Bob Pettit</a></li>
<li>F <a href="/wiki/Dolph_Schayes" title="Dolph Schayes">Dolph Schayes</a></li>
<li>F <a href="/wiki/Paul_Arizin" title="Paul Arizin">Paul Arizin</a></li>
<li>F <a href="/wiki/Joe_Fulks" title="Joe Fulks">Joe Fulks</a></li>
<li>C <a href="/wiki/Bill_Russell" title="Bill Russell">Bill Russell</a></li>
<li>C <a href="/wiki/George_Mikan" title="George Mikan">George Mikan</a></li>
<li>G <a href="/wiki/Bob_Cousy" title="Bob Cousy">Bob Cousy</a></li>
<li>G <a href="/wiki/Bill_Sharman" title="Bill Sharman">Bill Sharman</a></li>
<li>G <a href="/wiki/Bob_Davies" title="Bob Davies">Bob Davies</a></li>
<li>G <a href="/wiki/Sam_Jones_(basketball)" title="Sam Jones (basketball)">Sam Jones</a></li>
<li>Coach <a href="/wiki/Red_Auerbach" title="Red Auerbach">Red Auerbach</a></li>
</ul>
<p>Bill Russell was the only unanimous selection to the team. All players of the 25th Anniversary Team have been inducted to Naismith Basketball Hall Of Fame. Furthermore, all nominees of the 25th Anniversary Team besides Feerick and Zaslofky have also been inducted into Naismith Basketball Hall Of Fame.</p>
<p>In 1980, only four players of the 25th Anniversary Team were selected into <a href="/wiki/NBA_35th_Anniversary_Team" title="NBA 35th Anniversary Team">NBA 35th Anniversary Team</a> (Pettit, Cousy, Russell, and Mikan). However, along with them, four other players of this team (Arizin, Jones, Schayes, Sharman) were selected into <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/NBA_50_Greatest_Players" title="NBA 50 Greatest Players">NBA 50 Greatest Players</a> of All-Time in 1996.</p>
<p>Some of the 25th Anniversary Team players and nominees appear on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/NBA_Live" title="NBA Live">NBA Live</a> video game series (since 2000) as players of <a href="/wiki/NBA_Live_Legend_All-Stars_Teams" title="NBA Live Legend All-Stars Teams">NBA Live Legend All-Stars Teams</a> (50's All-Stars or 60's All-Stars).</p>
