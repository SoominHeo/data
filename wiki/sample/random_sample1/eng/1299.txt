<p><b>Im Soeun</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 임소은; born October 7, 1996), better known by her <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage name</a> <b>NC.A</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 앤씨아, <b>N</b>ew <b>C</b>reative <b>A</b>rtist),<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-arirang_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-arirang-3">[3]</a></sup> is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Singer" title="Singer">singer</a>. She debuted in August 2013 with "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/My_Student_Teacher" title="My Student Teacher">My Student Teacher</a>".</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=NC.A&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>NC.A was born on October 7, 1996.</p>
<p>She attended the Osan Middle School and attended Hanlim Multi Art School, where she graduated in February 2015.</p>
<p>Towards her graduation in junior high school, she started going to a music academy. She performed there and someone had it video-taped and uploaded online. The video ended up in the hands of Chae Jong-ju, the CEO of JJHolic Media who later called her in for an audition. Upon passing the auditions, she became a trainee.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-arirang_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-arirang-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2012, she performed in a concert of her label mate <a href="/wiki/Yurisangja" title="Yurisangja">Yurisangja</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-allkpop_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-allkpop-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2013:_Debut_with_.22My_Student_Teacher.22.2C_.22Oh_My_God.22">2013: Debut with "My Student Teacher", "Oh My God"</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=NC.A&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title='Edit section: 2013: Debut with "My Student Teacher", "Oh My God"'>edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On August 11, 2013, NC.A released her debut digital single "My Student Teacher".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-allkpop_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-allkpop-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> The song, which lyrics talked about high school life, was composed by Park Seung-hwa of Yurisangja, written by Lee Ji-won and edited by Seo Jung-jin.</p>
<p>She portrayed the role of a high school student in <a href="/wiki/TVN_(South_Korea)" title="TVN (South Korea)">tvN</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Reply_1994" title="Reply 1994">Reply 1994</a></i>. The drama's producing director <a href="/wiki/Shin_Won-ho_(director)" title="Shin Won-ho (director)">Shin Won-ho</a> saw a pre-debut photo and asked her to audition for the drama.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>On November 15, she released her second single "Oh My God", this time she appeared in the music video.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2014:_.22Hello_Baby.22.2C_Scent_of_NC.A.2C_.22Crazy_You.22.2C_.22Hoo_Hoo_Hoo.22">2014: "Hello Baby", <i>Scent of NC.A</i>, "Crazy You", "Hoo Hoo Hoo"</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=NC.A&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title='Edit section: 2014: "Hello Baby", Scent of NC.A, "Crazy You", "Hoo Hoo Hoo"'>edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On March 21, 2014, NC.A released her digital single "Hello Baby". Following this single, NC.A released her first mini-album on April 9.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> Along with Sungwon from A-Prince, NC.A will be an MC of the new music show <i>Ranking Reformat</i> which will air from Monday to Friday at 12AM KST. The premiere was on April 7.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> On the same date, the teaser of the MV "I'm Different" was released on NC.A's Official YouTube channel. On April 9, they released the music video for "I'm Different".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 28 August NC.A posted on Twitter a message announcing she would release a new single on 2 September.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 2 September NC.A released the digital single "Crazy You" featuring Sims of <a href="/wiki/M.I.B_(band)" title="M.I.B (band)">MIB</a> as a surprise for fans, NC.A released the music video on 20 September.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 18 December NC.A released the MV for <i>Hoo Hoo Hoo</i>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2015.E2.80.93present:_.22Coming_Soon.22.2C_.22Cinderella_Time.22.2C_.22Vanilla_Shake.22.2C_.22Time_To_Be_A_Woman.22">2015–present: "Coming Soon", "Cinderella Time", "Vanilla Shake", "Time To Be A Woman"</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=NC.A&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title='Edit section: 2015–present: "Coming Soon", "Cinderella Time", "Vanilla Shake", "Time To Be A Woman"'>edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 31 December NC.A posted the teaser for her new single "Coming Soon" on her YouTube channel, announcing that it would come out 5 January. <i>Coming Soon</i> is a song about a girl who wants to confess her feelings to the boy she's been admiring. She held her debut stage of "Coming Soon" on KBS's Music Bank 2 January. The song and MV were released 5 January. "Coming Soon" is NC.A's first try at rapping since her debut.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 21 February NC.A announced that she would be coming back with a ballad song on March.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<p>Through NC.A's official Facebook and Twitter it was announced on 25 March that the following day she would release her new single <i>Cinderella Time</i> and they posted the concept images. The single and MV were released on 26 March and in the MV <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yook_Sungjae" title="Yook Sungjae">Sungjae</a> from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/BTOB" title="BTOB">BTOB</a> and Dino from Halo appear.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup></p>
<p>It was announced that NC.A would make her comeback on July <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup> Through her official Facebook, NC.A posted a teaser for <i>Vanilla Shake</i> showing her in a short haircut and later posted that it would be released the 20th of the same month.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 20 July NC.A released her new song <i>Vanilla Shake</i> and Jeong Jae Yong and <a href="/wiki/NS_Yoon-G" title="NS Yoon-G">NS Yoon-G</a> were featured in the MV.</p>
<p>She released the OST "Instinct" for the drama <i>The Law of the Jungle</i> on the 23rd.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup></p>
<p>NC.A was cast in the drama <i>Three by Three</i> together with <a href="/wiki/Fiestar" title="Fiestar">Fiestar</a>'s Jei and <a href="/wiki/Dal_Shabet" title="Dal Shabet">Dal Shabet</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Jiyul_(singer)" title="Jiyul (singer)">Jiyul</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-donga1_18-0"><a href="#cite_note-donga1-18">[18]</a></sup></p>
<p>Through the project to fund albums <i>Makestar</i>, NC.A asked her fans to fund her first full album that will be released in May, 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Makestar_19-0"><a href="#cite_note-Makestar-19">[19]</a></sup></p>
