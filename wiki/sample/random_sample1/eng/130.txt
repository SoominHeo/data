<p>The <b>1842 general election</b> organized the sixth legislature of the <a href="/wiki/July_Monarchy" title="July Monarchy">July Monarchy</a>. The election was held on 9 July.</p>
<p>Only citizens paying taxes were eligible to vote.</p>
