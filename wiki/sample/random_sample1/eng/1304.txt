<p><b>NGC 11</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Spiral_galaxy" title="Spiral galaxy">spiral galaxy</a> located in the <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_(constellation)" title="Andromeda (constellation)">Andromeda</a> constellation. It is located at <a href="/wiki/Right_ascension" title="Right ascension">right ascension</a> 00<sup>h</sup> 08<sup>m</sup> 42.5<sup>s</sup>; <a href="/wiki/Declination" title="Declination">declination</a> +37° 26′ 53″; under <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/J2000.0" title="J2000.0">J2000.0</a> coordinates.</p>
