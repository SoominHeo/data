<p><b>NGC 39</b> (also known as <b>UGC 114</b>, <b>MCG 5-1-52</b>, <b>ZWG 499.76</b> or <b>PGC 852</b>) is a <a href="/wiki/Spiral_galaxy" title="Spiral galaxy">spiral galaxy</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Constellation" title="Constellation">constellation</a> <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_(constellation)" title="Andromeda (constellation)">Andromeda</a>. It was discovered in 1790.</p>
