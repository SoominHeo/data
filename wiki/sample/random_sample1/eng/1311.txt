<p><b>NGC 43</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Lenticular_galaxy" title="Lenticular galaxy">lenticular galaxy</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_(constellation)" title="Andromeda (constellation)">Andromeda</a> constellation. It has a diameter of approximately 27 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kiloparsec" title="Kiloparsec">kiloparsecs</a> (88,000 <a href="/wiki/Light-year" title="Light-year">light-years</a>) and was discovered by <a href="/wiki/John_Herschel" title="John Herschel">John Herschel</a> in 1827.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
