<p><b>NGC 46</b> is an F8 <a href="/wiki/Star" title="Star">star</a> in Pisces, which was identified on October 22, 1852 by <a href="/wiki/Edward_Joshua_Cooper" title="Edward Joshua Cooper">Edward Joshua Cooper</a>, and he incorrectly identified it as a nebula. The star is 962 ± 281 light years away. <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NGCO_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-NGCO-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
