<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Navras</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<div id="contentSub"></div>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>"<b>Navras</b>" is a song from <i><a href="/wiki/The_Matrix_Revolutions" title="The Matrix Revolutions">The Matrix Revolutions</a></i> <a href="/wiki/Soundtrack" title="Soundtrack">soundtrack</a> by <a href="/wiki/Goa_trance" title="Goa trance">goa trance</a> band <a href="/wiki/Juno_Reactor" title="Juno Reactor">Juno Reactor</a>, composed by <a href="/wiki/Don_Davis_(composer)" title="Don Davis (composer)">Don Davis</a> and <a href="/wiki/Juno_Reactor" title="Juno Reactor">Juno Reactor</a> featuring vocal elements by <a href="/wiki/Lakshmi_Shankar" title="Lakshmi Shankar">Lakshmi Shankar</a> and <a href="/wiki/Azam_Ali" title="Azam Ali">Azam Ali</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The song contains an adaption of the <a href="/wiki/Pavamana_Mantra" title="Pavamana Mantra"><i>Asato ma</i> mantra</a> found in the <a href="/wiki/Hindu" title="Hindu">Hindu</a> sacred text the <a href="/wiki/Brihadaranyaka_Upanishad" title="Brihadaranyaka Upanishad">Brihadaranyaka Upanishad</a>, section I.iii.28, used in the soundtrack of the movie <i><a href="/wiki/The_Matrix_Revolutions" title="The Matrix Revolutions">The Matrix Revolutions</a></i>. The song was also a remix of an earlier track called Neodammerung. The name <i>Navras</i> might refer to the notion of nine <i><a href="/wiki/Rasa_(aesthetics)" title="Rasa (aesthetics)">rasas</a></i> (<i>nava rasa</i>) in Sanskrit aesthetics.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (January 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<dl>
<dd>
<dl>
<dd><i>asato mā sad gamaya<br/>
tamaso mā jyotir gamaya<br/>
mṛtyor mā amṛtaṁ gamaya</i></dd>
</dl>
</dd>
</dl>
<dl>

From darkness lead me to light<br/>

</dl>
