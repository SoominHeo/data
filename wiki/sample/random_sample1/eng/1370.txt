<p>"<b>Pop Master</b>" is the 24th <a href="/wiki/Single_(music)" title="Single (music)">single</a> by Japanese singer and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Voice_actress" title="Voice actress">voice actress</a> <a href="/wiki/Nana_Mizuki" title="Nana Mizuki">Nana Mizuki</a>, released on April 13, 2011, together with her 23rd single, "<a href="/wiki/Scarlet_Knight_(song)" title="Scarlet Knight (song)">Scarlet Knight</a>".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Trivia">Trivia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Pop_Master&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Trivia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>The music video from <b><span style="color:red">Pop Master</span></b> was recorded live at <a href="/wiki/Nippon_Budokan" title="Nippon Budokan">Nippon Budokan</a>, and featured three of <i>Cherry Boys</i> musicians: <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kenji_Kitajima&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kenji Kitajima (page does not exist)">Kenji Kitajima</a>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Itaru_Watanabe&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Itaru Watanabe (page does not exist)">Itaru Watanabe</a> (Guitars) and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ryuta_Sakamoto&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ryuta Sakamoto (page does not exist)">Ryuta Sakamoto</a> (Bass). It was a homage to <a href="/wiki/CNN" title="CNN">CNN</a> graphics, entitled <i>Mizuki Nana News Network</i> or <i>MNN</i>.</li>
<li>The song was performed at <a href="/wiki/62nd_NHK_K%C5%8Dhaku_Uta_Gassen" title="62nd NHK Kōhaku Uta Gassen">62nd NHK Kōhaku Uta Gassen</a> on December 31, 2011<span style="display:none"> (<span class="bday dtstart published updated">2011-12-31</span>)</span>.</li>
</ul>
