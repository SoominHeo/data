<p>The first <b>Federal elections</b> were held in Germany on 3 March 1871.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NS_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-NS-1">[1]</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/National_Liberal_Party_(Germany)" title="National Liberal Party (Germany)">National Liberal Party</a> emerged as the largest party in the <a href="/wiki/Reichstag_(German_Empire)" title="Reichstag (German Empire)">Reichstag</a>, with 125 of the 382 seats.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Voter turnout was just 52.0%.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NS_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-NS-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
