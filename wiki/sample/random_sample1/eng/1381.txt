<p><i><b>Parallel Universe</b></i> is the eighth <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Studio_album" title="Studio album">studio album</a> by Japanese group <a href="/wiki/Garnet_Crow" title="Garnet Crow">Garnet Crow</a>. The album was released on December 8, 2010, by <a href="/wiki/Giza_Studio" title="Giza Studio">Giza Studio</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The lead single, "<a href="/wiki/Over_Drive_(Garnet_Crow_song)" title="Over Drive (Garnet Crow song)">Over Drive</a>", was released on April 14, 2010. The album's limited edition comes with a DVD of the band's concert, "GARNET CROW Symphonic Concert 2010 ~All Lovers~". The album reached #10 rank in Oricon for first week with 15,773 sold copies. It charted for 7 weeks and sold 22,911 copies.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
