<p><i><b>Pink Rocket</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">핑크 로켓</span>) is the second <a href="/wiki/Extended_play" title="Extended play">mini-album</a> by South Korean <a href="/wiki/Girl_group" title="Girl group">girl group</a> <a href="/wiki/Dal_Shabet" title="Dal Shabet">Dal Shabet</a>, released April 14, 2011. "Pink Rocket" was used as the lead single, and the official music video was released on April 13.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Promotions for "Pink Rocket" began on April 14 on <i>M! Countdown</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
