<p><b>Nowcom</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korea_Exchange" title="Korea Exchange">KRX</a>: <a class="external text" href="http://eng.krx.co.kr/por_eng/m2/m2_1/m2_1_1/JHPENG02001_01.jsp?isu_cd=A026330" rel="nofollow">026330</a>) founded in 1994, was a Korean IT Company. Effective 2011, this corporate entity changed its name to <b>AfreecaTV Co., Ltd</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korea_Exchange" title="Korea Exchange">KRX</a>: <a class="external text" href="http://eng.krx.co.kr/por_eng/m2/m2_1/m2_1_1/JHPENG02001_01.jsp?isu_cd=A067160" rel="nofollow">067160</a>) after the AfreecaTV Co.,Ltd-ZettaMedia split.</p>
<p>The first service of Nowcom in South Korea was <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Nownuri&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Nownuri (page does not exist)">Nownuri</a></i> which is VT-based online BBS started in 1994 and currently they operate <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Afreeca" title="Afreeca">Afreeca</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/TalesRunner" title="TalesRunner">TalesRunner</a></i>, <i>O2Jam</i> and <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=ClubBox&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="ClubBox (page does not exist)">ClubBox</a></i> as well as <i>Nownuri</i>.</p>
<p>Nowcom was merged by <b><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Wins_Technet&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Wins Technet (page does not exist)">Wins Technet</a></b>, a listed IT company, at January 2008 and listed on <a href="/wiki/KOSDAQ" title="KOSDAQ">KOSDAQ</a> with its original name though and worked together for 3 years, but finally they have been separated again to two companies as they were. Nowcom is still listed on KOSDAQ.</p>
<p>On July 2011, Nowcom announced that its assets would be split into one publicly traded company and the other privately held company.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Another firm known as <b>ZettaMedia</b> was also established at that date and is a new spin-off company created during the split.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Split">Split</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nowcom&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Split">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On July 2011, Nowcom announced that, after concerns from business differences between AfreecaTV and ClubBox, Nowcom's assets would be split into one publicly traded company, one oriented towards webcasting and online game development, and the other privately held company towards contents, media,and web storage. The split formally took place on July, 2011; where the present Nowcom was renamed AfreecaTV Co., Ltd. and consists primarily of <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Afreeca" title="Afreeca">Afreeca</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/TalesRunner" title="TalesRunner">TalesRunner</a></i>, and <i>O2Jam</i>, while a new ZettaMedia was formed to take on <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Nownuri&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Nownuri (page does not exist)">Nownuri</a></i>, PDBox, and <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=ClubBox&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="ClubBox (page does not exist)">ClubBox</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
