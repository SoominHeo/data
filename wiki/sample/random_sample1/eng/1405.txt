<p><i><b>Red Motion</b></i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> is the fourth <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Single_album" title="Single album">single album</a> of <a href="/wiki/South_Korean" title="South Korean">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Girl_group" title="Girl group">girl group</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/AOA_(band)" title="AOA (band)">AOA</a>. It was released on October 14, 2013 by <a href="/wiki/FNC_Entertainment" title="FNC Entertainment">FNC Entertainment</a>. "Confused" was used as the single album's lead song.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Release">Release</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Red_Motion&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Release">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>After the summer release of "Moya" from the band's sub-unit <i>AOA Black</i>, AOA teased new music for an autumn release by showing fans concept images from the albums photoshoot. These images showed the girls outfitted in matching white lace dresses in bright white rooms creating an angelic look.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> A video concept teaser was later released through YouTube on October 3, 2013 and showed the girls in the white lace blind folds from the images.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> A second teaser released on October 7, 2013 showed scenes from the upcoming music video for "Confused". The clip showed all the girls dancing to the chorus of the song, showing off the groups pin-sharp movements.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>AOA released their follow-up <i>Red Motion</i>, featuring the sultry dance title song "Confused". The release also featured a second song, "We Belong Together". Both songs were co-written by the leader of the group, Jimin.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Promotion">Promotion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Red_Motion&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Promotion">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On October 10, 2013 AOA appeared for their comeback stage on <a href="/wiki/M_Countdown" title="M Countdown">M Countdown</a> promoting their song "Confused". The girls also showcased the songs through several other programs including <a href="/wiki/Inkigayo" title="Inkigayo">Inkigayo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Show_Champion" title="Show Champion">Show Champion</a>, <a href="/wiki/Show!_Music_Core" title="Show! Music Core">Show! Music Core</a>, <a href="/wiki/The_Show_(South_Korean_TV_series)" title="The Show (South Korean TV series)">MTV's The Show</a> and <a href="/wiki/Music_Bank_(TV_series)" title="Music Bank (TV series)">Music Bank</a>.</p>
