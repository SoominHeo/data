<p>"<b>River</b>" is the 14th major single by the Japanese idol group <a href="/wiki/AKB48" title="AKB48">AKB48</a>, released on 21 October 2009.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-akihabara48blog_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-akihabara48blog-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-kingrecordsprofile_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-kingrecordsprofile-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> It was the first AKB48's single to top the Oricon weekly singles chart,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> having sold 179,000 copies in its first week.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-oricon20091027_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-oricon20091027-6">[6]</a></sup> Thus it became the group's best selling single, beating "<a href="/wiki/Namida_Surprise!" title="Namida Surprise!">Namida Surprise!</a>", which by then had sold 144,000 copies in 18 weeks.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-oricon20091027_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-oricon20091027-6">[6]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Release">Release</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=River_(AKB48_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Release">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The single was released in two versions: Regular Edition <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">通常盤</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> (CD+DVD, catalog number KIZM-43/4); and Theater Edition <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">劇場盤</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> (CD only, catalog number NMAX-1087). The bonuses for the first-press limited edition included a handshake event ticket for various locations (Sendai, Nagoya, Osaka, Hiroshima, Fukuoka, Sapporo, Tokyo), as well as a voting card for the <i>AKB48 Request Hour Set List Best 100 2010</i>. On the theater edition, the bonuses included a handshake event ticket (Tokyo Big Sight, SKE48 Theater), a special performance ticket lottery (live stage performances, karaoke competition, AKB meeting), and a random member photo.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (August 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>

<p>"<b>River</b>" was the first single of the Indonesian idol group <a href="/wiki/JKT48" title="JKT48">JKT48</a>, released on 11 May 2013 under the label <a href="/wiki/Media_Nusantara_Citra" title="Media Nusantara Citra">Hits Records</a>. It has been performed by selected members from JKT48's Team J and JKT48 trainees. The single is <a href="/wiki/JKT48" title="JKT48">JKT48</a>'s version of <a href="/wiki/AKB48" title="AKB48">AKB48</a>'s song.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (August 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
