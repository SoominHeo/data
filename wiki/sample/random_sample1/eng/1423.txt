<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Computing">Computing</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=RS&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Computing">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
