<p>"<b>Re-sublimity</b>" is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kotoko_(singer)" title="Kotoko (singer)">Kotoko</a>'s second maxi single under Geneon Entertainment, released on November 17, 2004. The title track was used as an opening theme for the anime series <i><a href="/wiki/Kannazuki_no_Miko" title="Kannazuki no Miko">Kannazuki no Miko</a></i> while "Agony", its B-side, was used as the ending theme. "Suppuration -core-" was used as a soundtrack for the anime and a rearrangement of the song "Suppuration" appears in her <i>Hane Live Tour 2004 Limited Album</i>. The single peaked at number eight on the Oricon charts and charted for 12 weeks.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Since it sold 50,119 copies, this is considered Kotoko's best selling single.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (November 2012)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
