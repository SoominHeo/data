<p><b>SBS MTV</b> is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> music channel. Being a South Korean version of American <a href="/wiki/MTV" title="MTV">MTV</a>, it features Korean pop artists, international music, news, and a few reality programs. It also broadcasts programs originally from the American MTV, along with a few Asian programs.</p>
<p>The channel is currently owned by SBS Viacom LLC, a <a href="/wiki/Joint_venture" title="Joint venture">joint venture</a> of <a href="/wiki/SBS_Medianet" title="SBS Medianet">SBS Medianet</a> and <a href="/wiki/Viacom_International_Media_Networks" title="Viacom International Media Networks">Viacom International Media Networks</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=SBS_MTV&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>From 1994 to 1999, MTV, through a partnership deal, showed programs on <a href="/wiki/CJ_Group" title="CJ Group">the CheilJedang group</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Mnet_(TV_channel)" title="Mnet (TV channel)">Mnet</a> network.</p>
<p>In January 2001, the MTV block returned on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ongamenet" title="Ongamenet">OnGameNet</a>, then owned by <a href="/wiki/Orion_Confectionery" title="Orion Confectionery">Orion Group</a>'s <a href="/wiki/On-Media" title="On-Media">On-Media</a>.</p>
<p>In July 2001, On-Media and Viacom launched <b>MTV Korea</b>. Their partnership ended in 2008.</p>
<p>In 2008, MTV Korea was acquired by C&amp;M.</p>
<p>In September 2011, <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Broadcasting_System" title="Seoul Broadcasting System">SBS</a>, a South Korean commercial broadcaster, became the official South Korean partner of <a href="/wiki/Viacom" title="Viacom">Viacom</a>. With this, MTV became a part of SBS, and renamed <b>SBS MTV</b> in November 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
