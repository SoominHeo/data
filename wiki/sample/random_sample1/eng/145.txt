<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Shooting at the 1896 Summer Olympics</h1>

<p>At the <a href="/wiki/1896_Summer_Olympics" title="1896 Summer Olympics">1896 Summer Olympics</a>, five <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Shooting_sports" title="Shooting sports">sport shooting</a> events were contested. These events took place at the newly constructed shooting range at <a href="/wiki/Kallithea" title="Kallithea">Kallithea</a>. They were organized and prepared by the Sub-Committee for Shooting. 61 shooters from seven nations competed.</p>
