<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Weightlifting at the 1896 Summer Olympics</h1>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><div class="tleft"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Weightlifting_pictogram.svg" title="Weightlifting"><img alt="Weightlifting" class="thumbborder" data-file-height="300" data-file-width="300" height="100" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/ac/Weightlifting_pictogram.svg/100px-Weightlifting_pictogram.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/ac/Weightlifting_pictogram.svg/150px-Weightlifting_pictogram.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/ac/Weightlifting_pictogram.svg/200px-Weightlifting_pictogram.svg.png 2x" width="100"/></a></div>
<p>At the <a href="/wiki/1896_Summer_Olympics" title="1896 Summer Olympics">1896 Summer Olympics</a>, two <a href="/wiki/Olympic_weightlifting" title="Olympic weightlifting">weightlifting</a> events were contested (men's one hand lift and two hand lift). The top two places were won by the same two men in each event, though their order was reversed for the two events. The <a href="/wiki/Bronze_medal" title="Bronze medal">bronze medals</a> were split by the two <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greek</a> weightlifters. A total of seven men from five nations competed.</p>
