<p><b>STX Offshore &amp; Shipbuilding</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: STX조선해양) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> shipbuilding company. It is the world's fourth largest shipbuilder,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> owning <a href="/wiki/STX_Europe" title="STX Europe">STX Europe</a>, Europe's second largest shipbuilding group. It has built about 700 ships 40 years in <a href="/wiki/Busan" title="Busan">Busan</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jinhae" title="Jinhae">Jinhae</a>.</p>
<p>The company filled for bankruptcy on 27th, May 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
