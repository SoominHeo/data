<p><b>Super Junior-K.R.Y.</b> is the first official sub-group of South Korean boy band <a href="/wiki/Super_Junior" title="Super Junior">Super Junior</a>. Super Junior-K.R.Y. formed in 2006 and contains three Super Junior's main vocalists, <a href="/wiki/Yesung" title="Yesung">Yesung</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ryeowook" title="Ryeowook">Ryeowook</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kyuhyun" title="Kyuhyun">Kyuhyun</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-smtown_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-smtown-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The trio's first official performance was on November 5, 2006, on <a href="/wiki/Korean_Broadcasting_System" title="Korean Broadcasting System">KBS's</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Music_Bank_(TV_series)" title="Music Bank (TV series)">Music Bank</a></i>, performing "The One I Love", theme song to the Korean television drama <i>Hyena</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Super_Junior-K.R.Y.&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2006.E2.80.932011:_Debut.2C_Soundtrack_participation_and_Asia_Tour">2006–2011: Debut, Soundtrack participation and Asia Tour</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Super_Junior-K.R.Y.&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 2006–2011: Debut, Soundtrack participation and Asia Tour">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Super Junior-K.R.Y. contributed to two tracks in the original soundtrack of <i><a href="/wiki/Hyena_(soundtrack)" title="Hyena (soundtrack)">Hyena</a></i>. The trio also performed in two additional original soundtracks, one track in both the soundtracks of <i>Snow Flower</i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Billie_Jean,_Look_at_Me_(soundtrack)" title="Billie Jean, Look at Me (soundtrack)">Billie Jean, Look at Me</a></i>.</p>
<p>Super Junior-K.R.Y. held their first concert tour in Asia in 2010–2011. The tour commenced in Tokyo in August 2010 and continued onto <a href="/wiki/Kobe" title="Kobe">Kobe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Taipei" title="Taipei">Taipei</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fukuoka" title="Fukuoka">Fukuoka</a>, and Seoul, concluding in Nanjing, China.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Over 22,000 people attended the tour.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Super Junior members <a href="/wiki/Lee_Donghae" title="Lee Donghae">Donghae</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sungmin" title="Sungmin">Sungmin</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Heechul" title="Heechul">Heechul</a> made special guest appearances at the Seoul concerts, as well as labelmates <a href="/wiki/Shinee" title="Shinee">Shinee</a> and <a href="/wiki/TRAX_(band)" title="TRAX (band)">TRAX</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2012.E2.80.932013:_Winter_concert_and_Japanese_debut">2012–2013: Winter concert and Japanese debut</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Super_Junior-K.R.Y.&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 2012–2013: Winter concert and Japanese debut">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Super Junior K.R.Y. Special Winter Concert 2012 was held in <a href="/wiki/Yokohama" title="Yokohama">Yokohama</a> on November 19–21 with 15,000 fans in the audience.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Yokohama2_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-Yokohama2-6">[6]</a></sup> Super Junior K.R.Y. released some tracks and a teaser video from their upcoming Japanese single "<a href="/wiki/Promise_You_(Super_Junior-K.R.Y_song)" title="Promise You (Super Junior-K.R.Y song)">Promise You</a>" for the first time at the concerts.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> Tickets for the concerts in Yokohama went on sale on October 27 and were sold out in three seconds.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> There were performances in <a href="/wiki/Kobe" title="Kobe">Kobe</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tokyo" title="Tokyo">Tokyo</a> in December and January. The tour mobilised 36,000 people in Tokyo, with a total of 72,000 in all three cities and nine concerts. The application for the tickets was over 1,500,000, and proved their popularity in Japan.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<p>"Promise You" was released on January 23, 2013 and debuted at number two on Oricon's daily singles chart. In total, the single album sold 40,645 copies on its first day of sales on January 23.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> In the next day the single album ranked 1st place by selling 15,197 copies.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> In the first week of sales "Promise You" sold 69,067 copies, ranking 2nd place in Oricon Weekly Chart.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<p>On May 6, 2013 member Yesung was enlisted for his mandatory military service. It was made the sub-group hiatus from their activities.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2015.E2.80.9316:_Japan_tour.2C_Join_Hands_and_Asia_tour">2015–16: Japan tour, <i>Join Hands</i> and Asia tour</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Super_Junior-K.R.Y.&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2015–16: Japan tour, Join Hands and Asia tour">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Yesung was completed his mandatory military service on May 4, 2015. The sub-group was continued their activities as trio with held Japan tour started in <a href="/wiki/Yokohama" title="Yokohama">Yokohama</a> on June 2–3 and had 11 total performances in arenas in Kobe, Fukuoka, and Nagoya.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-kpopstarz_13-0"><a href="#cite_note-kpopstarz-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
<p>In July 2015, SM Entertainment announced Super Junior-K.R.Y. kick-started their Asia tour in Seoul at the Olympic Hall in Seoul on August 22 and 23.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup> The trio released Japanese single Japan titled "Join Hands" on 5 August 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup>Member Ryeowook enlisted for his military service on 11 October 2016 and Kyuhyun will enlist for military service in 2017. It was made the sub-group terminated their activities during the member enlistment.</p>
