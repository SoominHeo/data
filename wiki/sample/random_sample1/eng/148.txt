<p>The <b>men's shot put</b> was one of two throwing events on the <a href="/wiki/Athletics_at_the_1896_Summer_Olympics" title="Athletics at the 1896 Summer Olympics">Athletics at the 1896 Summer Olympics</a> programme. 7 athletes took part in the <a href="/wiki/Shot_put" title="Shot put">shot put</a> competition on 7 April. The two <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greek</a> athletes both won medals, with Gouskos battling closely with Garrett of the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> for the longest distance.</p>
