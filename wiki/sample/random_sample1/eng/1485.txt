<p><i><b>Secret Garden</b></i> is the third <a href="/wiki/Extended_play" title="Extended play">mini-album</a> by South Korean <a href="/wiki/Girl_group" title="Girl group">girl group</a> <a href="/wiki/Apink" title="Apink">Apink</a>, released on July 5, 2013. This is their first official release as six-member group and their returned after 14 months since they last promoted their last album "Unee Anee" with "Hush" and "Bubibu" in May 2012. They made first comeback stage on Mnet’s “<a href="/wiki/M_Countdown" title="M Countdown">M! Countdown</a>" one day before the official release. The theme of the album is healing, aim to that will soothe the listeners’ eyes and ears. The album consist of five new songs including the title track “NoNoNo” produced by <a href="/wiki/Shinsadong_Tiger" title="Shinsadong Tiger">Shinsadong Tiger</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Release_and_Promotion">Release and Promotion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Secret_Garden_(EP)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Release and Promotion">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On June 23, A Cube Entertainment announced that Apink will return with a new mini album "Secret Garden" through a video teaser released on their YouTube channel.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-teaser1_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-teaser1-1">[1]</a></sup> The teaser showed six girls, confirming Apink will continue as six without adding new member after Yookyung left the group in April. "Secret Garden" will be their first official release as six-member group and their returned after 14 months since they last promoted their last album "Une Année" with "Hush" and "Bubibu" in May 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-teaser1_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-teaser1-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>On June 28, a second video teaser was released, showing the girls each holding a bouquet of flowers in a green meadow. The teaser revealed the date of upcoming album release will be on July 5.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> It was later revealed that the background music used for the video teasers were snippets of two of the tracks on their up-coming mini-album, titled "Secret Garden" and "U You".</p>
<p>On July 3, it was stated that the group's title track will be "NoNoNo", a song composed by Shinsadong Tiger and the full music video will be released together with the album release on July 5. In a preview of their upcoming music video of the song, Apink members can be seen dancing and singing a small part of their song.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MV_teaser_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-MV_teaser-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>One day before the music video and mini-album was released, Apink performed a snippet of "Lovely Day", a track on their album, in addition to a full performance of "NoNoNo" on Mnet's <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/M!_Countdown" title="M! Countdown">M! Countdown</a></i> on July 4. This was followed by additional comebacks on music programs including <a href="/wiki/Korean_Broadcasting_System" title="Korean Broadcasting System">KBS</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Music_Bank_(TV_series)" title="Music Bank (TV series)">Music Bank</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Munhwa_Broadcasting_Corporation" title="Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation">MBC</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Show!_Music_Core" title="Show! Music Core">Show! Music Core</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Broadcasting_System" title="Seoul Broadcasting System">SBS</a>'s <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Music_Trend" title="The Music Trend">Inkigayo</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-music_show_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-music_show-4">[4]</a></sup> On July 9, Apink's "NoNoNo" won 1st place on Music Bank.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Music_Bank_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Music_Bank-5">[5]</a></sup> On July 5, they released the physical mini album, digital single and the music video for "NoNoNo".</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Concept">Concept</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Secret_Garden_(EP)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Concept">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In regards to the theme of their album, A Cube Entertainment stated "The theme of the Secret Garden album is healing, and more than anything shocking, it’ll be an album that will make you feel refreshed. You can anticipate Apink’s improved skills that improved over the year of preparation."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MV_teaser_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-MV_teaser-3">[3]</a></sup> They added, "With the meaning of “Apink’s Establishment”, this album “Secret Garden” will not be a transformation, but it will be an album filled with an innocent music genre, perfect for the girls, that will soothe the listeners’ eyes and ears."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Album_Info_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-Album_Info-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>On July 10, Apink members sent healing messages for their fans. In the picture uploaded by A Cube, each of Apink member can be seen holding a personally decorated for their fans. their healing song "NoNoNo" revealed each of their own healing messages through their agency.The members wrote messages such as Naeun brightened up their day by telling fans to "Cheer UP!" Chorong assured, "Everything will be okay~ Pat Pat." Namjoo gave encouraging advice with the message: "Always Smile." Bomi suggested a "Positive mindset." Eunji let fans know, "I'm by your side. I love you," and Hayoung declared, "I will be your light." <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Stage_Outfit">Stage Outfit</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Secret_Garden_(EP)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Stage Outfit">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>For "Secret Garden" promotion, Apink incorporated 2013 summer fashion trend for their stage outfit. The aim is to achieve a girlish and feminine look that matched with the album concept and summer season. Some of fashion concept such as “Stripe &amp; Lace”, a combination of Black &amp; White striped pattern pants with lace and sporty baseball uniform for the top to finish a girlish look; “Pastel color”, warm and bright pastel color outfit that perfect for summer season;“Flower pattern &amp; Crop”, a trendy crop top with flower pattern that will give a feminine look.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Album_Cover_and_Artwork">Album Cover and Artwork</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Secret_Garden_(EP)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Album Cover and Artwork">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Apink released album jacket photos teaser through Naver Music online on June 27 through Naver Music Online, revealed both group and individual close up shots as they posed in a green field. On July 2, album cover for "Secret Garden" was released showing Apink member setting up a lemonade stand for the summer.and showed off their innocent and pure image.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-album_cover_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-album_cover-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p>Member Naeun had personally designed the album cover of Apink’s new 3rd mini album ‘Secret Garden’. After the album release, many fans asked if one of Apink member, Naeun participated in the album cover design after seeing the name Son Naeun credited for ‘Art Work &amp; Design’. On July 11, A Cube replied, “The Son Naeun listed on the credits is indeed the same Son Naeun from Apink. She draws really well, so we suggested that she could design the album cover this time. She personally agreed to it in a positive light and went ahead with it. Everyone will be able to find for themselves the art work that Son Naeun did within the album.”<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Songs_and_Music_Video">Songs and Music Video</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Secret_Garden_(EP)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Songs and Music Video">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="NoNoNo">NoNoNo</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Secret_Garden_(EP)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: NoNoNo">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/No_No_No_(Apink_song)" title="No No No (Apink song)">No No No (Apink song)</a></div>
<p>The song was written and produced by <a href="/wiki/Shinsadong_Tiger" title="Shinsadong Tiger">Shinsadong Tiger</a> and the music video was created by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ZanyBros" title="ZanyBros">ZanyBros</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Zany_Bros._12-0"><a href="#cite_note-Zany_Bros.-12">[12]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Shinsadong_Tiger" title="Shinsadong Tiger">Shinsadong Tiger</a> previously worked with Apink for "My My" and "Wishlist".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-album_cover_10-1"><a href="#cite_note-album_cover-10">[10]</a></sup> There are two version for the song music video, the full version released on July 5, while the dance choreography version on July 11.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Dance_Ver_13-0"><a href="#cite_note-Dance_Ver-13">[13]</a></sup> "NoNoNo" was the group's highest charting single, peaking at number 2 on 'Billboard's <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/K-Pop_Hot_100" title="K-Pop Hot 100">K-Pop Hot 100</a> and number 3 on Gaon Single Chart.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Billboard1_14-0"><a href="#cite_note-Billboard1-14">[14]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GaonWeek29_15-0"><a href="#cite_note-GaonWeek29-15">[15]</a></sup> On the year-end charts, "NoNoNo" placed at number 12 on the K-Pop Hot 100,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BB_YE_16-0"><a href="#cite_note-BB_YE-16">[16]</a></sup> and number 33 on Gaon's digital chart.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gaondigitalyearend_17-0"><a href="#cite_note-gaondigitalyearend-17">[17]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gaonyearend_18-0"><a href="#cite_note-gaonyearend-18">[18]</a></sup> A Japanese version of the song was released on October 22, 2014.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Secret_Garden">Secret Garden</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Secret_Garden_(EP)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Secret Garden">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>"Secret Garden" composed by ZigZag Note is a pop ballad song with a medium tempo that showcases Apink member's vocals.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup> The lyrics portrayed emotion of a girl who finally ready to open her heart to someone she love.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Album_Info_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-Album_Info-6">[6]</a></sup> On July 20, the group released the music video for their song, which was also created by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ZanyBros" title="ZanyBros">ZanyBros</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Music_Bank_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-Music_Bank-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Zany_Bros._12-1"><a href="#cite_note-Zany_Bros.-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="U_You">U You</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Secret_Garden_(EP)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: U You">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Music video for "U You" first revealed exclusively for the fans during Apink’s first fan meeting on August 31. It was later officially released on September 3 at their YouTube channel. Using the similar set with title song "NoNoNo", the video showed Apink members having fun playing together.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-20"><a href="#cite_note-20">[20]</a></sup></p>
