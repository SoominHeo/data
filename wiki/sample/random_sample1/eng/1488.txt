<p><b>"Show Me Your Love"</b> is a joint <a href="/wiki/CD_single" title="CD single">CD single</a> by <a href="/wiki/TVXQ" title="TVXQ">TVXQ</a> and <a href="/wiki/Super_Junior" title="Super Junior">Super Junior 05</a>, released on December 15, 2005. The bilingual single in <a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a> and <a href="/wiki/English_language" title="English language">English</a> peaked at #1 on the MIAK K-pop album charts and sold 49,945 units by the end of the year.</p>
<p>The rap lyrics for "Show Me Your Love" was written by Super Junior 05 members <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Heechul" title="Heechul">Heechul</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shindong" title="Shindong">Shindong</a> and <a href="/wiki/Eunhyuk" title="Eunhyuk">Eunhyuk</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Music_video">Music video</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Show_Me_Your_Love_(TVXQ_and_Super_Junior_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Music video">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The music video starts with TVXQ and Super Junior taking photos for a winter photoshoot. Super Junior becomes exhausted and they retire to their seats. TVXQ brings up their spirit by starting the song, saying that TVXQ now has a new family, Super Junior. The two groups sing together under snow, in chapels, on ships, and other similar places that are surrounded by Christmas spirits. Most of the filming took place in Lotte World.</p>
