<p>The <b>men's team horizontal bar</b> was the second of eight <a href="/wiki/Gymnastics" title="Gymnastics">gymnastics</a> events on the <a href="/wiki/Gymnastics_at_the_1896_Summer_Olympics" title="Gymnastics at the 1896 Summer Olympics">Gymnastics at the 1896 Summer Olympics</a> programme. It was conducted on 9 April. Only one team, from <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>, competed.</p>
