<p>"<b>Silent Bible</b>" is the 22nd <a href="/wiki/Single_(music)" title="Single (music)">single</a> by Japanese singer and <a href="/wiki/Voice_acting_in_Japan" title="Voice acting in Japan">voice actress</a> <a href="/wiki/Nana_Mizuki" title="Nana Mizuki">Nana Mizuki</a>, released on February 10, 2010 by <a href="/wiki/King_Records_(Japan)" title="King Records (Japan)">King Records</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> This is the first single which songs were featured in two different <a href="/wiki/Video_game" title="Video game">video games</a> created by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Namco_Bandai" title="Namco Bandai">Namco Bandai</a> Games.</p>
