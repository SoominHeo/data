<p><i><b>SpeXial</b></i> is Taiwanese <a href="/wiki/Mandopop" title="Mandopop">Mandopop</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Boyband" title="Boyband">boyband</a> <a href="/wiki/SpeXial" title="SpeXial">SpeXial</a>'s debut Mandarin studio album produced by Jeremy Ji, a famous Taiwanese Mandopop songwriter. It was released on December 7, 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-fan_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-fan-1">[1]</a></sup> The first promotional single is a lyric song "Celebrate Loneliness" (慶祝寂寞).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> "Gone Mad" (發飆) - SpeXial version, the theme song of Idol Drama <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=KO_one_2&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="KO one 2 (page does not exist)">KO one 2</a></i>, is the second promotional single. The third promotional single is an electronic dance song "Super Style",<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> which is also the theme song of Idol Drama <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=KO_One_Re-act&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="KO One Re-act (page does not exist)">KO One Re-act</a></i>. This album was funded by “2012 Funding the Production and Marketing of Outstanding Popular Music” of Bureau of Audiovisual and Music Industry Development.</p>
