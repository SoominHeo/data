<p><i><b>Steelheart</b></i> was the first full-length album by American <a href="/wiki/Rock_music" title="Rock music">rock</a> band <a href="/wiki/Steelheart" title="Steelheart">Steelheart</a>. It was released on May 10, 1990 and later re-released with a new cover in 1991. It reached #40 on the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_200" title="Billboard 200"><i>Billboard</i> 200</a>. The album was certified Gold by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/RIAA" title="RIAA">RIAA</a> in 1991.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Note that the Bruce Dickinson who co-produced this album is an American producer, not the singer for British heavy metal band <a href="/wiki/Iron_Maiden" title="Iron Maiden">Iron Maiden</a>.</p>
