<p>"<b>Sweet Dreams (Ola Ola E)</b>" is a song recorded by German <a href="/wiki/Eurodance" title="Eurodance">Eurodance</a> group <a href="/wiki/La_Bouche" title="La Bouche">La Bouche</a>. It was originally released in March 1994 as the lead single from their album of the same name. It was released in North America in January 1996. It reached number one in both <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a> and on the Canadian dance chart and peaked at number 13 in the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>. In Australia, "Sweet Dreams" peaked at number eight and was certified certified gold by <a href="/wiki/Australian_Recording_Industry_Association" title="Australian Recording Industry Association">ARIA</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aria96_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-aria96-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
