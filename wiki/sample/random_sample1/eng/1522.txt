<p><b>TESIS Aviation Enterprise</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Cargo_airline" title="Cargo airline">cargo airline</a> based in Moscow, Russia. It operated domestic and international charter cargo flights from Moscow to destinations including China, India, <a href="/wiki/United_Arab_Emirates" title="United Arab Emirates">United Arab Emirates</a> and Turkey. It also flies passenger charters around the world. It was established and started operations in November 1992. Its main bases were <a href="/wiki/Domodedovo_International_Airport" title="Domodedovo International Airport">Domodedovo International Airport</a>, Moscow and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Irkutsk_Airport" title="Irkutsk Airport">Irkutsk Airport</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ATM_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-ATM-1">[1]</a></sup> The Russian aviation authority is suspending flights effective 17 October 2008.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The airline has not been taken over by Russian aviation fuel supplier TOAP that has blamed the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Credit_crisis" title="Credit crisis">credit crisis</a> for the failure of the plan. It has then suspended operations with its two remaining B747-200F freighters on October 17, 2008.</p>
