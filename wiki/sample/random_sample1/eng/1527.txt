<p><i><b>Time</b></i> (stylized as <i><b>TIME</b></i>) is the sixth Japanese studio album (twelfth overall) by South Korean pop duo <a href="/wiki/TVXQ" title="TVXQ">Tohoshinki</a>, released by <a href="/wiki/Avex_Trax" title="Avex Trax">Avex Trax</a> on March 6, 2013. The record was released in four physical versions, each with a theme cover – Version A (Past), a CD+DVD version with music videos; Version B (Present), another CD+DVD version with off-shot movies and live performances; Version C (Future), a CD only version with two bonus tracks; and Version D, a <i>Bigeast</i> fan club limited edition. Musically, <i>Time</i> is primarily an <a href="/wiki/Electropop" title="Electropop">electropop</a> album, with <a href="/wiki/Dubstep" title="Dubstep">dubstep</a>, <a href="/wiki/Electronic_dance_music" title="Electronic dance music">electronic dance music</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Contemporary_R%26B" title="Contemporary R&amp;B">R&amp;B</a> influences.</p>
<p><i>Time</i> was Tohoshinki's fastest-selling studio album, selling over 160,000 copies on the first day and over 240,000 copies within the first week of release.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-allkpop1_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-allkpop1-1">[1]</a></sup> The album was also Tohoshinki's third consecutive album to debut at the top of the <a href="/wiki/Oricon_Albums_Chart" title="Oricon Albums Chart">Oricon Albums Chart</a> and the <i><a href="/wiki/Billboard_(magazine)" title="Billboard (magazine)">Billboard</a></i> Japan Top Albums.</p>
<p>The album spawned four hit singles, all of which peaked within the Top 2 on the Oricon Weekly Charts and sold over 150,000 copies each.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Time_(TVXQ_album)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It was first announced that Tohoshinki would be releasing their sixth Japanese album on January 16, 2013 through their official Japanese site. The album's title was revealed to be <i>Time</i> and that its release date was March 6, 2013. It was also stated that the album would come in four versions: CD version, 2 CD+DVD versions, and a special BigEast version.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Commercial_performance">Commercial performance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Time_(TVXQ_album)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Commercial performance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The album was commercial success selling 160,719 copies during the first day of release, thus becoming their fastest-selling Japanese studio album and second-fastest selling Japanese release to date.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-allkpop1_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-allkpop1-1">[1]</a></sup> It sold a further 35,930 copies on the second day, bringing total sales over the first 48 hours to 196,649 copies.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-oricon1_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-oricon1-3">[3]</a></sup> It sold a further 13,781 copies on the third day of sale,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> bringing the three-day sales total to 210,430 copies. Displaying their immense popularity in Japan, this album also marked TVXQ's 2nd grab for the first place on the Oricon Monthly Chart with over 277,000 copies sold, beating out Kiss My FT2, and others. Until now, Time has sold over 281,260 copies.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-oricon2_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-oricon2-5">[5]</a></sup> As of June 21, 2013, Time is the fourth biggest-selling album of the year in Japan with total sales of 292,632 copies according to Oricon.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
