<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">TNA Against All Odds</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Against All Odds</b> was an annual <a href="/wiki/Professional_wrestling" title="Professional wrestling">professional wrestling</a> <a href="/wiki/Pay-per-view" title="Pay-per-view">pay-per-view</a> (PPV) <a href="/wiki/List_of_TNA_pay-per-view_events" title="List of TNA pay-per-view events">event</a> held by <a href="/wiki/Total_Nonstop_Action_Wrestling" title="Total Nonstop Action Wrestling">Total Nonstop Action Wrestling</a> in the month of February. It was established in 2005 as TNA's fourth pay-per-view event as part of the inaugural series of monthly pay-per-views put on by the promotion. All Against All Odds PPVs but one have been held at the Impact! Zone. In December 2012, TNA announced that the event was canceled.</p>
