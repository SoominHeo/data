<p><i><b>The Very Best Of</b></i> (released as <i><b>The Complete Greatest Hits</b></i> in the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">UK</a>, <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>, and <a href="/wiki/New_Zealand" title="New Zealand">New Zealand</a>) is a two-disc compilation album by the <a href="/wiki/Eagles_(band)" title="Eagles (band)">Eagles</a>, released in <a href="/wiki/2003_in_music" title="2003 in music">2003</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> This album combines all tracks that appeared on the two previously released Eagles greatest hits albums <i>(<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Their_Greatest_Hits_(1971-1975)" title="Their Greatest Hits (1971-1975)">Their Greatest Hits (1971-1975)</a></i> &amp; <i><a href="/wiki/Eagles_Greatest_Hits,_Vol._2" title="Eagles Greatest Hits, Vol. 2">Eagles Greatest Hits, Vol. 2</a>)</i>, along with other singles not included on the first two compilations, album tracks and the new track "Hole in the World".</p>
<p><i>The Very Best Of</i> was also released as a limited-edition three-disc set with the third disc being a bonus DVD containing the video for the new song "<a href="/wiki/Hole_in_the_World" title="Hole in the World">Hole in the World</a>," as well as a making of the video featurette and "Backstage Pass to Farewell 1."</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Commercial_performance">Commercial performance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Very_Best_Of_(Eagles_album)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Commercial performance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The album debuted on the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_200" title="Billboard 200"><i>Billboard</i> 200</a> on November 8, 2003 at number 3, with 162,000 copies sold. It spent 62 weeks on the chart. The album was certified and awarded gold, platinum, and double platinum records by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/RIAA" title="RIAA">RIAA</a> on December 17, 2003, and on December 13, 2004, it achieved triple platinum status. The album has sold over 5 million units in the United States.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-the_boot_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-the_boot-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>As of December 2007, it has spent over 325 weeks in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Irish_Album_Charts" title="Irish Album Charts">Irish Album Charts</a>, effectively not having left the chart since its release. In the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">UK</a> the album (as <i>The Complete Greatest Hits</i>) entered the charts on November 1, 2003 at its initial number 27 peak position, the album did however re-enter the charts in June 2006 when it peaked at number 9 on the <a href="/wiki/UK_Albums_Chart" title="UK Albums Chart">UK Albums Chart</a>.</p>
