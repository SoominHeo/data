<p>The <b>1972 UEFA European Football Championship</b> final tournament was held in <a href="/wiki/Belgium" title="Belgium">Belgium</a>. This was the fourth <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UEFA_European_Football_Championship" title="UEFA European Football Championship">European Football Championship</a>, held every four years and endorsed by <a href="/wiki/UEFA" title="UEFA">UEFA</a>. The final tournament took place between 14 and 18 June 1972.</p>
<p>At the time, only four countries could play the final tournament which meant that there were only the semi-finals, the final and the third place match.</p>
<p>The hosts were only announced after the qualifying round, which meant all teams had to participate in the qualification process for the final stage.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Belgium was chosen among three candidates; the other bids came from England and Italy,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> whose teams did not reach the semi-finals.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Germany_national_football_team" title="Germany national football team">West Germany</a> won the tournament, beating the <a href="/wiki/Soviet_Union_national_football_team" title="Soviet Union national football team">Soviet Union</a> 3–0 in the final, with goals coming from <a href="/wiki/Gerd_M%C3%BCller" title="Gerd Müller">Gerd Müller</a> (twice) and <a href="/wiki/Herbert_Wimmer" title="Herbert Wimmer">Herbert Wimmer</a> at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Heysel_Stadium" title="Heysel Stadium">Heysel Stadium</a> in <a href="/wiki/Brussels" title="Brussels">Brussels</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying_round">Qualifying round</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=UEFA_Euro_1972&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Qualifying round">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/UEFA_Euro_1972_qualifying" title="UEFA Euro 1972 qualifying">UEFA Euro 1972 qualifying</a></div>
<p>The qualifying round was played throughout 1970 and 1971 (group phase), and 1972 (quarter-finals). There were eight qualifying groups of four teams each. The matches were played in a home-and-away basis. Victories were worth 2 points, draws 1 point, and defeats 0 points. Only group winners could qualify for the quarter-finals. The quarter-finals were played in two legs on a home-and-away basis. The winners of the quarter-finals would go through to the final tournament.</p>
