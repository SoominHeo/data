<p>The draw for the <b>qualifiers for <a href="/wiki/UEFA_Euro_1988" title="UEFA Euro 1988">UEFA Euro 1988</a></b> took place on 14 February 1986, in <a href="/wiki/Frankfurt" title="Frankfurt">Frankfurt</a>. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/West_Germany_national_football_team" title="West Germany national football team">West Germany</a> qualified automatically as hosts of the competition. 32 teams entered the draw.</p>
<p>The qualifiers, consisting of 32 teams divided into seven groups; three of four teams and four of five teams, were played in 1986 and 1987. Each group winner progressed to the finals.</p>
