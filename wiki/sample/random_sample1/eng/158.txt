<p>The <b>men's 200 metres</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Sprint_(running)" title="Sprint (running)">sprinting</a> event on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Athletics_(sport)" title="Athletics (sport)">athletics programme</a> at the <a href="/wiki/1900_Summer_Olympics" title="1900 Summer Olympics">1900 Summer Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>. It was held on July 22, 1900, well after most of the rest of the athletics events. The 1900 Games were the first time the 200 metres was contested. The races were held on a track of 500 metres in circumference. Eight athletes from seven nations competed.</p>

<p><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/William_Holland_(athlete)" title="William Holland (athlete)">William Holland</a> set the first Olympic record when he won the first heat of the first round in 24.0 seconds. In the final <a href="/wiki/Walter_Tewksbury" title="Walter Tewksbury">Walter Tewksbury</a> set a new Olympic record with 22.2 seconds.</p>
