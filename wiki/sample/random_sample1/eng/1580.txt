<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">1965–66 European Cup Winners' Cup</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

</div>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <b>season 1965-66</b> of the <a href="/wiki/UEFA_Cup_Winners%27_Cup" title="UEFA Cup Winners' Cup">European Cup Winners' Cup</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> club tournament was won by <a href="/wiki/Borussia_Dortmund" title="Borussia Dortmund">Borussia Dortmund</a> in an <a href="/wiki/Overtime_(sports)#Association_football" title="Overtime (sports)">extra-time</a> final victory at <a href="/wiki/Hampden_Park" title="Hampden Park">Hampden Park</a> against <a href="/wiki/Liverpool_F.C." title="Liverpool F.C.">Liverpool</a>. This edition of the European Cup Winners' Cup was the very first competition in history to apply the <a href="/wiki/Away_goals_rule" title="Away goals rule">away goals rule</a>.</p>
