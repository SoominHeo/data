<p>The <b>men's triple jump</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Track_and_field" title="Track and field">track &amp; field athletics</a> event at the <a href="/wiki/1900_Summer_Olympics" title="1900 Summer Olympics">1900 Summer Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>. It was held on July 16, 1900. 13 athletes from six nations competed in the <a href="/wiki/Triple_jump" title="Triple jump">triple jump</a>.</p>
