<p><b><a href="/wiki/UTC_Power" title="UTC Power">UTC</a>+09:00</b> is an identifier for a <a href="/wiki/UTC_offset" title="UTC offset">time offset from UTC</a> of +09.</p>
<p>During Japan occupations (of <a href="/wiki/Japanese_occupation_of_British_Borneo" title="Japanese occupation of British Borneo">Borneo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japanese_occupation_of_Burma" title="Japanese occupation of Burma">Burma</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japanese_occupation_of_Hong_Kong" title="Japanese occupation of Hong Kong">Hong Kong</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japanese_occupation_of_the_Dutch_East_Indies" title="Japanese occupation of the Dutch East Indies">Dutch East Indies</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japanese_occupation_of_Malaya" title="Japanese occupation of Malaya">Malaya</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japanese_occupation_of_the_Philippines" title="Japanese occupation of the Philippines">Philippines</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japanese_occupation_of_Singapore" title="Japanese occupation of Singapore">Singapore</a>, and <a href="/wiki/French_Indochina" title="French Indochina">French Indochina</a>), it was used as a common time with Tokyo until the fall of <a href="/wiki/Empire_of_Japan" title="Empire of Japan">Empire of Japan</a>.</p>
