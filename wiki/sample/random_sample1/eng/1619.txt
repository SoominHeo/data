<p><b>U Airlines</b> (<a href="/wiki/Thai_language" title="Thai language">Thai</a>: <span lang="th" xml:lang="th">ยู แอร์ไลน์</span>) is an <a href="/wiki/Airline" title="Airline">airline</a> based in <a href="/wiki/Thailand" title="Thailand">Thailand</a>, which began operations in April 2012. At first U Airlines operated only charter flights, but it plans to offer scheduled services.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=U_Airlines&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>U Airlines Co. Ltd. was founded in April 2012 with <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Serm_Srisuwan&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Serm Srisuwan (page does not exist)">Serm Srisuwan</a> as the President of the company. The head office of U Airlines is located at 246 Time Square Building 15th FL Sukhumwit 12 Klong Toei, Bangkok. In August 2012, U Airlines received its first aircraft, an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Airbus_A320-200" title="Airbus A320-200">Airbus A320-200</a> and the inaugural flight was on September 16, 2012 with Bangkok-Yancheng route at <a href="/wiki/Don_Mueang_International_Airport" title="Don Mueang International Airport">Don Mueang International Airport</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Fleet">Fleet</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=U_Airlines&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Fleet">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>As of August 2014, U Airlines has stored it sole aircraft.</p>
