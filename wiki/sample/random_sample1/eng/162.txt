<p>One person from <b><a href="/wiki/Peru" title="Peru">Peru</a></b> competed at the <b><a href="/wiki/1900_Summer_Olympics" title="1900 Summer Olympics">1900 Summer Olympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sports-reference_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-sports-reference-1">[1]</a></sup> However, the <a href="/wiki/International_Olympic_Committee" title="International Olympic Committee">International Olympic Committee</a> currently does not show Peru as having participated in the 1900 Games.</p>
