<p><i><b>Unplugged</b></i> is the eighth album by <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Korean_hip_hop" title="Korean hip hop">hip-hop</a> duo <a href="/wiki/Leessang" title="Leessang">Leessang</a>. The album was released on May 25, 2012. The album contains 13 songs.</p>
