<p>The <b>WAFU U-20 Championship</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">association football</a> tournament that is contested between West African nations. It was funded by <a href="/wiki/Economic_Community_of_West_African_States" title="Economic Community of West African States">The Economic Community Of West African States</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
