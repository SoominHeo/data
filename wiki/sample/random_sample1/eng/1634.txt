<p><b>WASP-12</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Apparent_magnitude" title="Apparent magnitude">magnitude</a> 11 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yellow_dwarf" title="Yellow dwarf">yellow dwarf</a> <a href="/wiki/Star" title="Star">star</a> located approximately 800 <a href="/wiki/Light-year" title="Light-year">light-years</a> away in the <a href="/wiki/Constellation" title="Constellation">constellation</a> <a href="/wiki/Auriga_(constellation)" title="Auriga (constellation)">Auriga</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SIMBAD_1-10"><a href="#cite_note-SIMBAD-1">[1]</a></sup> WASP-12 has a <a href="/wiki/Mass" title="Mass">mass</a> and <a href="/wiki/Radius" title="Radius">radius</a> similar to the <a href="/wiki/Sun" title="Sun">Sun</a>. Its planet <a href="/wiki/WASP-12b" title="WASP-12b">WASP-12b</a> has a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Retrograde_orbit" title="Retrograde orbit">retrograde orbit</a> around WASP-12.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Planetary_system">Planetary system</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=WASP-12&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Planetary system">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
