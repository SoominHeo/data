<p><i><b>Warchest</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Box_set" title="Box set">box set</a> from the American <a href="/wiki/Heavy_metal_music" title="Heavy metal music">heavy metal</a> band <a href="/wiki/Megadeth" title="Megadeth">Megadeth</a>. <i>Warchest</i> features five discs (4 CD + DVD) and features studio tracks, demos and live recordings of a number of the band's songs from its <a href="/wiki/Megadeth_discography" title="Megadeth discography">first ten studio albums and rarities EP</a>. The box set was released on October 9, 2007 by <a href="/wiki/EMI" title="EMI">EMI</a>/<a href="/wiki/Capitol_Records" title="Capitol Records">Capitol Records</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ReleaseDate_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-ReleaseDate-1">[1]</a></sup> and sold around 1,100 copies in the United States in its first week.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WarchestUS1stWeekSales_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-WarchestUS1stWeekSales-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>While the content on discs 1-3 is assorted, disc 4 was recorded live at Wembley Arena, London, England, on October 14, 1990. The location and date is incorrectly listed as October 16, 1990 in the liner notes.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2013)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> The "Clash Of The Titans Tour" also featured <a href="/wiki/Suicidal_Tendencies" title="Suicidal Tendencies">Suicidal Tendencies</a>, <a href="/wiki/Anthrax_(American_band)" title="Anthrax (American band)">Anthrax</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slayer" title="Slayer">Slayer</a> during the European leg. Disc 5 is a <a href="/wiki/DVD" title="DVD">DVD</a>, and features a concert recorded at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hammersmith_Odeon" title="Hammersmith Odeon">Hammersmith Odeon</a>, London, England, on September 30, 1992. Its audio was encoded to stereo <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Linear_pulse-code_modulation" title="Linear pulse-code modulation">Linear PCM</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2013)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>

<p>Jason Birchmeier of Allmusic noted that the set offers "something for everyone -- from longtime fans to neophytes". Birchmeyer reserved special praise for disc 4, a 1990 concert at Wembley Arena in London. He described the concert as "amazing" and noted the studio separation of the guitars, and an appearance by Sean Harris of <a href="/wiki/Diamond_Head_(band)" title="Diamond Head (band)">Diamond Head</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-AMReview_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-AMReview-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
