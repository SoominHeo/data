<p><i><b>Who Am I</b></i> is the second full-length album released by <a href="/wiki/B1A4" title="B1A4">B1A4</a> under <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=WM_Entertainment&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="WM Entertainment (page does not exist)">WM Entertainment</a>. The album was released on January 13, 2014 by WM Entertainment and their distributing label <a href="/wiki/Pony_Canyon" title="Pony Canyon">Pony Canyon Korea</a>. The title song of the album, "Lonely (없구나), was produced by leader Jinyoung along with 7 other tracks in the album. CNU also took part in the composition with 2 self-produced songs.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-allkpop_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-allkpop-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Release_and_promotion">Release and promotion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Who_Am_I_(B1A4_album)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Release and promotion">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On December 31, 2013, WM Entertainment released teaser photos for the B1A4's 2nd Album on their official website, including a group picture and an individual teaser picture for each member.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-allkpop2_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-allkpop2-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>On January 2, B1A4 released a music video teaser for Lonely (없구나).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-allkpop3_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-allkpop3-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>On January 6, B1A4 released a music video teaser drama version for Lonely (없구나).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-allkpop4_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-allkpop4-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>On January 9, B1A4 released a medley teaser previewing 15 seconds of each song.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-allkpop5_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-allkpop5-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
