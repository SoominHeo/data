<p><i><b>Wir ham' noch lange nicht genug</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a> for <i>We haven't had our fill by a long shot</i>) is the <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> <a href="/wiki/Hard_rock" title="Hard rock">Hard rock</a> band <a href="/wiki/B%C3%B6hse_Onkelz" title="Böhse Onkelz">Böhse Onkelz</a> 8th album. It was released in 1991.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Track_notes">Track notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wir_ham%E2%80%99_noch_lange_nicht_genug&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Track notes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Nur_die_Besten_sterben_jung">Nur die Besten sterben jung</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wir_ham%E2%80%99_noch_lange_nicht_genug&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Nur die Besten sterben jung">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This song was written in memoriam to Andreas "Trimmi" Trimborn, the band's best friend. He was murdered at 16 June 1990, after the Onkelz and Trimmi were gone in to the "Speak Easy", a tavern in Frankfurt-Sachsenhausen.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Ganz_egal">Ganz egal</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wir_ham%E2%80%99_noch_lange_nicht_genug&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Ganz egal">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The murderer of Trimmi wasn't condemned by the court. Stephan Weidner would appeal to his certain one.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Zeig.27_mir_den_Weg">Zeig' mir den Weg</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wir_ham%E2%80%99_noch_lange_nicht_genug&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Zeig' mir den Weg">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>"If you know all better, show me the way" A song against the press, who condemn the Onkelz further as a Nazi-band.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="3.2752.22">3'52"</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wir_ham%E2%80%99_noch_lange_nicht_genug&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: 3'52&quot;">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Instrumental-Track. The band couldn't find a name for this track and named it to its duration.</p>
