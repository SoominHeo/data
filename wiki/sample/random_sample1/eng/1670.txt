<p><i>XS</i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">XS엑세스</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Manhwa" title="Manhwa">manhwa</a> by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Song_Ji-Hyung&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Song Ji-Hyung (page does not exist)">Song Ji-Hyung</a> (<span lang="ko-Hang">송지형</span>) that was serialized in <a href="/wiki/Booking_(manhwa)" title="Booking (manhwa)">Booking</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Haksan_Culture_Company" title="Haksan Culture Company">Haksan Culture Company</a> and licensed by <a href="/wiki/Dark_Horse_Comics" title="Dark Horse Comics">Dark Horse Comics</a>.</p>
