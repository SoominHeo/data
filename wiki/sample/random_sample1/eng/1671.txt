<p><b>XUXU</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Liqueur" title="Liqueur">liqueur</a> produced in <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a> which derives its flavor from the <a href="/wiki/Strawberry" title="Strawberry">strawberry</a> fruit, with a small amount of lime juice.</p>
<p>The beverage is produced by mixing strawberry mash with vodka. Each bottle of Xuxu contains 66% fruit. XUXU contains no added sugar, only sugar found in the fruit mash. XUXU is produced by the German company <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Georg_Hemmeter_GmbH&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Georg Hemmeter GmbH (page does not exist)">Georg Hemmeter GmbH</a>. Xuxu was awarded Double Gold with a 96-point score at the <a href="/wiki/Spirits_ratings" title="Spirits ratings">World-Spirits Award</a> in Austria.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
