<p>The <b>men's 110 metres hurdles</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Track_and_field" title="Track and field">track and field athletics</a> event held as part of the <a href="/wiki/Athletics_at_the_1904_Summer_Olympics" title="Athletics at the 1904 Summer Olympics">Athletics at the 1904 Summer Olympics</a> programme. It was the third time the event was held. 8 hurdlers from at least 2 nations participated. The competition was held on September 3, 1904.</p>
