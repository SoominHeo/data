<p>The <b>men's triathlon</b> was an unusual event conducted during the <a href="/wiki/1904_Summer_Olympics" title="1904 Summer Olympics">1904 Summer Olympics</a>. The <a href="/wiki/International_Olympic_Committee" title="International Olympic Committee">International Olympic Committee</a> lists it as part of the <a href="/wiki/Athletics_at_the_1904_Summer_Olympics" title="Athletics at the 1904 Summer Olympics">Athletics at the 1904 Summer Olympics</a> programme, though other sources list it among the <a href="/wiki/Gymnastics_at_the_1904_Summer_Olympics" title="Gymnastics at the 1904 Summer Olympics">Gymnastics at the 1904 Summer Olympics</a> events. Gymnasts rather than track and field athletes competed. However, the three events of the triathlon were decidedly athletic rather than gymnastic in nature, comprising the long jump, the shot put, and the 100 yard dash.</p>
<p>118 athletes from 3 nations competed.</p>
<p>The results from this event, as well as the <a href="/wiki/Gymnastics_at_the_1904_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_triathlon" title="Gymnastics at the 1904 Summer Olympics – Men's triathlon">gymnastics triathlon event</a>, were used to determine scores for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gymnastics_at_the_1904_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_all-around" title="Gymnastics at the 1904 Summer Olympics – Men's all-around">gymnastics all-around event</a>. Nevertheless, the International Olympic Committee lists the medals won in this event as being part of the sport of Athletics.</p>
