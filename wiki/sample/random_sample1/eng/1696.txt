<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">T-diaeresis</h1>

<p><b>ẗ</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Diacritic" title="Diacritic">modified</a> letter of the <a href="/wiki/Latin_alphabet" title="Latin alphabet">Latin alphabet</a>, derived from the letter T with a <a href="/wiki/Diaeresis_(diacritic)" title="Diaeresis (diacritic)">diaeresis</a> on it. It is used in the <a href="/wiki/ISO_233" title="ISO 233">ISO 233</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_transliteration" title="Arabic transliteration">transliteration of Arabic</a> to represent <i>tāʼ marbūṭa</i> (ﺓ, ﺔ), and also in the <a href="/wiki/Uralic_Phonetic_Alphabet" title="Uralic Phonetic Alphabet">Uralic Phonetic Alphabet</a> to represent a <a href="/wiki/Tenuis_consonant" title="Tenuis consonant">tenuis</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Interdental" title="Interdental">interdental</a> <a href="/wiki/Stop_consonant" title="Stop consonant">stop</a> <span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)">[t̪͆]</span>.</p>
<p>Only the minuscule form exists in <a href="/wiki/Unicode" title="Unicode">Unicode</a> as a distinct character. The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Majuscule" title="Majuscule">majuscule</a> must be formed with a combination of <a href="/wiki/T" title="T">T</a> and a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Combining_diacritic" title="Combining diacritic">combining diacritic</a> (T̈), and because of this may not display correctly when using some <a href="/wiki/Font" title="Font">fonts</a> or systems.</p>
