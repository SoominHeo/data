<p><b>Radical 4</b> meaning <b>"slash"</b> or <b>"bend"</b> is one of six of the 214 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_Kangxi_radicals" title="List of Kangxi radicals">Kangxi radicals</a> that are composed of only one stroke.</p>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/Kangxi_Dictionary" title="Kangxi Dictionary">Kangxi Dictionary</a> there are 33 characters (out of 40,000) to be found under this <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Radical_(Chinese_character)" title="Radical (Chinese character)">radical</a>. It is highly similar to the Japanese kana, <a href="/wiki/No_(kana)" title="No (kana)">no</a> (ノ).</p>
<p>Radical 4 is one of the eight elements of <a href="/wiki/Eight_Principles_of_Yong" title="Eight Principles of Yong">永</a> (永字八法 Yǒngzì Bāfǎ) and thus basic to <a href="/wiki/Chinese_calligraphy" title="Chinese calligraphy">Chinese calligraphy</a>.</p>
