<p><b>Kagoshima Castle</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">鹿児島城</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>kagoshima jō</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>, also known as <b>Tsurumaru Castle</b>, is a <a href="/wiki/Japanese_castle" title="Japanese castle">Japanese castle</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kagoshima,_Kagoshima" title="Kagoshima, Kagoshima">Kagoshima City</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kagoshima_Prefecture" title="Kagoshima Prefecture">Kagoshima Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kagoshima_Castle&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This castle was constructed by <a href="/wiki/Shimazu_Iehisa" title="Shimazu Iehisa">Shimazu Iehisa</a> in 1601. In 1600 <a href="/wiki/Shimazu_Yoshihiro" title="Shimazu Yoshihiro">Shimazu Yoshihiro</a>, the father of Iehisa, was defeated at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Sekigahara" title="Battle of Sekigahara">Battle of Sekigahara</a> as a daimyo of the western alliance, by the eastern alliance led by <a href="/wiki/Tokugawa_Ieyasu" title="Tokugawa Ieyasu">Tokugawa Ieyasu</a>. This castle was built after the defeat and in the severe political tension with Ieyasu.</p>
<p>This castle is notable for the small scale and fairly poor quality as a main castle of one of the richest <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Daimyo" title="Daimyo">daimyo</a> in Japan. It is said that Shimazu was afraid of giving the Tokugawa an excuse to attack Shimazu territory by making too large a castle.</p>
