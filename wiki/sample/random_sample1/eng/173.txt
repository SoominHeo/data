<p><b><a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Netherlands</a></b> competed at the <b><a href="/wiki/1906_Intercalated_Games" title="1906 Intercalated Games">1906 Intercalated Games</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Athens" title="Athens">Athens</a>, <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greece</a>. Sixteen athletes, all men, competed in eight events in two sports.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sports-reference_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-sports-reference-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
