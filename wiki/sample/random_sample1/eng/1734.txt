<p><b>Garion</b> (Korean: 가리온) is an influential <a href="/wiki/Korean_hip_hop" title="Korean hip hop">South Korea hip hop</a> group that pioneered Korea's underground hip-hop scene. The group currently consists of rappers MC Meta and Naachal. They are notable for rapping entirely in <a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Garion_(band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Debut">Debut</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Garion_(band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Debut">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Garion formed in 1998 after Naachal and MC Meta met at a concert where MC Meta was performing as a member of the hip hop group Blex.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">[1]</a></sup> They named themselves Garion after a mythical white horse with a black mane that lives in <a href="/wiki/Paektu_Mountain" title="Paektu Mountain">Mount Paektu</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:1_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-:1-4">[4]</a></sup> Producer J-U joined the group in November.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_album_and_first_major_award">First album and first major award</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Garion_(band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: First album and first major award">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Garion released their first album, <i>Garion</i>, in January 2004. Most songs on the album are from the group's hip hop club days in the 1990s and 2000s.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> The album was not a mainstream hit but received praise from Korea's underground hip hop scene for its groundbreaking raps that were entirely in Korean.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">[1]</a></sup> It was later chosen as one of the 100 "Masterpieces of Korean Popular Music."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2005, they released the single, "무투" ("Mutu"), which went on to win Best Hip Hop Song at the 2006 <a href="/wiki/Korean_Music_Awards" title="Korean Music Awards">Korean Music Awards</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_album">Second album</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Garion_(band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Second album">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On June 24, 2010, it was officially announced that Garion will come back with their second album called <i>Garion2</i> (가리온2) in August.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> Nevertheless, because of Naachal's health problem and MC Meta's personal problems, they announced that they are expecting to release their album in September and that DJ Skip became their new assistant DJ.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> The album was released in October.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> After the release, it was applauded by the masses and the two members actively held concerts. Before long, Naachal became a rap teacher in FnC academy and is teaching students until now.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> In 2011, Garion2 (가리온2) received 'The Best Album of the Year', 'The Best Hip-hop Album of the Year', and 'The Best Hip-hop song of the Year' in the <a href="/wiki/Korean_Music_Awards" title="Korean Music Awards">Korean Music Awards</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> In 2012, MC Meta and Naachal became the founders of the crew 'Bulhanddang' (불한당, 不汗党).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Members">Members</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Garion_(band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Members">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="MC_Meta">MC Meta</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Garion_(band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: MC Meta">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>MC Meta had many solo career for a long time and even continued after Garion was formed. He had some collaboration works with DJ Soulscape and Da Crew (다 크루). He often held a class in Haja Center and taught hip-hop. Some of these students made a label called Soul Company (소울 컴퍼니), and thinking MC Meta as a spiritual leader. From April 2011 to August, MC Meta released four singles, collaborated with DJ Wreckx.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup> Before long, studio album was released and was also praised by the listeners.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Naachal">Naachal</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Garion_(band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Naachal">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Naachal didn't have any solo careers until the release of 《Mutu(무투(武鬪)》. He was busy on his school but also there was a rumor that Naachal didn't prefer featuring on another track. He also had some bad health problems. However, after the release of 《Mutu(무투(武鬪)》, he participated in many songs and on May 2009, released a project album called Golden Boy Training Academy with Issac Squab.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="J-U">J-U</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Garion_(band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: J-U">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>J-U started his career as a producer in 1995 in Germany and when he returned to Korea, he participated in concerts in hip-hop club Honey. Then he moved to Club MP and met with MC Meta and Naachal. After his succession from Garion, people thought he quit his career as a producer.</p>
