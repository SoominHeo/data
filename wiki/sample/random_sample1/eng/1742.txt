<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Hikari_Studio">Hikari Studio</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Hikari Studio">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Hikari Studio <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">光写真館</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Hikari Shashinkan</i>)</span> is the photography studio that the Hikari family owns and where Tsukasa Kadoya develops his photographs. The various <a href="/wiki/Theatrical_scenery" title="Theatrical scenery">backdrops</a> located in the studio allow it to transport its occupants to a mutltitude of parallel worlds. Its interior and exterior automatically transform to blend into the surroundings when moving across dimensions.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep1a_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep1a-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep1b_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep1b-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Tsukasa_Kadoya">Tsukasa Kadoya</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Tsukasa Kadoya">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Decade_(character)" title="Kamen Rider Decade (character)">Kamen Rider Decade (character)</a></div>
<p>Tsukasa Kadoya <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">門矢 士</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kadoya Tsukasa</i>)</span> is an arrogant, young man without any memory of his past. As a <a href="/wiki/Photographer" title="Photographer">photographer</a>, his photos never come out right, which is later revealed to be a sign of his world being slowly destroyed by the convergence of the other Kamen Riders' worlds. To save his world, he transforms into Kamen Rider Decade <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">仮面ライダーディケイド</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kamen Raidā Dikeido</i>)</span>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep1a_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep1a-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep1b_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep1b-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Tsukasa Kadoya is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Masahiro_Inoue" title="Masahiro Inoue">Masahiro Inoue</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">井上 正大</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Inoue Masahiro</i>)</span>. As a child, Tsukasa is portrayed by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Riku_Ichikawa&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Riku Ichikawa (page does not exist)">Riku Ichikawa</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">市川 理矩</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Ichikawa Riku</i>)</span>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Natsumi_Hikari">Natsumi Hikari</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Natsumi Hikari">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Natsumi Hikari <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">光 夏海</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Hikari Natsumi</i>)</span>, referred to as "Natsumikan" <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">夏蜜柑</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Natsumikan</i>, Summer <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mikan" title="Mikan">Mikan</a>)</span> by Tsukasa, is a young woman who works at the photo studio under her grandfather, having lived with him since her childhood. .<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep1a_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep1a-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep1b_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep1b-2">[2]</a></sup> Prior to the series, Natsumi was a member of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dropping_out" title="Dropping out">Taigaku</a> Club <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">退学クラブ</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Taigaku Kurabu</i>)</span> or TG Club <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">TGクラブ</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Tī Jī Kurabu</i>)</span> for short, a group of dropouts who got bored with school and wanted to find out their own answers. But they never carry out their intent and return to school, promising to meet again years later.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep20a_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep20a-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep20b_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep20b-4">[4]</a></sup> At the start, Natsumi begins to have nightmares of Decade prior to finding Decade's belt and giving it to Tsukasa. Fully aware of world's endangerment, Natsumi decides to accompany Tsukasa on his journey to prevent her dream from coming true.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep1a_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-ep1a-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep1b_2-3"><a href="#cite_note-ep1b-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>She occasionally keeps Tsukasa in line with the Hikari Family Secret Laughing <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Acupuncture_point" title="Acupuncture point">Pressure Point</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">光家秘伝・笑いのツボ</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Hikari-ke Hiden: Warai no Tsubo</i>)</span> but she had also used the technique on other people such as Yusuke and her grandfather.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep15a_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep15a-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep15b_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep15b-6">[6]</a></sup> She also appears to have feelings for Tsukasa, or at least cares for his well-being, which is hinted during her conversation with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Kuuga_(Decade)" title="Kamen Rider Kuuga (Decade)">Yusuke Onodera</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep3a_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep3a-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep3b_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep3b-8">[8]</a></sup> Despite Narutaki's offer to save her, she refuses and chooses to see Decade's journey to the end.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep7a_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep7a-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep7b_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep7b-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p>However she starts worrying during the time in the World of Hibiki after hearing from Narutaki that Decade's action is actually destroying the world.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep19a_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep19a-11">[11]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep19b_12-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep19b-12">[12]</a></sup> While in the World of Shinkenger, Natsumi assures Tsukasa that the Photo Studio is his home before her neardeath experience by Apollo Geist in the World of RX, learning that Tsukasa used some of his life to save her later. It is only after their adventures in the World of Amazon that Natsumi ponders if there's a point behind their journey.</p>
<p>In <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_%C3%97_Kamen_Rider_W_%26_Decade:_Movie_War_2010#Decade:_The_Last_Story" title="Kamen Rider × Kamen Rider W &amp; Decade: Movie War 2010">Kamen Rider Decade: The Last Story</a></i>, Natsumi becomes Kamen Rider Kivala <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">仮面ライダーキバーラ</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kamen Raidā Kibāra</i>)</span> with the use of Kivala, but unlike Kiva, the belt does not materialize first and it does not require Kivala's bite. Instead, it is a kiss to the user's forehead, which then creates a heart-shaped emblem and she becomes the Kivala Belt <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">キバーラベルト</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kibāra Beruto</i>)</span> after transformation. Also different is that Kivala does not perch on her belt during the transformation. As Kamen Rider Kivala, Natsumi is armed with a sword called the Kivala Saber <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">キバーラサーベル</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kibāra Sāberu</i>)</span> with which she uses to perform her finishing attack, the Sonic Stab <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">ソニックスタッブ</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Sonikku Sutabbu</i>)</span>. Natsumi/Kivala also uses the butt of the Kivala Sabre to perform a variation on Natsumi's trademark "Hikari Secret Technique-Laughter Pressure Point" with a Shocker Combatant as the target. Natsumi transforms into Kamen Rider Kivala to stop Decade Violent Emotion's rampage, being described as the only one able to do so. She later transforms to help Decade, Kuuga, and Diend in order to defeat the rest of Super Shocker, as well as rescue her grandfather from the Super Crisis Fortress. Like Kiva, Kamen Rider Kivala's motif is that of a <a href="/wiki/Bat" title="Bat">bat</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Oh141_13-0"><a href="#cite_note-Oh141-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
<p>Natsumi Hikari is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Kanna_Mori" title="Kanna Mori">Kanna Mori</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">森 カンナ</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Mori Kanna</i>)</span>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Eijiro_Hikari">Eijiro Hikari</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Eijiro Hikari">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Eijiro Hikari <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">光 栄次郎</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Hikari Eijirō</i>)</span> is the owner of the Hikari Studio and Natsumi's grandfather. When he discovers that his photo studio is the means of accessing the A.R. Worlds by accident, he is not surprised but amused at the fact and is glad to assist Tsukasa in his mission. Whenever they arrive to a different world, he cooks a different meal each time. That is also the explanation for Eijiro becoming Shinigami in the previous movie.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MovieWars2010DCShiniComment_21-0"><a href="#cite_note-MovieWars2010DCShiniComment-21">[21]</a></sup> He revives the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters_in_the_New_Worlds#Neo_Organism" title="List of Kamen Rider Decade characters in the New Worlds">Neo Organism</a> to defeat Kamen Rider Decade once and for all.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Oh141_13-1"><a href="#cite_note-Oh141-13">[13]</a></sup> However, Super Doctor Shinigami reverts to Eijiro as the Memory ejects out of him caused by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Double_(character)" title="Kamen Rider Double (character)">Kamen Rider Double</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Double_(character)#HeatMetal" title="Kamen Rider Double (character)">HeatMetal</a>'s HardMammother Maximum Drive and he is saved by Natsumi in her Kamen Rider Kiva-la form. <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Kivala">Kivala</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Kivala">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Kivala <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">キバーラ</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kibāra</i>)</span> is a white-colored female Kivat originally from <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters_in_the_Nine_Worlds#World_of_Kiva" title="List of Kamen Rider Decade characters in the Nine Worlds">the World of Kiva</a>, where she is <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Kiva_characters#Kivat-bat_the_3rd" title="List of Kamen Rider Kiva characters">Kivat-bat the 3rd</a>'s younger sister. She is extremely tiny, small enough to be hidden in an adult's closed fist. She learns of Decade from Narutaki, and uses her power to bring Yusuke into her world, and then sends Tsukasa into another to do battle with <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_555_characters#Masato_Kusaka" title="List of Kamen Rider 555 characters">Kaixa</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep4a_16-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep4a-16">[16]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep4b_17-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep4b-17">[17]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep5a_22-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep5a-22">[22]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep5b_23-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep5b-23">[23]</a></sup> She later ends up traveling with the Hikari Studio, though remaining in contact with Narutaki with only Natsumi aware of it.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep7a_9-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep7a-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep7b_10-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep7b-10">[10]</a></sup> She seems to like Yusuke the most out of the traveling group as she is delighted when he returns to travel with them after they leave the World of Agito.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep13a_24-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep13a-24">[24]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep13b_25-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep13b-25">[25]</a></sup> In the World of Hibiki, she assists <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Ryuki_characters#Takeshi_Asakura" title="List of Kamen Rider Ryuki characters">Ouja</a> in summoning a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Makamou" title="Makamou">Bakegani</a> to be used on Decade as Gyuki was not getting the job done, placing her overall goals further into question.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep19a_11-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep19a-11">[11]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep19b_12-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep19b-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<p>In <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_%C3%97_Kamen_Rider_W_%26_Decade:_Movie_War_2010" title="Kamen Rider × Kamen Rider W &amp; Decade: Movie War 2010">Kamen Rider × Kamen Rider W &amp; Decade: Movie War 2010</a></i>, Kiva-la is revealed to be the means for Natsumi to assume her Kamen Rider form: Kamen Rider Kivala.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Oh141_13-2"><a href="#cite_note-Oh141-13">[13]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Narutaki">Narutaki</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Narutaki">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Narutaki <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">鳴滝</span>)</span> is the main <a href="/wiki/Antagonist" title="Antagonist">antagonist</a> of the series, a mysterious man who refers to himself as a <a href="/wiki/Prophet" title="Prophet">prophet</a> and is able to freely travel the worlds with immunity to the convergence of the ten worlds.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep1a_1-5"><a href="#cite_note-ep1a-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep1b_2-5"><a href="#cite_note-ep1b-2">[2]</a></sup> He also has the ability to move freely between the worlds and summon Riders from other worlds. He firmly believes that Decade should not exist in any world, tending to say "curse you Decade!" <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">おのれディケイド!</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Onore Dikeido!</i>)</span> while tricking the Riders of various worlds to kill him.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep3a_7-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep3a-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep3b_8-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep3b-8">[8]</a></sup> Arranging a meeting with Natsumi, Narutaki proceeds to tell her that her dreams are tied to the Rider War that will awaken Decade's true nature.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep7a_9-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep7a-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep7b_10-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep7b-10">[10]</a></sup> After Decade defied his premonitions of doom in the previous nine worlds, Narutaki decides to personally deal with him in the World of Hibiki by summoning a Bakegani, revealing to Natsumi that Tsukasa's actions are actually destroying the worlds rather than saving them as they were told, and that only she can stop the madness now.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep19a_11-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep19a-11">[11]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep19b_12-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep19b-12">[12]</a></sup> However at the World of Negatives, Narutaki expresses his joy over Decade finding himself a world which he can exist in.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep20a_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep20a-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep20b_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep20b-4">[4]</a></sup> In the World of the Rider War, he asks for Tsukasa's help to stop Apollo Geist from speeding up the destruction of all worlds, but this doesn't change his belief that Tsukasa is the true cause of the destruction, or his desire to see Tsukasa killed.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep30a_26-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep30a-26">[26]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep30b_27-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep30b-27">[27]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep31a_28-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep31a-28">[28]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep31b_29-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep31b-29">[29]</a></sup></p>
<p>In <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_%C3%97_Kamen_Rider_W_%26_Decade:_Movie_War_2010#Decade:_The_Last_Story" title="Kamen Rider × Kamen Rider W &amp; Decade: Movie War 2010">Kamen Rider Decade: The Last Story</a></i>, Narutaki poses as Colonel Zol <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">ゾル大佐</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Zoru Taisa</i>)</span>, an executive member of the evil Super Shocker organization. The original Zol is the leader of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Shocker_(Kamen_Rider)" title="Shocker (Kamen Rider)">Shocker</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> branch and has the ability to transform into the Inhumanoid Gold Werewolf <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">黄金狼男</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Ōgon Ōkami Otoko</i>)</span>, but Narutaki as Zol does not transform in the movie due to his lack of cybernetic implants. However, when Doras fell from the Super Crisis Fortress and knocks him out of his way, he reverts to Narutaki and curses Decade for causing a new calamity born from the Neo Organism's antics before running off into another dimension.</p>
<p>Narutaki resurfaces again during the events of <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_%C3%97_Super_Sentai:_Super_Hero_Taisen" title="Kamen Rider × Super Sentai: Super Hero Taisen">Kamen Rider × Super Sentai: Super Hero Taisen</a></i> as Doktor G <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">ドクトルG</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Dokutoru Gē</i>)</span>, a knight armored general of the newly reformed Dai-Shocker based on the real Doktor G of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Destron_(Kamen_Rider)" title="Destron (Kamen Rider)">Destron</a>'s Germany branch. Like the original Doktor G, he wields an axe in battle and is able to assume the form of the Inhumanoid <a href="/wiki/Crab" title="Crab">Kani</a> Laser <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">カニレーザー</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kani Rēzā</i>)</span>. He was defeated by the card-using Kamen Riders using the Goseigers' Tensou Cards before he reverts to Narutaki as he curses Decade again before departing into another dimension.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Oh141_13-3"><a href="#cite_note-Oh141-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
<p>He appeared once again during the crossover special between <a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Gaim" title="Kamen Rider Gaim">Kamen Rider Gaim</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ressha_Sentai_ToQger" title="Ressha Sentai ToQger">Ressha Sentai ToQger</a>. He gave Kota a Rainbow Pass, tells him to give the Conductor and Ticket his regards and leaves, but not before cryptically warning him and Right about an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Heisei_Riders_vs._Sh%C5%8Dwa_Riders:_Kamen_Rider_Taisen_feat._Super_Sentai" title="Heisei Riders vs. Shōwa Riders: Kamen Rider Taisen feat. Super Sentai">inevitable war between Kamen Riders</a>.</p>
<p>Narutaki is portrayed by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Tatsuhito_Okuda&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Tatsuhito Okuda (page does not exist)">Tatsuhito Okuda</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">奥田 達士</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Okuda Tatsuhito</i>)</span>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Daiki_Kaito">Daiki Kaito</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Daiki Kaito">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Diend" title="Kamen Rider Diend">Kamen Rider Diend</a></div>
<p>Daiki Kaito <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">海東 大樹</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kaitō Daiki</i>)</span> is a mysterious young man who travels through the Kamen Riders' worlds in the hunt for their treasures as Kamen Rider Diend <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">仮面ライダーディエンド</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kamen Raidā Diendo</i>)</span>, possessing the power to summon Riders to serve him. He seems to know more about Tsukasa than Tsukasa himself does, being an ally one minute and an enemy the next when it suited his thieving needs.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep11a_30-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep11a-30">[30]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep11b_31-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep11b-31">[31]</a></sup> He first reveals himself to Tsukasa during his travels to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters_(Nine_Worlds)#World_of_Blade" title="List of Kamen Rider Decade characters (Nine Worlds)">World of Blade</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep9a_32-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep9a-32">[32]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep9b_33-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep9b-33">[33]</a></sup> and reveals that he is Diend while in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters_(Nine_Worlds)#World_of_Faiz" title="List of Kamen Rider Decade characters (Nine Worlds)">World of Faiz</a>, following Tsukasa and the Hikari Studio ever since.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep11a_30-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep11a-30">[30]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep11b_31-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep11b-31">[31]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep10a_34-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep10a-34">[34]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep10b_35-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep10b-35">[35]</a></sup> Prior to the beginning of the series, Daiki had left his home world (<a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters_in_the_New_Worlds#World_of_Diend" title="List of Kamen Rider Decade characters in the New Worlds">World of Diend</a>) for crimes he committed there, only to return when the Hikari Studio group finds its way there.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep22a_36-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep22a-36">[36]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep22b_37-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep22b-37">[37]</a></sup></p>
<p>Daiki Kaito is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Kimito_Totani" title="Kimito Totani">Kimito Totani</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">戸谷 公人</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Totani Kimito</i>)</span>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Dai-Shocker">Dai-Shocker</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Dai-Shocker">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The mysterious Dai-Shocker <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">大ショッカー</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Dai Shokkā</i>, "Great Shocker")</span> organization is a conglomation of all villain organizations who set into motion a plan to take over all dimensions due to someone providing the means to do so. Based on the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters_in_the_New_Worlds#World_of_Decade" title="List of Kamen Rider Decade characters in the New Worlds">World of Decade</a>, their insignia is double-headed version of Shocker's eagle crest, but with the "DCD" written on it that hints at Decade's ties to them. In addition to <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters#Eijiro_Hikari" title="List of Kamen Rider Decade characters">Doctor Shinigami</a> and <a href="#Narutaki">Doktor G</a>, there are many other members of Dai-Shocker that come from previous Kamen Rider Series. They sent their agents out across the worlds for universal domination. During the events of <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_%C3%97_Super_Sentai:_Super_Hero_Taisen" title="Kamen Rider × Super Sentai: Super Hero Taisen">Kamen Rider × Super Sentai: Super Hero Taisen</a></i>, Tsukasa Kadoya reforms Dai-Shocker and becomes their Great Leader once more in order to take down the <a href="/wiki/Super_Sentai" title="Super Sentai">Super Sentai</a> teams and the <a href="/wiki/Space_Empire_Zangyack" title="Space Empire Zangyack">Dai-Zangyack</a> group led by <a href="/wiki/Gokaigers#Captain_Marvelous" title="Gokaigers">Captain Marvelous</a>. However, it is revealed that it was a plot between Dai-Shocker and Dai-Zangyack all along to have the Super Sentai and the Kamen Riders wipe out each other. Both groups of villains are defeated after Tsukasa and Marvelous reveal to them that they knew about their scheme all the time, having the heroes they supposedly "destroyed" sent to an alternate dimension and summoned back all together for the final battle with them.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Apollo_Geist">Apollo Geist</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Apollo Geist">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The first member of Dai-Shocker Tsukasa confronts, Apollo Geist <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">アポロガイスト</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Aporo Gaisuto</i>)</span> is the Security Chief of Dai-Shocker, hastening the worlds' convergence to make invasion easy for his organization. He normally assumes the form of a man called Guy <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">ガイ</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Gai</i>)</span> until he invokes "Apollo Change" <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">アポロチェンジ</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Aporo Chenji</i>)</span>. Originally from the <a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_X" title="Kamen Rider X">World of X-Rider</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">Xライダーの世界</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Ekkusu Raidā no Sekai</i>)</span>, he was revived by the <a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_X#G.O.D." title="Kamen Rider X">Government Of Darkness</a> after being killed by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_X_(character)" title="Kamen Rider X (character)">X-Rider</a>. However, his lifespan is short as a result and thus uses the Perfecter to take the life force out of people to augment his own. When the Perfecter is stolen from him by Diend<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep26a_38-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep26a-38">[38]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep26b_39-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep26b-39">[39]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep27a_40-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep27a-40">[40]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep27b_41-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep27b-41">[41]</a></sup> and then destroyed by Decade,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep28a_42-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep28a-42">[42]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep28b_43-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep28b-43">[43]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep29a_44-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep29a-44">[44]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep29b_45-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep29b-45">[45]</a></sup> Apollo Geist eventually comes up with the plan to marry Yuki, the Fangire Queen, becoming a Fangire as a result. As a result of his transformation, he now becomes Super Apollo Geist <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">スーパーアポロガイスト</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Sūpā Aporo Gaisuto</i>)</span> with the ability to remove other realities from existence.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep30a_26-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep30a-26">[26]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep30b_27-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep30b-27">[27]</a></sup> Using his power to revive the monsters from the Nine Worlds and attempting to make Natsumi his bride after Yuki is destroyed by Decade Complete Form, Super Apollo Geist fights Decade and Diend until help arrives in the form of Hibiki and Kiva. Kiva and Hibiki distract Super Apollo Geist long enough for him to be mortally wounded by Decade Complete Form with Diend's power, Geist swearing that he will "revive as the greatest nuisance in the universe" before he is destroyed. Later revived during the events of <i>Super Hero Taisen</i>, Apollo Geist is destroyed again by <a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Fourze" title="Kamen Rider Fourze">Kamen Rider Fourze</a> in Magnet States.</p>
<p>Guy is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Kazuhisa_Kawahara_(actor)" title="Kazuhisa Kawahara (actor)">Kazuhisa Kawahara</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">川原 和久</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kawahara Kazuhisa</i>)</span>. In <i>Super Hero Taisen</i>, Apollo Geist is voiced by Kimito Totani who portrays Daiki Kaito.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Llumu_Qhimil">Llumu Qhimil</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Llumu Qhimil">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Ten-Faced Demon Llumu Qhimil <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">十面鬼ユム・キミル</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Jūmenki Yumu Kimiru</i>)</span> is the leader of <a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Amazon#Geddon" title="Kamen Rider Amazon">Geddon</a> and Dai-Shocker's designated ruler of the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters_in_the_New_Worlds#World_of_Amazon" title="List of Kamen Rider Decade characters in the New Worlds">World of Amazon</a>, developing the mindset that Kamen Riders are the enemies of the human race, rather than Geddon. He has the GaGa Armlet and seeks Amazon's GiGi Armlet to gain their full power and use the unlimited ancient <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Inca" title="Inca">Inca</a> powers to complete his master plan of converting humans into monsters. He normally resembles a torso attached to a large boulder, but he can also assume a humanoid form adorned with the faces of the ten Heisei Kamen Riders from which he can access their powers and counter any of the ten Heisei Kamen Riders attacks. Though he gains the GiGi Armlet, he loses both it and the GaGa Armlet to the Kamen Riders with Decade Complete Form weakening him with Faiz Blaster Form before Amazon destroys him with the Super Dai Setsudan.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep28a_42-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep28a-42">[42]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep28b_43-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep28b-43">[43]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep29a_44-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep29a-44">[44]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep29b_45-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep29b-45">[45]</a></sup></p>
<p>Llumu Qhimil is voiced by <a href="/wiki/Hideo_Ishikawa" title="Hideo Ishikawa">Hideo Ishikawa</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">石川 英郎</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Ishikawa Hideo</i>)</span>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Ambassador_Hell">Ambassador Hell</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Ambassador Hell">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Ambassador Hell <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">地獄大使</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Jigoku Taishi</i>)</span> is an executive member of the terrorist organization <a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider#Shocker" title="Kamen Rider">Shocker</a>, originally from the <a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider" title="Kamen Rider">World of Kamen Rider</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">仮面ライダーの世界</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kamen Raidā no Sekai</i>)</span> and donning a new black armor. He is able to assume the form of the Inhumanoid Garagaranda <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">ガラガランダ</span>)</span>, a <a href="/wiki/Rattlesnake" title="Rattlesnake">rattlesnake</a> monster. In the final battle, he fights Kamen Rider 1, 2, Kabuto, and Black RX. He is killed when Riders 1 and 2 use a Rider Double Kick on him after being stabbed by Kabuto and impaled by Black RX, he praises Dai-Shocker before finally succumbing to his wounds and dying in a huge explosion.</p>
<p>Ambassador Hell is portrayed by <a href="/wiki/Ren_Osugi" title="Ren Osugi">Ren Osugi</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">大杉 漣</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Ōsugi Ren</i>)</span> while Garagaranda is voiced by <a href="/wiki/Kenichi_Suzumura" title="Kenichi Suzumura">Kenichi Suzumura</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">鈴村 健一</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Suzumura Ken'ichi</i>)</span>.</p>

<p>General Jark <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">ジャーク将軍</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Jāku Shōgun</i>)</span> is a supreme commander of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Crisis_Empire" title="Crisis Empire">Crisis Empire</a> from the <a href="#World_of_Black_RX">World of Black RX</a>. <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Sou_Yaguruma">Sou Yaguruma</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=20" title="Edit section: Sou Yaguruma">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_KickHopper" title="Kamen Rider KickHopper">Kamen Rider KickHopper</a></div>
<p>Sou Yaguruma <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">矢車 想</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Yaguruma Sō</i>)</span> is an insane young man who transformed into Kamen Rider KickHopper <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">仮面ライダーキックホッパー</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kamen Raidā Kikku Hoppā</i>)</span>. <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Shun_Kageyama">Shun Kageyama</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=21" title="Edit section: Shun Kageyama">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_PunchHopper" title="Kamen Rider PunchHopper">Kamen Rider PunchHopper</a></div>
<p>Shun Kageyama <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">影山 瞬</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kageyama Shun</i>)</span> is a young man who transformed into Kamen Rider PunchHopper <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">仮面ライダーパンチホッパー</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kamen Raidā Panchi Hoppā</i>)</span>. In <i>Decade</i>, PunchHopper is a wildcard Kamen Rider who uses a fighting style themed around punching attacks.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep3a_7-4"><a href="#cite_note-ep3a-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep3b_8-4"><a href="#cite_note-ep3b-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep2a_14-3"><a href="#cite_note-ep2a-14">[14]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep2b_15-3"><a href="#cite_note-ep2b-15">[15]</a></sup></p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Masato_Uchiyama" title="Masato Uchiyama">Masato Uchiyama</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">内山 眞人</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Uchiyama Masato</i>)</span> reprises his role as Shun Kageyama from <i>Kamen Rider Kabuto</i>, voicing PunchHopper.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Masato_Kusaka">Masato Kusaka</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=22" title="Edit section: Masato Kusaka">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Kaixa" title="Kamen Rider Kaixa">Kamen Rider Kaixa</a></div>
<p>Masato Kusaka <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">草加 雅人</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kusaka Masato</i>)</span> is a selfish young man who transformed into Kamen Rider Kaixa <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">仮面ライダーカイザ</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kamen Raidā Kaiza</i>)</span>. In <i>Decade</i>, Kaixa is a wildcard Kamen Rider who attacks Decade after he is taken to another world by Kiva-la. He is under the impression that Decade is a hindrance to his own agenda and seeks to take the Decadriver from him. He eventually concedes when Decade is returned to the World of Kiva.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep4a_16-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep4a-16">[16]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep4b_17-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep4b-17">[17]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep5a_22-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep5a-22">[22]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep5b_23-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep5b-23">[23]</a></sup></p>
<p>Kamen Rider Kaixa reappears briefly in later airings of the series finale, using his Side Basshar to attack Decade and Wataru's group. He is seemingly killed in the chaos afterwards alongside many other Riders.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep31a_28-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep31a-28">[28]</a></sup> In <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_%C3%97_Kamen_Rider_W_%26_Decade:_Movie_War_2010#Decade:_The_Last_Story" title="Kamen Rider × Kamen Rider W &amp; Decade: Movie War 2010">Kamen Rider Decade: The Last Story</a></i>, Decade is shown to hold a Side Basshar card, labeled as a Kaixa card.</p>
<p><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kohei_Murakami" title="Kohei Murakami">Kohei Murakami</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">村上 幸平</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Murakami Kōhei</i>)</span> reprises his role as Masato Kusaka from <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_555" title="Kamen Rider 555">Kamen Rider 555</a></i>, voicing Kaixa.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Takeshi_Asakura">Takeshi Asakura</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=23" title="Edit section: Takeshi Asakura">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Ouja" title="Kamen Rider Ouja">Kamen Rider Ouja</a></div>
<p>Takeshi Asakura <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">浅倉 威</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Asakura Takeshi</i>)</span> is a sadistic criminal who transformed into Kamen Rider Ouja <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">仮面ライダー王蛇</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kamen Raidā Ōja</i>)</span>. In <i>Decade</i>, Ouja is summoned in the World of Hibiki by Kiva-la, wielding a <a href="/wiki/Kanab%C5%8D" title="Kanabō">kanabō</a> which he uses to summon a Bakegani from the mountain side after knocking Kiva-la aside.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep19a_11-3"><a href="#cite_note-ep19a-11">[11]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep19b_12-3"><a href="#cite_note-ep19b-12">[12]</a></sup> Asakura later appears in the World of Decade to prevent Dai-Shocker from taking over, but soon defects to the side of Dai-Shocker.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Decade_movie_20-2"><a href="#cite_note-Decade_movie-20">[20]</a></sup></p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Takashi_Hagino" title="Takashi Hagino">Takashi Hagino</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">萩野 崇</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Hagino Takashi</i>)</span> reprises his role as Takeshi Asakura from <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Ryuki" title="Kamen Rider Ryuki">Kamen Rider Ryuki</a></i>, voicing Ouja.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Kotaro_Minami">Kotaro Minami</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=24" title="Edit section: Kotaro Minami">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main articles: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Black_(character)" title="Kamen Rider Black (character)">Kamen Rider Black (character)</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Black_RX_(character)" title="Kamen Rider Black RX (character)">Kamen Rider Black RX (character)</a></div>
<p>Kotaro Minami <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">南 光太郎</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Minami Kōtarō</i>)</span> is a man who transformed into Kamen Rider Black <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">仮面ライダーBLACK</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kamen Raidā Burakku</i>)</span> and later evolved into Kamen Rider Black RX <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">仮面ライダーBLACK RX</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kamen Raidā Burakku Āru Ekkusu</i>)</span>. In addition to his appearances in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters_in_the_New_Worlds#World_of_Black_RX" title="List of Kamen Rider Decade characters in the New Worlds">World of Black RX</a> and <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters_in_the_New_Worlds#World_of_Black" title="List of Kamen Rider Decade characters in the New Worlds">World of Black</a> episodes, he also appears untransformed at the end of <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Decade:_All_Riders_vs._Dai-Shocker" title="Kamen Rider Decade: All Riders vs. Dai-Shocker">Kamen Rider Decade: All Riders vs. Dai-Shocker</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Decade_movie_20-3"><a href="#cite_note-Decade_movie-20">[20]</a></sup></p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Tetsuo_Kurata" title="Tetsuo Kurata">Tetsuo Kurata</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">倉田 てつを</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kurata Tetsuo</i>)</span> reprises his role as Kotaro Minami from <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Black" title="Kamen Rider Black">Kamen Rider Black</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Black_RX" title="Kamen Rider Black RX">Kamen Rider Black RX</a></i>.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Shotaro_Hidari_.26_Philip">Shotaro Hidari &amp; Philip</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=26" title="Edit section: Shotaro Hidari &amp; Philip">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Double_(character)" title="Kamen Rider Double (character)">Kamen Rider Double (character)</a></div>
<p>Detective Shotaro Hidari <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">左 翔太郎</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Shōtarō Hidari</i>)</span> and his partner Philip <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">フィリップ</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Firippu</i>)</span> are two young men from the city of Futo who fight the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dopant_(Kamen_Rider)" title="Dopant (Kamen Rider)">Dopant</a> menace as Kamen Rider Double <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">仮面ライダーダブル</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kamen Raidā Daburu</i>)</span>. In <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Decade:_All_Riders_vs._Dai-Shocker" title="Kamen Rider Decade: All Riders vs. Dai-Shocker">Kamen Rider Decade: All Riders vs. Dai-Shocker</a></i>, Double has a cameo appearance where he fights Shadow Moon. Double also appears in <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_%C3%97_Kamen_Rider_W_%26_Decade:_Movie_War_2010" title="Kamen Rider × Kamen Rider W &amp; Decade: Movie War 2010">Movie War 2010</a></i> to battle Super Shocker with Decade.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Decade_movie_20-5"><a href="#cite_note-Decade_movie-20">[20]</a></sup><br/></p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Kazuma_Kenzaki">Kazuma Kenzaki</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=27" title="Edit section: Kazuma Kenzaki">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Blade_(character)" title="Kamen Rider Blade (character)">Kamen Rider Blade (character)</a></div>
<p>Kazuma Kenzaki <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">剣崎 一真</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kenzaki Kazuma</i>)</span> is the original Kamen Rider Blade <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">仮面ライダーブレイド</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kamen Raidā Bureido</i>)</span> as well as the second Joker who appears about 5 years after the series original ending in the World of Rider War as the A.R. Worlds continue to fuse. He is first seen with Natsumi Hikari, pointing out to her that Dai-Shocker isn't the real enemy. As Natsumi asks in question, he had already left the scene.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep30a_26-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep30a-26">[26]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep30b_27-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep30b-27">[27]</a></sup> He then appears before Asumu, Wataru, Yusuke Onodera, and Daiki Kaito and says that their true enemy is Tsukasa, using them to force him to leave while he can or destroy him if he refuses. It is noteworthy that Kenzaki is able to transform directly to King Form using just the Change Beetle card alone as the belt now uses Kamen Rider Leangle's "Open Up!" feature, rather than the original "Turn Up!" feature, projecting a gold version of the transformation screen that depicts the Evolution Caucasus beetle. As Kenzaki attempts to force Tsukasa to leave the world, he refuses, transforming into Kamen Rider Decade. But Tsukasa proved no match as Kenzaki overpowers him throughout the entire fight. Kenzaki then directly uses The Royal Straight Flush to finish the fight as he severely injures Tsukasa. He then assumes King Form and teams up with Wataru Kurenai and the rest of the Kamen Riders to fight Tsukasa in the Rider War.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep31a_28-3"><a href="#cite_note-ep31a-28">[28]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep31b_29-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep31b-29">[29]</a></sup></p>
<p>In <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_%C3%97_Kamen_Rider_W_%26_Decade:_Movie_War_2010#Decade:_The_Last_Story" title="Kamen Rider × Kamen Rider W &amp; Decade: Movie War 2010">Kamen Rider × Kamen Rider W &amp; Decade: Movie War 2010: Decade: The Last Story</a></i>, Kenzaki in his regular Ace Form teams up with Shinji Kido, Kamen Rider Ryuki, to fight Tsukasa. But Tsukasa gains the upper hand as he transforms into Decade Violent Emotion. As he overpowers both of them, Tsukasa turns Kenzaki into "Blade Blade" and kills Shinji. With Kenzaki stunned, Tsukasa finally activates his "Final Attack Ride" and kills Kenzaki as well, resulting in both Kamen Riders being turned into cards. He is later revived, as well as the rest of the Kamen Riders after Tsukasa is voluntarily destroyed. But Kenzaki mysteriously disappears as he did not take part in the final battle against Super Shocker. Instead, it would be Kazuma Kendate, Kenzaki's alternate reality version to help Tsukasa and the rest of the Kamen Riders in the battle against Super Shocker, assuming King Form with Decade's K-Touch.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Takayuki_Tsubaki" title="Takayuki Tsubaki">Takayuki Tsubaki</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">椿 隆之</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Tsubaki Takayuki</i>)</span> reprises his role as Kazuma Kenzaki from <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Blade" title="Kamen Rider Blade">Kamen Rider Blade</a></i>.</p>
