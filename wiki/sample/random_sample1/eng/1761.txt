<p><b>Dario Oliviero</b> (born 12 July 1972), better known by his stage name <b>Gabriel Garko</b>, is an Italian actor and former fashion model.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gabriel_Garko&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Turin" title="Turin">Turin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Piedmont" title="Piedmont">Piedmont</a>, <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a> to a Venetian father and Sicilian mother, Garko was raised in the nearby suburb of <a href="/wiki/Settimo_Torinese" title="Settimo Torinese">Settimo Torinese</a>. His film credits include <i><a href="/wiki/Callas_Forever" title="Callas Forever">Callas Forever</a></i> (2002),<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and <i><a href="/wiki/L%27onore_e_il_rispetto" title="L'onore e il rispetto">L'onore e il rispetto</a></i> (2006) a television mini-series where he portrayed a ruthless fictitious <a href="/wiki/Sicilian_Mafia" title="Sicilian Mafia">Mafia</a> boss "Tonio Fortebracci". In Tinto Brass' <i>Senso '45</i> he appears full-frontal naked in a scene when he goes to the sea with lead actress Anna Galiena.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> He starred alongside actresses <a href="/wiki/Serena_Autieri" title="Serena Autieri">Serena Autieri</a> and <a href="/wiki/Manuela_Arcuri" title="Manuela Arcuri">Manuela Arcuri</a>. In 2010 he had the main role of "Nito Valdi" in another mini-series <i>Il peccato e la vergogna</i>, which was set in Italy during <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Manuela Arcuri was his co-star in this series as well. In 2011, he starred in the television mini-series <i>Viso d'angelo</i> (<i>Angel Face</i>), a police thriller.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
