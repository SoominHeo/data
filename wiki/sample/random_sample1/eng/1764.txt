<p><i><b>Gavy NJ</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: 가비엔제이) is a Korean pop ballad female trio that debuted in 2005 and which is currently signed to Goodfellas Entertainment. In 2006 Gavy NJ was included on the Goodberry Entertainment (their former label) 7 members project group "Gavy NJ Project Group H7 Miin", renamed in 2010 to "H7 Miin Project The b". The group is the longest-running Korean girl group (with more than 2 members), surpassing Jewelry.</p>
<p>In early 2009, Jung Hye Min left the group and was replaced by Misty. On November 17, 2011, it was announced that Hee Young would leave the group after finishing the promotion of the digital single "Gaseyo". In April, 2012, it was announced that Misty had also left, however two new members joined the group.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Current">Current</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gavy_NJ&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Current">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><b>Jenny</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 제니) was born as Kim Da Rae (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 김다래) on the 14th of August, 1988 (age 28) in South Korea.</p>
<p>Darae joined Gavy N.J in 2012 and became the vocal and leader of the group. Darae posts regularly on <a href="/wiki/Instagram" title="Instagram">Instagram</a> her account is <a class="external free" href="http://instagram.com/dal_zzang" rel="nofollow">http://instagram.com/dal_zzang</a>.</p>
<p><b>Gunji</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 건지) was born as Kim Gun Ji (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 김건지) on the 28th of February, 1992 (age 24) in South Korea.</p>
<p>Gunji joined Gavy N.J in 2012 along with Jenny becoming the vocal along with being the maknae of the group. Gunji posts on <a href="/wiki/Instagram" title="Instagram">Instagram</a> at <a class="external free" href="https://www.instagram.com/GUN_DIII/" rel="nofollow">https://www.instagram.com/GUN_DIII/</a>.</p>
<p><b>Seorin</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 서린) was born as Kang Soo Ji (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 강수지) on the 8th of April, 1988 (age 28) in South Korea.</p>
<p>Sooji joined Gavy N.J in 2016 just before their recent comeback with SHUBIRUBIRUB. Sooji is the vocal of the group. She along with the other members posts on <a href="/wiki/Instagram" title="Instagram">Instagram</a> at <a class="external free" href="https://www.instagram.com/sjk_0709/" rel="nofollow">https://www.instagram.com/sjk_0709/</a>.</p>
<p>If you wish to learn more about these lovely girls, personality wise, they do regular broadcasts on VLive <a class="external free" href="http://www.vlive.tv/channels/F091D3" rel="nofollow">http://www.vlive.tv/channels/F091D3</a>.</p>
