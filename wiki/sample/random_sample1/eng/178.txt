<p><b><a href="/wiki/Austria" title="Austria">Austria</a></b> competed at the <b><a href="/wiki/1908_Summer_Olympics" title="1908 Summer Olympics">1908 Summer Olympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. Austrian and Hungarian results at early Olympic Games are generally kept separate despite the union of the two nations as <a href="/wiki/Austria-Hungary" title="Austria-Hungary">Austria-Hungary</a> at the time.</p>
