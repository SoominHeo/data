<p><b>Kashimada Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">鹿島田駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kashimada-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a railway station on the <a href="/wiki/Nambu_Line" title="Nambu Line">Nambu Line</a> in <a href="/wiki/Saiwai-ku,_Kawasaki" title="Saiwai-ku, Kawasaki">Saiwai Ward</a> of <a href="/wiki/Kawasaki,_Kanagawa" title="Kawasaki, Kanagawa">Kawasaki, Kanagawa</a>, Japan, operated by <a href="/wiki/East_Japan_Railway_Company" title="East Japan Railway Company">East Japan Railway Company</a> (JR East).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lines">Lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kashimada_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kashimada Station is served by the 35.5 km <a href="/wiki/Nambu_Line" title="Nambu Line">Nambu Line</a> from <a href="/wiki/Kawasaki_Station" title="Kawasaki Station">Kawasaki</a> to <a href="/wiki/Tachikawa_Station" title="Tachikawa Station">Tachikawa</a>, and is 4.1 km from the southern terminus of the line at Kawasaki.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_layout">Station layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kashimada_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Station layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kashimada Station has two opposed <a href="/wiki/Side_platform" title="Side platform">side platforms</a> serving two tracks, connected by a footbridge to an elevated station building.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-kawashima2009_Vol.1_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-kawashima2009_Vol.1-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kashimada_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The station opened on March 9, 1927.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-teishajo1998_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-teishajo1998-2">[2]</a></sup> With the privatization of <a href="/wiki/Japanese_National_Railways" title="Japanese National Railways">Japanese National Railways</a> (JNR) on April 1, 1987, the station came under the control of JR East.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-teishajo1998_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-teishajo1998-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Passenger_statistics">Passenger statistics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kashimada_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Passenger statistics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
