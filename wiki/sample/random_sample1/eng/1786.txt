<p><i><b>Gymnocanthus</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Genus" title="Genus">genus</a> of <a href="/wiki/Cottidae" title="Cottidae">sculpins</a>, fishes in the family <a href="/wiki/Cottidae" title="Cottidae">Cottidae</a>.</p>
<p>Of the seven recognized <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> in this genus, five are native to the northern <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Ocean" title="Pacific Ocean">Pacific Ocean</a>, whereas one lives in arctic waters adjacent to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Atlantic" title="Atlantic">Atlantic</a>, and one in the central Atlantic:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li><i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Gymnocanthus_detrisus&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Gymnocanthus detrisus (page does not exist)">Gymnocanthus detrisus</a></i> <small><a href="/wiki/Charles_Henry_Gilbert" title="Charles Henry Gilbert">C. H. Gilbert</a> &amp; <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Charles_Victor_Burke&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Charles Victor Burke (page does not exist)">Burke</a>, 1912</small></li>
<li><i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Gymnocanthus_galeatus&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Gymnocanthus galeatus (page does not exist)">Gymnocanthus galeatus</a></i> <small><a href="/wiki/Tarleton_Hoffman_Bean" title="Tarleton Hoffman Bean">T. H. Bean</a>, 1881</small> (Armorhead sculpin)</li>
<li><i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Gymnocanthus_herzensteini&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Gymnocanthus herzensteini (page does not exist)">Gymnocanthus herzensteini</a></i> <small><a href="/wiki/David_Starr_Jordan" title="David Starr Jordan">D. <li><i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Gymnocanthus_intermedius&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Gymnocanthus intermedius (page does not exist)">Gymnocanthus intermedius</a></i> <small>(<a href="/wiki/Coenraad_Jacob_Temminck" title="Coenraad Jacob Temminck">Temminck</a> &amp; <a href="/wiki/Hermann_Schlegel" title="Hermann Schlegel">Schlegel</a>, 1843)</small></li>

C. H. Reinhardt</a>, 1830)</small> (Arctic staghorn sculpin)</li>
<li><i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Gymnocanthus_vandesandei&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Gymnocanthus vandesandei (page does not exist)">Gymnocanthus vandesandei</a></i> <small><a href="/wiki/Max_Poll" title="Max Poll">Poll</a>, 1949</small></li>
