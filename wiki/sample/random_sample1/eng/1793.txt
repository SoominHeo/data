<p><b>Gaoshu Township</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">高樹鄉</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Gāoshù Xiāng</span></i>; <a href="/wiki/Wade%E2%80%93Giles" title="Wade–Giles">Wade–Giles</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-wadegile" xml:lang="zh-Latn-wadegile">Kao<sup>1</sup>-shu<sup>4</sup> Hsiang<sup>1</sup></span></i>; <a href="/wiki/Pe%CC%8Dh-%C5%8De-j%C4%AB" title="Pe̍h-ōe-jī">Pe̍h-ōe-jī</a>: <i><span lang="nan" xml:lang="nan">Ko-chhiū-hiong</span></i>; <a href="/wiki/Pha%CC%8Dk-fa-s%E1%B9%B3" title="Pha̍k-fa-sṳ">Pha̍k-fa-sṳ</a>: <i>Kô-su-hiông</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Township_(Taiwan)" title="Township (Taiwan)">rural township</a> in <a href="/wiki/Pingtung_County" title="Pingtung County">Pingtung County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Taiwan" title="Taiwan">Taiwan</a>.</p>
