<p>The <b>Chengcing Lake Baseball Stadium</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">澄清湖棒球場</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Chéngqīng Hú Bàngqiú Chǎng</span></i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Baseball" title="Baseball">baseball</a> <a href="/wiki/Stadium" title="Stadium">stadium</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Niaosong_District" title="Niaosong District">Niaosong District</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kaohsiung_City" title="Kaohsiung City">Kaohsiung City</a>, <a href="/wiki/Taiwan" title="Taiwan">Taiwan</a>, on the lakeside of <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Cheng-ching_Lake&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Cheng-ching Lake (page does not exist)">Cheng-ching Lake</a>. The stadium opened in 1999 and has been the home of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kaohsiung-Pingtung_Fala" title="Kaohsiung-Pingtung Fala">Kaohsiung-Pingtung Fala</a> (1999–2002), <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/First_Financial_Holdings_Agan" title="First Financial Holdings Agan">First Financial Holdings Agan</a> (2003), <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/La_New_Bears" title="La New Bears">La New Bears</a> (2004-2010), and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/EDA_Rhinos" title="EDA Rhinos">EDA Rhinos</a> (2013-present).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
