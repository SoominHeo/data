<p><b>Gaochang</b> (<a href="/wiki/Uyghur_language" title="Uyghur language">Uyghur</a>: <span lang="ug" style="font-family:'UKIJ Tuz','UKIJ Nasq','UKIJ Basma','UKIJ_Mac Basma','UKIJ Zilwa','UKIJ Esliye','UKIJ Tuz Basma','UKIJ Tuz Kitab','UKIJ Tuz Gezit','UKIJ Tuz Qara','UKIJ Tuz Qara','UKIJ Tuz Tor','UKIJ Kesme','UKIJ Kesme Tuz','UKIJ Qara','UKIJ Basma Aq','UKIJ Basma Qara','UKIJ Basma Tuz','UKIJ Putuk','UKIJ Tuz Xet','UKIJ Tom Xet','UKIJ Tuz Jurnal','UKIJ Arabic','UKIJ CJK','UKIJ Ekran','UKIJ_Mac Ekran','UKIJ Teng','UKIJ Tor','UKIJ Tuz Tom','UKIJ Mono Keng','UKIJ Mono Tar','UKIJ Nokia','UKIJ SimSun','UKIJ Yanfon','UKIJ Qolyazma','UKIJ Saet','UKIJ Nasq Zilwa','UKIJ Sulus','UKIJ Sulus Tom','UKIJ 3D','UKIJ Diwani','UKIJ Diwani Yantu','UKIJ Diwani Tom','UKIJ Esliye Tom','UKIJ Esliye Qara','UKIJ Jelliy','UKIJ Kufi','UKIJ Kufi Tar','UKIJ Kufi Uz','UKIJ Kufi Yay','UKIJ Merdane','UKIJ Ruqi','UKIJ Mejnuntal','UKIJ Junun','UKIJ Moy Qelem','UKIJ Chiwer Kesme','UKIJ Orxun-Yensey','UKIJ Elipbe','UKIJ Qolyazma Tez','UKIJ Qolyazma Tuz','UKIJ Qolyazma Yantu','UKIJ Ruqi Tuz',FZWWBBOT_Unicode,FZWWHQHTOT_Unicode,'Microsoft Uighur'; font-size:135%;" xml:lang="ug">قاراھوجا, Қарахоҗа</span>‎, <a href="/wiki/Uyghur_Latin_alphabet" title="Uyghur Latin alphabet">ULY</a>: <i>Qarahoja</i> ; <a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">高昌</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Gāochāng</span></i>), is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/DPRC" title="DPRC">district</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Oasis" title="Oasis">oasis</a> city of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Turpan_Prefecture" title="Turpan Prefecture">Turpan</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Xinjiang" title="Xinjiang">Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region</a> of the <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">People's Republic of China</a>. <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Name">Name</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gaochang_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Name">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>According to the municipal government of <a href="/wiki/Turpan" title="Turpan">Turpan</a>, the district is named after the ancient city of <a href="/wiki/Gaochang" title="Gaochang">Gaochang</a>. It was also proposed that the district be named after <a href="/wiki/Ayding_Lake" title="Ayding Lake">Ayding Lake</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gaochang_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The district was created on 12 April 2015 after <a href="/wiki/Turpan" title="Turpan">Turpan</a> was upgraded from a <a href="/wiki/County-level_city" title="County-level city">county-level city</a> into a <a href="/wiki/Prefecture-level_city" title="Prefecture-level city">prefecture-level city</a>. The district has the same size and population as the former Turpan county-level city.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
