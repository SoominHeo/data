<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Outline of Middle-earth</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The following <a href="/wiki/Outline_(list)" title="Outline (list)">outline</a> is provided as an overview of and topical guide about Tolkien's fantasy universe:</p>
<p><i><b><a href="/wiki/Middle-earth" title="Middle-earth">Middle-earth</a></b></i> – fictional setting of the majority of author <a href="/wiki/J._R._R._Tolkien" title="J. R. R. Tolkien">J. R. R. Tolkien</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Tolkien%27s_legendarium" title="Tolkien's legendarium">fantasy writings</a>. <i><a href="/wiki/The_Hobbit" title="The Hobbit">The Hobbit</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Lord_of_the_Rings" title="The Lord of the Rings">The Lord of the Rings</a></i> take place entirely in Middle-earth, as does much of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Silmarillion" title="The Silmarillion">The Silmarillion</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Unfinished_Tales" title="Unfinished Tales">Unfinished Tales</a></i>. The term <i>Middle-earth</i> is also a nickname of the entirety of Tolkien's creation, instead of the more appropriate, but less known terms <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arda_(Middle-earth)" title="Arda (Middle-earth)">Arda</a> which refers to Tolkien's world (including <a href="/wiki/Astronomical_object" title="Astronomical object">celestial bodies</a>), and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/E%C3%A4" title="Eä">Eä</a>, which refers to the universe.</p>

<li><a href="/wiki/Fiction" title="Fiction">Fiction</a> – form of narrative which deals, in part or in whole, with events that are not factual, but rather, imaginary and invented by its author(s). </ul>
</li>
<li>a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fictional_setting" title="Fictional setting">fictional setting</a>
<ul>
<li>a <a href="/wiki/Fictional_universe" title="Fictional universe">fictional universe</a>
<ul>
<li>a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Constructed_world" title="Constructed world">constructed world</a>
<ul>
<li>a <a href="/wiki/Fantasy_world" title="Fantasy world">fantasy world</a></li>
</ul>

</ul>

</ul>

</ul>
