<p><b>Kajikazawaguchi Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">鰍沢口駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kajikazawaguchi-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/Train_station" title="Train station">railway station</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Minobu_Line" title="Minobu Line">Minobu Line</a> of <a href="/wiki/Central_Japan_Railway_Company" title="Central Japan Railway Company">Central Japan Railway Company</a> (JR Central) located in the town of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ichikawamisato,_Yamanashi" title="Ichikawamisato, Yamanashi">Ichikawamisato</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nishiyatsushiro_District,_Yamanashi" title="Nishiyatsushiro District, Yamanashi">Nishiyatsushiro District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yamanashi_Prefecture" title="Yamanashi Prefecture">Yamanashi Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>. The station is located 66.8 rail kilometers from the southern <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Terminal_station" title="Terminal station">terminus</a> of the Minobu Line at <a href="/wiki/Fuji_Station" title="Fuji Station">Fuji Station</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kajikazawaguchi_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kajikazawaguchi Station was opened on December 17, 1927 as <b>Kajikazawa-Kurosawa Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">鰍沢黒沢駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kajikazawa-Kurosawa-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> on the Fuji-Minobu Line. It was renamed to its current name on October 1, 1938. The line came under control of the <a href="/wiki/Japanese_Government_Railways" title="Japanese Government Railways">Japanese Government Railways</a> on May 1, 1941. Scheduled freight operations were discontinued in 1972. The JGR became the JNR (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Japan_National_Railway" title="Japan National Railway">Japan National Railway</a>) after World War II. Along with the division and <a href="/wiki/Privatization" title="Privatization">privatization</a> of JNR on April 1, 1987, the station came under the control and operation of the <a href="/wiki/Central_Japan_Railway_Company" title="Central Japan Railway Company">Central Japan Railway Company</a>. The station has been unattended since March 2012.</p>
