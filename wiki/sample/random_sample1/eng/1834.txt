<p>The <b>Roman Catholic Diocese of Nagoya</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latin_language" title="Latin language">Latin</a>: <span lang="la" xml:lang="la"><i>Nagoyën(sis)</i></span>, <a href="/wiki/Japanese_language" title="Japanese language">Japanese</a>: <span lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">カトリック名古屋教区</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Diocese" title="Diocese">diocese</a> located in the city of <a href="/wiki/Nagoya" title="Nagoya">Nagoya</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Ecclesiastical_province" title="Ecclesiastical province">Ecclesiastical province</a> of <a href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Archdiocese_of_Osaka" title="Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Osaka">Osaka 大阪</a> in Japan.</p>
