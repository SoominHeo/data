<p><b>Ngatpang</b> is one of <a href="/wiki/Palau" title="Palau">Palau</a>'s sixteen <a href="/wiki/States_of_Palau" title="States of Palau">states</a>. It comprises an area of around 47 square kilometers in the west of Palau's largest island, <a href="/wiki/Babeldaob" title="Babeldaob">Babeldaob</a>, facing onto <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ngeremeduu_Bay&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ngeremeduu Bay (page does not exist)">Ngeremeduu Bay</a>. The state capital is <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Oikuul&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Oikuul (page does not exist)">Oikuul</a>. It has a <a href="/wiki/Population" title="Population">population</a> of 464, making it Palau's 5th largest state in population.</p>
