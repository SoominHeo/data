<p>The seventh edition of the <b>South American Championship</b> was held in <a href="/wiki/Montevideo" title="Montevideo">Montevideo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Uruguay" title="Uruguay">Uruguay</a> from October 29 to December 2, 1923.</p>
<p>The participating countries were <a href="/wiki/Argentina_national_football_team" title="Argentina national football team">Argentina</a>, <a href="/wiki/Brazil_national_football_team" title="Brazil national football team">Brazil</a>, <a href="/wiki/Paraguay_national_football_team" title="Paraguay national football team">Paraguay</a> and <a href="/wiki/Uruguay_national_football_team" title="Uruguay national football team">Uruguay</a>. <a href="/wiki/Chile_national_football_team" title="Chile national football team">Chile</a> withdrew for the second time since the tournament was created.</p>
<p>The tournament was also used as a qualifier for the <a href="/wiki/Football_at_the_1924_Summer_Olympics" title="Football at the 1924 Summer Olympics">1924 Summer Olympics</a>.</p>
