<p><b>Federal elections</b> were held in Germany on 4 May 1924.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NS_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-NS-1">[1]</a></sup> The Reichstag had been dissolved on 13 March 1924. The <a href="/wiki/Social_Democratic_Party_of_Germany" title="Social Democratic Party of Germany">Social Democratic Party</a> remained the largest party in the <a href="/wiki/Reichstag_(Weimar_Republic)" title="Reichstag (Weimar Republic)">Reichstag</a> after winning 100 of the 472 seats.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Voter turnout was 77.4%.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
