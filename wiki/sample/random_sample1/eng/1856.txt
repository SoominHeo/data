<p><b>Garzê County</b> (<a href="/wiki/Tibetan_alphabet" title="Tibetan alphabet">Tibetan</a>: <span lang="bo" style="font-family: 'Qomolangma-Dunhuang', 'Qomolangma-Uchen Sarchen', 'Qomolangma-Uchen Sarchung', 'Qomolangma-Uchen Suring', 'Qomolangma-Uchen Sutung', 'Qomolangma-Title', 'Qomolangma-Subtitle', 'Qomolangma-Woodblock', 'DDC Uchen', Kailash, Jomolhari, 'TCRC Youtso Unicode', 'Tibetan Machine Uni', 'Noto Sans Tibetan', 'Microsoft Himalaya'; font-size:1.25em; vertical-align:top; word-wrap: break-word;">དཀར་མཛེས་རྫོང།</span>, <a href="/wiki/Wylie_transliteration" title="Wylie transliteration">Wylie</a>: <i>dkar mdzes rdzong</i>, <a href="/wiki/Tibetan_pinyin" title="Tibetan pinyin">ZYPY</a>: Garzê Zong; <a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans">甘孜县</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Gānzī Xiàn</span></i>) is one of the 18 subdivisions of the <a href="/wiki/Garz%C3%AA_Tibetan_Autonomous_Prefecture" title="Garzê Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture">Garzê Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture</a>, in northwestern <a href="/wiki/Sichuan" title="Sichuan">Sichuan</a> province, China. The <a href="/wiki/Yalong_River" title="Yalong River">Yalong River</a> passes just south of the town Garzê, also known as Ganzi, the capital town of the county, which has some 9,000 inhabitants (2008), many of them ethnic Tibetans, and is famous for its Tibetan lamasery. Historically, it is part of the Tibetan cultural region of <a href="/wiki/Kham" title="Kham">Kham</a> and now defunct province of <a href="/wiki/Xikang" title="Xikang">Xikang</a> (or Sikang). It lies on the northern section of the Sichuan-Tibet Highway.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Garz%C3%AA_County&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Due to its elevation, Garzê County has a <a href="/wiki/Monsoon" title="Monsoon">monsoon</a>-influenced <a href="/wiki/Humid_continental_climate" title="Humid continental climate">humid continental climate</a> (<a href="/wiki/K%C3%B6ppen_climate_classification" title="Köppen climate classification">Köppen</a> <i>Dwb</i>), with cold but very dry winters, and warm summers with frequent rain. The monthly 24-hour average temperature ranges from −4.4 °C (24.1 °F) in January to 13.9 °C (57.0 °F) in July, while the annual mean is 5.64 °C (42.2 °F). Over two-thirds of the annual precipitation of 660 mm (26.0 in) occurs from June thru September. With monthly percent possible sunshine ranging from 49% in July to 74% in November and December, the county seat receives abundant, outside of summer, sunshine, totalling 2,620 hours annually. The <a href="/wiki/Diurnal_temperature_variation" title="Diurnal temperature variation">diurnal temperature variation</a> is large, averaging 14.5 °C (26.1 °F) annually.</p>
