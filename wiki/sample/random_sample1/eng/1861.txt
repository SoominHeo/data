<p><b>Gal Gadot-Varsano</b> (<span class="nowrap"><span class="IPA nopopups"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_English" title="Help:IPA for English">/<span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="/ˈ/ primary stress follows">ˈ</span><span title="'g' in 'guy'">ɡ</span><span title="/æ/ short 'a' in 'bad'">æ</span><span title="'l' in 'lie'">l</span></span> <span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="'g' in 'guy'">ɡ</span><span title="/ə/ 'a' in 'about'">ə</span><span title="/ˈ/ primary stress follows">ˈ</span><span title="'d' in 'dye'">d</span><span title="/oʊ/ long 'o' in 'code'">oʊ</span><span title="'t' in 'tie'">t</span></span>/</a></span></span> <span title="English pronunciation respelling"><a href="/wiki/Help:Pronunciation_respelling_key" title="Help:Pronunciation respelling key"><i><b><span class="smallcaps"><span style="font-variant: small-caps; text-transform: lowercase;">GAL</span></span></b></i></a></span> <span title="English pronunciation respelling"><a href="/wiki/Help:Pronunciation_respelling_key" title="Help:Pronunciation respelling key"><i>gə-<b><span class="smallcaps"><span style="font-variant: small-caps; text-transform: lowercase;">DOHT</span></span></b></i></a></span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Hebrew_language" title="Hebrew language">Hebrew</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="he">גל גדות</span>‎‎, <small>pronounced </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Hebrew" title="Help:IPA for Hebrew">[ˈgal gaˈdot]</a></span>; born 30 April 1985)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-allmoviebio_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-allmoviebio-3">[3]</a></sup> is an Israeli actress and model. As an actress, Gadot is known for her role as <a href="/wiki/Gisele_Yashar" title="Gisele Yashar">Gisele Yashar</a> in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Fast_and_the_Furious_(film_series)" title="The Fast and the Furious (film series)">The Fast and the Furious</a></i> film series. In 2016, Gadot began playing the role of <a href="/wiki/Wonder_Woman" title="Wonder Woman">Wonder Woman</a> in the <a href="/wiki/DC_Extended_Universe" title="DC Extended Universe">DC Extended Universe</a>, starting with <i><a href="/wiki/Batman_v_Superman:_Dawn_of_Justice" title="Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice">Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice</a></i> and continuing as the lead in <i><a href="/wiki/Wonder_Woman_(2017_film)" title="Wonder Woman (2017 film)">Wonder Woman</a></i> (2017).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>In past years, she has been ranked as one of the highest earning models in <a href="/wiki/Israel" title="Israel">Israel</a>, behind <a href="/wiki/Bar_Refaeli" title="Bar Refaeli">Bar Refaeli</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> She is the face of <a href="/wiki/Gucci" title="Gucci">Gucci</a>'s Bamboo perfume.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hollywoodreporter.com_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-hollywoodreporter.com-7">[7]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gal_Gadot&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Gadot was born and raised in <a href="/wiki/Rosh_HaAyin" title="Rosh HaAyin">Rosh HaAyin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Israel" title="Israel">Israel</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> In <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hebrew" title="Hebrew">Hebrew</a>, her first name means "wave" and her surname means "riverbanks".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> She is the daughter of Irit (née Weiss), a teacher, and Michael Gadot, an engineer. Her parents were both <a href="/wiki/Sabra_(person)" title="Sabra (person)">born in Israel</a>, and they had <a href="/wiki/Hebraization_of_surnames" title="Hebraization of surnames">Hebraized</a> their surname from "Greenstein" to "Gadot".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> Her maternal grandparents were <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Holocaust" title="Holocaust">Holocaust</a> survivors.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> Her ancestry is Polish-Jewish, Austrian-Jewish, German-Jewish, and Czech-Jewish.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup> Her high school major was <a href="/wiki/Biology" title="Biology">biology</a>. She says that in high school she was successful at basketball due to her height.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ynet_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Ynet-1">[1]</a></sup> As an adult, she started studying <a href="/wiki/Law" title="Law">law</a> twice at the <a href="/wiki/Interdisciplinary_Center_Herzliya" title="Interdisciplinary Center Herzliya">Reichman</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ynet_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-Ynet-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Military_service">Military service</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gal_Gadot&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Military service">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>From the age of 20, Gadot served for two years as an enlisted soldier of the <a href="/wiki/Israel_Defense_Forces" title="Israel Defense Forces">Israel Defense Forces</a>. There, she excelled in a grueling three-month boot camp that prepared her to serve as a combat trainer.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-glamour.com_14-0"><a href="#cite_note-glamour.com-14">[14]</a></sup> She says of her time in the army: "You give two or three years, and it's not about you. You give your freedom away for a while. You learn discipline and respect. The things I've been through as a soldier prepared me to deal with career things as well".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-glamour.com_14-1"><a href="#cite_note-glamour.com-14">[14]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gal_Gadot&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright">

<p>At the age of 18, Gadot won the 2004 <a href="/wiki/Miss_Israel" title="Miss Israel">Miss Israel</a> competition, and next competed in the <a href="/wiki/Miss_Universe_2004" title="Miss Universe 2004">2004 Miss Universe</a> pageant in <a href="/wiki/Ecuador" title="Ecuador">Ecuador</a>. Following her participation in the competitions, she joined the <a href="/wiki/Israel_Defense_Forces" title="Israel Defense Forces">Israeli Army</a> where, as a citizen of Israel, she completed her two years of mandatory military service.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup></p>
<p>After leaving the army, Gadot studied law. It was when she had completed her first year of school that a casting director called her to audition for the part of <a href="/wiki/Bond_girl" title="Bond girl">Bond girl</a> <a href="/wiki/Camille_Montes" title="Camille Montes">Camille Montes</a> in the film <i><a href="/wiki/Quantum_of_Solace" title="Quantum of Solace">Quantum of Solace</a></i>. Although she lost the part to <a href="/wiki/Olga_Kurylenko" title="Olga Kurylenko">Olga Kurylenko</a>, the same casting director hired Gadot for the role of Gisele in <i><a href="/wiki/Fast_%26_Furious_(2009_film)" title="Fast &amp; Furious (2009 film)">Fast &amp; Furious</a></i>. Gadot said, "I think the main reason was that [the director] <a href="/wiki/Justin_Lin" title="Justin Lin">Justin Lin</a> really liked that I was in the military, and he wanted to use my knowledge of weapons."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-blogs.forward.com_17-0"><a href="#cite_note-blogs.forward.com-17">[17]</a></sup> Gadot performed her own stunts in the <i>Fast &amp; Furious</i> films.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-JewishJournal_18-0"><a href="#cite_note-JewishJournal-18">[18]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2007, the 21 year old Gadot took a part in the <i><a href="/wiki/Maxim_(magazine)" title="Maxim (magazine)">Maxim</a></i> photo shoot "Women of the Israeli Army", and as such she featured on the cover of the <i><a href="/wiki/New_York_Post" title="New York Post">New York Post</a></i>. In April 2012, <i>Shalom Life</i> ranked her Number 5 on its list of "the 50 most talented, intelligent, funny, and gorgeous Jewish women in the world", behind model <a href="/wiki/Bar_Refaeli" title="Bar Refaeli">Bar Refaeli</a> and actress <a href="/wiki/Eva_Green" title="Eva Green">Eva Green</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup> In 2014, Gadot was one of two Israeli actresses, along with <a href="/wiki/Odeya_Rush" title="Odeya Rush">Odeya Rush</a>, listed as an upcoming leading lady by <i><a href="/wiki/InStyle" title="InStyle">InStyle</a></i> magazine.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-20"><a href="#cite_note-20">[20]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Modeling">Modeling</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gal_Gadot&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Modeling">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>She has led international campaigns for <a href="/wiki/Captain_Morgan" title="Captain Morgan">Captain Morgan</a> rum, <a href="/wiki/Gucci" title="Gucci">Gucci</a> fragrances and Vine Vera skincare ranges, and advertised <a href="/wiki/Jaguar_Cars" title="Jaguar Cars">Jaguar Cars</a>. She is the face of Gucci's Bamboo perfume brand,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hollywoodreporter.com_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-hollywoodreporter.com-7">[7]</a></sup> of <a href="/wiki/Huawei" title="Huawei">Huawei</a> smartphones, and of <a href="/wiki/Miss_Sixty" title="Miss Sixty">Miss Sixty</a>. She has featured as the covergirl on <i><a href="/wiki/Cosmopolitan_(magazine)" title="Cosmopolitan (magazine)">Cosmopolitan</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Glamour_(magazine)" title="Glamour (magazine)">Glamour</a></i>, <i>Bride Magazine</i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Entertainment_Weekly" title="Entertainment Weekly">Entertainment Weekly</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Urban_Male_Magazine" title="Urban Male Magazine">UMM</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Cleo_(magazine)" title="Cleo (magazine)">Cleo</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Fashion_(magazine)" title="Fashion (magazine)">Fashion</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Lucire" title="Lucire">Lucire</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/FHM" title="FHM">FHM</a></i>. Gadot has been the main model for fashion brand <a href="/wiki/Castro_(clothing)" title="Castro (clothing)">Castro</a> in 2008–2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-21"><a href="#cite_note-21">[21]</a></sup> In 2013, her annual modelling salary was estimated at <a href="/wiki/Israeli_new_shekel" title="Israeli new shekel">NIS</a> 2.4 million, which was ahead of a number of other famous Israeli models such as <a href="/wiki/Esti_Ginzburg" title="Esti Ginzburg">Esti Ginzburg</a> and <a href="/wiki/Shlomit_Malka" title="Shlomit Malka">Shlomit Malka</a>, although significantly behind <a href="/wiki/Bar_Refaeli" title="Bar Refaeli">Bar Refaeli</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-22"><a href="#cite_note-22">[22]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Acting">Acting</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gal_Gadot&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Acting">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p>In 2008, Gadot starred in the Israeli drama <i>Bubot</i>. She appeared as Gisele in <i><a href="/wiki/Fast_%26_Furious_(2009_film)" title="Fast &amp; Furious (2009 film)">Fast &amp; Furious</a></i>, the fourth film in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Fast_and_the_Furious_(film_series)" title="The Fast and the Furious (film series)"><i>The Fast &amp; the Furious</i> franchise</a>, having won the role over six other actresses.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-23"><a href="#cite_note-23">[23]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-24"><a href="#cite_note-24">[24]</a></sup> In 2010, she had a small role in the action-adventure <i><a href="/wiki/Knight_and_Day" title="Knight and Day">Knight and Day</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-25"><a href="#cite_note-25">[25]</a></sup> Earlier that year, she appeared in the film <i><a href="/wiki/Date_Night" title="Date Night">Date Night</a></i> as Natanya, the girlfriend of <a href="/wiki/Mark_Wahlberg" title="Mark Wahlberg">Mark Wahlberg</a>'s character. 2011 brought her back to <i>The Fast &amp; the Furious</i> franchise, reprising her role as Gisele in <i><a href="/wiki/Fast_Five" title="Fast Five">Fast Five</a></i>. In 2013, Gadot played Gisele again in <i><a href="/wiki/Fast_%26_Furious_6" title="Fast &amp; Furious 6">Fast &amp; Furious 6</a></i>.</p>

<p>Gadot played <a href="/wiki/Wonder_Woman" title="Wonder Woman">Wonder Woman</a> in the movie <i><a href="/wiki/Batman_v_Superman:_Dawn_of_Justice" title="Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice">Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice</a></i> (2016), and is signed for two future sequels which include the <i><a href="/wiki/Justice_League_(film)" title="Justice League (film)">Justice League</a></i> and a solo <i><a href="/wiki/Wonder_Woman_(2017_film)" title="Wonder Woman (2017 film)">Wonder Woman</a></i> film.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-26"><a href="#cite_note-26">[26]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-27"><a href="#cite_note-27">[27]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-28"><a href="#cite_note-28">[28]</a></sup> Gadot received <a href="/wiki/Swordsmanship" title="Swordsmanship">swordsmanship</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chinese_martial_arts" title="Chinese martial arts">Kung Fu</a> <a href="/wiki/Kickboxing" title="Kickboxing">kickboxing</a>, <a href="/wiki/Capoeira" title="Capoeira">capoeira</a> and <a href="/wiki/Brazilian_jiu-jitsu" title="Brazilian jiu-jitsu">Brazilian jiu-jitsu</a> training in preparation for the role.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-29"><a href="#cite_note-29">[29]</a></sup> Gadot's performance as the superhero, which is the first time for the character on film, was hailed as one of the best parts of the film.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-30"><a href="#cite_note-30">[30]</a></sup></p>
<p>Also in 2016, she had a small role in <a href="/wiki/John_Hillcoat" title="John Hillcoat">John Hillcoat</a>'s crime-thriller <i><a href="/wiki/Triple_9" title="Triple 9">Triple 9</a></i>, where she starred along with <a href="/wiki/Kate_Winslet" title="Kate Winslet">Kate Winslet</a> and <a href="/wiki/Aaron_Paul" title="Aaron Paul">Aaron Paul</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-31"><a href="#cite_note-31">[31]</a></sup> Later that year, she co-starred in the thriller film <i><a href="/wiki/Criminal_(2016_film)" title="Criminal (2016 film)">Criminal</a></i>, as the wife of <a href="/wiki/Ryan_Reynolds" title="Ryan Reynolds">Ryan Reynolds</a>' character,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-32"><a href="#cite_note-32">[32]</a></sup> alongside <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Costner" title="Kevin Costner">Kevin Costner</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gary_Oldman" title="Gary Oldman">Gary Oldman</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Tommy_Lee_Jones" title="Tommy Lee Jones">Tommy Lee Jones</a>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-33"><a href="#cite_note-33">[33]</a></sup> Costner has said that the day he and Gadot met, they filmed a scene in which he was to tie her up and wrap her mouth with tape as a <a href="/wiki/Gag" title="Gag">gag</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-34"><a href="#cite_note-34">[34]</a></sup> Her final film of 2016 was the comedy <i><a href="/wiki/Keeping_Up_with_the_Joneses_(film)" title="Keeping Up with the Joneses (film)">Keeping Up with the Joneses</a></i>, in which she played a secret agent, alongside <a href="/wiki/Isla_Fisher" title="Isla Fisher">Isla Fisher</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jon_Hamm" title="Jon Hamm">Jon Hamm</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-35"><a href="#cite_note-35">[35]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gal_Gadot&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Gadot married Israeli real estate developer Yaron Varsano on 28 September 2008.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-36"><a href="#cite_note-36">[36]</a></sup> The couple owned a luxury hotel in the <a href="/wiki/Neve_Tzedek" title="Neve Tzedek">Neve Tzedek</a> district of <a href="/wiki/Tel_Aviv" title="Tel Aviv">Tel Aviv</a>, which they sold to <a href="/wiki/Roman_Abramovich" title="Roman Abramovich">Roman Abramovich</a> in 2015 for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/USD" title="USD">$</a>26 million.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-37"><a href="#cite_note-37">[37]</a></sup> The couple have a daughter, born in 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-38"><a href="#cite_note-38">[38]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-39"><a href="#cite_note-39">[39]</a></sup> In November 2016, Gadot announced she and Varsano are expecting their second child.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-40"><a href="#cite_note-40">[40]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-41"><a href="#cite_note-41">[41]</a></sup></p>
<p>She is a motorcycle rider and owns a black 2006 <a href="/wiki/Ducati_Monster" title="Ducati Monster">Ducati Monster</a>-S2R.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-42"><a href="#cite_note-42">[42]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2016, Wonder Woman played by Gadot (along with <a href="/wiki/Lynda_Carter" title="Lynda Carter">Lynda Carter</a>) was named an Honorary Ambassador for the Empowerment of Women &amp; Girls by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UN" title="UN">United Nations</a>. Subsequently, Gadot was invited to give a speech at the UN in New York City.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-43"><a href="#cite_note-43">[43]</a></sup></p>
