<p><b>Cymothoida</b> is the name of a suborder of <a href="/wiki/Isopoda" title="Isopoda">isopod</a> <a href="/wiki/Crustacean" title="Crustacean">crustaceans</a> with a mostly <a href="/wiki/Carnivore" title="Carnivore">carnivorous</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Parasite" title="Parasite">parasitic</a> lifestyle. It contains more than 2,700 described species in four <a href="/wiki/Taxonomic_rank#Ranks_in_zoology" title="Taxonomic rank">superfamilies</a>. Members of the suborder are characterised by their specialised mouthparts which include a mandible with a tooth-like process which is adapted for cutting or slicing.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
