<p><b>Gala-Yuzawa Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">ガーラ湯沢駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Gāra-yuzawa-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a seasonal railway station on the <a href="/wiki/Gala-Yuzawa_Line" title="Gala-Yuzawa Line">Gala-Yuzawa Line</a> in <a href="/wiki/Yuzawa,_Niigata" title="Yuzawa, Niigata">Yuzawa, Niigata</a>, Japan, operated by <a href="/wiki/East_Japan_Railway_Company" title="East Japan Railway Company">East Japan Railway Company</a> (JR East).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lines">Lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gala-Yuzawa_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The station is served by the <a href="/wiki/Gala-Yuzawa_Line" title="Gala-Yuzawa Line">Gala-Yuzawa Line</a> from <a href="/wiki/Echigo-Yuzawa_Station" title="Echigo-Yuzawa Station">Echigo-Yuzawa Station</a>, and forms the only station on this 1.8 km branch line. Services are classed as "<a href="/wiki/Limited_express" title="Limited express">Limited express</a>", although they are actually extensions of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Joetsu_Shinkansen" title="Joetsu Shinkansen">Joetsu Shinkansen</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Tanigawa_(train)" title="Tanigawa (train)">Tanigawa</a></i> services. The station is open to passengers only during the winter ski season, usually from mid-December until April.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Layout">Layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gala-Yuzawa_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The station consists of an <a href="/wiki/Island_platform" title="Island platform">island platform</a> serving two tracks.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-railfan467_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-railfan467-1">[1]</a></sup> The station has a "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Midori_no_Madoguchi" title="Midori no Madoguchi">Midori no Madoguchi</a>" staffed ticket office.</p>
