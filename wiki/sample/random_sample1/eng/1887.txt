<p><b>Gambassi Terme</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Florence" title="Province of Florence">Province of Florence</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Tuscany" title="Tuscany">Tuscany</a>, located about 35 kilometres (22 mi) southwest of <a href="/wiki/Florence" title="Florence">Florence</a>. It has a population of 4,890 inhabitants.</p>

<ul>
<li>The church of <i>San Giovanni Battista</i>, in the <i>frazione</i> of Varna, which houses a replica of <a href="/wiki/Andrea_del_Sarto" title="Andrea del Sarto">Andrea del Sarto</a>'s <i>Madonna with Child and Saints</i>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Romanesque_architecture" title="Romanesque architecture">Romanesque</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Pieve" title="Pieve">Pieve</a></i> of <i>Santa Maria Assunta</i> (12th century)</li>
<li><i>Parco Comunale</i>, former garden of the Villa Sinnai.</li>
</ul>
