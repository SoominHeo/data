<p><b>Gangnam-gu Office Station</b> is a station on <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Subway_Line_7" title="Seoul Subway Line 7">Seoul Subway Line 7</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Bundang_Line" title="Bundang Line">Bundang Line</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gangnam-gu" title="Gangnam-gu">Gangnam</a> district of <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a>.</p>
<p>The station serves the southern area of <a href="/wiki/Apgujeong-dong" title="Apgujeong-dong">Apgujeong-dong</a>, and is closer to various parts of Apgujeong than <a href="/wiki/Apgujeong_Station" title="Apgujeong Station">Apgujeong Station</a>.</p>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Gangnam_District" title="Gangnam District">Gangnam</a> - <a href="/wiki/University_of_California,_Riverside" title="University of California, Riverside">University of California, Riverside</a> (GNUCR) International Education Center and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/SK_Corporation" title="SK Corporation">SK Blue Hub</a> building are adjacent to the station. The eponymous Gangnam-gu local government district headquarters is a short distance to the east of the station.</p>
