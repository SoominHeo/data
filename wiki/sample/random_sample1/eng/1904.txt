<p><b>Gangneung Yeongdong College</b> is a private technical college in Seongsan-myeon, a rural area of <a href="/wiki/Gangneung" title="Gangneung">Gangneung</a> city in northeastern <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. It takes its name from the <a href="/wiki/Yeongdong_(region)" title="Yeongdong (region)">Yeongdong</a> region, in which it is located.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Campus">Campus</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gangneung_Yeongdong_University&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Campus">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>There are twelve major buildings on the campus, which covers an area of 113,000 square metres.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gangneung_Yeongdong_University&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The school officially opened on June 1, 1963, as Gangneung Superior Technical School of Nursing (강릉간호고등기술학교). It had a maximum enrollment of 90 students. Enrollment was raised to 120 in 1966, and doubled to 240 in 1971. It was redesignated a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Technical_college" title="Technical college">technical college</a> in 1973.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Organization">Organization</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gangneung_Yeongdong_University&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Organization">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>As a technical college, the school provides courses of study of two or three years in length. Two-year courses are provided by the Divisions of Health (departments of Optometry and Beauty), Hospitality, Social Service and Industry. Three-year courses are provided by the Divisions of Health, Nursing, and Social Service (department of <a href="/wiki/Early_childhood_education" title="Early childhood education">early childhood education</a>).</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Students_and_faculty">Students and faculty</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gangneung_Yeongdong_University&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Students and faculty">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Entering classes now average about 1,500 students, of which more than 90% study in regular daytime classes. The remainder attend <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/night_school" title="wikt:night school">night school</a>.</p>
