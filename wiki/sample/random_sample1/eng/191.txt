<p><b>!!!</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Dance-punk" title="Dance-punk">dance-punk</a> band that formed in <a href="/wiki/Sacramento,_California" title="Sacramento, California">Sacramento</a>, California, in 1996 by lead singer Nic Offer. Its name is most commonly pronounced "Chk Chk Chk" (<span class="nowrap"><span class="IPA nopopups"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_English" title="Help:IPA for English">/<span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="/tʃ/ 'ch' in 'china'">tʃ</span><span title="'k' in 'kind'">k</span><span title="/./ syllable break">.</span><span title="/tʃ/ 'ch' in 'china'">tʃ</span><span title="'k' in 'kind'">k</span><span title="/./ syllable break">.</span><span title="/tʃ/ 'ch' in 'china'">tʃ</span><span title="'k' in 'kind'">k</span></span>/</a></span></span>). Members of !!! came from other local bands such as The Yah Mos, Black Liquorice and Popesmashers. They are currently based in <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sacramento,_California" title="Sacramento, California">Sacramento</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Portland,_Oregon" title="Portland, Oregon">Portland, Oregon</a>. The band's sixth full-length album, <i><a href="/wiki/As_If_(album)" title="As If (album)">As If</a></i>, was released in October 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ChkChkChkOrigin-DeliMag_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-ChkChkChkOrigin-DeliMag-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NPRatc07_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-NPRatc07-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background_and_history">Background and history</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=!!!&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background and history">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>!!! is an American band formed in the summer of 1995 by the merger of part of the group Black Liquorice and Popesmashers. After a successful joint tour, the two teams decided to mix the disco-funk with more aggressive sounds and integrate the <a href="/wiki/Hardcore_punk" title="Hardcore punk">hardcore</a> singer Nic Offer from the Yah Mos. The band's name was inspired by the subtitles of the movie <i><a href="/wiki/The_Gods_Must_Be_Crazy" title="The Gods Must Be Crazy">The Gods Must Be Crazy</a></i>, in which the <a href="/wiki/Click_consonant" title="Click consonant">clicking sounds</a> of the <a href="/wiki/San_people" title="San people">Bushmens</a>' <a href="/wiki/Khoisan_languages" title="Khoisan languages">Khoisan language</a> were represented as "!". However, as the bandmembers themselves say, !!! is pronounced by repeating thrice any monosyllabic sound. "Chk Chk Chk" is the most common pronunciation,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NPRatc07_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-NPRatc07-2">[2]</a></sup> and the URL of their official website and the title of their <a href="/wiki/Myspace" title="Myspace">Myspace</a> page suggest it is the preferred pronunciation.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-myspace_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-myspace-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>The band's full-length debut record came out in 2000 as a self-titled album on the label <a href="/wiki/Gold_Standard_Laboratories" title="Gold Standard Laboratories">Gold Standard Laboratories</a>. This was followed in 2003 by the single "Me and Giuliani Down By the School Yard", a lengthy track combining <a href="/wiki/House_music" title="House music">house</a> beats with sinewy <a href="/wiki/Bassline" title="Bassline">basslines</a>, <a href="/wiki/Psychedelia" title="Psychedelia">psychedelic</a> guitars, and simple lyrics which quote the title song of the musical <i><a href="/wiki/Footloose_(musical)" title="Footloose (musical)">Footloose</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>A second full-length, <i><a href="/wiki/Louden_Up_Now" title="Louden Up Now">Louden Up Now</a></i>, was released on <a href="/wiki/Touch_and_Go_Records" title="Touch and Go Records">Touch and Go</a> in America and on <a href="/wiki/Warp_(record_label)" title="Warp (record label)">Warp Records</a> in Europe in June 2004. In June 2005 !!! released a new <a href="/wiki/Extended_play" title="Extended play">EP</a> covering "<a href="/wiki/Holiday_(The_Magnetic_Fields_album)" title="Holiday (The Magnetic Fields album)">Take Ecstasy with Me</a>" by <a href="/wiki/The_Magnetic_Fields" title="The Magnetic Fields">The Magnetic Fields</a>, and "Get Up" by <a href="/wiki/Nate_Dogg" title="Nate Dogg">Nate Dogg</a>. The following December, the original drummer for the band, Mikel Gius, was struck and killed by a car while riding his bike.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>They released their third album, <i><a href="/wiki/Myth_Takes" title="Myth Takes">Myth Takes</a></i> in 2007.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PF-MTr_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-PF-MTr-8">[8]</a></sup></p>

<p>!!! is composed of Mario Andreoni (guitar), Dan Gorman (<a href="/wiki/Horn_section" title="Horn section">horns</a>/percussion/<a href="/wiki/Musical_keyboard" title="Musical keyboard">keys</a>), Nic Offer (vocals), Tyler Pope (bass/various electronic devices), and Allan Wilson (horns/percussion/keys). Touring members include Shannon Funchess (vocals), Sean McGahan (various electronic devices/sound manipulation) and Paul Quattrone (drums). The band also shared membership with the similar, defunct group <a href="/wiki/Out_Hud" title="Out Hud">Out Hud</a> (including Tyler Pope, who has played with <a href="/wiki/LCD_Soundsystem" title="LCD Soundsystem">LCD Soundsystem</a> and written music for <a href="/wiki/Cake_(band)" title="Cake (band)">Cake</a>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<p>Vocalist and drummer John Pugh officially left the band in July 2007 to concentrate on his new band Free Blood.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> Shannon Funchess stood in for Pugh during much of their 2007 tour.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
