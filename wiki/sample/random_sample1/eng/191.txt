<p>At the <b><a href="/wiki/1928_Winter_Olympics" title="1928 Winter Olympics">1928 Winter Olympics</a></b> one individual <b><a href="/wiki/Nordic_combined" title="Nordic combined">Nordic combined</a></b> event was contested. It was held on Friday, February 17, 1928 (<a href="/wiki/Cross-country_skiing" title="Cross-country skiing">cross-country skiing</a>) and on Saturday, February 18, 1928 (<a href="/wiki/Ski_jumping" title="Ski jumping">ski jumping</a>). Unlike today the ski jump was the last event held. Both events were also individual medal events.</p>
