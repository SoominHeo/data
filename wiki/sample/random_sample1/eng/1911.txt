<p><b>Kang Jung-hun</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">강정훈</span>; born 16 December 1987) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Forward_(association_football)" title="Forward (association football)">forward</a> for <a href="/wiki/Busan_Transportation_Corporation_FC" title="Busan Transportation Corporation FC">Busan Transportation Corporation</a> on loan from <a href="/wiki/FC_Seoul" title="FC Seoul">FC Seoul</a> in the <a href="/wiki/K_League_Classic" title="K League Classic">K League Classic</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kang_Jung-hun&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kang was <a href="/wiki/FC_Seoul" title="FC Seoul">FC Seoul</a>'s first choice from the 2010 draft intake,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and made his professional debut on 25 April 2010 in Seoul's away <a href="/wiki/2010_K-League" title="2010 K-League">league</a> match against <a href="/wiki/Gyeongnam_FC" title="Gyeongnam FC">Gyeongnam FC</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Aside from a few <a href="/wiki/2010_Korean_League_Cup" title="2010 Korean League Cup">League Cup</a> matches, Kang was unused for the remainder of the 2010 season. Kang continued with FC Seoul into the <a href="/wiki/2011_K-League" title="2011 K-League">2011 K-League</a> season, and scored his first professional goal against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jeonbuk_Hyundai_Motors" title="Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors">Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
