<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Gangcheng</b> (钢城区; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i>Gāngchéng Qū</i>) is a district of <a href="/wiki/Laiwu" title="Laiwu">Laiwu</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/People%27s_Republic_of_China" title="People's Republic of China">Chinese</a> province of <a href="/wiki/Shandong" title="Shandong">Shandong</a>.</p>
<p>Taishan has an area of 336 km² and around 240,000 inhabitants (2003). It is the centre and administrative capital of Tai'an.</p>
