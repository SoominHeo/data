<p><i><b>The Frog Kingdom</b></i> (Chinese: 青蛙王国) is a 2013 Chinese animated <a href="/wiki/Adventure_film" title="Adventure film">adventure</a> drama film directed by <a href="/wiki/Nelson_Shin" title="Nelson Shin">Nelson Shin</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> A <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> dub of the film was released on <a href="/wiki/Video_on_demand" title="Video on demand">video on demand</a> and on DVD in the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> on June 30, 2015 by <a href="/wiki/Lionsgate" title="Lionsgate">Lionsgate</a> and Grindstone Entertainment Group.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-:0-3">[3]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Synopsis">Synopsis</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Frog_Kingdom&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Synopsis">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>When The Frog King announces that the winner of the annual Froglympics will win his daughter’s hand in marriage, she runs away instead. Disguised as a commoner, Princess Froglegs befriends street vendor, Freddie and begins training with him for the games—determined that she will win and thwart her father’s plans of marriage. Meanwhile, the villainous serpent has ideas of his own to ruin the Froglympics and take over the kingdom. With a little luck, and a little help from their trusty sidekicks Bestie and Boogie, Freddie and Princess Froglegs set out to save Frog Kingdom from the evil serpent.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Characters">Characters</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Frog_Kingdom&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Characters">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bella_Thorne" title="Bella Thorne">Bella Thorne</a> as Geake (Princess Froglegs) - The Princess of the kingdom. She recently disguise herself as a man with a mustache and wears a cap of her head to cover her flower, to sneak out with her maid and best friend and the competition.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Cameron_Dallas" title="Cameron Dallas">Cameron Dallas</a> as Raindrop (Freddy) - A traveling hermit along with his tadpole friend. He joins the competition and becomes partners with Geake, unaware that she's a girl and the princess. He serves as Geake's love interest.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Rob_Schneider" title="Rob Schneider">Rob Schneider</a> as One Eye</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Drake_Bell" title="Drake Bell">Drake Bell</a> as Announcer</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Keith_David" title="Keith David">Keith David</a> as King</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Release">Release</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Frog_Kingdom&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Release">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The film was released in <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a> on December 28, 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-auto_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-auto-4">[4]</a></sup> An English dub of the film featuring <a href="/wiki/Cameron_Dallas" title="Cameron Dallas">Cameron Dallas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bella_Thorne" title="Bella Thorne">Bella Thorne</a> and <a href="/wiki/Drake_Bell" title="Drake Bell">Drake Bell</a> was released in the United States on June 30, 2015 on DVD and through <a href="/wiki/Video_on_demand" title="Video on demand">video on demand</a> by <a href="/wiki/Lionsgate" title="Lionsgate">Lionsgate</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-:0-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Sequel">Sequel</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Frog_Kingdom&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Sequel">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
