<p><b>Gabrielle Christian</b> (born <b>Gabrielle Christine Horchler</b>; July 30, 1984)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Facts_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-Facts-1">[1]</a></sup> is an American actress and singer best known for her portrayal of <a href="/wiki/Spencer_Carlin" title="Spencer Carlin">Spencer Carlin</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_N" title="The N">The N</a> original series <i><a href="/wiki/South_of_Nowhere" title="South of Nowhere">South of Nowhere</a></i>, and her portrayal of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Colby_Robson" title="Colby Robson">Colby Robson</a> in the web series <i><a href="/wiki/Girltrash!" title="Girltrash!">Girltrash!</a></i> and its musical prequel film <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Girltrash!:_All_Night_Long" title="Girltrash!: All Night Long">Girltrash!: All Night Long</a></i>. In addition, she has also guest starred on <i><a href="/wiki/Drake_%26_Josh" title="Drake &amp; Josh">Drake &amp; Josh</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Windfall_(television_series)" title="Windfall (television series)">Windfall</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Without_a_Trace" title="Without a Trace">Without a Trace</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/What_Should_You_Do%3F" title="What Should You Do?">What Should You Do?</a></i> along with several minor roles in a variety of other shows.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_and_career">Early life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gabrielle_Christian&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Christian was born <b>Gabrielle Christine Horchler</b> in <a href="/wiki/Washington,_D.C." title="Washington, D.C.">Washington, D.C.</a> to parents Gabe and Joani. She grew up in <a href="/wiki/Cheverly,_Maryland" title="Cheverly, Maryland">Cheverly, Maryland</a>. She began performing in plays at age 12. She has four sisters (Gen, Steph, Jules, Ili). Christian acquired her first acting job while she still attending high school at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Eleanor_Roosevelt_High_School_(Greenbelt,_Maryland)" title="Eleanor Roosevelt High School (Greenbelt, Maryland)">Eleanor Roosevelt High School</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Greenbelt,_Maryland" title="Greenbelt, Maryland">Greenbelt, Maryland</a>, on the television series <i><a href="/wiki/Young_Americans_(TV_series)" title="Young Americans (TV series)">Young Americans</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Facts_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-Facts-1">[1]</a></sup> She studied one and half years of theater at the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Pittsburgh" title="University of Pittsburgh">University of Pittsburgh</a>, before relocating to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Los_Angeles,_California" title="Los Angeles, California">Los Angeles, California</a> after landing roles on several television series.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Facts_1-4"><a href="#cite_note-Facts-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gabrielle_Christian&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Christian relocated to Los Angeles after landing roles on the TV series <i><a href="/wiki/What_Should_You_Do%3F" title="What Should You Do?">What Should You Do?</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Without_a_Trace" title="Without a Trace">Without a Trace</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Drake_%26_Josh" title="Drake &amp; Josh">Drake &amp; Josh</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Film_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Film-3">[3]</a></sup> Her most notable role was portraying <a href="/wiki/Spencer_Carlin" title="Spencer Carlin">Spencer Carlin</a> on <a href="/wiki/TeenNick" title="TeenNick">The N’s</a> hit television series <i><a href="/wiki/South_of_Nowhere" title="South of Nowhere">South of Nowhere</a></i> which aired for three seasons between 2005 and 2008.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Facts_1-5"><a href="#cite_note-Facts-1">[1]</a></sup> Outside of <i>South Of Nowhere</i>, Christian appeared in <i><a href="/wiki/CSI:_Miami" title="CSI: Miami">CSI: Miami</a></i> as Amy Hobbs in the Season six episode "<a href="/wiki/CSI:_Miami_(season_6)#Episodes" title="CSI: Miami (season 6)">Permanent Vacation</a>."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Film_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-Film-3">[3]</a></sup> In 2006 she appeared as Miranda Keyton in the "<a href="/wiki/Windfall_(TV_series)#Episode_list" title="Windfall (TV series)">Truth Be Told and Priceless</a>" episodes of <i><a href="/wiki/Windfall_(TV_series)" title="Windfall (TV series)">Windfall</a></i>. In 2007 Christian portrayed Colby Robson in <a href="/wiki/Angela_Robinson" title="Angela Robinson">Angela Robinson</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Girltrash!" title="Girltrash!">Girltrash!</a></i> web-series alongside <i>South of Nowhere</i> co-star Mandy Musgrave. In 2008 she appeared in the "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_Numb3rs_episodes_(season_4)#Episodes" title="List of Numb3rs episodes (season 4)">Atomic No. 33</a>" episode of <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Numb3rs" title="Numb3rs">Numb3rs</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Film_3-2"><a href="#cite_note-Film-3">[3]</a></sup> The same year she appeared in the web-series <i>3Way</i> as Cindy Shims, however her episode "Ladycops" was not posted as a regular episode during the duration of the series. In 2010 she appeared as Justine Grogan, the daughter of Abbey Grogan (<a href="/wiki/Jennifer_Grey" title="Jennifer Grey">Jennifer Grey</a>), on the "<a href="/wiki/Unplanned_Parenthood_(House)" title="Unplanned Parenthood (House)">Unplanned Parenthood</a>" episode of <i><a href="/wiki/House_(TV_series)" title="House (TV series)">House</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> In August 2011 she guest starred on <i><a href="/wiki/The_Protector_(TV_series)" title="The Protector (TV series)">The Protector</a></i> as lab assistant Laurel Nash. Christian has two films yet to be released the first being <i>You're Cordially Uninvited</i> where she portrays Rose, a woman who is engaged to another woman (Lisa), but wishes not to have to face her parents and their reaction. The second film yet to be released is the musical prequel to the <i>Girltrash!</i> web-series titled <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Girltrash:_All_Night_Long" title="Girltrash: All Night Long">Girltrash: All Night Long</a></i> where she will once again portray Colby Robson.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Film_3-3"><a href="#cite_note-Film-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>On June 2, 2011 Christian released her first single entitled "Kissing Mandy" to <a href="/wiki/ITunes" title="ITunes">iTunes</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-KissingMandy_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-KissingMandy-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gabrielle_Christian&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Christian helps her mother with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sudden_Infant_Death_Syndrome" title="Sudden Infant Death Syndrome">Sudden Infant Death Syndrome</a> (SIDS) organization. This organization was created by her mother after the loss of her son in 1991. She uses the surname Christian in memory of her brother. Her mother has written a book, often known as the SIDS "Bible". The book features a poem Christian wrote when she was eight.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Facts_1-6"><a href="#cite_note-Facts-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Christian became the spokesperson for the organization FAIR Fund,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FAIR_FUND_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-FAIR_FUND-6">[6]</a></sup> which works to engage youth in the areas of <a href="/wiki/Human_trafficking" title="Human trafficking">human trafficking</a>, <a href="/wiki/Domestic_violence" title="Domestic violence">domestic violence</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Sexual_assault" title="Sexual assault">sexual assault</a> prevention. She hosts cocktail events to showcase jewelry made by girls participating in the JewelGirls program.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-jewelGirlz_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-jewelGirlz-7">[7]</a></sup> Christian's father is from <a href="/wiki/Hungary" title="Hungary">Hungary</a>. She speaks fluent <a href="/wiki/Hungarian_language" title="Hungarian language">Hungarian</a>, conversational <a href="/wiki/French_language" title="French language">French</a>, and basic <a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-AE_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-AE-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>Christian maintains close friendships with fellow <i>South of Nowhere</i> cast members <a href="/wiki/Maeve_Quinlan" title="Maeve Quinlan">Maeve Quinlan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mandy_Musgrave" title="Mandy Musgrave">Mandy Musgrave</a>. Christian married longtime boyfriend Justin Mentzer on July 11, 2009 at Saint Timothy's Chapel in Mentzer's home town of <a href="/wiki/Drummond,_Montana" title="Drummond, Montana">Drummond, Montana</a> with the reception held at the Mentzer's family ranch.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TIESTHEKNOT_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-TIESTHEKNOT-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
