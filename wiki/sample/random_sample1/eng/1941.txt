<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Military">Military</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gunner&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Military">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>Gunner, an <a href="/wiki/Automatic_rifle" title="Automatic rifle">automatic rifleman</a> specialist trained in using <a href="/wiki/Light_machine_gun" title="Light machine gun">light machine gun</a> or <a href="/wiki/Squad_automatic_weapon" title="Squad automatic weapon">squad automatic weapon</a> as part of a fireteam</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gunner_(artillery)" title="Gunner (artillery)">Gunner (artillery)</a>, member of the crew operating a crew-served weapon, such as an artillery piece</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gunner_(rank)" title="Gunner (rank)">Gunner (rank)</a>, rank equivalent to private in a British Commonwealth artillery corps</li>
<li>Gunner, non-commissioned member of the British <a href="/wiki/RAF_Regiment" title="RAF Regiment">RAF Regiment</a></li>
<li>Gunner, <a href="/wiki/Sailor" title="Sailor">sailor</a> responsible for a ship's cannons</li>
<li>Gunner, gunnery <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Warrant_Officer" title="Warrant Officer">Warrant Officer</a> of the British Royal Navy or Royal Marines</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Air_gunner" title="Air gunner">Air gunner</a>, or aerial gunner, a member of an aircrew who operates a machine gun or auto-cannon</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Marine_Gunner_(rank)" title="Marine Gunner (rank)">Marine Gunner</a>, Warrant Officer in the United States Marine Corps</li>
<li>The Gunners, a nickname for the British <a href="/wiki/Royal_Artillery" title="Royal Artillery">Royal Artillery</a></li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Sports">Sports</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gunner&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Sports">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gunner_(American_football)" title="Gunner (American football)">Gunner (American football)</a>, an American football player tasked with sprinting to tackle a kick returner</li>
<li>Edinburgh Gunners, nickname and former official name for <a href="/wiki/Edinburgh_Rugby" title="Edinburgh Rugby">Edinburgh Rugby</a>, a Scottish rugby team</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gunner_(wrestler)" title="Gunner (wrestler)">Gunner (wrestler)</a>, professional wrestler also known as Phill Shatter</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gunner_Scott" title="Gunner Scott">Gunner Scott</a>, professional wrestler also known as Brent Albright</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gunners_F.C." title="Gunners F.C.">Gunners F.C.</a>, Zimbabwean football club</li>
<li>"The Gunner", nickname given to former Pittsburgh Pirates announcer <a href="/wiki/Bob_Prince" title="Bob Prince">Bob Prince</a></li>
<li>The Gunners, nickname for <a href="/wiki/Arsenal_F.C." title="Arsenal F.C.">Arsenal F.C.</a>, a London football club</li>
</ul>
