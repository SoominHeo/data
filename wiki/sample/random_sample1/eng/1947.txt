<p><b>Geojehaemaji Station</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">거제해맞이역</span>; <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">Hanja</a>: <span lang="ko-Hani" xml:lang="ko-Hani">巨堤해맞이驛</span>) is a railway station of the <a href="/wiki/Donghae_Line" title="Donghae Line">Donghae Line</a> in <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Geoje-dong&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Geoje-dong (page does not exist)">Geoje-dong</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yeonje_District" title="Yeonje District">Yeonje District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Busan" title="Busan">Busan</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. The station is unrelated to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Geoje_Station_(Busan_Metro)" title="Geoje Station (Busan Metro)">Geoje Station</a> of Busan Metro.</p>
