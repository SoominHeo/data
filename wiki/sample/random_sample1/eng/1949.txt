<p>"<b>Geojitmal</b>" (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko" xml:lang="ko">거짓말</span>, "Lie") is the debut single by <a href="/wiki/Koreans" title="Koreans">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Girl_group" title="Girl group">girl group</a> T-ara released on July 27, 2009. All the tracks, excluding "Lie (Part.2)", from the single were later included on the group's debut album, <i><a href="/wiki/Absolute_First_Album" title="Absolute First Album">Absolute First Album</a></i>. The first version was re-titled as "Lie (Dance Ver.)" and the second part "Lie (Slow Ver.)".</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lie_(T-ara_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Although the members of <a href="/wiki/T-ara" title="T-ara">T-ara</a> had previously participated in two songs earlier in the year, <i>Lie</i> was the official debut single released by the group.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
