<p><b><a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a></b> competed at the <b><a href="/wiki/1928_Summer_Olympics" title="1928 Summer Olympics">1928 Summer Olympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Amsterdam" title="Amsterdam">Amsterdam</a>, <a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Netherlands</a>. 21 athletes (19 men and 2 women) competed in 16 events across two sports (athletics and fencing).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sports-reference_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-sports-reference-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
