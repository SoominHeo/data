<p><b>Geochagundo</b>, also <b>Geocha Gundo</b>, are an <a href="/wiki/Archipelago" title="Archipelago">archipelago</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> in the <a href="/wiki/Yellow_Sea" title="Yellow Sea">Yellow Sea</a>, located about 30 km (19 mi) south-west of <a href="/wiki/Jindo_(island)" title="Jindo (island)">Jindo</a>, in the administrative divisions of Donggeochado-ri and Seogeochado-ri, <a href="/wiki/Jodo-myeon" title="Jodo-myeon">Jodo-myeon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jindo_County" title="Jindo County">Jindo County</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Jeolla_Province" title="South Jeolla Province">South Jeolla Province</a>.</p>
<p>Geochagundo consists of the islands <a href="/wiki/Donggeochado" title="Donggeochado">Donggeochado</a> (동거차도), Mangdo (망도), Bukdo (북도), Sangsongdo (상송도), Hasongdo (하송도), Songdo (송도), <a href="/wiki/Seogeochado" title="Seogeochado">Seogeochado</a> (서거차도), Sangjukdo (상죽도, 웃대섬), Hajukdo (하죽도, 아랫대섬), Hangdo (항도).</p>
