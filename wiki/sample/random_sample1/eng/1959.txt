<p>This is the <a href="/wiki/Discography" title="Discography">discography</a> of <a href="/wiki/South_Korean" title="South Korean">South Korean</a> girl group <a href="/wiki/Girl%27s_Day" title="Girl's Day">Girl's Day</a>. The group debuted in July 9, 2010 with the EP <i>Girl's Day Party #1</i>, with the song "Tilt My Head" as their first single.</p>
