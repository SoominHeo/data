<p><b><a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a></b> competed at the <b><a href="/wiki/1928_Summer_Olympics" title="1928 Summer Olympics">1928 Summer Olympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Amsterdam" title="Amsterdam">Amsterdam</a>, <a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Netherlands</a>. The men's <a href="/wiki/Field_hockey" title="Field hockey">field hockey</a> team won the gold medal, beginning a streak that continued through the <a href="/wiki/1956_Summer_Olympics" title="1956 Summer Olympics">1956 Games</a>.</p>
