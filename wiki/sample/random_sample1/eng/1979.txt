<p>The <b>Bezirk Gera</b> was a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_the_German_Democratic_Republic" title="Administrative divisions of the German Democratic Republic">district</a> (<i>Bezirk</i>) of <a href="/wiki/East_Germany" title="East Germany">East Germany</a>. The administrative seat and main town was <a href="/wiki/Gera" title="Gera">Gera</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bezirk_Gera&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The district was established, with the other 13, on July 25, 1952, replacing the old <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> <a href="/wiki/States_of_Germany" title="States of Germany">states</a>. After October 3, 1990, it was disestablished due to <a href="/wiki/German_reunification" title="German reunification">German reunification</a>, becoming again part of the state of <a href="/wiki/Thuringia" title="Thuringia">Thuringia</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bezirk_Gera&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Position">Position</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bezirk_Gera&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Position">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Bezirk Gera had borders with the <i>Bezirke</i> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Suhl_(Bezirk)" title="Suhl (Bezirk)">Suhl</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Erfurt_(Bezirk)" title="Erfurt (Bezirk)">Erfurt</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Halle_(Bezirk)" title="Halle (Bezirk)">Halle</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Leipzig_(Bezirk)" title="Leipzig (Bezirk)">Leipzig</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Karl-Marx-Stadt_(Bezirk)" title="Karl-Marx-Stadt (Bezirk)">Karl-Marx-Stadt</a>, as well as with <a href="/wiki/West_Germany" title="West Germany">West Germany</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Subdivision">Subdivision</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bezirk_Gera&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Subdivision">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The <i>Bezirk</i> was divided into 13 <i>Kreise</i>: 2 urban districts (<i>Stadtkreise</i>) and 11 rural districts (<i>Landkreise</i>):</p>
<ul>
<li><b>Urban districts :</b> <a href="/wiki/Gera" title="Gera">Gera</a>; <a href="/wiki/Jena" title="Jena">Jena</a>.</li>
<li><b>Rural districts :</b> <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kreis_Eisenberg&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kreis Eisenberg (page does not exist)">Eisenberg</a>; <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kreis_Gera-Land&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kreis Gera-Land (page does not exist)">Gera-Land</a>; <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kreis_Greiz&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kreis Greiz (page does not exist)">Greiz</a>; <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kreis_Jena-Land&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kreis Jena-Land (page does not exist)">Jena-Land</a>; <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kreis_Lobenstein&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kreis Lobenstein (page does not exist)">Lobenstein</a>; <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kreis_P%C3%B6%C3%9Fneck&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kreis Pößneck (page does not exist)">Pößneck</a>; <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kreis_Rudolstadt&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kreis Rudolstadt (page does not exist)">Rudolstadt</a>; <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kreis_Saalfeld&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kreis Saalfeld (page does not exist)">Saalfeld</a>; <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kreis_Schleiz&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kreis Schleiz (page does not exist)">Schleiz</a>; <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kreis_Stadtroda&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kreis Stadtroda (page does not exist)">Stadtroda</a>; <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kreis_Zeulenroda&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kreis Zeulenroda (page does not exist)">Zeulenroda</a>.</li>
</ul>
