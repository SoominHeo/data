<p><b>Gabriel Macht</b> (born January 22, 1972) is an American actor. Macht is known for playing <a href="/wiki/Spirit_(comics)" title="Spirit (comics)">The Spirit</a> in the <a href="/wiki/The_Spirit_(film)" title="The Spirit (film)">eponymous 2008 film adaptation</a>, and for his role as Harvey Specter on the <a href="/wiki/USA_Network" title="USA Network">USA Network</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Suits_(TV_series)" title="Suits (TV series)">Suits</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gabriel_Macht&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Macht was born in <a href="/wiki/The_Bronx" title="The Bronx">The Bronx</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/New_York_(state)" title="New York (state)">New York</a>, the son of Suzanne Victoria Pulier, a museum curator and archivist, and actor <a href="/wiki/Stephen_Macht" title="Stephen Macht">Stephen Macht</a>. He has three siblings, Jesse, a musician (who appeared on <i><a href="/wiki/The_Next_Great_American_Band" title="The Next Great American Band">The Next Great American Band</a></i>), Ari Serbin, and Julie. Both of his parents are <a href="/wiki/Jews" title="Jews">Jewish</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Macht was raised in <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a> from the age of five. After graduating <a href="/wiki/Beverly_Hills_High_School" title="Beverly Hills High School">Beverly Hills High School</a>, he attended <a href="/wiki/Carnegie_Mellon_College_of_Fine_Arts" title="Carnegie Mellon College of Fine Arts">Carnegie Mellon College of Fine Arts</a>, where he graduated in 1994.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> During his time at Carnegie Mellon he became a member of <a href="/wiki/Delta_Upsilon" title="Delta Upsilon">Delta Upsilon</a> fraternity.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gabriel_Macht&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Macht was nominated for the <a href="/wiki/Young_Artist_Award" title="Young Artist Award">Best Young Motion Picture Actor Award</a> after playing his first role at age eight in the film <i><a href="/wiki/Why_Would_I_Lie%3F" title="Why Would I Lie?">Why Would I Lie?</a></i> under the <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage name</a> Gabriel Swann.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> He has appeared in many film and television roles including <i><a href="/wiki/A_Love_Song_for_Bobby_Long" title="A Love Song for Bobby Long">A Love Song for Bobby Long</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Because_I_Said_So_(film)" title="Because I Said So (film)">Because I Said So</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Recruit" title="The Recruit">The Recruit</a></i>, and <i>Archangel</i>. For the 2001 film <i><a href="/wiki/Behind_Enemy_Lines_(2001_film)" title="Behind Enemy Lines (2001 film)">Behind Enemy Lines</a></i>, Macht spent a week at sea filming on the flight deck, corridors, and hangar bay #3 of the <i>USS Carl Vinson</i>. Macht played the title role in <a href="/wiki/Frank_Miller_(comics)" title="Frank Miller (comics)">Frank Miller</a>'s <a href="/wiki/The_Spirit_(film)" title="The Spirit (film)">2008 adaptation</a> of <a href="/wiki/Will_Eisner" title="Will Eisner">Will Eisner</a>'s comic creation <i><a href="/wiki/Spirit_(comics)" title="Spirit (comics)">The Spirit</a></i>. Although the film was a failure upon theatrical release, the film and Macht himself both gained a <a href="/wiki/Cult_following" title="Cult following">cult following</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>In July 2010, it was announced that Macht had signed on to star in the new <a href="/wiki/USA_Network" title="USA Network">USA Network</a> drama <i><a href="/wiki/Suits_(TV_series)" title="Suits (TV series)">Suits</a></i>, which was originally known under the working title <i>A Legal Mind</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> The show has been a success for Gabriel and USA Network, having just been renewed for a sixth season of 16 episodes in July 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> Although not listed in the credits on the official USA Network page, Macht's name along with co-star <a href="/wiki/Patrick_J._Adams" title="Patrick J. Adams">Patrick J. Adams</a>' name appeared as "co-producers" during the end credits of each episode during season 3.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gabriel_Macht&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Macht married Australian-born actress <a href="/wiki/Jacinda_Barrett" title="Jacinda Barrett">Jacinda Barrett</a> in 2004.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> The couple's first child, a girl, Satine Anais Geraldine Macht, was born August 20, 2007 in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> They had a second child, a son named Luca, on February 26, 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<p>Macht has been the best friend of his <i><a href="/wiki/Suits_(TV_series)" title="Suits (TV series)">Suits</a></i> co-star <a href="/wiki/Sarah_Rafferty" title="Sarah Rafferty">Sarah Rafferty</a> for over 20 years, ever since they met in 1993 at the Williamstown Theatre Festival. Macht is a vegetarian and practices <a href="/wiki/Sustainable_living" title="Sustainable living">green living</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
