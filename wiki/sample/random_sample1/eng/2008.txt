<p><b>Getter Ragnila Jaani</b> (born 3 February 1993)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is an Estonian singer and actress. She represented <a href="/wiki/Estonia_in_the_Eurovision_Song_Contest_2011" title="Estonia in the Eurovision Song Contest 2011">Estonia</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Eurovision_Song_Contest_2011" title="Eurovision Song Contest 2011">Eurovision Song Contest 2011</a> with the song "<a href="/wiki/Rockefeller_Street" title="Rockefeller Street">Rockefeller Street</a>".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Getter_Jaani&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Getter was born and raised in <a href="/wiki/Tallinn" title="Tallinn">Tallinn</a>. In 2015, she graduated <a href="/wiki/Night_school" title="Night school">night school</a> and was awarded a <a href="/wiki/High_school_diploma" title="High school diploma">high school diploma</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Getter_Jaani&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Getter_Jaani_20120515_by_Ahsoous.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="2454" data-file-width="2756" height="196" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/79/Getter_Jaani_20120515_by_Ahsoous.jpg/220px-Getter_Jaani_20120515_by_Ahsoous.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/79/Getter_Jaani_20120515_by_Ahsoous.jpg/330px-Getter_Jaani_20120515_by_Ahsoous.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/79/Getter_Jaani_20120515_by_Ahsoous.jpg/440px-Getter_Jaani_20120515_by_Ahsoous.jpg 2x" width="220"/></a>
<div class="thumbcaption">
<div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Getter_Jaani_20120515_by_Ahsoous.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>
Jaani performing in May 2012.</div>
</div>
</div>
<p>Jaani first became publicly known in Estonia as a participant in <a href="/wiki/Eesti_otsib_superstaari_(season_3)" title="Eesti otsib superstaari (season 3)">the third season</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Eesti_otsib_superstaari" title="Eesti otsib superstaari">Eesti otsib superstaari</a></i>, the Estonian version of <i><a href="/wiki/Pop_Idol" title="Pop Idol">Pop Idol</a></i>. She finished fourth and her performance gained her role of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sharpay_Evans" title="Sharpay Evans">Sharpay Evans</a> in the Estonian version of <i><a href="/wiki/High_School_Musical" title="High School Musical">High School Musical</a></i>.</p>
<p>Jaani represented <a href="/wiki/Estonia_in_the_Eurovision_Song_Contest_2011" title="Estonia in the Eurovision Song Contest 2011">Estonia</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Eurovision_Song_Contest_2011" title="Eurovision Song Contest 2011">Eurovision Song Contest 2011</a>, with her entry "<a href="/wiki/Rockefeller_Street" title="Rockefeller Street">Rockefeller Street</a>" coming in 24th place in the final. That same year, she participated as a celebrity contestant on <i><a href="/wiki/Tantsud_t%C3%A4htedega" title="Tantsud tähtedega">Tantsud tähtedega</a></i>, the Estonian version of <i><a href="/wiki/Dancing_with_the_Stars" title="Dancing with the Stars">Dancing with the Stars</a></i>.</p>
<p>In 2012, Jaani presented the scores <a href="/wiki/Estonia_in_the_Eurovision_Song_Contest_2012" title="Estonia in the Eurovision Song Contest 2012">on behalf of Estonia</a> for the grand-finals of the <a href="/wiki/Eurovision_Song_Contest_2012" title="Eurovision Song Contest 2012">Eurovision Song Contest 2012</a> in <a href="/wiki/Baku" title="Baku">Baku</a>, Azerbaijan.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-voting_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-voting-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2013, Jaani landed the role of Sandy in the musical <i><a href="/wiki/Grease_(musical)" title="Grease (musical)">Grease</a></i>.</p>
