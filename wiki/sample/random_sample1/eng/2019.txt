<p>The <b>Glirinae</b> are a <a href="/wiki/Subfamily" title="Subfamily">subfamily</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dormice" title="Dormice">dormice</a> (Gliridae); it contains two extant genera, each having one species:</p>
<p>Subfamily <b>Glirinae</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Genus <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Glirulus" title="Glirulus">Glirulus</a></i>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Japanese_dormouse" title="Japanese dormouse">Japanese dormouse</a>, <i>Glirulus japonicus</i></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Genus <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Glis_(genus)" title="Glis (genus)">Glis</a></i>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Edible_dormouse" title="Edible dormouse">Edible dormouse</a>, <i>Glis glis</i></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
