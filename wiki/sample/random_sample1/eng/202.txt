<p><b><a href="/wiki/Curling" title="Curling">Curling</a></b> was a <a href="/wiki/Demonstration_sport" title="Demonstration sport">demonstration sport</a> at the <b><a href="/wiki/1932_Winter_Olympics" title="1932 Winter Olympics">1932 Winter Olympics</a></b>. The venue was the <a href="/wiki/Herb_Brooks_Arena" title="Herb Brooks Arena">Olympic Indoor Arena</a> in <a href="/wiki/Lake_Placid,_New_York" title="Lake Placid, New York">Lake Placid, New York</a>. Eight teams from two countries (4 American teams and 4 Canadian Teams) competed in this event. Only men's curling was contested at these Olympics. The matches were held on February 4 and 5, 1932. Each of the Canadian teams played against each of the American teams.</p>

<p>The medalists were from the Canadian provinces of <a href="/wiki/Manitoba" title="Manitoba">Manitoba</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ontario" title="Ontario">Ontario</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Quebec" title="Quebec">Quebec</a>. The highest placed American team was from <a href="/wiki/Connecticut" title="Connecticut">Connecticut</a> in 4th place. The other American teams were from New York, Michigan and Massachusetts.</p>
