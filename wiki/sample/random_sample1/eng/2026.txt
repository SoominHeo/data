<p><i><b>He Who Can't Marry</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">결혼 못하는 남자</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Gyeolhon Mothaneun Namja</span></i>) is a 2009 <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> television series starring <a href="/wiki/Ji_Jin-hee" title="Ji Jin-hee">Ji Jin-hee</a>, <a href="/wiki/Uhm_Jung-hwa" title="Uhm Jung-hwa">Uhm Jung-hwa</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kim_So-eun" title="Kim So-eun">Kim So-eun</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yoo_Ah-in" title="Yoo Ah-in">Yoo Ah-in</a> and <a href="/wiki/Yang_Jung-a" title="Yang Jung-a">Yang Jung-a</a>. A romantic comedy about an extremely stubborn and inflexible 40-year-old bachelor, it is a <a href="/wiki/Remake" title="Remake">remake</a> of the 2006 <a href="/wiki/Japanese_television_drama" title="Japanese television drama">Japanese drama</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Kekkon_Dekinai_Otoko" title="Kekkon Dekinai Otoko">Kekkon Dekinai Otoko</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=He_Who_Can%27t_Marry_(2009_TV_series)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Architect Jae-hee (<a href="/wiki/Ji_Jin-hee" title="Ji Jin-hee">Ji Jin-hee</a>) has the looks and the money, but he's over forty and still a bachelor. Despite being great marriage material on paper, his blunt personality and precise lifestyle turn women off. Jae-hee just can't seem to get married—until he meets his equal, single 40-year-old doctor Moon-jung (<a href="/wiki/Uhm_Jung-hwa" title="Uhm Jung-hwa">Uhm Jung-hwa</a>), who spends most of her time at work doing overtime and covering for colleagues. Romance may be in the air yet for the unmarriageable Jae-hee, but there's also his longtime colleague Ki-ran (<a href="/wiki/Yang_Jung-a" title="Yang Jung-a">Yang Jung-a</a>) and young neighbor Yoo-jin (<a href="/wiki/Kim_So-eun" title="Kim So-eun">Kim So-eun</a>) to consider.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
