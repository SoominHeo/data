<p><b>The Kyungpook National University Hospital</b> is located in <a href="/wiki/Daegu" title="Daegu">Daegu</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. It was derived from the DaeGuDongIn Hospital which had opened in 1907. It has played as centered hospital of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gyeongsangbuk-do" title="Gyeongsangbuk-do">Gyeongsangbuk-do</a>. As 1952 <a href="/wiki/Kyungpook_National_University" title="Kyungpook National University">Kyungpook National University</a> is established, it is following to the Kyungpook National University Hospitals. It is known to Korean as important general hospitals.</p>
