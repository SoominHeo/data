<p><b>Kyŏngsŏng County</b> is a <i>kun</i>, or county, on the central coast of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/North_Hamgyong" title="North Hamgyong">North Hamgyong</a>, <a href="/wiki/North_Korea" title="North Korea">North Korea</a>. The administrative center is located in <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ky%C5%8Fngs%C5%8Fng-%C5%ADp&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kyŏngsŏng-ŭp (page does not exist)">Kyŏngsŏng-ŭp</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kyongsong_County&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>To the west and southwest, Kyŏngsŏng is flanked by mountains, while to the east it borders <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ky%C5%8Fngs%C5%8Fng_Bay&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kyŏngsŏng Bay (page does not exist)">Kyŏngsŏng Bay</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Sea_of_Japan" title="Sea of Japan">Sea of Japan</a> <a href="/wiki/Sea_of_Japan_naming_dispute" title="Sea of Japan naming dispute">(East Sea of Korea)</a>. In the coastal area a number of small plains are found, including <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ky%C5%8Fngs%C5%8Fng_Plain&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kyŏngsŏng Plain (page does not exist)">Kyŏngsŏng Plain</a>. Numerous small streams flow into the Sea of Japan, including the Kwangmoch'ŏn, Segolch'ŏn, Pokkokch'ŏn, and Och'onch'ŏn.</p>
<p>There are also numerous hot springs in the area. Eighty percent of the county is composed of mountain forests, of which 58.9% is evergreen and 39.9% is deciduous.</p>
<p>Due to its location on the Sea of Japan, Kyŏngsŏng's climate is generally mild, but the region is prone to fog. The annual average temperature is 6.6 °C (43.9 °F), −8.2 °C (17.2 °F) in January and 21.1 °C (70.0 °F) in August. The average annual rainfall is 648 mm (25.5 in) along the coast, but 800 mm (31 in) in the west.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Economy">Economy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kyongsong_County&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Economy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Agriculture_and_fishery">Agriculture and fishery</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kyongsong_County&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Agriculture and fishery">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Rice farming is carried out in the eastern region of Kyŏngsŏng. In the orchards, pears are the predominant crop. Small-scale fishing is also carried out.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Manufacturing">Manufacturing</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kyongsong_County&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Manufacturing">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The area is known in North Korea as a center of ceramics manufacture.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Tourism">Tourism</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kyongsong_County&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Tourism">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Tourist attractions include Kyŏngsŏng Castle and Kyŏngsŏng Nammun.</p>
