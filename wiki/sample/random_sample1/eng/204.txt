<p>At the <a href="/wiki/1932_Winter_Olympics" title="1932 Winter Olympics">1932 Winter Olympics</a>, three <a href="/wiki/Figure_skating" title="Figure skating">figure skating</a> events were contested. The competitions were held from Monday, February 8 to Friday, February 12, 1932. It was the first time the events were held indoors.</p>
