<p><b>Gyongin National University of Education Station</b> is located at Gyeyang district of <a href="/wiki/Incheon" title="Incheon">Incheon</a> metropolitan city in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. The station was opened in 1999 of which the name was Incheon National university of education station. In 2003, the term was changed following the alteration of university. The university changed its name into Gyeongin National University of Education.</p>
