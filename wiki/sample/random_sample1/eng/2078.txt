<p><b>Heonjong of Goryeo</b> (1 August 1084 - 6 November 1097) (r. 1094–1095) was the fourteenth <a href="/wiki/Monarch" title="Monarch">monarch</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Goryeo" title="Goryeo">Goryeo</a> dynasty of <a href="/wiki/Korea" title="Korea">Korea</a>. He was the eldest son of <a href="/wiki/Seonjong_of_Goryeo" title="Seonjong of Goryeo">king Seonjong</a>. According to the <i><a href="/wiki/Goryeosa" title="Goryeosa">Goryeosa</a></i>, he was a bright child and excelled in writing by the age of 9.</p>
<p>In the year of his accession, Heonjong was faced by the rebellion of <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Yi_Ja-ui&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Yi Ja-ui (page does not exist)">Yi Ja-ui</a>, which was quickly put down. In the following year, he fell ill and gave up the throne to <a href="/wiki/Sukjong_of_Goryeo" title="Sukjong of Goryeo">king Sukjong</a>, his uncle.</p>
