<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Churches">Churches</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gornji_Petrovci&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Churches">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:152px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Cerkev_sv._Trojice_v_Gornjih_Petrovcih.JPG"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="1600" data-file-width="1067" height="225" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/27/Cerkev_sv._Trojice_v_Gornjih_Petrovcih.JPG/150px-Cerkev_sv._Trojice_v_Gornjih_Petrovcih.JPG" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/27/Cerkev_sv._Trojice_v_Gornjih_Petrovcih.JPG/225px-Cerkev_sv._Trojice_v_Gornjih_Petrovcih.JPG 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/27/Cerkev_sv._Trojice_v_Gornjih_Petrovcih.JPG/300px-Cerkev_sv._Trojice_v_Gornjih_Petrovcih.JPG 2x" width="150"/></a>
<div class="thumbcaption">
<div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Cerkev_sv._Trojice_v_Gornjih_Petrovcih.JPG" title="Enlarge"></a></div>
Holy Trinity Church</div>

<p>There are two churches in Gornji Petrovci. The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic" title="Roman Catholic">Roman Catholic</a> <a href="/wiki/Parish_church" title="Parish church">parish church</a> is dedicated to the <a href="/wiki/Trinity" title="Trinity">Holy Trinity</a> and is a structure that originated in the late 13th century, but was rebuilt on a number of occasions in the following centuries, preserving certain features from each phase. The relatively short <a href="/wiki/Nave" title="Nave">nave</a> is <a href="/wiki/Romanesque_architecture" title="Romanesque architecture">Romanesque</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Baroque" title="Baroque">Baroque</a> internal furnishings. The sanctuary is <a href="/wiki/Gothic_architecture" title="Gothic architecture">Late Gothic</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> The local <a href="/wiki/Lutheranism" title="Lutheranism">Lutheran</a> church is a large single-nave building and is one of the largest Lutheran churches in Prekmurje. It was built in 1804 and renovated in 1894.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
