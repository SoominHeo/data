<p><b>Kōriyama Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">郡山駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kōriyama eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/Train_station" title="Train station">train station</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Kansai_Main_Line" title="Kansai Main Line">Kansai Main Line</a> (<a href="/wiki/Yamatoji_Line" title="Yamatoji Line">Yamatoji Line</a>) of <a href="/wiki/West_Japan_Railway_Company" title="West Japan Railway Company">West Japan Railway Company</a> (JR West) in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yamato-K%C5%8Driyama,_Nara" title="Yamato-Kōriyama, Nara">Yamato-Kōriyama</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nara_Prefecture" title="Nara Prefecture">Nara Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</p>

<ul>
<li>There are 2 <a href="/wiki/Side_platform" title="Side platform">side platforms</a> serving 2 tracks on the ground level, and ticket gates are located over the platforms and tracks.</li>
