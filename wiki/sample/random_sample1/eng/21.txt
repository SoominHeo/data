<p>The <b>Type 051C</b> or <b><i>Luzhou</i> class</b> destroyer is a long-range air-defence guided missile destroyer built by <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a> in its ongoing effort to create a true blue water navy. The ship uses the hull design of the older <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Luhai_class_destroyer" title="Luhai class destroyer">Type 051B</a> (<i>Luhai</i> class), but is equipped with the advanced Russian <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/SA-10A_Grumble#Sea-based_S-300F_.28SA-N-6.29" title="SA-10A Grumble">S-300FM</a> air defence missiles systems. Currently, two ships of this class have been launched and deployed by <a href="/wiki/People%27s_Liberation_Army_Navy" title="People's Liberation Army Navy">People's Liberation Army Navy</a> <a href="/wiki/North_Sea_Fleet" title="North Sea Fleet">North Sea Fleet</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Program">Program</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Type_051C_destroyer&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Program">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The construction of the <i>Luzhou</i> class destroyer at of the China’s northern <a href="/wiki/Dalian" title="Dalian">Dalian</a> Shipyard was revealed in 2004 with the help of Internet photographs. A disadvantage of the Type 051C is that it lacks the stealthier radar cross-section found on the newer Chinese warships. The ship also possesses less advanced steam turbine propulsion compared to gas turbine propulsion present in all newer Chinese destroyers. Type 051C is a stop gap measure to provide long range area air defense to the fleet by mating the mature hull design with proven SAM system that is more capable than that of <a href="/wiki/Type_052B_destroyer" title="Type 052B destroyer">Type 052B destroyer</a>. Production of this class stopped once more advanced <a href="/wiki/Type_052C_destroyer" title="Type 052C destroyer">Type 052C destroyer</a> becomes available.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Missiles">Missiles</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Type_051C_destroyer&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Missiles">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <i>Luzhou</i> class uses the advanced Russian <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/SA-10A_Grumble#Sea-based_S-300F_.28SA-N-6.29" title="SA-10A Grumble">S-300FM</a> air defence missile system with <a href="/wiki/Track-via-missile" title="Track-via-missile">track-via-missile</a> homing guided by a single Tombstone radar. The missile has a maximum range of 150 km and an operating altitude of 10 m - 27 km.</p>
<p>The anti-ship abilities possessed by the <i>Luzhou</i> class includes 8 indigenous <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/C-803" title="C-803">YJ-83</a> (C-803) <a href="/wiki/Anti-ship_missile" title="Anti-ship missile">Anti-ship missiles</a>. This <a href="/wiki/Anti-ship_missile" title="Anti-ship missile">anti-ship missile</a> has a range of 150+ km and approaches its target in sea skimming mode at a speed of <a href="/wiki/Mach_number" title="Mach number">Mach</a> 1.5. As a secondary role, the missile can also be used against land targets.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Guns">Guns</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Type_051C_destroyer&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Guns">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The ship uses two <a href="/wiki/Type_730_CIWS" title="Type 730 CIWS">Type 730 CIWS</a> guns for air defence. The Type 730 is a highly effective <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/CIWS" title="CIWS">CIWS</a> that is also installed on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Guangzhou_class_destroyer" title="Guangzhou class destroyer">Type 052B</a> (<i>Guangzhou</i> class) and Type 052C/052D destroyers, and the <a href="/wiki/Type_054A_frigate" title="Type 054A frigate">Type 054A</a> (<i>Jiangkai</i> II class) frigates. The ship also has 100 mm gun based on a <a href="/wiki/French_100_mm_naval_gun" title="French 100 mm naval gun">French design</a> for use against surface targets.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Propulsion">Propulsion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Type_051C_destroyer&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Propulsion">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The ship is powered by two indigenous steam turbine engines.</p>
