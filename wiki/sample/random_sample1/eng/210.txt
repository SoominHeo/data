<p>The <big>'</big> character has number 39 in ASCII and code U+0027 in Unicode. It is used as:</p>
<ul>
<li><b><a href="/wiki/Apostrophe" title="Apostrophe">Apostrophe</a></b> (as straight version of the <big>’</big> character);</li>
<li><b><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Single_quotation_mark" title="Single quotation mark">Single quotation mark</a></b> (as straight version of the <big>‘</big> and <big>’</big> characters).</li>
</ul>
