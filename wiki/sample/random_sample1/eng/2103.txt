<p><i><b>Confession</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">고백</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Gobaek</span></i>) is a 2015 South Korean film starring <a href="/wiki/Kim_Young-ho_(actor)" title="Kim Young-ho (actor)">Kim Young-ho</a>, Yoon In-jo and <a href="/wiki/Choi_Cheol-ho" title="Choi Cheol-ho">Choi Cheol-ho</a>. The mystery film, directed by <a href="/wiki/Jung_Young-bae" title="Jung Young-bae">Jung Young-bae</a>, is about a man who tries to uncover the truth about his past.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Confession_(2015_film)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sang-woo (<a href="/wiki/Kim_Young-ho_(actor)" title="Kim Young-ho (actor)">Kim Young-ho</a>) lost his memory after an accident. He becomes suspicious about his past, and when he finds out his wife's affair, he becomes obsessed to uncover the truth.</p>
