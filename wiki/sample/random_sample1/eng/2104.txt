<p><b>Kō Bunyū</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_characters" title="Chinese characters">Chinese</a>: 黃文雄; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">Pinyin</a>: <i>Huáng Wén Xióng</i>; <a href="/wiki/Pe%CC%8Dh-%C5%8De-j%C4%AB" title="Pe̍h-ōe-jī">Pe̍h-ōe-jī</a>: <i>N̂g Bûn-hiông</i>; <a href="/wiki/Japanese_language" title="Japanese language">Japanese</a>: <span lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">黄文雄</span>, <i>Kō Bun'yū</i>; born 1938) is a controversial <a href="/wiki/Taiwanese_people" title="Taiwanese people">Taiwanese</a> author, now resident in <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>, who is well known for his staunch anti-<a href="/wiki/Mainland_China" title="Mainland China">Mainland Chinese</a> stance and for penning a number of highly controversial books about Chinese, Japanese and Korean history and culture.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Kō has been accused of being a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Historical_revisionism_(negationism)" title="Historical revisionism (negationism)">revisionist</a> and a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nationalist" title="Nationalist">nationalist</a>, and has been accused of playing down <a href="/wiki/Japanese_war_crimes" title="Japanese war crimes">Japanese war crimes</a> against China and Korea. His works often claim that Taiwan and Korea owe their present-day successes, and much of their culture, to Japan.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Books_by_K.C5.8D">Books by Kō</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ko_Bunyu&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Books by Kō">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><i>The ugly Chinaman</i> (<a href="/wiki/Japanese_language" title="Japanese language">Japanese</a>: <span lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">醜い中国人</span>; <i>Minikui Chūgoku-jin</i>)</li>
<li><i>The fabricated history of Japan</i> (捏造された日本史; <i>Netsuzō sareta Nihon-shi</i>)</li>
<li><i>Japan - falling into the trap of <a href="/wiki/Sinocentrism" title="Sinocentrism">Sinocentrism</a></i> (中華思想の罠に嵌った日本; <i>Chūka shisō no wana ni hamatta Nihon</i>)</li>
<li><i>The Japanese people's virtue which has been erased from history - About this country's "Inheritance of the Spirit" which is now being revived</i> (歴史から消された日本人の美徳―今蘇るこの国の“心の遺産”とは; Rekishi kara kesareta Nihon-jin no bitoku - Ima yomigaeru kono kuni no "kokoro no isan" to wa)</li>
<li><i>The curse of Sinocentrism lurking within <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chinese_character" title="Chinese character">Chinese character</a> culture</i> (漢字文明にひそむ中華思想の呪縛; Kanji bunmei ni hisomu Chūka shisō no jubaku)</li>
<li><i>South Korea was built by the Japanese</i> (韓国は日本人がつくった; Kankoku wa Nihon-jin ga tsukutta)</li>
<li><i>Taiwan was built by the Japanese people</i> (台湾は日本人がつくった; Taiwan wa Nihon-jin ga tsukutta)</li>
<li><i>The <a href="/wiki/Bushido" title="Bushido">Bushido</a> spirit bequeathed to Taiwan by the Japanese people</i> (日本人が台湾に遺した武士道精神; Nihon-jin ga Taiwan ni nokoshita Bushidō seishin)</li>
<li><i>The unknown truth about the <a href="/wiki/Second_Sino-Japanese_War" title="Second Sino-Japanese War">Second Sino-Japanese War</a></i> (日中戦争知られざる真実; Nicchū Sensō no shirarezaru shinjitsu)</li>
<li><i>The inheritance of <a href="/wiki/Manchukuo" title="Manchukuo">Manchukuo</a></i> (満州国の遺産; <i>Manshūkoku no isan</i>)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li>'<i>How the Chinese are taking advantage of Japanese confusion</i> (つけあがるな中国人うろたえるな日本人; Tsukeagaru na Chūgoku-jin urotaeru na Nihon-jin)</li>
<li><i>How far will China continue to crack?</i> (どこまで中国に喰われ続けるのか; Doko made Chūgoku ni kuware-tsuzukeru no ka)</li>
<li><i>7 reasons why China cannot prevail over Japan even if it dies trying</i> (中国が死んでも日本に勝てない7つの理由; Chūgoku ga shinde mo Nihon ni katenai nanatsu no riyū)</li>
</ul>
<p>Kō also provided the script for the <a href="/wiki/Manga" title="Manga">manga</a> <i>An Introduction to China: A Study of Our Bothersome Neighbors</i> (マンガ中国入門 やっかいな隣人の研究; Manga Chūgoku nyūmon: Yakkai na rinjin no kenkyū), which displayed all of the traits that he has been accused of above.</p>
<p>Kō is currently a professor at <a href="/wiki/Takushoku_University" title="Takushoku University">Takushoku University</a>.</p>
