<p>The <b>18 kilometre <a href="/wiki/Cross-country_skiing" title="Cross-country skiing">cross-country skiing</a></b> event was part of the <a href="/wiki/Cross-country_skiing_at_the_1936_Winter_Olympics" title="Cross-country skiing at the 1936 Winter Olympics">cross-country skiing at the 1936 Winter Olympics</a> programme. It was the fourth appearance of the event. The competition was held on Wednesday, February 12, 1936.</p>
<p>Seventy-five cross-country skiers from 22 nations competed.</p>
