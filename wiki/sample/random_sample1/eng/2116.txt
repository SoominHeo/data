<p><b>Go Se-won</b> (born December 13, 1977) is a South Korean actor. Go began his acting career in <a href="/wiki/Musical_theatre" title="Musical theatre">musical theatre</a>, then made the leap to television in 2007. He has played supporting roles in several <a href="/wiki/Korean_drama" title="Korean drama">Korean dramas</a> such as <i><a href="/wiki/Three_Brothers_(TV_series)" title="Three Brothers (TV series)">Three Brothers</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Cinderella%27s_Sister" title="Cinderella's Sister">Cinderella's Sister</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Three_Sisters_(2010_TV_series)" title="Three Sisters (2010 TV series)">Three Sisters</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/You_Don%27t_Know_Women" title="You Don't Know Women">You Don't Know Women</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> and <i>The Moon and Stars for You</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Go has also appeared in 13 seasons of cable <a href="/wiki/Sitcom" title="Sitcom">sitcom</a> <i>Rude Miss Young-ae</i>. In 2013, he was cast in his first onscreen leading role in the daily melodrama <i><a href="/wiki/Crazy_Love_(TV_series)" title="Crazy Love (TV series)">Crazy Love</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
