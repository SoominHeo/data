<p>The <b><a href="/wiki/Single_skating" title="Single skating">men's individual skating</a></b> event was held as part of the <a href="/wiki/Figure_skating_at_the_1936_Winter_Olympics" title="Figure skating at the 1936 Winter Olympics">figure skating at the 1936 Winter Olympics</a>. It was the sixth appearance of the event, which had previously been held twice at the Summer Olympics in 1908 and 1920 and at all three Winter Games from 1924 onward. The competition was held from Sunday, February 9 to Friday, February 14, 1936.</p>
<p>Twenty-five figure skaters from twelve nations competed.</p>
