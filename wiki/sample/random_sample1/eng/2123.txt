<p><b>Koshigoe Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">腰越駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Koshigoe-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Railway_station" title="Railway station">railway station</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Enoshima_Electric_Railway" title="Enoshima Electric Railway">Enoshima Electric Railway</a> (Enoden) located in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamakura,_Kanagawa" title="Kamakura, Kanagawa">Kamakura</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kanagawa_Prefecture" title="Kanagawa Prefecture">Kanagawa Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>. It is 3.9 kilometers from the terminus of the Enoden at <a href="/wiki/Fujisawa_Station" title="Fujisawa Station">Fujisawa Station</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Koshigoe_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Koshigoe Station was opened on June 20, 1903 as <b>Yato Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">谷戸駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Yato-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>. It was renamed to its present name on July 15, 1948. In 1993, the platform was lengthened to accept three-car trains.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_layout">Station layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Koshigoe_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Station layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
