<p><b>Goyang Citizen FC</b> is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> club based in the city of <a href="/wiki/Goyang" title="Goyang">Goyang</a>. It was founded because of the disappointment created by <a href="/wiki/Goyang_KB_Kookmin_Bank_FC" title="Goyang KB Kookmin Bank FC">Goyang KB Kookmin Bank FC</a>, which decided not to elevate to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/K-League" title="K-League">K-League</a> after winning the <a href="/wiki/Korea_National_League" title="Korea National League">Korea National League</a> in 2006. It is a member of the <a href="/wiki/K3_League" title="K3 League">K3 League</a>, an amateur league and the fourth tier of league football in South Korea.</p>
<p>2008 was its first season of participation in the Korean football league setup.</p>
