<p><b>Go Eun-ah</b> (born Bang Hyo-jin on October 28, 1988) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> actress. She is best known as Keum-shil in the TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Golden_Apple_(TV_series)" title="Golden Apple (TV series)">Golden Apple</a></i>.</p>
<p>Her brother <a href="/wiki/Mir_(South_Korean_singer)" title="Mir (South Korean singer)">Mir</a> is a member of the boyband <a href="/wiki/MBLAQ" title="MBLAQ">MBLAQ</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
