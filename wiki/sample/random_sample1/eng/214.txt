<p><b><a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a></b> competed at the <b><a href="/wiki/1936_Summer_Olympics" title="1936 Summer Olympics">1936 Summer Olympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Berlin" title="Berlin">Berlin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>. 32 competitors, 28 men and 4 women, took part in 26 events in 7 sports.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sports-reference_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-sports-reference-1">[1]</a></sup> Australian athletes have competed in every <a href="/wiki/Summer_Olympic_Games" title="Summer Olympic Games">Summer Olympic Games</a>.</p>
