<p><b>Kotaro Koizumi</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">小泉 孝太郎</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Koizumi Kōtarō</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born July 10, 1978 in <a href="/wiki/Yokosuka,_Kanagawa" title="Yokosuka, Kanagawa">Yokosuka</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kanagawa_Prefecture" title="Kanagawa Prefecture">Kanagawa</a>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/Japanese_people" title="Japanese people">Japanese</a> <a href="/wiki/Actor" title="Actor">actor</a> and the eldest son of the 87th <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_Japan" title="Prime Minister of Japan">Japanese Prime Minister</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Junichir%C5%8D_Koizumi" title="Junichirō Koizumi">Junichirō Koizumi</a>, and Kayoko Miyamoto.</p>
<p>He studied <a href="/wiki/Economics" title="Economics">economics</a> at <a href="/wiki/Night_school" title="Night school">night classes</a> of <a href="/wiki/Nihon_University" title="Nihon University">Nihon University</a>, but dropped out.</p>
