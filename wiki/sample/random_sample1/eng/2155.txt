<p>The <b>scarlet skimmer</b> or <b>crimson darter</b> (<i>Crocothemis servilia</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> of <a href="/wiki/Dragonfly" title="Dragonfly">dragonfly</a> of the family <a href="/wiki/Libellulidae" title="Libellulidae">Libellulidae</a>, native to <a href="/wiki/East_Asia" title="East Asia">east</a> and <a href="/wiki/Southeast_Asia" title="Southeast Asia">southeast Asia</a> and introduced to <a href="/wiki/Jamaica" title="Jamaica">Jamaica</a>, <a href="/wiki/Florida" title="Florida">Florida</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Hawaii" title="Hawaii">Hawaii</a>. The naiads are commonly used as food in parts of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yunnan,_China" title="Yunnan, China">Yunnan, China</a>.</p>
<p>There are two known subspecies; <i>Crocothemis servilia servilia</i> Drury, 1773 <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gbif_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-gbif-2">[2]</a></sup> and <i>Crocothemis servilia mariannae</i> Kiauta, 1983.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gbif2_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-gbif2-3">[3]</a></sup> <i>C. s. mariannae</i> lacks the mid-dorsal black stripe.</p>
