<p><b>Goldin Finance 117</b>, also known as <i><b>China 117 Tower</b></i>, (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">中国117大厦</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Skyscraper" title="Skyscraper">skyscraper</a> under construction in <a href="/wiki/Tianjin" title="Tianjin">Tianjin</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/People%27s_Republic_of_China" title="People's Republic of China">China</a>. The tower is expected to be 597 m (1,959 ft) with 117 <a href="/wiki/Storey" title="Storey">stories</a>. Construction began in 2008, and the building was scheduled to be completed in 2014, becoming the second tallest building in China, surpassing the <a href="/wiki/Shanghai_World_Financial_Center" title="Shanghai World Financial Center">Shanghai World Financial Center</a>. Construction was suspended in January 2010. Construction resumed in 2011, with completion estimated in 2017. The building was topped out on September 8, 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Design">Design</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Goldin_Finance_117&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Design">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The top was supposed to be a fin like shape. Many years later, the fin like shape was replaced by a diamond.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Urban_exploration">Urban exploration</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Goldin_Finance_117&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Urban exploration">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2015, two Russian and Chinese <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Urban_explorer" title="Urban explorer">urban explorers</a> climbed the tower and construction crane.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2016, a Russian couple of Ivan Birkus (Kuzenetsov) and Angela Nikolau climbed the under-construction tower through stairs and climbed out to a crane on the tower's top. The climb video<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> received over 600k views as of September 2016 and attracted worldwide media attention.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> It was claimed in the video that "No one has ever climbed it", which is apparently incorrect in view of the earlier climb in 2015.</p>
