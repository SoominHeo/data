<div class="hatnote" role="note">This is a <a href="/wiki/Chinese_name" title="Chinese name">Chinese name</a>; the <a href="/wiki/Chinese_surname" title="Chinese surname">family name</a> is <a href="/wiki/Gong_(surname)" title="Gong (surname)">Gong</a>.</div>
<p><b>Gong Du</b> was a bandit operating against the warlord <a href="/wiki/Cao_Cao" title="Cao Cao">Cao Cao</a> during the late <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Han_Dynasty" title="Han Dynasty">Han Dynasty</a> of <a href="/wiki/History_of_China" title="History of China">Chinese history</a>. In 200, Cao Cao's rival <a href="/wiki/Yuan_Shao" title="Yuan Shao">Yuan Shao</a> sent <a href="/wiki/Liu_Bei" title="Liu Bei">Liu Bei</a> to <a href="/wiki/Runan_County" title="Runan County">Runan</a> to support Gong Du in his operations. The combined forces of Liu Bei and Gong Du were able to kill Cao Cao's general <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Cai_Yang&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Cai Yang (page does not exist)">Cai Yang</a> (蔡陽), who was sent to resist them. In 201 Cao Cao himself came to attack; Liu Bei fled south to <a href="/wiki/Liu_Biao" title="Liu Biao">Liu Biao</a> and Gong Du's men scattered.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="In_fiction">In fiction</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gong_Du&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: In fiction">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Luo_Guanzhong" title="Luo Guanzhong">Luo Guanzhong</a>'s historical novel <i><a href="/wiki/Romance_of_the_Three_Kingdoms" title="Romance of the Three Kingdoms">Romance of the Three Kingdoms</a></i>, Gong Du was described as a former <a href="/wiki/Yellow_Turban_Rebellion" title="Yellow Turban Rebellion">Yellow Turban rebel</a> allied with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Liu_Pi_(Han_Dynasty)" title="Liu Pi (Han Dynasty)">Liu Pi</a>, another Yellow Turban. Following the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Guandu" title="Battle of Guandu">Battle of Guandu</a> during the year of 200, Gong Du cooperated with Liu Bei and Yuan Shao by attacking the rear of Cao Cao's army; however Gong Du ended up being slain by <a href="/wiki/Xiahou_Yuan" title="Xiahou Yuan">Xiahou Yuan</a>.</p>
