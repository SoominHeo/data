<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">Football</a></b> at the <a href="/wiki/1951_Asian_Games" title="1951 Asian Games">1951 Asian Games</a> was held in New Delhi, India from March 5 to March 11, 1951.
