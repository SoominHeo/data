<p><b>Gong O-Kyun</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko" xml:lang="ko">공오균</span>) is a former <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> player.</p>
<p>He played for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Daejeon_Citizen" title="Daejeon Citizen">Daejeon Citizen</a> for 10 seasons and appeared in 291 games for this club, scored 38 goals and made 18 assists. In 2009, he also joined the Australian semi-professional club <a href="/wiki/Sunshine_Coast_FC" title="Sunshine Coast FC">Sunshine Coast FC</a>.</p>
