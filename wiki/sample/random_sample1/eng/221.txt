<p><b><a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>'s</b> second <a href="/wiki/Winter_Olympic_Games" title="Winter Olympic Games">Winter Olympic Games</a> appearance was at the <b><a href="/wiki/1952_Winter_Olympics" title="1952 Winter Olympics">1952 Winter Olympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Oslo" title="Oslo">Oslo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Norway" title="Norway">Norway</a>. It was 16 years since Australia's last Winter Games, as the <a href="/wiki/1940_Winter_Olympics" title="1940 Winter Olympics">1940</a> and <a href="/wiki/1944_Winter_Olympics" title="1944 Winter Olympics">1944 Winter Olympics</a> were cancelled, and Australia did not compete in the <a href="/wiki/1948_Winter_Olympics" title="1948 Winter Olympics">1948 Winter Olympics</a>. Australia sent nine athletes and competed in <a href="/wiki/Alpine_skiing" title="Alpine skiing">alpine skiing</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cross-country_skiing" title="Cross-country skiing">cross-country skiing</a>, <a href="/wiki/Figure_skating" title="Figure skating">figure skating</a> and <a href="/wiki/Speed_skating" title="Speed skating">speed skating</a>. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nancy_Hallam" title="Nancy Hallam">Nancy Hallam</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gweneth_Molony" title="Gweneth Molony">Gweneth Molony</a> were Australia's first women athletes at the Winter Olympics.</p>

<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Cross-country_skiing_at_the_1952_Winter_Olympics" title="Cross-country skiing at the 1952 Winter Olympics">Cross-country skiing at the 1952 Winter Olympics</a></div>
<p>Along with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Karl_Sch%C3%BCssler" title="Karl Schüssler">Karl Schüssler</a> of <a href="/wiki/West_Germany" title="West Germany">West Germany</a>, Bruce and Cedric gave up between the 30.5 km mark and 42 km mark in the 50 km race.</p>
<p>The winner of the 18 km won in a time of 1:01:34, whereas Bruce Haslingden took 1:29:58 and Cedric Sloane timed 1:32:39. The third-last competitor, <a href="/wiki/John_H._Caldwell" title="John H. Caldwell">John H. Caldwell</a> (USA) took 1:25:42, and a total of five competitors retired during the race.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-OfficialReport_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-OfficialReport-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
