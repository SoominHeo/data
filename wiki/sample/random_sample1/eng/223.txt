<p>The <b>South American Championship 1953</b> was a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> tournament held in <a href="/wiki/Peru" title="Peru">Peru</a> and won by <a href="/wiki/Paraguay" title="Paraguay">Paraguay</a> with <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a> second.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Argentina_national_football_team" title="Argentina national football team">Argentina</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Colombia_national_football_team" title="Colombia national football team">Colombia</a> withdrew from the tournament.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Francisco_Molina" title="Francisco Molina">Francisco Molina</a> from <a href="/wiki/Chile" title="Chile">Chile</a> became top scorer of the tournament with 8 goals.</p>
