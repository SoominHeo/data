<p><b>Kwaksan Station</b> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Railway_station" title="Railway station">railway station</a> in Kwaksan-ŭp, <a href="/wiki/Kwaksan_County" title="Kwaksan County">Kwaksan County</a>, <a href="/wiki/North_Pyongan_Province" title="North Pyongan Province">North P'yŏngan Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/North_Korea" title="North Korea">North Korea</a>. It is on located on the <a href="/wiki/Pyongui_Line" title="Pyongui Line">P'yŏngŭi Line</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Korean_State_Railway" title="Korean State Railway">Korean State Railway</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Hayato_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Hayato-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
