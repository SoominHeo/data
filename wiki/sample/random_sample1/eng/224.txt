<p>The three teams in this group played against each other once. The group winner <a href="/wiki/South_Korea_national_football_team" title="South Korea national football team">South Korea</a> qualified for the fifth <a href="/wiki/FIFA_World_Cup" title="FIFA World Cup">FIFA World Cup</a> held in <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Switzerland</a>. <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Taipei_national_football_team" title="Chinese Taipei national football team">Republic of China</a> withdrew the qualification.</p>
