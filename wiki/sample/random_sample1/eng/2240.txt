<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Catholic Kwandong University Gymnasium</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Kwandong Hockey Centre</b> (가톨릭 관동대학교 아이스하키II 경기장) is a future ice skating arena which will be established on the grounds of <a href="/wiki/Catholic_Kwandong_University" title="Catholic Kwandong University">Catholic Kwandong University</a> in <a href="/wiki/Gangneung" title="Gangneung">Gangneung</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gangwon_Province_(South_Korea)" title="Gangwon Province (South Korea)">Gangwon-do</a>. During the <a href="/wiki/2018_Winter_Olympics" title="2018 Winter Olympics">2018 Winter Olympics</a>, it will be one of the two venues for Ice Hockey.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The seating capacity is 6,000. There will be one ice rink (60m x 30m), four floors aboveground and one underground level.</p>
<p>Construction has started in June 2014 and should be completed in October 2016.</p>
