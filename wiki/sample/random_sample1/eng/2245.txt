<p><b>Esther Kwan Wing-ho</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">关咏荷</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">關詠荷</span>), born 16 July 1964 is a <a href="/wiki/Hong_Kong" title="Hong Kong">Hong Kong</a> ex <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Actress" title="Actress">actress</a> contracted to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Television_Broadcasts_Limited" title="Television Broadcasts Limited">TVB</a> and <a href="/wiki/Asia_Television" title="Asia Television">ATV</a></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Esther_Kwan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kwan began her acting career after high school at <a href="/wiki/Asia_Television" title="Asia Television">ATV</a> from 1988 to 1993. She moved over to TVB in 1993 and has been at the broadcaster on and off ever since. Kwan met Hong Kong actor <a href="/wiki/Nick_Cheung" title="Nick Cheung">Nick Cheung</a> while she was still working for ATV. The couple married on 8 November 2004 in <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>. Their daughter, Brittany Cheung (張童) was born on 24 January 2006. In 2008, Kwan left acting to spend more time with her daughter.</p>
<p>She is fluent in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Standard_Cantonese" title="Standard Cantonese">Cantonese</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mandarin_Chinese" title="Mandarin Chinese">Mandarin</a> and <a href="/wiki/English_language" title="English language">English</a>.</p>
