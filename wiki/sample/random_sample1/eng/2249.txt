<p>The <b>Guam national futsal team</b> represents <a href="/wiki/Guam" title="Guam">Guam</a> in international <a href="/wiki/Futsal" title="Futsal">futsal</a> competitions and is controlled by the <a href="/wiki/Guam_Football_Association" title="Guam Football Association">Guam Football Association</a>. They are affiliated to the <a href="/wiki/Asian_Football_Confederation" title="Asian Football Confederation">Asian Football Confederation</a>'s <a href="/wiki/East_Asian_Football_Federation" title="East Asian Football Federation">East Asian Football Federation</a> region.</p>
