<p>The <b>Gwangyang Football Stadium</b>, nicknamed "<b>Dragon Dungeon</b>", is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football-specific_stadium" title="Football-specific stadium">football-specific stadium</a> in <a href="/wiki/Gwangyang" title="Gwangyang">Gwangyang</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. It is currently used mostly for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> matches and is the home stadium of <a href="/wiki/Jeonnam_Dragons" title="Jeonnam Dragons">Jeonnam Dragons</a>. The stadium holds 13,496 spectators. It was built in 1992 and opened in 1993.</p>
