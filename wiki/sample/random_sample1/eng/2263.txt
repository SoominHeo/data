<p><b>Gwangju Metro</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">광주 도시철도</span>; <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">Hanja</a>: <span lang="ko-Hani" xml:lang="ko-Hani">光州都市鐵道</span>) is the <a href="/wiki/Rapid_transit" title="Rapid transit">rapid transit</a> system of <a href="/wiki/Gwangju" title="Gwangju">Gwangju</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>, operated by the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Gwangju_Metropolitan_Rapid_Transit_Corporation&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Gwangju Metropolitan Rapid Transit Corporation (page does not exist)">Gwangju Metropolitan Rapid Transit Corporation</a> (GRTC, or Gwangju Metro). The subway network first opened in 2004 with 14 stations. The Gwangju Metro consists of one line, serving 20 operational stations, and operating on 20.1 kilometres (12.5 mi) of route.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Line_1">Line 1</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gwangju_Metro&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Line 1">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Gwangju_Metro_Line_1" title="Gwangju Metro Line 1">Gwangju Metro Line 1</a></div>
<p>As of 2012 Line 1 consists of twenty stations. The section between Sotae and Nokdong operates as a separate shuttle.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-urbanrail_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-urbanrail-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Expansions_and_plans">Expansions and plans</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gwangju_Metro&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Expansions and plans">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Plan_to_extend_Line_1">Plan to extend Line 1</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gwangju_Metro&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Plan to extend Line 1">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Gwangju City Government has a plan to extend the current Line 1 southward to <a href="/wiki/Hwasun_County" title="Hwasun County">Hwasun</a> and northward to <a href="/wiki/Naju" title="Naju">Naju</a>. Recently, the Presidential Committee on Balanced National Development promised to include the Line 1 extension in its '5+2 extensive economic zone' project.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Plan_of_lines">Plan of lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gwangju_Metro&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Plan of lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The original plan of Line 2 was a circle type, but recently the Gwangju City Government suggested to consider a so-called 'South-North type' as well. Now the Gwangju City Government is holding a public hearing.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li>Route of Circle Line (Plan 1) : Baekyoon-gwangjang (백운광장 a.k.a. white cloud square) - Namgwangju Station (남광주역) - Gwangju Station (광주역) - Gwangju Bus Terminal (터미널) - Market (시장) - Sangmu (상무) - Baegun-gwangjang (백운광장) - Hyocheon Station (효천역) (27.4 km)</li>
<li>Route of South-North Line (Plan 2) : Hyocheon Station (효천역) - Baekyoon-gwangjang (백운광장 a.k.a. white cloud square) - Namgwangju Station (남광주역) - Chosun University (조선대) - Gwangju Station (광주역) - Chonnam National University (전남대) - Ilgongno (일곡로) - Bonchon-industrial park (본촌공업단지) - Cheomdan-area (첨단지구) (24.58 km)</li>
</ul>
