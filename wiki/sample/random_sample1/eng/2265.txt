<p><b>Gwaebangsan</b> is a mountain in the city of <a href="/wiki/Gangneung" title="Gangneung">Gangneung</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gangwon-do_(South_Korea)" title="Gangwon-do (South Korea)">Gangwon-do</a> in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. It has an elevation of 339 m (1,112 ft).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
