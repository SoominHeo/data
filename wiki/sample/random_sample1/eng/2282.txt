<p>An <b>educational institution</b> is a place where people of different ages gain an <a href="/wiki/Education" title="Education">education</a>, including preschools, childcare, elementary schools, and universities. They provide a variety of <a href="/wiki/Learning_environment" title="Learning environment">learning environments</a> and <a href="/wiki/Learning_space" title="Learning space">learning spaces</a>.</p>
