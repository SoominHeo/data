<p>The <b>2nd <a href="/wiki/Pan_Arab_Games" title="Pan Arab Games">Pan Arab Games</a></b> were held in <a href="/wiki/Beirut" title="Beirut">Beirut</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lebanon" title="Lebanon">Lebanon</a> between October 12 and October 28, 1957.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> 914 athletes from 10 countries participated in events in 12 sports.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
