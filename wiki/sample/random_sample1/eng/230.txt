<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">All-Ukrainian Political Party "Ecology and Social Protection"</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

</div>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <b>All-Ukrainian Political Party "Ecology and Social Protection"</b> (<a href="/wiki/Ukrainian_language" title="Ukrainian language">Ukrainian</a>: <span lang="uk" xml:lang="uk">„Всеукраїнська Політична партія – Екологія та Соціальний захист“</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Political_party" title="Political party">political party</a> in <a href="/wiki/Ukraine" title="Ukraine">Ukraine</a> registered in November 2003.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-DATA_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-DATA-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The party did not participate in national elections till 2007.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-DATA_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-DATA-1">[1]</a></sup> In the <a href="/wiki/Ukrainian_parliamentary_election,_2007" title="Ukrainian parliamentary election, 2007">2007 parliamentary elections</a>, the party failed as part of the <a href="/wiki/Christian_Bloc" title="Christian Bloc">Christian Bloc</a> to win parliamentary representation.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-DATA_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-DATA-1">[1]</a></sup> The party did not participate in the <a href="/wiki/Ukrainian_parliamentary_election,_2012" title="Ukrainian parliamentary election, 2012">2012 parliamentary elections</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> And again the party did not participate in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2014_Ukrainian_parliamentary_election" title="2014 Ukrainian parliamentary election">2014 Ukrainian parliamentary election</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
