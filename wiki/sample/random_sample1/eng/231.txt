<p><b>Hunminjeongeum</b> (훈민정음) is a <a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a> language <a href="/wiki/Word_processor" title="Word processor">word processor</a> designed by <a href="/wiki/Samsung_Electronics" title="Samsung Electronics">Samsung Electronics</a>. Its <a href="/wiki/Filename_extension" title="Filename extension">filename extension</a> is <i>.gul</i>. It was first released in 1992 as South Korea's first fully corporate-sponsored word processor.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
