<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1959_Pan_American_Games&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The games were originally awarded to <a href="/wiki/Cleveland" title="Cleveland">Cleveland</a>. Cleveland withdrew from hosting the games due to financial reasons. The original backup-host, <a href="/wiki/Guatemala" title="Guatemala">Guatemala</a>, withdrew even before Cleveland. <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A3o_Paulo" title="São Paulo">São Paulo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a> and Chicago competed to replace Cleveland as the host of the Pan American Games. Chicago defeated São Paulo 13-6 in the Pan American Games Committee vote held August 3, 1957. São Paulo later would go on to host the <a href="/wiki/1963_Pan_American_Games" title="1963 Pan American Games">following edition</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ChicagoStyle_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-ChicagoStyle-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Organization">Organization</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1959_Pan_American_Games&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Organization">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Once Chicago took over the game following Cleveland's withdrawal, there were 18 months left to organize the games. The games were held on-schedule nonetheless. The games were the first Pan American Games to be held in the <a href="/wiki/Northern_Hemisphere" title="Northern Hemisphere">Northern Hemisphere's</a> <a href="/wiki/Summer" title="Summer">summer</a>. The previous two editions were held in March.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ChicagoStyle_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-ChicagoStyle-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Games">The Games</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1959_Pan_American_Games&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: The Games">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The games opened on August 27, 1959, in sunny 90 °F (32 °C) heat before 40,000 people in <a href="/wiki/Chicago" title="Chicago">Chicago</a>, <a href="/wiki/Illinois" title="Illinois">Illinois</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> at <a href="/wiki/Soldier_Field" title="Soldier Field">Soldier Field</a>.</p>
