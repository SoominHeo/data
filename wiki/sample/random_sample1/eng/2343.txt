<p><b>Masato Kudo</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">工藤 壮人</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kudou Masato</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born 6 May 1990)</span> is a Japanese <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> player who currently plays for <a href="/wiki/Sanfrecce_Hiroshima" title="Sanfrecce Hiroshima">Sanfrecce Hiroshima</a> in <a href="/wiki/J1_League" title="J1 League">J1 League</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Masato_Kudo&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On 23 September 2010, Kudo was selected for the Japan Under-21 squad for the <a href="/wiki/2010_Asian_Games" title="2010 Asian Games">2010 Asian Games</a> held in <a href="/wiki/Guangzhou" title="Guangzhou">Guangzhou</a>, China.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 23 May 2013, Kudo was called up to <a href="/wiki/Japan_national_football_team" title="Japan national football team">Japan</a>'s senior team for the first time prior to an <a href="/wiki/Exhibition_game" title="Exhibition game">international friendly</a> against <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria_national_football_team" title="Bulgaria national football team">Bulgaria</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
