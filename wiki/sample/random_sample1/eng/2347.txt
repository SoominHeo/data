<p><b>Guloudajie Station</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans">鼓楼大街站</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant">鼓樓大街站</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Gǔlóudàjiē Zhàn</span></i>; literally: "Gulou Street") is a <a href="/wiki/Metro_station" title="Metro station">station</a> on <a href="/wiki/Line_2,_Beijing_Subway" title="Line 2, Beijing Subway">Line 2</a> and <a href="/wiki/Line_8,_Beijing_Subway" title="Line 8, Beijing Subway">Line 8</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Beijing_Subway" title="Beijing Subway">Beijing Subway</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The connection to line 8 opened on December 30, 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
