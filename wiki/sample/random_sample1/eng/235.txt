<p>The <b>1960 Japan Series</b> was the <a href="/wiki/Nippon_Professional_Baseball" title="Nippon Professional Baseball">Nippon Professional Baseball</a> (NPB) championship series for the <a href="/wiki/1960_Nippon_Professional_Baseball_season" title="1960 Nippon Professional Baseball season">1960 season</a>. It was the 11th <a href="/wiki/Japan_Series" title="Japan Series">Japan Series</a> and featured the <a href="/wiki/Pacific_League" title="Pacific League">Pacific League</a> champions, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Daimai_Orions" title="Daimai Orions">Daimai Orions</a>, against the <a href="/wiki/Central_League" title="Central League">Central League</a> champions, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Taiyo_Whales" title="Taiyo Whales">Taiyo Whales</a>.</p>
