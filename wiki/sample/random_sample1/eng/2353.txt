<p><b>Kurosawa Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">黒沢駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kurosawa-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/East_Japan_Railway_Company" title="East Japan Railway Company">JR East</a> railway station located in <a href="/wiki/Yokote,_Akita" title="Yokote, Akita">Yokote</a>, <a href="/wiki/Akita_Prefecture" title="Akita Prefecture">Akita Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lines">Lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kurosawa_Station_(Yokote)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kurosawa Station is served by the <a href="/wiki/Kitakami_Line" title="Kitakami Line">Kitakami Line</a>, and is located 44.3 km from the terminus of the line at <a href="/wiki/Kitakami_Station" title="Kitakami Station">Kitakami Station</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_layout">Station layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kurosawa_Station_(Yokote)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Station layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kurosawa Station has a single <a href="/wiki/Island_platform" title="Island platform">island platform</a> serving bi-directional traffic. The station is unattended.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kurosawa_Station_(Yokote)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kurosawa Station opened on November 27, 1921 as a station on the <a href="/wiki/Japanese_Government_Railways" title="Japanese Government Railways">Japanese Government Railways</a> (JGR), serving the village of <a href="/wiki/Sannai,_Akita" title="Sannai, Akita">Sannai, Akita</a>. The JGR became the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Japan_National_Railways" title="Japan National Railways">Japan National Railways</a> (JNR) after World War II. The station was absorbed into the JR East network upon the <a href="/wiki/Privatization" title="Privatization">privatization</a> of the JNR on April 1, 1987.</p>
