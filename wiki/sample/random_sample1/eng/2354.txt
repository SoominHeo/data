<p><b>Kurosaki Station</b> (黒崎駅) is a station in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yahata_Nishi-ku,_Kitakyushu" title="Yahata Nishi-ku, Kitakyushu">Yahata Nishi-ku, Kitakyushu</a> serving <a href="/wiki/Kagoshima_Main_Line" title="Kagoshima Main Line">Kagoshima Main Line</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fukuhoku_Yutaka_Line" title="Fukuhoku Yutaka Line">Fukuhoku Yutaka Line</a> and <a href="/wiki/Chikuh%C5%8D_Electric_Railroad_Line" title="Chikuhō Electric Railroad Line">Chikuhō Electric Railroad Line</a>. It was first opened in 1891. Express trains, including the <a href="/wiki/Sonic_(train)" title="Sonic (train)">Sonic</a>, stop here.</p>

<p>JR Kurosaki Station is the station serving <a href="/wiki/Kagoshima_Main_Line" title="Kagoshima Main Line">Kagoshima Main Line</a> and terminus of <a href="/wiki/Fukuhoku_Yutaka_Line" title="Fukuhoku Yutaka Line">Fukuhoku Yutaka Line</a>. The station consist of 2 island serving four track and handles an average of 15,000 passengers a day. At the south exit of the station there is a <a href="/wiki/Pedway" title="Pedway">pedway</a> that also serves as a station square, connecting the Izutsuya department store on the east side, the commercial area on the south side.</p>
