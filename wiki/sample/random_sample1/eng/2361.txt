<p>The <b>coppery titi</b> (<i>Callicebus cupreus</i>) is a species of <a href="/wiki/Titi" title="Titi">titi</a>, a type of <a href="/wiki/New_World_monkey" title="New World monkey">New World monkey</a>, from <a href="/wiki/South_America" title="South America">South America</a>. It is found in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Amazon_Rainforest" title="Amazon Rainforest">Amazon</a> of <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a> and <a href="/wiki/Peru" title="Peru">Peru</a>, and perhaps northern <a href="/wiki/Bolivia" title="Bolivia">Bolivia</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-msw3_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-msw3-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
