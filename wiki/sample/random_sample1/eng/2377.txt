<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kushida_River" title="Kushida River">Kushida River</a> (櫛田川, Kushida-gawa), a major river that flows through central Mie Prefecture on the island of Honshū, Japan</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/144P/Kushida" title="144P/Kushida">144P/Kushida</a>, a periodic comet discovered in January, 1994</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/147P/Kushida-Muramatsu" title="147P/Kushida-Muramatsu">147P/Kushida-Muramatsu</a>, a quasi-Hilda comet discovered in 1993</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/Akira_Kushida" title="Akira Kushida">Akira Kushida</a> (born 1948), a Japanese vocalist</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Reiki_Kushida" title="Reiki Kushida">Reiki Kushida</a>, a Japanese astronomer</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Takashi_Kushida" title="Takashi Kushida">Takashi Kushida</a> (born 1935), a Japanese aikido master</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Yoshio_Kushida" title="Yoshio Kushida">Yoshio Kushida</a> (born 1957), a Japanese astronomer</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Yuki_Kushida" title="Yuki Kushida">Yuki Kushida</a>, an English-speaking actress and teacher of Japanese origin</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Yujiro_Kushida" title="Yujiro Kushida">Yujiro Kushida</a> (born 1983), a Japanese professional wrestler currently competing in New Japan Pro Wrestling</li>
</ul>
