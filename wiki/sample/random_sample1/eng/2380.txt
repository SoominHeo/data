<p><b>Guardea</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Terni" title="Province of Terni">Province of Terni</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Umbria" title="Umbria">Umbria</a>, located about 60 km south of <a href="/wiki/Perugia" title="Perugia">Perugia</a> and about 30 km west of <a href="/wiki/Terni" title="Terni">Terni</a>.</p>
<p>Among the churches is the parish church of <a href="/wiki/Santi_Pietro_e_Cesareo" title="Santi Pietro e Cesareo">Santi Pietro e Cesareo</a> and the church of <a href="/wiki/Sant%27Egidio,_Gardea" title="Sant'Egidio, Gardea">Sant'Egidio</a>.</p>
<p>Guardea borders the following municipalities: <a href="/wiki/Alviano" title="Alviano">Alviano</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Amelia_(Italy)" title="Amelia (Italy)">Amelia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Avigliano_Umbro" title="Avigliano Umbro">Avigliano Umbro</a>, <a href="/wiki/Civitella_d%27Agliano" title="Civitella d'Agliano">Civitella d'Agliano</a>, <a href="/wiki/Montecastrilli" title="Montecastrilli">Montecastrilli</a>, <a href="/wiki/Montecchio" title="Montecchio">Montecchio</a>.</p>
