<p><b>Guido Marilungo</b> (born August 9, 1989) is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Italian_people" title="Italian people">Italian</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Forward_(association_football)" title="Forward (association football)">striker</a> for <a href="/wiki/Empoli_F.C." title="Empoli F.C.">Empoli</a> on loan from <a href="/wiki/Atalanta_B.C." title="Atalanta B.C.">Atalanta</a>. He is known for his lightning pace and powerful right foot and eye for goal. He hugely admires both Summer Zibdeh and Alessandro "Gigi" Pirolini.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Guido_Marilungo&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Montegranaro" title="Montegranaro">Montegranaro</a>, <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Fermo" title="Province of Fermo">Province of Fermo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marche" title="Marche">Marche</a>, he moved his first footsteps with his hometown club until the age of 14, when he was noted by FIFA agent <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Silvio_Pagliari&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Silvio Pagliari (page does not exist)">Silvio Pagliari</a> who proposed him to <a href="/wiki/U.C._Sampdoria" title="U.C. Sampdoria">Sampdoria</a>, which successively chose to sign the player and include him in their <i>Giovanissimi Nazionali</i> (under-15) squad for the 2003–04 season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> He was pivotal in the 2007–08 Sampdoria under-19 double (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Campionato_Primavera" title="Campionato Primavera">Campionato Primavera</a> and <a href="/wiki/Coppa_Italia_Primavera" title="Coppa Italia Primavera">Coppa Italia Primavera</a>).</p>
<p>On January 11, 2009, he made his first team debut in a <a href="/wiki/Serie_A" title="Serie A">Serie A</a> home league game as a second-half substitute against <a href="/wiki/U.S._Citt%C3%A0_di_Palermo" title="U.S. Città di Palermo">Palermo</a>, a 2–0 loss for the Genoese club.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Three months later, exactly on April 26, 2009, he made his first start in a 3–3 draw with <a href="/wiki/Cagliari_Calcio" title="Cagliari Calcio">Cagliari</a>, with Marilungo scoring two goals, the first ones in his senior career.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>On August 27, 2009, Marilungo joined <a href="/wiki/Serie_B" title="Serie B">Serie B</a> outfit <a href="/wiki/U.S._Lecce" title="U.S. Lecce">Lecce</a> on a one-year loan spell.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> On 12 September 2010, he signed a 5-year contract to stay with Sampdoria,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> but only four months later, he joined <a href="/wiki/Atalanta_B.C." title="Atalanta B.C.">Atalanta</a> in a 4½-year deal for €4.7 million.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> Sampdoria relegated at the end of 2010–11 season, with Atlanta promoted to Serie A at the same time.</p>
<p>On 17 January 2014 Marilungo was signed by Serie B club <a href="/wiki/A.C._Cesena" title="A.C. Cesena">A.C. Cesena</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Guido_Marilungo&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On 8 September 2009 he made his debut with the <a href="/wiki/Italy_national_under-21_football_team" title="Italy national under-21 football team">Italy U-21</a> team in a game against <a href="/wiki/Luxembourg_national_under-21_football_team" title="Luxembourg national under-21 football team">Luxembourg</a>. He scored 2 goals in 7 appearances with the U-21 team.</p>
