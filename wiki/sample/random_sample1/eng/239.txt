<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Trivia">Trivia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=.gi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Trivia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Because of the coincidence in abbreviation, it has also been used for some official domains for the Spanish city <a href="/wiki/Girona" title="Girona">Girona</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> This limited use began prior to the creation of the <a href="/wiki/.cat" title=".cat">.cat</a> TLD because of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Catalan_people" title="Catalan people">Catalan</a> reluctance to use domains under the Spanish country code TLD <a href="/wiki/.es" title=".es">.es</a>. Subsequent rules changes prevent new 2nd level registrations directly under .gi for entities not connected with Gibraltar.</p>
