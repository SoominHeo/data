<p><b>Ku Hyun-jun</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">구현준</span>; born December 13, 1993) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> player who currently plays as a left back for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Busan_I%27Park" title="Busan I'Park">Busan I'Park</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/K-League" title="K-League">K-League</a>.</p>
