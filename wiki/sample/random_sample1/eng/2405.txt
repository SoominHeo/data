<p>The <b>State Ethnic Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China</b> (<b>SEAC</b>) is a cabinet-level department under the <a href="/wiki/State_Council_of_the_People%27s_Republic_of_China" title="State Council of the People's Republic of China">State Council of the People's Republic of China</a> and responsible for relations between the Central Government and <a href="/wiki/Ethnic_minorities_in_China" title="Ethnic minorities in China">ethnic minorities in China</a>.</p>
