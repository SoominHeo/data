<p><b>Freeway No. 4</b> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Freeway" title="Freeway">freeway</a>, which begins in <a href="/wiki/Qingshui_District" title="Qingshui District">Qingshui, Taichung</a> on the Provincial Highway No. 17. and ends in <a href="/wiki/Fengyuan_District" title="Fengyuan District">Fengyuan District</a> on the Provincial Highway No. 3.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notes">Notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Freeway_4_(Taiwan)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Notes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Completed in November 2001.</p>
<p>There is a plan to extend the route from Fengyuan End south to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Xinshe,_Taichung" title="Xinshe, Taichung">Xinshe, Taichung</a>, Dakeng (in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Taichung_City" title="Taichung City">Taichung City</a>), and <a href="/wiki/Taiping_District,_Taichung" title="Taiping District, Taichung">Taiping District</a>. It will connect to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Freeway_No._3_(Taiwan)" title="Freeway No. 3 (Taiwan)">Freeway No. 3</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wufeng,_Taichung" title="Wufeng, Taichung">Wufeng, Taichung</a>.</p>
