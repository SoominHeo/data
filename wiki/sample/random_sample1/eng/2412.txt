<p><b>National Route 5</b> (国道5号) is a major <a href="/wiki/National_highways_of_Japan" title="National highways of Japan">highway</a> on the island of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hokkaid%C5%8D" title="Hokkaidō">Hokkaidō</a> in <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>. Its southern terminus is in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hakodate,_Hokkaid%C5%8D" title="Hakodate, Hokkaidō">Hakodate</a> and its northern end in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sapporo,_Hokkaid%C5%8D" title="Sapporo, Hokkaidō">Sapporo</a>. En route, it passes through <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Otaru,_Hokkaid%C5%8D" title="Otaru, Hokkaidō">Otaru</a>.</p>
