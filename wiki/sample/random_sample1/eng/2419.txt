<p><b>Jeonju National Museum</b> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/National_museum" title="National museum">national museum</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Jeonju" title="Jeonju">Jeonju</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jeollabuk-do" title="Jeollabuk-do">Jeollabuk-do</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. It opened on October 26, 1990 as the ninth South Korean national museum.</p>
<p>The Main Building comprises three permanent exhibits, an Archeology room, a Fine Art room, a Folklore room, and one special exhibit room.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
