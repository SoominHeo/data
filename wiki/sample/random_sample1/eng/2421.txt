<p>The <b>Hong Kong and Macau Affairs Office of the State Council</b> is an administrative agency of the <a href="/wiki/State_Council_of_the_People%27s_Republic_of_China" title="State Council of the People's Republic of China">State Council of the People's Republic of China</a> responsible for promoting cooperation and coordination of political, economic and cultural ties between <a href="/wiki/Mainland_China" title="Mainland China">Mainland China</a> and the Chinese <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Special_Administrative_Region_of_the_People%27s_Republic_of_China" title="Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China">Special Administrative Regions</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hong_Kong" title="Hong Kong">Hong Kong</a> and <a href="/wiki/Macau" title="Macau">Macau</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Administration">Administration</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hong_Kong_and_Macau_Affairs_Office&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Administration">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The agency was established in 1978, and has been headed by the Director of the Hong Kong and Macau Affairs Office. There are deputy directors which assist the director in running the office.</p>
<p>The agency answers to the State Council of the People's Republic of China as well as the <a href="/wiki/Central_Coordination_Group_for_Hong_Kong_and_Macau_Affairs" title="Central Coordination Group for Hong Kong and Macau Affairs">Central Coordination Group for Hong Kong and Macau Affairs</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Communist_Party_of_China" title="Communist Party of China">Communist Party of China</a>.</p>
