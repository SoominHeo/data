<p><b>Gunnel MÃ¤rtha IngegÃ¤rd Lindblom</b> (born 18 December 1931) is a Swedish film actress and director.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SFI_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-SFI-1">[1]</a></sup> As an actress she has been particularly associated with the work of <a href="/wiki/Ingmar_Bergman" title="Ingmar Bergman">Ingmar Bergman</a>, though in 1965 she performed the lead role in <i><a href="/wiki/Miss_Julie" title="Miss Julie">Miss Julie</a></i> for BBC Television. She also played the key-role of The Mummy in Bergman's staging of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Strindberg" title="Strindberg">Strindberg</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/The_Ghost_Sonata" title="The Ghost Sonata">The Ghost Sonata</a></i> in 1998-2000, a performance that earned her much critical acclaim.</p>
<p>She appeared on stage as Tintomara's mother in <a href="/wiki/Carl_Jonas_Love_Almqvist" title="Carl Jonas Love Almqvist">Carl Almqvist</a>'s play <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Drottningens_juvelsmycke" title="Drottningens juvelsmycke">Drottningens juvelsmycke</a></i> (English: <i>The Queen's Tiara</i>), staged at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dramaten" title="Dramaten">Dramaten</a> for the theatre's 100-year jubilee in 2008. In 2009, she directed the <a href="/wiki/Jon_Fosse" title="Jon Fosse">Jon Fosse</a> play <i>Flicka i gul regnjacka (Girl in Yellow Raincoat)</i> at the <a href="/wiki/Royal_Dramatic_Theatre" title="Royal Dramatic Theatre">Royal Dramatic Theatre</a>, starring <a href="/wiki/Stina_Ekblad" title="Stina Ekblad">Stina Ekblad</a> and <a href="/wiki/Irene_Lindh" title="Irene Lindh">Irene Lindh</a>, which premiered on 9 October.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<p>Lindblom is married to senior lecturer Sture Helander (who was Ingmar Bergman's personal physician).<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
