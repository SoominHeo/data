<p><b>Kwon Jin-ah</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">권진아</span>; born July 18, 1997) is a South Korean singer, songwriter and guitarist. She was known for the third place of <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Broadcasting_System" title="Seoul Broadcasting System">SBS</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/K-pop_Star_3" title="K-pop Star 3">K-pop Star Season 3</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-star_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-star-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kwon_Jin-ah&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2013.E2.80.9315:_K-pop_Star_3_and_solo_activities">2013–15: K-pop Star 3 and solo activities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kwon_Jin-ah&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 2013–15: K-pop Star 3 and solo activities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/K-pop_Star_3" title="K-pop Star 3">K-pop Star 3</a></div>
<p>In 2013, Kwon Jin-ah became a contestant of <i><a href="/wiki/K-pop_Star_3" title="K-pop Star 3">K-pop Star 3</a></i> and won third place in the competition. On May 13, 2014, <a href="/wiki/Antenna_Music" title="Antenna Music">Antenna Music</a> announced their exclusive contract with Kwon Jin-ah.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> On June 19, she released a single titled, "I Only See You" for SBS drama <i>You're All Surrounded</i>. In November 2014, it was confirmed that she would feature in a song from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yoo_Hee-yeol" title="Yoo Hee-yeol">Yoo Hee-yeol</a>'s <i>De Capo</i> album. The song was titled "She Said".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> In 2015, she and <a href="/wiki/Rap_Monster" title="Rap Monster">Rap Monster</a> from [BTS (band)|Bangtan Boys]] featured in the song "U" on <a href="/wiki/Primary_(musician)" title="Primary (musician)">Primary</a>'s 2015 album, <i>2-1</i>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2016.E2.80.93present:_Debut">2016–present: Debut</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kwon_Jin-ah&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 2016–present: Debut">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In July 2016, Antenna Music announced that Kwon Jin-ah will be debut in September.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> She and Sam Kim joined Antenna Music project, with <b>Love Antenna</b> releasing as a digital single, and <i>Until Here</i> on July 18.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> On September 10, 2016 Antenna Music announced that Kwon Jin-ah will make her official debut with the release debut studio album <i>One Strange Night</i> on September 19.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
