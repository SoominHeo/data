<p>The <b>silent dormouse</b> (<i>Graphiurus surdus</i>) is a species of <a href="/wiki/Rodent" title="Rodent">rodent</a> in the family <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gliridae" title="Gliridae">Gliridae</a>. It is found in <a href="/wiki/Cameroon" title="Cameroon">Cameroon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo" title="Democratic Republic of the Congo">Democratic Republic of the Congo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Equatorial_Guinea" title="Equatorial Guinea">Equatorial Guinea</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Gabon" title="Gabon">Gabon</a>. Its natural <a href="/wiki/Habitat" title="Habitat">habitat</a> is subtropical or tropical moist lowland <a href="/wiki/Forest" title="Forest">forests</a>.</p>
