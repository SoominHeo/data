<p><i><b>Kage Kara Mamoru!</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">陰からマモル</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>lit. Mamoru from the Shadows, or "I Will Protect You from the Shadows</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a series of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Light_novels" title="Light novels">light novels</a> written by Achi Taro. The light novels had ended in March 2008 with the release of Volume 12, but as of July 2009, a new series entitled <i><b>Motto! Kage Kara Mamoru!</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">もっと!陰からマモル!</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>lit. More! Mamoru the Shadow</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> has started serialization. It features manga and anime adaptations. The main character is Mamoru Kagemori, a <a href="/wiki/Ninja" title="Ninja">ninja</a> who is sworn to protect his neighbor Yūna Konnyaku.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Story">Story</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kage_Kara_Mamoru!&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Story">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Kagemori family has secretly protected its neighbor, the Konnyaku family, for 400 years. Mamoru has been the protector of the daughter, Yūna Konnyaku, since they were in kindergarten. When Yūna is in trouble, Mamoru quickly puts on his ninja suit and transforms into a competent ninja; able to protect her from harm. Unfortunately, this poses quite a challenge as Yūna, being both clumsy and absent-minded, often stumbles into situations where Mamoru must once again come to her rescue.</p>
<p>On the first day of their new school term, a model airplane accidentally makes a dive towards Yuna, but it is quickly stopped by a thrown <a href="/wiki/Kunai" title="Kunai">kunai</a>. At school, Airi Sawagashi invites Yūna to go bowling, but is unhappy when Mamoru also comes.</p>
<p>At the bowling alley, Yūna wanders into the men's restroom and interrupts a yakuza deal involving fake dolls. As a result, they kidnap her and take her to their boss. Mamoru follows them, takes out the gang, and rescues Yūna.</p>
<p>The story continues as new characters enter Yūna and Mamoru's lives with both good and evil intentions, wacky adventures, hints of romance, and confrontations with the strangest of adversaries.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Characters">Characters</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kage_Kara_Mamoru!&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Characters">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<dl>
<dt>Mamoru Kagemori <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">陰守 マモル</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kagemori Mamoru</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><i>Voiced by:</i> <a href="/wiki/Atsushi_Kisaichi" title="Atsushi Kisaichi">Atsushi Kisaichi</a> (Japanese)</dd>
</dl>
<p>Mamoru Kagemori is a plain boy with disheveled hair and thick glasses, which are actually a disguise. He is the son of a ninja family which has been protecting the Konnyaku family for 400 years. He reveals his true self (without glasses) when he transforms into a ninja to protect Yūna from harm and is very lazy except when he's saving Yūna. He has been friends with Yūna since they were children, and has also been in the same class as her since kindergarten. He has a fear of bears, which developed when he tried to protect Yamame from a bear when they were children. His name: Mamoru, means protect in Japanese.</p>
<dl>
<dt>Yūna Konnyaku <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">紺若 ゆうな</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Konnyaku Yūna</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><i>Voiced by:</i> <a href="/wiki/Mai_Nakahara" title="Mai Nakahara">Mai Nakahara</a> (Japanese)</dd>
</dl>
<p>Yūna is Mamoru's childhood friend and neighbor. She is a quite clumsy and often ditzy girl. These qualities get her into many troublesome situations. Since she has known Mamoru since kindergarten, Mamoru knows everything about her, except for the object that Yūna looks at every night before bed - a photo of herself and Mamoru which she cherishes. It is hinted that she is in love with Mamoru, but is afraid to admit her love to him. She is shown to be jealous of Mamoru interacting with other girls as seen when she prevented Sawagashi and Tsubaki from holding his hands as they slept at the zoo.</p>
<dl>
<dt>Airi Sawagashi <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">沢菓 愛里</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Sawagashi Airi</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><i>Voiced by:</i> <a href="/wiki/Ry%C5%8Dko_Shintani" title="Ryōko Shintani">Ryōko Shintani</a> (Japanese)</dd>
</dl>
<p>Airi, a wealthy girl, is a classmate and friend of Mamoru and Yūna. She discourages any relationship between Yūna and Mamoru, because, according to her, childhood friends who become lovers are generally unsuccessful. However, this may be a ploy since she has feelings for Kagemori, whom she derisively calls 'Ahoru'. She occasionally gives Mamoru hints that she likes him, which he is oblivious to, and she vents her frustration by hitting Mamoru.</p>
<dl>
<dt>Tsubaki Mapputatsu <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">真双津 椿</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Mapputatsu Tsubaki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><i>Voiced by:</i> <a href="/wiki/Sachiko_Kojima" title="Sachiko Kojima">Sachiko Kojima</a> (Japanese)</dd>
</dl>
<p>Tsubaki is a <a href="/wiki/Miko" title="Miko">miko</a> swordswoman. Her sword can cut anything but <a href="/wiki/Nata_de_coco" title="Nata de coco">nata de coco</a>. She was hired by the yakuza boss in order avenge his earlier defeat at Mamoru's hands. After finding out the truth about the yakuza boss, she exacts her revenge on him. She also decides to enter Mamoru's school to become more worldly. After Mamoru cheers her up when she was depressed about being old-fashioned, she develops feelings for him.</p>
<dl>
<dt>Yamame Hattori <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">服部 山芽</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Hattori Yamame</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><i>Voiced by:</i> <a href="/wiki/Ai_Shimizu" title="Ai Shimizu">Ai Shimizu</a> (Japanese)</dd>
</dl>
<p>Yamame is a ninja and a relative of Mamoru. She remembers Mamoru comforting her when she was little and is quite disappointed after seeing the glasses-wearing person he has become. When Mamoru saves her from an animal man, Chin Panji, she realizes that he hasn't really changed. The next day, Yamame transfers to his school. She is also responsible for Mamoru's fear of bears.</p>
<dl>
<dt>Hotaru Kumogakure <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">雲隠 ホタル</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kumogakure Hotaru</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><i>Voiced by:</i> Erina Furukawa (Japanese)</dd>
</dl>
<p>Hotaru is a ninja. She confesses her love to Mamoru when they first meet. She and her brother are scheming to defeat Mamoru (a member of the strongest ninja clan) in order to avenge their clan's disgrace in losing to Yamame at a ninja athletic meet. Her feelings for him prevent her from finishing off the wounded Mamoru and she instead administers some of her family's secret medicine mouth-to-mouth thus allowing him to defeat her brother. Afterwards, she moves into the neighborhood and starts attending the same school.</p>
<dl>
<dt>Bluemaru <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">ぶる丸</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Burumaru</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
</dl>
<p>Kagemori family's <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bull_terrier" title="Bull terrier">bull terrier</a> ninja dog. Despite his cute appearance and lazy nature, Bluemaru is actually a very skilled canine and is able to use various ninja weapons in his mouth.</p>
<dl>
<dt>Sakurako Kagemori <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">陰守 桜子</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kagemori Sakurako</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
</dl>
<p>Sakurako is Mamoru's mother. She is a ninja and is in charge of protecting Yūna's mother.</p>
<dl>
<dt>Kengo Kagemori <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">陰守 堅護</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kagemori Kengo</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
</dl>
<p>Kengo is Mamoru's father. He is a ninja and is in charge of protecting Yūna's father.</p>
<dl>
<dt>Miminosuke <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">耳の介</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Miminosuke</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
</dl>
<p>Yamame's ninja rabbit companion.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Anime">Anime</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kage_Kara_Mamoru!&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Anime">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The light novels were adapted into a 12 episodes anime series by the <a href="/wiki/Group_TAC" title="Group TAC">Group TAC</a> studio and produced by <a href="/wiki/Aniplex" title="Aniplex">Aniplex</a>. This anime series was broadcast on TV Tokyo between January 7, 2006 and March 25, 2006 with last two episodes aired the same day.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ja-Broadcast_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Ja-Broadcast-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The anime opening theme is "Million Love" <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">ミリオン・ラブ</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> sung by <a href="/wiki/Mai_Nakahara" title="Mai Nakahara">Mai Nakahara</a> and the ending theme is "Rainy Beat" sung by <a href="/wiki/Ry%C5%8Dko_Shintani" title="Ryōko Shintani">Ryōko Shintani</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sachiko_Kojima" title="Sachiko Kojima">Sachiko Kojima</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ai_Shimizu" title="Ai Shimizu">Ai Shimizu</a>, and Erina Furukawa.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Manga">Manga</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kage_Kara_Mamoru!&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Manga">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The five volumes of the manga adaption were released by the publisher <a href="/wiki/Media_Factory" title="Media Factory">Media Factory</a>. The manga adaption was first serialized between November 2005 to April 2006 in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seinen" title="Seinen">seinen</a> magazine <i><a href="/wiki/Monthly_Comic_Flapper" title="Monthly Comic Flapper">Monthly Comic Flapper</a></i> and from August 2006 in <i><a href="/wiki/Monthly_Comic_Alive" title="Monthly Comic Alive">Monthly Comic Alive</a></i>.</p>
<p>Between July 16, 2008 and January 21, 2009, <a href="/wiki/DrMaster" title="DrMaster">DrMaster</a> published the English translation of the first three volumes of this series under the name of <i>Mamoru the Shadow Protector</i>.</p>
<p>In Indonesia, the manga has been licensed by <a href="/wiki/Elex_Media_Komputindo" title="Elex Media Komputindo">Elex Media Komputindo</a>, under the title <i>The Bodyguard</i>.</p>
