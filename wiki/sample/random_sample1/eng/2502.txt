<p><b>Granada</b> (<small>Spanish pronunciation: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Spanish" title="Help:IPA for Spanish">[ɡɾaˈnaða]</a></span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Departments_of_Nicaragua" title="Departments of Nicaragua">department</a> in <a href="/wiki/Nicaragua" title="Nicaragua">Nicaragua</a>. It covers an area of 929 km² and has a population of 190,600 (2005 census). The capital is the city of <a href="/wiki/Granada,_Nicaragua" title="Granada, Nicaragua">Granada</a>, one of North America's oldest cities</p>
