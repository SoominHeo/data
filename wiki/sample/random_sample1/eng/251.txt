<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">East German general election, 1971</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">
					Jump to:					<a href="#mw-head">navigation</a>, 					<a href="#p-search">search</a>
</div>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Legislative elections</b> were held in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/German_Democratic_Republic" title="German Democratic Republic">German Democratic Republic</a> on 14 November 1971. 434 deputies to the <a href="/wiki/Volkskammer" title="Volkskammer">Volkskammer</a> were elected, with all of them being candidates of the single-list <a href="/wiki/National_Front_(East_Germany)" title="National Front (East Germany)">National Front</a>. 584 Front candidates were put forward, with 434 being elected. The allocation of seats remained unchanged from previous elections.</p>
<p>Out of 11,401,090 registered electors, 11,227,535 (98.5%) voted, with 11,207,388 (99.5%) voting for Front candidates. 3,196 ballots were spoiled.</p>
