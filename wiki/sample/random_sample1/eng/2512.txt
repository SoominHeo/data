<p>The <b>gracile shrew tenrec</b> (<i>Microgale gracilis</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> of <a href="/wiki/Mammal" title="Mammal">mammal</a> in the family <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tenrecidae" title="Tenrecidae">Tenrecidae</a>. It is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Endemic" title="Endemic">endemic</a> to <a href="/wiki/Madagascar" title="Madagascar">Madagascar</a>. Its natural <a href="/wiki/Habitat" title="Habitat">habitat</a> is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Subtropical" title="Subtropical">subtropical</a> or tropical moist <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Montane_forest" title="Montane forest">montane forests</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-iucn_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-iucn-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
