<p>The <b>Grand Hyatt Seoul</b> is a 5-star luxury <a href="/wiki/Hyatt" title="Hyatt">Hyatt</a> hotel in <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. Located in landscaped gardens in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yongsan" title="Yongsan">Yongsan</a> district, on the historic Mount <a href="/wiki/Namsan_(Seoul)" title="Namsan (Seoul)">Namsan</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> the hotel has 602 rooms and suites and several F&amp;B outlets.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Facilities">Facilities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Grand_Hyatt_Seoul&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Facilities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The hotel's restaurants include the <i>Paris Grill</i> which serves European cuisine, <i>The Chinese Restaurant</i>, <i>J.J. Mahoney’s</i> with live music,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> <i>The Paris Bar</i> and <i>Helicon Song Bar</i>.</p>
<p>The Presidential Suite is 325 square-meter with seven rooms and floor-to-ceiling windows on the 20th floor which provide sprawling skyline views of Seoul.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="In_media">In media</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Grand_Hyatt_Seoul&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: In media">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The hotel was used as the filming location for <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Broadcasting_System" title="Seoul Broadcasting System">Seoul Broadcasting System (SBS)</a>'s drama <i><a href="/wiki/Lovers_in_Paris" title="Lovers in Paris">Lovers in Paris</a></i>, namely the swimming pool, the lobby where the two leads kiss, The Paris Bar, where male lead, Han Ki-joo played by <a href="/wiki/Park_Shin-yang" title="Park Shin-yang">Park Shin-yang</a> sang to Kang Tae-young played by <a href="/wiki/Kim_Jung-eun" title="Kim Jung-eun">Kim Jung-eun</a>, J.J. Mahoney's, where the college hockey party held and the grand ballroom, where the engagement ceremony was held.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
