<p><b>Grégory Mallet</b> (born 21 March 1984)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">[1]</a></sup> is a French Olympic swimmer.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gr%C3%A9gory_Mallet&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mallet swam for France at the <a href="/wiki/France_at_the_2008_Summer_Olympics" title="France at the 2008 Summer Olympics">2008 Olympics</a>, where he was part of France's silver-medal winning <a href="/wiki/Swimming_at_the_2008_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_4_%C3%97_100_metre_freestyle_relay" title="Swimming at the 2008 Summer Olympics – Men's 4 × 100 metre freestyle relay">4 × 100 m freestyle relay</a> after he swam in the preliminary heats.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> At the <a href="/wiki/2012_Summer_Olympics" title="2012 Summer Olympics">2012 Summer Olympics</a> he also swam in the final of the <a href="/wiki/Swimming_at_the_2012_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_4_%C3%97_200_metre_freestyle_relay" title="Swimming at the 2012 Summer Olympics – Men's 4 × 200 metre freestyle relay">men's 4 × 200 m freestyle relay</a> to help France win the silver medal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
