<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <b>K League 'FAN'tastic Player</b> is an annual award given to the player who is judged to have been the best of the year in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. The word 'Fan'tastic means that a fanstasic player is chosen by fans. The award has been presented since the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/K-League_2009" title="K-League 2009">2009</a> season and the winner is chosen by an online vote by K-League fans.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
