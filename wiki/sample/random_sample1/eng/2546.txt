<p><b>Grottolella</b> is a town and <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Avellino" title="Province of Avellino">province of Avellino</a>, <a href="/wiki/Campania" title="Campania">Campania</a>, <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>.<br/>
It is famous for its fraction <i>Pozzo del Sale</i>; years ago there was a <a href="/wiki/Water_well" title="Water well">water well</a> here, with <a href="/wiki/Saline_water" title="Saline water">saline water</a> inside, from which people used to get <a href="/wiki/Salt" title="Salt">salt</a> for free.</p>
