<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Consorts of the Kings of Greece</b> were women married to the rulers of the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Greece" title="Kingdom of Greece">Kingdom of Greece</a> during their reign. All monarchs of modern Greece were male.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Greek consorts wore the title, <i><a href="/wiki/Queen_consort" title="Queen consort">Queen</a> of the Hellenes</i> and the style, <a href="/wiki/Majesty" title="Majesty">Majesty</a>. The following queens were spouses of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_Kings_of_Greece" title="List of Kings of Greece">kings of modern Greece</a> between 1836 and 1973:</p>
