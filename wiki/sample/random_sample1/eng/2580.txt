<p><i><b>Kamen Rider × Super Sentai: Super Hero Taisen</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">仮面ライダー×スーパー戦隊 スーパーヒーロー大戦</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kamen Raidā × Supā Sentai Supā Hīrō Taisen</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a 2012 <a href="/wiki/Film" title="Film">film</a> which features a crossover between the <a href="/wiki/Super_Sentai" title="Super Sentai">Super Sentai</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Series" title="Kamen Rider Series">Kamen Rider Series</a>.</p>
<p>The protagonists of <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Decade" title="Kamen Rider Decade">Kamen Rider Decade</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Kaizoku_Sentai_Gokaiger" title="Kaizoku Sentai Gokaiger">Kaizoku Sentai Gokaiger</a></i> are featured, but the casts of <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Fourze" title="Kamen Rider Fourze">Kamen Rider Fourze</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_OOO" title="Kamen Rider OOO">Kamen Rider OOO</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Tokumei_Sentai_Go-Busters" title="Tokumei Sentai Go-Busters">Tokumei Sentai Go-Busters</a></i> also participate. Other returning cast members include <a href="/wiki/Rina_Akiyama" title="Rina Akiyama">Rina Akiyama</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kenjir%C5%8D_Ishimaru" title="Kenjirō Ishimaru">Kenjirō Ishimaru</a> reprising their roles from <i><a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Den-O" title="Kamen Rider Den-O">Kamen Rider Den-O</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>In its first week in theaters, <i>Super Hero Taisen</i> opened at the #1 spot in the Japanese box offices, edging out <i><a href="/wiki/Detective_Conan:_The_Eleventh_Striker" title="Detective Conan: The Eleventh Striker">Detective Conan: The Eleventh Striker</a></i>. The DVD and Blu-ray was released on October 21, 2012 and was aired as part of <a href="/wiki/TV_Asahi" title="TV Asahi">TV Asahi</a>'s <i>Super Hero and Heroine Summer Vacation</i> special on August 25, 2013.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Story">Story</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kamen_Rider_%C3%97_Super_Sentai:_Super_Hero_Taisen&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Story">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The film begins with Captain Marvelous of the <a href="/wiki/Gokaigers" title="Gokaigers">Gokaigers</a> confronting and defeating the first seven Kamen Riders before <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Decade_(character)" title="Kamen Rider Decade (character)">Tsukasa Kadoya</a> appears before him. While Marvelous swears to him he will not rest before all Kamen Riders are destroyed, Tsukasa claims he will not stop until all Super Sentai are vanquished. While watching Earth from the moon, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Fourze_(character)" title="Kamen Rider Fourze (character)">Kamen Rider Fourze</a>, <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Fourze_characters#Yuki_Jojima" title="List of Kamen Rider Fourze characters">Yuki</a> and <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Fourze_characters#Miu_Kazashiro" title="List of Kamen Rider Fourze characters">Miu</a> see a series of shooting stars that turn out to be a <a href="/wiki/Space_Empire_Zangyack" title="Space Empire Zangyack">Zangyack fleet</a>. Within moments, Amanogawa High is invaded by Zangyack militants as Gentaro comes to <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Fourze_characters#Shun_Daimonji" title="List of Kamen Rider Fourze characters">Shun</a>'s aid before being blasted away from the Kamen Rider Club to an area where he meets Captain Marvelous. Introducing himself as <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Fourze_characters#Kengo_Utahoshi" title="List of Kamen Rider Fourze characters">Kengo</a> and Yuki arrive while turning down Gentaro's friendship offer, Marvelous reveals his intent to kill Gentaro as becomes Gokai Red. Transforming in response, Kamen Rider Fourze is overpowered by Gokai Red before sicking the soldiers on him. But the Gokai Galleon crew arrive and take out the Zangyack soldiers before they attempt to talk Marvelous out of attacking the Kamen Riders. But Gokai Red is unconvinced as he summons his new ship, the Gigant Horse, and calls in Warz Gill, Ackdos Gill, <a href="/wiki/Brajira_of_the_Messiah" title="Brajira of the Messiah">Brajira of the Messiah</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Himitsu_Sentai_Gorenger#The_Black_Cross_Army" title="Himitsu Sentai Gorenger">Black Cross King</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gen_J%C5%ABken#Long" title="Gen Jūken">Long</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Infershia_Pantheon#Dagon" title="Infershia Pantheon">Hades God Dagon</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Negative_Syndicate#Ashu_Tribe.2FQuesters" title="Negative Syndicate">Questers</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Gedoushu#Doukoku" title="Gedoushu">Chimatsuri Doukoku</a>. Though <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Meteor" title="Kamen Rider Meteor">Kamen Rider Meteor</a> arrives to lend a hand, Gokai Red retaliates by summoning <a href="/wiki/Choudenshi_Bioman#Silva" title="Choudenshi Bioman">Rider Hunter Silva</a> to cripple Meteor before inflicting the death blow and tells his crew that Kamen Rider Decade has already taken the lives of the Gorengers and some of the other Super Sentai teams.</p>
<p>At their city's Kazami district, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Go-Busters" title="Go-Busters">Go-Busters</a> find people being attacked by various Kamen Rider monsters under <a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_V3#Destron" title="Kamen Rider V3">Doktor G</a> of the revived <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters#Dai-Shocker" title="List of Kamen Rider Decade characters">Dai-Shocker</a>. Doktor G then introduces the Go-Busters to Dai Shocker's Great Leader, Tsukasa as he transforms into Decade to overpower them. But before he can finish them off, the Gokai Galleon crew arrive and demand. However, Decade explains that their captain started this before the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Black_characters#Crisis_Empire" title="List of Kamen Rider Black characters">Crisis Fortress</a> appears overhead with Doktor G summoning <a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_X#G.O.D." title="Kamen Rider X">Apollo Geist</a>, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fangire#Bat" title="Fangire">Bat Fangire</a>, the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Blade_characters#Hajime_Aikawa" title="List of Kamen Rider Blade characters">Joker</a>, <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Black_characters#General_Jark" title="List of Kamen Rider Black characters">General Jark</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_ZO#Neo_Organism" title="Kamen Rider ZO">Neo Organism Doras</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Stronger#General_Shadow" title="Kamen Rider Stronger">General Shadow</a>, <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters#Llumu_Qhimil" title="List of Kamen Rider Decade characters">Llumu Qhimil</a>, and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Orphnoch#Orphnoch_King" title="Orphnoch">Arch Orphnoch</a> to weaken the Gokaigers so Decade kills Luka, Ahim and Gai as Joe and Don are forced to retreat.</p>
<p>Elsewhere, Brajira and various Goseiger monsters attack <a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_OOO" title="Kamen Rider OOO">Kamen Rider OOO</a> as the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_OOO_characters#Akira_Date" title="List of Kamen Rider OOO characters">Double</a> <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_OOO_characters#Shintaro_Goto" title="List of Kamen Rider OOO characters">Births</a> arrive to provide him back up before Silva arrives. Taking out the two Births while fighting Kamen Rider Diend, Gokai Red is about kill his opponent when OOO sacrifices himself to save the other Kamen Rider. Taking <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_OOO_characters#Hina_Izumi" title="List of Kamen Rider OOO characters">Hina</a> and the Core Medals on her person to the Crisis Fortress, Kaito confronts Tsukasa after he gave his forces the order to go after the Goseigers. Kaito manages to take Tsukasa's camera, refusing to support his friend as he and Hina are forced to retreat with <a href="/wiki/Birth_of_the_10th!_Kamen_Riders_All_Together!!#Badan_Empire" title="Birth of the 10th! Kamen Riders All Together!!">Tiger-Roid</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Super-1" title="Kamen Rider Super-1">Komathunder</a> sent after them as they run into Joe and Doc as they debate over their next course. At Hina's behest, Kaito becomes Diend to wipe out the Dai-Shocker monsters. Learning that he is a Kamen Rider, despite his affirmation he is on no one's side in the conflict, Joe attempts to take out his frustrations on Kaito as he reveals Marvelous's intentions while he spares Joe's life upon beating him. From there, Don offers that they all join forces to solve what is truly going on with Marvelous and Tsukasa.</p>
<p>Seeing that finding one of them is the only way to find answers, Kaito takes the others to Decade as he kills the Goseigers. Though they came only to talk, Joe is intent on fighting as Doras defeats him. However, Marvelous destroys the monster to save Joe and tells his first mate that this is for survival as only one group of heroes can exist. Telling Joe that Akarenger knows the true story, Marvelous tells him not to interfere as he confronts Tsukasa. With their amassed armies ordered to stand back and watch, Marvelous and Tsukasa transform and do battle. With both Gokai Red and Decade evenly matched, the fight ends in a stalemate with Joe attacking Kaito but lacking the will to kill him while admitting he does not want to see any more death. When they decide to find Akarenger, with Joe pointing out that he is dead, Kaito decides to buy them a ride on the DenLiner with the cup of pudding he stole from <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Den-O_characters#Momotaros" title="List of Kamen Rider Den-O characters">Momotaros</a> during their <a href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_%C3%97_Kamen_Rider_%C3%97_Kamen_Rider_The_Movie:_Cho-Den-O_Trilogy#Episode_Yellow" title="Kamen Rider × Kamen Rider × Kamen Rider The Movie: Cho-Den-O Trilogy">last meeting</a>. Arriving in the winter of 1976 to watch his battle with Baseball Mask, Akarenger reveals that he knows why they come and ask them to take him to their time as he is joined by the <a href="/wiki/Ky%C5%8Dry%C5%AB_Sentai_Zyuranger" title="Kyōryū Sentai Zyuranger">Zyurangers</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dai_Sentai_Goggle_V" title="Dai Sentai Goggle V">Goggle V</a>, <a href="/wiki/Denji_Sentai_Megaranger" title="Denji Sentai Megaranger">Megarangers</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dekarangers" title="Dekarangers">Dekarangers</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Choujuu_Sentai_Liveman" title="Choujuu Sentai Liveman">Livemen</a>. But <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_1" title="Kamen Rider 1">Kamen Rider 1</a> suddenly arrives with Kamen Riders <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Kuuga_(character)" title="Kamen Rider Kuuga (character)">Kuuga</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Faiz" title="Kamen Rider Faiz">Faiz</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Kabuto_(character)" title="Kamen Rider Kabuto (character)">Kabuto</a>, Kiva, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamen_Rider_Double_(character)" title="Kamen Rider Double (character)">Double</a> with a battle ensuing where only Akarenger and Kamen Rider 1 remain.</p>
<p>However, Akaranger and Kamen Rider 1 are revealed to be Gokai Red and Decade in disguise as both planned to flush out the remaining opposition and finally finish the other off. Mortified with Marvelous's drive to sacrifice their own, a betrayed Joe engages his captain with Kaito protecting him before being knocked out by Decade. With both Kaito and Joe taken out, Gokai Red and Decade resume their epic battle have been before Doktor G and Silva arrive and reveal that Dai-Shocker and Dai-Zangyack have been playing them both in order to remove any opposition to combining their fortresses into Big Machine to take over the world. However, Marvelous and Tsukasa knew of the treachery and played along while sending those they seemingly defeated into a dimensional rift until the time to spring their trap arrived. Joined by Marvelous's crew, OOO, and the real Akarenger and Kamen Rider 1 as they transform, Decade and Gokai Red are joined by Fourze, Meteor, the Go-Busters, and every other Kamen Rider and Super Sentai team to stand against the Shocker-Zangyack Alliance. However, as Go-Buster Oh is formed to fight the enlarged Warz and Akudos while he and Gokaigers defeats General Shadow, Decade is horrified to find a revenge-driven Kaito as he forms Big Machine to teach everyone a lesson as the mecha kills Akudos and Warz.</p>
<p>Entering Big Machine, Diend uses it on Go-Buster Oh with Fourze saving Yellow Buster. Back on Earth, the Gokaigers gain OOO Ranger Keys to take out Silva while Doktor G becomes Kani Laser to overpower the Kamen Riders before the Goseigers give Decade, Ryuki, and Blade their Tensou Cards to overpower the Destron monster prior to using their signature moves to finish him. However, Doktor G is revealed to be Narutaki as he leaves while telling Decade that he will never stop until he is dead. Back in space, using Saturn's gravity as a boost, Fourze installs the Drill Switch Super-3 on Go-Buster Oh to destroy Big Machine. Soon after, as Eiji and Hina are reunited, the Gokai Galleon crew watch Fourze befriending the Go-Busters. Marvelous then joins up with his crew sees the ultimate treasure being friendship between the Kamen Riders and the Super Sentai teams while accepting Eiji's friendship. As for Kaito, having survived the Big Machine's destruction but badly injured, gives Tsukasa back his camera and tells him to find his own path before accepting Tsukasa's friendship in his own way while leaving.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hh0312_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-hh0312-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-toei40_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-toei40-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Internet_spin-off_films">Internet spin-off films</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kamen_Rider_%C3%97_Super_Sentai:_Super_Hero_Taisen&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Internet spin-off films">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>To promote the movie, Toei released a series of Internet clips under the collective title <i>Net Movie: Kamen Rider × Super Sentai: Super Hero Taihen: Who Is the Culprit?!</i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">ネット版 仮面ライダー×スーパー戦隊 スーパーヒーロー大変 ～犯人はダレだ？！～</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Nettoban Kamen Raidā × Supā Sentai Supā Hīrō Taihen ~Hannin wa Dare da?!~</i>)</span> with a total of 10 webisodes. The web series is written by Shōji Yonemura and Shinichiro Shirakura, and directed by Hiroyuki Kato. <a href="/wiki/Toei_Company" title="Toei Company">Toei Tokusatsu BB</a> and TV Asahi began distribution on April 1, 2012 before releasing them on DVD on August 10, 2012. There are four different formats for the web movies:</p>
<ul>
<li>"Kamen Rider Murder Case, You're the Detective!" <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">仮面ライダー殺人事件 名探偵は君だ！</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kamen Raidā Satsujin Jiken Mei Tantei wa Kimi da!</i>)</span>: Kamen Rider Fourze investigates the deaths of Momotaros and Kamen Riders Decade and Double. Though thinking it was the action of a Super Sentai member, the culprit is revealed to be Eitoku who was attempting to kill Seiji Takaiwa and become the suit actor of the lead Kamen Riders.</li>
<li>"Super Sentai Murder Case, You're the Detective!" <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">スーパー戦隊殺人事件 名探偵は君だ！</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Sūpā Sentai Satsujin Jiken Mei Tantei wa Kimi da!</i>)</span>: The Go-Busters investigate the deaths of Gokai Pink, Gosei Pink, and Shinken Pink. Though thinking it was the action of a grasshopper based Kamen Riders, Kamen Rider OOO included as his Batta Medal was among the clues the pink Sentai heroines had on them, the culprit is revealed to be Hiroyuki Kato who killed them in a crime of passion.</li>
<li>"Special Ops Detective Buddy Go!" <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">特命探偵バディゴー！</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Tokumei Tantei Badi Gō!</i>)</span>: Finding himself in a predicament over a letter from a group and fearing he would be sent to the Dark Nebula if he fails, Hayami is taken by a Buddyloid to the Energy Management Center's hangar 07 where the robot's Go-Buster partner helps solve the mystery with humorous results.</li>
<li>"The Mature Narumi Detective Agency" <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">オトナの鳴海探偵事務所</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Otona no Narumi Tantei Jimusho</i>)</span>: <a href="/wiki/List_of_Kamen_Rider_Decade_characters#Narutaki" title="List of Kamen Rider Decade characters">Narutaki</a> of <i>Kamen Rider Decade</i> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Imagin#Urataros" title="Imagin">Urataros</a> of <i>Kamen Rider Den-O</i> form a detective agency to help answer the questions that <a href="/wiki/Gokaigers#Gai_Ikari" title="Gokaigers">Gokai Silver</a> of <i>Kaizoku Sentai Gokaiger</i> asks while providing serious background information about the Super Sentai and the Kamen Riders.</li>
</ul>
