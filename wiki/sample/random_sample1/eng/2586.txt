<p><i><b>A Dramatic Night</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">극적인 하룻밤</span>) is a 2015 South Korean <a href="/wiki/Romantic_comedy_film" title="Romantic comedy film">romantic comedy film</a> directed by Ha Ki-ho.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-variety_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-variety-1">[1]</a></sup> It was released on December 3, 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
