<p><b><a href="/wiki/Israel" title="Israel">Israel</a></b> competed at the <b><a href="/wiki/1972_Summer_Olympics" title="1972 Summer Olympics">1972 Summer Olympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Munich" title="Munich">Munich</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Germany" title="West Germany">West Germany</a>, which began on August 26. On September 5 and 6, in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Munich_Massacre" title="Munich Massacre">Munich Massacre</a>, 11 members of the Israeli delegation—5 athletes, 2 referees, and 4 coaches (names bolded on this page)—were taken hostage by PLO terrorists and murdered. The remainder of the team left Munich on September 7.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Shaul_Ladany" title="Shaul Ladany">Shaul Ladany</a>, a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Holocaust_survivor" title="Holocaust survivor">Holocaust survivor</a>, competed in the <a href="/wiki/Athletics_at_the_1972_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_50_kilometres_walk" title="Athletics at the 1972 Summer Olympics – Men's 50 kilometres walk">50-kilometer walk</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sports-reference1_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-sports-reference1-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-jewsinsports1_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-jewsinsports1-3">[3]</a></sup> He had been imprisoned in <a href="/wiki/Bergen-Belsen_concentration_camp" title="Bergen-Belsen concentration camp">Bergen-Belsen concentration camp</a> as a child, and wore a <a href="/wiki/Star_of_David" title="Star of David">Star of David</a> on his warm-up jersey.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-google2464_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-google2464-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-seattlepi1972_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-seattlepi1972-5">[5]</a></sup> When he was congratulated by locals on his fluent German, he responded: "I learned it in Bergen-Belsen".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-seattlepi1972_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-seattlepi1972-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> He survived the Munich massacre by jumping off a balcony.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sports-reference1_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-sports-reference1-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
