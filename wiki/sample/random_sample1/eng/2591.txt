<p><b>Gleb Sergeyevich Galperin</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Глеб Серге́евич Гальперин</span>) (born May 25, 1985 in <a href="/wiki/Moscow" title="Moscow">Moscow</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russian</a> <a href="/wiki/Diving" title="Diving">diver</a>. Competing in the <a href="/wiki/2008_Summer_Olympics" title="2008 Summer Olympics">2008 Summer Olympics</a>, he won a bronze medal in the <a href="/wiki/Diving_at_the_2008_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_synchronized_10_metre_platform" title="Diving at the 2008 Summer Olympics – Men's synchronized 10 metre platform">men's synchronized 10 metre platform</a> with teammate <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dmitriy_Dobroskok" title="Dmitriy Dobroskok">Dmitriy Dobroskok</a>, and also a bronze medal in the individual <a href="/wiki/Diving_at_the_2008_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_10_metre_platform" title="Diving at the 2008 Summer Olympics – Men's 10 metre platform">men's 10 metre platform</a> event. He also competed in the <a href="/wiki/2004_Summer_Olympics" title="2004 Summer Olympics">2004 Summer Olympics</a> and <a href="/wiki/2012_Summer_Olympics" title="2012 Summer Olympics">2012 Summer Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
