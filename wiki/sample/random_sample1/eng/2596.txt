<p><b>Glodeni</b> (<small>Romanian pronunciation: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Romanian" title="Help:IPA for Romanian">[ɡloˈdenʲ]</a></span>; <a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Глодяны</span>) is a city in the northwest of <a href="/wiki/Moldova" title="Moldova">Moldova</a>; it is the seat of <a href="/wiki/Glodeni_District" title="Glodeni District">Glodeni District</a>. Its population as of 2012 was 11,600. One village, Stîrcea, is administered by the city.</p>
