<p>The <b>1973 CONCACAF Championship</b>, the sixth edition of the <a href="/wiki/CONCACAF_Championship" title="CONCACAF Championship">CONCACAF Championship</a>, was held in <a href="/wiki/Haiti" title="Haiti">Haiti</a> from 29 November to 18 December. All matches were played at <a href="/wiki/Stade_Sylvio_Cator" title="Stade Sylvio Cator">Stade Sylvio Cator</a> in <a href="/wiki/Port-au-Prince" title="Port-au-Prince">Port-au-Prince</a>. This is the first edition to double as qualification for the World Cup. Haiti became winners for the first time in the CONCACAF region and qualified for <a href="/wiki/1974_FIFA_World_Cup" title="1974 FIFA World Cup">West Germany '74</a>. The North, Central American and Caribbean zone was allocated 1 place (out of 16) in the World Cup.</p>
