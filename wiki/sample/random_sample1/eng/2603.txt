<p><b>Gliese 1002</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Red_dwarf" title="Red dwarf">red dwarf</a> star. It is located relatively near our <a href="/wiki/Sun" title="Sun">Sun</a>, at a distance of 15.31 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Light_year" title="Light year">light years</a>, in the constellation <a href="/wiki/Cetus" title="Cetus">Cetus</a>.</p>
<p>This appears to be a relatively quiescent star, and no <a href="/wiki/Flare_star" title="Flare star">flare activity</a> has been detected.</p>
