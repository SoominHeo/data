<p><b>HD 50281</b> (also known as <b>Gliese 250</b>) is a <a href="/wiki/Binary_star" title="Binary star">binary star</a> system with a relatively high <a href="/wiki/Proper_motion" title="Proper motion">proper motion</a>. The primary star is a <a href="/wiki/Main_sequence" title="Main sequence">main sequence</a> orange dwarf star, while the smaller companion is a <a href="/wiki/Red_dwarf" title="Red dwarf">red dwarf</a>. The two stars are separated by 58 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arcsecond" title="Arcsecond">arcsecs</a>, which, at the estimated distance, is equivalent to about 500 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Astronomical_Unit" title="Astronomical Unit">AUs</a>.</p>
