<p><b>Functionalism</b> may refer to:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Functionalism_(architecture)" title="Functionalism (architecture)">Functionalism (architecture)</a>, the principle that architects should design a building based on the purpose of that building</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Functionalism_in_international_relations" title="Functionalism in international relations">Functionalism in international relations</a>, a theory that arose during the inter-War period</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Functionalism_(philosophy_of_mind)" title="Functionalism (philosophy of mind)">Functionalism (philosophy of mind)</a>, a theory of the mind in contemporary philosophy</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Functionalism_versus_intentionalism" title="Functionalism versus intentionalism">Functionalism versus intentionalism</a>, a historiographical debate about the origins of the Holocaust</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Structural_functionalism" title="Structural functionalism">Structural functionalism</a>, a theoretical tradition within sociology and anthropology</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Biological_functionalism" title="Biological functionalism">Biological functionalism</a>, an anthropological paradigm</li>
</ul>
