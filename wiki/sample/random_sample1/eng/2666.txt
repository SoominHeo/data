<p>The <b>Winnowing Basket</b> mansion (箕宿, <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: Jī Xiù) is one of the <a href="/wiki/Twenty-Eight_Mansions" title="Twenty-Eight Mansions">Twenty-Eight Mansions</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Chinese_constellations" title="Chinese constellations">Chinese constellations</a>. It is one of the eastern mansions of the <a href="/wiki/Azure_Dragon" title="Azure Dragon">Azure Dragon</a>.</p>
