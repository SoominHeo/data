<p><b>Mamoru Kishida</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">岸田 護</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kishida Mamoru</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born May 10, 1981 in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Suita" title="Suita">Suita</a>, <a href="/wiki/Osaka" title="Osaka">Osaka</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/Japanese_people" title="Japanese people">Japanese</a> professional <a href="/wiki/Baseball" title="Baseball">baseball</a> <a href="/wiki/Starting_pitcher" title="Starting pitcher">starting pitcher</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Orix_Buffaloes" title="Orix Buffaloes">Orix Buffaloes</a> in Japan's <a href="/wiki/Nippon_Professional_Baseball" title="Nippon Professional Baseball">Nippon Professional Baseball</a>. He made his debut in <a href="/wiki/2006_in_baseball" title="2006 in baseball">2006</a> and wears the uniform number 14.</p>
