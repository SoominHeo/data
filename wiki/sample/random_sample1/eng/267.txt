<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Diving at the 1976 Summer Olympics</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>At the <b><a href="/wiki/1976_Summer_Olympics" title="1976 Summer Olympics">1976 Summer Olympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Montreal" title="Montreal">Montreal</a>, four <b><a href="/wiki/Diving" title="Diving">diving</a></b> events were contested during a competition that took place at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Olympic_Pool_(Montreal)" title="Olympic Pool (Montreal)">Montréal Olympic Pool</a>, from 19 to 27 July (July 24, rest day), comprising 82 divers from 22 nations.</p>
