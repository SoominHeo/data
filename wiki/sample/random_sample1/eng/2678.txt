<p><b>Giorgi Asanidze</b> (<a href="/wiki/Georgian_language" title="Georgian language">Georgian</a>: <span lang="ka" xml:lang="ka">გიორგი ასანიძე</span>; born August 30, 1975 in <a href="/wiki/Sachkhere" title="Sachkhere">Sachkhere</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/Georgia_(country)" title="Georgia (country)">Georgian</a> <a href="/wiki/Olympic_weightlifting" title="Olympic weightlifting">weightlifter</a> who competed in the men's 85 kg at the <a href="/wiki/Weightlifting_at_the_2000_Summer_Olympics" title="Weightlifting at the 2000 Summer Olympics">2000 Summer Olympics</a> (where he won a bronze medal) and <a href="/wiki/Weightlifting_at_the_2004_Summer_Olympics" title="Weightlifting at the 2004 Summer Olympics">2004 Summer Olympics</a> (where he won a gold medal).</p>
<p>He was elected to the <a href="/wiki/Parliament_of_Georgia" title="Parliament of Georgia">Parliament of Georgia</a> from the <a href="/wiki/United_National_Movement_(Georgia)" title="United National Movement (Georgia)">United National Movement</a> in 2004. He was reelected in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Georgian_legislative_election,_2008" title="Georgian legislative election, 2008">May 2008 Parliamentary election</a> from <a href="/wiki/Tbilisi" title="Tbilisi">Tbilisi</a>’s Gldani constituency on the same party ticket.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
