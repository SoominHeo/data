<p><br/>
Year <b>77 BC</b> was a year of the <a href="/wiki/Roman_calendar" title="Roman calendar">pre-Julian Roman calendar</a>. At the time it was known as the <b>Year of the Consulship of Brutus and Lepidus</b> (or, less frequently, <b>year 677 <i><a href="/wiki/Ab_urbe_condita" title="Ab urbe condita">Ab urbe condita</a></i></b>). The denomination 77 BC for this year has been used since the early medieval period, when the <a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a> <a href="/wiki/Calendar_era" title="Calendar era">calendar era</a> became the prevalent method in Europe for naming years.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=77_BC&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Roman_Republic">Roman Republic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=77_BC&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Roman Republic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Aemilius_Lepidus_(consul_78_BC)" title="Marcus Aemilius Lepidus (consul 78 BC)">Marcus Aemilius Lepidus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Roman_consul" title="Roman consul">Roman consul</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Leader" title="Leader">leader</a> of the democratic party, is defeated by <a href="/wiki/Quintus_Lutatius_Catulus_(Capitolinus)" title="Quintus Lutatius Catulus (Capitolinus)">Quintus Lutatius Catulus</a> outside <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a>. The remnants of the rebels are wiped out by <a href="/wiki/Pompey" title="Pompey">Gnaeus Pompeius</a> in <a href="/wiki/Etruria" title="Etruria">Etruria</a>.</li>
<li>Lepidus, with some 21,000 troops, manages to escape to <a href="/wiki/Sardinia" title="Sardinia">Sardinia</a>. Soon afterwards he becomes ill and dies, his battered army, now under command by <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Perperna" title="Marcus Perperna">Marcus Perperna</a>, sails on to the <a href="/wiki/Iberian_Peninsula" title="Iberian Peninsula">Iberian Peninsula</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li>Pompeius marches along the <i><a href="/wiki/Via_Domitia" title="Via Domitia">Via Domitia</a></i> through <a href="/wiki/Gallia_Narbonensis" title="Gallia Narbonensis">Gallia Narbonensis</a> crossing the <a href="/wiki/Pyrenees" title="Pyrenees">Pyrenees</a> to <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>. He joins with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Quintus_Metellus_Pius" title="Quintus Metellus Pius">Quintus Metellus Pius</a> to suppress the revolt of <a href="/wiki/Quintus_Sertorius" title="Quintus Sertorius">Quintus Sertorius</a>, but is at first unsuccessful.</li>
</ul>
