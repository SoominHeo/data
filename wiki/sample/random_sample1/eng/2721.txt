<p><b>Giza Studio</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">ギザ ステューディオ</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Giza Suteyudio</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> <a href="/wiki/Record_label" title="Record label">record label</a>.</p>
<p>It started as an indie label in the early 1990s under the name <b>G</b>arage <b>I</b>ndies <b>Z</b>apping <b>A</b>ssociation, but soon <a href="/wiki/Being_Inc." title="Being Inc.">Being Inc.</a> bought it as their sister label company. Giza Studio is located in <a href="/wiki/Osaka" title="Osaka">Osaka</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a> and has been producing successful artists such as <a href="/wiki/Mai_Kuraki" title="Mai Kuraki">Mai Kuraki</a>, <a href="/wiki/Garnet_Crow" title="Garnet Crow">Garnet Crow</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rina_Aiuchi" title="Rina Aiuchi">Rina Aiuchi</a>, etc. since their commercial service on September 1, 1998. Along with other Being Group's sister labels (Zain Records, Rooms Records, B-Gram Records, BMFC, Vermillion Records and Northern Music), the studio's main task is to create music productivity by hiring talented artists, composers and engineers. The label's creative design (such as CD-jacket, photography, cinematography) is handled by Mod's House, while J-Disc is in charge of all the distribution and promotion of the sales.</p>
<p>Giza's artists have their songs made opening and ending themes frequently for <a href="/wiki/Anime" title="Anime">anime</a> like <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Case_Closed_(manga)" title="Case Closed (manga)">Meitantei Conan</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tantei_Gakuen_Q" title="Tantei Gakuen Q">Tantei Gakuen Q</a>.</p>
<p>Sub-labels include <b>D-Go</b> (<a href="/wiki/Rock_music" title="Rock music">rock music</a>) and <b>Giza Jazz</b> (<a href="/wiki/Jazz" title="Jazz">jazz</a>). It also owns an <a href="/wiki/Independent_record_label" title="Independent record label">indie imprint</a>, <b><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Tent_House_(record_label)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Tent House (record label) (page does not exist)">Tent House</a></b>.</p>
