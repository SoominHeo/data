<p>The <b>Kitakyushu Monorail</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">北九州モノレール</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kitakyūshū monorēru</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/Monorail" title="Monorail">monorail</a> system in the city of <a href="/wiki/Kitakyushu" title="Kitakyushu">Kitakyushu</a> in <a href="/wiki/Fukuoka_Prefecture" title="Fukuoka Prefecture">Fukuoka Prefecture</a>, Japan, operated by Kitakyushu Urban Monorail Co. Ltd. <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">北九州高速鉄道株式会社</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kitakyūshū Kōsoku Tetsudō Kabushiki-gaisha</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, literally "Kitakyūshū Rapid Transit Co. Ltd.")</span>.</p>
<p>The <b>Kokura Line</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">小倉線</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kokura-sen</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is the only line. It runs the 8.8 km (5.5 mi) between <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kokura_station" title="Kokura station">Kokura station</a> and Kikugaoka station in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kokura_Minami_ward" title="Kokura Minami ward">Kokura Minami ward</a> in about 18 minutes. It was opened on January 9, 1985, between the station now called Heiwadori and Kikugaoka, and extended about 300 metres (1000 ft) to Kokura station from Heiwadori on April 1, 1998.</p>
<p>The monorail is owned by the city of Kitakyushu, and did not begin to make a profit until the extension to Kokura station was completed. This had been resisted by the businesses in Uomachi shopping district who feared they would lose customers, but that did not happen.</p>
<p>Once a year, there are beer parties and wine parties held on the monorail, bookings made in advance. <a href="/wiki/Santa_Claus" title="Santa Claus">Santa Claus</a> boards the train on December 24.</p>
