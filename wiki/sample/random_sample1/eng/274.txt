<p><i>Cantonale</i> elections to renew <a href="/wiki/Cantons_of_France" title="Cantons of France">canton</a> general councillors were held in France on 14 and 21 March 1982. The left, in power since 1981, lost 8 and 98 seats to the right, which controlled 59 presidencies out of 95. The Socialists only lost 10 seats, but the Communists lost 45.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Electoral_system">Electoral system</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=French_cantonal_elections,_1982&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Electoral system">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The cantonales elections use the same system as the regional or legislative elections. There is a 10% threshold (10% of <i>registered</i> voters) needed to proceed to the second round.</p>
