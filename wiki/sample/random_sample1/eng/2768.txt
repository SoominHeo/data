<p><b>Kim Gwang-seok</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko" xml:lang="ko">김광석</span>; born 12 February 1983) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> player who since 2007 has played for <a href="/wiki/Pohang_Steelers" title="Pohang Steelers">Pohang Steelers</a>.</p>
