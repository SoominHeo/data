<p><b>Paul Geun-Sang Kim</b> (also <b>Keun-Sang</b> and similar; born 1952) is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Anglican" title="Anglican">Anglican</a> bishop of the Diocese of <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Primate_(bishop)" title="Primate (bishop)">Primate</a> of the Province of Korea.</p>
<p>He was ordained in 1980 and was elected bishop in January 2008, to take the place of the soon-to-retire, Archbishop <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Francis_Kyung_Jo_Park&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Francis Kyung Jo Park (page does not exist)">Francis Kyung Jo Park</a>. He was consecrated on May 27, 2008 and succeeded Archbishop Francis on January 15, 2009. In 2010, to succeed the Primate of Korea, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Solomon_Jong_Mo_Yoon&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Solomon Jong Mo Yoon (page does not exist)">Solomon Jong Mo Yoon</a>, he was elected Primate of the Province of Korea.</p>
