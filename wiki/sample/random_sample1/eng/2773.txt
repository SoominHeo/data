<p><b>Kim Geun-Chul</b> (born June 24, 1983) is <a href="/wiki/South_Korean" title="South Korean">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays for <a href="/wiki/PTT_Rayong_F.C." title="PTT Rayong F.C.">PTT Rayong</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Thai_Division_1_League" title="Thai Division 1 League">Thai Division 1 League</a>. He was regarded as one of the decent young midfielders. And he had a competitive relationship with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Daejeon_Citizen" title="Daejeon Citizen">Daejeon Citizen</a> midfielder <a href="/wiki/Kwon_Jip" title="Kwon Jip">Kwon Jip</a>, who had a role of playmaker in Dongbuk High school.</p>
<p>Kim previously played for <a href="/wiki/Shonan_Bellmare" title="Shonan Bellmare">Shonan Bellmare</a> in the <a href="/wiki/J2_League" title="J2 League">J2 League</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
