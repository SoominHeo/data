<p><b>Kim Dam</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">hangul</a>:김담, <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">hanja</a>:金淡, 1416 - 1464) was <a href="/wiki/Korea" title="Korea">Korean</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Joseon_Dynasty" title="Joseon Dynasty">Joseon Dynasty</a>'s a politician, astronomer, scientist. His nickname was Musongheon (무송헌, 撫松軒). He contributed to <a href="/wiki/Korea" title="Korea">Korean</a> literature, geography, wolryeok<sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="margin-left:0.1em; white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Please_clarify" title="Wikipedia:Please clarify"><span title="The text near this tag may need clarification or removal of jargon. (November 2011)">clarification needed</span></a></i>]</sup> calculations.</p>
