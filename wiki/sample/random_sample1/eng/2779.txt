<p><b>Kim Dong-young</b> (born 1988) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> actor. He starred in films such as <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=The_Last_Ride_(2016_film)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="The Last Ride (2016 film) (page does not exist)">The Last Ride</a></i> (2016) and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Age_of_Shadows" title="The Age of Shadows">The Age of Shadows</a></i> (2016), also television drama <a href="/wiki/Drinking_Solo" title="Drinking Solo">Drinking Solo</a> (2016).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
