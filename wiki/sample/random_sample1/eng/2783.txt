<p><b>Kim Min-seok</b> (born January 24, 1990) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> actor. He had supporting roles in television series such as <i><a href="/wiki/Shut_Up_Flower_Boy_Band" title="Shut Up Flower Boy Band">Shut Up Flower Boy Band</a></i> (2012) and <i><a href="/wiki/Who_Are_You:_School_2015" title="Who Are You: School 2015">Who Are You: School 2015</a></i> (2015) and in 2016 featured in <i><a href="/wiki/Descendants_of_the_Sun" title="Descendants of the Sun">Descendants of the Sun</a></i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and <i><a href="/wiki/Doctor_Crush" title="Doctor Crush">Doctor Crush</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
