<p><b>Kim Byeong-Jun</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 김병준, <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">Hanja</a>: 金秉俊) (born February 8, 1988 in <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Short_track_speed_skating" title="Short track speed skating">short track speed skater</a>.</p>
