<p><b><a href="/wiki/Colombia" title="Colombia">Colombia</a></b> competed at the <b><a href="/wiki/1984_Summer_Olympics" title="1984 Summer Olympics">1984 Summer Olympics</a></b> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Los_Angeles,_California" title="Los Angeles, California">Los Angeles</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>. 39 competitors, 36 men and 3 women, took part in 34 events in 8 sports.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sports-reference_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-sports-reference-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
