<p><b>Kim Seon-il</b>, sometimes spelled as <b>Kim Sun-il</b> (born 11 June 1985) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">defender</a>.</p>
<p>Playing as central defender, or left-back, he came to Serbia in summer 2011<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> after having played with <a href="/wiki/Suwon_Samsung_Bluewings" title="Suwon Samsung Bluewings">Suwon Samsung Bluewings</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/K-League" title="K-League">K-League</a> and winning the <a href="/wiki/2010_Korean_FA_Cup" title="2010 Korean FA Cup">2010 Korean FA Cup</a> with them.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-soccerway_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-soccerway-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Kim Seon-il played with <a href="/wiki/Dongguk_University" title="Dongguk University">Dongguk University</a> in 2008<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> before moving to Bluewings at the end of the year.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>He made his league debut with <a href="/wiki/FK_Radni%C4%8Dki_Ni%C5%A1" title="FK Radnički Niš">FK Radnički Niš</a> on October 1, 2011, in a <a href="/wiki/Serbian_First_League" title="Serbian First League">Serbian First League</a> round 8 match against <a href="/wiki/FK_Donji_Srem" title="FK Donji Srem">FK Donji Srem</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-soccerway_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-soccerway-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>He is a former member of the <a href="/wiki/South_Korea_national_under-20_football_team" title="South Korea national under-20 football team">South Korea national under-20 football team</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (April 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
