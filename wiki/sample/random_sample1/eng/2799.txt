<p><b>Kim Soo-yun</b> (born 30 August 1989) is a South Korean <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> player for <a href="/wiki/Hwacheon_KSPO_WFC" title="Hwacheon KSPO WFC">Hwacheon KSPO WFC</a> and the <a href="/wiki/South_Korea_women%27s_national_football_team" title="South Korea women's national football team">South Korean national team</a>. She participated at the <a href="/wiki/2015_FIFA_Women%27s_World_Cup" title="2015 FIFA Women's World Cup">2015 FIFA Women's World Cup</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
