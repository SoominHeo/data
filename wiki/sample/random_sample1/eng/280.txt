<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Mascot">Mascot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1985_Southeast_Asian_Games&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Mascot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="floatright"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:1985_Seagames_mascot.jpg"><img alt="1985 Seagames mascot.jpg" data-file-height="205" data-file-width="123" height="205" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/1/1d/1985_Seagames_mascot.jpg" width="123"/></a></div>
<p>The official 1985 SEA Games mascot was a <a href="/wiki/Siamese_cat" title="Siamese cat">Siamese cat</a>. The Siamese is one of the first distinctly recognised breeds of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Oriental_cat" title="Oriental cat">Oriental cat</a>. It is called Wichien-maat (<a href="/wiki/Thai_language" title="Thai language">Thai</a>: <span lang="th" xml:lang="th">วิเชียรมาศ</span>) in Thailand.</p>
