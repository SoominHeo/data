<p><b>Kim Shin</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">김신</span>; born 30 March 1995) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Forward_(association_football)" title="Forward (association football)">forward</a> for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chungju_Hummel" title="Chungju Hummel">Chungju Hummel</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kim_Shin&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kim joined <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jeonbuk_Hyundai" title="Jeonbuk Hyundai">Jeonbuk Hyundai</a> in 2014 and made his debut in the <a href="/wiki/2014_AFC_Champions_League" title="2014 AFC Champions League">2014 AFC Champions League</a> match against <a href="/wiki/Yokohama_F._Marinos" title="Yokohama F. Marinos">Yokohama F. Marinos</a> on 15 April.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> He penned a 2-year loan contract with <a href="/wiki/Ligue_1" title="Ligue 1">Ligue 1</a> side <a href="/wiki/Olympique_Lyonnais" title="Olympique Lyonnais">Olympique Lyonnais</a> in summer 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
