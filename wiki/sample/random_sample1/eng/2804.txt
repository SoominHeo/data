<p><b>Kim A-joong</b> (born October 16, 1982) is a South Korean <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Actress" title="Actress">actress</a>, <a href="/wiki/Model_(person)" title="Model (person)">model</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Singer" title="Singer">singer</a>. She graduated from <a href="/wiki/Dongduk_Women%27s_University" title="Dongduk Women's University">Dongduk Women's University</a> and attended <a href="/wiki/Korea_University" title="Korea University">Korea University</a> and the <a href="/wiki/International_Hotel_Management_Institute_Switzerland" title="International Hotel Management Institute Switzerland">International Hotel Management Institute Switzerland</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> She is best known for playing the main character in the 2006 critically acclaimed romance comedy <i><a href="/wiki/200_Pounds_Beauty" title="200 Pounds Beauty">200 Pounds Beauty</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kim_Ah-joong&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kim Ah-joong is a well-known Korean actress/model/singer who rose to stardom from her roles in numerous films and commercials. She is best known for her role as a large girl (Han-na) who undergoes cosmetic surgery makeover to become a pop sensation in the highly successful 2006 South Korean comedy movie <i><a href="/wiki/200_Pounds_Beauty" title="200 Pounds Beauty">200 Pounds Beauty</a></i>. For this role, she was awarded Best Actress in the 2007 <a href="/wiki/Grand_Bell_Awards" title="Grand Bell Awards">Grand Bell Awards</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> She did her own singing in the movie, including "Beautiful Girl" in the opening credits music and the Korean version of the song "<a href="/wiki/Maria_(Blondie_song)" title="Maria (Blondie song)">Maria</a>".</p>
<p>Prior to that, she appeared in a few other TV series including the popular <i><a href="/wiki/Bizarre_Bunch" title="Bizarre Bunch">Bizarre Bunch</a></i> in 2005 by <a href="/wiki/Korean_Broadcasting_System" title="Korean Broadcasting System">KBS</a>.</p>
<p>In December 2012, Kim co-starred with <a href="/wiki/Ji_Sung" title="Ji Sung">Ji Sung</a> in romantic comedy film <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/My_PS_Partner" title="My PS Partner">My PS Partner</a></i> as Yoon-jung. It is about a woman who in an attempt to spice up her five-year relationship, tries to have phone sex with her boyfriend but accidentally gets Hyun Seung on the line instead.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>She joined Kim Rae-won in SBS television series <i><a href="/wiki/Punch_(2014_TV_series)" title="Punch (2014 TV series)">Punch</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2016, she was the main female lead for SBS drama, Wanted.</p>
