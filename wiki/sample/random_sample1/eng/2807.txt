<p><b>Kim Young-hee</b> (born 17 May 1963) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> former <a href="/wiki/Basketball" title="Basketball">basketball</a> player who competed in the <a href="/wiki/1984_Summer_Olympics" title="1984 Summer Olympics">1984 Summer Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sref_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-sref-1">[1]</a></sup> Born in <a href="/wiki/Ulsan" title="Ulsan">Ulsan</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gyeongsangnam-do" title="Gyeongsangnam-do">Gyeongsangnam-do</a>, Kim is the elder of two children, with a younger brother.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
