<p><b>Kim Eun-Sun</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">김은선</span>; <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">Hanja</a>: 金恩宣; born 30 March 1988) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as <a href="/wiki/Midfielder" title="Midfielder">midfielder</a> for <a href="/wiki/Suwon_Samsung_Bluewings" title="Suwon Samsung Bluewings">Suwon Samsung Bluewings</a> in the <a href="/wiki/K_League_Classic" title="K League Classic">K League Classic</a>.</p>
