<p><b>Kim Igyo</b> (1764–1832) was a scholar-official and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Uuijeong&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Uuijeong (page does not exist)">Uuijeong</a> of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Joseon_Dynasty" title="Joseon Dynasty">Joseon Dynasty</a> <a href="/wiki/Korea" title="Korea">Korea</a>.</p>
<p>He was also <a href="/wiki/Diplomat" title="Diplomat">diplomat</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ambassador" title="Ambassador">ambassador</a>, representing <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Joseon_Dynasty" title="Joseon Dynasty">Joseon</a> interests in the 12th <a href="/wiki/Edo_period" title="Edo period">Edo period</a> diplomatic mission to the <a href="/wiki/Tokugawa_shogunate" title="Tokugawa shogunate">Tokugawa shogunate</a> in Japan.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="1811_mission_to_Japan">1811 mission to Japan</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kim_Igyo&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: 1811 mission to Japan">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kim Igyo was the leader selected by <a href="/wiki/Sunjo_of_Joseon" title="Sunjo of Joseon">Sunjo of Joseon</a> to head a mission to Japan in 1811.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> This diplomatic mission functioned to the advantage of both the Japanese and the Koreans as a channel for maintainining a political foundation for trade.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>This delegation was explicitly identified by the Joseon court as a "Communication Envoy" (<i>tongsinsa</i>). The mission was understood to signify that relations were "normalized."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>The Joseon monarch's ambassador and retinue traveled only as far as <a href="/wiki/Tsushima_Island" title="Tsushima Island">Tsushima</a>. The representatives of <a href="/wiki/Shogun" title="Shogun">Shogun</a> <a href="/wiki/Tokugawa_Ienari" title="Tokugawa Ienari">Ienari</a> met the mission on the island which is located in the middle of the <a href="/wiki/Korea_Strait" title="Korea Strait">Korea Strait</a> between the <a href="/wiki/Korean_Peninsula" title="Korean Peninsula">Korean Peninsula</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kyushu" title="Kyushu">Kyushu</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
