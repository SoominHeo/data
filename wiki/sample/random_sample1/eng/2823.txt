<p><b>Kim Jung-hyun</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">김정현</span>; born June 28, 1976) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> actor. He starred in TV series such as <i><a href="/wiki/Gwanggaeto,_The_Great_Conqueror" title="Gwanggaeto, The Great Conqueror">Gwanggaeto, The Great Conqueror</a></i> (2011), <i><a href="/wiki/Dangerous_Women_(2011_TV_series)" title="Dangerous Women (2011 TV series)">Dangerous Women</a></i> (2011), <i><a href="/wiki/My_Lover,_Madame_Butterfly" title="My Lover, Madame Butterfly">My Lover, Madame Butterfly</a></i> (2012), <i><a href="/wiki/Empress_Ki_(TV_series)" title="Empress Ki (TV series)">Empress Ki</a></i> (2013) and <i><a href="/wiki/My_Mother_is_a_Daughter-in-law" title="My Mother is a Daughter-in-law">My Mother is a Daughter-in-law</a></i> (2015).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
