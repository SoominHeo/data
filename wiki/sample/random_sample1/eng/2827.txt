<p><b>Kim Ji-hyun</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 김지현; born August 16, 1972) is a singer and actress. She is the member of the popular 90's Korean pop group, <a href="/wiki/Roo%27ra" title="Roo'ra">Roo'ra</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The group was active from 1994 to 2001. The group is currently involved in various concerts with other 90's singers.</p>
