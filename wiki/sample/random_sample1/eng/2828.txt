<p><b>Kim Ji-hoon</b> (born 9 August 1984) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Gymnastics" title="Gymnastics">gymnast</a>. Kim was part of the South Korean team that won the bronze medal in the team event at the <a href="/wiki/Gymnastics_at_the_2006_Asian_Games" title="Gymnastics at the 2006 Asian Games">2006 Asian Games</a>. He also competed at the <a href="/wiki/Gymnastics_at_the_2008_Summer_Olympics" title="Gymnastics at the 2008 Summer Olympics">2008 Summer Olympics</a> and the <a href="/wiki/2012_Summer_Olympics" title="2012 Summer Olympics">2012 Summer Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
