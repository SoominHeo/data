<p><b>Kim Cha-Youn</b> (born February 10, 1981) is a female <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Team_handball" title="Team handball">handball</a> player who competed in the <a href="/wiki/2004_Summer_Olympics" title="2004 Summer Olympics">2004 Summer Olympics</a>.</p>
<p>In 2004 she won the silver medal with the South Korean team. She played all seven matches and scored 19 goals.</p>
