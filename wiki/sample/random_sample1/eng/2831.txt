<p><b>Kim Chul-soo</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: 김철수; born July 6, 1952 <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>) is a former <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">Defender</a>.</p>
<p>He started professional career at <a href="/wiki/Pohang_Steelers" title="Pohang Steelers">POSCO FC</a> in 1975. He was squad of <a href="/wiki/South_Korea_national_football_team" title="South Korea national football team">South Korea national football team</a> in <a href="/wiki/1976_AFC_Asian_Cup_qualification" title="1976 AFC Asian Cup qualification">1976 AFC Asian Cup qualification</a>.</p>
<p>He taught <a href="/wiki/Lee_Young-pyo" title="Lee Young-pyo">Lee Young-pyo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Park_Ji-sung" title="Park Ji-sung">Park Ji-sung</a> in elementary School.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>He started abroad coaching career in <a href="/wiki/Philippines" title="Philippines">Philippines</a> since 2006.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
