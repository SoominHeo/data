<p><b>Kim Tae-woo</b> (born 15 April 1971) is a South Korean actor. After his breakthrough in blockbuster <a href="/wiki/War_film" title="War film">war film</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Joint_Security_Area_(film)" title="Joint Security Area (film)">Joint Security Area</a></i>, Kim became best known for his leading roles in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arthouse_film" title="Arthouse film">arthouse films</a>, such as those directed by <a href="/wiki/Hong_Sang-soo" title="Hong Sang-soo">Hong Sang-soo</a>, namely <i><a href="/wiki/Woman_Is_the_Future_of_Man" title="Woman Is the Future of Man">Woman Is the Future of Man</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Woman_on_the_Beach" title="Woman on the Beach">Woman on the Beach</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Like_You_Know_It_All" title="Like You Know It All">Like You Know It All</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
