<p><b>Kim Tae-Ho</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">김태호</span>; born 22 September 1989) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)#Full_Back" title="Defender (association football)">full back</a> for <a href="/wiki/FC_Anyang" title="FC Anyang">FC Anyang</a> in <a href="/wiki/K_League_Challenge" title="K League Challenge">K League Challenge</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kim_Tae-ho_(footballer)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was selected by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chunnam_Dragons" title="Chunnam Dragons">Chunnam Dragons</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2013_K_League_Classic" title="2013 K League Classic">2013 K League</a> draft.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> He made his debut in the league match against <a href="/wiki/Daegu_FC" title="Daegu FC">Daegu FC</a> on 10 March 2013.</p>
