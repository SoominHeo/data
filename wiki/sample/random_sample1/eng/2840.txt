<p><b>Kim Hae-Woon</b> (born December 25, 1973) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> former <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> <a href="/wiki/Goalkeeper_(association_football)" title="Goalkeeper (association football)">goalkeeper</a> and currently goalkeeper coach of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Henan_Construction" title="Henan Construction">Henan Construction</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kim_Hae-woon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kim has represented South Korea at youth level but, although part of the <a href="/wiki/South_Korea_national_football_team" title="South Korea national football team">senior team</a> squads (e.g. he was the third choice goalkeeper for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/East_Asian_Cup_2003" title="East Asian Cup 2003">2003 East Asian Cup</a>) he never went on to play a match for them.</p>
<p>In 1996 he made his debut for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seongnam_Ilhwa_Chunma" title="Seongnam Ilhwa Chunma">Ilhwa Chunma</a> (then based in <a href="/wiki/Cheonan" title="Cheonan">Cheonan</a> and known as <i>Cheonan Ilhwa Chunma</i>). Before the start of the 2000 season, Kim, together with the entire club moved to <a href="/wiki/Seongnam" title="Seongnam">Seongnam</a>. The move was highly successful and the club (now known as <i>Seongnam Ilhwa Chunma</i>) took over from <a href="/wiki/Suwon_Samsung_Bluewings" title="Suwon Samsung Bluewings">Suwon Samsung Bluewings</a> as the dominant force in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/K-league" title="K-league">Korean soccer</a>.</p>
<p>By the end of the 2007 season, Kim had played 197 times for the Ilhwa Chunma franchise and in that time conceded 212 goals.</p>
<p>On 23 May 2009, He announced his retirement.</p>
<p>He is appointed to the U-18 team goalkeeper coach of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jeonbuk_Hyundai_Motors" title="Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors">Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors</a> on 3 March 2010.</p>
