<p><b>Kim Hyun-Soo</b> (born March 13, 1973) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> former <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">footballer</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)#Centre_back" title="Defender (association football)">center back</a>. He is currently the First Team coach of <a href="/wiki/Daegu_FC" title="Daegu FC">Daegu FC</a>.</p>
<p>Kim started club career with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pusan_Daewoo_Royals" title="Pusan Daewoo Royals">Pusan Daewoo Royals</a> and was part of the <a href="/wiki/South_Korea_national_football_team" title="South Korea national football team">South Korea national football team</a>. He played at <a href="/wiki/1998_FIFA_World_Cup_qualification" title="1998 FIFA World Cup qualification">1998 FIFA World Cup qualification</a>.</p>
