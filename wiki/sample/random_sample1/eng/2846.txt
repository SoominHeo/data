<p><b>Gitdaebong</b> is a mountain of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jeollanam-do" title="Jeollanam-do">Jeollanam-do</a>, southwestern <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. It has an elevation of 368 metres.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
