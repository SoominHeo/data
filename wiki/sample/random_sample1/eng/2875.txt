<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Nagai Park</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Nagai Park</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">長居公園</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Nagai kōen</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a large park located in <a href="/wiki/Higashisumiyoshi-ku,_Osaka" title="Higashisumiyoshi-ku, Osaka">Higashisumiyoshi-ku</a>, <a href="/wiki/Osaka" title="Osaka">Osaka</a>. Its facilities include three multipurpose sports stadia, including 50,000-seat <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nagai_Stadium" title="Nagai Stadium">Nagai Stadium</a>, a baseball field, <a href="/wiki/Nagai_Botanical_Garden" title="Nagai Botanical Garden">Nagai Botanical Garden</a>, which boasts over 1,000 species of trees and flowers, a swimming pool and gymnasium, and a tract of preserved local forest. It also hosts a large number of cherry trees, and is a popular area for picnics during cherry blossom (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sakura" title="Sakura">Sakura</a>) season in early spring.</p>
