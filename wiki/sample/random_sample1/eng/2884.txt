<p><b>Nanoarchaeota</b> (Greek, "dwarf or tiny ancient one") are a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Phylum_(biology)" title="Phylum (biology)">phylum</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Archaea" title="Archaea">Archaea</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> This phylum currently has only one representative, <i><a href="/wiki/Nanoarchaeum_equitans" title="Nanoarchaeum equitans">Nanoarchaeum equitans</a></i>.</p>
