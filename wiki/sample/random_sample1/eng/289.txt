<p><b><a href="/wiki/Fiji" title="Fiji">Fiji</a></b> competed in the <a href="/wiki/Winter_Olympic_Games" title="Winter Olympic Games">Winter Olympic Games</a> for the first time at the <b><a href="/wiki/1988_Winter_Olympics" title="1988 Winter Olympics">1988 Winter Olympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Calgary" title="Calgary">Calgary, Alberta</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>.</p>
<p>The country was represented by was <a href="/wiki/Rusiate_Rogoyawa" title="Rusiate Rogoyawa">Rusiate Rogoyawa</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Cross-country_skiing" title="Cross-country skiing">cross-country skiing</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
