<p><b>Namayingo District</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Districts_of_Uganda" title="Districts of Uganda">district</a> in <a href="/wiki/Eastern_Region,_Uganda" title="Eastern Region, Uganda">Eastern Uganda</a>. The district is named after its 'chief town', <a href="/wiki/Namayingo" title="Namayingo">Namayingo</a>, where the district headquarters are located.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Location">Location</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Namayingo_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Location">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Namayingo District is located along the <a href="/wiki/Equator" title="Equator">Equator</a>. It is bordered by <a href="/wiki/Bugiri_District" title="Bugiri District">Bugiri District</a> to the northwest, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Busia_District,_Uganda" title="Busia District, Uganda">Busia District</a> to the northeast, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Republic_of_Kenya" title="Republic of Kenya">Republic of Kenya</a> to the east and southeast, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Republic_of_Tanzania" title="Republic of Tanzania">Republic of Tanzania</a> to the south and <a href="/wiki/Mayuge_District" title="Mayuge District">Mayuge District</a> to the west and southwest. <a href="/wiki/Namayingo" title="Namayingo">Namayingo</a>, where the district headquarters are located, is approximately 95 kilometres (59 mi), by road, southeast of <a href="/wiki/Jinja,_Uganda" title="Jinja, Uganda">Jinja</a>, the largest city in the <a href="/wiki/Busoga_sub-region" title="Busoga sub-region">Busoga sub-region</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> This location lies approximately 38 kilometres (24 mi), by road, south of <a href="/wiki/Bugiri" title="Bugiri">Bugiri</a>, the nearest large town.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The coordinates of the district are:00 17N, 33 51E.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Overview">Overview</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Namayingo_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Overview">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The district was established by Act of Parliament and began functioning on 1 July 2010. Priotr to that it was part of <a href="/wiki/Bugiri_District" title="Bugiri District">Bugiri District</a>. Namayingo District is part of <a href="/wiki/Busoga" title="Busoga">Busoga sub-region</a>. Other districts in Busoga include: 1. <a href="/wiki/Buyende_District" title="Buyende District">Buyende District</a> 2. <a href="/wiki/Iganga_District" title="Iganga District">Iganga District</a> 3. <a href="/wiki/Jinja_District" title="Jinja District">Jinja District</a> 4. <a href="/wiki/Kaliro_District" title="Kaliro District">Kaliro District</a> 5. <a href="/wiki/Kamuli_District" title="Kamuli District">Kamuli District</a> 6. <a href="/wiki/Luuka_District" title="Luuka District">Luuka District</a> 7. <a href="/wiki/Mayuge_District" title="Mayuge District">Mayuge District</a> and 8. <a href="/wiki/Namutumba_District" title="Namutumba District">Namutumba District</a>.</p>
<p>Prior to 2000, the two main activities in the district were (a) subsistence agriculture on the mainland and (b) commercial fishing on the lakeshores and on the islands in <a href="/wiki/Lake_Victoria" title="Lake Victoria">Lake Victoria</a>. During he 21st century, <a href="/wiki/Gold" title="Gold">gold</a> deposits have been discovered in the district. This has attracted many new residents to the area, performing such activities as: miners, washers, grinders, middlemen, buyers as well as salesmen of the hardware used in the artisanal gold mining industry, such as pick axes, shovels, pails, basins, ropes and ladders.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Population">Population</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Namayingo_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Population">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The 1991 national population census estimated the district population at about 68,000. In 2002, the next national census put the population of the district at approximately 175,000. The district annual population growth rate was calculated at 2.9%. It is estimated that the population of Namayingo District was approximately 232,300 in 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
