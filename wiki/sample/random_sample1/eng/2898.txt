<p><b>Certhioidea</b> is a superfamily belonging to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Infraorder" title="Infraorder">infraorder</a> <a href="/wiki/Passerida" title="Passerida">Passerida</a> containing wrens and their allies. It was created in 2004 by Cracraft et al. to house a clade of four families that were removed from the superfamily <a href="/wiki/Sylvioidea" title="Sylvioidea">Sylvioidea</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
