<p><b><a href="/wiki/Guam" title="Guam">Guam</a></b> competed in the <a href="/wiki/Summer_Olympic_Games" title="Summer Olympic Games">Summer Olympic Games</a> for the first time at the <b><a href="/wiki/1988_Summer_Olympics" title="1988 Summer Olympics">1988 Summer Olympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">Korea</a>. The Guam National Olympic Committee was formed in 1976 and recognized by the IOC (International Olympic Committee) in 1986.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
