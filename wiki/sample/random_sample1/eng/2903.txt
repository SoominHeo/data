<p><b>Nabaruru Batiri</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Gilbertese_language" title="Gilbertese language">I-Kiribati</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">Defender</a>. He represents Kiribati at <a href="/wiki/Kiribati_national_football_team" title="Kiribati national football team">international level</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NFT_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-NFT-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nabaruru_Batiri&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2003_Pacific_Games">2003 Pacific Games</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nabaruru_Batiri&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 2003 Pacific Games">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Batiri made four appearances at the <a href="/wiki/Men%27s_football_at_the_2003_South_Pacific_Games" title="Men's football at the 2003 South Pacific Games">2003 South Pacific Games</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NFT_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-NFT-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2011_Pacific_Games">2011 Pacific Games</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nabaruru_Batiri&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 2011 Pacific Games">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Batiri played 4 games, one as a substitute at the <a href="/wiki/Men%27s_Football_at_the_2011_Pacific_Games" title="Men's Football at the 2011 Pacific Games">2011 Pacific Games</a>, the 3-0 loss to <a href="/wiki/Cook_Islands_national_football_team" title="Cook Islands national football team">Cook Islands</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> the 1-17 losses to both <a href="/wiki/Papua_New_Guinea_national_football_team" title="Papua New Guinea national football team">Papua New Guinea</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Tahiti_national_football_team" title="Tahiti national football team">Tahiti</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> and the 9-0 loss to <a href="/wiki/Fiji_national_football_team" title="Fiji national football team">Fiji</a>, where he appeared as a substitute.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
