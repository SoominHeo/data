<p>The <b>Navegantes del Magallanes</b> (<i>Magallanes Navigators</i>) are a baseball team in the <a href="/wiki/Venezuelan_Professional_Baseball_League" title="Venezuelan Professional Baseball League">Venezuelan Professional Baseball League</a>. Based in <a href="/wiki/Valencia,_Carabobo" title="Valencia, Carabobo">Valencia</a>, the <i>Navegantes</i> have won twelve LVBP championships and two <a href="/wiki/Caribbean_Series" title="Caribbean Series">Caribbean Series</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Mascot">Mascot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Navegantes_del_Magallanes&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Mascot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Capy, is the official <a href="/wiki/Mascot" title="Mascot">mascot</a> of the <b>Navegantes del Magallanes</b> Baseball team. The bird wears a navigator costume. He has a girlfriend named Lola, a very charming parrot.</p>
