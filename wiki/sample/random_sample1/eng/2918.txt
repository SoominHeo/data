<p>The <b>Nawa Insect Museum</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">名和昆虫博物館</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Nawa Konchū Hakubutsukan</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a museum in the city of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gifu,_Gifu" title="Gifu, Gifu">Gifu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gifu_Prefecture" title="Gifu Prefecture">Gifu Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>. It is operated by the Nawa Insect Research Center. </p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nawa_Insect_Museum&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Yasushi_Nawa" title="Yasushi Nawa">Yasushi Nawa</a>, the man who discovered the Gifu Butterfly and is known as "The Insect Man," opened the Nawa Insect Research Center in April 1896. The center was moved to its present location in <a href="/wiki/Gifu_Park" title="Gifu Park">Gifu Park</a> in 1904, and became the Nawa Insect Museum in 1919, the first insect museum in Japan.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It currently houses over 300,000 specimens of 18,000 different insect species.</p>
<p>The building that houses the museum was designed by Goichi Takeda in a modern western style that was quite rare at the time.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-map_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-map-2">[2]</a></sup> The building is now a Tangible Cultural Property of the prefecture.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Access">Access</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nawa_Insect_Museum&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Access">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>From <a href="/wiki/Central_Japan_Railway_Company" title="Central Japan Railway Company">JR</a> <a href="/wiki/Gifu_Station" title="Gifu Station">Gifu Station</a> (Bus Platform 11) or <a href="/wiki/Meitetsu_Gifu_Station" title="Meitetsu Gifu Station">Meitetsu Gifu Station</a> (Bus Platform 4), board a bus operated by <a href="/wiki/Gifu_Bus" title="Gifu Bus">Gifu Bus</a> that is heading towards Nagara. Get off the bus at "Gifu Koen, Rekishi Hakubutsukan-mae," approximately 15 minutes from the train stations.</p>
