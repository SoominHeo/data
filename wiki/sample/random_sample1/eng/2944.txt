<p><b>Nakanoshima Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">中野島駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Nakanoshima-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Railway_station" title="Railway station">railway station</a> operated by <a href="/wiki/East_Japan_Railway_Company" title="East Japan Railway Company">JR East's</a> <a href="/wiki/Nambu_Line" title="Nambu Line">Nambu Line</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Tama-ku,_Kawasaki" title="Tama-ku, Kawasaki">Tama-ku, Kawasaki</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>. It is 19.5 kilometers from the terminus of the Nambu Line at <a href="/wiki/Kawasaki_Station" title="Kawasaki Station">Kawasaki Station</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nakanoshima_Station_(Kanagawa)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Nakanoshima Station was opened on November 1, 1927, as a station on the Nambu Railway. Freight operations began in 1929. The Nambu Railway was <a href="/wiki/Nationalization" title="Nationalization">nationalized</a> on April 1, 1944, and the station came under the control of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Japan_National_Railways" title="Japan National Railways">Japan National Railways</a> (JNR). The station was relocated 400 meters towards <a href="/wiki/Noborito_Station" title="Noborito Station">Noborito Station</a> in December 1945. Freight operations were discontinued after 1955. After the <a href="/wiki/Privatization" title="Privatization">privatization</a> of the JNR on April 1, 1987, the station was absorbed into the JR East network.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_layout">Station layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nakanoshima_Station_(Kanagawa)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Station layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Nakanoshima Station has two opposed <a href="/wiki/Side_platform" title="Side platform">side platforms</a> serving two tracks, connected by an <a href="/wiki/Overpass" title="Overpass">overpass</a>. The station is attended, but is a <a href="/wiki/Kan%27i_itaku_station" title="Kan'i itaku station">Kan'i itaku station</a> operated by the East Japan Eco Access Co., Ltd</p>
