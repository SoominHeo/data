<p><b>Kengo Nakamura</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">中村 憲剛</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Nakamura Kengo</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born 31 October 1980)</span> is a Japanese <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> midfielder who currently plays for <a href="/wiki/Kawasaki_Frontale" title="Kawasaki Frontale">Kawasaki Frontale</a> of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/J._League" title="J. League">J. League</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kengo_Nakamura&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He has a bachelor of arts (English) from <a href="/wiki/Chuo_University" title="Chuo University">Chuo University</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (November 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Playing_style">Playing style</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kengo_Nakamura&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Playing style">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>K.Nakamura is a playmaking midfielder usually employed by <a href="/wiki/Kawasaki_Frontale" title="Kawasaki Frontale">Kawasaki Frontale</a> on the central <a href="/wiki/Midfield" title="Midfield">midfield</a>.</p>
<p>Besides passing, Nakamura is also an adept dribbler with determination and strength. He has managed to score through dribbling past defenses for his club. His goals and assists were instrumental in taking Kawasaki Frontale to the knock-out stages of the ACL 2007.</p>
