<p>The <b>1990 Japan Series</b> was the <a href="/wiki/Nippon_Professional_Baseball" title="Nippon Professional Baseball">Nippon Professional Baseball</a> (NPB) championship series for the 1990 season. It was the 41st <a href="/wiki/Japan_Series" title="Japan Series">Japan Series</a> and featured the <a href="/wiki/Pacific_League" title="Pacific League">Pacific League</a> champion <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seibu_Lions" title="Seibu Lions">Seibu Lions</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Central_League" title="Central League">Central League</a> champion <a href="/wiki/Yomiuri_Giants" title="Yomiuri Giants">Yomiuri Giants</a>. Seibu won the PL pennant for the seventh time in nine years to reach the series, and Yomiuri dominated the CL to return to the series after winning it the year before. Played at <a href="/wiki/Tokyo_Dome" title="Tokyo Dome">Tokyo Dome</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seibu_Lions_Stadium" title="Seibu Lions Stadium">Seibu Lions Stadium</a>, the Lions swept the heavily favored Giants in four games to win the franchise's 10th Japan Series title. Seibu slugger and former MLB player <a href="/wiki/Orestes_Destrade" title="Orestes Destrade">Orestes Destrade</a> was named <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Japan_Series_Most_Valuable_Player" title="Japan Series Most Valuable Player">Most Valuable Player</a> of the series. The series was played between October 20 and October 24 with <a href="/wiki/Home_advantage" title="Home advantage">home field advantage</a> going to the Central League.</p>
