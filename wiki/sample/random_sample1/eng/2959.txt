<p><i><b>Natalie</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">나탈리</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Natalri</span></i>; <a href="/wiki/McCune%E2%80%93Reischauer" title="McCune–Reischauer">MR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Nat‘alli</span></i>) is a 2010 South Korean <a href="/wiki/Romance_film" title="Romance film">romance</a> <a href="/wiki/Mystery_film" title="Mystery film">mystery</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Erotic_film" title="Erotic film">erotic</a> film co-written, directed and produced by Ju Kyung-jung. It was the first Korean <a href="/wiki/3D_film" title="3D film">3D film</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>The film's title refers to a famous female nude piece by sculptor Jun-hyuk (<a href="/wiki/Lee_Sung-jae" title="Lee Sung-jae">Lee Sung-jae</a>). The model of the piece has remained a great mystery in the art world, but a young critic (<a href="/wiki/Kim_Ji-hoon_(actor_born_1981)" title="Kim Ji-hoon (actor born 1981)">Kim Ji-hoon</a>) claims to have also loved the <a href="/wiki/Femme_fatale" title="Femme fatale">femme fatale</a> (Park Hyun-jin) who had been Jun-hyuk's muse.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Natalie_(film)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mi-ran, a beautiful dance student (Park Hyun-jin), becomes the model, muse and lover of her philandering sculptor-professor Jun-hyuk (<a href="/wiki/Lee_Sung-jae" title="Lee Sung-jae">Lee Sung-jae</a>). When Mi-ran realizes that she will never be more than a model for Jun-hyuk, she leaves him for her stalker-cum-fellow student Min-woo (<a href="/wiki/Kim_Ji-hoon_(actor_born_1981)" title="Kim Ji-hoon (actor born 1981)">Kim Ji-hoon</a>).</p>
<p>The story takes place 10 years later, when Min-woo and Jun-hyuk reunite as art critic and interviewee, and the two compare their conflicting memories of Mi-ran, who seems to have since disappeared.</p>
