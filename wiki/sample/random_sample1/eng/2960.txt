<p><b>Nathalia Goyannes Dill Orrico</b>, better known as <b>Nathalia Dill</b> (born March 24, 1986), is a <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazilian</a> television and cinema actress. She has played the lead role in three telenovelas, the lead antagonist role in another two and the lead role in 2012's film <i>Artificial Paradises</i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nathalia_Dill&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born in the city of <a href="/wiki/Rio_de_Janeiro" title="Rio de Janeiro">Rio de Janeiro</a>, Nathalia Dill played the antagonist role as Débora Rios in the telenovela <i><a href="/wiki/Malha%C3%A7%C3%A3o" title="Malhação">Malhação</a></i> from 2007 to 2009.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Entrevista_exclusiva_com_Nath.C3.A1lia_Dill_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Entrevista_exclusiva_com_Nath.C3.A1lia_Dill-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Novo_Rumo_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Novo_Rumo-3">[3]</a></sup> She then played the lead role in the <a href="/wiki/Rede_Globo" title="Rede Globo">Rede Globo</a> telenovela <i>Paraíso</i>, by Benedito Ruy Barbosa, where she played the role of Maria Rita (nicknamed <i>Santinha</i>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Nath.C3.A1lia_Dill_fica_loira.2C_para_viver_sua_1.C2.AA_protagonista_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Nath.C3.A1lia_Dill_fica_loira.2C_para_viver_sua_1.C2.AA_protagonista-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Nat.C3.A1lia_Dill_ser.C3.A1_protagonista_da_novela_.27Para.C3.ADso.27_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Nat.C3.A1lia_Dill_ser.C3.A1_protagonista_da_novela_.27Para.C3.ADso.27-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Nat.C3.A1lia_Dill_ser.C3.A1_protagonista_da_novela_Para.C3.ADso_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-Nat.C3.A1lia_Dill_ser.C3.A1_protagonista_da_novela_Para.C3.ADso-6">[6]</a></sup> Nathalia Dill was cast to play the lead role in the telenovela <i>Escrito nas Estrelas</i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Nathalia_Dill_ser.C3.A1_protagonista_de_nova_novela_das_seis_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-Nathalia_Dill_ser.C3.A1_protagonista_de_nova_novela_das_seis-7">[7]</a></sup> in which she played the character Viviane.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Nathalia_Dill_grava_novela_em_morro_carioca_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-Nathalia_Dill_grava_novela_em_morro_carioca-8">[8]</a></sup> Her character, named Doralice, was part of the main love triangle of 2011 Rede Globo's telenovela <i>Cordel Encantado</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> Nathalia Dill played one of the lead roles, Érica, in the 2011 film <i>Paraísos Artificiais</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> She plays Débora in the 2012 telenovela <a href="/wiki/Avenida_Brasil_(telenovela)" title="Avenida Brasil (telenovela)">Avenida Brasil</a>, by <a href="/wiki/Jo%C3%A3o_Emanuel_Carneiro" title="João Emanuel Carneiro">João Emanuel Carneiro</a>, her character falls in love to Jorge, played by <a href="/wiki/Cau%C3%A3_Reymond" title="Cauã Reymond">Cauã Reymond</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> Nathalia Dill played the lead antagonist for the second time in her career in the 2013 Rede Globo telenovela <a href="/wiki/Joia_Rara" title="Joia Rara">Joia Rara</a>, her character is named as Sílvia.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-joiarara_12-0"><a href="#cite_note-joiarara-12">[12]</a></sup> She starred as the journalist Laura in the 2014 telenovela <a href="/wiki/Alto_Astral" title="Alto Astral">Alto Astral</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-AltoAstral_13-0"><a href="#cite_note-AltoAstral-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
