<p><b>Natalia Cordova-Buckley</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">Mexican</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Actress" title="Actress">actress</a> best known for portraying <a href="/wiki/Yo-Yo_Rodriguez" title="Yo-Yo Rodriguez">Yo-Yo Rodriguez</a> on <i><a href="/wiki/Agents_of_S.H.I.E.L.D." title="Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.">Marvel's Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_career">Early career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Natalia_Cordova-Buckley&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Cordova-Buckley began her career as a ballet dancer. Finding the occupation too restricting she decided to become an actress instead. Her grandfather is <a href="/wiki/Pancho_C%C3%B3rdova" title="Pancho Córdova">Pancho Córdova</a> an actor in his own right. She moved to the U.S. and studied at <a href="/wiki/University_of_North_Carolina_School_of_the_Arts" title="University of North Carolina School of the Arts">University of North Carolina School of the Arts</a> and later earned her first American role in <a href="/wiki/McFarland,_USA" title="McFarland, USA">McFarland, USA</a>. She almost quit acting until she won the role of <a href="/wiki/Yo-Yo_Rodriguez" title="Yo-Yo Rodriguez">Elena Rodriguez</a> on <i><a href="/wiki/Agents_of_S.H.I.E.L.D." title="Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.">Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.</a></i>, gaining recognition in the process.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
