<p><b>Natalie Vértiz González</b> (born September 29, 1991)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> is a Peruvian-American actress, TV Host, model and beauty pageant titleholder who represented <a href="/wiki/Peru" title="Peru">Peru</a> at <a href="/wiki/Miss_Universe_2011" title="Miss Universe 2011">Miss Universe 2011</a>. Vértiz was born in <a href="/wiki/Lima" title="Lima">Lima</a> and was raised in the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Pageants">Pageants</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Natalie_V%C3%A9rtiz&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Pageants">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Natalie, who is a resident of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pompano_Beach" title="Pompano Beach">Pompano Beach</a>, <a href="/wiki/Florida" title="Florida">Florida</a>, competed in <a href="/wiki/Miss_Per%C3%BA_2011" title="Miss Perú 2011">Miss Perú 2011</a>, representing USA Perú (the Peruvian community in the United States),<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> where she was one of 12 finalists and obtained the title of Miss Perú 2011, as well as the <i>Best Body</i> and <i>Miss Silhouette</i> awards. Her nutritionists and trainers, Christopher Rios, Daniel Rios and Xavier Rios, succeeded with his advisement towards her winning of the "Best Body" Award.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>Natalie represented <a href="/wiki/Peru" title="Peru">Peru</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Miss_Universe_2011" title="Miss Universe 2011">Miss Universe 2011</a> pageant,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> broadcast live from <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A3o_Paulo" title="São Paulo">São Paulo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a> on September 12, 2011 but failed to place.</p>
<p>On March 17, 2014 Vértiz and boyfriend Jacobo (Yaco) Eskenazi Álvarez welcomed their first child together, son Liam. Eskenazi proposed to Vertiz live on the competition show, "Esto es Guerra". <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jewish" title="Jewish">Jewish</a>-raised Eskenazi and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Catholic" title="Catholic">Catholic</a>-raised Vértiz married in a <a href="/wiki/Civil_marriage" title="Civil marriage">civil ceremony</a> on July 11, 2015. Their marriage was televised live on America Television . <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
