<p><i><b>Phoniscus</b></i> is a genus of <a href="/wiki/Vesper_bat" title="Vesper bat">vesper bat</a> in the family <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vespertilionidae" title="Vespertilionidae">Vespertilionidae</a>. It contains the following species:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dubious_trumpet-eared_bat" title="Dubious trumpet-eared bat">Dubious trumpet-eared bat</a> (<i>Phoniscus aerosa</i>)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Groove-toothed_bat" title="Groove-toothed bat">Groove-toothed bat</a> (<i>Phoniscus atrox</i>)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Peters%27s_trumpet-eared_bat" title="Peters's trumpet-eared bat">Peters's trumpet-eared bat</a> (<i>Phoniscus jagorii</i>)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Golden-tipped_bat" title="Golden-tipped bat">Golden-tipped bat</a> (<i>Phoniscus papuensis</i>)</li>
</ul>
