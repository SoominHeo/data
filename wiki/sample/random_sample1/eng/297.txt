<p>The <b>1992 <a href="/wiki/Dynasty_Cup" title="Dynasty Cup">Dynasty Cup</a></b> was a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> competition for the top four teams of <a href="/wiki/East_Asia" title="East Asia">East Asia</a>. The second edition of the Dynasty Cup was held from 22 to 29 August 1992 in <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. The competition was won by <a href="/wiki/Japan_national_football_team" title="Japan national football team">Japan</a>.</p>
