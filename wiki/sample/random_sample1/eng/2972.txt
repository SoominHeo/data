<p><b>Nakdonggang Station</b> (<small>Korean pronunciation: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Korean" title="Help:IPA for Korean">[nakt͈oŋgaŋ]</a></span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Train_station" title="Train station">railway station</a> in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. It is on <a href="/wiki/Gyeongjeon_Line" title="Gyeongjeon Line">Gyeongjeon Line</a>.</p>
