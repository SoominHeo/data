<p><b>Nangong</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%8D%97%E5%AE%AB" title="wikt:南宫">南宫</a></span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%8D%97%E5%AE%AE" title="wikt:南宮">南宮</a></span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Nángōng</span></i>) is a city in the <a href="/wiki/North_China" title="North China">northern</a> province of <a href="/wiki/Hebei" title="Hebei">Hebei</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. It is administered by the <a href="/wiki/Prefecture-level_city" title="Prefecture-level city">prefecture-level city</a> of <a href="/wiki/Xingtai" title="Xingtai">Xingtai</a>.</p>
<p>The city's population was 82,500 as of 2005.</p>
