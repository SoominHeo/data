<p><b>Nanxiong</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">南雄</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Nánxióng</span></i>) historically <b>Namyung</b> is a <a href="/wiki/County-level_city" title="County-level city">county-level city</a> of northern <a href="/wiki/Guangdong" title="Guangdong">Guangdong</a> province, People's Republic of China, bordering <a href="/wiki/Jiangxi" title="Jiangxi">Jiangxi</a> to the north, east and southeast. It is under the administration of <a href="/wiki/Shaoguan" title="Shaoguan">Shaoguan</a> City.</p>
