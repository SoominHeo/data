<p><b>Nanhai District</b>, is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/District_(PRC_and_ROC)" title="District (PRC and ROC)">district</a> of <a href="/wiki/Foshan" title="Foshan">Foshan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Guangdong" title="Guangdong">Guangdong</a>, China. Its government is the first to have developed <a href="/wiki/E-government" title="E-government">e-government</a> <a href="/wiki/Informatization" title="Informatization">informatization</a> at the county level in China.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nanhai_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The town was established in 1271 by two brothers carrying their father's bowls. They were fleeing south from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mongol" title="Mongol">Mongols</a> on a <a href="/wiki/Bamboo" title="Bamboo">bamboo</a> raft when a violent storm shipwrecked them and broke all the bowls. The brothers settled down there and the position of the wreck is commemorated by a shrine. This area was named Broken Bowls Point. 15 February 1921 eastern part of Nanhai County was ceded to the newly established City of <a href="/wiki/Guangzhou" title="Guangzhou">Guangzhou</a> which became part of what is now western part of <a href="/wiki/Liwan_District" title="Liwan District">Liwan</a>. On 26 June 1951 Foshan Town (present <a href="/wiki/Chancheng_District" title="Chancheng District">Chancheng</a>) was ceded to the newly established City of <a href="/wiki/Foshan" title="Foshan">Foshan</a>. Nanhai County was upgraded into a <a href="/wiki/County-level_city" title="County-level city">county-level city</a> on 2 September 1992 until 8 December 2002 Nanhai was consolidated as a district of Foshan.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Administration_division">Administration division</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nanhai_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Administration division">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Nanhai was a <a href="/wiki/County-level_city" title="County-level city">county-level city</a> until December 8, 2002, when it became a district of <a href="/wiki/Foshan" title="Foshan">Foshan</a> <a href="/wiki/Prefecture-level_city" title="Prefecture-level city">prefecture-level city</a>.</p>
