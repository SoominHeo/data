<p><b>Plagiochilaceae</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Family_(biology)" title="Family (biology)">family</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Liverworts" title="Liverworts">liverworts</a> in <a href="/wiki/Order_(biology)" title="Order (biology)">order</a> <a href="/wiki/Jungermanniales" title="Jungermanniales">Jungermanniales</a>. There may be anywhere from 500 to 1300 species, most of them from the tropics, but the exact number is still under revision. The family also has a wide distribution in temperate and arctic areas.</p>
