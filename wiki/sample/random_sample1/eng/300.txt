<p>The <b>1992 Japan Series</b> was the <a href="/wiki/Nippon_Professional_Baseball" title="Nippon Professional Baseball">Nippon Professional Baseball</a> (NPB) championship series for the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=1992_Nippon_Professional_Baseball_season&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="1992 Nippon Professional Baseball season (page does not exist)">1992 season</a>. It was the 43rd <a href="/wiki/Japan_Series" title="Japan Series">Japan Series</a> and featured the <a href="/wiki/Pacific_League" title="Pacific League">Pacific League</a> champion <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seibu_Lions" title="Seibu Lions">Seibu Lions</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Central_League" title="Central League">Central League</a> champion <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yakult_Swallows" title="Yakult Swallows">Yakult Swallows</a>. Seibu won their third consecutive PL pennant in convincing fashion to reach the series, and Yakult finished the season atop the competitive CL for the first time since 1978. Played at <a href="/wiki/Meiji_Jingu_Stadium" title="Meiji Jingu Stadium">Meiji Jingu Stadium</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seibu_Lions_Stadium" title="Seibu Lions Stadium">Seibu Lions Stadium</a>, the Lions defeated the Swallows four games to three in the <a href="/wiki/Playoff_format#Best-of-seven_playoff" title="Playoff format">best-of-seven series</a> to win the franchise's 11th Japan Series title. Seibu's <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sawamura_Award" title="Sawamura Award">Sawamura Award</a> winner <a href="/wiki/Takehiro_Ishii" title="Takehiro Ishii">Takehiro Ishii</a> was named <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Japan_Series_Most_Valuable_Player" title="Japan Series Most Valuable Player">Most Valuable Player</a> of the series. The series was played between October 17 and October 26 with <a href="/wiki/Home_advantage" title="Home advantage">home field advantage</a> going to the Central League.</p>
