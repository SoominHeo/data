<p>The <b>oreos</b> are a <a href="/wiki/Family_(biology)" title="Family (biology)">family</a>, the <b>Oreosomatidae</b>, of marine <a href="/wiki/Fish" title="Fish">fish</a>. Most species are found in the Southern Hemisphere, inhabiting <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Continental_slope" title="Continental slope">continental slopes</a> down to about 1,000 m (3,300 ft) deep.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-EoF_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-EoF-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
