<p>The <b>South German Offensive</b> is the general name of one of the final offensives of <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a> in <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>. The offensive was led by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/US_Seventh_Army" title="US Seventh Army">Seventh</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/US_Third_Army" title="US Third Army">Third</a> armies of the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> along with the <a href="/wiki/First_Army_(France)" title="First Army (France)">First Army</a> of <a href="/wiki/Provisional_Government_of_the_French_Republic" title="Provisional Government of the French Republic">France</a>. <a href="/wiki/Soviet_Union" title="Soviet Union">Soviet</a> troops linked up with American forces in <a href="/wiki/Czechoslovakia" title="Czechoslovakia">Czechoslovakia</a> notably in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Slivice" title="Battle of Slivice">Battle of Slivice</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (June 2013)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> The offensive was made by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/US_6th_Army_Group" title="US 6th Army Group">US 6th Army Group</a> to protect the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/US_12th_Army_Group" title="US 12th Army Group">12th Army Group's</a> right flank and to prevent a German last stand in the <a href="/wiki/Alps" title="Alps">Alps</a>. However German resistance was much more fierce than in the north, which slowed the 6th Army Group's progress. However, by the end of April, many German divisions surrendered without a fight to the advancing American forces to avoid the inevitable destruction. The VI Corps of the Seventh Army linked up with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/US_Fifth_Army" title="US Fifth Army">US Fifth Army</a>, which fought through <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>, in the Alps as the Third Army advanced into <a href="/wiki/Austria" title="Austria">Austria</a> and <a href="/wiki/Czechoslovakia" title="Czechoslovakia">Czechoslovakia</a>, where it linked up with Soviet forces advancing from the east. Fighting continued a few days after the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Surrender_of_Germany" title="Surrender of Germany">Surrender of Germany</a> on 8 May, due to German forces fighting west to surrender to the Americans instead of the Soviets.</p>
