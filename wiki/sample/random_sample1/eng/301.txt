<p><b>South Africa</b> competed at the <a href="/wiki/Summer_Olympic_Games" title="Summer Olympic Games">Summer Olympic Games</a> for the first time since <a href="/wiki/1960_Summer_Olympics" title="1960 Summer Olympics">1960</a> at the <b><a href="/wiki/1992_Summer_Olympics" title="1992 Summer Olympics">1992 Summer Olympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Barcelona" title="Barcelona">Barcelona</a>, Spain. South Africa was permitted to re-join the Olympic Movement after its citizens <a href="/wiki/South_African_apartheid_referendum,_1992" title="South African apartheid referendum, 1992">voted to abolish apartheid</a>. 93 competitors, 68 men and 25 women, took part in 87 events in 19 sports.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sports-reference_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-sports-reference-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The flag was carried at the opening ceremony by marathon runner <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Jan_Tau&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Jan Tau (page does not exist)">Jan Tau</a>.</p>
