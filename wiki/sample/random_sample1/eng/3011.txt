<p>The <b>Southern Region</b> of <a href="/wiki/Malawi" title="Malawi">Malawi</a> is an area of Malawi. It covers an area of 31,753 km². Its capital city is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Blantyre,_Malawi" title="Blantyre, Malawi">Blantyre</a>. In 2008, its population was 5,876,784.</p>
<p>Of the 28 <a href="/wiki/Districts_of_Malawi" title="Districts of Malawi">districts</a> in Malawi, 13 are located within the Southern Region. They are: <a href="/wiki/Balaka_District" title="Balaka District">Balaka</a>, <a href="/wiki/Blantyre_District" title="Blantyre District">Blantyre</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chikhwawa_District" title="Chikhwawa District">Chikhwawa</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chiradzulu_District" title="Chiradzulu District">Chiradzulu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Machinga_District" title="Machinga District">Machinga</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mangochi_District" title="Mangochi District">Mangochi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mulanje_District" title="Mulanje District">Mulanje</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mwanza_District" title="Mwanza District">Mwanza</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neno_District" title="Neno District">Neno</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nsanje_District" title="Nsanje District">Nsanje</a>, <a href="/wiki/Phalombe_District" title="Phalombe District">Phalombe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Thyolo_District" title="Thyolo District">Thyolo</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Zomba_District" title="Zomba District">Zomba</a>.</p>
