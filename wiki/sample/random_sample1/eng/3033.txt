<p>The <b>South Sea</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko" xml:lang="ko">남해</span>, <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">hanja</a>: <span lang="ko" xml:lang="ko"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%8D%97" title="wiktionary:南">南</a><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%B5%B7" title="wiktionary:海">海</a></span> ) is the region of ocean near <a href="/wiki/Korean_Peninsula" title="Korean Peninsula">Korea</a> that is bounded by the southwestern part of the <a href="/wiki/Sea_of_Japan" title="Sea of Japan">East Sea</a> and by the southeastern part of the <a href="/wiki/Yellow_Sea" title="Yellow Sea">West Sea</a>. The name is not known and used outside <a href="/wiki/Korean_Peninsula" title="Korean Peninsula">Korea</a>.</p>
