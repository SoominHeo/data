<p><i><b>My Daughter, Geum Sa-wol</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">내 딸 금사월</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Nae ttal Geum Sa-wol</span></i>) is a 2015 <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Korean_drama" title="Korean drama">television series</a> starring <a href="/wiki/Baek_Jin-hee" title="Baek Jin-hee">Baek Jin-hee</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jeon_In-hwa" title="Jeon In-hwa">Jeon In-hwa</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yoon_Hyun-min" title="Yoon Hyun-min">Yoon Hyun-min</a>, <a href="/wiki/Park_Se-young" title="Park Se-young">Park Se-young</a> and <a href="/wiki/Do_Sang-woo" title="Do Sang-woo">Do Sang-woo</a>. It aired on <a href="/wiki/Munhwa_Broadcasting_Corporation" title="Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation">MBC</a> on Saturdays and Sundays 21:45 for 51 episodes from September 5, 2015 until February 28, 2016. Although the drama enjoyed high viewership ratings, with the finale earning national viewership of 33.6%, critics described the drama as "absurdly unrealistic".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
