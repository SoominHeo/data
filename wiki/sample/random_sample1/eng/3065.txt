<p><b>Neoguri</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko" xml:lang="ko">너구리</span> lit. <a href="/wiki/Raccoon_dog" title="Raccoon dog">Raccoon dog</a>) is a brand of <a href="/wiki/Ramyeon" title="Ramyeon">ramyeon</a> produced by <a href="/wiki/Nongshim" title="Nongshim">Nongshim</a> in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a> since 1982.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> It is exported to over 80 different countries,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and is the fourth highest selling brand of noodles in South Korea.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
