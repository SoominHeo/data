<p><b><a href="/wiki/Taekwondo" title="Taekwondo">Taekwondo</a></b> took place from October 8 to October 9 at the <b><a href="/wiki/1994_Asian_Games" title="1994 Asian Games">1994 Asian Games</a></b> in Aki Ward Sports Center, <a href="/wiki/Hiroshima" title="Hiroshima">Hiroshima</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>. Men's competitions held in eight weight categories for each. Each country was limited to having 4 athletes for the entire competition.</p>
