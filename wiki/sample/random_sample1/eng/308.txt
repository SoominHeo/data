<p>The <b>2nd FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m)</b> was an international <a href="/wiki/Swimming_(sport)" title="Swimming (sport)">swimming</a> meet held on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Copacabana_Beach" title="Copacabana Beach">Copacabana Beach</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rio_de_Janeiro,_Brazil" title="Rio de Janeiro, Brazil">Rio de Janeiro, Brazil</a>, 30 November until 3 December 1995.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The meet featured 32 events swum in a <a href="/wiki/Short_course" title="Short course">short course</a> (25m) pool.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>The United States did poorly, as the meet dates fell in the middle (rather than the end) of the USA's annual competition season (particularly the college season), the USA had just hosted the <a href="/wiki/1995_Pan_Pacific_Swimming_Championships" title="1995 Pan Pacific Swimming Championships">1995 Pan Pacs</a>, and the USA would host the <a href="/wiki/1996_Summer_Olympics" title="1996 Summer Olympics">1996 Summer Olympics</a>.</p>
