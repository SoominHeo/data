<p><b>The anthem without a title</b> was the untitled <a href="/wiki/National_anthem" title="National anthem">national anthem</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Netherlands_Antilles" title="Netherlands Antilles">Netherlands Antilles</a>. The anthem was written in English by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Zahira_Hiliman&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Zahira Hiliman (page does not exist)">Zahira Hiliman</a> from <a href="/wiki/Sint_Maarten" title="Sint Maarten">Sint Maarten</a> and translated into the native Papiamento language by Lucille Berry-Haseth from <a href="/wiki/Cura%C3%A7ao" title="Curaçao">Curaçao</a>. The anthem was written in two of the three official languages of the Netherlands Antilles, <a href="/wiki/English_language" title="English language">English</a> and <a href="/wiki/Papiamento" title="Papiamento">Papiamento</a>; <a href="/wiki/Dutch_language" title="Dutch language">Dutch</a> was the third official language. It was adopted in 2000. In addition to this anthem, many of the islands in the Netherlands Antilles had their own anthems.</p>
<p>On 10 October 2010, the Netherlands Antilles was <a href="/wiki/Dissolution_of_the_Netherlands_Antilles" title="Dissolution of the Netherlands Antilles">dissolved</a> into <a href="/wiki/Cura%C3%A7ao" title="Curaçao">Curaçao</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sint_Maarten" title="Sint Maarten">Sint Maarten</a> and the three public bodies of the <a href="/wiki/Caribbean_Netherlands" title="Caribbean Netherlands">Caribbean Netherlands</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lyrics">Lyrics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Anthem_without_a_title&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lyrics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Papiamentu_lyrics">Papiamentu lyrics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Anthem_without_a_title&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Papiamentu lyrics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>1. <span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">Sinku prenda den laman, e islanan di nos,</span></p>
<p><span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">dòrnando e korona di un aliansa ideal.</span></p>
<p><span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">Ounke hende i kultura tur koló nan tin , nos mes a</span></p>
<p><span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">forma ùn famia den tur libertad.</span></p>
<p><b>Bridge</b></p>
<p><span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">Pesei nos tur ta alsa bos ku amor i den union</span></p>
<p><b>Chorus</b></p>
<p><span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">Antia Neerlandes, bunitesa sin igual.</span></p>
<p><span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">Ku orguyo mi ta defendé mi patria tan stimá.</span></p>
<p><span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">Antia Neerlandes, p’abo tur mi lealtat.</span></p>
<p><span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">Pa semper lo mi keda fiel, pais bendishoná</span></p>
<p>2. <span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">Un shelu semper kla, laman ta invitá, e islanan ta</span></p>
<p><span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">wowo dje kadena di unidat Idiomanan distinto, papiá</span></p>
<p><span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">Ku komprenshon, mes ternura: "Sweet antilles"</span></p>
<p><span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">"Dushi Antia ta ",</span></p>
<p>3. <span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">Ser parti di nos tera, ta orguyo sin midí,</span></p>
<p><span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">Antia Neerlandes stimá, ku tur sinseridat,</span></p>
<p><span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">Pues nos ta rearfimá ku amor i dignidat,</span></p>
<p><span lang="pap" xml:lang="pap">ku, dios dilanti, nos ta sirbi huntu nos pais.</span></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="English_lyrics">English lyrics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Anthem_without_a_title&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: English lyrics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>1. Our island in the sea, like gems they seem to be,</p>
<p>outstanding from a golden crown of blissful royalty.</p>
<p>Though their people and their culture colorful may seem,</p>
<p>they yet uniquely blend to be just one family.</p>
<p><b>Bridge</b></p>
<p>So we, your people raise our voice in love and unity</p>
<p><b>Chorus</b></p>
<p>Dear Netherlands Antilles, so beautiful to me.</p>
<p>I’m proud to be a part of you, a patriot I shall be.</p>
<p>Yes Netherlands Antilles, I pledge my loyalty,</p>
<p>To you always will be true; I say may God bless you.</p>
<p>2. So blessed with sunny skies and clear welcoming seas,</p>
<p>each island like a link that forms this chain of unity.</p>
<p>May differ in our language, yet meet on common ground,</p>
<p>When some say "Sweet Antilles", some say "Dushi Antia ta".</p>
<p>3. Yes, proud are we to be identified with you,</p>
<p>dear Netherlands Antilles, to you we will be true.</p>
<p>So we declare and vow, with dignity and love,</p>
<p>Our nation we will always serve, may God keep us as one.</p>
<p><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Dutch_Translation">Dutch Translation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Anthem_without_a_title&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Dutch Translation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>1. Ons eiland in de zee, net als edelstenen ze lijken te zijn,</p>
<p>uitstaand van een gouden kroon van gelukzalige royalty's.</p>
<p>Hoewel hun mensen en hun cultuur kleurrijke mogen lijken,</p>
<p>zij toch unieke mix tot slechts een familie.</p>
<p><b>Brug</b></p>
<p>Zo zijn wij, uw mensen te verhogen onze stem in de liefde en eenheid</p>
<p><b>Koor</b></p>
<p>Beste Nederlandse Antillen, zo mooi voor mij.</p>
<p>Ik ben er trots op een deel van jou, een patriot zal ik zijn.</p>
<p>Ja Nederlandse Antillen, beloof ik mijn loyaliteit,</p>
<p>Om u altijd waar zijn, ik zeg moge God u zegenen.</p>
<p>2. Zo gezegend met zonnige luchten en heldere gastvrije zeeën,</p>
<p>elk eiland als een link die deze keten van eenheid vormen.</p>
<p>Kunnen verschillen in onze taal, maar toch ontmoeten op gemeenschappelijke grond,</p>
<p>Wanneer sommigen "Zoet Antillen" zeggen, zeggen sommigen 'Dushi Antia ta ".</p>
<p>3. Ja, trots zijn we worden geïdentificeerd met u,</p>
<p>lieve Nederlandse Antillen, aan u zullen wij waar zijn.</p>
<p>Dus we verklaren en gelofte, met waardigheid en liefde,</p>
<p>Onze natie zullen we altijd dienen, moge God houden ons als een.</p>
