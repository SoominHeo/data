<p><b>Nergal crater</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Impact_crater" title="Impact crater">crater</a> on <a href="/wiki/Jupiter" title="Jupiter">Jupiter</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Natural_satellite" title="Natural satellite">moon</a> <a href="/wiki/Ganymede_(moon)" title="Ganymede (moon)">Ganymede</a>. It has a distinctive <a href="/wiki/Ejecta" title="Ejecta">ejecta</a> blanket surrounding it that's darker nearer the craters and brighter further away. The inner region of the ejecta is characterized by a lobate appearance indicative of the flow of a liquid (or slushy) substance over the surface. The flow was probably icy surface material melted by the energy released during the impact that formed the crater.</p>
