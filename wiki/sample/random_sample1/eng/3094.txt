<p><b>Nevin Galmarini</b> (born 4 December 1986) is a Swiss <a href="/wiki/Snowboard" title="Snowboard">snowboarder</a>. In 2009, he won the Swiss nationalships and became Swiss champion in parallel <a href="/wiki/Giant_slalom" title="Giant slalom">giant slalom</a>. He competed in the parallel <a href="/wiki/Giant_slalom" title="Giant slalom">giant slalom</a> for Switzerland during the <a href="/wiki/2010_Winter_Olympics" title="2010 Winter Olympics">2010 Winter Olympic</a> games in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vancouver,_Canada" title="Vancouver, Canada">Vancouver, Canada</a> and in the <a href="/wiki/2014_Winter_Olympics" title="2014 Winter Olympics">2014 Winter Olympic</a> games in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sochi,_Russia" title="Sochi, Russia">Sochi, Russia</a> where he won the silver medal. He currently resides in <a href="/wiki/Ardez" title="Ardez">Ardez</a>, <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Switzerland</a>. .</p>
