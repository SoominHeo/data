<p><b>Nestor Khergiani</b> (<a href="/wiki/Georgian_language" title="Georgian language">Georgian</a>: <span lang="ka" xml:lang="ka">ნესტორ ხერგიანი</span>, born July 20, 1975) is a <a href="/wiki/Georgia_(country)" title="Georgia (country)">Georgian</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Judoka" title="Judoka">judoka</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> He obtained silver medal at the <a href="/wiki/2004_Summer_Olympics" title="2004 Summer Olympics">2004 Summer Olympics</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Judo_at_the_2004_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_60_kg" title="Judo at the 2004 Summer Olympics – Men's 60 kg">-60 kg event</a> where he lost in the final to <a href="/wiki/Tadahiro_Nomura" title="Tadahiro Nomura">Tadahiro Nomura</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Khergiani won silver in <a href="/wiki/2007_World_Judo_Championships" title="2007 World Judo Championships">2007 World Judo Championships</a> and bronze in <a href="/wiki/1999_World_Judo_Championships" title="1999 World Judo Championships">1999 World Judo Championships</a>. At the <a href="/wiki/European_Judo_Championships" title="European Judo Championships">European Judo Championships</a> he won gold in <a href="/wiki/1998_European_Judo_Championships" title="1998 European Judo Championships">1998</a> and <a href="/wiki/2003_European_Judo_Championships" title="2003 European Judo Championships">2003</a>, bronze in <a href="/wiki/1999_European_Judo_Championships" title="1999 European Judo Championships">1999</a>, <a href="/wiki/2000_European_Judo_Championships" title="2000 European Judo Championships">2000</a>, <a href="/wiki/2001_European_Judo_Championships" title="2001 European Judo Championships">2001</a>, <a href="/wiki/2002_European_Judo_Championships" title="2002 European Judo Championships">2002</a>, <a href="/wiki/2007_European_Judo_Championships" title="2007 European Judo Championships">2007</a>, <a href="/wiki/2008_European_Judo_Championships" title="2008 European Judo Championships">2008</a> and <a href="/wiki/2009_European_Judo_Championships" title="2009 European Judo Championships">2009</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>Khergiani has been a scholarship holder with the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Olympic_Solidarity&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Olympic Solidarity (page does not exist)">Olympic Solidarity</a> program since August 2001.</p>
