<p><b>Neha Marda</b> (born 23 September 1985) is an Indian television actress, popularly known as <i>Urmi</i> for her role in the <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Doli_Armaano_ki&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Doli Armaano ki (page does not exist)">Doli Armaano ki</a></i> series broadcast by <a href="/wiki/Zee_TV" title="Zee TV">Zee TV</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> She also played the role of Gehna in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Colors_TV" title="Colors TV">Colors TV</a>'s "<a href="/wiki/Balika_Vadhu" title="Balika Vadhu">Balika Vadhu</a>" .<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Neha_Marda&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Neha is a native of Mumbai .On 10 February 2012, in Kolkata, she married Ayushman Agrawal, a <a href="/wiki/Patna" title="Patna">Patna</a>-based <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Businessman" title="Businessman">businessman</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> in an arranged marriage.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Performances">Performances</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Neha_Marda&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Performances">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Neha Marda is popularly known as <i>Gehna</i> for her role in the <i><a href="/wiki/Balika_Vadhu" title="Balika Vadhu">Balika Vadhu</a></i> series. According to Jyothi Venkatesh, Neha said, "she watched the CDs of Bandit Queen to get into the skin of her character Gehna in Balika Vadhu."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>She appeared in the dance shows <i>Boogie Woogie</i> and <i>Kaboom</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
