<p><b>Neha Sharma</b> (<small>pronounced </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Hindi_and_Urdu" title="Help:IPA for Hindi and Urdu">[nɛːɦaː ʃrmaː]</a></span>; born 21 November 1987) is an Indian film actress and model. A native of <a href="/wiki/Bihar" title="Bihar">Bihar</a>, Sharma attended the <a href="/wiki/Mount_Carmel_School_Bhagalpur" title="Mount Carmel School Bhagalpur">Mount Carmel School</a> in <a href="/wiki/Bhagalpur" title="Bhagalpur">Bhagalpur</a> and pursued a course in fashion design<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Neha_Sharma_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Neha_Sharma-2">[2]</a></sup> from the <a href="/wiki/National_Institute_of_Fashion_Technology" title="National Institute of Fashion Technology">National Institute of Fashion Technology</a> (NIFT) in <a href="/wiki/New_Delhi" title="New Delhi">New Delhi</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>Sharma's first role was in the Telugu film <i><a href="/wiki/Chirutha" title="Chirutha">Chirutha</a></i>, released in 2007. Her first Hindi film was <a href="/wiki/Mohit_Suri" title="Mohit Suri">Mohit Suri</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Crook_(film)" title="Crook (film)">Crook</a></i>, released in 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Sharma made a cameo appearance in <a href="/wiki/Kunal_Kohli" title="Kunal Kohli">Kunal Kohli</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Teri_Meri_Kahaani" title="Teri Meri Kahaani">Teri Meri Kahaani</a></i>. She was highly appreciated in the semi-hit <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kyaa_Super_Kool_Hai_Hum" title="Kyaa Super Kool Hai Hum">Kyaa Super Kool Hai Hum</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span></h2>
<p>Sharma was born on 21 November 1987 in <a href="/wiki/Bhagalpur" title="Bhagalpur">Bhagalpur</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bihar" title="Bihar">Bihar</a>. Her father <a href="/wiki/Ajit_Sharma" title="Ajit Sharma">Ajit Sharma</a>, is a businessman turned politician and MLA of <a href="/wiki/Bhagalpur" title="Bhagalpur">Bhagalpur</a>, of <a href="/wiki/Indian_National_Congress" title="Indian National Congress">Indian National Congress</a> party. Sharma has campaigned for her father in various elections.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> Sharma has three siblings. Sharma confessed being severely asthmatic in childhood and always unwell and weak physically. She also claimed to be completely cured of <a href="/wiki/Asthma" title="Asthma">asthma</a> with the blessing of a family in Hyderabad.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-timesofindia_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-timesofindia-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span></h2>
<p>Her hobbies are cooking, listening to music, reading and dance.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> Sharma is trained in the Indian classical dance form called <a href="/wiki/Kathak" title="Kathak">Kathak</a>. Apart from that she has also learned street hip hop, Latin dancing-salsa, merengue, jive and jazz from Pineapple dance studio, London.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-timesofindia_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-timesofindia-7">[7]</a></sup> She considers <a href="/wiki/Kate_Moss" title="Kate Moss">Kate Moss</a> as her style inspiration. Sharma also aspires to launch her own clothing label.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
