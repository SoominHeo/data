<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Nie Weiguo</h1>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><div class="hatnote" role="note">This is a <a href="/wiki/Chinese_name" title="Chinese name">Chinese name</a>; the <a href="/wiki/Chinese_surname" title="Chinese surname">family name</a> is <a href="/wiki/Nie_(surname)" title="Nie (surname)">Nie</a>.</div>
<p><b>Nie Weiguo</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans">聂卫国</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant">聶衛國</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Niè Wèigúo</span></i>; born August 1952) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/People%27s_Republic_of_China" title="People's Republic of China">Chinese</a> politician. He is the director of the Engineering Oversight Office of the <a href="/wiki/Three_Gorges_Dam" title="Three Gorges Dam">Three Gorges Dam</a> project.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-dam_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-dam-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Nie has occupied numerous <a href="/wiki/Communist_Party_of_China" title="Communist Party of China">Communist Party of China</a> positions in <a href="/wiki/Sichuan" title="Sichuan">Sichuan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Chongqing" title="Chongqing">Chongqing</a>. He received a degree in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Political_economics" title="Political economics">political economics</a> from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Central_Party_School" title="Central Party School">Central Party School</a> in 1996. From 2005 to 2010, Nie was the political commissar of the <a href="/wiki/Xinjiang_Production_and_Construction_Corps" title="Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps">Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps</a>. He has been a member of the <a href="/wiki/16th_Central_Committee_of_the_Communist_Party_of_China" title="16th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China">16th</a> and <a href="/wiki/17th_Central_Committee_of_the_Communist_Party_of_China" title="17th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China">17th</a> <a href="/wiki/Central_Committee_of_the_Communist_Party_of_China" title="Central Committee of the Communist Party of China">Central Committees of the Communist Party of China</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-xinhua_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-xinhua-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
