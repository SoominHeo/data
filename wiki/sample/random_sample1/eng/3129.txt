<p><b>Duke Wu of Lu</b> was a ruler of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/State_of_Lu" title="State of Lu">State of Lu</a> during the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Western_Zhou_Dynasty" title="Western Zhou Dynasty">Western Zhou Dynasty</a>. His ancestral name was Ji, and given name Ao. He succeeded his brother, Duke Shen of Lu, as the ninth ruler of Lu.</p>
<p>In spring of the ninth year of his reign, he paid a visit to <a href="/wiki/King_Xuan_of_Zhou" title="King Xuan of Zhou">King Xuan of Zhou</a> with his <a href="/wiki/Heir_apparent" title="Heir apparent">heir apparent</a> Kuo and younger son, Xi. King Xuan appreciated Xi greatly, and despite the objections of his councillor <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Zhongshan_Fu&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Zhongshan Fu (page does not exist)">Zhongshan Fu</a>, he decided to intervene and appoint Xi as the heir apparent over his elder brother. In summer, the three returned to Lu, and Duke Wu died shortly after.</p>
