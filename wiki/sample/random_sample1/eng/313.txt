<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><div class="hatnote" role="note">"-phobia" redirects here. For the class of psychological disorders, see <a href="/wiki/Phobia" title="Phobia">Phobia</a>.</div>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/English_language" title="English language">English</a> suffixes <b>-phobia</b>, <b>-phobic</b>, <b>-phobe</b> (from <a href="/wiki/Ancient_Greek" title="Ancient Greek">Greek</a> φόβος <i>phobos</i>, "fear") occur in technical usage in <a href="/wiki/Psychiatry" title="Psychiatry">psychiatry</a> to construct words that describe irrational, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Abnormal" title="Abnormal">abnormal</a>, unwarranted, persistent, or disabling <a href="/wiki/Fear" title="Fear">fear</a> as a mental disorder (e.g. <a href="/wiki/Agoraphobia" title="Agoraphobia">agoraphobia</a>), in <a href="/wiki/Chemistry" title="Chemistry">chemistry</a> to describe chemical aversions (e.g. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hydrophobic" title="Hydrophobic">hydrophobic</a>), in <a href="/wiki/Biology" title="Biology">biology</a> to describe organisms that dislike certain conditions (e.g. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Acidophobia" title="Acidophobia">acidophobia</a>), and in <a href="/wiki/Medicine" title="Medicine">medicine</a> to describe hypersensitivity to a stimulus, usually sensory (e.g. <a href="/wiki/Photophobia" title="Photophobia">photophobia</a>). In common usage, they also form words that describe dislike or hatred of a particular thing or subject (e.g. <a href="/wiki/Homophobia" title="Homophobia">homophobia</a>). The suffix is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Antonym" title="Antonym">antonymic</a> to <a href="/wiki/-phil-" title="-phil-">-phil-</a>.</p>
<p>For more information on the psychiatric side, including how psychiatry groups phobias such as agoraphobia, social phobia, or simple phobia, see <a href="/wiki/Phobia" title="Phobia">phobia</a>. The following lists include words ending in <i>-phobia</i>, and include fears that have acquired names. In some cases, the naming of phobias has become a <a href="/wiki/Word_game" title="Word game">word game</a>, of notable example being a 1998 humorous article published by <i><a href="/wiki/BBC_News" title="BBC News">BBC News</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-bbc_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-bbc-1">[1]</a></sup> In some cases, a word ending in <i>-phobia</i> may have an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Antonym" title="Antonym">antonym</a> with the suffix <i><a href="/wiki/-phil-" title="-phil-">-phil-</a></i>, e.g. <a href="/wiki/Anti-German_sentiment" title="Anti-German sentiment">Germanophobe</a> / <a href="/wiki/Germanophile" title="Germanophile">Germanophile</a>.</p>
<p>A large number of <i>-phobia</i> lists circulate on the Internet, with words collected from indiscriminate sources, often copying each other. Also, a number of psychiatric websites exist that at the first glance cover a huge number of phobias, but in fact use a standard text to fit any phobia and reuse it for all unusual phobias by merely changing the name. Sometimes it leads to bizarre results, such as suggestions to cure "prostitute phobia".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Such practice is known as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Content_spamming" title="Content spamming">content spamming</a> and is used to attract <a href="/wiki/Web_search_engine" title="Web search engine">search engines</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Psychological_conditions">Psychological conditions</span></h2>
<p>Specialists may prefer to avoid the suffix <i>-phobia</i> and use more descriptive terms such as <a href="/wiki/Personality_disorder" title="Personality disorder">personality disorders</a>, <a href="/wiki/Anxiety_disorder" title="Anxiety disorder">anxiety disorders</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Avoidant_personality_disorder" title="Avoidant personality disorder">avoidant personality disorder</a>.</p>
<div class="hatnote plainlinks" role="note">This list is <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Lists#Incomplete_lists" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Lists">incomplete</a>; you can help by <a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=List_of_phobias&amp;action=edit">expanding it</a>.</div>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="A">A</span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ablutophobia" title="Ablutophobia">Ablutophobia</a> – fear of <a href="/wiki/Bathing" title="Bathing">bathing</a>, <a href="/wiki/Washing" title="Washing">washing</a>, or <a href="/wiki/Cleanliness" title="Cleanliness">cleaning</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Acousticophobia" title="Acousticophobia">Acousticophobia</a> – fear of noise – a branch of <a href="/wiki/Phonophobia" title="Phonophobia">phonophobia</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Acrophobia" title="Acrophobia">Acrophobia</a> – fear of <a href="/wiki/Height" title="Height">heights</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Aerophobia" title="Aerophobia">Aerophobia</a> - fear of flying</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Agoraphobia" title="Agoraphobia">Agoraphobia</a> – fear of open places</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Agrizoophobia" title="Agrizoophobia">Agrizoophobia</a> – fear of <a href="/wiki/Wild_animal" title="Wild animal">wild animals</a>, a branch of <a href="/wiki/Zoophobia" title="Zoophobia">zoophobia</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Agyrophobia" title="Agyrophobia">Agyrophobia</a> – the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Irrational_fear" title="Irrational fear">irrational fear</a> that crossing streets will cause bodily harm to oneself, even if no actual threat is posed.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Palindrome" title="Palindrome">Aibohphobia</a> - fear of palindromes; words, phrases, numbers or other sequence of characters which can be read the same backward or forward.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Aichmophobia" title="Aichmophobia">Aichmophobia</a> – fear of sharp or pointed objects (such as a <a href="/wiki/Sewing_needle" title="Sewing needle">needle</a> or <a href="/wiki/Knife" title="Knife">knife</a>)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ailurophobia" title="Ailurophobia">Ailurophobia</a> – fear of <a href="/wiki/Cat" title="Cat">cats</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Algophobia" title="Algophobia">Algophobia</a> – fear of pain</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Amychophobia" title="Amychophobia">Amychophobia</a> – fear of being scratched</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Androphobia" title="Androphobia">Androphobia</a> - fear of <a href="/wiki/Man" title="Man">adult men</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Campbell2009_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-Campbell2009-7">[7]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Anthropophobia" title="Anthropophobia">Anthropophobia</a> – fear of people or the company of people, a form of <a href="/wiki/Social_anxiety_disorder" title="Social anxiety disorder">social phobia</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Aquaphobia" title="Aquaphobia">Aquaphobia</a> – fear of <a href="/wiki/Water" title="Water">water</a>. Distinct from <a href="/wiki/Hydrophobe" title="Hydrophobe">hydrophobia</a>, a scientific property that makes chemicals averse to interaction with water, as well as an archaic name for <a href="/wiki/Rabies" title="Rabies">rabies</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Arachnophobia" title="Arachnophobia">Arachnophobia</a> – fear of <a href="/wiki/Spider" title="Spider">spiders</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Astraphobia" title="Astraphobia">Astraphobia</a> – fear of <a href="/wiki/Thunder" title="Thunder">thunder</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lightning" title="Lightning">lightning</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Autophobia" title="Autophobia">Autophobia</a> – fear of isolation<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Aviophobia" title="Aviophobia">Aviophobia</a>, aviatophobia – fear of <a href="/wiki/Flight" title="Flight">flying</a></li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="B">B</span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Barophobia" title="Barophobia">Barophobia</a> – fear of <a href="/wiki/Gravity" title="Gravity">gravity</a> – there are two forms: fear of being crushed by gravity's pressure when it becomes too great, or fear that gravity will disappear causing everything and everyone to simply float away – also, fear of being overweight.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Campbell2009-p116_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-Campbell2009-p116-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-CTI_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-CTI-10">[10]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Fear_of_falling" title="Fear of falling">Basophobia</a> (also "basiphobia") – fear associated with <a href="/wiki/Astasia-abasia" title="Astasia-abasia">astasia-abasia</a> (fear of walking/standing erect) and a fear of falling</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Blood-injection-injury_type_phobia" title="Blood-injection-injury type phobia">Blood-injection-injury type phobia</a> – a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/DSM-IV" title="DSM-IV">DSM-IV</a> subtype of specific phobias</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="C">C</span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Chemophobia" title="Chemophobia">Chemophobia</a> – fear of <a href="/wiki/Chemical_substance" title="Chemical substance">chemicals</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chiroptophobia" title="Chiroptophobia">Chiroptophobia</a> – fear of <a href="/wiki/Bat" title="Bat">bats</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chlorophobia" title="Chlorophobia">Chlorophobia</a> – fear of the color <a href="/wiki/Green" title="Green">green</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lovescare_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-lovescare-11">[11]</a></sup></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chromophobia_(fear)" title="Chromophobia (fear)">Chromophobia</a>, chromatophobia – fear of <a href="/wiki/Color" title="Color">colors</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Chronophobia" title="Chronophobia">Chronophobia</a> – fear of time and time moving forward</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chrysophobia" title="Chrysophobia">Chrysophobia</a> – fear of the color <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Orange_(color)" title="Orange (color)">orange</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lovescare_11-1"><a href="#cite_note-lovescare-11">[11]</a></sup></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cibophobia" title="Cibophobia">Cibophobia</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sitophobia" title="Sitophobia">sitophobia</a> – aversion to <a href="/wiki/Food" title="Food">food</a>, synonymous to <a href="/wiki/Anorexia_nervosa" title="Anorexia nervosa">anorexia nervosa</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Claustrophobia" title="Claustrophobia">Claustrophobia</a> – fear of having no escape and being closed in</li>
<li>Cleithrophobia – fear of being trapped<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Coimetrophobia" title="Coimetrophobia">Coimetrophobia</a> – fear of <a href="/wiki/Cemetery" title="Cemetery">cemeteries</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Colorphobia" title="Colorphobia">Colorphobia</a> – fear or a strong aversion towards a particular color</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Coprophobia" title="Coprophobia">Coprophobia</a> – fear of <a href="/wiki/Feces" title="Feces">feces</a> or <a href="/wiki/Defecation" title="Defecation">defecation</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Campbell2009_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-Campbell2009-7">[7]</a></sup></li>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biology.2C_chemistry">Biology, chemistry</span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Biology" title="Biology">Biologists</a> use a number of <i>-phobia/-phobic</i> terms to describe predispositions by plants and animals against certain conditions. For antonyms, see <a href="/wiki/-phil-#Biology" title="-phil-">here</a></p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Acidophobe" title="Acidophobe">Acidophobia/Acidophobic</a> – preference for non-acidic conditions</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Heliophobia" title="Heliophobia">Heliophobia</a>/Heliophobic – aversion to <a href="/wiki/Sunlight" title="Sunlight">sunlight</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hydrophobe" title="Hydrophobe">Hydrophobia/Hydrophobic</a> – a property of being repelled by water</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Lipophobicity" title="Lipophobicity">Lipophobicity</a> – a property of fat rejection (sometimes also called <i>lipophobia</i>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Oleophobic" title="Oleophobic">Oleophobicity</a> – a property of oil rejection</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Photophobia_(biology)" title="Photophobia (biology)">Photophobia (biology)</a> – a negative <a href="/wiki/Phototaxis" title="Phototaxis">phototaxis</a> or <a href="/wiki/Phototropism" title="Phototropism">phototropism</a> response, or a tendency to stay out of the light</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Superhydrophobe" title="Superhydrophobe">Superhydrophobe</a> – the property given to materials that are extremely difficult to get wet</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Thermophobia" title="Thermophobia">Thermophobia</a> – aversion to <a href="/wiki/Heat" title="Heat">heat</a></li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Prejudices_and_discrimination">Prejudices and discrimination</span></h2>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Further information: <a href="/wiki/List_of_anti-cultural,_anti-national,_and_anti-ethnic_terms" title="List of anti-cultural, anti-national, and anti-ethnic terms">List of anti-cultural, anti-national, and anti-ethnic terms</a></div>
<p>The suffix <i>-phobia</i> is used to coin terms that denote a particular anti-ethnic or anti-demographic sentiment, such as <a href="/wiki/Anti-Americanism" title="Anti-Americanism">Americanophobia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Anti-Europeanism" title="Anti-Europeanism">Europhobia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Francophobia" title="Francophobia">Francophobia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hispanophobia" title="Hispanophobia">Hispanophobia</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Indophobia" title="Indophobia">Indophobia</a>. Often a synonym with the prefix "anti-" already exists (e.g. <a href="/wiki/Anti-Polish_sentiment" title="Anti-Polish sentiment">Polonophobia</a> vs. <a href="/wiki/Anti-Polish_sentiment" title="Anti-Polish sentiment">anti-Polonism</a>). Anti-religious sentiments are expressed in terms such as <a href="/wiki/Anti-Christian_sentiment" title="Anti-Christian sentiment">Christianophobia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Islamophobia" title="Islamophobia">Islamophobia</a>.</p>
<p>Other prejudices include:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Albanophobia" title="Albanophobia">Albanophobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Albanians" title="Albanians">Albanians</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Anglophobia" title="Anglophobia">Anglophobia</a> – fear/dislike of England or English culture</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Discrimination_against_atheists" title="Discrimination against atheists">Atheophobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Atheism" title="Atheism">atheists</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Biphobia" title="Biphobia">Biphobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Bisexuality" title="Bisexuality">bisexuality</a> or bisexuals</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Anti-Christian_sentiment" title="Anti-Christian sentiment">Christianophobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Christianity" title="Christianity">Christians</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ephebiphobia" title="Ephebiphobia">Ephebiphobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Youth" title="Youth">youth</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Anti-German_sentiment" title="Anti-German sentiment">Germanophobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Germans" title="Germans">Germans</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gerontophobia" title="Gerontophobia">Gerontophobia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gerascophobia" title="Gerascophobia">gerascophobia</a> – fear/dislike of aging or the <a href="/wiki/Old_age" title="Old age">elderly</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Heterophobia" title="Heterophobia">Heterophobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Heterosexuality" title="Heterosexuality">heterosexuals</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Anti-Hinduism" title="Anti-Hinduism">Hinduphobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Hindu" title="Hindu">Hindus</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hispanophobia" title="Hispanophobia">Hispanophobia</a> – fear/dislike of Hispanic people, Hispanic culture and the Spanish language</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Homophobia" title="Homophobia">Homophobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Homosexuality" title="Homosexuality">homosexuality</a>, homosexuals, or gays (as opposed to lesbians)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Indophobia" title="Indophobia">Indophobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Indian_culture" title="Indian culture">Indian culture</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Islamophobia" title="Islamophobia">Islamophobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Muslim" title="Muslim">Muslims</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Antisemitism" title="Antisemitism">Judeophobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Jews" title="Jews">Jews</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Lesbophobia" title="Lesbophobia">Lesbophobia</a> – fear/dislike of lesbians</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Racism" title="Racism">Negrophobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Black_people" title="Black people">black people</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Anti-Japanese_sentiment" title="Anti-Japanese sentiment">Nipponophobia</a> – fear/dislike of the <a href="/wiki/Japanese_people" title="Japanese people">Japanese</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Anti-Korean_sentiment" title="Anti-Korean sentiment">Koryophobia</a> – fear/dislike of the <a href="/wiki/Koreans" title="Koreans">Koreans</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pedophobia" title="Pedophobia">Pedophobia</a> – fear/dislike of children</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Anti-Polish_sentiment" title="Anti-Polish sentiment">Polonophobia</a> – fear/dislike of the <a href="/wiki/Poles" title="Poles">Polish</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Mentalism_(discrimination)" title="Mentalism (discrimination)">Psychophobia</a> – fear/dislike of mental illness or the <a href="/wiki/Mental_disorder" title="Mental disorder">mentally ill</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Russophobia" title="Russophobia">Russophobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Russians" title="Russians">Russians</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Shiaphobia" title="Shiaphobia">Shiaphobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Shiites" title="Shiites">Shiites</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sinophobia" title="Sinophobia">Sinophobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Chinese_people" title="Chinese people">Chinese people</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sunniphobia" title="Sunniphobia">Sunniphobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sunnis" title="Sunnis">Sunnis</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Transphobia" title="Transphobia">Transphobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Transgender" title="Transgender">transgender</a> people</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Anti-Turkism" title="Anti-Turkism">Turcophobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Turkish_people" title="Turkish people">Turks</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Xenophobia" title="Xenophobia">Xenophobia</a> – fear/dislike of <a href="/wiki/Alien_(law)" title="Alien (law)">foreigners</a> or <a href="/wiki/Extraterrestrial_life" title="Extraterrestrial life">extraterrestrials</a></li>
</ul>
