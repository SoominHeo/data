<p><b>Norður-Þingeyjarsýsla</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Counties_of_Iceland" title="Counties of Iceland">county</a> in northeastern <a href="/wiki/Iceland" title="Iceland">Iceland</a>. It included <a href="/wiki/Rifstangi" title="Rifstangi">Rifstangi</a>, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Extreme_points_of_Iceland" title="Extreme points of Iceland">northernmost point</a> in mainland Iceland.</p>
