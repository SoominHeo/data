<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Normandy Campaign</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <b>Battle of Normandy</b> or <b>Normandy Campaign</b> includes the following:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Operation_Overlord" title="Operation Overlord">Operation Overlord</a> – The Western Allied campaign in France from June 6 – August 25, 1944
<ul>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Invasion_of_Normandy" title="Invasion of Normandy">Invasion of Normandy</a>, or "Operation Neptune" – The initial part of Overlord, from June 6 – mid-July 1944
<ul>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Normandy_landings" title="Normandy landings">Normandy landings</a> on June 6, 1944</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Operation_Cobra" title="Operation Cobra">Operation Cobra</a>, the breakout from Normandy, starting on July 25, 1944</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>The "Battle of Normandy" is the official term for the British, French and Canadian military campaign lasting from June 6 – September 1, 1944.</p>
