<p>The <b>Nordderby</b> or <b>North derby</b> is a match between <a href="/wiki/Hamburger_SV" title="Hamburger SV">Hamburger SV</a> and <a href="/wiki/SV_Werder_Bremen" title="SV Werder Bremen">Werder Bremen</a>, the two most successful and popular clubs in Northern Germany. The game was first contested in 1927 with HSV recording a 4-1 away win. Since the founding of the Bundesliga in 1963, the match has been held twice every season, except in 1980–81, because Werder had been relegated to the 2. Bundesliga in the previous season. To date, 146 games have been contested with Werder holding a slight lead of 56 wins to HSV's 53. 41 games have ended in a draw.</p>
