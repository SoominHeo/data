<p><b>Nobukata Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">延方駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Nobukata-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>} is a <a href="/wiki/East_Japan_Railway_Company" title="East Japan Railway Company">JR East</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Railway_station" title="Railway station">railway station</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Itako,_Ibaraki" title="Itako, Ibaraki">Itako</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ibaraki_Prefecture" title="Ibaraki Prefecture">Ibaraki Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lines">Lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nobukata_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Nobukata Station is served by the <a href="/wiki/Kashima_Line" title="Kashima Line">Kashima Line</a>, and is located 10.4 km from the official starting point of the line at <a href="/wiki/Katori_Station" title="Katori Station">Katori Station</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_layout">Station layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nobukata_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Station layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Nobukata Station has one elevated <a href="/wiki/Island_platform" title="Island platform">island platform</a> with the station building underneath. The station is unattended.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nobukata_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Nobukata Station was opened on 20 August 1970. The station was absorbed into the JR East network upon the <a href="/wiki/Privatization" title="Privatization">privatization</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Japanese_National_Railways" title="Japanese National Railways">Japanese National Railways</a> (JNR) on 1 April 1987.</p>
