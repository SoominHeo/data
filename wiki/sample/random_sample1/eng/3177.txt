<p><b>Novy Urengoy</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Но́вый Уренго́й</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">city</a> in <a href="/wiki/Yamalo-Nenets_Autonomous_Okrug" title="Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Okrug">Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Okrug</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>. Population: <span class="nowrap">104,107 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2010)" title="Russian Census (2010)">2010 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2010Census_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-2010Census-3">[3]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">94,456 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2002)" title="Russian Census (2002)">2002 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PopCensus_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-PopCensus-5">[5]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">93,235 (<a href="/wiki/Soviet_Census_(1989)" title="Soviet Census (1989)">1989 Census</a>)</span>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census1989_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-Census1989-6">[6]</a></sup> It is the second largest city in the autonomous okrug after <a href="/wiki/Noyabrsk" title="Noyabrsk">Noyabrsk</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2010Census_3-2"><a href="#cite_note-2010Census-3">[3]</a></sup></p>

<p>It was founded in 1975 after the discovery of the <a href="/wiki/Urengoy_gas_field" title="Urengoy gas field">Urengoy gas field</a>, one of the largest in Russia.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (June 2010)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> Town status was granted to it in 1980.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Administrative_and_municipal_status">Administrative and municipal status</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Novy_Urengoy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Administrative and municipal status">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Within the <a href="/wiki/Subdivisions_of_Russia#Administrative_divisions" title="Subdivisions of Russia">framework of administrative divisions</a>, it is incorporated as the <b><a href="/wiki/City_of_federal_subject_significance" title="City of federal subject significance">city of okrug significance</a> of Novy Urengoy</b>—an administrative unit with the status equal to that of the <a href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_Yamalo-Nenets_Autonomous_Okrug" title="Administrative divisions of Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Okrug">districts</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ref928_1-4"><a href="#cite_note-Ref928-1">[1]</a></sup> As a <a href="/wiki/Subdivisions_of_Russia#Municipal_divisions" title="Subdivisions of Russia">municipal division</a>, the city of okrug significance of Novy Urengoy is incorporated as <b>Novy Urengoy Urban Okrug</b>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ref931_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-Ref931-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Climate">Climate</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Novy_Urengoy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Climate">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Novy Urengoy has a <a href="/wiki/Subarctic_climate" title="Subarctic climate">subarctic climate</a> (<a href="/wiki/K%C3%B6ppen_climate_classification" title="Köppen climate classification">Köppen climate classification</a> <i>Dfc</i>). Winters are severely cold and long with average temperatures from −30 °C (−22 °F) to −22 °C (−8 °F) in January, while summers are mild and brief with average temperatures from +10 °C (50 °F) to +18 °C (64 °F) in July. <a href="/wiki/Precipitation" title="Precipitation">Precipitation</a> is moderate, and is somewhat higher in summer than at other times of the year.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Economy">Economy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Novy_Urengoy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Economy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A major industry of the city is oil and gas production, with one of the largest <a href="/wiki/Urengoy_gas_field" title="Urengoy gas field">gas fields</a> in the world in the area and substantial prospects for further exploration. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Government_owned_corporation" title="Government owned corporation">Government owned</a> company <a href="/wiki/Gazprom" title="Gazprom">Gazprom</a> is the main local employer.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Transportation">Transportation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Novy_Urengoy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Transportation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The city is situated on the <a href="/wiki/Tyumen" title="Tyumen">Tyumen</a>–Novy Urengoy railway line. Traveling north from Tyumen, Novy Urengoy is the last station of significance. <a href="/wiki/Noyabrsk" title="Noyabrsk">Noyabrsk</a> is the previous station of significance.</p>
<p>Novy Urengoy is along the <a href="/wiki/Salekhard%E2%80%93Igarka_Railway" title="Salekhard–Igarka Railway">Salekhard–Igarka Railway</a>, "Dead Road ". Extending from Novy Urengoy to <a href="/wiki/Nadym" title="Nadym">Stary Nadym</a> is one section of the Railway of Death that is used as an important freight railway.</p>
<p>The city is served by the <a href="/wiki/Novy_Urengoy_Airport" title="Novy Urengoy Airport">Novy Urengoy Airport</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Sports">Sports</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Novy_Urengoy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Sports">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Fakel_Novy_Urengoy" title="Fakel Novy Urengoy">Fakel</a> is a men's volleyball team competing in the <a href="/wiki/Russian_Volleyball_Super_League" title="Russian Volleyball Super League">Russian Volleyball Super League</a> and playing at the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Gazodobytchik_Sports_Center&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Gazodobytchik Sports Center (page does not exist)">Gazodobytchik Sports Center</a>. In the 2008-09 season they finished 3-rd in the competition, behind <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Zenit_Kazan" title="Zenit Kazan">Zenit Kazan</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Iskra_Odintsovo" title="Iskra Odintsovo">Iskra Odintsovo</a>.</p>
