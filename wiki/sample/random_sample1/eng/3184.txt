<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Places">Places</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=North_Cape&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Places">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Nordkapp" title="Nordkapp">Nordkapp</a>, a municipality in northern Norway</li>

<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/North_Cape,_Norway" title="North Cape, Norway">North Cape, Norway</a>, a cape at the northernmost part of Magerøya island in Finnmark county, Norway</li>

<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/North_Cape,_New_Zealand" title="North Cape, New Zealand">North Cape, New Zealand</a>, a cape at the northern end of the North Auckland Peninsula in the North Island, New Zealand</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/North_Cape,_Wisconsin" title="North Cape, Wisconsin">North Cape, Wisconsin</a>, an unincorporated community in Racine County, Wisconsin, United States</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Northern_Cape" title="Northern Cape">Northern Cape</a>, a province of South Africa</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/North_Cape,_South_Australia" title="North Cape, South Australia">North Cape, South Australia</a>, a locality on Kangaroo Island in Australia</li>
</ul>
