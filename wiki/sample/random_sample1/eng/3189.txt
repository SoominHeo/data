<p><b>Noah Cornelius Marmaduke Huntley</b> (born 7 September 1974) is an English actor. He played <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Will_Curtis" title="Will Curtis">Will Curtis</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Holby_City" title="Holby City">Holby City</a></i>.</p>

<p>Huntley was born in <a href="/wiki/Wiston,_West_Sussex" title="Wiston, West Sussex">Wiston, West Sussex</a>, the son of Karen and Graham Huntley.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> He was educated at <a href="/wiki/Windlesham_House_School" title="Windlesham House School">Windlesham House School</a> in <a href="/wiki/Pulborough" title="Pulborough">Pulborough</a>, Sussex, at <a href="/wiki/Leighton_Park_School" title="Leighton Park School">Leighton Park School</a> in <a href="/wiki/Berkshire" title="Berkshire">Berkshire</a>, and at <a href="/wiki/Our_Lady_of_Sion_School" title="Our Lady of Sion School">Our Lady of Sion School</a> in <a href="/wiki/Worthing" title="Worthing">Worthing</a>.</p>
<p>His early acting career included roles in <i><a href="/wiki/28_Days_Later" title="28 Days Later">28 Days Later</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/True_Blue_(1996_film)" title="True Blue (1996 film)">True Blue</a></i>. He later appeared in <i><a href="/wiki/Snow_White_and_the_Huntsman" title="Snow White and the Huntsman">Snow White and the Huntsman</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Dracula_Untold" title="Dracula Untold">Dracula Untold</a></i>. On television, he played <a href="/wiki/Luke_McAllister" title="Luke McAllister">Luke McAllister</a> from 1993 to 1995 in the British soap opera <i><a href="/wiki/Emmerdale" title="Emmerdale">Emmerdale</a></i>. He played Will Curtis in <i><a href="/wiki/Holby_City" title="Holby City">Holby City</a></i> for the 2004–05 season, and Michael Dillon in the <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> drama <i><a href="/wiki/Dream_Team_(TV_series)" title="Dream Team (TV series)">Dream Team</a></i>.</p>
