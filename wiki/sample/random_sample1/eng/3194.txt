<p>The <b>Bezirk Neubrandenburg</b> was a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_the_German_Democratic_Republic" title="Administrative divisions of the German Democratic Republic">district</a> (<i>Bezirk</i>) of <a href="/wiki/East_Germany" title="East Germany">East Germany</a>. The administrative seat and the main town was <a href="/wiki/Neubrandenburg" title="Neubrandenburg">Neubrandenburg</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bezirk_Neubrandenburg&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The district was established, with the other 13, on July 25, 1952, substituting the old German <a href="/wiki/States_of_Germany" title="States of Germany">states</a>. After October 3, 1990, it was disestablished due to the <a href="/wiki/German_reunification" title="German reunification">German reunification</a>, becoming again mostly part of the state of <a href="/wiki/Mecklenburg-Vorpommern" title="Mecklenburg-Vorpommern">Mecklenburg-Vorpommern</a>. Also rural districts of Prenzlau and Templin (on 3 October 1990), municipalities of Nechlin, Wollschow, Woddow, Bagemühl and Grünberg with city of Brüssow in Pasewalk district and municipalities of Fahrenholz, Güterberg, Jagow, Lemmersdorf, Lübbenow, Milow, Trebenow, Wilsickow, Wismar (Uckerland) and Wolfshagen in Strasburg one part of the one of <a href="/wiki/Brandenburg" title="Brandenburg">Brandenburg</a> on 9 May 1992.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bezirk_Neubrandenburg&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Position">Position</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bezirk_Neubrandenburg&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Position">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Bezirk Neubrandenburg bordered with the <i>Bezirke</i> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rostock_(Bezirk)" title="Rostock (Bezirk)">Rostock</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Schwerin_(Bezirk)" title="Schwerin (Bezirk)">Schwerin</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Potsdam_(Bezirk)" title="Potsdam (Bezirk)">Potsdam</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Frankfurt_(Bezirk)" title="Frankfurt (Bezirk)">Frankfurt (Oder)</a>. It bordered also with <a href="/wiki/Poland" title="Poland">Poland</a> and a little part of it was located by the <a href="/wiki/Szczecin_Lagoon" title="Szczecin Lagoon">Stettin Bay</a>, a lagoon separated from the <a href="/wiki/Baltic_Sea" title="Baltic Sea">Baltic Sea</a>.</p>
