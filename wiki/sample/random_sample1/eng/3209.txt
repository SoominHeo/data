<p>The <b>Nokia X6</b> is a music-oriented <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Capacitive_touchscreen" title="Capacitive touchscreen">capacitive touchscreen</a> <a href="/wiki/Smartphone" title="Smartphone">smartphone</a> and portable entertainment device by <a href="/wiki/Nokia" title="Nokia">Nokia</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It was announced in early September 2009 during Nokia World 2009 in Germany.</p>
<p>The X6 replaces the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nokia_5800" title="Nokia 5800">Nokia 5800</a> as Nokia's flagship music-centred model.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Both still slot below some high-end touchscreen models like <a href="/wiki/Nokia_N97" title="Nokia N97">Nokia N97</a>.</p>
<p>The X6 and the <a href="/wiki/Nokia_X3-00" title="Nokia X3-00">Nokia X3-00</a> are the first devices in newly installed <a href="/wiki/Nokia_Xseries" title="Nokia Xseries">Nokia Xseries</a>. Before the Xseries, Nokia's music-centred devices were branded <a href="/wiki/XpressMusic" title="XpressMusic">XpressMusic</a>.</p>
<p>The original X6 includes the Comes With Music program and a licence for unlimited free downloads from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nokia_Music_Store" title="Nokia Music Store">Nokia Music Store</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The Comes With Music version shipped in late 2009 for an estimated retail price of £529.99 or €605.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Hardware_revisions">Hardware revisions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nokia_X6&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Hardware revisions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A version without Comes With Music support at €200 less was released on 23 February 2010. This version has 16 GB of on-board storage. Another cheaper variation of the X6 also without Comes With Music support was released by Nokia in mid-2010. This version has 8 GB of on-board storage and was released in <a href="/wiki/Asia" title="Asia">Asia</a> and North America. The 32 GB version was not released in India instead the 8 GB and 16 GB versions were provided with Ovi Music Unlimited offer.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Appearance">Appearance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nokia_X6&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Appearance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The X6 is notable for its slimmer body than the 5800 (13.8 mm) and 35 hours of continuous music playback and its fast 3g network. For social networking, it supports easy access to <a href="/wiki/Facebook" title="Facebook">Facebook</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/MySpace" title="MySpace">MySpace</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ovi_(Nokia)" title="Ovi (Nokia)">Ovi</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yahoo_IM" title="Yahoo IM">Yahoo IM</a>, <a href="/wiki/YouTube" title="YouTube">YouTube</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vkontakte" title="Vkontakte">VK</a>, <a href="/wiki/Windows_Live" title="Windows Live">Windows Live</a> and more.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Features">Features</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nokia_X6&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Features">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>WCDMA, GPRS/EDGE, HSDPA up to 3.6 Mbit/s</li>
<li>Size: 111 × 51 × 13.8 mm</li>
<li>Display: 3.2-inch (8.1 cm) 16:9 widescreen nHD, 231 ppi, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Capacitive_touchscreen" title="Capacitive touchscreen">capacitive touchscreen</a>.</li>
<li>Scratch-resistant screen</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/GPS_Phone" title="GPS Phone">Integrated</a> and <a href="/wiki/Assisted_GPS" title="Assisted GPS">Assisted</a> <a href="/wiki/Global_Positioning_System" title="Global Positioning System">GPS</a></li>
<li>5-megapixel camera with auto-focus and Carl Zeiss optics, dual LED flash</li>
<li>High speed <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Micro-USB" title="Micro-USB">Micro-USB</a> connector (Micro-B receptacle)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wireless_LAN" title="Wireless LAN">Wireless LAN</a> (WLAN)</li>
</ul>

<li>Downloadable Symbian (sis), Java applications, and widgets</li>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Operating_times">Operating times</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nokia_X6&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Operating times">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<li>Music playback: up to 35 hours</li>
<li>Video playback: up to 4 hours</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Firmware_updates">Firmware updates</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nokia_X6&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Firmware updates">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A new software update has been released for Nokia X6-00, version 40.0.002,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> which brings a new version of Ovi Maps and Ovi Store, improved browser and improved chat in Ovi Contacts.</p>
<ul>
<li>New web browser version 7.3.1.33</li>
<li>Updated Mail for Exchange</li>
<li>Updated Shazam</li>
<li>Usability improvements</li>
<li>Smileys in Messaging</li>
<li>Swipe to unlock screen and keys</li>
</ul>
<p>However the updates are not possible via the phone and require a Windows computer to perform. Nokia was willing to update the devices by mail for those that did not have a Windows PC.</p>
