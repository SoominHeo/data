<p>The <b>1998 South American Women's Football Championship</b> (Campeonato Sudamericano de Fútbol Femenino 1998) was held in <a href="/wiki/Mar_del_Plata" title="Mar del Plata">Mar del Plata</a>, <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentina</a> between March 1 &amp; 15. It was the third staging of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sudamericano_Femenino" title="Sudamericano Femenino">Sudamericano Femenino</a> and determined the <a href="/wiki/CONMEBOL" title="CONMEBOL">CONMEBOL</a>'s qualifiers for the <a href="/wiki/1999_FIFA_Women%27s_World_Cup" title="1999 FIFA Women's World Cup">1999 FIFA Women's World Cup</a>. The winner <a href="/wiki/Brazil_women%27s_national_football_team" title="Brazil women's national football team">Brazil</a> qualified directly. The runner-up <a href="/wiki/Argentina_women%27s_national_football_team" title="Argentina women's national football team">Argentina</a> played against <a href="/wiki/Mexico_women%27s_national_football_team" title="Mexico women's national football team">Mexico</a> in two play-off matches for qualification.</p>
<p>This was the first Sudamericano Femenino to feature all 10 CONMEBOL confederations' women's national teams.</p>
