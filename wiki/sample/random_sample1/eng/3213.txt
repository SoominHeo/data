<p><b>Notre Dame</b> commonly refers to:</p>
<ul>
<li><i>Notre Dame</i> (Our Lady), a French title for <a href="/wiki/Mary,_mother_of_Jesus" title="Mary, mother of Jesus">Mary, mother of Jesus</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Notre_Dame_de_Paris" title="Notre Dame de Paris">Notre Dame de Paris</a>, the most famous church with this name</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/University_of_Notre_Dame" title="University of Notre Dame">University of Notre Dame</a>, Indiana, United States
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Notre_Dame_Fighting_Irish" title="Notre Dame Fighting Irish">Notre Dame Fighting Irish</a>, this university's athletic teams
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Notre_Dame_Fighting_Irish_football" title="Notre Dame Fighting Irish football">Notre Dame Fighting Irish football</a>, by far the most notable Notre Dame team</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Notre_Dame_Educational_Association" title="Notre Dame Educational Association">Notre Dame Educational Association</a>, a network of Notre Dame Schools in the Philippines</li>
</ul>
