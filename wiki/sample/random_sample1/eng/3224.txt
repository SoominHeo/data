<p>In <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Alpha_taxonomy" title="Alpha taxonomy">taxonomy</a>, the <b>Chaetophorales</b> are an <a href="/wiki/Order_(biology)" title="Order (biology)">order</a> of <a href="/wiki/Green_algae" title="Green algae">green algae</a>, specifically the class <a href="/wiki/Chlorophyceae" title="Chlorophyceae">Chlorophyceae</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Algaebase_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Algaebase-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
