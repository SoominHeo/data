<p><b>Nongseong Station</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">농성역</span>; <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">Hanja</a>: <span lang="ko-Hani" xml:lang="ko-Hani">農城驛</span>) is a station of the <a href="/wiki/Gwangju_Metro_Line_1" title="Gwangju Metro Line 1">Gwangju Metro Line 1</a> in <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Nongseong-dong&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Nongseong-dong (page does not exist)">Nongseong-dong</a>, <a href="/wiki/Seo_District,_Gwangju" title="Seo District, Gwangju">Seo District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gwangju" title="Gwangju">Gwangju</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>.</p>
