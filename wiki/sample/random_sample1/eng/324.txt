<p>The domain name <b>moe</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Top-level_domain" title="Top-level domain">top-level domain</a> (TLD) in the <a href="/wiki/Domain_Name_System" title="Domain Name System">Domain Name System</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Internet" title="Internet">Internet</a>. Its name comes from the <a href="/wiki/Japanese_language" title="Japanese language">Japanese</a> <a href="/wiki/Slang" title="Slang">slang</a> word <i><a href="/wiki/Moe_(slang)" title="Moe (slang)">moe</a></i>, indicating its intended purpose in the marketing of products or services deemed <i>moe</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-CR-news1_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-CR-news1-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=.moe&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Interlink began developing the <span style="font-family: monospace, monospace;">moe</span> <a href="/wiki/Top-level_domain" title="Top-level domain">top-level domain</a> (TLD) in 2012. On November 13, 2013, <a href="/wiki/ICANN" title="ICANN">ICANN</a> and Interlink entered into a <a href="/wiki/Domain_name_registry" title="Domain name registry">registry</a> agreement under which Interlink operates the <span style="font-family: monospace, monospace;">moe</span> TLD.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Interlink sponsored a contest held between April 11 and May 6, 2014 to design the domain's logo.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> The general registration period began on July 22, 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-CR-news1_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-CR-news1-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
