<p><i><b>Leu</b></i> (or <i><b>Low</b></i>) <i>sieu</i> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">娄宿</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">婁宿</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Lóu Xiù</span>) meaning "the Train of a garment", was one of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Twenty-eight_mansions" title="Twenty-eight mansions">twenty-eight lunar mansions</a> of the traditional <a href="/wiki/Chinese_astronomy" title="Chinese astronomy">Chinese astronomy</a>. It was one of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_Tiger_(Chinese_constellation)" title="White Tiger (Chinese constellation)">White Tiger</a> of the West （西方白虎）.</p>
