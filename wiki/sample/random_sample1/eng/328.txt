<p><b><a href="/wiki/October" title="October">October</a></b> <b><a href="/wiki/1999" title="1999">1999</a></b> was the tenth month of that common year. The month, which began on a <a href="/wiki/Friday" title="Friday">Friday</a>, ended on a <a href="/wiki/Sunday" title="Sunday">Sunday</a> after 31 days.</p>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Pakistan" title="Pakistan">Pakistani</a> Prime Minister <a href="/wiki/Nawaz_Sharif" title="Nawaz Sharif">Nawaz Sharif</a> attempts to dismiss Army Chief General <a href="/wiki/Pervez_Musharraf" title="Pervez Musharraf">Pervez Musharraf</a> and install <a href="/wiki/Inter-Services_Intelligence" title="Inter-Services Intelligence">ISI</a> director <a href="/wiki/Ziauddin_Butt" title="Ziauddin Butt">Ziauddin Butt</a> in his place. Senior Army generals refuse to accept the dismissal. Musharraf, who was out of the country, attempts to return in a commercial airliner. Sharif orders the Karachi airport to not allow the plane to land. The generals lead a <a href="/wiki/Coup_d%27%C3%A9tat" title="Coup d'état">coup</a>, ousting Sharif's administration and taking over the airport. The plane lands with only a few minutes of fuel to spare, and Musharraf takes control of the government.</li>
<li>The 6 billionth <a href="/wiki/Person" title="Person">person</a> in the world, according to the <a href="/wiki/United_Nations" title="United Nations">UN</a> is born in <a href="/wiki/Sarajevo" title="Sarajevo">Sarajevo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bosnia_and_Herzegovina" title="Bosnia and Herzegovina">Bosnia and Herzegovina</a>.</li>
