<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">New Zealand national baseball team</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <b>New Zealand national baseball team</b>, also known as the <b>Diamondblacks</b>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is the representative team at international level for <a href="/wiki/New_Zealand" title="New Zealand">New Zealand</a>. The team is controlled by <a href="/wiki/Baseball_New_Zealand" title="Baseball New Zealand">Baseball New Zealand</a>, the country's governing body. The team competes in the <a href="/wiki/Oceania_Baseball_Championship" title="Oceania Baseball Championship">Oceania Championship</a>, where in 2007 withdrew from play giving Australia an automatic berth into the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Baseball_at_the_2008_Summer_Olympics_-_Qualification" title="Baseball at the 2008 Summer Olympics - Qualification">Final qualification tournament</a> for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2008_Olympics" title="2008 Olympics">2008 Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The "Diamondblacks" nickname is <a href="/wiki/New_Zealand_national_team_nomenclature_based_on_the_%22All_Blacks%22" title='New Zealand national team nomenclature based on the "All Blacks"'>one of many</a> national team nicknames related to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/All_Blacks" title="All Blacks">All Blacks</a>.</p>
<p>Former Major League baseball infielder and current Los Angeles Dodgers third base coach Chris Woodward managed New Zealand in the 2017 World Baseball Classic qualification tournament in Australia in 2016 which did not get past the qualification round for the 2017 tournament.</p>
