<p><i><b>Campsis</b></i> (trumpet creeper, trumpet vine) is a <a href="/wiki/Genus" title="Genus">genus</a> of <a href="/wiki/Flowering_plant" title="Flowering plant">flowering plants</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Family_(biology)" title="Family (biology)">family</a> <a href="/wiki/Bignoniaceae" title="Bignoniaceae">Bignoniaceae</a>, <a href="/wiki/Native_plant" title="Native plant">native</a> to woodland in China and North America.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It consists of two <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a>, both of which are vigorous <a href="/wiki/Deciduous" title="Deciduous">deciduous</a> <a href="/wiki/Perennial_plant" title="Perennial plant">perennial</a> <a href="/wiki/Vine" title="Vine">climbers</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Hunter2010_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Hunter2010-2">[2]</a></sup> clinging by <a href="/wiki/Aerial_root" title="Aerial root">aerial roots</a>, and producing large trumpet-shaped flowers in the summer.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Fowler1997_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Fowler1997-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Kirsten2001_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Kirsten2001-4">[4]</a></sup> They are <a href="/wiki/Hardiness_(plants)" title="Hardiness (plants)">hardy</a> but require the shelter of a warm wall in full sun.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Fowler1997_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-Fowler1997-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
