<p><b>Nina Proll</b> (born 12 January 1974) is an Austrian actress. She has appeared in more than 50 films and television shows since 1995. She starred in the film <i><a href="/wiki/September_(2003_film)" title="September (2003 film)">September</a></i>, which was screened in the <a href="/wiki/Un_Certain_Regard" title="Un Certain Regard">Un Certain Regard</a> section at the <a href="/wiki/2003_Cannes_Film_Festival" title="2003 Cannes Film Festival">2003 Cannes Film Festival</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-festival-cannes.com_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-festival-cannes.com-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 1999 she received the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Marcello_Mastroianni_Award" title="Marcello Mastroianni Award">Marcello Mastroianni Award</a> given out at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Venice_International_Film_Festival" title="Venice International Film Festival">Venice International Film Festival</a>.</p>
<p>Proll participated in the third season of the Austrian television dance competition <i><a href="/wiki/Dancing_Stars_(Austrian_TV_series)" title="Dancing Stars (Austrian TV series)">Dancing Stars</a></i> in 2007, coming in 5th place.</p>
