<p><b>Nicole Saba</b> (Nicole Chaba Elias) (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar" xml:lang="ar">نيكول سابا</span>‎‎) is a singer and actress from <a href="/wiki/Lebanon" title="Lebanon">Lebanon</a>.</p>
<p>From 1998-2001, Nicole Saba was a member of the Lebanese pop group <a href="/wiki/The_4_Cats" title="The 4 Cats">The 4 Cats</a>, She replaced "Roula Bahnam" who officially shot with the band in their first video "Ashra HdashEtnash", Nicole released 2 albums with the 4Cats "Tic - Tick" and "Layl Nhar" and shot 4 video clips with them "Yanassini, Kan Ezaman, Layl Nhar and Maba2a Eida" and shot the band's commercial for LUX Shower Gel. Then embarked on a solo singing as well as an acting career. Her debut album was released in 2004, and her first film came out in 2003. She has subsequently had success with additional roles in Egyptian-made films.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4cats1_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-4cats1-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Nicole was given the award for the best singer in 2004 by <i><a href="/wiki/Sayidaty" title="Sayidaty">Sayidaty</a></i> magazine<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (June 2008)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Filmography">Filmography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nicole_Saba&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Filmography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>2003: <i>El Tagroba Al Denemarkeya</i> (<i><a href="/wiki/The_Danish_Experience" title="The Danish Experience">The Danish Experience</a></i>) was Nicole's first acting experience.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-acting1_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-acting1-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-act2_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-act2-3">[3]</a></sup> Nicole was chosen by the Egyptian actor <a href="/wiki/Adel_Emam" title="Adel Emam">Adel Emam</a> to co-lead the movie with him. Nicole played the role of a Danish girl called "Anita" who went to stay for a while with an Egyptian family "Kadri and his sons" as she changes their lives totally, the movie was a hit in 2003 and made it to the top at the box office and was well accepted by the press.</li>
<li>2006: <i>Tomn Dastet Ashrar</i> was Nicole's second experience in acting as she joined a group of stars such as Yasmien Abdel Aziz, Mohamed Ragab and Khaled Saleh as they all come together to form a big robbery group and on the way they help their friend who falls in love. Nicole plays "Lily" the one responsible to help their friend with his love story.</li>
<li>2006: <i>Kesit el Hai El Sha'abi</i> (Arabic: قصة الحي الشعبي) (The Story of the Common Neighborhood)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-story1_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-story1-4">[4]</a></sup> is Nicole's third acting experience, and is considered to be the first movie she plays the main heroine in. In this movie Nicole plays as the daughter of an old singer who her bands plans to continue playing their music as they start searching for Nicole to take her mother's place. Although the movie wasn't a hit at the box office but it was still a good step for Nicole to make as she experienced playing as an upper Egyptian woman, and many other different characters that she never had the chance to play before.</li>
<li>2008: <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_BabyDoll_Night_(film)" title="The BabyDoll Night (film)">The BabyDoll Night (film)</a></i> (<i>Leilet El-Baby Doll</i>) (Arabic: ليلة البيبي دول)</li>
<li>2008: <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Special_Missions_(film)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Special Missions (film) (page does not exist)">Special Missions (film)</a></i> (<i>Amaleyat Khasaa</i>) (Arabic: عمليات خاصة)</li>
<li>2009: <i>Al-Saffah</i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-saffahreview_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-saffahreview-5">[5]</a></sup> (<i>The Criminal</i> or <i>The Serial Killer</i>) (Arabic: السفاح)</li>
<li>2012: <i><a href="/wiki/Baba_(2012_film)" title="Baba (2012 film)">Baba (film)</a></i> (<i>Baba</i>) (Arabic: بابا)</li>
<li>2013: <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Samir_Abou_Alneil,_(Samir_the_father_of_the_Nile)_(film)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Samir Abou Alneil, (Samir the father of the Nile) (film) (page does not exist)">Samir Abou Alneil, (Samir the father of the Nile) (film)</a></i> (<i>Sameer Abool Neeel</i>) (Arabic: سمير ابو النيل)</li>
</ul>
