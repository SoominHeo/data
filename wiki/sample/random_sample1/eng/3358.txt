<p><b>Nicole Lyn Oliver</b> (born February 22, 1970) is a Canadian <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Actress" title="Actress">actress</a>. She is best known for her roles as Princess Celestia and Cheerilee in <i><a href="/wiki/My_Little_Pony:_Friendship_Is_Magic" title="My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic">My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic</a></i> and Zoe Trent in <i><a href="/wiki/Littlest_Pet_Shop_(2012_TV_series)" title="Littlest Pet Shop (2012 TV series)">Littlest Pet Shop</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nicole_Oliver&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Oliver was born in <a href="/wiki/Ottawa" title="Ottawa">Ottawa</a>, Ontario. She has a <a href="/wiki/Bachelor_of_Fine_Arts" title="Bachelor of Fine Arts">Bachelor of Fine Arts</a> from <a href="/wiki/York_University" title="York University">York University</a>, a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Masters_degree" title="Masters degree">Masters degree</a> in Communication from <a href="/wiki/Royal_Roads_University" title="Royal Roads University">Royal Roads University</a>, and additional training from the <a href="/wiki/British_American_Drama_Academy" title="British American Drama Academy">British American Drama Academy</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nicole_Oliver&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A prominent member of the Union of BC Performers, Oliver served on its executive board (2002–2005), chaired several committees, and continues to be part of the negotiation team during <a href="/wiki/Collective_bargaining" title="Collective bargaining">collective bargaining</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nicole_Oliver&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Oliver is married to Chris Ainscough, an award-winning film composer. They live in <a href="/wiki/Vancouver" title="Vancouver">Vancouver</a> with their two sons.</p>
