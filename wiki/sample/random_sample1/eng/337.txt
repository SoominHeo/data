<p><b>January 2</b> is the second day of the year in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 363 days remaining until the end of the year (364 in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>). This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Monday, Wednesday or Saturday (58 in 400 years each) than on Thursday or Friday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Tuesday or Sunday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=January_2&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/366" title="366">366</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Alemanni" title="Alemanni">Alemanni</a> cross the frozen <a href="/wiki/Rhine" title="Rhine">Rhine</a> in large numbers, invading the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Empire" title="Roman Empire">Roman Empire</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/533" title="533">533</a> – Mercurius becomes <a href="/wiki/Pope_John_II" title="Pope John II">Pope John II</a>, the first pope to adopt a new name upon elevation to the <a href="/wiki/Pope" title="Pope">papacy</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1492" title="1492">1492</a> – <a href="/wiki/Reconquista" title="Reconquista">Reconquista</a>: the <a href="/wiki/Emirate_of_Granada" title="Emirate of Granada">Emirate of Granada</a>, the last <a href="/wiki/Moors" title="Moors">Moorish</a> stronghold in Spain, surrenders.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1777" title="1777">1777</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Revolutionary_War" title="American Revolutionary War">American Revolutionary War</a>: American forces under the command of <a href="/wiki/George_Washington" title="George Washington">George Washington</a> repulsed a British attack at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Assunpink_Creek" title="Battle of the Assunpink Creek">Battle of the Assunpink Creek</a> near <a href="/wiki/Trenton,_New_Jersey" title="Trenton, New Jersey">Trenton, New Jersey</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1788" title="1788">1788</a> – <a href="/wiki/Georgia_(U.S._state)" title="Georgia (U.S. state)">Georgia</a> becomes the fourth state to ratify the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Constitution" title="United States Constitution">United States Constitution</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1791" title="1791">1791</a> – <a href="/wiki/Big_Bottom_massacre" title="Big Bottom massacre">Big Bottom massacre</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Ohio_Country" title="Ohio Country">Ohio Country</a>, marking the beginning of the <a href="/wiki/Northwest_Indian_War" title="Northwest Indian War">Northwest Indian War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1818" title="1818">1818</a> – The British <a href="/wiki/Institution_of_Civil_Engineers" title="Institution of Civil Engineers">Institution of Civil Engineers</a> is founded.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1833" title="1833">1833</a> – <a href="/wiki/Reassertion_of_British_sovereignty_over_the_Falkland_Islands_(1833)" title="Reassertion of British sovereignty over the Falkland Islands (1833)">Reassertion of British sovereignty over the Falkland Islands</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1860" title="1860">1860</a> – The discovery of the planet <a href="/wiki/Vulcan_(hypothetical_planet)" title="Vulcan (hypothetical planet)">Vulcan</a> is announced at a meeting of the <a href="/wiki/French_Academy_of_Sciences" title="French Academy of Sciences">French Academy of Sciences</a> in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris, France</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1863" title="1863">1863</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Civil_War" title="American Civil War">American Civil War</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Stones_River" title="Battle of Stones River">Battle of Stones River</a> (a.k.a. Battle of Murfreesboro) resumes in <a href="/wiki/Middle_Tennessee" title="Middle Tennessee">central Tennessee</a> after a day's respite, resulting in a significant Union victory.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1865" title="1865">1865</a> – <a href="/wiki/Uruguayan_War" title="Uruguayan War">Uruguayan War</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Paysand%C3%BA" title="Siege of Paysandú">Siege of Paysandú</a> ends as <a href="/wiki/Empire_of_Brazil" title="Empire of Brazil">Brazilian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Colorado_Party_(Uruguay)" title="Colorado Party (Uruguay)">Coloradans</a> capture <a href="/wiki/Paysand%C3%BA" title="Paysandú">Paysandú</a>, <a href="/wiki/Uruguay" title="Uruguay">Uruguay</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1900" title="1900">1900</a> – American statesman and diplomat <a href="/wiki/John_Hay" title="John Hay">John Hay</a> announces the <a href="/wiki/Open_Door_Policy" title="Open Door Policy">Open Door Policy</a> to promote trade with China.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1905" title="1905">1905</a> – <a href="/wiki/Russo-Japanese_War" title="Russo-Japanese War">Russo-Japanese War</a>: The Russian garrison surrenders at <a href="/wiki/L%C3%BCshunkou_District" title="Lüshunkou District">Port Arthur</a>, China.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1920" title="1920">1920</a> – The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Second_Palmer_Raid" title="Second Palmer Raid">second Palmer Raid</a> takes place with another 6,000 suspected communists and anarchists arrested and held without trial. These raids take place in several U.S. cities.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1941" title="1941">1941</a> – <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a>: <a href="/wiki/Strategic_bombing_during_World_War_II" title="Strategic bombing during World War II">German bombing</a> severely damages the <a href="/wiki/Llandaff_Cathedral" title="Llandaff Cathedral">Llandaff Cathedral</a> in <a href="/wiki/Cardiff" title="Cardiff">Cardiff</a>, Wales, United Kingdom.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1942" title="1942">1942</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Federal_Bureau_of_Investigation" title="Federal Bureau of Investigation">Federal Bureau of Investigation</a> (FBI) convicts 33 members of a German spy ring headed by <a href="/wiki/Fritz_Joubert_Duquesne" title="Fritz Joubert Duquesne">Fritz Joubert Duquesne</a> in the largest espionage case in United States history—the <a href="/wiki/Duquesne_Spy_Ring" title="Duquesne Spy Ring">Duquesne Spy Ring</a>.</li>
<li>1942 – World War II: <a href="/wiki/Manila" title="Manila">Manila</a>, Philippines is captured by Japanese forces.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1945" title="1945">1945</a> – World War II: <a href="/wiki/Nuremberg" title="Nuremberg">Nuremberg</a>, Germany is severely bombed by <a href="/wiki/Allies_of_World_War_II" title="Allies of World War II">Allied</a> forces.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1949" title="1949">1949</a> – <a href="/wiki/Luis_Mu%C3%B1oz_Mar%C3%ADn" title="Luis Muñoz Marín">Luis Muñoz Marín</a> becomes the first democratically elected <a href="/wiki/Governor_of_Puerto_Rico" title="Governor of Puerto Rico">Governor of Puerto Rico</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1955" title="1955">1955</a> – <a href="/wiki/Panama" title="Panama">Panamanian</a> president <a href="/wiki/Jos%C3%A9_Antonio_Rem%C3%B3n_Cantera" title="José Antonio Remón Cantera">José Antonio Remón Cantera</a> is assassinated.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1959" title="1959">1959</a> – <i><a href="/wiki/Luna_1" title="Luna 1">Luna 1</a></i>, the first spacecraft to reach the vicinity of the Moon and to orbit the Sun, is launched by the <a href="/wiki/Soviet_Union" title="Soviet Union">Soviet Union</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1963" title="1963">1963</a> – <a href="/wiki/Vietnam_War" title="Vietnam War">Vietnam War</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Viet_Cong" title="Viet Cong">Viet Cong</a> wins its first <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Ap_Bac" title="Battle of Ap Bac">major victory</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1967" title="1967">1967</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ronald_Reagan" title="Ronald Reagan">Ronald Reagan</a> sworn in as <a href="/wiki/Governor_of_California" title="Governor of California">Governor of California</a></li>
