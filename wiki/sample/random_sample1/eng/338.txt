<p><b>January 9</b> is the ninth day of the year in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 356 days remaining until the end of the year (357 in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>). This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Monday, Wednesday or Saturday (58 in 400 years each) than on Thursday or Friday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Tuesday or Sunday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=January_9&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/475" title="475">475</a> – <a href="/wiki/List_of_Byzantine_emperors" title="List of Byzantine emperors">Byzantine Emperor</a> <a href="/wiki/Zeno_(emperor)" title="Zeno (emperor)">Zeno</a> is forced to flee his capital at <a href="/wiki/Constantinople" title="Constantinople">Constantinople</a>, and his general, <a href="/wiki/Basiliscus" title="Basiliscus">Basiliscus</a> gains control of the empire.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/681" title="681">681</a> – <a href="/wiki/Twelfth_Council_of_Toledo" title="Twelfth Council of Toledo">Twelfth Council of Toledo</a>: King <a href="/wiki/Erwig" title="Erwig">Erwig</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Visigoths" title="Visigoths">Visigoths</a> initiates a council in which he implements diverse measures against the Jews in Spain.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1127" title="1127">1127</a> – <a href="/wiki/Jin%E2%80%93Song_Wars" title="Jin–Song Wars">Jin–Song Wars</a>: Invading <a href="/wiki/Jurchen_people" title="Jurchen people">Jurchen</a> soldiers from the <a href="/wiki/Jin_dynasty_(1115%E2%80%931234)" title="Jin dynasty (1115–1234)">Jin dynasty</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jingkang_Incident" title="Jingkang Incident">besiege and sack</a> <a href="/wiki/Kaifeng" title="Kaifeng">Bianjing</a> (Kaifeng), the capital of the <a href="/wiki/Song_dynasty" title="Song dynasty">Song dynasty</a> of China, and abduct <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Emperor_Qinzong_of_Song" title="Emperor Qinzong of Song">Emperor Qinzong of Song</a> and others, ending the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Northern_Song_dynasty" title="Northern Song dynasty">Northern Song dynasty</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1150" title="1150">1150</a> – <a href="/wiki/Wanyan_Liang" title="Wanyan Liang">Wanyan Liang</a> and other court officials murder <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Xizong_of_Jin" title="Emperor Xizong of Jin">Emperor Xizong of Jin</a>. Wanyan Liang succeeds him as emperor.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1349" title="1349">1349</a> – The Jewish population of <a href="/wiki/Basel" title="Basel">Basel</a>, believed by the residents to be the cause of the ongoing <a href="/wiki/Black_Death" title="Black Death">Black Death</a>, is <a href="/wiki/Basel_massacre" title="Basel massacre">rounded up and incinerated</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1431" title="1431">1431</a> – Judges' investigations for the trial of <a href="/wiki/Joan_of_Arc" title="Joan of Arc">Joan of Arc</a> begin in <a href="/wiki/Rouen" title="Rouen">Rouen, France</a>, the seat of the English occupation government.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1760" title="1760">1760</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ahmad_Shah_Durrani" title="Ahmad Shah Durrani">Ahmad Shah Durrani</a> defeats the <a href="/wiki/Maratha" title="Maratha">Marathas</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Indian_Campaign_of_Ahmad_Shah_Durrani" title="Indian Campaign of Ahmad Shah Durrani">Battle of Barari Ghat</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1788" title="1788">1788</a> – <a href="/wiki/Connecticut" title="Connecticut">Connecticut</a> becomes the fifth state to ratify the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Constitution" title="United States Constitution">Constitution</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1792" title="1792">1792</a> – <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Jassy" title="Treaty of Jassy">Treaty of Jassy</a> between Russian and Ottoman Empire is signed.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1793" title="1793">1793</a> – <a href="/wiki/Jean-Pierre_Blanchard" title="Jean-Pierre Blanchard">Jean-Pierre Blanchard</a> becomes the first person to fly in a <a href="/wiki/Balloon" title="Balloon">balloon</a> in the United States.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1799" title="1799">1799</a> – <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_the_United_Kingdom" title="Prime Minister of the United Kingdom">British Prime Minister</a> <a href="/wiki/William_Pitt_the_Younger" title="William Pitt the Younger">William Pitt the Younger</a> introduces an <a href="/wiki/Income_tax" title="Income tax">income tax</a> of two <a href="/wiki/Shilling" title="Shilling">shillings</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Pound_sterling" title="Pound sterling">pound</a> to raise funds for Great Britain's war effort in the <a href="/wiki/Napoleonic_Wars" title="Napoleonic Wars">Napoleonic Wars</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1806" title="1806">1806</a> – <a href="/wiki/Horatio_Nelson,_1st_Viscount_Nelson" title="Horatio Nelson, 1st Viscount Nelson">Admiral Horatio Lord Nelson</a> receives a <a href="/wiki/State_funeral" title="State funeral">state funeral</a> and is interred in <a href="/wiki/St_Paul%27s_Cathedral" title="St Paul's Cathedral">St Paul's Cathedral</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1816" title="1816">1816</a> – Sir <a href="/wiki/Humphry_Davy" title="Humphry Davy">Humphry Davy</a> tests <a href="/wiki/Davy_lamp" title="Davy lamp">his safety lamp</a> for miners at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hebburn_Colliery" title="Hebburn Colliery">Hebburn Colliery</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1822" title="1822">1822</a> – The Portuguese prince <a href="/wiki/Pedro_I_of_Brazil" title="Pedro I of Brazil">Pedro I of Brazil</a> decides to stay in <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a> against the orders of the Portuguese King <a href="/wiki/John_VI_of_Portugal" title="John VI of Portugal">João VI</a>, beginning the Brazilian independence process.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1839" title="1839">1839</a> – The <a href="/wiki/French_Academy_of_Sciences" title="French Academy of Sciences">French Academy of Sciences</a> announces the <a href="/wiki/Daguerreotype" title="Daguerreotype">Daguerreotype</a> <a href="/wiki/Photography" title="Photography">photography</a> process.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1857" title="1857">1857</a> – The 7.9 <a href="/wiki/Moment_magnitude_scale" title="Moment magnitude scale">Mw</a> <a href="/wiki/1857_Fort_Tejon_earthquake" title="1857 Fort Tejon earthquake">Fort Tejon earthquake</a> shakes <a href="/wiki/Central_California" title="Central California">Central</a> and <a href="/wiki/Southern_California" title="Southern California">Southern California</a> with a maximum <a href="/wiki/Mercalli_intensity_scale" title="Mercalli intensity scale">Mercalli intensity</a> of IX (<i>Violent</i>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1858" title="1858">1858</a> – <a href="/wiki/Anson_Jones" title="Anson Jones">Anson Jones</a>, the last President of the <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Texas" title="Republic of Texas">Republic of Texas</a>, commits suicide.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1861" title="1861">1861</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Civil_War" title="American Civil War">American Civil War</a>: "<a href="/wiki/Star_of_the_West" title="Star of the West">Star of the West</a>" incident occurs near <a href="/wiki/Charleston,_South_Carolina" title="Charleston, South Carolina">Charleston, South Carolina</a>.</li>
<li>1861 – <a href="/wiki/Mississippi" title="Mississippi">Mississippi</a> becomes the second state to secede from the Union before the outbreak of the American Civil War.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1863" title="1863">1863</a> – American Civil War: The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Fort_Hindman" title="Battle of Fort Hindman">Battle of Fort Hindman</a> begins in <a href="/wiki/Arkansas" title="Arkansas">Arkansas</a>.</li>
