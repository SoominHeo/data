<p><b>Nicholas "Nicky" Katt</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> (born May 11, 1970) is an <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">American</a> actor known for his role as unorthodox teacher Harry Senate on <a href="/wiki/David_E._Kelley" title="David E. Kelley">David E. Kelley</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Fox_Broadcasting_Company" title="Fox Broadcasting Company">Fox</a> <a href="/wiki/Drama" title="Drama">drama</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Boston_Public" title="Boston Public">Boston Public</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nicky_Katt&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Katt has costarred in many <a href="/wiki/Film" title="Film">movies</a> and received critical praise for performances in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Limey" title="The Limey">The Limey</a></i> and <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/SubUrbia" title="SubUrbia">SubUrbia</a></i>. Katt often plays unsympathetic characters, such as an irascible motorhead in <i><a href="/wiki/Dazed_and_Confused_(film)" title="Dazed and Confused (film)">Dazed and Confused</a></i> with <a href="/wiki/Ben_Affleck" title="Ben Affleck">Ben Affleck</a>, an ill-fated racist child <a href="/wiki/Rape" title="Rape">rapist</a> in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/A_Time_to_Kill_(film)" title="A Time to Kill (film)">A Time to Kill</a></i> and an embittered business rival in <i><a href="/wiki/Boiler_Room_(film)" title="Boiler Room (film)">Boiler Room</a></i>. He was originally cast to star in the <a href="/wiki/American_Broadcasting_Company" title="American Broadcasting Company">ABC</a> Spring 2006 replacement series <i><a href="/wiki/The_Evidence_(TV_series)" title="The Evidence (TV series)">The Evidence</a></i>, but was replaced by <a href="/wiki/Rob_Estes" title="Rob Estes">Rob Estes</a>. Katt was a child actor as well, appearing as <a href="/wiki/Marc_Singer" title="Marc Singer">Marc Singer</a>'s son on the TV series <i><a href="/wiki/V_(1984_TV_series)" title="V (1984 TV series)">V</a></i>. He also lent his voice in gaming as the voice of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Atton_Rand" title="Atton Rand">Atton Rand</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Wars_Knights_of_the_Old_Republic_II:_The_Sith_Lords" title="Star Wars Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords">Star Wars Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords</a></i>.</p>
