<p><b>Emi Nitta</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">新田 恵海</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Nitta Emi</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born December 10, 1985)</span> is a Japanese <a href="/wiki/Voice_acting_in_Japan" title="Voice acting in Japan">voice actress</a> and singer from <a href="/wiki/Nagano_Prefecture" title="Nagano Prefecture">Nagano Prefecture</a> affiliated with the management company S.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-s-prof_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-s-prof-1">[1]</a></sup> She became a voice actress after passing an audition organized by S.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-s-prof_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-s-prof-1">[1]</a></sup> She is best known for her roles as Honoka Kōsaka in <i><a href="/wiki/Love_Live!" title="Love Live!">Love Live!</a></i> and Tokoha Anjō in <i><a href="/wiki/Cardfight!!_Vanguard" title="Cardfight!! Vanguard">Cardfight!! Vanguard</a> G</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-s-prof_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-s-prof-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ann-1_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-ann-1-2">[2]</a></sup> She made her anime voice acting debut as Ricca Morizono in <i><a href="/wiki/Da_Capo_III" title="Da Capo III">Da Capo III</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-s-prof_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-s-prof-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-koe-1_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-koe-1-3">[3]</a></sup> She made an appearance at the Bangkok Comic Con in July 2014.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Emi_Nitta&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In February 2015, Nitta's talent agency announced she would be suspending activities for two months to receive treatment for her <a href="/wiki/Vocal_fold_nodule" title="Vocal fold nodule">vocal cord polyps</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> She returned to work mid-April after non-invasive treatment<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2016, Weekly Asahi Geinou ran an article accusing Nitta of appearing in an <a href="/wiki/Pornography" title="Pornography">AV video</a> prior to her voice acting debut, due to similarities in looks and the placement of certain moles between Nitta and the AV actress in question.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> In response, S-inc published a statement denying Nitta's involvement.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ANN_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-ANN-7">[7]</a></sup> Due to threats from fans who remain convinced Nitta appeared in the video, security was increased at her live appearances.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Emi_Nitta&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Nitta was born in Nagano Prefecture. As well as singing, she also plays the piano. Her hobbies include visiting cafes, <a href="/wiki/Karaoke" title="Karaoke">karaoke</a>, and studying dinosaurs.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
