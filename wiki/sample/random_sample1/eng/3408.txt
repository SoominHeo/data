<p><b>Daniel Núñez</b> (born 12 September 1958 in <a href="/wiki/Santiago_de_Cuba" title="Santiago de Cuba">Santiago de Cuba</a>) is a Cuban <a href="/wiki/Olympic_weightlifting" title="Olympic weightlifting">weightlifter</a>.</p>
<p>He won a gold medal in the <i>bantamweight class</i> at the <a href="/wiki/1979_Pan_American_Games" title="1979 Pan American Games">1979 Pan American Games</a> in <a href="/wiki/San_Juan,_Puerto_Rico" title="San Juan, Puerto Rico">San Juan</a> and at the <a href="/wiki/1980_Summer_Olympics" title="1980 Summer Olympics">1980 Summer Olympics</a> in Moscow.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sports-ref_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-sports-ref-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Núñez set a world record for the <a href="/wiki/Snatch_(weightlifting)" title="Snatch (weightlifting)">snatch</a> at the <a href="/wiki/1982_Central_American_and_Caribbean_Games" title="1982 Central American and Caribbean Games">1982 Central American and Caribbean Games</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>At the <a href="/wiki/1983_Pan_American_Games" title="1983 Pan American Games">1983 Pan American Games</a> Núñez and three other lifters were stripped of their medals for use of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Anabolic_steroids" title="Anabolic steroids">anabolic steroids</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nyt-1983_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-nyt-1983-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
