<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Daniela Yordanova</b> (<a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_language" title="Bulgarian language">Bulgarian</a>: Даниела Йорданова)(born 8 March 1976 in <a href="/wiki/Slivnitsa" title="Slivnitsa">Slivnitsa</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgarian</a> middle distance runner who specializes in the <a href="/wiki/1500_metres" title="1500 metres">1500 metres</a>. She tested positive for <a href="/wiki/Testosterone_(medication)" title="Testosterone (medication)">testosterone</a> in an out-of-competition doping test in <a href="/wiki/Sofia" title="Sofia">Sofia</a> on 13 June 2008, which eventually got her kicked out of the 2008 Summer Olympics. <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
