<p><b>Danilo Pantić</b> (<a href="/wiki/Serbian_Cyrillic_alphabet" title="Serbian Cyrillic alphabet">Serbian Cyrillic</a>: Данило Пантић; born 26 October 1996) is a Serbian <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Midfielder" title="Midfielder">midfielder</a> for <a href="/wiki/Eredivisie" title="Eredivisie">Eredivisie</a> club <a href="/wiki/S.B.V._Excelsior" title="S.B.V. Excelsior">Excelsior</a>, on loan from <a href="/wiki/Premier_League" title="Premier League">Premier League</a> club <a href="/wiki/Chelsea_F.C." title="Chelsea F.C.">Chelsea</a>. He has represented <a href="/wiki/Serbia_national_football_team" title="Serbia national football team">Serbia</a> at multiple youth levels.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Danilo_Panti%C4%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Partizan">Partizan</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Danilo_Panti%C4%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Partizan">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Pantić made his debut on 26 May 2013 in a league fixture against <a href="/wiki/FK_Spartak_Subotica" title="FK Spartak Subotica">Spartak Subotica</a>, replacing <a href="/wiki/Vladimir_Volkov" title="Vladimir Volkov">Vladimir Volkov</a> in the 70th minute.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>After a disagreement with Partizan over contract extension proposals, he signed a one-and-a-half year contract with Partizan on December 5, 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Juventus_F.C." title="Juventus F.C.">Juventus</a> had scouted Pantić since early 2014, allegedly monitoring his status due to his short contract.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> After hearing about interest from Juventus, Pantić told <i><a href="/wiki/Ve%C4%8Dernje_novosti" title="Večernje novosti">Večernje novosti</a></i> that he wished to extend his contract with Partizan before leaving Serbia.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Chelsea">Chelsea</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Danilo_Panti%C4%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Chelsea">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 13 July 2015 Pantić signed for <a href="/wiki/Chelsea_F.C." title="Chelsea F.C.">Chelsea</a> for a reported fee of £1.25 million, on a four-year deal and was immediately loaned out to <a href="/wiki/Eredivisie" title="Eredivisie">Eredivisie</a> side <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vitesse_Arnhem" title="Vitesse Arnhem">Vitesse Arnhem</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Loan_to_Vitesse">Loan to Vitesse</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Danilo_Panti%C4%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Loan to Vitesse">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>He made his Vitesse debut 17 days later in a <a href="/wiki/UEFA_Europa_League" title="UEFA Europa League">UEFA Europa League</a> third qualifying round first leg against <a href="/wiki/Southampton_F.C." title="Southampton F.C.">Southampton</a> at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/St._Mary%27s_Stadium" title="St. Mary's Stadium">St. Mary's</a>, replacing <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Valeri_Kazaishvili" title="Valeri Kazaishvili">Valeri Kazaishvili</a> for the final 23 minutes of a 3–0 defeat.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> On 9 August 2015, Pantić made his league debut in a 1–1 draw with <a href="/wiki/Willem_II_(football_club)" title="Willem II (football club)">Willem II</a>, replacing <a href="/wiki/Valeri_Qazaishvili" title="Valeri Qazaishvili">Valeri Qazaishvili</a> in the 71st minute.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 3 March 2016, after being supposedly 'frozen' out of the Vitesse starting eleven, Pantić stated his passion for a return to London: "I want to come back to Chelsea, just to train. Why am I in Vitesse if I am not good enough? I am [going] crazy! I've made no mistake. If I had a chance and blew it, I would keep quiet. Maybe in Vitesse they don't like us <a href="/wiki/Serbia" title="Serbia">Serbs</a>: <a href="/wiki/Nemanja_Mati%C4%87" title="Nemanja Matić">Nemanja Matić</a> did not get a chance here either and <a href="/wiki/Uro%C5%A1_%C4%90ur%C4%91evi%C4%87" title="Uroš Đurđević">Uroš Đurđević</a> played 22 matches in a year and a half."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p>Following this interview, Pantić was suspended by first-team manager <a href="/wiki/Rob_Maas" title="Rob Maas">Rob Maas</a> indefinitely.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Loan_to_Excelsior">Loan to Excelsior</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Danilo_Panti%C4%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Loan to Excelsior">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>On 12 August 2016, Pantić joined Excelsior on loan for the 2016–17 campaign.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> On 17 September 2016, Pantić made his debut against <a href="/wiki/Willem_II_(football_club)" title="Willem II (football club)">Willem II</a> off the bench coming on after half-time as Excelsior went on to score a late goal to earn a 1–1 draw.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> In the following match, on 20 September, he made his full debut, playing a full 90 minutes in a 1–0 win over <a href="/wiki/BVV_Barendrecht" title="BVV Barendrecht">BVV Barendrecht</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2016%E2%80%9317_KNVB_Cup" title="2016–17 KNVB Cup">KNVB Cup</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Danilo_Panti%C4%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Youth">Youth</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Danilo_Panti%C4%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Youth">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Pantić has represented Serbia at the <a href="/wiki/Serbia_national_under-17_football_team" title="Serbia national under-17 football team">U17</a> and <a href="/wiki/Serbia_national_under-19_football_team" title="Serbia national under-19 football team">U19</a> levels.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup> On 10 October 2014, Pantić scored his first and second goal for the U19 side in the opening twenty minutes against <a href="/wiki/San_Marino_national_under-19_football_team" title="San Marino national under-19 football team">San Marino U19</a> to help Serbia to a 4–0 victory in their opening match of the <a href="/wiki/2015_UEFA_European_Under-19_Championship_qualification" title="2015 UEFA European Under-19 Championship qualification">2015 UEFA Euro U19 Championship Qualifiers</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup> Pantić has captained at the U19 level, captaining for the first time on 12 October 2014, against <a href="/wiki/Armenia_national_under-19_football_team" title="Armenia national under-19 football team">Armenia U19</a> in a <a href="/wiki/2015_UEFA_European_Under-19_Championship_qualification" title="2015 UEFA European Under-19 Championship qualification">UEFA Euro U19 Championship Qualifier</a> match, which ended in a 1–0 victory for the Serbian side.</p>
