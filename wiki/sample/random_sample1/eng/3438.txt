<p><b>Damir Kahriman</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Serbian_Cyrillic_language" title="Serbian Cyrillic language">Serbian Cyrillic</a>: <span lang="sr" xml:lang="sr"><span class="Unicode">Дамир Кахриман</span></span>; born 19 November 1984) is a <a href="/wiki/Serbia" title="Serbia">Serbian</a> professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Goalkeeper_(association_football)" title="Goalkeeper (association football)">Goalkeeper</a>. He currently plays for <a href="/wiki/Red_Star_Belgrade" title="Red Star Belgrade">Red Star Belgrade</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Serbian_SuperLiga" title="Serbian SuperLiga">Serbian SuperLiga</a>.</p>
<p>He was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Serbia_national_under-21_football_team" title="Serbia national under-21 football team">Serbia national under-21 football team</a>, especially during the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2007_UEFA_European_Under-21_Football_Championship" title="2007 UEFA European Under-21 Football Championship">2007 UEFA European Under-21 Football Championship</a> held in the <a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Netherlands</a> in which <a href="/wiki/Serbia_national_under-21_football_team" title="Serbia national under-21 football team">Serbia</a> reached the final and the keeper was selected for the tournament's starting eleven.</p>
