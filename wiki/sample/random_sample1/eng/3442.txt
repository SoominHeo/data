<p><b>Davide Bariti</b> (born 7 July 1991) is an Italian <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Midfielder" title="Midfielder">midfielder</a> for <a href="/wiki/Lega_Pro" title="Lega Pro">Lega Pro</a> club <a href="/wiki/Lupa_Roma_F.C." title="Lupa Roma F.C.">Lupa Roma</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Davide_Bariti&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Carrarese">Carrarese</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Davide_Bariti&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Carrarese">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Born in <a href="/wiki/La_Spezia" title="La Spezia">La Spezia</a>, Liguria, Bariti started his career at nearby Tuscan team <a href="/wiki/Carrarese_Calcio" title="Carrarese Calcio">Carrarese</a>. He was the member of <i>Allievi B</i> U16 team in 2006–07 season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> In January 2009 Bariti made his first team debut.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Triestina">Triestina</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Davide_Bariti&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Triestina">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 31 August 2009 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/U.S._Triestina_Calcio" title="U.S. Triestina Calcio">Triestina</a> signed Bariti in <a href="/wiki/Co-ownership_(football)" title="Co-ownership (football)">co-ownership deal</a> for €150,000<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[nb 1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[2]</a></sup> and sent him back to <a href="/wiki/Lega_Pro_Seconda_Divisione" title="Lega Pro Seconda Divisione">Lega Pro Seconda Divisione</a>. In June 2010 Triestina bought Bariti outright for undisclosed free. Bariti played 22 games (5 starts) for Triestina in <a href="/wiki/2010%E2%80%9311_Serie_B" title="2010–11 Serie B">2010–11 Serie B</a>. The team relegated at the end of season.</p>
<p>Bariti played the first two rounds of <a href="/wiki/2011%E2%80%9312_Coppa_Italia" title="2011–12 Coppa Italia">2011–12 Coppa Italia</a> before leaving Triestina. He scored the 1st and 11th kick of Triestina in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Penalty_shootout_(association_football)" title="Penalty shootout (association football)">penalty shootout</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PSO_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-PSO-5">[nb 2]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Vicenza">Vicenza</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Davide_Bariti&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Vicenza">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 31 August 2011 Triestina sold Bariti to <a href="/wiki/Vicenza_Calcio" title="Vicenza Calcio">Vicenza</a> for free (along with <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Marco_Franceschin&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Marco Franceschin (page does not exist)">Marco Franceschin</a> for undisclosed fee) and the club re-sold half of the registration rights of Bariti to <a href="/wiki/S.S.C._Napoli" title="S.S.C. Napoli">Napoli</a>, for €550,000 in 3-year deal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Napoli2012bilancio_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-Napoli2012bilancio-6">[4]</a></sup> On the same day Bariti returned to the city of <a href="/wiki/Vicenza" title="Vicenza">Vicenza</a> for <a href="/wiki/2011%E2%80%9312_Serie_B" title="2011–12 Serie B">2011–12 Serie B</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Napoli">Napoli</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Davide_Bariti&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Napoli">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 22 June 2012 Napoli acquired Bariti outright after Vicenza were also relegated.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[7]</a></sup> However, Vicenza would only received half of the "card" of Mario Sgambato as compensation.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[8]</a></sup> On 31 August 2012, Bariti was loaned out to <a href="/wiki/Lega_Pro_Prima_Divisione" title="Lega Pro Prima Divisione">Lega Pro Prima Divisione</a> club <a href="/wiki/U.S._Avellino_1912" title="U.S. Avellino 1912">Avellino</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[9]</a></sup></p>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/2013%E2%80%9314_Serie_A" title="2013–14 Serie A">2013–14 Serie A</a> Bariti was a player for Napoli's first team. He made two appearances.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Lupa_Roma">Lupa Roma</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Davide_Bariti&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Lupa Roma">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In 2014 Bariti was released by Napoli. He was signed by <a href="/wiki/Lega_Pro" title="Lega Pro">Lega Pro</a> club <a href="/wiki/Lupa_Roma_F.C." title="Lupa Roma F.C.">Lupa Roma</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[10]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Davide_Bariti&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Bariti played against Slovakia and Slovenia in <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=2008%E2%80%9309_Mirop_Cup&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="2008–09 Mirop Cup (page does not exist)">2008–09 Mirop Cup</a> for <a href="/wiki/Italy_Lega_Pro_representative_teams" title="Italy Lega Pro representative teams">Italy U20 Lega Pro team</a> in April and November 2009.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[11]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[12]</a></sup> He also played the next match, against Malta in <a href="/wiki/2009%E2%80%9311_International_Challenge_Trophy" title="2009–11 International Challenge Trophy">2009–11 International Challenge Trophy</a> in December 2009.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[13]</a></sup> Bariti also played for Italy U20 lega Pro in <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=2009_Trofeo_Dossena&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="2009 Trofeo Dossena (page does not exist)">2009</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=2010_Trofeo_Dossena&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="2010 Trofeo Dossena (page does not exist)">2010 Trofeo Dossena</a>, winning <a href="/wiki/New_York_Magic" title="New York Magic">New York Magic</a> (men <a href="/wiki/Super-20_League" title="Super-20 League">S-20 team</a>)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[14]</a></sup> and the youth team of <a href="/wiki/Sport_Club_Internacional" title="Sport Club Internacional">Internacional</a> in the final respectively.</p>
<p>Bariti played once for U20 A team in the last match of 2010–11 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Italy/Germany/Switzerland_under-20_Four_Nations_Tournament" title="Italy/Germany/Switzerland under-20 Four Nations Tournament">Four Nations</a>, in June 2011.</p>
<p>In January 2012 Bariti received his first <a href="/wiki/Italy_national_under-21_football_team" title="Italy national under-21 football team">U21</a> call-up, for a training camp in Rome.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[15]</a></sup></p>
