<p><b>David Rodríguez Lombán</b> (born 5 June 1987) is a Spanish professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays for <a href="/wiki/Granada_CF" title="Granada CF">Granada CF</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)#Centre-back" title="Defender (association football)">central defender</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=David_Lomb%C3%A1n&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Avil%C3%A9s" title="Avilés">Avilés</a>, <a href="/wiki/Asturias" title="Asturias">Asturias</a>, Lombán finished his graduation with <a href="/wiki/Valencia_CF" title="Valencia CF">Valencia CF</a>'s youth system, and made his senior debuts with <a href="/wiki/Valencia_CF_Mestalla" title="Valencia CF Mestalla">the reserves</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2006%E2%80%9307_Segunda_Divisi%C3%B3n_B" title="2006–07 Segunda División B">2006–07 season</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Segunda_Divisi%C3%B3n_B" title="Segunda División B">Segunda División B</a>. On 15 December 2007 he appeared in his first official game with the main squad, coming on as a second-half <a href="/wiki/Substitute_(association_football)" title="Substitute (association football)">substitute</a> in a 0–3 <a href="/wiki/La_Liga" title="La Liga">La Liga</a> home loss against <a href="/wiki/FC_Barcelona" title="FC Barcelona">FC Barcelona</a>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> in October of the following year, he suffered a <a href="/wiki/Knee" title="Knee">knee</a> injury which kept him sidelined for six months.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 2 July 2009, Lombán joined <a href="/wiki/Segunda_Divisi%C3%B3n" title="Segunda División">Segunda División</a> side <a href="/wiki/UD_Salamanca" title="UD Salamanca">UD Salamanca</a> in a <a href="/wiki/2009%E2%80%9310_Segunda_Divisi%C3%B3n" title="2009–10 Segunda División">season</a>-long loan deal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> One year later, the <a href="/wiki/Free_agent" title="Free agent">free agent</a> moved to fellow league club <a href="/wiki/Xerez_CD" title="Xerez CD">Xerez CD</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 11 July 2012, Lombán signed for <a href="/wiki/FC_Barcelona_B" title="FC Barcelona B">FC Barcelona B</a> also in the second level.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> On 8 July of the following year he terminated his contract with the <a href="/wiki/Catalonia" title="Catalonia">Catalans</a> and moved to <a href="/wiki/Elche_CF" title="Elche CF">Elche CF</a>, <a href="/wiki/2012%E2%80%9313_Segunda_Divisi%C3%B3n" title="2012–13 Segunda División">freshly promoted to the top level</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 10 August 2015, after his team <a href="/wiki/2014%E2%80%9315_La_Liga" title="2014–15 La Liga">suffered administrative relegation</a>, Lombán joined fellow league club <a href="/wiki/Granada_CF" title="Granada CF">Granada CF</a> after agreeing to a three-year deal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
