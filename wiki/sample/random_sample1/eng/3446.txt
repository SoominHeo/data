<p><b>David Mazouz</b> (<span class="nowrap"><span class="IPA nopopups"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_English" title="Help:IPA for English">/<span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="'d' in 'dye'">d</span><span title="/ə/ 'a' in 'about'">ə</span><span title="/ˈ/ primary stress follows">ˈ</span><span title="'v' in 'vie'">v</span><span title="/iː/ long 'e' in 'seed'">iː</span><span title="'d' in 'dye'">d</span></span> <span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="'m' in 'my'">m</span><span title="/ə/ 'a' in 'about'">ə</span><span title="/ˈ/ primary stress follows">ˈ</span><span title="'z' in 'Zion'">z</span><span title="/uː/ long 'oo' in 'food'">uː</span><span title="'z' in 'Zion'">z</span></span>/</a></span></span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-David_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-David-1">[1]</a></sup> born February 19, 2001)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> is an American actor. An early role of his was that of the young autistic Jacob "Jake" Bohm in the TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Touch_(TV_series)" title="Touch (TV series)">Touch</a></i>. He currently portrays a young <a href="/wiki/Batman" title="Batman">Bruce Wayne</a> in <a href="/wiki/Fox_Broadcasting_Company" title="Fox Broadcasting Company">Fox</a>'s TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Gotham_(TV_series)" title="Gotham (TV series)">Gotham</a></i>, based on the <a href="/wiki/DC_Comics" title="DC Comics">DC Comics</a> alter ego of the superhero <a href="/wiki/Batman" title="Batman">Batman</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=David_Mazouz&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>David Mazouz was born in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Los_Angeles,_California" title="Los Angeles, California">Los Angeles, California</a>, to Rachel, a psychotherapist, and Michael Mazouz, a physician. He has a sister, Rebecca. Mazouz and his family are <a href="/wiki/Shabbat" title="Shabbat">Sabbath</a>-observing, <a href="/wiki/Kashrut" title="Kashrut">Kosher</a>-keeping, observant <a href="/wiki/Jews" title="Jews">Jews</a>, of <a href="/wiki/Sephardi_Jews" title="Sephardi Jews">Sephardi</a> descent.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-jjournal1_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-jjournal1-4">[4]</a></sup> He has been a student at <a href="/wiki/Shalhevet_High_School" title="Shalhevet High School">Shalhevet High School</a> since 2015<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> and appeared in a music video promoting the school.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=David_Mazouz&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mazouz started his career in commercials with <a href="/wiki/Kiefer_Sutherland" title="Kiefer Sutherland">Kiefer Sutherland</a> at age eight.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-David_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-David-1">[1]</a></sup> In 2012, He again co- starred with Sutherland when he landed the role of mute Jacob "Jake" Bohm in the TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Touch_(TV_series)" title="Touch (TV series)">Touch</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Touch_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-Touch-7">[7]</a></sup> Mazouz has acted in several television series including: <i><a href="/wiki/Mike_%26_Molly" title="Mike &amp; Molly">Mike &amp; Molly</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Gotham_(TV_series)" title="Gotham (TV series)">Gotham</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Office_(U.S._TV_series)" title="The Office (U.S. TV series)">The Office</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Criminal_Minds" title="Criminal Minds">Criminal Minds</a></i>. He has also appeared in the ABC show <i><a href="/wiki/Private_Practice_(TV_series)" title="Private Practice (TV series)">Private Practice</a></i> as the adopted brother of Betsy.</p>
<p>In 2014, Mazouz was cast in the role of <a href="/wiki/Batman" title="Batman">Bruce Wayne</a> in <a href="/wiki/Fox_Broadcasting_Company" title="Fox Broadcasting Company">Fox</a>'s TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Gotham_(TV_series)" title="Gotham (TV series)">Gotham</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Batman_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-Batman-8">[8]</a></sup> In 2016, he had a role in the horror films <i><a href="/wiki/The_Darkness_(film)" title="The Darkness (film)">The Darkness</a></i>, as Michael Taylor,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Mazouz_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-Mazouz-9">[9]</a></sup> and <i><a href="/wiki/Incarnate_(film)" title="Incarnate (film)">Incarnate</a></i>, as Cameron.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-film_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-film-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
