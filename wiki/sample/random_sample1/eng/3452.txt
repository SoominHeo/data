<p><b>Dashiqiao</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">大石桥</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">大石橋</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Dàshíqiáo</span></i>; literally: "Great Stone Bridge") is a city in south-central <a href="/wiki/Liaoning" title="Liaoning">Liaoning</a> province of <a href="/wiki/Northeast_China" title="Northeast China">Northeast China</a>. It is under the administration of <a href="/wiki/Yingkou" title="Yingkou">Yingkou</a> City, which is 23 kilometres (14 mi) to the west.</p>
