<p><b>Dayu County</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chinese_character" title="Chinese character">Chinese</a>: 大余县; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">Pinyin</a>: Dàyú Xiàn) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/County_(People%27s_Republic_of_China)" title="County (People's Republic of China)">county</a> in western <a href="/wiki/Ganzhou" title="Ganzhou">Ganzhou</a> Municipality, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jiangxi_Province" title="Jiangxi Province">Jiangxi Province</a>.It is known for The Capital of <a href="/wiki/Tungsten" title="Tungsten">Tungsten</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Statistics">Statistics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dayu_County&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Statistics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Dayu has an area of 1367.63 km². Total population is 280,000.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Administration">Administration</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dayu_County&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Administration">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The county executive, legislature, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Basic_People%27s_Court" title="Basic People's Court">judiciary</a> are at Nanan Town (南安镇), together with the <a href="/wiki/Communist_Party_of_China" title="Communist Party of China">CPC</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Public_Security_Bureau" title="Public Security Bureau">PSB</a> branches.</p>
<p>The county oversees 17 towns &amp; townships.</p>
