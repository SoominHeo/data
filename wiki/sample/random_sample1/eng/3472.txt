<p><b>Dynasty Cup</b> was an <a href="/wiki/East_Asia" title="East Asia">East Asian</a> international association football tournament. The tournament was held every 2 or 3 years. It was hosted by <a href="/wiki/Beijing" title="Beijing">Beijing</a> in 1990 and 1992, <a href="/wiki/Hong_Kong" title="Hong Kong">Hong Kong</a> in 1995, <a href="/wiki/Yokohama" title="Yokohama">Yokohama</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tokyo" title="Tokyo">Tokyo</a> in 1998.</p>
<p>In 2002 the <a href="/wiki/East_Asian_Football_Federation" title="East Asian Football Federation">East Asian Football Federation</a> was founded and in 2003 the <a href="/wiki/EAFF_E-1_Football_Championship" title="EAFF E-1 Football Championship">East Asian Football Championship</a> was established as successor tournament.</p>
