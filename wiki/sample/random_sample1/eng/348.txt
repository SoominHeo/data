<li><a href="/wiki/Yemen" title="Yemen">Yemen</a>: An <a href="/wiki/AGM-114_Hellfire" title="AGM-114 Hellfire">AGM-114 Hellfire</a> missile launched by an American <a href="/wiki/Unmanned_aerial_vehicle" title="Unmanned aerial vehicle">drone airplane</a> destroyed a car carrying what the United States claims were six members of <a href="/wiki/Al-Qaeda" title="Al-Qaeda">al-Qaeda</a>, including the mastermind of the <a href="/wiki/USS_Cole_(DDG-67)" title="USS Cole (DDG-67)">USS <i>Cole</i></a> attack, <a href="/wiki/Qaed_Salim_Sinan_al-Harethi" title="Qaed Salim Sinan al-Harethi">Qaed Salim Sinan al-Harethi</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>: Turkish <a href="/wiki/Justice_and_Development_Party_(Turkey)" title="Justice and Development Party (Turkey)">Justice and Development Party</a> (AK Party) has won the Turkish general election. The AK Party campaigned on economic and social issues, and downplayed its Islamist origins. The AK Party's chairman <a href="/wiki/Recep_Tayyip_Erdo%C4%9Fan" title="Recep Tayyip Erdoğan">Recep Tayyip Erdoğan</a> is banned from holding political office, so someone else will become <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Turkish_Prime_Minister" title="Turkish Prime Minister">Turkish Prime Minister</a>. Opponents of the AK Party have expressed concerns that the AK Party's victory may threaten the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Secular" title="Secular">secular</a> nature of the Turkish state.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Human_rights" title="Human rights">Human rights</a>: <a href="/wiki/Amnesty_International" title="Amnesty International">Amnesty International</a> has released a 74-page report accusing the <a href="/wiki/Israel_Defense_Forces" title="Israel Defense Forces">Israel Defense Forces</a> of <a href="/wiki/War_crime" title="War crime">war crimes</a> and human rights violations.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Internet" title="Internet">Internet</a>: <a href="/wiki/Top-level_domain" title="Top-level domain">Country code top-level domain</a> administrators have started to talk about taking back control of their parts of the <a href="/wiki/Domain_name" title="Domain name">domain name</a> system that has been controlled by <a href="/wiki/ICANN" title="ICANN">ICANN</a> since the death of <a href="/wiki/Jon_Postel" title="Jon Postel">Jon Postel</a>. They have complained that ICANN is unaccountable, dictatorial and unresponsive to users' needs.</li>
</ul>

The act creates a new <a href="/wiki/United_States_Department_of_Homeland_Security" title="United States Department of Homeland Security">Department of Homeland Security</a> and is the largest government reorganization since the <a href="/wiki/National_Security_Act_of_1947" title="National Security Act of 1947">National Security Act of 1947</a> more than 50 years before.</li>
<li>A group of European scientists has announced that they intend to use the <a href="/wiki/Very_Large_Telescope" title="Very Large Telescope">Very Large Telescope</a> to take pictures of the lunar module bases remaining on the Moon, in order to debunk the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Apollo_moon_landing_conspiracy_theory" title="Apollo moon landing conspiracy theory">Apollo moon landing conspiracy theory</a> that states that the <a href="/wiki/Apollo_program" title="Apollo program">Apollo moon landings</a> were a <a href="/wiki/Hoax" title="Hoax">hoax</a>.</li>
