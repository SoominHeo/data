<p><b>Taihei Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">太平駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Taihei-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a railway station on the <a href="/wiki/Sassh%C5%8D_Line" title="Sasshō Line">Sasshō Line</a> in <a href="/wiki/Kita-ku,_Sapporo" title="Kita-ku, Sapporo">Kita-ku, Sapporo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hokkaido" title="Hokkaido">Hokkaido</a>, Japan, operated by the <a href="/wiki/Hokkaido_Railway_Company" title="Hokkaido Railway Company">Hokkaido Railway Company</a> (JR Hokkaido). The station is numbered G06.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lines">Lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Taihei_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Taihei Station is served by the <a href="/wiki/Sassh%C5%8D_Line" title="Sasshō Line">Sasshō Line</a> (Gakuen Toshi Line) from <a href="/wiki/Sapporo_Station" title="Sapporo Station">Sapporo</a> to <a href="/wiki/Shin-Totsukawa_Station" title="Shin-Totsukawa Station">Shin-Totsukawa</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_layout">Station layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Taihei_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Station layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The station has two <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Side_platforms" title="Side platforms">side platforms</a> serving two tracks, connected by a passenger footbridge.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-dj340_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-dj340-2">[2]</a></sup> The station has automated ticket machines and <a href="/wiki/Kitaca" title="Kitaca">Kitaca</a> card readers.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The station is unattended.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Taihei_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
