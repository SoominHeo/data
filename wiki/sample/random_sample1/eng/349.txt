<p><b><a href="/wiki/June" title="June">June</a></b> <b><a href="/wiki/2002" title="2002">2002</a></b> was the sixth month of that common year. The month, which began on a <a href="/wiki/Saturday" title="Saturday">Saturday</a>, ended on a <a href="/wiki/Sunday" title="Sunday">Sunday</a> after 30 days.</p>

<li><a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Sweden</a> passes a law that allows <a href="/wiki/Homosexuality" title="Homosexuality">homosexual</a> couples to apply for both the national and the international <a href="/wiki/Adoption" title="Adoption">adoption</a> of children.</li>
