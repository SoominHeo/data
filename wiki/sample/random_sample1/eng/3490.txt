<p><b>Multi-index notation</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Mathematical_notation" title="Mathematical notation">mathematical notation</a> that simplifies formulas used in <a href="/wiki/Multivariable_calculus" title="Multivariable calculus">multivariable calculus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Partial_differential_equation" title="Partial differential equation">partial differential equations</a> and the theory of <a href="/wiki/Distribution_(mathematics)" title="Distribution (mathematics)">distributions</a>, by generalising the concept of an integer <a href="/wiki/Index_notation" title="Index notation">index</a> to an ordered <a href="/wiki/Tuple" title="Tuple">tuple</a> of indices.</p>
