<p><b>Dacheng</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">大城县</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Dàichéng</span></i>; literally: "Great City") is a county of <a href="/wiki/Hebei" title="Hebei">Hebei</a>, China. It is under the administration of <a href="/wiki/Langfang" title="Langfang">Langfang</a> City.</p>
