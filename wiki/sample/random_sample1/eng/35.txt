<p><b>Francesco III <a href="/wiki/House_of_Gonzaga" title="House of Gonzaga">Gonzaga</a></b> (10 March 1533 – 22 February 1550) was <a href="/wiki/Duke_of_Mantua" title="Duke of Mantua">Duke of Mantua</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Marquess_of_Montferrat" title="Marquess of Montferrat">Marquess of Montferrat</a> from 1540 until his death. He was the eldest son of <a href="/wiki/Federico_II_Gonzaga,_Duke_of_Mantua" title="Federico II Gonzaga, Duke of Mantua">Federico II Gonzaga, Duke of Mantua</a> and his wife <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Margaret_Paleologina" title="Margaret Paleologina">Margaret Paleologina</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Francesco_III_Gonzaga,_Duke_of_Mantua&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On 22 October 1549, he married <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Catherine_of_Austria_(1533-1572)" title="Catherine of Austria (1533-1572)">Catherine of Habsburg</a>, a daughter of <a href="/wiki/Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Holy Roman Emperor">Holy Roman Emperor</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ferdinand_I_of_Germany" title="Ferdinand I of Germany">Ferdinand I</a>.</p>
