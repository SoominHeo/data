<p>Year <b>1064</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MLXIV</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Thursday" title="Leap year starting on Thursday">leap year starting on Thursday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_area">By area</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1064&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By area">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Americas">Americas</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1064&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Americas">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sunset_Crater_Volcano" title="Sunset Crater Volcano">Sunset Crater Volcano</a> (present-day <a href="/wiki/Arizona" title="Arizona">Arizona</a>) first erupts.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1064&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Great_Seljuq_Empire" title="Great Seljuq Empire">Seljuk</a> Turks storm Anatolia, taking <a href="/wiki/Caesarea" title="Caesarea">Caesarea</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ani" title="Ani">Ani</a>, marking the beginning of Turkish incursions into <a href="/wiki/Anatolia" title="Anatolia">Anatolia</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Georgia" title="Kingdom of Georgia">Kingdom of Georgia</a> takes over <a href="/wiki/Samshvilde" title="Samshvilde">Samshvilde</a>, the capital of the neighboring <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Tashir-Dzoraget" title="Kingdom of Tashir-Dzoraget">Kingdom of Tashir-Dzoraget</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1064&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_9" title="June 9">June 9</a> – <a href="/wiki/Coimbra" title="Coimbra">Coimbra</a> is taken by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ferdinand_I_of_Castile" title="Ferdinand I of Castile">King Ferdinand</a> of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Castile" title="Kingdom of Castile">Castile</a>.</li>
<li>European warriors go to Spain to participate to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/War_of_Barbastro" title="War of Barbastro">siege of Barbastro</a>. This expedition is sanctioned by the <a href="/wiki/Pope_Alexander_II" title="Pope Alexander II">Pope</a> and is now regarded as an early form of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Crusade" title="Crusade">Crusade</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Harold_Godwinson" title="Harold Godwinson">Harold Godwinson</a> is shipwrecked at <a href="/wiki/Ponthieu" title="Ponthieu">Ponthieu</a>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=County_of_Normandy&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="County of Normandy (page does not exist)">Normandy</a> and taken captive by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Guy_I_of_Ponthieu" title="Guy I of Ponthieu">Count Guy</a>.</li>
<li>Construction of the <a href="/wiki/Piazza_dei_Miracoli#Duomo" title="Piazza dei Miracoli">Duomo</a> at <a href="/wiki/Pisa" title="Pisa">Pisa</a> in <a href="/wiki/Tuscany" title="Tuscany">Tuscany</a> begins.</li>
</ul>
