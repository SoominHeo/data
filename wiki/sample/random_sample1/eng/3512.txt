<p><b>Takaharu</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">高原町</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Takaharu-chō</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/List_of_towns_in_Japan" title="List of towns in Japan">town</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Nishimorokata_District,_Miyazaki" title="Nishimorokata District, Miyazaki">Nishimorokata District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Miyazaki_Prefecture" title="Miyazaki Prefecture">Miyazaki Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</p>
<p>As of September 1, 2011, the town has an estimated <a href="/wiki/Population" title="Population">population</a> of 9,887 and the <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> of 116 persons per km². The total area is 85.38 km².</p>
