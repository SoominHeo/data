<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Date Yoshimura</b> (伊達 吉村, July 23, 1680 – February 8, 1752) was a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Daimyo" title="Daimyo">daimyo</a> (feudal lord) in the 18th century, from 1703 to 1743.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Rathbone_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Rathbone-1">[1]</a></sup> He became daimyo at age of 23 in 1703, retired in 1743 at age of 42.</p>
