<p><b>Al Dhahirah</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar" xml:lang="ar">محافظة الظاهرة</span>‎‎; <i><b>Muḥāfaẓat aẓ-Ẓāhirah</b></i>) is one of the governorates (<i><a href="/wiki/Muhafazah" title="Muhafazah">muhafazah</a></i>) of <a href="/wiki/Oman" title="Oman">Oman</a>. It was previously a region (<i><a href="/wiki/Mintaqah" title="Mintaqah">mintaqah</a></i>). It became a governorate on 28 October 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Provinces">Provinces</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ad_Dhahirah_Governorate&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Provinces">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Adh Dhahirah Governorate consists of three <a href="/wiki/Provinces_of_Oman" title="Provinces of Oman">provinces</a> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wilayat" title="Wilayat">wilayat</a>):</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ibri" title="Ibri">Ibri</a></li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Yanqul&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Yanqul (page does not exist)">Yanqul</a></li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Dhank&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Dhank (page does not exist)">Dhank</a></li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Regional_Subdivision_Review_before_2007">Regional Subdivision Review before 2007</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ad_Dhahirah_Governorate&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Regional Subdivision Review before 2007">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Until October 2006, two more former wilayat (Provinces) were part of this region: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Al_Buraymi" title="Al Buraymi">Al Buraymi</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mahdha" title="Mahdha">Mahdha</a>. <a href="/wiki/Al_Buraimi_Governorate" title="Al Buraimi Governorate">Al Buraimi Governorate</a> was created from those two wilayats in October, 2006, as an new governorate. As well, a third wilaya (Province), of <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Al_Sinaihah&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Al Sinaihah (page does not exist)">Al Sinaihah</a> was created from rural parts of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Al_Buraymi" title="Al Buraymi">Al Buraymi</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mahdha" title="Mahdha">Mahdha</a>.</p>
