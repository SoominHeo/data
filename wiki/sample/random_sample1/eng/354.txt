<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Football at the 2002 Asian Games</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

</div>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">Football</a></b> at the <b><a href="/wiki/2002_Asian_Games" title="2002 Asian Games">2002 Asian Games</a></b> was held in <a href="/wiki/Busan" title="Busan">Busan</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a> from September 27 to October 13, 2002.</p>
<p>Age limit for the men teams is under-23, same as the age limit in football competitions in <a href="/wiki/Olympic_Games" title="Olympic Games">Olympic Games</a>, while three overage players are allowed among each squad.</p>
