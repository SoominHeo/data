<p><b><a href="/wiki/Taekwondo" title="Taekwondo">Taekwondo</a></b> took place from October 10 to October 13 at the <b><a href="/wiki/2002_Asian_Games" title="2002 Asian Games">2002 Asian Games</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Busan" title="Busan">Busan</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. Men's and women's competitions held in eight weight categories for each. All competition took place at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gudeok_Gymnasium" title="Gudeok Gymnasium">Gudeok Gymnasium</a>. Each country except the host nation was limited to having 6 men and 6 women.</p>
