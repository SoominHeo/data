<div class="hatnote" role="note">This is a <a href="/wiki/Chinese_name" title="Chinese name">Chinese name</a>; the <a href="/wiki/Chinese_surname" title="Chinese surname">family name</a> is <a href="/wiki/T%C3%A1ng_(surname)" title="Táng (surname)">Tang</a>.</div>
<p><b>Tang Zi</b> was a military general of <a href="/wiki/Eastern_Wu" title="Eastern Wu">Eastern Wu</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Three_Kingdoms" title="Three Kingdoms">Three Kingdoms</a> period of <a href="/wiki/History_of_China" title="History of China">Chinese history</a>. Once, Tang was sent to lead reinforcements to assist <a href="/wiki/Zhuge_Dan" title="Zhuge Dan">Zhuge Dan</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Three_Rebellions_in_Shouchun" title="Three Rebellions in Shouchun">rebellion</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Cao_Wei" title="Cao Wei">Cao Wei</a> regent <a href="/wiki/Sima_Zhao" title="Sima Zhao">Sima Zhao</a>. After Zhuge's defeat, Tang defected to Cao Wei and rose to a prominent position in the military later.</p>
