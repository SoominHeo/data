<p><b>Daegu Yangnyeongsi Festival</b> is a festival of <a href="/wiki/Daegu" title="Daegu">Daegu</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. The festival is held every May. The main events include a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Medicinal_herb" title="Medicinal herb">Medicinal herb</a> party, cutting medicinal herbs contest and a free medical check-up by <a href="/wiki/Oriental_medicine" title="Oriental medicine">oriental medicine</a>.</p>
