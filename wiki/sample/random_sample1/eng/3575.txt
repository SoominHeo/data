<p><i><b>Hello Counselor</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">안녕하세요</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Annyeonghaseyo</span></i>) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> reality show first that debuted in November 2010. It is hosted by <a href="/wiki/Shin_Dong-yup_(entertainer)" title="Shin Dong-yup (entertainer)">Shin Dong-yup</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Lee_Young-ja_(comedian)" title="Lee Young-ja (comedian)">Lee Young-ja</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Cultwo" title="Cultwo">Cultwo</a> (<a href="/wiki/Jung_Chan-woo_(comedian)" title="Jung Chan-woo (comedian)">Jung Chan-woo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kim_Tae-gyun_(comedian)" title="Kim Tae-gyun (comedian)">Kim Tae-gyun</a>). <a href="/wiki/Choi_Tae-joon" title="Choi Tae-joon">Choi Tae-joon</a> joined the show as host starting August 29, 2016. According to the show's official description through <a href="/wiki/Korean_Broadcasting_System" title="Korean Broadcasting System">KBS</a>, <i>Hello Counselor</i> is a talk show with an emphasis on regular people, regardless of age or gender, that aims to help take down communication barriers by sharing stories about life.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-KBSWorld_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-KBSWorld-1">[1]</a></sup> The program airs every Monday on <a href="/wiki/KBS_World_(TV_channel)" title="KBS World (TV channel)">KBS World</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Four_Helpers"><i>Four Helpers</i></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hello_Counselor&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Four Helpers">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In January 2016, KBS threatened legal action against <a href="/wiki/Shanghai_Media_Group" title="Shanghai Media Group">Shanghai Media Group</a> for their show <i>Four Helpers</i> (<i>四大名助</i>), claiming that the show plagiarized <i>Hello Counselor'</i>s set design, format, voting and scoring system, and other aspects of the show. A statement issued by KBS stated that they had demanded the immediate termination of the show's broadcast on the grounds publication right infringement and that, if the broadcasts continued, they would involve the Chinese <a href="/wiki/State_Administration_of_Press,_Publication,_Radio,_Film_and_Television" title="State Administration of Press, Publication, Radio, Film and Television">State Administration of Press, Publication, Radio, Film and Television</a> and pursue legal action.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
