<p>The <b>2004 IBAF Women's Baseball World Cup</b> was held from July 30 to August 8, 2004 in <a href="/wiki/Edmonton" title="Edmonton">Edmonton, Alberta</a>, Canada and won by the United States. It was the first <a href="/wiki/Women%27s_Baseball_World_Cup" title="Women's Baseball World Cup">Women's Baseball World Cup</a> and was sanctioned by the <a href="/wiki/International_Baseball_Federation" title="International Baseball Federation">International Baseball Federation</a>.</p>
<p>Competing teams were <a href="/wiki/Australia_women%27s_national_baseball_team" title="Australia women's national baseball team">Australia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada_women%27s_national_baseball_team" title="Canada women's national baseball team">Canada</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Taipei_women%27s_national_baseball_team" title="Chinese Taipei women's national baseball team">Chinese Taipei (Taiwan)</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan_women%27s_national_baseball_team" title="Japan women's national baseball team">Japan</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States_women%27s_national_baseball_team" title="United States women's national baseball team">USA</a>. <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgaria</a> were scheduled to compete, but withdrew before the tournament.</p>
