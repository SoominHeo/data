<p><b>Daran Norris</b> (born <b>Daran Morrison Nordlund</b>; November 1, 1964) is an American film, television and voice actor. He has appeared or voiced characters in more than 400 <a href="/wiki/Film" title="Film">films</a>, <a href="/wiki/Video_game" title="Video game">video games</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Television_program" title="Television program">television programs</a>, including: Gordy in <i><a href="/wiki/Ned%27s_Declassified_School_Survival_Guide" title="Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide">Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide</a></i>; <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Woody_Goodman" title="Woody Goodman">Cliff McCormack</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Veronica_Mars" title="Veronica Mars">Veronica Mars</a></i>; the voices of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cosmo_and_Wanda" title="Cosmo and Wanda">Cosmo</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jorgen_Von_Strangle" title="Jorgen Von Strangle">Jorgen Von Strangle</a>, and <a href="/wiki/List_of_The_Fairly_OddParents_characters#Mr._and_Mrs._Turner" title="List of The Fairly OddParents characters">Mr. Turner</a> in the <i><a href="/wiki/The_Fairly_OddParents" title="The Fairly OddParents">The Fairly OddParents</a></i>; Buddha Bob in <i><a href="/wiki/Big_Time_Rush" title="Big Time Rush">Big Time Rush</a></i>; and Knock Out in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Transformers_Prime" title="Transformers Prime">Transformers Prime</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Daran_Norris&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Ferndale,_Washington" title="Ferndale, Washington">Ferndale, Washington</a>, Norris graduated from <a href="/wiki/Ferndale_High_School_(Washington)" title="Ferndale High School (Washington)">Ferndale High School</a> in 1983, and married <a href="/wiki/Voice_acting" title="Voice acting">voice actress</a> <a href="/wiki/Mary_Elizabeth_McGlynn" title="Mary Elizabeth McGlynn">Mary Elizabeth McGlynn</a> in 1988. They divorced in 2012. He is a Christian.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-voicechaser_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-voicechaser-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-filmreference_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-filmreference-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tvguide_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-tvguide-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Daran_Norris&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Norris has been an actor since 1988, beginning such work in television commercials for <a href="/wiki/Pop-Tarts" title="Pop-Tarts">Pop-Tarts</a>, and moving into film, TV, and voice work in early-mid 90s.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Live_action">Live action</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Daran_Norris&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Live action">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>His first film was <i><a href="/wiki/Hobgoblins_(film)" title="Hobgoblins (film)">Hobgoblins</a></i> in 1988. Afterwards, he moved to television.</p>
<p>Norris is known for the role as <a href="/wiki/List_of_Ned%27s_Declassified_School_Survival_Guide_characters#Gordy" title="List of Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide characters">Gordy</a> in 44 episodes of <i><a href="/wiki/Ned%27s_Declassified_School_Survival_Guide" title="Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide">Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LAT1_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-LAT1-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-book1_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-book1-5">[5]</a></sup> Gordy is the "school's lazy, wacky janitor" who always goads <a href="/wiki/List_of_Ned%27s_Declassified_School_Survival_Guide_characters#.23Ned_Bigby" title="List of Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide characters">Ned</a> into rash actions<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-DVDT1_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-DVDT1-6">[6]</a></sup> while acting as Ned's go-to guy for advice.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-DVDizzy_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-DVDizzy-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>He portrayed <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cliff_McCormack" title="Cliff McCormack">Cliff McCormack</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Public_defender" title="Public defender">public defender</a> and family friend in the television drama <i><a href="/wiki/Veronica_Mars" title="Veronica Mars">Veronica Mars</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LAT1_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-LAT1-4">[4]</a></sup> The character of McCormack served as an ally to <a href="/wiki/Keith_Mars" title="Keith Mars">Keith</a> and <a href="/wiki/Veronica_Mars_(character)" title="Veronica Mars (character)">Veronica Mars</a> and a client of Mars Investigations. He has known <i>Mars</i> creator <a href="/wiki/Rob_Thomas_(writer)" title="Rob Thomas (writer)">Rob Thomas</a> since he was three.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>Most recently, Norris recurs as <a href="/wiki/List_of_Big_Time_Rush_characters#Buddha_Bob" title="List of Big Time Rush characters">Buddha Bob</a> in the 2010 series <i><a href="/wiki/Big_Time_Rush" title="Big Time Rush">Big Time Rush</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-voicechaser_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-voicechaser-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-filmreference_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-filmreference-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tvguide_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-tvguide-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2015, it was announced that Norris will star as <a href="/wiki/Phil_Hartman" title="Phil Hartman">Phil Hartman</a> in the film biopic, directed by <a href="/wiki/Jason_Priestley" title="Jason Priestley">Jason Priestley</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Voice_acting">Voice acting</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Daran_Norris&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Voice acting">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Among his voice projects, Norris voiced characters in 22 episodes of <i><a href="/wiki/Codename:_Kids_Next_Door" title="Codename: Kids Next Door">Codename: Kids Next Door</a></i> from 2002 through 2007, and 18 episodes of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Marvelous_Misadventures_of_Flapjack" title="The Marvelous Misadventures of Flapjack">The Marvelous Misadventures of Flapjack</a></i> from 2008 through 2009. He is noted for voicing the lead <a href="/wiki/The_Replacements_(TV_series)#Main_characters" title="The Replacements (TV series)">Dick Daring</a> in 71 episodes of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Replacements_(TV_series)" title="The Replacements (TV series)">The Replacements</a></i> from 2006 through 2009, and for voicing <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mr._and_Mrs._Turner" title="Mr. and Mrs. Turner">Mr. Turner</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cosmo_and_Wanda" title="Cosmo and Wanda">Cosmo</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jorgen_Von_Strangle" title="Jorgen Von Strangle">Jorgen Von Strangle</a>, for <i><a href="/wiki/The_Fairly_OddParents" title="The Fairly OddParents">The Fairly OddParents</a></i> franchise from 2001 to present,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LAT1_4-2"><a href="#cite_note-LAT1-4">[4]</a></sup> including <a href="/wiki/List_of_The_Fairly_OddParents_episodes" title="List of The Fairly OddParents episodes">126 episodes</a> of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Television_series" title="Television series">television series</a>, six <a href="/wiki/The_Fairly_OddParents#The_Fairly_OddParents_special_episodes" title="The Fairly OddParents">specials</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Abra-Catastrophe!" title="Abra-Catastrophe!">seven</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/TV_movies" title="TV movies">TV movies</a>, as well as voicing these same characters in the related <a href="/wiki/The_Fairly_OddParents#Jimmy_Timmy_Power_Hour_trilogy" title="The Fairly OddParents">Jimmy Timmy Power Hour</a> trilogy: <i><a href="/wiki/The_Jimmy_Timmy_Power_Hour" title="The Jimmy Timmy Power Hour">The Jimmy Timmy Power Hour</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Jimmy_Timmy_Power_Hour_2:_When_Nerds_Collide" title="The Jimmy Timmy Power Hour 2: When Nerds Collide">The Jimmy Timmy Power Hour 2: When Nerds Collide</a></i>, and <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Jimmy_Timmy_Power_Hour_3:_The_Jerkinators" title="The Jimmy Timmy Power Hour 3: The Jerkinators">The Jimmy Timmy Power Hour 3: The Jerkinators</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-voicechaser_1-4"><a href="#cite_note-voicechaser-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-filmreference_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-filmreference-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tvguide_3-2"><a href="#cite_note-tvguide-3">[3]</a></sup> In October 2010, Norris voiced the <a href="/wiki/Decepticon" title="Decepticon">Decepticon</a> 'Knock Out' in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Hub_(TV_network)" title="The Hub (TV network)">The Hub TV network</a> original series, <i><a href="/wiki/Transformers:_Prime" title="Transformers: Prime">Transformers: Prime</a></i>. He has also been a voice over in the popular anime show Ghost in the Shell: Stand Alone Complex. Most recently, Norris recurs as the voice of <a href="/wiki/T.U.F.F._Puppy#The_Chief" title="T.U.F.F. Puppy">The Chief</a> and The Chameleon in the 2010 series <i><a href="/wiki/T.U.F.F._Puppy" title="T.U.F.F. Puppy">T.U.F.F. Puppy</a></i>. He also does the voices of <a href="/wiki/Barnyard_Dawg" title="Barnyard Dawg">Barnyard Dawg</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wile_E._Coyote_and_Road_Runner" title="Wile E. Coyote and Road Runner">Wile E. Coyote</a> in the new TV series, <a href="/wiki/The_Looney_Tunes_Show" title="The Looney Tunes Show">The Looney Tunes Show</a>. Norris also played Mr. Turner in the special live-action <a href="/wiki/Television_film" title="Television film">television film</a> <i><a href="/wiki/A_Fairly_Odd_Movie:_Grow_Up,_Timmy_Turner!" title="A Fairly Odd Movie: Grow Up, Timmy Turner!">A Fairly Odd Movie: Grow Up, Timmy Turner!</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-voicechaser_1-5"><a href="#cite_note-voicechaser-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-filmreference_2-3"><a href="#cite_note-filmreference-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tvguide_3-3"><a href="#cite_note-tvguide-3">[3]</a></sup> He has recently done commercials for <a href="/wiki/Burger_King" title="Burger King">Burger King</a>.</p>
