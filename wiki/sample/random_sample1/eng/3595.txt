<p>The <b>Taiwan Chi Yuan Culture Foundation</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: 台灣棋院文化基金會 <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">Pinyin</a>:Táiwān Qíyuàn Wénhuà Jījīnhuì ), also known as the <b>Taiwan Go Association</b>, is a professional <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Go_(board_game)" title="Go (board game)">Go</a> association in <a href="/wiki/Taiwan" title="Taiwan">Taiwan</a>. The Taiwan Qiyuan was created on March 4, 2000 with initial funding provided by Mr. Weng Ming Xian (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>:翁明顯 <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">Pinyin</a>: Wēng Míng Xiǎn) who also served as the first chairman of the association. The chairman is Chen Guoxing. It ranks professionals and runs professional qualification exams for players in Taiwan.</p>
