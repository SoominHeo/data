<p>Year <b>1077</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MLXXVII</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Sunday" title="Common year starting on Sunday">common year starting on Sunday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1077&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_area">By area</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1077&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By area">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Africa">Africa</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1077&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Africa">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Almoravids" title="Almoravids">Almoravids</a> complete the conquest of the <a href="/wiki/Ghana_Empire" title="Ghana Empire">Ghana Empire</a>, and reach Spain.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1077&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seljuk_Turks" title="Seljuk Turks">Seljuk Turks</a> capture <a href="/wiki/%C4%B0znik" title="İznik">Nicaea</a>.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Suleyman_I_of_R%C3%BBm" title="Suleyman I of Rûm">Suleyman I of Rûm</a> becomes the leader of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seljuk_Sultanate_of_R%C3%BBm" title="Seljuk Sultanate of Rûm">Seljuk Sultanate of Rûm</a> in modern <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Anushtegin_Gharchai" title="Anushtegin Gharchai">Anushtegin Gharchai</a> becomes, as a Seljuk vassal, leader of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Khwarezmid_Empire" title="Khwarezmid Empire">Khwarezmid Empire</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1077&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_26" title="January 26">January 26</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Walk_to_Canossa" title="Walk to Canossa">Walk to Canossa</a>: <a href="/wiki/Henry_IV,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Henry IV, Holy Roman Emperor">Henry IV, Holy Roman Emperor</a>, visits <a href="/wiki/Pope_Gregory_VII" title="Pope Gregory VII">Pope Gregory VII</a> as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Penitent" title="Penitent">penitent</a>, asking him to remove sentence of <a href="/wiki/Excommunication" title="Excommunication">excommunication</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_28" title="January 28">January 28</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Excommunication" title="Excommunication">excommunication</a> of <a href="/wiki/Henry_IV,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Henry IV, Holy Roman Emperor">Henry IV, Holy Roman Emperor</a> is lifted.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_3" title="April 3">April 3</a>
<ul>
<li>The first Parliament of <a href="/wiki/Friuli" title="Friuli">Friuli</a> is created.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Henry_IV,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Henry IV, Holy Roman Emperor">Henry IV, Holy Roman Emperor</a> grants the county of <a href="/wiki/Friuli" title="Friuli">Friuli</a>, with ducal status, to <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Sigaerd&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Sigaerd (page does not exist)">Sigaerd</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Patriarch_of_Aquileia" title="Patriarch of Aquileia">Patriarch of Aquileia</a>.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Robert_Curthose" title="Robert Curthose">Robert Curthose</a> instigates his first insurrection against his father, <a href="/wiki/William_the_Conqueror" title="William the Conqueror">William the Conqueror</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Normandy" title="Normandy">Normandy</a>.</li>
<li>The kingdom of <a href="/wiki/Duklja" title="Duklja">Duklja</a> is founded.</li>
<li>The first recorded <a href="/wiki/Trial_by_combat" title="Trial by combat">Trial by combat</a> is held in <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a>.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vsevolod_of_Kiev" title="Vsevolod of Kiev">Vsevolod of Kiev</a> becomes the prince of <a href="/wiki/Kievan_Rus%27" title="Kievan Rus'">Kievan Rus'</a>.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Alfonso_VI_of_Castile" title="Alfonso VI of Castile">Alfonso VI of Castile</a> conquers <a href="/wiki/Coria,_C%C3%A1ceres" title="Coria, Cáceres">Coria</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-picard2000_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-picard2000-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Pope_Gregory_VII" title="Pope Gregory VII">Pope Gregory VII</a> places the island of <a href="/wiki/Corsica" title="Corsica">Corsica</a> under the secular rule of the archbishop of <a href="/wiki/Pisa" title="Pisa">Pisa</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hugh_I,_Duke_of_Burgundy" title="Hugh I, Duke of Burgundy">Hugh of Burgundy</a> supports the <a href="/wiki/Sancho_Ram%C3%ADrez" title="Sancho Ramírez">king of Aragon</a> in his conquest of the castle of <a href="/wiki/Secastilla" title="Secastilla">Muñones</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Ahmad_al-Muqtadir" title="Ahmad al-Muqtadir">emir of Zaragossa</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cruzadas_aragon_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-cruzadas_aragon-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
