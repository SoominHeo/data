<p>The <b>10th <a href="/wiki/Pan_Arab_Games" title="Pan Arab Games">Pan Arab Games</a></b> was an international <a href="/wiki/Multi-sport_event" title="Multi-sport event">multi-sport event</a> which took place in <a href="/wiki/Algiers" title="Algiers">Algiers</a>, <a href="/wiki/Algeria" title="Algeria">Algeria</a>, between 24 September and 10 October 2004. It witnessed the participation of all <a href="/wiki/Arab_League" title="Arab League">Arab League</a> members for the first time – 22 countries participated in 26 sports.</p>
<p>The event was originally scheduled for 2003, but was postponed for a year due to the damage caused by the <a href="/wiki/2003_Boumerd%C3%A8s_earthquake" title="2003 Boumerdès earthquake">2003 Boumerdès earthquake</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
