<p><b>Daejeon Station</b> is on <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea's</a> high-speed <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/KTX" title="KTX">KTX</a> railway network, 166.6 km south of <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Station" title="Seoul Station">Seoul Station</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Daejeon_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The station opened on January 1, 1905 and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/KTX" title="KTX">KTX</a> trains on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gyeongbu_Line_(KTX)" title="Gyeongbu Line (KTX)">Gyeongbu Line</a> began services on April 1, 2004. The station inspired a romantic blues ballad entitled "Daejeon Blues" <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> that has been preferred by musicians throughout Asia and has become a Korean classic. The melody of the song is played on the speakers of the subway trains upon arriving at Daejeon Station.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Services">Services</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Daejeon_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Services">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Daejeon Station serves all <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/KTX" title="KTX">KTX</a> trains on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gyeongbu_Line_(KTX)" title="Gyeongbu Line (KTX)">Gyeongbu Line</a>. It also has express services and local services on the normal speed <a href="/wiki/Gyeongbu_Line" title="Gyeongbu Line">Gyeongbu Line</a>. The station is served by the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Daejeon_Line&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Daejeon Line (page does not exist)">Daejeon Line</a>, a short line connecting Daejeon Station with <a href="/wiki/Seodaejeon_Station" title="Seodaejeon Station">Seodaejeon Station</a>, and also by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Daejeon_Subway" title="Daejeon Subway">Daejeon Subway</a>. Underground shopping can be found connected to Daejeon Station.</p>
