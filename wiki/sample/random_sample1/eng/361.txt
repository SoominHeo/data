<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Athletics">Athletics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Antigua_and_Barbuda_at_the_2004_Summer_Olympics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Athletics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Athletics_at_the_2004_Summer_Olympics" title="Athletics at the 2004 Summer Olympics">Athletics at the 2004 Summer Olympics</a></div>
<p>Athletes from Antigua &amp; Barbuda have so far achieved qualifying standards in the following athletics events (up to a maximum of 3 athletes in each event at the 'A' Standard, and 1 at the 'B' Standard).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<dl>
<dt><small>Key</small></dt>
</dl>
<ul>
<li><small><b>Note</b>–Ranks given for track events are within the athlete's heat only</small></li>
<li><small><b>Q</b> = Qualified for the next round</small></li>
<li><small><b>q</b> = Qualified for the next round as a fastest loser <i>or</i>, in field events, by position without achieving the qualifying target</small></li>
<li><small><b>NR</b> = National record</small></li>
<li><small>N/A = Round not applicable for the event</small></li>
<li><small>Bye = Athlete not required to compete in round</small></li>
</ul>
