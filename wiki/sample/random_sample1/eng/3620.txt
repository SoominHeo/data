<p>The <b>Korea Basketball Association</b> (KBA) (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko">대한농구협회</span>) is the governing body of <a href="/wiki/Basketball" title="Basketball">basketball</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Republic_of_Korea" title="Republic of Korea">Republic of Korea</a>. Formed in 1925, it is based in <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a>. The KBA is a member of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/International_Basketball_Federation" title="International Basketball Federation">International Basketball Federation</a> (FIBA) and <a href="/wiki/FIBA_Asia" title="FIBA Asia">FIBA Asia</a>. The current president of the federation is Pang Yul.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-KBA_Central_Board_Members_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-KBA_Central_Board_Members-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The federation also organizes the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Korea_national_basketball_team" title="Korea national basketball team">Korea national basketball team</a> and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Korea_women%27s_national_basketball_team" title="Korea women's national basketball team">Korea women's national basketball team</a>.</p>
