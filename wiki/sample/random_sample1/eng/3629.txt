<p>The <b>16th South Korean Presidential Election</b> took place on 19 December 2002. The election was won by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Roh_Moo-Hyun" title="Roh Moo-Hyun">Roh Moo-Hyun</a> of the ruling <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Millennium_Democratic_Party" title="Millennium Democratic Party">Millennium Democratic Party</a>, who defeated <a href="/wiki/Lee_Hoi-chang" title="Lee Hoi-chang">Lee Hoi-chang</a> of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grand_National_Party" title="Grand National Party">Grand National Party</a> by just over half a million votes.</p>
