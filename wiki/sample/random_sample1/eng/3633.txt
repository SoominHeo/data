<p>This is a <b>list of diplomatic missions in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a></b>. There are currently 110 embassies and 2 Representative Offices in <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a>, and some countries maintain consulates (not including honorary consulates) in cities other than Seoul as well. Several other countries have diplomatic ties with South Korea but do not operate embassies in Seoul maintain non-resident embassies, mostly in <a href="/wiki/Tokyo" title="Tokyo">Tokyo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Beijing" title="Beijing">Beijing</a> or elsewhere.</p>
