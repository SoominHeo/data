<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Policies">Policies</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=New_Right_(South_Korea)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Policies">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="School_textbooks">School textbooks</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=New_Right_(South_Korea)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: School textbooks">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Some well-known politicians from the "old right" <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grand_National_Party" title="Grand National Party">GNP</a> indicated commonality with the New Right groups, including <a href="/wiki/Park_Geun-hye" title="Park Geun-hye">Park Geun-hye</a> endorse the <i>anti-North Korean</i> New Right's version of <i>alternative Korean History textbooks</i> through a foundation called <a class="extiw" href="https://ko.wikipedia.org/wiki/%EA%B5%90%EA%B3%BC%EC%84%9C%ED%8F%AC%EB%9F%BC" title="ko:교과서포럼">Text Book Forum</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Lee_Myung-bak_government" title="Lee Myung-bak government">Lee Myung-bak government</a>'s <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Education,_Science_and_Technology" title="Ministry of Education, Science and Technology">Ministry of Education, Science and Technology</a> has tried to implant the next Korean New Right version of Korean history textbook for the public school usage.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The NR's groups' demanding pressure to use their <i>hawkish</i> Korean history textbooks has eventually make them inconsistently incompatible with the <i>dovish</i> ethics textbooks.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Conservative_education">Conservative education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=New_Right_(South_Korea)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Conservative education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The New Right establishments often opposes the liberal <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Korean_Teachers_%26_Education_Workers%27_Union" title="Korean Teachers &amp; Education Workers' Union">Korean Teachers &amp; Education Workers' Union</a>.</p>
