<p><i><b>Love Yunge from the Desert</b></i> is a 2015 <a href="/wiki/History_of_China" title="History of China">Chinese historical</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Romantic_comedy" title="Romantic comedy">romantic comedy</a> television series based on <a href="/wiki/Tong_Hua_(writer)" title="Tong Hua (writer)">Tong Hua</a>'s novel <i><a href="/wiki/Song_in_the_Clouds" title="Song in the Clouds">Song in the Clouds</a></i>. The television series was produced by <a href="/wiki/Yu_Zheng" title="Yu Zheng">Yu Zheng</a> and written by Shen Zhining, and starring <a href="/wiki/Angelababy" title="Angelababy">Angelababy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Du_Chun" title="Du Chun">Du Chun</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lu_Yi_(actor)" title="Lu Yi (actor)">Lu Yi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chen_Xiao" title="Chen Xiao">Chen Xiao</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yang_Rong_(actress)" title="Yang Rong (actress)">Yang Rong</a>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Su_Qing_(actress)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Su Qing (actress) (page does not exist)">Su Qing</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bao_Bei%27er" title="Bao Bei'er">Bao Bei'er</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Rachel_Momo&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Rachel Momo (page does not exist)">Rachel Momo</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> It is a sequel to <i><a href="/wiki/Sound_of_the_Desert_(TV_series)" title="Sound of the Desert (TV series)">Sound of the Desert</a></i> (<span lang="zh-Hans">《风中奇缘》</span>). <i>Love Yunge From The Desert</i> began airing on September 13, 2015 on <a href="/wiki/Hunan_Television" title="Hunan Television">Hunan Television</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Love_Yunge_from_the_Desert&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During the Western Han Dynasty, an eight-year-old Emperor Zhao of Han meets a girl Huo Yun Ge (daughter of Huo Qu Bing and Jin Yu from Ballad of the Desert), who rescued him from the cold of the desert. Ten years later, Yun Ge is now a beautiful young woman, who could not forget the boy she saved. She ventures to find her Ling Ge Ge (Brother Ling) who has also not forgotten her after all these years. However, she mistakens Liu Xun for Liu Fuling when she sees a memento that Liu Fuling also has. Heartbroken that Liu Xun already has Xu Pingjun, she tries to let go. Huo Yun Ge later meets a handsome Meng Jue, only later to find herself embroiled in a power struggles within the imperial palace. Only later on does she come to realize that Liu Fuling is actually the Ling Ge Ge that she has been searching for all along. By then, she had already fallen for Meng Jue.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Production">Production</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Love_Yunge_from_the_Desert&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Production">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On March 18, 2013, author of the original novel Tong Hua announced the film rights has been bought by <a href="/wiki/Yu_Zheng" title="Yu Zheng">Yu Zheng</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Principal_photography" title="Principal photography">Principal photography</a> started on April 3, 2013 and wrapped in July 3.</p>
<p>This television series shot the scene in <a href="/wiki/Hengdian_World_Studios" title="Hengdian World Studios">Hengdian World Studios</a>, <a href="/wiki/Zhejiang" title="Zhejiang">Zhejiang</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>On August 18, 2014, the producer released the music video for its theme song, <i>Silk</i>, which was sung by well-known Chinese singer <a href="/wiki/Li_Yuchun" title="Li Yuchun">Li Yuchun</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>On August 11, 2015, the producer released the trailer.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
