<p>These are the results of the <b>men's <a href="/wiki/Foil_(fencing)" title="Foil (fencing)">foil</a> team</b> competition in <b><a href="/wiki/Fencing_at_the_2004_Summer_Olympics" title="Fencing at the 2004 Summer Olympics">fencing at the 2004 Summer Olympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Athens" title="Athens">Athens</a>. A total of 30 men from eight nations competed in this event. Competition took place in the Fencing Hall at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Helliniko_Olympic_Complex" title="Helliniko Olympic Complex">Helliniko Olympic Complex</a> on August 21.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Tournament_results">Tournament results</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Fencing_at_the_2004_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_team_foil&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Tournament results">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The team competition was a <a href="/wiki/Single-elimination_tournament" title="Single-elimination tournament">single-elimination tournament</a> among the eight teams. Quarterfinal losers continued to play classification matches to determine final placement from first to eighth. Each team match consisted of a set of nine individual matches, comprising a full <a href="/wiki/Round-robin_tournament" title="Round-robin tournament">round-robin</a> schedule among the three fencers on each team.</p>
