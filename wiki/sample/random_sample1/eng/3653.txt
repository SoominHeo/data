<p>A <b>deluge</b> is a large downpour of <a href="/wiki/Rain" title="Rain">rain</a>, often a <a href="/wiki/Flood" title="Flood">flood</a>.</p>
<p><b>Deluge</b> may also refer to:</p>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Flood_myth" title="Flood myth">Flood myth</a>, mythic floods in general, involving Gilgamesh, and others</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Deluge_(prehistoric)" title="Deluge (prehistoric)">Deluge (prehistoric)</a>, prehistoric great floods, some of which may have inspired deluge myths</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Genesis_flood_narrative" title="Genesis flood narrative">Genesis flood narrative</a>, of the Bible, in which Noah builds an ark</li>
</ul>
