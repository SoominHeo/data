<p><b>The Wizarding World of Harry Potter</b> is a chain of themed areas at <a href="/wiki/Universal_Parks_%26_Resorts" title="Universal Parks &amp; Resorts">Universal Parks &amp; Resorts</a> based on the <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a></i> media franchise, adapting elements from the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">film series</a> and novels by <a href="/wiki/J._K._Rowling" title="J. K. Rowling">J. K. Rowling</a>. The areas were designed by <a href="/wiki/Universal_Creative" title="Universal Creative">Universal Creative</a> from an exclusive <a href="/wiki/License" title="License">license</a> with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Warner_Bros._Entertainment" title="Warner Bros. Entertainment">Warner Bros. Entertainment</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A <i>Harry Potter</i> themed attraction at a Universal Studios park or a <a href="/wiki/Walt_Disney_Parks_and_Resorts" title="Walt Disney Parks and Resorts">Disney park</a> was rumored in 2003.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-jimhill030814_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-jimhill030814-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tpi030712_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-tpi030712-9">[9]</a></sup> However, the rights to the Harry Potter franchise had been acquired by <a href="/wiki/Warner_Bros." title="Warner Bros.">Warner Bros.</a>, who denied all rumors.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-os030808_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-os030808-10">[10]</a></sup> In January 2007, <a href="/wiki/About.com" title="About.com">About.com</a> reported a rumor from a "highly credible source" that the <a href="/wiki/Islands_of_Adventure" title="Islands of Adventure">Islands of Adventure</a> park's <a href="/wiki/Islands_of_Adventure#The_Lost_Continent" title="Islands of Adventure">Lost Continent</a> area was going to be re-themed "to the stories and characters of one of the most popular children's franchises".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-about070128_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-about070128-11">[11]</a></sup> Other sources followed up in the next few days with unofficial confirmation that the new area would involve <i>Harry Potter</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-screamscape070201_12-0"><a href="#cite_note-screamscape070201-12">[12]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-jimhill070205_13-0"><a href="#cite_note-jimhill070205-13">[13]</a></sup> On May 31, 2007, Universal, in partnership with Warner Bros., officially announced <i>The Wizarding World of Harry Potter</i> would be added to Islands of Adventure.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-bbc070531_14-0"><a href="#cite_note-bbc070531-14">[14]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-guardian070531_15-0"><a href="#cite_note-guardian070531-15">[15]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-usatoday070531_16-0"><a href="#cite_note-usatoday070531-16">[16]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-stpetersburg070601_17-0"><a href="#cite_note-stpetersburg070601-17">[17]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Parks">Parks</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Parks">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Universal_Orlando_Resort">Universal Orlando Resort</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Universal Orlando Resort">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter_(Universal_Orlando_Resort)" title="The Wizarding World of Harry Potter (Universal Orlando Resort)">The Wizarding World of Harry Potter (Universal Orlando Resort)</a></div>
<p>At the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Universal_Orlando_Resort" title="Universal Orlando Resort">Universal Orlando Resort</a> in <a href="/wiki/Orlando,_Florida" title="Orlando, Florida">Orlando, Florida</a>, Islands Of Adventure and Universal Studios have Wizarding World of Harry Potter themed environments. Both parks include <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express_(Universal_Orlando_Resort)" title="Hogwarts Express (Universal Orlando Resort)">Hogwarts Express</a> stations allowing passengers to take the train reach both theme parks aboard the full-scale replica of the train that appears in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">film series</a>.</p>
<p>At the <a href="/wiki/Islands_of_Adventure" title="Islands of Adventure">Islands of Adventure</a> theme park, the Wizarding World of Harry Potter opened June 18, 2010. It includes re-creation of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hogsmeade" title="Hogsmeade">Hogsmeade</a> and three rides. The flagship attraction is <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Forbidden_Journey" title="Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey">Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey</a></i>, which exists within a re-creation of <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts" title="Hogwarts">Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry</a>. Other rides include <i><a href="/wiki/Dragon_Challenge" title="Dragon Challenge">Dragon Challenge</a></i>, a pair of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Inverted_roller_coasters" title="Inverted roller coasters">inverted roller coasters</a>, <i><a href="/wiki/Flight_of_the_Hippogriff" title="Flight of the Hippogriff">Flight of the Hippogriff</a></i>, a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Family_roller_coaster" title="Family roller coaster">family roller coaster</a>, and the Hogwarts Express. Other attractions include Ollivander's Wand Shop, Triwizard Spirit Rally, and the Frog Choir. Hogsmeade containing many gift shops and restaurants from the book series including Dervish and Banges, Honeydukes, Ollivanders, the Three Broomsticks, and the Hog's Head.</p>
<p>At the <a href="/wiki/Universal_Studios_Florida" title="Universal Studios Florida">Universal Studios Florida</a> theme park, <i>The Wizarding World of Harry Potter</i> opened July 8, 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-orlandosentinel20140717_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-orlandosentinel20140717-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-huffington140709_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-huffington140709-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tpinsider140708_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-tpinsider140708-4">[4]</a></sup> It includes re-creation of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Diagon_Alley" title="Diagon Alley">Diagon Alley</a> and connecting alleys, as well as, a small section of <a href="/wiki/Muggle" title="Muggle">Muggle</a> <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>. The rides are <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Escape_from_Gringotts" title="Harry Potter and the Escape from Gringotts">Harry Potter and the Escape from Gringotts</a></i> roller coaster ride and the <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts_Express_(Universal_Orlando_Resort)" title="Hogwarts Express (Universal Orlando Resort)">Hogwarts Express</a>. Other attractions include Ollivander's Wand Shop, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Tales_of_Beedle_the_Bard" title="The Tales of Beedle the Bard">The Tales of Beedle the Bard</a></i> show and Celestina Warbeck and the Banshees performance. The area also containing many shops and restaurants from the book series including <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Leaky_Cauldron_(pub)" title="The Leaky Cauldron (pub)">The Leaky Cauldron</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ollivanders_Wand_Shop" title="Ollivanders Wand Shop">Ollivanders Wand Shop</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Weasleys%27_Wizard_Wheezes" title="Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes">Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Borgin_and_Burkes" title="Borgin and Burkes">Borgin and Burkes</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Madam_Malkin%27s_Robes_for_All_Occasions" title="Madam Malkin's Robes for All Occasions">Madam Malkin's Robes for All Occasions</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wiseacre%27s_Wizarding_Equipment" title="Wiseacre's Wizarding Equipment">Wiseacre's Wizarding Equipment</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Florean_Fortescue%27s_Ice_Cream_Parlour" title="Florean Fortescue's Ice Cream Parlour">Florean Fortescue's Ice Cream Parlour</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Universal_Studios_Japan">Universal Studios Japan</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Universal Studios Japan">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter_(Universal_Studios_Japan)" title="The Wizarding World of Harry Potter (Universal Studios Japan)">The Wizarding World of Harry Potter (Universal Studios Japan)</a></div>
<p>At the <a href="/wiki/Universal_Studios_Japan" title="Universal Studios Japan">Universal Studios Japan</a> theme park in <a href="/wiki/Osaka" title="Osaka">Osaka</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>, <i>The Wizarding World of Harry Potter</i> opened on July 15, 2014. It includes the village of Hogsmeade, <i>Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey</i> ride, and <i>Flight of the Hippogriff</i> roller coaster.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-orlando20140418_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-orlando20140418-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cnn20140716_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-cnn20140716-6">[6]</a></sup> Two features in the Japanese park not found in Orlando are <a href="/wiki/Hogwarts" title="Hogwarts">Hogwarts</a>' Black Lake and live owls.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cnn20140716_6-2"><a href="#cite_note-cnn20140716-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Universal_Studios_Hollywood">Universal Studios Hollywood</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Universal Studios Hollywood">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/The_Wizarding_World_of_Harry_Potter_(Universal_Studios_Hollywood)" title="The Wizarding World of Harry Potter (Universal Studios Hollywood)">The Wizarding World of Harry Potter (Universal Studios Hollywood)</a></div>
<p>The Wizarding World of Harry Potter opened at <a href="/wiki/Universal_Studios_Hollywood" title="Universal Studios Hollywood">Universal Studios Hollywood</a> on April 7, 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup> The flagship attraction is the fight-simulating ride <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Forbidden_Journey" title="Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey">Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey</a></i>, though compared to the Orlando version, the Hollywood version incorporates 3D technology. Visitors wear Quidditch-inspired 3D goggles throughout the ride for an enhanced ride experience.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup></p>
