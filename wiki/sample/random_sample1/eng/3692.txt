<p><b>Dêgê County</b> (<a href="/wiki/Tibetan_alphabet" title="Tibetan alphabet">Tibetan</a>: <span lang="bo" style="font-family: 'Qomolangma-Dunhuang', 'Qomolangma-Uchen Sarchen', 'Qomolangma-Uchen Sarchung', 'Qomolangma-Uchen Suring', 'Qomolangma-Uchen Sutung', 'Qomolangma-Title', 'Qomolangma-Subtitle', 'Qomolangma-Woodblock', 'DDC Uchen', Kailash, Jomolhari, 'TCRC Youtso Unicode', 'Tibetan Machine Uni', 'Noto Sans Tibetan', 'Microsoft Himalaya'; font-size:1.25em; vertical-align:top; word-wrap: break-word;" xml:lang="bo">སྡེ་དགེ</span>, <a href="/wiki/Wylie_transliteration" title="Wylie transliteration">Wylie</a>: <i>sde dge</i>, <a href="/wiki/Tibetan_pinyin" title="Tibetan pinyin">ZYPY</a>: Dêgê; <a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">德格</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Dégé</span></i>) was formerly one of the <a href="/wiki/Kham" title="Kham">Kham</a> region's five independent kingdoms - the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Derge" title="Kingdom of Derge">Kingdom of Derge</a> - and is now administered as a county in <a href="/wiki/Garz%C3%AA_Tibetan_Autonomous_Prefecture" title="Garzê Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture">Garzê Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture</a> in China's <a href="/wiki/Sichuan" title="Sichuan">Sichuan</a> province. Its county seat is the town of <a href="/wiki/Derge" title="Derge">Derge</a>.</p>
