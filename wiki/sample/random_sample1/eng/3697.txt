<p><i><b>Codonopsis</b></i> is a genus of <a href="/wiki/Flowering_plant" title="Flowering plant">flowering plant</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Campanulaceae" title="Campanulaceae">Campanulaceae</a>. As currently recognized, <i>Codonopsis</i> includes two other groups sometimes separated as distinct genera, i.e. <i>Campanumoea</i> and <i>Leptocodon.</i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-connie_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-connie-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> The enlarged genus <i>Codonopsis</i> is widespread across eastern, southern, central, and southeastern <a href="/wiki/Asia" title="Asia">Asia</a>, including <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Russian_Far_East" title="Russian Far East">Russian Far East</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kazakhstan" title="Kazakhstan">Kazakhstan</a>, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Indian_Subcontinent" title="Indian Subcontinent">Indian Subcontinent</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>, <a href="/wiki/Indochina" title="Indochina">Indochina</a>, <a href="/wiki/Indonesia" title="Indonesia">Indonesia</a>, etc.</p>
