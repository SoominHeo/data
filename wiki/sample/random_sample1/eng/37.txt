<p><b>October 30</b> is the 303rd day of the year (304th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 62 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Tuesday, Friday or Sunday (58 in 400 years each) than on Wednesday or Thursday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Monday or Saturday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=October_30&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/637" title="637">637</a> – <a href="/wiki/Antioch" title="Antioch">Antioch</a> surrenders to the Muslim forces under <a href="/wiki/Rashidun_Caliphate" title="Rashidun Caliphate">Rashidun Caliphate</a> after the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Iron_Bridge" title="Battle of the Iron Bridge">Battle of the Iron Bridge</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/758" title="758">758</a> – <a href="/wiki/Guangzhou" title="Guangzhou">Guangzhou</a> is sacked by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arab" title="Arab">Arab</a> and <a href="/wiki/Persian_people" title="Persian people">Persian</a> <a href="/wiki/Piracy" title="Piracy">pirates</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1137" title="1137">1137</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Rignano" title="Battle of Rignano">Battle of Rignano</a> between <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ranulf_of_Apulia" title="Ranulf of Apulia">Ranulf of Apulia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Roger_II_of_Sicily" title="Roger II of Sicily">Roger II of Sicily</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1270" title="1270">1270</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Eighth_Crusade" title="Eighth Crusade">Eighth Crusade</a> and siege of <a href="/wiki/Tunis" title="Tunis">Tunis</a> end by an agreement between <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Charles_I_of_Sicily" title="Charles I of Sicily">Charles I of Sicily</a> (brother to King <a href="/wiki/Louis_IX_of_France" title="Louis IX of France">Louis IX of France</a>, who had died months earlier) and the <a href="/wiki/Sultan" title="Sultan">sultan</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tunis" title="Tunis">Tunis</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1340" title="1340">1340</a> – <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Portugal" title="Kingdom of Portugal">Portuguese</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Castile" title="Kingdom of Castile">Castilian</a> forces halt a <a href="/wiki/Marinid_dynasty" title="Marinid dynasty">Marinid</a> invasion at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_R%C3%ADo_Salado" title="Battle of Río Salado">Battle of Río Salado</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1485" title="1485">1485</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Henry_VII_of_England" title="Henry VII of England">Henry VII of England</a> is crowned.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1501" title="1501">1501</a> – <a href="/wiki/Banquet_of_Chestnuts" title="Banquet of Chestnuts">Ballet of Chestnuts</a>: A banquet held by <a href="/wiki/Cesare_Borgia" title="Cesare Borgia">Cesare Borgia</a> in the Papal Palace where fifty prostitutes or courtesans are in attendance for the entertainment of the guests.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1657" title="1657">1657</a> – <a href="/wiki/Spanish_Empire" title="Spanish Empire">Spanish</a> forces fail to retake <a href="/wiki/Jamaica" title="Jamaica">Jamaica</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Ocho_Rios" title="Battle of Ocho Rios">Battle of Ocho Rios</a> during the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Anglo-Spanish_War_(1654%E2%80%9360)" title="Anglo-Spanish War (1654–60)">Anglo-Spanish War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1806" title="1806">1806</a> – Believing he is facing a much larger force, Prussian Lieutenant General Friedrich von Romberg, commanding 5,300 men, <a href="/wiki/Capitulation_of_Stettin" title="Capitulation of Stettin">surrendered the city of Stettin</a> to 800 French soldiers commanded by <a href="/wiki/Antoine_Charles_Louis_de_Lasalle" title="Antoine Charles Louis de Lasalle">General Lassalle</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1817" title="1817">1817</a> – The independent government of <a href="/wiki/Venezuela" title="Venezuela">Venezuela</a> is established by <a href="/wiki/Sim%C3%B3n_Bol%C3%ADvar" title="Simón Bolívar">Simón Bolívar</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1831" title="1831">1831</a> – In <a href="/wiki/Southampton_County,_Virginia" title="Southampton County, Virginia">Southampton County, Virginia</a>, escaped slave <a href="/wiki/Nat_Turner" title="Nat Turner">Nat Turner</a> is captured and arrested for leading the bloodiest <a href="/wiki/Nat_Turner%27s_slave_rebellion" title="Nat Turner's slave rebellion">slave rebellion</a> in United States history.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1863" title="1863">1863</a> – Danish Prince Vilhelm arrives in <a href="/wiki/Athens" title="Athens">Athens</a> to assume his throne as <a href="/wiki/George_I_of_Greece" title="George I of Greece">George I</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/King_of_the_Hellenes" title="King of the Hellenes">King of the Hellenes</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1864" title="1864">1864</a> – <a href="/wiki/Second_Schleswig_War" title="Second Schleswig War">Second Schleswig War</a> ends. Denmark renounces all claim to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Schleswig" title="Schleswig">Schleswig</a>, <a href="/wiki/Holstein" title="Holstein">Holstein</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lauenburg_(Elbe)" title="Lauenburg (Elbe)">Lauenburg</a>, which come under <a href="/wiki/Prussia" title="Prussia">Prussian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Austrian_Empire" title="Austrian Empire">Austrian</a> administration.</li>
<li>1864 – <a href="/wiki/Helena,_Montana" title="Helena, Montana">Helena, Montana</a> is founded after four <a href="/wiki/Prospecting" title="Prospecting">prospectors</a> discover <a href="/wiki/Gold" title="Gold">gold</a> at "Last Chance Gulch".</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1888" title="1888">1888</a> – <a href="/wiki/Rudd_Concession" title="Rudd Concession">Rudd Concession</a> granted by King <a href="/wiki/Lobengula" title="Lobengula">Lobengula</a> of <a href="/wiki/Matabeleland" title="Matabeleland">Matabeleland</a> to agents of <a href="/wiki/Cecil_Rhodes" title="Cecil Rhodes">Cecil Rhodes</a> led by <a href="/wiki/Charles_Rudd" title="Charles Rudd">Charles Rudd</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1894" title="1894">1894</a> – Domenico Melegatti obtains a patent for a procedure to be applied in producing <a href="/wiki/Pandoro" title="Pandoro">pandoro</a> industrially.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1905" title="1905">1905</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Czar" title="Czar">Czar</a> <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_II_of_Russia" title="Nicholas II of Russia">Nicholas II of Russia</a> issues the <a href="/wiki/October_Manifesto" title="October Manifesto">October Manifesto</a>, granting the Russian peoples basic civil liberties and the right to form a <a href="/wiki/State_Duma_(Russian_Empire)" title="State Duma (Russian Empire)">duma</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1918" title="1918">1918</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a> signs an <a href="/wiki/Armistice" title="Armistice">armistice</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Allies_of_World_War_I" title="Allies of World War I">Allies</a>, ending the <a href="/wiki/World_War_I" title="World War I">First World War</a> in the Middle East.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1920" title="1920">1920</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Communist_Party_of_Australia" title="Communist Party of Australia">Communist Party of Australia</a> is founded in Sydney.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1925" title="1925">1925</a> – <a href="/wiki/John_Logie_Baird" title="John Logie Baird">John Logie Baird</a> creates Britain's first <a href="/wiki/Television_transmitter" title="Television transmitter">television transmitter</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1929" title="1929">1929</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Standseilbahn_Stuttgart" title="Standseilbahn Stuttgart">Stuttgart Cable Car</a> is constructed in <a href="/wiki/Stuttgart" title="Stuttgart">Stuttgart</a>, Germany.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1938" title="1938">1938</a> – <a href="/wiki/Orson_Welles" title="Orson Welles">Orson Welles</a> broadcasts his radio play of <a href="/wiki/H._G._Wells" title="H. G. Wells">H. G.
