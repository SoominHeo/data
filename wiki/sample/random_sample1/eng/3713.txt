<p><b>Deogyang-gu</b> is a ward in <a href="/wiki/Goyang" title="Goyang">Goyang</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. It is noticeably less affluent than the other two wards in Goyang.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Administration">Administration</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Deogyang-gu&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Administration">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Deogyang-gu is divided into 19 <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Subdivisions_of_South_Korea" title="Subdivisions of South Korea">dong</a></i> (동, "neighborhoods"):</p>
<ul>
<li>Jugyo-dong</li>
<li>Seongsa 1 and 2 dong</li>
<li>Wonsin-dong (combination from Wondang-dong and Sinwon-dong)</li>
<li>Heungdo-dong (combination from Wonheung-dong and Donae-dong)</li>
<li>Sindo-dong (divided into Samsong-dong and Ogeum-dong)</li>
<li>Changneung-dong (divided into Dongsan-dong and Yongdu-dong)</li>
<li>Hyoja-dong (divided into Hyoja-dong, Jichuk-dong and Bukhan-dong)</li>
<li>Hwajeon-dong (divided into Hwajeon-dong, Deogeun-dong and Hyangdong-dong)</li>
<li>Daedeok-dong (contain Hyeoncheon-dong)</li>
<li>Haengsin 1 to 3 dong (Haengsin 2-dong [Gangmae-dong])</li>
<li>Haengju-dong (divided into Haengjunae-dong, Haengjuoe-dong, Todang-dong and Daejang-dong)</li>
<li>Neunggok-dong (divided into Naegok-dong and Sinpyeong-dong)</li>
<li>Hwajeong 1 and 2 dong</li>
<li>Gwansan-dong (divided into Gwansan-dong and Naeyu-dong)</li>
<li>Goyang-dong (divided into Goyang-dong, Byeokje-dong, Daeja-dong and Seonyu-dong )</li>
</ul>
