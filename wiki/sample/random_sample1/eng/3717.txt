<p><b>Dundas West</b> is a station on the <a href="/wiki/Line_2_Bloor%E2%80%93Danforth" title="Line 2 Bloor–Danforth">Bloor–Danforth subway line</a> in <a href="/wiki/Toronto" title="Toronto">Toronto</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ontario" title="Ontario">Ontario</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>. It is located just north of <a href="/wiki/Bloor_Street" title="Bloor Street">Bloor Street West</a> at the corner of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dundas_Street_(Toronto)" title="Dundas Street (Toronto)">Dundas Street</a> and Edna Avenue. The station is about 200 metres west of <a href="/wiki/Bloor_GO_Station" title="Bloor GO Station">Bloor GO Station</a> on the <a href="/wiki/GO_Transit" title="GO Transit">GO Transit</a> <a href="/wiki/Kitchener_line" title="Kitchener line">Kitchener line</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Union_Pearson_Express" title="Union Pearson Express">Union Pearson Express</a>.</p>
<p>Wi-Fi service is available at this station.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> A <a href="/wiki/McDonald%27s_Canada" title="McDonald's Canada">McDonald's</a> restaurant serves the station, with access from both the fare-paid and non-fare-paid areas of the station's upper level, and there is a <a href="/wiki/Gateway_Newstands" title="Gateway Newstands">Gateway Newstand</a> on the mezzanine level.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Overview">Overview</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dundas_West_(TTC)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Overview">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft">

<p>To the east of the station, the subway runs in a twin bored tunnel until just before the next station (<a href="/wiki/Lansdowne_(TTC)" title="Lansdowne (TTC)">Lansdowne</a>). This allowed the tracks to pass underneath nearby railway lines without disturbing them during construction. To the west, the tracks follow a short "cut and cover" tunnel before emerging outside at the <i>Dorval Portal</i>. Trains run in open-air until they enter Keele Station.</p>
<p>South of the subway platforms, underground, are the four tunnels that comprise the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vincent_Subway_Yard" title="Vincent Subway Yard">Vincent Subway Yard</a>. This station is also home to the Subway Track Maintenance Office, located on the Mezzanine level.</p>
<p>Nearby landmarks include <a href="/wiki/The_Crossways,_Toronto" title="The Crossways, Toronto">The Crossways</a> residential and retail complex, <a href="/wiki/Bishop_Marrocco/Thomas_Merton_Catholic_Secondary_School" title="Bishop Marrocco/Thomas Merton Catholic Secondary School">Bishop Marrocco/Thomas Merton Catholic Secondary School</a>, The Lithuanian House banquet hall, <a href="/wiki/Roncesvalles,_Toronto" title="Roncesvalles, Toronto">Roncesvalles</a> and <a href="/wiki/The_Junction" title="The Junction">The Junction</a> neighbourhoods.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dundas_West_(TTC)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Dundas West station opened in 1966 as a part of the initial segment of the Bloor-Danforth line between <a href="/wiki/Keele_(TTC)" title="Keele (TTC)">Keele</a> and <a href="/wiki/Woodbine_(TTC)" title="Woodbine (TTC)">Woodbine</a>.</p>
<p>In 2002 as part of a scheduled reconstruction of the streetcar tracks on Dundas Street, a second streetcar track and platform was added in an effort to improve reliability on both the <a href="/wiki/504_King" title="504 King">504 King</a> and <a href="/wiki/505_Dundas" title="505 Dundas">505 Dundas</a> streetcar routes. Until the completion of the second track, a streetcar waiting in the station on either route could hold up vehicles on the other. At the same time, elevators were added, making the station wheelchair-accessible.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Connection_to_Bloor_GO_station">Connection to Bloor GO station</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dundas_West_(TTC)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Connection to Bloor GO station">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Metrolinx plans to build a direct connection to Dundas West station to the nearby Bloor GO Station with a new pedestrian tunnel from the east end of the subway platforms.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Currently customers transferring from the TTC to GO/UPX need to walk 200 metres east along city streets from the only station entrance, at the west end of the subway platforms.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TorStar2015-05-22_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-TorStar2015-05-22-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
