<p><b>Anderson Vital da Silva</b> (born July 1, 1988 in <a href="/wiki/Volta_Redonda" title="Volta Redonda">Volta Redonda</a>), also known as <b>Dedé</b>, is a <a href="/wiki/Brazilians" title="Brazilians">Brazilian</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Centre_back" title="Centre back">centre back</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sweeper_(football)" title="Sweeper (football)">sweeper</a> for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cruzeiro_EC" title="Cruzeiro EC">Cruzeiro</a> and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Brazilian_national_football_team" title="Brazilian national football team">Brazilian national football team</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ded%C3%A9_(footballer,_born_1988)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Vasco_da_Gama">Vasco da Gama</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ded%C3%A9_(footballer,_born_1988)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Vasco da Gama">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After a poor spell <a href="/wiki/Campeonato_Carioca" title="Campeonato Carioca">Campeonato Carioca</a> for <a href="/wiki/Volta_Redonda_Futebol_Clube" title="Volta Redonda Futebol Clube">Volta Redonda</a> in 2009, Dedé was loaned with a buy-out clause to the <i><a href="/wiki/CR_Vasco_da_Gama" title="CR Vasco da Gama">Giant of the Hill</a></i>. In <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Club_de_Regatas_Vasco_da_Gama" title="Club de Regatas Vasco da Gama">Vasco da Gama</a>, Dedé the Centreback won his first title as a professional, the <a href="/wiki/2009_Campeonato_Brasileiro_S%C3%A9rie_B" title="2009 Campeonato Brasileiro Série B">Campeonato Brasileiro Serie B in 2009</a>, despite not having had many chances in the first team.</p>
<p>In 2010, Dedé started the year on the bench and had very few chances to show his skills at <a href="/wiki/CR_Vasco_da_Gama" title="CR Vasco da Gama">Vasco da Gama</a>. However, in a game against <a href="/wiki/Esporte_Clube_Vit%C3%B3ria" title="Esporte Clube Vitória">Vitória</a>, <a href="/wiki/2010_Copa_do_Brasil" title="2010 Copa do Brasil">in the quarter-final of the Copa do Brasil</a>, Dedé the Centreback was selected due to the lack of available defenders and subsequently had his contract (which would end in a month) renewed until the end of 2010. He played very well and after a string of solid and impressive displays, became the defensive backbone of <a href="/wiki/CR_Vasco_da_Gama" title="CR Vasco da Gama">Vasco da Gama</a>, becoming a fan favourite, heavily cheered by <a href="/wiki/CR_Vasco_da_Gama" title="CR Vasco da Gama">Vasco da Gama's</a> supporters.</p>
<p>In October 2010, the buy-out clause was exercised, and subsequently Dedé the Centreback and <a href="/wiki/CR_Vasco_da_Gama" title="CR Vasco da Gama">Vasco da Gama</a> signed an agreement until the end of 2014. In the same year, Dedé won the award of best right centre back in Brasileirão. In 2011, formed a rock solid duo with <a href="/wiki/Anderson_Martins" title="Anderson Martins">Anderson Martins</a> that won the <a href="/wiki/2011_Copa_do_Brasil" title="2011 Copa do Brasil">2011 Copa do Brasil</a>, with both players being elected the best central defenders of the <a href="/wiki/2011_Campeonato_Carioca" title="2011 Campeonato Carioca">2011 Campeonato Carioca</a>.</p>
<p>Currently, Dedé is considered one of the best <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">centre backs</a> in <a href="/wiki/Football_in_Brazil" title="Football in Brazil">Brazilian football</a>, with great acclaim from critics and fans. With great performances and the big identification with the <a href="/wiki/CR_Vasco_da_Gama" title="CR Vasco da Gama">Vasco da Gama's</a> fans, Dedé acquired several nicknames including: (<i>"<a href="/wiki/Mythology" title="Mythology">The Myth</a>"</i>), <i>"<a href="/wiki/Franz_Beckenbauer" title="Franz Beckenbauer">Dedéckenbauer</a>"</i> and even <i>"Dedélícia"</i> (<i>"Dedélicious"</i>, by <a href="/wiki/Female" title="Female">female fans</a>). Dedé has become one of the great idols of the <a href="/wiki/CR_Vasco_da_Gama" title="CR Vasco da Gama">Vasco da Gama</a> in the 21st Century, turning up the campaign poster boy <i>"Marco1924"</i> (promotional clock that celebrates the victory of <a href="/wiki/CR_Vasco_da_Gama" title="CR Vasco da Gama">Vasco da Gama</a> against <a href="/wiki/Racism" title="Racism">racism</a> in <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a>), along with <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Prass" title="Fernando Prass">Fernando Prass</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> On July 11, was elected by <a href="/wiki/Sistema_Brasileiro_de_Televis%C3%A3o" title="Sistema Brasileiro de Televisão">SBT</a> and a popular vote as one of the 100 greatest Brazilians of all time.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> On one of his last games for Vasco da Gama, he kissed the club's emblem to show his love for the club even with his transfer.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Cruzeiro">Cruzeiro</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ded%C3%A9_(footballer,_born_1988)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Cruzeiro">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On April 17, 2013, Dedé was confirmed as new player for <a href="/wiki/Cruzeiro_Esporte_Clube" title="Cruzeiro Esporte Clube">Cruzeiro</a>, the cruzerio club is chidis monastery <a href="/wiki/Cruzeiro_Esporte_Clube" title="Cruzeiro Esporte Clube"><i>Celestes</i></a> paid a fee of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Brazilian_Real" title="Brazilian Real">R$</a>14 million plus midfielder Allison. <a href="/wiki/Wellington_Paulista" title="Wellington Paulista">Wellington Paulista</a>, on loan with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/West_Ham_United" title="West Ham United">West Ham</a> was also included in the negotiation. Dedé became the most expensive player ever purchased by <a href="/wiki/Cruzeiro_Esporte_Clube" title="Cruzeiro Esporte Clube">Cruzeiro</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> In an interview just after his transfer he stated he never intended to leave Vasco, but he knew his transfer would put an end to the financial problems the club was having, which led him to the decision to leave. He also stated that he was very sad to leave the club and even though he is leaving Vasco, his heart is staying.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>Introduced in a <a href="/wiki/Supermarket" title="Supermarket">supermarket</a> in <a href="/wiki/Belo_Horizonte" title="Belo Horizonte">Belo Horizonte</a>, home of his new <a href="/wiki/Cruzeiro_Esporte_Clube" title="Cruzeiro Esporte Clube">club</a>, Dedé appeared before journalists in an innovative publicity stunt.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> He later had his transfer blocked by <a href="/wiki/Federa%C3%A7%C3%A3o_de_Futebol_do_Estado_do_Rio_de_Janeiro" title="Federação de Futebol do Estado do Rio de Janeiro">FERJ</a>, main football institution from <a href="/wiki/Rio_de_Janeiro" title="Rio de Janeiro">Rio de Janeiro</a>, the state of his former <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Club_de_Regatas_Vasco_da_Gama" title="Club de Regatas Vasco da Gama">Vasco da Gama</a>. According to FERJ, the reason for the blockade was due to debts owed by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Club_de_Regatas_Vasco_da_Gama" title="Club de Regatas Vasco da Gama">cruz-maltinos</a> to the Brazilian Federal Justice. While this debt does not be paid, the defender must not keep playing for any club.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> Despite these problems, <a href="/wiki/Esporte_Clube_Cruzeiro" title="Esporte Clube Cruzeiro"><i>cruzeirenses</i></a>, are not worried, considering them a matter that must be resolved by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Clube_de_Regatas_Vasco_da_Gama" title="Clube de Regatas Vasco da Gama">Vasco</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 16 January 2015, it was reported that Dedé will have to undergo a surgery in his right knee, he is expected to return after 6 to 8 months.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ded%C3%A9_(footballer,_born_1988)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On 25 July 2011, after numerous great performances by <a href="/wiki/CR_Vasco_da_Gama" title="CR Vasco da Gama">Vasco da Gama</a>, Dedé was selected for the first time for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Brazilian_national_football_team" title="Brazilian national football team">Brazilian national football team</a>. This was for the friendly match against <a href="/wiki/Germany_national_football_team" title="Germany national football team">Germany</a>, on the first call by the coach <a href="/wiki/Mano_Menezes" title="Mano Menezes">Mano Menezes</a> after their elimination in the <a href="/wiki/2011_Copa_Am%C3%A9rica" title="2011 Copa América">2011 Copa América</a>. On 18 August, Dedé was selected again, this time for the friendly match against <a href="/wiki/Ghana_national_football_team" title="Ghana national football team">Ghana</a>. Although he did not play in any of the matches. In September 5, Dedé was selected for <a href="/wiki/Supercl%C3%A1sico_de_las_Am%C3%A9ricas" title="Superclásico de las Américas">Superclásico de las Américas</a>, where only players who played in <a href="/wiki/Football_in_Brazil" title="Football in Brazil">Brazilian football</a> were selected. On September 14, Dedé finally debuted in Superclásico de las Américas.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> On 15 October, in a 2-0 friendly win against Zambia played in China, Dedé scored the second goal, heading in a free-kick cross by <a href="/wiki/Neymar" title="Neymar">Neymar</a>.</p>
