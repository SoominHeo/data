<p><b>Dédougou</b> is a city located in western <a href="/wiki/Burkina_Faso" title="Burkina Faso">Burkina Faso</a>. It is the capital city of <a href="/wiki/Mouhoun_Province" title="Mouhoun Province">Mouhoun Province</a> and <a href="/wiki/Boucle_du_Mouhoun_Region" title="Boucle du Mouhoun Region">Boucle du Mouhoun Region</a>. The main ethnic groups are the <a href="/wiki/Marka_people" title="Marka people">Marka</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Bwa_people" title="Bwa people">Bwa</a>. The population of Dédougou was 37,793 in 2006; 18,778 were male and 19,015 were female.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-c2006_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-c2006-1">[1]</a></sup> It is the 9th largest city in Burkina Faso.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-c2006_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-c2006-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
