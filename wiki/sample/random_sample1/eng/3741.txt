<p><b>Devon Windsor</b> (born March 7, 1994 at <a href="/wiki/St._Louis" title="St. Louis">St. Louis</a>, <a href="/wiki/Missouri" title="Missouri">Missouri</a>) is an American model.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Devon_Windsor&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Devon Windsor grew up in St. Louis, Missouri, where she attended <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/MICDS" title="MICDS">Mary Institute and St. Louis Country Day School</a> from a young age. She was discovered when she was 14 years old by <a href="/wiki/Suzy_Gorman" title="Suzy Gorman">Suzy Gorman</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-marie_claire_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-marie_claire-1">[1]</a></sup> a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fashion_photographer" title="Fashion photographer">fashion photographer</a> who recommended her to a local agency, West Model &amp; Talent Management,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-stltoday_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-stltoday-2">[2]</a></sup> which made her sign a contract with them and Vision Model Management. This new agency sent her to <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a> in order to attend castings and shooting for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Roxy_(clothing)" title="Roxy (clothing)">Roxy</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-marie_claire_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-marie_claire-1">[1]</a></sup> There she was noticed by <a href="/wiki/IMG_Models" title="IMG Models">IMG Models</a> which decided to sign her to their new-yorker agency.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-marie_claire_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-marie_claire-1">[1]</a></sup> One of her first jobs after that was an <a href="/wiki/Editorial" title="Editorial">editorial</a> for the fashion magazine <i><a href="/wiki/Oyster_(magazine)" title="Oyster (magazine)">Oyster</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Following her graduation from high school, Devon moved to New York to model full-time.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Devon_Windsor&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Her first big fashion show was for <a href="/wiki/Prada" title="Prada">Prada</a> in September 2013. During <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fitting_model" title="Fitting model">fittings</a>, her hair was dyed bleach blonde.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> She liked it and decided to keep them that way, which has become her main feature. The same year, she started walking for the <a href="/wiki/Victoria%27s_Secret_Fashion_Show" title="Victoria's Secret Fashion Show">Victoria's Secret Fashion Show</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-stl_today_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-stl_today-6">[6]</a></sup> and advertised <a href="/wiki/Moschino" title="Moschino">Love Moschino</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-stl_today_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-stl_today-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2014, she walked the <a href="/wiki/Victoria%27s_Secret_Fashion_Show" title="Victoria's Secret Fashion Show">Victoria's Secret Fashion Show</a> again, wearing wings and advertised <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jean_Paul_Gaultier" title="Jean Paul Gaultier">Jean Paul Gaultier</a> and <a href="/wiki/Max_Mara" title="Max Mara">Max Mara</a>.</p>
<p>In 2015, she advertised <a href="/wiki/Balmain_(fashion_house)" title="Balmain (fashion house)">Balmain</a> alongside <a href="/wiki/Cindy_Bruna" title="Cindy Bruna">Cindy Bruna</a>.</p>
