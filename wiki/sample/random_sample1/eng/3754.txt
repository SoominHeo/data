<p><b>Lani Desmonet "Desi" Lydic</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Lani_D_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Lani_D-1">[1]</a></sup> (born June 30, 1981) is an American <a href="/wiki/Actor" title="Actor">actress</a> who is currently a correspondent on <i><a href="/wiki/The_Daily_Show" title="The Daily Show">The Daily Show</a></i> with <a href="/wiki/Trevor_Noah" title="Trevor Noah">Trevor Noah</a>. She also stars as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Guidance_counselor" title="Guidance counselor">guidance counselor</a> Valerie Marks on the <a href="/wiki/MTV" title="MTV">MTV</a> <a href="/wiki/Comedy-drama" title="Comedy-drama">comedy-drama</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Awkward_(TV_series)" title="Awkward (TV series)">Awkward</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Awkward._2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Awkward.-2">[2]</a></sup> She got her start in the 2001 <a href="/wiki/Parody_film" title="Parody film">parody film</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Not_Another_Teen_Movie" title="Not Another Teen Movie">Not Another Teen Movie</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Buddy_TV_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Buddy_TV-3">[3]</a></sup> She also starred in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Spike_(TV_channel)" title="Spike (TV channel)">Spike</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mini-series" title="Mini-series">mini-series</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Invasion_Iowa" title="Invasion Iowa">Invasion Iowa</a></i> alongside <a href="/wiki/William_Shatner" title="William Shatner">William Shatner</a>, and the parody series <i><a href="/wiki/The_Real_Wedding_Crashers" title="The Real Wedding Crashers">The Real Wedding Crashers</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Desi_Credits_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Desi_Credits-4">[4]</a></sup> She appeared as Shea Seger in the 2011 film <i><a href="/wiki/We_Bought_a_Zoo" title="We Bought a Zoo">We Bought a Zoo</a></i> alongside <a href="/wiki/Matt_Damon" title="Matt Damon">Matt Damon</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scarlett_Johansson" title="Scarlett Johansson">Scarlett Johansson</a>, and in the 2013 film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Babymakers" title="The Babymakers">The Babymakers</a></i> with <a href="/wiki/Olivia_Munn" title="Olivia Munn">Olivia Munn</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Buddy_TV_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-Buddy_TV-3">[3]</a></sup> Lydic also appeared as one half of a <a href="/wiki/Lesbian" title="Lesbian">lesbian</a> couple on an episode of the <a href="/wiki/Disney_Channel" title="Disney Channel">Disney Channel</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Good_Luck_Charlie" title="Good Luck Charlie">Good Luck Charlie</a></i> in 2014. She joined Trevor Noah's lineup of correspondents for <i>The Daily Show</i> on September 29, 2015.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Desi_Lydic&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Lydic was born in <a href="/wiki/Summit,_New_Jersey" title="Summit, New Jersey">Summit, New Jersey</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Summit_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Summit-5">[5]</a></sup> and grew up in <a href="/wiki/Louisville,_Kentucky" title="Louisville, Kentucky">Louisville, Kentucky</a>. As a child, she enrolled in the Young Actors Institute at the <a href="/wiki/Youth_Performing_Arts_School" title="Youth Performing Arts School">Youth Performing Arts School</a> and starred in a <a href="/wiki/Kroger" title="Kroger">Kroger</a> commercial. She later moved to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Los_Angeles,_California" title="Los Angeles, California">Los Angeles, California</a>, and became an <a href="/wiki/Improvisation" title="Improvisation">improvisational</a> actor, performing at <a href="/wiki/The_Groundlings" title="The Groundlings">The Groundlings</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Improv_Olympic" title="Improv Olympic">Improv Olympic</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Out_at_the_Wedding_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-Out_at_the_Wedding-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Desi_Lydic&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
