<p><b>Deon McCaulay</b> (born September 20, 1987) is a Belizean international <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays for <a href="/wiki/Verdes_FC" title="Verdes FC">Verdes FC</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Premier_League_of_Belize" title="Premier League of Belize">Premier League of Belize</a>. He previously played for <a href="/wiki/Atlanta_Silverbacks" title="Atlanta Silverbacks">Atlanta Silverbacks</a> <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Deon_McCaulay&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>McCaulay has played in Belize for <a href="/wiki/Kremandala" title="Kremandala">Kremandala</a>, <a href="/wiki/FC_Belize" title="FC Belize">FC Belize</a> and <a href="/wiki/Belize_Defence_Force_FC" title="Belize Defence Force FC">Belize Defence Force</a>; in Costa Rica he had a trial with <a href="/wiki/Puntarenas_F.C." title="Puntarenas F.C.">Puntarenas</a>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> he played in Honduras for <a href="/wiki/Deportes_Savio" title="Deportes Savio">Deportes Savio</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NFT_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-NFT-3">[3]</a></sup> He has also played for <a href="/wiki/Belmopan_Bandits" title="Belmopan Bandits">Belmopan Bandits</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Premier_League_of_Belize" title="Premier League of Belize">Premier League of Belize</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>In February 2013 he had a trial with the <a href="/wiki/Portland_Timbers" title="Portland Timbers">Portland Timbers</a> of <a href="/wiki/Major_League_Soccer" title="Major League Soccer">Major League Soccer</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Dike_injury_leaves_Porter.2C_Timbers_looking_at_new_trialist_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Dike_injury_leaves_Porter.2C_Timbers_looking_at_new_trialist-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>In March 2014 he joined the <a href="/wiki/Atlanta_Silverbacks" title="Atlanta Silverbacks">Atlanta Silverbacks</a> of <a href="/wiki/North_American_Soccer_League" title="North American Soccer League">North American Soccer League</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Deon_McCaulay&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>McCaulay made his international debut for <a href="/wiki/Belize_national_football_team" title="Belize national football team">Belize</a> in 2007.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NFT_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-NFT-3">[3]</a></sup> He was a member of Belize's FIFA World Cup qualifying campaign for both the 2010 and 2014 editions,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FIFA_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-FIFA-7">[7]</a></sup> and he scored the first goal and first hat-trick of the <a href="/wiki/2014_FIFA_World_Cup_qualification" title="2014 FIFA World Cup qualification">2014 FIFA World Cup qualifying campaign</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> He is Belize's all-time top scorer,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> with 13 goals in FIFA World Cup qualifying,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FIFA_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-FIFA-7">[7]</a></sup> as well as two goals at the <a href="/wiki/2007_UNCAF_Nations_Cup" title="2007 UNCAF Nations Cup">2007 UNCAF Nations Cup</a> and one goal at the <a href="/wiki/2013_Copa_Centroamericana" title="2013 Copa Centroamericana">2013 Copa Centroamericana</a>. He was also the world-wide top goalscorer in 2014 FIFA World Cup qualification along with <a href="/wiki/Luis_Su%C3%A1rez" title="Luis Suárez">Luis Suárez</a> and <a href="/wiki/Robin_van_Persie" title="Robin van Persie">Robin van Persie</a> with 11 goals. He scored a hat-trick of those 11 in a 5–2 rout of <a href="/wiki/Montserrat_national_football_team" title="Montserrat national football team">Montserrat</a> in June 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-UNCAF_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-UNCAF-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
