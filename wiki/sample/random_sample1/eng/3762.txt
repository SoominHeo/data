<p>The <b>Deir el-Balah Governorate</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar" xml:lang="ar">محافظة دير البلح</span>‎‎ <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="DIN 31635 Arabic">Muḥāfaẓat Dayr al Balaḥ</span></i>) is one of 16 <a href="/wiki/Governorates_of_Palestine" title="Governorates of Palestine">Governorates of Palestine</a> in the central <a href="/wiki/Gaza_Strip" title="Gaza Strip">Gaza Strip</a> which is administered by <a href="/wiki/State_of_Palestine" title="State of Palestine">Palestine</a> aside from its border with Israel, airspace and maritime territory. Its total land area consists of 56 sq. kilometers. According to the <a href="/wiki/Palestinian_Central_Bureau_of_Statistics" title="Palestinian Central Bureau of Statistics">Palestinian Central Bureau of Statistics</a>, in mid-year 2006 it had a population of 208,716 inhabitants distributed between eight localities.</p>
