<p><b>David Ramsey</b> (born November 17, 1971) is an <a href="/wiki/Americans" title="Americans">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Actor" title="Actor">actor</a>, best known for his roles in the <a href="/wiki/Showtime_(TV_network)" title="Showtime (TV network)">Showtime</a> TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Dexter_(TV_series)" title="Dexter (TV series)">Dexter</a></i> as <a href="/wiki/List_of_Dexter_characters#Anton_Briggs" title="List of Dexter characters">Anton Briggs</a>, the film <i><a href="/wiki/Mother_and_Child_(2009_film)" title="Mother and Child (2009 film)">Mother and Child</a></i> (2009) as Joseph, and the CW show <i><a href="/wiki/Arrow_(TV_series)" title="Arrow (TV series)">Arrow</a></i> as John Diggle.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=David_Ramsey&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ramsey was born in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Detroit,_Michigan" title="Detroit, Michigan">Detroit, Michigan</a>. He graduated from <a href="/wiki/Wayne_State_University" title="Wayne State University">Wayne State University</a>. From 1997 to 1998, he starred in the <a href="/wiki/UPN" title="UPN">UPN</a> sitcom <i><a href="/wiki/Good_News_(TV_series)" title="Good News (TV series)">Good News</a></i>, as pastor David Randolph. In 2000, he starred as <a href="/wiki/Muhammad_Ali" title="Muhammad Ali">Muhammad Ali</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Fox_Broadcasting_Company" title="Fox Broadcasting Company">Fox</a> television movie <i><a href="/wiki/Ali:_An_American_Hero" title="Ali: An American Hero">Ali: An American Hero</a></i>. That year, he appeared in <i><a href="/wiki/Pay_It_Forward_(film)" title="Pay It Forward (film)">Pay It Forward</a></i> and started a recurring role in <i><a href="/wiki/For_Your_Love_(TV_series)" title="For Your Love (TV series)">For Your Love</a></i>. In 2001, he starred as Vince Lee in the South African comedy film "Mr Bones". He has also starred in recurring roles in <i><a href="/wiki/All_of_Us" title="All of Us">All of Us</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_West_Wing" title="The West Wing">The West Wing</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/CSI:_Crime_Scene_Investigation" title="CSI: Crime Scene Investigation">CSI: Crime Scene Investigation</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Ghost_Whisperer" title="Ghost Whisperer">Ghost Whisperer</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Wildfire_(TV_series)" title="Wildfire (TV series)">Wildfire</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Hollywood_Residential" title="Hollywood Residential">Hollywood Residential</a></i>. In 2008 to 2009, he appeared in 17 episodes of <i><a href="/wiki/Dexter_(TV_series)" title="Dexter (TV series)">Dexter</a></i> as Anton Briggs, a pot-smoking <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Confidential_informant" title="Confidential informant">confidential informant</a> who has an affair with <a href="/wiki/Debra_Morgan" title="Debra Morgan">Debra Morgan</a> in Season 3 and at the start of Season 4. In 2010, he appeared in an episode of <i><a href="/wiki/Grey%27s_Anatomy" title="Grey's Anatomy">Grey's Anatomy</a></i> and in the short-lived NBC courtroom drama series <i><a href="/wiki/Outlaw_(TV_series)" title="Outlaw (TV series)">Outlaw</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> In 2012, he has been pulling double duty, appearing in the main cast of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_CW_Television_Network" title="The CW Television Network">CW's</a> superhero show <i><a href="/wiki/Arrow_(TV_series)" title="Arrow (TV series)">Arrow</a></i>, as John Diggle, a military veteran who is Oliver's partner, confidant, and bodyguard, while also starring in the TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Blue_Bloods_(TV_series)" title="Blue Bloods (TV series)">Blue Bloods</a></i> as Mayor Carter Poole.</p>
<p>Ramsey is an accomplished martial artist, with a <a href="/wiki/Black_belt_(martial_arts)" title="Black belt (martial arts)">black belt</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jeet_kune_do" title="Jeet kune do">jeet kune do</a>. He has also studied <a href="/wiki/Boxing" title="Boxing">boxing</a> and <a href="/wiki/Taekwondo" title="Taekwondo">tae kwon do</a>, and trained in <a href="/wiki/Kickboxing" title="Kickboxing">kickboxing</a> under <a href="/wiki/Benny_Urquidez" title="Benny Urquidez">Benny "Jet" Urquidez</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
