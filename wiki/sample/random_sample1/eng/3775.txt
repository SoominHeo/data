<p><b>David Barron</b> is a British film producer, best known for his involvement in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)"><i>Harry Potter</i> film series</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=David_Barron_(film_producer)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>David Barron worked as a producer on <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows_%E2%80%93_Part_1" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 1">Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 1</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Deathly_Hallows_%E2%80%93_Part_2" title="Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 2">Part 2</a></i>. He previously served as a producer on <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Half-Blood_Prince_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (film)">Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Order_of_the_Phoenix_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)">Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix</a></i>. He was also an executive producer on both <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Chamber_of_Secrets_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (film)">Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_and_the_Goblet_of_Fire_(film)" title="Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (film)">Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire</a>.</i> Barron has worked in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Entertainment_industry" title="Entertainment industry">entertainment industry</a> for more than 25 years, beginning his career in commercials before moving into television and film production. In addition to his work as a producer, he has held a wide range of posts, including <a href="/wiki/Location_manager" title="Location manager">location manager</a>, <a href="/wiki/Assistant_director" title="Assistant director">assistant director</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Production_manager" title="Production manager">production manager</a> and production supervisor, working on such films as <i><a href="/wiki/The_French_Lieutenant%27s_Woman_(film)" title="The French Lieutenant's Woman (film)">The French Lieutenant's Woman</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Killing_Fields_(film)" title="The Killing Fields (film)">The Killing Fields</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Revolution_(1985_film)" title="Revolution (1985 film)">Revolution</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Legend_(1985_film)" title="Legend (1985 film)">Legend</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Princess_Bride_(film)" title="The Princess Bride (film)">The Princess Bride</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/Franco_Zeffirelli" title="Franco Zeffirelli">Franco Zeffirelli</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Hamlet_(1990_film)" title="Hamlet (1990 film)">Hamlet</a></i>.</p>
<p>In 1991, Barron was appointed executive in charge of production on <a href="/wiki/George_Lucas" title="George Lucas">George Lucas</a>' ambitious television project <i><a href="/wiki/The_Young_Indiana_Jones_Chronicles" title="The Young Indiana Jones Chronicles">The Young Indiana Jones Chronicles</a></i>. The following year, he served as the line producer on the feature <i><a href="/wiki/The_Muppet_Christmas_Carol" title="The Muppet Christmas Carol">The Muppet Christmas Carol</a></i>. In 1993, Barron joined <a href="/wiki/Kenneth_Branagh" title="Kenneth Branagh">Kenneth Branagh</a>'s production team as associate producer and unit production manager on <i><a href="/wiki/Mary_Shelley%27s_Frankenstein_(film)" title="Mary Shelley's Frankenstein (film)">Mary Shelley's Frankenstein</a>.</i> That film began an association with Branagh, with Barron going on to produce the director's films <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/A_Midwinter%27s_Tale" title="A Midwinter's Tale">A Midwinter's Tale</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Hamlet_(1996_film)" title="Hamlet (1996 film)">Hamlet</a></i> and <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Love%27s_Labour%27s_Lost_(2000_film)" title="Love's Labour's Lost (2000 film)">Love's Labour's Lost</a></i>. Barron also produced <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Parker" title="Oliver Parker">Oliver Parker</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Othello_(1995_film)" title="Othello (1995 film)">Othello</a></i>, in which Branagh starred with <a href="/wiki/Laurence_Fishburne" title="Laurence Fishburne">Laurence Fishburne</a>. In spring 1999, he formed his own company, Contagious Films, with British director <a href="/wiki/Paul_Weiland" title="Paul Weiland">Paul Weiland</a>. Barron more recently launched Runaway Fridge Films and Beagle Pug Films.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
