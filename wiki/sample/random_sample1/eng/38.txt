<p><b>October 9</b> is the 282nd day of the year (283rd in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 83 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Tuesday, Friday or Sunday (58 in 400 years each) than on Wednesday or Thursday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Monday or Saturday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=October_9&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/768" title="768">768</a> – <a href="/wiki/Carloman_I" title="Carloman I">Carloman I</a> and <a href="/wiki/Charlemagne" title="Charlemagne">Charlemagne</a> are crowned kings of the <a href="/wiki/Francia" title="Francia">Franks</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1238" title="1238">1238</a> – <a href="/wiki/James_I_of_Aragon" title="James I of Aragon">James I of Aragon</a> conquers <a href="/wiki/Valencia" title="Valencia">Valencia</a> and founds the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Valencia" title="Kingdom of Valencia">Kingdom of Valencia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1264" title="1264">1264</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Castile" title="Kingdom of Castile">Kingdom of Castile</a> conquers the city of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jerez" title="Jerez">Jerez</a>, which had been under Muslim occupation since <a href="/wiki/711" title="711">711</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1410" title="1410">1410</a> – The first known mention of the <a href="/wiki/Prague_astronomical_clock" title="Prague astronomical clock">Prague astronomical clock</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1446" title="1446">1446</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">hangul</a> alphabet is published in Korea.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1514" title="1514">1514</a> – Marriage of <a href="/wiki/Louis_XII_of_France" title="Louis XII of France">Louis XII of France</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mary_Tudor,_Queen_of_France" title="Mary Tudor, Queen of France">Mary Tudor</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1582" title="1582">1582</a> – Because of the implementation of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>, this day does not exist in this year in Italy, Poland, Portugal and Spain.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1594" title="1594">1594</a> – The army of the <a href="/wiki/Portuguese_Empire" title="Portuguese Empire">Portuguese Empire</a> is annihilated by the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Kandy" title="Kingdom of Kandy">Kingdom of Kandy</a> on <a href="/wiki/Sri_Lanka" title="Sri Lanka">Sri Lanka</a>, bringing an end to the <a href="/wiki/Campaign_of_Danture" title="Campaign of Danture">Campaign of Danture</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1604" title="1604">1604</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Supernova_1604" title="Supernova 1604">Supernova 1604</a>, the most recent supernova to be observed in the <a href="/wiki/Milky_Way" title="Milky Way">Milky Way</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1635" title="1635">1635</a> – Founder of <a href="/wiki/Rhode_Island" title="Rhode Island">Rhode Island</a> <a href="/wiki/Roger_Williams" title="Roger Williams">Roger Williams</a> is banished from the <a href="/wiki/Massachusetts_Bay_Colony" title="Massachusetts Bay Colony">Massachusetts Bay Colony</a> as a religious dissident after he speaks out against punishments for religious offenses and giving away <a href="/wiki/Native_Americans_in_the_United_States" title="Native Americans in the United States">Native American</a> land.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1701" title="1701">1701</a> – The Collegiate School of Connecticut (later renamed <a href="/wiki/Yale_University" title="Yale University">Yale University</a>) is chartered in <a href="/wiki/Old_Saybrook,_Connecticut" title="Old Saybrook, Connecticut">Old Saybrook, Connecticut</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1708" title="1708">1708</a> – <a href="/wiki/Peter_the_Great" title="Peter the Great">Peter the Great</a> defeats the Swedes at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Lesnaya" title="Battle of Lesnaya">Battle of Lesnaya</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1740" title="1740">1740</a> – <a href="/wiki/Dutch_East_Indies" title="Dutch East Indies">Dutch colonists</a> and various slave groups begin <a href="/wiki/1740_Batavia_massacre" title="1740 Batavia massacre">massacring</a> ethnic <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Indonesians" title="Chinese Indonesians">Chinese</a> in <a href="/wiki/Jakarta" title="Jakarta">Batavia</a>, eventually killing 10,000 and leading to a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Java_War_(1741%E2%80%9343)" title="Java War (1741–43)">two-year-long war</a> throughout <a href="/wiki/Java" title="Java">Java</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1760" title="1760">1760</a> – <a href="/wiki/Seven_Years%27_War" title="Seven Years' War">Seven Years' War</a>: Russian forces occupy Berlin.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1790" title="1790">1790</a> – An <a href="/wiki/List_of_earthquakes_in_Algeria" title="List of earthquakes in Algeria">earthquake</a> shakes northern <a href="/wiki/Algeria" title="Algeria">Algeria</a> with a maximum <a href="/wiki/Mercalli_intensity_scale" title="Mercalli intensity scale">Mercalli intensity</a> of X (<i>Extreme</i>) causing severe damage and a tsunami in the Mediterranean Sea. Three-thousand people were killed.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1799" title="1799">1799</a> – Sinking of <a href="/wiki/HMS_Lutine_(1779)" title="HMS Lutine (1779)">HMS <i>Lutine</i></a> with the loss of 240 men and a cargo worth £1,200,000.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1804" title="1804">1804</a> – <a href="/wiki/Hobart" title="Hobart">Hobart</a>, capital of <a href="/wiki/Tasmania" title="Tasmania">Tasmania</a>, is founded.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1806" title="1806">1806</a> – <a href="/wiki/Prussia" title="Prussia">Prussia</a> begins the <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Fourth_Coalition" title="War of the Fourth Coalition">War of the Fourth Coalition</a> against France.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1812" title="1812">1812</a> – <a href="/wiki/War_of_1812" title="War of 1812">War of 1812</a>: In a naval engagement on <a href="/wiki/Lake_Erie" title="Lake Erie">Lake Erie</a>, American forces capture two British ships: <a href="/wiki/HMS_Detroit_(1812)" title="HMS Detroit (1812)">HMS <i>Detroit</i></a> and <a href="/wiki/HMS_Caledonia_(1807)" title="HMS Caledonia (1807)">HMS <i>Caledonia</i></a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1820" title="1820">1820</a> – <a href="/wiki/Guayaquil" title="Guayaquil">Guayaquil</a> declares independence from Spain.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1824" title="1824">1824</a> – <a href="/wiki/Slavery" title="Slavery">Slavery</a> is abolished in <a href="/wiki/Costa_Rica" title="Costa Rica">Costa Rica</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1831" title="1831">1831</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ioannis_Kapodistrias" title="Ioannis Kapodistrias">Ioannis Kapodistrias</a>, the first head of state of independent Greece is assassinated.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1834" title="1834">1834</a> – Opening of the <a href="/wiki/Dublin_and_Kingstown_Railway" title="Dublin and Kingstown Railway">Dublin and Kingstown Railway</a>, the first public railway on the island of Ireland.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1845" title="1845">1845</a> – <a href="/wiki/John_Henry_Newman" title="John Henry Newman">John Henry Newman</a> is received into the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Church" title="Roman Catholic Church">Roman Catholic Church</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1847" title="1847">1847</a> – Slavery is abolished in <a href="/wiki/Saint_Barth%C3%A9lemy" title="Saint Barthélemy">Saint Barthélemy</a> and all remaining slaves are freed.</li>
