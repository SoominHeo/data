<p><b><a href="/wiki/December" title="December">December</a></b> <b><a href="/wiki/2007" title="2007">2007</a></b> was the twelfth and final month of that common year. The month, which began on a <a href="/wiki/Saturday" title="Saturday">Saturday</a>, ended on a <a href="/wiki/Monday" title="Monday">Monday</a> after 31 days.</p>
