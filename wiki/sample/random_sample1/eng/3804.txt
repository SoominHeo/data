<p><b>Temmabashi Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">天満橋駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Tenmabashi-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a railway station in <a href="/wiki/Ch%C5%AB%C5%8D-ku,_Osaka" title="Chūō-ku, Osaka">Chūō-ku, Osaka</a>, Japan, operated by the <a href="/wiki/Private_railway" title="Private railway">private railway</a> operator <a href="/wiki/Keihan_Electric_Railway" title="Keihan Electric Railway">Keihan Electric Railway</a> and <a href="/wiki/Osaka_Municipal_Subway" title="Osaka Municipal Subway">Osaka Municipal Subway</a>.</p>

<p>There are two ticket gates in the east and west. The Osaka Municipal Subway Tanimachi Line is close to the east gate. The station consists of two <a href="/wiki/Side_platform" title="Side platform">side platforms</a> and one <a href="/wiki/Island_platform" title="Island platform">island platform</a> serving four tracks. The two tracks in the north are used for trains to and from Nakanoshima, and the 2 in the south for trains to and from Yodoyabashi.</p>
