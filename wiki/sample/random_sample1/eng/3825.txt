<p><b>Tōgō</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">東郷町</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Tōgō-chō</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/List_of_towns_in_Japan" title="List of towns in Japan">town</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Aichi_District,_Aichi" title="Aichi District, Aichi">Aichi District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Aichi_Prefecture" title="Aichi Prefecture">Aichi Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</p>
<p>As of May 2015, the town had an estimated <a href="/wiki/Population" title="Population">population</a> of 42,695 and a <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> of 2370 persons per km². The total area was 18.03 square kilometres (6.96 sq mi).</p>
