<p><b>Doggy Style Records</b> (formerly known as <b>Dogghouse Records</b>) is an American <a href="/wiki/Record_label" title="Record label">record label</a> founded by <a href="/wiki/Rapping" title="Rapping">rapper</a> <a href="/wiki/Snoop_Dogg" title="Snoop Dogg">Snoop Dogg</a> in 1995. It is named after Snoop's debut album, <i><a href="/wiki/Doggystyle" title="Doggystyle">Doggystyle</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Doggy_Style_Records&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<table class="plainlinks metadata ambox mbox-small-left ambox-content" role="presentation">
<tr>
<td class="mbox-image"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg"><img alt="[icon]" data-file-height="31" data-file-width="44" height="14" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg/20px-Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg/30px-Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg/40px-Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg.png 2x" width="20"/></a></td>

<p>On July 6, 1995, Doggy Style Records, Inc. was registered with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/California_Secretary_of_State" title="California Secretary of State">California Secretary of State</a> as business entity number C1923139.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> After Snoop Dogg was acquitted of murder charges on February 20, 1996, he and the mother of his son and their kennel of 20 pit bulls moved into a 5,000-square-foot (460 m<sup>2</sup>) home in the hills of <a href="/wiki/Claremont,_California" title="Claremont, California">Claremont, California</a> and by August 1996 Doggy Style Records, a subsidiary of <a href="/wiki/Death_Row_Records" title="Death Row Records">Death Row Records</a>, signed <a href="/wiki/The_Gap_Band" title="The Gap Band">The Gap Band</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Charlie_Wilson_(singer)" title="Charlie Wilson (singer)">Charlie Wilson</a> as one of the record label's first artists.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The record label was founded by Snoop Dogg at the end of his self-described "servitude" at Master P's <a href="/wiki/No_Limit_Records" title="No Limit Records">No Limit Records</a>. The idea of an independent record label came up when Snoop left <a href="/wiki/Death_Row_Records" title="Death Row Records">Death Row</a> earlier than finishing his second album <a href="/wiki/Tha_Doggfather" title="Tha Doggfather">Tha Doggfather</a>. Although completing it by himself and producing the tracks alone, the vision of his own label remained unrealised. Much advertising was done by Snoop in interviews, as well as wearing "Dogghouse-branded" leather jackets and jewelry made in the shape of the Doggy Style Records logo. Finally it was independently founded in late 1999/early 2000. It started as a place to house many artists that had ties with Snoop in the past but were not currently signed to a label, as well as some newcomers. However, Snoop only used the label for his own releases, and as a result many artists left. For a time, though, several former <a href="/wiki/Death_Row_Records" title="Death Row Records">Death Row</a> artists who collaborated with Snoop in the early '90s were signed to the label. Its main purpose is still to endorse all copyrights for the recorded song from then on.</p>
<p>Originally it was distributed through <a href="/wiki/Universal_Music_Group" title="Universal Music Group">Universal Music Group</a>, the main shareholder of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vivendi_Universal" title="Vivendi Universal">Vivendi Universal</a> SA that embraced <a href="/wiki/MCA_Inc." title="MCA Inc.">MCA Inc.</a> record label the one to release Doggy Style Records as its sub-agency with Snoop being the executive in August 2001. After this in October 2005 Snoop has formed a partnership with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Koch_Records" title="Koch Records">Koch Records</a> in preparation for the release of <i>Snoop Dogg Presents <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Welcome_to_tha_Chuuch_-_Da_Album" title="Welcome to tha Chuuch - Da Album">Welcome to tha Chuuch - Da Album</a></i>, as well as other albums.</p>
