<p><b>Camel toe,</b> or <b>cameltoe,</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Slang" title="Slang">slang</a> term that refers to the outline of a woman's <a href="/wiki/Labia_majora" title="Labia majora">labia majora</a> in tightly fitting clothes.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Owing to a combination of anatomical factors and the tightness of the fabric covering the area, the crotch and <a href="/wiki/Mons_pubis" title="Mons pubis">mons pubis</a> may display a shape resembling the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Metatarsus" title="Metatarsus">forefoot</a> of a <a href="/wiki/Camel" title="Camel">camel</a>. Camel toe commonly occurs as a result of wearing tight-fitting clothes, such as <a href="/wiki/Shorts" title="Shorts">shorts</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shorts#Styles" title="Shorts">hotpants</a> or swimwear.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nyt_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-nyt-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
