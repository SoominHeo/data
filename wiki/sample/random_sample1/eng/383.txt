<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Supreme_Court_of_Russia" title="Supreme Court of Russia">supreme court</a> declares the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Romanov" title="House of Romanov">imperial dynasty</a> victims of political repression, marking the official rehabilitation of the house of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Romanov" title="Romanov">Romanov</a>. The decision overturns a lower court ruling that classified the killings as plain murder, and exonerates Emperor <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_II_of_Russia" title="Nicholas II of Russia">Nicholas II</a> and his family of the alleged crimes the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bolshevik" title="Bolshevik">Bolshevik</a> regime used to justify their killing. <a class="external text" href="http://africa.reuters.com/world/news/usnTRE4901WV.html" rel="nofollow">(Reuters)</a><sup class="noprint Inline-Template"><span style="white-space: nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Link_rot" title="Wikipedia:Link rot"><span title=" Dead link since June 2015">dead link</span></a></i>]</span></sup></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> <a href="/wiki/United_States_Senate" title="United States Senate">Senate</a> passes the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Indo-US_civilian_nuclear_agreement" title="Indo-US civilian nuclear agreement">civilian nuclear agreement</a> with <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a> by a vote of 86–13. India has not signed the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nuclear_Non-Proliferation_Treaty" title="Nuclear Non-Proliferation Treaty">Non-Proliferation Treaty</a>, but may now undertake nuclear trade to the States <a class="external text" href="http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/" rel="nofollow">(TOI)</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/National_Transportation_Safety_Board" title="National Transportation Safety Board">National Transportation Safety Board</a> reports that a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Metrolink_(Southern_California)" title="Metrolink (Southern California)">Metrolink</a> engineer sent a <a href="/wiki/Text_messaging" title="Text messaging">text message</a> 22 seconds before the <a href="/wiki/2008_Chatsworth_train_collision" title="2008 Chatsworth train collision">Chatsworth train collision</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Los_Angeles,_California" title="Los Angeles, California">Los Angeles</a>, <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>, that killed 25 people. <a class="external text" href="http://cbs2.com/local/Robert.Sanchez.Text.2.830304.html" rel="nofollow">(CBS)</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/List_of_mayors_and_lord_mayors_of_Melbourne" title="List of mayors and lord mayors of Melbourne">Mayor</a> of <a href="/wiki/Melbourne" title="Melbourne">Melbourne</a> <a href="/wiki/John_So" title="John So">John So</a> announces his resignation. <a class="external text" href="http://www.news.com.au/heraldsun/story/0,21985,24433359-661,00.html" rel="nofollow">(news.com.au)</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/United_States_Army" title="United States Army">United States Army</a> <a href="/wiki/General_officer" title="General officer">General</a> <a href="/wiki/David_D._McKiernan" title="David D. McKiernan">David D. McKiernan</a>, the commander of <a href="/wiki/NATO" title="NATO">NATO</a> forces in <a href="/wiki/Afghanistan" title="Afghanistan">Afghanistan</a>, says that a greater military presence is "needed as quickly as possible." <a class="external text" href="http://edition.cnn.com/2008/WORLD/asiapcf/10/01/afghan.troops/index.html?section=cnn_latest" rel="nofollow">(CNN)</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Subprime_mortgage_crisis" title="Subprime mortgage crisis">Subprime mortgage crisis</a>:
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/United_States_House_of_Representatives" title="United States House of Representatives">U.S. Representative</a> <a href="/wiki/Carol_Shea-Porter" title="Carol Shea-Porter">Carol Shea-Porter</a> says that "more than 400 economists, including <a href="/wiki/Nobel_Memorial_Prize_in_Economic_Sciences" title="Nobel Memorial Prize in Economic Sciences">Nobel laureates</a>, appealed to <a href="/wiki/United_States_Congress" title="United States Congress">Congress</a> to slow down and make sure [they] got [the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Proposed_bailout_of_United_States_financial_system" title="Proposed bailout of United States financial system">bailout bill</a>] right". <a class="external text" href="http://www.nhpr.org/node/17839" rel="nofollow">(NHPR)</a> <a class="external text" href="http://www.nashuatelegraph.com/apps/pbcs.dll/article?AID=/20080930/NEWS01/309309923/-1/news" rel="nofollow">(Nashua Telegraph)</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Swedish</a> <a href="/wiki/Minister_for_Finance_(Sweden)" title="Minister for Finance (Sweden)">Minister for Finance</a> <a href="/wiki/Anders_Borg" title="Anders Borg">Anders Borg</a> slams the culture of "greed" exemplified by <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">U.S.</a> financial institutions and its role in precipitating the current financial crisis. <a class="external text" href="http://www.thelocal.se/14656/20080930/" rel="nofollow">(The Local)</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/United_States_Senate" title="United States Senate">U.S. Senate</a> approves <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/HR1424" title="HR1424">HR1424</a>, a revised version of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Proposed_bailout_of_United_States_financial_system" title="Proposed bailout of United States financial system">proposed bailout</a> of the nation's <a href="/wiki/Finance" title="Finance">financial system</a>. <a class="external text" href="http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/americas/7647622.stm" rel="nofollow">(BBC)</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>A new <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Military_of_the_United_States" title="Military of the United States">U.S. Armed Forces</a> <a href="/wiki/Unified_Combatant_Command" title="Unified Combatant Command">Unified Combatant Command</a> for <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">Africa</a>—<a href="/wiki/United_States_Africa_Command" title="United States Africa Command">AFRICOM</a>—is created. Main functions of AFRICOM include fighting terror, securing <a href="/wiki/Petroleum" title="Petroleum">oil</a> supplies in Africa, and supporting <a href="/wiki/Foreign_policy_of_the_United_States" title="Foreign policy of the United States">U.S. foreign policy</a> in the region where <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/People%27s_Republic_of_China" title="People's Republic of China">Chinese</a> influence is growing. <a class="external text" href="http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/africa/7644994.stm" rel="nofollow">(BBC)</a> <a class="external text" href="http://www.thenation.com/doc/20071119/glover_lee" rel="nofollow">(The Nation)</a></li>
<li>A <a href="/wiki/2008_Agartala_bombings" title="2008 Agartala bombings">series of 4 blasts</a> set off in <a href="/wiki/Agartala" title="Agartala">Agartala</a>, capital of the <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">Indian</a> state of <a href="/wiki/Tripura" title="Tripura">Tripura</a>, killing at least 4 people and injuring 100. <a class="external text" href="http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/4_dead_76_hurt_as_serial_blasts_rip_Tripura/articleshow/3550593.cms" rel="nofollow">Times of India</a></li>
</ul>
