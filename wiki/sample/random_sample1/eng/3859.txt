<p><b>Dom Dom</b> is a Japanese <a href="/wiki/Fast_food_restaurant" title="Fast food restaurant">fast food restaurant</a> chain operated by Orange Food Court, Inc. Dom Dom was the first hamburger chain to open in Japan, with the first restaurant opening in February 1970.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
