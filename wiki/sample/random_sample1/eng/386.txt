<p>The <b>2008 <a href="/wiki/AFC_Challenge_Cup" title="AFC Challenge Cup">AFC Challenge Cup</a></b> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> tournament was hosted by India and played from 30 July – 13 August 2008. <a href="/wiki/India_national_football_team" title="India national football team">India</a> won the final against <a href="/wiki/Tajikistan_national_football_team" title="Tajikistan national football team">Tajikistan</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> India was also the winner of the fair play award and the India <a href="/wiki/Captain_(association_football)" title="Captain (association football)">captain</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Baichung_Bhutia" title="Baichung Bhutia">Baichung Bhutia</a> won the most valuable player award.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Chinese_Taipei" title="Chinese Taipei">Chinese Taipei</a> was originally designated the host, but could not guarantee it would meet the <a href="/wiki/Asian_Football_Confederation" title="Asian Football Confederation">AFC</a>'s standards for hosting the tournament.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Thailand was first chosen as host but withdrew due to lack of time to prepare.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The winners of the 2008 and 2010 competitions will qualify directly for the <a href="/wiki/2011_AFC_Asian_Cup" title="2011 AFC Asian Cup">2011 Asian Cup</a>. This competition is exclusive to members of the <i>emerging nations</i> class of the AFC. However, in the previous edition, <a href="/wiki/Bangladesh_national_football_team" title="Bangladesh national football team">Bangladesh</a> and <a href="/wiki/India_national_football_team" title="India national football team">India</a> were invited from the <i>developing nations</i> class - Bangladesh actually hosting the tournament, and India sending their <a href="/wiki/India_national_under-20_football_team" title="India national under-20 football team">under-20 team</a>. India and Bangladesh have again been invited for the 2008 edition of the tournament, along with <a href="/wiki/North_Korea_national_football_team" title="North Korea national football team">North Korea</a>, <a href="/wiki/Myanmar_national_football_team" title="Myanmar national football team">Myanmar</a> and <a href="/wiki/Turkmenistan_national_football_team" title="Turkmenistan national football team">Turkmenistan</a> who are also a part of the <i>developing nations</i> class.</p>
<p>Laos withdrew from the competition on 2 May 2008.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Palestine withdrew from the competition on 14 May 2008.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>Due to the poor conditions of the pitch at <a href="/wiki/Lal_Bahadur_Shastri_Stadium" title="Lal Bahadur Shastri Stadium">Lal Bahadur Shastri Stadium</a>, the AFC moved the majority of the matches to another venue. It was decided that ten matches would be played at the <a href="/wiki/Gachibowli_Athletic_Stadium" title="Gachibowli Athletic Stadium">Gachibowli Athletic Stadium</a>, and two at the LBS Stadium.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> Due to incessant rains in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hyderabad,_India" title="Hyderabad, India">Hyderabad</a> in the days leading up to the final, the <a href="/wiki/Asian_Football_Confederation" title="Asian Football Confederation">AFC</a> changed the venue of the final and the third place play-off to the <a href="/wiki/Ambedkar_Stadium" title="Ambedkar Stadium">Ambedkar Stadium</a> in <a href="/wiki/New_Delhi" title="New Delhi">New Delhi</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Tournament">Tournament</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_AFC_Challenge_Cup&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Tournament">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Qualification saw the top team from each of the four qualification groups qualify for the tournament proper, bringing the total number of participating teams in the centralized finals to eight. In addition, <a href="/wiki/India_national_football_team" title="India national football team">India</a> (tournament hosts), <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Korea_DPR_national_football_team" title="Korea DPR national football team">Korea DPR</a>, <a href="/wiki/Turkmenistan_national_football_team" title="Turkmenistan national football team">Turkmenistan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Myanmar_national_football_team" title="Myanmar national football team">Myanmar</a> qualified automatically.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Mongolia_national_football_team" title="Mongolia national football team">Mongolia</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Timor_Leste_national_football_team" title="Timor Leste national football team">Timor-Leste</a> decided not to take part.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Tie-breaking_criteria">Tie-breaking criteria</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_AFC_Challenge_Cup&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Tie-breaking criteria">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Where two or more teams end the group stage with the same number of points, their ranking is determined by the following criteria:</p>
<ol>
<li>points earned in the matches between the teams concerned;</li>
<li>goal difference in the matches between the teams concerned;</li>
<li>number of goals scored in the group matches between the teams concerned;</li>
<li>goal difference in all group matches;</li>
<li>number of goals scored in all group matches;</li>
<li>kicks from the penalty mark (if only two teams are level and they are both on the field of play);</li>
<li>fewer yellow and red cards received in the group matches;</li>
<li>drawing of lots by the organising committee.</li>
</ol>
