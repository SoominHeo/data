<p>The <b>Dominican Republic national basketball team</b> is the men's basketball team that represents the <a href="/wiki/Dominican_Republic" title="Dominican Republic">Dominican Republic</a> in international competition. In 2011 and 2012, <a href="/wiki/John_Calipari" title="John Calipari">John Calipari</a>, the head coach of the <a href="/wiki/Kentucky_Wildcats_men%27s_basketball" title="Kentucky Wildcats men's basketball">University of Kentucky men's basketball</a> team, served as the head coach of the team.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The current coach is <a href="/wiki/Orlando_Antigua" title="Orlando Antigua">Orlando Antigua</a>, a former Calipari assistant and the current head coach of the <a href="/wiki/South_Florida_Bulls_men%27s_basketball" title="South Florida Bulls men's basketball">University of South Florida</a>. The team placed third in the <a href="/wiki/2011_FIBA_Americas_Championship" title="2011 FIBA Americas Championship">2011 FIBA Americas Championship</a> and fourth in the <a href="/wiki/2012_FIBA_World_Olympic_Qualifying_Tournament_for_Men" title="2012 FIBA World Olympic Qualifying Tournament for Men">2012 FIBA World Olympic Qualifying Tournament</a>, one position shy of qualifying for the 2012 Olympics.</p>
