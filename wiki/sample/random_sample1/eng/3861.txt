<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">List of Roman Catholic dioceses in the Dominican Republic</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic" title="Roman Catholic">Roman Catholic</a> Church in the <a href="/wiki/Dominican_Republic" title="Dominican Republic">Dominican Republic</a> comprises two <a href="/wiki/Ecclesiastical_province" title="Ecclesiastical province">ecclesiastical provinces</a>, each headed by an <a href="/wiki/Archbishop" title="Archbishop">archbishop</a>. Each province is subdivided into an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Archdiocese" title="Archdiocese">archdiocese</a> and <a href="/wiki/Diocese" title="Diocese">dioceses</a> (nine in total), each headed by a <a href="/wiki/Bishop" title="Bishop">bishop</a> or an archbishop.</p>
