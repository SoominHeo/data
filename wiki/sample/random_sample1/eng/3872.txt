<p>The <b>Tōbu Utsunomiya Line</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">東武宇都宮線</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Tōbu Utsunomiya-sen</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a 24.3-kilometre railway line in <a href="/wiki/Tochigi_Prefecture" title="Tochigi Prefecture">Tochigi Prefecture</a>, Japan, owned and operated by the <a href="/wiki/Private_railway" title="Private railway">private railway</a> operator <a href="/wiki/Tobu_Railway" title="Tobu Railway">Tobu Railway</a>. It connects <a href="/wiki/Shin-Tochigi_Station" title="Shin-Tochigi Station">Shin-Tochigi Station</a> in <a href="/wiki/Tochigi,_Tochigi" title="Tochigi, Tochigi">Tochigi</a> with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tobu_Utsunomiya_Station" title="Tobu Utsunomiya Station">Tobu Utsunomiya Station</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Utsunomiya,_Tochigi" title="Utsunomiya, Tochigi">Utsunomiya</a>.</p>
<p>Shin-Tochigi Station offers connections to the <a href="/wiki/T%C5%8Dbu_Nikk%C5%8D_Line" title="Tōbu Nikkō Line">Tōbu Nikkō Line</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Tobu_Railway#T.C5.8Dbu_Main_Lines" title="Tobu Railway">Tobu Main Line</a> network.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=T%C5%8Dbu_Utsunomiya_Line&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The entire line opened in 1931, electrified at 1,500 V DC.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-terada2013_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-terada2013-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>From 17 March 2012, station numbering was introduced on all Tobu lines, with Tobu Utsunomiya Line stations adopting the prefix "TN" in orange.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tobu20120209_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-tobu20120209-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
