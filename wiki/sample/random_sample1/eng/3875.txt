<p><b>Dobrich Province</b> (<a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_language" title="Bulgarian language">Bulgarian</a>: <span lang="bg">Област Добрич, <i>Oblast Dobrich</i></span>, former name Dobrich <a href="/wiki/Okrug" title="Okrug">okrug</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/Province" title="Province">province</a> in northeastern <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgaria</a>, part of <a href="/wiki/Southern_Dobruja" title="Southern Dobruja">Southern Dobruja</a> geographical region. It is divided into 8 municipalities. At the 2011 census, it had a population of 186,016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-divisions_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-divisions-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-population_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-population-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-pop-stat-divisions_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-pop-stat-divisions-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Population">Population</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dobrich_Province&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Population">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Dobrich province had a <a href="/wiki/Population" title="Population">population</a> of 215,232 (215,217 also given) according to a 2001 <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a>, of which <span class="sortkey" style="display:none">7001493000000000000♠</span>49.3% were <a href="/wiki/Male" title="Male">male</a> and <span class="sortkey" style="display:none">7001507000000000000♠</span>50.7% were <a href="/wiki/Female" title="Female">female</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NSI_Census_2001_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-NSI_Census_2001-7">[7]</a></sup> At the end of 2009, the population of the province, announced by the Bulgarian National Statistical Institute, numbered 199,705<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-divisions_3-2"><a href="#cite_note-divisions-3">[3]</a></sup> of which <span class="sortkey" style="display:none">7001238000000000000♠</span>23.8% are inhabitants aged over 60 years.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NSI_age_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-NSI_age-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
