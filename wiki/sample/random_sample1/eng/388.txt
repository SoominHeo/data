<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">2008 FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m)</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

</div>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/FINA_World_Swimming_Championships_(25_m)" title="FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m)">FINA World Swimming Championships (25 m)</a></div>
<p>The <b>9th <a href="/wiki/FINA" title="FINA">FINA</a> Swimming World Championships (25 m)</b> were held at the <a href="/wiki/Manchester_Arena" title="Manchester Arena">Manchester Arena</a>, in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Manchester,_England" title="Manchester, England">Manchester, United Kingdom</a> 9-13 April 2008.</p>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> topped the medal table with 10 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gold_medals" title="Gold medals">gold medals</a>, though host team <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a> took home the most medals (24).</p>
