<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualification">Qualification</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Badminton_at_the_2008_Summer_Olympics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Qualification">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">For more details on this topic, see <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Badminton_at_the_2008_Summer_Olympics_-_Qualification" title="Badminton at the 2008 Summer Olympics - Qualification">Badminton at the 2008 Summer Olympics - Qualification</a>.</div>
<p>The qualification for 2008 Summer Olympics began from May 2007 until April 2008. During that period, all of tournaments sanctioned by the <a href="/wiki/Badminton_World_Federation" title="Badminton World Federation">Badminton World Federation</a> (except test events) awarded points to qualify for Olympic Games.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Draw">Draw</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Badminton_at_the_2008_Summer_Olympics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Draw">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Draw of the competition was held on July 26, 2008 in Beijing Henan Plaza Hotel at 16:00 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/China_Standard_Time" title="China Standard Time">CST</a> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UTC%2B8" title="UTC+8">UTC+8</a>). The draw was originally scheduled on August 4, 2008, just five days before the start of the competition. However it was brought forward to ensure better television broadcast scheduling.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
