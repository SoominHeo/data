<p><b>Tōkai</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">東海市</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Tōkai-shi</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/Cities_of_Japan" title="Cities of Japan">city</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Aichi_Prefecture" title="Aichi Prefecture">Aichi Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</p>
<p>As of May 2015, the city had an estimated <a href="/wiki/Population" title="Population">population</a> of 111,950 and a <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> of 2580 persons per km². The total area was 43.43 square kilometres (16.77 sq mi).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=T%C5%8Dkai,_Aichi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Tōkai is located in the far northwestern neck of <a href="/wiki/Chita_Peninsula" title="Chita Peninsula">Chita Peninsula</a> in southern Aichi Prefecture, and is bordered by <a href="/wiki/Ise_Bay" title="Ise Bay">Ise Bay</a> to the east, and the metropolis of Nagoya to the north.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Neighboring_municipalities">Neighboring municipalities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=T%C5%8Dkai,_Aichi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Neighboring municipalities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Aichi Prefecture

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=T%C5%8Dkai,_Aichi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The city of Tōkai was established on April 1, 1969 through the merger of the former towns of Yokosuka and Ueno (both from <a href="/wiki/Chita_District,_Aichi" title="Chita District, Aichi">Chita District</a>).</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Economy">Economy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=T%C5%8Dkai,_Aichi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Economy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Tōkai has a strong industrial base along its coastline, dominated by a large <a href="/wiki/Steel_mill" title="Steel mill">steel mill</a> owned by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nippon_Steel" title="Nippon Steel">Nippon Steel</a>.</p>
