<p>The first <b><a href="/wiki/World_record" title="World record">world record</a></b> in the <b><a href="/wiki/Decathlon" title="Decathlon">decathlon</a></b> was recognized by the <a href="/wiki/International_Association_of_Athletics_Federations" title="International Association of Athletics Federations">International Association of Athletics Federations</a> in 1922.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-iaaf_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-iaaf-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>As of 23 June 2012, 36 men's world records have been ratified by the IAAF in the event.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-iaaf_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-iaaf-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The first world record in the women's decathlon was recognized by the IAAF in 2004. As of 21 June 2009, two world records have been ratified by the IAAF in the event.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-iaaf_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-iaaf-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

In 1986, Thompson's <a href="/wiki/110_metres_hurdles" title="110 metres hurdles">110 metres hurdles</a> time was revised from 14.34 to 14.33 and his score increased by one point, so he retrospectively joined Hingsen as record holder under the old tables and replaced him from the date of the new tables.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[6]</a></sup></span></li>
