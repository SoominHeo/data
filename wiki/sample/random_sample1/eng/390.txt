<p>The <b>women's <a href="/wiki/Water_polo" title="Water polo">water polo</a> tournament at the <a href="/wiki/2008_Summer_Olympics" title="2008 Summer Olympics">2008 Summer Olympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Beijing" title="Beijing">Beijing</a> was held from 11 August to 21 August at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Beijing_National_Aquatics_Centre" title="Beijing National Aquatics Centre">Beijing's Ying Tung Pool</a>.</p>
<p>Teams from eight nations competed, seeded into two groups for the preliminary round. 20 games were played, 12 of them in the preliminary round (each team played the other teams in the group). Eight games were played in the final round.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Format">Format</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Water_polo_at_the_2008_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Women%27s_tournament&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Format">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The tournament featured eight teams, separated into two groups of four teams.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Each team played the other three teams in its pool once in a round-robin format. The first-place team in each pool qualified directly for the semifinal, the second- and third-place teams moved on to the quarterfinal round, and the fourth-place teams played each other in a seventh place game.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-format_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-format-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>The winners of the quarterfinal games moved on to the semifinals to face the top teams from each pool, while the quarterfinal losers played a fifth place classification game. The semifinal winners played in the gold medal game, while the losers played each other for the bronze.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-format_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-format-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
