<p><b>Tokyo Prefecture</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">東京府</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, とうきょうふ｜Tōkyō-fu <i>or</i> Tōkei-fu)</span> is a former <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> government entity which existed between 1869 and 1943.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nepyo_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-nepyo-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Tokyo_Prefecture&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:202px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Tokyo_Prefectural_Office_and_Tokyo_City_Hall.JPG"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="354" data-file-width="506" height="140" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cc/Tokyo_Prefectural_Office_and_Tokyo_City_Hall.JPG/200px-Tokyo_Prefectural_Office_and_Tokyo_City_Hall.JPG" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cc/Tokyo_Prefectural_Office_and_Tokyo_City_Hall.JPG/300px-Tokyo_Prefectural_Office_and_Tokyo_City_Hall.JPG 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cc/Tokyo_Prefectural_Office_and_Tokyo_City_Hall.JPG/400px-Tokyo_Prefectural_Office_and_Tokyo_City_Hall.JPG 2x" width="200"/></a>
<div class="thumbcaption">

<p>In 1943, the prefecture's municipalities, including the traditional <a href="/wiki/Tokyo_City" title="Tokyo City">city of Tokyo</a>, became part of the newly created <a href="/wiki/Tokyo" title="Tokyo">Tokyo Metropolis</a> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">東京都</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Tōkyō-to</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> or "<a href="/wiki/Greater_Tokyo_Area" title="Greater Tokyo Area">Greater Tokyo Metropolitan Area</a>".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nepyo_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-nepyo-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
