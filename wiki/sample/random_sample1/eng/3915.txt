<p><b>Tōtōmi-Ichinomiya Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">遠江一宮駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Tōtōmi-Ichinomiya-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a railway station in <a href="/wiki/Mori,_Shizuoka" title="Mori, Shizuoka">Mori, Shizuoka</a>, Japan, operated by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tenry%C5%AB_Hamanako_Railroad" title="Tenryū Hamanako Railroad">Tenryū Hamanako Railroad</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lines">Lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=T%C5%8Dt%C5%8Dmi-Ichinomiya_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Tōtōmi-Ichinomiya Station is served by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tenry%C5%AB_Hamanako_Railroad_Tenry%C5%AB_Hamanako_Line" title="Tenryū Hamanako Railroad Tenryū Hamanako Line">Tenryū Hamanako Railroad Tenryū Hamanako Line</a>. It is 16.4 km from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Terminal_station" title="Terminal station">terminus</a> of the line at <a href="/wiki/Kakegawa_Station" title="Kakegawa Station">Kakegawa Station</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_layout">Station layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=T%C5%8Dt%C5%8Dmi-Ichinomiya_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Station layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Tōtōmi-Ichinomiya Station has two opposing <a href="/wiki/Side_platform" title="Side platform">side platforms</a> serving two tracks. The station is not staffed full-time.</p>

<p>Tōtōmi-Ichinomiya Station opened on June 1, 1940, when the section of the <a href="/wiki/Japanese_National_Railways" title="Japanese National Railways">Japanese National Railways</a> (JNR) Futamata Line was extended from <a href="/wiki/Ensh%C5%AB-Mori_Station" title="Enshū-Mori Station">Enshū-Mori Station</a> to <a href="/wiki/Kanasashi_Station" title="Kanasashi Station">Kanasashi Station</a>. Scheduled freight services were discontinued from August 1962, and small parcel service in June 1970. After the <a href="/wiki/Privatization" title="Privatization">privatization</a> of JNR on March 15, 1987, the station came under the control of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tenry%C5%AB_Hamanako_Railroad_Tenry%C5%AB_Hamanako_Line" title="Tenryū Hamanako Railroad Tenryū Hamanako Line">Tenryū Hamanako Line</a>.</p>
