<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">States of the German Confederation</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

The German Confederation, 1815–1866 (in <a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a>)</div>
</div>
</div>
<p>The <b>States of the German Confederation</b> were those member states that from 20 June 1815 were part of the <a href="/wiki/German_Confederation" title="German Confederation">German Confederation</a>, which lasted, with some changes in the member states, until 24 August 1866, under the presidency of the Austrian imperial <a href="/wiki/House_of_Habsburg" title="House of Habsburg">House of Habsburg</a>, which was represented by an Austrian presidential envoy to the Federal <a href="/wiki/Diet_(assembly)" title="Diet (assembly)">diet</a> in <a href="/wiki/Free_City_of_Frankfurt" title="Free City of Frankfurt">Frankfurt</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Explanation">Explanation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=States_of_the_German_Confederation&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Explanation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On the whole, its territory nearly coincided with that remaining in the <a href="/wiki/Holy_Roman_Empire" title="Holy Roman Empire">Holy Roman Empire</a> at the outbreak of the <a href="/wiki/French_Revolution" title="French Revolution">French Revolution</a>, with the notable exception of Belgium. Except for the two rival major powers, <a href="/wiki/Habsburg_Monarchy" title="Habsburg Monarchy">Habsburg</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Prussia" title="Kingdom of Prussia">Prussia</a>, and the western left bank of the Rhine (which France had annexed, with tiny <a href="/wiki/County_of_Katzenelnbogen" title="County of Katzenelnbogen">Katzenelnbogen</a>), the other member states or their precursors, making up most of present <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>, had been within <a href="/wiki/Napoleon" title="Napoleon">Napoleon's</a> <a href="/wiki/Confederation_of_the_Rhine" title="Confederation of the Rhine">Confederation of the Rhine</a>.</p>
<ol>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Austrian_Empire" title="Austrian Empire">Austrian Empire</a>, excluding the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Hungary" title="Kingdom of Hungary">Kingdom of Hungary</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Principality_of_Transylvania_(1711%E2%80%931867)" title="Principality of Transylvania (1711–1867)">Principality of Transylvania</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Croatia_(Habsburg)" title="Kingdom of Croatia (Habsburg)">Croatia</a> (all of which became parts of the <a href="/wiki/Apostolic_King" title="Apostolic King">apostolic kingdom</a> of Hungary within the Danubian <a href="/wiki/Austria-Hungary" title="Austria-Hungary">Dual Monarchy</a>), the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Lombardy-Venetia" title="Kingdom of Lombardy-Venetia">Kingdom of Lombardy-Venetia</a> (constituting parts lost to Italy in 1859- viz. 1866), <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Bukovina" title="Duchy of Bukovina">Bukovina</a>, the kingdoms of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Dalmatia" title="Kingdom of Dalmatia">Dalmatia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Galicia_and_Lodomeria" title="Kingdom of Galicia and Lodomeria">Galicia</a>
<ol>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Bohemia" title="Kingdom of Bohemia">Kingdom of Bohemia</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Archduchy_of_Austria" title="Archduchy of Austria">Archduchy of Austria</a> (split into <a href="/wiki/Upper_Austria" title="Upper Austria">Upper Austria</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lower_Austria" title="Lower Austria">Lower Austria</a> in 1849)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Margraviate_of_Moravia" title="Margraviate of Moravia">Margraviate of Moravia</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Salzburg" title="Grand Duchy of Salzburg">Grand Duchy of Salzburg</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Carinthia" title="Duchy of Carinthia">Duchy of Carinthia</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Carniola" title="Duchy of Carniola">Duchy of Carniola</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Upper_and_Lower_Silesia" title="Duchy of Upper and Lower Silesia">Duchy of Upper and Lower Silesia</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Styria" title="Duchy of Styria">Duchy of Styria</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Austrian_Littoral" title="Austrian Littoral">Littoral</a> (consisting of <a href="/wiki/Gorizia_and_Gradisca" title="Gorizia and Gradisca">Gorizia and Gradisca</a>, <a href="/wiki/Istria" title="Istria">Istria</a> and <a href="/wiki/Trieste" title="Trieste">Trieste</a>)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/County_of_Tyrol" title="County of Tyrol">County of Tyrol</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Vorarlberg" title="Vorarlberg">Vorarlberg</a></li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Prussia" title="Kingdom of Prussia">Kingdom of Prussia</a> (without <a href="/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Posen" title="Grand Duchy of Posen">Posen</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Province_of_East_Prussia" title="Province of East Prussia">East Prussia</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Province_of_West_Prussia" title="Province of West Prussia">West Prussia</a>)
<ol>
<li><a href="/wiki/Province_of_Brandenburg" title="Province of Brandenburg">Brandenburg</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Province_of_Pomerania_(1815%E2%80%931945)" title="Province of Pomerania (1815–1945)">Pomerania</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Rhine_Province" title="Rhine Province">Rhine Province</a> (until 1822, <a href="/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_the_Lower_Rhine" title="Grand Duchy of the Lower Rhine">Lower Rhine</a> and <a href="/wiki/Province_of_J%C3%BClich-Cleves-Berg" title="Province of Jülich-Cleves-Berg">Jülich-Cleves-Berg</a>)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Province_of_Saxony" title="Province of Saxony">Saxony</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Province_of_Silesia" title="Province of Silesia">Silesia</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Province_of_Westphalia" title="Province of Westphalia">Westphalia</a></li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Bavaria" title="Kingdom of Bavaria">Kingdom of Bavaria</a> (the third largest member)</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Saxony" title="Kingdom of Saxony">Kingdom of Saxony</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Hanover" title="Kingdom of Hanover">Kingdom of Hanover</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_W%C3%BCrttemberg" title="Kingdom of Württemberg">Kingdom of Württemberg</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Electorate_of_Hesse" title="Electorate of Hesse">Electorate of Hesse</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Baden" title="Grand Duchy of Baden">Grand Duchy of Baden</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Hesse" title="Grand Duchy of Hesse">Grand Duchy of Hesse</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Holstein" title="Duchy of Holstein">Duchy of Holstein</a> (in personal union with the Kingdom of <a href="/wiki/Denmark" title="Denmark">Denmark</a>, was not a former member of the Confederation of the Rhine)
<ol>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Schleswig" title="Duchy of Schleswig">Duchy of Schleswig</a> (a fief of Denmark and together with the Duchy of Holstein in personal union with the Kingdom of Denmark, was not a former member of either the <a href="/wiki/Holy_Roman_Empire" title="Holy Roman Empire">Holy Roman Empire</a> or the Confederation of the Rhine. The secessionist (pro-German) government of Schleswig-Holstein (1848–51) joined Schleswig to the Confederation. This was not recognized by the Confederation and the Danish government, and the peace settlement in 1851 specified that Schleswig was not a member.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Luxembourg" title="Grand Duchy of Luxembourg">Grand Duchy of Luxembourg</a> (lost over <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Luxembourg_(province)" title="Luxembourg (province)">half of its territory in the west</a> to Belgium in the breakup of the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom_of_the_Netherlands" title="United Kingdom of the Netherlands">United Kingdom of the Netherlands</a> in 1839, and thereby causing <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Limburg_(1839-1866)" title="Duchy of Limburg (1839-1866)">Duchy of Limburg</a> to become a member.)</li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Limburg_(1839-1866)" title="Duchy of Limburg (1839-1866)">Duchy of Limburg</a> (became a member in 1839 as a compensation for territorial losses in the Grand Duchy of Luxemburg that were caused by the breakup of the United Kingdom of the Netherlands.)</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Brunswick" title="Duchy of Brunswick">Duchy of Brunswick</a> (prior Brunswick-Lunenburgian <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Principality_of_Wolfenb%C3%BCttel" title="Principality of Wolfenbüttel">Principality of Wolfenbüttel</a>)</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Mecklenburg-Schwerin" title="Grand Duchy of Mecklenburg-Schwerin">Grand Duchy of Mecklenburg-Schwerin</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Nassau" title="Duchy of Nassau">Duchy of Nassau</a></li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Saxe-Weimar-Eisenach" title="Grand Duchy of Saxe-Weimar-Eisenach">Grand Duchy of Saxe-Weimar-Eisenach</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Saxe-Gotha-Altenburg" title="Saxe-Gotha-Altenburg">Duchy of Saxe-Gotha-Altenburg</a> (to 1825)</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Saxe-Coburg-Saalfeld" title="Saxe-Coburg-Saalfeld">Duchy of Saxe-Coburg-Saalfeld</a> (to 1826)</li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saxe-Coburg-Gotha" title="Saxe-Coburg-Gotha">Duchy of Saxe-Coburg and Gotha</a> (from 1826)</li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Saxe-Meiningen" title="Duchy of Saxe-Meiningen">Duchy of Saxe-Meiningen</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Saxe-Hildburghausen" title="Saxe-Hildburghausen">Duchy of Saxe-Hildburghausen</a> (<a href="/wiki/Saxe-Altenburg" title="Saxe-Altenburg">Saxe-Altenburg</a> from 1826)</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Mecklenburg-Strelitz" title="Grand Duchy of Mecklenburg-Strelitz">Grand Duchy of Mecklenburg-Strelitz</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Oldenburg" title="Duchy of Oldenburg">Duchy of Oldenburg</a> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grand_Duke" title="Grand Duke">Grand Duchy</a> from 1829)</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Anhalt-Dessau" title="Anhalt-Dessau">Duchy of Anhalt-Dessau</a> (<a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Anhalt" title="Duchy of Anhalt">Duchy of Anhalt</a> from 1863)</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Anhalt-Bernburg" title="Anhalt-Bernburg">Duchy of Anhalt-Bernburg</a> (to 1863)</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Anhalt-K%C3%B6then" title="Anhalt-Köthen">Duchy of Anhalt-Köthen</a> (to 1847)</li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Principality_of_Schwarzburg-Sondershausen" title="Principality of Schwarzburg-Sondershausen">Principality of Schwarzburg-Sondershausen</a></li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Principality_of_Schwarzburg-Rudolstadt" title="Principality of Schwarzburg-Rudolstadt">Principality of Schwarzburg-Rudolstadt</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Hohenzollern-Hechingen" title="Hohenzollern-Hechingen">Principality of Hohenzollern-Hechingen</a> (merged in Prussia in 1850)</li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Principality_of_Liechtenstein" title="Principality of Liechtenstein">Principality of Liechtenstein</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen" title="Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen">Principality of Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen</a> (merged in Prussia in 1850)</li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Principality_of_Waldeck" title="Principality of Waldeck">Principality of Waldeck and Pyrmont</a></li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Principality_of_Reuss_Senior_Line" title="Principality of Reuss Senior Line">Principality of Reuss Senior Line</a></li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Principality_of_Reuss_Junior_Line" title="Principality of Reuss Junior Line">Principality of Reuss Junior Line</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Principality_of_Schaumburg-Lippe" title="Principality of Schaumburg-Lippe">Principality of Schaumburg-Lippe</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Principality_of_Lippe" title="Principality of Lippe">Principality of Lippe</a></li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Landgraviate_of_Hesse-Homburg" title="Landgraviate of Hesse-Homburg">Landgraviate of Hesse-Homburg</a> (from 1817)</li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Lauenburg" title="Duchy of Lauenburg">Duchy of Lauenburg</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Free_City_of_L%C3%BCbeck" title="Free City of Lübeck">Free and Hanseatic City of Lübeck</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Free_City_of_Frankfurt" title="Free City of Frankfurt">Free City of Frankfurt upon Main</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Bremen_(state)" title="Bremen (state)">Free Hanseatic City of Bremen</a> (still a constitutive state of federal Germany)</li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Free_and_Hanseatic_City_of_Hamburg" title="Free and Hanseatic City of Hamburg">Free and Hanseatic City of Hamburg</a> (still a constitutive state of federal Germany)</li>
</ol>
<p>The four free cities were <a href="/wiki/Republic" title="Republic">republics</a> by constitution, while all the others were monarchies, some <a href="/wiki/Constitutional_monarchy" title="Constitutional monarchy">constitutional</a> and some <a href="/wiki/Absolute_monarchy" title="Absolute monarchy">absolutist</a>.</p>
