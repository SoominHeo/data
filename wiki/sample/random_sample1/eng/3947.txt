<p>This is a <b>list of <a href="/wiki/Political_party" title="Political party">political parties</a> in <a href="/wiki/Politics_of_Germany" title="Politics of Germany">Germany</a></b>.</p>
<p>The Parliament of Germany, the <a href="/wiki/Bundestag" title="Bundestag">Bundestag</a>, has a plural <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Multi-party" title="Multi-party">multi-party</a> system, with two major parties, the <a href="/wiki/Social_Democratic_Party_of_Germany" title="Social Democratic Party of Germany">Social Democratic Party of Germany</a> (SPD) and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Christian_Democratic_Union_(Germany)" title="Christian Democratic Union (Germany)">Christian Democratic Union</a> (CDU), with its sister party, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Christian_Social_Union_of_Bavaria" title="Christian Social Union of Bavaria">Christian Social Union</a> (CSU) in the same parliamentary group, also known as <a href="/wiki/CDU/CSU" title="CDU/CSU">CDU/CSU</a> or the Union.</p>
<p>Germany also has a number of minor parties, most importantly <a href="/wiki/The_Left_(Germany)" title="The Left (Germany)">The Left</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Alliance_%2790/The_Greens" title="Alliance '90/The Greens">Alliance '90/The Greens</a>. The federal government of Germany usually consisted of a <a href="/wiki/Coalition_government" title="Coalition government">coalition</a> of a major and a minor party, most typically CDU/CSU and <a href="/wiki/Free_Democratic_Party_(Germany)" title="Free Democratic Party (Germany)">Free Democratic Party</a> (FDP), or a '<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Red-green_alliance" title="Red-green alliance">red-green alliance</a>' of the SPD and Greens. From 1966 to 1969, from 2005 to 2009 and again since 2013, the federal government consisted of a <a href="/wiki/Grand_coalition_(Germany)" title="Grand coalition (Germany)">Grand Coalition</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> In 2013, the FDP was voted out of the national Parliament, and in the following months also out of some state Parliaments such that it is not participating in any governments any longer.</p>
<p>Coalitions in the <a href="/wiki/Bundestag" title="Bundestag">Bundestag</a> and state legislators are often described by party colors. Party colors are the Social Democratic Party being red, the Alliance '90/The Greens green, the Free Democratic Party yellow, the Left dark red, red or purple, and the CDU/CSU black or blue.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
