<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Don Balón Award</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

</div>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>From 1976<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> until 2010, the Spanish sports magazine <i><a href="/wiki/Don_Bal%C3%B3n" title="Don Balón">Don Balón</a></i> conferred the <b>Don Balón Award</b> (<i>Premio Don Balón</i>) to the best Spanish player, the best foreign player, the best referee and the breakthrough player in <a href="/wiki/La_Liga" title="La Liga">La Liga</a>. These awards ceased to exist after the closure of the magazine in 2011.</p>
