<p>The <b><i>Dolgorae</i> class</b> submarine (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 돌고래급 잠수정) was a type of <a href="/wiki/Midget_submarine" title="Midget submarine">midget submarine</a> designed and acquired for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ROK_Navy" title="ROK Navy">ROK Navy</a>. All three units are now retired.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>These midget submarines were the first South Korean submarines of any type. They were acquired primarily to obtain initial experience with the basics of operating a submarine force. The secondary mission to train surface ASW ships in the detection of North Korean midget submarines.</p>
<p>With the Type 209 project completed there is no need for these submarines.</p>
