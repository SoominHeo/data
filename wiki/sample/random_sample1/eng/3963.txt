<p><b>Dolsando</b> or <b>Dolsan Island</b> is tenth-largest <a href="/wiki/Island" title="Island">island</a> in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a> and largest island in <a href="/wiki/Yeosu" title="Yeosu">Yeosu</a> city. It is the principal island of <a href="/wiki/Dolsan" title="Dolsan">Dolsan</a> town, on the southeastern coast of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/South_Jeolla" title="South Jeolla">South Jeolla</a> province, South Korea.</p>
