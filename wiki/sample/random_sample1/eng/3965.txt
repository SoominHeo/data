<p><i><b>Collective Invention</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">돌연변이</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Dolyeon Byeoni</span></i>; lit. "Mutant") is a South Korean <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Black_comedy_film" title="Black comedy film">black comedy film</a> written and directed by Kwon Oh-kwang.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> It is about a hapless fish-man's meteoric rise to celebrity — and subsequent fall from grace.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-CI1_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-CI1-5">[5]</a></sup> The film was screened in the Vanguard section of the <a href="/wiki/2015_Toronto_International_Film_Festival" title="2015 Toronto International Film Festival">2015 Toronto International Film Festival</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Collective_Invention&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A medical experiment gone wrong and a man mutates due to side effects from an experimental drug administered to him, turning him into a fish-man. When his supposedly girlfriend posts the story of her 'fishy' boyfriend on the internet, a wannabe reporter starts digging for the truth, covering the news about the mutant fish-man.</p>
