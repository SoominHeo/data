<p><b><a href="/wiki/Dolphin" title="Dolphin">Dolphin</a></b> usually refers to any member of the mammal families <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Delphinidae" title="Delphinidae">Delphinidae</a></i> (oceanic dolphins) and <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Platanistoidea" title="Platanistoidea">Platanistoidea</a></i> (river dolphins).</p>
<p>It can also refer to either species in the fish family <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Coryphaenidae" title="Coryphaenidae">Coryphaenidae</a></i>:</p>
<ul>
<li>the <a href="/wiki/Mahi-mahi" title="Mahi-mahi">mahi-mahi</a> or common dolphinfish (<i>Coryphaena hippurus</i>)</li>
<li>the <a href="/wiki/Pompano_dolphinfish" title="Pompano dolphinfish">pompano dolphinfish</a> (<i>Coryphaena equiselis</i>)</li>
</ul>
