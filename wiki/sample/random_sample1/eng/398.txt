<p>The <b>2009 FA Community Shield</b> was the 87th <a href="/wiki/FA_Community_Shield" title="FA Community Shield">FA Community Shield</a>, an annual <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> match contested by the winners of the previous season's <a href="/wiki/Premier_League" title="Premier League">Premier League</a> and <a href="/wiki/FA_Cup" title="FA Cup">FA Cup</a> competitions. The match was contested at <a href="/wiki/Wembley_Stadium" title="Wembley Stadium">Wembley Stadium</a>, <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, on 9 August 2009,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> and contested by <a href="/wiki/2008%E2%80%9309_Premier_League" title="2008–09 Premier League">2008–09 Premier League</a> champions <a href="/wiki/Manchester_United_F.C." title="Manchester United F.C.">Manchester United</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Chelsea_F.C." title="Chelsea F.C.">Chelsea</a> as the winners of the <a href="/wiki/2008%E2%80%9309_FA_Cup" title="2008–09 FA Cup">2008–09 FA Cup</a>, a repeat of the <a href="/wiki/2007_FA_Community_Shield" title="2007 FA Community Shield">2007 match</a>. The game ended in a 2–2 draw – the goals coming from <a href="/wiki/Nani" title="Nani">Nani</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wayne_Rooney" title="Wayne Rooney">Wayne Rooney</a> for Manchester United, and from <a href="/wiki/Ricardo_Carvalho" title="Ricardo Carvalho">Ricardo Carvalho</a> and <a href="/wiki/Frank_Lampard" title="Frank Lampard">Frank Lampard</a> for Chelsea – with Chelsea winning 4–1 on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Penalty_shootout_(association_football)" title="Penalty shootout (association football)">penalties</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>This was also the first time since 1998 that Chelsea had won a professional match on penalties; The last instance was a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_League_Cup" title="Football League Cup">League Cup</a> quarter-final against <a href="/wiki/Ipswich_Town_F.C." title="Ipswich Town F.C.">Ipswich Town</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (February 2012)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
