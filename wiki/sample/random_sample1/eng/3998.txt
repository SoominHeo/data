<p><b>East Darfur State</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar" xml:lang="ar">ولاية شرق دارفور</span>‎‎ Wilāyat Šarq Dārfūr; Sharq Darfur) is one of the <a href="/wiki/States_of_Sudan" title="States of Sudan">states</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sudan" title="Sudan">Sudan</a>, and one of five comprising the <a href="/wiki/Darfur" title="Darfur">Darfur</a> region. It was created in January 2012 as a result of the ongoing peace process for the wider Darfur region.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The state capital is <a href="/wiki/Ed_Daein" title="Ed Daein">Ed Daein</a>. The state was formed from land that was part of the state of <a href="/wiki/South_Darfur" title="South Darfur">South Darfur</a>.</p>
