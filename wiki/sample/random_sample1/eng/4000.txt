<p><b>Dongdaemun Station</b> is a station on the <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Subway_Line_1" title="Seoul Subway Line 1">Seoul Subway Line 1</a> and <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Subway_Line_4" title="Seoul Subway Line 4">Line 4</a>. It is named after <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dongdaemun" title="Dongdaemun">one of the four great gates</a> of the circular wall surrounding ancient Seoul, and is situated on the eastern end of <a href="/wiki/Jongno" title="Jongno">Jongno</a> ("Bell Street"). This station is also close to <a href="/wiki/Dongdaemun_Market" title="Dongdaemun Market">Dongdaemun Market</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>In December 2010 the station is recorded as having the third highest WiFi data consumption of all the <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Metropolitan_Subway" title="Seoul Metropolitan Subway">Seoul Metropolitan Subway</a> stations, following <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Express_Bus_Terminal_Station,_Seoul" title="Express Bus Terminal Station, Seoul">Express Bus Terminal Station</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sadang_Station" title="Sadang Station">Sadang Station</a>, and followed by <a href="/wiki/Jamsil_Station" title="Jamsil Station">Jamsil Station</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jongno_3-ga_Station" title="Jongno 3-ga Station">Jongno 3-ga Station</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
