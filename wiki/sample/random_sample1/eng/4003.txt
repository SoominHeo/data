<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">East London derby</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">
					Jump to:					<a href="#mw-head">navigation</a>, 					<a href="#p-search">search</a>
</div>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <b>East London derby</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is a football match that takes place between two of either <a href="/wiki/Dagenham_%26_Redbridge_F.C." title="Dagenham &amp; Redbridge F.C.">Dagenham &amp; Redbridge</a>, <a href="/wiki/Leyton_Orient_F.C." title="Leyton Orient F.C.">Leyton Orient</a> and <a href="/wiki/West_Ham_United_F.C." title="West Ham United F.C.">West Ham United</a>. This derby rarely takes place because, as is the case with the <a href="/wiki/Millwall_F.C.%E2%80%93West_Ham_United_F.C._rivalry" title="Millwall F.C.–West Ham United F.C. rivalry">Dockers Derby</a>, the clubs are usually in different leagues.</p>
<p>The last time West Ham United and Leyton Orient shared a competitive meeting was on 31 January 1987 in the <a href="/wiki/FA_Cup" title="FA Cup">FA Cup</a> which West Ham won 4–1 with their goals coming from <a href="/wiki/Frank_McAvennie" title="Frank McAvennie">McAvennie</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tony_Cottee" title="Tony Cottee">Cottee</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Keen" title="Kevin Keen">Keen</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Parris" title="George Parris">Parris</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> West Ham and Dagenham &amp; Redbridge have never met since the latter was formed in 1992 from the merger of <a href="/wiki/Redbridge_Forest_F.C." title="Redbridge Forest F.C.">Redbridge Forest</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dagenham_F.C." title="Dagenham F.C.">Dagenham</a>. However, Leyton Orient and Dagenham &amp; Redbridge meet occasionally in the lower leagues. They played each other in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_League_Two" title="Football League Two">League Two</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2015%E2%80%9316_Football_League_Two" title="2015–16 Football League Two">2015–16 season</a>, with Leyton Orient winning both matches.</p>
