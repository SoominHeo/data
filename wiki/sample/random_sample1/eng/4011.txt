<p><b>Dongbu Group</b> is a large <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Chaebol" title="Chaebol">chaebol</a></i> (conglomerate), producing <a href="/wiki/Industry" title="Industry">industry</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chemical" title="Chemical">chemical</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Shipping" title="Shipping">shipping</a>, <a href="/wiki/Insurance" title="Insurance">insurance</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Financial" title="Financial">financial</a> products. It was established in 1969.</p>
