<p>The <b>Dongseongno Festival</b> is a festival held in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jung-gu,_Daegu" title="Jung-gu, Daegu">Daegu Jung-gu</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. The festival is held every May and includes a song festival, a <a href="/wiki/Fashion_show" title="Fashion show">fashion show</a>, and concerts. <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
