<p><b>Dong-ah Institute of Media and Arts</b> (formerly known as <b>Dong-Ah Broadcasting College</b>) is a technical college in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a> specialized in training professionals in the fields of media design, production, and communications. The campus is situated in Samjuk-myeon, <a href="/wiki/Anseong" title="Anseong">Anseong</a> City, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gyeonggi" title="Gyeonggi">Gyeonggi</a> province, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. The name has been changed to Dong-Ah Institute of Media and Arts as January 15, 2007.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Dong-ah Institute of Media and Arts was ranked 171th (tied with <a href="/wiki/Jeju_National_University" title="Jeju National University">Jeju National University</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kumoh_National_Institute_of_Technology" title="Kumoh National Institute of Technology">Kumoh National Institute of Technology</a>) in Asia by QS Asian Universities Ranking in 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-QS_Asian_Universities_Ranking_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-QS_Asian_Universities_Ranking-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dong-ah_Institute_of_Media_and_Arts&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The school was opened in 1997 as Dong-ah Broadcasting Specialized College. On January 15, 2007 the school was renamed Dong-ah Institute Of Media and Arts.</p>
<p>Dong-ah has a student-exchange program with <a href="/wiki/Illinois_State_University" title="Illinois State University">Illinois State University</a> where Korean students can graduate with a degree from an American University and ISU students can spend a semester or more abroad experiencing the Korean culture. Dong-ah also has a student-exchange program with <a href="/wiki/California_State_University,_Fullerton" title="California State University, Fullerton">California State University, Fullerton</a> called the DIMA program and caters especially to Communications majors.</p>
