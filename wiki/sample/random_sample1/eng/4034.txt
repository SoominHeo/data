<p><i><b>Dongju: The Portrait of a Poet</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korea</a>: 동주) is a 2016 South Korean <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Black-and-white" title="Black-and-white">black-and-white</a> <a href="/wiki/Biographical_film" title="Biographical film">biographical</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Period_film" title="Period film">period</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Drama_film" title="Drama film">drama film</a> directed by <a href="/wiki/Lee_Joon-ik" title="Lee Joon-ik">Lee Joon-ik</a> about <a href="/wiki/Yun_Dong-ju" title="Yun Dong-ju">Yun Dong-ju</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-han_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-han-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> It was released in South Korea on February 17, 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-han_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-han-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Summary">Summary</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dongju:_The_Portrait_of_a_Poet&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Summary">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
