<p><b>Callionymoidei</b> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Suborder" title="Suborder">suborder</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Perciformes" title="Perciformes">Perciformes</a>, the largest <a href="/wiki/Order_(biology)" title="Order (biology)">order</a> of fish. The suborder includes the <a href="/wiki/Dragonet" title="Dragonet">Dragonets</a>.</p>
