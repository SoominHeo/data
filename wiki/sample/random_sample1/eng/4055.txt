<p><i><b>The Story of Two Women</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">두 여자 이야기</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Duyeoja iyagi</span></i>) is a <a href="/wiki/1994_in_film" title="1994 in film">1994</a> <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> film directed by <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jung-gook" title="Lee Jung-gook">Lee Jung-gook</a>. It was awarded Best Film at the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Bell_Awards" title="Grand Bell Awards">Grand Bell Awards</a> ceremony.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Other awards won by the film included Best New Director for Lee, Best Screenplay and Best New Actress for <a href="/wiki/Yoon_Yoo-sun" title="Yoon Yoo-sun">Yoon Yoo-sun</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-KMDB_Korean_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-KMDB_Korean-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
