<p><b>Lottioidea</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Family_(biology)" title="Family (biology)">superfamily</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sea_snail" title="Sea snail">sea snails</a> or limpets, <a href="/wiki/Marine_(ocean)" title="Marine (ocean)">marine</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gastropod" title="Gastropod">gastropod</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mollusk" title="Mollusk">mollusks</a> in the clade <a href="/wiki/Patellogastropoda" title="Patellogastropoda">Patellogastropoda</a>, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/True_limpets" title="True limpets">true limpets</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="2005_taxonomy">2005 taxonomy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lottioidea&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: 2005 taxonomy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>There are three families within Lottioidea in the <a href="/wiki/Taxonomy_of_the_Gastropoda_(Bouchet_%26_Rocroi,_2005)" title="Taxonomy of the Gastropoda (Bouchet &amp; Rocroi, 2005)">taxonomy of the Gastropoda by Bouchet &amp; Rocroi (2005)</a>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Family <a href="/wiki/Lottiidae" title="Lottiidae">Lottiidae</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/J._E._Gray" title="J. E. Gray">J. E. Gray</a>, 1840</li>
<li>Family <a href="/wiki/Acmaeidae" title="Acmaeidae">Acmaeidae</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/E._Forbes" title="E. Forbes">E. Forbes</a>, 1850</li>
<li>Family <a href="/wiki/Lepetidae" title="Lepetidae">Lepetidae</a> J. E. Gray, 1850</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="2007_taxonomy">2007 taxonomy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lottioidea&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 2007 taxonomy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Based on <a href="/wiki/Patellogastropoda#2007_taxonomy" title="Patellogastropoda">molecular research on the Patellogastropoda by Nakano &amp; Ozawa (2007)</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Nakano_2007_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Nakano_2007-3">[3]</a></sup> there were eight families in Lottioidea:</p>
<ul>
<li>superfamily Lottioidea
<ul>
<li>family <a href="/wiki/Acmaeidae" title="Acmaeidae">Acmaeidae</a> <small>Forbes, 1850</small></li>
<li>family <a href="/wiki/Nacellidae" title="Nacellidae">Nacellidae</a> <small>Thiele, 1891</small></li>
<li>family <a href="/wiki/Lepetidae" title="Lepetidae">Lepetidae</a> <small>Gray, 1850</small></li>
<li>family <a href="/wiki/Pectinodontidae" title="Pectinodontidae">Pectinodontidae</a> <small>Pilsbry, 1891</small></li>
<li>family <a href="/wiki/Lottiidae" title="Lottiidae">Lottiidae</a> <small>Gray, 1840</small></li>
<li>family <a href="/wiki/Neolepetopsidae" title="Neolepetopsidae">Neolepetopsidae</a> <small>McLean, 1990</small></li>
<li>† family <a href="/wiki/Daminilidae" title="Daminilidae">Daminilidae</a></li>
<li>† family <a href="/wiki/Lepetopsidae" title="Lepetopsidae">Lepetopsidae</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dt>Families brought into synonymy</dt>
</dl>
<ul>
<li>Bertiniidae <small>Jousseaume, 1883</small>: synonym of Nacellidae <small>Thiele, 1891</small></li>
<li>Lottidae [sic] : synonym of Lottiidae <small>Gray, 1840</small></li>
<li>Propilidiidae: synonym of Lepetidae <small>Gray, 1850</small></li>
</ul>
