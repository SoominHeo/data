<p><b>Dorbod Mongol Autonomous County</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">杜尔伯特蒙古族自治县</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Dù'ěrbótè měnggǔzú Zìzhìxiàn</span></i>; <a href="/wiki/Mongolian_language" title="Mongolian language">Mongolian</a>: <span style="display:inline-block; font-weight:normal; font-family:'Menk Hawang Tig', 'Menk Qagan Tig', 'Menk Garqag Tig', 'Menk Har_a Tig', 'Menk Scnin Tig', 'Oyun Gurban Ulus Tig', 'Oyun Qagan Tig', 'Oyun Garqag Tig', 'Oyun Har_a Tig', 'Oyun Scnin Tig', 'Mongolian BT', 'Mongolian Baiti', 'Noto Sans Mongolian', 'Mongol Usug', 'Mongolian White', 'MongolianScript', 'Code2000', 'Menksoft Qagan'; font-size: 1.5em; line-height: 1em; writing-mode: vertical-lr; -webkit-writing-mode: vertical-lr; -o-writing-mode: vertical-lr; -ms-writing-mode: tb-lr; writing-mode: tb-lr; vertical-align:text-top; max-height: 2.5em; text-align: left; word-wrap: normal;">ᠳᠥᠷᠪᠡᠳ ᠮᠣᠩᠭᠣᠯ ᠥᠪᠡᠷᠲᠡᠭᠡᠨ ᠵᠠᠰᠠᠬᠤ ᠰᠢᠶᠠᠨ</span> ) is an administrative subdivision of the <a href="/wiki/Provinces_of_China" title="Provinces of China">province</a> of <a href="/wiki/Heilongjiang" title="Heilongjiang">Heilongjiang</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/People%27s_Republic_of_China" title="People's Republic of China">China</a>. It is under the jurisdiction of the <a href="/wiki/Prefecture-level_city" title="Prefecture-level city">prefecture-level city</a> of <a href="/wiki/Daqing" title="Daqing">Daqing</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dorbod_Mongol_Autonomous_County&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The population of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/County_(People%27s_Republic_of_China)" title="County (People's Republic of China)">county</a> was 243,829 in 1999.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>It is named after the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/D%C3%B6rbet_(Borjigin_clan)" title="Dörbet (Borjigin clan)">Dörbed Mongols</a>.</p>
