<p><b>Donggang</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">东港</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">東港</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Dōnggǎng</span></i>; literally: "Eastern Port") is a city in the southeast of <a href="/wiki/Liaoning" title="Liaoning">Liaoning</a> Province in Northeast China. Situated on the coast of the <a href="/wiki/Yellow_Sea" title="Yellow Sea">Yellow Sea</a> at the mouth of the <a href="/wiki/Yalu_River" title="Yalu River">Yalu River</a>, it is located near the maritime border with <a href="/wiki/North_Korea" title="North Korea">North Korea</a>. Administratively, it is a <a href="/wiki/County-level_city" title="County-level city">county-level city</a> of <a href="/wiki/Dandong" title="Dandong">Dandong</a>, which lies 35 kilometres (22 mi) to the northeast.</p>
