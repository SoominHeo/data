<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">2010 FIFA World Cup Group F</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/2010_FIFA_World_Cup" title="2010 FIFA World Cup">2010 FIFA World Cup</a></div>
<p><b>Group F of the <a href="/wiki/2010_FIFA_World_Cup" title="2010 FIFA World Cup">2010 FIFA World Cup</a></b> began on 14 June 2010 and ended on 24 June 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The group consisted of <a href="/wiki/2006_FIFA_World_Cup" title="2006 FIFA World Cup">2006</a> winner <a href="/wiki/Italy_national_football_team" title="Italy national football team">Italy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Paraguay_national_football_team" title="Paraguay national football team">Paraguay</a>, <a href="/wiki/New_Zealand_national_football_team" title="New Zealand national football team">New Zealand</a> and <a href="/wiki/Slovakia_national_football_team" title="Slovakia national football team">Slovakia</a>. Italy and Paraguay previously met in the first round of the <a href="/wiki/1950_FIFA_World_Cup" title="1950 FIFA World Cup">1950 tournament</a>, with Italy winning 2–0; neither qualified for the next round.</p>
<p>Paraguay topped the group with five points and advanced to face <a href="/wiki/Japan_national_football_team" title="Japan national football team">Japan</a>, the runners-up in <a href="/wiki/2010_FIFA_World_Cup_Group_E" title="2010 FIFA World Cup Group E">Group E</a>. Slovakia finished as runners-up with four points and advanced to meet the <a href="/wiki/Netherlands_national_football_team" title="Netherlands national football team">Netherlands</a>, who won Group E. Italy were eliminated from the group with just two points, behind underdogs New Zealand, who drew all three of their matches to finish on three points. New Zealand ended up being the only unbeaten team at the finals.</p>
