<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en"><i>Dnestrovskaya Pravda</i></h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><i><b>Dnestrovskaya Pravda</b></i> is a Russian-language newspaper from <a href="/wiki/Tiraspol" title="Tiraspol">Tiraspol</a>, the capital of <a href="/wiki/Transnistria" title="Transnistria">Transnistria</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-home_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-home-1">[1]</a></sup> It was founded by the Tiraspol City Council of popular deputies in 1941. This is the oldest <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Periodical_publication" title="Periodical publication">periodical publication</a> in the region. Its name means <i>Dniester Truth</i>; taking it title from the <a href="/wiki/Dniester" title="Dniester">Dniester</a> river which flows through the city.</p>
<p>The paper is printed three times a week (Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday) and consists of only four folded <a href="/wiki/ISO_216#A_series" title="ISO 216">A3</a>-sized sheets. It is often cited as an authoritative source for news from Transnistria by foreign news agencies such as <a href="/wiki/BASA-press" title="BASA-press">Basa</a>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Flux_(news_agency)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Flux (news agency) (page does not exist)">Flux</a> and <a href="/wiki/Interfax" title="Interfax">Interfax</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (February 2007)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<p>Its editor is Tatyana Mikhailovna Rudenko (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cyrillic" title="Cyrillic">Cyrillic</a>: Татьяна Михайловна Руденко).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
