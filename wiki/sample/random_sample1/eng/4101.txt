<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dnieper_(disambiguation)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dnieper_River" title="Dnieper River">Dnieper River</a> or <b>Dnipro</b> river, river flowing through Russia, Belarus and Ukraine to the Black Sea.
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnieper_Rapids" title="Dnieper Rapids">Dnieper Rapids</a> are the historic rapids on the Dnieper river.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnieper-Bug_Estuary" title="Dnieper-Bug Estuary">Dnieper-Bug Estuary</a>, estuary, or liman, of two rivers: the Dnieper and the Southern Bug.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnieper-Donets_Rift" title="Dnieper-Donets Rift">Dnieper-Donets Rift</a>, the east-west running rift in the Sarmatian Craton.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnieper_Upland" title="Dnieper Upland">Dnieper Upland</a>, southeastern European plain occupying the territory between the Dnieper and Bug rivers.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnieper%E2%80%93Bug_Canal" title="Dnieper–Bug Canal">Dnieper–Bug Canal</a> (alternately spelled Dnepr-Bug Canal), or the Dneprovsko-Bugsky Canal is the longest inland ship canal in Belarus.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnieper_Reservoir" title="Dnieper Reservoir">Dnieper Reservoir</a>, a water reservoir on the Dnieper River in Ukraine.
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnieper_Hydroelectric_Station" title="Dnieper Hydroelectric Station">Dnieper Hydroelectric Station</a>, the largest hydroelectric power station on the Dnieper River, placed in Zaporizhia, Ukraine.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnieper_Ukraine" title="Dnieper Ukraine">Dnieper Ukraine</a>, central Ukraine under the Russian Empire.</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Sports">Sports</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dnieper_(disambiguation)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Sports">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/BC_Dnipro" title="BC Dnipro">BC Dnipro</a>, a Ukrainian basketball club based in the city of <a href="/wiki/Dnipropetrovsk" title="Dnipropetrovsk">Dnipro</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnipro-Arena" title="Dnipro-Arena">Dnipro-Arena</a>, a football stadium in Dnipropetrovsk, Ukraine.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/FC_Cherkaskyi_Dnipro" title="FC Cherkaskyi Dnipro">FC Cherkaskyi Dnipro</a>, a professional Ukrainian football club from the city of Cherkasy.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/FC_Dnepr_Smolensk" title="FC Dnepr Smolensk">FC Dnepr Smolensk</a>, a Russian football club.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/FC_Dnipro_Dnipropetrovsk" title="FC Dnipro Dnipropetrovsk">FC Dnipro Dnipropetrovsk</a>, a Ukrainian football club.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/FC_Dnipro-75_Dnipropetrovsk" title="FC Dnipro-75 Dnipropetrovsk">FC Dnipro-75 Dnipropetrovsk</a>, a Ukrainian football club.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/FC_MKK-Dnepr_Rogachev" title="FC MKK-Dnepr Rogachev">FC MKK-Dnepr Rogachev</a>, a Belarusian football club based in Rogachev, Gomel Oblast.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/FC_Dnepr_Mogilev" title="FC Dnepr Mogilev">FC Dnepr Mogilev</a>, a Belarusian football club.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnipro_Dnipropetrovsk_(bandy)" title="Dnipro Dnipropetrovsk (bandy)">Dnipro Dnipropetrovsk</a> (Ukrainian: "Дніпро" Дніпропетровськ), a bandy club from Dnipropetrovsk in Ukraine.</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transportation">Transportation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dnieper_(disambiguation)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Transportation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnipro_(Kiev_Metro)" title="Dnipro (Kiev Metro)">Dnipro (Kiev Metro)</a>, a Metro station in Kiev.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dnepr_(motorcycle)" title="Dnepr (motorcycle)">Dnepr (motorcycle)or</a> Dnipro (Ukrainian: Дніпро), the brand name of a motorcycle produced in Kyiv, Ukraine.</li>
<li><b>Dnepr</b> was the call sign of the Soviet <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_T-7" title="Soyuz T-7">Soyuz T-7</a> mission to space station Salyut 7.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnepr_(rocket)" title="Dnepr (rocket)">Dnepr (rocket)</a>, a space launch vehicle named after the Dnieper River.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/SS-18_Satan" title="SS-18 Satan">SS-18 Satan</a>, the Dnepr missile.</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Military">Military</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dnieper_(disambiguation)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Military">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Dnieper" title="Battle of the Dnieper">Battle of the Dnieper</a> was a military campaign that took place in 1943 on the Eastern Front of World War II.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnestr_radar" title="Dnestr radar">Dnestr radar</a> (Russian: Днестр) and <b>Dnepr radar</b> (Russian: Днепр), both known by the NATO reporting name Hen House are the first generation of Soviet space surveillance and early warning radars.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnieper%E2%80%93Carpathian_Offensive" title="Dnieper–Carpathian Offensive">Dnieper–Carpathian Offensive</a> was a strategic offensive of Soviet Armed Forces.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnieper_Flotilla" title="Dnieper Flotilla">Dnieper Flotilla</a>, the name given to the various naval flotillas on the Dnieper River.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dnipro-1_Regiment" title="Dnipro-1 Regiment">Dnipro-1 Regiment</a>, a Special Tasks Patrol Police regiment subordinated to Ministry of Internal Affairs of Ukraine.</li>
</ul>
