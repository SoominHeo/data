<p><b>Dragoș Grigore</b> (born 7 September 1986) is a <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romanian</a> professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)#Centre-back" title="Defender (association football)">central defender</a> for Qatari side <a href="/wiki/Al-Sailiya_SC" title="Al-Sailiya SC">Al-Sailiya SC</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Romania_national_football_team" title="Romania national football team">Romania national team</a>.</p>
