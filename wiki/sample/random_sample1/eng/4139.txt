<p><i><b>The End</b></i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> (<a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a>: <span lang="es"><i><b>Fin</b></i></span>) is a 2012 Spanish <a href="/wiki/Thriller_film" title="Thriller film">thriller film</a> directed by Jorge Torregrossa and based on David Monteagudo's novel <i>Fin</i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Cuore_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Cuore-2">[2]</a></sup> with a screenplay by <a href="/wiki/Sergio_G._S%C3%A1nchez" title="Sergio G. Sánchez">Sergio G. Sánchez</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jorge_Guerricaechevarr%C3%ADa" title="Jorge Guerricaechevarría">Jorge Guerricaechevarría</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TIFF_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-TIFF-3">[3]</a></sup> The film was produced by <a href="/wiki/Antena_3_(Spain)" title="Antena 3 (Spain)">Antena 3 Films</a>. It was first screened at the <a href="/wiki/2012_Toronto_International_Film_Festival" title="2012 Toronto International Film Festival">2012 Toronto International Film Festival</a> in September<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TIFF_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-TIFF-3">[3]</a></sup> and later opened in Spain on 23 November of the same year.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Butaca_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Butaca-4">[4]</a></sup> The film also marks the screen debut of model <a href="/wiki/Andr%C3%A9s_Velencoso" title="Andrés Velencoso">Andrés Velencoso</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-A3tv_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-A3tv-5">[5]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_End_(2012_film)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A group of old friends gets together for a weekend in a mountain cabin. It has been several years since they last met, yet nothing seems to have changed between them. But lurking behind the laughter and stories is a murky episode from their past that continues to haunt them.</p>
<p>A strange, sudden incident alters their plans, leaving them stranded and with no means of communication with the outside world. On their way for help, the group starts to disintegrate, just as a new natural order is revealed before their astonished eyes.</p>
