<p><i><b>D-Day</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">디데이</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Di Dei</span></i>) is a 2015 <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Korean_drama" title="Korean drama">television series</a> that airs on <a href="/wiki/JTBC" title="JTBC">jTBC</a> on Fridays and Saturdays at 20:40 for 20 episodes beginning September 18, 2015.</p>
