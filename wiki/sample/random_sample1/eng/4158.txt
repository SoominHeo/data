<p>The <b>Moldovan National Division</b> (<a href="/wiki/Romanian_language" title="Romanian language">Romanian</a>: <span lang="ro" xml:lang="ro"><i>Divizia Națională</i></span>) is the top division of <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">association football</a> in <a href="/wiki/Moldova" title="Moldova">Moldova</a>. There are 11 teams in the competition. At the end of the season, the bottom club is relegated to the <a href="/wiki/Moldovan_%22A%22_Division" title='Moldovan "A" Division'>"A" Division</a> and replaced by the lower league's champion and runner-up.</p>
<p>The competition was established in 1992, when the country became independent from the <a href="/wiki/Soviet_Union" title="Soviet Union">Soviet Union</a>.</p>
