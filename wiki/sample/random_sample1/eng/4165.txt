<p><b>Diyarbakır Province</b> (<a href="/wiki/Turkish_language" title="Turkish language">Turkish</a>: <span lang="tr" xml:lang="tr"><i>Diyarbakır ili</i></span>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kurdish_language" title="Kurdish language">Kurdish</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ku" xml:lang="ku">Parêzgeha Amed</span>‎) is a <a href="/wiki/Provinces_of_Turkey" title="Provinces of Turkey">province</a> in southeastern <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a> . The province covers an area of 15,355 km² and its population is 1,528,958. The provincial capital is the city of <a href="/wiki/Diyarbak%C4%B1r" title="Diyarbakır">Diyarbakır</a>.</p>
<p>It has been home to many civilisations and the surrounding area including itself is home to many Mesolithic era stone carvings and artifacts. The province has been ruled by <a href="/wiki/Hurrians" title="Hurrians">Hurrians</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hittites" title="Hittites">Hittites</a>, <a href="/wiki/Assyria" title="Assyria">Assyria</a>, <a href="/wiki/Armenians" title="Armenians">Armenians</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Persia" title="Persia">Persia</a>, Macedonian and Seleucid <a href="/wiki/Greeks" title="Greeks">Greeks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a>, <a href="/wiki/Parthia" title="Parthia">Parthia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Byzantium" title="Byzantium">Byzantium</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sassanid_Persia" title="Sassanid Persia">Sassanid Persia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Arabs" title="Arabs">Arabs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Seljuk_Empire" title="Seljuk Empire">Seljuk Empire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mongol_Empire" title="Mongol Empire">Mongol Empire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Safavid_dynasty" title="Safavid dynasty">Safavid dynasty</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a> (as the sequential <a href="/wiki/Diyarbekir_Eyalet" title="Diyarbekir Eyalet">Diyarbekir Eyalet</a> and <a href="/wiki/Diyarbekir_Vilayet" title="Diyarbekir Vilayet">Diyarbekir Vilayet</a>), and finally the modern <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Republic_of_Turkey" title="Republic of Turkey">Republic of Turkey</a>.</p>
<p>The majority of the province's population is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kurd" title="Kurd">Kurdish</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
