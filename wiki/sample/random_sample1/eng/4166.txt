<p><i><b>Dear Sister</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">ディア・シスター</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a Japanese <a href="/wiki/List_of_romantic_comedy_television_series" title="List of romantic comedy television series">love comedy</a> <a href="/wiki/Japanese_television_drama" title="Japanese television drama">drama series</a>. It premiered on October 16, 2014 on <a href="/wiki/Fuji_Television" title="Fuji Television">Fuji Television</a>. The theme song is "Happiness", by <a href="/wiki/Che%27Nelle" title="Che'Nelle">Che'Nelle</a> from her album <i>@Che'Nelle World</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dear_Sister&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Misaki and Hazuki are sisters. When they were younger, Misaki did not study well, but she had a charming personality which endeared her to others. Older sister Hazuki was meticulous, but she wasn't very good socially. Hazuki was also jealous of her younger sister, believing her mother only loved Misaki. After graduating from high school, Misaki left home and the family has not heard from her since.</p>
<p>Hazuki is now 29 years old and works at a local government office. Her boyfriend works in the same office and they hope to marry soon. Suddenly, Misaki, who is now 27 years old, appears in front of Hazuki and they begin to live together. Misaki has a secret that she can't tell anyone.</p>
