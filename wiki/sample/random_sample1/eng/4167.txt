<p><b>Diego Martín Novaretti</b>, (born 9 May 1985), sometimes known as <b>El Flaco</b>, is an Argentine <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">defender</a> for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Club_Leon" title="Club Leon">Club Leon</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Diego_Novaretti&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_Atl.C3.A9tico_Belgrano">Club Atlético Belgrano</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Diego_Novaretti&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Club Atlético Belgrano">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>He made his professional debut with <a href="/wiki/Club_Atl%C3%A9tico_Belgrano" title="Club Atlético Belgrano">Club Atlético Belgrano</a> in 2006. Novaretti is known for his height and quickness.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Toluca_F.C.">Toluca F.C.</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Diego_Novaretti&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Toluca F.C.">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It was rumoured that Novaretti might join <a href="/wiki/Deportivo_Toluca_F.C." title="Deportivo Toluca F.C.">Deportivo Toluca</a> since the departure of <a href="/wiki/Paulo_da_Silva" title="Paulo da Silva">Paulo da Silva</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Jos%C3%A9_Manuel_de_la_Torre" title="José Manuel de la Torre">José Manuel de la Torre</a> expressed a great interest in acquiring him for the team. On 22 July 2009, it became official that Novaretti was to join Toluca. But currently he won't play as he escaped from his last club without paying anything for the transfer. He made his debut on 6 September 2009 against Morelia. He played the whole 90 minutes. A game that finished 1–0 For Toluca. He scored his first goal for Toluca on 4 April 2010 against Estudiantes Tecos. A game that finished 5–0 for Toluca.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="S.S._Lazio">S.S. Lazio</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Diego_Novaretti&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: S.S. Lazio">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In the summer of 2013, Novaretti arrived as a free agent joining <a href="/wiki/S.S._Lazio" title="S.S. Lazio">Lazio</a> from <a href="/wiki/Deportivo_Toluca_F.C." title="Deportivo Toluca F.C.">Toluca</a>. Novaretti signed a 4-year deal at the club. Novaretti was originally targeted by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Manchester_City" title="Manchester City">Manchester City</a>, but their interest waned after <a href="/wiki/Roberto_Mancini" title="Roberto Mancini">Roberto Mancini</a> was relieved of his duties.</p>
<p>He made his debut for Lazio in <a href="/wiki/Serie_A" title="Serie A">Serie A</a> on 25 August 2013 in Lazio's 2-1 win against Udinese. While 19 September 2013, Novaretti made his debut in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Europa_League" title="Europa League">Europa League</a>, against <a href="/wiki/Legia_Warsaw" title="Legia Warsaw">Legia Warsaw</a>; Novaretti replaced <a href="/wiki/Keita_Bald%C3%A9_Diao" title="Keita Baldé Diao">Keita Baldé Diao</a> as a substitute. In his first season Novaretti made 17 appearances in all competitions during the 2013/14 season for <a href="/wiki/S.S._Lazio" title="S.S. Lazio">Lazio</a>.</p>
