<p><b>Deon Hemmings</b> (born 9 October 1968 in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saint_Ann,_Jamaica" title="Saint Ann, Jamaica">Saint Ann, Jamaica</a>) is a former female <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/400m_Hurdles" title="400m Hurdles">400 metres hurdler</a>.</p>
<p>Hemmings was the first ever <a href="/wiki/Jamaica" title="Jamaica">Jamaican</a> woman to win an <a href="/wiki/Olympic_Gold" title="Olympic Gold">Olympic Gold</a> when she won the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/400m_Hurdles" title="400m Hurdles">400m Hurdles</a> at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1996_Olympics" title="1996 Olympics">1996 Olympics</a> breaking the Olympic record which stood to 2004. Hemmings also won two silver medals at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2000_Olympics" title="2000 Olympics">2000 Olympics</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/400m_Hurdles" title="400m Hurdles">400m Hurdles</a> and 4x400m Relay (together with <a href="/wiki/Sandie_Richards" title="Sandie Richards">Sandie Richards</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Catherine_Scott-Pomales" title="Catherine Scott-Pomales">Catherine Scott-Pomales</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lorraine_Graham" title="Lorraine Graham">Lorraine Graham</a>).</p>
<p>Hemmings also won a Silver medal in the <a href="/wiki/1994_Commonwealth_Games" title="1994 Commonwealth Games">1994 Commonwealth Games</a>, Bronze Medal in the 1995 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/World_Athletics_Championships" title="World Athletics Championships">World Athletics Championships</a>, Silver in the 1997 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/World_Athletics_Championships" title="World Athletics Championships">World Athletics Championships</a> and Bronze in the 1999 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/World_Athletics_Championships" title="World Athletics Championships">World Athletics Championships</a> all in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/400m_Hurdles" title="400m Hurdles">400m Hurdles</a>.</p>
<p>Hemmings retired in 2003 and married <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Michael_McCatty&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Michael McCatty (page does not exist)">Michael McCatty</a> in 2004.</p>
