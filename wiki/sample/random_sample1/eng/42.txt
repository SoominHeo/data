<p><br/>
Year <b>1163</b> (<b><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Roma%D8%B9n_numerals&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Romaعn numerals (page does not exist)">MCLXIII</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Tuesday" title="Common year starting on Tuesday">common year starting on Tuesday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1163&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Owain_Gwynedd" title="Owain Gwynedd">Owain Gwynedd</a> is recognized as ruler of <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Wales</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Silesia" title="Silesia">Silesian</a> duchies accept the suzerainty of the <a href="/wiki/Holy_Roman_Empire" title="Holy Roman Empire">Holy Roman Empire</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Norwegian_Law_of_Succession" title="Norwegian Law of Succession">Law of Succession</a> is introduced in <a href="/wiki/Norway" title="Norway">Norway</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Council_of_Tours" title="Council of Tours">Council of Tours</a>: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Albigensians" title="Albigensians">Albigensians</a> are named and condemned as heretics.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Loccum_Abbey" title="Loccum Abbey">Loccum Abbey</a> in <a href="/wiki/Hanover" title="Hanover">Hanover</a> is founded as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cistercian" title="Cistercian">Cistercian</a> house by <a href="/wiki/Cornwall" title="Cornwall">Cornwall</a>.</li>
<li>The Guanfuchang salt-fields (官富場) in <a href="/wiki/Hong_Kong" title="Hong Kong">Hong Kong</a>, nowadays <a href="/wiki/To_Kwa_Wan" title="To Kwa Wan">To Kwa Wan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kowloon_Bay" title="Kowloon Bay">Kowloon Bay</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kwun_Tong" title="Kwun Tong">Kwun Tong</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lam_Tin" title="Lam Tin">Lam Tin</a> districts, are first officially operated by the <a href="/wiki/Song_dynasty" title="Song dynasty">Song dynasty</a>.</li>
<li>The first stone of <a href="/wiki/Notre_Dame_de_Paris" title="Notre Dame de Paris">Paris's Notre Dame Cathedral</a> is set by <a href="/wiki/Pope_Alexander_III" title="Pope Alexander III">Pope Alexander III</a>.</li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Almohads" title="Almohads">Almohads</a> finish driving the <a href="/wiki/Normans" title="Normans">Normans</a> out of <a href="/wiki/North_Africa" title="North Africa">North Africa</a>.</li>
</ul>
