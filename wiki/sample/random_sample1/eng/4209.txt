<p>The <b>Douliou Baseball Stadium</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">斗六棒球場</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Dòuliù Bàngqiú Chǎng</span></i>) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Baseball_stadium" title="Baseball stadium">baseball stadium</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Douliu,_Yunlin" title="Douliu, Yunlin">Douliu City</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yunlin_County" title="Yunlin County">Yunlin County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Taiwan" title="Taiwan">Taiwan</a>. It was opened in 2005 and has a capacity of 15,000 people.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
