<p><b>Radda in Chianti</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Siena" title="Province of Siena">Province of Siena</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Tuscany" title="Tuscany">Tuscany</a>, located about 35 kilometres (22 miles) southeast of <a href="/wiki/Florence" title="Florence">Florence</a> and about 15 km (9 miles) north of <a href="/wiki/Siena" title="Siena">Siena</a>.</p>
<p>Radda in Chianti borders the following municipalities: <a href="/wiki/Castellina_in_Chianti" title="Castellina in Chianti">Castellina in Chianti</a>, <a href="/wiki/Castelnuovo_Berardenga" title="Castelnuovo Berardenga">Castelnuovo Berardenga</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cavriglia" title="Cavriglia">Cavriglia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gaiole_in_Chianti" title="Gaiole in Chianti">Gaiole in Chianti</a>, <a href="/wiki/Greve_in_Chianti" title="Greve in Chianti">Greve in Chianti</a>.</p>
