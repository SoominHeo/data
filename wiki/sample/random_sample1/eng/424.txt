<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Semifinals">Semifinals</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Freestyle_skiing_at_the_2010_Winter_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_ski_cross&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Semifinals">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
