<p><b>L'Abergement-Clémenciat</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Communes_of_France" title="Communes of France">commune</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Departments_of_France" title="Departments of France">department</a> of <a href="/wiki/Ain" title="Ain">Ain</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Regions_of_France" title="Regions of France">region</a> of <a href="/wiki/Auvergne-Rh%C3%B4ne-Alpes" title="Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes">Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes</a> in eastern <a href="/wiki/Metropolitan_France" title="Metropolitan France">Metropolitan</a> <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=L%27Abergement-Cl%C3%A9menciat&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Chalaronne" title="Chalaronne">Chalaronne</a> flows west-northwest through the southern part of the commune.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Toponomy">Toponomy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=L%27Abergement-Cl%C3%A9menciat&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Toponomy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>There are 4 <a href="/wiki/Communes_of_France" title="Communes of France">communes</a> in <a href="/wiki/Ain" title="Ain">Ain</a> with the name of Jura on a river. The other three are <a href="/wiki/Le_Petit-Abergement" title="Le Petit-Abergement">Le Petit-Abergement</a>, <a href="/wiki/Le_Grand-Abergement" title="Le Grand-Abergement">Le Grand-Abergement</a>, and <a href="/wiki/L%27Abergement-de-Varey" title="L'Abergement-de-Varey">L'Abergement-de-Varey</a>. It seems to designate agricultural concessions in the 11th-15th centuries.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=L%27Abergement-Cl%C3%A9menciat&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It was once a large timber plantation.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Population">Population</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=L%27Abergement-Cl%C3%A9menciat&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Population">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2012, Abergement-Clémenciat had 777 inhabitants (an increase of 8% over 1999). The town occupied the 11,540th place nationally, while it was 11,370th in 1999, and the 197th at the departmental level of 419 municipalities.</p>
<p>Changes in the number of inhabitants is known throughout the population censuses conducted in Abergement and Clémenciat in 1793 and Abergement-Clémenciat since 1861. The maximum population was reached in 2006 with 811 inhabitants.</p>
