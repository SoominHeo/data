<p><b>Laohekou</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">老河口</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Lǎohékǒu</span></i>; literally: "old river mouth") is a <a href="/wiki/County-level_city" title="County-level city">county-level city</a> in northwestern <a href="/wiki/Hubei" title="Hubei">Hubei</a> province, People's Republic of China. It is located on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Han_River_(Hanshui)" title="Han River (Hanshui)">Han River (Hanshui)</a>, near the <a href="/wiki/Henan" title="Henan">Henan</a> border.</p>
<p>The entire county-level city has an area of 1,032 square kilometres (398 sq mi) and a population of 490,000 (2002). It falls under the jurisdiction of <a href="/wiki/Xiangyang" title="Xiangyang">Xiangyang</a> City. The area includes the city of Laohekou proper, which has an area of 27 square kilometres (10 sq mi).</p>
<p>Before the Communist Revolution, the city was the seat of the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Diocese_of_Laohekou" title="Roman Catholic Diocese of Laohekou">Roman Catholic Bishop of Laohekou</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Laohekou&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Laohekou has a <a href="/wiki/Monsoon" title="Monsoon">monsoon</a>-influenced, four season <a href="/wiki/Humid_subtropical_climate" title="Humid subtropical climate">humid subtropical climate</a> (<a href="/wiki/K%C3%B6ppen_climate_classification" title="Köppen climate classification">Köppen</a> <i>Cfa</i>), with cold, damp (but comparatively dry), winters, and hot, humid summers. The monthly 24-hour average temperature ranges from 2.6 °C (36.7 °F) in January to 27.3 °C (81.1 °F) in July, while the annual mean is 15.53 °C (60.0 °F). A majority of the annual precipitation of 835 mm (32.9 in) occurs from June to September. With monthly percent possible sunshine ranging from 33% in January to 46% in August, the city receives 1,762 hours of bright sunshine annually; January thru March is the cloudiest period of the year.</p>
