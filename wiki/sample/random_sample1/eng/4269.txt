<p><b>Raimond Aumann</b> (born 12 October 1963 in <a href="/wiki/Augsburg" title="Augsburg">Augsburg</a> (District Oberhausen), <a href="/wiki/West_Germany" title="West Germany">West Germany</a>) was a German <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> His nickname is <b>Balu</b> (Germanized version of <a href="/wiki/Baloo" title="Baloo">Baloo</a> the bear in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jungle_Book" title="Jungle Book">Jungle Book</a>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>He was a goalkeeper in the <a href="/wiki/Bundesliga" title="Bundesliga">Bundesliga</a> between 1982 and 1994.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The first two years he was only reserve keeper of <a href="/wiki/FC_Bayern_Munich" title="FC Bayern Munich">Bayern Munich</a> (first choice was <a href="/wiki/Jean-Marie_Pfaff" title="Jean-Marie Pfaff">Jean-Marie Pfaff</a>). In 1984 he became number 1 for the first time until his injury in November 1985. When Jean-Marie Pfaff left Bayern Munich in 1988, Aumann finally became number 1. He was considered one of the best German goalkeepers at that time.</p>
<p>With FC Bayern Munich he won six German championships and twice the German Cup before he transferred in 1994 to <a href="/wiki/Be%C5%9Fikta%C5%9F_J.K." title="Beşiktaş J.K.">Beşiktaş</a>, where he was to help them to a 1995 league victory. He retired from football in November 1995.</p>
<p>Aumann played four times internationally in 1989 and 1990.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> He was a member of Germany's squad for the <a href="/wiki/1990_FIFA_World_Cup" title="1990 FIFA World Cup">1990 World Cup</a>, but did not play in the tournament.</p>
<p>He is currently the supporters and fan club coordinator for <a href="/wiki/FC_Bayern_Munich" title="FC Bayern Munich">Bayern Munich</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
