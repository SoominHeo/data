<p><b><a href="/wiki/Swimming_(sport)" title="Swimming (sport)">Swimming</a></b> at the <b><a href="/wiki/2010_Asian_Games" title="2010 Asian Games">2010 Asian Games</a></b> was held at the Aoti Aquatics Centre<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> in <a href="/wiki/Guangzhou" title="Guangzhou">Guangzhou, China</a> from November 13 to 18, 2010. This Aquatics discipline had 38 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Long_course" title="Long course">long course</a> events: 19 for males and 19 for females.</p>
