<p>The <b>coat of arms of <a href="/wiki/Liberia" title="Liberia">Liberia</a></b> consists of a shield containing a picture of 19th century ship arriving in Liberia. The ship symbolizes the ships which brought the freed slaves from the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> to Liberia. Above the shield the national motto of Liberia appears on a scroll: <i>The love of liberty brought us here</i>, and below the shield another scroll contains the official name of the country, Republic of Liberia.</p>
<p>The plow and the shovel represent the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dignity_of_labor" title="Dignity of labor">dignity of labor</a> and hard work through which the nation will prosper. The rising sun in the background represents the birth of a nation. The palm tree, the nation's most versatile source of food, represents prosperity.</p>
<p>The white dove with a scroll represents the breath of peace.</p>
