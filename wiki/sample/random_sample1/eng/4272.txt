<p><b>LivePlanet</b> is a production company that invests in content for television and new media platforms. It was founded in 2000.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=LivePlanet&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The company was started by <a href="/wiki/Ben_Affleck" title="Ben Affleck">Ben Affleck</a>, <a href="/wiki/Matt_Damon" title="Matt Damon">Matt Damon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chris_Moore_(film_producer)" title="Chris Moore (film producer)">Chris Moore</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sean_Bailey_(producer)" title="Sean Bailey (producer)">Sean Bailey</a>. Affleck and Damon ended their film production deal on December 31, 2007 and Bailey disassociated in 2008 to form Idealogy Inc., his own film production company, at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Walt_Disney_Motion_Pictures_Group" title="Walt Disney Motion Pictures Group">Disney</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Chris_Moore_(film_producer)" title="Chris Moore (film producer)">Chris Moore</a> was CEO of LivePlanet from 2000 through 2002, followed by <a href="/wiki/Larry_Tanz" title="Larry Tanz">Larry Tanz</a>, who served as CEO and President from 2003 through 2007.</p>
<p>The company is currently helmed by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Keith_Quinn_(producer)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Keith Quinn (producer) (page does not exist)">Keith Quinn</a>.</p>
<p>The company is well known for television's <i><a href="/wiki/Project_Greenlight" title="Project Greenlight">Project Greenlight</a></i>, Affleck's directorial debut, <i><a href="/wiki/Gone_Baby_Gone" title="Gone Baby Gone">Gone Baby Gone</a></i>, and the documentary <i><a href="/wiki/Running_the_Sahara" title="Running the Sahara">Running the Sahara</a></i>.</p>
