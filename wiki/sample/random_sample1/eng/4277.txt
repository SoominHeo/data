<p><b>Ryan P. Tatusko</b> (born Match 27, 1985) is an American <a href="/wiki/Professional_baseball" title="Professional baseball">professional baseball</a> <a href="/wiki/Pitcher" title="Pitcher">pitcher</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Hanwha_Eagles" title="Hanwha Eagles">Hanwha Eagles</a> in <a href="/wiki/Korea" title="Korea">Korea</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Professional_career">Professional career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryan_Tatusko&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Professional career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Texas_Rangers">Texas Rangers</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryan_Tatusko&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Texas Rangers">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Tatusko was the <a href="/wiki/Texas_Rangers_(baseball)" title="Texas Rangers (baseball)">Texas Rangers</a> eighteen-round pick in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2007_Major_League_Baseball_Draft" title="2007 Major League Baseball Draft">2007 Major League Baseball Draft</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Washington_Nationals">Washington Nationals</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryan_Tatusko&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Washington Nationals">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Tatusko was traded from the Rangers along with <a href="/wiki/Tanner_Roark" title="Tanner Roark">Tanner Roark</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Washington_Nationals" title="Washington Nationals">Washington Nationals</a> for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Christian_Guzman" title="Christian Guzman">Christian Guzman</a> on July 30, 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hanwha_Eagles">Hanwha Eagles</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryan_Tatusko&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Hanwha Eagles">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Tatusko was sold to the <a href="/wiki/Hanwha_Eagles" title="Hanwha Eagles">Hanwha Eagles</a> in June 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
