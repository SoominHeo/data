<p><b>Athletes from <a href="/wiki/Kuwait" title="Kuwait">Kuwait</a></b> participated in the <b><a href="/wiki/2010_Asian_Games" title="2010 Asian Games">16th Asian Games</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Guangzhou" title="Guangzhou">Guangzhou</a>, <a href="/wiki/Guangdong" title="Guangdong">Guangdong</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/People%27s_Republic_of_China" title="People's Republic of China">China</a> from 12 November to 27 November 2010. These athletes competed under the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Olympic_flag" title="Olympic flag">Olympic flag</a> because the <a href="/wiki/Kuwait_Olympic_Committee" title="Kuwait Olympic Committee">Kuwait Olympic Committee</a> had been suspended by the <a href="/wiki/International_Olympic_Committee" title="International Olympic Committee">International Olympic Committee</a> in January 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> A total of 195 athletes competed, of which 19 were women.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-official_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-official-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
