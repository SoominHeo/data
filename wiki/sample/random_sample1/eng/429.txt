<p><b><a href="/wiki/Triathlon" title="Triathlon">Triathlon</a></b> at the <b><a href="/wiki/2010_Asian_Games" title="2010 Asian Games">2010 Asian Games</a></b> was held in <a href="/wiki/Guangzhou" title="Guangzhou">Guangzhou</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a> from November 13 to 14, 2010. Both men and women competed on the Guangzhou Triathlon Venue.</p>
<p>The triathlon contains three components; a 1.5 km (0.93 mi) swim, 40 km (25 mi) cycle, and a 10 km (6.2 mi) run.</p>
