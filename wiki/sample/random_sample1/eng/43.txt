<p><br/>
Year <b>1176</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MCLXXVI</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Thursday" title="Leap year starting on Thursday">leap year starting on Thursday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1176&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>January – The <a href="/wiki/Assize_of_Northampton" title="Assize of Northampton">Assize of Northampton</a> is enacted in <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_22" title="May 22">May 22</a> – The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hashshashin" title="Hashshashin">Hashshashin</a> attempt to murder <a href="/wiki/Saladin" title="Saladin">Saladin</a> near <a href="/wiki/Aleppo" title="Aleppo">Aleppo</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_29" title="May 29">May 29</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Legnano" title="Battle of Legnano">Battle of Legnano</a>: <a href="/wiki/Frederick_I,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Frederick I, Holy Roman Emperor">Frederick I, Holy Roman Emperor</a>, is defeated by the <a href="/wiki/Lombard_League" title="Lombard League">Lombard League</a>, leading to the <i>pactum Anagninum</i> (the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Agreement_of_Anagni" title="Agreement of Anagni">Agreement of Anagni</a>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_17" title="September 17">September 17</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Myriokephalon" title="Battle of Myriokephalon">Battle of Myriokephalon</a>: The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seljuq_Turks" title="Seljuq Turks">Seljuq Turks</a> defeat the Byzantine Emperor <a href="/wiki/Manuel_I_Komnenos" title="Manuel I Komnenos">Manuel I Komnenos</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Al-Adil_I" title="Al-Adil I">Al-Adil I</a>, the Muslim ruler of <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>, suppresses a revolt by the Christian <a href="/wiki/Copts" title="Copts">Copts</a> in the city of <a href="/wiki/Qift" title="Qift">Qift</a>, hanging nearly 3,000 of them on the trees around the city.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Raynald_of_Ch%C3%A2tillon" title="Raynald of Châtillon">Raynald of Châtillon</a> is ransomed from prison in <a href="/wiki/Aleppo" title="Aleppo">Aleppo</a>.</li>
<li>Construction begins on a stone-built <a href="/wiki/London_Bridge" title="London Bridge">London Bridge</a>.</li>
<li>The first recorded Welsh <a href="/wiki/Eisteddfod" title="Eisteddfod">Eisteddfod</a> is held by <a href="/wiki/Rhys_ap_Gruffydd" title="Rhys ap Gruffydd">Rhys ap Gruffydd</a> at <a href="/wiki/Cardigan,_Ceredigion" title="Cardigan, Ceredigion">Cardigan</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Unkei" title="Unkei">Unkei</a> completes his <a href="/wiki/Dainichi_Nyorai_(Enj%C5%8D-ji)" title="Dainichi Nyorai (Enjō-ji)">Dainichi Nyorai (Enjō-ji)</a> statue, now a <a href="/wiki/National_Treasure_(Japan)" title="National Treasure (Japan)">National Treasure of Japan</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sens_Cathedral" title="Sens Cathedral">Sens Cathedral</a> installs an <i>horologe</i>, presumed to be an early form of clock.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Carthusians" title="Carthusians">Carthusians</a> are approved as a religious order.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Births">Births</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1176&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Births">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
