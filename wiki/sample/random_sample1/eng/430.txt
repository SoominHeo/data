<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b><a href="/wiki/Handball" title="Handball">Handball</a></b> at the <b><a href="/wiki/2010_Asian_Games" title="2010 Asian Games">2010 Asian Games</a></b> was held in <a href="/wiki/Guangzhou" title="Guangzhou">Guangzhou</a>, <a href="/wiki/Guangdong" title="Guangdong">Guangdong</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a> from November 13 to 26, 2010.
