<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Union of Latin American Parties</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <b>Union of Latin American Parties</b> (<a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a>: <span lang="es" xml:lang="es"><i>Unión de Partidos Latinoamericanos</i></span>) is an alliance of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Centre-right" title="Centre-right">centre-right</a> <a href="/wiki/Political_party" title="Political party">political parties</a> in <a href="/wiki/Latin_America" title="Latin America">Latin America</a> and <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>. It is affiliated with the global <a href="/wiki/International_Democrat_Union" title="International Democrat Union">International Democrat Union</a>.</p>
<p>UPLA has twenty-two member parties (including observers and an associate) in eighteen of the region's twenty-one countries.</p>
