<p><b>Lapland</b> may refer to:</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Places">Places</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lapland&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Places">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/S%C3%A1pmi" title="Sápmi">Sápmi</a>, a cultural region stretching over northern Fennoscandia, including parts of Sweden, Finland, Norway, and Russia</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Lapland_(Finland)" title="Lapland (Finland)">Lapland (Finland)</a> (<i>Lappi</i>/<i>Lappland</i>), a Finnish region</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Lapland_(former_province_of_Finland)" title="Lapland (former province of Finland)">Lapland (former province of Finland)</a> (<i>Lappi</i>/<i>Lappland</i>), a province of Finland 1938–2009</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Lapland_(electoral_district)" title="Lapland (electoral district)">Lapland (electoral district)</a>, an electoral district in Finland</li>
<li>Norwegian Lapland, Northern Norway, <a href="/wiki/Finnmark" title="Finnmark">Finnmark</a>, formerly known as Danish Lapland (including Troms)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Lapland_(Sweden)" title="Lapland (Sweden)">Lapland (Sweden)</a>, a Swedish historical province.</li>
<li>Russian Lapland, a former name and a sporadic marketing term for <a href="/wiki/Murmansk_Oblast" title="Murmansk Oblast">Murmansk Oblast</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Laplandiya" title="Laplandiya">Laplandiya</a> (lit. "Lapland"), a rural locality (a railway station) in Murmansk Oblast, Russia.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Lapland,_Indiana" title="Lapland, Indiana">Lapland, Indiana</a>, a town in the United States.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Lapland,_Nova_Scotia" title="Lapland, Nova Scotia">Lapland, Nova Scotia</a>, a community in Lunenburg district, Nova Scotia.</li>
</ul>
