<p>The <b>Province of Lapland</b> (<a href="/wiki/Finnish_language" title="Finnish language">Finnish</a>: <span lang="fi" xml:lang="fi"><i>Lapin lääni</i></span>, <a href="/wiki/Swedish_language" title="Swedish language">Swedish</a>: <span lang="sv" xml:lang="sv"><i>Lapplands län</i></span>) was a <a href="/wiki/Provinces_of_Finland" title="Provinces of Finland">province</a> of <a href="/wiki/Finland" title="Finland">Finland</a> from 1938 to 2009.</p>
<p>It was established in 1938, when it was separated from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Province_of_Oulu" title="Province of Oulu">Province of Oulu</a>. After the Second World War, the <a href="/wiki/Pechengsky_District" title="Pechengsky District">Petsamo</a> municipality (former province) and part of the <a href="/wiki/Salla" title="Salla">Salla</a> municipality were ceded to the <a href="/wiki/Soviet_Union" title="Soviet Union">Soviet Union</a>.</p>
