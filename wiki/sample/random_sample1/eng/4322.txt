<p><i><b>3000 Whys of Blue Cat</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">蓝猫淘气3000问</span>) is the first large-scale <a href="/wiki/Chinese_animation" title="Chinese animation">Chinese animated series</a>-<a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">American</a>-<a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canadian</a> in <a href="/wiki/Mainland_China" title="Mainland China">mainland China</a> with an emphasis on <a href="/wiki/Science" title="Science">science</a>. The series is produced by "Beijing Sunchime Happy Culture Company". The show remains one of the longest running children's cartoon series in the world. It is most commonly referred to as "Lan Mao", or "Blue Cat". An English version was made for the first episode titled "The Adventures of the Blue Cat" and was made as a pilot for a series titled <i>Lucky's Adventures</i> but due to lack of interest, the series was cancelled.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> The show uses a combination of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2D_animation" title="2D animation">2D animation</a> and <a href="/wiki/Computer-generated_imagery" title="Computer-generated imagery">CGI</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=3000_Whys_of_Blue_Cat&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The series is mostly known for its successful line of companion <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Children%27s_book" title="Children's book">children's books</a>. As of October 2004, the series has spawned 36 series of children's books spanning 452 volumes in all. Rights for some of the books have been sold to <a href="/wiki/Taiwan" title="Taiwan">Taiwan</a>. Other intellectual properties rights managers from <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a> and <a href="/wiki/Thailand" title="Thailand">Thailand</a> have shown interests in the books. It was initially marketed to be the <a href="/wiki/Mickey_Mouse" title="Mickey Mouse">Mickey Mouse</a> of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Far_east" title="Far east">far east</a>, though the artistic style is more similar to <a href="/wiki/Tom_and_Jerry" title="Tom and Jerry">Tom and Jerry</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Story">Story</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=3000_Whys_of_Blue_Cat&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Story">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lan_Mao" title="Lan Mao">Blue Cat</a> is blue, because the color signifies dreams. Blue Cat is always curious and explores different parts of the world in any given time era with his imagination.</p>
