<p><b>Arthur Lounsbery</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">ランズベリー・アーサー</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Ranzuberī Āsā</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born September 12, California, U.S.)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/Voice_acting_in_Japan" title="Voice acting in Japan">Japanese voice actor</a> and narrator affiliated with <a href="/wiki/81_Produce" title="81 Produce">81 Produce</a>. His major roles include Ryugel Baran in <i><a href="/wiki/Inazuma_Eleven_GO_(manga)" title="Inazuma Eleven GO (manga)">Inazuma Eleven GO: Galaxy</a></i>, and Kaito Tsukigami in <i><a href="/wiki/Star-Myu" title="Star-Myu">Star-Myu</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Arthur_Lounsbery&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Lounsbery was born in California, but his family moved to <a href="/wiki/Kanagawa_Prefecture" title="Kanagawa Prefecture">Kanagawa Prefecture</a> when he was approximately 1 1/2 years old.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:5_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-:5-1">[1]</a></sup> He graduated from high school when he was supposed to be in his first year of high school. Hence, he was able to take the university examinations when he was supposed to be in his second year.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:5_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-:5-1">[1]</a></sup> He was recommended a voice acting job after graduating from high school by his co-worker from his part-time job. That co-worker of his was a freshly debut voice actor at that time.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ddnavi_interview_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-ddnavi_interview-2">[2]</a></sup> Lounsbery graduated from <i>Amusement Media Academy</i> in Tokyo, under Voice Actor Talent Department, in 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> His first work when he was still in the academy was <i><a href="/wiki/The_Accidental_Couple" title="The Accidental Couple">The Accidental Couple</a></i>, his first official debut after entering his agency <a href="/wiki/81_Produce" title="81 Produce">81 Produce</a> was <i><a href="/wiki/Sket_Dance" title="Sket Dance">Sket Dance</a>.</i> His first regular role was as Ryugel Baran in <i><a href="/wiki/Inazuma_Eleven_GO_(manga)" title="Inazuma Eleven GO (manga)">Inazuma Eleven GO: Galaxy</a>.</i> His lead and breakout role in <i><a href="/wiki/Star-Myu" title="Star-Myu">Star-Myu</a></i> as Kaito Tsukigami marks the first time he produces multiple character songs. He has been featured in popular Japanese voice actor magazines like <i>Voice Newtype</i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> <i>Prince Animage</i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> and <i>Seiyuu Junon</i>.</p>
<p>In addition to voice acting, Lounsbery is part of 81 Produce's live-action unit <i>soffive</i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:4_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-:4-6">[6]</a></sup> and Animate Channel's group <i>8P</i> (or <i>eight piece</i>). In April 2017, he will reprise his role as Kaito Tsukigami in <i>Musical Star-Myu,</i> which is the stage musical adaptation of the anime <i>Star-Myu</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-:0-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:3_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-:3-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
