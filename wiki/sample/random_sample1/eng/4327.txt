<p><b>Lantian County</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">蓝田县</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">藍田縣</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Lántián Xiàn</span></i>) is under the administration of <a href="/wiki/Xi%27an" title="Xi'an">Xi'an</a>, the capital of <a href="/wiki/Shaanxi" title="Shaanxi">Shaanxi</a> province, China. It is the easternmost and second-most spacious (after <a href="/wiki/Zhouzhi_County" title="Zhouzhi County">Zhouzhi County</a>) of the 13 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/County-level_division" title="County-level division">county-level divisions</a> of Xi'an. The county borders the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Prefecture-level_cities" title="Prefecture-level cities">prefecture-level cities</a> of <a href="/wiki/Weinan" title="Weinan">Weinan</a> to the northeast and <a href="/wiki/Shangluo" title="Shangluo">Shangluo</a> to the southeast, <a href="/wiki/Lintong_District" title="Lintong District">Lintong District</a> to the north, <a href="/wiki/Chang%27an_District,_Xi%27an" title="Chang'an District, Xi'an">Chang'an District</a> to the west, and <a href="/wiki/Baqiao_District" title="Baqiao District">Baqiao District</a> to the northwest.</p>
