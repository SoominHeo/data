<p><b>Liaodong Bay</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">辽东湾</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">遼東灣</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Liáodōng Wān</span></i>) is one of the three <a href="/wiki/Headlands_and_bays" title="Headlands and bays">bays</a> forming the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bohai_Gulf" title="Bohai Gulf">Bohai Gulf</a>, the innermost gulf of the <a href="/wiki/Yellow_Sea" title="Yellow Sea">Yellow Sea</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Northeast_China" title="Northeast China">northeast China</a>. It borders <a href="/wiki/Liaoning" title="Liaoning">Liaoning</a> province.</p>
<p>The three bays are <a href="/wiki/Laizhou_Bay" title="Laizhou Bay">Laizhou Bay</a> to the south, Liaodong Bay to the north, and <a href="/wiki/Bohai_Bay" title="Bohai Bay">Bohai Bay</a> to the west.</p>
