<p>The <b>2011 BWF World Championships</b> was the 19th tournament of the <a href="/wiki/BWF_World_Championships" title="BWF World Championships">BWF World Championships</a>, a global tournament in the sport of badminton. It was held at <a href="/wiki/Wembley_Arena" title="Wembley Arena">Wembley Arena</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>, from August 8 to August 14, 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>China clean swept all the titles for a record third time and became the first nation to successfully defend all the titles won from the previous edition. Scotland won their first medal from mixed pair and India won their medal in women's doubles and their seconds worlds medal after 28 years.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
