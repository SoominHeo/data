<p><i><b>Love Roma</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">ラブロマ</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Rabu Roma</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a Japanese <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Romantic_comedy" title="Romantic comedy">Romantic comedy</a></i> <a href="/wiki/Manga" title="Manga">manga</a> series written and illustrated by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Minoru_Toyoda&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Minoru Toyoda (page does not exist)">Minoru Toyoda</a>. It began serialization in 2003 <i>Afternoon</i> a Japanese manga magazine. The first bound volume was released in September 2003 in Japan; as of December 2005, the story concluded with five volumes released. The manga was licensed by <a href="/wiki/Del_Rey_Books" title="Del Rey Books">Del Rey</a>, with the first volume being released on August 30, 2005.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The story is about a boy, Hoshino, who to attract the girl he likes, Negishi, chooses to be completely honest.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Love_Roma&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the middle of class, in front of everybody, Hoshino approaches Negishi (who doesn't even know his name) with "Negishi, I like you. Please go out with me" and is obviously refused; she refuses to go on a date too, so they settle for walking home together, and the whole class starts laughing and clapping their hands (a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Leitmotiv" title="Leitmotiv">leitmotiv</a> in their story, marking every significant advancement in their relationship). The day after their walk, Negishi approaches Hoshino with "Hoshino, I like you. Please go out with me."</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Characters">Characters</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Love_Roma&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Characters">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<dl>
<dt>Hajime Hoshino <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">星野一</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Hoshino Hajime</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd>Undoubtedly an honest guy who always says what he thinks. He loved Negishi since the first moment he saw her, because she was saying and doing what he too had in his heart, but he didn't have the courage to act upon according to his feelings; from that moment on, he decided he'd always be honest.</dd>
</dl>
<dl>
<dt>Yumiko Negishi <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">根岸由美子</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Negishi Yumiko</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd>The girl Hoshino loves; she has more common sense than he does, and thus she often slaps or punches him out of embarrassment. She gets a little embarrassed when Hoshino's and her relationship is often put on display to the high school public. Negishi is a horrible cook who can barely make any edible food for anyone including Hoshino. She thinks that she is like the <i>tsukkomi</i> in a <a href="/wiki/Manzai" title="Manzai">Manzai</a>, where Hoshino is the <i>boke</i>; in a panel, though, Hoshino claims to be the <i>tsukkomi</i>.</dd>
</dl>
<dl>
<dt>Yoko Meguro <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">目黒陽子</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Meguro Yoko</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd>Negishi's best friend (Hoshino at first calls her "Negishi's friend" and never uses her name), intriguing and scheming; she often thinks of absurd plans for Hoshino, and is the primary source of comedy in the story. Although her pranks often make their relationship more difficult, she really roots for them, and accepts going on a double date with Tsukahara, leaving the couple alone as soon as they don't notice.</dd>
</dl>
<dl>
<dd>She is very often depicted drinking tomato (or other vegetables) juice with a straw, or sucking a <a href="/wiki/Lollipop" title="Lollipop">lollipop</a>. When the class claps their hands, she is often depicted waving her hand like a <a href="/wiki/Music_director" title="Music director">music director</a>.</dd>
</dl>
<dl>
<dt>Toshio Tsukahara <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">塚原敏男</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Tsukahara Toshio</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd>Hoshino's best friend, <i>cool</i> as the author tags him in the character box. Soon after Hoshino's proposal, he gives him a <a href="/wiki/Condom" title="Condom">condom</a>, but Hoshino doesn't understand he was teasing him and thanks him deeply. He represents the rational counterpart to Hoshino. Although he generally sees through Yoko's pranks he stays silent, preferring to be the onlooker.</dd>
</dl>
<dl>
<dt>Wada <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">和田さん</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd>A member of the Occult Research Club, often involved by Yoko in her plans, generally for fortune-telling, as her predictions are always wrong.</dd>
</dl>
<dl>
<dt>Yoshitsune <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">水元義経</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd>A member of Tsukahara's band and Hoshino's classmate; likes dirty talk, and his advice to Hoshino is generally sex-related.</dd>
</dl>
