<p>The <b>Rushmore Plaza Civic Center</b> is a 150,000-square-foot (14,000 m<sup>2</sup>) <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Exhibition_center" title="Exhibition center">exhibition center</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Rapid_City,_South_Dakota" title="Rapid City, South Dakota">Rapid City, South Dakota</a>. It contains a 10,000 seat, multi-purpose arena. It was built in 1977. The grand opening event was a concert by <a href="/wiki/Elvis_Presley" title="Elvis Presley">Elvis Presley</a> on June 21, 1977. That concert was filmed for a CBS television special that aired in September. The concert was during the singer's final tour before his death on August 16, 1977.</p>
<p>The Rushmore Plaza Civic Center is home to many large events including the Black Hills Stock Show and Rodeo,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Lakota Nations Invitational, and the Black Hills Homebuilders Expo. It also showcases many motorcycles during the <a href="/wiki/Sturgis_Motorcycle_Rally" title="Sturgis Motorcycle Rally">Sturgis Motorcycle Rally</a>. It was home to the <a href="/wiki/Rapid_City_Thrillers" title="Rapid City Thrillers">Rapid City Thrillers</a> <a href="/wiki/Basketball" title="Basketball">basketball</a> team. The Civic Center is the main event center for western <a href="/wiki/South_Dakota" title="South Dakota">South Dakota</a>.</p>
<p>It is currently managed by Brian Maliske and has plans for expansion for a new arena that will house a hockey team and music concerts as well, but those plans were voted down by the citizens of the city. The new arena was designed to double the capacity of the Civic Center, and was completed in 2008. It allows two simultaneous events to occur under one building, and it used to be largest venue center in <a href="/wiki/South_Dakota" title="South Dakota">South Dakota</a>, until it was supplanted by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Denny_Sanford_PREMIER_Center" title="Denny Sanford PREMIER Center">Denny Sanford PREMIER Center</a>. The new arena features <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Luxury_boxes" title="Luxury boxes">luxury boxes</a> for events and the Rapid City Rush home games. The new arena hosts the <a href="/wiki/Rapid_City_Rush" title="Rapid City Rush">Rapid City Rush</a> of the <a href="/wiki/ECHL" title="ECHL">ECHL</a>.</p>
