<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="List_of_missions">List of missions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Running_Man_missions_in_2010&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: List of missions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race_missions">Race missions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Running_Man_missions_in_2010&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Race missions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>These missions are the main feature of <i>Running Man</i>, which involves racing to complete a mission first or chasing each other to eliminate the other team in a landmark.</p>
<div class="NavFrame collapsed" style="border:none;">
<div class="NavHead" style="background:transparent;text-align:left;background:#F5ECCE;">List of race missions </div>
<div class="NavContent" style="background:transparent;text-align:left;">
<ul>
<li><i>Race mission</i> (레이스 미션) [Episode 1–6] - The main mission of <i>Running Man</i>, it involves teams racing to search for <a href="/wiki/Box" title="Box">capsules</a> with a secret code (episode 1), "Golden Pigs" with money (episode 2-5) or <i>Running Man Ball</i> (episode 6) throughout the show.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep1_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep1-2">[2]</a></sup>
<ul>
<li><i>Bells Hide and Seek</i> (방울 숨바꼭질) [Episode 7–25,76,84,85,104,115,169,171,172,208] - <i>Bells Hide and Seek</i> is a shortened version of the race mission. A "chasing" team wearing bells must catch the "mission" team by taking their <a href="/wiki/Name_tag" title="Name tag">name tags</a> from their backs in an allotted time. The "mission" team will need to find hidden items in the landmark and not be caught by the "chasing" team before the allotted time ends.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> It was called <i>Big Fight! Hide and Seek Race</i> (대격돌! 숨바꼭질 레이스) in episode 7 and <i>Alarms Hide and Seek</i> (알람 달고 숨바꼭질) in episode 8, where <a href="/wiki/Alarm_clock" title="Alarm clock">alarms</a> were used on the Mission Team instead of bells on the Chasing Team.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Staple_missions">Staple missions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Running_Man_missions_in_2010&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Staple missions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>These missions were a main part of the show and played in many episodes along with the <i>race mission</i>.</p>
<div class="NavFrame collapsed" style="border:none;">
<div class="NavHead" style="background:transparent;text-align:left;background:#F5DA81;">List of other missions </div>
<div class="NavContent" style="background:transparent;text-align:left;">
<ul>
<li><i>Photo Zone Game</i> (포토존 게임) [Episode 1,2,5,7,53,94,115,154,203] - Referred to as <i>Wedding Photo Zone</i> (웨딩 포토존) in episode 1 and <i>Photo Zone on Water</i> (수중 포토존) in episode 5, teams must appear in a designated area with their faces showing when a photo is taken.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep1_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep1-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Leisure Time for a Cup of Tea</i> (차 한잔의 여유) [Episode 6-10] - In an attempt to keep the cast awake during the late hours, teams will roll a <a href="/wiki/Dice" title="Dice">die</a> to determine the amount of ingredients in that episodes drink. Small games may be played to determine which team goes first. The team who finishes their drinks first will win the game.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tea_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-tea-6">[6]</a></sup>
<ul>
<li><i>Flip the Coin</i> (동전 뒤집기) [Episode 9,116] - A game played during <i>Leisure Time for a Cup of Tea</i>, members must flip a <a href="/wiki/Coin" title="Coin">coin</a> by hitting around it.</li>
<li><i>Drawing from Memory</i> (기억력 그림 그리기) [Episode 10] - A game played during <i>Leisure Time for a Cup of Tea</i>, members of both teams must choose a painting and create a drawing themselves from memory for another member of their team to search for.</li>
<li><i>Toss the Coin</i> (동전 던지기) [Episode 10] - A game played during <i>Leisure Time for a Cup of Tea</i>, members of each team must toss a coin into a bowl of water from a distance.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><i>Find the Thief</i> (도둑잡기) [Episode 10-14] - A game where cards are distributed to all members, one card says "Thief" on it and the rest say "Running Man". The object of the game is to avoid getting the "Thief" card. During each round, the person next will choose one of two cards from the previous person, and continuing the process with all players. Afterwards, games are played to determine who is first place and they will be exempt from the game and receive a "Running Ball". Their card is given to another player of their choice. After 2 rounds, the final round winner can mix up the seating arrangements of the players. At the end, the person with the "Thief" card loses and all remaining players receive a "Running Ball".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup>
<ul>
<li><i>Parcel Box Jenga</i> (소포상자 뽑기) [Episode 11] - A game played during <i>Find the Thief</i>, members play <a href="/wiki/Jenga" title="Jenga">Jenga</a> using big parcel boxes.</li>
<li><i>Word Chain</i> (끝말 잇기) [Episode 11,13] - A game played during <i>Find the Thief</i>, members must say words that start with the end of the previous word. It was called <i>Two Words Word Chain</i> (두자 끝말잇기) in episode 13.</li>
<li><i>Design Ladder</i> (디자인 사다리 타기) [Episode 12] - A game played during <i>Find the Thief</i>, members design ladders and choose which ladder to take. The winning ladder takes them to the <i>Running Ball</i>.</li>
<li><i>Quick Wit Game</i> (눈치 게임) [Episode 12,116] - A game played during <i>Find the Thief</i>, members must stand and accumulatively say numbers. The last to say the number or those who say it at the same time will lose.</li>
<li><i>Zero Game</i> (제로 게임) [Episode 13] - A game played during <i>Find the Thief</i>, a member calls a number from 0-7 and the remaining members may lift their microphone. If the number called matches with the corresponding number of microphones, he/she wins.</li>
<li><i>3.6.9 Bbi-Bbo</i> (3.6.9 삐뽀) [Episode 14] - A game played during <i>Find the Thief</i>, members calls numbers accumulatively, when a number contains 3, 6 or 9, he/she must call "bbi-bbo" corresponding to the number of 3, 6 or 9's.</li>
<li><i>I Came Alone</i> (혼자왔습니다) [Episode 14] - A game played during <i>Find the Thief</i>, one member calls numbers and the corresponding number of numbers must stand up and say "(Number of people) have come" or "I came alone" if it's just one person.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><i>Challenge 1 vs. X</i> (대결 1:X) (where X is the number of members on that episode) [Episode 11,12,14-25,28,34] - A mission where one person is against the rest of the cast. Either the one person or the remaining cast is trying to complete a mission without the other(s) knowing. In some episodes, all members know of the mission. If the one person/group succeeds, everyone but the person/group will receive a <i>Running Ball</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1vs_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-1vs-8">[8]</a></sup>
<ul>
<li><i>Raise Ji-hyo's heartbeat!</i> (지효의 심장을 뛰게하라!) [Episode 15] - Members must raise Song Ji-hyo's <a href="/wiki/Cardiac_cycle" title="Cardiac cycle">heartbeat</a> past 130 <a href="/wiki/Heart_rate" title="Heart rate">bpm</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Detective! Find the Culprit!</i> (명탐정! 범인을 찾아라) [Episode 18] - One member is designated as the <a href="/wiki/Culprit" title="Culprit">culprit</a> and needs to cause the team to lose, similar to <i><a href="/wiki/X-Man_(TV_series)" title="X-Man (TV series)">X-man</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep18_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep18-10">[10]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>General Knowledge Battle with Haha</i> (하하와의 지식배틀) [Episode 19] - Haha provides nine questions which he knows the answer to. The members must answer at least 5 questions correctly.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Make Jae-suk Lie</i> (재석이 거짓을 말하게 하라) [Episode 20] - Members must force Yoo Jae-suk, who is connected to a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lie_detector_test" title="Lie detector test">lie detector test</a>, to lie 3 times with a series of questions.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Copy Shim Hyung-rae!</i> (심형래 따라잡기!) [Episode 23] - Members must correctly act out Shim Hyung-rae's <a href="/wiki/Slapstick" title="Slapstick">slapstick</a> acts with him judging.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Other_missions">Other missions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Running_Man_missions_in_2010&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Other missions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>These missions are other missions used in landmarks that add variety to the show besides the <i>race missions</i> and <i>staple missions</i>. Many of these missions are related to the landmark and are only played once.</p>
<div class="NavFrame collapsed" style="border:none;">
<div class="NavHead" style="background:transparent;text-align:left;background:#F7D358;">List of other missions </div>
<div class="NavContent" style="background:transparent;text-align:left;">
<ul>
<li><i>Hanger Race</i> (행거 레이스) [Episode 1] - The teams wear clothing on <a href="/wiki/Clothes_hanger" title="Clothes hanger">hangers</a> hooked on to racks racing to a turning point and back.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep1_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep1-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Lips Muk-chi-ba</i> (입술 묵찌빠) [Episode 1] - Members face off in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Muk-chi-ba" title="Muk-chi-ba">muk-chi-ba</a> using their lips, wearing hats that keep their face in close proximity.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep1_2-3"><a href="#cite_note-ep1-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Chicken Fight vs. 50</i> (닭싸움 vs 50) [Episode 1] - The teams face off with a group of 50 citizens in "chicken fight".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep1_2-4"><a href="#cite_note-ep1-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Cheering Squad Speed Quiz</i> (응원단 스피드 퀴즈) [Episode 2] - A member of each team will need to describe/act out words/phrases to the other members of their team amidst a loud and disruptive cheering squad interfering.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep2_14-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep2-14">[14]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Cumulative Height Diving</i> (높이 합산 다이빙) [Episode 2-3] - Members choose the height they will <a href="/wiki/Diving" title="Diving">dive</a> from and will be cumulative of each other. The team with the most cumulative height will win.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep2_14-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep2-14">[14]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>SBS Telepathy Relay Center</i> (SBS 텔레파시 중계석) [Episode 3] - Two members of each team are wearing loud <a href="/wiki/Headphones" title="Headphones">headphones</a>. One member can see a list of questions and the other member can see a list of answers. They must match the correct question and answer without being able to hear or communicate with each other.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep3_15-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep3-15">[15]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Daerong Daerong Penalty Shootout</i> (대롱대롱 승부차기) [Episode 3] - Teams must shoot into the other teams <a href="/wiki/Goal_(sport)" title="Goal (sport)">goal</a> while the <a href="/wiki/Goalkeeper" title="Goalkeeper">goalkeeper</a> is hanging on to the top bar of the goal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep3_15-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep3-15">[15]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Human Rocket on Water</i> (수중 인간로켓) [Episode 4] - Each member must "shoot" a member over a horizontal bar at designated heights.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep4_16-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep4-16">[16]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Garbage Can Human Curling</i> (쓰레기통 인간 컬링) [Episode 4-5,109] - Members sit in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Garbage_cans" title="Garbage cans">garbage cans</a> and are pushed by their members to reach a certain point, like <a href="/wiki/Curling" title="Curling">curling</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep4_16-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep4-16">[16]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Escape the Full Bus</i> (만원버스를 탈출하라) [Episode 5] - A member of each team face off to escape from a <a href="/wiki/Bus" title="Bus">bus</a> filled with citizens first.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Find the Citizen Running Man</i> (시민 런닝맨을 찾아라) [Episode 6] - The teams must find the <i>Citizen Running Man</i> in the crowd.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep6_18-0"><a href="#cite_note-ep6-18">[18]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Metal Bucket Tower Race</i> (깡통타워 달리기) [Episode 6] - A member of each team face off racing through obstacles to a turning point and back while walking on metal buckets.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep6_18-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep6-18">[18]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>One Heart One Mind Badminton</i> (일심동체 배드민턴) [Episode 6] - Teams face off in <a href="/wiki/Badminton" title="Badminton">badminton</a> while being tied to each other.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep6_18-2"><a href="#cite_note-ep6-18">[18]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Gwanghwamun Love Song</i> (광화문 연가) [Episode 7] - As a warm-up mission, a member of each team must find a citizen to wear hats that are stuck together and have eye contact for 10 seconds.</li>
<li><i>Jongno Road 3 Set Competition</i> (종로거리 3종 경기) [Episode 8] - A series of 3 games relating to past times including breaking <a href="/wiki/Chopsticks" title="Chopsticks">chopsticks</a> with their hands, and <a href="/wiki/Tug_of_war" title="Tug of war">tug of war</a> with plastic clips clipped on to a part of their face.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tea_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-tea-6">[6]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Roller Coaster Duet Karaoke</i> (롤러코스터 듀엣 노래방) [Episode 9,111] - Two members of each team ride on a <a href="/wiki/Roller_coaster" title="Roller coaster">roller coaster</a> and need to sing a song.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Running Man Auction</i> (런닝맨 경매) [Episode 10, 166] - Members bid on items that can benefit them during that episode with punishments.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-20"><a href="#cite_note-20">[20]</a></sup> In episode 166, members bid for passbooks.</li>
<li><i>Eat Samples and Gain 1kg!</i> (시식, 1kg을 늘려라!) [Episode 16] - Three teams go around to eat samples and must gain exactly 1 kg together.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-21"><a href="#cite_note-21">[21]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Perform the Band Songs!</i> (밴드 연주곡을 맞혀라!) [Episode 17] - Three teams try to recruit real music students from a group of imposters to help them perform songs with one member trying to correctly guess them.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-22"><a href="#cite_note-22">[22]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Love's Cruise</i> (사랑의 유람선) [Episode 18] - Members must form two couples to receive <i>Running Balls</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-23"><a href="#cite_note-23">[23]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Running Man Korean Food Contest</i> (런닝맨 한식 경연대회) [Episode 19] - Three teams choose <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ingredients" title="Ingredients">ingredients</a> to create <a href="/wiki/Dish_(food)" title="Dish (food)">dishes</a> for a foreign <a href="/wiki/Adjudicator" title="Adjudicator">judge</a> panel.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-24"><a href="#cite_note-24">[24]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Running Man Ridiculous Forecast</i> (런닝맨 황당예보) [Episode 20] - Three teams race to get <a href="/wiki/Raincoat" title="Raincoat">raincoats</a> and must memorize a ridiculous <a href="/wiki/Weather_forecasting" title="Weather forecasting">weather forecast</a> and correctly present it in front of a <a href="/wiki/Chroma_key" title="Chroma key">bluescreen</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-25"><a href="#cite_note-25">[25]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Run Running Man!</i> (달려라 런닝맨!) [Episode 21] - Three teams must race to complete missions and ride on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/KTX" title="KTX">KTX</a> trains to the next station on time.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-26"><a href="#cite_note-26">[26]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Christmas Presents</i> (크리스마스 선물) [Episode 22] - Three teams must find the most popular <a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> presents of a certain topic.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-27"><a href="#cite_note-27">[27]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Lift Quiz</i> (리프트 퀴즈) [Episode 23] - Three teams must correctly answer words/phrases being described by one member on a <a href="/wiki/Ski_lift" title="Ski lift">ski lift</a> while running up a hill.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-28"><a href="#cite_note-28">[28]</a></sup></li>
</ul>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Fake_missions">Fake missions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Running_Man_missions_in_2010&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Fake missions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<li><i>Stamp Quickly</i> (우편 도장 빨리찍기) [Episode 11] - Members must quickly <a href="/wiki/Postmark" title="Postmark">stamp</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Envelopes" title="Envelopes">envelopes</a>. <li><i>Running Man Speed Quiz</i> (런닝맨 스피드 퀴즈) [Episode 16] - Members must answer 10 words/phrases in 100 seconds with one member describe/act out words/phrases to the other members. The members already know the words/phrases and must force a loss without <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kwon_Yuri" title="Kwon Yuri">Yuri</a> knowing.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-30"><a href="#cite_note-30">[30]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Spelling Test</i> (받아쓰기 시험) [Episode 17] - Members must correctly spell words dictated to them. The members believe they are trying to force <a href="/wiki/Jung_Yong-hwa" title="Jung Yong-hwa">Jung Yong-hwa</a> to be last, but the real mission is for Jung Yong-hwa to get help on 3 words from the members.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-31"><a href="#cite_note-31">[31]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Relay Song Singing</i> (릴레이 노래부르기) [Episode 18] - Members must sing songs related to a certain topic. The real mission was for the culprit (Song Ji-hyo) to pretend to lose.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ep18_10-1"><a href="#cite_note-ep18-10">[10]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Raise Kim Je-dong's Heartbeat</i> (김제동의 심박수를 높여라) [Episode 21] - Members must raise <a href="/wiki/Kim_Je-dong" title="Kim Je-dong">Kim Je-dong</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Cardiac_cycle" title="Cardiac cycle">heartbeat</a> past 130 <a href="/wiki/Heart_rate" title="Heart rate">bpm</a>. The real mission is for the members to make Kim Je-dong say the phrases assigned to them.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-32"><a href="#cite_note-32">[32]</a></sup></li>
<li><i>Kim Min-jong's Immortal Works</i> (김민종의 불후의 명작) [Episode 22] - Members must act out scenes of <a href="/wiki/Kim_Min-jong" title="Kim Min-jong">Kim Min-jong</a>'s <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Films" title="Films">films</a> with him judging. The real mission is for all the members to change their shirt from a red <a href="/wiki/Santa_Claus" title="Santa Claus">Santa Claus</a> to a green Santa Claus.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-33"><a href="#cite_note-33">[33]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
