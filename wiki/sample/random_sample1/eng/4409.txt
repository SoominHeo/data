<p>The second season of American <a href="/wiki/List_of_animated_television_series" title="List of animated television series">animated television series</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Regular_Show" title="Regular Show">Regular Show</a></i> originally aired on <a href="/wiki/Cartoon_Network" title="Cartoon Network">Cartoon Network</a> in the United States. The season was 28 episodes long, starting with "Ello Gov'nor", and ending with the season finale "Karaoke Video". The season was storyboarded and written by Sean Szeles, Shion Takeuchi, Benton Connor, Calvin Wong, Paul Scarlata, Kat Morris, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/J.G._Quintel" title="J.G. Quintel">J.G. Quintel</a>, Mike Roth, John Infantino, Minty Lewis, Henry Yu, and Dennis Messmer, while being produced by <a href="/wiki/Cartoon_Network_Studios" title="Cartoon Network Studios">Cartoon Network Studios</a>.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Production">Production</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Regular_Show_(season_2)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Production">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
