<p><b>Renate Krößner</b> (born 17 May 1945 in <a href="/wiki/Osterode_am_Harz" title="Osterode am Harz">Osterode am Harz</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lower_Saxony" title="Lower Saxony">Lower Saxony</a>) is a German actress. She has appeared in such films as <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Nordkurve&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Nordkurve (page does not exist)">Nordkurve</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Solo_Sunny" title="Solo Sunny">Solo Sunny</a></i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and <i><a href="/wiki/Alles_auf_Zucker!" title="Alles auf Zucker!">Alles auf Zucker!</a></i>. In television she has appeared on <i><a href="/wiki/Tatort" title="Tatort">Tatort</a></i>, <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Bruder_Esel&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Bruder Esel (page does not exist)">Bruder Esel</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Stubbe_-_Von_Fall_zu_Fall" title="Stubbe - Von Fall zu Fall">Stubbe - Von Fall zu Fall</a></i> and <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Einmal_Bulle,_immer_Bulle&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Einmal Bulle, immer Bulle (page does not exist)">Einmal Bulle, immer Bulle</a></i>.</p>
<p>For her role in <i>Solo Sunny</i>, she won the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Silver_Bear_for_Best_Actress" title="Silver Bear for Best Actress">Silver Bear for Best Actress</a> at the <a href="/wiki/30th_Berlin_International_Film_Festival" title="30th Berlin International Film Festival">30th Berlin International Film Festival</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Berlinale_1980_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Berlinale_1980-2">[2]</a></sup> In 1991, she was a member of the jury at the <a href="/wiki/41st_Berlin_International_Film_Festival" title="41st Berlin International Film Festival">41st Berlin International Film Festival</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Berlinale_1991_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Berlinale_1991-3">[3]</a></sup> In 1993, Krößner won a <a href="/wiki/Deutscher_Filmpreis" title="Deutscher Filmpreis">Deutscher Filmpreis</a> Best Actress award for her work in <i>Nordkurve</i>.</p>
