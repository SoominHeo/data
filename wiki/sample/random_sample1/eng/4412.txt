<p><b>René Orlando Houseman</b> (born 19 July 1953) is a retired <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentine</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a>, who played as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)_positions" title="Football (soccer) positions">right winger</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ren%C3%A9_Houseman&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Houseman played for <a href="/wiki/Defensores_de_Belgrano" title="Defensores de Belgrano">Defensores de Belgrano</a>, <a href="/wiki/Club_Atl%C3%A9tico_Hurac%C3%A1n" title="Club Atlético Huracán">Huracán</a>, <a href="/wiki/Club_Atl%C3%A9tico_River_Plate" title="Club Atlético River Plate">River Plate</a>, <a href="/wiki/Colo-Colo" title="Colo-Colo">Colo-Colo</a> (in Chile), <a href="/wiki/AmaZulu_F.C." title="AmaZulu F.C.">Amazulu</a> (in South Africa), <a href="/wiki/Club_Atl%C3%A9tico_Independiente" title="Club Atlético Independiente">Independiente</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Excursionistas&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Excursionistas (page does not exist)">Excursionistas</a>.</p>
<p>During his international career he also played for <a href="/wiki/Argentina_national_football_team" title="Argentina national football team">Argentina</a>, and participated at the <a href="/wiki/1974_FIFA_World_Cup" title="1974 FIFA World Cup">1974 FIFA World Cup</a>, where he scored three goals. His greatest achievement in football was being a member of the <a href="/wiki/1978_FIFA_World_Cup" title="1978 FIFA World Cup">1978 FIFA World Cup</a> winning team and scoring in the 6-0 win over Peru. His career was cut short due to problems with <a href="/wiki/Alcoholism" title="Alcoholism">alcoholism</a>, with which he has been struggling for most of his life.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
