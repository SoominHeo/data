<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Rémy Card</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<div id="contentSub"></div>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Rémy Card</b> is a French <a href="/wiki/Software_developer" title="Software developer">software developer</a> who is noted for his contributions to the <a href="/wiki/Linux_kernel" title="Linux kernel">Linux kernel</a>.</p>
<p>He is credited as one of the primary developers of the <a href="/wiki/Extended_file_system" title="Extended file system">Extended file system</a> (ext) and <a href="/wiki/Ext2" title="Ext2">Second Extended file system</a> (ext2) for Linux.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ibm_ext4_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-ibm_ext4-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
