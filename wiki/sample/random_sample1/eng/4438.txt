<p><b>Levan Shengelia</b> (<a href="/wiki/Georgian_language" title="Georgian language">Georgian</a>: <span lang="ka" xml:lang="ka">ლევან შენგელია</span>, born 27 October 1995) is a <a href="/wiki/Georgia_(country)" title="Georgia (country)">Georgian</a> professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> player, currently playing for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Daejeon_Citizen" title="Daejeon Citizen">Daejeon Citizen</a> in <a href="/wiki/K_League_Challenge" title="K League Challenge">K League Challenge</a>.</p>
