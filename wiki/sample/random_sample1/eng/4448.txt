<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Wrestlepalooza</h1>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>In <a href="/wiki/Professional_wrestling" title="Professional wrestling">professional wrestling</a>, <b>Wrestlepalooza</b> was an event held by the <a href="/wiki/Philadelphia" title="Philadelphia">Philadelphia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pennsylvania" title="Pennsylvania">Pennsylvania</a>, United States-based <a href="/wiki/Professional_wrestling_promotion" title="Professional wrestling promotion">professional wrestling promotion</a> <a href="/wiki/Extreme_Championship_Wrestling" title="Extreme Championship Wrestling">Extreme Championship Wrestling</a> annually in 1995, 1997, 1998 and 2000. The 1998 iteration aired on <a href="/wiki/Pay-per-view" title="Pay-per-view">pay-per-view</a>. The footage from the four Wrestlepalooza events is owned by <a href="/wiki/WWE" title="WWE">WWE</a>.</p>

<p><b>Wrestlepalooza (1995)</b> was a <a href="/wiki/House_show" title="House show">live event</a> that took place on August 5, 1995 in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ECW_Arena" title="ECW Arena">ECW Arena</a> in <a href="/wiki/Philadelphia" title="Philadelphia">Philadelphia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pennsylvania" title="Pennsylvania">Pennsylvania</a>, US. The commentator for the event was <a href="/wiki/Joey_Styles" title="Joey Styles">Joey Styles</a>. The event later aired in <a href="/wiki/Broadcast_syndication" title="Broadcast syndication">syndication</a> on <i><a href="/wiki/ECW_Hardcore_TV" title="ECW Hardcore TV">ECW Hardcore TV</a></i>.</p>

<p><b>Wrestlepalooza (1998)</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Pay-per-view" title="Pay-per-view">pay-per-view</a> event that took place on May 3, 1998 in the Cobb County Civic Center in <a href="/wiki/Marietta,_Georgia" title="Marietta, Georgia">Marietta, Georgia</a>, US. The commentator for the event was <a href="/wiki/Joey_Styles" title="Joey Styles">Joey Styles</a>.</p>
<p>The event features the final TV appearance of the <a href="/wiki/Junkyard_Dog" title="Junkyard Dog">Junkyard Dog</a>, who died one month later in a car accident. JYD was introduced, along with <a href="/wiki/Bob_Armstrong" title="Bob Armstrong">Bob Armstrong</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dick_Slater" title="Dick Slater">Dick Slater</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Masked_Superstar" title="The Masked Superstar">the Masked Superstar</a>, in tribute to their legacy as Southern wrestling stars.</p>
