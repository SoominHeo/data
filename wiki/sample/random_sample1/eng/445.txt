<p>The <b>2012–13 <a href="/wiki/ISU_Speed_Skating_World_Cup" title="ISU Speed Skating World Cup">ISU Speed Skating World Cup</a></b>, officially the <i><a href="/wiki/Essent" title="Essent">Essent</a> ISU World Cup Speed Skating 2012–2013</i>, was a series of international <a href="/wiki/Speed_skating" title="Speed skating">speed skating</a> competitions which ran the entire season. The season started on 16 November 2012 in <a href="/wiki/Heerenveen" title="Heerenveen">Heerenveen</a>, Netherlands, and ended with the final on 10 March 2013, also in Heerenveen. In total, nine competition weekends were held at eight different locations, twelve cups were contested (six for men, and six for women), and 82 races took place.</p>
<p>This season world cup points was awarded in the mass start event. Additionally, the team sprint was contested as a demonstration event at both Heerenveen competitions. The World Cup is organized by the <a href="/wiki/International_Skating_Union" title="International Skating Union">International Skating Union</a> (ISU).</p>
