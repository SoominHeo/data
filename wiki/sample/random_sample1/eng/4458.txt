<p><b>Leonor Andrade</b> (born 13 September 1994)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is a <a href="/wiki/Portuguese_people" title="Portuguese people">Portuguese</a> singer and actress. She <a href="/wiki/Portugal_in_the_Eurovision_Song_Contest_2015" title="Portugal in the Eurovision Song Contest 2015">represented Portugal</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Eurovision_Song_Contest_2015" title="Eurovision Song Contest 2015">Eurovision Song Contest 2015</a> with the song "<a href="/wiki/H%C3%A1_um_mar_que_nos_separa" title="Há um mar que nos separa">Há um mar que nos separa</a>".</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Leonor_Andrade&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2014, Andrade participated in season two of <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/A_Voz_de_Portugal" title="A Voz de Portugal">The Voice Portugal</a></i>. She was eliminated on episode 13.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-The_Voice_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-The_Voice-2">[2]</a></sup> After <i>The Voice</i>, Andrade joined the cast of <a href="/wiki/RTP1" title="RTP1">RTP1</a> <a href="/wiki/Telenovela" title="Telenovela">telenovela</a> <i><a href="/wiki/%C3%81gua_de_Mar" title="Água de Mar">Água de Mar</a></i>, where she played Joana Luz.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-.C3.81gua_de_Mar_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-.C3.81gua_de_Mar-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 19 February 2015, Andrade was announced as one of the twelve participants of <a href="/wiki/Festival_da_Can%C3%A7%C3%A3o" title="Festival da Canção">Festival da Canção 2015</a> with the song "<a href="/wiki/H%C3%A1_um_mar_que_nos_separa" title="Há um mar que nos separa">Há um mar que nos separa</a>".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> On 7 March 2015, she won the competition and thus was selected to <a href="/wiki/Portugal_in_the_Eurovision_Song_Contest_2015" title="Portugal in the Eurovision Song Contest 2015">represent Portugal</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Eurovision_Song_Contest_2015" title="Eurovision Song Contest 2015">Eurovision Song Contest 2015</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
