<p><b>Leonid Arnoldovich Fedun</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Леонид Арнольдович Федун</span>; born 5 April 1956) is a Russian <a href="/wiki/Billionaire" title="Billionaire">billionaire</a> businessman. His estimated wealth as of 2016 is $4.8 billion. Fedun, a former Russian military officer, is a close associate of <a href="/wiki/Vagit_Alekperov" title="Vagit Alekperov">Vagit Alekperov</a>, the CEO of Russian oil company <a href="/wiki/Lukoil" title="Lukoil">Lukoil</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-forbes.ru.2010_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-forbes.ru.2010-2">[2]</a></sup> Fedun owns 9.2% of Lukoil shares<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> and the Russian football club <a href="/wiki/FC_Spartak_Moscow" title="FC Spartak Moscow">Spartak Moscow</a>. He was awarded of the Order For Merit to the Fatherland, 4th Order and the Order of Honour.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Leonid_Fedun&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In November 1992, he went into business and founded the company Neftkonsult LLP. In 1993, Fedun was officially Dismissed from the Russian military and within the same year became the CEO of <a href="/wiki/Vostok_Aviation_Company" title="Vostok Aviation Company">JSC</a>. In 1996, Fedun became the Vice President of LUKoil Inc. Fedun met Alekperov in the 1980s while he was a teacher in <a href="/wiki/Kogalym" title="Kogalym">Kogalym</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Siberia" title="Siberia">Siberian</a> city.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> In May 2006 Fedun was one of the two main owners of <a href="/wiki/IFD_Kapital_Group" title="IFD Kapital Group">IFD Kapital Group</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
