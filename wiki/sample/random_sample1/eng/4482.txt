<p>The <a href="/wiki/Districts_of_Portugal" title="Districts of Portugal">District</a> of <b>Leiria</b> (<small>Portuguese pronunciation: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Portuguese" title="Help:IPA for Portuguese">[lɐjˈɾi.ɐ]</a></span>, <a href="/wiki/Portuguese_language" title="Portuguese language">Portuguese</a>: <span lang="pt" xml:lang="pt"><i>Distrito de Leiria</i></span>) is located in Central <a href="/wiki/Portugal" title="Portugal">Portugal</a>. The District Capital is the city of <a href="/wiki/Leiria" title="Leiria">Leiria</a> which is also the largest city and most populous city. The district is composed by 16 municipalities: [C = city/<i>cidade</i> T = town/<i>vila</i>]</p>
