<p><b>Lake View Cemetery</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Cemetery" title="Cemetery">cemetery</a> located on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seattle,_Washington" title="Seattle, Washington">Seattle</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Washington_(U.S._state)" title="Washington (U.S. state)">Washington</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Capitol_Hill,_Seattle,_Washington" title="Capitol Hill, Seattle, Washington">Capitol Hill</a> just north of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Volunteer_Park,_Seattle" title="Volunteer Park, Seattle">Volunteer Park</a>. It is named for its view of <a href="/wiki/Lake_Washington" title="Lake Washington">Lake Washington</a> to the east. It was founded in 1872 as the Seattle <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Masonic" title="Masonic">Masonic</a> Cemetery.</p>
