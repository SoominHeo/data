<p><b>Lezhë County</b> (<a href="/wiki/Albanian_language" title="Albanian language">Albanian</a>: <span lang="sq" xml:lang="sq"><i>Qarku i Lezhës</i></span>) is one of the 12 <a href="/wiki/Counties_of_Albania" title="Counties of Albania">counties</a> of <a href="/wiki/Albania" title="Albania">Albania</a>. The population at the 2011 census was 134,027, in an area of 1620 km².<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-census11_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-census11-1">[1]</a></sup> Its capital is the city <a href="/wiki/Lezh%C3%AB" title="Lezhë">Lezhë</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Administrative_divisions">Administrative divisions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lezh%C3%AB_County&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Administrative divisions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lezh%C3%AB_County&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>According to the last national census from 2011 this county has 134,027 inhabitants. Ethnic groups in the county include:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-census11_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-census11-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Albanians" title="Albanians">Albanians</a> = 116,469 (86.90%)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Greeks" title="Greeks">Greeks</a> = 41 (0.03%)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Macedonians_(ethnic_group)" title="Macedonians (ethnic group)">Macedonians</a> = 9 (0.01%)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Montenegrins" title="Montenegrins">Montenegrins</a> = 8 (0.01%)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Aromanians" title="Aromanians">Aromanians</a> = 15 (0.01%)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Romani_people" title="Romani people">Romani</a> = 187 (0,14%)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Balkan_Egyptians" title="Balkan Egyptians">Egyptians</a> = 240 (0,18%)</li>
<li>others = 18 (0.01%)</li>
<li>no answer = 17,040 (12.71%)</li>
</ul>
