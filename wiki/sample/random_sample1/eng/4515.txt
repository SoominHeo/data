<p><i><b>Wreckers</b></i> is a 2011 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Drama_film" title="Drama film">drama film</a> written and directed by D. R. Hood. The film's cast includes <a href="/wiki/Claire_Foy" title="Claire Foy">Claire Foy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Benedict_Cumberbatch" title="Benedict Cumberbatch">Benedict Cumberbatch</a> with <a href="/wiki/Shaun_Evans" title="Shaun Evans">Shaun Evans</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peter_McDonald_(actor)" title="Peter McDonald (actor)">Peter McDonald</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sinead_Matthews" title="Sinead Matthews">Sinead Matthews</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
