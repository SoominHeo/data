<p><b>Lena Söderberg</b> (born 31 March 1951) appeared as a <a href="/wiki/Playboy_Playmate" title="Playboy Playmate">Playmate</a> in the November 1972 issue of <i><a href="/wiki/Playboy" title="Playboy">Playboy</a></i> magazine,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> under the name <b>Lenna Sjööblom</b>. Her <a href="/wiki/Centerfold" title="Centerfold">centerfold</a> was photographed by <a href="/wiki/Dwight_Hooker" title="Dwight Hooker">Dwight Hooker</a>.</p>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Woody_Allen" title="Woody Allen">Woody Allen</a>'s 1973 comedy <i><a href="/wiki/Sleeper_(1973_film)" title="Sleeper (1973 film)">Sleeper</a></i>, Woody's character Miles Monroe, after being <a href="/wiki/Cryonics" title="Cryonics">cryogenically frozen</a> for 200 years and revived, is asked to look at a series of 20th century artifacts. One of them is Söderberg's centerfold; Miles decides to keep it, to "study it and give you a full report."</p>
<p>A section of her centerfold (known as <a href="/wiki/Lenna" title="Lenna">Lenna</a>) is often used to test algorithms in <a href="/wiki/Digital_image_processing" title="Digital image processing">digital image processing</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-JH_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-JH-2">[2]</a></sup> She was a guest at the 50th annual Conference of the <a href="/wiki/Society_for_Imaging_Science_and_Technology" title="Society for Imaging Science and Technology">Society for Imaging Science and Technology</a> in 1997 where she gave a presentation about herself.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Because of the ubiquity of her Playboy photo scan, she has been called the "first lady of the internet".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Cs.cmu.edu_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Cs.cmu.edu-5">[5]</a></sup> The title was given to her by Jeff Seideman in a press release he issued announcing her appearance at the 50th annual IS&amp;T Conference.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (February 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
