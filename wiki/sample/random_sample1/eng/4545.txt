<p>The <b>Metropolitan Borough of Rotherham</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Metropolitan_borough" title="Metropolitan borough">metropolitan borough</a> of <a href="/wiki/South_Yorkshire" title="South Yorkshire">South Yorkshire</a>, England. It is named after its largest town, <a href="/wiki/Rotherham" title="Rotherham">Rotherham</a>, but also spans the outlying towns of <a href="/wiki/Maltby,_South_Yorkshire" title="Maltby, South Yorkshire">Maltby</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rawmarsh" title="Rawmarsh">Rawmarsh</a>, <a href="/wiki/Swinton,_South_Yorkshire" title="Swinton, South Yorkshire">Swinton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wath-upon-Dearne" title="Wath-upon-Dearne">Wath-upon-Dearne</a>, and also <a href="/wiki/Dinnington,_South_Yorkshire" title="Dinnington, South Yorkshire">Dinnington</a> and <a href="/wiki/Laughton-en-le-Morthen" title="Laughton-en-le-Morthen">Laughton</a> as well as a suburban and rural element composed of hills, escarpments and broad valleys.</p>
<p>The district was formed on 1 April 1974, under the <a href="/wiki/Local_Government_Act_1972" title="Local Government Act 1972">Local Government Act 1972</a>, as a merger of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/County_Borough_of_Rotherham" title="County Borough of Rotherham">County Borough of Rotherham</a>, with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Maltby_Urban_District" title="Maltby Urban District">Maltby</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rawmarsh_Urban_District" title="Rawmarsh Urban District">Rawmarsh</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Swinton_Urban_District" title="Swinton Urban District">Swinton</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wath_upon_Dearne_Urban_District" title="Wath upon Dearne Urban District">Wath-upon-Dearne</a> <a href="/wiki/Urban_district_(Great_Britain_and_Ireland)" title="Urban district (Great Britain and Ireland)">urban districts</a> along with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rotherham_Rural_District" title="Rotherham Rural District">Rotherham Rural District</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kiveton_Park_Rural_District" title="Kiveton Park Rural District">Kiveton Park Rural District</a>.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Rotherham_Metropolitan_Borough_Council" title="Rotherham Metropolitan Borough Council">Rotherham Metropolitan Borough Council</a> is one of the safest <a href="/wiki/Labour_Party_(UK)" title="Labour Party (UK)">Labour</a> councils in the United Kingdom, although the number of Labour council seats dropped from 92% to 79% in 2014 following the <a href="/wiki/Rotherham_child_sexual_exploitation_scandal" title="Rotherham child sexual exploitation scandal">Rotherham child sexual exploitation scandal</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-r2008_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-r2008-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
