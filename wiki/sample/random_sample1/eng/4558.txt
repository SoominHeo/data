<p><b>Laura Fraser</b> (born 24 July 1976) is a Scottish actress. She is best known for portraying Door in the <a href="/wiki/Urban_fantasy" title="Urban fantasy">urban fantasy</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Neverwhere" title="Neverwhere">Neverwhere</a></i>, Kate in the film <i><a href="/wiki/A_Knight%27s_Tale" title="A Knight's Tale">A Knight's Tale</a></i>, Cat MacKenzie in the <a href="/wiki/Drama" title="Drama">drama</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Lip_Service_(TV_series)" title="Lip Service (TV series)">Lip Service</a></i> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lydia_Rodarte-Quayle" title="Lydia Rodarte-Quayle">Lydia Rodarte-Quayle</a> in the <a href="/wiki/AMC_(TV_channel)" title="AMC (TV channel)">AMC</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Crime_drama" title="Crime drama">crime drama</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Breaking_Bad" title="Breaking Bad">Breaking Bad</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Laura_Fraser&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Fraser was born in <a href="/wiki/Glasgow" title="Glasgow">Glasgow</a>, the daughter of Rose, a college <a href="/wiki/Lecturer" title="Lecturer">lecturer</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nurse" title="Nurse">nurse</a>, and Alister Fraser, a <a href="/wiki/Screenwriter" title="Screenwriter">screenwriter</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Businessman" title="Businessman">businessman</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> She attended <a href="/wiki/Hillhead_High_School" title="Hillhead High School">Hillhead High School</a> and is a former member of the <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Youth_Theatre" title="Scottish Youth Theatre">Scottish Youth Theatre</a>. She trained at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Royal_Scottish_Academy_of_Music_and_Drama" title="Royal Scottish Academy of Music and Drama">Royal Scottish Academy of Music and Drama</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Laura_Fraser&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Fraser's first big break was playing Door in the <a href="/wiki/BBC" title="BBC">BBC</a>'s dark fantasy series <i><a href="/wiki/Neverwhere" title="Neverwhere">Neverwhere</a></i> in 1996. She starred in the BBC film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Tribe_(1998_film)" title="The Tribe (1998 film)">The Tribe</a></i> (1998), and played Lavinia in <i><a href="/wiki/Titus_(film)" title="Titus (film)">Titus</a></i> (1999) as well as Justine in 'Virtual Sexuality' (1999) and Candice in <a href="/wiki/Kevin_%26_Perry_Go_Large" title="Kevin &amp; Perry Go Large">Kevin &amp; Perry Go Large</a> (2000). She also appeared in the films <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/A_Knight%27s_Tale_(film)" title="A Knight's Tale (film)">A Knight's Tale</a></i> (2001) and <i><a href="/wiki/Vanilla_Sky" title="Vanilla Sky">Vanilla Sky</a></i> (2001). She starred in the BBC <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Period_drama" title="Period drama">period drama</a> serials <i><a href="/wiki/Anthony_Trollope#Trollope.27s_works_on_television" title="Anthony Trollope">He Knew He Was Right</a></i> (2004), <i><a href="/wiki/Casanova_(2005_TV_serial)" title="Casanova (2005 TV serial)">Casanova</a></i> (2005), and <i><a href="/wiki/Reichenbach_Falls_(film)" title="Reichenbach Falls (film)">Reichenbach Falls</a></i> (2007). She starred in the British comedy film <i><a href="/wiki/Nina%27s_Heavenly_Delights" title="Nina's Heavenly Delights">Nina's Heavenly Delights</a></i> (2006). She played Claire Bellington in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ITV_Network" title="ITV Network">ITV</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Talk_to_Me_(TV_series)" title="Talk to Me (TV series)">Talk to Me</a></i> (2007).</p>
<p>Fraser starred as the <a href="/wiki/Florence_Nightingale" title="Florence Nightingale">title character</a> in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Florence_Nightingale_(television_drama)" title="Florence Nightingale (television drama)">Florence Nightingale</a></i>, broadcast on <a href="/wiki/BBC_One" title="BBC One">BBC One</a> in June 2008. From 2010 to 2012, she starred as Cat in the <a href="/wiki/BBC_Three" title="BBC Three">BBC Three</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Lip_Service_(TV_series)" title="Lip Service (TV series)">Lip Service</a></i>. In 2010, it was announced that she would portray <a href="/wiki/List_of_Homeland_characters#Jessica_Brody" title="List of Homeland characters">Jessica Brody</a> in the pilot for the <a href="/wiki/Showtime_(TV_network)" title="Showtime (TV network)">Showtime</a> <a href="/wiki/Political_thriller" title="Political thriller">political thriller</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Homeland_(TV_series)" title="Homeland (TV series)">Homeland</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> She was replaced on the show by <a href="/wiki/Morena_Baccarin" title="Morena Baccarin">Morena Baccarin</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> From 2012 to 2013, she portrayed <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lydia_Rodarte-Quayle" title="Lydia Rodarte-Quayle">Lydia Rodarte-Quayle</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Breaking_Bad_(season_5)" title="Breaking Bad (season 5)">final season</a> of the <a href="/wiki/AMC_(TV_channel)" title="AMC (TV channel)">AMC</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Crime_drama" title="Crime drama">crime drama</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Breaking_Bad" title="Breaking Bad">Breaking Bad</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Laura_Fraser&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Fraser is a <a href="/wiki/Teetotalism" title="Teetotalism">teetotaler</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> She married actor <a href="/wiki/Karl_Geary" title="Karl Geary">Karl Geary</a> in 2003. They have one daughter, Lila, one son Jem and live in Glasgow, Scotland. <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
