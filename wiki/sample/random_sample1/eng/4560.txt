<p><b>Laurent Franco Ciman</b> (born 5 August 1985) is a Belgian professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">defender</a> for <a href="/wiki/Major_League_Soccer" title="Major League Soccer">Major League Soccer</a> club <a href="/wiki/Montreal_Impact" title="Montreal Impact">Montreal Impact</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Belgium_national_football_team" title="Belgium national football team">Belgium national football team</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Laurent_Ciman&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ciman began his career in the youth system of <a href="/wiki/R._Charleroi_S.C." title="R. Charleroi S.C.">Charleroi</a> and made his first team debut in 2004. His play with Charleroi attracted the interest of the top Belgian clubs. Ciman was transferred from <a href="/wiki/R._Charleroi_S.C." title="R. Charleroi S.C.">Charleroi</a> to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Club_Brugge_K.V." title="Club Brugge K.V.">Club Brugge</a> in June 2008 and signed a three-year contract for the Belgian team, he was loaned to <a href="/wiki/K.V._Kortrijk" title="K.V. Kortrijk">Kortrijk</a> for the 2009–10 season. On 16 June 2010 he joined <a href="/wiki/Standard_Li%C3%A8ge" title="Standard Liège">Standard Liège</a> on a four-year contract.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Upon joining Standard Ciman established him as a starter and helped the club capture the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2010-11_Belgian_Cup" title="2010-11 Belgian Cup">2010-11 Belgian Cup</a>. He made a total of 194 appearances for the club, scoring eight goals, before leaving the club in the winter of 2015.</p>
<p>Ciman signed a three-year deal with <a href="/wiki/Major_League_Soccer" title="Major League Soccer">Major League Soccer</a> club <a href="/wiki/Montreal_Impact" title="Montreal Impact">Montreal Impact</a> on 22 January 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> In his first season with the team, he was named <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/MLS_Defender_of_the_Year" title="MLS Defender of the Year">MLS Defender of the Year</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> In July 2016, he was included in the roster for the <a href="/wiki/2016_MLS_All-Star_Game" title="2016 MLS All-Star Game">2016 MLS All-Star Game</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Laurent_Ciman&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ciman was a member of the Belgian squad which came fourth at the <a href="/wiki/Football_at_the_2008_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_tournament" title="Football at the 2008 Summer Olympics – Men's tournament">2008 Olympics</a>.</p>
<p>Ciman also played for the <a href="/wiki/Belgium_national_under-21_football_team" title="Belgium national under-21 football team">National U21</a> side and was called up by national coach <a href="/wiki/Georges_Leekens" title="Georges Leekens">Georges Leekens</a> for the friendly match against <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria_national_football_team" title="Bulgaria national football team">Bulgaria</a> on 19 May 2010, during which he made his debut.</p>
<p>On 13 May 2014, Ciman was named in Belgium's squad for the <a href="/wiki/2014_FIFA_World_Cup" title="2014 FIFA World Cup">2014 FIFA World Cup</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> He was unused in the tournament, in which Belgium reached the quarter-finals. In the next major tournament, <a href="/wiki/UEFA_Euro_2016" title="UEFA Euro 2016">UEFA Euro 2016</a> he was also part of the squad. This time, he played in the opening game of the competition, a 2–0 defeat to <a href="/wiki/Italy_national_football_team" title="Italy national football team">Italy</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
