<p><b>Lauren Anne Landa</b> is an American <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Voice_actress" title="Voice actress">voice actress</a> who has provided voices for <a href="/wiki/Anime" title="Anime">anime</a> and video games.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Her most known role is <a href="/wiki/List_of_BlazBlue_characters#Litchi_Faye-Ling" title="List of BlazBlue characters">Litchi Faye-Ling</a> in the <i><a href="/wiki/BlazBlue" title="BlazBlue">BlazBlue</a></i> fighting video game series. She also voiced Arf and Chrono in the <i>Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha</i> series; <a href="/wiki/List_of_The_Familiar_of_Zero_characters#Kirche" title="List of The Familiar of Zero characters">Kirche</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Familiar_of_Zero" title="The Familiar of Zero">The Familiar of Zero</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-anime_five_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-anime_five-4">[4]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Kasumi_(Dead_or_Alive)" title="Kasumi (Dead or Alive)">Kasumi</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Dead_or_Alive_5" title="Dead or Alive 5">Dead or Alive 5</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/List_of_Puella_Magi_Madoka_Magica_characters#Kyoko_Sakura" title="List of Puella Magi Madoka Magica characters">Kyoko Sakura</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Puella_Magi_Madoka_Magica" title="Puella Magi Madoka Magica">Puella Magi Madoka Magica</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/List_of_Attack_on_Titan_characters#Annie_Leonhart" title="List of Attack on Titan characters">Annie Leonhart</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Attack_on_Titan" title="Attack on Titan">Attack on Titan</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-magic_city_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-magic_city-1">[1]</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Sailor_Neptune" title="Sailor Neptune">Michiru Kaioh/Sailor Neptune</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Viz_Media" title="Viz Media">Viz Media</a> dub of <i><a href="/wiki/Sailor_Moon" title="Sailor Moon">Sailor Moon</a></i>.</p>
