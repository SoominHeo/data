<p><b>Rory Cochrane</b> (born February 28, 1972) is an American <a href="/wiki/Actor" title="Actor">actor</a>. He is known for playing Ron Slater in <i><a href="/wiki/Dazed_and_Confused_(film)" title="Dazed and Confused (film)">Dazed and Confused</a></i>, Lucas in <i><a href="/wiki/Empire_Records" title="Empire Records">Empire Records</a></i>, Lee Schatz in <i><a href="/wiki/Argo_(2012_film)" title="Argo (2012 film)">Argo</a></i>, Freck in <i><a href="/wiki/A_Scanner_Darkly_(film)" title="A Scanner Darkly (film)">A Scanner Darkly</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/Tim_Speedle" title="Tim Speedle">Tim Speedle</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/CSI:_Miami" title="CSI: Miami">CSI: Miami</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rory_Cochrane&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Cochrane's first roles included a part in a docudrama about drugs on <i>Saturday Night with <a href="/wiki/Connie_Chung" title="Connie Chung">Connie Chung</a></i> (1989) and an appearance in an episode of <i><a href="/wiki/H.E.L.P." title="H.E.L.P.">H.E.L.P.</a></i> (1990). He then made his film debut (with about fifteen seconds' screen time) in <i><a href="/wiki/A_Kiss_Before_Dying_(1991_film)" title="A Kiss Before Dying (1991 film)">A Kiss Before Dying</a></i>, followed by his first substantial role as <a href="/wiki/Jeff_Goldblum" title="Jeff Goldblum">Jeff Goldblum</a>'s son in <i><a href="/wiki/Fathers_%26_Sons_(film)" title="Fathers &amp; Sons (film)">Fathers &amp; Sons</a></i>.</p>
<p>Cochrane played major <a href="/wiki/Role#Role_in_Theatre_and_the_Dramatic_Arts" title="Role">roles</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Film" title="Film">films</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Empire_Records" title="Empire Records">Empire Records</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Dazed_and_Confused_(film)" title="Dazed and Confused (film)">Dazed and Confused</a></i>, with <a href="/wiki/Jason_London" title="Jason London">Jason London</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ben_Affleck" title="Ben Affleck">Ben Affleck</a>, and appeared in <i><a href="/wiki/Hart%27s_War" title="Hart's War">Hart's War</a></i> starring <a href="/wiki/Bruce_Willis" title="Bruce Willis">Bruce Willis</a> and <a href="/wiki/Colin_Farrell" title="Colin Farrell">Colin Farrell</a>. He also appeared in the <a href="/wiki/Richard_Linklater" title="Richard Linklater">Richard Linklater</a> film <i><a href="/wiki/A_Scanner_Darkly_(film)" title="A Scanner Darkly (film)">A Scanner Darkly</a></i> (2006). Cochrane reprised his role as Tim Speedle on <i>CSI: Miami</i> in the episode "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bang,_Bang,_Your_Debt" title="Bang, Bang, Your Debt">Bang, Bang, Your Debt</a>", as a hallucination to Detective <a href="/wiki/Eric_Delko" title="Eric Delko">Eric Delko</a>.</p>
<p>In 2015, Cochrane had a supporting role as Boston mobster <a href="/wiki/Stephen_Flemmi" title="Stephen Flemmi">Stephen Flemmi</a> in the true-crime film <i><a href="/wiki/Black_Mass_(film)" title="Black Mass (film)">Black Mass</a></i>, which starred <a href="/wiki/Johnny_Depp" title="Johnny Depp">Johnny Depp</a>.</p>
