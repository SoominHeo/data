<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Romain_Duris&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>, son of an engineer-architect father and a dancer mother, Duris studied arts at university but decided to follow a career in music forming a jazz-funk band. Music remains a major interest. He was noticed in the street by a casting director whilst waiting in a queue in 1993 and was offered a part in the 1994 <a href="/wiki/C%C3%A9dric_Klapisch" title="Cédric Klapisch">Cédric Klapisch</a> film <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Le_p%C3%A9ril_jeune" title="Le péril jeune">Le péril jeune</a></i>. He has since worked with Klapisch regularly. His first role however was in a pop video for <a href="/wiki/Princess_Erika" title="Princess Erika">Princess Erika</a>, <i>Faut qu'j'travaille</i>, (I need to work) in which he plays the role of a small-time gangster.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Faut_qu.27j.27travaille_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Faut_qu.27j.27travaille-1">[1]</a></sup> His sister is pianist Caroline Duris, who performs on the soundtrack of the 2005 film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Beat_That_My_Heart_Skipped" title="The Beat That My Heart Skipped">The Beat That My Heart Skipped</a></i>. Before going into acting, he started an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Acid-jazz" title="Acid-jazz">acid-jazz</a> band where he was a drummer. Duris lives in Paris near La Bastille with his actress girlfriend <a href="/wiki/Olivia_Bonamy" title="Olivia Bonamy">Olivia Bonamy</a> with whom he has a son, Luigi, born 10 February 2009.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Romain_Duris&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Duris is best known for his role as the French exchange student Xavier Rousseau in <i><a href="/wiki/L%27Auberge_Espagnole" title="L'Auberge Espagnole">L'Auberge Espagnole</a></i> and <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Russian_Dolls" title="The Russian Dolls">The Russian Dolls</a>.</i> He has a reputation for versatility, having played a thug in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Beat_That_My_Heart_Skipped" title="The Beat That My Heart Skipped">The Beat That My Heart Skipped</a></i>. He has played a variety of roles ranging from gangsters as in <a href="/wiki/Dobermann_(film)" title="Dobermann (film)">Dobermann</a> through romantic leads, <i><a href="/wiki/Heartbreaker_(2010_film)" title="Heartbreaker (2010 film)">Heartbreaker</a></i> to action heroes in <i><a href="/wiki/Ars%C3%A8ne_Lupin_(2004_film)" title="Arsène Lupin (2004 film)">Arsène Lupin</a></i>.</p>
