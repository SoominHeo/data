<p><i><b>I Need Romance</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">로맨스가 필요해</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Romaenseuga Pilyohae</span></i>) is a 2011 <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> romantic comedy series starring <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jo_Yeo-jeong" title="Jo Yeo-jeong">Jo Yeo-jeong</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kim_Jeong-hoon" title="Kim Jeong-hoon">Kim Jeong-hoon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Choi_Yeo-jin" title="Choi Yeo-jin">Choi Yeo-jin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Choi_Song-hyun" title="Choi Song-hyun">Choi Song-hyun</a> and <a href="/wiki/Choi_Jin-hyuk" title="Choi Jin-hyuk">Choi Jin-hyuk</a>. It aired on <a href="/wiki/TVN_(South_Korea)" title="TVN (South Korea)">tvN</a> from June 13 to August 2, 2011 on Mondays and Tuesdays at 23:00 for 16 episodes.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The cable drama revolves around three thirty-something career women and their complicated love lives. It was popular with female viewers in their 20s and 30s for depicting contemporary women in a realistic way.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=I_Need_Romance&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Funny, romantic, and just a little racy, <i>I Need Romance</i> follows the <i><a href="/wiki/Sex_and_the_City" title="Sex and the City">Sex and the City</a></i>-like adventures of three thirty-something best friends looking for love in modern-day Seoul.</p>
