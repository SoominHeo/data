<p><br/>
Year <b>1194</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MCXCIV</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Saturday" title="Common year starting on Saturday">common year starting on Saturday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1194&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_4" title="February 4">February 4</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Richard_I_of_England" title="Richard I of England">Richard I of England</a> is ransomed from <a href="/wiki/Henry_VI,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Henry VI, Holy Roman Emperor">Henry VI, Holy Roman Emperor</a>.</li>
<li>c. <a href="/wiki/February_10" title="February 10">February 10</a> – <a href="/wiki/Henry_Marshal_(bishop_of_Exeter)" title="Henry Marshal (bishop of Exeter)">Henry Marshal</a> is nominated <a href="/wiki/Bishop_of_Exeter" title="Bishop of Exeter">Bishop of Exeter</a> in <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_10" title="March 10">March 10</a> – Sultan <a href="/wiki/Toghrul_III" title="Toghrul III">Toghrul III</a> is defeated and killed in battle with <a href="/wiki/Ala_ad-Din_Tekish" title="Ala ad-Din Tekish">Ala ad-Din Tekish</a> near <a href="/wiki/Rey,_Iran" title="Rey, Iran">Rey</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Persia" title="Persia">Persia</a>, ending the <a href="/wiki/Seljuq_dynasty" title="Seljuq dynasty">Seljuq dynasty</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hamedan" title="Hamedan">Hamedan</a>; the Great <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seljuq_Empire" title="Seljuq Empire">Seljuq Empire</a> passes to the <a href="/wiki/Khwarazmian_dynasty" title="Khwarazmian dynasty">Khwarazmian dynasty</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_12" title="March 12">March 12</a>–<a href="/wiki/March_28" title="March 28">28</a> – King Richard returns to <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a> and besieges <a href="/wiki/Nottingham_Castle" title="Nottingham Castle">Nottingham Castle</a> to reclaim it from his brother <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/John_of_England" title="John of England">John</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Cassell.27s_Chronology_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Cassell.27s_Chronology-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_17" title="April 17">April 17</a> – Second coronation of Richard I at <a href="/wiki/Winchester" title="Winchester">Winchester</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-CBH_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-CBH-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_2" title="May 2">May 2</a> – The port of <a href="/wiki/Portsmouth" title="Portsmouth">Portsmouth</a> in England is granted a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Royal_Charter" title="Royal Charter">Royal Charter</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_12" title="May 12">May 12</a> – After settling affairs in England, Richard I leaves for <a href="/wiki/Barfleur" title="Barfleur">Barfleur</a> in Normandy to reclaim lands lost to <a href="/wiki/Philip_II_of_France" title="Philip II of France">Philip II of France</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Cassell.27s_Chronology_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-Cassell.27s_Chronology-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_10" title="June 10">June 10</a> – A fire at <a href="/wiki/Chartres_Cathedral" title="Chartres Cathedral">Chartres Cathedral</a> leads to the start of its rebuilding.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_3" title="July 3">July 3</a> – Battle of Fréteval: <a href="/wiki/Richard_I_of_England" title="Richard I of England">Richard I of England</a> reconquers his French fiefdoms from <a href="/wiki/Philip_II_of_France" title="Philip II of France">Philip II</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Cassell.27s_Chronology_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-Cassell.27s_Chronology-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_5" title="July 5">July 5</a> – <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Guangzong_of_Song" title="Emperor Guangzong of Song">Emperor Guangzong of Song</a> <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a> is forced to give up his throne.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_20" title="November 20">November 20</a> – <a href="/wiki/Palermo" title="Palermo">Palermo</a> in Sicily falls to <a href="/wiki/Henry_VI,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Henry VI, Holy Roman Emperor">Henry VI, Holy Roman Emperor</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/December_25" title="December 25">December 25</a> – <a href="/wiki/Henry_VI,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Henry VI, Holy Roman Emperor">Henry VI</a> is crowned king of <a href="/wiki/Sicily" title="Sicily">Sicily</a>.</li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Danes_(ancient_people)" title="Danes (ancient people)">Danes</a> attack <a href="/wiki/Estonia" title="Estonia">Estonia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/History_of_the_Jews_in_England_(1066%E2%80%931290)#Ordinance_of_the_Jewry.2C_1194" title="History of the Jews in England (1066–1290)">Ordinance of the Jewry in England</a>: Strict records are to be kept of financial transactions by Jews in England for taxation purposes.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Yellow_River" title="Yellow River">Yellow River</a> of <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a> experiences a major course change, taking over the <a href="/wiki/Huai_River" title="Huai River">Huai River</a> drainage system for the next 700 years.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></li>
<li>Beginning of a three-year famine in Northern France due to catastrophic rains and hail storms. <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Baldwin2006_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-Baldwin2006-6">[6]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hunac_Ceel" title="Hunac Ceel">Hunac Ceel</a> drives the <a href="/wiki/Itza_people" title="Itza people">Itza</a> out of <a href="/wiki/Chichen_Itza" title="Chichen Itza">Chichen Itza</a>, forcing them to start the <a href="/wiki/Peten_Itza_Kingdom" title="Peten Itza Kingdom">Itza Kingdom</a> on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lake_Peten_Itza" title="Lake Peten Itza">Lake Peten Itza</a>.</li>
</ul>
