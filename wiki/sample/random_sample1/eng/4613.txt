<p><b>Roberto Vitiello</b> (born 8 May 1983) is an <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)#Full-back" title="Defender (association football)">right back</a> and serves as captain for Sicilian <a href="/wiki/Serie_A" title="Serie A">Serie A</a> club <a href="/wiki/U.S._Citt%C3%A0_di_Palermo" title="U.S. Città di Palermo">Palermo</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Roberto_Vitiello&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Vitiello started his career at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Parma_F.C." title="Parma F.C.">Parma</a>, in 2002, he participated in <a href="/wiki/Torneo_di_Viareggio" title="Torneo di Viareggio">Torneo di Viareggio</a> with Parma and highly rated.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> He then awarded no.34 of the first team.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>After loaned to <a href="/wiki/A.C._Cesena" title="A.C. Cesena">Cesena</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Serie_C1" title="Serie C1">Serie C1</a>, he was loaned to <a href="/wiki/Vicenza_Calcio" title="Vicenza Calcio">Vicenza</a> of <a href="/wiki/Serie_B" title="Serie B">Serie B</a> in July 2003, as a compensation of <a href="/wiki/Giuseppe_Cardone" title="Giuseppe Cardone">Giuseppe Cardone</a> and <a href="/wiki/Christian_Maggio" title="Christian Maggio">Christian Maggio</a>. In summer 2004 the deal became permanent. In July 2006 he was sold to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rimini_Calcio_F.C." title="Rimini Calcio F.C.">Rimini</a> of <a href="/wiki/Serie_B" title="Serie B">Serie B</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>In June 2010 he was hired by newly relegated Serie B team <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/A.C._Siena" title="A.C. Siena">Siena</a>, signed a 3-year contract.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>In the January 2014 goes to <a href="/wiki/U.S._Citt%C3%A0_di_Palermo" title="U.S. Città di Palermo">Palermo</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Italian_football_scandal">Italian football scandal</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Roberto_Vitiello&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Italian football scandal">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Due to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2011_Italian_football_scandal" title="2011 Italian football scandal">2011 Italian football scandal</a>, he was suspended for 4 year on 11 August 2012.</p>
