<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Robin_Weigert&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Weigert was born in <a href="/wiki/Washington,_D.C." title="Washington, D.C.">Washington, D.C.</a>, the daughter of Dionne Laufman and Berlin-born Wolfgang Oscar Weigert, a psychiatrist.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> She is of Jewish heritage.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> After graduating from <a href="/wiki/Brandeis_University" title="Brandeis University">Brandeis University</a> in 1991, Weigert attended <a href="/wiki/New_York_University" title="New York University">New York University</a>, earning a <a href="/wiki/Master_of_Fine_Arts" title="Master of Fine Arts">Master of Fine Arts</a> degree in the <a href="/wiki/Tisch_School_of_the_Arts#Graduate_Acting_Program" title="Tisch School of the Arts">Graduate Acting Program</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Tisch_School_of_the_Arts" title="Tisch School of the Arts">Tisch School of the Arts</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> After a decade as a stage actress based in New York City, she moved to Los Angeles and has appeared in various movies, TV shows and mini-series.</p>
<p>Weigert is best known for her much-lauded portrayal of the unkempt, foul-mooded, foul-mouthed drunkard <a href="/wiki/Calamity_Jane" title="Calamity Jane">Calamity Jane</a> in the <a href="/wiki/HBO" title="HBO">HBO</a> television series <i><a href="/wiki/Deadwood_(TV_series)" title="Deadwood (TV series)">Deadwood</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> In 2004, Weigert was nominated for an Emmy for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series for the role.</p>
<p>In 2010, it was announced that Weigert would be joining the cast of the FX drama <i><a href="/wiki/Sons_of_Anarchy" title="Sons of Anarchy">Sons of Anarchy</a></i>. She plays the motorcycle club's lawyer.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> As of 2014, she plays a recurring role on <i><a href="/wiki/Chicago_P.D._(TV_series)" title="Chicago P.D. (TV series)">Chicago P.D.</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
