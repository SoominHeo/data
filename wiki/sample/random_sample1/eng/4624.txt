<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="The_city_of_Roosendaal">The city of Roosendaal</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Roosendaal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: The city of Roosendaal">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Under King <a href="/wiki/Louis_Bonaparte" title="Louis Bonaparte">Lodewijk Napoléon</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Holland" title="Kingdom of Holland">Kingdom of Holland</a>, Roosendaal received <a href="/wiki/City_rights_in_the_Low_Countries" title="City rights in the Low Countries">city rights</a> in 1809.</p>
<p>The official International ABBA Fan club has been based in Roosendaal since 1986, and since the turn of the century, the city has also played host to the annual convention of the International <a href="/wiki/ABBA" title="ABBA">ABBA</a> Fan Club where for one weekend every April, hundreds of people from all over the world attend the event.</p>
<p><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Nispen&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Nispen (page does not exist)">Nispen</a> merged with Roosendaal<sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Manual_of_Style/Dates_and_numbers#Chronological_items" title="Wikipedia:Manual of Style/Dates and numbers"><span title="The time period mentioned near this tag is ambiguous. (April 2015)">when?</span></a></i>]</sup> to form the municipality <a href="/wiki/Roosendaal_en_Nispen" title="Roosendaal en Nispen">Roosendaal en Nispen</a>. On 1 January 1997 the municipalities Roosendaal en Nispen and <a href="/wiki/Wouw" title="Wouw">Wouw</a> merged into the municipality now simply known as Roosendaal.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Roosendaal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Roosendaal goes back to the 12th and 13th century. The name Rosendaele was first mentioned in a document of 1268. Roosendaal was always a part of <a href="/wiki/North_Brabant" title="North Brabant">North Brabant</a>. In the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Ages" title="Middle Ages">Middle Ages</a>, Roosendaal grew as a result of the turf business, but the <a href="/wiki/Eighty_Years%27_War" title="Eighty Years' War">Eighty Years' War</a> (1568–1648) put an end to the growth as Roosendaal and Wouw were suffering from itinerant combat troops that plundered and ravaged everything they came across. For decades the countryside of Roosendaal was abandoned.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Sports">Sports</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Roosendaal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Sports">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The leading <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">soccer</a> team of the city is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/RBC_Roosendaal" title="RBC Roosendaal">RBC Roosendaal</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transport">Transport</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Roosendaal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Transport">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><b>Railway station:</b> <a href="/wiki/Roosendaal_railway_station" title="Roosendaal railway station">Roosendaal</a></li>
</ul>
<p>The city serves as a regional railway hub: the <a href="/wiki/Zwolle" title="Zwolle">Zwolle</a>-Roosendaal Intercity service starts and ends here, the international train from <a href="/wiki/Amsterdam" title="Amsterdam">Amsterdam</a> to <a href="/wiki/Brussels" title="Brussels">Brussels</a> calls at Roosendaal, as does the Amsterdam-<a href="/wiki/Vlissingen" title="Vlissingen">Vlissingen</a> (Flushing) intercity. There is also a stop-train service to <a href="/wiki/Antwerp" title="Antwerp">Antwerp</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Medical_Care">Medical Care</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Roosendaal&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Medical Care">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Bravis Ziekenhuis Roosendaal is the main general hospital for residents of Roosendaal and surrounding areas. Bravis Ziekenhuis offers specialised medical care provided by a broad team of medical specialists. Special units include: <a href="/wiki/Cardiology" title="Cardiology">Cardiology</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Dermatology" title="Dermatology">Dermatology</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Gynaecology" title="Gynaecology">Gynaecology</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Oncology" title="Oncology">Oncology</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Ophthalmology" title="Ophthalmology">Ophthalmology</a> <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Intensive_Care" title="Intensive Care">Intensive Care</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
