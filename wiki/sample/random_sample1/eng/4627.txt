<p><b>Rosoman</b> (<a href="/wiki/Macedonian_language" title="Macedonian language">Macedonian</a>: <span lang="mk" xml:lang="mk"><span class="unicode haudio"><span class="fn"><span style="white-space:nowrap"><a href="/wiki/File:Mk-Rosoman.ogg" title="About this sound"><img alt="About this sound" data-file-height="20" data-file-width="20" height="11" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Loudspeaker.svg/11px-Loudspeaker.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Loudspeaker.svg/17px-Loudspeaker.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Loudspeaker.svg/22px-Loudspeaker.svg.png 2x" width="11"/></a> </span><a class="internal" href="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/19/Mk-Rosoman.ogg" title="Mk-Rosoman.ogg">Росоман</a></span> <small class="metadata audiolinkinfo" style="cursor:help;">(<a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Media_help" title="Wikipedia:Media help"><span style="cursor:help;">help</span></a>·<a href="/wiki/File:Mk-Rosoman.ogg" title="File:Mk-Rosoman.ogg"><span style="cursor:help;">info</span></a>)</small></span></span>) is a municipality in central <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Macedonia" title="Republic of Macedonia">Republic of Macedonia</a>. <i><a href="/wiki/Rosoman" title="Rosoman">Rosoman</a></i> is also the name of the village where the municipal seat is found. Rosoman Municipality is part of the <a href="/wiki/Vardar_Statistical_Region" title="Vardar Statistical Region">Vardar Statistical Region</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rosoman_Municipality&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The municipality borders <a href="/wiki/Gradsko_Municipality" title="Gradsko Municipality">Gradsko Municipality</a> to the north, <a href="/wiki/Negotino_Municipality" title="Negotino Municipality">Negotino Municipality</a> to the east, <a href="/wiki/%C4%8Ca%C5%A1ka_Municipality" title="Čaška Municipality">Čaška Municipality</a> to the west, and <a href="/wiki/Kavadarci_Municipality" title="Kavadarci Municipality">Kavadarci Municipality</a> to the south.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rosoman_Municipality&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The municipality has a population of 4,141 according to the last national census in 2002, with density of 31,16 people/km².<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> In the census in 1994, 4,328 people were registered as residents of this municipality. Ethnic groups in the municipality:</p>
<ul>
<li>Macedonians = 3,694 (89.2%)</li>
<li>Serbs = 409 (9.9%)</li>
<li>others = 38 (0.9%)</li>
</ul>
