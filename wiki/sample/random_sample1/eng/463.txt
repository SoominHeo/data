<p>The <b>2013 EAFF Women's East Asian Cup</b> was the 4th edition of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/EAFF_Women%27s_East_Asian_Cup" title="EAFF Women's East Asian Cup">EAFF Women's East Asian Cup</a>. There were three competition rounds.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-eaff-120420_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-eaff-120420-1">[1]</a></sup> The final round was won by North Korea.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> In August 2012, Australia accepted an invitation to take part.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-AusEntry_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-AusEntry-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
