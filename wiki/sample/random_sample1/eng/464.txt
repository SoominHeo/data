<p>The <b>2013 East Asian Games</b>, officially known as the VI <a href="/wiki/East_Asian_Games" title="East Asian Games">East Asian Games</a>, was an <a href="/wiki/International" title="International">international</a> <a href="/wiki/Multi-sport_event" title="Multi-sport event">multi-sport event</a> that took place in <a href="/wiki/Tianjin" title="Tianjin">Tianjin</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>, between 6 October and 15 October 2013. 2,422 Athletes from nine <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=East_aria&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="East aria (page does not exist)">East Asian</a> nations competed in 254 events in 24 sports.</p>
