<p>The <b>Los Angeles Master Chorale</b> is a professional <a href="/wiki/Choir" title="Choir">chorus</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Los_Angeles,_California" title="Los Angeles, California">Los Angeles, California</a>, and one the resident companies of both the <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles_Music_Center" title="Los Angeles Music Center">Los Angeles Music Center</a> and <a href="/wiki/Walt_Disney_Concert_Hall" title="Walt Disney Concert Hall">Walt Disney Concert Hall</a>. It was founded in 1964 by <a href="/wiki/Roger_Wagner" title="Roger Wagner">Roger Wagner</a> to be one of the three original resident companies of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Music_Center_of_Los_Angeles_County" title="Music Center of Los Angeles County">Music Center of Los Angeles County</a>. <a href="/wiki/Grant_Gershon" title="Grant Gershon">Grant Gershon</a> has been its music director since 2001, replacing <a href="/wiki/Paul_Salamunovich" title="Paul Salamunovich">Paul Salamunovich</a>.</p>
<p>The Master Chorale performs about ten times per year in its own season. It has presented more than 450 concerts, including early choral music to contemporary compositions. Noted for presenting numerous world, U.S. and West Coast premieres, the chorus has commissioned 24 and premiered 40 new works.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (June 2011)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> The Master Chorale regularly performs with the <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles_Philharmonic" title="Los Angeles Philharmonic">Los Angeles Philharmonic</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Hollywood_Bowl_Orchestra" title="Hollywood Bowl Orchestra">Hollywood Bowl Orchestra</a>, both at the Music Center and at the Hollywood Bowl, with such leading conductors as <a href="/wiki/Gustavo_Dudamel" title="Gustavo Dudamel">Gustavo Dudamel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Esa-Pekka_Salonen" title="Esa-Pekka Salonen">Esa-Pekka Salonen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Zubin_Mehta" title="Zubin Mehta">Zubin Mehta</a>, <a href="/wiki/Andr%C3%A9_Previn" title="André Previn">André Previn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Boulez" title="Pierre Boulez">Pierre Boulez</a>, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Tilson_Thomas" title="Michael Tilson Thomas">Michael Tilson Thomas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Roger_Norrington" title="Roger Norrington">Roger Norrington</a>, among many others. Notable guest conductors have included <a href="/wiki/Robert_Shaw_(conductor)" title="Robert Shaw (conductor)">Robert Shaw</a>, <a href="/wiki/Helmuth_Rilling" title="Helmuth Rilling">Helmuth Rilling</a>, <a href="/wiki/Margaret_Hillis" title="Margaret Hillis">Margaret Hillis</a>, Robert Page and <a href="/wiki/Richard_Westenburg" title="Richard Westenburg">Richard Westenburg</a>. It served as the chorus for <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles_Opera" title="Los Angeles Opera">Los Angeles Opera</a> during that organization's early years before it had established its own in-house chorus. It also performs in community concerts throughout Southern California.</p>
<p>The Chorale originally performed at the <a href="/wiki/Dorothy_Chandler_Pavilion" title="Dorothy Chandler Pavilion">Dorothy Chandler Pavilion</a>, but since 2003 the group's principal concert venue has been <a href="/wiki/Walt_Disney_Concert_Hall" title="Walt Disney Concert Hall">Walt Disney Concert Hall</a>. <a href="/wiki/Morten_Lauridsen" title="Morten Lauridsen">Morten Lauridsen</a> was its composer-in-residence from 1994 through 2001.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="With_the_Hollywood_Bowl_Orchestra">With the Hollywood Bowl Orchestra</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Los_Angeles_Master_Chorale&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: With the Hollywood Bowl Orchestra">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Motion_pictures">Motion pictures</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Los_Angeles_Master_Chorale&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Motion pictures">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Salamunovich also conducted the Los Angeles Master Chorale on the soundtracks of numerous major motion pictures, including <i><a href="/wiki/A.I._Artificial_Intelligence" title="A.I. Artificial Intelligence">A.I. Artificial Intelligence</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/My_Best_Friend%27s_Wedding" title="My Best Friend's Wedding">My Best Friend's Wedding</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dracula_(1992_film)" title="Dracula (1992 film)">Bram Stoker's Dracula</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Sum_of_All_Fears_(film)" title="The Sum of All Fears (film)">The Sum of All Fears</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Waterworld" title="Waterworld">Waterworld</a></i>. Gershon conducted the Chorale on the soundtracks for the motion pictures <i><a href="/wiki/Lady_in_the_Water" title="Lady in the Water">Lady in the Water</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/License_to_Wed" title="License to Wed">License to Wed</a></i>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Awards_and_recognition">Awards and recognition</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Los_Angeles_Master_Chorale&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Awards and recognition">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In June 2003, the Master Chorale received the prestigious ASCAP/Chorus America Award for Adventurous Programming. In 2008, one of the Chorale’s highly successful outreach program, “Voices Within,” earned the coveted Chorus America Education Outreach Award. The Master Chorale with <a href="/wiki/Paul_Salamunovich" title="Paul Salamunovich">Paul Salamunovich</a> conducting, received a Grammy nomination for Best Choral Performance for the recording Lux Aeterna in 1998.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Education_programs">Education programs</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Los_Angeles_Master_Chorale&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Education programs">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Since 1990, the Master Chorale has held the Los Angeles Master Chorale High School Choir Festival, one of the largest high school choir festivals in the nation.</p>
<p>"Voices Within," another of the Chorale's highly successful outreach programs, is an in-depth, ten-week, in-school residency program for fifth and six grade students that helps children who have little or no musical background to “find their voices.” With the assistance of a teaching artist, lyricist, and composer who work with teams of students in the classroom, children write music and lyrics to create original songs.</p>
<p>The Master Chorale provides education outreach to approximately 13,000 children each year.</p>
