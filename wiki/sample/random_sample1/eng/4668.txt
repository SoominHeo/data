<p><b>Roccastrada</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Grosseto" title="Province of Grosseto">Province of Grosseto</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Tuscany" title="Tuscany">Tuscany</a>, located about 90 kilometres (56 mi) south of <a href="/wiki/Florence" title="Florence">Florence</a> and about 25 kilometres (16 mi) north of <a href="/wiki/Grosseto" title="Grosseto">Grosseto</a>, between the <a href="/wiki/Maremma" title="Maremma">Maremma</a> plain and the <a href="/wiki/Colline_Metallifere" title="Colline Metallifere">Colline Metallifere</a>.</p>
