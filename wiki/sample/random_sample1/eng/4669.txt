<p><b>Roccasicura</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Isernia" title="Province of Isernia">Province of Isernia</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Molise" title="Molise">Molise</a>, located about 40 kilometres (25 mi) northwest of <a href="/wiki/Campobasso" title="Campobasso">Campobasso</a> and about 11 kilometres (7 mi) north of <a href="/wiki/Isernia" title="Isernia">Isernia</a>. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 610 and an area of 29.0 square kilometres (11.2 sq mi).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-istat_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-istat-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Roccasicura borders the following municipalities: <a href="/wiki/Carovilli" title="Carovilli">Carovilli</a>, <a href="/wiki/Forl%C3%AC_del_Sannio" title="Forlì del Sannio">Forlì del Sannio</a>, <a href="/wiki/Isernia" title="Isernia">Isernia</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Miranda,_Italy" title="Miranda, Italy">Miranda</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vastogirardi" title="Vastogirardi">Vastogirardi</a>.</p>
