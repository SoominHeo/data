<p><b>Wagner Lopes</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">呂比須 ワグナー</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Lopes Wagner</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born January 29, 1969)</span> is a former <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> player. He played for <a href="/wiki/Japan_national_football_team" title="Japan national football team">Japan national team</a>. Although he was born in Brazil, he is a naturalised Japanese citizen.</p>
<p>Lopes was an important member of Japan's <a href="/wiki/1998_FIFA_World_Cup" title="1998 FIFA World Cup">1998 FIFA World Cup</a> squad and was best remembered playing alongside <a href="/wiki/Hidetoshi_Nakata" title="Hidetoshi Nakata">Hidetoshi Nakata</a> at <a href="/wiki/Shonan_Bellmare" title="Shonan Bellmare">Bellmare Hiratsuka</a>.</p>
<p>He played for many Japanese clubs including <a href="/wiki/Yokohama_F._Marinos" title="Yokohama F. Marinos">Nissan Motors</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kashiwa_Reysol" title="Kashiwa Reysol">Kashiwa Reysol</a>, <a href="/wiki/Honda_FC" title="Honda FC">Honda</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shonan_Bellmare" title="Shonan Bellmare">Bellmare Hiratsuka</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nagoya_Grampus" title="Nagoya Grampus">Nagoya Grampus Eight</a>, <a href="/wiki/FC_Tokyo" title="FC Tokyo">FC Tokyo</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Avispa_Fukuoka" title="Avispa Fukuoka">Avispa Fukuoka</a>.</p>
<p>He served as manager at <a href="/wiki/Paulista_Futebol_Clube" title="Paulista Futebol Clube">Paulista FC</a> in 2010 and 2011.</p>
