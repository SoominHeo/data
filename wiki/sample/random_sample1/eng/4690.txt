<p><b>Ron Garney</b> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Comic_book_writer/artist" title="Comic book writer/artist">comic book writer/artist</a>, known for his work on books such as <i><a href="/wiki/JLA_(comic_book)" title="JLA (comic book)">JLA</a></i>,<i><a href="/wiki/The_Amazing_Spider-Man" title="The Amazing Spider-Man">The Amazing Spider-Man</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Silver_Surfer" title="Silver Surfer">Silver Surfer</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Hulk_(comics)" title="Hulk (comics)">Hulk</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Daredevil_(Marvel_Comics_series)" title="Daredevil (Marvel Comics series)">Daredevil</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Captain_America" title="Captain America">Captain America</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ron_Garney&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Garney has worked on <i><a href="/wiki/JLA_(comic_book)" title="JLA (comic book)">JLA</a></i>,<i><a href="/wiki/The_Amazing_Spider-Man" title="The Amazing Spider-Man">The Amazing Spider-Man</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Daredevil_(Marvel_Comics_series)" title="Daredevil (Marvel Comics series)">Daredevil</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Ghost_Rider_(comics)" title="Ghost Rider (comics)">Ghost Rider</a></i> volume 3, <i><a href="/wiki/Wolverine_(comic_book)" title="Wolverine (comic book)">Wolverine</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Captain_America" title="Captain America">Captain America</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/X-Men" title="X-Men">X-Men</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Silver_Surfer" title="Silver Surfer">Silver Surfer</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Hulk_(comics)" title="Hulk (comics)">Hulk</a></i>. He has also written for <i>Hulk</i> in collaboration with <a href="/wiki/Jerry_Ordway" title="Jerry Ordway">Jerry Ordway</a>.</p>
<p>Garney's late 2000s projects include <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Skaar_(character)" title="Skaar (character)">Skaar: Son of Hulk</a></i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> and <i><a href="/wiki/Wolverine:_Weapon_X" title="Wolverine: Weapon X">Wolverine: Weapon X</a></i>. Garney worked as the Costume illustrator on the 2007 <a href="/wiki/Will_Smith" title="Will Smith">Will Smith</a> film <i><a href="/wiki/I_Am_Legend_(film)" title="I Am Legend (film)">I Am Legend</a></i>, and the 2010 <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Cage" title="Nicolas Cage">Nicolas Cage</a> fantasy film, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Sorcerer%27s_Apprentice_(2010_film)" title="The Sorcerer's Apprentice (2010 film)">The Sorcerer's Apprentice</a></i>.</p>
