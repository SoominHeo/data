<p><i><b>RollerCoaster Tycoon 3D</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Construction_and_management_simulation" title="Construction and management simulation">construction and management simulation</a> <a href="/wiki/Video_game" title="Video game">video game</a> developed by <a href="/wiki/N-Space" title="N-Space">n-Space</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Nintendo_3DS" title="Nintendo 3DS">Nintendo 3DS</a>. The game is a spin-off of the <a href="/wiki/RollerCoaster_Tycoon_(series)" title="RollerCoaster Tycoon (series)"><i>RollerCoaster Tycoon</i> series</a> and the first portable game in the franchise.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Initial_reveal_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Initial_reveal-3">[3]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Gameplay">Gameplay</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=RollerCoaster_Tycoon_3D&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Gameplay">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The gameplay is similar and borrow elements from previous installments of <i><a href="/wiki/RollerCoaster_Tycoon_(series)" title="RollerCoaster Tycoon (series)">RollerCoaster Tycoon</a></i>, as players are able build <a href="/wiki/Roller_coaster" title="Roller coaster">roller coasters</a> in a 3D space, construct various attractions, and generally various aspects of the park. The game also has features from the previous game such the CoasterCam and themed areas, but does not have other features such as water attractions, fireworks, and adjustable terrain.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Reception">Reception</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=RollerCoaster_Tycoon_3D&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Reception">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>RollerCoaster Tycoon 3D</i> has received mostly negative reviews, with many critics citing that the game does not hold up to its predecessors due to its unappealing graphics, lack of mechanics from previous games, a poorly designed interface, and a generally dull experience. <i>RollerCoaster Tycoon 3D</i> currently has a score of 39 out of 100 on <a href="/wiki/Metacritic" title="Metacritic">Metacritic</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
