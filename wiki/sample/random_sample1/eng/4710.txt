<p><b>Lotte Insurance Co, Ltd.</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hangeul" title="Hangeul">hangeul</a>:롯데손해보험) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Insurance" title="Insurance">insurance</a> company (previously known as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hanwha_Daehan_Life_Insurance" title="Hanwha Daehan Life Insurance">Hanwha Daehan Life Insurance</a>), headquartered in <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a>, <a href="/wiki/Korea" title="Korea">Korea</a> which was established in 1946. The damage insurance group division of the company controls a large portion of the South Korean <a href="/wiki/Telephone_card" title="Telephone card">calling card</a> market under the low-price online automobile insurance brand name <b>How Much</b>.</p>
