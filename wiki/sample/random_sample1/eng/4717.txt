<p>The <b>Ryonggang Line</b> is a non-electrified standard-gauge secondary line of the <a href="/wiki/North_Korea" title="North Korea">North Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Korean_State_Railway" title="Korean State Railway">State Railway</a> running from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ryonggang" title="Ryonggang">Ryonggang</a> on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/P%27y%C5%8Fngnam_Line" title="P'yŏngnam Line">P'yŏngnam Line</a> to <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Mayong_(DPRK)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Mayong (DPRK) (page does not exist)">Mayŏng</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Hayato_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Hayato-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Services">Services</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryonggang_Line&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Services">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A local train, <b>734</b>, operates on the entirety of this line from <a href="/wiki/Mayong_Station" title="Mayong Station">Mayŏng</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ryonggang_Station" title="Ryonggang Station">Ryonggang</a>, continuing on to <a href="/wiki/Kangso_Station" title="Kangso Station">Kangsŏ</a>; the return trip is train number <b>733</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Hayato_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Hayato-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Most freight on the line is outbound from a mine at Mayŏng and a granite quarry on the Yangmak Branch.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Yangmak_Branch">Yangmak Branch</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryonggang_Line&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Yangmak Branch">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This is a non-electrified, freight-only branch running along the west side of the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ryongho_Reservoir&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ryongho Reservoir (page does not exist)">Ryongho Reservoir</a> to serve a number of industries. The industrial trackage continues for a ways beyond <a href="/wiki/Yangmak_Station" title="Yangmak Station">Yangmak Station</a> to the Yangmak granite quarry.</p>
