<p><b>Rusa II</b> was <a href="/wiki/Monarch" title="Monarch">king</a> of <a href="/wiki/Urartu" title="Urartu">Urartu</a> between around 680 BC and 639 BC. It was during his reign that the massive fortress complex, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Karmir-Blur" title="Karmir-Blur">Karmir-Blur</a>, was constructed.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>A cuneiform inscription has been found commemorating the king building a canal to channel water to the city of Quarlini from the Ildaruni (<a href="/wiki/Hrazdan_River" title="Hrazdan River">Hrazdan River</a>).</p>
