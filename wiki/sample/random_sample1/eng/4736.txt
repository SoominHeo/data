<p><b>Lusail</b> is the newest <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Planned_city" title="Planned city">planned city</a> in <a href="/wiki/Qatar" title="Qatar">Qatar</a>, located on the coast, in the northern part of the municipality of <a href="/wiki/Al_Daayen" title="Al Daayen">Al Daayen</a>. Lusail is located about 23 km north of the city centre of <a href="/wiki/Doha" title="Doha">Doha</a>, just north of the <a href="/wiki/West_Bay_Lagoon" title="West Bay Lagoon">West Bay Lagoon</a>, on over 35 km² and will eventually have a population of up to 260,000 people. It is planned to have marinas, residential areas, island resorts, commercial districts, luxury shopping and leisure facilities, and a golf course community, man made islands and several <a href="/wiki/Entertainment" title="Entertainment">entertainment</a> districts. Construction is still ongoing. It is being developed by the state-controlled developer <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Qatari_Diar_Real_Estate_Investment_Company" title="Qatari Diar Real Estate Investment Company">Qatari Diar Real Estate Investment Company</a> as well as <a href="/wiki/Parsons_Corporation" title="Parsons Corporation">Parsons Corporation</a>.</p>
<p>The planned <a href="/wiki/Lusail_Iconic_Stadium" title="Lusail Iconic Stadium">Lusail Iconic Stadium</a>, with a capacity of 80,000+ people, will host the opening and final matches of the <a href="/wiki/2022_FIFA_World_Cup" title="2022 FIFA World Cup">2022 FIFA World Cup</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The stadium's design is inspired from the sail of a traditional <a href="/wiki/Dhow" title="Dhow">dhow</a> as the city will be located north of Doha on the eastern coast, which is a historic focal point of pearling vessels. After the FIFA World Cup, the stadium will be used to host other sporting and cultural events. <a href="/wiki/Lusail_Sports_Arena" title="Lusail Sports Arena">Lusail Sports Arena</a> hosted matches of the <a href="/wiki/2015_World_Men%27s_Handball_Championship" title="2015 World Men's Handball Championship">2015 World Men's Handball Championship</a>.</p>
