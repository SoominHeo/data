<p><b>Louis Ozawa Changchien</b> (born October 11, 1975) is an American actor, best known for his role in the <a href="/wiki/2010_in_film" title="2010 in film">2010</a> film <i><a href="/wiki/Predators_(film)" title="Predators (film)">Predators</a></i> and 2012 <i><a href="/wiki/The_Bourne_Legacy_(film)" title="The Bourne Legacy (film)">The Bourne Legacy</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_and_education">Early life and education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Louis_Ozawa_Changchien&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life and education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Changchien was born in <a href="/wiki/Queens" title="Queens">Queens, New York</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aubreymag_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-aubreymag-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-asiancemag_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-asiancemag-2">[2]</a></sup> and raised in both <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a> and <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-asiancemag_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-asiancemag-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sro_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-sro-3">[3]</a></sup> the son of a <a href="/wiki/Japanese_people" title="Japanese people">Japanese</a> mother, a jewelry designer, and a <a href="/wiki/Taiwanese_people" title="Taiwanese people">Taiwanese</a> father.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sro_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-sro-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> He attended <a href="/wiki/Riverdale_Country_School" title="Riverdale Country School">Riverdale Country School</a> as well as <a href="/wiki/Stuyvesant_High_School" title="Stuyvesant High School">Stuyvesant High School</a> and <a href="/wiki/Brown_University" title="Brown University">Brown University</a>, where he holds an <a href="/wiki/Master_of_Fine_Arts" title="Master of Fine Arts">MFA</a> in acting.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-asiancemag_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-asiancemag-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Louis_Ozawa_Changchien&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Changchien first appeared in the 1999 film <i>On the Q.T.</i> as Kenneth. Since then, he has appeared in many popular films, including <i><a href="/wiki/Robot_Stories" title="Robot Stories">Robot Stories</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Fair_Game_(2010_film)" title="Fair Game (2010 film)">Fair Game</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Predators_(film)" title="Predators (film)">Predators</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Bourne_Legacy_(film)" title="The Bourne Legacy (film)">The Bourne Legacy</a></i>. He also has made guest appearances on such shows as, <i><a href="/wiki/Law_%26_Order" title="Law &amp; Order">Law &amp; Order</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/3_lbs." title="3 lbs.">3 lbs.</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Heroes_and_Villains_(TV_series)" title="Heroes and Villains (TV series)">Heroes and Villains</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Lights_Out_(2011_TV_series)" title="Lights Out (2011 TV series)">Lights Out</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Blue_Bloods_(TV_series)" title="Blue Bloods (TV series)">Blue Bloods</a></i>.</p>
