<p><b>Luiz Philipe Lima de Oliveira</b>, known as <b>Muralha</b> (born January 21, 1993 in <a href="/wiki/Rio_de_Janeiro" title="Rio de Janeiro">Rio de Janeiro</a>), is a <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazilian</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> <a href="/wiki/Midfielder#Deep-lying_playmaker" title="Midfielder">centre midfielder</a>. He currently plays for <a href="/wiki/K_League_Classic" title="K League Classic">K League</a> club <a href="/wiki/Pohang_Steelers" title="Pohang Steelers">Pohang Steelers</a> on loan from <a href="/wiki/Clube_de_Regatas_do_Flamengo" title="Clube de Regatas do Flamengo">Flamengo</a>.</p>
