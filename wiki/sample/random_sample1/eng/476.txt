<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">2014 South Korea floods</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <b>2014 South Korea floods</b> were a series of floods in late August 2014 caused by heavy rainfall around the <a href="/wiki/Honam" title="Honam">Honam</a> and <a href="/wiki/Yeongnam" title="Yeongnam">Yeongnam</a>. This flood season killed about 10 people, and caused many accidents during 18 August to 25 August. In some places, The rain fell over 50 mm in an hour.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Cause_of_the_rainfall">Cause of the rainfall</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_South_Korea_floods&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Cause of the rainfall">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Primarily, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jangma" title="Jangma">Jangma</a> and other heavy rain seasons occur during June to July. But this rain occurred in August because the heavy rainfall moved to South Korea.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="August_18">August 18</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_South_Korea_floods&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: August 18">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The heavy rainfall passed between <a href="/wiki/North_Pacific_High" title="North Pacific High">North Pacific High</a> and <a href="/wiki/Siberian_High" title="Siberian High">Siberian High</a>, so the rain started to fall on <a href="/wiki/Honam" title="Honam">Honam</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jeju_Island" title="Jeju Island">Jeju Island</a>. In <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yeonggwang" title="Yeonggwang">Yeonggwang</a>, nine houses were flooded because of the rain, and many other accidents occurred. A few hours later, the cloud moved to the <a href="/wiki/Yeongnam" title="Yeongnam">Yeongnam</a>. There were many accidents and records in Yeongnam: 284.5 mm of precipitation in <a href="/wiki/Yangsan" title="Yangsan">Yangsan</a>, a sand-collapsing accident in <a href="/wiki/Geoje" title="Geoje">Geoje</a>, and two collapsing accidents in <a href="/wiki/Busan" title="Busan">Busan</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Precipitation_records">Precipitation records</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_South_Korea_floods&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Precipitation records">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Daily precipitation
<ul>

<li><a href="/wiki/Busan" title="Busan">Busan</a> 249 mm</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gochang" title="Gochang">Gochang</a> 230.6 mm</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ulsan" title="Ulsan">Ulsan</a> 211 mm</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Jeongeup" title="Jeongeup">Jeongeup</a> 193 mm</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Hourly precipitation
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yeonggwang" title="Yeonggwang">Yeonggwang</a> 59.5 mm</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Yangsan" title="Yangsan">Yangsan</a> 50 mm</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ulsan" title="Ulsan">Ulsan</a> 48.3 mm</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Suncheon" title="Suncheon">Suncheon</a> 47.5 mm</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gimhae" title="Gimhae">Gimhae</a> 35.5 mm</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="August_20">August 20</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_South_Korea_floods&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: August 20">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The rainfall weakened on August 19, so there was less precipitation except in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jeju_island" title="Jeju island">Jeju island</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gangwon_Province_(South_Korea)" title="Gangwon Province (South Korea)">Gangwon</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gyeongbuk" title="Gyeongbuk">Gyeongbuk</a>. But it strengthened the next day, so rain fell in Jeju island and <a href="/wiki/South_Sea_(Korea)" title="South Sea (Korea)">South Sea</a> seaside. The rain was less than on August 18, but many accidents occurred because the ground was weakened.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="August_21">August 21</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_South_Korea_floods&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: August 21">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In August 21, the rain was expanded to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gyeonggi" title="Gyeonggi">Gyeonggi</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gangwon_Province_(South_Korea)" title="Gangwon Province (South Korea)">Gangwon</a> because the rainfall moved north. A reservoir in <a href="/wiki/Yeongcheon" title="Yeongcheon">Yeongcheon</a> was destroyed because of the rain, and in <a href="/wiki/Daegu" title="Daegu">Daegu</a>, one child disappeared, and another died because the river overflowed.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="August_25">August 25</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_South_Korea_floods&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: August 25">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Flooding_of_the_bus">Flooding of the bus</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_South_Korea_floods&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Flooding of the bus">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At 14:20, the bus in the <a href="/wiki/Masanhappo-gu" title="Masanhappo-gu">Masanhappo-gu</a> was flooded. It was going to the <i>Jindong-Pachulso</i>, but the road was restricted because of the rain, so the bus took the detour to farm road. In the process of detouring, the bus fell into the overflowed stream. Seven people on the bus perished.</p>
