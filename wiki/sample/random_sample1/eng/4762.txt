<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Louise_Monot&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Known as the face of the French cosmetics company <a href="/wiki/Bourjois" title="Bourjois">Bourjois</a>, she is also known for portraying one of the schoolgirls, Marine Lavor, on the television series, <i><a href="/wiki/La_vie_devant_nous" title="La vie devant nous">La vie devant nous</a></i>. Monot also appeared in the film <i><a href="/wiki/OSS_117:_Lost_in_Rio" title="OSS 117: Lost in Rio">OSS 117: Lost in Rio</a></i>.</p>
<p>She is a cast-member on the <a href="/wiki/Amazon_Studios" title="Amazon Studios">Amazon Studios</a> <a href="/wiki/Science_fiction" title="Science fiction">science fiction</a> <a href="/wiki/Drama" title="Drama">drama</a> series, <i><a href="/wiki/The_After" title="The After">The After</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Louise_Monot&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She's in couple with French actor <a href="/wiki/Samir_Boitard" title="Samir Boitard">Samir Boitard</a> since 2014. In October 2016, they announce waiting for their first child via the social networks.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
