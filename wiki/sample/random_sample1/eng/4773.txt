<p><b>Luca Cigarini</b> (<small>Italian pronunciation: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Italian" title="Help:IPA for Italian">[ˈluːka tʃiɡaˈriːni]</a></span>; born 20 June 1986) is an Italian professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays for <a href="/wiki/U.C._Sampdoria" title="U.C. Sampdoria">Sampdoria</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Midfielder" title="Midfielder">midfielder</a>.</p>
<p>He is nicknamed <i>il Professore</i> (The Professor) due to his intelligent reading of the game. The Italian sports newspaper <i><a href="/wiki/La_Gazzetta_dello_Sport" title="La Gazzetta dello Sport">La Gazzetta dello Sport</a></i> has also stated that Cigarini has a similar vision of the game to that of fellow Italian midfielder <a href="/wiki/Andrea_Pirlo" title="Andrea Pirlo">Andrea Pirlo</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Luca_Cigarini&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Parma">Parma</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Luca_Cigarini&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Parma">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>He was one of the notable youth products in the 2005 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Parma_F.C." title="Parma F.C.">Parma</a> squad, alongside <a href="/wiki/Daniele_Dessena" title="Daniele Dessena">Daniele Dessena</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Atalanta">Atalanta</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Luca_Cigarini&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Atalanta">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After Parma was relegated to Serie B in June 2008, he was sold to <a href="/wiki/Atalanta_B.C." title="Atalanta B.C.">Atalanta</a> in <a href="/wiki/Co-ownership_(football)" title="Co-ownership (football)">join-ownership bid</a>, for €4.5 million on 11 July.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Napoli">Napoli</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Luca_Cigarini&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Napoli">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 3 July 2009 <a href="/wiki/S.S.C._Napoli" title="S.S.C. Napoli">Napoli</a> signed the talented and hard-working playmaker from <a href="/wiki/Atalanta_B.C." title="Atalanta B.C.">Atalanta</a> on a five-year deal for €10.5 million.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Atalanta paid Parma €3.5 million plus half the registration rights of <a href="/wiki/Alessio_Manzoni" title="Alessio Manzoni">Alessio Manzoni</a> to sign Cigarini outright.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 2 August 2010, <a href="/wiki/S.S.C._Napoli" title="S.S.C. Napoli">Napoli</a> sent Cigarini to <a href="/wiki/Sevilla_FC" title="Sevilla FC">Sevilla FC</a> on a loan deal for the 2010-2011 season for €292,500,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> upon conclusion of the season, Sevilla will have the option to purchase the midfielder for €7 million.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (June 2011)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Return_to_Atalanta">Return to Atalanta</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Luca_Cigarini&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Return to Atalanta">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Cigarini returned to Atalanta on loan in summer 2011 for free.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> On 4 July 2012 the loan was renewed for €1.25 million loan fee.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 5 July 2013, after spending two seasons on loan with <a href="/wiki/Atalanta_B.C." title="Atalanta B.C.">Atalanta</a>, a co-ownership deal was agreed to with <a href="/wiki/S.S.C._Napoli" title="S.S.C. Napoli">Napoli</a> for €2.75 million.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Napoli2014bilancio_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-Napoli2014bilancio-9">[9]</a></sup> Cigarini continued to play for <a href="/wiki/Atalanta_B.C." title="Atalanta B.C.">Atalanta</a> for the upcoming <a href="/wiki/2013%E2%80%9314_Serie_A" title="2013–14 Serie A">2013–14 Serie A</a> season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> Cigarini scored his first goal of the season against <a href="/wiki/S.S._Lazio" title="S.S. Lazio">Lazio</a> on 20 October. In June 2014 Atalanta acquired Cigarini outright for €2.35 million.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Napoli2014bilancio_9-1"><a href="#cite_note-Napoli2014bilancio-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> Atalanta paid €5.75 million transfer fee in total to re-acquire Cigarini.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Sampdoria">Sampdoria</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Luca_Cigarini&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Sampdoria">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After spending five years with Atalanta, Cigarini made the switch from <a href="/wiki/Bergamo" title="Bergamo">Bergamo</a> to <a href="/wiki/Genoa" title="Genoa">Genoa</a>, signing a permanent deal with <a href="/wiki/U.C._Sampdoria" title="U.C. Sampdoria">Sampdoria.</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Luca_Cigarini&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On 7 September 2007, Cigarini scored his first ever goal (which was also the winning goal) with the <a href="/wiki/Italy_national_under-21_football_team" title="Italy national under-21 football team">Italy U-21</a> squad in a 2–1 victory over the <a href="/wiki/Faroe_Islands_national_football_team" title="Faroe Islands national football team">Faroe Islands</a> in a UEFA U-21 Qualifying match. He was also part of <a href="/wiki/Pierluigi_Casiraghi" title="Pierluigi Casiraghi">Pierluigi Casiraghi</a>'s Olympic team that won the <a href="/wiki/2008_Toulon_Tournament" title="2008 Toulon Tournament">2008 Toulon Tournament</a>. He also played for Italy in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2009_UEFA_European_Under-21_Football_Championship" title="2009 UEFA European Under-21 Football Championship">2009 UEFA European Under-21 Football Championship</a>.</p>
<p>Cigarini received his first call-up to the <a href="/wiki/Italy_national_football_team" title="Italy national football team">senior</a> team on 30 August 2010. New coach <a href="/wiki/Cesare_Prandelli" title="Cesare Prandelli">Cesare Prandelli</a> named him in the squad for the upcoming <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Euro_2012_qualifying" title="Euro 2012 qualifying">Euro 2012 qualifiers</a> as an injury replacement for <a href="/wiki/Claudio_Marchisio" title="Claudio Marchisio">Claudio Marchisio</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
