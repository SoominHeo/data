<p><b>Lucas Daniel Romero</b> (born April 18, 1994) is an <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentine</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> <a href="/wiki/Midfielder" title="Midfielder">midfielder</a> who plays for <a href="/wiki/Cruzeiro_Esporte_Clube" title="Cruzeiro Esporte Clube">Cruzeiro</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Campeonato_Brasileiro_S%C3%A9rie_A" title="Campeonato Brasileiro Série A">Campeonato Brasileiro Série A</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lucas_Romero&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Romero was born and raised in a humble neighborhood in <a href="/wiki/Loma_Hermosa" title="Loma Hermosa">Loma Hermosa</a>, <a href="/wiki/Greater_Buenos_Aires" title="Greater Buenos Aires">Greater Buenos Aires</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> He started playing youth football in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Club_Atl%C3%A9tico_V%C3%A9lez_S%C3%A1rsfield" title="Club Atlético Vélez Sársfield">Vélez Sársfield</a> when he was 7 years old.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lucas_Romero&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Romero made his debut for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Club_Atl%C3%A9tico_V%C3%A9lez_S%C3%A1rsfield" title="Club Atlético Vélez Sársfield">Vélez Sársfield</a> in 2012 at 18 years of age, coming on as a substitute in a 0–0 draw with <a href="/wiki/Estudiantes_de_La_Plata" title="Estudiantes de La Plata">Estudiantes de La Plata</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-inicial12_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-inicial12-4">[4]</a></sup> In his first season with the first team, Romero helped Vélez to win the <a href="/wiki/2012%E2%80%9313_Argentine_Primera_Divisi%C3%B3n_season#Torneo_Inicial" title="2012–13 Argentine Primera División season">2012 Inicial</a> championship, playing 13 games (including 4 as a starter replacing injured <a href="/wiki/Francisco_Cerro" title="Francisco Cerro">Francisco Cerro</a>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-inicial12_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-inicial12-4">[4]</a></sup> The midfielder also entered the field in Vélez's victory over <a href="/wiki/Newell%27s_Old_Boys" title="Newell's Old Boys">Newell's Old Boys</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2012%E2%80%9313_Argentine_Primera_Divisi%C3%B3n_season#Superfinal" title="2012–13 Argentine Primera División season">2012–13 Superfinal</a> and was a starter in his team's victory over <a href="/wiki/Arsenal_de_Sarand%C3%AD" title="Arsenal de Sarandí">Arsenal de Sarandí</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2013_Supercopa_Argentina" title="2013 Supercopa Argentina">2013 Supercopa Argentina</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lucas_Romero&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Romero played with the <a href="/wiki/Argentina_national_under-20_football_team" title="Argentina national under-20 football team">Argentina national under-20 football team</a> the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2013_South_American_Youth_Championship" title="2013 South American Youth Championship">2013 South American Youth Championship</a>, in which his team was eliminated in the first round.</p>
