<p><b>Lucas Zen</b> (born June 17, 1991 in Rio de Janeiro), is a Brazilian <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Defensive_midfielder" title="Defensive midfielder">defensive midfielder</a>. He currently plays for <a href="/wiki/Paysandu_Sport_Club" title="Paysandu Sport Club">Paysandu</a>, on loan from <a href="/wiki/Botafogo_de_Futebol_e_Regatas" title="Botafogo de Futebol e Regatas">Botafogo</a>.</p>
