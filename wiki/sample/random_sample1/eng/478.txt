<p><b><a href="/wiki/Albania" title="Albania">Albania</a></b> competed at the <a href="/wiki/2014_Winter_Olympics" title="2014 Winter Olympics">2014 Winter Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Sochi" title="Sochi">Sochi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a> from 7 to 23 February 2014. The team consisted of two athletes competing in <a href="/wiki/Alpine_skiing" title="Alpine skiing">alpine skiing</a> and for the first time a female athlete.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-a_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-a-4">[4]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Alpine_skiing">Alpine skiing</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Albania_at_the_2014_Winter_Olympics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Alpine skiing">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Alpine_skiing_at_the_2014_Winter_Olympics" title="Alpine skiing at the 2014 Winter Olympics">Alpine skiing at the 2014 Winter Olympics</a></div>
<p>According to the final quota allocation released on January 20, 2014, Albania had two athletes in qualification position.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-quota_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-quota-5">[5]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Erjon_Tola" title="Erjon Tola">Erjon Tola</a> was scheduled to compete in his third consecutive games, but had to withdraw because he broke his arm in training.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>On February 18, <a href="/wiki/Suela_M%C3%ABhilli" title="Suela Mëhilli">Suela Mëhilli</a> finished the giant slalom race in 60th position (out of 74 competitors who finished).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> She did not finish the slalom race after starting the first run.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
