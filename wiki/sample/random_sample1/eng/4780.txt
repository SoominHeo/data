<p><b>Luco dei Marsi</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/Town" title="Town">town</a> in the Province of <a href="/wiki/L%27Aquila" title="L'Aquila">L'Aquila</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Abruzzo" title="Abruzzo">Abruzzo</a> region of <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>. It is part of the <a href="/wiki/Marsica" title="Marsica">Marsica</a>.</p>
<p>The town was probably founded by the Roman Emperor <a href="/wiki/Claudius" title="Claudius">Claudius</a> to house workers in the drying of the <i>Lacus Fucinus</i> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lake_Fucino" title="Lake Fucino">Lake Fucino</a>). The name derives from a nearby wood, <i><a href="/wiki/Lucus_Angitiae" title="Lucus Angitiae">Lucus Angitiae</a></i>, "Sacred Grove of <a href="/wiki/Angitia" title="Angitia">Angitia</a>," referring to a divine sorceress of the <a href="/wiki/Marsi" title="Marsi">Marsi</a> Italic tribe.</p>
<p>During the Middle Ages it was a fief of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/D%27Avalos" title="D'Avalos">d'Avalos</a> and then of the <a href="/wiki/Colonna_family" title="Colonna family">Colonna family</a></p>
