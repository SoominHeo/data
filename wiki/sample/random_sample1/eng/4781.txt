<p>The <b>Municipality of Lukovica</b> (<a href="/wiki/Slovene_language" title="Slovene language">Slovene</a>: <span lang="sl" xml:lang="sl"><i>Občina Lukovica</i></span>) is a municipality in the eastern part of the <a href="/wiki/Upper_Carniola" title="Upper Carniola">Upper Carniola</a> region in <a href="/wiki/Slovenia" title="Slovenia">Slovenia</a>. It lies on the main road from <a href="/wiki/Ljubljana" title="Ljubljana">Ljubljana</a> to <a href="/wiki/Celje" title="Celje">Celje</a> and has been an independent municipality since 1995.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
