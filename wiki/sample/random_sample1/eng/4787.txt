<p><b>Lukian Araújo de Almeida</b> or simply <b>Lukian</b> (born September 21, 1991 in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nova_Rosalandia" title="Nova Rosalandia">Nova Rosalandia</a>), is a <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazilian</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Forward_(association_football)#Striker" title="Forward (association football)">striker</a> for <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Busan_IPark_Football_Club&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Busan IPark Football Club (page does not exist)">Busan IPark FC</a> in the <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> professional league, <a href="/wiki/K_League_Challenge" title="K League Challenge">K League Challenge</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lukian_Ara%C3%BAjo_de_Almeida&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He joined <a href="/wiki/K_League_Challenge" title="K League Challenge">K League Challenge</a> side <a href="/wiki/Bucheon_FC_1995" title="Bucheon FC 1995">Bucheon FC</a> in July 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> He scored 4 goals in 22 games that season for Bucheon 1995.</p>
