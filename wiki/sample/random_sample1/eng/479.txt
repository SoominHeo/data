<p><b><a href="/wiki/Georgia_(country)" title="Georgia (country)">Georgia</a></b> competed at the <a href="/wiki/2014_Winter_Olympics" title="2014 Winter Olympics">2014 Winter Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Sochi" title="Sochi">Sochi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a> from 7–23 February 2014. The Georgian team consists of four athletes in two sports.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-a_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-a-4">[4]</a></sup> The team also consists of seven officials.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> Georgia's government only decided to send athletes and coaches but no government officials due to the breakup in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Georgia_(country)%E2%80%93Russia_relations" title="Georgia (country)–Russia relations">relations with Russia</a> in the aftermath of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2008_South_Ossetia_War" title="2008 South Ossetia War">August 2008 war</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-a_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-a-4">[4]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Flagbearer">Flagbearer</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Georgia_at_the_2014_Winter_Olympics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Flagbearer">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Originally <a href="/wiki/Figure_skating" title="Figure skating">figure skater</a> <a href="/wiki/Elene_Gedevanishvili" title="Elene Gedevanishvili">Elene Gedevanishvili</a> was supposed to be the flagbearer.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> However, because of training schedules, Gedevanishvili was scheduled to arrive after the <a href="/wiki/2014_Winter_Olympics_opening_ceremony" title="2014 Winter Olympics opening ceremony">opening ceremony</a>. Therefore, <a href="/wiki/Alpine_skiing" title="Alpine skiing">alpine skier</a> <a href="/wiki/Nino_Tsiklauri" title="Nino Tsiklauri">Nino Tsiklauri</a> was selected to replace her.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-b_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-b-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
