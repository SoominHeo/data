<p><b>Ruth Vega Fernandez</b> (née Ruth Fernández Martín; born 12 April 1977 on <a href="/wiki/Canary_Islands" title="Canary Islands">Canary Islands</a>) is a Swedish-Spanish actress. She is known for her roles in <i><a href="/wiki/Call_Girl_(2012_film)" title="Call Girl (2012 film)">Call Girl</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Gentlemen_(2014_film)" title="Gentlemen (2014 film)">Gentlemen</a></i></p>
<p>She has studied at <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Balettakademien&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Balettakademien (page does not exist)">Balettakademien</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
