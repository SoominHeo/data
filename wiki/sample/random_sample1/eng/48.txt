<p><b>November 11</b> is the 315th day of the year (316th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 50 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Wednesday, Friday or Sunday (58 in 400 years each) than on Monday or Tuesday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Thursday or Saturday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=November_11&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/308" title="308">308</a> – At <a href="/wiki/Carnuntum" title="Carnuntum">Carnuntum</a>, Emperor <i>emeritus</i> <a href="/wiki/Diocletian" title="Diocletian">Diocletian</a> confers with <a href="/wiki/Galerius" title="Galerius">Galerius</a>, <i><a href="/wiki/Augustus_(honorific)" title="Augustus (honorific)">Augustus</a></i> of the East, and <a href="/wiki/Maximian" title="Maximian">Maximianus</a>, the recently returned former <i>Augustus</i> of the West, in an attempt to end the <a href="/wiki/Civil_wars_of_the_Tetrarchy" title="Civil wars of the Tetrarchy">civil wars of the Tetrarchy</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1100" title="1100">1100</a> – <a href="/wiki/Henry_I_of_England" title="Henry I of England">Henry I of England</a> marries <a href="/wiki/Matilda_of_Scotland" title="Matilda of Scotland">Matilda of Scotland</a>, the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Malcolm_III_of_Scotland" title="Malcolm III of Scotland">Malcolm III of Scotland</a> and a direct descendant of the Saxon king <a href="/wiki/Edmund_Ironside" title="Edmund Ironside">Edmund Ironside</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1215" title="1215">1215</a> – The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fourth_Lateran_Council" title="Fourth Lateran Council">Fourth Lateran Council</a> meets, defining the doctrine of <a href="/wiki/Transubstantiation" title="Transubstantiation">transubstantiation</a>, the process by which bread and wine are, by that doctrine, said to transform into the body and blood of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Christ" title="Christ">Christ</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1500" title="1500">1500</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Granada_(1500)" title="Treaty of Granada (1500)">Treaty of Granada</a>: <a href="/wiki/Louis_XII_of_France" title="Louis XII of France">Louis XII of France</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ferdinand_II_of_Aragon" title="Ferdinand II of Aragon">Ferdinand II of Aragon</a> agree to divide the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Naples" title="Kingdom of Naples">Kingdom of Naples</a> between them.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1620" title="1620">1620</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Mayflower_Compact" title="Mayflower Compact">Mayflower Compact</a> is signed in what is now <a href="/wiki/Provincetown_Harbor" title="Provincetown Harbor">Provincetown Harbor</a> near <a href="/wiki/Cape_Cod" title="Cape Cod">Cape Cod</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1634" title="1634">1634</a> – Following pressure from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Anglican" title="Anglican">Anglican</a> bishop <a href="/wiki/John_Atherton" title="John Atherton">John Atherton</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Irish_House_of_Commons" title="Irish House of Commons">Irish House of Commons</a> passes <i>An Act for the Punishment for the Vice of <a href="/wiki/Buggery" title="Buggery">Buggery</a></i>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1673" title="1673">1673</a> – Second <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Khotyn_(1673)" title="Battle of Khotyn (1673)">Battle of Khotyn</a> in <a href="/wiki/Ukraine" title="Ukraine">Ukraine</a>: <a href="/wiki/Polish%E2%80%93Lithuanian_Commonwealth" title="Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth">Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth</a> forces under the command of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jan_III_Sobieski" title="Jan III Sobieski">Jan Sobieski</a> defeat the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman</a> army. In this battle, rockets made by <a href="/wiki/Kazimierz_Siemienowicz" title="Kazimierz Siemienowicz">Kazimierz Siemienowicz</a> are successfully used.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1675" title="1675">1675</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gottfried_Leibniz" title="Gottfried Leibniz">Gottfried Leibniz</a> demonstrates <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Integral_calculus" title="Integral calculus">integral calculus</a> for the first time to find the area under the graph of <i>y</i> = <i>ƒ</i>(<i>x</i>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1724" title="1724">1724</a> – <a href="/wiki/Joseph_Blake_(criminal)" title="Joseph Blake (criminal)">Joseph Blake</a>, alias Blueskin, a highwayman known for attacking "Thief-Taker General" (and thief) <a href="/wiki/Jonathan_Wild" title="Jonathan Wild">Jonathan Wild</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Old_Bailey" title="Old Bailey">Old Bailey</a>, is hanged in London.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1750" title="1750">1750</a> – <a href="/wiki/Lhasa_riot_of_1750" title="Lhasa riot of 1750">Riots break out</a> in <a href="/wiki/Lhasa" title="Lhasa">Lhasa</a> after the murder of the <a href="/wiki/Tibet" title="Tibet">Tibetan</a> regent.</li>
<li>1750 – The F.H.C. Society, also known as the <a href="/wiki/Flat_Hat_Club" title="Flat Hat Club">Flat Hat Club</a>, is formed at Raleigh Tavern, <a href="/wiki/Williamsburg,_Virginia" title="Williamsburg, Virginia">Williamsburg, Virginia</a>. It is the first college fraternity.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1778" title="1778">1778</a> – <a href="/wiki/Cherry_Valley_massacre" title="Cherry Valley massacre">Cherry Valley massacre</a>: <a href="/wiki/Loyalist_(American_Revolution)" title="Loyalist (American Revolution)">Loyalists</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seneca_nation" title="Seneca nation">Seneca</a> Indian forces attack a fort and village in eastern New York during the <a href="/wiki/American_Revolutionary_War" title="American Revolutionary War">American Revolutionary War</a>, killing more than forty civilians and soldiers.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1805" title="1805">1805</a> – <a href="/wiki/Napoleonic_Wars" title="Napoleonic Wars">Napoleonic Wars</a>: <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_D%C3%BCrenstein" title="Battle of Dürenstein">Battle of Dürenstein</a>: Eight thousand French troops attempt to slow the retreat of a vastly superior Russian and Austrian force.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1813" title="1813">1813</a> – <a href="/wiki/War_of_1812" title="War of 1812">War of 1812</a>: <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Crysler%27s_Farm" title="Battle of Crysler's Farm">Battle of Crysler's Farm</a>: British and Canadian forces defeat a larger American force, causing the Americans to abandon their Saint Lawrence campaign.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1831" title="1831">1831</a> – In <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jerusalem,_Virginia" title="Jerusalem, Virginia">Jerusalem, Virginia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nat_Turner" title="Nat Turner">Nat Turner</a> is hanged after inciting a violent <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Slave_uprising" title="Slave uprising">slave uprising</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1839" title="1839">1839</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Virginia_Military_Institute" title="Virginia Military Institute">Virginia Military Institute</a> is founded in <a href="/wiki/Lexington,_Virginia" title="Lexington, Virginia">Lexington, Virginia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1864" title="1864">1864</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Civil_War" title="American Civil War">American Civil War</a>: General <a href="/wiki/William_Tecumseh_Sherman" title="William Tecumseh Sherman">William Tecumseh Sherman</a> begins burning <a href="/wiki/Atlanta_in_the_American_Civil_War" title="Atlanta in the American Civil War">Atlanta</a> to the ground in preparation for his <a href="/wiki/Sherman%27s_March_to_the_Sea" title="Sherman's March to the Sea">march to the sea</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1865" title="1865">1865</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Sinchula" title="Treaty of Sinchula">Treaty of Sinchula</a> is signed whereby <a href="/wiki/Bhutan" title="Bhutan">Bhutan</a> cedes the areas east of the <a href="/wiki/Teesta_River" title="Teesta River">Teesta River</a> to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/British_East_India_Company" title="British East India Company">British East India Company</a>.</li>
