<p><i><b>Roommate</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko">룸메이트</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korean" title="South Korean">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Reality_television" title="Reality television">reality show</a>; formerly part of <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Broadcasting_System" title="Seoul Broadcasting System">SBS</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Good_Sunday" title="Good Sunday">Good Sunday</a></i> lineup. It was first scheduled to be aired on April 20, 2014, but due to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sinking_of_the_MV_Sewol" title="Sinking of the MV Sewol">Sewol ferry tragedy</a> and the temporary broadcast halt, the airing date was pushed to May 4, 2014. The show features eleven celebrities living together in a <i>share house</i>, sharing common spaces such as the kitchen, living room, and washrooms, as well as household tasks.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The house features sixty cameras and five bedrooms.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Six of the original eleven roommates have left the show ending the first season of <i>Roommate</i> on September 14, 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Seven new roommates have been added for a brand new second season beginning September 21, 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> With the return of <i><a href="/wiki/K-pop_Star" title="K-pop Star">K-pop Star</a></i> to <i><a href="/wiki/Good_Sunday" title="Good Sunday">Good Sunday</a></i>, <i>Roommate</i> moved to airing every Tuesday at 11:15 pm as a stand-alone program starting November 25, 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tues_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-tues-5">[5]</a></sup> The second season was cancelled due to low ratings with the final episode airing on April 14, 2015. It was confirmed that there will be no season 3.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Season_1">Season 1</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Roommate_(TV_series)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Season 1">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The original roommates are <a href="/wiki/Lee_So-ra_(model)" title="Lee So-ra (model)">Lee So-ra</a>, <a href="/wiki/Park_Bom" title="Park Bom">Park Bom</a> and Song Ga-yeon, <a href="/wiki/Jo_Se-ho" title="Jo Se-ho">Jo Se-ho</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lee_Dong-wook" title="Lee Dong-wook">Lee Dong-wook</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nana_(singer)" title="Nana (singer)">Nana</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hong_Soo-hyun" title="Hong Soo-hyun">Hong Soo-hyun</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Park_Chan-yeol" title="Park Chan-yeol">Park Chan-yeol</a> and <a href="/wiki/Shin_Sung-woo" title="Shin Sung-woo">Shin Sung-woo</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Park_Min-woo_(actor)" title="Park Min-woo (actor)">Park Min-woo</a> and <a href="/wiki/Seo_Kang-joon" title="Seo Kang-joon">Seo Kang-joon</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> This mix of singers, actors/actresses, comedians, models, DJs and martial artists who have little reality and variety program experience is expected to create new undiscovered characters and laughter.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> On July 24, 2014, Baek Jung Ryeol of SBS announced that Park Bom has not participated in recordings since July 11, 2014, due to her amphetamine scandal and has officially left the program, with her final episode airing on July 27, 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup> On August 20, 2014, it was announced that Lee So-ra will leave the program due to the schedule conflicts.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup> On August 27, 2014, Song Ga-yeon was also confirmed to leave the show to focus on her career.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup> On September 1, 2014, Shin Sung-woo announced on Twitter he will be leaving the show.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup> On September 11, 2014, it was announced that Hong Soo-hyun will also be leaving to focus on her acting career.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup> On September 12, 2014, it was announced that Park Chan-yeol will also be leaving the show due to his busy schedule.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup> With more than half of the original members leaving the show, the producers announced a brand new season with new members alongside the remaining members of season 1.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Season_2">Season 2</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Roommate_(TV_series)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Season 2">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A new season of <i>Roommate</i> was announced following the departure of several members.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-20"><a href="#cite_note-20">[20]</a></sup> This season's concept is <i>Home share, Whole share</i>, where the members not only live together, but learn and share from each other. The new cast will consist of the remaining five roommates and seven new roommates. The new members feature a broad spectrum of the celebrities ranging from veteran stars to foreigners to rookie stars. The new roommates are <a href="/wiki/Sunny_(singer)" title="Sunny (singer)">Sunny</a>, <a href="/wiki/Joon_Park" title="Joon Park">Park Joon-hyung</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jackson_Wang" title="Jackson Wang">Jackson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ryohei_Otani" title="Ryohei Otani">Ryohei Otani</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lee_Guk-joo" title="Lee Guk-joo">Lee Guk-joo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bae_Jong-ok" title="Bae Jong-ok">Bae Jong-ok</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Heo_Youngji" title="Heo Youngji">Heo Young-ji</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-21"><a href="#cite_note-21">[21]</a></sup> With the return of <i><a href="/wiki/K-pop_Star" title="K-pop Star">K-pop Star</a></i> to <i>Good Sunday</i>, it was announced that <i>Roommate</i> will move to Tuesday nights airing at 11:15 pm replacing <i><a href="/wiki/Magic_Eye_(TV_series)" title="Magic Eye (TV series)">Magic Eye</a></i> beginning November 25, 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tues_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-tues-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>Roommate season 2 ended on April 14, 2015, after 26 episodes. Although preparations were being made for a new season, Roommate was confirmed to be cancelled due to low ratings; the last episode aired on April 14, 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-22"><a href="#cite_note-22">[22]</a></sup></p>

<th>21</th>
<td>September 21, 2014</td>
<td><b>"Which Star Are You From?"</b>
<p>As the new season of <i>Roommate</i> commences with new members, the group slowly begins to break the awkward and unfamiliarity by getting to know each other. The new members introduced themselves to the producers and the audiences first. The casts later arrive at their new home and are assigned to their respective rooms.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr style="text-align:center;">
<th>22</th>
<td>October 5, 2014</td>
<td><b>"me Will Like you."</b>
<p>In order to get to know each other better, the members draw maps of their brains and talk about their profession, characteristics and personal interests. Nana, Guk-joo and Se-ho leave home to Se-ho's parents' house to get food and on the way, they learnt that it was actually Nana's birthday while Dong-wook, Jong-ok, Joon-hyung, Ryohei and Min-woo take a historic tour around Seongbuk-gu with the "Roommate" team. Later, Jong-ok and Sunny set out to learn pilates. At night, the members gather to plan a surprise party for Nana before she arrives home.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr style="text-align:center;">
<th>23</th>
<td>October 12, 2014</td>
<td><b>"24 Hours Is Not Enough."</b>
<p>The episode starts off with Guk-joo cooking for her roommates and as they sit down to eat, Guk-joo talks about how she became a comedian. Later, the members go out for a picnic, playing games and doling out punishments to the losers. After the picnic, Dong-wook takes Ryohei, Jackson and Young-ji out for some shopping which led to Dong-wook being exhausted at the end. After the members return home, Song Ga-yeon pays a visit to their home. The episode ends with Jackson and Joon-hyung teaching the members English and acting out scenes by speaking English.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr style="text-align:center;">
<th>24</th>
<td>October 19, 2014</td>
<td><b>"Reply Old Love."</b>
<p>The episode begins with Sunny, Ryohei, Joon-hyung, and Jackson going on an outing to pick lotus roots which was planned to be their guests' dinner. When they return to the house, the group prepares dinner as the guests start to arrive. Joon-hyung’s cousin, Danny Ahn (one of the members of <a href="/wiki/G.o.d" title="G.o.d">g.o.d</a>), was the first to arrive followed by Ryohei's friend, Chae Yeon (a singer/actress). While the members were entertaining their guests, Sunny arrives with another guest, Yoona from Girls' Generation. Close to the end, Nana returns home to see that Cucumber's belly was swollen and the members immediately send her to a 24-hour veterinary hospital.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr style="text-align:center;">
<th>25</th>
<td>October 26, 2014</td>
<td><b>"How To Get Closer With Your Neighbors?"</b>
<p>Jong-ok, Joon-hyung, Jackson, Sunny and Ryohei head out to Bukjeong Village, a nearby neighborhood that is called the last shantytown in Seoul where they tour and meet the locals, while Nana, Se-ho, Guk-joo, and Kang-joon set out to the Finnish Ambassador’s home. After meeting their neighbors, the members gather together and decorate their rooms. For dinner, the members prepare fresh thornback ray and 5-year-old kimchi sent by Young-ji's uncle. Meanwhile, Guk-joo joins Baek Ji-young's performance on stage. At the end of the episode, Jong-ok teaches the members about acting and a few members act out the famous play of Shakespeare, Romeo and Juliet.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr style="text-align:center;">
<th>26</th>
<td>November 2, 2014</td>
<td><b>"Lee Soon-jae Visits Roommate."</b>
<p>Sunny and Jong-ok visit the theater where two very revered veteran actors were performing while Joon-hyung, Se-ho, Jackson, Nana, Guk-joo and Young-ji learn Taekkyeon, a traditional Korean martial art. After watching the performance, Sunny and Jong-ok went home with a guest, Lee Soon-jae. At dinner time, the roommates were all gathered at the dinner table sharing old memories of their past and bringing up old graduation photos. The next morning, Guk-joo and Young-ji are on breakfast duty while Jong-ok wakes up early in the morning to harvest some peppers that are grown in the backyard. Towards the end of the episode, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/G.o.d_(South_Korean_band)" title="G.o.d (South Korean band)">g.o.d</a> have their encore concert with close to 40,000 people in attendance, including fellow members of <i>Roommate</i>, Min-woo, Se-ho, and Kang-joon who came to show their support for Joon-hyung and g.o.d.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr style="text-align:center;">
<th>27</th>
<td>November 9, 2014</td>
<td><b>"Boys' Time vs Girls' Time."</b>
<p>A "Boys and Girls" outing. Nana, Sunny, and Guk-joo go out for a leisurely stroll that includes a tarot reading, while Ryohei, Joon-hyung, Jackson, and Se-ho decide to go skateboarding. Later, Se-ho invites trot singer Park Hyun-bin to give some singing lessons to Ryohei. Park Hyun-bin leaves on a good note and the roommates begin to start their night time routines. While everyone else seemed to be asleep, Se-ho, Guk-joo, and Jackson were wide awake. They decide to surprise Dong-wook at his filming site.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr style="text-align:center;">
<th>28</th>
<td>November 16, 2014</td>
<td><b>"Kimchi War."</b>
<p>Joon-hyung, Jackson, Dong-wook and Young-ji head to the market to pick up supplies when the <i>Roommate</i> members decide to make kimchi. Back at the house, the roommates receive the cabbage delivery and get to work peeling vegetables for the kimchi. Se-ho invites Choi Hong-man to help them with making kimchi. When eating dinner, the members gather around to talk about Hong-man's ideal type. Halfway through, Guk-joo's friend, trot goddess Hong Jin-young stops by for a visit. Meanwhile, Guk-joo appears on a radio show and was paid a surprise visit by Kang-joon.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr style="text-align:center;">
<th>29</th>
<td>November 25, 2014</td>
<td><b>"Family's Here."</b>
<p>Dong-wook, Jackson, Young-ji, Jong-ok, Min-woo and Nana leave to catch eels where the members get into a fun mud fight. Meanwhile, Joon-hyung, Guk-joo, Ryohei and Sunny leave to learn pansori. When they return home at night, Guk-joo brings her brother, Sunkyu, with her. When the members gather together and cook eels in their backyard, Goo Ha-ra from girl group Kara arrives. Young-ji gets tearful when Hara talks about her (Youngji) to her roommates. As the night gets deeper, the members and guests have a dance battle. At the end of the episode, a few members reenact their past auditions.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr style="text-align:center;">
<th>30</th>
<td>December 2, 2014</td>
<td><b>"We Won't Stop."</b>
<p>The episode starts off with Guk-joo preparing dinner for her roommates. After having their dinner, the members go upstairs to watch <i>Roommate</i> but were shortly interrupted by the doorbell. Apparently, Jong-ok’s best friends, actress/singer Yoon Hyun-sook and actress/model Byun Jung-soo have come to pay a visit. Later in the episode, Jackson, Ryohei and Se-ho join a marathon which requires the contestants to climb sixty floors of stairs in less than twenty minutes. Sunny, Nana and Guk-joo go along with them to cheer them on at the beginning of the race (at the starting point) and waited for them on the 60th floor. After that, the <i>Roommate</i> members go out for an outing. Several days later, Guk-joo and Kang-joon visit Jackson for the showcase of GOT7's first official album, in which Kang-joon played a prank on him.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr style="text-align:center;">
<th>31</th>
<td>December 9, 2014</td>
<td><b>"Between Beans And Music."</b>
<p>One of Ryohei's friends sent huge sacks of beans for the <i>Roommate</i> members which they decided to make tofu and noodles with. To do that, they need a millstone and a large pot which falls to Young-ji, Jackson, Se-ho, and Dong-wook to accomplish. The members who stayed at home helped with sorting out the good beans from the bad beans. During their tofu making, Se-ho gets a phone call from Hong Seok-chun who later pays a visit to the <i>Roommate</i> members and helps them out with making tofu. Later in the evening, Guk-joo gets tearful when she talks about her career and the support she gets from the family.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr style="text-align:center;">
