<p><b>Rongxian</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">容县</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Róngxiàn</span></i>; also called <b>Rong Xian</b> or <b>Rong County</b>) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/County_(People%27s_Republic_of_China)" title="County (People's Republic of China)">county</a> in <a href="/wiki/Yulin,_Guangxi" title="Yulin, Guangxi">Yulin</a>, in the autonomous region of <a href="/wiki/Guangxi" title="Guangxi">Guangxi</a>, in southern <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. Its population is approximately 700,000.<a class="external autonumber" href="http://www.chinatoday.com.cn/English/e2002/e20029/rongxian.htm" rel="nofollow">[1]</a></p>
<p>Duqiao Mountain (都峤山), located in Rongxian, is a famous <a href="/wiki/Taoism" title="Taoism">Taoist</a> sanctuary.<a class="external autonumber" href="http://www.chinatoday.com.cn/English/e2002/e20029/rongxian.htm" rel="nofollow">[2]</a> Other tourist sites include the Zhenwu Pavilion (真武阁).</p>
