<p><b>Rong'an County</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">融安县</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">融安縣</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Róng'ān Xiàn</span></i>; <a href="/wiki/Standard_Zhuang" title="Standard Zhuang">Standard Zhuang</a>: <span lang="za" xml:lang="za">Yungz'anh Yen</span>) is under the administration of <a href="/wiki/Liuzhou" title="Liuzhou">Liuzhou</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Guangxi_Zhuang_Autonomous_Region" title="Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region">Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region</a>, China. It borders the <a href="/wiki/Prefecture-level_city" title="Prefecture-level city">prefecture-level city</a> of <a href="/wiki/Guilin" title="Guilin">Guilin</a> to the east.</p>
