<p><b>Longyang Road Station</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">龙阳路站</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">龍陽路站</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Lóngyáng Lù Zhàn</span></i>) is the name of a station that serves both the <a href="/wiki/Shanghai_Maglev_Train" title="Shanghai Maglev Train">Shanghai Maglev Train</a> and Lines <a href="/wiki/Line_2,_Shanghai_Metro" title="Line 2, Shanghai Metro">2</a>, <a href="/wiki/Line_7,_Shanghai_Metro" title="Line 7, Shanghai Metro">7</a> and <a href="/wiki/Line_16,_Shanghai_Metro" title="Line 16, Shanghai Metro">Line 16</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Shanghai_Metro" title="Shanghai Metro">Shanghai Metro</a>. It provides a quick transfer between the metro system and the Maglev train to and from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pudong_International_Airport" title="Pudong International Airport">Pudong International Airport</a>.</p>
