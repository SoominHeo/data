<p><b>Ludivine Sagnier</b> is a French actress and model, who has appeared in films since 1989. She was nominated for two <a href="/wiki/C%C3%A9sar_Award" title="César Award">César Awards</a> for <a href="/wiki/C%C3%A9sar_Award_for_Best_Supporting_Actress" title="César Award for Best Supporting Actress">Best Supporting Actress</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Swimming_Pool_(film)" title="Swimming Pool (film)">Swimming Pool</a></i> (2003) and <i><a href="/wiki/A_Secret" title="A Secret">Un secret</a></i> (2007).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ludivine_Sagnier&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sagnier was born on 3 July 1979 in <a href="/wiki/La_Celle-Saint-Cloud" title="La Celle-Saint-Cloud">La Celle-Saint-Cloud</a>, in the <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/D%C3%A9partement" title="Département">département</a></i> of <a href="/wiki/Yvelines" title="Yvelines">Yvelines</a>, France; her mother is a retired secretary and her father is a professor of English at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Paris_University" title="Paris University">Paris University</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
