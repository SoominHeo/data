<p>The <b>Renault Trucks D</b> is a range of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Medium_duty_truck" title="Medium duty truck">medium duty trucks</a> for distribution manufactured by the French truckmaker Renault Trucks.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-AW_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-AW-1">[1]</a></sup> It was launched in 2013 to replace the Midlum, the Premium Distribution<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> and the Access.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Characteristics">Characteristics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Renault_Trucks_D&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Characteristics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft">

<p>The D incorporates automatic transmission as standard in all its versions and new comfort and security features.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-AW_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-AW-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Engines">Engines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Renault_Trucks_D&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Engines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The D offers two <a href="/wiki/European_emission_standards" title="European emission standards">Euro 6</a> engines, the DTI 5 and the DTI 8.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-AW_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-AW-1">[1]</a></sup> The DTI 5 is a 5 L inline-four engine with a power output of 210 hp (157 kW) and a torque of 800 N·m (590 lb·ft) or 240 hp (179 kW) and 900 N·m (660 lb·ft). The DTI 8 is an 8 L straight-six engine with a power output of 250 hp (186 kW) and a torque of 950 N·m (700 lb·ft); 280 hp (209 kW) and 1,050 N·m (770 lb·ft) or 320 hp (239 kW) and 1,200 N·m (890 lb·ft).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>Both engines includes an EGR system to reduce <a href="/wiki/NOx" title="NOx">NOx</a> emissions.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Range">Range</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Renault_Trucks_D&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Range">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The D range includes three models:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-AW_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-AW-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li><i>Renault Trucks D</i> (from 10 <a href="/wiki/Ton" title="Ton">t</a> to 18 t, 2.1 <a href="/wiki/Metre" title="Metre">metres</a> wide cab)</li>
<li><i>Renault Trucks D Wide</i> (from 16 t to 26 t, 2.3 metres wide cab)</li>
<li><i>Renault Trucks D Access</i> (low entry cab, from 18 t to 26 t)</li>
</ul>
