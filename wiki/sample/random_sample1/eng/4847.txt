<p><b>Le Chesnay</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Communes_of_France" title="Communes of France">commune</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Yvelines" title="Yvelines">Yvelines</a> <a href="/wiki/Departments_of_France" title="Departments of France">department</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%C3%8Ele-de-France_(region)" title="Île-de-France (region)">Île-de-France</a> <a href="/wiki/Regions_of_France" title="Regions of France">region</a> in north-central <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>. It is located in the western suburbs of <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>, 16.7 km (10.4 mi) from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kilometre_Zero" title="Kilometre Zero">center of Paris</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Le_Chesnay&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:212px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Le_Chesnay_%C3%89glise_Saint-Antoine2.JPG"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="2576" data-file-width="1932" height="280" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5c/Le_Chesnay_%C3%89glise_Saint-Antoine2.JPG/210px-Le_Chesnay_%C3%89glise_Saint-Antoine2.JPG" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5c/Le_Chesnay_%C3%89glise_Saint-Antoine2.JPG/315px-Le_Chesnay_%C3%89glise_Saint-Antoine2.JPG 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5c/Le_Chesnay_%C3%89glise_Saint-Antoine2.JPG/420px-Le_Chesnay_%C3%89glise_Saint-Antoine2.JPG 2x" width="210"/></a>
<div class="thumbcaption">
<div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Le_Chesnay_%C3%89glise_Saint-Antoine2.JPG" title="Enlarge"></a></div>
Saint-Antoine-de-Padoue</div>
</div>
</div>
<p>On 1 July 1815, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Napoleon_I_of_France" title="Napoleon I of France">Napoleon</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Grande_Arm%C3%A9e" title="Grande Armée">Grande Armée</a> fought its last battle in <a href="/wiki/Rocquencourt" title="Rocquencourt">Rocquencourt</a> and Le Chesnay. After the defeat of <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Waterloo" title="Battle of Waterloo">Waterloo</a> on 18 June 1815, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Emmanuel,_marquis_de_Grouchy" title="Emmanuel, marquis de Grouchy">Grouchy</a>'s army withdrew to <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> via <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Namur_(city)" title="Namur (city)">Namur</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dinant" title="Dinant">Dinant</a>, reaching Paris on 29 June, a few days before the <a href="/wiki/Prussia" title="Prussia">Prussians</a>, who camped at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Versailles_(city)" title="Versailles (city)">Versailles</a>.</p>
<p>While negotiating the final <a href="/wiki/Armistice" title="Armistice">armistice</a>, <a href="/wiki/R%C3%A9mi_Joseph_Isidore_Exelmans" title="Rémi Joseph Isidore Exelmans">Exelmans</a> was ordered to attack the Prussians at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Versailles_(city)" title="Versailles (city)">Versailles</a> on 1 July 1815. Under attack the Prussians retreated from Versailles and headed east, but were blocked by the French at <a href="/wiki/V%C3%A9lizy-Villacoublay" title="Vélizy-Villacoublay">Vélizy</a>. They failed to re-enter Versailles and headed for <a href="/wiki/Saint-Germain-en-Laye" title="Saint-Germain-en-Laye">Saint-Germain-en-Laye</a>. Their first squadron came under fire at the entrance of Rocquencourt and attempted to escape through the fields. They were forced into a small, narrow street in Le Chesnay and killed or captured. However, the main body of the Prussian army succeeded in reaching Saint-Germain. (From <a class="external text" href="http://www.crwflags.com/fotw/flags/fr-78-ro.html" rel="nofollow">Presentation of Rocquencourt</a>)</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transport">Transport</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Le_Chesnay&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Transport">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Le Chesnay is served by no station of the Paris Métro, RER, or suburban rail network. The closest station to Le Chesnay is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Versailles_%E2%80%93_Rive_Droite_(SNCF)" title="Versailles – Rive Droite (SNCF)">Versailles – Rive Droite</a> station on the <a href="/wiki/Transilien_Paris_%E2%80%93_Saint-Lazare" title="Transilien Paris – Saint-Lazare">Transilien Paris – Saint-Lazare</a> suburban rail line. This station is located in the neighboring city of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Versailles_(city)" title="Versailles (city)">Versailles</a>, 2 km (1.2 mi) from the town center of Le Chesnay.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="LDS_Temple">LDS Temple</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Le_Chesnay&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: LDS Temple">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In April 2017, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Church_of_Jesus_Christ_of_Latter-day_Saints" title="Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints">Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints</a> will open its first <a href="/wiki/Temple_(LDS_Church)" title="Temple (LDS Church)">temple</a> in France to the public prior to its dedication in May.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The temple is located in Le Chesnay with grounds abutting the gardens of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ch%C3%A2teau_de_Versailles" title="Château de Versailles">Château de Versailles</a>.</p>
