<p><b>Lương Xuân Trường</b> (born 28 April 1995) is a <a href="/wiki/Vietnam" title="Vietnam">Vietnamese</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Midfielder#Central_midfielder" title="Midfielder">Central midfielder</a> for <a href="/wiki/Gangwon_FC" title="Gangwon FC">Gangwon FC</a>, on loan from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/V-League_(Vietnam)" title="V-League (Vietnam)">V-League (Vietnam)</a> club <a href="/wiki/Ho%C3%A0ng_Anh_Gia_Lai_F.C." title="Hoàng Anh Gia Lai F.C.">Hoàng Anh Gia Lai F.C.</a></p>
<p>Xuân Trường is a product of <a href="/wiki/Hoang_Anh_Gia_Lai_%E2%80%93_Arsenal_JMG_Academy" title="Hoang Anh Gia Lai – Arsenal JMG Academy">HAGL – Arsenal JMG Academy</a> and was promoted to <a href="/wiki/Ho%C3%A0ng_Anh_Gia_Lai_F.C." title="Hoàng Anh Gia Lai F.C.">Hoàng Anh Gia Lai F.C.</a> first team in 2015, he was also given the captain arm band. Xuân Trường is praised for his vision, passing and free-kick ability.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=L%C6%B0%C6%A1ng_Xu%C3%A2n_Tr%C6%B0%E1%BB%9Dng&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born on 28 April 1995 in Ỷ La, <a href="/wiki/Tuy%C3%AAn_Quang" title="Tuyên Quang">Tuyên Quang</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tuy%C3%AAn_Quang" title="Tuyên Quang">Tuyên Quang Province</a> in a family of 2 children, Xuân Trường is the second child, above him is an older sister.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> His father Lương Bách Chiến who works in Tuyên Quang Power Company is a former amateur footballer in the army team, he's also the manager of Tuyên Quang Power Company's male football team.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Xuân Trường started play football around the age of five, trained by his father. He represented Tuyên Quang Province at several youth football competitions.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=L%C6%B0%C6%A1ng_Xu%C3%A2n_Tr%C6%B0%E1%BB%9Dng&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Ho.C3.A0ng_Anh_Gia_Lai_F.C.">Hoàng Anh Gia Lai F.C.</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=L%C6%B0%C6%A1ng_Xu%C3%A2n_Tr%C6%B0%E1%BB%9Dng&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Hoàng Anh Gia Lai F.C.">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="HAGL_-_Arsenal_JMG_Academy">HAGL - Arsenal JMG Academy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=L%C6%B0%C6%A1ng_Xu%C3%A2n_Tr%C6%B0%E1%BB%9Dng&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: HAGL - Arsenal JMG Academy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In 2007, when playing for Tuyên Quang Province U-13 team at a youth football competition in <a href="/wiki/Hanoi" title="Hanoi">Hanoi</a>, Xuân Trường's team happened to be at the same hotel with <a href="/wiki/Hoang_Anh_Gia_Lai_%E2%80%93_Arsenal_JMG_Academy" title="Hoang Anh Gia Lai – Arsenal JMG Academy">HAGL – Arsenal JMG Academy</a>'s coaches who were there searching for candidates for the academy. Xuân Trường decided to take the trial and he got accepted into the academy.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>In November 2012, Xuân Trường along with <a href="/wiki/Nguy%E1%BB%85n_C%C3%B4ng_Ph%C6%B0%E1%BB%A3ng" title="Nguyễn Công Phượng">Nguyễn Công Phượng</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nguy%E1%BB%85n_Tu%E1%BA%A5n_Anh" title="Nguyễn Tuấn Anh">Nguyễn Tuấn Anh</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tr%E1%BA%A7n_H%E1%BB%AFu_%C4%90%C3%B4ng_Tri%E1%BB%81u" title="Trần Hữu Đông Triều">Trần Hữu Đông Triều</a> were four of the players from the academy received the invitation to train with <a href="/wiki/Arsenal_F.C." title="Arsenal F.C.">Arsenal F.C.</a> U-17 team.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> In the letter, Arsenal's performance supervisor, <a href="/wiki/Steve_Morrow" title="Steve Morrow">Steve Morrow</a> stated that coach <a href="/wiki/Ars%C3%A8ne_Wenger" title="Arsène Wenger">Arsène Wenger</a> was impressed with the academy's players when they beat Arsenal U-17 team in January.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Ho.C3.A0ng_Anh_Gia_Lai_first_team">Hoàng Anh Gia Lai first team</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=L%C6%B0%C6%A1ng_Xu%C3%A2n_Tr%C6%B0%E1%BB%9Dng&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Hoàng Anh Gia Lai first team">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Xuân Trường was promoted to <a href="/wiki/Ho%C3%A0ng_Anh_Gia_Lai_F.C." title="Hoàng Anh Gia Lai F.C.">Hoàng Anh Gia Lai F.C.</a> first team along with other academy's player from the first selection in 2015 when the club revolutionized their first team, most of their first team players were released, only a few was kept to guide young player from the academy.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> Xuân Trường was promoted to first team along with other academy's player from the first selection,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> he was also given the captain arm band.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
<p>Xuân Trường made his <a href="/wiki/V.League_1" title="V.League 1">V.League 1</a> debut on 4 January 2015 at the opening game of the season against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sanna_Khanh_Hoa_F.C." title="Sanna Khanh Hoa F.C.">Sanna Khanh Hoa F.C.</a>. He score his first league goal in this game as <a href="/wiki/Ho%C3%A0ng_Anh_Gia_Lai_F.C." title="Hoàng Anh Gia Lai F.C.">Hoàng Anh Gia Lai F.C.</a> won the game 4–2.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> In February, Xuân Trường suffered an injury in the seventh round game against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/XSKT_Can_Tho_F.C." title="XSKT Can Tho F.C.">XSKT Can Tho F.C.</a> which kept him out for 4 months,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup> he made his come back on June in the thirteenth round game against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Becamex_Binh_Duong_F.C." title="Becamex Binh Duong F.C.">Becamex Binh Duong F.C.</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Incheon_United_F.C.">Incheon United F.C.</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=L%C6%B0%C6%A1ng_Xu%C3%A2n_Tr%C6%B0%E1%BB%9Dng&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Incheon United F.C.">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 28 December 2015, Xuân Trường joined <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Incheon_United_F.C." title="Incheon United F.C.">Incheon United F.C.</a> on a 2 years loan deal with the alleged transfer fee of US$300,000. He became the first <a href="/wiki/Vietnamese_people" title="Vietnamese people">Vietnamese</a> player to play in <a href="/wiki/K_League" title="K League">K League</a> He made his very first debut game on 23 May 2016 versus <a href="/wiki/Gwangju_FC" title="Gwangju FC">Gwangju FC</a>, he was named in the starting eleven of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Incheon_United_F.C." title="Incheon United F.C.">Incheon United F.C.</a>, played 60 minutes and was replaced. After that <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Incheon_United_F.C." title="Incheon United F.C.">Incheon United F.C.</a> lost by 1 goal to nil.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Gangwon_FC">Gangwon FC</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=L%C6%B0%C6%A1ng_Xu%C3%A2n_Tr%C6%B0%E1%BB%9Dng&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Gangwon FC">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 21 December 2016, Xuân Trường moved from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Incheon_United_F.C." title="Incheon United F.C.">Incheon United</a> to <a href="/wiki/Gangwon_FC" title="Gangwon FC">Gangwon FC</a> on 1 year loan deal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=L%C6%B0%C6%A1ng_Xu%C3%A2n_Tr%C6%B0%E1%BB%9Dng&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Vietnam_under-19">Vietnam under-19</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=L%C6%B0%C6%A1ng_Xu%C3%A2n_Tr%C6%B0%E1%BB%9Dng&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Vietnam under-19">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Xuân Trường was the captain of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vietnam_national_under-19_football_team" title="Vietnam national under-19 football team">Vietnam U-19</a> at <a href="/wiki/2013_AFF_U-19_Youth_Championship" title="2013 AFF U-19 Youth Championship">2013 AFF U-19 Youth Championship</a>, he missed out on the final due to injury as his team lost to the host Indonesia U-19 in the penalty shoot-out.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup></p>
<p>Xuân Trường represented U-19 team<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup> in all of their competitions throughout 2013 to 2014 such as: <a href="/wiki/2014_AFC_U-19_Championship_qualification" title="2014 AFC U-19 Championship qualification">2014 AFC U-19 Championship qualification</a> where his team came out as Group F winner after they shockingly beat Australia U-19 (5-1),<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/2014_AFF_U-19_Youth_Championship" title="2014 AFF U-19 Youth Championship">2014 AFF U-19 Youth Championship</a> where they finished second, and <a href="/wiki/2014_AFC_U-19_Championship" title="2014 AFC U-19 Championship">2014 AFC U-19 Championship</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Vietnam_under-23">Vietnam under-23</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=L%C6%B0%C6%A1ng_Xu%C3%A2n_Tr%C6%B0%E1%BB%9Dng&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Vietnam under-23">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In February 2015, Xuân Trường was called up to <a href="/wiki/Vietnam_national_under-23_football_team" title="Vietnam national under-23 football team">Vietnam U-23</a> training camp for <a href="/wiki/2016_AFC_U-23_Championship_qualification" title="2016 AFC U-23 Championship qualification">2016 AFC U-23 Championship qualification</a> but was returned to his club later as the injury he suffered prior the training camp was much more serious than expected.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-20"><a href="#cite_note-20">[20]</a></sup></p>
