<p>The <b>2014 <a href="/wiki/Asian_Para_Games" title="Asian Para Games">Asian Para Games</a></b> also known as the <b>Second Asian Para Games</b> was a parallel sport event for Asian athletes with disability. The event was held in <a href="/wiki/Incheon" title="Incheon">Incheon</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>, opened 18 October 2014 and ended on 24 October 2014.</p>
<p>About 4,500 athletes from 41 countries competed in 23 sports. Sailing debuts for the first time since the <a href="/wiki/2006_FESPIC_Games" title="2006 FESPIC Games">2006 FESPIC Games</a>, <a href="/wiki/Malaysia" title="Malaysia">Malaysia</a> was dissolved.</p>
