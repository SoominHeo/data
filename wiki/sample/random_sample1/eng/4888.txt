<p><i><b>Nurse Angel Ririka SOS</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">ナースエンジェルりりかＳＯＳ</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Nāsu Enjeru Ririka EsuŌEsu</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a Japanese <a href="/wiki/Sh%C5%8Djo" title="Shōjo">shōjo</a> <a href="/wiki/Manga" title="Manga">manga</a> series created by music producer and television writer <a href="/wiki/Yasushi_Akimoto" title="Yasushi Akimoto">Yasushi Akimoto</a> and manga creator <a href="/wiki/Koi_Ikeno" title="Koi Ikeno">Koi Ikeno</a>. The manga was serialized in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ribon_Magazine" title="Ribon Magazine">Ribon Magazine</a></i> from January 1995 to June 1996, and subsequently collected into four bound volumes published by <a href="/wiki/Shueisha" title="Shueisha">Shueisha</a>. The story follows Ririka Moriya, an elementary school student who receives the power to transform into the legendary guardian, Nurse Angel. As Nurse Angel, she is the only person capable of protecting the Earth from world-destroying invaders.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Gallop_(studio)" title="Gallop (studio)">Studio Gallop</a> adapted the manga into a 35-episode <a href="/wiki/Anime" title="Anime">anime</a> TV series,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gallop_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-gallop-1">[1]</a></sup> which premiered in Japan in July 1995, airing on <a href="/wiki/TV_Tokyo" title="TV Tokyo">TV Tokyo</a> affiliates. Additional media produced include novelizations and a theatrical musical<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hakuhinkan_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-hakuhinkan-2">[2]</a></sup> that aired in digest form in the show's time slot after its conclusion.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Introduction">Introduction</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nurse_Angel_Ririka_SOS&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Introduction">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The heroine, Ririka Moriya, is a bubbly (but slightly clumsy) 4th grader who has a crush on Nozomu Kanō, a transfer student from <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. On her 10th birthday, Kanō (whom Ririka always calls Kanō-<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Senpai" title="Senpai">senpai</a>) gives her a magical cap, which allows Ririka to transform into the heavenly guardian, Nurse Angel. Kanō-senpai tells her that he is really named Kanon, and he comes from the planet Queen Earth, which has been overrun by the evil organization Dark Joker. Dark Joker has poisoned that world, and so he was sent to find the legendary Nurse Angel, who would save both <a href="/wiki/Earth" title="Earth">Earth</a> and Queen Earth from destruction by finding Flower of Life (命の花 <i>Inochi no Hana</i>). With help from Kanō-senpai and her friend and neighbor Seiya Uzaki, she soon learns to fight as Nurse Angel to protect her friends.</p>
