<p><b>November 1</b> is the 305th day of the year (306th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 60 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Tuesday, Thursday or Sunday (58 in 400 years each) than on Friday or Saturday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Monday or Wednesday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=November_1&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/365" title="365">365</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Alemanni" title="Alemanni">Alemanni</a> cross the <a href="/wiki/Rhine" title="Rhine">Rhine</a> and invade <a href="/wiki/Gaul" title="Gaul">Gaul</a>. Emperor <a href="/wiki/Valentinian_I" title="Valentinian I">Valentinian I</a> moves to Paris to command the army and defend the Gallic cities.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/996" title="996">996</a> – Emperor <a href="/wiki/Otto_III,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Otto III, Holy Roman Emperor">Otto III</a> issues a deed to Gottschalk, <a href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Archdiocese_of_Munich_and_Freising" title="Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Munich and Freising">Bishop of Freising</a>, which is the oldest known document using the name <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ostarr%C3%AEchi" title="Ostarrîchi">Ostarrîchi</a></i> (Austria in <a href="/wiki/Old_High_German" title="Old High German">Old High German</a>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1141" title="1141">1141</a> – <a href="/wiki/Empress_Matilda" title="Empress Matilda">Empress Matilda</a>'s reign as 'Lady of the English' ends with <a href="/wiki/Stephen,_King_of_England" title="Stephen, King of England">Stephen of Blois</a> regaining the title of King of England.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1179" title="1179">1179</a> – <a href="/wiki/Philip_II_of_France" title="Philip II of France">Philip II</a> is <a href="/wiki/Coronation" title="Coronation">crowned</a> King of France.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1214" title="1214">1214</a> – The port city of <a href="/wiki/Sinop,_Turkey" title="Sinop, Turkey">Sinope</a> <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Sinope" title="Siege of Sinope">surrenders</a> to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seljuq_Turks" title="Seljuq Turks">Seljuq Turks</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1348" title="1348">1348</a> – The anti-royalist <a href="/wiki/Union_of_Valencia" title="Union of Valencia">Union of Valencia</a> attacks the Jews of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Murviedro" title="Murviedro">Murviedro</a> on the pretext that they are <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Serf" title="Serf">serfs</a> of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/King_of_Valencia" title="King of Valencia">King of Valencia</a> and thus "royalists".</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1503" title="1503">1503</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Julius_II" title="Pope Julius II">Pope Julius II</a> is elected.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1512" title="1512">1512</a> – The ceiling of the <a href="/wiki/Sistine_Chapel" title="Sistine Chapel">Sistine Chapel</a>, painted by <a href="/wiki/Michelangelo" title="Michelangelo">Michelangelo</a>, is exhibited to the public for the first time.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1520" title="1520">1520</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Strait_of_Magellan" title="Strait of Magellan">Strait of Magellan</a>, the passage immediately south of mainland South America connecting the Pacific and the Atlantic Oceans, is first discovered and navigated by European explorer <a href="/wiki/Ferdinand_Magellan" title="Ferdinand Magellan">Ferdinand Magellan</a> during the first recorded <a href="/wiki/Circumnavigation" title="Circumnavigation">circumnavigation</a> voyage.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1555" title="1555">1555</a> – French <a href="/wiki/Huguenot" title="Huguenot">Huguenots</a> establish the <a href="/wiki/France_Antarctique" title="France Antarctique">France Antarctique</a> colony in present-day <a href="/wiki/Rio_de_Janeiro" title="Rio de Janeiro">Rio de Janeiro</a>, Brazil.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1570" title="1570">1570</a> – The <a href="/wiki/All_Saints%27_Flood_(1570)" title="All Saints' Flood (1570)">All Saints' Flood</a> devastates the Dutch coast.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1604" title="1604">1604</a> – <a href="/wiki/William_Shakespeare" title="William Shakespeare">William Shakespeare</a>'s tragedy <i><a href="/wiki/Othello" title="Othello">Othello</a></i> is performed for the first time, at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Whitehall_Palace" title="Whitehall Palace">Whitehall Palace</a> in London.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1611" title="1611">1611</a> – Shakespeare's play <i><a href="/wiki/The_Tempest" title="The Tempest">The Tempest</a></i> is performed for the first time, at Whitehall Palace in London.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1612" title="1612">1612</a> – During the <a href="/wiki/Time_of_Troubles" title="Time of Troubles">Time of Troubles</a>, Polish troops are expelled from Moscow's <a href="/wiki/Kitay-gorod" title="Kitay-gorod">Kitay-gorod</a> by Russian troops under the command of <a href="/wiki/Dmitry_Pozharsky" title="Dmitry Pozharsky">Dmitry Pozharsky</a> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/22_October" title="22 October">22 October</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Old_Style" title="Old Style">O.S.</a>) .</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1683" title="1683">1683</a> – The British Crown <a href="/wiki/Province_of_New_York" title="Province of New York">colony of New York</a> is subdivided into 12 <a href="/wiki/List_of_counties_in_New_York" title="List of counties in New York">counties</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1688" title="1688">1688</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/William_III_of_Orange" title="William III of Orange">William III of Orange</a> sets out a second time from <a href="/wiki/Hellevoetsluis" title="Hellevoetsluis">Hellevoetsluis</a> in the Netherlands to seize the crowns of England, Scotland and Ireland from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/King_James_II_of_England" title="King James II of England">King James II of England</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Glorious_Revolution" title="Glorious Revolution">Glorious Revolution</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1755" title="1755">1755</a> – In Portugal, <a href="/wiki/Lisbon" title="Lisbon">Lisbon</a> is <a href="/wiki/1755_Lisbon_earthquake" title="1755 Lisbon earthquake">totally devastated</a> by a massive earthquake and <a href="/wiki/Tsunami" title="Tsunami">tsunami</a>, killing between 60,000 and 90,000 people.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1765" title="1765">1765</a> – The British Parliament enacts the <a href="/wiki/Stamp_Act_1765" title="Stamp Act 1765">Stamp Act</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Thirteen_Colonies" title="Thirteen Colonies">Thirteen Colonies</a> in order to help pay for British military operations in North America.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1790" title="1790">1790</a> – <a href="/wiki/Edmund_Burke" title="Edmund Burke">Edmund Burke</a> publishes <i><a href="/wiki/Reflections_on_the_Revolution_in_France" title="Reflections on the Revolution in France">Reflections on the Revolution in France</a></i>, in which he predicts that the <a href="/wiki/French_Revolution" title="French Revolution">French Revolution</a> will end in a disaster.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1800" title="1800">1800</a> – <a href="/wiki/John_Adams" title="John Adams">John Adams</a> becomes the first President of the United States to live in the Executive Mansion (later renamed the <a href="/wiki/White_House" title="White House">White House</a>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1805" title="1805">1805</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Napoleon_I" title="Napoleon I">Napoleon Bonaparte</a> invades <a href="/wiki/Austrian_Empire" title="Austrian Empire">Austria</a> during the <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Third_Coalition" title="War of the Third Coalition">War of the Third Coalition</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1814" title="1814">1814</a> – <a href="/wiki/Congress_of_Vienna" title="Congress of Vienna">Congress of Vienna</a> opens to re-draw the European political map after the defeat of France in the <a href="/wiki/Napoleonic_Wars" title="Napoleonic Wars">Napoleonic Wars</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1848" title="1848">1848</a> – In <a href="/wiki/Boston" title="Boston">Boston</a>, <a href="/wiki/Massachusetts" title="Massachusetts">Massachusetts</a>, the first <a href="/wiki/Medical_school" title="Medical school">medical school</a> for women, Boston Female Medical School (which later merged with the <a href="/wiki/Boston_University" title="Boston University">Boston University</a> School of Medicine), opens.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1861" title="1861">1861</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Civil_War" title="American Civil War">American Civil War</a>: U.S. President <a href="/wiki/Abraham_Lincoln" title="Abraham Lincoln">Abraham Lincoln</a> appoints <a href="/wiki/George_B._McClellan" title="George B. McClellan">George B.
