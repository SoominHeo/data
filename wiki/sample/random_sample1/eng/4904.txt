<p><b>Rebecca Elizabeth Breeds</b> (born 17 June 1987) is an Australian actress known for her roles as Cassie Cometti in the third series of <i><a href="/wiki/Blue_Water_High" title="Blue Water High">Blue Water High</a></i>, as series regular <a href="/wiki/Ruby_Buckton" title="Ruby Buckton">Ruby Buckton</a> in the soap opera <i><a href="/wiki/Home_and_Away" title="Home and Away">Home and Away</a></i> and as Aurora de Martel in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Originals_(TV_series)" title="The Originals (TV series)">The Originals</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rebecca_Breeds&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Breeds was born in Sydney.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> She attended <a href="/wiki/St_Andrew%27s_Cathedral_School" title="St Andrew's Cathedral School">St Andrew's Cathedral School</a>, where she was made Drama Captain.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Inspired_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Inspired-5">[5]</a></sup> For six months, she studied a double degree in music and performing arts at <a href="/wiki/University_of_New_South_Wales" title="University of New South Wales">University of New South Wales</a>, before deferring her studies for a film role.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Inspired_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-Inspired-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rebecca_Breeds&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Breeds began her acting career with appearances in television commercials. She landed her first lead role as Leah Pointin in the 2008 Australian film <i><a href="/wiki/Newcastle_(film)" title="Newcastle (film)">Newcastle</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>Breeds played Cassie Cometti in the third series of <i><a href="/wiki/Blue_Water_High" title="Blue Water High">Blue Water High</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Jensen_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-Jensen-8">[8]</a></sup> It was during filming on <i>Blue Water High</i> that she successfully auditioned for the role of <a href="/wiki/Ruby_Buckton" title="Ruby Buckton">Ruby Buckton</a> on <i>Home and Away</i>, and her first scenes aired in June 2008.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Jensen_8-1"><a href="#cite_note-Jensen-8">[8]</a></sup> For her portrayal of Ruby, Breeds earned two <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Logie_Award" title="Logie Award">Logie Award</a> nominations, including <a href="/wiki/Logie_Award_for_Best_Actress" title="Logie Award for Best Actress">Most Popular Actress</a> in 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Logies_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-Logies-9">[9]</a></sup> In June 2012, <i><a href="/wiki/TV_Week" title="TV Week">TV Week</a>'s</i> Erin Miller reported that Breeds had filmed her final scenes and had left <i>Home and Away</i> after four years.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tvweek20120616_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-tvweek20120616-10">[10]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-digspy20120612_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-digspy20120612-11">[11]</a></sup> The actress made her last appearance as Ruby during the episode broadcast on 15 August 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-digspy20120915_12-0"><a href="#cite_note-digspy20120915-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<p>In August 2012, Nellie Andreeva from Deadline.com announced that Breeds had been cast in <a href="/wiki/Rob_Greenberg" title="Rob Greenberg">Rob Greenberg</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/We_Are_Men" title="We Are Men">We Are Men</a></i> as Abby Russo. <i>We Are Men</i> marked her first US television role, however, the show was cancelled after poor ratings.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-deadline20120831_13-0"><a href="#cite_note-deadline20120831-13">[13]</a></sup> Breeds also appears in the 2013 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hindi-language" title="Hindi-language">Hindi-language</a> <a href="/wiki/Bollywood" title="Bollywood">Bollywood</a> film <i><a href="/wiki/Bhaag_Milkha_Bhaag" title="Bhaag Milkha Bhaag">Bhaag Milkha Bhaag</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2015, Breeds appeared in the sixth season of <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pretty_Little_Liars_(TV_series)" title="Pretty Little Liars (TV series)">Pretty Little Liars</a></i> and was cast as Aurora in the supernatural series <i><a href="/wiki/The_Originals_(TV_series)" title="The Originals (TV series)">The Originals</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup> Breeds played <a href="/wiki/Molly_Meldrum" title="Molly Meldrum">Molly Meldrum</a>'s fiancée Camille in the 2016 miniseries <i><a href="/wiki/Molly_(miniseries)" title="Molly (miniseries)">Molly</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup> On 19 February 2016, it was announced that Breeds will star in <i>Miranda's Rights</i>, a legal soap about a group of lawyers who work and live together. Breeds was cast as the title character Miranda Coale after a competitive audition process.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rebecca_Breeds&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2009, Breeds began dating her <i>Home and Away</i> co-star <a href="/wiki/Luke_Mitchell" title="Luke Mitchell">Luke Mitchell</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Engaged_18-0"><a href="#cite_note-Engaged-18">[18]</a></sup> The couple announced their engagement in May 2012 and they married in January 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Engaged_18-1"><a href="#cite_note-Engaged-18">[18]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lifeyahoo20120514_19-0"><a href="#cite_note-lifeyahoo20120514-19">[19]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-dailytel20130106_20-0"><a href="#cite_note-dailytel20130106-20">[20]</a></sup></p>
