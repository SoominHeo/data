<p><b>Lisboa Region</b> (<a href="/wiki/Portuguese_language" title="Portuguese language">Portuguese</a>: <span lang="pt" xml:lang="pt"><i>Região de Lisboa</i></span>) is one of the seven <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/NUTS_II" title="NUTS II">NUTS II</a> designated regions of <a href="/wiki/Portugal" title="Portugal">Portugal</a>, which includes two <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/NUTS_III" title="NUTS III">NUTS III</a> subregions: <a href="/wiki/Grande_Lisboa" title="Grande Lisboa">Greater Lisbon</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pen%C3%ADnsula_de_Set%C3%BAbal" title="Península de Setúbal">Peninsula of Setúbal</a>. The region covers an area of 3001.95 km² (the smallest region on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mainland_Portugal" title="Mainland Portugal">mainland Portugal</a>) <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> and includes a population of 2,815,851 inhabitants according to the 2011 census (the most populated region in Portugal),<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-INE_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-INE-4">[4]</a></sup> a density of 1039 inhabitants/km².</p>
<p>Considered as representing the <a href="/wiki/Lisbon_Metropolitan_Area" title="Lisbon Metropolitan Area"><b>Lisboa Metropolitan Region</b></a>. It is a region of significant importance in industry (light and heavy), services, and it is highly urbanized.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lisboa_Region&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Prior to 2002, the area was included within the NUTS II region of <a href="/wiki/Lisboa_e_Vale_do_Tejo" title="Lisboa e Vale do Tejo">Lisbon and Tagus Valley</a> (that also included three other subregions).</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="NUTS_II_region_and_area_of_intervention_of_the_CCDRLVT">NUTS II region and area of intervention of the CCDRLVT</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lisboa_Region&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: NUTS II region and area of intervention of the CCDRLVT">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Despite the territorial configuration for statistical purposes (National Statistical System in Portugal), in force since 2007, matching the NUTS II the Lisbon, Region Greater Lisbon (AML) - composed only NUTSIII Greater Lisbon and Setúbal Peninsula - the area of intervention of the CCDRLVT - Steering Committee and Regional Development, abbreviated to CCDR - (the Lisbon and the Tagus Valley), continues to be composed of 5 NUTSIII (Sub-regions: Greater Lisbon, Setúbal Peninsula, Middle Tagus, and Lezíria West Coast).</p>
<p>For the Regional Funds, management responsibilities under the policy of the European Union in Portugal, this regions it's the region of Lisbon that consists of Grande Lisboa and Península de Setúbal, for regional planning (Run, monitor and evaluate, at regional level, policies on environment, nature conservation, land management and city) the region is called Lisbon and the Tagus Valley (LVT), composed by 5 NUTSIII (Sub-regions: Greater Lisbon, Setúbal Peninsula, Middle Tagus, and Lezíria West Coast)."</p>
