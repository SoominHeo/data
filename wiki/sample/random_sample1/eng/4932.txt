<p><b>Riadh Ben-Khemais Bouazizi</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar" xml:lang="ar">رياض بن خميس بوعزيزي</span>‎‎) (born 8 April 1973) is a Tunisian <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Central_midfielder" title="Central midfielder">Central midfielder</a>.</p>
<p>Bouazizi played for <a href="/wiki/Bursaspor" title="Bursaspor">Bursaspor</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gaziantepspor" title="Gaziantepspor">Gaziantepspor</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kayseri_Erciyesspor" title="Kayseri Erciyesspor">Kayseri Erciyesspor</a> in the Turkish <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Super_Lig" title="Super Lig">Super Lig</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>He has 83 caps for the <a href="/wiki/Tunisia_national_football_team" title="Tunisia national football team">national team</a>, and was called up to the <a href="/wiki/2006_FIFA_World_Cup" title="2006 FIFA World Cup">2006 World Cup</a>. He also played at the World Cups in <a href="/wiki/1998_FIFA_World_Cup" title="1998 FIFA World Cup">1998</a> and <a href="/wiki/2002_FIFA_World_Cup" title="2002 FIFA World Cup">2002</a>. In addition, he was on the winning Tunisian team at the <a href="/wiki/2004_African_Cup_of_Nations" title="2004 African Cup of Nations">2004 African Cup of Nations</a>.</p>
