<p><b>Real Colorado Foxes</b> were an American <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">soccer</a> team based in <a href="/wiki/Highlands_Ranch,_Colorado" title="Highlands Ranch, Colorado">Highlands Ranch, Colorado</a>, United States. Founded in 2008, the team played in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/USL_Premier_Development_League" title="USL Premier Development League">USL Premier Development League</a> (PDL), the fourth tier of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/American_Soccer_Pyramid" title="American Soccer Pyramid">American Soccer Pyramid</a>, in the Heartland Division of the Central Conference.</p>
<p>The team played its home games at <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Shea_Stadium_(Denver)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Shea Stadium (Denver) (page does not exist)">Shea Stadium</a>, where they have played since 2009. The team's colors were red, black and white.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Real_Colorado_Foxes&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Foxes joined the PDL in 2009,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and played their first ever game on May 9, 2009, against <a href="/wiki/Kansas_City_Brass" title="Kansas City Brass">Kansas City Brass</a>. The Foxes won the game 3–2, with the first goal in franchise history being scored by <a href="/wiki/Chris_Salvaggione" title="Chris Salvaggione">Chris Salvaggione</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2011, the team qualified for its first <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lamar_Hunt_US_Open_Cup" title="Lamar Hunt US Open Cup">Lamar Hunt US Open Cup</a> by winning the first 4 games of the regular season. The Foxes then advanced to the second round by knocking out <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/USASA" title="USASA">USASA</a>’s <a href="/wiki/DV8_Defenders" title="DV8 Defenders">DV8 Defenders</a> of <a href="/wiki/Redwood_City,_California" title="Redwood City, California">Redwood City, California</a> with a 5–0 convincing victory to fall short to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kitsap_Pumas" title="Kitsap Pumas">Kitsap Pumas</a>, another seasoned PDL side in <a href="/wiki/Bremerton,_Washington" title="Bremerton, Washington">Bremerton, Washington</a>, by a score of 3–1.</p>
