<p><b>Lionel Cox</b> (born 11 July 1981) is a Belgian <a href="/wiki/Shooting_sport" title="Shooting sport">sports shooter</a>. He competed in the <a href="/wiki/Shooting_at_the_2012_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_50_metre_rifle_prone" title="Shooting at the 2012 Summer Olympics – Men's 50 metre rifle prone">men's 50 metre rifle prone</a> event at the <a href="/wiki/2012_Summer_Olympics" title="2012 Summer Olympics">2012 Summer Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-london2012_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-london2012-1">[1]</a></sup> He qualified as second for the final with 8 shooters with a score of 599 out of 600. He finished the final as second, winning the silver medal.</p>
