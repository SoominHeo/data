<p><b>Lijin County</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">利津县</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">利津縣</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Lìjīn Xiàn</span></i>) is a county of <a href="/wiki/Shandong" title="Shandong">Shandong</a> province, People's Republic of China, situated on the northwestern (left) bank of the <a href="/wiki/Yellow_River" title="Yellow River">Yellow River</a>. It is under the administration of <a href="/wiki/Dongying" title="Dongying">Dongying</a> City.</p>
<p>The population in 1999 was 289 593.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
