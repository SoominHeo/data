<p><b>Richard Cansino</b> (born August 10, 1953) is an American voice actor. He is also known as <b>Richard Hayworth</b> because he is the nephew of <a href="/wiki/Rita_Hayworth" title="Rita Hayworth">Rita Hayworth</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and occasionally goes by the name <b>Steve Davis</b> after the <a href="/wiki/Steve_Davis" title="Steve Davis">British snooker player of the same name</a>. Richard is arguably best known for his portrayal of <a href="/wiki/Himura_Kenshin" title="Himura Kenshin">Kenshin Himura</a> from the anime adaptation of <a href="/wiki/Rurouni_Kenshin" title="Rurouni Kenshin">Rurouni Kenshin</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> Additionally, Richard is also known for his performances as Izumo Kamizuki from the popular <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Naruto_(anime)" title="Naruto (anime)">Naruto series</a> and Legato Bluesummers from <a href="/wiki/Trigun" title="Trigun">Trigun</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
