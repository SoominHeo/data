<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wide_Awake_(2007_film)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 1980s South Korea, a young boy is traumatised after experiencing <a href="/wiki/Anesthesia_awareness" title="Anesthesia awareness">anesthesia awareness</a> during heart surgery, and no-one believes his story afterwards. Twenty-five years later, the doctors and nurses who operated on him begin to die under mysterious circumstances. Dr. Ryu Jae-woo, a surgeon married to Hee-jin, believes that the boy he remembers from his childhood is responsible for the deaths. The leading suspects are Lee Myeong-suk, who has been stalking Dr. Ryu, and the seemingly unhinged Uk-hwan. Hypnosis specialist Oh Chi-hoon also seems to know something about these deaths.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Release">Release</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wide_Awake_(2007_film)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Release">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Wide Awake</i> was released in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a> on 8 August 2007,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Koreanfilm.org_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Koreanfilm.org-1">[1]</a></sup> and its opening weekend was ranked fourth at the box office with 238,819 admissions.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The film went on to receive a total of 677,939 admissions nationwide,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Koreanfilm.org_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Koreanfilm.org-1">[1]</a></sup> with a gross (as of 16 September 2007) of US$4,569,126.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
