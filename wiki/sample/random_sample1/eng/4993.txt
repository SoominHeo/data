<p>A <b><a href="/wiki/Reaper" title="Reaper">reaper</a></b> is a farm tool or machine for harvesting grain. It comes from the word "<a class="mw-redirect mw-disambig" href="/wiki/Reap_(disambiguation)" title="Reap (disambiguation)">reap</a>", which means "to take". A <a href="/wiki/Reaper-binder" title="Reaper-binder">Reaper-binder</a>, is a later version of the original reaping machine that includes the binding operation.</p>
<p>Due to the association with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grim_Reaper" title="Grim Reaper">Grim Reaper</a>, the reaper is a common symbol of <a href="/wiki/Death" title="Death">death</a>.</p>
