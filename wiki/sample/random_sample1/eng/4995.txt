<p><i><b>Rhipidomys</b></i> is a genus of <a href="/wiki/Rodent" title="Rodent">rodents</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Cricetidae" title="Cricetidae">Cricetidae</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MSW3_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-MSW3-1">[1]</a></sup> comprising at least 18 <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> of climbing mouse:</p>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Southern_Climbing_Mouse" title="Southern Climbing Mouse">Southern Climbing Mouse</a> (<i>Rhipidomys austrinus</i>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cariri_Climbing_Mouse" title="Cariri Climbing Mouse">Cariri Climbing Mouse</a> (<i>Rhipidomys cariri</i>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cauca_Climbing_Mouse" title="Cauca Climbing Mouse">Cauca Climbing Mouse</a> (<i>Rhipidomys caucensis</i>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Coues%27s_Climbing_Mouse" title="Coues's Climbing Mouse">Coues's Climbing Mouse</a> (<i>Rhipidomys couesi</i>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Eastern_Amazon_Climbing_Mouse" title="Eastern Amazon Climbing Mouse">Eastern Amazon Climbing Mouse</a> (<i>Rhipidomys emiliae</i>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Buff-bellied_Climbing_Mouse" title="Buff-bellied Climbing Mouse">Buff-bellied Climbing Mouse</a> (<i>Rhipidomys fulviventer</i>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gardner%27s_Climbing_Mouse" title="Gardner's Climbing Mouse">Gardner's Climbing Mouse</a> (<i>Rhipidomys gardneri</i>)</li>
<li>(<i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Rhipidomys_ipukensis&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Rhipidomys ipukensis (page does not exist)">Rhipidomys ipukensis</a>)</i></li>
<li>(<i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Rhipidomys_itoan&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Rhipidomys itoan (page does not exist)">Rhipidomys itoan</a></i>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Broad-footed_Climbing_Mouse" title="Broad-footed Climbing Mouse">Broad-footed Climbing Mouse</a> (<i>Rhipidomys latimanus</i>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White-footed_Climbing_Mouse" title="White-footed Climbing Mouse">White-footed Climbing Mouse</a> (<i>Rhipidomys leucodactylus</i>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/MacConnell%27s_Climbing_Mouse" title="MacConnell's Climbing Mouse">MacConnell's Climbing Mouse</a> (<i>Rhipidomys macconnelli</i>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cerrado_Climbing_Mouse" title="Cerrado Climbing Mouse">Cerrado Climbing Mouse</a> (<i>Rhipidomys macrurus</i>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Atlantic_Forest_Climbing_Mouse" title="Atlantic Forest Climbing Mouse">Atlantic Forest Climbing Mouse</a> (<i>Rhipidomys mastacalis</i>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Peruvian_Climbing_Mouse" title="Peruvian Climbing Mouse">Peruvian Climbing Mouse</a> (<i>Rhipidomys modicus</i>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Splendid_Climbing_Mouse" title="Splendid Climbing Mouse">Splendid Climbing Mouse</a> (<i>Rhipidomys nitela</i>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yellow-bellied_Climbing_Mouse" title="Yellow-bellied Climbing Mouse">Yellow-bellied Climbing Mouse</a> (<i>Rhipidomys ochrogaster</i>)</li>
