<p>On 13 March 2016, three gunmen opened fire at a beach resort in <a href="/wiki/Grand-Bassam" title="Grand-Bassam">Grand-Bassam</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ivory_Coast" title="Ivory Coast">Ivory Coast</a>, killing at least 19 people and injuring 33 others.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:1_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-:1-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-:0-5">[5]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Attack">Attack</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2016_Grand-Bassam_shootings&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Attack">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Three armed assailants<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> attacked the Étoile du Sud hotel which, according to <a href="/wiki/Agence_France-Presse" title="Agence France-Presse">Agence France-Presse</a>, was occupied by numerous <a href="/wiki/Expatriate" title="Expatriate">expats</a> at the time.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[nb 1]</a></sup> According to officials, 15 civilians and 3 special forces soldiers were killed.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[9]</a></sup></p>
<p>The attackers were described as African, armed with <a href="/wiki/Kalashnikov_rifle" title="Kalashnikov rifle">Kalashnikov rifles</a> and grenade belts and dressed in casual clothes and <a href="/wiki/Balaclava_(clothing)" title="Balaclava (clothing)">balaclavas</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:1_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-:1-4">[4]</a></sup> A shootout occurred between the attackers and police forces as the assailants reached La Paillote Hotel. Local residents and tourists were evacuated by army personnel from the beach to nearby hotels, which were temporarily placed on lockdown.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:2_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-:2-11">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Associated_Press" title="Associated Press">Associated Press</a> quoted Ivorian Government officials as saying that security forces have killed six armed men.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[11]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[12]</a></sup> The terrorists allegedly shouted "<a href="/wiki/Takbir" title="Takbir">Allahu Akbar</a>".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-telegraph_14-0"><a href="#cite_note-telegraph-14">[13]</a></sup> An American embassy delegation was at Grand-Bassam on the date of the attack, but the US Embassy in Abidjan said on Twitter that there is "no evidence that U.S. citizens were targeted nor confirmed reports of any U.S [<i><a href="/wiki/Sic" title="Sic">sic</a></i>] citizens as harmed."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[14]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[15]</a></sup></p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Al-Qaeda_in_the_Islamic_Maghreb" title="Al-Qaeda in the Islamic Maghreb">Al-Qaeda in the Islamic Maghreb</a> (AQIM) and <a href="/wiki/Al-Mourabitoun_(militant_group)" title="Al-Mourabitoun (militant group)">Al-Mourabitoun</a> claimed responsibility for the attack.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Bloomberg_17-0"><a href="#cite_note-Bloomberg-17">[16]</a></sup> On 17 March, AQIM released the names of the attackers: Hamza al-Fulani and Abu Adam al-Ansari from al-Mourabitoun and Abderrahmane al-Fulani from the "Emirate of the Desert".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-StarAfrica_18-0"><a href="#cite_note-StarAfrica-18">[17]</a></sup></p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="International_reactions">International reactions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2016_Grand-Bassam_shootings&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: International reactions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><span class="flagicon"><img alt="" class="thumbborder" data-file-height="500" data-file-width="1000" height="12" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/c/cf/Flag_of_Canada.svg/23px-Flag_of_Canada.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/c/cf/Flag_of_Canada.svg/35px-Flag_of_Canada.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/c/cf/Flag_of_Canada.svg/46px-Flag_of_Canada.svg.png 2x" width="23"/> </span><a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>: <a href="/wiki/Global_Affairs_Canada" title="Global Affairs Canada">Global Affairs Canada</a> stated that "Canada condemns the terrorist attack at Grand-Bassam beach resort in Côte d'Ivoire. Our thoughts and prayers are with you." <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-29"><a href="#cite_note-29">[28]</a></sup></li>
<li><span class="flagicon"><img alt="" class="thumbborder" data-file-height="600" data-file-width="900" height="15" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/c/c3/Flag_of_France.svg/23px-Flag_of_France.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/c/c3/Flag_of_France.svg/35px-Flag_of_France.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/c/c3/Flag_of_France.svg/45px-Flag_of_France.svg.png 2x" width="23"/> </span><a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>: <a href="/wiki/President_of_France" title="President of France">President</a> <a href="/wiki/Fran%C3%A7ois_Hollande" title="François Hollande">François Hollande</a> stated that he "strongly condemns the cowardly attack that caused the death of at least ten civilians, including at least one French national, and several members of the security forces in Grand-Bassam".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-30"><a href="#cite_note-30">[29]</a></sup> He also stated that "France will bring its logistical support and intelligence to Ivory Coast to find the attackers. It will pursue and intensify its cooperation with its partners in the fight against terrorism."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-31"><a href="#cite_note-31">[30]</a></sup></li>
<li><span class="flagicon"><img alt="" class="thumbborder" data-file-height="900" data-file-width="1350" height="15" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/4/41/Flag_of_India.svg/23px-Flag_of_India.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/4/41/Flag_of_India.svg/35px-Flag_of_India.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/4/41/Flag_of_India.svg/45px-Flag_of_India.svg.png 2x" width="23"/> </span><a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a>: <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_India" title="Prime Minister of India">Prime Minister</a> <a href="/wiki/Narendra_Modi" title="Narendra Modi">Narendra Modi</a> condemned the attacks and stated his thoughts were with the families of the victims and wished the injured a speedy recovery.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-32"><a href="#cite_note-32">[31]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-33"><a href="#cite_note-33">[32]</a></sup></li>
<li><span class="flagicon"><img alt="" class="thumbborder" data-file-height="600" data-file-width="1200" height="12" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/a/ae/Flag_of_the_United_Kingdom.svg/23px-Flag_of_the_United_Kingdom.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/a/ae/Flag_of_the_United_Kingdom.svg/35px-Flag_of_the_United_Kingdom.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/a/ae/Flag_of_the_United_Kingdom.svg/46px-Flag_of_the_United_Kingdom.svg.png 2x" width="23"/> </span><a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>: <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_the_United_Kingdom" title="Prime Minister of the United Kingdom">Prime Minister</a> <a href="/wiki/David_Cameron" title="David Cameron">David Cameron</a> stated on Twitter: "I'm appalled by the devastating terror attacks in Ankara and the Ivory Coast. My thoughts are with all those affected."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-34"><a href="#cite_note-34">[33]</a></sup></li>
<li><span class="flagicon"><img alt="" class="thumbborder" data-file-height="650" data-file-width="1235" height="12" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/a/a4/Flag_of_the_United_States.svg/23px-Flag_of_the_United_States.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/a/a4/Flag_of_the_United_States.svg/35px-Flag_of_the_United_States.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/a/a4/Flag_of_the_United_States.svg/46px-Flag_of_the_United_States.svg.png 2x" width="23"/> </span><a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>: In a statement by the <a href="/wiki/United_States_National_Security_Council" title="United States National Security Council">National Security Council</a> of the White House, the U.S. condemned the terrorist attack in Grand-Bassam, Côte d'Ivoire. Furthermore, it extended deep condolences to the loved ones of the victims and that their thoughts and prayers also are with the injured. It also commended "the bravery of the Ivorian and French security personnel who responded to the situation and prevented even worse loss of life."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-35"><a href="#cite_note-35">[34]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
