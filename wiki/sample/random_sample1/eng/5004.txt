<p><b>Rick Hoffman</b> (born June 12, 1970) is an American actor. He is best known for portraying Patrick Van Dorn in <i><a href="/wiki/Jake_in_Progress" title="Jake in Progress">Jake in Progress</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/List_of_Suits_characters#Louis_Litt" title="List of Suits characters">Louis Litt</a> in the legal drama series <i><a href="/wiki/Suits_(TV_series)" title="Suits (TV series)">Suits</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rick_Hoffman&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Hoffman was born in <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a> and grew up in <a href="/wiki/Roslyn_Heights,_New_York" title="Roslyn Heights, New York">Roslyn Heights, New York</a>. He is the son of Gail and Charlie Hoffman and is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jewish" title="Jewish">Jewish</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> He has a younger brother, Jeff. He graduated from <a href="/wiki/The_Wheatley_School" title="The Wheatley School">The Wheatley School</a> in <a href="/wiki/Old_Westbury,_New_York" title="Old Westbury, New York">Old Westbury, New York</a> before attending the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Arizona" title="University of Arizona">University of Arizona</a>. After graduating he moved to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Los_Angeles,_California" title="Los Angeles, California">Los Angeles, California</a> to start an acting career.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rick_Hoffman&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Hoffman was cast in his first role, as a security guard in <i><a href="/wiki/Conspiracy_Theory_(film)" title="Conspiracy Theory (film)">Conspiracy Theory</a></i>, in 1997. He had some other small roles until he received a main role as Freddie Sacker on Darren Star's short-lived Wall Street series <i><a href="/wiki/The_Street_(2000_TV_series)" title="The Street (2000 TV series)">The $treet</a></i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> in 2000, which was pulled from the airwaves after seven episodes, but which allowed him to quit his job waiting tables and move back to New York. His later TV roles include Terry Loomis on the short-lived <a href="/wiki/Steven_Bochco" title="Steven Bochco">Steven Bochco</a> law drama <i><a href="/wiki/Philly_(TV_series)" title="Philly (TV series)">Philly</a></i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> in 2001-2002, Jerry Best in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Bernie_Mac_Show" title="The Bernie Mac Show">The Bernie Mac Show</a></i> (2002-2005),<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IMDb_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-IMDb-5">[5]</a></sup> Patrick Van Dorn on the John Stamos comedy <i><a href="/wiki/Jake_in_Progress" title="Jake in Progress">Jake in Progress</a></i> (2004-2005)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IMDb_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-IMDb-5">[5]</a></sup> and Chase Chapman on the ABC comedy <i><a href="/wiki/Samantha_Who%3F" title="Samantha Who?">Samantha Who?</a></i> (2007-2009). In 2011, he acted in a starring role as Louis Litt in the <a href="/wiki/USA_Network" title="USA Network">USA Network</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Suits_(TV_series)" title="Suits (TV series)">Suits</a></i>, which has continued ever since. His TV/film work includes <i><a href="/wiki/The_Day_After_Tomorrow" title="The Day After Tomorrow">The Day After Tomorrow</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Blood_Work_(film)" title="Blood Work (film)">Blood Work</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Hostel_(2005_film)" title="Hostel (2005 film)">Hostel</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Cellular_(film)" title="Cellular (film)">Cellular</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Condemned" title="The Condemned">The Condemned</a></i>. He has also appeared as a guest in <i><a href="/wiki/CSI:_Miami" title="CSI: Miami">CSI: Miami</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Chuck_(TV_series)" title="Chuck (TV series)">Chuck</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Pretender_(TV_series)" title="The Pretender (TV series)">The Pretender</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Law_%26_Order:_SVU" title="Law &amp; Order: SVU">Law &amp; Order: SVU</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/NCIS_(TV_series)" title="NCIS (TV series)">NCIS</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Crossing_Jordan" title="Crossing Jordan">Crossing Jordan</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Without_a_Trace" title="Without a Trace">Without a Trace</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Practice" title="The Practice">The Practice</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/CSI:NY" title="CSI:NY">CSI:NY</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/CSI:_Crime_Scene_Investigation" title="CSI: Crime Scene Investigation">CSI: Crime Scene Investigation</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Lie_to_Me" title="Lie to Me">Lie to Me</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Monk_(TV_series)" title="Monk (TV series)">Monk</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Mentalist" title="The Mentalist">The Mentalist</a></i>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rick_Hoffman&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Hoffman is originally from Long Island, New York. His first child, a son, was born in December 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
