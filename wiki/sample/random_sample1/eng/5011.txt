<p><b>Linsey Dawn McKenzie</b> (born 7 August 1978<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Stenson_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Stenson-2">[2]</a></sup> in <a href="/wiki/London_Borough_of_Brent" title="London Borough of Brent">Brent</a>, <a href="/wiki/Middlesex" title="Middlesex">Middlesex</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-search.findmypast.co.uk_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-search.findmypast.co.uk-1">[1]</a></sup>) is an English <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Glamour_model" title="Glamour model">glamour model</a>, pornographic performer, and television personality who made her topless modelling debut in the <i><a href="/wiki/Sunday_Sport" title="Sunday Sport">Sunday Sport</a></i> tabloid newspaper on her 16th birthday in 1994. Known for her naturally large 34GG breasts, she went on to feature in a wide range of adult magazines, websites, broadcast media, and videos, including <a href="/wiki/Hardcore_pornography" title="Hardcore pornography">hardcore pornography</a> productions after 2000.</p>
<p>She has attained minor celebrity status in the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>, where she has appeared on a number of mainstream television programmes, such as <i><a href="/wiki/They_Think_It%27s_All_Over_(TV_series)" title="They Think It's All Over (TV series)">They Think It's All Over</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/I%27m_Famous_and_Frightened!" title="I'm Famous and Frightened!">I'm Famous and Frightened!</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Weakest_Link" title="The Weakest Link">The Weakest Link</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Four_Weddings" title="Four Weddings">Celebrity Four Weddings</a></i>. She has also appeared in documentaries about the lives of glamour models and was featured in <a href="/wiki/Martin_Gooch" title="Martin Gooch">Martin Gooch</a>'s 2002 film <i>Arthur's Amazing Things</i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Linsey_Dawn_McKenzie&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In early 1994 when McKenzie was 15, she sent sample glamour photographs to local modelling agencies. She began her modelling career glamour modeling and swimsuit work, intending to progress to topless modelling when she turned 16 later that year.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Stenson_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-Stenson-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>As McKenzie approached her 16th birthday, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Sunday_Sport" title="The Sunday Sport">The Sunday Sport</a></i> tabloid newspaper expressed an interest in turning her topless debut into a media event. Throughout June and July 1994, it published provocative pictures of McKenzie, touting her 34GG bra size and counting down the days until it could legally show her topless.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Burniston_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Burniston-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>After she turned 16, McKenzie continued to model topless for <i>The Daily Sport</i> and <i>The Sunday Sport.</i> She also made <i><a href="/wiki/Page_3" title="Page 3">Page 3</a></i> girl appearances in tabloid newspapers <i><a href="/wiki/The_Sun_(United_Kingdom)" title="The Sun (United Kingdom)">The Sun</a></i> and <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Daily_Star_(United_Kingdom)" title="Daily Star (United Kingdom)">The Daily Star</a></i>; posed topless in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lads%27_mags" title="Lads' mags">lads' mags</a> such as <i><a href="/wiki/Loaded_(magazine)" title="Loaded (magazine)">Loaded</a></i>; appeared fully nude in British <a href="/wiki/Softcore_pornography" title="Softcore pornography">softcore</a> magazines such as <i><a href="/wiki/Mayfair_(magazine)" title="Mayfair (magazine)">Mayfair</a></i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> and <i><a href="/wiki/Men_Only" title="Men Only">Men Only</a></i>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> and performed in a variety of low-budget softcore videos.</p>
<p>On 29 July 1995, shortly before her 17th birthday, McKenzie did a topless <a href="/wiki/Streaking" title="Streaking">streak</a> at a televised <a href="/wiki/England_cricket_team" title="England cricket team">England</a> v. <a href="/wiki/West_Indies_cricket_team" title="West Indies cricket team">West Indies</a> <a href="/wiki/Cricket" title="Cricket">cricket</a> match at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Old_Trafford_(cricket)" title="Old Trafford (cricket)">Old Trafford</a>. Wearing only a <a href="/wiki/Thong_(clothing)" title="Thong (clothing)">thong</a> and a pair of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Athletic_shoes" title="Athletic shoes">trainers</a>, she ran onto the field with the words "Only Teasing" written across her breasts.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>After McKenzie turned 18 in 1996, she made her North American modelling debut in the <a href="/wiki/Breast_fetishism" title="Breast fetishism">breast fetishism</a> magazine <i>Score</i>, which marked the occasion by making McKenzie the <a href="/wiki/Cover_girl" title="Cover girl">cover girl</a> of its December 1996 "50th Anniversary" issue.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>In the late 1990s, McKenzie was frequently featured on the adult-oriented channels <a href="/wiki/L!VE_TV" title="L!VE TV">L!VE TV</a> and <a href="/wiki/Television_X" title="Television X">Television X</a>. She continued to appear regularly in adult-oriented magazines, DVDs, Internet sites, and broadcast media until late 2004, when she took a break from modelling to have a child and undergo a breast reduction operation.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Linsey_Dawn_McKenzie&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>McKenzie was born in <a href="/wiki/London_Borough_of_Brent" title="London Borough of Brent">Brent</a>, <a href="/wiki/Middlesex" title="Middlesex">Middlesex</a> and grew up in <a href="/wiki/Wallington,_London" title="Wallington, London">Wallington</a>, <a href="/wiki/Surrey" title="Surrey">Surrey</a>. She is the youngest of three children and her parents, Tony and Lesley McKenzie, divorced in 1987.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Stenson_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-Stenson-2">[2]</a></sup> In 1996, at the age of 17, McKenzie became involved in an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Extramarital_affair" title="Extramarital affair">extramarital affair</a> with 27-year-old footballer <a href="/wiki/Dean_Holdsworth" title="Dean Holdsworth">Dean Holdsworth</a>, who was then a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Forward_(football)" title="Forward (football)">striker</a> for <a href="/wiki/Wimbledon_F.C." title="Wimbledon F.C.">Wimbledon F.C.</a> The relationship received widespread attention in the British media.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-20"><a href="#cite_note-20">[20]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 1998, she became engaged to actor <a href="/wiki/Michael_Greco_(actor)" title="Michael Greco (actor)">Michael Greco</a>, who at the time was playing the role of <a href="/wiki/Beppe_di_Marco" title="Beppe di Marco">Beppe di Marco</a> in the <a href="/wiki/BBC" title="BBC">BBC</a> soap opera <i><a href="/wiki/EastEnders" title="EastEnders">EastEnders</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-21"><a href="#cite_note-21">[21]</a></sup> She twice became pregnant over the course of her 15-month relationship with Greco, but miscarried both times.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-22"><a href="#cite_note-22">[22]</a></sup></p>
<p>Claiming to have grown disillusioned with footballers and celebrities, McKenzie began dating 21-year-old window cleaner Terry Canty in 2000.
