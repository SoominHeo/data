<p><b>Lindsey Wixson</b> (born April 11, 1994) is an American fashion model. She is known for her unique looks, defined in part by her "bee-stung lips", "rosebud pout", and gapped front teeth.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-newgirl_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-newgirl-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Southern_Belle_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Southern_Belle-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> She is best known for her campaigns with designers as <a href="/wiki/Chanel" title="Chanel">Chanel</a>, <a href="/wiki/John_Galliano" title="John Galliano">John Galliano</a>, <a href="/wiki/Versace" title="Versace">Versace Vanitas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Miu_Miu" title="Miu Miu">Miu Miu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jill_Stuart" title="Jill Stuart">Jill Stuart</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alexander_McQueen" title="Alexander McQueen">Alexander McQueen</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WWD_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-WWD-5">[5]</a></sup> As of July 2014<sup class="plainlinks noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Lindsey_Wixson&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup>, Wixson currently ranks 20th on the <i>Top 50 Models Women</i> list by MODELS.com.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lindsey_Wixson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Wixson was born in <a href="/wiki/Wichita,_Kansas" title="Wichita, Kansas">Wichita, Kansas</a> on April 11, 1994 to Thomas, who works installing <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Satellite_dishes" title="Satellite dishes">satellite dishes</a>, and Michele, a <a href="/wiki/School_bus" title="School bus">school bus</a> <a href="/wiki/Bus_driver" title="Bus driver">driver</a>. She has a sister, Kirsten. Wixson inherited her gapped teeth from her father.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WWD_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-WWD-5">[5]</a></sup> As a young girl she wanted to be either a <a href="/wiki/Chef" title="Chef">chef</a> or a lawyer.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-W_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-W-7">[7]</a></sup> In <a href="/wiki/Middle_school" title="Middle school">middle school</a>, Wixson was bullied, having her <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Diastema_(dentistry)" title="Diastema (dentistry)">tooth gap</a> called a "parking lot", making her self-conscious about her looks.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Southern_Belle_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-Southern_Belle-3">[3]</a></sup> At 12 years old and 5'7", strangers would come up to her to tell her she could be a model,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WWD_5-2"><a href="#cite_note-WWD-5">[5]</a></sup> so she started researching it as a possible career.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Southern_Belle_3-2"><a href="#cite_note-Southern_Belle-3">[3]</a></sup> Around the same time, she was inspired by an article in the <a href="/wiki/Reader%27s_Digest" title="Reader's Digest">Reader's Digest</a> about <a href="/wiki/Bill_Gates" title="Bill Gates">Bill Gates</a> and other millionaires, stating that "it was talking about how they took the chance and they took their opportunities and they took it to a whole other level". Wixson decided to become a model,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WWD_5-3"><a href="#cite_note-WWD-5">[5]</a></sup> and studied old copies of <i><a href="/wiki/Vogue_(magazine)" title="Vogue (magazine)">Vogue</a></i>, looking at the careers of supermodels like <a href="/wiki/Gisele_B%C3%BCndchen" title="Gisele Bündchen">Gisele</a>, <a href="/wiki/Christy_Turlington" title="Christy Turlington">Christy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kate_Moss" title="Kate Moss">Kate</a>. Wixson first signed with a local agent in Kansas, but had no success, so her parents made the decision to take out a loan and drive down to Los Angeles.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Southern_Belle_3-3"><a href="#cite_note-Southern_Belle-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lindsey_Wixson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Breakthrough.2C_2009.E2.80.9310">Breakthrough, 2009–10</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lindsey_Wixson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Breakthrough, 2009–10">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Wixson began her modeling career in 2009 when she signed with Vision Models in Los Angeles. In June 2009, she went to New York where she was rejected by <a href="/wiki/IMG_Models" title="IMG Models">IMG Models</a> and several other agencies because casting directors thought her looks would not translate well on and off the <a href="/wiki/Runway" title="Runway">runway</a>, except for one: <a href="/wiki/Marilyn_Agency" title="Marilyn Agency">Marilyn Agency</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-newgirl_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-newgirl-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nymag.com_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-nymag.com-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> At just 15, she was hand-selected by <a href="/wiki/Steven_Meisel" title="Steven Meisel">Steven Meisel</a> after finding a video of her on Models.com and booked her for <i><a href="/wiki/Vogue_(magazine)" title="Vogue (magazine)">Vogue</a></i> Italia,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WWD_5-4"><a href="#cite_note-WWD-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-W_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-W-7">[7]</a></sup> her first job.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Southern_Belle_3-4"><a href="#cite_note-Southern_Belle-3">[3]</a></sup> Her runway debut was at the Spring 2010 <a href="/wiki/New_York_Fashion_Week" title="New York Fashion Week">New York Fashion Week</a> walking for Cynthia Steffe.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nymag.com_8-1"><a href="#cite_note-nymag.com-8">[8]</a></sup> Wixson then went to <a href="/wiki/Milan_Fashion_Week" title="Milan Fashion Week">Milan</a> where she opened the spring 2010 <a href="/wiki/Prada" title="Prada">Prada</a> show and then closed for <a href="/wiki/Miu_Miu" title="Miu Miu">Miu Miu</a> in <a href="/wiki/Paris_Fashion_Week" title="Paris Fashion Week">Paris</a>, both as <a href="/wiki/Runway_(fashion)#Exclusive" title="Runway (fashion)">exclusives</a>, thus making her one of the most successful newcomers that season. By September, <i>W</i> editor <a href="/wiki/Stefano_Tonchi" title="Stefano Tonchi">Stefano Tonchi</a> ran a full-page close-up of her lips and the gap in her teeth, her two most arresting features, in his first issue of <i><a href="/wiki/W_(magazine)" title="W (magazine)">W</a></i> magazine.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WWD_5-5"><a href="#cite_note-WWD-5">[5]</a></sup> She then became the face of <a href="/wiki/Miu_Miu" title="Miu Miu">Miu Miu</a>'s Spring 2010 campaign shot by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mert_Alas_and_Marcus_Piggott" title="Mert Alas and Marcus Piggott">Mert Alas and Marcus Piggott</a>. A press release about the campaign called her the "embodiment of a free-spirit on the cusp of womanhood" (she would stay with the brand for a two-season run).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> But, Wixson has her limits in what she models, during a <i><a href="/wiki/W_(magazine)" title="W (magazine)">W</a></i> editorial shoot titled "Run of Show" shot by <a href="/wiki/Craig_McDean" title="Craig McDean">Craig McDean</a> she requested a bra because her top was see-through, <i>W</i> notes that a lot of models wouldn't have done that.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-W_7-2"><a href="#cite_note-W-7">[7]</a></sup> <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/LIFE_magazine" title="LIFE magazine">LIFE magazine</a></i> photographed Wixson as she worked at <a href="/wiki/New_York_Fashion_Week" title="New York Fashion Week">New York Fashion Week</a>. The story chronicles the life of how a 15-year-old teen model navigates her way through fittings, hair, makeup and the runway.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> A selection of 10 unpublished photographs from the <i>LIFE magazine</i> shoot were displayed at <a href="/wiki/Sotheby%27s" title="Sotheby's">Sotheby's</a> in an exhibition titled "I Remain, You Desire" in 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> She ended the season with the fall/winter campaigns for <a href="/wiki/Miu_Miu" title="Miu Miu">Miu Miu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jill_Stuart" title="Jill Stuart">Jill Stuart</a> <i>Beauty</i>, <a href="/wiki/Barneys_New_York" title="Barneys New York">Barney's</a>, <a href="/wiki/Versace" title="Versace">Versace</a> <i>Vanitas</i> Fragrance, and <a href="/wiki/John_Galliano" title="John Galliano">Galliano</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-advertising_13-0"><a href="#cite_note-advertising-13">[13]</a></sup> She also walked in a combined 44 shows in <a href="/wiki/New_York_Fashion_Week" title="New York Fashion Week">New York</a>, <a href="/wiki/Paris_Fashion_Week" title="Paris Fashion Week">Paris</a> and <a href="/wiki/Milan_Fashion_Week" title="Milan Fashion Week">Milan</a> in the fall.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WWD_5-6"><a href="#cite_note-WWD-5">[5]</a></sup> And booked her first major magazine cover, for the <i><a href="/wiki/I-D" title="I-D">i-D</a></i> fall 2010 issue.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup></p>

<p>Proclaimed as the modelling world's biggest rising star,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Southern_Belle_3-5"><a href="#cite_note-Southern_Belle-3">[3]</a></sup> Wixson started off the spring/summer 2011 season in campaigns for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mulberry" title="Mulberry">Mulberry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Barneys_New_York" title="Barneys New York">Barney's</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alexander_McQueen" title="Alexander McQueen">Alexander McQueen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jill_Stuart" title="Jill Stuart">Jill Stuart</a>, Jill Stuart's <i>Night Jewels</i> Fragrance, Jill Stuart <i>Beauty</i>, and <a href="/wiki/Versace" title="Versace">Versace</a> <i>Vanitas</i> Fragrance.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-advertising_13-1"><a href="#cite_note-advertising-13">[13]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup> As well as modeled for <a href="/wiki/Chanel" title="Chanel">Chanel</a>’s spring makeup collection.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup> She did her first editorial work for <i>Vogue</i>, a two-model shoot with <a href="/wiki/Coco_Rocha" title="Coco Rocha">Coco Rocha</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WWD_5-7"><a href="#cite_note-WWD-5">[5]</a></sup> Her <a href="/wiki/Talent_agent" title="Talent agent">agent</a>, Cheri Bowen at Marilyn Agency, said that Wixson is more interested in quality than quantity, "She's incredibly intelligent about the business. She's not about selling out".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WWD_5-8"><a href="#cite_note-WWD-5">[5]</a></sup> Wixson made her national television appearance on <a href="/wiki/CNN" title="CNN">CNN</a>, being introduced a "teen supermodel" at 17, as the cameras and reporter <a href="/wiki/Alina_Cho" title="Alina Cho">Alina Cho</a> followed her home to Wichita to film her prom experience.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-prom_18-0"><a href="#cite_note-prom-18">[18]</a></sup> Wixson has walked for a number of key labels this season, from <a href="/wiki/Rodarte" title="Rodarte">Rodarte</a> in <a href="/wiki/New_York_Fashion_Week" title="New York Fashion Week">New York</a> to <a href="/wiki/Zac_Posen" title="Zac Posen">Zac Posen</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jean_Paul_Gaultier" title="Jean Paul Gaultier">Gaultier</a> in <a href="/wiki/Paris_Fashion_Week" title="Paris Fashion Week">Paris</a>, but due to an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Allergic_reaction" title="Allergic reaction">allergic reaction</a> to the make-up she had to cut her stint short at Paris Fashion Week, missing out on a number of the city's big shows.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-20"><a href="#cite_note-20">[20]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2013, Wixson joined <a href="/wiki/The_Society_Management" title="The Society Management">The Society Management</a> in New York and <a href="/wiki/Elite_Model_Management" title="Elite Model Management">Elite Model Management</a> in Paris.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-21"><a href="#cite_note-21">[21]</a></sup> In January of the same year she was also featured in the psychedelic band <a href="/wiki/Animal_Collective" title="Animal Collective">Animal Collective</a>'s video for their song Applesauce from Centipede Hz. During the 2014 Paris Fashion Week, she modelled for Elie Saab and Atelier Versace, and for the Chanel Haute Couture show in Salzburg. She also graced the covers of Russian, Korean, Japanese and Ukrainian <i>Vogue</i>. In December, Wixson graced the cover of <i><a href="/wiki/Elle_(magazine)" title="Elle (magazine)">Elle</a></i> Japan, shot by <a href="/wiki/Karl_Lagerfeld" title="Karl Lagerfeld">Karl Lagerfeld</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Continued_Success.2C_2015.E2.80.93present">Continued Success, 2015–present</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lindsey_Wixson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Continued Success, 2015–present">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In 2015, Wixson appeared in campaigns for Chanel, Versace, <a href="/wiki/Urban_Outfitters" title="Urban Outfitters">Urban Outfitters</a>, <a href="/wiki/H%26M" title="H&amp;M">H&amp;M</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fendi" title="Fendi">Fendi</a>. She again graced the covers of Korean and Russian <i>Vogue</i> as well as <i>Vogue</i> Turkey. That season, she walked for designers including Chanel, Fendi, <a href="/wiki/Roland_Mouret" title="Roland Mouret">Roland Mouret</a>, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Kors" title="Michael Kors">Michael Kors</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vera_Wang" title="Vera Wang">Vera Wang</a>, <a class="mw-disambig" href="/wiki/Balmain" title="Balmain">Balmain</a>, <a href="/wiki/Diane_von_F%C3%BCrstenberg" title="Diane von Fürstenberg">Diane von Fürstenberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Max_Mara" title="Max Mara">Max Mara</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tom_Ford" title="Tom Ford">Tom Ford</a>.</p>
<p>In 2016, Wixson fronted campaigns for <a href="/wiki/Jill_Stuart" title="Jill Stuart">Jill Stuart</a> and <a href="/wiki/River_Island" title="River Island">River Island</a>. She walked the Chanel Resort wear show in <a href="/wiki/Cuba" title="Cuba">Cuba</a>, Chanel, Balmain and Gaultier at Paris Fashion Week and <a href="/wiki/Moschino" title="Moschino">Moschino</a> and Max Mara at <a href="/wiki/Milan_Fashion_Week" title="Milan Fashion Week">Milan Fashion Week</a>. She also graced the cover of Russian <i><a href="/wiki/Harper%27s_Bazaar" title="Harper's Bazaar">Harper's Bazaar</a></i>.</p>
<p>Wixson began 2017 fronting a campaign for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dior" title="Dior">Dior</a>, walking the Spring 2017 Chanel Haute Couture Show as well as appearing on the cover of Mexican <i>Harper's Bazaar</i>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lindsey_Wixson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Wixson's hobbies include art and writing. When she finishes modeling Wixson said she would love to become a pilot. Her model friends include <a href="/wiki/Daphne_Groeneveld" title="Daphne Groeneveld">Daphne Groeneveld</a>, <a href="/wiki/Camille_Rowe" title="Camille Rowe">Camille Rowe</a>, Ethan Green and Aymeline Valade. Wixson currently has her <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/GED" title="GED">GED</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (April 2011)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> Wixson expressed that modelling really helped build her confidence and is inspired by <a href="/wiki/Kate_Moss" title="Kate Moss">Kate Moss</a>'s fashion style.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Southern_Belle_3-6"><a href="#cite_note-Southern_Belle-3">[3]</a></sup> In 2011, her <a href="/wiki/Prom" title="Prom">prom</a> dress was designed especially for her by <a href="/wiki/Jason_Wu" title="Jason Wu">Jason Wu</a>, the same designer who designed <a href="/wiki/Michelle_Obama" title="Michelle Obama">Michelle Obama</a>'s dress for the inauguration.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-prom_18-1"><a href="#cite_note-prom-18">[18]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-22"><a href="#cite_note-22">[22]</a></sup></p>
