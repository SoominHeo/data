<p><b>Lily Monica Donaldson</b> (born 27 January 1987) is a British fashion <a href="/wiki/Model_(person)" title="Model (person)">model</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lily_Donaldson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Lily_Donaldson2.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="1600" data-file-width="1200" height="293" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b7/Lily_Donaldson2.jpg/220px-Lily_Donaldson2.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b7/Lily_Donaldson2.jpg/330px-Lily_Donaldson2.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b7/Lily_Donaldson2.jpg/440px-Lily_Donaldson2.jpg 2x" width="220"/></a>
<div class="thumbcaption">
<div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Lily_Donaldson2.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>
Lily Donaldson modeling at New York Fashion Week 2007</div>
</div>
</div>
<p>Donaldson was born in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, and educated at the <a href="/wiki/Camden_School_for_Girls" title="Camden School for Girls">Camden School for Girls</a>, living in <a href="/wiki/Kentish_Town" title="Kentish Town">Kentish Town</a> with her parents.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> She has a younger sister named Aurelia.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-femail_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-femail-2">[2]</a></sup> She was scouted by <a href="/wiki/Select_Model_Management" title="Select Model Management">Select Model Management</a> in 2003 at the age of 16 while shopping in <a href="/wiki/Camden_Town" title="Camden Town">Camden</a>, and went on to debut in the Fall/Winter 2004 season walking shows for designers such as <a href="/wiki/Chanel" title="Chanel">Chanel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Louis_Vuitton" title="Louis Vuitton">Louis Vuitton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Moschino" title="Moschino">Moschino</a>, <a href="/wiki/Missoni" title="Missoni">Missoni</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jill_Sander" title="Jill Sander">Jill Sander</a>.</p>
<p>Donaldson has notably landed big campaigns for fashion houses such as <a href="/wiki/Burberry" title="Burberry">Burberry</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gucci" title="Gucci">Gucci</a>, <a href="/wiki/Versace" title="Versace">Versace</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fendi" title="Fendi">Fendi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lanvin_(company)" title="Lanvin (company)">Lanvin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Calvin_Klein" title="Calvin Klein">Calvin Klein</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dior" title="Dior">Dior</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jil_Sander_(brand)" title="Jil Sander (brand)">Jil Sander</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dolce_%26_Gabbana" title="Dolce &amp; Gabbana">Dolce &amp; Gabbana</a>, <a href="/wiki/Balenciaga" title="Balenciaga">Balenciaga</a>, <a href="/wiki/Roberto_Cavalli" title="Roberto Cavalli">Roberto Cavalli</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Boss" title="Hugo Boss">Hugo Boss</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alexander_McQueen" title="Alexander McQueen">Alexander McQueen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Max_Mara" title="Max Mara">Max Mara</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shiatzy_Chen" title="Shiatzy Chen">Shiatzy Chen</a>, <a href="/wiki/H%26M" title="H&amp;M">H&amp;M</a>, <a href="/wiki/Topshop" title="Topshop">Topshop</a>, <a href="/wiki/Aldo_Group" title="Aldo Group">Aldo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kenzo" title="Kenzo">Kenzo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Anna_Sui" title="Anna Sui">Anna Sui</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Gap_Inc." title="Gap Inc.">Gap</a>.</p>
<p>In the May 2007 issue of American <i>Vogue</i>, she was featured on the cover with fellow models <a href="/wiki/Doutzen_Kroes" title="Doutzen Kroes">Doutzen Kroes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Caroline_Trentini" title="Caroline Trentini">Caroline Trentini</a>, <a href="/wiki/Raquel_Zimmermann" title="Raquel Zimmermann">Raquel Zimmermann</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sasha_Pivovarova" title="Sasha Pivovarova">Sasha Pivovarova</a>, <a href="/wiki/Agyness_Deyn" title="Agyness Deyn">Agyness Deyn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Coco_Rocha" title="Coco Rocha">Coco Rocha</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hilary_Rhoda" title="Hilary Rhoda">Hilary Rhoda</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chanel_Iman" title="Chanel Iman">Chanel Iman</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Jessica_Stam" title="Jessica Stam">Jessica Stam</a> as the "World's Next Top Models".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> Later, in the September 2007 issue of <a href="/wiki/Vogue_(British_magazine)" title="Vogue (British magazine)">British <i>Vogue</i></a>, she was named a "Head Girl", a model to watch out for in the upcoming season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nymag_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-nymag-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>Donaldson has appeared on 28 international covers of <i><a href="/wiki/Vogue_(magazine)" title="Vogue (magazine)">Vogue</a></i> which includes the British, American, Italia, China, Australia, Japan, Turkey, Taiwan, Korea, Portugal, Spain and Russian editions. In 2010, she made her <a href="/wiki/Victoria%27s_Secret_Fashion_Show" title="Victoria's Secret Fashion Show">Victoria's Secret Fashion Show</a> debut<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> and also walked in the 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, and 2016 shows.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2009, <i><a href="/wiki/Vogue_Paris" title="Vogue Paris">Vogue Paris</a></i> declared her one of the top 30 models of the 2000s.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> Donaldson is the face of the new <a href="/wiki/Monsoon_Accessorize" title="Monsoon Accessorize">Monsoon Accessorize</a> campaign for Spring Summer 2011.</p>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/2012_Summer_Olympics_closing_ceremony" title="2012 Summer Olympics closing ceremony">2012 Summer Olympics closing ceremony</a>, Donaldson was one of the British models wearing <a href="/wiki/Bespoke" title="Bespoke">bespoke</a> fashions created by British designers specifically for the event. The other models were <a href="/wiki/Naomi_Campbell" title="Naomi Campbell">Naomi Campbell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kate_Moss" title="Kate Moss">Kate Moss</a>, <a href="/wiki/David_Gandy" title="David Gandy">David Gandy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Karen_Elson" title="Karen Elson">Karen Elson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jourdan_Dunn" title="Jourdan Dunn">Jourdan Dunn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Georgia_May_Jagger" title="Georgia May Jagger">Georgia May Jagger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Stella_Tennant" title="Stella Tennant">Stella Tennant</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> She wore a gold ball gown by <a href="/wiki/Vivienne_Westwood" title="Vivienne Westwood">Vivienne Westwood</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lily_Donaldson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She is close friends with models <a href="/wiki/Gemma_Ward" title="Gemma Ward">Gemma Ward</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Irina_Lazareanu" title="Irina Lazareanu">Irina Lazareanu</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> the latter with whom she shot a <a href="/wiki/Mulberry_(company)" title="Mulberry (company)">Mulberry</a> campaign.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
<p>After five years living in <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York</a>, Donaldson moved back to London in late 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup></p>
<p>Fleetwood Mac legend <a href="/wiki/Mick_Fleetwood" title="Mick Fleetwood">Mick Fleetwood</a> is Lily's great-uncle.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup></p>
