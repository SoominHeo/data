<p><b>Marguerite MacIntyre</b> (born May 11, 1965) is an American actress. She performed in her first play at 16 and has appeared in regional theatre productions ranging from <i><a href="/wiki/Fahrenheit_451" title="Fahrenheit 451">Fahrenheit 451</a></i> at the Falcon Theatre to <i>South Pacific</i> at Goodspeed. She has also appeared in <a href="/wiki/Broadway_theatre" title="Broadway theatre">Broadway</a> or <a href="/wiki/Off-Broadway" title="Off-Broadway">Off-Broadway</a> productions of <i>Jane Eyre</i>, <i>City of Angels</i>, <i>No Way to Treat a Lady</i>, <i>Annie Warbucks</i>, <i>Weird Romance</i>, and <i>Mata Hari</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-imdb_bio_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-imdb_bio-1">[1]</a></sup> She starred on <i><a href="/wiki/The_Vampire_Diaries" title="The Vampire Diaries">The Vampire Diaries</a></i> as Sheriff Forbes until season six</p>
