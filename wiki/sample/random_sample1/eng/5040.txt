<p><b>Lichinomycetes</b> are a <a href="/wiki/Class_(biology)" title="Class (biology)">class</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ascomycete" title="Ascomycete">ascomycete</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fungi" title="Fungi">fungi</a>. It includes the single order <a href="/wiki/Lichinales" title="Lichinales">Lichinales</a>. Most species are lichenized. The class was circumscribed in 2004.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Reeb_2004_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Reeb_2004-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
