<p>The <b>Manado fruit bat</b> (<i>Rousettus bidens</i> or <i>Boneia bidens</i>) is a species of <a href="/wiki/Megabat" title="Megabat">megabat</a> in the family <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pteropodidae" title="Pteropodidae">Pteropodidae</a>. It is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Endemic" title="Endemic">endemic</a> to <a href="/wiki/Indonesia" title="Indonesia">Indonesia</a> (<a href="/wiki/Sulawesi" title="Sulawesi">Sulawesi</a>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Bon%C3%A9&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Boné (page does not exist)">Boné</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>).</p>
