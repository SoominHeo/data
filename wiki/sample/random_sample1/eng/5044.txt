<p><b>Manasi Moghe</b> (born 29 August 1991) is an <a href="/wiki/Indian_people" title="Indian people">Indian</a> actress, engineer, model and beauty pageant titleholder who was crowned <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Indian_Diva_2013" title="Indian Diva 2013">Miss Diva 2013</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> and represented <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a> at <a href="/wiki/Miss_Universe_2013" title="Miss Universe 2013">Miss Universe 2013</a> in <a href="/wiki/Moscow" title="Moscow">Moscow</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a> on 9 November 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Manasi_Moghe&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Manasi Moghe was born in <a href="/wiki/Indore" title="Indore">Indore</a>, <a href="/wiki/Madhya_Pradesh" title="Madhya Pradesh">Madhya Pradesh</a>, India into a <a href="/wiki/Marathi_people" title="Marathi people">Marathi</a> family. She completed her schooling from Sri Sathya Sai Vidya Vihar, Indore. She had done graduation B.E. in Electronics &amp; Telecommunication from <a href="/wiki/St._Vincent_Pallotti_College_of_Engineering_and_Technology" title="St. Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering and Technology">St. Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering and Technology, Nagpur</a>. Her parents are doctors working with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Coal_India_Limited" title="Coal India Limited">Coal India Limited</a> at <a href="/wiki/Chandrapur" title="Chandrapur">Chandrapur</a>, <a href="/wiki/Maharashtra" title="Maharashtra">Maharashtra</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Miss_Universe_2013">Miss Universe 2013</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Manasi_Moghe&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Miss Universe 2013">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Miss_Universe_2013" title="Miss Universe 2013">Miss Universe 2013</a> Pageant was held at <a href="/wiki/Crocus_City_Hall" title="Crocus City Hall">Crocus City Hall</a>, <a href="/wiki/Moscow" title="Moscow">Moscow</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>. Finale was on 9 November 2013 and Manasi Moghe placed among the Top Ten finalists.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Miss_Diva_-_2013">Miss Diva - 2013</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Manasi_Moghe&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Miss Diva - 2013">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:322px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:MIss_Diva_2013_Manasi_Moghe.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="4320" data-file-width="3240" height="427" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/db/MIss_Diva_2013_Manasi_Moghe.jpg/320px-MIss_Diva_2013_Manasi_Moghe.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/db/MIss_Diva_2013_Manasi_Moghe.jpg/480px-MIss_Diva_2013_Manasi_Moghe.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/db/MIss_Diva_2013_Manasi_Moghe.jpg/640px-MIss_Diva_2013_Manasi_Moghe.jpg 2x" width="320"/></a>

Manasi Moghe after being crowned the first Miss Diva Universe in 2013</div>

<p>The <a href="/wiki/Miss_Diva_-_2013" title="Miss Diva - 2013">Miss Diva - 2013</a> finale, held on 5 September 2013 Thursday night at Hotel Westin Mumbai Garden City, saw 14 finalists, including Manasi Moghe, competing against each other to win the title.</p>
<p>Manasi Moghe was crowned Miss Diva 2013, while <a href="/wiki/Gurleen_Grewal" title="Gurleen Grewal">Gurleen Grewal</a> was declared Miss Diva International and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Shristi_Rana" title="Shristi Rana">Srishti Rana</a> was declared Miss Diva Asia Pacific World.</p>
<p>Manasi then competed at <a href="/wiki/Miss_Universe_2013" title="Miss Universe 2013">Miss Universe 2013</a> in Moscow, finishing in the Top 10.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Femina_Miss_India_2013">Femina Miss India 2013</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Manasi_Moghe&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Femina Miss India 2013">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Manasi was one of the 21 finalists of <a href="/wiki/Femina_Miss_India_2013" title="Femina Miss India 2013">Femina Miss India 2013</a>. She got Wild Card Entry to the Pageant where she won Miss Active sub-title.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Film_career">Film career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Manasi_Moghe&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Film career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Manasi made her cinematic debut in a <a href="/wiki/Marathi_language" title="Marathi language">Marathi language</a> film title <i>Bugadi Maazi Sandli Ga</i> in the year 2014. She paired up with Kashyap Parulekar in the film. She is starring in a film opposite superstar <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ankush_Choudhary" title="Ankush Choudhary">Ankush Choudhary</a> in an upcoming Marathi film directed by <a href="/wiki/Satish_Rajwade" title="Satish Rajwade">Satish Rajwade</a>.</p>
