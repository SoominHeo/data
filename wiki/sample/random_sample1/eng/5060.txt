<p><i><b>Madame Antoine</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">마담 앙트완</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Madam Angteuwan</span></i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Korean_drama" title="Korean drama">South Korean television series</a> starring <a href="/wiki/Sung_Joon" title="Sung Joon">Sung Joon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Han_Ye-seul" title="Han Ye-seul">Han Ye-seul</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Jeong_Jinwoon" title="Jeong Jinwoon">Jeong Jinwoon</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The drama was originally scheduled to be aired on <a href="/wiki/JTBC" title="JTBC">JTBC</a> on November 27, 2015 but was delayed and premiered on January 22, 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Madame_Antoine&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Go Hye-rim (<a href="/wiki/Han_Ye-seul" title="Han Ye-seul">Han Ye-seul</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/Fortune-telling" title="Fortune-telling">fortune teller</a> and cafe owner who goes by the name Madame Antoine. She claims her fortune telling abilities are based on a spiritual connection to the famous French Queen <a href="/wiki/Marie_Antoinette" title="Marie Antoinette">Marie Antoinette</a>. Although by her own admission she lacks any true psychic connection to the queen, she is a skilled <a href="/wiki/Cold_reading" title="Cold reading">cold reader</a> with a knack for following hunches and picking up on details.</p>
<p>Choi Soo-hyeon (<a href="/wiki/Sung_Joon" title="Sung Joon">Sung Joon</a>) is a psychotherapist specializing in women's psychology and known as the 'Soul Doctor' amongst Hollywood stars. Although his <a href="/wiki/Stanford_University" title="Stanford University">Stanford</a> professorship and good looks make him a highly eligible bachelor, his past research and life experiences have left him skeptical that true love actually exists. Choi Soo-hyun moves into the space above Go Hye-rim's office and starts a psychology practice where he designs an experiment intending to prove his hypothesis about the nature of true love. His decision to name his practice Madame Antoine leads to a scuffle with Go Hye-rim, whom he then tricks into taking part in his experiment.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Cast">Cast</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Madame_Antoine&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Cast">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sung_Joon" title="Sung Joon">Sung Joon</a> as Choi Soo-hyun</li>
</ul>
<p>Choi Soo-hyun is a man in his 30s and a psychology professor from Stanford University. He has a cold personality and doesn't believe in love. He opens a psychology center on the 2nd floor above Go Hye-rim's cafe. He decides to secretly use her in his experiment (also called Madame Antoine) on how women truly feel about love. Although, the experiment is about a women falling in love with one of the 3 suitors (including Soo-Hyun) he eagerly wants Hye-rim to fall in love with him in revenge for her being rude towards him. He plays the rich man in his 30s for the experiment.</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Han_Ye-seul" title="Han Ye-seul">Han Ye-seul</a> as Go Hye-rim</li>
</ul>
<p>Hye-rim is a con-artist and fortune-teller in her early 30s. She runs a cafe called "Madame Antoine", where she makes people believe that she has special connections with the ghost of <a href="/wiki/Marie_Antoinette" title="Marie Antoinette">Marie Antoinette</a>. She cons people so that she can pay for daughter's tuition. She despises Soo-hyun for opening a psychological center above her cafe, coincidentally named "Madame Antoine". She believes people will misunderstand between her cafe &amp; fortune telling place and Soo-hyun psychology center. Even though her job is just deceiving people, she has a good talent on observing things.</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Jeong_Jinwoon" title="Jeong Jinwoon">Jeong Jinwoon</a> as Choi Seung-chan<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-_3-0"><a href="#cite_note--3">[3]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<p>Seung-chan is a former rookie rising baseball star and the younger half-brother of Choi Soo-hyun. After he quits being baseball, he decides to move in with his older brother after hearing Soo-hyun came back from America. Initially, his brother rejects him from staying with him, but then agrees to let him in if Seungchan takes part in his experiment "Madame Antoine" while competing with Soo-hyun and Ji-ho in return. He play the role of a man with an athlete vibe in his 20s for the experiment.</p>
<ul>
<li>Lee Joo-hyung as Won Ji-ho</li>
</ul>
<p>Won Ji-ho is a genius that has an IQ of 210. Because of his lack in communication and understanding to people, he decides to go to psychology field and become Soo-hyun's student. He is a very innocent guy also behaves stiff and child-like. He was ordered by Soo-hyun to become a part of the experiment "Madame Antoine" where he competes with Soo-hyun and Seung-chan for Hye-rim's heart. He ends up liking Go Yoo-rim. He plays the role of the flower-boy in his 20s for the experiment.</p>
<ul>
<li>Hwang Seung-eon as Go Yoo-rim</li>
</ul>
<p>Go Yoo-rim is Hye-rim's little sister who works as a camera lady and also helps at her sister's cafe. At first, she helps Ji-ho into getting her older sister fall in with him but ends up falling for him herself. Thus, she becomes jealous of Hye-rim. She takes advantage of Ji-ho's innocence and child-like behavior, by "teaching him how to ..."</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Chang_Mi-hee" title="Chang Mi-hee">Jang Mi-hee</a> as Bae Mi-ran</li>
</ul>
<p>She is psychologist in her old age. After her plan of retiring from jobs, she decided to work on 'Madame Antoine' center after she met Seung-chan when she pay a visit to the center as Soo-hyun supervisor. A feeling of being youth comeback again after she met Seung-chan and cheering her up.</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Byun_Hee-bong" title="Byun Hee-bong">Byun Hee-bong</a> as President Kim</li>
</ul>
<p>He is a successful CEO of a big cosmetics company, he is also the owner of the building Hye-rim and Soo-hyun work in. Hye-rim thought President Kim is just an average old landlord but find out that he's a rich man. He seems amused by Hye-rim talent at observing on things and her job as a fortune teller. He is very nice to Hye-rim and helps her whenever she needs him.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Cameo_Appearances">Cameo Appearances</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Madame_Antoine&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Cameo Appearances">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kim_Jae-kyung" title="Kim Jae-kyung">Kim Jae-kyung</a> as Jyu-ni</li>
</ul>
<p>She is a famous singer that gifted with beauty. But she actually is a singer with a mental issue that craving peoples attention, she get jealous of Hye-rim because she get to enjoy herself with 3 good looking guys in 'Madame Antoine' center. Her character is kind of stingy and childish but actually she is not a bad person at all.</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Lee_Sun-bin_(actress)" title="Lee Sun-bin (actress)">Lee Sun-bin</a> as Ma-Ri</li>
</ul>
<p>She is the South Korea representative as a gymnastic athlete. She almost lost her ability to see after she received many hates from the anti-fans.</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Go_Joo-won" title="Go Joo-won">Go Joo-won</a> as Hye-Rim's ex-husband</li>
</ul>
<p>He divorced to Hye-rim because he doesn't feel happy with her anymore and already found a new woman. Now, he live with his new wife also with his and Hye-rim's daughter in America.</p>
