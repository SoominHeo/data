<p><b><a href="/wiki/Judo" title="Judo">Judo</a></b> at the <b><a href="/wiki/2016_Summer_Olympics" title="2016 Summer Olympics">2016 Summer Olympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/Rio_de_Janeiro" title="Rio de Janeiro">Rio de Janeiro</a> took place from 6 to 12 August at the <a href="/wiki/Carioca_Arena_2" title="Carioca Arena 2">Carioca Arena 2</a> inside the <a href="/wiki/Barra_Olympic_Park" title="Barra Olympic Park">Barra Olympic Park</a> in <a href="/wiki/Barra_da_Tijuca" title="Barra da Tijuca">Barra da Tijuca</a>. Around 386 judoka competed in 14 events (seven each for both men and women).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualification">Qualification</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Judo_at_the_2016_Summer_Olympics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Qualification">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Judo_at_the_2016_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Qualification" title="Judo at the 2016 Summer Olympics – Qualification">Judo at the 2016 Summer Olympics – Qualification</a></div>
<p>Similar to 2012 format, qualification was based on the world ranking list prepared by <a href="/wiki/International_Judo_Federation" title="International Judo Federation">International Judo Federation</a> as of May 30, 2016. A total of 252 athletes directly qualified through the ranking with only the top 22 men or top 14 women in each division, ensuring that each NOC was subjected to a limit of one judoka per division.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Competition_schedule">Competition schedule</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Judo_at_the_2016_Summer_Olympics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Competition schedule">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>There were two sessions of competition on each day of the 2016 Olympic Judo program. The first session (Elimination &amp; Quarterfinal) was conducted from 10:00 to 13:00 <a href="/wiki/Time_in_Brazil#Bras.C3.ADlia_time.2C_BRT_.28UTC.E2.88.9203:00.29" title="Time in Brazil">BRT</a>, and the second session (repechage, semifinal, Bronze medal and Gold medal) was conducted from 15:30 to 18:10 <a href="/wiki/Time_in_Brazil#Bras.C3.ADlia_time.2C_BRT_.28UTC.E2.88.9203:00.29" title="Time in Brazil">BRT</a>.</p>
