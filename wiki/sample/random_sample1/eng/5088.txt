<p><b>Marco Sanchez</b> is an American actor, producer, and writer.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Marco_Sanchez&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The son of <a href="/wiki/Cuba" title="Cuba">Cuban</a> immigrants, and the youngest of 4 siblings, Marco Sanchez was born in Los Angeles, California, and raised in <a href="/wiki/Palm_Desert,_California" title="Palm Desert, California">Palm Desert, California</a>. Having studied theater throughout junior high and high school, he graduated from the UCLA School of Theater, Film, and Television in 1992.</p>
<p>His television work includes series regular roles on <a href="/wiki/Steven_Spielberg" title="Steven Spielberg">Steven Spielberg's</a> <i><a href="/wiki/SeaQuest_DSV" title="SeaQuest DSV">seaQuest DSV</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Walker,_Texas_Ranger" title="Walker, Texas Ranger">Walker, Texas Ranger</a></i>, recurring roles on <i><a href="/wiki/NCIS_(TV_series)" title="NCIS (TV series)">NCIS</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Client_List_(TV_series)" title="The Client List (TV series)">The Client List</a></i>, as well as numerous guest star appearances on shows such as <i><a href="/wiki/24_(TV_series)" title="24 (TV series)">24</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Desperate_Housewives" title="Desperate Housewives">Desperate Housewives</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/ER_(TV_series)" title="ER (TV series)">ER</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Mentalist" title="The Mentalist">The Mentalist</a></i>.</p>
<p>His film performances include <a href="/wiki/Showtime_(TV_network)" title="Showtime (TV network)">Showtime</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/The_Last_Debate" title="The Last Debate">The Last Debate</a></i> (directed by <a href="/wiki/John_Badham" title="John Badham">John Badham</a>), <i><a href="/wiki/Illusion_(2004_film)" title="Illusion (2004 film)">Illusion</a></i> (written and directed by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Michael_Goorjian" title="Michael Goorjian">Michael Goorjian</a>), <i><a href="/wiki/The_Rookie_(2002_film)" title="The Rookie (2002 film)">The Rookie</a></i> (with <a href="/wiki/Dennis_Quaid" title="Dennis Quaid">Dennis Quaid</a>), and most recently in <i><a href="/wiki/Super_8_(film)" title="Super 8 (film)">Super 8</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Trek_Into_Darkness" title="Star Trek Into Darkness">Star Trek Into Darkness</a></i> (both directed by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/J.J._Abrams" title="J.J. Abrams">J.J. Abrams</a>).</p>
<p>In 2006, Marco co-founded <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Lyceum_Films&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Lyceum Films (page does not exist)">Lyceum Films</a> with actor/director <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Michael_Goorjian" title="Michael Goorjian">Michael Goorjian</a>, and Muse Media Center founder <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Noah_Veneklasen&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Noah Veneklasen (page does not exist)">Noah Veneklasen</a>. Marco’s producing credits with Lyceum include: <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Players%27_Club" title="Players' Club">Players' Club</a></i>, <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=5_Wishes&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="5 Wishes (page does not exist)">5 Wishes</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_War_Prayer" title="The War Prayer">The War Prayer</a></i> (for which he also wrote the adapted screenplay), <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=The_Shift_(video)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="The Shift (video) (page does not exist)">The Shift</a></i>, and <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Tales_of_Everyday_Magic&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Tales of Everyday Magic (page does not exist)">Tales of Everyday Magic</a></i>.</p>
