<p><b>Marco Engelhardt</b> (born 2 December 1980) is a German professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Midfielder#Defensive_midfielder" title="Midfielder">defensive midfielder</a> for <a href="/wiki/TSG_1899_Hoffenheim_II" title="TSG 1899 Hoffenheim II">1899 Hoffenheim II</a>.</p>

<p>He previously played for <a href="/wiki/Karlsruher_SC" title="Karlsruher SC">Karlsruher SC</a>, <a href="/wiki/1._FC_N%C3%BCrnberg" title="1. FC Nürnberg">1. FC Nürnberg</a> and <a href="/wiki/1._FC_Kaiserslautern" title="1. FC Kaiserslautern">1. FC Kaiserslautern</a>. On 3 March 2007, he played his first Bundesliga match for Nürnberg against <a href="/wiki/Arminia_Bielefeld" title="Arminia Bielefeld">Arminia Bielefeld</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> On 19 December 2008 he announced his return to <a href="/wiki/Karlsruher_SC" title="Karlsruher SC">Karlsruher SC</a>, signing a contract until 30 June 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
