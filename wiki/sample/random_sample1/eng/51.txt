<p><b>November 27</b> is the 331st day of the year (332nd in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 34 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Tuesday, Friday or Sunday (58 in 400 years each) than on Wednesday or Thursday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Monday or Saturday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=November_27&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/AD_25" title="AD 25">AD 25</a> – <a href="/wiki/Luoyang" title="Luoyang">Luoyang</a> is <a href="/wiki/History_of_the_Han_dynasty#Reconsolidation_under_Guangwu" title="History of the Han dynasty">declared</a> capital of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Eastern_Han_dynasty" title="Eastern Han dynasty">Eastern Han dynasty</a> by <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Guangwu_of_Han" title="Emperor Guangwu of Han">Emperor Guangwu of Han</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/176" title="176">176</a> – Emperor <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Aurelius" title="Marcus Aurelius">Marcus Aurelius</a> grants his son <a href="/wiki/Commodus" title="Commodus">Commodus</a> the rank of "<a href="/wiki/Imperator" title="Imperator">Imperator</a>" and makes him Supreme Commander of the <a href="/wiki/Roman_legion" title="Roman legion">Roman legions</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/395" title="395">395</a> – <a href="/wiki/Rufinus_(consul)" title="Rufinus (consul)">Rufinus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Praetorian_prefecture_of_the_East" title="Praetorian prefecture of the East">praetorian prefect of the East</a>, is murdered by <a href="/wiki/Goths" title="Goths">Gothic</a> mercenaries under <a href="/wiki/Gainas" title="Gainas">Gainas</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/511" title="511">511</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Clovis_I" title="Clovis I">Clovis I</a> dies at <a href="/wiki/Lutetia" title="Lutetia">Lutetia</a> and is buried in the <a href="/wiki/Abbey_of_St_Genevieve" title="Abbey of St Genevieve">Abbey of St Genevieve</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/602" title="602">602</a> – Emperor <a href="/wiki/Maurice_(emperor)" title="Maurice (emperor)">Maurice</a> is forced to watch his five sons be executed before being <a href="/wiki/Decapitation" title="Decapitation">beheaded</a> himself.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1095" title="1095">1095</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Urban_II" title="Pope Urban II">Pope Urban II</a> declares the <a href="/wiki/First_Crusade" title="First Crusade">First Crusade</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Council_of_Clermont" title="Council of Clermont">Council of Clermont</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1703" title="1703">1703</a> – The first <a href="/wiki/Eddystone_Lighthouse" title="Eddystone Lighthouse">Eddystone Lighthouse</a> is destroyed in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Storm_of_1703" title="Great Storm of 1703">Great Storm of 1703</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1727" title="1727">1727</a> – The foundation stone to the <a href="/wiki/Jerusalem_Church_(Berlin)" title="Jerusalem Church (Berlin)">Jerusalem Church</a> in Berlin is laid.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1807" title="1807">1807</a> – The Portuguese Royal Family <a href="/wiki/History_of_Portugal_(1777%E2%80%931834)#Napoleonic_invasions" title="History of Portugal (1777–1834)">leaves Lisbon to escape from Napoleonic troops</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1810" title="1810">1810</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Berners_Street_hoax" title="Berners Street hoax">Berners Street hoax</a> was perpetrated by <a href="/wiki/Theodore_Hook" title="Theodore Hook">Theodore Hook</a> in the <a href="/wiki/City_of_Westminster" title="City of Westminster">City of Westminster</a>, London.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1815" title="1815">1815</a> – Adoption of <a href="/wiki/Constitution_of_the_Kingdom_of_Poland" title="Constitution of the Kingdom of Poland">Constitution of the Kingdom of Poland</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1830" title="1830">1830</a> – Saint <a href="/wiki/Catherine_Labour%C3%A9" title="Catherine Labouré">Catherine Labouré</a> experiences a <a href="/wiki/Marian_apparition" title="Marian apparition">Marian apparition</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1835" title="1835">1835</a> – <a href="/wiki/James_Pratt_and_John_Smith" title="James Pratt and John Smith">James Pratt and John Smith</a> are hanged in London; they are the last two to be executed for sodomy in England.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1839" title="1839">1839</a> – In <a href="/wiki/Boston" title="Boston">Boston</a>, <a href="/wiki/Massachusetts" title="Massachusetts">Massachusetts</a>, the <a href="/wiki/American_Statistical_Association" title="American Statistical Association">American Statistical Association</a> is founded.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1856" title="1856">1856</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Luxembourg_Coup_of_1856" title="Luxembourg Coup of 1856">Coup of 1856</a> leads to <a href="/wiki/Luxembourg" title="Luxembourg">Luxembourg</a>'s unilateral adoption of a new, reactionary <a href="/wiki/Constitution_of_Luxembourg" title="Constitution of Luxembourg">constitution</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1863" title="1863">1863</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Civil_War" title="American Civil War">American Civil War</a>: <a href="/wiki/Confederate_States_of_America" title="Confederate States of America">Confederate</a> cavalry leader <a href="/wiki/John_Hunt_Morgan" title="John Hunt Morgan">John Hunt Morgan</a> and several of his men escape the <a href="/wiki/Ohio_Penitentiary" title="Ohio Penitentiary">Ohio Penitentiary</a> and return safely to the <a href="/wiki/Confederate_States_of_America" title="Confederate States of America">South</a>.</li>
<li>1863 – American Civil War: <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Mine_Run" title="Battle of Mine Run">Battle of Mine Run</a>: <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">Union</a> forces under General <a href="/wiki/George_Meade" title="George Meade">George Meade</a> take up positions against troops led by <a href="/wiki/Confederate_States_of_America" title="Confederate States of America">Confederate</a> General <a href="/wiki/Robert_E._Lee" title="Robert E. Lee">Robert E. Lee</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1868" title="1868">1868</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Indian_Wars" title="American Indian Wars">American Indian Wars</a>: <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Washita_River" title="Battle of Washita River">Battle of Washita River</a>: <a href="/wiki/United_States_Army" title="United States Army">United States Army</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lieutenant_Colonel" title="Lieutenant Colonel">Lieutenant Colonel</a> <a href="/wiki/George_Armstrong_Custer" title="George Armstrong Custer">George Armstrong Custer</a> leads an attack on <a href="/wiki/Cheyenne" title="Cheyenne">Cheyenne</a> living on reservation land.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1886" title="1886">1886</a> – German judge <a href="/wiki/Emil_Hartwich" title="Emil Hartwich">Emil Hartwich</a> sustains fatal injuries in a <a href="/wiki/Duel" title="Duel">duel</a>, which would become the background for <a href="/wiki/Theodor_Fontane" title="Theodor Fontane">Theodor Fontane</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Effi_Briest" title="Effi Briest">Effi Briest</a></i>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1895" title="1895">1895</a> – At the Swedish–Norwegian Club in Paris, <a href="/wiki/Alfred_Nobel" title="Alfred Nobel">Alfred Nobel</a> signs his <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Last_will_and_testament" title="Last will and testament">last will and testament</a>, setting aside his estate to establish the <a href="/wiki/Nobel_Prize" title="Nobel Prize">Nobel Prize</a> after he dies.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1896" title="1896">1896</a> – <a href="/wiki/Also_sprach_Zarathustra_(Strauss)" title="Also sprach Zarathustra (Strauss)">Also sprach Zarathustra</a> by <a href="/wiki/Richard_Strauss" title="Richard Strauss">Richard Strauss</a> is first performed.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1901" title="1901">1901</a> – The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/U.S._Army_War_College" title="U.S. Army War College">U.S. Army War College</a> is established.</li>
