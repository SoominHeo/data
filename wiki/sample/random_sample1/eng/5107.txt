<p><b>Marie Vinck</b> (born January 3, 1983) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Belgian" title="Belgian">Belgian</a>-<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Flemish_language" title="Flemish language">Flemish</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Actress" title="Actress">actress</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Voice_actress" title="Voice actress">voice actress</a>. She started acting in 1993 at the age of 10 in the Flemish TV series, <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Mother,_why_do_we_live%3F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Mother, why do we live? (page does not exist)">Mother, why do we live?</a></i>, which Marie's mother has performed a leading role in the series. In 1998, she acted out again with her mother in the Dutch-Flemish movie: <i><a href="/wiki/Dandelion_Game" title="Dandelion Game">Dandelion Game</a></i>.</p>
<p>Marie Vinck has also given the Flemish <a href="/wiki/Dubbing_(filmmaking)" title="Dubbing (filmmaking)">dubbing</a> voice to <a href="/wiki/Emma_Watson" title="Emma Watson">Emma Watson</a>'s role as <a href="/wiki/Hermione_Granger" title="Hermione Granger">Hermione Granger</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(film_series)" title="Harry Potter (film series)">Harry Potter films</a>, along with her colleagues <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Stef_Aerts&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Stef Aerts (page does not exist)">Stef Aerts</a> who gave the Flemish voice to <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Radcliffe" title="Daniel Radcliffe">Daniel Radcliffe</a>'s role as <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(character)" title="Harry Potter (character)">Harry Potter</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Wouter_Hendrickx&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Wouter Hendrickx (page does not exist)">Wouter Hendrickx</a> who gave the Flemish voice to <a href="/wiki/Rupert_Grint" title="Rupert Grint">Rupert Grint</a>'s role as <a href="/wiki/Ron_Weasley" title="Ron Weasley">Ron Weasley</a>.</p>
<p>Marie currently lives in <a href="/wiki/Antwerp" title="Antwerp">Antwerp</a>, and she happens to have a relationship with Stef Aerts.</p>
