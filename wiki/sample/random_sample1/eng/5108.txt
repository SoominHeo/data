<p><b>Marina Comas</b> (born September 1, 1996) is a Spanish actress. Her credits include the television series <i><a href="/wiki/Polseres_vermelles" title="Polseres vermelles">Polseres vermelles</a></i> and the films <i><a href="/wiki/Black_Bread" title="Black Bread">Black Bread</a></i> and <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Els_Nens_Salvatges" title="Els Nens Salvatges">Els Nens Salvatges</a></i>.</p>
<p>Comas was born in <a href="/wiki/Santa_Maria_de_Besora" title="Santa Maria de Besora">Santa Maria de Besora</a>, <a href="/wiki/Osona" title="Osona">Osona</a>..</p>
