<p><b>Marina Carolina Pérez</b> (born July 31, 1984, in <a href="/wiki/Madrid" title="Madrid">Madrid</a>, <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Spanish_people" title="Spanish people">Spanish</a> <a href="/wiki/Model_(person)" title="Model (person)">model</a> known for her eyes. She has been the face for <a href="/wiki/Jimmy_Choo" title="Jimmy Choo">Jimmy Choo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alessandro_Dell%27Acqua" title="Alessandro Dell'Acqua">Alessandro Dell'Acqua</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pucci" title="Pucci">Pucci</a>, <a href="/wiki/Moschino" title="Moschino">Moschino</a>, <a href="/wiki/Giorgio_Armani" title="Giorgio Armani">Giorgio Armani</a>, <a href="/wiki/St._John_(clothing)" title="St. John (clothing)">St. John</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Marc_Jacobs" title="Marc Jacobs">Marc Jacobs</a> campaigns.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NYM_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-NYM-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_and_career">Early life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Marina_P%C3%A9rez&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Martina_Perez.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="3264" data-file-width="2448" height="227" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b5/Martina_Perez.jpg/170px-Martina_Perez.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b5/Martina_Perez.jpg/255px-Martina_Perez.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b5/Martina_Perez.jpg/340px-Martina_Perez.jpg 2x" width="170"/></a>

<p>Encouraged by her mother, Pérez began modeling at the age of 11 posing for catalogs and ads. She made her catwalk debut at the fall Madrid runway for Miguel Palacio, and appeared on her first magazine cover, for Spanish <a href="/wiki/Elle_(magazine)" title="Elle (magazine)">Elle</a>. Recognition came when she was presented with the <a href="/wiki/L%27Or%C3%A9al" title="L'Oréal">L'Oréal</a> modeling award at the <a href="/wiki/Cibeles_Madrid_Fashion_Week" title="Cibeles Madrid Fashion Week">Cibeles Madrid Fashion Week</a> and since then she has not stopped working.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> A break into fashion at the international level came in late 2001 after she moved to New York at age 18. In September 2003 she debuted in <a href="/wiki/New_York_Fashion_Week" title="New York Fashion Week">New York Fashion Week</a> for <a href="/wiki/Betsey_Johnson" title="Betsey Johnson">Betsey Johnson</a>. The <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/New_York_Magazine" title="New York Magazine">New York Magazine</a></i> reported that Pérez commanded over $8,000 per runway show in 2004 and,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NYM_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-NYM-2">[2]</a></sup> it was said she earned <a href="/wiki/Euro" title="Euro">€</a> 5,000 for the Milan shows.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Her runway credentials include walking for designers: <a href="/wiki/Christian_Dior" title="Christian Dior">Christian Dior</a>, <a href="/wiki/Louis_Vuitton" title="Louis Vuitton">Louis Vuitton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yves_Saint_Laurent_(designer)" title="Yves Saint Laurent (designer)">Yves Saint Laurent</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alexander_McQueen" title="Alexander McQueen">Alexander McQueen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chlo%C3%A9" title="Chloé">Chloé</a>, <a href="/wiki/Givenchy" title="Givenchy">Givenchy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Prada" title="Prada">Prada</a>, <a href="/wiki/Balenciaga" title="Balenciaga">Balenciaga</a>, <a href="/wiki/Miu_Miu" title="Miu Miu">Miu Miu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jeanne_Lanvin" title="Jeanne Lanvin">Lanvin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dolce_%26_Gabbana" title="Dolce &amp; Gabbana">Dolce &amp; Gabbana</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dries_van_Noten" title="Dries van Noten">Dries van Noten</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rick_Owens" title="Rick Owens">Rick Owens</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rodarte" title="Rodarte">Rodarte</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marni" title="Marni">Marni</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gucci" title="Gucci">Gucci</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Thakoon" title="Thakoon">Thakoon</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Nina_Ricci_(designer)" title="Nina Ricci (designer)">Nina Ricci</a> in <a href="/wiki/New_York_Fashion_Week" title="New York Fashion Week">New York</a>, <a href="/wiki/London_Fashion_Week" title="London Fashion Week">London</a>, <a href="/wiki/Milan_Fashion_Week" title="Milan Fashion Week">Milan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Paris_Fashion_Week" title="Paris Fashion Week">Paris</a>. She has opened shows for <a href="/wiki/Proenza_Schouler" title="Proenza Schouler">Proenza Schouler</a> f/w 06 in New York and for <a href="/wiki/Christopher_Kane" title="Christopher Kane">Christopher Kane</a> s/s 07 in London. Pérez has worked with high-calibre photographers such as <a href="/wiki/Steven_Meisel" title="Steven Meisel">Steven Meisel</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Inez_van_Lamsweerde_and_Vinoodh_Matadin" title="Inez van Lamsweerde and Vinoodh Matadin">Inez and Vinoodh</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mert_and_Marcus" title="Mert and Marcus">Mert n Marcus</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Patrick_Demarchelier" title="Patrick Demarchelier">Patrick Demarchelier</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> She has been the cover of magazines, <i><a href="/wiki/Vogue_(magazine)" title="Vogue (magazine)">Vogue</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dazed_%26_Confused_(magazine)" title="Dazed &amp; Confused (magazine)">Dazed &amp; Confused</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Num%C3%A9ro" title="Numéro">Numéro</a></i> and <i>10 Magazine</i>. She has appeared on the cover of Italian <i>Vogue</i> and twice on Spanish <i>Vogue</i>. In 2008 she replaced <a href="/wiki/Angelina_Jolie" title="Angelina Jolie">Angelina Jolie</a> as one of the faces of <a href="/wiki/St._John_(clothing)" title="St. John (clothing)">St. John</a> alongside <a href="/wiki/Hilary_Rhoda" title="Hilary Rhoda">Hilary Rhoda</a> and <a href="/wiki/Caroline_Winberg" title="Caroline Winberg">Caroline Winberg</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FMD_1-4"><a href="#cite_note-FMD-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NYM_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-NYM-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Her younger sister Carla is also a model. Pérez has a tattoo on the inside of her right wrist.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FMD_1-5"><a href="#cite_note-FMD-1">[1]</a></sup> Her natural hair color is blonde.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
