<p><b>María Pujalte</b> (<a href="/wiki/A_Coru%C3%B1a" title="A Coruña">Corunna</a>, December 22, 1966) is a <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spanish</a> actress.</p>
<p>She studied singing, drama and self-expression through movement in <a href="/wiki/Santiago_de_Compostela" title="Santiago de Compostela">Santiago de Compostela</a> and at the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Scuola_Internazionale_dell%27Attore_Comico&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Scuola Internazionale dell'Attore Comico (page does not exist)">Scuola Internazionale dell'Attore Comico</a> from <a href="/wiki/Reggio_Emilia" title="Reggio Emilia">Reggio Emilia</a> with a scholarship of the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Deputation_of_Corunna&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Deputation of Corunna (page does not exist)">Deputation of Corunna</a>.</p>
<p>She has been a member of <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Centro_Dram%C3%A1tico_Galego&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Centro Dramático Galego (page does not exist)">Centro Dramático Galego</a> and of the theatre groups <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Moucho_Clerc&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Moucho Clerc (page does not exist)">Moucho Clerc</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Compa%C3%B1%C3%ADa_de_Mar%C3%ADas&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Compañía de Marías (page does not exist)">Compañía de Marías</a>.</p>
