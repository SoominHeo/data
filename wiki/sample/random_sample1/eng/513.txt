<p><b>Winter Universiade 2017</b>, the XXVIII Winter <a href="/wiki/Universiade" title="Universiade">Universiade</a>, is a multi sport winter event which will take place in <a href="/wiki/Almaty" title="Almaty">Almaty</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kazakhstan" title="Kazakhstan">Kazakhstan</a>. On 29 November 2011 FISU announced that Winter Universiade 2017 will be hosted in Almaty.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Student-athletes from all over the world between the ages of 17 and 28 will flock to Almaty to take part in Winter Universiade organized by FISU.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Universiade_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Universiade-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Host-City_Candidates">Host-City Candidates</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Winter_Universiade&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Host-City Candidates">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>There were two candidates to host the event.</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Almaty" title="Almaty">Almaty</a>. This will be the third time that a former soviet country will host the Winter Universiade. Russia hosted two times the Summer Universiade – Moscow (1973), Kazan (2013) and will host the Winter Universiade two years later in <a href="/wiki/Krasnoyarsk" title="Krasnoyarsk">Krasnoyarsk</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Trentino" title="Trentino">Trentino</a>, Italy. Until 2011, Italy hosted the Universiade 9 times (4 Summer Universiades and 5 Winter Universiades). A week before the announcement of results of the bidding campaign, in connection with the economic crisis, the Italian government has decided to withdraw its candidacy.</li>
</ul>
<p>«Given the difficult economic situation in Italy and the high financial obligations to the International Federation for the moment, the Council of the Autonomous Province of Trento sees no possibility to raise funds for the Universiade in 2017 and therefore removes the candidature of the city.»</p>
<p>Later <a href="/wiki/Trentino" title="Trentino">Trentino</a> was invited to host the <a href="/wiki/2013_Winter_Universiade" title="2013 Winter Universiade">2013 Winter Universiade</a>, after <a href="/wiki/Maribor" title="Maribor">Maribor</a> withdrew to host the event.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>Formal decision on the Universiade in Almaty was taken on a vote of the <a class="external text" href="http://www.fisu.net/en/International-university-sports-federation-3137.html" rel="nofollow">International University Sports Federation (FISU)</a> on November 29, 2011, which was held in Brussels (Belgium).</p>
<p>Thus, due to the lack of competitors, Almaty was acclaimed the host city of the 28th Winter Universiade in 2017.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Venues">Venues</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Winter_Universiade&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Venues">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Almaty">Almaty</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Winter_Universiade&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Almaty">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Earlier it was reported that Kazakhstan will spend $950 million on Winter Universiade 2017. An Ice Palace, two ice rinks, an Athletic Village and international airport terminal and many more other sport and non-sport <a class="extiw" href="https://ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%A1%D0%BF%D0%BE%D1%80%D1%82%D0%B8%D0%B2%D0%BD%D1%8B%D0%B5_%D0%BE%D0%B1%D1%8A%D0%B5%D0%BA%D1%82%D1%8B_%D0%97%D0%B8%D0%BC%D0%BD%D0%B5%D0%B9_%D0%A3%D0%BD%D0%B8%D0%B2%D0%B5%D1%80%D1%81%D0%B8%D0%B0%D0%B4%D1%8B_2017" title="ru:Спортивные объекты Зимней Универсиады 2017">venues</a> will be built in Almaty for the event. The cost is considered small, and it is because some of the objects had already been built for the <a href="/wiki/2011_Asian_Winter_Games" title="2011 Asian Winter Games">7th Asian Winter Games</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> part of the competition which also took place in Almaty, and certain existing and remodeled venues. Kazakhstan chose bandy, ski jumping, Nordic combined, freestyle skiing and speed skating as optional sports, with bandy being in the form of demonstration. However, after tightening the budget, bandy was removed.</p>

<p>In January 17, it was announced the cancellation of pairs competition in the figure skating due to the low number of entrants. Only two pairs from Russia were registered in the event, which forced the cancellation of the same, according to the rules of FISU.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cancel_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-cancel-6">[6]</a></sup></p>

<p>The logo of the Winter Universiade 2017 was created considering the following images:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li>Letter "U" – is an essential attribute of all Universiade logos</li>
<li>Wings – symbolizes the aspiration to win</li>
</ul>
<p>The logo was created in bright and soft colors that emphasize the modernity and the eco-friendly approach, which is one of the most important elements of the general concept of the Games.</p>
