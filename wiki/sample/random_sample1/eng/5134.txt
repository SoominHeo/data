<p><i><b>Mamoritai (White Wishes)</b></i> <span style="font-weight:normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">まもりたい ～White Wishes～</span><span class="t_nihongo_help"><sup><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span style="color:#00e; font:bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration:none; padding:0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup></span>, lit. Want to Protect (White Wishes))</span> is <a href="/wiki/BoA" title="BoA">BoA</a>'s twenty-ninth<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SNE_BOA_29th_single_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-SNE_BOA_29th_single-1">[1]</a></sup> Japanese single. It was released on December 9, 2009,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Release_date_mention_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Release_date_mention-2">[2]</a></sup> in three formats, Limited Tales of Graces Edition, CD-only and CD+DVD.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>"Mamoritai (White Wishes)" is featured as the theme song of the game <i><a href="/wiki/Tales_of_Graces" title="Tales of Graces">Tales of Graces</a></i>.</p>
