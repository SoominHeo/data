<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Format">Format</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_FIFA_World_Cup_qualification_%E2%80%93_CONCACAF_Fifth_Round&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Format">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A total of six teams which advanced from the <a href="/wiki/2018_FIFA_World_Cup_qualification_%E2%80%93_CONCACAF_Fourth_Round" title="2018 FIFA World Cup qualification – CONCACAF Fourth Round">fourth round</a> (the three group winners and the three group runners-up) will play against each other home-and-away in a <a href="/wiki/Round-robin_tournament" title="Round-robin tournament">round-robin format</a> in a single group. The top three teams in the group will qualify for the <a href="/wiki/2018_FIFA_World_Cup" title="2018 FIFA World Cup">2018 FIFA World Cup</a>, and the fourth-placed team will advance to the <a href="/wiki/2018_FIFA_World_Cup_qualification_(inter-confederation_play-offs)" title="2018 FIFA World Cup qualification (inter-confederation play-offs)">inter-confederation play-offs</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-approval_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-approval-1">[1]</a></sup> This stage is referred to as the <i>Hexagonal</i> or <i>Hex</i>, and has been used by CONCACAF to determine its World Cup finals entrants since the <a href="/wiki/1998_FIFA_World_Cup_qualification_(CONCACAF)" title="1998 FIFA World Cup qualification (CONCACAF)">qualification tournament</a> for the <a href="/wiki/1998_FIFA_World_Cup" title="1998 FIFA World Cup">1998 FIFA World Cup</a>.</p>
<p>The draw for the fifth round (to decide the fixtures) was held on 8 July 2016, 10:00 <a href="/wiki/Eastern_Time_Zone" title="Eastern Time Zone">EDT</a> (<a href="/wiki/UTC%E2%88%9204:00" title="UTC−04:00">UTC−4</a>), at the CONCACAF headquarters in <a href="/wiki/Miami_Beach,_Florida" title="Miami Beach, Florida">Miami Beach</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-draw_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-draw-5">[5]</a></sup> As the draw was held before the <a href="/wiki/2018_FIFA_World_Cup_qualification_%E2%80%93_CONCACAF_Fourth_Round" title="2018 FIFA World Cup qualification – CONCACAF Fourth Round">fourth round</a> was completed, the identities of the fourth round qualifiers were not known at the time of the draw, except for Mexico which were by then already confirmed as winners of Group A.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Standings">Standings</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_FIFA_World_Cup_qualification_%E2%80%93_CONCACAF_Fifth_Round&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Standings">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<table class="wikitable collapsible collapsed">
<tr>
<th><a href="/wiki/2018_FIFA_World_Cup_qualification#Tiebreakers" title="2018 FIFA World Cup qualification">2018 FIFA World Cup qualification tiebreakers</a></th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>In league format, the ranking of teams in each group is based on the following criteria (regulations Articles 20.6 and 20.7):<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-regulations_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-6">[6]</a></sup>
<ol>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Points_(association_football)" title="Points (association football)">Points</a> (3 points for a win, 1 point for a draw, 0 points for a loss)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Goal_difference" title="Goal difference">Goal difference</a></li>
<li>Goals scored</li>
<li>Points in matches between tied teams</li>
<li>Goal difference in matches between tied teams</li>
<li>Goals scored in matches between tied teams</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Away_goals_rule" title="Away goals rule">Away goals</a> scored in matches between tied teams (if the tie is only between two teams in home-and-away league format)</li>
<li>Fair play points
<ul>
<li>first yellow card: minus 1 point</li>
<li>indirect red card (second yellow card): minus 3 points</li>
<li>direct red card: minus 4 points</li>
