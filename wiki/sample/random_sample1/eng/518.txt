<p>The compactness of the venues of the <a href="/wiki/2018_Winter_Olympics" title="2018 Winter Olympics">2018 Winter Olympics</a> and Paralympics in <a href="/wiki/Pyeongchang_County" title="Pyeongchang County">Pyeongchang</a> has been one of the winning arguments of the bid. The Games are gathered around two main venues, the mountain resort of <a href="/wiki/Alpensia_Resort" title="Alpensia Resort">Alpensia</a> for the outdoor sports (Nordic and alpine skiing, bobsled...) and the coastal city of <a href="/wiki/Gangneung" title="Gangneung">Gangneung</a> for the indoor sports (figure skating, hockey, curling...). In addition there are two mountain stand alone venues.</p>
<p>The Games will have a total of 13 competition venues – six of which will be new. The speed skating rink construction having started in October 2014, all of the new venues are now under construction.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Pyeongchang_Mountain_cluster">Pyeongchang Mountain cluster</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Venues_of_the_2018_Winter_Olympics_and_Paralympics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Pyeongchang Mountain cluster">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Alpensia_Sports_Park">Alpensia Sports Park</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Venues_of_the_2018_Winter_Olympics_and_Paralympics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Alpensia Sports Park">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Alpensia_Resort" title="Alpensia Resort">Alpensia Resort</a> in <a href="/wiki/Daegwallyeong-myeon" title="Daegwallyeong-myeon">Daegwallyeong-myeon</a> will be the focus of the 2018 Pyeongchang Winter Olympics.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-pyeongchang2018webvolume2_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-pyeongchang2018webvolume2-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Pyeongchang_Olympic_Stadium" title="Pyeongchang Olympic Stadium">Pyeongchang Olympic Stadium</a> – Ceremonies <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-autogenerated1_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-autogenerated1-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Alpensia_Ski_Jumping_Stadium" title="Alpensia Ski Jumping Stadium">Alpensia Ski Jumping Centre</a> – Ski jumping, Nordic combined</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Alpensia_Biathlon_Centre" title="Alpensia Biathlon Centre">Alpensia Biathlon Centre</a> – Biathlon</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Alpensia_Cross-Country_Centre" title="Alpensia Cross-Country Centre">Alpensia Cross-Country Centre</a> – Cross-country skiing, Nordic combined</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Alpensia_Sliding_Centre" title="Alpensia Sliding Centre">Alpensia Sliding Centre</a> – Luge, Bobsleigh, and Skeleton</li>
<li>Olympic Village</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Yongpyong_Resort" title="Yongpyong Resort">Yongpyong Alpine Centre</a> – Alpine Skiing (Slalom, Giant Slalom)</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Stand-alone_venues">Stand-alone venues</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Venues_of_the_2018_Winter_Olympics_and_Paralympics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Stand-alone venues">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>

<li><a href="/wiki/Jeongseon_Alpine_Centre" title="Jeongseon Alpine Centre">Jeongseon Alpine Centre</a> – Alpine skiing (downhill, Super-G, and Combined)</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Gangneung_Coastal_cluster">Gangneung Coastal cluster</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Venues_of_the_2018_Winter_Olympics_and_Paralympics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Gangneung Coastal cluster">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The coastal cluster is located in the city of <a href="/wiki/Gangneung" title="Gangneung">Gangneung</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Gangneung_Olympic_Park" title="Gangneung Olympic Park">Gangneung Olympic Park</a> will include the following four venues:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gangneung_Hockey_Centre" title="Gangneung Hockey Centre">Gangneung Hockey Centre</a> – Ice Hockey (men´s tournament)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gangneung_Curling_Centre" title="Gangneung Curling Centre">Gangneung Curling Centre</a> – Curling</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gangneung_Oval" title="Gangneung Oval">Gangneung Oval</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-autogenerated1_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-autogenerated1-3">[3]</a></sup> - Speed skating</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gangneung_Ice_Arena" title="Gangneung Ice Arena">Gangneung Ice Arena</a> – Short track speed skating and Figure skating</li>
</ul>
<p>In addition, a standalone venue is located on the grounds of Kwandong University:</p>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kwandong_Hockey_Centre" title="Kwandong Hockey Centre">Kwandong Hockey Centre</a> - Ice Hockey (women´s tournament)</li>
</ul>
