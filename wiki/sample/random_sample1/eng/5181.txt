<p><b>Mauro Emanuel Icardi Rivero</b> (born 19 February 1993) is an Argentine professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Forward_(association_football)#Striker" title="Forward (association football)">striker</a> and serves as <a href="/wiki/Captain_(association_football)" title="Captain (association football)">captain</a> for Italian club <a href="/wiki/Inter_Milan" title="Inter Milan">Internazionale</a>.</p>
<p>He started his career in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/FC_Barcelona_Youth#La_Masia" title="FC Barcelona Youth">youth system</a> at <a href="/wiki/FC_Barcelona" title="FC Barcelona">Barcelona</a> before moving to <a href="/wiki/U.C._Sampdoria" title="U.C. Sampdoria">Sampdoria</a>, where he began his professional career. He joined Inter in 2013, and during the <a href="/wiki/2014%E2%80%9315_Serie_A" title="2014–15 Serie A">2014–15 season</a>, he was the joint winner of the <i><a href="/wiki/Capocannoniere" title="Capocannoniere">Capocannoniere</a></i> title, awarded to the top scorer in <a href="/wiki/Serie_A" title="Serie A">Serie A</a>.</p>
<p>Icardi is also an international with <a href="/wiki/Argentina_national_football_team" title="Argentina national football team">Argentina</a> having made his senior debut in October 2013.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mauro_Icardi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_career">Early career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mauro_Icardi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Icardi was born in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rosario" title="Rosario">Rosario</a>, <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentina</a> and moved to <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Canary_Islands" title="Canary Islands">Canary Islands</a> when he was 6. He began his football career with <a href="/wiki/UD_Vecindario" title="UD Vecindario">Vecindario</a> in <a href="/wiki/Gran_Canaria" title="Gran Canaria">Gran Canaria</a>, and scored over 500 goals in their youth categories.</p>
<p>In 2007, he was disputed over by <a href="/wiki/FC_Barcelona" title="FC Barcelona">Barcelona</a> and <a href="/wiki/Real_Madrid_C.F." title="Real Madrid C.F.">Real Madrid</a>, after offers from <a href="/wiki/Valencia_CF" title="Valencia CF">Valencia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sevilla_FC" title="Sevilla FC">Sevilla</a>, <a href="/wiki/RCD_Espanyol" title="RCD Espanyol">Espanyol</a>, <a href="/wiki/Deportivo_de_La_Coru%C3%B1a" title="Deportivo de La Coruña">Deportivo La Coruña</a>, <a href="/wiki/Arsenal_F.C." title="Arsenal F.C.">Arsenal</a> and <a href="/wiki/Liverpool_F.C." title="Liverpool F.C.">Liverpool</a>. Barcelona won the race for Icardi, and signed with him until 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Icardi joined the Catalan club at the start of <a href="/wiki/2008%E2%80%9309_FC_Barcelona_season" title="2008–09 FC Barcelona season">2008–09 season</a> and was included on the U-17 team. He was promoted to the <a href="/wiki/FC_Barcelona_Juvenil_A" title="FC Barcelona Juvenil A">U-19 team</a> the following season before joining <a href="/wiki/U.C._Sampdoria" title="U.C. Sampdoria">Sampdoria</a> on loan in January 2011. Icardi later stated that leaving Barcelona was not a bad decision, convinced that his decision to move on was the right one.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Sampdoria">Sampdoria</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mauro_Icardi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Sampdoria">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 11 January 2011, Sampdoria confirmed Icardi had signed with the club on loan<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-a_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-a-3">[3]</a></sup> until the end of the season. After a successful six-month loan for <i>la Samp</i>, scoring 13 goals in 19 games with the Primavera team, the Italian side utilised the option to buy Icardi for €400,000 in July 2011, signing a three-year deal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-a_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-a-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> In 2011–12 season, he scored 19 goals in the reserve league Group A, as the joint-third topscorer of the league along with <a href="/wiki/Gonzalo_Barreto" title="Gonzalo Barreto">Gonzalo Barreto</a> of Group C.</p>
<p>On 12 May 2012, he made his first-team debut, against <a href="/wiki/S.S._Juve_Stabia" title="S.S. Juve Stabia">Juve Stabia</a>, after coming off the bench to replace <a href="/wiki/Bruno_Fornaroli" title="Bruno Fornaroli">Bruno Fornaroli</a> in the 75th minute of the match. Ten minutes later, Icardi scored his first professional goal to win the match 2–1 for Sampdoria.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 26 September 2012, he made his <a href="/wiki/Serie_A" title="Serie A">Serie A</a> debut for Sampdoria against <a href="/wiki/A.S._Roma" title="A.S. Roma">Roma</a>, substituting the Paraguayan midfielder <a href="/wiki/Marcelo_Estigarribia" title="Marcelo Estigarribia">Marcelo Estigarribia</a> in the 49th minute.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> On 12 November, Icardi scored his first goal in Serie A in the <a href="/wiki/Derby_della_Lanterna" title="Derby della Lanterna">Derby della Lanterna</a> against <a href="/wiki/Genoa_C.F.C." title="Genoa C.F.C.">Genoa</a> in an eventual 3–1 win.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> On 6 January 2013, he scored two goals against <a href="/wiki/Juventus_F.C." title="Juventus F.C.">Juventus</a> in Sampdoria's 2–1 upset at <a href="/wiki/Juventus_Stadium" title="Juventus Stadium">Juventus Stadium</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> It was Juventus's second ever loss in the Juventus Stadium since the construction.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 27 January 2013, he scored four goals in a six-goal thrashing of fellow strugglers <a href="/wiki/Delfino_Pescara_1936" title="Delfino Pescara 1936">Pescara</a>, lifting the <a href="/wiki/Genoa" title="Genoa">Genoese</a> side clear of the <a href="/wiki/2012%E2%80%9313_Serie_A" title="2012–13 Serie A">Serie A</a> relegation zone.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> On 18 May, during the final match of <a href="/wiki/2012%E2%80%9313_Serie_A" title="2012–13 Serie A">2012–13 season</a>, Icardi scored again against Juventus, helping the team to beat the eventual champions of Italy 3–2 at <a href="/wiki/Stadio_Luigi_Ferraris" title="Stadio Luigi Ferraris">Stadio Luigi Ferraris</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Internazionale">Internazionale</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mauro_Icardi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Internazionale">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In late April, it was announced that Icardi would join <a href="/wiki/Inter_Milan" title="Inter Milan">Inter</a> before the beginning of the <a href="/wiki/2013%E2%80%9314_Serie_A" title="2013–14 Serie A">2013–14 season</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> The deal was also later confirmed by Sampdoria board.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup> The transfer fee was €6.5 million for 50% registration rights.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Inter2014bilancio_14-0"><a href="#cite_note-Inter2014bilancio-14">[14]</a></sup> Icardi was officially unveiled as an Inter player on 16 July along with Algerian striker <a href="/wiki/Ishak_Belfodil" title="Ishak Belfodil">Ishak Belfodil</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup> where he was allocated squad number 9.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ESPN_16-0"><a href="#cite_note-ESPN-16">[16]</a></sup> He said that moving to Inter is a "dream come true",<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ESPN_16-1"><a href="#cite_note-ESPN-16">[16]</a></sup> adding that "he had a lot of offers, but he wanted only Inter".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ESPN_FC_17-0"><a href="#cite_note-ESPN_FC-17">[17]</a></sup></p>
<p>He made his unofficial debut one day later during the 3–0 away win against <a href="/wiki/Trentino" title="Trentino">Trentino</a> Select XI by playing in the first half of the 3–0 away win.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup> He scored his first goal for Inter in their 1–1 pre-season <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Exhibition_match" title="Exhibition match">friendly</a> draw with <a href="/wiki/Bundesliga" title="Bundesliga">Bundesliga</a> club <a href="/wiki/Hamburger_SV" title="Hamburger SV">Hamburger SV</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup> netting after less than two minutes on the field and being assisted by <a href="/wiki/Fredy_Guar%C3%ADn" title="Fredy Guarín">Fredy Guarín</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-20"><a href="#cite_note-20">[20]</a></sup></p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="2013.E2.80.9314_season">2013–14 season</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mauro_Icardi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 2013–14 season">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>On 25 August 2013, Icardi made his Inter official debut, coming on as a second-half <a href="/wiki/Substitute_(association_football)" title="Substitute (association football)">substitute</a> in a 2–0 home success over <a href="/wiki/Genoa_C.F.C." title="Genoa C.F.C.">Genoa</a>, hitting the crossbar late in the game;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-21"><a href="#cite_note-21">[21]</a></sup> he scored his first league goal for the <i>Nerazzurri</i> on 14 September, netting the first of a 1–1 home draw against <a href="/wiki/Juventus_F.C." title="Juventus F.C.">Juventus</a>, after coming on as a substitute in the 73rd minute.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-22"><a href="#cite_note-22">[22]</a></sup></p>
<p>Due to injury, Icardi missed most of the games of the first part of the season,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-23"><a href="#cite_note-23">[23]</a></sup> only returning to first-team matches in February. On 14 February 2014, during the away match against <a href="/wiki/ACF_Fiorentina" title="ACF Fiorentina">Fiorentina</a> at <a href="/wiki/Stadio_Artemio_Franchi" title="Stadio Artemio Franchi">Stadio Artemio Franchi</a>, Icardi returned in the field after four months and scored the winner after only ten minutes.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-24"><a href="#cite_note-24">[24]</a></sup> Later, on 5 April, Icardi scored a brace during the 2–2 home draw against <a href="/wiki/Bologna_F.C._1909" title="Bologna F.C. 1909">Bologna</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-25"><a href="#cite_note-25">[25]</a></sup> That was his first brace of his Inter career. This was followed by another brace during the 0–4 away win against his former side <a href="/wiki/U.C._Sampdoria" title="U.C. Sampdoria">Sampdoria</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-26"><a href="#cite_note-26">[26]</a></sup> Before the match, he was booed by the home fans, but he quieted them down by scoring in the 13th minute from a cross from <a href="/wiki/Rodrigo_Palacio" title="Rodrigo Palacio">Palacio</a>, making a negative gesture which saw him booked.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-27"><a href="#cite_note-27">[27]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 10 May 2014, in <a href="/wiki/Javier_Zanetti" title="Javier Zanetti">Javier Zanetti</a>'s last competitive match at the <a href="/wiki/San_Siro" title="San Siro">San Siro</a>, in which Inter defeated <a href="/wiki/S.S._Lazio" title="S.S. Lazio">Lazio</a> 4–1 to secure a place in <a href="/wiki/2014%E2%80%9315_UEFA_Europa_League" title="2014–15 UEFA Europa League">Europa League</a> play-off for next season, Icardi scored the second goal of the match in the 37th minute after an assist from <a href="/wiki/Mateo_Kova%C4%8Di%C4%87" title="Mateo Kovačić">Kovačić</a> and went to celebrate with Zanetti.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-28"><a href="#cite_note-28">[28]</a></sup> He ended his first season with <i>Nerazzurri</i> with 23 matches in all competitions, including 22 in the league, scoring 9 goals in the process with an average of one goal in every 145 minutes.</p>
<p>On 10 June 2014, Inter and Sampdoria reached the deal to terminate the co-ownership for another €6.5 million,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Inter2014bilancio_14-1"><a href="#cite_note-Inter2014bilancio-14">[14]</a></sup> making Inter was the sole employer of Icardi in the football field.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-29"><a href="#cite_note-29">[29]</a></sup></p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="2014.E2.80.9315_season">2014–15 season</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mauro_Icardi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: 2014–15 season">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>On 20 August 2014, in Inter's first match of <a href="/wiki/2014%E2%80%9315_Inter_Milan_season" title="2014–15 Inter Milan season">the season</a> against Iceland's <a href="/wiki/Stjarnan" title="Stjarnan">Stjarnan</a> for first leg of play-off round of the <a href="/wiki/2014%E2%80%9315_UEFA_Europa_League" title="2014–15 UEFA Europa League">2014–15 Europa League</a>, Icardi opened a 3–0 away win.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-30"><a href="#cite_note-30">[30]</a></sup> That was his first European goal, in his European debut. In the returning leg, he came in as a second-half substitute and scored two times in a 6–0 win at the <a href="/wiki/San_Siro" title="San Siro">San Siro</a>, to advance his team into the group stage 9–0 on aggregate.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-31"><a href="#cite_note-31">[31]</a></sup></p>
<p>During the second match of the league season on 14 September, he scored his second <a href="/wiki/Serie_A" title="Serie A">Serie A</a> hat-trick in a 7–0 thrashing of <a href="/wiki/U.S._Sassuolo_Calcio" title="U.S. Sassuolo Calcio">Sassuolo</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-32"><a href="#cite_note-32">[32]</a></sup> Ten days later, Icardi was injured and substituted in the 24th minute for <a href="/wiki/Dani_Osvaldo" title="Dani Osvaldo">Dani Osvaldo</a> during the 2–0 home win against <a href="/wiki/Atalanta_B.C." title="Atalanta B.C.">Atalanta</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-33"><a href="#cite_note-33">[33]</a></sup> On 26 October, Icardi netted his first Serie A goal for over a month, the winner on a penalty kick against <a href="/wiki/A.C._Cesena" title="A.C. Cesena">Cesena</a> and giving Inter their first away win of the league.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-34"><a href="#cite_note-34">[34]</a></sup> He was decisive again three days later, scoring a 90th-minute penalty kick won by <a href="/wiki/Zdravko_Kuzmanovi%C4%87" title="Zdravko Kuzmanović">Zdravko Kuzmanović</a> in a 1–0 win against <a href="/wiki/U.C._Sampdoria" title="U.C. Sampdoria">Sampdoria</a>, giving his former side their first loss of 2014–15 season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-35"><a href="#cite_note-35">[35]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 9 November, Icardi scored twice after two assists by Palacio during a 2–2 home draw against <a href="/wiki/Hellas_Verona_F.C." title="Hellas Verona F.C.">Hellas Verona</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-36"><a href="#cite_note-36">[36]</a></sup> This draw caused the sacking of coach <a href="/wiki/Walter_Mazzarri" title="Walter Mazzarri">Walter Mazzarri</a>, who was replaced with the returning <a href="/wiki/Roberto_Mancini" title="Roberto Mancini">Roberto Mancini</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-37"><a href="#cite_note-37">[37]</a></sup> On 6 January of the following year, in the first match of the second part of the season, Icardi netted the only goal for his team during the 1–1 draw against <a href="/wiki/Juventus_F.C." title="Juventus F.C.">Juventus</a> at <a href="/wiki/Juventus_Stadium" title="Juventus Stadium">Juventus Stadium</a>; the goal came in 64th minute after a pass from the Colombian midfielder <a href="/wiki/Fredy_Guar%C3%ADn" title="Fredy Guarín">Fredy Guarín</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-38"><a href="#cite_note-38">[38]</a></sup> During that match, he had a conflict with his strike partner Osvaldo who was angry with him after Icardi decided to shoot instead of passing to his teammate who was in a better position to score.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-39"><a href="#cite_note-39">[39]</a></sup> Due to this, Osvaldo left the team in the end of January.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-40"><a href="#cite_note-40">[40]</a></sup> Five days later, Icardi was decisive in Inter's 3–1 home win against <a href="/wiki/Genoa_C.F.C." title="Genoa C.F.C.">Genoa</a>, scoring the second goal and earlier making an overhead kick (saved by goalkeeper) which was turned into an assist for Palacio.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-41"><a href="#cite_note-41">[41]</a></sup></p>
<p>In the match against Sassuolo, Inter lost 1–3 with Icardi scoring the only goal for his team after a mistake from the Sassuolo defence. After the match, he along with Guarín had a conflict with the fans at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mapei_Stadium" title="Mapei Stadium">Mapei Stadium</a>. They went to throw their shirt to the fans who refused and threw them back at them, spewing insults at them.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-42"><a href="#cite_note-42">[42]</a></sup> On 7 February, Icardi responded by scoring twice in a 3–0 home win against <a href="/wiki/U.S._Citt%C3%A0_di_Palermo" title="U.S. Città di Palermo">Palermo</a>, refusing to celebrate in the process.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-43"><a href="#cite_note-43">[43]</a></sup> After the match Icardi told to the media that the reason he did not celebrate was not the conflict with the fans but failing to find a way to renew his contract with the club directors. He was later criticized by Mancini for not celebrating.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-44"><a href="#cite_note-44">[44]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 11 April, he scored the first goal during the 0–3 away win against Verona, helping Inter get their first win after six consecutive matches.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-45"><a href="#cite_note-45">[45]</a></sup> He scored twice on the final day in a 4–3 win over <a href="/wiki/Empoli_F.C." title="Empoli F.C.">Empoli</a> and finished the season as the joint <a href="/wiki/Capocannoniere" title="Capocannoniere">Serie A top goalscorer</a> with 22 goals with Verona's <a href="/wiki/Luca_Toni" title="Luca Toni">Luca Toni</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-top_46-0"><a href="#cite_note-top-46">[46]</a></sup> Although Inter failed to qualify for European competition for the first time since 1999, Icardi's form attracted attention from abroad, however he signed a new four-year contract at the season's end.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-47"><a href="#cite_note-47">[47]</a></sup></p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="2015.E2.80.9316_season:_Captain">2015–16 season: Captain</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mauro_Icardi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: 2015–16 season: Captain">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Before the start of <a href="/wiki/2015%E2%80%9316_Inter_Milan_season" title="2015–16 Inter Milan season">2015–16 season</a>, it was announced that Icardi would be the new captain of Inter, replacing <a href="/wiki/Andrea_Ranocchia" title="Andrea Ranocchia">Andrea Ranocchia</a> who lost his status after a poor 2014–15 season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-48"><a href="#cite_note-48">[48]</a></sup> In his first match as skipper, he was taken off after 15 minutes during the league win against <a href="/wiki/Atalanta_B.C." title="Atalanta B.C.">Atalanta</a>; Icardi was substituted for <a href="/wiki/Stevan_Joveti%C4%87" title="Stevan Jovetić">Stevan Jovetić</a>, who scored the winning goal in the third minute of added time.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-49"><a href="#cite_note-49">[49]</a></sup> After the examinations, it was announced that Icardi suffered a thigh injury and he missed the following league match against <a href="/wiki/Carpi_F.C._1909" title="Carpi F.C. 1909">Carpi</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-50"><a href="#cite_note-50">[50]</a></sup> He made his comeback on 13 September during the 1–0 home win against the cross-town rivals of <a href="/wiki/A.C._Milan" title="A.C. Milan">Milan</a> and opened his scoring account one week later against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/A.C._Chievo_Verona" title="A.C. Chievo Verona">Chievo Verona</a>, helping Inter to clinch the 4th consecutive victory.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-51"><a href="#cite_note-51">[51]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-52"><a href="#cite_note-52">[52]</a></sup> On 27 September, Icardi was again on the scoresheet but this time in a 1–4 defeat versus <a href="/wiki/ACF_Fiorentina" title="ACF Fiorentina">Fiorentina</a>, who ended Inter's undefeated streak of the 2015–16 season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-53"><a href="#cite_note-53">[53]</a></sup></p>
<p>One month later, on 28 October, following the 0–1 away win over <a href="/wiki/Bologna_F.C._1909" title="Bologna F.C. 1909">Bologna</a> where Icardi scored the only goal of the match following an assist from <a href="/wiki/Adem_Ljaji%C4%87" title="Adem Ljajić">Adem Ljajić</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-54"><a href="#cite_note-54">[54]</a></sup> he complained about lack of services to him, accusing his teammates in the process. Icardi told the media that: "In ten matches I've received four chances to score, and I've taken three. I think that is a good average."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-55"><a href="#cite_note-55">[55]</a></sup> After that, he was benched for the following league match against <a href="/wiki/A.S._Roma" title="A.S. Roma">Roma</a>, where Inter won, again a 1–0 victory.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-56"><a href="#cite_note-56">[56]</a></sup> After the game, coach <a href="/wiki/Roberto_Mancini" title="Roberto Mancini">Mancini</a> said that leaving Icardi on the bench was a "tactical decision", adding that Icardi "must improve his game".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-57"><a href="#cite_note-57">[57]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-58"><a href="#cite_note-58">[58]</a></sup> He returned to the starting lineup in the next league match against <a href="/wiki/Torino_F.C." title="Torino F.C.">Torino</a>, where Inter grabbed another 1–0 victory.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-59"><a href="#cite_note-59">[59]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 22 November, in his 100th <a href="/wiki/Serie_A" title="Serie A">Serie A</a> appearance, he scored in a 4–0 win over the minnows of <a href="/wiki/Frosinone_Calcio" title="Frosinone Calcio">Frosinone</a>, helping Inter to seal its biggest victory of the season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-60"><a href="#cite_note-60">[60]</a></sup> On 13 December, Icardi netted his fist brace of the season, in the 0–4 thrashing of <a href="/wiki/Udinese_Calcio" title="Udinese Calcio">Udinese</a>, benefiting in both occasions from the mistakes of Udinese defenders.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-61"><a href="#cite_note-61">[61]</a></sup> In Inter's final match of 2015, Icardi's goal was not enough to avoid the 2–1 home loss against <a href="/wiki/S.S._Lazio" title="S.S. Lazio">Lazio</a>, but still the team managed to end the year in the first place.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-62"><a href="#cite_note-62">[62]</a></sup> At the end of the year, Icardi was ranked the 82nd best footballer in the world by the English newspaper <i><a href="/wiki/The_Guardian" title="The Guardian">The Guardian</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-63"><a href="#cite_note-63">[63]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 6 January 2016, Icardi finished <a href="/wiki/Ivan_Peri%C5%A1i%C4%87" title="Ivan Perišić">Ivan Perišić</a>'s cross from close range for the only goal away against <a href="/wiki/Empoli_F.C." title="Empoli F.C.">Empoli</a>, putting Inter on top of Serie A.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-64"><a href="#cite_note-64">[64]</a></sup> A month later, on 3 February, he scored his 50th Inter goal in all competitions in a 1–0 home win over <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/A.C._Chievo_Verona" title="A.C. Chievo Verona">Chievo</a>, returning Inter to its winning ways after four consecutive matches.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-65"><a href="#cite_note-65">[65]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-66"><a href="#cite_note-66">[66]</a></sup> In February 2016, it was reported by several newspapers that Inter had rejected a €30 million bid from <a href="/wiki/Manchester_United_F.C." title="Manchester United F.C.">Manchester United</a> to sign Icardi.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-67"><a href="#cite_note-67">[67]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-68"><a href="#cite_note-68">[68]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-69"><a href="#cite_note-69">[69]</a></sup> On 9 April, he scored his 50th <a href="/wiki/Serie_A" title="Serie A">Serie A</a> goal in a 1–0 away win over <a href="/wiki/Frosinone_Calcio" title="Frosinone Calcio">Frosinone</a>, making his 100th Inter appearance in the process.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-70"><a href="#cite_note-70">[70]</a></sup></p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="2016.E2.80.9317_season">2016–17 season</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mauro_Icardi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: 2016–17 season">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Icardi started the season on 21 August by playing in the 2–0 away defeat to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/A.C._Chievo_Verona" title="A.C. Chievo Verona">Chievo</a> in the opening league match,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-71"><a href="#cite_note-71">[71]</a></sup> and scored his first goal of the season a week later during the 1–1 draw against <a href="/wiki/U.S._Citt%C3%A0_di_Palermo" title="U.S. Città di Palermo">Palermo</a> at home.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-72"><a href="#cite_note-72">[72]</a></sup> On 11 September, Icardi scored both goals, including a last-minute winner, as Inter overturn the score against newly promoted side <a href="/wiki/Delfino_Pescara_1936" title="Delfino Pescara 1936">Pescara</a> for the first win of the season under the new coach <a href="/wiki/Frank_de_Boer" title="Frank de Boer">Frank de Boer</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-73"><a href="#cite_note-73">[73]</a></sup> One week later, in the <a href="/wiki/Derby_d%27Italia" title="Derby d'Italia">Derby d'Italia</a> match against <a href="/wiki/Juventus_F.C." title="Juventus F.C.">Juventus</a>, Icardi scored an equalizer in the 68th minute, and provided the <a href="/wiki/Ivan_Peri%C5%A1i%C4%87" title="Ivan Perišić">Ivan Perišić</a> goal ten minutes later, as Inter overturn the score again to beat the lifetime rivals for the first time in league since November 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-74"><a href="#cite_note-74">[74]</a></sup> It was Icardi's seventh goal in eight appearances against Juventus, making thus his favourite opponent to score against.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-75"><a href="#cite_note-75">[75]</a></sup> This was followed by a brace in Inter's 2–0 win at <a href="/wiki/Empoli_F.C." title="Empoli F.C.">Empoli</a> three days later, taking the lead of goalscorers table with six goals in six appearances; it was also the first time that he has scored in four consecutive Serie A matches.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-76"><a href="#cite_note-76">[76]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-77"><a href="#cite_note-77">[77]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 7 October, Icardi signed a new contract extension with Inter until June 2021, with a buy-out clause of €110 million.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-78"><a href="#cite_note-78">[78]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-79"><a href="#cite_note-79">[79]</a></sup> With the new deal, Icardi will earn €4,5 million per season plus various bonuses based on his performances, goals and image rights. Shortly after signing the contract, Icardi stated: "I'm really happy to have signed this new contract that will keep me at the club until 2021. My dream is to win trophies with this team. I heard lots of things during the transfer window, as you always do, but the club was on my side. I have my agent and wife Wanda to thank for this renewal and we're all pleased at home."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-80"><a href="#cite_note-80">[80]</a></sup></p>
<p>Icardi scored a brace to beat Torino 2–1 at the San Siro, giving Inter their first win in their last 5 games.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-81"><a href="#cite_note-81">[81]</a></sup> Then, he continued with his outrageous form, scoring back-to-back home braces against <a href="/wiki/F.C._Crotone" title="F.C. Crotone">Crotone</a> and <a href="/wiki/ACF_Fiorentina" title="ACF Fiorentina">Fiorentina</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-82"><a href="#cite_note-82">[82]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-83"><a href="#cite_note-83">[83]</a></sup> In the last match of 2016, Icardi scored another brace against <a href="/wiki/S.S._Lazio" title="S.S. Lazio">Lazio</a> to help Inter finish the year on a high.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-84"><a href="#cite_note-84">[84]</a></sup> He finished the first part of the season with 14 goals and 5 assists, being the most efficient striker in top 5 leagues, overtaking stars such <a href="/wiki/Cristiano_Ronaldo" title="Cristiano Ronaldo">Cristiano Ronaldo</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lionel_Messi" title="Lionel Messi">Lionel Messi</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-85"><a href="#cite_note-85">[85]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-86"><a href="#cite_note-86">[86]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/The_Guardian" title="The Guardian">The Guardian</a> named him the world's 49th best player, winning 33 positions compare to last year ranking.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-87"><a href="#cite_note-87">[87]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 15 January 2017, in the 3–1 home win against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/A.C._Chievo_Verona" title="A.C. Chievo Verona">Chievo</a> which was the fifth consecutive win in league, Icardi scored his 15th league goal of the season becoming the first Inter player to score at least 15 goals in three consecutive seasons since <a href="/wiki/Zlatan_Ibrahimovi%C4%87" title="Zlatan Ibrahimović">Zlatan Ibrahimović</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cristian_Vieri" title="Cristian Vieri">Cristian Vieri</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-88"><a href="#cite_note-88">[88]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-89"><a href="#cite_note-89">[89]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mauro_Icardi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In August 2012, Icardi made his debut for the <a href="/wiki/Argentina_national_under-20_football_team" title="Argentina national under-20 football team">Argentina Under-20</a> side in a match against <a href="/wiki/Germany_national_under-21_football_team" title="Germany national under-21 football team">Germany</a>.</p>
<p>Following <a href="/wiki/Italy_national_football_team" title="Italy national football team">Italy national football team</a> manager <a href="/wiki/Cesare_Prandelli" title="Cesare Prandelli">Cesare Prandelli</a>'s interest in Icardi's performances, in February 2013, Icardi stated his wish to play for the <a href="/wiki/Argentina_national_football_team" title="Argentina national football team">Argentina senior team</a>, despite also being eligible to play for Italy, saying: "I'm very grateful to Italian football for the opportunity it has given me, I'd also like to thank Prandelli for the nice words he expressed about me. But I have to be honest and sincere - I'm Argentine, I feel Argentine, and I've always dreamed of wearing the shirt of my nation. To actually do that would be the maximum for me."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-90"><a href="#cite_note-90">[90]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 15 October 2013 he played his first game for the main squad, coming on as an 82nd-minute substitute for <a href="/wiki/Augusto_Fern%C3%A1ndez" title="Augusto Fernández">Augusto Fernández</a> in a 2–3 <a href="/wiki/FIFA_World_Cup" title="FIFA World Cup">FIFA World Cup</a> qualifier loss away to <a href="/wiki/Uruguay_national_football_team" title="Uruguay national football team">Uruguay</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-91"><a href="#cite_note-91">[91]</a></sup></p>
<p>In April 2016, Icardi was excluded from <a href="/wiki/Gerardo_Martino" title="Gerardo Martino">Gerardo Martino</a>'s preliminary squad for the <a href="/wiki/Copa_Am%C3%A9rica_Centenario" title="Copa América Centenario">Copa América Centenario</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-92"><a href="#cite_note-92">[92]</a></sup> although the Argentine manager later named him to Argentina's preliminary under-23 squad for the <a href="/wiki/Football_at_the_2016_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_tournament" title="Football at the 2016 Summer Olympics – Men's tournament">2016 Summer Olympics</a> in Rio de Janeiro.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-93"><a href="#cite_note-93">[93]</a></sup> However, Inter did not release Icardi to participate in the Olympics.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-94"><a href="#cite_note-94">[94]</a></sup></p>
<p>In October 2016, rumours started to speculate in Argentina that <a href="/wiki/Lionel_Messi" title="Lionel Messi">Lionel Messi</a> was behind Icardi's exclusion from the national team due to his friendship with <a href="/wiki/Maxi_L%C3%B3pez" title="Maxi López">Maxi López</a>, his wife's former partner.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-95"><a href="#cite_note-95">[95]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-96"><a href="#cite_note-96">[96]</a></sup> The national team coach <a href="/wiki/Edgardo_Bauza" title="Edgardo Bauza">Edgardo Bauza</a> stated that Icardi's off-field antics have nothing to do with him not being selected for Argentina and he would receive a call-up soon, adding that Messi does not "dictate" the team.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-97"><a href="#cite_note-97">[97]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-98"><a href="#cite_note-98">[98]</a></sup> However, he was omitted again for the <a href="/wiki/2018_FIFA_World_Cup_qualification_(CONMEBOL)" title="2018 FIFA World Cup qualification (CONMEBOL)">2018 FIFA World Cup qualification matches</a> against <a href="/wiki/Brazil_national_football_team" title="Brazil national football team">Brazil</a> and <a href="/wiki/Colombia_national_football_team" title="Colombia national football team">Colombia</a> in November.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-99"><a href="#cite_note-99">[99]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Style_of_play">Style of play</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mauro_Icardi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Style of play">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Nicknamed <i>El Niño del partido</i>, and <i>O cañito</i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Chi_.C3.A8_Mauro_Icardi_100-0"><a href="#cite_note-Chi_.C3.A8_Mauro_Icardi-100">[100]</a></sup> Icardi is a quick, tactically intelligent, and physically strong striker, with good technique, attacking movement, positioning, and an eye for goal, who mainly operates in central areas inside the penalty area; an accurate and efficient finisher with his head and both feet inside the box, he is known for his ability to score from few touches, although he can also hold up the ball in order to create space or chances for other players, and play off of his team-mates.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Chi_.C3.A8_Mauro_Icardi_100-1"><a href="#cite_note-Chi_.C3.A8_Mauro_Icardi-100">[100]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Everything_you_need_to_know_about_Chelsea_and_Liverpool_target_Mauro_Icardi_101-0"><a href="#cite_note-Everything_you_need_to_know_about_Chelsea_and_Liverpool_target_Mauro_Icardi-101">[101]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-102"><a href="#cite_note-102">[102]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Internazionale.27s_Mauro_Icardi_begins_to_make_right_headlines_in_Fiorentina_win_103-0"><a href="#cite_note-Internazionale.27s_Mauro_Icardi_begins_to_make_right_headlines_in_Fiorentina_win-103">[103]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-104"><a href="#cite_note-104">[104]</a></sup> Although he was once criticised for his limited creative and defensive contribution, as well as his lack of movement outside the box,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Everything_you_need_to_know_about_Chelsea_and_Liverpool_target_Mauro_Icardi_101-1"><a href="#cite_note-Everything_you_need_to_know_about_Chelsea_and_Liverpool_target_Mauro_Icardi-101">[101]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Internazionale.27s_Mauro_Icardi_begins_to_make_right_headlines_in_Fiorentina_win_103-1"><a href="#cite_note-Internazionale.27s_Mauro_Icardi_begins_to_make_right_headlines_in_Fiorentina_win-103">[103]</a></sup> his work-rate has improved in recent seasons, and has seen him drift out to wide positions in order to create space or provide assists for teammates.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-105"><a href="#cite_note-105">[105]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-106"><a href="#cite_note-106">[106]</a></sup> Long regarded as a talented and promising prospect,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Everything_you_need_to_know_about_Chelsea_and_Liverpool_target_Mauro_Icardi_101-2"><a href="#cite_note-Everything_you_need_to_know_about_Chelsea_and_Liverpool_target_Mauro_Icardi-101">[101]</a></sup> in 2013, <a href="/wiki/Don_Bal%C3%B3n" title="Don Balón">Don Balón</a> listed him as one of the top 100 young players born after 1992.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-107"><a href="#cite_note-107">[107]</a></sup></p>
<p>Speaking of Icardi, Inter coach <a href="/wiki/Stefano_Pioli" title="Stefano Pioli">Stefano Pioli</a> stated that Icardi "is an animal in the box", and "always finding the right position",<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-108"><a href="#cite_note-108">[108]</a></sup> while the <a href="/wiki/1982_FIFA_World_Cup" title="1982 FIFA World Cup">1982 FIFA World Cup</a> winner <a href="/wiki/Paolo_Rossi" title="Paolo Rossi">Paolo Rossi</a> described Icardi as "deadly striker", adding that "even in the most complicated games he can create the right opportunity and take advantage of it."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-109"><a href="#cite_note-109">[109]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mauro_Icardi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Icardi is married to Argentine media personality <a href="/wiki/Wanda_Nara" title="Wanda Nara">Wanda Nara</a>. Nara was married to Icardi's former <a href="/wiki/U.C._Sampdoria" title="U.C. Sampdoria">Sampdoria</a> teammate <a href="/wiki/Maxi_L%C3%B3pez" title="Maxi López">Maxi López</a>, but the couple split after her relationship with Icardi was exposed. She and López began divorce proceedings in December 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-110"><a href="#cite_note-110">[110]</a></sup> Nara and Icardi subsequently married on 27 May 2014, not long after the divorce was finalised, at a small ceremony in <a href="/wiki/Buenos_Aires" title="Buenos Aires">Buenos Aires</a>, <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentina</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-111"><a href="#cite_note-111">[111]</a></sup> They have 2 daughters, Francesca, born on 19 January 2015,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-112"><a href="#cite_note-112">[112]</a></sup> and Isabella, born on 27 October 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-113"><a href="#cite_note-113">[113]</a></sup></p>
<p>During an April 2014 <a href="/wiki/Serie_A" title="Serie A">Serie A</a> match between Sampdoria and Inter, López notably refused to shake Icardi's hand, leading the press to dub the game the "Wanda derby".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-114"><a href="#cite_note-114">[114]</a></sup> Two years later, López repeated the history by again refusing to shake Icardi's hand during a Serie A match against <a href="/wiki/Torino_F.C." title="Torino F.C.">Torino</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-115"><a href="#cite_note-115">[115]</a></sup></p>
<p>Icardi is of <a href="/wiki/Italians" title="Italians">Italian</a> origin through his paternal ancestors named Icardi from <a href="/wiki/Piedmont" title="Piedmont">Piedmont</a> and Da Mezan from <a href="/wiki/Venice" title="Venice">Venice</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-116"><a href="#cite_note-116">[116]</a></sup> He has an Italian passport.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-117"><a href="#cite_note-117">[117]</a></sup> He features on the cover of the Italian version of <a href="/wiki/EA_Sports" title="EA Sports">EA Sports</a>' <i><a href="/wiki/FIFA_16" title="FIFA 16">FIFA 16</a></i>, alongside global cover star and compatriot <a href="/wiki/Lionel_Messi" title="Lionel Messi">Lionel Messi</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-118"><a href="#cite_note-118">[118]</a></sup> On 6 December 2015, Icardi was robbed of his £29,000 <a href="/wiki/Hublot" title="Hublot">Hublot</a> watch after Inter's 1–0 win over <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Genoa_CFC" title="Genoa CFC">Genoa</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-119"><a href="#cite_note-119">[119]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 11 October 2016, Icardi released his autobiography called <i>Sempre Avanti</i> (Always Forward) published by Sperling &amp; Kupfer. The book caused controversy as Icardi had described an incident which offended certain Inter fans, leading to a banner being displayed calling for Icardi to leave the club.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-120"><a href="#cite_note-120">[120]</a></sup> He was fined by the club and was forced to remove that chapter from the book.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-121"><a href="#cite_note-121">[121]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-122"><a href="#cite_note-122">[122]</a></sup></p>
