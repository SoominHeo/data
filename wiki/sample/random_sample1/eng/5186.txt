<p><i><a href="/wiki/My_Little_Pony:_Friendship_Is_Magic" title="My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic">My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic</a></i> is a 2010 animated television series developed by <a href="/wiki/Lauren_Faust" title="Lauren Faust">Lauren Faust</a>. The show premiered on the launch of The Hub in the United States on Sunday, October 10, 2010. As of October 22, 2016, 143 episodes have aired through the show's six seasons. All episodes are 22 minutes and a season comprises 26 episodes, with the exception of Season 3, which contained 13 episodes. <i>My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic</i> is produced by <a href="/wiki/Hasbro_Studios" title="Hasbro Studios">Hasbro Studios</a> in the United States and <a href="/wiki/DHX_Media" title="DHX Media">DHX Media/Vancouver</a> in Canada. In the United States, the series is rated <a href="/wiki/TV_Parental_Guidelines#TV-Y" title="TV Parental Guidelines">TV-Y</a>. A seventh season has been announced by Hasbro, with episodes to broadcast in early 2017. Like previous seasons with the exception of Season 3, Season 7 will consist of 26 episodes.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
