<p><b>Evergreen Mak Cheung-ching</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">麦长青</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">麥長青</span>, born 18 December 1967), better known as <b>Makbau</b> (麥包), is a Hong Kong <a href="/wiki/TVB" title="TVB">TVB</a> actor. He is lauded and best known for his villainous portrayal of the main antagonist, Leung Fei-fan (Fei Fan Gor), in <a href="/wiki/No_Regrets_(TV_series)" title="No Regrets (TV series)">No Regrets</a>, for which he won the <a href="/wiki/TVB_Anniversary_Award_for_Best_Supporting_Actor" title="TVB Anniversary Award for Best Supporting Actor">TVB Anniversary Award for Best Supporting Actor</a>. He also won the Best Actor in a Supporting Role award in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Asian_Television_Awards" title="Asian Television Awards">Asian Television Awards</a> 2011 for the same role. He is also known for his voiceover work for cartoons, as well a children show host for <i>Flash Fax and</i> <a href="/wiki/After_School_ICU" title="After School ICU">After School ICU</a>.</p>
