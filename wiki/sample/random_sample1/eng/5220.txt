<p><i><b>Microbe &amp; Gasoline</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/French_language" title="French language">French</a>: <span lang="fr"><i><b>Microbe et Gasoil</b></i></span>) is a 2015 French <a href="/wiki/Comedy_film" title="Comedy film">comedy film</a> written and directed by <a href="/wiki/Michel_Gondry" title="Michel Gondry">Michel Gondry</a>. The film follows the adventures of two teenagers who take a road trip in their self-assembled vehicle. The film stars newcomers Théophile Baquet and Ange Dargent, along with <a href="/wiki/Audrey_Tautou" title="Audrey Tautou">Audrey Tautou</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
