<p><b>Michael Groo Massee</b> (September 1, 1952 – October 20, 2016) was an American <a href="/wiki/Actor" title="Actor">actor</a>. Massee is perhaps best known for his roles as Funboy in the 1994 film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Crow_(1994_film)" title="The Crow (1994 film)">The Crow</a></i> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gentleman_(comics)" title="Gentleman (comics)">The Gentleman</a> in the 2012 film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Amazing_Spider-Man_(2012_film)" title="The Amazing Spider-Man (2012 film)">The Amazing Spider-Man</a></i>. He also reprised this role in the 2014 sequel <i><a href="/wiki/The_Amazing_Spider-Man_2" title="The Amazing Spider-Man 2">The Amazing Spider-Man 2</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Michael_Massee&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 1993, Massee portrayed the character Funboy in the film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Crow_(1994_film)" title="The Crow (1994 film)">The Crow</a></i>, starring <a href="/wiki/Brandon_Lee" title="Brandon Lee">Brandon Lee</a>. Massee was the actor who fired the shot that killed Lee by accident on the set in 1993, due to an improperly prepared prop gun. He was so traumatized by the event that he returned to New York and took a year off from acting. He refused to view the film. In an interview in 2005, 12 years after the incident, Massee revealed that he still had nightmares about it, going on to say, "I don't think you ever get over something like that."</p>
<p>In 1995, Massee played the cop Newton in the film <i><a href="/wiki/Tales_from_the_Hood" title="Tales from the Hood">Tales from the Hood</a></i>. He has provided the voice for the villain <a href="/wiki/Spellbinder_(DC_Comics)" title="Spellbinder (DC Comics)">Spellbinder</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Batman_(TV_series)" title="The Batman (TV series)">The Batman</a></i>, as well as appearing on the television series <i><a href="/wiki/24_(TV_series)" title="24 (TV series)">24</a></i> as the villain <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ira_Gaines" title="Ira Gaines">Ira Gaines</a>. He provided the voice of <a href="/wiki/Hulk_(comics)" title="Hulk (comics)">Bruce Banner</a> for the <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ultimate_Avengers" title="Ultimate Avengers">Ultimate Avengers</a></i> animated film, as well as the sequel <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ultimate_Avengers_2" title="Ultimate Avengers 2">Ultimate Avengers 2</a></i>. He appeared in the film <i><a href="/wiki/Seven_(1995_film)" title="Seven (1995 film)">Seven</a></i> alongside <a href="/wiki/Brad_Pitt" title="Brad Pitt">Brad Pitt</a> and <a href="/wiki/Morgan_Freeman" title="Morgan Freeman">Morgan Freeman</a>. He appeared in <a href="/wiki/David_Lynch" title="David Lynch">David Lynch</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Lost_Highway_(film)" title="Lost Highway (film)">Lost Highway</a></i> as Andy. He played the physical manifestation of Management (a.k.a. Lucius Belyakovin) on <a href="/wiki/HBO" title="HBO">HBO</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Carniv%C3%A0le" title="Carnivàle">Carnivàle</a></i>. He guest-starred in the <i><a href="/wiki/The_X-Files" title="The X-Files">The X-Files</a></i> episode "<a href="/wiki/The_Field_Where_I_Died" title="The Field Where I Died">The Field Where I Died</a>". He also appeared in the 2005 <a href="/wiki/NBC" title="NBC">NBC</a> TV miniseries <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Revelations_(TV_miniseries)" title="Revelations (TV miniseries)">Revelations</a></i> as the main antagonist. In 2006, he guest-starred in <i><a href="/wiki/Criminal_Minds" title="Criminal Minds">Criminal Minds</a></i> as a <a href="/wiki/Serial_killer" title="Serial killer">serial killer</a> on <a href="/wiki/Death_row" title="Death row">death row</a>. He also guest-starred in the <i><a href="/wiki/Law_%26_Order:_Criminal_Intent" title="Law &amp; Order: Criminal Intent">Law &amp; Order: Criminal Intent</a></i> episode "Reunion" as a rock star. He appeared in the American TV series <i><a href="/wiki/FlashForward" title="FlashForward">FlashForward</a></i> as villain Dyson Frost. He appeared as a hunter, Kubrick, on the series <i><a href="/wiki/Supernatural_(U.S._TV_series)" title="Supernatural (U.S. TV series)">Supernatural</a></i> in 2007.</p>
<p>Massee portrayed imprisoned serial killer Charles Hoyt in the first two seasons of the TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Rizzoli_%26_Isles" title="Rizzoli &amp; Isles">Rizzoli &amp; Isles</a></i>. He played the role of Gustav Fiers (alias <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gentleman_(comics)" title="Gentleman (comics)">The Gentleman</a>) in 2012's <i><a href="/wiki/The_Amazing_Spider-Man_(2012_film)" title="The Amazing Spider-Man (2012 film)">The Amazing Spider-Man</a></i> and reprised the role in the 2014 sequel <i><a href="/wiki/The_Amazing_Spider-Man_2" title="The Amazing Spider-Man 2">The Amazing Spider-Man 2</a></i>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life_and_death">Personal life and death</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Michael_Massee&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life and death">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Massee was born in Kansas City, Missouri in 1952.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nyt_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-nyt-1">[1]</a></sup> He and his wife Ellen together owned a clothing boutique in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Extra_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Extra-2">[2]</a></sup> He spoke French and appeared in 2014 in the French series <i>Interventions</i>.</p>
<p>He died of stomach cancer in Los Angeles on October 20, 2016 at the age of 64.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nyt_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-nyt-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
