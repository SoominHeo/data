<p><b>Michael Mosley</b> (born September 16, 1978) is an American television and film actor, best known for his roles as Drew Suffin on <i><a href="/wiki/Scrubs_(TV_series)" title="Scrubs (TV series)">Scrubs</a></i>, Ted Vanderway on <i><a href="/wiki/Pan_Am_(TV_series)" title="Pan Am (TV series)">Pan Am</a></i> and Johnny Farrell on <i><a href="/wiki/Sirens_(2014_TV_series)" title="Sirens (2014 TV series)">Sirens</a></i>.</p>
<p>Mosley was born in <a href="/wiki/Iowa_City,_Iowa" title="Iowa City, Iowa">Iowa City, Iowa</a>. He was engaged to actress <a href="/wiki/Anna_Camp" title="Anna Camp">Anna Camp</a> in September 2008; the couple wed in early 2010 and filed for divorce in April 2013 citing "irreconcilable differences".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-divorceDM_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-divorceDM-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
