<p><b>Michael Alan Welch</b> (born July 25, 1987) is an American television and film actor best known for the role of Luke Girardi on the television series <i><a href="/wiki/Joan_of_Arcadia" title="Joan of Arcadia">Joan of Arcadia</a></i> and for the role of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mike_Newton_(Twilight)" title="Mike Newton (Twilight)">Mike Newton</a> in the films <i><a href="/wiki/Twilight_(2008_film)" title="Twilight (2008 film)">Twilight</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Twilight_Saga:_New_Moon" title="The Twilight Saga: New Moon">New Moon</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Twilight_Saga:_Eclipse" title="The Twilight Saga: Eclipse">Eclipse</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Twilight_Saga:_Breaking_Dawn_%E2%80%93_Part_1" title="The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn – Part 1">Breaking Dawn</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Michael_Welch_(actor)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Welch was born in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Los_Angeles,_California" title="Los Angeles, California">Los Angeles, California</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and his father is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Protestant" title="Protestant">Protestant</a> and his mother <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jewish" title="Jewish">Jewish</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Welch is known for his role as the popular Mike Newton in <i>The Twilight Saga</i> film series. Although he auditioned for the part of Edward Cullen, he was cast in the role of Mike Newton.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> He's also well known for his role as Luke Girardi in the television series <i>Joan of Arcadia</i>, which ran for two seasons (2003–05).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>Welch was cast in the series <i>Z Nation</i> on Syfy, which began airing in the fall of 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> He also appeared in the 2014 film <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Boy_Meets_Girl_(2014)" title="Boy Meets Girl (2014)">Boy Meets Girl</a></i>, a romantic comedy which received many awards at LGBT film festivals in the U.S. and internationally.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Awards">Awards</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Michael_Welch_(actor)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Awards">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Los Angeles FirstGlance Film Festival (2011) • Won, Festival Award – Best Actor for Unrequited (2010) (Lucky Day Studios)</li>
<li>FilmOut San Diego (2014) • Won, FilmOut Festival Award – Best Actor for <i><a href="/wiki/Boy_Meets_Girl_(2014_film)" title="Boy Meets Girl (2014 film)">Boy Meets Girl</a></i>.</li>
</ul>
<p>Welch is the recipient of two Young Artist Awards. His first was for his performance in the film <i>Star Trek: Insurrection</i> at the age of 10. The second was for his work as Luke Girardi on the <i>Joan of Arcadia</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Michael_Welch_(actor)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Welch married Marissa Lefton in 2008. The couple separated in 2011, and jointly filed for divorce in California on February 14, 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> He married model girlfriend Samantha Maggio on Nov 5th, 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
