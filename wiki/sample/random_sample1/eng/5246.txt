<p><i><b>Ultimate Shooting Collection</b></i>, originally released in Japan as <i><b>Milestone Shooting Collection: Karous Wii</b></i>, is a compilation of <i><a href="/wiki/Karous" title="Karous">Karous</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Radirgy" title="Radirgy">Radirgy</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Chaos_Field" title="Chaos Field">Chaos Field</a></i>. This is the first non-Japanese release of <i>Karous</i> and <i>Radirgy</i>.</p>
<p>It was followed by a <i>Milestone Shooting Collection 2</i>, which retained the lineup of the original while adding <i><a href="/wiki/Radirgy_Noa" title="Radirgy Noa">Radirgy Noa</a></i> and <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Illvelo&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Illvelo (page does not exist)">Illvelo</a></i>.</p>
<p>The lineup of <i>Milestone Shooting Collection 2</i> was later released as a compilation for Xbox 360 under a new name, <i><b>Sakura Flamingo Archives</b></i>.</p>
