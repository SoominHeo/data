<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Masovia Governorate</h1>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Masovia Governorate</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Мазовская Губерния</span> <a href="/wiki/Polish_language" title="Polish language">Polish</a>: <span lang="pl" xml:lang="pl"><i>Gubernia mazowiecka</i></span>) was an administrative unit (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Governorates_of_the_Russian_Empire" title="Governorates of the Russian Empire">governorate</a>) of the <a href="/wiki/Congress_Poland" title="Congress Poland">Congress Poland</a>.</p>
<p>It was created in 1837 from the <a href="/wiki/Masovian_Voivodeship_(1526%E2%80%931795)" title="Masovian Voivodeship (1526–1795)">Masovian Voivodeship</a>; in 1844 it was merged into a larger <a href="/wiki/Warsaw_Governorate" title="Warsaw Governorate">Warsaw Governorate</a>.</p>
