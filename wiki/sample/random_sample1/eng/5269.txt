<p><b>Naoko Matsui</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">松井 菜桜子</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Matsui Naoko</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born <b>Naoko Ogawa</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">小川 菜桜子</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Ogawa Naoko</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>, April 4, 1961)</span> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seiy%C5%AB" title="Seiyū">Japanese voice actress</a> from <a href="/wiki/Hakodate,_Hokkaido" title="Hakodate, Hokkaido">Hakodate, Hokkaido</a>. Matsui was a member of <a href="/wiki/Production_Baobab" title="Production Baobab">Production Baobab</a> for 20 years before becoming a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Freelance" title="Freelance">freelancer</a>. She is also a voice acting instructor.</p>
<p>Matsui is most notable for the roles of <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Uru_Chie&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Uru Chie (page does not exist)">Uru Chie</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/High_School!_Kimengumi" title="High School! Kimengumi">High School! Kimengumi</a></i>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Rem_Ayanok%C5%8Dji&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Rem Ayanokōji (page does not exist)">Rem Ayanokōji</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Dream_Hunter_Rem" title="Dream Hunter Rem">Dream Hunter Rem</a></i>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Katsumi_Liqueur&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Katsumi Liqueur (page does not exist)">Katsumi Liqueur</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Silent_M%C3%B6bius" title="Silent Möbius">Silent Möbius</a></i>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Chiyoko_Wato&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Chiyoko Wato (page does not exist)">Chiyoko Wato</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Three-Eyed_One" title="The Three-Eyed One">The Three-Eyed One</a></i>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Runrun&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Runrun (page does not exist)">Runrun</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Mah%C5%8Djin_Guru_Guru" title="Mahōjin Guru Guru">Mahōjin Guru Guru</a></i>, Rabby in <i><a href="/wiki/Gall_Force" title="Gall Force">Gall Force Eternal Story</a></i>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Roux_Louka" title="Roux Louka">Roux Louka</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Mobile_Suit_Gundam_ZZ" title="Mobile Suit Gundam ZZ">Mobile Suit Gundam ZZ</a></i>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dorothy_Catalonia" title="Dorothy Catalonia">Dorothy Catalonia</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Mobile_Suit_Gundam_Wing" title="Mobile Suit Gundam Wing">Mobile Suit Gundam Wing</a></i>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Azusa_Shiratori" title="Azusa Shiratori">Azusa Shiratori</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Ranma_%C2%BD" title="Ranma ½">Ranma ½</a></i>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Miyako_Todaiji&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Miyako Todaiji (page does not exist)">Miyako Todaiji</a> in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kamikaze_Kaito_Jeanne" title="Kamikaze Kaito Jeanne">Kamikaze Kaito Jeanne</a></i>, and Sonoko Suzuki in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Detective_Conan" title="Detective Conan">Detective Conan</a></i>.</p>
<p>She was once married to fellow voice actor <a href="/wiki/Kenyu_Horiuchi" title="Kenyu Horiuchi">Kenyu Horiuchi</a> but they divorced.</p>
<p>Besides voice acting, Matsui is also a <a href="/wiki/Theatre" title="Theatre">theatre</a> actress and co-leader of a theatre group. At a younger age, she wanted to become a <a href="/wiki/Musical_theatre" title="Musical theatre">musical theatre</a> actress.</p>
<p>Matsui lives in <a href="/wiki/Tokyo" title="Tokyo">Tokyo</a>.</p>
