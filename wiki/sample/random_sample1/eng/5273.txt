<p><b>Liga de Elite</b> (Elite League), previously known as <b>Campeonato da 1ª Divisão do Futebol</b>, is the top division of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Associa%C3%A7%C3%A3o_de_Futebol_de_Macau" title="Associação de Futebol de Macau">Macau Football Association</a>, created in 1973. As with the <a href="/wiki/Hong_Kong_Premier_League" title="Hong Kong Premier League">Hong Kong Premier League</a>, it is separate from the mainland Chinese football league system, including the <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Super_League" title="Chinese Super League">Chinese Super League</a> and leagues below it. The league is generally played between January and June of each calendar year.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Liga_de_Elite_.282014.29">Liga de Elite (2014)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Liga_de_Elite&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Liga de Elite (2014)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the 2014 edition, <a href="/wiki/G.D._Lam_Pak" title="G.D. Lam Pak">Lam Pak</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lam_Ieng" title="Lam Ieng">Lam Ieng</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kuan_Tai" title="Kuan Tai">Kuan Tai</a> all forfeited from the 1st Division before the start of the competition.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Jornal_Tribuna_de_Macau_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Jornal_Tribuna_de_Macau-1">[1]</a></sup> Lam Pak and Lam Ieng withdrew due to the lack of backing from sponsors and Kuan Tai cited budget issues and could no longer afford to pay some of its players.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Jornal_Tribuna_de_Macau_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Jornal_Tribuna_de_Macau-1">[1]</a></sup> To solve the problem of 3 sudden vacancies, the <a href="/wiki/Macau_Football_Association" title="Macau Football Association">Macau Football Association</a> clarified that the 2 clubs that were relegated in 2013, Kei Lun and MFA Development, were invited to remain, and an additional third team would come from the 2nd Division.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ponto_Final_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Ponto_Final-2">[2]</a></sup> After careful consideration by the MFA, no team from the 2nd Division was picked, being deemed not competitive enough for the 1st Division.</p>
