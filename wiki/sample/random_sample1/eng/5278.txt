<p><b>Makedonska Kamenica</b> (<a href="/wiki/Macedonian_language" title="Macedonian language">Macedonian</a>: <span lang="mk" xml:lang="mk">Македонска Каменица</span> <span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Bulgarian_and_Macedonian" title="Help:IPA for Bulgarian and Macedonian">[maˈkɛdɔnska kaˈmɛnitsa]</a></span><small class="nowrap metadata"> (<a href="/wiki/File:Mk-Makedonska_Kamenica.ogg" title="File:Mk-Makedonska Kamenica.ogg"><img alt="" data-file-height="500" data-file-width="500" height="13" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/21/Speaker_Icon.svg/13px-Speaker_Icon.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/21/Speaker_Icon.svg/20px-Speaker_Icon.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/21/Speaker_Icon.svg/26px-Speaker_Icon.svg.png 2x" width="13"/></a> <a class="internal" href="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/2/2d/Mk-Makedonska_Kamenica.ogg" title="Mk-Makedonska Kamenica.ogg">listen</a>)</small>) is a municipality in eastern <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Macedonia" title="Republic of Macedonia">Republic of Macedonia</a>. <i><a href="/wiki/Makedonska_Kamenica" title="Makedonska Kamenica">Makedonska Kamenica</a></i> is also the name of the town where the municipal seat is found. The municipality is part of <a href="/wiki/Eastern_Statistical_Region" title="Eastern Statistical Region">Eastern Statistical Region</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Makedonska_Kamenica_Municipality&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The municipality borders <a href="/wiki/Kriva_Palanka_Municipality" title="Kriva Palanka Municipality">Kriva Palanka Municipality</a> in the north, <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgaria</a> and <a href="/wiki/Del%C4%8Devo_Municipality" title="Delčevo Municipality">Delčevo Municipality</a> to the east, <a href="/wiki/Ko%C4%8Dani_Municipality" title="Kočani Municipality">Kočani Municipality</a> to the west, and <a href="/wiki/Vinica_Municipality,_Macedonia" title="Vinica Municipality, Macedonia">Vinica Municipality</a> to the south.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Makedonska_Kamenica_Municipality&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Makedonska Kamenica Municipality had 8,110 residents at the 2002 Macedonian census.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Ethnic groups in the municipality:</p>
<ul>
<li>Macedonians = 8,055 (99.3%)</li>
<li>others = 55</li>
</ul>
