<p><b>Makole</b> (<small>pronounced </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Slovene" title="Help:IPA for Slovene">[ˈmaːkɔlɛ]</a></span>) is a settlement and a <a href="/wiki/Municipality" title="Municipality">municipality</a> in northeastern <a href="/wiki/Slovenia" title="Slovenia">Slovenia</a>. It lies in the <a href="/wiki/Dravinja" title="Dravinja">Dravinja</a> Valley. Traditionally the area was part of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lower_Styria" title="Lower Styria">Styria</a> region. It belonged to the Municipality of <a href="/wiki/Slovenska_Bistrica" title="Slovenska Bistrica">Slovenska Bistrica</a> until 2006, when it became an independent municipality. The municipality is now included in the <a href="/wiki/Drava_Statistical_Region" title="Drava Statistical Region">Drava Statistical Region</a>. It was first mentioned in written documents dating to 1375 and was also granted <a href="/wiki/Market_town" title="Market town">market rights</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Landmarks">Landmarks</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Makole&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Landmarks">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Parish_church" title="Parish church">parish church</a> in the settlement is dedicated to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saint_Andrew" title="Saint Andrew">Saint Andrew</a> and belongs to the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Archdiocese_of_Maribor" title="Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Maribor">Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Maribor</a>. It is a <a href="/wiki/Gothic_architecture" title="Gothic architecture">Gothic</a> building that was first mentioned in 1441. In the 18th century, it was restyled in the <a href="/wiki/Baroque_architecture" title="Baroque architecture">Baroque</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
