<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1210">1210</span></h3>
<div class="hatnote relarticle mainarticle selfreference noprint" role="note">This section is <a href="/wiki/Transclusion" title="Transclusion">transcluded</a> from <a href="/wiki/1210" title="1210">1210</a>. <span class="plainlinks" style="font-style: normal"><small>(<a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=1210&amp;action=edit">edit</a> | <a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=1210&amp;action=history">history</a>)</small></span></div>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="By_area">By area</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1210&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-1" title="1210">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1210&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-2" title="1210">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Emperor_Juntoku" title="Emperor Juntoku">Emperor Juntoku</a> succeeds <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Tsuchimikado" title="Emperor Tsuchimikado">Emperor Tsuchimikado</a> on the throne of <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Jochi" title="Jochi">Jochi</a>, eldest son of <a href="/wiki/Genghis_Khan" title="Genghis Khan">Genghis Khan</a>, leads a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mongol" title="Mongol">Mongol</a> campaign against the <a href="/wiki/Kyrgyz_people" title="Kyrgyz people">Kyrgyz</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Delhi_Sultanate" title="Delhi Sultanate">Delhi Sultanate</a> begins.</li>
</ul>
<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1210&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-3" title="1210">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li>May – The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Second_Parliament_of_Ravennika" title="Second Parliament of Ravennika">Second Parliament of Ravennika</a> is held at <a href="/wiki/Ravennika" title="Ravennika">Ravennika</a> in <a href="/wiki/Central_Greece" title="Central Greece">Central Greece</a>, resulting in a <a href="/wiki/Concordat" title="Concordat">concordat</a> between the princes of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Frankish_Greece" title="Frankish Greece">Frankish Greece</a> and the Roman Catholic clergy.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_18" title="July 18">July 18</a> – Former king <a href="/wiki/Sverker_II_of_Sweden" title="Sverker II of Sweden">Sverker II of Sweden</a> is defeated and killed by the reigning king <a href="/wiki/Eric_X_of_Sweden" title="Eric X of Sweden">Erik X</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Gestilren" title="Battle of Gestilren">Battle of Gestilren</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_18" title="November 18">November 18</a> – <a href="/wiki/Otto_IV,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Otto IV, Holy Roman Emperor">Otto IV, Holy Roman Emperor</a> is excommunicated by <a href="/wiki/Pope_Innocent_III" title="Pope Innocent III">Pope Innocent III</a> for invading southern Italy in defiance of the <a href="/wiki/Concordat_of_Worms" title="Concordat of Worms">Concordat of Worms</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_21" title="November 21">November 21</a> – King Eric X of <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Sweden</a> is crowned, which is the first known coronation of a Swedish king. Shortly thereafter, he marries the Danish princess <a href="/wiki/Richeza_of_Denmark" title="Richeza of Denmark">Richeza</a>, in order to strengthen his relationship to the Danish king <a href="/wiki/Valdemar_II_of_Denmark" title="Valdemar II of Denmark">Valdemar the Conqueror</a>. Valdemar used to support king Sverker II, but through the marriage, Valdemar makes peace with his former enemy, Eric.</li>
<li>King <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/John_I_of_England" title="John I of England">John I of England</a> raises £100,000 from church property as an extraordinary fiscal levy; the operation is described as an “inestimable and incomparable exaction” by contemporary sources.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Livonian_Crusade" title="Livonian Crusade">Livonian Crusade</a>: <a href="/wiki/Estonians" title="Estonians">Estonian</a> forces defeat the <a href="/wiki/Livonian_Brothers_of_the_Sword" title="Livonian Brothers of the Sword">Livonian Brothers of the Sword</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_%C3%9Cmera" title="Battle of Ümera">Battle of Ümera</a>.</li>
<li>The citadel of the <a href="/wiki/Acrocorinth" title="Acrocorinth">Acrocorinth</a> surrenders to the <a href="/wiki/Fourth_Crusade" title="Fourth Crusade">Crusaders</a> after a five-year siege.</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li><a href="/wiki/July_16" title="July 16">July 16</a> – The Christian kingdoms of Spain decisively defeat the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Almohads" title="Almohads">Almohads</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Navas_de_Tolosa" title="Battle of Navas de Tolosa">Battle of Navas de Tolosa</a> and the victory leaves the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Castile" title="Kingdom of Castile">Kingdom of Castile</a> in a difficult financial position as numerous soldiers have to be paid by the treasury.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-linehan1999_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-linehan1999-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li>December – <a href="/wiki/Frederick_II,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Frederick II, Holy Roman Emperor">Frederick II</a> of Hohenstaufen is crowned King of Germany with the support of <a href="/wiki/Pope_Innocent_III" title="Pope Innocent III">Pope Innocent III</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Children%27s_Crusade" title="Children's Crusade">Children's Crusade</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Holy_Land" title="Holy Land">Holy Land</a> is organised. <ul>

</ul>

<li>In Japan, <a href="/wiki/Kamo_no_Ch%C5%8Dmei" title="Kamo no Chōmei">Kamo no Chōmei</a> writes the <i><a href="/wiki/H%C5%8Dj%C5%8Dki" title="Hōjōki">Hōjōki</a></i>, one of the great works of classical Japanese prose.</li>

<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/John_of_England" title="John of England">John of England</a> impounds the revenue of all prelates appointed by bishops who had deserted him at his <a href="/wiki/Excommunication" title="Excommunication">excommunication</a>. He remains on good terms, however, with churchmen who stood by him, including Abbot Sampson, who this year bequeaths John his jewels.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></li>
<li>The Banner of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Las_Navas_de_Tolosa" title="Las Navas de Tolosa">Las Navas de Tolosa</a> is begun. It is a <a href="/wiki/Trophy" title="Trophy">trophy</a> of <a href="/wiki/Ferdinand_III_of_Castile" title="Ferdinand III of Castile">Ferdinand III of Castile</a>, and will end up in the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Museo_de_Telas_Medievales&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Museo de Telas Medievales (page does not exist)">Museo de Telas Medievales</a>.</li>

<li>The fall of <a href="/wiki/Argos" title="Argos">Argos</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Fourth_Crusade" title="Fourth Crusade">Crusaders</a> early in the year completes their conquest of the <a href="/wiki/Morea" title="Morea">Morea</a>. The city, along with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nauplia" title="Nauplia">Nauplia</a>, is given to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Otto_de_la_Roche" title="Otto de la Roche">Otto de la Roche</a> as a fief (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Argos_and_Nauplia" title="Argos and Nauplia">Argos and Nauplia</a>).</li>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1215">1215</span></h3>
<div class="hatnote relarticle mainarticle selfreference noprint" role="note">This section is <a href="/wiki/Transclusion" title="Transclusion">transcluded</a> from <a href="/wiki/1215" title="1215">1215</a>. <span class="plainlinks" style="font-style: normal"><small>(<a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=1215&amp;action=edit">edit</a> | <a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=1215&amp;action=history">history</a>)</small></span></div>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_4" title="March 4">March 4</a> – <a href="/wiki/Monarch" title="Monarch">King</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/John_of_England" title="John of England">John of England</a> makes an <a href="/wiki/Oath" title="Oath">oath</a> to <a href="/wiki/Pope_Innocent_III" title="Pope Innocent III">Pope Innocent III</a> as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Crusades" title="The Crusades">crusader</a> to gain his support.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_1" title="June 1">June 1</a> – <a href="/wiki/Mongol_conquest_of_the_Jin_dynasty" title="Mongol conquest of the Jin dynasty">Mongol conquest of the Jin dynasty</a>: after the long <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Zhongdu" title="Battle of Zhongdu">Battle of Zhongdu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Genghis_Khan" title="Genghis Khan">Genghis Khan</a>'s Mongols captured and torched <a href="/wiki/Beijing" title="Beijing">Beijing</a> (the city burns for more than a month).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_15" title="June 15">June 15</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/King_John_of_England" title="King John of England">King John of England</a> is forced by rebellious <a href="/wiki/Baron" title="Baron">barons</a> of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a> at <a href="/wiki/Runnymede" title="Runnymede">Runnymede</a> to put the <a href="/wiki/Great_Seal_of_the_Realm" title="Great Seal of the Realm">Great Seal of the Realm</a> on a set of articles confirming their rights and those of the towns and Church, and confirming the status of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Trial_by_jury_in_the_United_Kingdom" title="Trial by jury in the United Kingdom">trial by jury</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Cassell.27s_Chronology_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-Cassell.27s_Chronology-7">[7]</a></sup> which on <a href="/wiki/June_19" title="June 19">June 19</a> is confirmed as <a href="/wiki/Magna_Carta" title="Magna Carta">Magna Carta</a>.</li>
<li>August – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/King_John_of_England" title="King John of England">King John of England</a> rejects <a href="/wiki/Magna_Carta" title="Magna Carta">Magna Carta</a>, leading to the <a href="/wiki/First_Barons%27_War" title="First Barons' War">First Barons' War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_24" title="August 24">August 24</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Innocent_III" title="Pope Innocent III">Pope Innocent III</a> declares <a href="/wiki/Magna_Carta" title="Magna Carta">Magna Carta</a> invalid.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_11" title="November 11">November 11</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Fourth_Council_of_the_Lateran" title="Fourth Council of the Lateran">Fourth Council of the Lateran</a> gathers in <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a> under <a href="/wiki/Pope_Innocent_III" title="Pope Innocent III">Pope Innocent III</a>, who adopts the style "Vicar of Christ".</li>
<li>December – <a href="/wiki/First_Barons%27_War" title="First Barons' War">First Barons' War</a>: <a href="/wiki/Alexander_II_of_Scotland" title="Alexander II of Scotland">Alexander II of Scotland</a> invades <a href="/wiki/Northern_England" title="Northern England">northern England</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-CBH_6-4"><a href="#cite_note-CBH-6">[6]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Otto_IV,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Otto IV, Holy Roman Emperor">Otto IV, Holy Roman Emperor</a>, is excommunicated and forced to abdicate as Emperor and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/King_of_Burgundy" title="King of Burgundy">King of Burgundy</a>, replaced by <a href="/wiki/Frederick_II,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Frederick II, Holy Roman Emperor">Frederick II</a> (<a href="/wiki/King_of_the_Romans" title="King of the Romans">King of the Romans</a> 1212–1250).</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Dominican_Order" title="Dominican Order">Dominican Order</a> is founded, according to some sources.</li>
<li>Bhiksu Ananda of Kapitanagar completes writing the holy <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Buddhist" title="Buddhist">Buddhist</a> book <i>Arya Astasahasrika Prajnaparamita</i> (<a href="/wiki/Sutra" title="Sutra">sutra</a>) in gold ink in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ranjana_script" title="Ranjana script">Ranjana script</a>.</li>
<li>1215–1216 – The Macy Jug, from <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>, is made. It is now kept at The <a href="/wiki/Metropolitan_Museum_of_Art" title="Metropolitan Museum of Art">Metropolitan Museum of Art</a>, <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kalinga_Magha" title="Kalinga Magha">Kalinga Magha</a> from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kalinga,_India" title="Kalinga, India">Kalinga</a> Province in India lands in <a href="/wiki/Sri_Lanka" title="Sri Lanka">Sri Lanka</a> with a force of 24,000 men, to capture the city of <a href="/wiki/Polonnaruwa" title="Polonnaruwa">Polonnaruwa</a> and depose its King, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Parakrama_Pandya" title="Parakrama Pandya">Parakrama Pandya</a>.</li>
</ul>

The young <a href="/wiki/Henry_III_of_England" title="Henry III of England">Henry III of England</a> is crowned at <a href="/wiki/Gloucester" title="Gloucester">Gloucester</a> on <a href="/wiki/October_28" title="October 28">October 28</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Cassell.27s_Chronology_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-Cassell.27s_Chronology-7">[7]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_12" title="November 12">November 12</a> – William Marshal and the <a href="/wiki/Papal_legate" title="Papal legate">papal legate</a> to England, <a href="/wiki/Guala_Bicchieri" title="Guala Bicchieri">Guala Bicchieri</a>, issue a Charter of Liberties, based on the <a href="/wiki/Magna_Carta" title="Magna Carta">Magna Carta</a>, in the new King of England's name.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Cassell.27s_Chronology_7-2"><a href="#cite_note-Cassell.27s_Chronology-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dresden" title="Dresden">Dresden</a> receives <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/City_rights" title="City rights">city rights</a>.</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a>, <a href="/wiki/Roger_of_Wendover" title="Roger of Wendover">Roger of Wendover</a> begins to cover contemporary events in his continuation of the <a href="/wiki/Chronicle" title="Chronicle">chronicle</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Flores_Historiarum" title="Flores Historiarum">Flores Historiarum</a></i>.</li>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_9" title="April 9">April 9</a> – <a href="/wiki/Peter_II_of_Courtenay" title="Peter II of Courtenay">Peter II of Courtenay</a> is crowned emperor of the <a href="/wiki/Latin_Empire" title="Latin Empire">Latin Empire</a> of <a href="/wiki/Constantinople" title="Constantinople">Constantinople</a> at <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a>, by <a href="/wiki/Pope_Honorius_III" title="Pope Honorius III">Pope Honorius III</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_20" title="May 20">May 20</a> – <a href="/wiki/First_Barons%27_War" title="First Barons' War">First Barons' War</a> in <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>: Occupying French forces are defeated at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Lincoln_(1217)" title="Battle of Lincoln (1217)">Battle of Lincoln</a> by English royal troops led by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/William_Marshal,_1st_Earl_of_Pembroke_(2nd_Creation)" title="William Marshal, 1st Earl of Pembroke (2nd Creation)">William Marshal, Earl of Pembroke</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-CBH_6-7"><a href="#cite_note-CBH-6">[6]</a></sup> and survivors forced to flee south.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_6" title="June 6">June 6</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Henry_I_of_Castile" title="Henry I of Castile">Henry I of Castile</a> dies aged thirteen from the fall of a roof tile in <a href="/wiki/Palencia" title="Palencia">Palencia</a>, an event which his <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Regency_(government)" title="Regency (government)">regent</a>, <a href="/wiki/%C3%81lvaro_N%C3%BA%C3%B1ez_de_Lara_(died_1218)" title="Álvaro Núñez de Lara (died 1218)">Álvaro Núñez de Lara</a>, attempts to conceal. Henry's sister <a href="/wiki/Berengaria_of_Castile" title="Berengaria of Castile">Berengaria</a> succeeds to the throne.</li>
<li>June – Haakon Haakonsson becomes King <a href="/wiki/Haakon_IV_of_Norway" title="Haakon IV of Norway">Haakon IV of Norway</a> following the death of <a href="/wiki/Inge_II_of_Norway" title="Inge II of Norway">Inge II</a> and largely ends the <a href="/wiki/Civil_war_era_in_Norway" title="Civil war era in Norway">civil war era in Norway</a>, reigning until <a href="/wiki/1263" title="1263">1263</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_24" title="August 24">August 24</a> – First Barons' War: In the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Sandwich_(1217)" title="Battle of Sandwich (1217)">Battle of Sandwich</a> in the <a href="/wiki/English_Channel" title="English Channel">English Channel</a>, English forces destroy the French and the French mercenary <a href="/wiki/Eustace_the_Monk" title="Eustace the Monk">Eustace the Monk</a> is captured and beheaded.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_31" title="August 31">August 31</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ferdinand_III_of_Castile" title="Ferdinand III of Castile">Ferdinand</a> becomes <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Castile" title="Kingdom of Castile">King of Castile</a> on abdication of his mother <a href="/wiki/Berengaria_of_Castile" title="Berengaria of Castile">Berengaria</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_12" title="September 12">September 12</a> – First Barons' War in England ended by the <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Lambeth#Treaty_of_Kingston_.281217.29" title="Treaty of Lambeth">Treaty of Kingston upon Thames</a>: French and Scots are to leave England, and an amnesty is granted to rebels.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-CBH_6-8"><a href="#cite_note-CBH-6">[6]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_20" title="September 20">September 20</a> – <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Lambeth" title="Treaty of Lambeth">Treaty of Lambeth</a> signed ratifying the Treaty of Kingston.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_21" title="September 21">September 21</a> – <a href="/wiki/Livonian_Crusade" title="Livonian Crusade">Livonian Crusade</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Livonian_Brothers_of_the_Sword" title="Livonian Brothers of the Sword">Livonian Brothers of the Sword</a> and allied <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Livonian_people" title="Livonian people">Livs</a> and <a href="/wiki/Latvians" title="Latvians">Letts</a> defeat the <a href="/wiki/Estonians" title="Estonians">Estonian</a> army in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_St._Matthew%27s_Day" title="Battle of St. Matthew's Day">Battle of St. Matthew's Day</a> and kill their leader <a href="/wiki/Lembitu" title="Lembitu">Lembitu</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/October_18" title="October 18">October 18</a> – <a href="/wiki/Fifth_Crusade" title="Fifth Crusade">Fifth Crusade</a>, <a href="/wiki/Reconquista" title="Reconquista">Reconquista</a>: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Alc%C3%A1cer_do_Sal_Municipality" title="Alcácer do Sal Municipality">Alcácer do Sal</a> is reconquered by the troops of King <a href="/wiki/Afonso_II_of_Portugal" title="Afonso II of Portugal">Afonso II of Portugal</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-picard2000_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-picard2000-10">[10]</a></sup></li>
<li>November – In the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Castile" title="Kingdom of Castile">Kingdom of Castile</a>, former regent <a href="/wiki/%C3%81lvaro_N%C3%BA%C3%B1ez_de_Lara_(died_1218)" title="Álvaro Núñez de Lara (died 1218)">Álvaro Núñez de Lara</a> is captured and forced to relinquish all his castles.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-linehan1999_3-2"><a href="#cite_note-linehan1999-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Stefan_Nemanji%C4%87" title="Stefan Nemanjić">Stefan Nemanjić</a> is elevated to be first <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Serbia_(medieval)" title="Kingdom of Serbia (medieval)">King of the Serbian lands</a> by <a href="/wiki/Pope_Honorius_III" title="Pope Honorius III">Pope Honorius III</a> and crowned by Stefan's brother, <a href="/wiki/Archimandrite" title="Archimandrite">Archimandrite</a> <a href="/wiki/Saint_Sava" title="Saint Sava">Sava</a>, in <a href="/wiki/%C5%BDi%C4%8Da" title="Žiča">Žiča</a>.</li>
<li>A decree made in <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a> establishes that only Englishman can be clergy of <a href="/wiki/Lordship_of_Ireland" title="Lordship of Ireland">Ireland</a>.</li>
</ul>

<li><a href="/wiki/August_31" title="August 31">August 31</a> – <a href="/wiki/Al-Kamil" title="Al-Kamil">Al-Kamil</a> becomes the new <a href="/wiki/Sultan_of_Egypt" title="Sultan of Egypt">Egyptian Sultan</a> on the death of his father <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Al-Adil" title="Al-Adil">Al-Adil</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia_4">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1218&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-3" title="1218">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_24" title="May 24">May 24</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Fifth_Crusade" title="Fifth Crusade">Fifth Crusade</a> leaves <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Akko" title="Akko">Acre</a> for <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Damietta" title="Damietta">Damietta</a> is besieged by the <a href="/wiki/Knight" title="Knight">knights</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Fifth_Crusade" title="Fifth Crusade">Fifth Crusade</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Minamoto_no_Sanetomo" title="Minamoto no Sanetomo">Minamoto no Sanetomo</a> becomes <i><a href="/wiki/Udaijin" title="Udaijin">Udaijin</a></i> of <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kara-Khitan_Khanate" title="Kara-Khitan Khanate">Kara-Khitan Khanate</a> is destroyed by <a href="/wiki/Genghis_Khan" title="Genghis Khan">Genghis Khan</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Mongols" title="Mongols">Mongolian</a> cavalry.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Genghis_Khan" title="Genghis Khan">Genghis Khan</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Mongols" title="Mongols">Mongols</a>, under the leadership of his eldest son <a href="/wiki/Jochi" title="Jochi">Jochi</a>, conduct a second campaign against the <a href="/wiki/Kyrgyz_people" title="Kyrgyz people">Kyrgyz</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Genghis_Khan" title="Genghis Khan">Genghis Khan</a> proposes to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Khwarazm" title="Khwarazm">Khwarazm</a> <a href="/wiki/Shah" title="Shah">shah</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Persia" title="Persia">Persia</a> that he accept Mongol overlordship and establish trade relations.</li>
</ul>
<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe_5">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1218&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-4" title="1218">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li>March – The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Worcester" title="Treaty of Worcester">Treaty of Worcester</a> recognises <a href="/wiki/Llywelyn_the_Great" title="Llywelyn the Great">Llywelyn the Great</a> as regent of <a href="/wiki/South_Wales" title="South Wales">south Wales</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July" title="July">July</a> – <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>: In order to facilitate the movement of <a href="/wiki/Reconquista" title="Reconquista">Reconquista</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pope_Honorius_III" title="Pope Honorius III">Pope Honorius III</a> reverses <a href="/wiki/Pope_Innocent_III" title="Pope Innocent III">Innocent III</a>’s earlier judgement and declares <a href="/wiki/Ferdinand_III_of_Castile" title="Ferdinand III of Castile">Ferdinand III of Castile</a> legitimate heir to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Leon" title="Kingdom of Leon">Kingdom of Leon</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-linehan1999_3-3"><a href="#cite_note-linehan1999-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
</ul>

<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Markets">Markets</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1218&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-7" title="1218">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li>The northern French city of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rheims" title="Rheims">Rheims</a> emits the first recorded public <a href="/wiki/Life_annuity" title="Life annuity">life annuity</a> in medieval Europe. Theretofore, this type of instrument had been mostly issued by religious institutions. The emission by Rheims is the first evidence of a consolidation of public debt that is to become common in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Langue_d%27O%C3%AFl" title="Langue d'Oïl">Langue d'Oïl</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Low_Countries" title="Low Countries">Low Countries</a> and <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></li>
</ul>

<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saint_Pedro_Nolasco" title="Saint Pedro Nolasco">Saint Pedro Nolasco</a> founds the <a href="/wiki/Order_of_the_Blessed_Virgin_Mary_of_Mercy" title="Order of the Blessed Virgin Mary of Mercy">Order of the Blessed Virgin Mary of Mercy</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Barcelona,_Spain" title="Barcelona, Spain">Barcelona, Spain</a>.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_5" title="November 5">November 5</a> – <a href="/wiki/Damietta" title="Damietta">Damietta</a>, <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a> falls to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Crusaders" title="Crusaders">Crusaders</a> after a siege.</li>
<li>Saint <a href="/wiki/Francis_of_Assisi" title="Francis of Assisi">Francis of Assisi</a> introduces <a href="/wiki/Catholicism" title="Catholicism">Catholicism</a> into <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>, during the <a href="/wiki/Fifth_Crusade" title="Fifth Crusade">Fifth Crusade</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egyptian</a> city of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Al_Mansurah" title="Al Mansurah">Al Mansurah</a> is founded.</li>
</ul>
<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia_5">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1219&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-3" title="1219">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li>By letter, <a href="/wiki/Genghis_Khan" title="Genghis Khan">Genghis Khan</a> summons <a href="/wiki/Qiu_Chuji" title="Qiu Chuji">Qiu Chuji</a> (Chongchan) to visit him to advise him on the medicine of immortality (the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Philosopher%27s_Stone" title="Philosopher's Stone">Philosopher's Stone</a>)</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/H%C5%8Dj%C5%8D_clan" title="Hōjō clan">Hojo</a> family, vassals of the <a href="/wiki/Shogun" title="Shogun">Shogun</a>, reduce him to a figurehead.</li>
</ul>
<h6><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe_6">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1219&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-4" title="1219">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h6>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_15" title="June 15">June 15</a> – <a href="/wiki/Livonian_Crusade" title="Livonian Crusade">Livonian Crusade</a>: <a href="/wiki/Denmark" title="Denmark">Danish</a> crusaders led by King <a href="/wiki/Valdemar_II_of_Denmark" title="Valdemar II of Denmark">Valdemar II</a> conquer <a href="/wiki/Tallinn" title="Tallinn">Tallinn</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Lyndanisse" title="Battle of Lyndanisse">Battle of Lyndanisse</a>. What is to become the <a href="/wiki/Flag_of_Denmark" title="Flag of Denmark">flag of Denmark</a> allegedly falls from the sky during that battle. Their stronghold in Tallinn will help the Danes conquer the entire <a href="/wiki/Danish_Estonia" title="Danish Estonia">Northern Estonia</a>. The Dannebrog remains the national flag of Denmark.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/List_of_early_Lithuanian_dukes" title="List of early Lithuanian dukes">Twenty-four Lithuanian dukes and nobles</a> purportedly sign a peace treaty with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Halych-Volhynia" title="Halych-Volhynia">Halych-Volhynia</a>, stating common cause against invading <a href="/wiki/Christian" title="Christian">Christian</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Crusaders" title="Crusaders">Crusaders</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></li>
<li>Upon the death of <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Aymeric_of_Saint_Maur&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Aymeric of Saint Maur (page does not exist)">Aymeric of Saint Maur</a>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Alan_Marcell&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Alan Marcell (page does not exist)">Alan Marcell</a> becomes master of the <a href="/wiki/Knights_Templar" title="Knights Templar">Temple</a> in <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/East_Frisian_Islands" title="East Frisian Islands">East Frisian island</a> of <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Burchana&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Burchana (page does not exist)">Burchana</a> is broken up in a <a href="/wiki/North_Sea" title="North Sea">North Sea</a> flood.</li>
</ul>
