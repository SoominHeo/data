<p><b>Matam</b> is a region of <a href="/wiki/Senegal" title="Senegal">Senegal</a> (regional capitals have the same name as their respective regions).</p>
<p>It is a stark, flat, arid place bounded on the north by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/S%C3%A9n%C3%A9gal_River" title="Sénégal River">Sénégal River</a> and the south by the <a href="/wiki/Sahel" title="Sahel">Sahelian</a> plain studded with <a href="/wiki/Adansonia_digitata" title="Adansonia digitata">baobab</a> trees. Matam is populated by the tall <a href="/wiki/Fula_language" title="Fula language">Pulaar</a>-speaking <a href="/wiki/Toucouleur_people" title="Toucouleur people">Toucouleur</a> people who brought <a href="/wiki/Islam" title="Islam">Islam</a> to Senegal in the 11th century and gave us the music of <a href="/wiki/Baaba_Maal" title="Baaba Maal">Baaba Maal</a> in the 21st century.</p>
