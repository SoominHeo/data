<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Martin Reader</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Martin Reader</b> (born May 2, 1984 in <a href="/wiki/Comox_Valley" title="Comox Valley">Comox Valley</a>, <a href="/wiki/British_Columbia" title="British Columbia">British Columbia</a>)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is a retired <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canadian</a> male <a href="/wiki/Beach_volleyball" title="Beach volleyball">beach volleyball</a> player. He competed for Canada at the <a href="/wiki/Table_tennis_at_the_2012_Summer_Olympics" title="Table tennis at the 2012 Summer Olympics">2012 Summer Olympics</a> with <a href="/wiki/Josh_Binstock" title="Josh Binstock">Josh Binstock</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Reader announced his retirement from competition after the London Olympics.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Martin_Reader&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Reader was born in <a href="/wiki/Comox_Valley" title="Comox Valley">Comox Valley</a>, <a href="/wiki/British_Columbia" title="British Columbia">British Columbia</a>. His father, raised in <a href="/wiki/Uganda" title="Uganda">Uganda</a>, worked for the British African police and was a surfer.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
