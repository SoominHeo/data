<p><b>Mathildoidea</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Taxonomic_rank" title="Taxonomic rank">superfamily</a> of sea <a href="/wiki/Snail" title="Snail">snails</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marine_(ocean)" title="Marine (ocean)">marine</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gastropod" title="Gastropod">gastropod</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mollusk" title="Mollusk">mollusks</a> in the informal group <a href="/wiki/Lower_Heterobranchia" title="Lower Heterobranchia">Lower Heterobranchia</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
