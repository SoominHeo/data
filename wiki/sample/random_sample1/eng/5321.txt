<p><b>Maha Bandula Bridge</b> (sometimes spelled Mahabandoola Bridge) is a major bridge in <a href="/wiki/Yangon" title="Yangon">Yangon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Myanmar" title="Myanmar">Myanmar</a> built in 2001. It is named after General <a href="/wiki/Maha_Bandula" title="Maha Bandula">Maha Bandula</a>, and crosses <a href="/wiki/Pazundaung_Creek" title="Pazundaung Creek">Pazundaung Creek</a> just east of Yangon's central business district. It is accessed by <a href="/wiki/Maha_Bandula_Road" title="Maha Bandula Road">Maha Bandula Road</a>.</p>
