<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">203 Hill</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:203_Meter_Hill.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="1675" data-file-width="2416" height="153" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/ce/203_Meter_Hill.jpg/220px-203_Meter_Hill.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/ce/203_Meter_Hill.jpg/330px-203_Meter_Hill.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/ce/203_Meter_Hill.jpg/440px-203_Meter_Hill.jpg 2x" width="220"/></a>

203 Meter Hill, December 14, 1904</div>
</div>
</div>
<p><b>203 Hill</b> (in <a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">二零三高地</span> or in <a href="/wiki/Japanese_language" title="Japanese language">Japanese</a>: 二〇三高地) is a <a href="/wiki/High_ground" title="High ground">high ground</a> located in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lushunkou_District" title="Lushunkou District">Lushunkou District</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dalian_City" title="Dalian City">Dalian City</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Liaoning_Province" title="Liaoning Province">Liaoning Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. In 1904-1905, one of the fiercest battles was fought between the Japanese and <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russian</a> armies in the <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Port_Arthur" title="Siege of Port Arthur">Siege of Port Arthur</a>, during the <a href="/wiki/Russo-Japanese_War" title="Russo-Japanese War">Russo-Japanese War</a>.</p>
<p>It is so named because it is 203 metres (666 ft) <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Meters_above_sea_level" title="Meters above sea level">above sea level</a>.</p>
<p><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Maresuke_Nogi" title="Maresuke Nogi">Maresuke Nogi</a> after the battle used the same Chinese pronunciation to name it the "Mountain Where Your Souls Lie" (in <a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">爾靈山</span>; Erlingshan) in his famous poem:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<table class="wikitable" style="margin:auto">
<tr>
<th>爾靈山</th>
<th>Your Souls' Mountain</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td align="left">爾靈山嶮豈難攀<br/>
<p>男子功名期克艱<br/>
鐵血覆山山形改<br/>
萬人齊仰爾靈山</p>
</td>
<td align="left">Can we say it was easy to climb 203 Hill?<br/>
<p>Was it not difficult because men sought their honor?<br/>
The mountain has changed its shape, covered by iron and blood.<br/>
We all now equally look up in awe at Your Souls' Mountain.</p>
</td>
</tr>
</table>
