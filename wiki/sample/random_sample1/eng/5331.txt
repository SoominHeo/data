<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Max Eckert</b> (after 1934, <b>Max Eckert-Greifendorff</b>: 10 April 1868 in <a href="/wiki/Chemnitz" title="Chemnitz">Chemnitz</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Saxony" title="Kingdom of Saxony">Kingdom of Saxony</a> – 26 December 1938, in <a href="/wiki/Aachen" title="Aachen">Aachen</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> <a href="/wiki/Geographer" title="Geographer">geographer</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Max_Eckert-Greifendorff&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He received his education in <a href="/wiki/L%C3%B6bau" title="Löbau">Löbau</a> and <a href="/wiki/Berlin" title="Berlin">Berlin</a>, and taught for some time at Löbau and <a href="/wiki/Leipzig" title="Leipzig">Leipzig</a>. In 1903, he became <a href="/wiki/Privatdozent" title="Privatdozent">Privatdozent</a> at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kiel_University" title="Kiel University">Kiel University</a>. In 1907, he was appointed to the chair of geography in the Royal Technical High School of Aachen (Aix-la-Chapelle). He invented the six <a href="/wiki/Eckert_projection" title="Eckert projection">Eckert projections</a> and others.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Writings">Writings</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Max_Eckert-Greifendorff&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Writings">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He has published <i>Schulatlas</i> (School atlas, 45th ed., 1912), <i>Wesen und Aufgabe der Wirtschafts- und Verkehrsgeographie</i> (Essentials and purpose of economic and transportation geography, 1903), <i>Grundriss der Handelsgeographie</i> (Fundamentals of the geography of trade, 1905), <i>Leitfaden der Handelsgeographie</i> (Primer of the geography of trade, 3d ed., 1911), <i>Neue Entwürfe für Erdkarten</i> (New ideas for world maps, 1906), <i>Die Kartographie als Wissenschaft</i> (Cartography as a discipline, 1907), <i>The New Fields of Geography, especially Commercial Geography</i> (1907), <i>Geographisches Praktikum</i>, with <a href="/wiki/Otto_Kr%C3%BCmmel" title="Otto Krümmel">Otto Krümmel</a> (Geography in practice, 1908), <i>Fortschritt in der geographischen Erschliessung unsern Kolonien</i> (1908 et seq.), <i>Die Kartenprojektion</i> (Map projections, 1910), <i>Deutsche Kulturgeographie</i> (German cultural geography, 1912), <i>Wirtschaftsatlas der deutschen Kolonien</i> (Economic atlas of the German colonies, 1912), <i>Die wirtschaftliche Bedeutung des Panama-Kanals</i> (The economic significance of the <a href="/wiki/Panama_Canal" title="Panama Canal">Panama Canal</a>, 1913), <i>Die Metallverbreitung und Metallgewerbe der Welt</i> (World metal trade and distribution, 1913).</p>
