<p><b>Mandera County</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Counties_of_Kenya" title="Counties of Kenya">county</a> in the former <a href="/wiki/North_Eastern_Province_(Kenya)" title="North Eastern Province (Kenya)">North Eastern Province</a> of <a href="/wiki/Kenya" title="Kenya">Kenya</a>. Its capital and largest town is <a href="/wiki/Mandera" title="Mandera">Mandera</a>. The county has a population of 1,025,756 (2009 census)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> and an area of 25,797.7 km².</p>
