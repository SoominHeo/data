<p><b>Manhwabang</b> is a kind of <a href="/wiki/Coffeehouse" title="Coffeehouse">café</a> in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a> where people can read <a href="/wiki/Manhwa" title="Manhwa">manhwas</a>. Similarly, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> version of manhwabang is <a href="/wiki/Manga_cafe" title="Manga cafe">manga cafe</a>.</p>
