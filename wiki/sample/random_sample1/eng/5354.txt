<p>The <b>District of <a href="/wiki/Mallakast%C3%ABr" title="Mallakastër">Mallakastër</a></b> (<a href="/wiki/Albanian_language" title="Albanian language">Albanian</a>: <span lang="sq" xml:lang="sq"><i>Rrethi i Mallakastrës</i></span>) is one of the thirty-six <a href="/wiki/Districts_of_Albania" title="Districts of Albania">districts of Albania</a>, part of <a href="/wiki/Fier_County" title="Fier County">Fier County</a>. It has a population of 31,356<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> (2010 estimate), and an area of 393 km². It is in the south of the country, and its capital is <a href="/wiki/Ballsh" title="Ballsh">Ballsh</a>. The district consists of the following municipalities:</p>
