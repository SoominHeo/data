<p><b>Manga</b> is a town located in the province of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Zoundw%C3%A9ogo" title="Zoundwéogo">Zoundwéogo</a> in <a href="/wiki/Burkina_Faso" title="Burkina Faso">Burkina Faso</a>. It is the capital of Zoundwéogo Province and <a href="/wiki/Centre-Sud_Region" title="Centre-Sud Region">Centre-Sud Region</a>.</p>
