<p><br/>
<b>2055 (<a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MMLV</a>)</b> will be a <a href="/wiki/Common_year" title="Common year">common year</a> <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Friday" title="Common year starting on Friday">starting on Friday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> C) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>, the 2055th year of the <a href="/wiki/Common_Era" title="Common Era">Common Era</a> (CE) and <i><a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a></i> (AD) designations, the 55th year of the <a href="/wiki/3rd_millennium" title="3rd millennium">3rd millennium</a>, the 55th year of the <a href="/wiki/21st_century" title="21st century">21st century</a>, and the 6th year of the <a href="/wiki/2050s" title="2050s">2050s</a> decade.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Predicted_and_scheduled_events">Predicted and scheduled events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2055&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Predicted and scheduled events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>A 2006 design competition called “Hospitality Transformed: Resort Hotels in 2055" envisaged high-rise hotels that hang down from the Antarctic ice sheet.</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="In_fiction">In fiction</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2055&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: In fiction">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>This is the year that the <i><a href="/wiki/Days_of_Future_Past" title="Days of Future Past">Days of Future Past</a></i> story occurs.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Dexter%27s_Laboratory" title="Dexter's Laboratory">Dexter's Laboratory</a> movie, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ego_Trip_(Dexter%27s_Laboratory)" title="Ego Trip (Dexter's Laboratory)">Ego Trip</a></i>, has some scenes that take place in 2055.</li>
<li>The short story <i><a href="/wiki/A_Sound_of_Thunder" title="A Sound of Thunder">A Sound of Thunder</a></i> begins in this year.</li>
<li>The same film <a href="/wiki/A_Sound_of_Thunder_(film)" title="A Sound of Thunder (film)">from 2005</a> starts there based on ability of some companies having <a href="/wiki/Time_travel" title="Time travel">time machine</a>.</li>
<li>The protagonist of the film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Age_of_Stupid" title="The Age of Stupid">The Age of Stupid</a></i> is from this year.</li>
<li>The spaceship <i>Icarus II</i> leaves Earth to re-ignite the dying sun in this year in the movie <a href="/wiki/Sunshine_(2007_film)" title="Sunshine (2007 film)">Sunshine</a>.</li>
<li>The levels, "Traffic" and "Fission," in the 2014 video game, <i><a href="/wiki/Call_of_Duty:_Advanced_Warfare" title="Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare">Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare</a>,</i> take place in 2055.</li>
<li>The fifth and sixth episodes of the anime <i><a href="/wiki/Mnemosyne_(anime)" title="Mnemosyne (anime)">Rin: Daughters of Mnemosyne</a></i> are set in 2055.</li>
</ul>
