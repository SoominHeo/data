<p><b>Madison Lee De La Garza</b> <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">[1]</a></sup> (born December 28, 2001<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>) is an American actress. She is known for playing Juanita Solis on <i><a href="/wiki/Desperate_Housewives" title="Desperate Housewives">Desperate Housewives</a></i>. She is the younger half-sister of actress and singer <a href="/wiki/Demi_Lovato" title="Demi Lovato">Demi Lovato</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Madison_De_La_Garza&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Madison De La Garza <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">[1]</a></sup>was born to Ford dealership manager Edward "Eddie" De La Garza and former Dallas Cowboys Cheerleader<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Dianna_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Dianna-3">[3]</a></sup> Dianna De La Garza (born Dianna Lee Smith on August 8, 1962).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MotherLine_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-MotherLine-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="noprint Inline-Template noprint Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wikipedia:NOTRS" title="Wikipedia:NOTRS"><span title="This claim needs references to better sources. (August 2016)">better source needed</span></a></i>]</sup> Eddie is of Mexican descent.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Latina_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Latina-5">[5]</a></sup> She has two older half-sisters through Dianna's first marriage to Patrick Martin Lovato (1960–2013), Dallas Lovato (born February 4, 1988)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Dallas_Birthday_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-Dallas_Birthday-6">[6]</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Demi_Lovato" title="Demi Lovato">Demi Lovato</a> (born August 20, 1992).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-DemiWashingtonPost_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-DemiWashingtonPost-7">[7]</a></sup> De La Garza has faced bullying due to her weight and received threats online.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-DemiTaughtMaddie_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-DemiTaughtMaddie-8">[8]</a></sup> In addition to Juanita Solis on <i><a href="/wiki/Desperate_Housewives" title="Desperate Housewives">Desperate Housewives</a></i>, she played a younger version of her sister Demi's character Sonny Munroe in an episode of <i><a href="/wiki/Sonny_with_a_Chance" title="Sonny with a Chance">Sonny with a Chance</a></i>. She also skipped a grade at her school and has a current YouTube channel.</p>
