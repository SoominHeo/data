<p><b>Matt Bush</b> (born March 22, 1986) is an American actor, best known for the film <i><a href="/wiki/Adventureland_(film)" title="Adventureland (film)">Adventureland</a></i> and his <a href="/wiki/AT%26T" title="AT&amp;T">AT&amp;T</a> Rollover Minutes commercials. He starred in the TBS comedy <i><a href="/wiki/Glory_Daze_(TV_series)" title="Glory Daze (TV series)">Glory Daze</a></i> as Eli, a freshman who rushes the wildest fraternity on a 1980s college campus.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sbt_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-sbt-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Bush was born in <a href="/wiki/Pennsylvania" title="Pennsylvania">Pennsylvania</a> and raised in <a href="/wiki/Cherry_Hill,_New_Jersey" title="Cherry Hill, New Jersey">Cherry Hill, New Jersey</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sbt_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-sbt-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-yae_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-yae-2">[2]</a></sup> He is of Italian descent.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ortem_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-ortem-3">[3]</a></sup> His parents, Linda and Dennis Bush, run a magic act.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tsorei_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-tsorei-4">[4]</a></sup> Bush attended Beck Middle School, <a href="/wiki/Cherry_Hill_High_School_East" title="Cherry Hill High School East">Cherry Hill High School East</a>, and spent several semesters at <a href="/wiki/Rowan_University" title="Rowan University">Rowan University</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tsorei_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-tsorei-4">[4]</a></sup> He voiced the character of Pete "Petey" Kowalski in the 2005 videogame <i><a href="/wiki/Bully_(video_game)" title="Bully (video game)">Bully</a></i> (known as <i>Canis Canem Edit</i> outside of North America).</p>
<p>He was also seen in a number of AT&amp;T commercials as a member of a family arguing over the expiration of rollover minutes.</p>
