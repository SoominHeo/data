<p><i><b>Harukanaru Toki no Naka de 2</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">遙かなる時空の中で2</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>In a Distant Time 2</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is an <a href="/wiki/Otome_game" title="Otome game">otome</a> <a href="/wiki/Adventure" title="Adventure">adventure</a> game developed by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ruby_Party" title="Ruby Party">Ruby Party</a> and published by <a href="/wiki/Koei" title="Koei">Koei</a>. It was originally released for <a href="/wiki/Personal_computer" title="Personal computer">PC</a>, and has since been ported to <a href="/wiki/PlayStation_2" title="PlayStation 2">PlayStation 2</a> and <a href="/wiki/PlayStation_Portable" title="PlayStation Portable">PlayStation Portable</a>. <i>Harukanaru Toki no Naka de 2</i> is the second installment of the <i><a href="/wiki/Haruka:_Beyond_the_Stream_of_Time" title="Haruka: Beyond the Stream of Time">Haruka</a></i> series, and it is a part of Ruby Party's Neoromance label.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Story">Story</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Harukanaru_Toki_no_Naka_de_2&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Story">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Harukanaru Toki no Naka de 2</i> takes place 100 years after the prior game, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Harukanaru_Toki_no_Naka_de" title="Harukanaru Toki no Naka de">Harukanaru Toki no Naka de</a></i>. Kyō, which resembles <a href="/wiki/Kyoto" title="Kyoto">Kyoto</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Heian_period" title="Heian period">Heian period</a>, is in turmoil because of a power struggle going on between the <a href="/wiki/Emperor_of_Japan" title="Emperor of Japan">emperor</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Cloistered_rule" title="Cloistered rule">retired emperor</a>, and the people of Kyō are falling into desperation, fearing the coming of the end of the world. Karin, a modern-day high school girl, is summoned to be the Ryūjin no Miko <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">龍神の神子</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Priestess of the Dragon God</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> who will save Kyō. However, there is already a Ryūjin no Miko in Kyō who has been recognized by the retired emperor, and the eight men chosen as Karin's Hachiyō <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">八葉</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> do not believe that Karin is really the Miko.</p>

</li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dd>Seiryū of Heaven <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">天の青龍</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>. Yoritada is a swordsman in the retired emperor's army, the Hokumen no Bushi <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">北面の武士</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>. Having decided that he need only obey the commands of his master, Yoritada avoids acting on his own will.</dd>
