<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Melissa_Baker&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Melissa Baker was born in <a href="/wiki/Akron,_Ohio" title="Akron, Ohio">Akron, Ohio</a>, and grew up in nearby <a href="/wiki/Cuyahoga_Falls,_Ohio" title="Cuyahoga Falls, Ohio">Cuyahoga Falls</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> graduating from <a href="/wiki/Stow-Munroe_Falls_High_School" title="Stow-Munroe Falls High School">Stow-Munroe Falls High School</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> She had been a competitive <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gymnast" title="Gymnast">gymnast</a> in her youth.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Her family, which is at least partly <a href="/wiki/Greek_language" title="Greek language">Greek</a> in origin, still resides in Ohio. Her mother's name is Becky,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> and she has a twin brother, Brandon.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (January 2009)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Melissa_Baker&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Baker is considered curvaceous.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> She is represented by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Click_Model_Agency&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Click Model Agency (page does not exist)">Click Model Agency</a> in New York and Los Angeles, and by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Women%27s_Model_Management&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Women's Model Management (page does not exist)">Women's Model Management</a> in Milan and Paris.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MBFMD_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-MBFMD-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>She has modelled for <a href="/wiki/Abercrombie_%26_Fitch" title="Abercrombie &amp; Fitch">Abercrombie &amp; Fitch</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Express_(store)" title="Express (store)">Express</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/McDonalds" title="McDonalds">McDonalds</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sisley" title="Sisley">Sisley</a> and appeared on the cover of <i>Bridal Magazine</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MBFMD_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-MBFMD-7">[7]</a></sup> Baker has been photographed by <a href="/wiki/Bruce_Weber_(photographer)" title="Bruce Weber (photographer)">Bruce Weber</a>, <a href="/wiki/Terry_Richardson" title="Terry Richardson">Terry Richardson</a>, Sylvia Krivickova, Richard Phibbs, <a href="/wiki/Raphael_Mazzucco" title="Raphael Mazzucco">Raphael Mazzucco</a>, Antoine Vergas, <a href="/wiki/Miko_Lim" title="Miko Lim">Miko Lim</a> and many more. She has appeared in <i><a href="/wiki/V_(American_magazine)" title="V (American magazine)">V</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Maxim_(magazine)" title="Maxim (magazine)">Maxim</a></i>. She walked for the <a href="/wiki/Malan_Breton" title="Malan Breton">Malan Breton</a> Spring 2009 Runway collection during <a href="/wiki/New_York_Fashion_Week" title="New York Fashion Week">New York Fashion Week</a>.</p>
