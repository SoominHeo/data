<p><b>2072 (<a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MMLXXII</a>)</b> will be a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap year</a> <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Friday" title="Leap year starting on Friday">starting on Friday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> CB) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>, the 2072nd year of the <a href="/wiki/Common_Era" title="Common Era">Common Era</a> (CE) and <i><a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a></i> (AD) designations, the 72nd year of the <a href="/wiki/3rd_millennium" title="3rd millennium">3rd millennium</a>, the 72nd year of the <a href="/wiki/21st_century" title="21st century">21st century</a>, and the 3rd year of the <a href="/wiki/2070s" title="2070s">2070s</a> decade.</p>

This is determined when title character Jenny Wakeman, or XJ-9, reveals she was activated five years previously, prompting her friend Brad Carbunkle to ask if that meant she did not remember <a href="/wiki/Super_Bowl" title="Super Bowl">Super Bowl</a> C (100), which would have taken place in 2067, so if Jenny was activated that year, and the show takes place five years after, it would be set in that year.</li>
