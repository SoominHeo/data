<p><b>Mercedes Rodríguez Lambre</b> (<small>Spanish pronunciation: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Spanish" title="Help:IPA for Spanish">[merˈseðes ˈlambɾe]</a></span>; born October 5, 1992) is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Argentine" title="Argentine">Argentine</a> actress, singer, dancer and model. She is known to international audiences for playing the debut role of Ludmilla in the <a href="/wiki/Disney_Channel" title="Disney Channel">Disney Channel</a> original series <i><a href="/wiki/Violetta_(telenovela)" title="Violetta (telenovela)">Violetta</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cine3_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-cine3-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mercedes_Lambre&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mercedes Lambre was born on October 5, 1992 in <a href="/wiki/La_Plata" title="La Plata">La Plata</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Province_of_Buenos_Aires" title="Province of Buenos Aires">Province of Buenos Aires</a>, <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentina</a>, where she trained in acting, singing and dancing. She studied theater with Lito Cruz, Gaston Marioni, Augusto Britez, and Alejandro Orduna and is currently studying with Monica Bruni. She also studied voice for four years with Professor Gabriel Giangrante CEFOA Academy in La Plata.</p>
<p>Regarding her training in dance, she majored in jazz dance, street dance and Spanish dances. She studied jazz dance with Juan Mallach for three years and with Gustavo Carrizo; street dance with Daniela Perez for two years in CICLUS and Spanish dances with Analia Flebes Sanchez for three years.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>She began her career as a model for pay TV signal <a class="extiw" href="https://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/Util%C3%ADsima_Satelital" title="es:Utilísima Satelital">Utilísima</a> being Violetta her first appearance in a fiction for television,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> where she plays Ludmila,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> the cool and glamorous girl of "Studio 21" which makes her the ideal girlfriend for "Leon" (<a href="/wiki/Jorge_Blanco_(actor)" title="Jorge Blanco (actor)">Jorge Blanco</a>). She starred in the second <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Violetta_(TV_series)" title="Violetta (TV series)"><i>Violetta</i></a>'s music video, "Juntos somos más", which premiered on May 1, 2012 on Disney Channel.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (June 2012)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
