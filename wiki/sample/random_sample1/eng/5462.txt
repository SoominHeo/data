<p><b>Mercedes Mason</b> (<span class="nowrap"><span class="IPA nopopups"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_English" title="Help:IPA for English">/<span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="'m' in 'my'">m</span><span title="/eɪ/ long 'a' in 'base'">eɪ</span><span title="'s' in 'sigh'">s</span><span title="/ɒ/ short 'o' in 'body'">ɒ</span><span title="'n' in 'no'">n</span></span>/</a></span></span>; born <b>Masöhn</b>), is a Swedish actress known for playing the role of <a href="/wiki/List_of_minor_Chuck_characters#Zondra_Rizzo" title="List of minor Chuck characters">Zondra</a> in the American television series <i><a href="/wiki/Chuck_(TV_series)" title="Chuck (TV series)">Chuck</a></i> and the role of Isabel Zambada<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> in the American <a href="/wiki/Procedural_drama" title="Procedural drama">procedural drama</a> <i><a href="/wiki/The_Finder_(U.S._TV_series)" title="The Finder (U.S. TV series)">The Finder</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-The_Finder_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-The_Finder-5">[5]</a></sup> She starred in the 2011 American <a href="/wiki/Horror_film" title="Horror film">horror film</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Quarantine_2:_Terminal" title="Quarantine 2: Terminal">Quarantine 2: Terminal</a></i>. She played Louise Leonard in the 2012–13 American supernatural drama <i><a href="/wiki/666_Park_Avenue" title="666 Park Avenue">666 Park Avenue</a></i> and Talia Del Campo in <i><a href="/wiki/NCIS:_Los_Angeles" title="NCIS: Los Angeles">NCIS: Los Angeles</a></i>. She is currently a regular on <a href="/wiki/AMC_(TV_channel)" title="AMC (TV channel)">AMC</a>'s television series <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fear_The_Walking_Dead" title="Fear The Walking Dead">Fear The Walking Dead</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mercedes_Masohn&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mason was one of two lead actresses in the <i><a href="/wiki/The_Finder_(U.S._TV_series)" title="The Finder (U.S. TV series)">The Finder</a></i> during its lone season in 2012. She played <a href="/wiki/United_States_Marshals_Service" title="United States Marshals Service">Deputy U.S. Marshal</a> Isabel Zambada, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Romantic_interest" title="Romantic interest">romantic interest</a> of <a href="/wiki/Title_role" title="Title role">title character</a> Walter Sherman (<a href="/wiki/Geoff_Stults" title="Geoff Stults">Geoff Stults</a>). Both she and <a href="/wiki/Maddie_Hasson" title="Maddie Hasson">Maddie Hasson</a> filled the void left by the departure of <a href="/wiki/Saffron_Burrows" title="Saffron Burrows">Saffron Burrows</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> the female lead in the series' <a href="/wiki/Television_pilot" title="Television pilot">backdoor pilot</a> which was the 19th installment of Season 6 of <i><a href="/wiki/Bones_(TV_series)" title="Bones (TV series)">Bones</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2012 she co-starred on the ABC supernatural drama series <i><a href="/wiki/666_Park_Avenue" title="666 Park Avenue">666 Park Avenue</a></i>, which was canceled midway through its season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BuckleyMasohn_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-BuckleyMasohn-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MasohnBuckley_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-MasohnBuckley-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<p>In August 2015, Mason debuted in her role as Ofelia Salazar, on the <i><a href="/wiki/The_Walking_Dead_(TV_series)" title="The Walking Dead (TV series)">Walking Dead</a></i> companion series, <i><a href="/wiki/Fear_the_Walking_Dead" title="Fear the Walking Dead">Fear the Walking Dead</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p>In Spring 2014, she debuted her role of DEA Special Agent Talia Del Campo on the <a href="/wiki/CBS" title="CBS">CBS</a> series <a href="/wiki/NCIS:_Los_Angeles" title="NCIS: Los Angeles">NCIS: Los Angeles</a>, which has since become a recurring role.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mercedes_Masohn&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mason was born in <a href="/wiki/Link%C3%B6ping" title="Linköping">Linköping</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Sweden</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-jk42616_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-jk42616-11">[11]</a></sup> and grew up in the neighborhood of <a href="/wiki/Ryd,_Link%C3%B6ping" title="Ryd, Linköping">Ryd, Linköping</a>. She moved to the United States with her family at the age of 12.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> She was raised around the Chicago area. She currently lives in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>, <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a> with her husband <a href="/wiki/David_Denman" title="David Denman">David Denman</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup></p>
<p>Mason has applied for U.S. citizenship. She took the U.S. <a href="/wiki/Citizenship_test" title="Citizenship test">citizenship test</a> in April 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-jk42616_11-1"><a href="#cite_note-jk42616-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
<p>In addition to English, Mason speaks Swedish, French, Spanish, and Persian.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup> However, on the Swedish talk show "<a href="/wiki/Skavlan" title="Skavlan">Skavlan</a>" on september 30 2016, she declared that she no longer speaks Swedish, but she does still understand it.</p>
