<p><b>Marysville</b> is a city in and the <a href="/wiki/County_seat" title="County seat">county seat</a> of <a href="/wiki/Marshall_County,_Kansas" title="Marshall County, Kansas">Marshall County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kansas" title="Kansas">Kansas</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR6_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-GR6-6">[6]</a></sup> As of the <a href="/wiki/2010_United_States_Census" title="2010 United States Census">2010 census</a>, the city population was 3,294.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census2010_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-Census2010-7">[7]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Marysville,_Kansas&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Pony_Express_Marysville_KS.JPG"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="2304" data-file-width="3841" height="132" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ee/Pony_Express_Marysville_KS.JPG/220px-Pony_Express_Marysville_KS.JPG" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ee/Pony_Express_Marysville_KS.JPG/330px-Pony_Express_Marysville_KS.JPG 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ee/Pony_Express_Marysville_KS.JPG/440px-Pony_Express_Marysville_KS.JPG 2x" width="220"/></a>
<div class="thumbcaption">
<div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Pony_Express_Marysville_KS.JPG" title="Enlarge"></a></div>
Pony Express Station, 2010</div>
</div>
</div>
<p>Marysville was laid out in 1855 by Francis J. Marshall, and designated in that same year the county seat. It was incorporated as a city in 1861.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>Marysville was located on the <a href="/wiki/Oregon_Trail" title="Oregon Trail">Oregon Trail</a> and the route of the <a href="/wiki/Pony_Express" title="Pony Express">Pony Express</a>, the St. Joe Road, the Overland Stage, The Military Road, and the Otoe-Missouria Trail. British explorer <a href="/wiki/Richard_Francis_Burton" title="Richard Francis Burton">Richard Francis Burton</a> en route to California in 1860 noted: "Passing by Marysville, in old maps Palmetto City, a country-town which thrives by selling whiskey to ruffians of all descriptions ..."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> The old Pony Express Station still stands in downtown Marysville.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p>Marysville owes much of its prosperity to the <a href="/wiki/Union_Pacific_Railroad" title="Union Pacific Railroad">Union Pacific Railroad</a>. The railroad has been a major employer in the town for nearly 100 years. Over 60 trains pass through the town daily and the railroad recently completed an underpass which redirects the tracks below <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/US-36" title="US-36">US-36</a>, rather than crossing the highway and backing up traffic.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (March 2011)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<p>Marysville is also known as the "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Black_Squirrel" title="Black Squirrel">Black Squirrel</a> City" due to an isolated community of all-black squirrels that make their homes in the town. The squirrels are said to be the result of escapees from a traveling circus.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-skyways_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-skyways-11">[11]</a></sup></p>

<p>Marysville is located at <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Marysville%2C_Kansas&amp;params=39_50_41_N_96_38_33_W_type:city"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">39°50′41″N</span> <span class="longitude">96°38′33″W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">39.84472°N 96.64250°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">39.84472; -96.64250</span></span></span></a></span> (39.844624, -96.642505).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR1_12-0"><a href="#cite_note-GR1-12">[12]</a></sup> According to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Census_Bureau" title="United States Census Bureau">United States Census Bureau</a>, the city has a total area of 4.62 square miles (11.97 km<sup>2</sup>), of which 4.58 square miles (11.86 km<sup>2</sup>) is land and 0.04 square miles (0.10 km<sup>2</sup>) is water.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Gazetteer_files_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Gazetteer_files-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2010_census">2010 census</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Marysville,_Kansas&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2010 census">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As of the <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FactFinder_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-FactFinder-2">[2]</a></sup> of 2010, there were 3,294 people, 1,468 households, and 859 families residing in the city. The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 719.2 inhabitants per square mile (277.7/km<sup>2</sup>). There were 1,646 housing units at an average density of 359.4 per square mile (138.8/km<sup>2</sup>). The racial makeup of the city was 96.7% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">White</a>, 0.3% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/African_American_(U.S._Census)" title="African American (U.S. Census)">African American</a>, 0.2% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Native_American_(U.S._Census)" title="Native American (U.S. Census)">Native American</a>, 0.6% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Asian_(U.S._Census)" title="Asian (U.S. Census)">Asian</a>, 0.8% from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Race_(U.S._Census)" title="Race (U.S. Census)">other races</a>, and 1.4% from two or more races. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)">Hispanic</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)">Latino</a> of any race were 2.6% of the population.</p>
<p>There were 1,468 households of which 27.2% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 46.1% were <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married couples</a> living together, 8.4% had a female householder with no husband present, 4.0% had a male householder with no wife present, and 41.5% were non-families. 38.1% of all households were made up of individuals and 18.1% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.20 and the average family size was 2.89.</p>
<p>The median age in the city was 41.8 years. 24% of residents were under the age of 18; 7% were between the ages of 18 and 24; 22.2% were from 25 to 44; 26.4% were from 45 to 64; and 20.5% were 65 years of age or older. The gender makeup of the city was 47.9% male and 52.1% female.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2000_census">2000 census</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Marysville,_Kansas&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 2000 census">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As of the <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR2_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-GR2-4">[4]</a></sup> of 2000, there were 3,271 people, 1,437 households, and 865 families residing in the city. The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 1,004.2 people per square mile (387.4/km²). There were 1,614 housing units at an average density of 495.5 per square mile (191.2/km²). The racial makeup of the city was 98.04% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">White</a>, 0.15% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/African_American_(U.S._Census)" title="African American (U.S. Census)">African American</a>, 0.34% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Native_American_(U.S._Census)" title="Native American (U.S. Census)">Native American</a>, 0.49% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Asian_(U.S._Census)" title="Asian (U.S. Census)">Asian</a>, 0.15% from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Race_(United_States_Census)" title="Race (United States Census)">other races</a>, and 0.83% from two or more races. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)">Hispanic</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)">Latino</a> of any race were 0.73% of the population.</p>
<p>There were 1,437 households out of which 26.9% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 51.1% were <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married couples</a> living together, 6.5% had a female householder with no husband present, and 39.8% were non-families. 36.5% of all households were made up of individuals and 20.2% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.19 and the average family size was 2.87.</p>
<p>In the city the population was spread out with 22.7% under the age of 18, 7.1% from 18 to 24, 24.6% from 25 to 44, 22.2% from 45 to 64, and 23.4% who were 65 years of age or older. The median age was 42 years. For every 100 females there were 89.7 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 85.3 males.</p>
<p>The median income for a household in the city was $31,250, and the median income for a family was $40,427. Males had a median income of $28,065 versus $18,063 for females. The <a href="/wiki/Per_capita_income" title="Per capita income">per capita income</a> for the city was $19,196. About 5.7% of families and 8.5% of the population were below the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Poverty_line" title="Poverty line">poverty line</a>, including 8.5% of those under age 18 and 4.3% of those age 65 or over.</p>
