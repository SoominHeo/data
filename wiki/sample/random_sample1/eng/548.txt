<p><b>2079 (<a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MMLXXIX</a>)</b> will be a <a href="/wiki/Common_year" title="Common year">common year</a> <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Sunday" title="Common year starting on Sunday">starting on Sunday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> A) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>, the 2079th year of the <a href="/wiki/Common_Era" title="Common Era">Common Era</a> (CE) and <i><a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a></i> (AD) designations, the 79th year of the <a href="/wiki/3rd_millennium" title="3rd millennium">3rd millennium</a>, the 79th year of the <a href="/wiki/21st_century" title="21st century">21st century</a>, and the 10th and last year of the <a href="/wiki/2070s" title="2070s">2070s</a> decade.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Predicted_and_scheduled_events">Predicted and scheduled events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2079&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Predicted and scheduled events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>May 1 - A <a href="/wiki/Solar_eclipse_of_May_1,_2079" title="Solar eclipse of May 1, 2079">total solar eclipse</a> is predicted for <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a> in the United States, as well as <a href="/wiki/Nova_Scotia" title="Nova Scotia">Nova Scotia</a> in Canada.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li>June 6 - The smalldatetime fields in <a href="/wiki/SQL" title="SQL">SQL</a>-Server databases will wrap around to January 1, 1900.</li>
<li>August 11 - <a href="/wiki/Mercury_(planet)" title="Mercury (planet)">Mercury</a> occults <a href="/wiki/Mars" title="Mars">Mars</a> for the first time since 578. It is difficult to observe, but is the best observable occultation of a planet by another in the 21st century.</li>
</ul>
