<p><b>Messgram</b> (<span lang="ko">메스그램</span>) is a Korean <a href="/wiki/Hard_rock" title="Hard rock">hard rock</a> band based in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hongdae_area" title="Hongdae area">Hongdae</a>, Seoul. Formed in the winter of 2011, the group was founded by Soojin Lee, who is a drummer in the band, with a goal of creating high quality music in Korean <a href="/wiki/Indie_music_scene" title="Indie music scene">indie music scene</a>. They released their first EP, <i>This Is A Mess But It's Us</i>, on April 8 2014.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Messgram&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Formation_and_first_EP_.282011-present.29">Formation and first EP (2011-present)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Messgram&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Formation and first EP (2011-present)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The project of making ultimate sound was first started by Soojin Lee. After the disbandment of his former band, Move Your Eyes, he was seeking for members to create a new band. Yushik Shin, who was a former member of T.V. Animals, was having a period of rest after the disbandment of his band. Soojin and Yushik gathered and began carrying forward the project. Soojin convinced Jahnny Shin, a former member of Move Your Eyes, to join the project and Yushik convinced Seth Kang, a former member of T.V. Animals, to join. Along with YK joining the project, they formed a band named Messgram in the winter of 2011. They explained that the mess in the band name means a disordered condition and the gram means a unit that gathers the mess, in other words, people with a different style and taste gathered and formed a band, a unity that organizes a mess that each individual has created. <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Musistance_Artist_Interview_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Musistance_Artist_Interview-1">[1]</a></sup> After the three years of preparation, the band released their debut EP, <i>This Is A Mess But It's Us</i>, on April 8 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> In early November 2014, they drew public's attention by making a remake of <a href="/wiki/Seo_Taiji" title="Seo Taiji">Seo Taiji</a>'s single <i>Soyeokdong</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Messgram_Seotaiji_Remake_News_Article_1_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Messgram_Seotaiji_Remake_News_Article_1-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Messgram_Seotaiji_Remake_News_Article_2_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Messgram_Seotaiji_Remake_News_Article_2-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Musical_style">Musical style</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Messgram&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Musical style">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Messgram's music have adapted to a wide variation of musical types such as early 90's <a href="/wiki/Post-grunge" title="Post-grunge">post-grunge</a>, <a href="/wiki/Heavy_metal_music" title="Heavy metal music">metal</a>, <a href="/wiki/Trance" title="Trance">trance</a> and <a href="/wiki/Post-hardcore" title="Post-hardcore">post-hardcore</a> forging into a new genre.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Musical_Style_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-Musical_Style-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
