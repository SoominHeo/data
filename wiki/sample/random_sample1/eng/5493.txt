<p><b>Mayday</b> or <b>May Day</b> usually refers to:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Mayday" title="Mayday">Mayday</a>, a distress signal.</li>

<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/May_Day_(labor_commemoration)" title="May Day (labor commemoration)">May Day (labor commemoration)</a> or <a href="/wiki/International_Workers%27_Day" title="International Workers' Day">International Workers' Day</a>, an international labor celebration held on May 1.</li>
