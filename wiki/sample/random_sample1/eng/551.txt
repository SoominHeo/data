<p><b>2082 (<a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MMLXXXII</a>)</b> will be a <a href="/wiki/Common_year" title="Common year">common year</a> <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Thursday" title="Common year starting on Thursday">starting on Thursday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> D) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>, the 2082nd year of the <a href="/wiki/Common_Era" title="Common Era">Common Era</a> (CE) and <i><a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a></i> (AD) designations, the 82nd year of the <a href="/wiki/3rd_millennium" title="3rd millennium">3rd millennium</a>, the 82nd year of the <a href="/wiki/21st_century" title="21st century">21st century</a>, and the 3rd year of the <a href="/wiki/2080s" title="2080s">2080s</a> decade.</p>
