<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Mephisto Prize</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">
					Jump to:					<a href="#mw-head">navigation</a>, 					<a href="#p-search">search</a>
</div>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <b>Mephisto Prize</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">メフィスト賞</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Mefisuto Shō</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a Japanese <a href="/wiki/Literary_award" title="Literary award">literary award</a> for unpublished <a href="/wiki/Genre_fiction" title="Genre fiction">genre fiction</a> novels, mainly for <a href="/wiki/Mystery_fiction" title="Mystery fiction">mystery</a> novels. It was established in 1996 by the editors of <i>Mephisto</i> magazine and is awarded on an irregular basis. The winning work is published by <a href="/wiki/Kodansha" title="Kodansha">Kodansha</a> and the winner receives a statue of <a href="/wiki/Sherlock_Holmes" title="Sherlock Holmes">Sherlock Holmes</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Winners_whose_works_are_available_in_English">Winners whose works are available in English</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mephisto_Prize&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Winners whose works are available in English">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<dl>
<dt>Writers engaged in The BBB</dt>
</dl>
<p>The BBB is a publishing group in which some Mephisto Prize winners participate. The group was established in 2012 by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ryusui_Seiryoin" title="Ryusui Seiryoin">Ryusui Seiryoin</a> and other Mephisto Prize winners to translate their works into English and publish them as ebooks.</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hiroshi_Mori_(writer)" title="Hiroshi Mori (writer)">Hiroshi Mori</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ryusui_Seiryoin" title="Ryusui Seiryoin">Ryusui Seiryoin</a> (Chief Editing Officer of The BBB)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li>Kenichi Sobu</li>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Mephisto_.28magazine.29"><i>Mephisto</i> (magazine)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mephisto_Prize&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Mephisto (magazine)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i><b>Mephisto</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">メフィスト</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a Japanese literary magazine of <a href="/wiki/Genre_fiction" title="Genre fiction">genre fiction</a>, mainly of <a href="/wiki/Mystery_fiction" title="Mystery fiction">mystery fiction</a>. It has been published triannually by <a href="/wiki/Kodansha" title="Kodansha">Kodansha</a> since April 1994.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Koji_Suzuki" title="Koji Suzuki">Koji Suzuki</a>'s <i>Promenade of the Gods</i> was serialized in the magazine from 1995 to 2002. The first three stories of <a href="/wiki/Nisio_Isin" title="Nisio Isin">Nisio Isin</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Monogatari_(series)" title="Monogatari (series)">Monogatari</a></i> series were published in the magazine in 2005-2006.</p>
