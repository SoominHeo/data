<p><b>2084 (<a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MMLXXXIV</a>)</b> will be a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap year</a> <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Saturday" title="Leap year starting on Saturday">starting on Saturday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> BA) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>, the 2084th year of the <a href="/wiki/Common_Era" title="Common Era">Common Era</a> (CE) and <i><a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a></i> (AD) designations, the 84th year of the <a href="/wiki/3rd_millennium" title="3rd millennium">3rd millennium</a>, the 84th year of the <a href="/wiki/21st_century" title="21st century">21st century</a>, and the 5th year of the <a href="/wiki/2080s" title="2080s">2080s</a> decade.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Predicted_and_scheduled_events">Predicted and scheduled events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2084&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Predicted and scheduled events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>October 26 – Lease held by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pitjantjatjara_people" title="Pitjantjatjara people">Pitjantjatjara</a> on <a href="/wiki/Uluru" title="Uluru">Uluru</a> to <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australian</a> government set to expire.</li>
<li>November 10 – <a href="/wiki/Transit_of_Earth_from_Mars" title="Transit of Earth from Mars">Transit of Earth</a> as seen from <a href="/wiki/Mars" title="Mars">Mars</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Mars2084_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Mars2084-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="In_fiction">In fiction</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2084&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: In fiction">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>According to the storyline of the 1982 arcade video game <i><a href="/wiki/Robotron:_2084" title="Robotron: 2084">Robotron: 2084</a></i>, in 2084 man perfects the Robotrons, a robot species so advanced that man is inferior to his own creation.</li>
<li>In the X-COM Storyline, the Fourth Alien War (AW-IV) starts in 2084.</li>
<li>The video game <i><a href="/wiki/Remember_Me_(video_game)" title="Remember Me (video game)">Remember Me</a></i> is set in Paris 2084.</li>
<li>The events described by a fictional version of <a href="/wiki/Evel_Knievel" title="Evel Knievel">Evel Knievel</a> in the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Midnight_Brown&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Midnight Brown (page does not exist)">Midnight Brown</a> song <i>2084</i>. Including things like the TV series <i>Dog Monkey vs. Monkey Dog</i> and streets made of syrup.</li>
<li>In the 1985 <a href="/wiki/Soviet_Union" title="Soviet Union">Soviet</a> science fiction television <a href="/wiki/Miniseries" title="Miniseries">miniseries</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Guest_from_the_Future" title="Guest from the Future">Guest from the Future</a></i> a schoolboy travels from the year 1984 to the year 2084, there is a forecast of some technical and scientific achievements up to the year 2084.</li>
<li>The 1990 science fiction film <i><a href="/wiki/Total_Recall_(1990_film)" title="Total Recall (1990 film)">Total Recall</a></i> is set in 2084.</li>
<li>The <i><a href="/wiki/Doctor_Who" title="Doctor Who">Doctor Who</a></i> story <i><a href="/wiki/Warriors_of_the_Deep" title="Warriors of the Deep">Warriors of the Deep</a></i> takes place in 2084.</li>
<li>A war in <a href="/wiki/Ayreon" title="Ayreon">Ayreon</a>'s universe is set in 2084, said to be the "Sixth Extinction" by the "Forever" aliens (the "Fifth Extinction" was that of the dinosaurs 65 million years prior), causing the extinction of mankind save for the colony on Mars. Due to Mars' incapacity to support life, the Mars colony is effectively doomed, and after the death of its last inhabitant, the last human being, he is re-created as a new soul, the new Universal Migrator.</li>
<li><i>2084: Tomorrow is Today</i>, a futuristic novel by John William McMullen set in the year 2084 in which the world has spiraled from economic collapse, limited nuclear war, and global anarchy into a United World under the protection of The Program.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Diana_Wynne_Jones" title="Diana Wynne Jones">Diana Wynne Jones</a>' short story <i>No One</i> begins with a reference to the year being 2084</li>
<li>In episode 11.16 of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Simpsons" title="The Simpsons">The Simpsons</a></i>, Lisa receives a sticker saying to vote for a gay president in 2084, which, according to them, is a realistic goal.</li>
</ul>
