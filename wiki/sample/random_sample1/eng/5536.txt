<p><b>Mela Lee</b> is an American <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Voice_actress" title="Voice actress">voice actress</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Singer" title="Singer">singer</a> in the Los Angeles area. She first got into voice acting when she auditioned for <i><a href="/wiki/Vampire_Princess_Miyu" title="Vampire Princess Miyu">Vampire Princess Miyu</a></i>, but the director had her read for <i><a href="/wiki/Saint_Tail" title="Saint Tail">Saint Tail</a></i>, and she was cast as the title character Meimi Haneoka, whom assumes the secret identity of Saint Tail.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-capsule_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-capsule-1">[1]</a></sup> She would later land the vampire role of Yuki Cross in the <i><a href="/wiki/Vampire_Knight" title="Vampire Knight">Vampire Knight series</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/List_of_Fate/stay_night_characters#Rin_Tohsaka" title="List of Fate/stay night characters">Rin Tosaka</a>, the heroine of the <i><a href="/wiki/Fate/stay_night" title="Fate/stay night">Fate/stay night</a></i> series. Outside of voice acting, Lee has been involved in a band called Magnolia Memoir where she composes the music and lyrics.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-capsule_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-capsule-1">[1]</a></sup> She and fellow voice actress <a href="/wiki/Erica_Lindbeck" title="Erica Lindbeck">Erica Lindbeck</a> host their own web series called <i>Lindbeck and Lee</i> with local voice actor guests.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
