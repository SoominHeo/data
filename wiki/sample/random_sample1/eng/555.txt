<p><b>2096 (<a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MMXCVI</a>)</b> will be a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap year</a> <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Sunday" title="Leap year starting on Sunday">starting on Sunday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> AG) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>, the 2096th year of the <a href="/wiki/Common_Era" title="Common Era">Common Era</a> (CE) and <i><a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a></i> (AD) designations, the 96th year of the <a href="/wiki/3rd_millennium" title="3rd millennium">3rd millennium</a>, the 96th year of the <a href="/wiki/21st_century" title="21st century">21st century</a>, and the 7th year of the <a href="/wiki/2090s" title="2090s">2090s</a> decade.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Predicted_and_scheduled_events">Predicted and scheduled events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2096&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Predicted and scheduled events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>February 29 – First time that <a href="/wiki/Ash_Wednesday" title="Ash Wednesday">Ash Wednesday</a> falls on February 29.</li>
<li>2096 is the last leap year before <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2100" title="2100">2100</a>, which will not be a leap year.</li>
<li>The dwarf planet <a href="/wiki/2015_RR245" title="2015 RR245">2015 RR245</a> will make its closest approach to the Sun.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li>Events in the <i><a href="/wiki/SimCity_(1989_video_game)" title="SimCity (1989 video game)">SimCity</a></i> video game <a href="/wiki/Las_Vegas_Valley" title="Las Vegas Valley">Las Vegas</a> scenario where extraterrestrial attack the <a href="/wiki/Town" title="Town">town</a> take place in this year.</li>
<li>The character Cy-5 from the video game, <i><a href="/wiki/War_Gods_(video_game)" title="War Gods (video game)">War Gods</a></i>, comes from this year.</li>
