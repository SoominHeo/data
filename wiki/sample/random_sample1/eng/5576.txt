<p><b>Estadio José Dellagiovanna</b> (widely known as <i>Coloso de Victoria</i>) is a multi-use <a href="/wiki/Stadium" title="Stadium">stadium</a> in <a href="/wiki/Victoria,_Buenos_Aires" title="Victoria, Buenos Aires">Victoria</a>, <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentina</a>. It is currently used mostly for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> matches. The stadium holds 26,282 people and was built in 1936.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>It is used as the home of <a href="/wiki/Club_Atl%C3%A9tico_Tigre" title="Club Atlético Tigre">Club Atlético Tigre</a>.</p>
<p>In 2006, work was finished to convert the remaining wood stands to cement.</p>
