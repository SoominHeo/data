<p><b>Monique Ganderton</b> (born August 6, 1980) is a Canadian stunt woman and actress who works in television and film.</p>
<p>Ganderton was born in <a href="/wiki/Edmonton" title="Edmonton">Edmonton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alberta" title="Alberta">Alberta</a>. She started out in modeling before moving to stunt work. She has doubled <a href="/wiki/Tricia_Helfer" title="Tricia Helfer">Tricia Helfer</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rachel_Nichols_(actress)" title="Rachel Nichols (actress)">Rachel Nichols</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Leelee_Sobieski" title="Leelee Sobieski">Leelee Sobieski</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bridget_Moynahan" title="Bridget Moynahan">Bridget Moynahan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Daryl_Hannah" title="Daryl Hannah">Daryl Hannah</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Rebecca_Romijn" title="Rebecca Romijn">Rebecca Romijn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Famke_Janssen" title="Famke Janssen">Famke Janssen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kristanna_Loken" title="Kristanna Loken">Kristanna Loken</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> In 2009, she was cast as Alia, a recurring role in Season 9 of <i><a href="/wiki/Smallville" title="Smallville">Smallville</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> She stund double : <a href="/wiki/Charlize_Theron" title="Charlize Theron">Charlize Theron</a>, <a href="/wiki/Stana_Katic" title="Stana Katic">Stana Katic</a> , <a href="/wiki/Cassidy_Freeman" title="Cassidy Freeman">Cassidy Freeman</a> , <a href="/wiki/Sigourney_Weaver" title="Sigourney Weaver">Sigourney Weaver</a>, and <a class="external text" href="http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1244650/?ref_=ttfc_fc_cl_t12" rel="nofollow">more</a></p>
