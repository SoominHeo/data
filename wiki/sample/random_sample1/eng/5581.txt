<p><i><b>Early Single Box</b></i> is the first <a href="/wiki/Box_set" title="Box set">box set</a> compilation from <a href="/wiki/J-pop" title="J-pop">J-pop</a> idol group <a href="/wiki/Morning_Musume" title="Morning Musume">Morning Musume</a>, and was released December 15, 2004. It contains their first eight singles in their entirety, each on a separate disc with an additional bonus track each. It also includes a ninth disc with fifteen <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Instrumentals" title="Instrumentals">instrumentals</a> of some songs from their first three albums.</p>
<p>Although the original versions of the first six singles were pressed on 8 cm CDs, all of the ones included in the Early Single Box were 12 cm CDs.</p>
<p>All eight of the new versions of the singles were later reissued separately. The instrumental album is still exclusive to the box set.</p>
