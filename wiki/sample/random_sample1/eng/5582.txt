<p>"<b>Morning Coffee</b>" <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">モーニングコーヒー</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a song by <a href="/wiki/J-pop" title="J-pop">J-pop</a> idol group <a href="/wiki/Morning_Musume" title="Morning Musume">Morning Musume</a>, released as their first official single on January 28, 1998 as an 8 cm <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/CD" title="CD">CD</a>. It sold a total of 200,790 copies. In 2004, it was re-released as part of the <i><a href="/wiki/Early_Single_Box" title="Early Single Box">Early Single Box</a></i> and again in 2005 as a 12 cm CD. The lead vocalist of the single was <a href="/wiki/Natsumi_Abe" title="Natsumi Abe">Natsumi Abe</a>.</p>
<p>Early on, <a href="/wiki/Tsunku" title="Tsunku">Tsunku</a> had decided that <a href="/wiki/Kaori_Iida" title="Kaori Iida">Kaori Iida</a> was going to be the lead vocalist. Before the recording on the second day, Tsunku changed his mind and told the group that he was going to change the lead vocals to Abe instead.</p>
<p>A re-arranged and re-recorded version of this song was featured as the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/B-side" title="B-side">B-side</a> for Morning Musume's 14th single, "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Souda!_We%27re_Alive" title="Souda! We're Alive">Souda! We're Alive</a>".</p>
<p>It is the group's only major-label single to exclusively feature the original members, as the second generation would join before the release of "<a href="/wiki/Summer_Night_Town" title="Summer Night Town">Summer Night Town</a>". It is also their first and only single to be released by the defunct <a href="/wiki/Up-Front_Group" title="Up-Front Group">Up-Front Works</a> label <a href="/wiki/Zetima" title="Zetima">One Up Music</a> and distributed through <a href="/wiki/Warner_Music_Group" title="Warner Music Group">Warner Music Japan</a>, as One Up Music would end its distribution deal with Warner and change its name to <b>Zetima</b> before the release of the group's next single. Distribution rights for Zetima would be given to <a href="/wiki/Sony_Music_Entertainment_Japan" title="Sony Music Entertainment Japan">Sony Music Japan</a>'s sub label <a href="/wiki/Epic_Records" title="Epic Records">Epic Records</a> for "<a href="/wiki/Summer_Night_Town" title="Summer Night Town">Summer Night Town</a>", Morning Musume's first album "<a href="/wiki/First_Time_(Morning_Musume_album)" title="First Time (Morning Musume album)">First Time</a>" and all of Zetima's subsequent releases.</p>
