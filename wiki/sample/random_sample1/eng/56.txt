<p><br/>
Year <b>1227</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MCCXXVII</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Friday" title="Common year starting on Friday">common year starting on Friday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1227&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>January
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Livonian_Crusade" title="Livonian Crusade">Livonian Crusade</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Livonian_Brothers_of_the_Sword" title="Livonian Brothers of the Sword">Livonian Brothers of the Sword</a> and their crusader allies cross the sea ice from mainland <a href="/wiki/Estonia" title="Estonia">Estonia</a> and defeat the last Estonian strongholds on <a href="/wiki/Muhu" title="Muhu">Muhu</a> and <a href="/wiki/Saaremaa" title="Saaremaa">Saaremaa</a> islands. This marks the end of the Estonian campaign in the Livonian Crusade. The Sword Brothers conquer <a href="/wiki/Danish_Estonia" title="Danish Estonia">Danish Estonia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tallinn" title="Tallinn">Tallinn</a> (<i>Reval</i>) is given town rights under <a href="/wiki/Riga" title="Riga">Riga</a> law.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Henry_III_of_England" title="Henry III of England">Henry III of England</a> declares himself of age and assumes power.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-CBH_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-CBH-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li>(approximate date) <a href="/wiki/Swedish%E2%80%93Novgorodian_Wars" title="Swedish–Novgorodian Wars">Swedish–Novgorodian Wars</a>: Grand Prince <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yaroslav_II_of_Russia" title="Yaroslav II of Russia">Yaroslav II</a> of <a href="/wiki/Vladimir,_Russia" title="Vladimir, Russia">Vladimir</a> leads an attack from the <a href="/wiki/Novgorod_Republic" title="Novgorod Republic">Novgorod Republic</a> on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Finnic_peoples" title="Finnic peoples">Finnic peoples</a> in eastern <a href="/wiki/Fennoscandia" title="Fennoscandia">Fennoscandia</a> called "Yem", whom he devastates.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_11" title="January 11">January 11</a> – The city of <a href="/wiki/Po%C5%BEega,_Croatia" title="Požega, Croatia">Požega</a> is first mentioned in a charter of <a href="/wiki/Andrew_II_of_Hungary" title="Andrew II of Hungary">Andrew II of Hungary</a>.</li>
<li>March – <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a> makes a truce with <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_France" title="Kingdom of France">France</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-CBH_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-CBH-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_19" title="March 19">March 19</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Gregory_IX" title="Pope Gregory IX">Pope Gregory IX</a> succeeds <a href="/wiki/Pope_Honorius_III" title="Pope Honorius III">Pope Honorius III</a> as the 178th <a href="/wiki/Pope" title="Pope">pope</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_24" title="November 24">November 24</a> – Prince <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Leszek_I_the_White" title="Leszek I the White">Leszek I the White</a>, <a href="/wiki/List_of_Polish_monarchs" title="List of Polish monarchs">High Duke of Poland</a>, is assassinated at an assembly of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Piast" title="Piast">Piast</a> dukes at <a href="/wiki/G%C4%85sawa" title="Gąsawa">Gąsawa</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/D%C5%8Dgen" title="Dōgen">Dōgen</a> receives <a href="/wiki/Dharma_transmission" title="Dharma transmission">Dharma transmission</a> and <i><a href="/wiki/Dharma_transmission" title="Dharma transmission">inka</a></i> from his master <a href="/wiki/Rujing" title="Rujing">Rujing</a> in China, settling his "life's quest of the great matter",<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> going on to introduce <a href="/wiki/S%C5%8Dt%C5%8D" title="Sōtō">Sōtō</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Zen_Buddhism" title="Zen Buddhism">Zen Buddhism</a> into his native <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</li>
