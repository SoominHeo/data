<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Morgane_Dubled&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Dubled was first scouted at the age of 16 by top scout G. Simon Chafik for <a href="/wiki/Elite_Model_Management" title="Elite Model Management">Elite Model Management</a> in her hometown of Nice, France, in 2001. After getting her <a href="/wiki/Baccalaur%C3%A9at" title="Baccalauréat">Baccalauréat</a> (scientific section), she attended the medical school Faculté de Médecine de Nice,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-UFR_Medecine_Nice_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-UFR_Medecine_Nice-2">[2]</a></sup> but after one year changed direction and moved to Paris for a year in <a href="/wiki/Kh%C3%A2gne" title="Khâgne">Hypokhâgne</a>, preparing the <a href="/wiki/%C3%89cole_Normale_Sup%C3%A9rieure" title="École Normale Supérieure">École Normale Supérieure</a>. She started modeling as a part-time job to pay for her studies, but soon her interest in the modeling world rose and she decided to do it full-time.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Vogue.fr_Profile_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Vogue.fr_Profile-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Modeling">Modeling</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Morgane_Dubled&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Modeling">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Since her first catwalk season in 2004, she has become a popular runway model, walking for the likes of <a href="/wiki/Armani" title="Armani">Armani</a>, <a href="/wiki/Calvin_Klein" title="Calvin Klein">Calvin Klein</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chanel" title="Chanel">Chanel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Christian_Lacroix" title="Christian Lacroix">Christian Lacroix</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dolce_%26_Gabbana" title="Dolce &amp; Gabbana">Dolce &amp; Gabbana</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dior" title="Dior">Dior</a>, <a href="/wiki/Elie_Saab" title="Elie Saab">Elie Saab</a>, <a href="/wiki/Emanuel_Ungaro" title="Emanuel Ungaro">Emanuel Ungaro</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fendi" title="Fendi">Fendi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gucci" title="Gucci">Gucci</a>, <a href="/wiki/Herm%C3%A8s" title="Hermès">Hermès</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jean-Paul_Gaultier" title="Jean-Paul Gaultier">Jean-Paul Gaultier</a>, <a href="/wiki/John_Galliano" title="John Galliano">John Galliano</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lacoste" title="Lacoste">Lacoste</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lanvin_(clothing)" title="Lanvin (clothing)">Lanvin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kenzo_(brand)" title="Kenzo (brand)">Kenzo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marc_Jacobs" title="Marc Jacobs">Marc Jacobs</a>, Marc by Marc Jacobs, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Kors" title="Michael Kors">Michael Kors</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nina_Ricci_(brand)" title="Nina Ricci (brand)">Nina Ricci</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ralph_Lauren" title="Ralph Lauren">Ralph Lauren</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sonia_Rykiel" title="Sonia Rykiel">Sonia Rykiel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Stella_McCartney" title="Stella McCartney">Stella McCartney</a>, <a href="/wiki/Versace" title="Versace">Versace</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Viktor_%26_Rolf" title="Viktor &amp; Rolf">Viktor &amp; Rolf</a>.</p>
<p>She has done editorial work with notable photographers like <a href="/wiki/Terry_Richardson" title="Terry Richardson">Terry Richardson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Craig_McDean" title="Craig McDean">Craig McDean</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mark_Abrahams" title="Mark Abrahams">Mark Abrahams</a>, John Akehurst, Eric Maillet, <a href="/wiki/David_Sims_(photographer)" title="David Sims (photographer)">David Sims</a>. She also graced the cover of Russian <i><a href="/wiki/Vogue_(magazine)" title="Vogue (magazine)">Vogue</a></i> and has done campaign work for Dior, Dolce &amp; Gabbana, just Cavalli, Jean-Paul Gaultier, and <a href="/wiki/Rochas" title="Rochas">Rochas</a> (Fragrance ad for "Soleil de Rochas", directed by <a href="/wiki/Bruno_Aveillan" title="Bruno Aveillan">Bruno Aveillan</a>).</p>
<p>She participated in the 2005, 2006, 2007, and 2008 <a href="/wiki/Victoria%27s_Secret" title="Victoria's Secret">Victoria's Secret</a> Fashion Shows.</p>
<p>Since 2009, she has walked only for Paris Fashion Week (and Couture).</p>
