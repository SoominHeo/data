<p><b>Takaji Mori</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">森 孝慈</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Mori Takaji</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, November 24, 1943 – July 17, 2011)</span> was a <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> player and manager. He played for <a href="/wiki/Japan_national_football_team" title="Japan national football team">Japan national team</a> at the <a href="/wiki/1968_Summer_Olympics" title="1968 Summer Olympics">1968 Summer Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
