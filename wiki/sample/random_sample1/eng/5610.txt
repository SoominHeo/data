<p>The <b>coat of arms of <a href="/wiki/Mauritius" title="Mauritius">Mauritius</a></b> are stipulated in the "<i>Mauritius Laws 1990 Vol.2 SCHEDULE (Section 2)</i>". The arms were designed by the <a href="/wiki/Mayor_of_Johannesburg" title="Mayor of Johannesburg">Mayor of Johannesburg</a> in 1906, Johann Van Der Puf. In the lower right quarter is a key and on the left-hand side is a white star, which are referred to in the Latin motto “Stella Clavisque Maris Indici” meaning “Star and Key of the Indian Ocean".</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Blazon">Blazon</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Coat_of_arms_of_Mauritius&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Blazon">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The armorial ensigns and supporters of Mauritius are described as:</p>
<p>(a) for arms-</p>
<ol>
<li>Quarterly azure and or.</li>
<li>In the first quarter a <a href="/wiki/Lymphad" title="Lymphad">lymphad</a> or.</li>
<li>In the second, 3 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Palm_(plant)" title="Palm (plant)">palm</a> trees vert.</li>
<li>In the third, a key in pale the wards downwards gules.</li>
<li>In the issuant, from the base a pile, and in chief a mullet argent.</li>
</ol>
<p>(b) for the supporters-</p>
<ol>
<li>On the dexter side, a <a href="/wiki/Dodo" title="Dodo">dodo</a> per bend sinister embattled gules and argent, and</li>
<li>On the sinister side, a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sambar_(deer)" title="Sambar (deer)">sambur deer</a> per bend embattled argent and gules, each supporting a sugar cane erect proper,</li>
</ol>
<p>(c) with the motto "Stella Clavisque Maris Indici” (<i>lat: Star and Key of the Indian Ocean</i>)</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Colour_code">Colour code</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Coat_of_arms_of_Mauritius&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Colour code">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>Azure - Royal Blue (Pantone Reflex Blue)</li>
<li>Or - Gold (Metallic Gold)</li>
<li>Vert - Emerald Green (Pantone Green)</li>
<li>Gules - Warm Red (Pantone 2X)</li>
<li>Argent - Silver (Metallic Silver) <a class="external autonumber" href="http://www.gov.mu/portal/site/abtmtius/menuitem.64c8475c9c8cb1984d57241079b521ca/" rel="nofollow">[1]</a></li>
</ul>
