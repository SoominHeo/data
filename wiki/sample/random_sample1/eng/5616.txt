<p><b>Masahiko Morifuku</b> (森福 允彦, born July 29, 1986 in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Toyohashi,_Aichi" title="Toyohashi, Aichi">Toyohashi, Aichi</a>) is a Japanese professional <a href="/wiki/Baseball" title="Baseball">baseball</a> <a href="/wiki/Pitcher" title="Pitcher">pitcher</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Fukuoka_SoftBank_Hawks" title="Fukuoka SoftBank Hawks">Fukuoka SoftBank Hawks</a> in Japan's <a href="/wiki/Nippon_Professional_Baseball" title="Nippon Professional Baseball">Nippon Professional Baseball</a>.</p>
