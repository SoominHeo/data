<p><b>Momonokawa Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">桃川駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Momonokawa-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/Train_station" title="Train station">railway station</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Chikuhi_Line" title="Chikuhi Line">Chikuhi Line</a> of <a href="/wiki/Kyushu_Railway_Company" title="Kyushu Railway Company">Kyushu Railway Company</a> (JR Kyushu), located in <a href="/wiki/Imari,_Saga" title="Imari, Saga">Imari</a>, <a href="/wiki/Saga_Prefecture" title="Saga Prefecture">Saga Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</p>
