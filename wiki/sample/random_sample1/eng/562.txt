<p>The <b>Type 216</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Submarine" title="Submarine">submarine</a> design concept announced by <a href="/wiki/Howaldtswerke-Deutsche_Werft" title="Howaldtswerke-Deutsche Werft">Howaldtswerke-Deutsche Werft</a> based on the Type <a href="/wiki/Type_212_submarine" title="Type 212 submarine">212</a>/<a href="/wiki/Type_214_submarine" title="Type 214 submarine">214</a>. It is a larger design targeted to meet the needs of the Australian <a href="/wiki/Collins-class_submarine_replacement_project" title="Collins-class submarine replacement project">Collins-class submarine replacement project</a>, also known as Sea 1000, and the needs of other countries possibly including India and Canada.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-uboatcanberratimes_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-uboatcanberratimes-3">[3]</a></sup> The design is double hulled with two decks, includes a fuel cell, Permasyn motor, and lithium-ion batteries.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ThyssenKrupp_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-ThyssenKrupp-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
