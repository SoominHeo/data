<p>The <b>list of motorways in the United Kingdom</b> is a complete list of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Motorway" title="Motorway">motorways</a> in the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>. Note that whilst <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain_road_numbering_scheme" title="Great Britain road numbering scheme">one numbering scheme is used for Great Britain</a>, roads in <a href="/wiki/Northern_Ireland" title="Northern Ireland">Northern Ireland</a> are allocated their own numbers on an ad hoc basis.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Motorways_under_construction_or_planned">Motorways under construction or planned</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_motorways_in_the_United_Kingdom&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Motorways under construction or planned">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The largest motorway project currently under construction in the UK is the £500m <a href="/wiki/M8_motorway_(Scotland)" title="M8 motorway (Scotland)">M8 Baillieston to Newhouse</a> completion project, which includes 6 miles of new motorway on the M8 as well as junction upgrades and widenings to the M73 and M74.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup> Work started in Spring 2014 with completion estimated for Spring 2017.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup></p>
<p>The £380m A1(M) Leeming - Barton upgrade<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup> also commenced construction in Spring 2014 with completion scheduled for Summer 2016.</p>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Queensferry_Crossing" title="Queensferry Crossing">Queensferry Crossing</a> and its approach roads are also being constructed as a motorway and will extend the <a href="/wiki/M90_motorway" title="M90 motorway">M90</a> to the M9 at junction 1a re-numbering the M9 spur when finished in 2016.</p>
<p>The only other new motorway constructions currently being planned in the UK is a motorway running south of the city of <a href="/wiki/Newport,_Wales" title="Newport, Wales">Newport</a>, south Wales. This motorway remains to be properly named, only being described as the <a href="/wiki/M4_relief_road" title="M4 relief road">M4 relief road</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Unbuilt_motorways">Unbuilt motorways</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_motorways_in_the_United_Kingdom&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Unbuilt motorways">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>There have been many plans for motorways in the UK that have not been built. Below is a list of plans that were not built (not exhaustive):</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/M12_motorway" title="M12 motorway">M12</a> in 3 forms:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup>
<ul>
<li>From the London end of the <a href="/wiki/M11_motorway" title="M11 motorway">M11</a> to <a href="/wiki/Brentwood,_Essex" title="Brentwood, Essex">Brentwood</a>;</li>
<li>From the London end of the <a href="/wiki/M11_motorway" title="M11 motorway">M11</a> to <a href="/wiki/Maplin_Sands" title="Maplin Sands">Maplin Sands</a> Airport (also not built);</li>
<li>From the <a href="/wiki/M25_motorway" title="M25 motorway">M25</a> (in the vicinity of the <a href="/wiki/M11_motorway" title="M11 motorway">M11</a>) to <a href="/wiki/Chelmsford" title="Chelmsford">Chelmsford</a>.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/M13_motorway" title="M13 motorway">M13</a>, from East London to <a href="/wiki/Maplin_Sands" title="Maplin Sands">Maplin Sands</a> airport passing <i>south</i> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Southend" title="Southend">Southend</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup></li>
<li>M17 <a href="/wiki/Castle_Donington" title="Castle Donington">Castle Donington</a> bypass, a short spur off the <a href="/wiki/M1_motorway" title="M1 motorway">M1</a>;</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/M31_motorway" title="M31 motorway">M31</a>, a bypass of the busiest section of the <a href="/wiki/M25_motorway" title="M25 motorway">M25</a>, cutting the corner for traffic from the West.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup></li>
<li>M59, <a href="/wiki/M58_motorway" title="M58 motorway">M58</a> junction 2 to south of <a href="/wiki/Preston,_Lancashire" title="Preston, Lancashire">Preston</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-20"><a href="#cite_note-20">[20]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/M64_motorway" title="M64 motorway">M64</a>, between the <a href="/wiki/M6_motorway" title="M6 motorway">M6</a> south of <a href="/wiki/Stoke-on-Trent" title="Stoke-on-Trent">Stoke-on-Trent</a> and the <a href="/wiki/M1_motorway" title="M1 motorway">M1</a> near <a href="/wiki/Derby" title="Derby">Derby</a>. Mostly built as the <a href="/wiki/A50_road" title="A50 road">A50</a>.</li>
<li>M601, which would have entered <a href="/wiki/Manchester" title="Manchester">Manchester</a> from the <a href="/wiki/M62_motorway" title="M62 motorway">M62</a> (now the <a href="/wiki/M60_motorway" title="M60 motorway">M60</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Irwell_Valley" title="Irwell Valley">Irwell Valley</a> at <a href="/wiki/Clifton,_Greater_Manchester" title="Clifton, Greater Manchester">Clifton</a> near <a href="/wiki/Pendlebury" title="Pendlebury">Pendlebury</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-21"><a href="#cite_note-21">[21]</a></sup></li>
<li>M650, Airedale Motorway from near <a href="/wiki/Silsden" title="Silsden">Silsden</a> to <a href="/wiki/Shipley,_West_Yorkshire" title="Shipley, West Yorkshire">Shipley</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-22"><a href="#cite_note-22">[22]</a></sup></li>
<li>A6(M) (or <a href="/wiki/M66_motorway" title="M66 motorway">M66</a> extension), which would have formed an eastern bypass for <a href="/wiki/Stockport" title="Stockport">Stockport</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-23"><a href="#cite_note-23">[23]</a></sup> As of March 2006, this is still under the planning stage as part of the SEMMMS (south east Manchester multi modal study) project (though not as motorway).</li>
<li>A556(M), which would have cut the corner between the M6 and M56 near <a href="/wiki/Warrington" title="Warrington">Warrington</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-24"><a href="#cite_note-24">[24]</a></sup></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chippenham,_Wiltshire" title="Chippenham, Wiltshire">Chippenham</a> Spur, off the <a href="/wiki/M4_motorway" title="M4 motorway">M4</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-25"><a href="#cite_note-25">[25]</a></sup></li>
<li>Strensham – <a href="/wiki/Solihull" title="Solihull">Solihull</a> motorway, a link between the <a href="/wiki/M50_motorway_(Great_Britain)" title="M50 motorway (Great Britain)">M50</a> and <a href="/wiki/M42_motorway" title="M42 motorway">M42</a> or <a href="/wiki/M69_motorway" title="M69 motorway">M69</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-26"><a href="#cite_note-26">[26]</a></sup></li>
<li>Most of the <a href="/wiki/M67_motorway" title="M67 motorway">M67</a> – from <a href="/wiki/Hattersley" title="Hattersley">Hattersley</a> to the <a href="/wiki/M1_motorway" title="M1 motorway">M1</a> via the <a href="/wiki/Hope_Valley,_Derbyshire" title="Hope Valley, Derbyshire">Hope Valley</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Peak_District" title="Peak District">Peak District</a> <a href="/wiki/National_parks_of_England_and_Wales" title="National parks of England and Wales">national park</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sheffield" title="Sheffield">Sheffield</a>. Various routes have been proposed but have yet to come to fruition due to controversy and/or expense.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-27"><a href="#cite_note-27">[27]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Sheffield_Hallam_(UK_Parliament_constituency)" title="Sheffield Hallam (UK Parliament constituency)">Sheffield Hallam</a> MP and former <a href="/wiki/Deputy_Prime_Minister_of_the_United_Kingdom" title="Deputy Prime Minister of the United Kingdom">Deputy Prime Minister</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nick_Clegg" title="Nick Clegg">Nick Clegg</a> has also been signalling his support for the scheme.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-28"><a href="#cite_note-28">[28]</a></sup> The latest proposal to extend the M67 is to tunnel under the <a href="/wiki/Peak_District" title="Peak District">Peak District</a> from <a href="/wiki/Hattersley" title="Hattersley">Hattersley</a> to <a href="/wiki/Stannington,_Sheffield" title="Stannington, Sheffield">Stannington</a> (an outer-suburb of <a href="/wiki/Sheffield" title="Sheffield">Sheffield</a>) and to link that tunnel to a new underground motorway providing links to the <a href="/wiki/M1_motorway" title="M1 motorway">M1</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-29"><a href="#cite_note-29">[29]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<p>Many cities had urban motorway plans, most of which were not built. <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-30"><a href="#cite_note-30">[30]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Newcastle_upon_Tyne" title="Newcastle upon Tyne">Newcastle</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-31"><a href="#cite_note-31">[31]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Liverpool" title="Liverpool">Liverpool</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-32"><a href="#cite_note-32">[32]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Manchester" title="Manchester">Manchester</a>/<a href="/wiki/Greater_Manchester" title="Greater Manchester">SELNEC</a> and <a href="/wiki/Glasgow" title="Glasgow">Glasgow</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-33"><a href="#cite_note-33">[33]</a></sup> all had extensive plans.</p>
