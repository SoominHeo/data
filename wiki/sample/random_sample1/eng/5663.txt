<p><b>Mokdong Stadium</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Sports_complex" title="Sports complex">sports complex</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Mok-dong" title="Mok-dong">Mok-dong</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yangcheon-gu" title="Yangcheon-gu">Yangcheon-gu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a>. It consists of <a href="/wiki/Multi-purpose_stadium" title="Multi-purpose stadium">multi-purpose stadium</a>, a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Baseball_stadium" title="Baseball stadium">baseball stadium</a>, and an <a href="/wiki/Ice_rink" title="Ice rink">Artificial ice rink</a>. It was opened on November 14, 1989. The main stadium hosted <a href="/wiki/K_League" title="K League">K League</a> football matches from 1993 to 2001.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Facilities">Facilities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mokdong_Stadium&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Facilities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Mokdong_Stadium">Mokdong Stadium</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mokdong_Stadium&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Mokdong Stadium">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It is multi-purpose stadium and currently used mostly for football and athletics and was the home stadium of <a href="/wiki/Jeju_United_FC" title="Jeju United FC">Bucheon SK</a> (Currently Jeju United FC) before they were moved to <a href="/wiki/Bucheon" title="Bucheon">Bucheon</a>. The stadium holds 20,236 people and opened in 1989.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Mokdong_Baseball_Stadium">Mokdong Baseball Stadium</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mokdong_Stadium&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Mokdong Baseball Stadium">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><i>For details, see <a href="/wiki/Mokdong_Baseball_Stadium" title="Mokdong Baseball Stadium">Mokdong Baseball Stadium</a>.</i></li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Mokdong_Ice_Rink">Mokdong Ice Rink</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mokdong_Stadium&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Mokdong Ice Rink">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Mokdong Ice Rink – it was used as the filming location for <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Broadcasting_System" title="Seoul Broadcasting System">Seoul Broadcasting System (SBS)</a>'s drama <i><a href="/wiki/Lovers_in_Paris" title="Lovers in Paris">Lovers in Paris</a></i>, where Ki-joo, played by <a href="/wiki/Park_Shin-yang" title="Park Shin-yang">Park Shin-yang</a>, and Soo-hyuk, played by <a href="/wiki/Lee_Dong-gun" title="Lee Dong-gun">Lee Dong-gun</a>, played ice hockey.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The venue was used by <a href="/wiki/Asia_League_Ice_Hockey" title="Asia League Ice Hockey">Asia League Ice Hockey</a> team <a href="/wiki/Daemyung_Sangmu" title="Daemyung Sangmu">Daemyung Sangmu</a> between 2013 and 2016.</li>
</ul>
