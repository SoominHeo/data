<b>Municipalities of Montenegro</b></div>
</div>

<p><a href="/wiki/Montenegro" title="Montenegro">Montenegro</a> is composed of 23 <a href="/wiki/Municipality" title="Municipality">municipalities</a> (<i><a href="/wiki/Op%C5%A1tina" title="Opština">opština</a></i> / општина), the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Old_Royal_Capital_Ceti%C7%8Ce" title="Old Royal Capital Cetiǌe">Old Royal Capital Cetiǌe</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Podgorica_Capital_City" title="Podgorica Capital City">Podgorica Capital City</a>. Podgorica is divided into two subdivisions called <i>urban municipalities</i> (градска општина / <i>gradska opština</i>).</p>
<p>Recently created:</p>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pet%C7%8Cica_Municipality" title="Petǌica Municipality">Petǌica Municipality</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gusi%C7%8Ce_Municipality" title="Gusiǌe Municipality">Gusiǌe Municipality</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<p>Urban municipalities:</p>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Podgorica_Municipality" title="Podgorica Municipality">Podgorica Municipality</a>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Golubovci" title="Golubovci">Urban Municipality of Golubovci</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Tuzi" title="Tuzi">Urban Municipality of Tuzi</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>The <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Union_of_Municipalities_of_Montenegro&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Union of Municipalities of Montenegro (page does not exist)">Union of Municipalities of Montenegro</a> is a national association of local authorities of Montenegro.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
