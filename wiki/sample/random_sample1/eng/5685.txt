<p>The <b>Montreal Jazz</b> (<a href="/wiki/French_language" title="French language">French</a>: <span lang="fr" xml:lang="fr"><i>Jazz de Montréal</i></span>) were a Canadian professional <a href="/wiki/Basketball" title="Basketball">basketball</a> team based in <a href="/wiki/Montreal" title="Montreal">Montreal</a>, <a href="/wiki/Quebec" title="Quebec">Quebec</a>, Canada. The team played its only season in the <a href="/wiki/National_Basketball_League_of_Canada" title="National Basketball League of Canada">National Basketball League of Canada</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2012-13_NBL_Canada_season" title="2012-13 NBL Canada season">2012-2013 season</a>, in place of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Laval_Kebs" title="Laval Kebs">Laval Kebs</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The Jazz played its home games at the <a href="/wiki/Centre_Pierre_Charbonneau" title="Centre Pierre Charbonneau">Centre Pierre Charbonneau</a>.</p>
<p>The Jazz did not play in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2013-14_NBL_Canada_season" title="2013-14 NBL Canada season">2013-14 NBL Canada season</a> after failing to secure a new ownership group.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Montreal_Jazz&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On October 26, 2012 NBL Canada announced that a Montreal expansion team would join the league in place of the recently folded Laval Kebs.</p>
<p>It was announced on November 1, 2012 that the team would be named the Montreal Jazz.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>After finishing the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2012-13_NBL_Canada_season" title="2012-13 NBL Canada season">2012-13 NBL Canada season</a> with a disappointing 2-38 record and without an owner, the NBL Canada Board of Governors decided to suspend the Jazz for the <a href="/wiki/2013%E2%80%9314_NBL_Canada_season" title="2013–14 NBL Canada season">2013-14 season</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
