<p>The <b>Marquises and Dukes of Montferrat</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> were the rulers of a territory in <a href="/wiki/Piedmont" title="Piedmont">Piedmont</a> south of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Po_River" title="Po River">Po</a> and east of <a href="/wiki/Turin" title="Turin">Turin</a> called <a href="/wiki/Montferrat" title="Montferrat">Montferrat</a>. The <a href="/wiki/March_of_Montferrat" title="March of Montferrat">March of Montferrat</a> was created by <a href="/wiki/Berengar_II_of_Italy" title="Berengar II of Italy">Berengar II of Italy</a> in 950 during a redistribution of power in the northwest of his kingdom. It was originally named after and held by the <a href="/wiki/Aleramici" title="Aleramici">Aleramici</a>. In 1574, Montferrat was raised to a <a href="/wiki/Duchy" title="Duchy">Duchy</a> by <a href="/wiki/Maximilian_II,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Maximilian II, Holy Roman Emperor">Maximilian II, Holy Roman Emperor</a> (see <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Montferrat" title="Duchy of Montferrat">Duchy of Montferrat</a>).</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Gonzaga_dynasty">Gonzaga dynasty</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_rulers_of_Montferrat&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Gonzaga dynasty">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/House_of_Gonzaga" title="House of Gonzaga">House of Gonzaga</a></div>
<p>In 1536 <a href="/wiki/Charles_V,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor">Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor</a> granted the marquisate, despite competing claims from Savoy and from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Marquis_of_Saluzzo" title="Marquis of Saluzzo">Marquis of Saluzzo</a>, to the Gonzagas. This was confirmed in 1559 by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Peace_of_Cateau-Cambr%C3%A9sis" title="Peace of Cateau-Cambrésis">Peace of Cateau-Cambrésis</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Frederick_II,_Duke_of_Mantua" title="Frederick II, Duke of Mantua">Frederick Gonzaga</a> (1536–1540), Duke of Mantua. Married to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Margaret_of_Montferrat" title="Margaret of Montferrat">Margaret of Montferrat</a>, daughter of William IX and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Anne_of_Alen%C3%A7on" title="Anne of Alençon">Anne of Alençon</a>, and Marchioness of Montferrat <a href="/wiki/Suo_jure" title="Suo jure">in her own right</a>.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Francesco_III_Gonzaga" title="Francesco III Gonzaga">Francis I</a> (1540–1550), Duke of Mantua, Marquess of Montferrat. Son of Margaret of Montferrat and Frederick Gonzaga.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Guglielmo_I_Gonzaga" title="Guglielmo I Gonzaga">William X</a> (1550–1587), Duke of Mantua, Marquess, then (from 1574) Duke of Montferrat. Son of Margaret of Montferrat and Frederick Gonzaga</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vincenzo_I_Gonzaga" title="Vincenzo I Gonzaga">Vincent I</a> (1587–1612), Duke of Mantua and Montferrat. Son of William X</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Francesco_IV_Gonzaga" title="Francesco IV Gonzaga">Francis II</a> (1612), Duke of Mantua and Montferrat. Son of Vincent I</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ferdinando_I_Gonzaga" title="Ferdinando I Gonzaga">Ferdinand I</a> (1612–26), Duke of Mantua and Montferrat. Son of Vincent I.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vincenzo_II_Gonzaga" title="Vincenzo II Gonzaga">Vincent II</a> (1626–27), Duke of Mantua and Montferrat. Son of Vincent I.</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Mantuan_Succession" title="War of the Mantuan Succession">War of the Mantuan Succession</a></i> (1627–1631) – a portion was lost to <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Savoy" title="Duchy of Savoy">Duchy of Savoy</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Maria_Gonzaga" title="Maria Gonzaga">Maria</a>, Duchess of Montferrat 1612–61, also Duchess of Mantua 1627–1631. Daughter of Francis II.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Charles_I,_Duke_of_Mantua_and_Montferrat" title="Charles I, Duke of Mantua and Montferrat">Charles I</a>, called "of Nevers", Duke of Montferrat (1627–1637), also Duke of Mantua and Nevers. Father-in-law of Maria, co-ruler with Maria and his son, Charles.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Charles_II,_Duke_of_Mantua_and_Montferrat" title="Charles II, Duke of Mantua and Montferrat">Charles II</a> (1637–1665). Also Duke of Nevers until 1659. Son of Maria, grandson of both Charles I and Francis II.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ferdinand_Charles,_Duke_of_Mantua_and_Montferrat" title="Ferdinand Charles, Duke of Mantua and Montferrat">Ferdinand Charles</a> (1665–1708), Duke of Montferrat and Mantua. Son of Charles II.</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notes">Notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_rulers_of_Montferrat&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Notes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="reflist" style="list-style-type: decimal;">
<ol class="references">
