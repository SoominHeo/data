<p>This is a <b>list of diplomatic missions of Moldova</b>. The landlocked country of <a href="/wiki/Moldova" title="Moldova">Moldova</a>, sandwiched between <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ukraine" title="Ukraine">Ukraine</a>, has a handful of embassies, mostly located in Europe.</p>
