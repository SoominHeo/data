<p>This page <b>lists diplomatic missions resident in <a href="/wiki/Maldives" title="Maldives">Maldives</a></b>. The capital, <a href="/wiki/Mal%C3%A9" title="Malé">Malé</a>, hosts 7 embassies and high commissioners. Several other countries have accredited ambassadors resident in other capitals or major cities in the region.</p>
