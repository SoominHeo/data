<p><br/>
Year <b>1230</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MCCXXX</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Tuesday" title="Common year starting on Tuesday">common year starting on Tuesday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1230&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_area">By area</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1230&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By area">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Africa">Africa</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1230&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Africa">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sundiata" title="Sundiata">Sundiata</a> starts to rule in <a href="/wiki/Mali" title="Mali">Mali</a> (approximate date).</li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/West_Africa" title="West Africa">West African</a> village of <a href="/wiki/Siby,_Mali" title="Siby, Mali">Siby</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sundiata_Keita" title="Sundiata Keita">Sundiata Keita</a>, founder of the <a href="/wiki/Mali_Empire" title="Mali Empire">Mali Empire</a>, forces the <a href="/wiki/Mandinka_people" title="Mandinka people">Malinkés</a> to bind themselves to each other by oath.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1230&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>August 10–12 – Sultan <a href="/wiki/Kayqubad_I" title="Kayqubad I">Kayqubad I</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sultanate_of_Rum" title="Sultanate of Rum">Rum</a> defeats Shah <a href="/wiki/Jalal_ad-Din_Mingburnu" title="Jalal ad-Din Mingburnu">Jalal ad-Din</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Persia" title="Persia">Persia</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Yass%C4%B1%C3%A7emen" title="Battle of Yassıçemen">Battle of Yassıçemen</a>, ending the <a href="/wiki/Khwarazmian_dynasty" title="Khwarazmian dynasty">Khwarazmian dynasty</a></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Sena_dynasty" title="Sena dynasty">Sena dynasty</a> of <a href="/wiki/Bengal" title="Bengal">Bengal</a> falls.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1230&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_9" title="March 9">March 9</a> – <a href="/wiki/Second_Bulgarian_Empire" title="Second Bulgarian Empire">Bulgarian</a> <a href="/wiki/Tsar" title="Tsar">tsar</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ivan_Asen_II" title="Ivan Asen II">Ivan Asen II</a> defeats the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Emperor_of_Thessalonica" title="Emperor of Thessalonica">Emperor of Thessalonica</a> <a href="/wiki/Theodore_Komnenos_Doukas" title="Theodore Komnenos Doukas">Theodore Komnenos Doukas</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Klokotnitsa" title="Battle of Klokotnitsa">Battle of Klokotnitsa</a>. In its aftermath, Bulgaria quickly extends its control over most of Theodore's domains in <a href="/wiki/Thrace" title="Thrace">Thrace</a>, <a href="/wiki/Macedonia_(region)" title="Macedonia (region)">Macedonia</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Albania" title="Albania">Albania</a>. The Latin <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Philippopolis" title="Duchy of Philippopolis">Duchy of Philippopolis</a> and the independent principality of <a href="/wiki/Alexius_Slav" title="Alexius Slav">Alexius Slav</a> are also captured and annexed into Bulgaria.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Iberian_Peninsula" title="Iberian Peninsula">Iberian Peninsula</a>: <a href="/wiki/Alfonso_IX_of_Le%C3%B3n" title="Alfonso IX of León">Alfonso IX</a> defeats <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ibn_Hud_al-Yamani&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ibn Hud al-Yamani (page does not exist)">Ibn Hud al-Yamani</a> (known as Almogàver by the Christians) at the battle of <a href="/wiki/Alange" title="Alange">Alange</a>. This success opens the road to <a href="/wiki/Badajoz" title="Badajoz">Badajoz</a> to the Leonese troops.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-linehan1999_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-linehan1999-2">[2]</a></sup> The Portuguese king <a href="/wiki/Sancho_II_of_Portugal" title="Sancho II of Portugal">Sancho II</a> continues his offensive southward and takes <a href="/wiki/Beja,_Portugal" title="Beja, Portugal">Beja</a>, <a href="/wiki/Juromenha" title="Juromenha">Juromenha</a>, <a href="/wiki/Serpa" title="Serpa">Serpa</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Moura_Municipality" title="Moura Municipality">Moura</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-picard2000_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-picard2000-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_24" title="September 24">September 24</a> – The Kingdoms of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Le%C3%B3n" title="Kingdom of León">León</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Galicia" title="Kingdom of Galicia">Galicia</a> unite with the Kingdoms of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Castile" title="Kingdom of Castile">Castile</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Toledo" title="Kingdom of Toledo">Toledo</a> under <a href="/wiki/Ferdinand_III_of_Castile" title="Ferdinand III of Castile">Ferdinand III</a>.</li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Teutonic_Knights" title="Teutonic Knights">Teutonic Knights</a> are invited into <a href="/wiki/Prussia" title="Prussia">Prussia</a> to forcibly convert the <a href="/wiki/Old_Prussians" title="Old Prussians">Prussians</a> and Yatvags to <a href="/wiki/Christianity" title="Christianity">Christianity</a>.</li>
</ul>
