<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Murad Giray</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<div id="contentSub"></div>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Murad Giray</b> aka <b>Murad Khan Girai</b> (<a href="/wiki/Crimean_Tatar_language" title="Crimean Tatar language">Crimean Tatar</a>: <span lang="crh" xml:lang="crh">Murad Geray مراد كراى</span>, <a href="/wiki/Turkish_language" title="Turkish language">Turkish</a>: <span lang="tr" xml:lang="tr"><i>Murat Giray</i></span>) (1627–1696) was a <a href="/wiki/List_of_Crimean_khans" title="List of Crimean khans">Crimean khan</a> who reigned from 1678 to 1683.</p>
<p>Grandson of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Selamet_I_Giray" title="Selamet I Giray">Selamet I Giray</a>, Murad replaced dethroned khan <a href="/wiki/Selim_I_Giray" title="Selim I Giray">Selim I Giray</a> in 1683. He participated in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Vienna" title="Battle of Vienna">Battle of Vienna</a> of 1683 and following the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman</a> defeat, he was held responsible by <a href="/wiki/Grand_Vizier" title="Grand Vizier">Grand Vizier</a> <a href="/wiki/Kara_Mustafa_Pasha" title="Kara Mustafa Pasha">Kara Mustafa Pasha</a> (himself later executed) and was dismissed as the khan, to be replaced by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Hac%C4%B1_II_Giray&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Hacı II Giray (page does not exist)">Hacı II Giray</a>.</p>
<p>He was exiled to the Saracheli farm near <a href="/wiki/Yambol" title="Yambol">Yambol</a> and died there in 1696.</p>
