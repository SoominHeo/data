<p><b>Arisa Murata</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">村田 愛里咲</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Murata Arisa</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born <span style="display:none">(<span class="bday">1990-10-17</span>)</span>October 17, 1990 in <a href="/wiki/Kitakyushu" title="Kitakyushu">Kitakyushu</a>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> <a href="/wiki/Freestyle_skiing" title="Freestyle skiing">freestyle skier</a>, specializing in <a href="/wiki/Mogul_skiing" title="Mogul skiing">moguls</a> .<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-fisprofile_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-fisprofile-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Murata competed at the <a href="/wiki/2010_Winter_Olympics" title="2010 Winter Olympics">2010 Winter Olympics</a> for Japan. She placed 11th in the qualifying round of the <a href="/wiki/Freestyle_skiing_at_the_2010_Winter_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Women%27s_moguls" title="Freestyle skiing at the 2010 Winter Olympics – Women's moguls">moguls</a>, advancing to the final, where she placed 8th.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sportsrefprofile_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-sportsrefprofile-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>As of April 2013, her best showing at the <a href="/wiki/FIS_Freestyle_World_Ski_Championships" title="FIS Freestyle World Ski Championships">World Championships</a> is 5th, in the <a href="/wiki/FIS_Freestyle_World_Ski_Championships_2013" title="FIS Freestyle World Ski Championships 2013">2013</a> dual moguls event.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-fisprofile_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-fisprofile-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Murata made her <a href="/wiki/FIS_Freestyle_Skiing_World_Cup" title="FIS Freestyle Skiing World Cup">World Cup</a> debut in February 2008. As of March 2013, she has one World Cup podium finish, taking bronze in the moguls event at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%C3%85re_Ski_Area" title="Åre Ski Area">Are</a> in <a href="/wiki/2011%E2%80%9312_FIS_Freestyle_Skiing_World_Cup" title="2011–12 FIS Freestyle Skiing World Cup">2011/12</a>. Her best World Cup overall finish in moguls is 15th, in <a href="/wiki/2012%E2%80%9313_FIS_Freestyle_Skiing_World_Cup" title="2012–13 FIS Freestyle Skiing World Cup">2012/13</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-fisprofile_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-fisprofile-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
