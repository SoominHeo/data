<p>The <i><b>Warriors</b></i> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Meta-series" title="Meta-series">meta-series</a>, known as <i><b>Musou</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">無双</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Musō</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, lit. "Unrivalled")</span> series in Japan, is an <a href="/wiki/Action_game" title="Action game">action game</a> series created by <a href="/wiki/Koei_Tecmo#Omega_Force" title="Koei Tecmo">Omega Force</a> and <a href="/wiki/Koei" title="Koei">Koei</a> (now <a href="/wiki/Koei_Tecmo" title="Koei Tecmo">Koei Tecmo</a>). The first title, <i><a href="/wiki/Dynasty_Warriors_(video_game)" title="Dynasty Warriors (video game)">Dynasty Warriors</a></i>, was premiered in Japan in 1997. The meta-series contains <i><a href="/wiki/Dynasty_Warriors" title="Dynasty Warriors">Dynasty Warriors</a></i> games, <i><a href="/wiki/Samurai_Warriors" title="Samurai Warriors">Samurai Warriors</a></i> games, and spin-off titles.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Dynasty_Warriors_series">Dynasty Warriors series</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Warriors_video_games&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Dynasty Warriors series">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i><b><a href="/wiki/Dynasty_Warriors" title="Dynasty Warriors">Dynasty Warriors</a></b></i>, known as <i><b>Sangokumusou</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">三國無双</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Sangokumusō</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, lit. "<a href="/wiki/Three_Kingdoms" title="Three Kingdoms">Three Kingdoms</a> Unrivalled")</span>, is the first and the largest sub-series of <i>Warriors</i> series. While the second and follow titles are named as <i><b>Shin · Sangokumusou</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">真・三國無双</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Shin · Sangokumusō</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, lit. "True · Three Kingdom Unrivalled")</span>, English localization remain naming <i>Dynasty Warriors</i>, made all English titles are a number ahead of their Japanese counterparts.</p>
