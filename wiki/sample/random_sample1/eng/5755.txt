<p><i><b>Myriad Colors Phantom World</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">無彩限のファントム・ワールド</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Musaigen no Fantomu Wārudo</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a Japanese <a href="/wiki/Fantasy" title="Fantasy">fantasy</a> novel series written by Sōichirō Hatano and illustrated by Shirabi. An <a href="/wiki/Anime" title="Anime">anime</a> adaptation aired between 7 January and 31 March 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TV_anime_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-TV_anime-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Myriad_Colors_Phantom_World&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the near future, the accidental release of an experimental virus causes an outbreak that changes the brain chemistry of every person in the world, allowing them to perceive extra-dimensional beings called "phantoms". In addition, some children born after the outbreak have developed special powers that allow them to battle and seal phantoms. Even though the vast majority of phantoms are harmless, many of these gifted children are placed in clubs and organizations dedicated to dealing with phantoms that prove to be nuisances or threats to humanity. The story revolves around Haruhiko Ichijo and his friends in the Phantom-hunting club of Hosea Academy, a private school for children with special abilities to seal phantoms, and their everyday life and struggles, dealing with phantoms.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Characters">Characters</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Myriad_Colors_Phantom_World&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Characters">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<dl>
<dt>Haruhiko Ichijo <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">一条 晴彦</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Ichijō Haruhiko</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><span style="font-weight: normal">Voiced by: <a href="/wiki/Hiro_Shimono" title="Hiro Shimono">Hiro Shimono</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TV_anime_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-TV_anime-1">[1]</a></sup></span></dd>
<dd>A first-year high school student and the main character. His power consists of summoning or sealing phantoms by drawing them in a sketchbook. Due to the library that is in his house, he has a lot of knowledge about numerous different subjects, but most of those facts are seen as useless by his teammates. His parents are separated but he hopes for them to be reunited as a family again. In the anime, most of the episodes begin with Haruhiko giving a brief explanation about certain topics.</dd>
</dl>
<dl>
<dt>Mai Kawakami <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">川神 舞</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kawakami Mai</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><span style="font-weight: normal">Voiced by: <a href="/wiki/Sumire_Uesaka" title="Sumire Uesaka">Sumire Uesaka</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Uesaka_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Uesaka-2">[2]</a></sup></span></dd>
<dd>A second-year high school student, Haruhiko's senior and original partner. She specializes in close combat and her ability consists of channeling elemental powers through her body, such as fire from her heart, earth from her spleen, metal from her lungs, water from her kidneys, and wood from her armpit. Mai has been known by Haruhiko to be hot headed and violent ever since she was a child.</dd>
</dl>
<dl>
<dt>Reina Izumi <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">和泉 玲奈</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Izumi Reina</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><span style="font-weight: normal">Voiced by: <a href="/wiki/Saori_Hayami" title="Saori Hayami">Saori Hayami</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TV_anime_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-TV_anime-1">[1]</a></sup></span></dd>
<dd>A first-year high school student and a new member of Haruhiko's team. She is a Phantom Eater, an unusual power that allows her to seal phantoms by consuming them. She has also been trained in basic self defense. She has a large appetite and constantly struggles with getting enough money to eat, despite coming from a wealthy household. She has an older sister who ran away from home due to their parents being very strict as well has having a strong dislike towards phantoms. She strongly admires Mai who she claims to resemble her older sister.</dd>
</dl>
<dl>
<dt>Koito Minase <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">水無瀬 小糸</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Minase Koito</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><span style="font-weight: normal">Voiced by: <a href="/wiki/Maaya_Uchida" title="Maaya Uchida">Maaya Uchida</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TV_anime_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-TV_anime-1">[1]</a></sup></span></dd>
<dd>A newly transferred student who is always wearing headphones. Her ability first manifested when she was in elementary school when a phantom attacked the rabbits she was assigned to care for in the schoolyard. She managed to seal the phantom with her ability, but in the process damaged a large portion of the school. This caused her friends and even her parents to fear her and she eventually developed the anti-social personality she has today. Her ability is a powerful sound attack using her voice, which can stun or seal phantoms. She tends to use a lot of sugar in her drinks.</dd>
</dl>
<dl>
<dt>Ruru <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">ルル</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><span style="font-weight: normal">Voiced by: <a href="/wiki/Azusa_Tadokoro" title="Azusa Tadokoro">Azusa Tadokoro</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TV_anime_1-4"><a href="#cite_note-TV_anime-1">[1]</a></sup></span></dd>
<dd>A friendly phantom in the form of a small fairy. She always follows Haruhiko and enjoys making fun of him and the other characters. This character is original to the anime.</dd>
</dl>
<dl>
<dt>Kurumi Kumamakura <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">熊枕 久瑠美</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kumamakura Kurumi</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><span style="font-weight: normal">Voiced by: <a href="/wiki/Misaki_Kuno" title="Misaki Kuno">Misaki Kuno</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TV_anime_1-5"><a href="#cite_note-TV_anime-1">[1]</a></sup></span></dd>
<dd>An anime-original character, she is a shy fourth grade student from the primary school division of Hosea Academy who looks up to Haruhiko's group. She always carries a teddy bear named Albrecht and has a very strong affinity with bears as almost everything associated with her, from her surname to her birthplace and even her favorite food, has bear(Kuma) in its name. Kurumi's ability enlarges Albrecht's size considerably and allows him to move on his own and fight. Like Minase, Kurumi's ability manifested at a very young age.</dd>
</dl>
<dl>
<dt>Shosuke Morohashi <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">諸橋 翔介</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Morohashi Shōsuke</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><span style="font-weight: normal">Voiced by: <a href="/wiki/Daisuke_Sakaguchi" title="Daisuke Sakaguchi">Daisuke Sakaguchi</a></span></dd>
<dd>Haruhiko's friend and classmate who is usually envious of him because all of his teammates are beautiful girls.</dd>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Anime">Anime</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Myriad_Colors_Phantom_World&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Anime">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>An anime television series aired between 7 January and 31 March 2016 on <a href="/wiki/Asahi_Broadcasting_Corporation" title="Asahi Broadcasting Corporation">ABC Asahi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tokyo_Metropolitan_Television" title="Tokyo Metropolitan Television">Tokyo MX</a>, <a href="/wiki/Television_Aichi_Broadcasting" title="Television Aichi Broadcasting">TV Aichi</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Nippon_BS_Broadcasting" title="Nippon BS Broadcasting">BS11</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[a]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[7]</a></sup> The series is directed by <a href="/wiki/Tatsuya_Ishihara" title="Tatsuya Ishihara">Tatsuya Ishihara</a> and written by <a href="/wiki/Fumihiko_Shimo" title="Fumihiko Shimo">Fumihiko Shimo</a>, with animation produced by <a href="/wiki/Kyoto_Animation" title="Kyoto Animation">Kyoto Animation</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Uesaka_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-Uesaka-2">[2]</a></sup> Kazumi Ikeda will handle the series' character designs, and will also serve as the chief animation director. Shinpei Sawa provides the designs for the Phantoms. The series' music is composed by Effy. Additionally, Ryuuta Nakagami serves as director of photography; Mikiko Watanabe is the series' art director; Kana Miyata provides the color key; Hiroshi Karata is in charge of accessories planning; and Yota Tsuruoka is the sound director. The opening theme song is "Naked Dive" by Screen Mode, and the ending theme is "Junshin Always" <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">純真Always</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, "Innocence Always")</span> by <a href="/wiki/Azusa_Tadokoro" title="Azusa Tadokoro">Azusa Tadokoro</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TV_anime_1-7"><a href="#cite_note-TV_anime-1">[1]</a></sup> The anime will be released on seven <a href="/wiki/Blu-ray" title="Blu-ray">Blu-ray</a> and DVD compilation volumes containing two episodes and one picture drama each between 6 April and 5 October 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-bddvd1_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-bddvd1-9">[8]</a></sup> An <a href="/wiki/Original_video_animation" title="Original video animation">original video animation</a> will be bundled with the seventh volume.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-OVA_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-OVA-10">[9]</a></sup> Crunchyroll will release the series on Blu-ray and DVD with an English dub.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[10]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[11]</a></sup></p>

During the trip, Haruhiko states that he hates summer, and wishes that he is inside his house reading... until the other boys point out about the swimsuits the girls would be wearing. When they arrived, they are told that even though there are a lot of visitors each year, a sudden appearance of a Phantom scares people away, so they are asked to exterminate the said Phantom. In return, the lodging and food fees will be free. That being said, when asked about what the Phantom did, the lodge house owner refused to say anything. The first day pass by without anything happened, and they thought that since there is no visitors, probably the Phantom had moved to somewhere else. With the illusion ended, Mai uses the sea to increase her water techniques, while Kurumi uses Albrecht to throw the Phantom in the air, which ends up eaten by Reina. With the case finally resolved, they feel that they can enjoy the rest of the vacation, until Haruhiko come back from the sea (he was swept away by the current when Mai and Albrecht attack the Phantom) without his swimming trunks, now really naked instead of just an illusion.
