<p><b>Mutnovsky</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Мутновский</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Complex_volcano" title="Complex volcano">complex volcano</a> located in the southern part of <a href="/wiki/Kamchatka_Peninsula" title="Kamchatka Peninsula">Kamchatka Peninsula</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>. It is one of the most active volcanoes of southern Kamchatka; the latest eruption was recorded in 2000. At the foot of the Mutnovsky lies a <a href="/wiki/Geyser" title="Geyser">geyser</a> field, popularly known as the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Lesser_Valley_of_Geysers&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Lesser Valley of Geysers (page does not exist)">Lesser Valley of Geysers</a>.</p>
