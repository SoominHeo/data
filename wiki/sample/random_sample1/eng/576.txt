<p>The discography of <a href="/wiki/2AM_(band)" title="2AM (band)">2AM</a> consists of three <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Studio_album" title="Studio album">studio albums</a>, three <a href="/wiki/Extended_play" title="Extended play">extended plays</a> and eighteen <a href="/wiki/Single_(music)" title="Single (music)">singles</a>. In addition, they released three promotional singles and eight commercial singles for their endorsement deals with various companies. 2AM debuted with their first single "This Song" in 2008.</p>
