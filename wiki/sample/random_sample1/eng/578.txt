<p><i><b>2PM of 2PM</b></i> is the fourth Japanese studio album by <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> boy band <a href="/wiki/2PM" title="2PM">2PM</a>. It was released in April 15, 2015, as their second album release under <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sony_Music_Japan" title="Sony Music Japan">Sony Music Japan</a> sublabel <a href="/wiki/Epic_Records_Japan" title="Epic Records Japan">Epic Records Japan</a> in three editions:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-official_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-official-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li>Regular edition: CD <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li>Limited Edition A: CD + DVD <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li>Limited Edition B: CD + CD <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-typeb_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-typeb-4">[4]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<p>There are 13 new tracks in this fourth Japanese studio album. Tracks from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Guilty_Love_(2PM_song)" title="Guilty Love (2PM song)">Guilty Love</a> are also included in this album.</p>
<p>The album debuted <a href="/wiki/List_of_Oricon_number-one_albums_of_2015" title="List of Oricon number-one albums of 2015">at number one</a> on the Weekly <a href="/wiki/Oricon_Albums_Chart" title="Oricon Albums Chart">Oricon Albums Chart</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-oricon_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-oricon-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>On May 20, 2015 2PM released a repackaged edition to "2PM of 2PM" with two editions:</p>
<ul>
<li>Regular edition: CD + new song "I know" <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></li>
<li>Limited Edition A: CD + DVD <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
