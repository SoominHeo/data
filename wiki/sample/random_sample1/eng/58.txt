<p><br/>
Year <b>1248</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MCCXLVIII</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Wednesday" title="Leap year starting on Wednesday">leap year starting on Wednesday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1248&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_26" title="April 26">April 26</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Gothic_architecture" title="Gothic architecture">Gothic</a> <a href="/wiki/Chapel" title="Chapel">chapel</a> <a href="/wiki/Sainte-Chapelle" title="Sainte-Chapelle">Sainte-Chapelle</a> is consecrated in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>, <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_15" title="August 15">August 15</a> – The foundation stone of <a href="/wiki/Cologne_Cathedral" title="Cologne Cathedral">Cologne Cathedral</a> is laid after an older cathedral on the site burns down on <a href="/wiki/April_30" title="April 30">April 30</a>. Construction is completed 632 years later, in <a href="/wiki/1880" title="1880">1880</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_25" title="August 25">August 25</a> – The Dutch city of <a href="/wiki/Ommen" title="Ommen">Ommen</a> receives <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/City_rights_in_the_Netherlands" title="City rights in the Netherlands">city rights</a> and <a href="/wiki/Defensive_wall" title="Defensive wall">fortification rights</a> from Otto III, <a href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Archdiocese_of_Utrecht" title="Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Utrecht">Archbishop of Utrecht</a>, after it has been pillaged at least twice by a local robber baron.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_23" title="November 23">November 23</a> – <a href="/wiki/Reconquista" title="Reconquista">Reconquista</a>: King <a href="/wiki/Ferdinand_III_of_Castile" title="Ferdinand III of Castile">Ferdinand III of Castile</a> recaptures the city of <a href="/wiki/Seville" title="Seville">Seville</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Moors" title="Moors">Moors</a>, ending the <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Seville" title="Siege of Seville">Siege of Seville</a>; this year also Prince <a href="/wiki/Alfonso_X_of_Castile" title="Alfonso X of Castile">Alfonso X of Castile</a> takes the city of <a href="/wiki/Alicante" title="Alicante">Alicante</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_24" title="November 24">November 24</a> – In the middle of the night a mass on the north side of <a href="/wiki/Mont_Granier" title="Mont Granier">Mont Granier</a> suddenly collapsed, in one of the largest historical rock slope failures known in Europe.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li>King <a href="/wiki/Louis_IX_of_France" title="Louis IX of France">Louis IX of France</a> launches the <a href="/wiki/Seventh_Crusade" title="Seventh Crusade">Seventh Crusade</a>, setting sail with an army of 20,000 toward <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Pope_Innocent_IV" title="Pope Innocent IV">Pope Innocent IV</a> grants the <a href="/wiki/Croatia" title="Croatia">Croats</a> permission to use their own language and script in <a href="/wiki/Liturgy" title="Liturgy">liturgy</a> (<i>see</i> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Glagolitic_alphabet" title="Glagolitic alphabet">Glagolitic alphabet</a>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Tallinn" title="Tallinn">Tallinn</a> (<i>Reval</i>) converts from <a href="/wiki/Riga" title="Riga">Riga</a> law to <a href="/wiki/L%C3%BCbeck_law" title="Lübeck law">Lübeck law</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Roger_Bacon" title="Roger Bacon">Roger Bacon</a> publishes the formula for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Black_powder" title="Black powder">black powder</a> in <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>.</li>
<li>Construction on the <a href="/wiki/Alhambra" title="Alhambra">Alhambra</a> palace in <a href="/wiki/Granada" title="Granada">Granada</a>, Spain, is begun by the <a href="/wiki/Nasrid_dynasty" title="Nasrid dynasty">Nasrid dynasty</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (August 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></li>
<li>The University of <a href="/wiki/Piacenza" title="Piacenza">Piacenza</a> is founded in Italy.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources.
