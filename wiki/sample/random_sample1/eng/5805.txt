<p><b>Mtskheta-Mtianeti</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Georgian_alphabet" title="Georgian alphabet">Georgian</a>: მცხეთა-მთიანეთი) is a region (<a href="/wiki/Mkhare" title="Mkhare">Mkhare</a>) in eastern <a href="/wiki/Georgia_(country)" title="Georgia (country)">Georgia</a> comprising the town of <a href="/wiki/Mtskheta" title="Mtskheta">Mtskheta</a>, which serves as a regional capital, together with its district and the adjoining mountainous areas.The western part is controlled by the breakaway <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Republic_of_South_Ossetia" title="Republic of South Ossetia">Republic of South Ossetia</a> since 1992 and the independence of Georgia.<br/>
The region comprises five <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Districts_of_Georgia_(country)" title="Districts of Georgia (country)">municipalities</a>:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Akhalgori_Municipality" title="Akhalgori Municipality">Akhalgori</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dusheti_Municipality" title="Dusheti Municipality">Dusheti</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Tianeti_Municipality" title="Tianeti Municipality">Tianeti</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Mtskheta_Municipality" title="Mtskheta Municipality">Mtskheta</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kazbegi_Municipality" title="Kazbegi Municipality">Kazbegi</a></li>
</ul>
