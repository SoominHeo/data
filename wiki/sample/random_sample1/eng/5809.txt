<p><b>Miguel Ángel Britos Cabrera</b> (born 17 July 1985) is a <a href="/wiki/Uruguay" title="Uruguay">Uruguayan</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)#Centre-back" title="Defender (association football)">centre back</a> for <a href="/wiki/Premier_League" title="Premier League">Premier League</a> team <a href="/wiki/Watford_F.C." title="Watford F.C.">Watford</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Miguel_Britos&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="F.C3.A9nix">Fénix</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Miguel_Britos&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Fénix">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Britos began his professional career playing with <a href="/wiki/Centro_Atl%C3%A9tico_F%C3%A9nix" title="Centro Atlético Fénix">Fénix</a> in 2005. After playing the <a href="/wiki/2005%E2%80%9306_Uruguayan_Primera_Divisi%C3%B3n" title="2005–06 Uruguayan Primera División">2005–06 Uruguayan Primera División</a> season, his team was relegated to the <a href="/wiki/Uruguayan_Segunda_Divisi%C3%B3n" title="Uruguayan Segunda División">Uruguayan Segunda División</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Juventud">Juventud</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Miguel_Britos&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Juventud">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 1 January 2006, Britos was transferred to <a href="/wiki/Juventud_de_Las_Piedras" title="Juventud de Las Piedras">Juventud</a>, where he achieved the promotion to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2007%E2%80%9308_Uruguayan_Primera_Divisi%C3%B3n" title="2007–08 Uruguayan Primera División">2007–08 Uruguayan Top Division season</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Wanderers">Wanderers</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Miguel_Britos&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Wanderers">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 1 July 2007, Britos signed a new contract with <a href="/wiki/Montevideo_Wanderers_F.C." title="Montevideo Wanderers F.C.">Montevideo Wanderers</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Bologna">Bologna</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Miguel_Britos&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Bologna">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Britos then moved to Italy on 22 July 2008 where he played for <a href="/wiki/Bologna_F.C._1909" title="Bologna F.C. 1909">Bologna</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Serie_A" title="Serie A">Serie A</a>, signing a five-year contract for a €2 million transfer fee.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> He made his Serie A debut on 21 September 2008 against <a href="/wiki/ACF_Fiorentina" title="ACF Fiorentina">Fiorentina</a>. On 21 February 2009, he scored his first Serie A goal against <a href="/wiki/Inter_Milan" title="Inter Milan">Inter</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Napoli">Napoli</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Miguel_Britos&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Napoli">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Britos' talent did not go unnoticed, and he was subsequently purchased from Bologna by <a href="/wiki/S.S.C._Napoli" title="S.S.C. Napoli">Napoli</a> on 12 July 2011 in a €9 million transfer deal, signing a four-year contract.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> He scored his first goal for Napoli in a 2–0 home win over <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/A.C._Chievo_Verona" title="A.C. Chievo Verona">Chievo</a> on 13 February 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> Growing into an invaluable asset for <a href="/wiki/Walter_Mazzarri" title="Walter Mazzarri">Walter Mazzarri</a>'s side, Britos featured in the <a href="/wiki/2012_Coppa_Italia_Final" title="2012 Coppa Italia Final">final</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Coppa_Italia" title="Coppa Italia">Coppa Italia</a> on 20 May 2012, as Napoli defeated Serie A champions <a href="/wiki/Juventus_F.C." title="Juventus F.C.">Juventus</a> 2–0.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 23 May 2015, he headbutted <a href="/wiki/%C3%81lvaro_Morata" title="Álvaro Morata">Álvaro Morata</a> in a 3–1 away defeat to Juventus, and was sent off.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Watford">Watford</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Miguel_Britos&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Watford">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 22 July 2015, Britos joined newly promoted English side <a href="/wiki/Watford_F.C." title="Watford F.C.">Watford</a> on a three-year deal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> He was sent off on his debut against <a href="/wiki/Preston_North_End_F.C." title="Preston North End F.C.">Preston North End</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_League_Cup" title="Football League Cup">League Cup</a> on 25 August 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup> Britos made his full Premier League debut on the 24 October 2015, completing the full 90 minutes and helping keep a clean sheet against <a href="/wiki/Stoke_City_F.C." title="Stoke City F.C.">Stoke City</a>.</p>
