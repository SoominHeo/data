<p>The <b>2sunhwan-ro</b>(<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">2순환로</span>), also called <b>Gwangju Loop 2</b>, is an 6-lane highway located in <a href="/wiki/Gwangju" title="Gwangju">Gwangju</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Republic_of_Korea" title="Republic of Korea">Republic of Korea</a>. this route circulates suburbs with Dountown of Gwangju, with a total length of 27.66 km (17.19 mi).</p>

<li>1 May 1998 : Gakhwa ~ Duam section opens to traffic.</li>
<li>1 January 2001 : Duam ~ Jiwon section opens to traffic.</li>
<li>5 March 2002 : Pungam ~ Seochang section opens to traffic.</li>
<li>15 October 2004 : Pungam ~ Hyodeok section opens to traffic.</li>
<li>15 May 2007 : Sanwol JCT ~ Seochang section opens to traffic.</li>
<li>19 September 2009 : Munheung JCT ~ Gakhwa section opens to traffic.</li>
</ul>
