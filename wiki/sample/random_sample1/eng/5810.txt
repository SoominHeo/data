<p><b>Miguel Ângelo Leonardo Vítor</b> (born 30 June 1989) is a Portuguese professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays for Israeli club <a href="/wiki/Hapoel_Be%27er_Sheva_F.C." title="Hapoel Be'er Sheva F.C.">Hapoel Be'er Sheva F.C.</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)#Centre-back" title="Defender (association football)">central defender</a>.</p>
<p>He spent most of his career with <a href="/wiki/S.L._Benfica" title="S.L. Benfica">Benfica</a> (mostly as a reserve) and <a href="/wiki/PAOK_FC" title="PAOK FC">PAOK</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Miguel_V%C3%ADtor&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Benfica">Benfica</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Miguel_V%C3%ADtor&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Benfica">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Vítor was born in <a href="/wiki/Torres_Vedras" title="Torres Vedras">Torres Vedras</a>. Having been promoted from <a href="/wiki/S.L._Benfica" title="S.L. Benfica">S.L. Benfica</a>'s junior squad for the <a href="/wiki/2007%E2%80%9308_Primeira_Liga" title="2007–08 Primeira Liga">2007–08 season</a> he made his debut for the first team in August 2007, appearing in both the <a href="/wiki/Primeira_Liga" title="Primeira Liga">Primeira Liga</a>, against <a href="/wiki/Vit%C3%B3ria_S.C." title="Vitória S.C.">Vitória de Guimarães</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/UEFA_Champions_League" title="UEFA Champions League">UEFA Champions League</a>, facing <a href="/wiki/F.C._Copenhagen" title="F.C. Copenhagen">F.C. Copenhagen</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2007%E2%80%9308_UEFA_Champions_League" title="2007–08 UEFA Champions League">third qualifying round</a>; this was because habitual starters <a href="/wiki/Luis%C3%A3o" title="Luisão">Luisão</a> and <a href="/wiki/David_Luiz" title="David Luiz">David Luiz</a> were sidelined with injuries.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Like <a href="/wiki/Romeu_Ribeiro" title="Romeu Ribeiro">Romeu Ribeiro</a>, Vítor was loaned to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Liga_de_Honra" title="Liga de Honra">second division</a> side <a href="/wiki/C.D._Aves" title="C.D. Aves">C.D. Aves</a> in January 2008 until the end of the season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Subsequently he returned to Benfica, being relatively used in <a href="/wiki/2008%E2%80%9309_Primeira_Liga" title="2008–09 Primeira Liga">his first full campaign</a>.</p>
<p>In early July 2010, Vítor joined English <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_League_Championship" title="Football League Championship">Championship</a> club <a href="/wiki/Leicester_City_F.C." title="Leicester City F.C.">Leicester City</a> in a <a href="/wiki/2010%E2%80%9311_Football_League" title="2010–11 Football League">season</a>-long loan, teaming up with compatriots <a href="/wiki/Moreno_(Portuguese_footballer)" title="Moreno (Portuguese footballer)">Moreno</a> and manager <a href="/wiki/Paulo_Sousa" title="Paulo Sousa">Paulo Sousa</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> He made his debut in a 2–1 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_League_Cup" title="Football League Cup">League Cup</a> win over <a href="/wiki/Portsmouth_F.C." title="Portsmouth F.C.">Portsmouth</a> at <a href="/wiki/Fratton_Park" title="Fratton Park">Fratton Park</a> on 21 September;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> on 6 November, he scored his first professional goal, heading from a <a href="/wiki/Martyn_Waghorn" title="Martyn Waghorn">Martyn Waghorn</a> <a href="/wiki/Indirect_free_kick" title="Indirect free kick">free kick</a> after 18 minutes, in a 2–0 win against <a href="/wiki/Barnsley_F.C." title="Barnsley F.C.">Barnsley</a> at <a href="/wiki/Oakwell" title="Oakwell">Oakwell</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 19 December 2010, Vítor suffered a <a href="/wiki/Pulled_hamstring" title="Pulled hamstring">pulled hamstring</a> in a 0–3 away defeat against <a href="/wiki/Ipswich_Town_F.C." title="Ipswich Town F.C.">Ipswich Town</a>, being sidelined for three months.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> He netted twice on his return, a 3–0 success at <a href="/wiki/Scunthorpe_United_F.C." title="Scunthorpe United F.C.">Scunthorpe United</a> on 12 March 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>Vítor subsequently returned to Benfica, being completely ostracized by manager <a href="/wiki/Jorge_Jesus" title="Jorge Jesus">Jorge Jesus</a> – as practically all Portuguese players – and even being demoted to <a href="/wiki/S.L._Benfica_B" title="S.L. Benfica B">the B-team</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> After he refused to renew his contract, he left as a <a href="/wiki/Free_agent" title="Free agent">free agent</a> in late May 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="PAOK">PAOK</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Miguel_V%C3%ADtor&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: PAOK">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 24 June 2013, Vítor signed with <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greek</a> club <a href="/wiki/PAOK_FC" title="PAOK FC">PAOK FC</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> He scored his first goal for his new team – also the side's first in <a href="/wiki/2013%E2%80%9314_Superleague_Greece" title="2013–14 Superleague Greece">the new season</a> – on 17 August, netting through a header in a 3–0 home win over <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Skoda_Xanthi_F.C." title="Skoda Xanthi F.C.">Skoda Xanthi FC</a>.</p>
<p>Vítor was first-choice during his spell in <a href="/wiki/Thessaloniki" title="Thessaloniki">Thessaloniki</a>, scoring two goals in 33 games in the <a href="/wiki/2014%E2%80%9315_Superleague_Greece" title="2014–15 Superleague Greece">2014–15 campaign</a> to help his team finish third. On 10 June 2016, it was announced he would be released as his contract would not be renewed.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hapoel_Be.27er_Sheva">Hapoel Be'er Sheva</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Miguel_V%C3%ADtor&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Hapoel Be'er Sheva">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 1 July 2016, <a href="/wiki/Free_agent" title="Free agent">free agent</a> Vítor signed a three-year contract with <a href="/wiki/Israeli_Premier_League" title="Israeli Premier League">Israeli Premier League</a> champions <a href="/wiki/Hapoel_Be%27er_Sheva_F.C." title="Hapoel Be'er Sheva F.C.">Hapoel Be'er Sheva FC</a>, for an undisclosed fee.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
