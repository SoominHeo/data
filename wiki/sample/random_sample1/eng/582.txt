<p><b>February 17</b> is the 48th day of the year in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 317 days remaining until the end of the year (318 in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>). This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Wednesday, Friday or Sunday (58 in 400 years each) than on Monday or Tuesday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Thursday or Saturday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=February_17&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/364" title="364">364</a> – Roman Emperor <a href="/wiki/Jovian_(emperor)" title="Jovian (emperor)">Jovian</a> dies after a reign of eight months. He is found dead in his tent at <a href="/wiki/Tyana" title="Tyana">Tyana</a> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Asia_Minor" title="Asia Minor">Asia Minor</a>) en route back to <a href="/wiki/Constantinople" title="Constantinople">Constantinople</a> in suspicious circumstances.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1370" title="1370">1370</a> – <a href="/wiki/Northern_Crusades" title="Northern Crusades">Northern Crusades</a>: <a href="/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Lithuania" title="Grand Duchy of Lithuania">Grand Duchy of Lithuania</a> and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Teutonic_Knights" title="Teutonic Knights">Teutonic Knights</a> meet in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Rudau" title="Battle of Rudau">Battle of Rudau</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1411" title="1411">1411</a> – Following the successful campaigns during the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Interregnum" title="Ottoman Interregnum">Ottoman Interregnum</a>, <a href="/wiki/Musa_%C3%87elebi" title="Musa Çelebi">Musa Çelebi</a>, one of the sons of <a href="/wiki/Bayezid_I" title="Bayezid I">Bayezid I</a>, becomes <a href="/wiki/Sultan" title="Sultan">Sultan</a> with the support of <a href="/wiki/Mircea_I_of_Wallachia" title="Mircea I of Wallachia">Mircea I of Wallachia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1500" title="1500">1500</a> – Duke <a href="/wiki/Frederick_I_of_Denmark" title="Frederick I of Denmark">Friedrich</a> and Duke <a href="/wiki/John,_King_of_Denmark" title="John, King of Denmark">Johann</a> attempt to subdue the <a href="/wiki/Peasant" title="Peasant">peasantry</a> of <a href="/wiki/Dithmarschen" title="Dithmarschen">Dithmarschen</a>, Denmark, in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hemmingstedt" title="Battle of Hemmingstedt">Battle of Hemmingstedt</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1600" title="1600">1600</a> – The philosopher <a href="/wiki/Giordano_Bruno" title="Giordano Bruno">Giordano Bruno</a> is burned alive, for <a href="/wiki/Heresy_in_Christianity" title="Heresy in Christianity">heresy</a>, at <a href="/wiki/Campo_de%27_Fiori" title="Campo de' Fiori">Campo de' Fiori</a> in Rome.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1621" title="1621">1621</a> – <a href="/wiki/Myles_Standish" title="Myles Standish">Myles Standish</a> is appointed as first commander of the English <a href="/wiki/Plymouth_Colony" title="Plymouth Colony">Plymouth Colony</a> in North America.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1753" title="1753">1753</a> – In Sweden February 17 is followed by <a href="/wiki/March_1" title="March 1">March 1</a> as the country moves from the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1801" title="1801">1801</a> – An <a href="/wiki/Electoral_College_(United_States)" title="Electoral College (United States)">electoral</a> tie between <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Jefferson" title="Thomas Jefferson">Thomas Jefferson</a> and <a href="/wiki/Aaron_Burr" title="Aaron Burr">Aaron Burr</a> is resolved when Jefferson is elected President of the United States and Burr, Vice President by the <a href="/wiki/United_States_House_of_Representatives" title="United States House of Representatives">United States House of Representatives</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1814" title="1814">1814</a> – <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Sixth_Coalition" title="War of the Sixth Coalition">War of the Sixth Coalition</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Mormant" title="Battle of Mormant">Battle of Mormant</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1819" title="1819">1819</a> – The United States House of Representatives passes the <a href="/wiki/Missouri_Compromise" title="Missouri Compromise">Missouri Compromise</a> for the first time.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1838" title="1838">1838</a> – <a href="/wiki/Weenen_massacre" title="Weenen massacre">Weenen massacre</a>: Hundreds of <a href="/wiki/Voortrekkers" title="Voortrekkers">Voortrekkers</a> along the Blaukraans River, <a href="/wiki/Natal,_South_Africa" title="Natal, South Africa">Natal</a> are killed by <a href="/wiki/Zulu_people" title="Zulu people">Zulus</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1854" title="1854">1854</a> – The United Kingdom recognizes the independence of the <a href="/wiki/Orange_Free_State" title="Orange Free State">Orange Free State</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1859" title="1859">1859</a> – <a href="/wiki/Cochinchina_Campaign" title="Cochinchina Campaign">Cochinchina Campaign</a>: The <a href="/wiki/French_Navy" title="French Navy">French Navy</a> captured the <a href="/wiki/Citadel_of_Saigon" title="Citadel of Saigon">Citadel of Saigon</a>, a fortress that was manned by 1,000 <a href="/wiki/Nguy%E1%BB%85n_dynasty" title="Nguyễn dynasty">Nguyễn dynasty</a> soldiers, en route to conquering <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saigon" title="Saigon">Saigon</a> and other regions of southern Viet Nam.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1863" title="1863">1863</a> – A group of citizens of <a href="/wiki/Geneva" title="Geneva">Geneva</a> founded an International Committee for Relief to the Wounded, which later became known as the <a href="/wiki/International_Committee_of_the_Red_Cross" title="International Committee of the Red Cross">International Committee of the Red Cross</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1864" title="1864">1864</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Civil_War" title="American Civil War">American Civil War</a>: The <a href="/wiki/H._L._Hunley_(submarine)" title="H. L. Hunley (submarine)"><i>H. L. Hunley</i></a> becomes the first submarine to <a href="/wiki/Sinking_of_USS_Housatonic" title="Sinking of USS Housatonic">engage and sink a warship</a>, the <a href="/wiki/USS_Housatonic_(1861)" title="USS Housatonic (1861)">USS <i>Housatonic</i></a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1865" title="1865">1865</a> – American Civil War: <a href="/wiki/Columbia,_South_Carolina" title="Columbia, South Carolina">Columbia, South Carolina</a>, is <a href="/wiki/Capture_of_Columbia" title="Capture of Columbia">burned</a> as <a href="/wiki/Confederate_States_of_America" title="Confederate States of America">Confederate</a> forces flee from advancing <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">Union</a> forces.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1871" title="1871">1871</a> – The victorious <a href="/wiki/Prussia" title="Prussia">Prussian</a> Army parades through Paris, France, after the end of the <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Paris_(1870%E2%80%9371)" title="Siege of Paris (1870–71)">Siege of Paris</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Franco-Prussian_War" title="Franco-Prussian War">Franco-Prussian War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1904" title="1904">1904</a> – <i><a href="/wiki/Madama_Butterfly" title="Madama Butterfly">Madama Butterfly</a></i> receives its première at <a href="/wiki/La_Scala" title="La Scala">La Scala</a> in <a href="/wiki/Milan" title="Milan">Milan</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1913" title="1913">1913</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Armory_Show" title="Armory Show">Armory Show</a> opens in New York City, displaying works of artists who are to become some of the most influential painters of the early 20th century.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1919" title="1919">1919</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Ukrainian_People%27s_Republic#Winter_1918.E2.80.9319" title="Ukrainian People's Republic">Ukrainian People's Republic</a> asks <a href="/wiki/Triple_Entente" title="Triple Entente">Entente</a> and the US for help fighting the <a href="/wiki/Bolsheviks" title="Bolsheviks">Bolsheviks</a>.</li>
