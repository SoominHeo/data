<p><b>Minami-Matsumoto Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">南松本駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Minami-Matsumoto-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a train station in the city of <a href="/wiki/Matsumoto,_Nagano" title="Matsumoto, Nagano">Matsumoto</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nagano_Prefecture" title="Nagano Prefecture">Nagano Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>, operated by <a href="/wiki/East_Japan_Railway_Company" title="East Japan Railway Company">East Japan Railway Company</a> (JR East)., with a freight terminal operated by the <a href="/wiki/Japan_Freight_Railway_Company" title="Japan Freight Railway Company">Japan Freight Railway Company</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lines">Lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Minami-Matsumoto_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Minami-Matsumoto Station is served by the <a href="/wiki/Shinonoi_Line" title="Shinonoi Line">Shinonoi Line</a> and is 10.9 kilometers from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Terminal_station" title="Terminal station">terminus</a> of the line at <a href="/wiki/Shiojiri_Station" title="Shiojiri Station">Shiojiri Station</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_layout">Station layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Minami-Matsumoto_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Station layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The station consists of a single ground-level <a href="/wiki/Island_platform" title="Island platform">island platform</a>, connected to the station building by a footbridge. The station has a <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Midori_no_Madoguchi" title="Midori no Madoguchi">Midori no Madoguchi</a></i> staffed ticket office.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Minami-Matsumoto_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Minami-Matumoto Station opened on 1 September 1944. With the privatization of <a href="/wiki/Japanese_National_Railways" title="Japanese National Railways">Japanese National Railways</a> (JNR) on 1 April 1987, the station came under the control of JR East.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Passenger_statistics">Passenger statistics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Minami-Matsumoto_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Passenger statistics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In fiscal 2015, the station was used by an average of 1584 passengers daily (boarding passengers only).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-jreast2015stats_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-jreast2015stats-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
