<p><b>Minami-Sunamachi Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">南砂町駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Minami-sunamachi-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a railway station in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/K%C5%8Dt%C5%8D,_Tokyo" title="Kōtō, Tokyo">Kōtō, Tokyo</a>, Japan. Its station number is T-15. The station opened on 29 March 1969, and consists of an <a href="/wiki/Island_platform" title="Island platform">island platform</a>.</p>
