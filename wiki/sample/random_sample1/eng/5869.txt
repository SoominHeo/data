<p><b>Susanna</b> "<b>Mini</b>" <b>Andén</b> (born 7 June 1978) is a Swedish <a href="/wiki/Model_(person)" title="Model (person)">model</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Actress" title="Actress">actress</a>, occasional host and producer.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mini_And%C3%A9n&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She was born in <a href="/wiki/Stockholm" title="Stockholm">Stockholm</a> and began modeling at the age of ten, joining <a href="/wiki/Elite_Model_Management" title="Elite Model Management">Elite Model Management</a> when she was fifteen. She has been on the cover of many fashion magazines including <i><a href="/wiki/Vogue_(magazine)" title="Vogue (magazine)">Vogue</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Marie_Claire" title="Marie Claire">Marie Claire</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Cosmopolitan_(magazine)" title="Cosmopolitan (magazine)">Cosmopolitan</a></i>, and <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ELLE" title="ELLE">ELLE</a></i>. She has been featured in fashion campaigns for <a href="/wiki/Calvin_Klein" title="Calvin Klein">Calvin Klein</a>, <a href="/wiki/Donna_Karan" title="Donna Karan">Donna Karan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Max_Azria" title="Max Azria">BCBG</a>, <a href="/wiki/Louis_Vuitton" title="Louis Vuitton">Louis Vuitton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hugo_Boss" title="Hugo Boss">Hugo Boss</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gucci" title="Gucci">Gucci</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Victoria%27s_Secret" title="Victoria's Secret">Victoria's Secret</a>. She is currently<sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Manual_of_Style/Dates_and_numbers#Chronological_items" title="Wikipedia:Manual of Style/Dates and numbers"><span title="The time period mentioned near this tag is ambiguous. (February 2013)">when?</span></a></i>]</sup> seen as the face for <a href="/wiki/Giorgio_Armani" title="Giorgio Armani">Giorgio Armani</a>'s perfume Armani Code for Women.</p>
<p>She was a <a href="/wiki/Judge" title="Judge">judge</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Miss_Universe" title="Miss Universe">Miss Universe</a> beauty pageant in 2001. She was the host of the Swedish section of <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Scandinavia%27s_Next_Top_Model" title="Scandinavia's Next Top Model">Scandinavia's Next Top Model</a></i> which premiered February 16, 2005. Andén has appeared in a handful of films and produced the 2003 short film <i>Buffoon</i>, which starred her husband <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Taber_Schroeder&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Taber Schroeder (page does not exist)">Taber Schroeder</a>. She appeared in <a href="/wiki/MyNetworkTV" title="MyNetworkTV">MyNetworkTV</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Fashion_House" title="Fashion House">Fashion House</a></i> in which she played a self-destructive model Tania Ford.</p>
<p>She married model Taber Schroeder in 2001, and they live together in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>.</p>
