<p><i><b>Mimi</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">미미</span>) is a 2014 <a href="/wiki/Korean_drama" title="Korean drama">South Korean television series</a> starring <a href="/wiki/Changmin" title="Changmin">Shim Chang-min</a> and <a href="/wiki/Moon_Ga-young" title="Moon Ga-young">Moon Ga-young</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It aired on cable channel <a href="/wiki/Mnet_(TV_channel)" title="Mnet (TV channel)">Mnet</a> from February 21 to March 14, 2014 on Fridays at 23:00 for 4 episodes.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>The horror/mystery/romance drama is inspired by the 2007 <a href="/wiki/Lee_Myung-se" title="Lee Myung-se">Lee Myung-se</a> film <i><a href="/wiki/M_(2007_film)" title="M (2007 film)">M</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mimi_(2014_TV_series)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Min-woo is a 28-year-old <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Introverted" title="Introverted">introverted</a> writer of <a href="/wiki/Webtoon" title="Webtoon">webtoons</a> who has partial <a href="/wiki/Amnesia" title="Amnesia">memory loss</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> One day, he finds a memo on his desk calendar, and because of it, writes a new webtoon titled <i>December 8</i>, which becomes an instant hit. But as the webtoon gets even more popular, Min-woo feels pressure from work and begins to suffer from severe headaches. He strives to recover his lost memories from the time he was an 18-year-old high school student, especially those of his first love, Mimi, as he unravels their mysterious fate.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Soundtrack">Soundtrack</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mimi_(2014_TV_series)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Soundtrack">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The official theme of the series, <i>Because I Love You</i>, was performed by <a href="/wiki/Wendy_(singer)" title="Wendy (singer)">Wendy (Son Seung-wan)</a>, who was at the time part of an <a href="/wiki/SM_Rookies" title="SM Rookies">SM Rookies</a> trio that included Irene (Bae Joo-hyun) and Seulgi (Kang Seul-gi). Their group would later become <a href="/wiki/Red_Velvet_(band)" title="Red Velvet (band)">Red Velvet</a> with the addition of <a href="/wiki/Joy_(singer)" title="Joy (singer)">Joy (Park Soo-young)</a> before their debut and then Yeri (Kim Ye-rim) after it.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_broadcast">International broadcast</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mimi_(2014_TV_series)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: International broadcast">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
