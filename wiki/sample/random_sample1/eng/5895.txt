<p>The <b>Misa Bridge</b> crosses the <a href="/wiki/Han_River_(Korea)" title="Han River (Korea)">Han River</a> in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a> and connects the cities of <a href="/wiki/Hanam" title="Hanam">Hanam</a> and <a href="/wiki/Namyangju" title="Namyangju">Namyangju</a>. It is part of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seoul-Chuncheon_Expressway" title="Seoul-Chuncheon Expressway">Seoul-Chuncheon Expressway</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
