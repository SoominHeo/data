<p><b>Misha</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Diminutive" title="Diminutive">diminutive</a> of the Russian name Михаил (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mikhail" title="Mikhail">Mikhail</a>). A <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hypocoristic" title="Hypocoristic">hypocoristic</a> of <a href="/wiki/Michael" title="Michael">Michael</a>, its English-language equivalent would be <a class="mw-disambig" href="/wiki/Mike_(disambiguation)" title="Mike (disambiguation)">Mike</a>. Non-Russian usages sometimes have it as a female name, and sometimes as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mischa_(disambiguation)" title="Mischa (disambiguation)">Mischa</a>.</p>
