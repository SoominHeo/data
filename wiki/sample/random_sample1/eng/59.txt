<p><br/>
Year <b>1251</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MCCLI</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Sunday" title="Common year starting on Sunday">common year starting on Sunday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1251&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1251&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1251&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>April – The first <a href="/wiki/Shepherds%27_Crusade" title="Shepherds' Crusade">Shepherds' Crusade</a>, a domestic <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_France" title="Kingdom of France">French</a> uprising in response to events in <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Seventh_Crusade" title="Seventh Crusade">Seventh Crusade</a>, occurs.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/December_26" title="December 26">December 26</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Alexander_III_of_Scotland" title="Alexander III of Scotland">Alexander III of Scotland</a> marries <a href="/wiki/Margaret_of_England" title="Margaret of England">Margaret</a>, daughter of King <a href="/wiki/Henry_III_of_England" title="Henry III of England">Henry III of England</a>, precipitating a power struggle between the two monarchs.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Andrew_de_Longjumeau" title="Andrew de Longjumeau">Andrew de Longjumeau</a>, dispatched two years earlier by King <a href="/wiki/Louis_IX_of_France" title="Louis IX of France">Louis IX of France</a> as an ambassador to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mongol" title="Mongol">Mongols</a>, meets the king in Palestine, with reports from the Mongols and <a href="/wiki/Tartary" title="Tartary">Tartary</a>; his mission is considered a failure.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Mindaugas" title="Mindaugas">Mindaugas</a> of <a href="/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Lithuania" title="Grand Duchy of Lithuania">Lithuania</a> is baptized, in prelude to his crowning as King of Lithuania in 1253.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Alexander_Nevsky" title="Alexander Nevsky">Alexander Nevsky</a> signs the first peace treaty between <a href="/wiki/Kievan_Rus%27" title="Kievan Rus'">Kievan Rus'</a> and <a href="/wiki/Norway" title="Norway">Norway</a>.</li>
<li>King <a href="/wiki/Conrad_IV_of_Germany" title="Conrad IV of Germany">Conrad IV of Germany</a> invades <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>, but fails to subdue the supporters of <a href="/wiki/Pope_Innocent_IV" title="Pope Innocent IV">Pope Innocent IV</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> city of <a href="/wiki/Berlin" title="Berlin">Berlin</a>, founded some 50 years earlier, receives its city charter.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ottokar_II_of_Bohemia" title="Ottokar II of Bohemia">Ottokar II of Bohemia</a>, later to become <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/King_of_Bohemia" title="King of Bohemia">King of Bohemia</a>, is elected <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rulers_of_Austria" title="Rulers of Austria">Duke of Austria</a>.</li>
<li>The earliest known manuscript of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Proverbs_of_Alfred" title="The Proverbs of Alfred">The Proverbs of Alfred</a></i>, a collection of sayings of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Alfred_the_Great" title="Alfred the Great">Alfred the Great</a>, is written.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1251&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/M%C3%B6ngke_Khan" title="Möngke Khan">Möngke Khan</a> is elected as the fourth <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Great_Khan" title="Great Khan">great Khan</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Mongol_Empire" title="Mongol Empire">Mongol Empire</a>.</li>
