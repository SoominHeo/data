<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Michel_Galabru&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Galabru appeared in more than 250 films and worked with <a href="/wiki/Film_director" title="Film director">directors</a> such as <a href="/wiki/Bertrand_Blier" title="Bertrand Blier">Bertrand Blier</a>, <a href="/wiki/Costa-Gavras" title="Costa-Gavras">Costa-Gavras</a>, <a href="/wiki/Luc_Besson" title="Luc Besson">Luc Besson</a> (for <i><a href="/wiki/Subway_(film)" title="Subway (film)">Subway</a></i>), and <a href="/wiki/Jean-Luc_Godard" title="Jean-Luc Godard">Jean-Luc Godard</a>. He is also known for his collaborations with <a href="/wiki/Louis_de_Fun%C3%A8s" title="Louis de Funès">Louis de Funès</a> in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Le_gendarme_de_Saint-Tropez" title="Le gendarme de Saint-Tropez">Le gendarme de Saint-Tropez</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Le_gendarme_se_marie" title="Le gendarme se marie">Le gendarme se marie</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Le_gendarme_et_les_extra-terrestres" title="Le gendarme et les extra-terrestres">Le gendarme et les extra-terrestres</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Le_gendarme_en_balade" title="Le gendarme en balade">Le gendarme en balade</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Le_gendarme_%C3%A0_New_York" title="Le gendarme à New York">Le gendarme à New York</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Le_gendarme_et_les_gendarmettes" title="Le gendarme et les gendarmettes">Le gendarme et les gendarmettes</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Le_petit_baigneur" title="Le petit baigneur">Le petit baigneur</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/L%27avare" title="L'avare">L'avare</a></i>, and <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nous_irons_a_Deauville" title="Nous irons a Deauville">Nous irons a Deauville</a></i> (with <a href="/wiki/Michel_Serrault" title="Michel Serrault">Michel Serrault</a>).</p>
<p>He worked with the actors <a href="/wiki/Ugo_Tognazzi" title="Ugo Tognazzi">Ugo Tognazzi</a> and <a href="/wiki/Michel_Serrault" title="Michel Serrault">Michel Serrault</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/La_Cage_aux_Folles_(film)" title="La Cage aux Folles (film)">La Cage aux Folles</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/La_Cage_aux_Folles_II" title="La Cage aux Folles II">La Cage aux Folles II</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/La_Cage_aux_Folles_3:_The_Wedding" title="La Cage aux Folles 3: The Wedding">La Cage aux Folles 3: The Wedding</a></i>; <i><a href="/wiki/Le_Viager" title="Le Viager">Le viager</a></i>; <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Les_gaspars&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Les gaspars (page does not exist)">Les gaspars</a></i>; <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Le_room_service&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Le room service (page does not exist)">Le room service</a></i>; <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Antonio_Vivaldi,_un_prince_%C3%A0_Venise&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Antonio Vivaldi, un prince à Venise (page does not exist)">Antonio Vivaldi, un prince à Venise</a></i>; and <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=L%27ibis_rouge&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="L'ibis rouge (page does not exist)">L'ibis rouge</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Awards">Awards</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Michel_Galabru&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Awards">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 1977, Galabru received a <a href="/wiki/C%C3%A9sar_Award" title="César Award">César</a> for Best Actor for his portrayal of Joseph Bouvier in <a href="/wiki/Bertrand_Tavernier" title="Bertrand Tavernier">Bertrand Tavernier</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/The_Judge_and_the_Assassin" title="The Judge and the Assassin">The Judge and the Assassin</a></i>.</p>
