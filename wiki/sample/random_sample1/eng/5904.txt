<p><b>Michelle Lynn Monaghan</b> (born March 23, 1976<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>) is an American actress known for her roles in <i><a href="/wiki/Kiss_Kiss_Bang_Bang" title="Kiss Kiss Bang Bang">Kiss Kiss Bang Bang</a></i> (2005), <i><a href="/wiki/Mission:_Impossible_III" title="Mission: Impossible III">Mission: Impossible III</a></i> (2006), <i><a href="/wiki/Gone_Baby_Gone" title="Gone Baby Gone">Gone Baby Gone</a></i> (2007), <i><a href="/wiki/Made_of_Honor" title="Made of Honor">Made of Honor</a></i> (2008), <i><a href="/wiki/Eagle_Eye" title="Eagle Eye">Eagle Eye</a></i> (2008), <i><a href="/wiki/Source_Code" title="Source Code">Source Code</a></i> (2011), and <i><a href="/wiki/True_Detective" title="True Detective">True Detective</a></i><span class="nowrap" style="padding-left:0.1em;">'s</span> <a href="/wiki/True_Detective_(season_1)" title="True Detective (season 1)">first season</a> (2014).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Michelle_Monaghan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Monaghan was born in <a href="/wiki/Winthrop,_Iowa" title="Winthrop, Iowa">Winthrop, Iowa</a>, the daughter of Sharon (nèe Harnel),<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> who ran a day care center in the family's home for forty years,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-mail-romcom_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-mail-romcom-3">[3]</a></sup> and Robert Monaghan, a factory worker.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-wcf-big_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-wcf-big-5">[5]</a></sup> She has two older brothers, Bob and John.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> Her parents brought up twelve foster children over a period of twelve years.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-mail-romcom_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-mail-romcom-3">[3]</a></sup> Monaghan has Irish and German ancestry, and was raised <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Catholic" title="Catholic">Catholic</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<p>She graduated from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/East_Buchanan_High_School_(Iowa)" title="East Buchanan High School (Iowa)">East Buchanan High School</a> in 1994,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-wcf-big_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-wcf-big-5">[5]</a></sup> where she was class president and acted in several plays.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> After graduation, she moved to Chicago to study journalism at <a href="/wiki/Columbia_College_Chicago" title="Columbia College Chicago">Columbia College Chicago</a> and began to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Model_(profession)" title="Model (profession)">model</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> She modeled in <a href="/wiki/Milan" title="Milan">Milan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Singapore" title="Singapore">Singapore</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tokyo" title="Tokyo">Tokyo</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hong_Kong" title="Hong Kong">Hong Kong</a>, as well as in the United States. With one semester remaining to complete her journalism degree in 1999, she left for New York to pursue an acting career, where she appeared as a model in several magazines and catalogs before making her acting debut.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>

<p>Monaghan's first notable appearances, though minor roles, were in episodes of <i><a href="/wiki/Young_Americans_(TV_series)" title="Young Americans (TV series)">Young Americans</a></i> (in which she appeared twice), and <i><a href="/wiki/Law_%26_Order:_Special_Victims_Unit" title="Law &amp; Order: Special Victims Unit">Law &amp; Order: Special Victims Unit</a></i>, came in 2001. She made her big screen debut that year in the small role of "Henrietta" in the movie <i>Perfume</i>. This was followed by another small role in <i><a href="/wiki/Unfaithful_(2002_film)" title="Unfaithful (2002 film)">Unfaithful</a></i> in 2002.</p>
<p>Monaghan got a big-break role in 2002 when she co-starred in the television series <i><a href="/wiki/Boston_Public" title="Boston Public">Boston Public</a></i> playing the role of Kimberly Woods. After guest-starring for a season, she returned to film, appearing in <i><a href="/wiki/It_Runs_in_the_Family_(2003_film)" title="It Runs in the Family (2003 film)">It Runs in the Family</a></i> in 2003, <i><a href="/wiki/Winter_Solstice_(film)" title="Winter Solstice (film)">Winter Solstice</a></i> in 2004, and starring with <a href="/wiki/Robert_Downey_Jr." title="Robert Downey Jr.">Robert Downey Jr.</a> and <a href="/wiki/Val_Kilmer" title="Val Kilmer">Val Kilmer</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Kiss_Kiss_Bang_Bang" title="Kiss Kiss Bang Bang">Kiss Kiss Bang Bang</a></i> in 2005. She also appears in <i><a href="/wiki/Constantine_(film)" title="Constantine (film)">Constantine</a></i> in 2005, but her role in that film was essentially cut, with her scenes viewable on the DVD under "deleted scenes."</p>
<p>In 2007, she co-starred with <a href="/wiki/Ben_Stiller" title="Ben Stiller">Ben Stiller</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Heartbreak_Kid_(2007_film)" title="The Heartbreak Kid (2007 film)">The Heartbreak Kid</a></i>, and with <a href="/wiki/Casey_Affleck" title="Casey Affleck">Casey Affleck</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Gone_Baby_Gone" title="Gone Baby Gone">Gone Baby Gone</a></i>. In 2008, she co-starred with <a href="/wiki/Patrick_Dempsey" title="Patrick Dempsey">Patrick Dempsey</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Made_of_Honor" title="Made of Honor">Made of Honor</a></i>, and co-starred with <a href="/wiki/Shia_LaBeouf" title="Shia LaBeouf">Shia LaBeouf</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Eagle_Eye" title="Eagle Eye">Eagle Eye</a></i>. She starred alongside <a href="/wiki/Jake_Gyllenhaal" title="Jake Gyllenhaal">Jake Gyllenhaal</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Source_Code" title="Source Code">Source Code</a></i>. She played Diane Ford in <i><a href="/wiki/Trucker_(film)" title="Trucker (film)">Trucker</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
<p>Monaghan starred in the critically acclaimed <a href="/wiki/HBO" title="HBO">HBO</a> anthology series <i><a href="/wiki/True_Detective" title="True Detective">True Detective</a></i>.</p>
<p>In April 2016, Monaghan was reported to have joined the principal cast of <a href="/wiki/Shawn_Christensen" title="Shawn Christensen">Shawn Christensen</a>'s independent feature film <i><a href="/wiki/Sidney_Hall_(film)" title="Sidney Hall (film)">Sidney Hall</a></i>, which began filming later that month.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SidneyHall_14-0"><a href="#cite_note-SidneyHall-14">[14]</a></sup> The film is set to premiere on January 25, 2017, at the <a href="/wiki/2017_Sundance_Film_Festival" title="2017 Sundance Film Festival">Sundance Film Festival</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SidneyHallPremiere_15-0"><a href="#cite_note-SidneyHallPremiere-15">[15]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Michelle_Monaghan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Monaghan met Australian graphic artist Peter White at a party in 2000. They married in <a href="/wiki/Sydney" title="Sydney">Sydney</a> five years later in August 2005 and live in New York.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup> She gave birth to their daughter in 2008,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup> and their son on October 30, 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2011, Monaghan had a mole on the back of her calf, which she had examined, then removed. It was diagnosed as <a href="/wiki/Skin_cancer" title="Skin cancer">skin cancer</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup></p>
