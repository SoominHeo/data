<p><b>Michelle Veintimilla</b> is an American film, television, and theater actress, best known for playing Janie in the film <i><a href="/wiki/Not_Cool_(film)" title="Not Cool (film)">Not Cool</a></i>. She has played the role of super-villain <a href="/wiki/Firefly_(DC_Comics)" title="Firefly (DC Comics)">Firefly</a> in the second season of the <a href="/wiki/Fox_Broadcasting_Company" title="Fox Broadcasting Company">Fox</a> television series <i><a href="/wiki/Gotham_(TV_series)" title="Gotham (TV series)">Gotham</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Michelle_Veintimilla&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Veintimilla was studying as a senior at <a href="/wiki/Carnegie_Mellon_University" title="Carnegie Mellon University">Carnegie Mellon University</a> when she got the job offer to perform in the musical <i>The Visit</i> in 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-June2015NYT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-June2015NYT-1">[1]</a></sup> She is currently playing the <a href="/wiki/DC_Comics" title="DC Comics">DC Comics</a>' super-villain <a href="/wiki/Firefly_(DC_Comics)" title="Firefly (DC Comics)">Bridgit Pike/Firefly</a> in the series <i><a href="/wiki/Gotham_(TV_series)" title="Gotham (TV series)">Gotham</a></i>. She would appear in two episodes of season two.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Aug2015TVL_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Aug2015TVL-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Veintimilla appeared on <a href="/wiki/Broadway_theatre" title="Broadway theatre">Broadway</a> in the musical version of <i>The Visit</i>, playing the younger version of <a href="/wiki/Chita_Rivera" title="Chita Rivera">Chita Rivera</a>'s character.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-June2015NYT_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-June2015NYT-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Aug2015TVL_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-Aug2015TVL-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
