<p><b>Michel Pensée Billong</b> (born 16 June 1973 in <a href="/wiki/Yaound%C3%A9" title="Yaoundé">Yaoundé</a>) is a former <a href="/wiki/Cameroon" title="Cameroon">Cameroonian</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">footballer</a> who played as a defender. He enjoyed a geographically varied career which took in spells with teams in his native <a href="/wiki/Cameroon" title="Cameroon">Cameroon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">Mexico</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>, <a href="/wiki/Portugal" title="Portugal">Portugal</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a> and <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. In England he scored once for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/MK_Dons" title="MK Dons">MK Dons</a> against <a href="/wiki/Barnsley_F.C." title="Barnsley F.C.">Barnsley</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> He was a part of the <a href="/wiki/Cameroon_national_football_team" title="Cameroon national football team">Cameroonian</a> <a href="/wiki/1998_FIFA_World_Cup" title="1998 FIFA World Cup">1998 FIFA World Cup</a> side.</p>
