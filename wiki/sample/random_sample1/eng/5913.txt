<p>The <b>Académie des Beaux-Arts</b> (<small>French pronunciation: ​</small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_French" title="Help:IPA for French">[lakadeˈmi de boˈzaʁ]</a></span>, <i>Academy of Fine Arts</i>) is a French <a href="/wiki/Learned_society" title="Learned society">learned society</a>. It is one of the five academies of the <a href="/wiki/Institut_de_France" title="Institut de France">Institut de France</a>. The current (2016) President of the Academy is <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%C3%89rik_Desmazi%C3%A8res&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Érik Desmazières (page does not exist)">Érik Desmazières</a> - a noted engraver and watercolorist.</p>
<p>The Academy was created in 1816 as a merger of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Acad%C3%A9mie_de_peinture_et_de_sculpture" title="Académie de peinture et de sculpture">Académie de peinture et de sculpture</a> (Academy of Painting and Sculpture, founded 1648), the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Acad%C3%A9mie_de_musique" title="Académie de musique">Académie de musique</a> (Academy of Music, founded in 1669) and the <a href="/wiki/Acad%C3%A9mie_royale_d%27architecture" title="Académie royale d'architecture">Académie d'architecture</a> (Academy of Architecture, founded in 1671).</p>
<p>Currently, the Académie des Beaux-Arts provides several awards including five dedicated prizes:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Liliane_Bettencourt" title="Liliane Bettencourt">Liliane Bettencourt</a> Prize for Choral Singing</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Simone_and_Cino_Del_Duca_Foundation" title="Simone and Cino Del Duca Foundation">Simone and Cino Del Duca Foundation</a> Prize for Music</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Pierre_Cardin" title="Pierre Cardin">Pierre Cardin</a> Prize for Design</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Fran%C3%A7ois-Victor_Noury&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="François-Victor Noury (page does not exist)">François-Victor Noury</a> Prize</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Fondation_Pierre_Gianadda" title="Fondation Pierre Gianadda">Fondation Pierre Gianadda</a> Prize</li>
</ul>
<p>Previously the Académie granted the <a href="/wiki/Prix_Rossini" title="Prix Rossini">Prix Rossini</a> for excellence in <a href="/wiki/Libretto" title="Libretto">libretto</a> or music composition.</p>
