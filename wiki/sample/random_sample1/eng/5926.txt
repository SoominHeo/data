<p><b>Myanmar women's national football team</b> is a female <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">association football</a> team representing <a href="/wiki/Myanmar" title="Myanmar">Myanmar</a> and controlled by <a href="/wiki/Myanmar_Football_Federation" title="Myanmar Football Federation">Myanmar Football Federation</a> (MFF). Myanmar, like <a href="/wiki/North_Korea_women%27s_national_football_team" title="North Korea women's national football team">North Korea</a>, has received more money from the state and improved its game recently, and had a goal of qualifying for the <a href="/wiki/2011_FIFA_Women%27s_World_Cup" title="2011 FIFA Women's World Cup">2011 FIFA Women's World Cup</a> in <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>. They failed, however, in the Asian qualifiers, not winning any matches, and are now focusing on the future.</p>
