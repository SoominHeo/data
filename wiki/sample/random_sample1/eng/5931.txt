<p><b>Miwa Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">三輪駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Miwa-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/Train_station" title="Train station">train station</a> of <a href="/wiki/West_Japan_Railway_Company" title="West Japan Railway Company">West Japan Railway Company</a> (JR-West) in <a href="/wiki/Sakurai,_Nara" title="Sakurai, Nara">Sakurai</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nara_Prefecture" title="Nara Prefecture">Nara</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>. Although the station is on the <a href="/wiki/Sakurai_Line" title="Sakurai Line">Sakurai Line</a> as rail infrastructure, it has been served by the <a href="/wiki/Sakurai_Line" title="Sakurai Line">Man-yō Mahoroba Line</a> since 2010 in terms of passenger train services.</p>
