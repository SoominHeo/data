<p><b>Erika Miyoshi</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">三好 絵梨香</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Miyoshi Erika</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born November 8, 1984 in <a href="/wiki/Sapporo" title="Sapporo">Sapporo</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hokkaid%C5%8D_Prefecture" title="Hokkaidō Prefecture">Hokkaidō Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>)</span> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Japanese_pop" title="Japanese pop">Japanese pop</a> singer and former member of the <a href="/wiki/Hello!_Project" title="Hello! Project">Hello! Project</a>-associated trio <a href="/wiki/V-u-den" title="V-u-den">v-u-den</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Erika_Miyoshi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In July 2003, Erika successfully passed the <i>Tsunku♂ Produce Hello! Project Shin Unit Audition</i>. In August 2004, she joined the group called <a href="/wiki/V-u-den" title="V-u-den">v-u-den</a> with former <a href="/wiki/Morning_Musume" title="Morning Musume">Morning Musume</a> member <a href="/wiki/Rika_Ishikawa" title="Rika Ishikawa">Rika Ishikawa</a> and <i>Hello! Project Egg Audition 2004</i> winner <a href="/wiki/Yui_Okada" title="Yui Okada">Yui Okada</a>.</p>
<p>In early 2006 she joined the new <a href="/wiki/Hello!_Project" title="Hello! Project">Hello! Project</a> kickball team Metro Rabbits H.P.</p>
<p>Miyoshi was cast as Kotomi Kanda in the Japanese film <i><a href="/wiki/Yo-Yo_Girl_Cop" title="Yo-Yo Girl Cop">Sukeban Deka: Codename = Asamiya Saki</a></i>, which was released on September 30, 2006.</p>
