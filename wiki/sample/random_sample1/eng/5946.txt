<p><b>Kaoru Mitsumune</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">光宗 薫</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Mitsumune Kaoru</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born April 26, 1993)</span> is a Japanese actress, model and a former member of <a href="/wiki/Japanese_idol" title="Japanese idol">Japanese idol</a> <a href="/wiki/Girl_group" title="Girl group">girl group</a> <a href="/wiki/AKB48" title="AKB48">AKB48</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kaoru_Mitsumune&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In March 2011, Mitsumune won the Grand Prix at the <a href="/wiki/Kobe_Collection" title="Kobe Collection">Kobe Collection</a> Model Audition 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> In December of the same year, she joined <a href="/wiki/AKB48" title="AKB48">AKB48</a> as part of the 13th generation trainee members.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> She made her television drama debut in <i><a href="/wiki/Ataru_(TV_series)" title="Ataru (TV series)">Ataru</a></i> aired on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tokyo_Broadcasting_System_Television" title="Tokyo Broadcasting System Television">TBS</a> in April 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tokyohive20120328_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-tokyohive20120328-4">[4]</a></sup> On October 24, 2012, she announced that she would leave the group due to her poor physical condition.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> She played the lead role for the first time in the film <i>Joshi Camera</i>, and it was released on November 24, 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>Mitsumune made a comeback to the entertainment industry appearing in the film <i>Gekijōban Ataru The First Love &amp; The Last Kill</i> released on September 14, 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> On April, 2014, it was announced that she belonged to the agency Flave Entertainment, and she resumed her activity in full swing.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
