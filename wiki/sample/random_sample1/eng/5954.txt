<p><b>Micah Sloat</b> (<small>pronounced </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_English" title="Help:IPA for English">/ˈmiːkə/</a></span>; born May 8, 1981) is an American theatre actor and musician, largely known for his leading role as Micah in the 2007 hit <a href="/wiki/Horror_film" title="Horror film">horror film</a>, <i><a href="/wiki/Paranormal_Activity" title="Paranormal Activity">Paranormal Activity</a></i>. He appeared in the horror sequel in 2010, <i><a href="/wiki/Paranormal_Activity_2" title="Paranormal Activity 2">Paranormal Activity 2</a></i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hr_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-hr-1">[1]</a></sup> and cameos in the Latino spin-off, <i><a href="/wiki/Paranormal_Activity:_The_Marked_Ones" title="Paranormal Activity: The Marked Ones">Paranormal Activity: The Marked Ones</a></i>, in 2014.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Micah_Sloat&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sloat was born in <a href="/wiki/Westport,_Connecticut" title="Westport, Connecticut">Westport, Connecticut</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lat_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-lat-2">[2]</a></sup> as the oldest of six siblings. He obtained his <a href="/wiki/Bachelor%27s_degree" title="Bachelor's degree">bachelor's degree</a> in Philosophy from <a href="/wiki/Skidmore_College" title="Skidmore College">Skidmore College</a> in 2004.</p>
<p>Sloat plays both rock and blues on the guitar and also sings with the world champion <a href="/wiki/Westminster_Chorus" title="Westminster Chorus">Westminster</a> barbershop chorus. In 2005, Sloat moved to Los Angeles and attended the <a href="/wiki/Musicians_Institute" title="Musicians Institute">Musicians Institute</a> in Hollywood and began studying acting.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hr_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-hr-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>He was still taking classes when he noticed a <a href="/wiki/Casting" title="Casting">casting</a> advertisement for <i><a href="/wiki/Paranormal_Activity" title="Paranormal Activity">Paranormal Activity</a></i> in the spring of 2006.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hr_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-hr-1">[1]</a></sup> The ad asked for unknown actors with the ability to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Improvise" title="Improvise">improvise</a> who could work during the night without knowing what the next scene would entail.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hr_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-hr-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Released widely in 2009, <i><a href="/wiki/Paranormal_Activity" title="Paranormal Activity">Paranormal Activity</a></i> proved to be an unexpected hit at the box office, grossing more than $190 million worldwide.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> He returned to the <a href="/wiki/Paranormal_Activity" title="Paranormal Activity"><i>Paranormal Activity</i> franchise</a> in the film's <a href="/wiki/Paranormal_Activity_2" title="Paranormal Activity 2">prequel</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> and had a <a href="/wiki/Cameo_appearance" title="Cameo appearance">cameo appearance</a> in the series' LA-based spin-off, <i><a href="/wiki/Paranormal_Activity:_The_Marked_Ones" title="Paranormal Activity: The Marked Ones">Paranormal Activity: The Marked Ones</a></i>, in 2014.</p>
