<p><b>Mihal Spasov Ashminov</b> (<a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_language" title="Bulgarian language">Bulgarian</a>: <span lang="bg" xml:lang="bg">Михал Спасов Ашминов</span>; born March 27, 1982) is a Bulgarian chef and television personality in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> He is master chef of <i>Zelen</i> in <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> He is also currently a cast member in the variety show <i><a href="/wiki/Please_Take_Care_of_My_Refrigerator" title="Please Take Care of My Refrigerator">Please Take Care of My Refrigerator</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal">Personal</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mihal_Ashminov&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Personal">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born to a Bulgarian father and a Polish mother in <a href="/wiki/Sofia" title="Sofia">Sofia</a>, the capital of Bulgaria. Worked at the Sheraton Hotel Balkan in Sofia before being transferred to a hotel in <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a>, where he opened his own restaurant and eventually got cast in <i><a href="/wiki/Please_Take_Care_of_My_Refrigerator" title="Please Take Care of My Refrigerator">Please Take Care of My Refrigerator</a></i>.</p>
