<p><b>Mykyta Ruslanovych Shevchenko</b> (<a href="/wiki/Ukrainian_language" title="Ukrainian language">Ukrainian</a>: <span lang="uk" xml:lang="uk">Микита Русланович Шевченко</span>, born 26 January 1993 in <a href="/wiki/Horlivka" title="Horlivka">Horlivka</a>, <a href="/wiki/Donetsk_Oblast" title="Donetsk Oblast">Donetsk Oblast</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ukraine" title="Ukraine">Ukraine</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/Ukrainians" title="Ukrainians">Ukrainian</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> <a href="/wiki/Goalkeeper_(association_football)" title="Goalkeeper (association football)">goalkeeper</a> who plays for <a href="/wiki/FC_Shakhtar_Donetsk" title="FC Shakhtar Donetsk">FC Shakhtar Donetsk</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Ukrainian_Premier_League" title="Ukrainian Premier League">Ukrainian Premier League</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mykyta_Shevchenko&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Shevchenko has been a member of different Ukrainian national youth football teams. He was called up as member of the <a href="/wiki/Ukraine_national_under-18_football_team" title="Ukraine national under-18 football team">Ukraine national under-18 football team</a> by coach <a href="/wiki/Oleh_Kuznetsov" title="Oleh Kuznetsov">Oleh Kuznetsov</a> in summer of 2010, but not play in any game.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Shevchenko was named as part of the senior <a href="/wiki/Ukraine_national_football_team" title="Ukraine national football team">Ukraine</a> squad for <a href="/wiki/UEFA_Euro_2016" title="UEFA Euro 2016">UEFA Euro 2016</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
