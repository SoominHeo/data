<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="As_a_family_name">As a family name</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Min_(Korean_name)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: As a family name">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The family name Min is written with only one hanja (<a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%96%94" title="wikt:閔">閔</a>). The 2000 South Korean Census found 142,572 people with this family name, up by 3.8 percent from 137,317 in the 1985 census. This increase was far smaller than the fifteen percent growth in the overall <a href="/wiki/Demographics_of_South_Korea" title="Demographics of South Korea">South Korean population</a> over the same period.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Census-1">[1]</a></sup> They traced their origins to only a single <i><a href="/wiki/Bon-gwan" title="Bon-gwan">bon-gwan</a></i>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Yeoheung-gun&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Yeoheung-gun (page does not exist)">Yeoheung-gun</a> (now in <a href="/wiki/Yeoju" title="Yeoju">Yeoju</a> City), <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gyeonggi-do" title="Gyeonggi-do">Gyeonggi-do</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sancheong-gun" title="Sancheong-gun">Sancheong-gun</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gyeongsangnam-do" title="Gyeongsangnam-do">Gyeongsangnam-do</a> was the place where they formed the highest concentration of the local population, with 1,100 people (2.88%), down from 2,243 (3.56%) in the 1985 census.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Census-1">[1]</a></sup> Historically, Min family is known for being one of the few clans which were in control of the government over the king during the Joseon Dynasty, along with Andong Kim and Pungyang Cho clan.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
