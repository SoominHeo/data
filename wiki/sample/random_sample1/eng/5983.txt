<p><b>Min Kyung-hoon</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">민경훈</span>; born October 6, 1984) is a South Korean singer. He is the lead vocalist of the band <a href="/wiki/Buzz_(band)" title="Buzz (band)">Buzz</a>. His first solo album, <i>Im·pres·sive</i>, was released on December 5, 2007. His second solo album, <i>Picnic</i>, was released on June 27, 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> In December 2015, he joined JTBC's new variety show, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Knowing_Bros_(TV_series)" title="Knowing Bros (TV series)">Knowing Bros</a></i>, as a cast member along with comedians <a href="/wiki/Kang_Ho-dong" title="Kang Ho-dong">Kang Ho-dong</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lee_Soo-geun" title="Lee Soo-geun">Lee Soo-geun</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
