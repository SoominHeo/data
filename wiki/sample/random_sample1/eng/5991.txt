<p><b>Min Yeong-chan</b> (1873–?), was scion of the powerful Min clan of the late <a href="/wiki/Joseon" title="Joseon">Joseon</a> period in Korea. Along with his elder brother <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Min_Yeong-hwan" title="Min Yeong-hwan">Min Yeong-hwan</a>, Yeong-chan served in a number of official positions towards the end of the Joseon dynasty. In 1900 he served as Korean commissioner to the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Paris_Universal_Exposition.&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Paris Universal Exposition. (page does not exist)">Paris Universal Exposition.</a></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Min_Yeong-chan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Min Yeong-chan was born in 1873 into the Yeoheung (여흥) Min clan. He was the younger brother of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Min_Yeong-hwan" title="Min Yeong-hwan">Min Yeong-hwan</a> and the cousin of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Queen_Min" title="Queen Min">Queen Min</a>, the consort of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gojong_of_the_Korean_Empire" title="Gojong of the Korean Empire">King Gojong</a> (Queen Min being the daughter of Min Yeong-chan's paternal uncle, Min Seung-ho). The Min clan rose to great prominence and power with its royal alliance. As such, Min Yeong-chan received the best education, studying alongside the future <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Emperor_Sunjong" title="Emperor Sunjong">Emperor Sunjong</a>. In 1889 Min passed the national civil service exam and was then named to an official position in the Hongmungwan (홍문관, 弘文館), of Office of Special Advisors. In 1897 he was named vice-president of the Hanseong Bank Corporation.</p>
<p>In 1900 Min served as royal commissioner to the <a href="/wiki/Exposition_Universelle_(1900)" title="Exposition Universelle (1900)">Paris Universal Exposition</a>.</p>
