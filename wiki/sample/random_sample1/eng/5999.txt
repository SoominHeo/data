<p>The <b>Line 4</b> is a new underground <a href="/wiki/Rapid_transit" title="Rapid transit">rapid transit</a> line under construction<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> in <a href="/wiki/Milan" title="Milan">Milan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>, part of the <a href="/wiki/Milan_Metro" title="Milan Metro">Milan Metro</a> system. The line was planned to be completed in 2015, as part of the works for the upcoming <a href="/wiki/Expo_2015" title="Expo 2015">Milan Expo</a>. However, delays in planning, financing<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> and construction works forced to postpone the opening, and the line is now expected to open in 2022.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-metro4milano_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-metro4milano-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-corriere_Jul15_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-corriere_Jul15-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-metropolitanamilanese_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-metropolitanamilanese-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>The line will be 15 km (9.3 mi) long with 21 stations.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-metropolitanamilanese_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-metropolitanamilanese-5">[5]</a></sup> The expected annual ridership is 87 million. The total estimated cost is about €1.7 billion. 786 million will be granted by the government, 512 million will come by private investors and 400 from the city of Milan.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lavori_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-lavori-6">[6]</a></sup> The line will feature completely automatic driverless trains<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> and is designed for a capacity of 24-28,000 passengers per hour in each direction.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MM1_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-MM1-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>The consortium <a href="/wiki/Azienda_Trasporti_Milanesi" title="Azienda Trasporti Milanesi">ATM</a> - <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Impregilo" title="Impregilo">Impregilo</a> will be responsible for the construction works.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lavori_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-lavori-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-jan12_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-jan12-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
