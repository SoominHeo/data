<p><br/>
Year <b>1262</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MCCLXII</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Sunday" title="Common year starting on Sunday">common year starting on Sunday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1262&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_area">By area</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1262&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By area">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1262&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>King <a href="/wiki/Mangrai" title="Mangrai">Mangrai</a> of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lanna" title="Lanna">Lanna</a> Kingdom (present day Northern Thailand, Shan State and Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Prefecture) founds the city of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chiang_Rai" title="Chiang Rai">Chiang Rai</a> as the kingdom's capital.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1262&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>King <a href="/wiki/Mindaugas" title="Mindaugas">Mindaugas</a> of <a href="/wiki/Lithuania" title="Lithuania">Lithuania</a> renounces <a href="/wiki/Christianity" title="Christianity">Christianity</a>, returning to his <a href="/wiki/Paganism" title="Paganism">pagan</a> roots and reverting to Grand Duke of Lithuania.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Icelandic_Commonwealth" title="Icelandic Commonwealth">Icelandic Commonwealth</a> enters into the <a href="/wiki/Old_Covenant_(Iceland)" title="Old Covenant (Iceland)">Old Covenant</a> (<i>Gissurarsáttmáli</i>) establishing a union with <a href="/wiki/Norway" title="Norway">Norway</a> and acknowledges King <a href="/wiki/Haakon_IV_of_Norway" title="Haakon IV of Norway">Haakon IV of Norway</a> as its ruler.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Strasbourg" title="Strasbourg">Strasbourg</a> becomes an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Imperial_Free_City" title="Imperial Free City">Imperial Free City</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Holy_Roman_Empire" title="Holy Roman Empire">Holy Roman Empire</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_topic">By topic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1262&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: By topic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Arts_and_culture">Arts and culture</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1262&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Arts and culture">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Adam_de_la_Halle" title="Adam de la Halle">Adam de la Halle</a> writes the first <a href="/wiki/Operetta" title="Operetta">operetta</a>, <i>"Le Jeu de la Feuillee"</i>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Markets">Markets</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1262&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Markets">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Venice" title="Republic of Venice">Venetian</a> Senate starts consolidating all of the city's outstanding debt into a single fund, later known as the <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Monte_Vecchio&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Monte Vecchio (page does not exist)">Monte Vecchio</a></i>. The holders of the newly created <i>prestiti</i> are promised a 5% annual coupon. These claims can be sold and quickly (before 1320) give rise to the first recorded secondary market for financial assets in medieval Europe.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
