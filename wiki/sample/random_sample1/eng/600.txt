<p>Year <b>370</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">CCCLXX</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Friday" title="Common year starting on Friday">common year starting on Friday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>. At the time, it was known as the <b>Year of the Consulship of Augustus and Valens</b> (or, less frequently, <b>year 1123 <i><a href="/wiki/Ab_urbe_condita" title="Ab urbe condita">Ab urbe condita</a></i></b>). The denomination 370 for this year has been used since the early medieval period, when the <a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a> <a href="/wiki/Calendar_era" title="Calendar era">calendar era</a> became the prevalent method in Europe for naming years.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=370&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=370&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Roman_Empire">Roman Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=370&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Roman Empire">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The presence of the <a href="/wiki/Saxons" title="Saxons">Saxons</a> in <a href="/wiki/Betuwe" title="Betuwe">Batavia</a> is noted by <a href="/wiki/Ammianus_Marcellinus" title="Ammianus Marcellinus">Ammianus Marcellinus</a>. They renew their attacks in northern <a href="/wiki/Gaul" title="Gaul">Gaul</a>. After modest success, a truce is signed and emperor <a href="/wiki/Valentinian_I" title="Valentinian I">Valentinian I</a> orders the Saxons to join the <a href="/wiki/Late_Roman_army" title="Late Roman army">Roman army</a>.</li>
<li>A law of Valentinian I and <a href="/wiki/Valens" title="Valens">Valens</a> bans marriages between <a href="/wiki/Roman_Empire" title="Roman Empire">Romans</a> and barbarians under penalty of death.</li>
<li>An <a href="/wiki/Edict" title="Edict">edict</a> issued by Valentinian I and Valens bans the importation of <a href="/wiki/Wine" title="Wine">wine</a> and <a href="/wiki/Olive_oil" title="Olive oil">olive oil</a> from areas controlled by the barbarians.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=370&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Huns" title="Huns">Huns</a> migrate westward from the <a href="/wiki/Volga_River" title="Volga River">Volga</a> into <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>, and subjugate the <a href="/wiki/Alans" title="Alans">Alans</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Ostrogoths" title="Ostrogoths">Ostrogoths</a>. With their arrival, a tradition of <a href="/wiki/Composite_bow" title="Composite bow">composite bows</a> is introduced.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Athanaric" title="Athanaric">Athanaric</a>, Gothic leader of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tervingi" title="Tervingi">Tervingi</a>, advances eastwards and takes up a defensive position along the banks of the <a href="/wiki/Dniester" title="Dniester">Dniester</a> (<a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a>).</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=370&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Former_Qin" title="Former Qin">Former Qin</a> conquers <a href="/wiki/Former_Yan" title="Former Yan">Former Yan</a> in <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>.</li>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_topic">By topic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=370&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: By topic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
