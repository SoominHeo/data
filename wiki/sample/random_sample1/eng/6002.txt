<p>The <b>Millennium Tower</b> is located on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sheikh_Zayed_Road" title="Sheikh Zayed Road">Sheikh Zayed Road</a> in <a href="/wiki/Dubai" title="Dubai">Dubai</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_Arab_Emirates" title="United Arab Emirates">United Arab Emirates</a>. The tower rises 285 m (935 ft) and has 60 floors. It was completed in 2006. The Millennium Tower contains 301 three-bedroom and 106 two-bedroom apartments. Located beside the tower is a 10-storey carpark with 471 parking bays.</p>
