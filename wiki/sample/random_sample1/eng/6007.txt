<p><b>M.I.L.K.</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">밀크</span> <i>Milk</i>) was a <a href="/wiki/K-pop" title="K-pop">K-pop</a> <a href="/wiki/Girl_group" title="Girl group">girl group</a> signed under <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/SM_Entertainment" title="SM Entertainment">SM Entertainment</a>'s sister label, BM Entertainment in 2001. They disbanded when Bae Yu-mi left the group in 2003. Now each member has gone off to do their own individual projects such as acting, singing, MCing, VJing, and <a href="/wiki/Model_(person)" title="Model (person)">modeling</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Members">Members</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Milk_(South_Korean_band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Members">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Seo_Hyun-jin">Seo Hyun-jin</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Milk_(South_Korean_band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Seo Hyun-jin">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Seo_Hyun-jin" title="Seo Hyun-jin">Seo Hyun-jin</a> was the lead singer and dancer of the group. After the group disbanded, she returned to the K-pop scene as a soloist. No official album or single has been confirmed, but she has already released four solo tracks featured on OSTs and the <a href="/wiki/SM_Town" title="SM Town">SM Town</a> compilations. After starring as the leading lady in the <a href="/wiki/TVXQ" title="TVXQ">TVXQ</a> drama <i>Dating on Earth</i>, Seo became more active in recent years as an actress, appearing in the television series <i><a href="/wiki/H.I.T_(TV_series)" title="H.I.T (TV series)">H.I.T</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Hwang_Jini_(TV_series)" title="Hwang Jini (TV series)">Hwang Jini</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Duo" title="The Duo">The Duo</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Feast_of_the_Gods_(TV_series)" title="Feast of the Gods (TV series)">Feast of the Gods</a></i>. She gained further recognition after her appearance in the drama <a href="/wiki/Let%27s_Eat_2" title="Let's Eat 2">Let's Eat 2</a>, and surged in popularity with her break-through role in 2016's <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/TvN" title="TvN">tvN</a> hit drama <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Another_Miss_Oh" title="Another Miss Oh">Another Miss Oh</a></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Kim_Bo-mi">Kim Bo-mi</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Milk_(South_Korean_band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Kim Bo-mi">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Kim Bo-mi gained popularity as the <a href="/wiki/SM_Town" title="SM Town">SM Town</a> <a href="/wiki/Video_vixen" title="Video vixen">video vixen</a> by starring as the lead female role in many SM artist's music videos, <a href="/wiki/Shinhwa" title="Shinhwa">Shinhwa</a> "Yo!" in 1999, before her debut with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/M.I.L.K." title="M.I.L.K.">M.I.L.K.</a></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Bae_Yu-mi">Bae Yu-mi</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Milk_(South_Korean_band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Bae Yu-mi">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Bae Yu-mi left <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/SM_Entertainment" title="SM Entertainment">SM Entertainment</a> in 2003, which resulted in the disbandment of the group. She joined SUM Entertainment after her departure. Yumi has been one of the more active of the former group members, having sung the opening theme to Japanese anime <i>Futakoi Alternative</i> and also having done work in dramas. However since 2006, she has not made any public appearances.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Park_Hee-von">Park Hee-von</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Milk_(South_Korean_band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Park Hee-von">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Park_Hee-von" title="Park Hee-von">Park Hee-von</a> was the leader of the group. After the disbandment of the group, Jae-young returned with the stage name, Park Hee-von. She has made several appearances in dramas and independent films. In a few M.I.L.K. songs, she was credited for writing some of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Korean_rap" title="Korean rap">Korean rap</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Verse_(popular_music)" title="Verse (popular music)">verse</a>. She married Yoon Se-young in 2016.</p>
