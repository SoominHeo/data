<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Mingshui County</h1>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Mingshui County</b> (明水县; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: Míngshuǐ Xiàn) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/County_(People%27s_Republic_of_China)" title="County (People's Republic of China)">county</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Heilongjiang_Province" title="Heilongjiang Province">Heilongjiang Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. It is under the jurisdiction of the <a href="/wiki/Prefecture-level_city" title="Prefecture-level city">prefecture-level city</a> of <a href="/wiki/Suihua" title="Suihua">Suihua</a>. Its population was 336,649 in 1999.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
