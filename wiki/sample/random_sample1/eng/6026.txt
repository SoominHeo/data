 •  HIP = <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hipparcos_Catalogue" title="Hipparcos Catalogue">Hipparcos Catalogue</a> designation number.<br/>

 •  Dec = <a href="/wiki/Declination" title="Declination">Declination</a> for the Epoch/Equinox J2000.0.</p>
