<p><b>Ba Đình</b> is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Districts_of_Vietnam" title="Districts of Vietnam">urban district</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hanoi" title="Hanoi">Hanoi</a>, the capital city of <a href="/wiki/Vietnam" title="Vietnam">Vietnam</a>. Ba Dinh is the political center of Vietnam. Most of the government offices and embassies are located here. It was formerly called the "French Quarter" (<i>Khu phố Pháp</i>), a name that is still used in travel literature.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
