<p><b>Barren Island</b> is an island located in the <a href="/wiki/Andaman_Sea" title="Andaman Sea">Andaman Sea</a>. It is the only confirmed <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Active_volcano" title="Active volcano">active volcano</a> in <a href="/wiki/South_Asia" title="South Asia">South Asia</a>. Along with the rest of the Andaman Islands, it is a part of the <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">Indian</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Union_Territory" title="Union Territory">Union Territory</a> of <a href="/wiki/Andaman_and_Nicobar_Islands" title="Andaman and Nicobar Islands">Andaman and Nicobar Islands</a>, and lies about 138 km (86 mi) northeast of the territory's capital, <a href="/wiki/Port_Blair" title="Port Blair">Port Blair</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Barren_Island_(Andaman_Islands)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The first recorded eruption of the volcano dates back to 1787. Since then, the volcano has erupted more than ten times, with the most recent one being in 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> After the first recorded eruption in 1787, further eruptions were recorded in 1789, 1795, 1803–04, and 1852. After nearly one and half <a href="/wiki/Century" title="Century">century</a> of dormancy, the <a href="/wiki/Island" title="Island">island</a> had another eruption in 1991 that lasted six <a href="/wiki/Month" title="Month">month</a>s and caused considerable damage. There were eruptions in 1994–95 and 2005–07, the latter considered to be linked to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2004_Indian_Ocean_earthquake" title="2004 Indian Ocean earthquake">2004 Indian Ocean earthquake</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> Based on Ar-Ar dating of samples from Barren Island it is now established that the oldest subaerial lava flows of the volcano is 1.6 million year old and the volcano is located on an oceanic crust which is ~106 million year old (Ray et al., Bull Volcanol 77: 57, 2015)<br/>
A Lighthouse that was established on 1993 was destroyed by the recent eruptions.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Barren_Island_(Andaman_Islands)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This volcanic island stands in the midst of a volcanic belt on the edge of the <a href="/wiki/Indian_Plate" title="Indian Plate">Indian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Burma_Plate" title="Burma Plate">Burmese</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tectonic" title="Tectonic">tectonic</a> plates. <a href="/wiki/Narcondam_Island" title="Narcondam Island">Narcondam Island</a> is a dormant volcano in the area, apart from volcanic <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seamounts" title="Seamounts">seamounts</a> like Alcock and Sewell. All the historical and recent eruptions (1789 and after) are confined within and around an active polygenetic <a href="/wiki/Cinder_cone" title="Cinder cone">cinder cone</a> in a 2 km (1.2 mi) wide <a href="/wiki/Caldera" title="Caldera">caldera</a> that was formed by the <a href="/wiki/Pleistocene" title="Pleistocene">Pleistocene</a> collapse of a primitive cone of a <a href="/wiki/Stratovolcano" title="Stratovolcano">stratovolcano</a>. The remnant of the primitive <a href="/wiki/Volcanic_cone" title="Volcanic cone">volcanic cone</a> forms a precipitous cliff around the island (commonly referred as <a href="/wiki/Caldera" title="Caldera">caldera</a> wall), with a break towards the west. The highest <a href="/wiki/Elevation" title="Elevation">elevation</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Island" title="Island">island</a> is 354 m (1,161 ft), with most of the primitive <a href="/wiki/Volcano" title="Volcano">volcano</a> <a href="/wiki/Underwater" title="Underwater">underwater</a> (standing on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seafloor" title="Seafloor">seafloor</a> 2,250 metres (7,380 ft) below <a href="/wiki/Sea_level" title="Sea level">sea level</a>). The island is 3 km (1.9 mi) in diameter, with a total surface area of 8.34 km<sup>2</sup> (3.22 sq mi).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Fauna">Fauna</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Barren_Island_(Andaman_Islands)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Fauna">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>True to its name, it is a barren area uninhabited by humans, though it has a small <a href="/wiki/Population" title="Population">population</a> of <a href="/wiki/Goat" title="Goat">goats</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> Also <a href="/wiki/Bird" title="Bird">bird</a>s, <a href="/wiki/Bat" title="Bat">bats</a> like <a href="/wiki/Pteropus" title="Pteropus">flying foxes</a> and a few <a href="/wiki/Rodent" title="Rodent">rodent</a> species such as <a href="/wiki/Rat" title="Rat">rats</a> are known to survive the harsh conditions.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Tourism">Tourism</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Barren_Island_(Andaman_Islands)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Tourism">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Scuba_diving">Scuba diving</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Barren_Island_(Andaman_Islands)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Scuba diving">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The waters surrounding Barren Island are reputed to be among the <a href="/wiki/World" title="World">world</a>'s top <a href="/wiki/Scuba_diving" title="Scuba diving">scuba diving</a> destinations. Major attractions here are the <a href="/wiki/Crystal" title="Crystal">crystal</a> clear visibility, <a href="/wiki/Manta_ray" title="Manta ray">Manta Ray</a>s, interesting <a href="/wiki/Basalt" title="Basalt">basalt</a> formations, <a href="/wiki/Topography" title="Topography">topography</a> of past <a href="/wiki/Lava" title="Lava">lava</a> flows and fast growing <a href="/wiki/Coral" title="Coral">coral</a> gardens. This dive destination is remote but can be accessed by either a live aboard ship or with scuba-operators based at <a href="/wiki/Havelock_Island" title="Havelock Island">Havelock Island</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Administration">Administration</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Barren_Island_(Andaman_Islands)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Administration">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It belongs to the <a href="/wiki/North_and_Middle_Andaman_district" title="North and Middle Andaman district">North and Middle Andaman</a> administrative <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Districts_of_India" title="Districts of India">district</a>, part of the <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">Indian</a> <a href="/wiki/Union_territory" title="Union territory">union territory</a> of <a href="/wiki/Andaman_and_Nicobar_Islands" title="Andaman and Nicobar Islands">Andaman and Nicobar Islands</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-census_12-0"><a href="#cite_note-census-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Barren_Island_(Andaman_Islands)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The island is uninhabited as of 2016.</p>
