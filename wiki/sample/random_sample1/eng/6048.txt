<p><b>Barbarossa</b>, a name meaning "red beard" in Italian, may refer to:</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="People">People</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Barbarossa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: People">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>Frederick Barbarossa, formally <a href="/wiki/Frederick_I,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Frederick I, Holy Roman Emperor">Frederick I, Holy Roman Emperor</a> (1122–1190)
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Barbarossa_city" title="Barbarossa city">Barbarossa city</a>, a nickname for five German cities that Emperor Barbarossa stayed in or near for some time</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Oru%C3%A7_Reis" title="Oruç Reis">Oruç Reis</a> (c. 1474–1518), called Barbarossa or Aruj, Ottoman Turkish captain and Bey of Algiers</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hayreddin_Barbarossa" title="Hayreddin Barbarossa">Hayreddin Barbarossa</a> (c. 1478–1546), Ottoman Turkish captain and Admiral-of-the-Fleet, brother of Oruç</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Theodore_Cotillo_Barbarossa" title="Theodore Cotillo Barbarossa">Theodore Cotillo Barbarossa</a> (1906–1992), American sculptor</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Luca_Barbarossa" title="Luca Barbarossa">Luca Barbarossa</a> (born 1961), Italian singer-songwriter</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/David_Barbarossa" title="David Barbarossa">David Barbarossa</a> (born 1961), British drummer</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li>Barbarossa, pirate and protagonist in the 1953 film <i><a href="/wiki/Raiders_of_the_Seven_Seas" title="Raiders of the Seven Seas">Raiders of the Seven Seas</a></i></li>
<li>Barbarossa (Vilhelm Jayther), a Titan in the Dune Legends prequel books</li>
<li>Hayreddin Barbarossa, a pirate captain in the Nintendo 3DS game <i><a href="/wiki/Bravely_Default" title="Bravely Default">Bravely Default</a></i>.</li>
<li>Barbarossa, a character in the book <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Thief_Lord" title="Thief Lord">Thief Lord</a></li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Operation_Barbarossa" title="Operation Barbarossa">Operation Barbarossa</a>, the Axis invasion of the Soviet Union in World War II, commencing 22 June 1941</li>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Ships">Ships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Barbarossa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Ships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Barbarossa_class_ocean_liner" title="Barbarossa class ocean liner"><i>Barbarossa</i> class ocean liner</a>, a class of ten German ocean liners built between 1896 and 1902
<ul>
<li>SS <i>Barbarossa</i>, seized by the U.S.A. during World War I and renamed <a href="/wiki/USS_Mercury_(ID-3012)" title="USS Mercury (ID-3012)">USS <i>Mercury</i> (ID-3012)</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/SMS_Kaiser_Barbarossa" title="SMS Kaiser Barbarossa">SMS <i>Kaiser Barbarossa</i></a>, a German pre-dreadnought battleship launched in 1900</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ottoman_battleship_Barbaros_Hayreddin" title="Ottoman battleship Barbaros Hayreddin"><i>Barbaros Hayreddin</i></a>, an Ottoman battleship of World War I</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bodacious_Space_Pirates#Space_Pirates" title="Bodacious Space Pirates"><i>The Barbaroosa</i></a>, A fictional pirate ship bearing a Letter of Marque, captained by Kenjo Kurihara from Miniskirt Pirates light novel series by Yūichi Sasamoto and corresponding anime Bodacious Space Pirates.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/RV_Barbaros_Hayreddin_Pa%C5%9Fa" title="RV Barbaros Hayreddin Paşa">RV <i>Barbaros Hayreddin Paşa</i></a>, a Turkish seismographic research/survey vessel owned and operated by the Turkish Petroleum Corp. (TPAO).</li>
</ul>
