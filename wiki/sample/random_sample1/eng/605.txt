<p><b>March 10</b> is the 69th day of the year (70th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 296 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Tuesday, Thursday or Saturday (58 in 400 years each) than on Sunday or Monday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Wednesday or Friday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=March_10&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/241_BC" title="241 BC">241 BC</a> – <a href="/wiki/First_Punic_War" title="First Punic War">First Punic War</a>: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Aegates_Islands" title="Battle of the Aegates Islands">Battle of the Aegates Islands</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Roman_Republic" title="Roman Republic">Romans</a> sink the <a href="/wiki/Carthage" title="Carthage">Carthaginian</a> fleet bringing the First Punic War to an end.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/298" title="298">298</a> – Roman Emperor <a href="/wiki/Maximian" title="Maximian">Maximian</a> concludes his campaign in <a href="/wiki/Africa_(Roman_province)" title="Africa (Roman province)">North Africa</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Berbers" title="Berbers">Berbers</a>, and makes a triumphal entry into <a href="/wiki/Carthage" title="Carthage">Carthage</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1607" title="1607">1607</a> – <a href="/wiki/Susenyos_I" title="Susenyos I">Susenyos I</a> defeats the combined armies of <a href="/wiki/Yaqob" title="Yaqob">Yaqob</a> and <a href="/wiki/Abuna" title="Abuna">Abuna</a> Petros II at the Battle of Gol in <a href="/wiki/Gojjam" title="Gojjam">Gojjam</a>, making him <a href="/wiki/Emperor_of_Ethiopia" title="Emperor of Ethiopia">Emperor of Ethiopia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1629" title="1629">1629</a> – <a href="/wiki/Charles_I_of_England" title="Charles I of England">Charles I of England</a> dissolves <a href="/wiki/Parliament_of_England" title="Parliament of England">Parliament</a>, beginning the eleven-year period known as the <a href="/wiki/Personal_Rule" title="Personal Rule">Personal Rule</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1735" title="1735">1735</a> – An agreement between <a href="/wiki/Nader_Shah" title="Nader Shah">Nader Shah</a> and Russia is signed near <a href="/wiki/Ganja,_Azerbaijan" title="Ganja, Azerbaijan">Ganja, Azerbaijan</a> and Russian troops are withdrawn from <a href="/wiki/Baku" title="Baku">Baku</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1762" title="1762">1762</a> – French <a href="/wiki/Huguenot" title="Huguenot">Huguenot</a> <a href="/wiki/Jean_Calas" title="Jean Calas">Jean Calas</a>, who had been wrongly convicted of killing his son, dies after being tortured by authorities; the event inspired <a href="/wiki/Voltaire" title="Voltaire">Voltaire</a> to begin a campaign for religious tolerance and legal reform.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1804" title="1804">1804</a> – <a href="/wiki/Louisiana_Purchase" title="Louisiana Purchase">Louisiana Purchase</a>: In <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/St._Louis,_Missouri" title="St. Louis, Missouri">St. Louis, Missouri</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Three_Flags_Day" title="Three Flags Day">formal ceremony</a> is conducted to transfer ownership of the <a href="/wiki/Louisiana_Territory" title="Louisiana Territory">Louisiana Territory</a> from France to the United States.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1814" title="1814">1814</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Napoleon_I_of_France" title="Napoleon I of France">Napoleon I of France</a> is defeated at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Laon" title="Battle of Laon">Battle of Laon</a> in France.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1816" title="1816">1816</a> – <a href="/wiki/Crossing_of_the_Andes" title="Crossing of the Andes">Crossing of the Andes</a>: A group of royalist scouts are captured during the <a href="/wiki/Action_of_Juncalito" title="Action of Juncalito">Action of Juncalito</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1830" title="1830">1830</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Royal_Netherlands_East_Indies_Army" title="Royal Netherlands East Indies Army">Royal Netherlands East Indies Army</a> is created.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1848" title="1848">1848</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Guadalupe_Hidalgo" title="Treaty of Guadalupe Hidalgo">Treaty of Guadalupe Hidalgo</a> is ratified by the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Senate" title="United States Senate">United States Senate</a>, ending the <a href="/wiki/Mexican%E2%80%93American_War" title="Mexican–American War">Mexican–American War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1861" title="1861">1861</a> – <a href="/wiki/El_Hadj_Umar_Tall" title="El Hadj Umar Tall">El Hadj Umar Tall</a> seizes the city of <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A9gou" title="Ségou">Ségou</a>, destroying the <a href="/wiki/Bamana_Empire" title="Bamana Empire">Bamana Empire</a> of <a href="/wiki/Mali" title="Mali">Mali</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1876" title="1876">1876</a> – <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Graham_Bell" title="Alexander Graham Bell">Alexander Graham Bell</a> makes the first successful telephone call by saying "Mr. Watson, come here, I want to see you."</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1891" title="1891">1891</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Almon_Strowger" title="Almon Strowger">Almon Strowger</a>, an undertaker in <a href="/wiki/Topeka,_Kansas" title="Topeka, Kansas">Topeka, Kansas</a>, patents the <a href="/wiki/Strowger_switch" title="Strowger switch">Strowger switch</a>, a device which led to the automation of telephone circuit switching.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1906" title="1906">1906</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Courri%C3%A8res_mine_disaster" title="Courrières mine disaster">Courrières mine disaster</a>, Europe's worst ever, kills 1099 miners in northern France.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1909" title="1909">1909</a> – By signing the <a href="/wiki/Anglo-Siamese_Treaty_of_1909" title="Anglo-Siamese Treaty of 1909">Anglo-Siamese Treaty of 1909</a>, <a href="/wiki/Thailand" title="Thailand">Thailand</a> relinquishes its <a href="/wiki/Sovereignty" title="Sovereignty">sovereignty</a> over the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Malay_states" title="Malay states">Malay states</a> of <a href="/wiki/Kedah" title="Kedah">Kedah</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kelantan" title="Kelantan">Kelantan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Perlis" title="Perlis">Perlis</a> and <a href="/wiki/Terengganu" title="Terengganu">Terengganu</a>, which become <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/British_protectorates" title="British protectorates">British protectorates</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1915" title="1915">1915</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Neuve_Chapelle" title="Battle of Neuve Chapelle">Battle of Neuve Chapelle</a> begins. This is the first large-scale operation by the <a href="/wiki/British_Army" title="British Army">British Army</a> in WWI.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1917" title="1917">1917</a> – Some <a href="/wiki/Provinces_of_the_Philippines" title="Provinces of the Philippines">provinces</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cities_of_the_Philippines" title="Cities of the Philippines">cities</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Philippines" title="Philippines">Philippines</a> are incorporated due to the ratification of Act No. 2711 or the Administrative Code of the Philippines.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1922" title="1922">1922</a> – <a href="/wiki/Mahatma_Gandhi" title="Mahatma Gandhi">Mahatma Gandhi</a> is arrested in India, tried for <a href="/wiki/Sedition" title="Sedition">sedition</a>, and sentenced to six years in prison, only to be released after nearly two years for an <a href="/wiki/Appendicitis" title="Appendicitis">appendicitis</a> operation.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1933" title="1933">1933</a> – An <a href="/wiki/1933_Long_Beach_earthquake" title="1933 Long Beach earthquake">earthquake in Long Beach, California</a> kills 115 people and causes an estimated $40 million in damage.</li>
