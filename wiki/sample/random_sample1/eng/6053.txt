<p><b>Vārkava Municipality</b> (<a href="/wiki/Latvian_language" title="Latvian language">Latvian</a>: <span lang="lv"><i>Vārkavas novads</i></span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Municipality" title="Municipality">municipality</a> in <a href="/wiki/Latgale" title="Latgale">Latgale</a>, <a href="/wiki/Latvia" title="Latvia">Latvia</a>. The municipality was formed in 2002 by <a href="/wiki/Merger_(politics)" title="Merger (politics)">merging</a> <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ro%C5%BEkalni_parish&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Rožkalni parish (page does not exist)">Rožkalni parish</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Upmale_parish&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Upmale parish (page does not exist)">Upmale parish</a>. In 2009 it absorbed <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/V%C4%81rkava_parish" title="Vārkava parish">Vārkava parish</a>, too; the administrative centre being <a href="/wiki/Vecv%C4%81rkava" title="Vecvārkava">Vecvārkava</a>.</p>
