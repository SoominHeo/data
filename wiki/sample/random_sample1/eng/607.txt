<p><b>March 27</b> is the 86th day of the year (87th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 279 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Tuesday, Friday or Sunday (58 in 400 years each) than on Wednesday or Thursday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Monday or Saturday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=March_27&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1309" title="1309">1309</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Clement_V" title="Pope Clement V">Pope Clement V</a> imposes excommunication and interdiction on Venice, and a general prohibition of all commercial intercourse with Venice, which had seized on Ferrara, a papal fiefdom.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1329" title="1329">1329</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_John_XXII" title="Pope John XXII">Pope John XXII</a> issues his <i>In Agro Dominico</i> condemning some writings of <a href="/wiki/Meister_Eckhart" title="Meister Eckhart">Meister Eckhart</a> as heretical.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1513" title="1513">1513</a> – Spanish explorer <a href="/wiki/Juan_Ponce_de_Le%C3%B3n" title="Juan Ponce de León">Juan Ponce de León</a> reaches the northern end of <a href="/wiki/The_Bahamas" title="The Bahamas">The Bahamas</a> on his first voyage to Florida.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1625" title="1625">1625</a> – <a href="/wiki/Charles_I_of_England" title="Charles I of England">Charles I</a> becomes King of England, Scotland and Ireland as well as claiming the title <a href="/wiki/English_claims_to_the_French_throne" title="English claims to the French throne">King of France</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1782" title="1782">1782</a> – <a href="/wiki/Charles_Watson-Wentworth,_2nd_Marquess_of_Rockingham" title="Charles Watson-Wentworth, 2nd Marquess of Rockingham">Charles Watson-Wentworth, 2nd Marquess of Rockingham</a> becomes <a href="/wiki/Prime_Minister_of_the_United_Kingdom" title="Prime Minister of the United Kingdom">Prime Minister of the United Kingdom</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1794" title="1794">1794</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Federal_government_of_the_United_States" title="Federal government of the United States">United States Government</a> establishes a permanent <a href="/wiki/United_States_Navy" title="United States Navy">navy</a> and authorizes the building of <a href="/wiki/Original_six_frigates_of_the_United_States_Navy" title="Original six frigates of the United States Navy">six frigates</a>.</li>
<li>1794 – Denmark and Sweden form a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Country_neutrality_(international_relations)" title="Country neutrality (international relations)">neutrality</a> compact.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1809" title="1809">1809</a> – <a href="/wiki/Peninsular_War" title="Peninsular War">Peninsular War</a>: A combined <a href="/wiki/First_French_Empire" title="First French Empire">Franco</a>-<a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Warsaw" title="Duchy of Warsaw">Polish</a> force defeats the Spanish in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Ciudad_Real" title="Battle of Ciudad Real">Battle of Ciudad Real</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1814" title="1814">1814</a> – <a href="/wiki/War_of_1812" title="War of 1812">War of 1812</a>: In central <a href="/wiki/Alabama" title="Alabama">Alabama</a>, U.S. forces under General <a href="/wiki/Andrew_Jackson" title="Andrew Jackson">Andrew Jackson</a> defeat the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Creek_(people)" title="Creek (people)">Creek</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Horseshoe_Bend_(1814)" title="Battle of Horseshoe Bend (1814)">Battle of Horseshoe Bend</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1836" title="1836">1836</a> – <a href="/wiki/Texas_Revolution" title="Texas Revolution">Texas Revolution</a>: <a href="/wiki/Goliad_massacre" title="Goliad massacre">Goliad massacre</a>: On the orders of General <a href="/wiki/Antonio_L%C3%B3pez_de_Santa_Anna" title="Antonio López de Santa Anna">Antonio López de Santa Anna</a>, the Mexican army <a href="/wiki/Goliad_massacre" title="Goliad massacre">massacres</a> 342 Texas POWs at <a href="/wiki/Goliad,_Texas" title="Goliad, Texas">Goliad, Texas</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1866" title="1866">1866</a> – President <a href="/wiki/Andrew_Johnson" title="Andrew Johnson">Andrew Johnson</a> vetoes the <a href="/wiki/Civil_Rights_Act_of_1866" title="Civil Rights Act of 1866">Civil Rights Act of 1866</a>. His veto is overridden by Congress and the bill passes into law on April 9.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1871" title="1871">1871</a> – The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1871_England_versus_Scotland_rugby_union_match" title="1871 England versus Scotland rugby union match">first</a> international <a href="/wiki/Rugby_football" title="Rugby football">rugby football</a> match, when <a href="/wiki/Scotland_national_rugby_union_team" title="Scotland national rugby union team">Scotland</a> defeats <a href="/wiki/England_national_rugby_union_team" title="England national rugby union team">England</a> in <a href="/wiki/Edinburgh" title="Edinburgh">Edinburgh</a> at <a href="/wiki/Raeburn_Place" title="Raeburn Place">Raeburn Place</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1884" title="1884">1884</a> – A mob in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cincinnati,_Ohio" title="Cincinnati, Ohio">Cincinnati, Ohio</a>, attacks members of a jury which had returned a verdict of <a href="/wiki/Manslaughter" title="Manslaughter">manslaughter</a> in what was seen as a clear case of <a href="/wiki/Murder" title="Murder">murder</a>; over the next few days the mob would <a href="/wiki/Cincinnati_riots_of_1884" title="Cincinnati riots of 1884">riot and eventually destroy the courthouse</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1886" title="1886">1886</a> – <a href="/wiki/Geronimo" title="Geronimo">Geronimo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Apache" title="Apache">Apache</a> warrior, surrenders to the U.S. Army, ending the main phase of the <a href="/wiki/Apache_Wars" title="Apache Wars">Apache Wars</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1899" title="1899">1899</a> – <a href="/wiki/Emilio_Aguinaldo" title="Emilio Aguinaldo">Emilio Aguinaldo</a> leads <a href="/wiki/Philippine_Revolutionary_Army" title="Philippine Revolutionary Army">Filipino forces</a> for the only time during the <a href="/wiki/Philippine%E2%80%93American_War" title="Philippine–American War">Philippine–American War</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Marilao_River" title="Battle of Marilao River">Battle of Marilao River</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1915" title="1915">1915</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Typhoid_Mary" title="Typhoid Mary">Typhoid Mary</a>, the first <a href="/wiki/Asymptomatic_carrier" title="Asymptomatic carrier">healthy carrier</a> of disease ever identified in the United States, is put in <a href="/wiki/Quarantine" title="Quarantine">quarantine</a>, where she would remain for the rest of her life.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1938" title="1938">1938</a> – <a href="/wiki/Second_Sino-Japanese_War" title="Second Sino-Japanese War">Second Sino-Japanese War</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Taierzhuang" title="Battle of Taierzhuang">Battle of Taierzhuang</a> begins, resulting several weeks later in the war's first major Chinese victory over Japan.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1941" title="1941">1941</a> – <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a>: <a href="/wiki/Yugoslavia" title="Yugoslavia">Yugoslavian</a> Air Force officers topple the <a href="/wiki/Axis_powers" title="Axis powers">pro-Axis</a> government in a <a href="/wiki/Yugoslav_coup_d%27%C3%A9tat" title="Yugoslav coup d'état">bloodless coup</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1943" title="1943">1943</a> – World War II: <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Komandorski_Islands" title="Battle of the Komandorski Islands">Battle of the Komandorski Islands</a>: In the <a href="/wiki/Aleutian_Islands" title="Aleutian Islands">Aleutian Islands</a> the battle begins when United States Navy forces intercept Japanese attempting to reinforce a <a href="/wiki/Garrison" title="Garrison">garrison</a> at <a href="/wiki/Kiska" title="Kiska">Kiska</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1945" title="1945">1945</a> – World War II: <a href="/wiki/Operation_Starvation" title="Operation Starvation">Operation Starvation</a>, the aerial mining of Japan's ports and waterways begins.
