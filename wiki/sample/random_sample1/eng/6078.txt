<p><b>Baohe District</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">包河区</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">包河區</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Bāohé Qū</span></i>) is a district of <a href="/wiki/Anhui" title="Anhui">Anhui Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. It is under the administration of <a href="/wiki/Hefei" title="Hefei">Hefei</a> city. The district has a surface of 294.94 km² and a population of 817,686 inhabitants.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It governs 7 subdistricts and 2 towns.</p>
