<p><b>March 29</b> is the 88th day of the year (89th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 277 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Tuesday, Thursday or Sunday (58 in 400 years each) than on Friday or Saturday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Monday or Wednesday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=March_29&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/502" title="502">502</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Gundobad" title="Gundobad">Gundobad</a> issues a new legal code (<i><a href="/wiki/Lex_Burgundionum" title="Lex Burgundionum">Lex Burgundionum</a></i>) at <a href="/wiki/Lyon" title="Lyon">Lyon</a> that makes <a href="/wiki/Gallo-Roman_culture" title="Gallo-Roman culture">Gallo-Romans</a> and <a href="/wiki/Burgundians" title="Burgundians">Burgundians</a> subject to the same <a href="/wiki/Law" title="Law">laws</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/845" title="845">845</a> – Paris is <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Paris_(845)" title="Siege of Paris (845)">sacked</a> by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Viking" title="Viking">Viking</a> raiders, probably under <a href="/wiki/Ragnar_Lodbrok" title="Ragnar Lodbrok">Ragnar Lodbrok</a>, who collects a huge ransom in exchange for leaving.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1430" title="1430">1430</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a> under <a href="/wiki/Murad_II" title="Murad II">Murad II</a> captures the <a href="/wiki/Byzantine_Empire" title="Byzantine Empire">Byzantine</a> city of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Thessalonica" title="Thessalonica">Thessalonica</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1461" title="1461">1461</a> – <a href="/wiki/Wars_of_the_Roses" title="Wars of the Roses">Wars of the Roses</a>: <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Towton" title="Battle of Towton">Battle of Towton</a>: <a href="/wiki/Edward_IV_of_England" title="Edward IV of England">Edward of York</a> defeats <a href="/wiki/Margaret_of_Anjou" title="Margaret of Anjou">Queen Margaret</a> to become King <a href="/wiki/Edward_IV_of_England" title="Edward IV of England">Edward IV of England</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1500" title="1500">1500</a> – <a href="/wiki/Cesare_Borgia" title="Cesare Borgia">Cesare Borgia</a> is given the title of <a href="/wiki/Captain_General_of_the_Church" title="Captain General of the Church">Captain General</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gonfalonier_of_the_Church" title="Gonfalonier of the Church">Gonfalonier</a> by his father <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rodrigo_Borgia" title="Rodrigo Borgia">Rodrigo Borgia</a> after returning from his conquests in the <a href="/wiki/Romagna" title="Romagna">Romagna</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1549" title="1549">1549</a> – The city of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Salvador_da_Bahia" title="Salvador da Bahia">Salvador da Bahia</a>, the first capital of <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a>, is founded.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1632" title="1632">1632</a> – <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Saint-Germain-en-Laye_(1632)" title="Treaty of Saint-Germain-en-Laye (1632)">Treaty of Saint-Germain</a> is signed returning <a href="/wiki/Quebec" title="Quebec">Quebec</a> to French control after the English had seized it in <a href="/wiki/1629" title="1629">1629</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1792" title="1792">1792</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Gustav_III_of_Sweden" title="Gustav III of Sweden">Gustav III of Sweden</a> dies after being shot in the back at a midnight <a href="/wiki/Masquerade_ball" title="Masquerade ball">masquerade ball</a> at <a href="/wiki/Stockholm" title="Stockholm">Stockholm</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Royal_Swedish_Opera" title="Royal Swedish Opera">Royal Opera</a> 13 days earlier. He is succeeded by <a href="/wiki/Gustav_IV_Adolf_of_Sweden" title="Gustav IV Adolf of Sweden">Gustav IV Adolf</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1806" title="1806">1806</a> – Construction is authorized of the Great National Pike, better known as the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cumberland_Road" title="Cumberland Road">Cumberland Road</a>, becoming the first <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> federal <a href="/wiki/Highway#United_States" title="Highway">highway</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1809" title="1809">1809</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Gustav_IV_Adolf_of_Sweden" title="Gustav IV Adolf of Sweden">Gustav IV Adolf of Sweden</a> <a href="/wiki/Abdication" title="Abdication">abdicates</a> after a <i><a href="/wiki/Coup_d%27%C3%A9tat" title="Coup d'état">coup d'état</a></i>. At the <a href="/wiki/Diet_of_Porvoo" title="Diet of Porvoo">Diet of Porvoo</a>, Finland's <a href="/wiki/Riksdag_of_the_Estates" title="Riksdag of the Estates">four Estates</a> pledge allegiance to <a href="/wiki/Alexander_I_of_Russia" title="Alexander I of Russia">Alexander I of Russia</a>, commencing the secession of the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Finland" title="Grand Duchy of Finland">Grand Duchy of Finland</a> from <a href="/wiki/Lands_of_Sweden" title="Lands of Sweden">Sweden</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1831" title="1831">1831</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Great_Bosnian_uprising" title="Great Bosnian uprising">Great Bosnian uprising</a>: <a href="/wiki/Bo%C5%A1njani" title="Bošnjani">Bosniaks</a> rebel against <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1847" title="1847">1847</a> – <a href="/wiki/Mexican%E2%80%93American_War" title="Mexican–American War">Mexican–American War</a>: United States forces led by General <a href="/wiki/Winfield_Scott" title="Winfield Scott">Winfield Scott</a> take <a href="/wiki/Veracruz_(city)" title="Veracruz (city)">Veracruz</a> after a <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Veracruz" title="Siege of Veracruz">siege</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1849" title="1849">1849</a> – The <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom_of_Great_Britain_and_Ireland" title="United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland">United Kingdom</a> annexes the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Punjab_region" title="Punjab region">Punjab</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1857" title="1857">1857</a> – <a href="/wiki/Sepoy" title="Sepoy">Sepoy</a> <a href="/wiki/Mangal_Pandey" title="Mangal Pandey">Mangal Pandey</a> of the 34th <a href="/wiki/Regiment" title="Regiment">Regiment</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bengal_Native_Infantry" title="Bengal Native Infantry">Bengal Native Infantry</a> mutinies against the <a href="/wiki/East_India_Company" title="East India Company">East India Company</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Company_rule_in_India" title="Company rule in India">rule in India</a> and inspires the protracted <a href="/wiki/Indian_Rebellion_of_1857" title="Indian Rebellion of 1857">Indian Rebellion of 1857</a>, also known as the Sepoy Mutiny.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1865" title="1865">1865</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Civil_War" title="American Civil War">American Civil War</a>: Federal forces under Major General <a href="/wiki/Philip_Sheridan" title="Philip Sheridan">Philip Sheridan</a> move to flank Confederate forces under <a href="/wiki/Robert_E._Lee" title="Robert E. Lee">Robert E. Lee</a> as the <a href="/wiki/Appomattox_Campaign" title="Appomattox Campaign">Appomattox Campaign</a> begins.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1867" title="1867">1867</a> – <a href="/wiki/Queen_Victoria" title="Queen Victoria">Queen Victoria</a> gives <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Royal_Assent" title="Royal Assent">Royal Assent</a> to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/British_North_America_Act" title="British North America Act">British North America Act</a> which establishes the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dominion_of_Canada" title="Dominion of Canada">Dominion of Canada</a> on <a href="/wiki/July_1" title="July 1">July 1</a>.</li>
