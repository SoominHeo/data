<p><i><b>Aconaemys</b></i> is a genus of <a href="/wiki/Rodent" title="Rodent">rodent</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Octodontidae" title="Octodontidae">Octodontidae</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MSW3_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-MSW3-1">[1]</a></sup> It contains the following species:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Chilean_rock_rat" title="Chilean rock rat">Chilean rock rat</a> (<i>Aconaemys fuscus</i>)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Porter%27s_rock_rat" title="Porter's rock rat">Porter's rock rat</a> (<i>Aconaemys porteri</i>)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sage%27s_rock_rat" title="Sage's rock rat">Sage's rock rat</a> (<i>Aconaemys sagei</i>)</li>
</ul>
