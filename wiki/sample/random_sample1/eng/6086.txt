<p><b>Bay</b> (<a href="/wiki/Somali_language" title="Somali language">Somali</a>: <span lang="so" xml:lang="so"><i>Baay</i></span>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar" xml:lang="ar">باي</span>‎‎) is an administrative region (<i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Regions_of_Somalia" title="Regions of Somalia">gobol</a></i>) in southern <a href="/wiki/Somalia" title="Somalia">Somalia</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Cwfsom_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Cwfsom-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Overview">Overview</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bay,_Somalia&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Overview">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It is bordered by the Somalian regions of <a href="/wiki/Bakool" title="Bakool">Bakool</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hiran,_Somalia" title="Hiran, Somalia">Hiran</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lower_Shebelle" title="Lower Shebelle">Lower Shebelle</a> (Shabeellaha Hoose), <a href="/wiki/Middle_Juba" title="Middle Juba">Middle Juba</a> (Jubbada Dhexe), and <a href="/wiki/Gedo" title="Gedo">Gedo</a>.</p>
<p>Baidoa used to be the capital of the old Upper <a href="/wiki/Jubaland" title="Jubaland">Juba</a> region, which today also includes Gedo and Bakool, as well as most parts of the Middle Juba region. Present regions were created in the 1970s by the then ruling military regime. Bay's current capital is <a href="/wiki/Baidoa" title="Baidoa">Baidoa</a>.</p>
