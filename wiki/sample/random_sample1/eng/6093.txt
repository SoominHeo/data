<p>"<b>Bye Bye</b>" (バイバイ) is the 19th single from Japanese singer <a href="/wiki/Ai_Otsuka" title="Ai Otsuka">Ai Otsuka</a>, and the fourth single to be released from her <i><a href="/wiki/Love_Letter_(Ai_Otsuka_album)" title="Love Letter (Ai Otsuka album)">Love Letter</a></i> album. It is her second re-cut single, and it will be the CM song for the <a href="/wiki/Asahi_Breweries" title="Asahi Breweries">Asahi Breweries</a> LTD new low-alcohol beverage, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=The_Asahi_Slat&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="The Asahi Slat (page does not exist)">The Asahi Slat</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
