<p><b>Baia e Latina</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Caserta" title="Province of Caserta">Province of Caserta</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Campania" title="Campania">Campania</a>, located about 50 kilometres (31 mi) north of <a href="/wiki/Naples" title="Naples">Naples</a> and about 25 kilometres (16 mi) northwest of <a href="/wiki/Caserta" title="Caserta">Caserta</a>.</p>
<p>Baia Latina borders the following municipalities: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Alife_(CE)" title="Alife (CE)">Alife</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dragoni" title="Dragoni">Dragoni</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pietravairano" title="Pietravairano">Pietravairano</a>, <a href="/wiki/Roccaromana" title="Roccaromana">Roccaromana</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sant%27Angelo_d%27Alife" title="Sant'Angelo d'Alife">Sant'Angelo d'Alife</a>.</p>
