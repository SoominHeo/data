<p><b>Battle of Bekeriyah</b> was a major battle of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saudi%E2%80%93Rashidi_War" title="Saudi–Rashidi War">Saudi–Rashidi War</a>, during the <a href="/wiki/Unification_of_Saudi_Arabia" title="Unification of Saudi Arabia">Unification of Saudi Arabia</a> campaign, between <a href="/wiki/Rashidi_dynasty" title="Rashidi dynasty">Rashidi</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ibn_Saud" title="Ibn Saud">Saudi</a> rebels. It occurred in June 1904 at the town of <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Bekeriyah&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Bekeriyah (page does not exist)">Bekeriyah</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Al-Qassim_Province" title="Al-Qassim Province">Qassim region</a>. After Ibn Saud's victory in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Dilam_(1903)" title="Battle of Dilam (1903)">Battle of Dilam</a>, Ibn Saud tried to expand his power by capturing Qassim, weakening his Rashidi enemies and their <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman</a> allies. The battle ended in Ha'il victory with heavy casualties on both sides.</p>
