<p><b>Baku National Stadium</b> is a 69,870-seat stadium,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-UEFA_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-UEFA-1">[1]</a></sup> designed and constructed to meet the international standards for stadiums set by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Union_of_European_Football_Associations" title="Union of European Football Associations">Union of European Football Associations</a> (UEFA), the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/International_Federation_of_Association_Football" title="International Federation of Association Football">International Federation of Association Football</a> (FIFA) and the <a href="/wiki/International_Association_of_Athletics_Federations" title="International Association of Athletics Federations">International Association of Athletics Federations</a> (IAAF). This is the <a href="/wiki/List_of_football_stadiums_in_Azerbaijan" title="List of football stadiums in Azerbaijan">largest stadium in the country</a>.</p>
<p>Construction of the 225,000-square-meter stadium on a 650,000-square-meter site was completed in February 2015. The six-story, 65.7 meter structure near <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Boyukshor_Lake" title="Boyukshor Lake">Boyukshor Lake</a>, <a href="/wiki/Baku" title="Baku">Baku</a>, <a href="/wiki/Azerbaijan" title="Azerbaijan">Azerbaijan</a>, opened on 6 March 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The main tenant of the stadium is Azerbaijan's <a href="/wiki/Azerbaijan_national_football_team" title="Azerbaijan national football team">national football team</a>, who moved from their previous home at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tofiq_Bahramov_Stadium" title="Tofiq Bahramov Stadium">Tofiq Bahramov Stadium</a>. In June 2015 the stadium served as the main venue for the <a href="/wiki/2015_European_Games" title="2015 European Games">European Games</a>, hosting the opening and closing ceremonies, and the athletic games. The venue is intended to host the quarterfinals and three of the group games for <a href="/wiki/UEFA_Euro_2020" title="UEFA Euro 2020">Euro 2020</a>. A green area is being developed around the stadium, which is accessible via public transport.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Baku_National_Stadium&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="The_ceremony_of_laying_the_foundation">The ceremony of laying the foundation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Baku_National_Stadium&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: The ceremony of laying the foundation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>June 6, 2011 in celebration of the 100th anniversary of Azerbaijani football was held a ceremony of laying the foundation of the Baku National Stadium. The ceremony was attended by President of Azerbaijan <a href="/wiki/Ilham_Aliyev" title="Ilham Aliyev">Ilham Aliyev</a> and his spouse <a href="/wiki/Mehriban_Aliyeva" title="Mehriban Aliyeva">Mehriban Aliyeva</a>, and FIFA President <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Joseph_Blatter" title="Joseph Blatter">Joseph Blatter</a> and UEFA President <a href="/wiki/Michel_Platini" title="Michel Platini">Michel Platini</a>, prominent members of the global football community, heads of various federations, football veterans and other guests. Azerbaijani President Ilham Aliyev was informed about the future plans for the construction of the stadium. Speaking at the ceremony, FIFA President Joseph S. Blatter said:</p>
<p><i>"This is really beautiful, grand building, and more than 60 thousand football fans coming to the stadium will be able to enjoy football. "</i></p>
<p>Then Ilham Aliyev put a symbolic metal capsule inside the ball. The son of the president, Heydar Aliyev, scoring a goal in a symbolic gate, dropped it into the foundation. Later, President Ilham Aliyev, FIFA President Joseph S. Blatter and UEFA President Michel Platini, switched on the installation of concrete pouring and signed on a soccer ball, which will be stored in the memory of the ceremony</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Construction">Construction</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Baku_National_Stadium&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Construction">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Despite the groundbreaking ceremony having taken place in 2011, the construction of the stadium started only in November 2012 with the excavation and backfilling of the stadium area.</p>
<p>The opening of the stadium took place on March 6, 2015, President Ilham Aliyev participated in the opening ceremony.</p>
<p>Location : Baku, Azerbaijan Client  : State Oil Company of Azerbaijan Republic (SOCAR) Contractor : TEKFEN Construction and Installation Co., INC.</p>
<p>Scope : Turn Key Design and Build of Baku Olympic Stadium Project</p>
<p>Commencement : 27.02.2013</p>
<p>Completion : 28.02.2015</p>
<p>Total Layout Area : 617,000m²</p>
<p>Stadium Capacity : 68,000 ea Seating</p>
<p>Seats at the Warm-up Stadium : 1,800 Other Buildings : MEP Building, Info center and Two External Toilet Buildings</p>
<p>Baku was ready to host the first ever European Games in 2015, with The National Stadium Project as the most important and prestigious project undertaken by Azerbaijan for the games. Main Stadium layout area is 87,000 m2, Total Confined Space 215,000 m2, Maximum Height from ground 60.08 m2, 6-Levels Building, Total Car Parking Area Capacity 3,078 cars and VVIP Suites with 126, VIP Suites with 500 spectator’s capacity. • Total 7,000,000 m³ soil movement completed • Piles: 2,469 pcs (Length: 35m, Diameter: 1.2m) • Total 360,000 m³ concrete poured • Reinforcement Steel: 55,000 tons • Formwork: 415,000² • Structural Steel: 21,000tons • Precast Concrete Elements: 8,186pcs • Cable: 2,000,000 m • Sanitary and Fire Piping: 120,000 m • Air Duct: 42,000 m² • Aerated Concrete Wall: 55,000 m² • Aluminum Joinery: 10,000 m² • Total 3300 trucks of materials had been transported • Total 60,000m² Façade and Roof Covering with ETFE • Total 400,000m3 Scaffolding</p>

<p>The project is being funded by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/State_Oil_Company_of_Azerbaijan_Republic" title="State Oil Company of Azerbaijan Republic">SOCAR</a>. A Turkish company <a href="/wiki/Tekfen_Construction_and_Installation" title="Tekfen Construction and Installation">Tekfen Construction and Installation Co.,Inc</a>, were given a Design&amp;Build contract.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>Along with the stadium, there were built a number of hotels, parking venue (total 3,617 car places), and green space (81,574 square meters).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>Stadium contains VVIP, VIP - CIP Suites total 127 each with 720 spectator's capacity, total special area of interior 25,200 m2 and Total Car Parking Area Capacity 3,078 cars. 1,800 Seating Capacity Warm Up Area, MEP Building, Info Centre and Two External Buildings are other building of Projects. Design and Engineering Service, Procurement and Logistic Services, Over All Turn Key Coordination Services, HSE Management Service, QA/QC Management Service, Construction Supervision Services and Planning and Project Controls Service.</p>

<p>The venue will host games for the <a href="/wiki/Azerbaijan_national_football_team" title="Azerbaijan national football team">Azerbaijan national football team</a> as well as concerts. Baku National Stadium fulfils <a href="/wiki/FIFA" title="FIFA">FIFA</a>'s and <a href="/wiki/UEFA" title="UEFA">UEFA</a>'s highest technical recommendations and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UEFA_elite_stadium" title="UEFA elite stadium">elite stadium obligations</a> for football stadiums and is automatically qualified to arrange tournaments and qualification games in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/European_Football_Championships" title="European Football Championships">European Football Championships</a>. The stadium hosted <a href="/wiki/Athletics_at_the_2015_European_Games" title="Athletics at the 2015 European Games">athletics</a> and ceremonies during <a href="/wiki/2015_European_Games" title="2015 European Games">2015 European Games</a> and will host Quarter-finals and Group stage matches during <a href="/wiki/UEFA_Euro_2020" title="UEFA Euro 2020">UEFA Euro 2020</a>.</p>
