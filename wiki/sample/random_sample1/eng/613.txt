<p>Year <b>401</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">CDI</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Tuesday" title="Common year starting on Tuesday">common year starting on Tuesday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>. At the time, it was known as the <b>Year of the Consulship of Vincentius and Fravitus</b> (or, less frequently, <b>year 1154 <i><a href="/wiki/Ab_urbe_condita" title="Ab urbe condita">Ab urbe condita</a></i></b>). The denomination 401 for this year has been used since the early medieval period, when the <a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a> <a href="/wiki/Calendar_era" title="Calendar era">calendar era</a> became the prevalent method in Europe for naming years.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=401&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=401&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Roman_Empire">Roman Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=401&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Roman Empire">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>Emperor <a href="/wiki/Arcadius" title="Arcadius">Arcadius</a> sends many gifts to the <a href="/wiki/Huns" title="Huns">Hunnish</a> chieftain <a href="/wiki/Uldin" title="Uldin">Uldin</a>, in appreciation of his victory over the <a href="/wiki/Goths" title="Goths">Goths</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gainas" title="Gainas">Gainas</a>. Arcadius then allies himself with the Huns.</li>
<li>Piracy is committed by <a href="/wiki/Slavery" title="Slavery">slavetraders</a> from <a href="/wiki/Galatia_(Roman_province)" title="Galatia (Roman province)">Galatia</a> (<a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>), along the coasts of <a href="/wiki/Africa_(Roman_province)" title="Africa (Roman province)">Africa</a>. The old <a href="/wiki/Legio_II_Adiutrix" title="Legio II Adiutrix">Legio II Adiutrix</a>, part of which had always been stationed at <a href="/wiki/Aquincum" title="Aquincum">Aquincum</a> (modern <a href="/wiki/Budapest" title="Budapest">Budapest</a>), is divided into two <i><a href="/wiki/Comitatenses" title="Comitatenses">comitatenses</a></i> and shipped to <a href="/wiki/Roman_Britain" title="Roman Britain">Britannia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Stilicho" title="Stilicho">Stilicho</a>, Roman general (<i><a href="/wiki/Magister_militum" title="Magister militum">magister militum</a></i>), leads his army in an extensive campaign against the <a href="/wiki/Vandals" title="Vandals">Vandals</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rhaetia" title="Rhaetia">Rhaetia</a> (<a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Switzerland</a>).</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Temple_of_Artemis" title="Temple of Artemis">Temple of Artemis</a> near <a href="/wiki/Sel%C3%A7uk" title="Selçuk">Selçuk</a> in <a href="/wiki/Ephesus" title="Ephesus">Ephesus</a> is dismantled.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_18" title="November 18">November 18</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Visigoths" title="Visigoths">Visigoths</a>, led by king <a href="/wiki/Alaric_I" title="Alaric I">Alaric I</a>, cross the <a href="/wiki/Alps" title="Alps">Alps</a> and invade northern <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>. Emperor <a href="/wiki/Honorius_(emperor)" title="Honorius (emperor)">Honorius</a> moves his capital to the coastal city of <a href="/wiki/Ravenna" title="Ravenna">Ravenna</a>, which is protected by <a href="/wiki/Marsh" title="Marsh">marshes</a> and strong <a href="/wiki/Fortification" title="Fortification">fortifications</a>. Stilicho begins the process of reclaiming the city.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Black_Sea">Black Sea</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=401&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Black Sea">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The <i><a href="/wiki/Chronicon_Paschale" title="Chronicon Paschale">Chronicon Pachale</a></i> (Paschal Chronicle) reports that the <a href="/wiki/Black_Sea" title="Black Sea">Black Sea</a> is completely frozen.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="China">China</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=401&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: China">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kumarajiva" title="Kumarajiva">Kumarajiva</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kucha" title="Kucha">Kuchean</a> Buddhist <a href="/wiki/Bhikkhu" title="Bhikkhu">monk</a>, arrives in <a href="/wiki/Chang%27an" title="Chang'an">Chang'an</a> and begins translating <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Buddhist" title="Buddhist">Buddhist</a> texts into <a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_topic">By topic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=401&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: By topic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Religion">Religion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=401&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Religion">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/December_19" title="December 19">December 19</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Anastasius_I" title="Pope Anastasius I">Pope Anastasius I</a> dies at <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a> after a 2-year reign, and is succeeded by <a href="/wiki/Pope_Innocent_I" title="Pope Innocent I">Innocent I</a> as the 40th <a href="/wiki/Pope" title="Pope">pope</a> proclaiming his universal power over the whole of <a href="/wiki/Christendom" title="Christendom">Christendom</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Mahabharata" title="Mahabharata">Mahabharata</a>, an <a href="/wiki/History_of_India" title="History of India">Indian</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sanskrit_literature" title="Sanskrit literature">Sanskrit</a> <a href="/wiki/Indian_epic_poetry" title="Indian epic poetry">epic</a>, is finalised.</li>
</ul>
