<div class="hatnote" role="note">This is a <a href="/wiki/Korean_name" title="Korean name">Korean name</a>; the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Korean_family_names" title="List of Korean family names">family name</a> is <a href="/wiki/Park_(Korean_surname)" title="Park (Korean surname)"><i>Park</i></a>.</div>
<p><b>Park Dong-Hyuk</b> (born 18 April 1979) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">football defender</a>, who currently plays for <a href="/wiki/K_League_Classic" title="K League Classic">K League Classic</a> club <a href="/wiki/Ulsan_Hyundai_FC" title="Ulsan Hyundai FC">Ulsan Hyundai</a>. His previous clubs are <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jeonbuk_Hyundai_Motors" title="Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors">Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gamba_Osaka" title="Gamba Osaka">Gamba Osaka</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kashiwa_Reysol" title="Kashiwa Reysol">Kashiwa Reysol</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dalian_Shide" title="Dalian Shide">Dalian Shide</a>. He was a part of <a href="/wiki/South_Korea_national_under-23_football_team" title="South Korea national under-23 football team">South Korea</a> who of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Summer_Olympics" title="Summer Olympics">Summer Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/2000_Summer_Olympics" title="2000 Summer Olympics">2000</a>.</p>
<p>On 16 December 2011, Park signed a contract with <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Super_League" title="Chinese Super League">Chinese Super League</a> club <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dalian_Shide" title="Dalian Shide">Dalian Shide</a>.</p>
