<p><b>Park Sang-min</b> (born October 9, 1970) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> actor. He is best known for <a href="/wiki/Im_Kwon-taek" title="Im Kwon-taek">Im Kwon-taek</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/General%27s_Son" title="General's Son">General's Son</a></i> trilogy and his roles in numerous <a href="/wiki/Korean_drama" title="Korean drama">television dramas</a> such as <i>My Lovely Fool</i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Giant_(TV_series)" title="Giant (TV series)">Giant</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Park has had run-ins with the law: a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Drunk_driving" title="Drunk driving">drunk driving</a> arrest in 2011,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> and a <a href="/wiki/Domestic_violence" title="Domestic violence">domestic violence</a> charge in 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p></p>
