<p><b>Park Sung-ho</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 박성호; born 17 July 1982) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> <a href="/wiki/Forward_(association_football)" title="Forward (association football)">forward</a>, who currently plays for <a href="/wiki/Ulsan_Hyundai_FC" title="Ulsan Hyundai FC">Ulsan Hyundai</a>. His previous clubs were <a href="/wiki/FC_Seoul" title="FC Seoul">Anyang LG Cheetahs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Busan_IPark" title="Busan IPark">Busan IPark</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Daejeon_Citizen" title="Daejeon Citizen">Daejeon Citizen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pohang_Steelers" title="Pohang Steelers">Pohang Steelers</a> and <a href="/wiki/J1_League" title="J1 League">J1 League</a> side <a href="/wiki/Vegalta_Sendai" title="Vegalta Sendai">Vegalta Sendai</a>.</p>
<p>On 3 November 2011, he was traded with <a href="/wiki/Kim_Dong-hee_(footballer)" title="Kim Dong-hee (footballer)">Kim Dong-hee</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lee_Seul-gi" title="Lee Seul-gi">Lee Seul-gi</a> of <a href="/wiki/Pohang_Steelers" title="Pohang Steelers">Pohang Steelers</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
