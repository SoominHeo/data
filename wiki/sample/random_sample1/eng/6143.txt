<p><b>Park Se-jin</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">박세진</span>; born 15 December 1995) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)#Full-back" title="Defender (association football)">full back</a> for <a href="/wiki/Daegu_FC" title="Daegu FC">Daegu FC</a> in <a href="/wiki/K_League_Challenge" title="K League Challenge">K League Challenge</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Park_Se-jin&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Park joined <a href="/wiki/K_League_Challenge" title="K League Challenge">K League Challenge</a> side <a href="/wiki/Daegu_FC" title="Daegu FC">Daegu FC</a> in January 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> He made his professional debut in the opening game of the <a href="/wiki/2016_K_League_Challenge" title="2016 K League Challenge">2016 season</a> against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Daejeon_Citizen" title="Daejeon Citizen">Daejeon Citizen</a> on 26 March.</p>
