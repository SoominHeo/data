<p><b>Park Eun-Kyung</b>(born January 25, 1975) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Field_hockey" title="Field hockey">field hockey</a> player.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> At the <a href="/wiki/2004_Summer_Olympics" title="2004 Summer Olympics">2004</a> and <a href="/wiki/2008_Summer_Olympics" title="2008 Summer Olympics">2008 Summer Olympics</a> she competed with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Korea_women%27s_national_field_hockey_team" title="Korea women's national field hockey team">Korea women's national field hockey team</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Field_hockey_at_the_2008_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Women%27s_tournament" title="Field hockey at the 2008 Summer Olympics – Women's tournament">women's tournament</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sref_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-sref-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
