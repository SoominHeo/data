<p><b>Park Eun-bin</b> (born September 4, 1992) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> actress. She debuted at the age of 7 and has starred in numerous <a href="/wiki/Korean_drama" title="Korean drama">TV dramas</a> as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Child_actress" title="Child actress">child actress</a>. She played her first leading role in time-traveling romance <i><a href="/wiki/Operation_Proposal" title="Operation Proposal">Operation Proposal</a></i> (2012).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
