<p><b>Park Jae-seung</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: 박재승, <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">Hanja</a>: 朴在昇, born 1 April 1923) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">defender</a> who played for the <a href="/wiki/South_Korea_national_football_team" title="South Korea national football team">South Korea</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1954_FIFA_World_Cup" title="1954 FIFA World Cup">1954 FIFA World Cup</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> He also played for <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Seoul_Army_Club&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Seoul Army Club (page does not exist)">Seoul Army Club</a>.</p>
