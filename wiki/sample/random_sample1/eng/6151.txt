<p><b>Park Chung-il</b> (박정일, born November 19, 1959, in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>) is a South Korean footballer. He was first goal scorer of <a href="/wiki/FC_Seoul" title="FC Seoul">FC Seoul</a>, then known as Lucky-Goldstar Hwangso.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
