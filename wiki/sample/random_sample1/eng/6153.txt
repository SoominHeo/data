<p><b>Park Jun-Tae</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko" xml:lang="ko">박준태</span>; born 2 December 1989) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Forward_(football)" title="Forward (football)">Forward</a>, who plays for <a href="/wiki/Busan_IPark" title="Busan IPark">Busan IPark</a> in the <a href="/wiki/K_League_Challenge" title="K League Challenge">K League Challenge</a>. He has previously played for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ulsan_Hyundai" title="Ulsan Hyundai">Ulsan Hyundai</a>, <a href="/wiki/Incheon_United_FC" title="Incheon United FC">Incheon United</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jeonnam_Dragons" title="Jeonnam Dragons">Jeonnam Dragons</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Park_Jun-tae&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Picked from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/K-League" title="K-League">K-League</a> draft by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ulsan_Hyundai" title="Ulsan Hyundai">Ulsan Hyundai</a> for the 2009 season, Park made the majority of his appearances in 2009 from the bench as a substitute. He featured in the <a href="/wiki/2009_AFC_Champions_League" title="2009 AFC Champions League">2009 AFC Champions League</a>, including a start in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ulsan_Hyundai" title="Ulsan Hyundai">Ulsan</a>'s away loss to Australian club <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Newcastle_Jets" title="Newcastle Jets">Newcastle Jets</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>After a single match for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ulsan_Hyundai" title="Ulsan Hyundai">Ulsan Hyundai</a> in 2010, he then spent the remainder of the 2010 season on loan to <a href="/wiki/Korea_National_League" title="Korea National League">National League</a> club <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ulsan_Hyundai_Mipo_Dockyard" title="Ulsan Hyundai Mipo Dockyard">Ulsan Hyundai Mipo Dockyard</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Park returned to the K-League with a shift to <a href="/wiki/Incheon_United_FC" title="Incheon United FC">Incheon United</a> for the 2011 season. On 17 April 2011, Park scored his first professional goal in the dying minutes of the match against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seongnam_Ilhwa_Chunma" title="Seongnam Ilhwa Chunma">Seongnam Ilhwa Chunma</a>, helping his side to a 2 -1 win.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
