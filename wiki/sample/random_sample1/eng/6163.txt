<p><b>Park Ha-na</b> (born July 25, 1985) is a South Korean actress. Park made her entertainment debut in 2003 as a member of the idol group <i>FUNNY</i>. Since then, she has turned to acting and was in the lead role in <i><a href="/wiki/Apgujeong_Midnight_Sun" title="Apgujeong Midnight Sun">Apgujeong Midnight Sun</a></i> (2014).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
