<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Park_Han-byul&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>As a student at Anyang Art High School,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Park posted photographs of herself on the internet, becoming an online celebrity due to her <a href="/wiki/Look-alike" title="Look-alike">close physical resemblance</a> to actress <a href="/wiki/Jun_Ji-hyun" title="Jun Ji-hyun">Jun Ji-hyun</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> After being signed by an entertainment agency, she made her acting debut in the 2003 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/K-Horror" title="K-Horror">horror</a> film <i><a href="/wiki/Wishing_Stairs" title="Wishing Stairs">Wishing Stairs</a></i>, her part requiring her to learn <a href="/wiki/Ballet" title="Ballet">ballet</a> over a two-month period of rigorous training.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>Having since appeared in a succession of <a href="/wiki/Korean_drama" title="Korean drama">television drama series</a>, Park returned to the big screen with a role in the 2008 film <i>Fate</i>. Her next project will be a film titled <i>Yoga School</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Lee_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Lee-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>In July 2012, Park co-starred with <a href="/wiki/Kim_Ji-seok_(actor)" title="Kim Ji-seok (actor)">Kim Ji-seok</a> in horror film <i>Two Moons</i>, playing the role of So Hee, a horror fiction novelist with a hidden secret.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Park_Han-byul&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In June 2009, <a href="/wiki/K-pop" title="K-pop">K-pop</a> singer <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seven_(South_Korean_singer)" title="Seven (South Korean singer)">Se7en</a> acknowledged on his website that he and Park had been in a relationship for seven years, with the couple having first met when they were seniors in high school. Se7en had previously denied rumors that they were a couple in order to protect Park's privacy and let the relationship grow naturally.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Lee_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-Lee-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> <span>On December 23, 2014, a statement was released by Park's agency stating that she and Se7en had ended their 12-year relationship earlier on in the year.</span><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
