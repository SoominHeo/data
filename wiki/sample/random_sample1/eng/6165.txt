<p><b>Park Hyung-jin</b> (朴亨鎮, 24 June 1990) is a South Korean <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> player. He currently plays for <a href="/wiki/V-Varen_Nagasaki" title="V-Varen Nagasaki">V-Varen Nagasaki</a> in the <a href="/wiki/J2_League" title="J2 League">J2 League</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-jleague_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-jleague-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>His elder brother, <a href="/wiki/Park_Jin-soo" title="Park Jin-soo">Park Jin-soo</a>, is also a football player.</p>
