<p>Year <b>426</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">CDXXVI</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Friday" title="Common year starting on Friday">common year starting on Friday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>. At the time, it was known as the <b>Year of the Consulship of Theodosius and Valentinianus</b> (or, less frequently, <b>year 1179 <i><a href="/wiki/Ab_urbe_condita" title="Ab urbe condita">Ab urbe condita</a></i></b>). The denomination 426 for this year has been used since the early medieval period, when the <a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a> <a href="/wiki/Calendar_era" title="Calendar era">calendar era</a> became the prevalent method in Europe for naming years.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=426&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=426&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Roman_Empire">Roman Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=426&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Roman Empire">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>Emperor <a href="/wiki/Theodosius_II" title="Theodosius II">Theodosius II</a> gives orders to destroy the buildings and pagan <a href="/wiki/Temple" title="Temple">temples</a> at <a href="/wiki/Olympia,_Greece" title="Olympia, Greece">Olympia</a> (<a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greece</a>). The <a href="/wiki/Statue_of_Zeus_at_Olympia" title="Statue of Zeus at Olympia">statue of Zeus</a> is brought to <a href="/wiki/Constantinople" title="Constantinople">Constantinople</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=426&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Flavius_Aetius" title="Flavius Aetius">Flavius Aetius</a>, Roman general (<i><a href="/wiki/Magister_militum" title="Magister militum">magister militum</a></i>), starts a 10-year campaign against the <a href="/wiki/Visigoths" title="Visigoths">Visigoths</a> in southern <a href="/wiki/Gaul" title="Gaul">Gaul</a>.</li>
<li>King <a href="/wiki/Gunderic" title="Gunderic">Gunderic</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Vandals" title="Vandals">Vandals</a> accepts the request of the <a href="/wiki/Alans" title="Alans">Alans</a> in <a href="/wiki/Hispania" title="Hispania">Hispania</a> to become their ruler (approximate date).</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Mesoamerica">Mesoamerica</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=426&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Mesoamerica">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/K%27inich_Yax_K%27uk%27_Mo%27" title="K'inich Yax K'uk' Mo'">K'inich Yax K'uk' Mo'</a> becomes the founder of the <a href="/wiki/Pre-Columbian_era" title="Pre-Columbian era">pre-Columbian</a> <a href="/wiki/Maya_civilization" title="Maya civilization">Maya civilization</a> at <a href="/wiki/Cop%C3%A1n" title="Copán">Copán</a> (modern <a href="/wiki/Honduras" title="Honduras">Honduras</a>).</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Religion">Religion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=426&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Religion">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Augustine_of_Hippo" title="Augustine of Hippo">Augustine of Hippo</a> publishes the <i>De Civitate Dei</i>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/City_of_God_(book)" title="City of God (book)">City of God</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Archbishop_Sisinnius_I_of_Constantinople" title="Archbishop Sisinnius I of Constantinople">Sisinnius</a> becomes <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Patriarch_of_Constantinople" title="Patriarch of Constantinople">Archbishop of Constantinople</a>.</li>
