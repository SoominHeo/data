<p><b>Banma County</b> is a county of <a href="/wiki/Qinghai" title="Qinghai">Qinghai Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. It is under the administration of <a href="/wiki/Golog_Tibetan_Autonomous_Prefecture" title="Golog Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture">Golog Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture</a>. The Red Army of the Chinese Communist Party passed through Banma in 1936 during the <a href="/wiki/Long_March" title="Long March">Long March</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-xinhuanet_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-xinhuanet-1">[1]</a></sup> The seat of Baima county is in <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Sailaitang_Town&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Sailaitang Town (page does not exist)">Sailaitang Town</a> (赛来塘镇).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-xinhuanet_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-xinhuanet-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
