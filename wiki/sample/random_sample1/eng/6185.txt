<p>The <b>Pansŏng Archipelago</b> is a chain of islands located off the coast of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cholsan" title="Cholsan">Cholsan</a> county, <a href="/wiki/North_Korea" title="North Korea">North Korea</a>. The chain includes North Korea's largest island, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sinmi-do" title="Sinmi-do">Sinmi-do</a>. The major islands in the archipelago are:</p>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sinmi-do" title="Sinmi-do">Sinmi-do</a> (신미도/身彌島)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ka-do&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ka-do (page does not exist)">Ka-do</a> (가도/椵島)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Tan-do&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Tan-do (page does not exist)">Tan-do</a>(탄도/炭島)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Honggon-do&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Honggon-do (page does not exist)">Honggon-do</a> (홍건도/洪建島)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Taehwa-do&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Taehwa-do (page does not exist)">Taehwa-do</a> (대화도/大和島)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Sohwa-do&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Sohwa-do (page does not exist)">Sohwa-do</a> (소화도/小和島)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Hoe-do&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Hoe-do (page does not exist)">Hoe-do</a> (회도/灰島)</li>
</ul>
<p>The archipelago also includes many smaller islands, including</p>
<ul>
<li>Nabi-som (나비섬)</li>
<li>Uri-do (우리도/牛里島)</li>
<li>Taekacha-do (대가차도/大加次島)</li>
<li>Sokacha-do (소가차도/小加次島)</li>
<li>Taedu-do (대두도/大豆島)</li>
<li>Pugun-do (부군도/府郡島)</li>
<li>Taejongjok-do (대정족도/大鼎足島)</li>
<li>Kom-do</li>
<li>Ung-do (웅도/熊島)</li>
<li>Mugunjang-do</li>
<li>Sari-yom (사리염)</li>
<li>Chi-do (지도/芝島)</li>
<li>Uri-do (우리도/于里島)</li>
<li>Samcha-do</li>
<li>Rap-do (랍도/蠟島)</li>
<li>Sorap-do (소랍도/小蠟島)</li>
</ul>
