<p><b>Ban Hyo-jung</b> (born Ban Man-hee on November 27, 1942) is a South Korean actress. She made her acting debut in 1964 with a <a href="/wiki/Bit_part" title="Bit part">bit part</a> in <a href="/wiki/Shin_Sang-ok" title="Shin Sang-ok">Shin Sang-ok</a>'s film <i>Rice</i>, and went on to a prolific career in <a href="/wiki/Korean_drama" title="Korean drama">television dramas</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
