<p><b>Valery Milagros Ortiz</b> (born August 1, 1984) is a <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">Puerto Rican</a> actress, dancer, singer, songwriter &amp; author who is known for her role as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Madison_Duarte" title="Madison Duarte">Madison Duarte</a> on the television series <i><a href="/wiki/South_of_Nowhere" title="South of Nowhere">South of Nowhere</a></i>, also for her role as Raquel Saldaña on VH1's "<a href="/wiki/Hit_the_Floor_(TV_series)" title="Hit the Floor (TV series)">Hit the Floor</a>".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ourhost_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Ourhost-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Valery_Ortiz&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ortiz was born in <a href="/wiki/San_Juan,_Puerto_Rico" title="San Juan, Puerto Rico">San Juan, Puerto Rico</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MissO_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-MissO-2">[2]</a></sup> and raised in <a href="/wiki/Orlando,_Florida" title="Orlando, Florida">Orlando, Florida</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-latina_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-latina-3">[3]</a></sup> Passionate to become a "ballerina" and she explored her dancing talents after receiving a scholarship to <i>The Southern Ballet Theater Performing Art Centre</i> in Orlando.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (October 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> She continued dancing but eased out of it in middle school as her interest in theatre and acting grew.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MissO_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-MissO-2">[2]</a></sup> She worked in Orlando as an extra on the <a href="/wiki/Universal_Studios" title="Universal Studios">Universal Studios</a> backlot.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Following in her older brother's footsteps, she was accepted into the <a href="/wiki/Dr._Phillips_High_School" title="Dr. Phillips High School">Dr. Phillips High School</a> performing arts magnet program.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-latina_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-latina-3">[3]</a></sup> After four years of study, Ortiz began working in several productions like Latins Anonymous (Orlando Fringe Festival), West Side Story as Maria, A Midsummer Night's Dream as Helena, The Vagina Monologues (Orlando Premiere) and The Edinburgh, Scotland Fringe Festival.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ourhost_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Ourhost-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Valery_Ortiz&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>While attending the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Central_Florida" title="University of Central Florida">University of Central Florida</a> for a Bachelor in Fine Arts, Ortiz was given an opportunity to work on television show, <a href="/wiki/Nickelodeon_Splat!" title="Nickelodeon Splat!">Splat!</a> as a host.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-latina_3-2"><a href="#cite_note-latina-3">[3]</a></sup> After the first season, a two week<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MrDreamz_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-MrDreamz-5">[5]</a></sup> road trip "vacation" to Los Angeles turned into permanent relocation to Los Angeles. Ortiz quickly started working on a local television show called LATV Live as a <a href="/wiki/VJ_(media_personality)" title="VJ (media personality)">VJ</a>.</p>
<p>While working at the <a href="/wiki/Hard_Rock_Cafe" title="Hard Rock Cafe">Hard Rock Cafe</a> as a hostess, Ortiz was cast as in the comedy <i><a href="/wiki/Date_Movie" title="Date Movie">Date Movie</a></i> as Jell-O, a <a href="/wiki/Jennifer_Lopez" title="Jennifer Lopez">Jennifer Lopez</a> impersonation.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> During the movie's filming, she was cast the television series <i><a href="/wiki/South_of_Nowhere" title="South of Nowhere">South of Nowhere</a>.</i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Celebzter_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-Celebzter-8">[8]</a></sup> Shortly after, she was cast in the pilot Emily's Reason's Why Not, which was picked up and aired in 2006.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ourhost_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-Ourhost-1">[1]</a></sup> She currently is an anchor for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Si_TV" title="Si TV">Si TV</a> and E! News. Ortiz was “One of the Top 25 Latinas of 2006” from <a href="/wiki/Maxim_(magazine)" title="Maxim (magazine)">Maxim en Español</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ourhost_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-Ourhost-1">[1]</a></sup> Ortiz currently portrays the role of "Raquel Saldana" in <a href="/wiki/Hit_the_Floor_(TV_series)" title="Hit the Floor (TV series)">Hit the Floor</a>, a new scripted <a href="/wiki/VH1" title="VH1">VH1</a> series that premiered May 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Celebzter_8-1"><a href="#cite_note-Celebzter-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Valery_Ortiz&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ortiz currently lives in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>, <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>. She enjoys reading, hiking, decorating, and writing.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ourhost_1-4"><a href="#cite_note-Ourhost-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
