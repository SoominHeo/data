<p><b>Lorenzo Rosseti</b> (born 5 August 1994) is an Italian professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays for <a href="/wiki/FC_Lugano" title="FC Lugano">FC Lugano</a> on loan from <a href="/wiki/Juventus_F.C." title="Juventus F.C.">Juventus</a>, as a <a href="/wiki/Forward_(association_football)#Striker" title="Forward (association football)">striker</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lorenzo_Rosseti&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rosseti was signed by defending Serie A champions <a href="/wiki/Juventus_F.C." title="Juventus F.C.">Juventus F.C.</a> in August 2014 as a free agent; he was released by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/A.C._Siena" title="A.C. Siena">Siena</a> in July after the club was not admit 2014–15 professional league. On 29 August 2014 Rosseti joined fellow Serie A club <a href="/wiki/Atalanta_B.C." title="Atalanta B.C.">Atalanta B.C.</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 21 July 2015 Rosseti was signed by Serie B club <a href="/wiki/A.C._Cesena" title="A.C. Cesena">A.C. Cesena</a> in a temporary deal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> On 6 August 2016 he was signed by <a href="/wiki/FC_Lugano" title="FC Lugano">FC Lugano</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> in a temporary deal after a month with Juventus's pre-season camp.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
