<p><b>Valesca Reis Santos</b>, better known as <b>Valesca Popozuda</b> (born October 6, 1978 in Rio de Janeiro, <a href="/wiki/Rio_de_Janeiro_(state)" title="Rio de Janeiro (state)">Rio de Janeiro</a>) is a Brazilian musician, dancer and former reality show contestant. She is the current singer for the Brazilian <a href="/wiki/Funk_carioca" title="Funk carioca">funk carioca</a> band Gaiola das Popozudas. Her name means "Big-butted Valesca" in Brazilian Portuguese.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Valesca_Popozuda&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Valesca was born in October 6, 1978, in the <a href="/wiki/Iraj%C3%A1" title="Irajá">Irajá</a> neighborhood, in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rio_de_Janeiro,_Rio_de_Janeiro" title="Rio de Janeiro, Rio de Janeiro">Rio de Janeiro, Rio de Janeiro</a>, Brazil. She has a son called Pablo (born in 1999)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and currently lives in her birth city.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Valesca_Popozuda&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>She is a founding member of the Brazilian funk carioca group Gaiola das Popozudas, which was created in the beginning of the 2000s (decade), whose most famous songs include "Late que eu tô passando" (Bark Because I'm Passing by), "Agora eu tô solteira" (Now I'm Single) and its uncensored version, "Agora eu virei puta" (Now I've Become a whore), and "Quero te dar" (I Want to Be Fucked by You). Valesca is noted for her slightly hoarse voice, for her irreverence and large buttocks (which are insured for R$5.000.000,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> have 550ml silicone implants in each side and are big enough to balance a glass<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>). She was the June 2009 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Playmate" title="Playmate">Playmate</a> of the Brazilian <a href="/wiki/Playboy" title="Playboy">Playboy</a>. In 2011, she was participated in the <a href="/wiki/A_Fazenda_4" title="A Fazenda 4">fourth edition</a> of the Brazilian reality show <a href="/wiki/A_Fazenda" title="A Fazenda">A Fazenda</a>, where she finished 4th. Valesca got along with everyone in the house, and was too welcomed by public due to, along with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Raquel_Pacheco" title="Raquel Pacheco">Raquel Pacheco</a>, <a href="/wiki/Monique_Evans" title="Monique Evans">Monique Evans</a> and <a href="/wiki/Joana_Machado" title="Joana Machado">Joana Machado</a> (the final three of the show), taking part of the most popular alliance from that season.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Controversies">Controversies</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Valesca_Popozuda&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Controversies">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Valesca, an admirer of the ex-<a href="/wiki/President_of_Brazil" title="President of Brazil">president</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lula" title="Lula">Lula</a>, has written a song dedicated to him, which includes a controversial statement: "conheci o Lula no <a href="/wiki/Complexo_do_Alem%C3%A3o" title="Complexo do Alemão">Complexo do Alemão</a>, e ele não tirou o olho do meu popozão", Brazilian Portuguese for "I met Lula at Complexo do Alemão and he didn't stop staring at my big <a href="/wiki/Buttocks" title="Buttocks">ass</a>".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Valeska_Popozuda_escreve_m.C3.BAsica_para_Lula_e_quer_lev.C3.A1-lo_ao_baile_funk_Valeska_Popozuda_escreve_m.C3.BAsica_para_Lula_e_quer_lev.C3.A1-lo_ao_baile_funk_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Valeska_Popozuda_escreve_m.C3.BAsica_para_Lula_e_quer_lev.C3.A1-lo_ao_baile_funk_Valeska_Popozuda_escreve_m.C3.BAsica_para_Lula_e_quer_lev.C3.A1-lo_ao_baile_funk-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
