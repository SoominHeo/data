Established in 1966, the <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> team played in the <a href="/wiki/S%C3%BCper_Lig" title="Süper Lig">Süper Lig</a> after achieving promotion having finished as runners-up of the <a href="/wiki/TFF_First_League" title="TFF First League">TFF First League</a> in <a href="/wiki/2013%E2%80%9314_TFF_First_League" title="2013–14 TFF First League">2013–14</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> The team's previous promotion was 40 years before that.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> Their stadium, the all-seater <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bal%C4%B1kesir_Atat%C3%BCrk_Stad%C4%B1" title="Balıkesir Atatürk Stadı">Balıkesir Atatürk Stadı</a>, has a capacity of 15,800.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
