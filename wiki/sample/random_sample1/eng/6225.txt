<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Inventors' Day</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Inventors' Day</b> is a day of the year set aside by a <a href="/wiki/Country" title="Country">country</a> to recognise the contributions of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Inventor" title="Inventor">inventors</a>. Not all countries recognise Inventors' Day. Those countries which do recognise an Inventors' Day do so with varying degrees of emphasis and on different days of the year.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="By_location">By location</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Inventors%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: By location">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Argentina">Argentina</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Inventors%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Argentina">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:152px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Ladislao_Biro_Argentina_Circa_1978.JPG"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="1143" data-file-width="1488" height="115" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/15/Ladislao_Biro_Argentina_Circa_1978.JPG/150px-Ladislao_Biro_Argentina_Circa_1978.JPG" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/15/Ladislao_Biro_Argentina_Circa_1978.JPG/225px-Ladislao_Biro_Argentina_Circa_1978.JPG 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/15/Ladislao_Biro_Argentina_Circa_1978.JPG/300px-Ladislao_Biro_Argentina_Circa_1978.JPG 2x" width="150"/></a>

<p>The Inventors' Day (<a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a>: <span lang="es" xml:lang="es"><i>Día del Inventor</i></span>) in <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentina</a> has been celebrated since 1986 and is held yearly on September 29, the birthday of the inventor of the <a href="/wiki/Ballpoint_pen" title="Ballpoint pen">ballpoint pen</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/L%C3%A1szl%C3%B3_J%C3%B3zsef_B%C3%ADr%C3%B3" title="László József Bíró">László József Bíró</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Austria.2C_Germany_and_Switzerland">Austria, Germany and Switzerland</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Inventors%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Austria, Germany and Switzerland">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:152px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Hedy_Lamarr_in_Dishonored_Lady_5.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="518" data-file-width="538" height="144" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d8/Hedy_Lamarr_in_Dishonored_Lady_5.jpg/150px-Hedy_Lamarr_in_Dishonored_Lady_5.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d8/Hedy_Lamarr_in_Dishonored_Lady_5.jpg/225px-Hedy_Lamarr_in_Dishonored_Lady_5.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d8/Hedy_Lamarr_in_Dishonored_Lady_5.jpg/300px-Hedy_Lamarr_in_Dishonored_Lady_5.jpg 2x" width="150"/></a>

<a href="/wiki/Hedy_Lamarr" title="Hedy Lamarr">Hedy Lamarr</a>, actress and inventor</div>

<p>The Inventors' Day (<a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a>: <span lang="de" xml:lang="de"><i>Tag der Erfinder</i></span>) in the German-speaking countries <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>, <a href="/wiki/Austria" title="Austria">Austria</a> and <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Switzerland</a> is celebrated on November 9, the birthday of the Austrian-born inventor and <a href="/wiki/Hollywood" title="Hollywood">Hollywood</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Actress" title="Actress">actress</a> <a href="/wiki/Hedy_Lamarr" title="Hedy Lamarr">Hedy Lamarr</a> whose main <a href="/wiki/Invention" title="Invention">invention</a> was the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Frequency_hopping" title="Frequency hopping">frequency-hopped spread spectrum</a> 1942.</p>
<p>The day was proclaimed by <a href="/wiki/Berlin" title="Berlin">Berlin</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Inventor" title="Inventor">inventor</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Entrepreneur" title="Entrepreneur">entrepreneur</a> <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Gerhard_Muthenthaler&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Gerhard Muthenthaler (page does not exist)">Gerhard Muthenthaler</a>. According to the website of the organisation<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> it intends to pursue the following goals:</p>
<ul>
<li>Encourage people towards their own ideas and for a change to the better</li>
<li>Remind people of forgotten inventors</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hungary">Hungary</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Inventors%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Hungary">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright">

<p><i>Hungarian Inventors' Day</i> (<a class="extiw" href="https://hu.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magyar_Feltal%C3%A1l%C3%B3k_Napja" title="hu:Magyar Feltalálók Napja">Magyar Feltalálók Napja</a>) is celebrated on June 13 in memoriam of <a href="/wiki/Albert_Szent-Gy%C3%B6rgyi" title="Albert Szent-Györgyi">Albert Szent-Györgyi</a> who registered his national patent about the synthesized <a href="/wiki/Vitamin_C" title="Vitamin C">Vitamin C</a> in 1941. It is celebrated by the Association of Hungarian Inventors (MAFE) since 2009.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Moldova">Moldova</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Inventors%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Moldova">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Since 1995, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Republic_of_Moldova" title="Republic of Moldova">Republic of Moldova</a> has celebrated an <b>Inventors' and Rationalizers' Day</b> annually at the end of June.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Russia">Russia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Inventors%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Russia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Since 1957, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a> has celebrated an <b>Inventors' and Rationalizers' Day</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">День изобретателя и рационализатора</span>) annually at the last Saturday of June.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Thailand">Thailand</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Inventors%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Thailand">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Thailand" title="Thailand">Thailand</a> recognises February 2 as <b>Inventors' Day</b> each year. The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Thai_Cabinet" title="Thai Cabinet">Thai Cabinet</a> set this date to commemorate the anniversary of His Majesty King <a href="/wiki/Bhumibol_Adulyadej" title="Bhumibol Adulyadej">Bhumibol Adulyadej</a>’s allocation of a patent for a <i>slow speed surface aerator</i> on February 2, 1993.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="United_Kingdom">United Kingdom</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Inventors%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: United Kingdom">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>While as of yet, the United Kingdom does not officially recognize an Inventor's Day, in 2014 <a href="/wiki/BT_Group" title="BT Group">BT</a> made a public appeal to celebrate "the first" National Inventor's Day on 2 December 2014. While many countries celebrate inventiveness on a significant day relating to one of that country's inventors, 2 December does not appear to have such a relationship. However, the day was also the release of a study by BT of the inventiveness of UK residents.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> As of 9 November, 2016 there has not been any announcement of a second or subsequent National Inventor's Day in the United Kingdom.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="United_States">United States</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Inventors%27_Day&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: United States">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p><a href="/wiki/Ronald_Reagan" title="Ronald Reagan">Ronald Reagan</a> as <a href="/wiki/President_of_the_United_States" title="President of the United States">President of the United States</a> proclaimed February 11, 1983 as <b>National Inventors' Day</b>, <a class="external text" href="http://www.reagan.utexas.edu/archives/speeches/1983/11283i.htm" rel="nofollow">Proclamation 5013</a>, to "...call upon the people of the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> to observe this day with appropriate ceremonies and activities".</p>
<p>In recognition of the enormous contribution <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Inventor" title="Inventor">inventors</a> make to the nation and the world, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Congress_of_the_United_States" title="Congress of the United States">Congress</a>, pursuant to Senate Joint Resolution 140 (Public Law 97 - 198), has designated February 11, the anniversary of the birth of the inventor <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Thomas_Alva_Edison" title="Thomas Alva Edison">Thomas Alva Edison</a>, who held over 1,000 <a href="/wiki/Patent" title="Patent">patents</a>, as <b>National Inventors' Day</b>.</p>
