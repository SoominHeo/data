<p><b>Hui of Balhae</b> (died 817) (r. 812?–817) was the eighth king of <a href="/wiki/Balhae" title="Balhae">Balhae</a>. He was the son of <a href="/wiki/Gang_of_Balhae" title="Gang of Balhae">King Gang</a>, and the younger brother of <a href="/wiki/Jeong_of_Balhae" title="Jeong of Balhae">King Jeong</a>. He actively cultivated relations with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tang_China" title="Tang China">Tang China</a>, and imported many aspects of Tang culture and governmental systems.</p>
