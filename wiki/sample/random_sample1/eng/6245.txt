<p><b>Bang Dae-du</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 방대두, <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">Hanja</a>: 方大斗; born October 14, 1954 in <a href="/wiki/Gyeongsan" title="Gyeongsan">Gyeongsan</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gyeongsangbuk-do" title="Gyeongsangbuk-do">Gyeongsangbuk-do</a>) is a retired <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Greco-Roman_wrestling" title="Greco-Roman wrestling">Greco-Roman wrestler</a>.</p>
<p>He received a bronze medal at the <a href="/wiki/1984_Summer_Olympics" title="1984 Summer Olympics">1984 Summer Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-db-ol1984-WRE_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-db-ol1984-WRE-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
