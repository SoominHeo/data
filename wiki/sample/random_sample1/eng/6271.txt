<p><i><b>Bad Piggies</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Puzzle_video_game" title="Puzzle video game">puzzle</a> <a href="/wiki/Video_game" title="Video game">video game</a> developed by <a href="/wiki/Rovio_Entertainment" title="Rovio Entertainment">Rovio Entertainment</a>, and was the company's first spin-off to <i><a href="/wiki/Angry_Birds" title="Angry Birds">Angry Birds</a></i>. The game launched on <a href="/wiki/Android_(operating_system)" title="Android (operating system)">Android</a>, <a href="/wiki/IOS" title="IOS">iOS</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Windows" title="Windows">Windows</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mac_OS" title="Mac OS">Mac</a> on September 27, 2012. It was released for <a href="/wiki/BlackBerry_10" title="BlackBerry 10">BlackBerry 10</a> on October 2013 and for <a href="/wiki/Windows_Phone" title="Windows Phone">Windows Phone</a> on April 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Unlike the Angry Birds games, the player assists the minion pigs in building contraptions that travel on land and in air to collect pieces of a map to ultimately capture and take away the Angry Birds' eggs.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> As of October 2012, <i>Bad Piggies</i> was the fastest-selling game on the <a href="/wiki/App_Store_(iOS)" title="App Store (iOS)">Apple App Store</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-th_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-th-3">[3]</a></sup> and the quickest one to reach the top of the app list in just three hours.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>In June 2016, Rovio made <i>Bad Piggies</i> <a href="/wiki/Free-to-play" title="Free-to-play">free-to-play</a>, since it previously had a purchase price.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Gameplay">Gameplay</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bad_Piggies&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Gameplay">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The objective of the game is to build a contraption that transports the pig from a starting point to the finish line, usually indicated by a map. Such contraptions are made of wood and/or iron (similar to <i>Angry Birds</i>), as well as other objects such as <a href="/wiki/Wheel" title="Wheel">wheels</a>, <a href="/wiki/Two-liter_bottle" title="Two-liter bottle">two-liter bottles</a>, <a href="/wiki/Umbrella" title="Umbrella">umbrellas</a> (to increase <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Air_resistance" title="Air resistance">air resistance</a>), <a href="/wiki/Electric_motor" title="Electric motor">motors</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mechanical_fan" title="Mechanical fan">fans</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/TNT" title="TNT">TNT</a>, <a href="/wiki/Balloon" title="Balloon">balloons</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rope" title="Rope">ropes</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Rocket" title="Rocket">rockets</a>.</p>
<p>Similar to <i><a href="/wiki/Angry_Birds_(video_game)" title="Angry Birds (video game)">Angry Birds</a></i>, the player can earn up to three stars per level, but stars are not earned by scoring points. Instead, players automatically earn one star for completing the level. For each level, the other two stars can be obtained by:</p>
<ul>
<li>Completing the level within a certain time limit (e.g. 10 seconds)</li>
<li>Collecting 1 or 2 "star boxes" on the playing field</li>
<li>Not using a certain part (e.g. a wheel, an umbrella, etc.)</li>
<li>Keeping the contraption intact</li>
<li>Transporting the King Pig along with the Freckled Pig</li>
<li>Transporting the Egg (this is mandatory in <i>Flight in the Night</i>)</li>
<li>Transporting the Halloween Candy (this is mandatory in <i>Tusk 'til Dawn</i>)</li>
</ul>
<p>For example, on level 1-1, a player can earn three stars by completing the level, collecting a star on the playing field and by keeping the vehicle intact. On some levels, it is impossible to earn all three stars at once, so the player must play the level two or more times in order to obtain three stars.</p>
<p>Additionally, "skulls" are placed throughout the levels, in which a player can unlock the skull level in the Sandbox by finding at least 10 out of the over 40 hidden skulls.</p>
<p>After the release of the remaining 30 levels of "Rise and Swine", Rovio added a "Feed the King" mini-game, in order to earn power-ups in the game. The player obtains one or more dessert(s) during gameplay, which they can use to feed King Pig to receive power-ups, such as sticky glue (makes your contraption more durable), motor boost, golden engineer, and super magnet; <a href="/wiki/Nut_(hardware)" title="Nut (hardware)">nuts</a>, and coins. The King Pig only has a slight chance of giving a power-up. The only other way to get power-ups is by purchasing them in their store.</p>
<p>In June 2016 with the change to free-to-play, there are 3 daily Loot Crates to collect parts (wooden crates contain 3 items, metal contain 4, and gold contain 8) for a new Custom Contraptions mode, which allows the player to (completely optional) change the appearance of parts used in the levels, ranging from a rarity of 1 to 3 stars; it has fullscreen video ads and a way to buy more parts.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="In_other_media">In other media</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bad_Piggies&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: In other media">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Television">Television</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bad_Piggies&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Television">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Piggy_Tales" title="Piggy Tales">Piggy Tales</a></div>
<p>On April 11, 2014, Rovio released "<a href="/wiki/Piggy_Tales" title="Piggy Tales">Piggy Tales</a>", a <a href="/wiki/Clay_animation" title="Clay animation">Clay Animation</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/TV_series" title="TV series">TV series</a> based on the game, and a spin-off of <i><a href="/wiki/Angry_Birds_Toons" title="Angry Birds Toons">Angry Birds Toons</a></i>. It features a closer glimpse of the life of the minion pigs.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Film">Film</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bad_Piggies&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Film">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The game's main character appears in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Angry_Birds_Movie" title="The Angry Birds Movie">The Angry Birds Movie</a></i>, going by the name Ross and voiced by <a href="/wiki/Tony_Hale" title="Tony Hale">Tony Hale</a>. He is one of Leonard's closest assistants in the pigs' schemes.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Reception">Reception</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bad_Piggies&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Reception">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The game has received positive reviews with a <a href="/wiki/Metacritic" title="Metacritic">Metacritic</a> score of 88/100 based on 24 reviews.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<p>Mark Brown of <i><a href="/wiki/Pocket_Gamer" title="Pocket Gamer">Pocket Gamer</a></i> gave the game a Silver Award and called it "creative, addictive, and absolutely packed with content", while lamenting that it felt "a little too safe and predictable".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/IGN" title="IGN">IGN</a> rated it the best mobile game of 2012.</p>
