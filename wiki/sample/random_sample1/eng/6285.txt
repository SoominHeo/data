<p><b>Bae Hyo-sung</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">배효성</span>; born 1 January 1982) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Defender_(football)" title="Defender (football)">defender</a> who plays for <a href="/wiki/Chungju_Hummel_FC" title="Chungju Hummel FC">Chungju Hummel</a> in <a href="/wiki/K_League_Challenge" title="K League Challenge">K League Challenge</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bae_Hyo-sung&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He began his professional career with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Busan_I%27Park" title="Busan I'Park">Busan I'Park</a> in 2004. In February 2011, he moved from Busan to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Incheon_United" title="Incheon United">Incheon United</a> as soon as he left the army-based team <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gwangju_Sangmu" title="Gwangju Sangmu">Gwangju Sangmu</a>. Although being a newly arriving player, coach <a href="/wiki/Huh_Jung-moo" title="Huh Jung-moo">Huh Jung-moo</a> named him as team captain for 2011 season. On 14 November 2011, he left Incheon for his hometown team <a href="/wiki/Gangwon_FC" title="Gangwon FC">Gangwon FC</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
