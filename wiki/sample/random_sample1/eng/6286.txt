<p><b>Baek</b>, also often spelled <b>Paek</b>, <b>Baik</b> or <b>Paik</b>, is of lord classed family name which can be traced as far as 8000 years between China and Korea <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Korean_family_name" title="Korean family name">Korean family name</a>. No one really know the true origin in which it started either in China or Korea as both history timeline were similar. In the year 2000, there were 351,275 people with this surname in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. The word means the color white.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.99.BD.2Fexistence">白/existence</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Baek&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: 白/existence">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>白(Bai) is pronounced "Beck" in Korean. The name has a Cantonese origin from the <a href="/wiki/Yuan_dynasty" title="Yuan dynasty">Yuan dynasty</a> and <a href="/wiki/Goryeo" title="Goryeo">Goryeo</a> dynasty.</p>
<ul>
<li>Baek Wu Kyung (白宇經) of the <a href="/wiki/Suwon" title="Suwon">Suwon</a> Baek clan, cousin of <a href="/wiki/Bai_Juyi" title="Bai Juyi">Bai Juyi</a> of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tang_Dynasty" title="Tang Dynasty">Tang Dynasty</a>, is the origin of this name.</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id=".E8.8B.A9.2Fextinction">苩/extinction</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Baek&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 苩/extinction">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Some <a href="/wiki/Baekje" title="Baekje">Baekje</a> refugees from the late <a href="/wiki/Silla" title="Silla">Silla</a> age had this surname.</p>
<ul>
<li>Paik Ga (苩加), <a href="/wiki/Mahan_confederacy" title="Mahan confederacy">Mahan</a> ruler</li>
<li>Paik Yong (苩龍), Silla general</li>
<li>Paik Ki (苩奇), Baekje general</li>
<li>Paik Ga (苩加), Baekje painter</li>
</ul>
