<p><b>Baek Sang Seung</b> (b. December 12, 1935 in Gyeongju) is the mayor of <a href="/wiki/Gyeongju" title="Gyeongju">Gyeongju</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. He was elected to the post in 2002. He is a member of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grand_National_Party" title="Grand National Party">Grand National Party</a>. He studied public administration at the undergraduate level at <a href="/wiki/Korea_University" title="Korea University">Korea University</a>, and attended graduate school in the same field at <a href="/wiki/Seoul_National_University" title="Seoul National University">Seoul National University</a>. Prior to attaining his present position, he held various positions in the <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a> municipal government.</p>
