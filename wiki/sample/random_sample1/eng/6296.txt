<p><b>Baekseok Station</b> is a station located in <a href="/wiki/Ilsan" title="Ilsan">Ilsan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Goyang" title="Goyang">Goyang</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gyeonggi-do" title="Gyeonggi-do">Gyeonggi-do</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> It is served by <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Subway_Line_3" title="Seoul Subway Line 3">Seoul Subway Line 3</a>. There are many stores and restaurants close to the station, including a <a href="/wiki/Costco" title="Costco">Costco</a> store.</p>
