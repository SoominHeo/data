<p><br/></p>
<p>Year <b>488</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">CDLXXXVIII</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Friday" title="Leap year starting on Friday">leap year starting on Friday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>. At the time, it was known as the <b>Year of the Consulship of Ecclesius and Sividius</b> (or, less frequently, <b>year 1241 <i><a href="/wiki/Ab_urbe_condita" title="Ab urbe condita">Ab urbe condita</a></i></b>). The denomination 488 for this year has been used since the early medieval period, when the <a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a> <a href="/wiki/Calendar_era" title="Calendar era">calendar era</a> became the prevalent method in Europe for naming years.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=488&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=488&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Byzantine_Empire">Byzantine Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=488&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Byzantine Empire">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>Emperor <a href="/wiki/Zeno_(emperor)" title="Zeno (emperor)">Zeno</a> regains power from the Roman <a href="/wiki/Catholicism" title="Catholicism">Catholic</a> usurper <a href="/wiki/Leontius_(usurper)" title="Leontius (usurper)">Leontius</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Isauria" title="Isauria">Isaurian</a> patrician <a href="/wiki/Illus" title="Illus">Illus</a>, who are captured and executed, ending a 4-year <a href="/wiki/Rebellion" title="Rebellion">rebellion</a> (see <a href="/wiki/484" title="484">484</a>).</li>
<li>Zeno orders <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Theodoric_the_Great" title="Theodoric the Great">Theodoric the Great</a> to overthrow his rival <a href="/wiki/Odoacer" title="Odoacer">Odoacer</a>, who has established himself as <a href="/wiki/King_of_Italy" title="King of Italy">king of Italy</a> (see <a href="/wiki/476" title="476">476</a>). He marches with an <a href="/wiki/Ostrogoths" title="Ostrogoths">Ostrogoth</a> army to the West.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=488&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>According to the <i><a href="/wiki/Anglo-Saxon_Chronicle" title="Anglo-Saxon Chronicle">Anglo-Saxon Chronicle</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Hengist_and_Horsa" title="Hengist and Horsa">Hengist</a> dies and is succeeded by his son <a href="/wiki/Oisc_of_Kent" title="Oisc of Kent">Oisc</a> as king of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Kent" title="Kingdom of Kent">Kent</a>.</li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/East_Roman_Empire" title="East Roman Empire">East Roman</a> Emperor <a href="/wiki/Zeno_(emperor)" title="Zeno (emperor)">Zeno</a> tasks the King of the <a href="/wiki/Ostrogoths" title="Ostrogoths">Ostrogoths</a>, <a href="/wiki/Theoderic_the_Great" title="Theoderic the Great">Theoderic the Great</a>, with conducting a campaign against <a href="/wiki/Odoacer" title="Odoacer">Odoacer</a>, whom he initially had recognised as his representative in <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>.</li>
<li>Among the peoples who live on the south bank of the <a href="/wiki/Danube" title="Danube">Danube</a> in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Noricum_ripense" title="Noricum ripense">Noricum ripense</a></i> and who are <i>de facto</i> ruled by the <a href="/wiki/Rugii" title="Rugii">Rugii</a>, whose empire has its centre near <a href="/wiki/Krems_an_der_Donau" title="Krems an der Donau">Krems</a> on the north bank, are Romii who had been evacuated earlier from Danube settlements above the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/River_Enns" title="River Enns">River Enns</a>. They include members of the <a href="/wiki/Severinus_of_Noricum" title="Severinus of Noricum">Severin</a> <a href="/wiki/Convent" title="Convent">convent</a>. Because some of the Rugii want to fight for East Rome against <a href="/wiki/Odoacer" title="Odoacer">Odoacer</a>, they destroy the Rugian Empire and allow the Romii to be evacuated to Italy by his brother, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hunulf" title="Hunulf">Hunulf</a>, in order to prevent the re-establishment of the Rugian Empire by a surviving prince. The northern <a href="/wiki/Danubian_Limes" title="Danubian Limes">Danubian Limes</a> of the Roman Empire are effectively abandoned. Even the relics of <a href="/wiki/Severinus_of_Noricum" title="Severinus of Noricum">Severinus of Noricum</a> are carried with them.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Gepids" title="Gepids">Gepids</a> capture <a href="/wiki/Belgrade" title="Belgrade">Belgrade</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Persia">Persia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=488&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Persia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kavadh_I" title="Kavadh I">Kavadh I</a> is <a href="/wiki/Coronation" title="Coronation">crowned</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Nobility" title="Nobility">nobles</a>, and succeeds his blind uncle <a href="/wiki/Balash" title="Balash">Balash</a> as the 19th king of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sassanid_Empire" title="Sassanid Empire">Persia</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=488&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
