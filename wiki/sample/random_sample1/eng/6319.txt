<p><b>Vanessa Marshall</b> (born October 19, 1969) is an American singer, actress and model. She is most active in voice-over roles for animated series, films and video games.</p>
<p>She is the daughter of actress <a href="/wiki/Joan_Van_Ark" title="Joan Van Ark">Joan Van Ark</a> and reporter John Marshall. She began voice-over work after being discovered at a one-woman show.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Vanessa_Marshall&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Marshall was born in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/New_York_City,_New_York" title="New York City, New York">New York City, New York</a>. She is the only child of <a href="/wiki/Joan_Van_Ark" title="Joan Van Ark">Joan Van Ark</a> and John Marshall.</p>
<p>She was married to voice actor <a href="/wiki/Andrew_Kishino" title="Andrew Kishino">Andrew Kishino</a> from 2001 to 2007. During their marriage, they founded Marsh-Kish Productions, a voice-over production company. Both have appeared as main characters in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Spectacular_Spider-Man_(TV_series)" title="The Spectacular Spider-Man (TV series)">The Spectacular Spider-Man</a></i>. Her acting in this has been compared to that of her mother in the 1970s <a href="/wiki/Spider-Woman" title="Spider-Woman">Spider-Woman</a> series.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
