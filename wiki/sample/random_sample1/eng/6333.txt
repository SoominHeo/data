<p>The <b>Virginia Wings</b> were a professional <a href="/wiki/Ice_hockey" title="Ice hockey">ice hockey</a> team based in <a href="/wiki/Norfolk,_Virginia" title="Norfolk, Virginia">Norfolk, Virginia</a> of the greater <a href="/wiki/Hampton_Roads" title="Hampton Roads">Hampton Roads</a> area. They were an affiliated farm team of the <a href="/wiki/Detroit_Red_Wings" title="Detroit Red Wings">Detroit Red Wings</a>, and a member of the <a href="/wiki/American_Hockey_League" title="American Hockey League">American Hockey League</a> for three seasons from <a href="/wiki/1972%E2%80%9373_AHL_season" title="1972–73 AHL season">1972–73</a> to <a href="/wiki/1974%E2%80%9375_AHL_season" title="1974–75 AHL season">1974–75</a>. The Wings were previously known as the <b>Tidewater Wings</b> for the <a href="/wiki/1971%E2%80%9372_AHL_season" title="1971–72 AHL season">1971–72</a> season. Their cross-state rivals in the AHL were the <a href="/wiki/Richmond_Robins" title="Richmond Robins">Richmond Robins</a>.</p>
<p>The Wings won the <a href="/wiki/John_D._Chick_Trophy" title="John D. Chick Trophy">John D. Chick Trophy</a> as south division champions of the regular season in 1974–75, which turned out to be their final season. After the Wings ceased operations, the <a href="/wiki/Hampton_Gulls" title="Hampton Gulls">Hampton Gulls</a> would represent eastern <a href="/wiki/Virginia" title="Virginia">Virginia</a> at the AHL level for the <a href="/wiki/1977%E2%80%9378_AHL_season" title="1977–78 AHL season">1977–78</a> season. The Wings franchise was granted suspension status until 1975, and then voluntarily withdrew from the league in 1976.</p>
<p><b>The franchise was replaced by:</b></p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hampton_Gulls" title="Hampton Gulls">Hampton Gulls</a> (1974–1977 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Southern_Hockey_League_(1973%E2%80%931977)" title="Southern Hockey League (1973–1977)">SHL</a>), (1977–1978 <a href="/wiki/American_Hockey_League" title="American Hockey League">AHL</a>)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Tidewater_Sharks" title="Tidewater Sharks">Tidewater Sharks</a> (1975–1977 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Southern_Hockey_League_(1973%E2%80%931977)" title="Southern Hockey League (1973–1977)">Southern Hockey League</a>)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hampton_Roads_Gulls" title="Hampton Roads Gulls">Hampton Roads Gulls</a> (1982–1983 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Atlantic_Coast_Hockey_League_(1981%E2%80%9387)" title="Atlantic Coast Hockey League (1981–87)">ACHL</a>)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hampton_Roads_Admirals" title="Hampton Roads Admirals">Hampton Roads Admirals</a> of the <a href="/wiki/ECHL" title="ECHL">ECHL</a> (1989–2000)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Norfolk_Admirals_(AHL)" title="Norfolk Admirals (AHL)">Norfolk Admirals</a> of the <a href="/wiki/American_Hockey_League" title="American Hockey League">AHL</a> (2000–15)</li>

</ul>
