<p><b>April 15</b> is the 105th day of the year (106th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 260 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Wednesday, Friday or Sunday (58 in 400 years each) than on Monday or Tuesday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Thursday or Saturday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=April_15&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/769" title="769">769</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Lateran_Council_(769)" title="Lateran Council (769)">Lateran Council</a> condemned the <a href="/wiki/Council_of_Hieria" title="Council of Hieria">Council of Hieria</a> and <a href="/wiki/Anathema" title="Anathema">anathematized</a> its <a href="/wiki/Iconoclasm" title="Iconoclasm">iconoclastic</a> rulings.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1071" title="1071">1071</a> – <a href="/wiki/Bari" title="Bari">Bari</a>, the last <a href="/wiki/Byzantine_Empire" title="Byzantine Empire">Byzantine</a> possession in southern Italy, <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Bari" title="Siege of Bari">is surrendered</a> to <a href="/wiki/Robert_Guiscard" title="Robert Guiscard">Robert Guiscard</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1395" title="1395">1395</a> – <a href="/wiki/Timur" title="Timur">Timur</a> defeats <a href="/wiki/Tokhtamysh" title="Tokhtamysh">Tokhtamysh</a> of the Golden Horde at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Terek_River" title="Battle of the Terek River">Battle of the Terek River</a>. The Golden Horde capital city, <a href="/wiki/Sarai_(city)" title="Sarai (city)">Sarai</a>, is razed to the ground and Timur installs a puppet ruler on the throne.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1450" title="1450">1450</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Formigny" title="Battle of Formigny">Battle of Formigny</a>: Toward the end of the <a href="/wiki/Hundred_Years%27_War" title="Hundred Years' War">Hundred Years' War</a>, the French attack and nearly annihilate English forces, ending English domination in Northern France.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1632" title="1632">1632</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Rain" title="Battle of Rain">Battle of Rain</a>: <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Swedes</a> under <a href="/wiki/Gustavus_Adolphus_of_Sweden" title="Gustavus Adolphus of Sweden">Gustavus Adolphus</a> defeat the <a href="/wiki/Holy_Roman_Empire" title="Holy Roman Empire">Holy Roman Empire</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Thirty_Years%27_War" title="Thirty Years' War">Thirty Years' War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1642" title="1642">1642</a> – <a href="/wiki/Irish_Confederate_Wars" title="Irish Confederate Wars">Irish Confederate Wars</a>: A <a href="/wiki/Confederate_Ireland" title="Confederate Ireland">Confederate Irish</a> militia is routed in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Kilrush" title="Battle of Kilrush">Battle of Kilrush</a> when it attempts to halt the progress of a <a href="/wiki/Royalist" title="Royalist">Royalist</a> Army.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1715" title="1715">1715</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Yamasee_War#Pocotaligo_massacre" title="Yamasee War">Pocotaligo Massacre</a> triggers the start of the <a href="/wiki/Yamasee_War" title="Yamasee War">Yamasee War</a> in <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Carolina" title="Province of Carolina">colonial South Carolina</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1738" title="1738">1738</a> – <i><a href="/wiki/Serse" title="Serse">Serse</a></i>, an <a href="/wiki/Italian_opera" title="Italian opera">Italian opera</a> by <a href="/wiki/George_Frideric_Handel" title="George Frideric Handel">George Frideric Handel</a> receives its premiere performance in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London, England</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1755" title="1755">1755</a> – <a href="/wiki/Samuel_Johnson" title="Samuel Johnson">Samuel Johnson</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/A_Dictionary_of_the_English_Language" title="A Dictionary of the English Language">A Dictionary of the English Language</a></i> is published in London.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1783" title="1783">1783</a> – Preliminary articles of peace ending the <a href="/wiki/American_Revolutionary_War" title="American Revolutionary War">American Revolutionary War</a> (or American War of Independence) are ratified.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1817" title="1817">1817</a> – <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Hopkins_Gallaudet" title="Thomas Hopkins Gallaudet">Thomas Hopkins Gallaudet</a> and <a href="/wiki/Laurent_Clerc" title="Laurent Clerc">Laurent Clerc</a> founded the <a href="/wiki/American_School_for_the_Deaf" title="American School for the Deaf">American School for the Deaf</a>, the first American school for deaf students, in <a href="/wiki/Hartford,_Connecticut" title="Hartford, Connecticut">Hartford, Connecticut</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1861" title="1861">1861</a> – President <a href="/wiki/Abraham_Lincoln" title="Abraham Lincoln">Abraham Lincoln</a> calls for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/President_Lincoln%27s_75,000_Volunteers" title="President Lincoln's 75,000 Volunteers">75,000 Volunteers</a> to quell the insurrection that soon became the <a href="/wiki/American_Civil_War" title="American Civil War">American Civil War</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1865" title="1865">1865</a> – President <a href="/wiki/Abraham_Lincoln" title="Abraham Lincoln">Abraham Lincoln</a> dies after being shot the previous evening by actor <a href="/wiki/John_Wilkes_Booth" title="John Wilkes Booth">John Wilkes Booth</a>. Vice President <a href="/wiki/Andrew_Johnson" title="Andrew Johnson">Andrew Johnson</a>, becomes President upon Lincoln's death.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1892" title="1892">1892</a> – The <a href="/wiki/General_Electric" title="General Electric">General Electric Company</a> is formed.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1896" title="1896">1896</a> – Closing ceremony of the <a href="/wiki/1896_Summer_Olympics" title="1896 Summer Olympics">Games of the I Olympiad</a> in <a href="/wiki/Athens" title="Athens">Athens</a>, Greece.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1900" title="1900">1900</a> – <a href="/wiki/Philippine%E2%80%93American_War" title="Philippine–American War">Philippine–American War</a>: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Filipino_people" title="Filipino people">Filipino</a> guerrillas launch a surprise attack on U.S. infantry and begin a <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Catubig" title="Siege of Catubig">four-day siege</a> of <a href="/wiki/Catubig,_Northern_Samar" title="Catubig, Northern Samar">Catubig</a>, <a href="/wiki/Philippines" title="Philippines">Philippines</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1907" title="1907">1907</a> – <a href="/wiki/Triangle_Fraternity" title="Triangle Fraternity">Triangle Fraternity</a> is founded at the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Illinois_at_Urbana%E2%80%93Champaign" title="University of Illinois at Urbana–Champaign">University of Illinois at Urbana–Champaign</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1912" title="1912">1912</a> – The British passenger liner <a href="/wiki/RMS_Titanic" title="RMS Titanic">RMS <i>Titanic</i></a> <a href="/wiki/Sinking_of_the_RMS_Titanic" title="Sinking of the RMS Titanic">sinks</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">North Atlantic</a> at 2:20 a.m., two hours and forty minutes after hitting an <a href="/wiki/Iceberg" title="Iceberg">iceberg</a>. Only 710 of 2,227 passengers and crew on board survive.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1920" title="1920">1920</a> – Two security guards are murdered during a robbery in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/South_Braintree,_Massachusetts" title="South Braintree, Massachusetts">South Braintree, Massachusetts</a>. <a href="/wiki/Anarchism" title="Anarchism">Anarchists</a> <a href="/wiki/Sacco_and_Vanzetti" title="Sacco and Vanzetti">Sacco and Vanzetti</a> would be convicted of and executed for the crime, amid much controversy.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1922" title="1922">1922</a> – U.S. Senator <a href="/wiki/John_B._Kendrick" title="John B. Kendrick">John B.
