<p><b>Panpulmonata</b> is a taxonomic <a href="/wiki/Clade" title="Clade">clade</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Snails" title="Snails">snails</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Slugs" title="Slugs">slugs</a> in the clade <a href="/wiki/Heterobranchia" title="Heterobranchia">Heterobranchia</a> within the clade <a href="/wiki/Euthyneura" title="Euthyneura">Euthyneura</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-J.C3.B6rger_2010_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-J.C3.B6rger_2010-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Panpulmonata was established as a new <a href="/wiki/Taxon" title="Taxon">taxon</a> by Jörger et al. in October 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-J.C3.B6rger_2010_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-J.C3.B6rger_2010-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The older name "<a href="/wiki/Pulmonata" title="Pulmonata">Pulmonata</a>" referred to a group of gastropods which were considered to be "air-breathers". This meaning certainly does not apply to the panpulmonate groups <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Acochlidia" title="Acochlidia">Acochlidia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sacoglossa" title="Sacoglossa">Sacoglossa</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pyramidelloidea" title="Pyramidelloidea">Pyramidelloidea</a>, and also was inaccurate when applied to some of the more traditional pulmonate taxa such as <a href="/wiki/Siphonarioidea" title="Siphonarioidea">Siphonarioidea</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hygrophila_(Gastropoda)" title="Hygrophila (Gastropoda)">Hygrophila</a>, most members of which lack permanently air-filled lungs.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-J.C3.B6rger_2010_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-J.C3.B6rger_2010-1">[1]</a></sup> However, the term Panpulmonata was chosen by Jörger et al. (2010) to provide some continuity in the terminology.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-J.C3.B6rger_2010_1-4"><a href="#cite_note-J.C3.B6rger_2010-1">[1]</a></sup> Panpulmonata consists of following taxa:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-J.C3.B6rger_2010_1-5"><a href="#cite_note-J.C3.B6rger_2010-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Siphonarioidea" title="Siphonarioidea">Siphonarioidea</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sacoglossa" title="Sacoglossa">Sacoglossa</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Glacidorboidea" title="Glacidorboidea">Glacidorboidea</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Amphiboloidea" title="Amphiboloidea">Amphiboloidea</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Pyramidelloidea" title="Pyramidelloidea">Pyramidelloidea</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hygrophila_(Gastropoda)" title="Hygrophila (Gastropoda)">Hygrophila</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Acochlidiacea" title="Acochlidiacea">Acochlidiacea</a> (mentioned as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Acochlidia" title="Acochlidia">Acochlidia</a>)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Eupulmonata" title="Eupulmonata">Eupulmonata</a>: <a href="/wiki/Stylommatophora" title="Stylommatophora">Stylommatophora</a>, <a href="/wiki/Systellommatophora" title="Systellommatophora">Systellommatophora</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ellobioidea" title="Ellobioidea">Ellobioidea</a>, <a href="/wiki/Otinoidea" title="Otinoidea">Otinoidea</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Trimusculoidea" title="Trimusculoidea">Trimusculoidea</a>.</li>
</ul>
