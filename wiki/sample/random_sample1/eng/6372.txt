<p>The following is a list of awards and nominations received by English actor <a href="/wiki/Benedict_Cumberbatch" title="Benedict Cumberbatch">Benedict Cumberbatch</a>. He has earned three <a href="/wiki/Laurence_Olivier_Award" title="Laurence Olivier Award">Laurence Olivier Award</a> nominations, winning <a href="/wiki/Laurence_Olivier_Award_for_Best_Actor" title="Laurence Olivier Award for Best Actor">Best Actor in a Play</a> for <i>Frankenstein</i>. He has also received four <a href="/wiki/Primetime_Emmy_Award" title="Primetime Emmy Award">Primetime Emmy Award</a> nominations, winning <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Primetime_Emmy_Award_for_Outstanding_Lead_Actor_in_a_Miniseries_or_a_Movie" title="Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actor in a Miniseries or a Movie">Outstanding Lead Actor in a Miniseries or a Movie</a> for <i>Sherlock</i>. His portrayal of Alan Turing in <i>The Imitation Game</i> earned him an <a href="/wiki/87th_Academy_Awards" title="87th Academy Awards">Academy Award nomination</a> for <a href="/wiki/Academy_Award_for_Best_Actor" title="Academy Award for Best Actor">Best Actor</a>. In addition, he has received six <a href="/wiki/British_Academy_of_Film_and_Television_Arts" title="British Academy of Film and Television Arts">British Academy of Film and Television Arts</a> nominations, five <a href="/wiki/Screen_Actors_Guild_Award" title="Screen Actors Guild Award">Screen Actors Guild Award</a> nominations and two <a href="/wiki/Golden_Globe_Award" title="Golden Globe Award">Golden Globe Award</a> nominations among others. In 2014, <i><a href="/wiki/Time_(magazine)" title="Time (magazine)">Time</a></i> magazine included him in its annual <a href="/wiki/Time_100" title="Time 100"><i>Time</i> 100</a> as one of the "Most Influential People in the World". He was appointed <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Commander_of_the_Order_of_the_British_Empire" title="Commander of the Order of the British Empire">Commander of the Order of the British Empire</a> (CBE) by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Queen_Elizabeth_II" title="Queen Elizabeth II">Queen Elizabeth II</a> in June 2015 for his services to the performing arts and to charity.</p>
