<p><b>Bethlehem University</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar">جامعة بيت لحم</span>‎‎) is the first university founded in the <a href="/wiki/West_Bank" title="West Bank">West Bank</a>. Established under Israeli rule in 1973, the university traces its roots to 1893 when the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/De_La_Salle_Christian_Brothers" title="De La Salle Christian Brothers">De La Salle Christian Brothers</a> opened schools in <a href="/wiki/Bethlehem" title="Bethlehem">Bethlehem</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jerusalem" title="Jerusalem">Jerusalem</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jaffa" title="Jaffa">Jaffa</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nazareth" title="Nazareth">Nazareth</a>, <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lebanon" title="Lebanon">Lebanon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jordan" title="Jordan">Jordan</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-UniversityWebsite_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-UniversityWebsite-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p></p>
