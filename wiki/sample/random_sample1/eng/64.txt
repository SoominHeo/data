<p><br/>
Year <b>1296</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MCCXCVI</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Sunday" title="Leap year starting on Sunday">leap year starting on Sunday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1296&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_30" title="March 30">March 30</a> – <a href="/wiki/Capture_of_Berwick_(1296)" title="Capture of Berwick (1296)">Capture of Berwick</a>: King <a href="/wiki/Edward_I_of_England" title="Edward I of England">Edward I of England</a> storms and captures <a href="/wiki/Berwick-upon-Tweed" title="Berwick-upon-Tweed">Berwick-upon-Tweed</a>, sacking what is at this time a <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Scotland" title="Kingdom of Scotland">Scottish</a> border town, with much bloodshed. He slaughters most residents, including those who flee to the churches.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_12" title="April 12">April 12</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Mangrai" title="Mangrai">Mangrai</a> the Great of Ngoenyang establishes a new capital by founding <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chiangmai" title="Chiangmai">Chiangmai</a> and founds the Mangrai Dynasty that ruled the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lanna" title="Lanna">Lanna</a> Kingdom of Chiangmai from 1296 to 1578. The <a href="/wiki/700th_Anniversary_Stadium" title="700th Anniversary Stadium">700th Anniversary Stadium</a> was built in remembrance of this foundation.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_27" title="April 27">April 27</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Dunbar_(1296)" title="Battle of Dunbar (1296)">Battle of Dunbar</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Scotland" title="Kingdom of Scotland">Scots</a> are defeated by <a href="/wiki/Edward_I_of_England" title="Edward I of England">Edward I of England</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_20" title="July 20">July 20</a> – <a href="/wiki/Jalal_ud_din_Firuz_Khilji" title="Jalal ud din Firuz Khilji">Jalal ud din Firuz Khilji</a> dies and <a href="/wiki/Alauddin_Khilji" title="Alauddin Khilji">Ala-Ud-Din-Khilji</a> comes to the throne of the <a href="/wiki/Delhi_Sultanate" title="Delhi Sultanate">Delhi Sultanate</a> in <a href="/wiki/Hindustan" title="Hindustan">Hindustan</a>.</li>
<li>The Chinese diplomat <a href="/wiki/Zhou_Daguan" title="Zhou Daguan">Zhou Daguan</a> spends a year at the court of the <a href="/wiki/Khmer_people" title="Khmer people">Khmer</a> King <a href="/wiki/Indravarman_III" title="Indravarman III">Indravarman III</a> at <a href="/wiki/Angkor" title="Angkor">Angkor</a> and pens a journal setting forth his observations.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Boniface_of_Verona" title="Boniface of Verona">Boniface of Verona</a> expels the <a href="/wiki/Byzantine_Empire" title="Byzantine Empire">Byzantines</a> from their last remaining strongholds on <a href="/wiki/Euboea" title="Euboea">Euboea</a>.</li>
</ul>
