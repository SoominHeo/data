<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Beverly</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Beverly</b> or <b>Beverley</b> is a given name that was at one time commonly a masculine given name but is now almost exclusively a feminine name. It is derived from an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/English_surname" title="English surname">English surname</a>, which was in turn taken from the place name, <a href="/wiki/Beverley" title="Beverley">Beverley</a>. The placename derives from Old English, combining the words <i>befer</i> "<a href="/wiki/Beaver" title="Beaver">beaver</a>" and <i>leah</i> "clearing".</p>
