<p><b>Beth Jean Riesgraf</b> (born August 24, 1978) is an American actress. She is known for her portrayal of Parker on the <a href="/wiki/TNT_(TV_channel)" title="TNT (TV channel)">TNT</a> television series <i><a href="/wiki/Leverage_(TV_series)" title="Leverage (TV series)">Leverage</a></i> (2008–12).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_and_education">Early life and education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Beth_Riesgraf&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life and education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Originally from <a href="/wiki/Minnesota" title="Minnesota">Minnesota</a>, Riesgraf graduated from <a href="/wiki/Cimarron-Memorial_High_School" title="Cimarron-Memorial High School">Cimarron-Memorial High School</a> in <a href="/wiki/Las_Vegas" title="Las Vegas">Las Vegas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nevada" title="Nevada">Nevada</a> in 1996.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Beth_Riesgraf&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Riesgraf appeared with then-fiancé Jason Lee as the character Natalie Duckworth on the show <i><a href="/wiki/My_Name_Is_Earl" title="My Name Is Earl">My Name Is Earl</a></i> in 2005 and 2007, and in <i><a href="/wiki/Alvin_and_the_Chipmunks_(film)" title="Alvin and the Chipmunks (film)">Alvin and The Chipmunks</a></i> as a mother in the supermarket in 2007. In 2008, she played the unnamed bride (presumed to be Catherine) in the official music video for "<a href="/wiki/Cath..." title="Cath...">Cath...</a>" by <a href="/wiki/Death_Cab_for_Cutie" title="Death Cab for Cutie">Death Cab for Cutie</a>. From 2008 to 2012, she played the main role of Parker in the TNT series <i><a href="/wiki/Leverage_(TV_series)" title="Leverage (TV series)">Leverage</a></i>. In 2015, Riesgraf starred in the USA Network series <i><a href="/wiki/Complications_(TV_series)" title="Complications (TV series)">Complications</a></i>, which follows a morally conflicted doctor's life. The show lasted for one season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Beth_Riesgraf&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Riesgraf was in a relationship with <a href="/wiki/Jason_Lee_(actor)" title="Jason Lee (actor)">Jason Lee</a> from 2001 to 2007 and the two were engaged to be married. With Lee, she had her first child at age 25, a son named Pilot Inspektor Riesgraf-Lee, on September 28, 2003.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
