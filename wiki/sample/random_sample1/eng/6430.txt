<p><i><b>Vesti segodnya</b></i> (News Today, <a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru"><i>Вести Сегодня</i></span>) is a daily (as per 2016) newspaper published in <a href="/wiki/Latvia" title="Latvia">Latvia</a>, formerly <b>Sovetskaya molodezh</b> and <b>SM segodnya</b>, the first among Russian-language dailies and tying third and fourth places among all dailies by subsciption, as per January 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
