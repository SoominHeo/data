<p><i><b>Baby Steps</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<a href="/wiki/Japanese_language" title="Japanese language">Japanese</a>: <span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">ベイビーステップ</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <a href="/wiki/Hepburn_romanization" title="Hepburn romanization">Hepburn</a>: <i>Beibī Suteppu</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a Japanese <a href="/wiki/Manga" title="Manga">manga</a> series by Hikaru Katsuki. It has been serialized in <a href="/wiki/Kodansha" title="Kodansha">Kodansha</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Weekly_Sh%C5%8Dnen_Magazine" title="Weekly Shōnen Magazine">Weekly Shōnen Magazine</a></i> since October 2007. The story is centered on Eiichirō Maruo, a first year honor student who one day decides that he is lacking exercise. He then joins the Southern Tennis Club (STC) where he begins his tennis journey. An <a href="/wiki/Anime" title="Anime">anime</a> television series adaptation of the series began airing in Japan in April 2014 with a second season began airing April 5, 2015. It also won the 38th <a href="/wiki/Kodansha_Manga_Award" title="Kodansha Manga Award">Kodansha Manga Award</a> in the Best Shōnen Manga category.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-38th_award_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-38th_award-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Story">Story</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Baby_Steps&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Story">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Eiichirō Maruo, a first year honor student, one day decides he is unhappy with the way things are and lacks exercise. He finds a flyer for the Tennis Club and decides to check it out. He is instantly captivated by it. With no prior experience and poor physical conditioning, he embarks on a tennis journey using his smarts, dedication and work ethic. He uses his inherent studious nature to develop an extremely strategic approach to tennis, taking notes on the habits and tendencies of his opponents thus allowing him to predict their shots before they make them.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Southern_Tennis_Club">Southern Tennis Club</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Baby_Steps&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Southern Tennis Club">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<dl>
<dt>Eiichirō Maruo <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">丸尾 栄一郎</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Maruo Eiichirō</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><span style="font-weight: normal">Voiced by: <a href="/wiki/Taishi_Murata" title="Taishi Murata">Taishi Murata</a></span></dd>
<dd>Eiichirō Maruo, also called Ei-chan <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">エーちゃん</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> by his friends, is the main <a href="/wiki/Protagonist" title="Protagonist">protagonist</a> of the series. He is an honor student who quickly became famous for his meticulous note-taking in class. During his first year of high school, however, he got into a rut studying all of the time and wanted to find a way to exercise. When he meets Natsu, a girl in the same year as him, her passion for tennis convinces him to try playing it. Like Natsu and their <i>senpai</i> Takuma, Ei-chan becomes a member of a prominent youth tennis program at Southern Tennis Club (STC). His years of experience in studying and taking notes help him in his path to become a tennis player. His aim is to become a professional tennis player. Ei-chan and Natsu's growing relationship is also a major part of the series.</dd>
</dl>
<dl>
<dt>Natsu Takasaki <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">鷹崎 奈津</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Takasaki Natsu</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><span style="font-weight: normal">Voiced by: <a href="/wiki/Minako_Kotobuki" title="Minako Kotobuki">Minako Kotobuki</a></span></dd>
<dd>Natsu Takasaki, also known as Nat-chan <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">なっちゃん</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>, is the main female <a href="/wiki/Protagonist" title="Protagonist">protagonist</a>. She is a member of Southern Tennis Club and has played tennis since she was very young. She studies at the same high school and in the same year as Maruo. She is aiming to become a professional player. Natsu is the exact opposite of Maruo tennis-wise. She is always very optimistic and encouraging toward Maruo, and the two become very close as the series progresses.</dd>
</dl>
<dl>
<dt>Takuma Egawa <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">江川 逞</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Egawa Takuma</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><span style="font-weight: normal">Voiced by: <a href="/wiki/Daisuke_Namikawa" title="Daisuke Namikawa">Daisuke Namikawa</a></span></dd>
<dd>Takuma is one year older than Ei-chan and Nat-chan and attends the same high school, where he is known as a delinquent. He and Natsu have known each other for a long time through tennis practice at STC. He showed a talent for tennis at a young age. He is one of the best junior players in Japan, and he aims to go pro. He is often antagonistic toward others, but he's also protective of Natsu.</dd>
</dl>
<dl>
<dt>Yukichi Fukazawa <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">深沢 諭吉</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Fukazawa Yukichi</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>

</dl>
<dl>
<dt>Yūsaku Miura <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">三浦優作</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Miura Yūsaku</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span></dt>
<dd><span style="font-weight: normal">Voiced by: <a href="/wiki/Taiten_Kusunoki" title="Taiten Kusunoki">Taiten Kusunoki</a></span></dd>
<dd>One of the boys' coaches at STC. He becomes very interested in helping Maruo after seeing his work ethic and how quickly he's able to improve.</dd>
