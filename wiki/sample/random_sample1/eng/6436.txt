<p>The <b>Beijing International Film Festival</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">北京国际电影节</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Film_festival" title="Film festival">film festival</a> in <a href="/wiki/Beijing" title="Beijing">Beijing</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. Founded in 2011, the film festival is supported and sponsored by the government of the Municipality of Beijing. The festival since its launch has been heavily attended by Hollywood executives, directors, producers and studio heads.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> As China's box office has expanded exponentially over the past decade, the festival is aiming to provide a high-profile forum for the interaction between the Chinese and the international film industries.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="2012_Festival">2012 Festival</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Beijing_International_Film_Festival&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: 2012 Festival">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Notable international guest speakers at the 2012 festival including <a href="/wiki/James_Cameron" title="James Cameron">James Cameron</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Cameron_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Cameron-3">[3]</a></sup> who unveiled the details of the upcoming <i>Avatar</i> sequels at the film festival;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Cameron_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-Cameron-3">[3]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Jim_Gianopulos" title="Jim Gianopulos">Jim Gianopulos</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-fest2012_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-fest2012-4">[4]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Jeremy_Renner" title="Jeremy Renner">Jeremy Renner</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tom_DeSanto" title="Tom DeSanto">Tom DeSanto</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jon_Landau" title="Jon Landau">Jon Landau</a>, who were there to discuss co-productions between Hollywood and Chinese film studios.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="2013_Festival">2013 Festival</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Beijing_International_Film_Festival&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 2013 Festival">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Notable guests at the 2013 festival includes Lucasfilm president <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kathleen_Kennedy_(film_producer)" title="Kathleen Kennedy (film producer)">Kathleen Kennedy</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-fest2013_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-fest2013-5">[5]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Paul_Hanneman" title="Paul Hanneman">Paul Hanneman</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-fest2013_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-fest2013-5">[5]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Keanu_Reeves" title="Keanu Reeves">Keanu Reeves</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-fest2013_5-2"><a href="#cite_note-fest2013-5">[5]</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Peter_Chan" title="Peter Chan">Peter Chan</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-fest2013_5-3"><a href="#cite_note-fest2013-5">[5]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Luc_Besson" title="Luc Besson">Luc Besson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jean-Jacques_Annaud" title="Jean-Jacques Annaud">Jean-Jacques Annaud</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Wolfgang_Petersen" title="Wolfgang Petersen">Wolfgang Petersen</a> also discussed their upcoming co-productions in China at the festival.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-fest2013_5-4"><a href="#cite_note-fest2013-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="2014_Festival">2014 Festival</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Beijing_International_Film_Festival&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 2014 Festival">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The 2014 festival saw <a href="/wiki/Jean_Reno" title="Jean Reno">Jean Reno</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Alfonso_Cuaron" title="Alfonso Cuaron">Alfonso Cuaron</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> Paramount Pictures COO Frederick Huntsberry,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Stone" title="Oliver Stone">Oliver Stone</a>, MPAA president <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Christopher_Dodd" title="Christopher Dodd">Christopher Dodd</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peter_Del_Vecho" title="Peter Del Vecho">Peter Del Vecho</a>, <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Saldanha" title="Carlos Saldanha">Carlos Saldanha</a>, <a href="/wiki/John_Woo" title="John Woo">John Woo</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Timur_Bekmambetov" title="Timur Bekmambetov">Timur Bekmambetov</a>, Peter Ziering, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Andres_Vicente_Gomez" title="Andres Vicente Gomez">Andres Vicente Gomez</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rajkumar_Hirani" title="Rajkumar Hirani">Rajkumar Hirani</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jim_Sheridan" title="Jim Sheridan">Jim Sheridan</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> The festival also saw a large representation from the <a href="/wiki/British_Film_Institute" title="British Film Institute">BFI</a>, the British Film Commission and BBC, who are aiming for a closer co-production agreement with China.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="2015_Festival">2015 Festival</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Beijing_International_Film_Festival&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2015 Festival">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The 2015 festival saw <a href="/wiki/Arnold_Schwarzenegger" title="Arnold Schwarzenegger">Arnold Schwarzenegger</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Darren_Aronofsky" title="Darren Aronofsky">Darren Aronofsky</a> <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> attend the event in April.</p>
