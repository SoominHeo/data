<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Neighbourhoods">Neighbourhoods</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wijchen&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Neighbourhoods">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Neighbourhoods in Wijchen include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Centre: Kloosterakker</li>
<li>Wijchen-Oost: Valendries, Oosterweg and Uilenboom.</li>
<li>Woezik: Veenhof and Saltshof.</li>
<li>Wijchen-Noord.</li>
<li>Achterlo: Homberg, Heilige Stoel and Kraaijenberg.</li>
<li>Wijchen-West: Blauwe Hof and Aalsburg.</li>
<li>Wijchen-Zuid: Abersland, Diepvoorde, Huissteden, Hoogmeer, De Ververt, De Geer, Elsland, De Weertjes, De Grippen, Zesakkers, Zevendreef, Sluiskamp, Oudelaan, and Kronenland.</li>
<li>Kerkeveld: De Gamert, De Meren, Diemenwei, De Flier, De Lingert.</li>
<li>Huurlingsedam.</li>
</ul>
<p>Streets in most neighbourhoods are numbered instead of named. This is not common practice in the Netherlands, therefore the street numbers are included in the <a href="/wiki/House_numbering" title="House numbering">house numbering</a>. E.g. someone living in Abersland, 11th street, house #05 will usually use Abersland 1105 in his address.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Wijchen_Castle_.28town_hall.29">Wijchen Castle (town hall)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wijchen&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Wijchen Castle (town hall)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Parts of Wijchen Castle (<i>Kasteel Wijchen</i> in Dutch) date from the 14th century, but it took its current <a href="/wiki/Mannerism" title="Mannerism">Mannerist</a> form in the years 1609–1629. It is surrounded by a <a href="/wiki/Moat" title="Moat">moat</a> and used to serve as the town hall. Currently, the town hall is on the other side of the road, while the castle is only used for important meetings.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
