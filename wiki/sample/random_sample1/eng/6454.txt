<p>The <b>Viet Nam Military History Museum</b>, set up on 17 July 1956, is one of seven <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/National_museum" title="National museum">national museums</a> in <a href="/wiki/Vietnam" title="Vietnam">Vietnam</a>. It covers 12,800 m<sup>2</sup>. It is situated in central <a href="/wiki/Hanoi" title="Hanoi">Hanoi</a>, opposite the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lenin_Park" title="Lenin Park">Lenin Park</a> and near the <a href="/wiki/Ho_Chi_Minh_Mausoleum" title="Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum">Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum</a>.</p>
<p>Entrance is 20,000 VND.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Items_on_outside_display">Items on outside display</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Vietnam_Military_History_Museum&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Items on outside display">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Items on display include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bell <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UH-1_Iroquois" title="UH-1 Iroquois">UH-1 Iroquois</a> H model</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Boeing_B-52_Stratofortress" title="Boeing B-52 Stratofortress">B-52G</a> wreckage of jet shot down during <a href="/wiki/Operation_Linebacker_II" title="Operation Linebacker II">Operation Linebacker II</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Cessna_A-37_Dragonfly" title="Cessna A-37 Dragonfly">Cessna A-37 Dragonfly</a> B model</li>
<li>Douglas <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/A-1_Skyraider" title="A-1 Skyraider">A-1 Skyraider</a> E and H models</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/M3_Half-track" title="M3 Half-track">M3 Half-track</a> purportedly used by Groupe Mobile 100 and captured in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Mang_Yang_Pass" title="Battle of Mang Yang Pass">Battle of Mang Yang Pass</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/M101_howitzer" title="M101 howitzer">M101 howitzer</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/M107_Self-Propelled_Gun" title="M107 Self-Propelled Gun">M107 Self-Propelled Gun</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/M113_armored_personnel_carrier" title="M113 armored personnel carrier">M113 armored personnel carrier</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/M114_155_mm_howitzer" title="M114 155 mm howitzer">M114 155 mm howitzer</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Mikoyan-Gurevich_MiG-17" title="Mikoyan-Gurevich MiG-17">Mikoyan-Gurevich MiG-17</a> F model</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Mikoyan-Gurevich_MiG-21" title="Mikoyan-Gurevich MiG-21">Mikoyan-Gurevich MiG-21</a> MF &amp; PFM</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/PT-76" title="PT-76">PT-76</a> amphibious light tank</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/SA-2" title="SA-2">SA-2</a> Guideline SAM</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/T-34" title="T-34">T-34</a> tank</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/T-54/55" title="T-54/55">T-54</a> tank</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/37_mm_automatic_air_defense_gun_M1939_(61-K)" title="37 mm automatic air defense gun M1939 (61-K)">37 mm automatic air defense gun M1939 (61-K)</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/57_mm_AZP_S-60" title="57 mm AZP S-60">57 mm AZP S-60</a></li>

<li><a href="/wiki/130_mm_towed_field_gun_M1954_(M-46)" title="130 mm towed field gun M1954 (M-46)">130 mm towed field gun M1954 (M-46)</a></li>
