<p>Year <b>549</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">DXLIX</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Friday" title="Common year starting on Friday">common year starting on Friday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>. The denomination 549 for this year has been used since the early medieval period, when the <a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a> <a href="/wiki/Calendar_era" title="Calendar era">calendar era</a> became the prevalent method in Europe for naming years.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=549&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=549&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Byzantine_Empire">Byzantine Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=549&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Byzantine Empire">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Siege_of_Rome_(549-550)" title="Siege of Rome (549-550)">Siege of Rome</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Ostrogoths" title="Ostrogoths">Ostrogoths</a> under <a href="/wiki/Totila" title="Totila">Totila</a> besiege <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a> for the third time, after <a href="/wiki/Belisarius" title="Belisarius">Belisarius</a> has returned to <a href="/wiki/Constantinople" title="Constantinople">Constantinople</a>. He offers a peace agreement, but this is rejected by Emperor <a href="/wiki/Justinian_I" title="Justinian I">Justinian I</a>.</li>
<li>Totila conquers the city of <a href="/wiki/Perugia" title="Perugia">Perugia</a> (<a href="/wiki/Central_Italy" title="Central Italy">Central Italy</a>) and stations a <a href="/wiki/Goths" title="Goths">Gothic</a> garrison. He takes bishop <a href="/wiki/Herculanus_of_Perugia" title="Herculanus of Perugia">Herculanus</a> prisoner, and orders him to be completely <a href="/wiki/Flaying" title="Flaying">flayed</a>. The Ostrogoth soldier asked to perform this gruesome execution shows pity, and <a href="/wiki/Decapitation" title="Decapitation">decapitates</a> Herculanus before the <a href="/wiki/Skin" title="Skin">skin</a> on every part of his body is removed.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li>In the <a href="/wiki/Circus_Maximus" title="Circus Maximus">Circus Maximus</a>, first and largest <a href="/wiki/Circus_(building)" title="Circus (building)">circus</a> in Rome, the last <a href="/wiki/Chariot_racing" title="Chariot racing">chariot races</a> are held.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=549&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Agila_I" title="Agila I">Agila I</a> succeeds <a href="/wiki/Theudigisel" title="Theudigisel">Theudigisel</a> as king of the <a href="/wiki/Visigoths" title="Visigoths">Visigoths</a>, after he is murdered by a group of conspirators during a <a href="/wiki/Banquet" title="Banquet">banquet</a> in <a href="/wiki/Seville" title="Seville">Seville</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Persia">Persia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=549&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Persia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>Spring – <a href="/wiki/Lazic_War" title="Lazic War">Lazic War</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Byzantine_army" title="Byzantine army">Byzantine army</a> under <a href="/wiki/Bessas_(general)" title="Bessas (general)">Bessas</a> combines forces with King <a href="/wiki/Gubazes_II_of_Lazica" title="Gubazes II of Lazica">Gubazes II</a>, and defeats the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sassanid_Empire" title="Sassanid Empire">Persians</a> in <a href="/wiki/Lazica" title="Lazica">Lazica</a> (modern <a href="/wiki/Georgia_(country)" title="Georgia (country)">Georgia</a>) in a surprise attack. </ul>
