<p><i><b>Ben 10: Omniverse</b></i> is an American <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cartoon_series" title="Cartoon series">animated television series</a> and the fourth installment of the <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ben_10_(franchise)" title="Ben 10 (franchise)">Ben 10 franchise</a></i>, which aired on <a href="/wiki/Cartoon_Network" title="Cartoon Network">Cartoon Network</a> from September 22, 2012 to November 14, 2014, in the United States.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Man of Action (group consisting of <a href="/wiki/Duncan_Rouleau" title="Duncan Rouleau">Duncan Rouleau</a>, <a href="/wiki/Joe_Casey" title="Joe Casey">Joe Casey</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Joe_Kelly_(comics)" title="Joe Kelly (comics)">Joe Kelly</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Steven_T._Seagle" title="Steven T. Seagle">Steven T. Seagle</a>) created the franchise.</p>
<p>The series was announced at Cartoon Network's Upfront in 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> Concept art, described as an homage to the original <i>Ben 10</i> series,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NewAliens_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-NewAliens-6">[6]</a></sup> designed by Derrick J. Wyatt<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Plot_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-Plot-7">[7]</a></sup> (<i><a href="/wiki/Transformers:_Animated" title="Transformers: Animated">Transformers: Animated</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Scooby-Doo!_Mystery_Incorporated" title="Scooby-Doo! Mystery Incorporated">Scooby-Doo! Mystery Incorporated</a></i>) was first unveiled at the 2012 UK Toy Fair.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>The series premiered on September 22, 2012, with a "sneak peek episode" that aired on August 1, 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Q2_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-Q2-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-date_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-date-11">[11]</a></sup> A "sneak peek" of the series aired after "Ben 10 Week" (March 19, 2012 – March 24, 2012).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span></h2>
<p>The series follows the adventures of sixteen-year-old Ben Tennyson, wielder of the Omnitrix, an extremely powerful watch-like device that allows Ben to change into a multitude of various aliens; each with their own special abilities and skills. The storylines alternate between that of eleven-year-old Ben (one year after <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ben_10_(TV_series)" title="Ben 10 (TV series)">the original series</a>) and sixteen-year-old Ben (a few months after <i><a href="/wiki/Ben_10:_Ultimate_Alien" title="Ben 10: Ultimate Alien">Ben 10: Ultimate Alien</a></i>). After Gwen leaves for college and Kevin leaves to be closer to her, sixteen-year-old Ben gets a new partner, a rookie by-the-book alien plumber named Rook Blonko (surname first). On a mission to explore a secret underground alien city named Undertown, Ben explores the quirkier side of things in the alien underground and discovers that enemies from his past are looking for a rematch.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NewAliens_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-NewAliens-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-date_11-1"><a href="#cite_note-date-11">[11]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
<p>In "T.G.I.S.", it is revealed that the <i><a href="/wiki/Ben_10" title="Ben 10">Ben 10</a></i> universe shares the same universe with <i><a href="/wiki/The_Secret_Saturdays" title="The Secret Saturdays">The Secret Saturdays</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Merchandise">Merchandise</span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Toy_line">Toy line</span></h3>
<p>A toy line manufactured by <a href="/wiki/Bandai" title="Bandai">Bandai</a> was originally shown off at Toy Fairs around the world. A possible unintentional leak of official images of the line was released on the website of department store <a href="/wiki/Kmart" title="Kmart">Kmart</a>. Figures that were revealed at Toy Fairs such as Bloxx,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-39"><a href="#cite_note-39">[39]</a></sup> Shocksquatch,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-40"><a href="#cite_note-40">[40]</a></sup> 16-year-old Ben Tennyson,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-41"><a href="#cite_note-41">[41]</a></sup> an 11-year old version of Ben,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-42"><a href="#cite_note-42">[42]</a></sup> and Ben's sidekick Rook<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-43"><a href="#cite_note-43">[43]</a></sup> were listed on the website. The vehicle play-set, "Rook's Truck", has won an award for Best Action Figures/Accessories at the 2012 London Toy Fair.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-44"><a href="#cite_note-44">[44]</a></sup> The Omnitrix Touch has a gray color instead of the white it features in the show.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Video_games">Video games</span></h2>
<p>A <a href="/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse_(video_game)" title="Ben 10: Omniverse (video game)">video game of the same name</a> is developed by <a href="/wiki/Vicious_Cycle_Software" title="Vicious Cycle Software">Vicious Cycle Studios</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Wii_U" title="Wii U">Wii U</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wii" title="Wii">Wii</a>, <a href="/wiki/PlayStation_3" title="PlayStation 3">PlayStation 3</a>, <a href="/wiki/Xbox_360" title="Xbox 360">Xbox 360</a> and by <a href="/wiki/1st_Playable_Productions" title="1st Playable Productions">1st Playable Productions</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Nintendo_3DS" title="Nintendo 3DS">Nintendo 3DS</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nintendo_DS" title="Nintendo DS">Nintendo DS</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-VG_45-0"><a href="#cite_note-VG-45">[45]</a></sup> The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Action_(genre)" title="Action (genre)">action</a> <a href="/wiki/Beat_%27em_up" title="Beat 'em up">beat 'em up</a> features 15 playable characters on DS and 3DS and 16 playable characters on the other systems. It also features a two-player co-op play.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-VG_45-1"><a href="#cite_note-VG-45">[45]</a></sup> The game follows Ben and Rook in a battle to defeat a fierce villain, named Malware, who has evil plans of destroying the world.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-VG_45-2"><a href="#cite_note-VG-45">[45]</a></sup></p>
<p><i>Omniverse</i> also spawned a <a href="/wiki/Ben_10:_Omniverse_2" title="Ben 10: Omniverse 2">second video game</a>, which was developed by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/High_Voltage_Studios" title="High Voltage Studios">High Voltage Studios</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Wii_U" title="Wii U">Wii U</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wii" title="Wii">Wii</a>, <a href="/wiki/PlayStation_3" title="PlayStation 3">PlayStation 3</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Xbox_360" title="Xbox 360">Xbox 360</a> and by <a href="/wiki/1st_Playable_Productions" title="1st Playable Productions">1st Playable Productions</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Nintendo_3DS" title="Nintendo 3DS">Nintendo 3DS</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-46"><a href="#cite_note-46">[46]</a></sup> This game takes place during the events of Frogs of War, where Ben must fight the Incursions to get back to Earth and stop Emperor Milleous, Attea, Dr. Psychobos, and the Way Bads.</p>
