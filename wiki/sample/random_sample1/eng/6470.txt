<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Benzoquinone</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">
					Jump to:					<a href="#mw-head">navigation</a>, 					<a href="#p-search">search</a>
</div>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Benzoquinone</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Quinone" title="Quinone">quinone</a> with a single <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Benzene_ring" title="Benzene ring">benzene ring</a>, of which there are only two:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1,4-Benzoquinone" title="1,4-Benzoquinone">1,4-Benzoquinone</a>, most commonly (also <i>para</i>-benzoquinone, <i>p</i>-benzoquinone, <i>para</i>-quinone, or just quinone)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1,2-Benzoquinone" title="1,2-Benzoquinone">1,2-Benzoquinone</a>, less commonly (also <i>ortho</i>-benzoquinone, <i>o</i>-benzoquinone, <i>ortho</i>-quinone)</li>
</ul>
<p><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Benzoquinones.png"><img alt="Benzoquinones.png" data-file-height="375" data-file-width="669" height="179" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/66/Benzoquinones.png/320px-Benzoquinones.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/66/Benzoquinones.png/480px-Benzoquinones.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/66/Benzoquinones.png/640px-Benzoquinones.png 2x" width="320"/></a></p>
<p>It has been found in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rhizomes" title="Rhizomes">rhizomes</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Iris_kemaonensis" title="Iris kemaonensis">Iris kemaonensis</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
