<p>Year <b>558</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">DLVIII</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Tuesday" title="Common year starting on Tuesday">common year starting on Tuesday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>. The denomination 558 for this year has been used since the early medieval period, when the <a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a> <a href="/wiki/Calendar_era" title="Calendar era">calendar era</a> became the prevalent method in Europe for naming years.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=558&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=558&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Byzantine_Empire">Byzantine Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=558&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Byzantine Empire">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_7" title="May 7">May 7</a> – In <a href="/wiki/Constantinople" title="Constantinople">Constantinople</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Dome" title="Dome">dome</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Hagia_Sophia" title="Hagia Sophia">Hagia Sophia</a> collapses due to an <a href="/wiki/Earthquake" title="Earthquake">earthquake</a>. Emperor <a href="/wiki/Justinian_I" title="Justinian I">Justinian I</a> orders the dome to be rebuilt.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=558&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Avars_(Carpathians)" title="Avars (Carpathians)">Avars</a> and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Slavic_peoples" title="Slavic peoples">Slavs</a> occupy the <a href="/wiki/Great_Hungarian_Plain" title="Great Hungarian Plain">Hungarian Plain</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Balkans" title="Balkans">Balkans</a>. The threat of Avar domination prompts the <a href="/wiki/Lombards" title="Lombards">Lombards</a> to <a href="/wiki/Human_migration" title="Human migration">migrate</a> to <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/December_13" title="December 13">December 13</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Chlothar_I" title="Chlothar I">Chlothar I</a> reunites the <a href="/wiki/Francia" title="Francia">Frankish Kingdom</a> after his brother <a href="/wiki/Childebert_I" title="Childebert I">Childebert I</a> dies, becoming sole ruler of the <a href="/wiki/Franks" title="Franks">Franks</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Conall_mac_Comgaill" title="Conall mac Comgaill">Conall mac Comgaill</a> becomes king of <a href="/wiki/D%C3%A1l_Riata" title="Dál Riata">Dál Riata</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Gaels" title="Gaels">Gaelic</a> overkingdom on the western coast of <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=558&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ist%C3%A4mi" title="Istämi">Istämi</a>, ruler of the <a href="/wiki/Western_Turkic_Khaganate" title="Western Turkic Khaganate">Western Turkic Khaganate</a>, establishes <a href="/wiki/Diplomacy" title="Diplomacy">diplomatic</a> relations with the <a href="/wiki/Byzantine_Empire" title="Byzantine Empire">Byzantine Empire</a>.</li>
