<p><b>56.com</b> is one of the largest <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Video_sharing" title="Video sharing">video sharing</a> websites in <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>, where users can upload, view and share <a href="/wiki/Video_clip" title="Video clip">video clips</a>.</p>
<p>A fully owned subsidiary of <a href="/wiki/Sohu" title="Sohu">Sohu</a>, the company is headquartered in <a href="/wiki/Tianhe_District" title="Tianhe District">Tianhe District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Guangzhou" title="Guangzhou">Guangzhou</a>, <a href="/wiki/Guangdong" title="Guangdong">Guangdong</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>The domain <i>56.com</i> attracted at least 3.2 million visitors annually by 2008 according to a <a href="/wiki/Compete.com" title="Compete.com">Compete.com</a> survey.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>Access to the Chinese online video provider 56.com was suspended since 18:00 on June 3, 2008. 56.com made an explanation at 10:00 on June 4, 16 hours after the website's access was stopped, and said that the website had experienced a server failure and was being repaired, but it did not give a clear time for when the site's services would be fully resumed. Some media, such as Hexun.com and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sina.com" title="Sina.com">Sina.com</a>, reported that as 56.com had frequently violated relevant government rules with its video content, it had therefore been issued a warning by the government and might be closed for a few days. However, this was denied by 56.com. By July 11, 2008, 56.com was back and operational.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>On December 31 2014, <a href="/wiki/Sohu" title="Sohu">Sohu</a> had reached a deal with compatriot social networking site operator <a href="/wiki/Renren" title="Renren">Renren</a> to acquire the 56.com. According to a source familiar with the deal, Sohu paid USD 25 million for 56.com.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
