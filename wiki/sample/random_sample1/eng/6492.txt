<p><b>Veles</b> (<a href="/wiki/Macedonian_language" title="Macedonian language">Macedonian</a>: <span lang="mk" xml:lang="mk">Велес</span> <span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Bulgarian_and_Macedonian" title="Help:IPA for Bulgarian and Macedonian">[ˈvɛːlɛs]</a></span><small class="nowrap metadata"> (<a href="/wiki/File:Mk-Veles.ogg" title="File:Mk-Veles.ogg"><img alt="" data-file-height="500" data-file-width="500" height="13" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/21/Speaker_Icon.svg/13px-Speaker_Icon.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/21/Speaker_Icon.svg/20px-Speaker_Icon.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/21/Speaker_Icon.svg/26px-Speaker_Icon.svg.png 2x" width="13"/></a> <a class="internal" href="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/3/3a/Mk-Veles.ogg" title="Mk-Veles.ogg">listen</a>)</small>) is a municipality in central part of <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Macedonia" title="Republic of Macedonia">Republic of Macedonia</a>. <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Veles_(city)" title="Veles (city)">Veles</a></i> is also the name of the town where the municipal seat is found. Veles Municipality is part of the <a href="/wiki/Vardar_Statistical_Region" title="Vardar Statistical Region">Vardar Statistical Region</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Veles_Municipality&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The municipality borders <a href="/wiki/%C4%8Ca%C5%A1ka_Municipality" title="Čaška Municipality">Čaška Municipality</a> and <a href="/wiki/Zelenikovo_Municipality" title="Zelenikovo Municipality">Zelenikovo Municipality</a> to the west, <a href="/wiki/Petrovec_Municipality" title="Petrovec Municipality">Petrovec Municipality</a> to the north, and <a href="/wiki/Gradsko_Municipality" title="Gradsko Municipality">Gradsko</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lozovo_Municipality" title="Lozovo Municipality">Lozovo</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Sveti_Nikole_Municipality" title="Sveti Nikole Municipality">Sveti Nikole</a> municipalities to the east.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Veles_Municipality&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>According to the last national census from 2002, this municipality had 55,108 inhabitants.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> At the census of 1994, it had 56,571 inhabitants. The majority are <a href="/wiki/Macedonians_(ethnic_group)" title="Macedonians (ethnic group)">Macedonians</a> (46,767 or 84.9%), while other ethnic groups include <a href="/wiki/Bosniaks" title="Bosniaks">Bosniaks</a> (2,406 or 4.4%), Albanians (2,299 or 4.2%), Turks (1,724 or 3.1%), and others.</p>
