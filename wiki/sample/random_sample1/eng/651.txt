<p>Year <b>584</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">DLXXXIV</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Saturday" title="Leap year starting on Saturday">leap year starting on Saturday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>. The denomination 584 for this year has been used since the early medieval period, when the <a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a> <a href="/wiki/Calendar_era" title="Calendar era">calendar era</a> became the prevalent method in Europe for naming years.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=584&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=584&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=584&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>September – King <a href="/wiki/Chilperic_I" title="Chilperic I">Chilperic I</a> dies after a 23-year reign over a territory extending from <a href="/wiki/Aquitaine" title="Aquitaine">Aquitaine</a> to the northern seacoast of what later will be <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>. He is stabbed to death while returning from a <a href="/wiki/Hunting" title="Hunting">hunt</a> near <a href="/wiki/Chelles,_Seine-et-Marne" title="Chelles, Seine-et-Marne">Chelles</a>. His wife <a href="/wiki/Fredegund" title="Fredegund">Fredegund</a> has paid for his <a href="/wiki/Assassination" title="Assassination">assassination</a>, seizes his wealth, flees to <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> with her son <a href="/wiki/Chlothar_II" title="Chlothar II">Chlothar II</a>, and persuades the <a href="/wiki/Nobility" title="Nobility">nobles</a> to accept him as legitimate heir while she serves as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Regency_(government)" title="Regency (government)">regent</a>, continuing her power struggles with <a href="/wiki/Guntram" title="Guntram">Guntram</a>, king of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Burgundy" title="Kingdom of Burgundy">Burgundy</a>, and her sister <a href="/wiki/Brunhilda_of_Austrasia" title="Brunhilda of Austrasia">Brunhilda</a>, queen mother of <a href="/wiki/Austrasia" title="Austrasia">Austrasia</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Lombards" title="Lombards">Lombards</a> re-establish a unified monarchy after a 10-year <a href="/wiki/Interregnum" title="Interregnum">interregnum</a> (<a href="/wiki/Rule_of_the_Dukes" title="Rule of the Dukes">Rule of the Dukes</a>). Threatened by a <a href="/wiki/Franks" title="Franks">Frankish</a> invasion that the <a href="/wiki/Dux" title="Dux">dukes</a> has provoked, they elect <a href="/wiki/Authari" title="Authari">Authari</a> (son of <a href="/wiki/Cleph" title="Cleph">Cleph</a>) as their king and give him the capital of <a href="/wiki/Pavia" title="Pavia">Pavia</a> (<a href="/wiki/Northern_Italy" title="Northern Italy">Northern Italy</a>).</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Visigoths" title="Visigoths">Visigoths</a> under King <a href="/wiki/Liuvigild" title="Liuvigild">Liuvigild</a> capture the city of <a href="/wiki/Seville" title="Seville">Seville</a>, after a <a href="/wiki/Siege" title="Siege">siege</a> of nearly 2 years. His rebellious son <a href="/wiki/Hermenegild" title="Hermenegild">Hermenegild</a> seeks refuge in a church at <a href="/wiki/C%C3%B3rdoba,_Andalusia" title="Córdoba, Andalusia">Córdoba</a>, but is arrested and banished to <a href="/wiki/Tarragona" title="Tarragona">Tarragona</a>. His wife <a href="/wiki/Ingund_(wife_of_Hermenegild)" title="Ingund (wife of Hermenegild)">Inguld</a> flees with her son to <a href="/wiki/Diocese_of_Africa" title="Diocese of Africa">Africa</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Exarchate_of_Ravenna" title="Exarchate of Ravenna">Exarchate of Ravenna</a> is founded, and organised into a group of <a href="/wiki/Duchy" title="Duchy">duchies</a>, mainly coastal cities on the <a href="/wiki/Italian_Peninsula" title="Italian Peninsula">Italian Peninsula</a>. The civil and military head of these <a href="/wiki/Byzantine_Empire" title="Byzantine Empire">Byzantine</a> territories is the <i><a href="/wiki/Exarch" title="Exarch">exarch</a></i> (governor) in <a href="/wiki/Ravenna" title="Ravenna">Ravenna</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Slavs" title="Slavs">Slavs</a> push south on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Balkan" title="Balkan">Balkan Peninsula</a> — partly in conjunction with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Avars_(Carpathians)" title="Avars (Carpathians)">Avars</a> under their ruler (<i><a href="/wiki/Khagan" title="Khagan">khagan</a></i>) <a href="/wiki/Bayan_I" title="Bayan I">Bayan I</a> — ravaging the cities <a href="/wiki/Athens" title="Athens">Athens</a> and <a href="/wiki/Corinth" title="Corinth">Corinth</a>, and threatening the <a href="/wiki/Walls_of_Constantinople" title="Walls of Constantinople">Long Walls</a> of <a href="/wiki/Constantinople" title="Constantinople">Constantinople</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li>King <a href="/wiki/Eboric" title="Eboric">Eboric</a> is deposed by his mother (second husband <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Andeca" title="Andeca">Andeca</a>) who becomes the new ruler of the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Galicia" title="Kingdom of Galicia">Kingdom of Galicia</a> (Northern <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>) and the <a href="/wiki/Suebi" title="Suebi">Suevi</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gundoald" title="Gundoald">Gundoald</a>, illegitimate son of <a href="/wiki/Chlothar_I" title="Chlothar I">Chlothar I</a>, tries to expend his territory from <a href="/wiki/Brive-la-Gaillarde" title="Brive-la-Gaillarde">Brive-la-Gaillarde</a> (Burgundy) and proclaims himself king (approximate date).</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Britain">Britain</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=584&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Britain">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ceawlin_of_Wessex#584:_Fethan_leag" title="Ceawlin of Wessex">Battle of Fethanleigh</a>: King <a href="/wiki/Ceawlin_of_Wessex" title="Ceawlin of Wessex">Ceawlin of Wessex</a> is defeated by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Britons_(Celtic_people)" title="Britons (Celtic people)">Britons</a>. He <a href="/wiki/Looting" title="Looting">ravages</a> the surrounding countryside in revenge (approximate date).</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=584&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>Emperor <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Wen_of_Sui" title="Emperor Wen of Sui">Wéndi</a> of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sui_Dynasty" title="Sui Dynasty">Sui Dynasty</a> organises the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Canal_(China)" title="Grand Canal (China)">Grand Canal</a>. He builds ships for transportation and <a href="/wiki/Cereal" title="Cereal">grain</a> stores are located at strategic points.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
