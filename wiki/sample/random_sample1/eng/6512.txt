<p><b>Bian Zhang</b> (died 186), originally named <b>Bian Yun</b>, was an official who lived in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Eastern_Han_dynasty" title="Eastern Han dynasty">Eastern Han dynasty</a> and served as the Magistrate of <a href="/wiki/Xin%27an_County" title="Xin'an County">Xin'an County</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 185, Beigong Boyu of the Qiang tribe <a href="/wiki/Liang_Province_Rebellion" title="Liang Province Rebellion">rebelled</a> in <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Liang_Province&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Liang Province (page does not exist)">Liang Province</a>. Beigong Boyu took Bian Zhang and <a href="/wiki/Han_Sui" title="Han Sui">Han Sui</a>, another Han official, hostage and stopped all attempts to return them. Bian Zhang was forced to participate in the rebellion or else he would be killed. He became the overall commander of the rebels, but was defeated in battle with <a href="/wiki/Dong_Zhuo" title="Dong Zhuo">Dong Zhuo</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sun_Jian" title="Sun Jian">Sun Jian</a> and died shortly after.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
