<p><b>Byun Jae-sub</b> (born on September 17, 1975) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> footballer</p>
<p>He graduated in <a href="/wiki/Jeonju_University" title="Jeonju University">Jeonju University</a>, He was the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/K-League_Top_Assistor_Award" title="K-League Top Assistor Award">K-League Top Assistor</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1999_K-League" title="1999 K-League">1999 season</a></p>
