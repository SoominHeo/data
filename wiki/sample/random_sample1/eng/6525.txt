<p><b>Byeongjeom Depot Line</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Rapid_transit_in_South_Korea" title="Rapid transit in South Korea">subway line</a> operated by <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Metropolitan_Subway" title="Seoul Metropolitan Subway">Seoul Metropolitan Subway</a>, in Seoul, South Korea. It is part of <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Metro" title="Seoul Metro">Seoul Metro</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Subway_Line_1" title="Seoul Subway Line 1">Line 1</a>.</p>
