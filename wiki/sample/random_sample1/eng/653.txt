<p>Year <b>589</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">DLXXXIX</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Saturday" title="Common year starting on Saturday">common year starting on Saturday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>. The denomination 589 for this year has been used since the early medieval period, when the <a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a> <a href="/wiki/Calendar_era" title="Calendar era">calendar era</a> became the prevalent method in Europe for naming years.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=589&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Byzantine_Empire">Byzantine Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=589&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Byzantine Empire">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Byzantine%E2%80%93Sassanid_War_of_572%E2%80%93591" title="Byzantine–Sassanid War of 572–591">Byzantine–Sassanid War</a>: A <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sassanid_Empire" title="Sassanid Empire">Persian</a> army under <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bahr%C4%81m_Chobin" title="Bahrām Chobin">Bahrām Chobin</a> captures the fortress city of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Silvan,_Turkey" title="Silvan, Turkey">Martyropolis</a> (modern <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>).</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=589&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_15" title="May 15">May 15</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Authari" title="Authari">Authari</a> marries <a href="/wiki/Theodelinda" title="Theodelinda">Theodelinda</a>, daughter of the <a href="/wiki/Bavaria" title="Bavaria">Bavarian</a> duke <a href="/wiki/Garibald_I_of_Bavaria" title="Garibald I of Bavaria">Garibald I</a>. A <a href="/wiki/Catholic_Church" title="Catholic Church">Catholic</a>, she has great influence at court and among the <a href="/wiki/Lombards" title="Lombards">Lombard</a> nobility.</li>
<li>King <a href="/wiki/Childebert_II" title="Childebert II">Childebert II</a> attempts to impose <a href="/wiki/Tax" title="Tax">taxes</a> on the citizens of <a href="/wiki/Tours" title="Tours">Tours</a>; Bishop <a href="/wiki/Gregory_of_Tours" title="Gregory of Tours">Gregory</a> successfully opposes this by claiming <a href="/wiki/State_immunity" title="State immunity">state immunity</a> instituted by <a href="/wiki/Fredegund" title="Fredegund">Fredegund</a>.</li>
<li>King <a href="/wiki/Guntram" title="Guntram">Guntram</a> sends an expedition into <a href="/wiki/Septimania" title="Septimania">Septimania</a> (Southern <a href="/wiki/Gaul" title="Gaul">Gaul</a>), in support of a rebellion by the Arian bishop <a href="/wiki/Athaloc" title="Athaloc">Athaloc</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Claudius,_Duke_of_Lusitania" title="Claudius, Duke of Lusitania">Claudius</a>, duke (<i><a href="/wiki/Dux" title="Dux">dux</a></i>) of <a href="/wiki/Lusitania" title="Lusitania">Lusitania</a>, defeats the <a href="/wiki/Franks" title="Franks">Franks</a> and <a href="/wiki/Burgundians" title="Burgundians">Burgundians</a> at <a href="/wiki/Carcassonne" title="Carcassonne">Carcassonne</a> (<a href="/wiki/Languedoc" title="Languedoc">Languedoc</a>) on the <a href="/wiki/Aude_(river)" title="Aude (river)">Aude River</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/October_17" title="October 17">October 17</a> – The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Adige_River" title="Adige River">Adige River</a> overflows its banks, flooding the church of <a href="/wiki/Zeno_of_Verona" title="Zeno of Verona">St. Zeno</a> and damaging the walls of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Verona,_Italy" title="Verona, Italy">Verona</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Bubonic_plague" title="Bubonic plague">plague</a> hits <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a>, and its victims include <a href="/wiki/Pope_Pelagius_II" title="Pope Pelagius II">Pope Pelagius II</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Persia">Persia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=589&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Persia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/First_Perso-Turkic_War" title="First Perso-Turkic War">First Perso-Turkic War</a>: The Sassanid Persians capture the cities <a href="/wiki/Balkh" title="Balkh">Balkh</a> and <a href="/wiki/Herat" title="Herat">Herat</a> (<a href="/wiki/Afghanistan" title="Afghanistan">Afghanistan</a>). They cross the <a href="/wiki/Amu_Darya" title="Amu Darya">Oxus River</a> and repulse a <a href="/wiki/G%C3%B6kt%C3%BCrks" title="Göktürks">Turkic</a> invasion.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=589&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Early_Imperial_China" title="Early Imperial China">Chinese Empire</a> is reunited under the leadership of Emperor <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Wen_of_Sui" title="Emperor Wen of Sui">Wéndi</a> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sui_Dynasty" title="Sui Dynasty">Sui Dynasty</a>), who defeats the Chen forces at <a href="/wiki/Jiankang" title="Jiankang">Jiankang</a> (modern <a href="/wiki/Nanjing" title="Nanjing">Nanjing</a>), ending the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chen_Dynasty" title="Chen Dynasty">Chen Dynasty</a> (the last of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Southern_and_Northern_Dynasties" title="Southern and Northern Dynasties">Southern Dynasties</a>) that has ruled since <a href="/wiki/557" title="557">557</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Yan_Zhitui" title="Yan Zhitui">Yan Zhitui</a>, scholar-official, makes the first reference to the use of <a href="/wiki/Toilet_paper" title="Toilet paper">toilet paper</a> in human history. It is used in the Chinese <a href="/wiki/Court_(royal)" title="Court (royal)">imperial court</a> and amongst the other wealthy citizens.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Tulan_Qaghan" title="Tulan Qaghan">Tulan Qaghan</a>, son of <a href="/wiki/Ishbara_Qaghan" title="Ishbara Qaghan">Ishbara Qaghan</a>, becomes the seventh ruler (<i><a href="/wiki/Khagan" title="Khagan">khagan</a></i>) of the <a href="/wiki/G%C3%B6kt%C3%BCrks" title="Göktürks">Turkic Khaganate</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_topic">By topic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=589&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: By topic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Religion">Religion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=589&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Religion">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Pope_Gregory_I" title="Pope Gregory I">Gregory</a>, <a href="/wiki/Archdeacon" title="Archdeacon">archdeacon</a> of Rome, converts <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> slaves on the Roman <a href="/wiki/Market_(place)" title="Market (place)">market</a>. He calls them <a href="/wiki/Angel" title="Angel">Angels</a> if they would be <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Christians" title="Christians">Christians</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Third_Council_of_Toledo" title="Third Council of Toledo">Third Council of Toledo</a>, called by King <a href="/wiki/Reccared_I" title="Reccared I">Reccared I</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Visigoths" title="Visigoths">Visigoths</a>, renounces <a href="/wiki/Arianism" title="Arianism">Arianism</a> and embraces <a href="/wiki/Catholicism" title="Catholicism">Catholicism</a>.</li>
<li>The Council of <a href="/wiki/Narbonne" title="Narbonne">Narbonne</a> is held. In Septimania, <a href="/wiki/Jews" title="Jews">Jews</a> are forbidden from chanting <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Psalm" title="Psalm">psalms</a> while burying their dead.</li>
</ul>
