<p><b>Borgo San Lorenzo</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Florence" title="Province of Florence">Province of Florence</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Tuscany" title="Tuscany">Tuscany</a>, located about 20 kilometres (12 mi) northeast of <a href="/wiki/Florence" title="Florence">Florence</a>. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 18,085 and an area of 146.1 square kilometres (56.4 sq mi).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-istat_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-istat-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<p>Borgo San Lorenzo borders the following municipalities: <a href="/wiki/Fiesole" title="Fiesole">Fiesole</a>, <a href="/wiki/Firenzuola" title="Firenzuola">Firenzuola</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marradi" title="Marradi">Marradi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Palazzuolo_sul_Senio" title="Palazzuolo sul Senio">Palazzuolo sul Senio</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pontassieve" title="Pontassieve">Pontassieve</a>, <a href="/wiki/San_Piero_a_Sieve" title="San Piero a Sieve">San Piero a Sieve</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Scarperia" title="Scarperia">Scarperia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vaglia" title="Vaglia">Vaglia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vicchio" title="Vicchio">Vicchio</a>.</p>
