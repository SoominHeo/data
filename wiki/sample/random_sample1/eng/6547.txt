<p><b>Penaeoidea</b> is the larger of the two <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Superfamily_(zoology)" title="Superfamily (zoology)">superfamilies</a> of <a href="/wiki/Dendrobranchiata" title="Dendrobranchiata">prawns</a>. It comprises eight families, three of which are known only from <a href="/wiki/Fossil" title="Fossil">fossils</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Grave_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Grave-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Aciculopoda_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Aciculopoda-2">[2]</a></sup> The fossil record of the group stretches back to <i><a href="/wiki/Aciculopoda" title="Aciculopoda">Aciculopoda</a></i>, discovered in <a href="/wiki/Famennian" title="Famennian">Famennian</a> sediments in <a href="/wiki/Oklahoma" title="Oklahoma">Oklahoma</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Aciculopoda_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-Aciculopoda-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li>† <a href="/wiki/Aciculopoda" title="Aciculopoda">Aciculopodidae</a> (1 genus, 1 species)</li>
<li>† <a href="/wiki/Aegeridae" title="Aegeridae">Aegeridae</a> (2 genera, 25 species)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Aristeidae&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Aristeidae (page does not exist)">Aristeidae</a> (10 genera, 28 species)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Benthesicymidae&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Benthesicymidae (page does not exist)">Benthesicymidae</a> (5 genera, 43 species)</li>
<li>† <a href="/wiki/Carpopenaeus" title="Carpopenaeus">Carpopenaeidae</a> (1 genus, 3 species)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Penaeidae" title="Penaeidae">Penaeidae</a> (48 genera, 286 species)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sicyoniidae" title="Sicyoniidae">Sicyoniidae</a> (1 genus, 53 species)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Solenoceridae&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Solenoceridae (page does not exist)">Solenoceridae</a> (9 genera, 86 species)</li>
</ul>
