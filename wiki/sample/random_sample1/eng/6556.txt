<p><b>Robert Moorhouse "Bobby" Coleman III</b> (born May 5, 1997) is an <a href="/wiki/Americans" title="Americans">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Actor" title="Actor">actor</a>. He is best known for his roles in the films <i><a href="/wiki/Martian_Child" title="Martian Child">Martian Child</a></i>, as the title character, and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Last_Song_(film)" title="The Last Song (film)">The Last Song</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bobby_Coleman&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Robert Coleman was born in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Los_Angeles,_California" title="Los Angeles, California">Los Angeles, California</a>, the son of Doris Berg and Robert Moorhouse Coleman, Jr. He is the younger brother of actress <a href="/wiki/Holliston_Coleman" title="Holliston Coleman">Holliston Coleman</a>, and lives with her family in the Los Angeles area.</p>
<p>Coleman began acting at the age of five in commercials, and has since appeared in several film and television productions. Coleman had brief appearances in a number of series such as <i><a href="/wiki/Medium_(TV_series)" title="Medium (TV series)">Medium</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/JAG_(TV_series)" title="JAG (TV series)">JAG</a></i>, before moving into film roles. He appeared in the feature films <i><a href="/wiki/Must_Love_Dogs" title="Must Love Dogs">Must Love Dogs</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Friends_with_Money" title="Friends with Money">Friends with Money</a></i>, and also had a recurring role in the television series <i><a href="/wiki/Surface_(TV_series)" title="Surface (TV series)">Surface</a></i>, before taking leading roles in the films <i><a href="/wiki/Glass_House:_The_Good_Mother" title="Glass House: The Good Mother">Glass House: The Good Mother</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Take_(film)" title="Take (film)">Take</a></i>. He played the title lead role in the film <i><a href="/wiki/Martian_Child" title="Martian Child">Martian Child</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> his second role alongside <a href="/wiki/John_Cusack" title="John Cusack">John Cusack</a> and is set to appear with his sister in <i>Proving Ground: From the Adventures of Captain Redlocks</i>, in which he will play the younger brother of his real-life sister. They are both set to star together again in the science-fiction adventure film, <i><a href="/wiki/Robosapien:_Rebooted" title="Robosapien: Rebooted">Robosapien: Rebooted</a></i>. He appeared in the 2010 film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Last_Song_(film)" title="The Last Song (film)">The Last Song</a></i> as Jonah Miller, the younger brother of <a href="/wiki/Miley_Cyrus" title="Miley Cyrus">Miley Cyrus</a>'s character.</p>
