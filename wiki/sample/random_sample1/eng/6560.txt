<p><b>Bosung Girls' High School</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko" xml:lang="ko">보성여자고등학교</span>) is a private girls high school located in <a href="/wiki/Yongsan-dong,_Seoul" title="Yongsan-dong, Seoul">Yongsan-dong</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yongsan_District" title="Yongsan District">Yongsan-gu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bosung_Girls%27_High_School&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The school was founded on October 10, 1907 under the name Bosung Girls' School (보성여학교). The current principal Nam Myung-hwal was appointed on February 7, 2013 as the school's 15th principal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-url_rel.ED.95.99.EA.B5.90_.EC.97.B0.ED.98.81_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-url_rel.ED.95.99.EA.B5.90_.EC.97.B0.ED.98.81-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
