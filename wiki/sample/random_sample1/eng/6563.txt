<p>The <b>Bosnian Wikipedia</b> (<a href="/wiki/Bosnian_language" title="Bosnian language">Bosnian</a>: <i>Wikipedia na bosanskom jeziku</i>) is the <a href="/wiki/Bosnian_language" title="Bosnian language">Bosnian language</a> version of <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia" title="Wikipedia">Wikipedia</a>, hosted by the <a href="/wiki/Wikimedia_Foundation" title="Wikimedia Foundation">Wikimedia Foundation</a>. As of September 30, 2016, it has over 70,000 articles. It was created on December 12, 2002 and its first article was <i><a class="extiw" href="https://bs.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matematika" title="bs:Matematika">Matematika</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Community">Community</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bosnian_Wikipedia&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Community">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On February 17, 2007, members of the Bosnian wiki community held the first in-person meet-up event in <a href="/wiki/Sarajevo" title="Sarajevo">Sarajevo</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Another meeting was held in the same year, followed by meetings in 2008 and 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
