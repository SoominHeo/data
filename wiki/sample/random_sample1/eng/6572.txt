<p><b>Boa Vista</b> is a <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_Cabo_Verde" title="Administrative divisions of Cabo Verde">concelho</a></i> (municipality) of <a href="/wiki/Cape_Verde" title="Cape Verde">Cape Verde</a>. It covers the whole island of <a href="/wiki/Boa_Vista,_Cape_Verde" title="Boa Vista, Cape Verde">Boa Vista</a>. Its capital is the town <a href="/wiki/Sal_Rei" title="Sal Rei">Sal Rei</a>. The municipality consists of two <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_Cabo_Verde" title="Administrative divisions of Cabo Verde">freguesias</a></i> (civil parishes):</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Santa_Isabel_(Boa_Vista)" title="Santa Isabel (Boa Vista)">Santa Isabel</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/S%C3%A3o_Jo%C3%A3o_Baptista_(Boa_Vista)" title="São João Baptista (Boa Vista)">São João Baptista</a></li>
</ul>
