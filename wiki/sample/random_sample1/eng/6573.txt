<p><b>Bo'ao</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">博鳌</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">博鰲</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Bó'áo</span></i>) is a town located next to the eastern coastal city of <a href="/wiki/Qionghai" title="Qionghai">Qionghai</a> in <a href="/wiki/Hainan" title="Hainan">Hainan</a> Province, People's Republic of China, near the mouth of the <a href="/wiki/Wanquan_River" title="Wanquan River">Wanquan River</a> where it discharges into the <a href="/wiki/South_China_Sea" title="South China Sea">South China Sea</a>. The town is 17 km (11 mi) away from Qionghai, 105 km (65 mi) away from <a href="/wiki/Haikou" title="Haikou">Haikou</a> and 180 km (110 mi) away from <a href="/wiki/Sanya" title="Sanya">Sanya</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Bo'ao is famous for the <a href="/wiki/Boao_Forum_for_Asia" title="Boao Forum for Asia">Boao Forum for Asia</a>, an international organisation whose venue is permanently located on Bo'ao's largest island, Dongyu Island (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">东屿岛</span>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
