<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Bojan Bazelli</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Bojan Bazelli</b> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Montenegrins_(ethnic_group)" title="Montenegrins (ethnic group)">Montenegrin</a> <a href="/wiki/Cinematographer" title="Cinematographer">cinematographer</a> and <a href="/wiki/Music_video_director" title="Music video director">music video director</a> born in <a href="/wiki/Herceg_Novi" title="Herceg Novi">Herceg Novi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Montenegro" title="Montenegro">Montenegro</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Kodak_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Kodak-1">[1]</a></sup> His credits include the films <i><a href="/wiki/The_Ring_(2002_film)" title="The Ring (2002 film)">The Ring</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Mr._%26_Mrs._Smith_(2005_film)" title="Mr. &amp; Mrs. Smith (2005 film)">Mr. &amp; Mrs. Smith</a></i>, and director <a href="/wiki/Adam_Shankman" title="Adam Shankman">Adam Shankman</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Hairspray_(2007_film)" title="Hairspray (2007 film)">Hairspray</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> as well as <a href="/wiki/Mariah_Carey" title="Mariah Carey">Mariah Carey</a>'s music video "<a href="/wiki/Vision_of_Love" title="Vision of Love">Vision of Love</a>". Other feature credits include <i><a href="/wiki/The_Rapture_(1991_film)" title="The Rapture (1991 film)">The Rapture</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Deep_Cover" title="Deep Cover">Deep Cover</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/King_of_New_York" title="King of New York">King of New York</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Kalifornia" title="Kalifornia">Kalifornia</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Dangerous_Beauty" title="Dangerous Beauty">Dangerous Beauty</a></i>.</p>
<p>Bazelli studied at <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Famu_Film_School&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Famu Film School (page does not exist)">Famu Film School</a> in <a href="/wiki/Prague" title="Prague">Prague</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Kodak_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Kodak-1">[1]</a></sup> Impressed with one of Bazelli’s student films, director <a href="/wiki/Abel_Ferrara" title="Abel Ferrara">Abel Ferrara</a> hired Bazelli to shoot <i><a href="/wiki/China_Girl_(1987_film)" title="China Girl (1987 film)">China Girl</a></i> in New York City. He subsequently filmed <i><a href="/wiki/King_of_New_York" title="King of New York">King of New York</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Body_Snatchers_(1993_film)" title="Body Snatchers (1993 film)">Body Snatchers</a></i> with Ferrara as well.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Kodak_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-Kodak-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>Bazelli received an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Independent_Spirit_Award" title="Independent Spirit Award">Independent Spirit Awards</a> in 1990 for his work on <i>King of New York</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Kodak_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-Kodak-1">[1]</a></sup> He was honored for Best Cinematography in both 1996 and 1998 at the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=American_Independent_Commercial_Producers&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="American Independent Commercial Producers (page does not exist)">American Independent Commercial Producers</a> (AICP), one of the few cinematographers to have received this honor twice. Bazelli also received a Gold <a href="/wiki/Clio_Awards" title="Clio Awards">Clio</a> for Best Cinematography in 1998, and the film <i>Kalifornia</i> was awarded Best Cinematography at the Montreal Film Festival.</p>
