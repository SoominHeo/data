<p><b>May 23</b> is the 143rd day of the year (144th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 222 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Monday, Wednesday or Saturday (58 in 400 years each) than on Thursday or Friday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Tuesday or Sunday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=May_23&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/844" title="844">844</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Clavijo" title="Battle of Clavijo">Battle of Clavijo</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Apostle" title="Apostle">Apostle</a> <a href="/wiki/James,_son_of_Zebedee" title="James, son of Zebedee">Saint James the Greater</a> is said to have miraculously appeared to a force of outnumbered <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Asturias" title="Kingdom of Asturias">Asturians</a> and aided them against the forces of the <a href="/wiki/Emirate_of_C%C3%B3rdoba" title="Emirate of Córdoba">Emir of Cordoba</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1430" title="1430">1430</a> – <a href="/wiki/Joan_of_Arc" title="Joan of Arc">Joan of Arc</a> is captured by the <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Burgundy" title="Duchy of Burgundy">Burgundians</a> while leading an army to raise the <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Compi%C3%A8gne" title="Siege of Compiègne">Siege of Compiègne</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1498" title="1498">1498</a> – <a href="/wiki/Girolamo_Savonarola" title="Girolamo Savonarola">Girolamo Savonarola</a> is burned at the stake in <a href="/wiki/Florence" title="Florence">Florence</a>, Italy.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1533" title="1533">1533</a> – The marriage of <a href="/wiki/List_of_English_monarchs#House_of_Tudor" title="List of English monarchs">King</a> <a href="/wiki/Henry_VIII_of_England" title="Henry VIII of England">Henry VIII</a> to <a href="/wiki/Catherine_of_Aragon" title="Catherine of Aragon">Catherine of Aragon</a> is declared null and void.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1568" title="1568">1568</a> – Dutch rebels led by <a href="/wiki/Louis_of_Nassau" title="Louis of Nassau">Louis of Nassau</a>, defeat <a href="/wiki/Jean_de_Ligne,_Duke_of_Arenberg" title="Jean de Ligne, Duke of Arenberg">Jean de Ligne, Duke of Arenberg</a>, and his loyalist troops in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Heiligerlee_(1568)" title="Battle of Heiligerlee (1568)">Battle of Heiligerlee</a>, opening the <a href="/wiki/Eighty_Years%27_War" title="Eighty Years' War">Eighty Years' War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1609" title="1609">1609</a> – Official ratification of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Virginia_Charter" title="Second Virginia Charter">Second Virginia Charter</a> takes place.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1618" title="1618">1618</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Defenestrations_of_Prague" title="Defenestrations of Prague">Second Defenestration of Prague</a> precipitates the <a href="/wiki/Thirty_Years%27_War" title="Thirty Years' War">Thirty Years' War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1701" title="1701">1701</a> – After being convicted of <a href="/wiki/Piracy" title="Piracy">piracy</a> and of murdering William Moore, Captain <a href="/wiki/William_Kidd" title="William Kidd">William Kidd</a> is hanged in London.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1706" title="1706">1706</a> – <a href="/wiki/John_Churchill,_1st_Duke_of_Marlborough" title="John Churchill, 1st Duke of Marlborough">John Churchill, 1st Duke of Marlborough</a>, defeats a French army under <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Marshal_of_France" title="Marshal of France">Marshal</a> <a href="/wiki/Fran%C3%A7ois_de_Neufville,_duc_de_Villeroy" title="François de Neufville, duc de Villeroy">François de Neufville, duc de Villeroy</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Ramillies" title="Battle of Ramillies">Battle of Ramillies</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1788" title="1788">1788</a> – <a href="/wiki/South_Carolina" title="South Carolina">South Carolina</a> ratifies the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Constitution" title="United States Constitution">United States Constitution</a> as the eighth American state.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1793" title="1793">1793</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Famars" title="Battle of Famars">Battle of Famars</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Flanders_Campaign" title="Flanders Campaign">Flanders Campaign</a> of the <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_First_Coalition" title="War of the First Coalition">War of the First Coalition</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1829" title="1829">1829</a> – <a href="/wiki/Accordion" title="Accordion">Accordion</a> patent granted to <a href="/wiki/Cyrill_Demian" title="Cyrill Demian">Cyrill Demian</a> in <a href="/wiki/Vienna" title="Vienna">Vienna</a>, <a href="/wiki/Austrian_Empire" title="Austrian Empire">Austrian Empire</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1844" title="1844">1844</a> – Declaration of the <a href="/wiki/B%C3%A1b" title="Báb">Báb</a> the evening before the 23rd: A merchant of Shiraz announces that he is a Prophet and founds a religious movement that would later be brutally crushed by the Persian government. He is considered to be a forerunner of the <a href="/wiki/Bah%C3%A1%27%C3%AD_Faith" title="Bahá'í Faith">Bahá'í Faith</a>, and Bahá'ís celebrate the day as a holy day.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1846" title="1846">1846</a> – <a href="/wiki/Mexican%E2%80%93American_War" title="Mexican–American War">Mexican–American War</a>: President <a href="/wiki/Mariano_Paredes_(President_of_Mexico)" title="Mariano Paredes (President of Mexico)">Mariano Paredes</a> of Mexico unofficially declares war on the United States.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1873" title="1873">1873</a> – The Canadian Parliament establishes the <a href="/wiki/North-West_Mounted_Police" title="North-West Mounted Police">North-West Mounted Police</a>, the forerunner of the <a href="/wiki/Royal_Canadian_Mounted_Police" title="Royal Canadian Mounted Police">Royal Canadian Mounted Police</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1900" title="1900">1900</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Civil_War" title="American Civil War">American Civil War</a>: Sergeant <a href="/wiki/William_Harvey_Carney" title="William Harvey Carney">William Harvey Carney</a> is awarded the <a href="/wiki/Medal_of_Honor" title="Medal of Honor">Medal of Honor</a> for his heroism in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Assault_on_the_Battery_Wagner" title="Assault on the Battery Wagner">Assault on the Battery Wagner</a> in 1863.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1907" title="1907">1907</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Unicameralism" title="Unicameralism">unicameral</a> <a href="/wiki/Parliament_of_Finland" title="Parliament of Finland">Parliament of Finland</a> gathers for its first plenary session.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1911" title="1911">1911</a> – The <a href="/wiki/New_York_Public_Library" title="New York Public Library">New York Public Library</a> is dedicated.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1915" title="1915">1915</a> – <a href="/wiki/World_War_I" title="World War I">World War I</a>: Italy joins the <a href="/wiki/Allies_of_World_War_I" title="Allies of World War I">Allies</a>, fulfilling its part of the <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_London_(1915)" title="Treaty of London (1915)">Treaty of London</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1932" title="1932">1932</a> – In <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a>, four students are shot and killed during a manifestation against the Brazilian dictator <a href="/wiki/Get%C3%BAlio_Vargas" title="Getúlio Vargas">Getúlio Vargas</a>, which resulted in the outbreak of the <a href="/wiki/Constitutionalist_Revolution" title="Constitutionalist Revolution">Constitutionalist Revolution</a> several weeks later.</li>

<li>1934 – The <a href="/wiki/Auto-Lite_strike" title="Auto-Lite strike">Auto-Lite strike</a> culminates in the "Battle of Toledo", a five-day melée between 1,300 troops of the <a href="/wiki/Ohio_National_Guard" title="Ohio National Guard">Ohio National Guard</a> and 6,000 picketers.</li>
