<p><b>Boeun Sangmu Women's Football Club</b>(<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: 보은 상무), founded in March 2007, is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Women%27s_association_football" title="Women's association football">women's football</a> club based in <a href="/wiki/Boeun_County" title="Boeun County">Boeun County</a>, <a href="/wiki/North_Chungcheong_Province" title="North Chungcheong Province">North Chungcheong Province</a>. <i>Sangmu (상무, 尙武)</i> is the sports division of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Military_of_South_Korea" title="Military of South Korea">Military of South Korea</a>.</p>
