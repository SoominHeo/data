<p><b>May 27</b> is the 147th day of the year (148th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 218 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Wednesday, Friday or Sunday (58 in 400 years each) than on Monday or Tuesday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Thursday or Saturday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=May_27&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/927" title="927">927</a> – Death of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Simeon_I_the_Great" title="Simeon I the Great">Simeon I the Great</a>, the first Bulgarian to be recognized as Emperor.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1120" title="1120">1120</a> – <a href="/wiki/Richard_III_of_Capua" title="Richard III of Capua">Richard III of Capua</a> is anointed as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Prince_of_Capua" title="Prince of Capua">Prince</a> two weeks before his untimely death.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1153" title="1153">1153</a> – <a href="/wiki/Malcolm_IV_of_Scotland" title="Malcolm IV of Scotland">Malcolm IV</a> becomes King of Scotland.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1199" title="1199">1199</a> – <a href="/wiki/John,_King_of_England" title="John, King of England">John</a> is crowned King of England.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1644" title="1644">1644</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Manchu" title="Manchu">Manchu</a> regent <a href="/wiki/Dorgon" title="Dorgon">Dorgon</a> defeats rebel leader <a href="/wiki/Li_Zicheng" title="Li Zicheng">Li Zicheng</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Shun_dynasty" title="Shun dynasty">Shun dynasty</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Shanhai_Pass" title="Battle of Shanhai Pass">Battle of Shanhai Pass</a>, allowing the Manchus to enter and conquer the capital city of Beijing.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1703" title="1703">1703</a> – <a href="/wiki/Tsar" title="Tsar">Tsar</a> <a href="/wiki/Peter_the_Great" title="Peter the Great">Peter the Great</a> founds the city of <a href="/wiki/Saint_Petersburg" title="Saint Petersburg">Saint Petersburg</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1798" title="1798">1798</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Oulart_Hill" title="Battle of Oulart Hill">Battle of Oulart Hill</a> takes place in <a href="/wiki/Wexford" title="Wexford">Wexford</a>, Ireland.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1799" title="1799">1799</a> – <a href="/wiki/War_of_the_Second_Coalition" title="War of the Second Coalition">War of the Second Coalition</a>: <a href="/wiki/Habsburg_Monarchy" title="Habsburg Monarchy">Austrian</a> forces <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Winterthur_(1799)" title="Battle of Winterthur (1799)">defeats</a> the <a href="/wiki/French_First_Republic" title="French First Republic">French</a> at <a href="/wiki/Winterthur" title="Winterthur">Winterthur</a>, Switzerland, securing control of the northeastern Swiss Plateau because of the town's location at the junction of seven cross-roads.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1813" title="1813">1813</a> – <a href="/wiki/War_of_1812" title="War of 1812">War of 1812</a>: In Canada, American forces capture <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Fort_George" title="Battle of Fort George">Fort George</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1849" title="1849">1849</a> – The Great Hall of <a href="/wiki/Euston_railway_station" title="Euston railway station">Euston station</a> in London is opened.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1860" title="1860">1860</a> – <a href="/wiki/Giuseppe_Garibaldi" title="Giuseppe Garibaldi">Giuseppe Garibaldi</a> begins his attack on <a href="/wiki/Palermo" title="Palermo">Palermo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sicily" title="Sicily">Sicily</a>, as part of the <a href="/wiki/Italian_unification" title="Italian unification">Italian unification</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1863" title="1863">1863</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Civil_War" title="American Civil War">American Civil War</a>: First Assault on the <a href="/wiki/Confederate_States_of_America" title="Confederate States of America">Confederate</a> works at the <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Port_Hudson" title="Siege of Port Hudson">Siege of Port Hudson</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1874" title="1874">1874</a> – The first group of <a href="/wiki/Dorsland_Trek" title="Dorsland Trek">Dorsland trekkers</a> under the leadership of <a href="/wiki/Gert_Alberts" title="Gert Alberts">Gert Alberts</a> leaves <a href="/wiki/Pretoria" title="Pretoria">Pretoria</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1883" title="1883">1883</a> – <a href="/wiki/Alexander_III_of_Russia" title="Alexander III of Russia">Alexander III</a> is crowned Tsar of Russia.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1896" title="1896">1896</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Fujita_scale" title="Fujita scale">F4</a>-strength <a href="/wiki/1896_St._Louis%E2%80%93East_St._Louis_tornado" title="1896 St. Louis–East St. Louis tornado">1896 St. Louis–East St. Louis tornado</a> hits in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/St._Louis,_Missouri" title="St. Louis, Missouri">St. Louis, Missouri</a>, and <a href="/wiki/East_St._Louis,_Illinois" title="East St. Louis, Illinois">East St. Louis, Illinois</a>, killing at least 255 people and causing $2.9 billion in damage (<a href="/wiki/1997" title="1997">1997</a> US dollars).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1905" title="1905">1905</a> – <a href="/wiki/Russo-Japanese_War" title="Russo-Japanese War">Russo-Japanese War</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Tsushima" title="Battle of Tsushima">Battle of Tsushima</a> begins.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1907" title="1907">1907</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/San_Francisco_plague_of_1900%E2%80%9304" title="San Francisco plague of 1900–04">Bubonic plague breaks out in San Francisco</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1908" title="1908">1908</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Khilafat_Day" title="Khilafat Day">Khilafat Day</a> – the day of establishment of Khilafat in Islam Ahmadiyya.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1917" title="1917">1917</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Benedict_XV" title="Pope Benedict XV">Pope Benedict XV</a> <a href="/wiki/Promulgation_(canon_law)" title="Promulgation (canon law)">promulgates</a> the <a href="/wiki/1917_Code_of_Canon_Law" title="1917 Code of Canon Law">1917 Code of Canon Law</a>, the first comprehensive codification of <a href="/wiki/Canon_law_(Catholic_Church)" title="Canon law (Catholic Church)">Catholic canon law</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Legal_history_of_the_Catholic_Church" title="Legal history of the Catholic Church">legal history of the Catholic Church</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1919" title="1919">1919</a> – The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/NC-4" title="NC-4">NC-4</a> aircraft arrives in <a href="/wiki/Lisbon" title="Lisbon">Lisbon</a> after completing the first <a href="/wiki/Transatlantic_flight" title="Transatlantic flight">transatlantic flight</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1927" title="1927">1927</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Ford_Motor_Company" title="Ford Motor Company">Ford Motor Company</a> ceases manufacture of the <a href="/wiki/Ford_Model_T" title="Ford Model T">Ford Model T</a> and begins to retool plants to make the <a href="/wiki/Ford_Model_A_(1927%E2%80%9331)" title="Ford Model A (1927–31)">Ford Model A</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1930" title="1930">1930</a> – The 1,046 feet (319 m) <a href="/wiki/Chrysler_Building" title="Chrysler Building">Chrysler Building</a> in New York City, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_tallest_buildings_in_the_world" title="List of tallest buildings in the world">tallest man-made structure</a> at the time, opens to the public.</li>
