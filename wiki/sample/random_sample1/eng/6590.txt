<p>The <b>President of the Republic of Botswana</b> is the <a href="/wiki/Head_of_state" title="Head of state">head of state</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Head_of_government" title="Head of government">head of government</a> of <a href="/wiki/Botswana" title="Botswana">Botswana</a>, as well as the <a href="/wiki/Commander-in-chief" title="Commander-in-chief">commander-in-chief</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Botswana_Defence_Force" title="Botswana Defence Force">Botswana Defence Force</a>, according to the <a href="/wiki/Constitution_of_Botswana" title="Constitution of Botswana">Constitution of Botswana</a>.</p>
