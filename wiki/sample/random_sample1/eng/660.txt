<p><b>May 4</b> is the 124th day of the year (125th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 241 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Monday, Wednesday or Friday (58 in 400 years each) than on Saturday or Sunday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Tuesday or Thursday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=May_4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1256" title="1256">1256</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Augustinians" title="Augustinians">Augustinian</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Monastic_order" title="Monastic order">monastic order</a> is constituted at the <a href="/wiki/Monastery_of_the_Holy_Saviour" title="Monastery of the Holy Saviour">Lecceto Monastery</a> when <a href="/wiki/Pope_Alexander_IV" title="Pope Alexander IV">Pope Alexander IV</a> issues a <a href="/wiki/Papal_bull" title="Papal bull">papal bull</a> <i>Licet ecclesiae catholicae</i>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1415" title="1415">1415</a> – Religious reformers <a href="/wiki/John_Wycliffe" title="John Wycliffe">John Wycliffe</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jan_Hus" title="Jan Hus">Jan Hus</a> are condemned as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Christian_heresy" title="Christian heresy">heretics</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Council_of_Constance" title="Council of Constance">Council of Constance</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1436" title="1436">1436</a> – Assassination of the Swedish rebel (later national hero) <a href="/wiki/Engelbrekt_Engelbrektsson" title="Engelbrekt Engelbrektsson">Engelbrekt Engelbrektsson</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1471" title="1471">1471</a> – <a href="/wiki/Wars_of_the_Roses" title="Wars of the Roses">Wars of the Roses</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Tewkesbury" title="Battle of Tewkesbury">Battle of Tewkesbury</a>: <a href="/wiki/Edward_IV_of_England" title="Edward IV of England">Edward IV</a> defeats a <a href="/wiki/House_of_Lancaster" title="House of Lancaster">Lancastrian</a> Army and kills <a href="/wiki/Edward_of_Westminster,_Prince_of_Wales" title="Edward of Westminster, Prince of Wales">Edward of Westminster, Prince of Wales</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1493" title="1493">1493</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Alexander_VI" title="Pope Alexander VI">Pope Alexander VI</a> divides the <a href="/wiki/New_World" title="New World">New World</a> between Spain and Portugal along the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Line_of_Demarcation" title="Line of Demarcation">Line of Demarcation</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1626" title="1626">1626</a> – Dutch explorer <a href="/wiki/Peter_Minuit" title="Peter Minuit">Peter Minuit</a> arrives in <a href="/wiki/New_Netherland" title="New Netherland">New Netherland</a> (present day <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Manhattan_Island" title="Manhattan Island">Manhattan Island</a>) aboard the <i>See Meeuw</i>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1675" title="1675">1675</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Charles_II_of_England" title="Charles II of England">Charles II of England</a> orders the construction of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Royal_Greenwich_Observatory" title="Royal Greenwich Observatory">Royal Greenwich Observatory</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1686" title="1686">1686</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Municipalities_of_the_Philippines" title="Municipalities of the Philippines">Municipality</a> of <a href="/wiki/Ilagan" title="Ilagan">Ilagan</a> is founded in the <a href="/wiki/Philippines" title="Philippines">Philippines</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1776" title="1776">1776</a> – <a href="/wiki/Rhode_Island" title="Rhode Island">Rhode Island</a> becomes the first American colony to renounce allegiance to King <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/George_III" title="George III">George III</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1799" title="1799">1799</a> – <a href="/wiki/Fourth_Anglo-Mysore_War" title="Fourth Anglo-Mysore War">Fourth Anglo-Mysore War</a>: The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Seringapatam" title="Battle of Seringapatam">Battle of Seringapatam</a>: The siege of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seringapatam" title="Seringapatam">Seringapatam</a> ends when the city is invaded and <a href="/wiki/Tipu_Sultan" title="Tipu Sultan">Tipu Sultan</a> killed by the besieging British army, under the command of <a href="/wiki/George_Harris,_1st_Baron_Harris" title="George Harris, 1st Baron Harris">General George Harris</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1814" title="1814">1814</a> – Emperor <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Napoleon_I_of_France" title="Napoleon I of France">Napoleon I of France</a> arrives at <a href="/wiki/Portoferraio" title="Portoferraio">Portoferraio</a> on the island of <a href="/wiki/Elba" title="Elba">Elba</a> to begin his exile.</li>
<li>1814 – King <a href="/wiki/Ferdinand_VII_of_Spain" title="Ferdinand VII of Spain">Ferdinand VII of Spain</a> signs the Decree of the 4th of May, returning Spain to <a href="/wiki/Absolute_monarchy" title="Absolute monarchy">absolutism</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1836" title="1836">1836</a> – Formation of <a href="/wiki/Ancient_Order_of_Hibernians" title="Ancient Order of Hibernians">Ancient Order of Hibernians</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1859" title="1859">1859</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Cornwall_Railway" title="Cornwall Railway">Cornwall Railway</a> opens across the <a href="/wiki/Royal_Albert_Bridge" title="Royal Albert Bridge">Royal Albert Bridge</a> linking <a href="/wiki/Devon" title="Devon">Devon</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cornwall" title="Cornwall">Cornwall</a> in England.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1865" title="1865">1865</a> – <a href="/wiki/Conclusion_of_the_American_Civil_War#Surrender_of_the_Confederate_departments_of_Alabama.2C_Mississippi_and_East_Louisiana_regiments_.28May_4.29" title="Conclusion of the American Civil War">Surrender</a> of the Confederate departments of Alabama, Mississippi and East Louisiana at <a href="/wiki/Citronelle,_Alabama" title="Citronelle, Alabama">Citronelle, Alabama</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1869" title="1869">1869</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Naval_Battle_of_Hakodate" title="Naval Battle of Hakodate">Naval Battle of Hakodate</a> is fought in Japan.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1871" title="1871">1871</a> – The <a href="/wiki/National_Association_of_Professional_Base_Ball_Players" title="National Association of Professional Base Ball Players">National Association</a>, the first professional baseball league, opens its first season in <a href="/wiki/Fort_Wayne,_Indiana" title="Fort Wayne, Indiana">Fort Wayne, Indiana</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1886" title="1886">1886</a> – <a href="/wiki/Haymarket_affair" title="Haymarket affair">Haymarket affair</a>: A bomb is thrown at policemen trying to break up a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Labor_movement" title="Labor movement">labor</a> rally in <a href="/wiki/Chicago" title="Chicago">Chicago</a>, United States, killing eight and wounding 60. The police fire into the crowd.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1904" title="1904">1904</a> – The United States begins construction of the <a href="/wiki/Panama_Canal" title="Panama Canal">Panama Canal</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1910" title="1910">1910</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Royal_Canadian_Navy" title="Royal Canadian Navy">Royal Canadian Navy</a> is created.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1912" title="1912">1912</a> – Italy occupies the Greek island of <a href="/wiki/Rhodes" title="Rhodes">Rhodes</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1919" title="1919">1919</a> – <a href="/wiki/May_Fourth_Movement" title="May Fourth Movement">May Fourth Movement</a>: Student demonstrations take place in <a href="/wiki/Tiananmen_Square" title="Tiananmen Square">Tiananmen Square</a> in Beijing, China, protesting the <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Versailles" title="Treaty of Versailles">Treaty of Versailles</a>, which transferred <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_China_(1912%E2%80%9349)" title="Republic of China (1912–49)">Chinese</a> territory to Japan.</li>
