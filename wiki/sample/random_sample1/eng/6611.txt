<p><i><b>Born to Kill</b></i> is a 1996 South Korean action film by Jang Hyun-soo, starring <a href="/wiki/Jung_Woo-sung" title="Jung Woo-sung">Jung Woo-sung</a> and <a href="/wiki/Shim_Eun-ha" title="Shim Eun-ha">Shim Eun-ha</a>.</p>
