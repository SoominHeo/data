<p><b>Bolognetta</b> is a stop of <a href="/wiki/Line_C_(Rome_Metro)" title="Line C (Rome Metro)">Line C</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Rome_Metro" title="Rome Metro">Rome Metro</a>. It is located along the <i>via Casilina</i> in the <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Roman</a> <a href="/wiki/Frazione" title="Frazione">frazione</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Finocchio" title="Finocchio">Finocchio</a>.</p>
<p>The stop was renovated and it re-opened as part of the new Metro line on 9 November 2014.</p>
