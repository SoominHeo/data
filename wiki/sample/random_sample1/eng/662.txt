<p>Year <b>607</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">DCVII</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Sunday" title="Common year starting on Sunday">common year starting on Sunday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>. The denomination 607 for this year has been used since the early medieval period, when the <a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a> <a href="/wiki/Calendar_era" title="Calendar era">calendar era</a> became the prevalent method in Europe for naming years.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=607&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=607&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=607&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Visigoths" title="Visigoths">Visigoths</a>, <a href="/wiki/Austrasia" title="Austrasia">Austrasians</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neustria" title="Neustria">Neustrians</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lombards" title="Lombards">Lombards</a> form an <a href="/wiki/Military_alliance" title="Military alliance">alliance</a> against King <a href="/wiki/Theuderic_II" title="Theuderic II">Theuderic II</a> of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Burgundy" title="Kingdom of Burgundy">Burgundy</a>, whose grandmother and sister has murdered Theuderic's wife Ermenberga, daughter of <a href="/wiki/Witteric" title="Witteric">Witteric</a>, king of the Visigoths. Fighting takes place around <a href="/wiki/Narbonne" title="Narbonne">Narbonne</a>, but little is known of the details or outcome (approximate date).</li>
<li>Queen <a href="/wiki/Brunhilda_of_Austrasia" title="Brunhilda of Austrasia">Brunhilda</a> has <a href="/wiki/Uncelen" title="Uncelen">Uncelen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Duke_of_Swabia" title="Duke of Swabia">Duke of Alemannia</a>, removed from office after his foot is cut off as <a href="/wiki/Revenge" title="Revenge">revenge</a> for <a href="/wiki/Protadius" title="Protadius">Protadius</a>' death (according to the <i><a href="/wiki/Lex_Alamannorum" title="Lex Alamannorum">Lex Alamannorum</a></i>).</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Britain">Britain</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=607&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Britain">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>King <a href="/wiki/Ceolwulf_of_Wessex" title="Ceolwulf of Wessex">Ceolwulf of Wessex</a> fights the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Sussex" title="Kingdom of Sussex">South Saxons</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-AS607_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-AS607-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=607&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_1" title="August 1">August 1</a> – Empress <a href="/wiki/Empress_Suiko" title="Empress Suiko">Suiko</a> appoints <a href="/wiki/Ono_no_Imoko" title="Ono no Imoko">Ono no Imoko</a> as official envoy to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sui_Dynasty" title="Sui Dynasty">Sui Court</a> (<a href="/wiki/Japanese_missions_to_Imperial_China" title="Japanese missions to Imperial China">Japanese missions to Imperial China</a>). She sends him to pay <a href="/wiki/Tribute" title="Tribute">tribute</a> to Emperor <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Yang_of_Sui" title="Emperor Yang of Sui">Yángdi</a>, and let him deliver the famous letter from prince-regent <a href="/wiki/Prince_Sh%C5%8Dtoku" title="Prince Shōtoku">Shōtoku</a> which begins: "The Son of Heaven where the sun rises (<a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>), to the <a href="/wiki/Mandate_of_Heaven" title="Mandate of Heaven">Son of Heaven</a> where the sun sets (<a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>), may good health be with you." (Traditional <a href="/wiki/Japanese_calendar" title="Japanese calendar">Japanese date</a>: July 3, 607).</li>
<li>Yángdi is offended by his general <a href="/wiki/Gao_Jiong" title="Gao Jiong">Gao Jiong</a>, who makes several comments critical of the emperor's policies, against <a href="/wiki/G%C3%B6kt%C3%BCrks" title="Göktürks">Tujue</a> submissive <a href="/wiki/Yami_Qaghan" title="Yami Qaghan">Yami Qaghan</a>. He is executed (<a href="/wiki/Decapitation" title="Decapitation">beheaded</a>), and Gao's sons are <a href="/wiki/Exile" title="Exile">exiled</a> to the border provinces (<a href="/wiki/North_China" title="North China">Northern China</a>).</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_topic">By topic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=607&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: By topic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Religion">Religion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=607&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Religion">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_19" title="February 19">February 19</a> – The vacancy (<i><a href="/wiki/Sede_vacante" title="Sede vacante">sede vacante</a></i>) that has existed on the <a href="/wiki/Holy_See" title="Holy See">papal throne</a>, since the death last year of <a href="/wiki/Pope_Sabinian" title="Pope Sabinian">Sabinian</a>, ends with the election of a <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a>-born <a href="/wiki/Deacon" title="Deacon">deacon</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Catholic_Church" title="Catholic Church">Catholic Church</a>. Pope <a href="/wiki/Pope_Boniface_III" title="Pope Boniface III">Boniface III</a> is appointed as the 66th <a href="/wiki/Pope" title="Pope">pope</a>, but dies the same year.</li>
<li>Emperor <a href="/wiki/Phocas" title="Phocas">Phocas</a> bestows the title "Universal Bishop" upon Boniface III, in an effort to improve relations with Rome.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/October_11" title="October 11">October 11</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Patriarch_Thomas_I_of_Constantinople" title="Patriarch Thomas I of Constantinople">Thomas I</a> is appointed as the 60th <a href="/wiki/Ecumenical_Patriarch_of_Constantinople" title="Ecumenical Patriarch of Constantinople">patriarch of Constantinople</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/H%C5%8Dry%C5%AB-ji" title="Hōryū-ji">Hōryū-ji</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Buddhist" title="Buddhist">Buddhist</a> temple in <a href="/wiki/Ikaruga,_Nara" title="Ikaruga, Nara">Ikaruga</a>, near <a href="/wiki/Nara_Prefecture" title="Nara Prefecture">Nara</a> (Japan), is constructed.</li>
</ul>
