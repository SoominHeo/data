<p><b>Bolívar</b> (<a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a>: <span lang="es"><i>Estado Bolívar</i></span>, <small>IPA: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Spanish" title="Help:IPA for Spanish">[esˈtaðo βoˈliβar]</a></span>), is one of the 23 <a href="/wiki/States_of_Venezuela" title="States of Venezuela">states</a> (<i><span lang="es">estados</span></i>) into which <a href="/wiki/Venezuela" title="Venezuela">Venezuela</a> is divided. The state capital city is <a href="/wiki/Ciudad_Bol%C3%ADvar" title="Ciudad Bolívar">Ciudad Bolívar</a>. Bolívar State covers a total surface area of 240,500 km² and as of the 2011 census, had a population of 1,410,964.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bol%C3%ADvar_(state)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The territory covered by present-day Bolívar was previously part of the <a href="/wiki/Guayana_Province" title="Guayana Province">Guayana Province</a>, a Province of the <a href="/wiki/Spanish_Empire" title="Spanish Empire">Spanish Empire</a> (from 1585) and later of Venezuela (until 1864, when the <a href="/wiki/Provinces_of_Venezuela" title="Provinces of Venezuela">Provinces of Venezuela</a> were replaced by the <a href="/wiki/States_of_Venezuela" title="States of Venezuela">States of Venezuela</a>, following the <a href="/wiki/Federal_War" title="Federal War">Federal War</a>).</p>
