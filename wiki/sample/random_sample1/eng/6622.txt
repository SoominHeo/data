<p><b>Volsk</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Вольск</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">town</a> in <a href="/wiki/Saratov_Oblast" title="Saratov Oblast">Saratov Oblast</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>, located on the right bank of the <a href="/wiki/Volga_River" title="Volga River">Volga River</a>, opposite the mouth of the <a href="/wiki/Bolshoy_Irgiz_River" title="Bolshoy Irgiz River">Bolshoy Irgiz</a> (a <a href="/wiki/Tributary" title="Tributary">tributary</a> of the Volga), 147 kilometers (91 mi) northeast from <a href="/wiki/Saratov" title="Saratov">Saratov</a>, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_center" title="Administrative center">administrative center</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Oblast" title="Oblast">oblast</a>. As of the <a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2010)" title="Russian Census (2010)">2010 Census</a>, its population was 66,508.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2010Census_7-2"><a href="#cite_note-2010Census-7">[7]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Volsk&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It was founded in 1699 as the <i><a href="/wiki/Sloboda" title="Sloboda">sloboda</a></i> of <b>Malykovka</b> (<span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Малыковка</span>) and was granted town status and renamed <b>Volgsk</b> (<span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Волгск</span>) in 1780.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gr_9-3"><a href="#cite_note-gr-9">[9]</a></sup> In the 19th century, the name gradually changed to a more pronounceable "Volsk".<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2010)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> After the <a href="/wiki/October_Revolution" title="October Revolution">October Revolution</a> of 1917, Volsk became a major center of <a href="/wiki/Cement" title="Cement">cement</a> production.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Administrative_and_municipal_status">Administrative and municipal status</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Volsk&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Administrative and municipal status">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Within the <a href="/wiki/Subdivisions_of_Russia#Administrative_divisions" title="Subdivisions of Russia">framework of administrative divisions</a>, Volsk serves as the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_center" title="Administrative center">administrative center</a> of <a href="/wiki/Volsky_District" title="Volsky District">Volsky District</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-OKATO_3-2"><a href="#cite_note-OKATO-3">[3]</a></sup> even though it is not a part of it.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-OKATO2_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-OKATO2-4">[4]</a></sup> As an administrative division, it is, together with the <a href="/wiki/Urban-type_settlement" title="Urban-type settlement">work settlement</a> of <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kleny_(urban-type_settlement),_Saratov_Oblast&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kleny (urban-type settlement), Saratov Oblast (page does not exist)">Kleny</a> and three <a href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">rural localities</a>, incorporated separately<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-OKATO2_4-2"><a href="#cite_note-OKATO2-4">[4]</a></sup> as the <b><a href="/wiki/City_of_federal_subject_significance" title="City of federal subject significance">town of oblast significance</a> of Volsk</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-OKATO2_4-3"><a href="#cite_note-OKATO2-4">[4]</a></sup>—an administrative unit with the status equal to that of the <a href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_Samara_Oblast" title="Administrative divisions of Samara Oblast">districts</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SaratovO_admlaw_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-SaratovO_admlaw-2">[2]</a></sup> As a <a href="/wiki/Subdivisions_of_Russia#Municipal_divisions" title="Subdivisions of Russia">municipal division</a>, the town of oblast significance of Volsk is incorporated within Volsky Municipal District as <b>Volsk Urban Settlement</b>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SaratovOVolskyD_mun_5-3"><a href="#cite_note-SaratovOVolskyD_mun-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
