<p><b>Volchansk</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Волча́нск</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">town</a> under the administrative jurisdiction of the <a href="/wiki/City_of_federal_subject_significance" title="City of federal subject significance">Town</a> of <a href="/wiki/Karpinsk" title="Karpinsk">Karpinsk</a> in <a href="/wiki/Sverdlovsk_Oblast" title="Sverdlovsk Oblast">Sverdlovsk Oblast</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>, located on the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Volchanka_River&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Volchanka River (page does not exist)">Volchanka River</a> (a right <a href="/wiki/Tributary" title="Tributary">tributary</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Sosva_River" title="Sosva River">Sosva</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Ob_River" title="Ob River">Ob</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Drainage_basin" title="Drainage basin">basin</a>), 452 kilometers (281 mi) north of <a href="/wiki/Yekaterinburg" title="Yekaterinburg">Yekaterinburg</a>, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_center" title="Administrative center">administrative center</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Oblast" title="Oblast">oblast</a>. Population: <span class="nowrap">10,010 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2010)" title="Russian Census (2010)">2010 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2010Census_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-2010Census-4">[4]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">11,043 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2002)" title="Russian Census (2002)">2002 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PopCensus_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-PopCensus-8">[8]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">14,814 (<a href="/wiki/Soviet_Census_(1989)" title="Soviet Census (1989)">1989 Census</a>)</span>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census1989_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-Census1989-9">[9]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Volchansk&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The town consists of two parts: Lesnaya Volchanka in the north (or Volchansk proper) and Volchanka in the south.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2013)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Volchansk&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Town status was granted to Volchansk in 1956.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gr_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-gr-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Administrative_and_municipal_status">Administrative and municipal status</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Volchansk&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Administrative and municipal status">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Within the <a href="/wiki/Subdivisions_of_Russia#Administrative_divisions" title="Subdivisions of Russia">framework of the administrative divisions</a>, it is, together with ten <a href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">rural localities</a>, subordinated to the <b><a href="/wiki/City_of_federal_subject_significance" title="City of federal subject significance">Town</a> of <a href="/wiki/Karpinsk" title="Karpinsk">Karpinsk</a></b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SverdlovskO_admlist_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-SverdlovskO_admlist-1">[1]</a></sup>—an administrative unit with the status equal to that of the <a href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_Sverdlovsk_Oblast" title="Administrative divisions of Sverdlovsk Oblast">districts</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SverdlovskO_admlaw_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-SverdlovskO_admlaw-10">[10]</a></sup> As a <a href="/wiki/Subdivisions_of_Russia#Municipal_divisions" title="Subdivisions of Russia">municipal division</a>, Volchansk, together with two rural localities under the administrative jurisdiction of the Town of Karpinsk, is incorporated as <b>Volchansky Urban Okrug</b>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SverdlovskO_mun_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-SverdlovskO_mun-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SverdlovskO_admlist_1-4"><a href="#cite_note-SverdlovskO_admlist-1">[1]</a></sup> Karpinsk and eight other rural localities are incorporated separately as Karpinsk Urban Okrug.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SverdlovskO_mun_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-SverdlovskO_mun-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Transportation">Transportation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Volchansk&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Transportation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">See also: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Volchansk_tram_system" title="Volchansk tram system">Volchansk tram system</a></div>
<p>A <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Volchansk_tram_system" title="Volchansk tram system">tram</a> system started operating in Volchansk on December 31, 1951. The total length of the tram line is about 7 kilometers (4.3 mi).</p>
