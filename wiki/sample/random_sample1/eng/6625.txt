<p><b>Volker Bruch</b> (born 9 March 1980) is a <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> television and film actor. He is best known internationally for a leading role in the television drama <i><a href="/wiki/Generation_War" title="Generation War">Generation War</a></i> (2013) as Wilhelm Winter.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_acting_career">Life and acting career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Volker_Bruch&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and acting career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>After graduating from high school in <a href="/wiki/Munich" title="Munich">Munich</a> he studied performing arts at the <a href="/wiki/Max_Reinhardt_Seminar" title="Max Reinhardt Seminar">Max Reinhardt Seminar</a> in <a href="/wiki/Vienna" title="Vienna">Vienna</a>. <a href="/wiki/Austria" title="Austria">Austria</a>. He is also known for roles in: <i><a href="/wiki/The_Reader_(2008_film)" title="The Reader (2008 film)">The Reader</a></i> (2008), <i><a href="/wiki/Female_Agents" title="Female Agents">Female Agents</a></i> (2008), <i><a href="/wiki/The_Baader_Meinhof_Complex" title="The Baader Meinhof Complex">The Baader Meinhof Complex</a></i> (2008) as <a href="/wiki/Stefan_Aust" title="Stefan Aust">Stefan Aust</a>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Red_Baron_(2008_film)" title="The Red Baron (2008 film)">The Red Baron</a></i> (2008) as <a href="/wiki/Lothar_von_Richthofen" title="Lothar von Richthofen">Oberleutnant Lothar Freiherr von Richthofen</a>, <i><a href="/wiki/Young_Goethe_in_Love" title="Young Goethe in Love">Young Goethe in Love</a></i> (2010) and <i><a href="/wiki/Nanga_Parbat_(film)" title="Nanga Parbat (film)">Nanga Parbat</a></i> (2010). Bruch has five siblings and lives in Munich.</p>
