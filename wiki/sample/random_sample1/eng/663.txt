<p>Year <b>617</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">DCXVII</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Saturday" title="Common year starting on Saturday">common year starting on Saturday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>. The denomination 617 for this year has been used since the early medieval period, when the <a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a> <a href="/wiki/Calendar_era" title="Calendar era">calendar era</a> became the prevalent method in Europe for naming years.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=617&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Byzantine_Empire">Byzantine Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=617&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Byzantine Empire">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Byzantine%E2%80%93Sassanid_War_of_602%E2%80%93628#Anatolia" title="Byzantine–Sassanid War of 602–628">Byzantine-Sassanid War</a>: The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sassanid_Empire" title="Sassanid Empire">Persian</a> army under <a href="/wiki/Shahin_Vahmanzadegan" title="Shahin Vahmanzadegan">Shahin Vahmanzadegan</a> conquers <a href="/wiki/Chalcedon" title="Chalcedon">Chalcedon</a> in <a href="/wiki/Anatolia" title="Anatolia">Anatolia</a>, and reaches the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bosporus" title="Bosporus">Bosporus</a>, threatening <a href="/wiki/Constantinople" title="Constantinople">Constantinople</a>. Emperor <a href="/wiki/Heraclius" title="Heraclius">Heraclius</a> begins peace negotiations, promising an annual <a href="/wiki/Tribute" title="Tribute">tribute</a> of a 1,000 talents of <a href="/wiki/Gold" title="Gold">gold</a> and <a href="/wiki/Silver" title="Silver">silver</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Greatrex-Lieu_p._II.2C_198_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Greatrex-Lieu_p._II.2C_198-1">[1]</a></sup> Shahin withdrews with his army to <a href="/wiki/Syria" title="Syria">Syria</a>, to focus on the invasion of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Diocese_of_Egypt_(Late_Antiquity)" title="Diocese of Egypt (Late Antiquity)">Egypt</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Avars_(Carpathians)" title="Avars (Carpathians)">Avars</a> send envoys to Constantinople for a meeting with Heraclius. He is warned about an <a href="/wiki/Ambush" title="Ambush">ambush</a>, and flees for safety behind the city walls in time. Angry at the failure to capture the <a href="/wiki/Byzantine_Empire" title="Byzantine Empire">Byzantine</a> emperor, the Avars plunder <a href="/wiki/Thrace" title="Thrace">Thrace</a> and return to the <a href="/wiki/Danube" title="Danube">Danube River</a>, carrying off 270,000 people.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=617&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grasulf_of_Friuli" title="Grasulf of Friuli">Grasulf II</a> becomes the Lombard <a href="/wiki/Dukes_and_margraves_of_Friuli" title="Dukes and margraves of Friuli">Duke of Friuli</a> (<a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>), after the assassination of his nephews, <a href="/wiki/Tasso_of_Friuli" title="Tasso of Friuli">Tasso</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kakko_of_Friuli" title="Kakko of Friuli">Kakko</a> ,in <a href="/wiki/Oderzo" title="Oderzo">Oderzo</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Britain">Britain</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=617&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Britain">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sigeberht_the_Little" title="Sigeberht the Little">Sigeberht</a> becomes king of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Essex" title="Kingdom of Essex">Essex</a>, after his father <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A6ward_of_Essex" title="Sæward of Essex">Sæward</a> and uncle <a href="/wiki/Sexred" title="Sexred">Sexred</a> are killed in battle against the <a href="/wiki/Wessex" title="Wessex">West Saxons</a>.</li>
<li>Heathens revolt in <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Kent" title="Kingdom of Kent">Kent</a> under King <a href="/wiki/Eadbald_of_Kent" title="Eadbald of Kent">Eadbald</a>. During the uprising <a href="/wiki/Justus" title="Justus">Justus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bishop_of_Rochester" title="Bishop of Rochester">bishop of Rochester</a>, flees to <a href="/wiki/Gaul" title="Gaul">Gaul</a>.</li>
<li>King <a href="/wiki/Edwin_of_Northumbria" title="Edwin of Northumbria">Edwin of Northumbria</a> invades and annexes the minor <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Britons_(Celtic_people)" title="Britons (Celtic people)">British</a> kingdom of <a href="/wiki/Elmet" title="Elmet">Elmet</a> (approximate date).</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=617&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_8" title="September 8">September 8</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Huoyi" title="Battle of Huoyi">Battle of Huoyi</a>: Rebel forces under <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Gaozu_of_Tang" title="Emperor Gaozu of Tang">Li Yuān</a> defeat the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sui_Dynasty" title="Sui Dynasty">Sui</a> troops near the <a href="/wiki/Fen_River" title="Fen River">Fen River</a>, and capture the <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">Chinese</a> capital, <a href="/wiki/Chang%27an" title="Chang'an">Chang'an</a>. Li has gained support from <a href="/wiki/Shibi_Khan" title="Shibi Khan">Shibi</a>, ruler (<i><a href="/wiki/Khagan" title="Khagan">khagan</a></i>) of the <a href="/wiki/G%C3%B6kt%C3%BCrks" title="Göktürks">Eastern Turks</a>, who secures his northern frontier and supplies him with 2,000 horses.</li>
<li>Winter – Li Yuan demotes Sui emperor <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Yang_of_Sui" title="Emperor Yang of Sui">Yángdi</a> to the status of <i><a href="/wiki/Taishang_Huang" title="Taishang Huang">Taishang Huang</a></i> (retired emperor), and declares <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Gong_of_Sui" title="Emperor Gong of Sui">Yang You</a> emperor, while he gives himself authority over the western Sui <a href="/wiki/Commandery_(China)" title="Commandery (China)">commanderies</a> (prefecture), under the title of "Prince of Tang".</li>
</ul>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Religion">Religion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=617&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Religion">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Mecca" title="Mecca">Meccans</a> begin a <a href="/wiki/Meccan_boycott_of_the_Hashemites" title="Meccan boycott of the Hashemites">boycott of the Banu Hashim</a> clan, to which <a href="/wiki/Islam" title="Islam">Islamic</a> prophet <a href="/wiki/Muhammad" title="Muhammad">Muhammad</a> belongs. A <a href="/wiki/Civil_war" title="Civil war">civil war</a> begins in <a href="/wiki/Medina" title="Medina">Medina</a>.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Bu%27ath" title="Battle of Bu'ath">Battle of Bu'ath</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Arabian_tribes_that_interacted_with_Muhammad" title="Arabian tribes that interacted with Muhammad">Arabic tribes</a> win an indecisive battle against the <a href="/wiki/Jews" title="Jews">Jewish</a> tribe of <a href="/wiki/Banu_Qurayza" title="Banu Qurayza">Banu Qurayza</a>, near the Medinan <a href="/wiki/Oasis" title="Oasis">oasis</a>.</li>
</ul>
