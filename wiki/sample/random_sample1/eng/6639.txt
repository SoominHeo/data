<p><b>Bongolava</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Regions_of_Madagascar" title="Regions of Madagascar">region</a> in central-western <a href="/wiki/Madagascar" title="Madagascar">Madagascar</a>. The capital of the region is <a href="/wiki/Tsiroanomandidy" title="Tsiroanomandidy">Tsiroanomandidy</a>. It had an estimated population of 457,368 in 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> <span class="citation-needed-content" style="padding-left:0.1em; padding-right:0.1em; color:#595959; border:1px solid #DDD;">The head of the region is Jean Claude Rabarijaona.</span><sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="margin-left:0.1em; white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2012)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> </p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bongolava&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The region is situated in central-western Madagascar. It is bordered by <a href="/wiki/Betsiboka" title="Betsiboka">Betsiboka</a>, <a href="/wiki/Melaky" title="Melaky">Melaky</a>, <a href="/wiki/Menabe" title="Menabe">Menabe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vakinankaratra" title="Vakinankaratra">Vakinankaratra</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Itasy" title="Itasy">Itasy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Analamanga" title="Analamanga">Analamanga</a>. The altitude ranges from 800 m (2,600 ft) to 1,500 m (4,900 ft).</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Administrative_divisions">Administrative divisions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bongolava&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Administrative divisions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Bongolava region is divided into two districts, which are sub-divided into 24 communes.</p>
