<p><b>Bongmyeong Station</b> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Subway_station" title="Subway station">station</a> on <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Subway_Line_1" title="Seoul Subway Line 1">Line 1</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Metropolitan_Subway" title="Seoul Metropolitan Subway">Seoul Metropolitan Subway</a>. It opened in December 2008.</p>
