<p><b>Bugae Station</b> is a subway station located in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bupyeong-gu" title="Bupyeong-gu">Bupyeong-gu</a>, a district in <a href="/wiki/Incheon" title="Incheon">Incheon</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. This station is on the <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Subway_Line_1" title="Seoul Subway Line 1">Seoul Subway Line 1</a>.</p>
