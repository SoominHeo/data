<p><i><b>Boonie Bears: To the Rescue</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh">熊出没之夺宝奇兵</span>) is a 2014 <a href="/wiki/Chinese_animation" title="Chinese animation">Chinese animated</a> <a href="/wiki/Comedy_film" title="Comedy film">comedy</a> <a href="/wiki/Adventure_film" title="Adventure film">adventure</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Family_film" title="Family film">family</a> film based on the animated television series <i><a href="/wiki/Boonie_Bears" title="Boonie Bears">Boonie Bears</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> A second film, <i><a href="/wiki/Boonie_Bears:_Mystical_Winter" title="Boonie Bears: Mystical Winter">Boonie Bears: Mystical Winter</a></i>, was released in 2015.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Reception">Reception</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Boonie_Bears:_To_the_Rescue&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Reception">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The film had grossed <a href="/wiki/Renminbi" title="Renminbi">RMB</a> 103 million (<a href="/wiki/United_States_dollar" title="United States dollar">US$</a>17 million) at the Chinese box office three days after being released. It had the highest-grossing opening weekend for a Chinese animated film and the third highest-grossing opening day for any animated film at the Chinese box office.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> More than a week after being released, it had grossed CN¥183 million (US$30.3 million). It is the highest grossing Chinese animated film in the country, beating the record previously held by <i><a href="/wiki/Mission_Incredible:_Adventures_on_the_Dragon%27s_Trail" title="Mission Incredible: Adventures on the Dragon's Trail">Mission Incredible: Adventures on the Dragon's Trail</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
