<p><b>Nils Allen "Booboo" Stewart, Jr.</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cambio1_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-cambio1-1">[1]</a></sup> (born January 21, 1994) is an American actor, dancer, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Model_(profession)" title="Model (profession)">model</a>, and singer, best known for playing <a href="/wiki/List_of_Twilight_characters#Seth_Clearwater" title="List of Twilight characters">Seth Clearwater</a>, a shapeshifter (wolf) in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Twilight_Saga_(film_series)" title="The Twilight Saga (film series)">The Twilight Saga</a></i>, and his role as Jay in <i><a href="/wiki/Descendants_(2015_film)" title="Descendants (2015 film)">Descendants</a></i> and its upcoming sequel, <i><a href="/wiki/Descendants_2" title="Descendants 2">Descendants 2</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Booboo_Stewart&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Nils Allen Stewart, Jr. was born in Beverly Hills, California. His father, Nils Allen Stewart, is a professional stuntman.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> He has <a href="/wiki/Japanese_people" title="Japanese people">Japanese</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chinese_people" title="Chinese people">Chinese</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Koreans" title="Koreans">Korean</a> ancestry on his mother's side along with <a href="/wiki/Scottish_people" title="Scottish people">Scottish</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Russian_people" title="Russian people">Russian</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Blackfoot_Confederacy" title="Blackfoot Confederacy">Blackfoot</a> ancestry on his father's side.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Stewart was in a band named "Echoes of Angels" and toured with his mother and grandmother, who were known as "TSC" (The Stewart Clan).</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_career">Early career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Booboo_Stewart&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Early career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During 2006–2010 Stewart appeared or starred in several <a href="/wiki/Independent_film" title="Independent film">independent</a>, <a href="/wiki/Direct-to-video" title="Direct-to-video">direct-to-video</a>, or <a href="/wiki/Television_film" title="Television film">TV films</a>. He also hosted six episodes of the children's show <i>Blue Dolphin Kids</i> in Hawaii, and did stunt work on several films including the 2006 film <i><a href="/wiki/Zoom_(2006_film)" title="Zoom (2006 film)">Zoom</a></i> and the 2007 film <i><a href="/wiki/Beowulf_(2007_film)" title="Beowulf (2007 film)">Beowulf</a></i>. He had roles on episodes of <i><a href="/wiki/Steve_Harvey%27s_Big_Time_Challenge" title="Steve Harvey's Big Time Challenge">Steve Harvey's Big Time Challenge</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/ER_(TV_series)" title="ER (TV series)">ER</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Dante%27s_Cove" title="Dante's Cove">Dante's Cove</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Everybody_Hates_Chris" title="Everybody Hates Chris">Everybody Hates Chris</a></i> and "<a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=RL_Stine%27s_The_Haunting_Hour&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="RL Stine's The Haunting Hour (page does not exist)">RL Stine's The Haunting Hour</a>". Stewart also toured with <a href="/wiki/Hannah_Montana" title="Hannah Montana">Hannah Montana</a>/<a href="/wiki/Miley_Cyrus" title="Miley Cyrus">Miley Cyrus</a> on her <a href="/wiki/Best_of_Both_Worlds_Tour" title="Best of Both Worlds Tour">Best of Both Worlds Tour</a> featuring the <a href="/wiki/Jonas_Brothers" title="Jonas Brothers">Jonas Brothers</a> and in the Camp Rock Freestyle Jam concert series. He recorded the opening theme for the <i><a href="/wiki/Disney_Channel_Games" title="Disney Channel Games">Disney Channel Games 2008</a></i> titled "Let's Go!", and in 2010 "<a href="/wiki/Under_the_Sea" title="Under the Sea">Under the Sea</a> (from <i><a href="/wiki/The_Little_Mermaid" title="The Little Mermaid">The Little Mermaid</a></i>)" for the Disney recompilation disc <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/DisneyMania_7" title="DisneyMania 7">DisneyMania 7</a></i>. He promoted clothes, as well as toys and games such as <i><a href="/wiki/Wii_Fit" title="Wii Fit">Wii Fit</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/Hot_Wheels" title="Hot Wheels">Hot Wheels</a>.He also played Roman in Lab Rats: Elite Force.</p>
<p>During 2006–2008, he was a member of the musical group <a href="/wiki/T-Squad" title="T-Squad">T-Squad</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2010-present:_Theatrical_debut">2010-present: Theatrical debut</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Booboo_Stewart&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2010-present: Theatrical debut">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In 2010, Stewart appeared in the movie <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Logan_(2010_film)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Logan (2010 film) (page does not exist)">Logan</a></i> and portrayed <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seth_Clearwater" title="Seth Clearwater">Seth Clearwater</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Twilight_Saga:_Eclipse" title="The Twilight Saga: Eclipse">The Twilight Saga: Eclipse</a></i>, the third installment of <a href="/wiki/The_Twilight_Saga_(film_series)" title="The Twilight Saga (film series)"><i>The Twilight Saga</i> film series</a>.</p>
<p>In 2012, Stewart starred in the movie <a href="/wiki/White_Frog" title="White Frog">White Frog</a> as Nick Young, a teen with Asperger's syndrome. In 2013, he played the lead role in <a href="/wiki/Running_Deer_(film)" title="Running Deer (film)">Running Deer</a>, an award-winning short film produced and directed by <a href="/wiki/Brent_Ryan_Green" title="Brent Ryan Green">Brent Ryan Green</a> through <a href="/wiki/Toy_Gun_Films" title="Toy Gun Films">Toy Gun Films</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-News_OK_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-News_OK-4">[4]</a></sup> Stewart also appeared in 2014's <i><a href="/wiki/X-Men:_Days_of_Future_Past" title="X-Men: Days of Future Past">X-Men: Days of Future Past</a></i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> acting as <a href="/wiki/Warpath_(comics)" title="Warpath (comics)">Warpath</a>.</p>
<p>In 2013 Stewart appeared in Space Warriors along side Danny Glover, Dermot Mulroney, Josh Lucas, Mira Sorvino. Directed by Sean McNamara</p>
<p>Stewart starred in the 2015 Disney Channel original movie <i><a href="/wiki/Descendants_(2015_film)" title="Descendants (2015 film)">Descendants</a></i> as Jay, the son of Jafar. He is set to reprise his role in the sequel, <i><a href="/wiki/Descendants_2" title="Descendants 2">Descendants 2</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>

<p>In 2010, he was named a Celebrity Ambassador by the <a href="/wiki/Muscular_Dystrophy_Association" title="Muscular Dystrophy Association">Muscular Dystrophy Association</a>. In his role, Stewart is helping raise awareness in the fight against muscle diseases. He is also part of MDA's "Make a Muscle, Make a Difference" PSA campaign, being featured in print and TV advertisements.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> He also travelled to <a href="/wiki/Perth" title="Perth">Perth</a>, <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Seven_Network" title="Seven Network">Channel 7</a> Telethon, raising money for the <a href="/wiki/Princess_Margaret_Hospital_for_Children" title="Princess Margaret Hospital for Children">Princess Margaret Hospital for Children</a>. Stewart and his sister Fivel also hosted free concerts on behalf of the national non-profit organization <a href="/wiki/Childhelp" title="Childhelp">Childhelp</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>He appeared in a <a href="/wiki/People_for_the_Ethical_Treatment_of_Animals" title="People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals">PETA</a> campaign, encouraging people to adopt animals rather than to buy them from pet stores.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> He is also involved in <a href="/wiki/Four_Green_Steps" title="Four Green Steps">Four Green Steps</a>, an environmental organization based in <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
