<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="In_mathematics">In mathematics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Subspace&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: In mathematics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>A <a href="/wiki/Space_(mathematics)" title="Space (mathematics)">space</a> inheriting all characteristics of a parent space.</li>
<li>A subset of a topological space endowed with the <a href="/wiki/Subspace_topology" title="Subspace topology">subspace topology</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Linear_subspace" title="Linear subspace">Linear subspace</a>, in linear algebra, a subset of a vector space that is closed under addition and scalar multiplication</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Flat_(geometry)" title="Flat (geometry)">Flat (geometry)</a>, a Euclidean subspace</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Affine_subspace" title="Affine subspace">Affine subspace</a>, a geometric structure that generalizes the affine properties of a flat</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Projective_space#Projective_subspace" title="Projective space">Projective subspace</a>, a geometric structure that generalizes a linear subspace of a vector space</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Multilinear_subspace" title="Multilinear subspace">Multilinear subspace</a> in multilinear algebra, a subset of a tensor space that is closed under addition and scalar multiplication</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="In_science_fiction">In science fiction</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Subspace&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: In science fiction">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Subspace_(fictional)" title="Subspace (fictional)">Subspace (fictional)</a> or hyperspace, a fictional parallel universe</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Subspace_(Star_Trek)" title="Subspace (Star Trek)">Subspace (<i>Star Trek</i>)</a>, a fictional feature of space-time that facilitates faster-than-light communication and transit</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="In_games">In games</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Subspace&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: In games">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/SubSpace_(video_game)" title="SubSpace (video game)"><i>SubSpace</i> (video game)</a>, a two-dimensional space shooter computer game</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Super_Smash_Bros._Brawl#Adventure_Mode:_The_Subspace_Emissary" title="Super Smash Bros. Brawl">The Subspace Emissary</a>, the two player Adventure mode in the video game <i>Super Smash Bros. Brawl</i></li>
<li><i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yume_K%C5%8Dj%C5%8D:_Doki_Doki_Panic" title="Yume Kōjō: Doki Doki Panic">Yume Kōjō: Doki Doki Panic</a></i> and its derivative, <i><a href="/wiki/Super_Mario_Bros._2" title="Super Mario Bros. 2">Super Mario Bros. 2</a></i>, both feature a realm called "Subspace" whose primary features include <a href="/wiki/Power-up" title="Power-up">power-up</a> items, health restoration bonuses, and coins used to purchase extra lives</li>
<li><i>SubSpace</i>, is the name of a level in the <a href="/wiki/PlayStation_(console)" title="PlayStation (console)">PlayStation</a> version of <a href="/wiki/Final_Doom" title="Final Doom">Final Doom</a></li>
</ul>
