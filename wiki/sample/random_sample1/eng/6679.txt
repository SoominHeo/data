<p><b>Busan Kyungsang College</b> is a college in the Yeonje-gu district of <a href="/wiki/Busan" title="Busan">Busan</a> Metropolitan City, in southeastern <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. The president is Park Seok-Yong. It carries a legal maximum enrollment of 3,700 students.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Academic_departments">Academic departments</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Busan_Kyungsang_College&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Academic departments">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The college offers training in technical and business-related fields.</p>
<ul>
<li>Department of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Business_Administration" title="Business Administration">Business Administration</a></li>
<li>Department of e-Commerce Tax Information</li>
<li>Department of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/International_Trade" title="International Trade">International Trade</a></li>
<li>Department of <a href="/wiki/Finance" title="Finance">Finance</a></li>
<li>Department of <a href="/wiki/Property_management" title="Property management">Real Estate Management</a></li>
<li>Department of Social Welfare Public Administration</li>
<li>Department of Tourism Japanese</li>
<li>Department of Tourism English</li>
<li>Department of Tourism Chinese</li>
<li>Department of Aircraft Service and Secretarial Science</li>
<li>Department of Early Childhood Education</li>
<li>Department of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fashion_Design" title="Fashion Design">Fashion Design</a></li>
<li>Department of Broadcasting and Art</li>
<li>Department of Multimedia Computing</li>
<li>Department of Internet Information Technology</li>
<li>Department of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Industrial_Design" title="Industrial Design">Industrial Design</a></li>
<li>Department of <a href="/wiki/Computer_animation" title="Computer animation">Digital Animation</a></li>
<li>Department of <a href="/wiki/Mobile_game" title="Mobile game">Mobile Games</a></li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dd>According to the school, this is the only mobile game department in South Korea.</dd>
</dl>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Busan_Kyungsang_College&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The school opened its doors on March 8, 1980. The Hwashin School Foundation which founded it was established in 1977. The current president was installed in 2001.</p>
