<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Turbocharger#boost" title="Turbocharger">Turbocharger boost</a>, positive manifold pressure in engines</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/Boost_converter" title="Boost converter">Boost converter</a>, an electrical circuit variation of a DC to DC converter, which increases (boosts) the voltage</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Boost_(C%2B%2B_libraries)" title="Boost (C++ libraries)">Boost (C++ libraries)</a>, a set of free peer-reviewed portable C++ libraries</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Boosting_(machine_learning)" title="Boosting (machine learning)">Boosting (machine learning)</a>, a supervised learning algorithm</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Intel_Turbo_Boost" title="Intel Turbo Boost">Intel Turbo Boost</a>, a technology that enables a processor to run above its base operating frequency</li>
