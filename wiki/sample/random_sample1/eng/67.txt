<p><br/>
Year <b>1317</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MCCCXVII</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Saturday" title="Common year starting on Saturday">common year starting on Saturday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1317&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="December">December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1317&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/December_10" title="December 10">December 10</a>–<a href="/wiki/December_11" title="December 11">11</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Birger,_King_of_Sweden" title="Birger, King of Sweden">Birger of Sweden</a> has his brothers, Dukes <a href="/wiki/Eric,_Duke_of_S%C3%B6dermanland" title="Eric, Duke of Södermanland">Eric</a> and <a href="/wiki/Valdemar,_Duke_of_Finland" title="Valdemar, Duke of Finland">Valdemar</a>, captured and thrown into a dungeon during the <a href="/wiki/Nyk%C3%B6ping_Banquet" title="Nyköping Banquet">Nyköping Banquet</a>, as a revenge for their imprisonment of him in the <a href="/wiki/H%C3%A5tuna_games" title="Håtuna games">Håtuna games</a> in <a href="/wiki/1306" title="1306">1306</a>. As the dukes soon starve to death in the dungeon, their followers rebel against the king, throwing Sweden into civil war, in which the king is deposed in <a href="/wiki/1318" title="1318">1318</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Date_unknown">Date unknown</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1317&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Date unknown">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Great_Famine_of_1315-1317" title="Great Famine of 1315-1317">Great Famine of 1315-1317</a> comes to an end.</li>
<li>Pope <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/John_XXII" title="John XXII">John XXII</a> erects the <a href="/wiki/Diocese" title="Diocese">dioceses</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Diocese_of_Lu%C3%A7on" title="Diocese of Luçon">Luçon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ancient_Diocese_of_Maillezais" title="Ancient Diocese of Maillezais">Maillezais</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Diocese_of_Tulle" title="Diocese of Tulle">Tulle</a> and issues the <a href="/wiki/Decretal" title="Decretal">decretal</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Spondent_Pariter" title="Spondent Pariter">Spondent Pariter</a></i> prohibiting <a href="/wiki/Alchemy" title="Alchemy">alchemy</a>, but not chemistry (which John himself had studied).</li>
</ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Deaths">Deaths</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1317&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Deaths">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
