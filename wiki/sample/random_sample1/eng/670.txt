<p>Year <b>684</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">DCLXXXIV</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Friday" title="Leap year starting on Friday">leap year starting on Friday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>. The denomination 684 for this year has been used since the early medieval period, when the <a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a> <a href="/wiki/Calendar_era" title="Calendar era">calendar era</a> became the prevalent method in Europe for naming years.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=684&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=684&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=684&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gistemar" title="Gistemar">Ghislemar</a>, mayor of the palace in <a href="/wiki/Neustria" title="Neustria">Neustria</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Burgundy" title="Kingdom of Burgundy">Burgundy</a>, dies after a 2-year reign, and is succeeded by his father <a href="/wiki/Waratton" title="Waratton">Waratton</a>. He makes peace between the three <a href="/wiki/Francia" title="Francia">Frankish kingdoms</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Britain">Britain</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=684&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Britain">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>King <a href="/wiki/Ecgfrith_of_Northumbria" title="Ecgfrith of Northumbria">Ecgfrith of Northumbria</a> sends an <a href="/wiki/Punitive_expedition" title="Punitive expedition">punitive expedition</a> to <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> under his <a href="/wiki/Ealdorman" title="Ealdorman">ealdorman</a> Berht, laying waste to the territory of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Meath" title="Kingdom of Meath">Meath</a>, ruled by High King <a href="/wiki/F%C3%ADnsnechta_Fledach" title="Fínsnechta Fledach">Fínsnechta Fledach</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Arabian_Empire">Arabian Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=684&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Arabian Empire">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>Caliph <a href="/wiki/Muawiya_II" title="Muawiya II">Muawiya II</a> dies at <a href="/wiki/Damascus" title="Damascus">Damascus</a>, after a brief reign that ends <a href="/wiki/Umayyad_Caliphate#Sufyanids" title="Umayyad Caliphate">Sufyanid rule</a>. A new <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Caliph" title="Caliph">caliph</a> is proclaimed in <a href="/wiki/Syria" title="Syria">Syria</a> amidst <a href="/wiki/Endemic_warfare" title="Endemic warfare">tribal wars</a>, but <a href="/wiki/Marwan_I" title="Marwan I">Marwan I</a> will reign until next year.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_18" title="August 18">August 18</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Marj_Rahit_(684)" title="Battle of Marj Rahit (684)">Battle of Marj Rahit</a>: Muslim <a href="/wiki/Partisan_(military)" title="Partisan (military)">partisans</a> under Marwan I defeat the supporters of <a href="/wiki/Abd_Allah_ibn_al-Zubayr" title="Abd Allah ibn al-Zubayr">Abd Allah ibn al-Zubayr</a> near Damascus, and cement <a href="/wiki/Umayyad_Caliphate" title="Umayyad Caliphate">Umayyad</a> control of Syria.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=684&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_3" title="January 3">January 3</a> – <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Zhongzong_of_Tang" title="Emperor Zhongzong of Tang">Zhong Zong</a> succeeds his father <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Gaozong_of_Tang" title="Emperor Gaozong of Tang">Gao Zong</a>, and becomes emperor of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tang_Dynasty" title="Tang Dynasty">Tang Dynasty</a>. His mother <a href="/wiki/Wu_Zetian" title="Wu Zetian">Wu Zetian</a> remains the power behind the <a href="/wiki/Throne" title="Throne">throne</a> in <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_27" title="February 27">February 27</a> – Wu Zetian replaces Zhong Zong in favor of his younger brother <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Ruizong_of_Tang" title="Emperor Ruizong of Tang">Rui Zong</a>. He becomes a <a href="/wiki/Puppet_ruler" title="Puppet ruler">puppet ruler</a>, and Zhong Zong is placed under <a href="/wiki/House_arrest" title="House arrest">house arrest</a>.</li>
<li>Summer – The <a href="/wiki/Pallava_dynasty" title="Pallava dynasty">Pallava Empire</a> (modern <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a>) invades the kingdom of <a href="/wiki/Sri_Lanka" title="Sri Lanka">Ceylon</a>. A Pallavan naval expedition employing <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tamil_people" title="Tamil people">Tamil</a> mercenaries ends the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_Sri_Lankan_monarchs#House_of_Moriya_.28463.E2.80.93691.29" title="List of Sri Lankan monarchs">Moriya Dynasty</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_7" title="September 7">September 7</a> – A large <a href="/wiki/Comet" title="Comet">comet</a> is observed in <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a> (it's Japan's oldest observation record of the <a href="/wiki/Halley%27s_Comet" title="Halley's Comet">Halley's Comet</a>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_13" title="November 13">November 13</a> – Emperor <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Tenmu" title="Emperor Tenmu">Tenmu</a> institutes eight titles of eight classes (<i><a href="/wiki/Kabane" title="Kabane">Yakusa-no-kabane</a></i>) in Japan.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_26" title="November 26">November 26</a> – A great <a href="/wiki/Earthquake" title="Earthquake">earthquake</a> strikes Japan. The people, houses, <a href="/wiki/Buddhist_temples_in_Japan" title="Buddhist temples in Japan">temples</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shinto_shrine" title="Shinto shrine">shrines</a> and domestic animals are greatly damaged.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Mesoamerica">Mesoamerica</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=684&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Mesoamerica">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_10" title="February 10">February 10</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/K%27inich_Kan_B%27alam_II" title="K'inich Kan B'alam II">K'inich Kan B'alam II</a> accedes to the rulership of the <a href="/wiki/Maya_civilization" title="Maya civilization">Maya</a> polity of <a href="/wiki/Palenque" title="Palenque">Palenque</a> (modern <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">Mexico</a>).</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_topic">By topic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=684&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: By topic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Religion">Religion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=684&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Religion">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Cuthbert" title="Cuthbert">Cuthbert</a> is elected <a href="/wiki/Bishop_of_Hexham" title="Bishop of Hexham">Bishop of Hexham</a>, and receives a visit from a large group under Ecgfrith. He agrees to return to <a href="/wiki/Lindisfarne" title="Lindisfarne">Lindisfarne</a> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Northumbria" title="Northumbria">Northumbria</a>) to take up duties.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_26" title="June 26">June 26</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Benedict_II" title="Pope Benedict II">Pope Benedict II</a> succeeds <a href="/wiki/Pope_Leo_II" title="Pope Leo II">Leo II</a> as the 81st <a href="/wiki/Pope" title="Pope">pope</a> of <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a>, after a period of <i><a href="/wiki/Sede_vacante" title="Sede vacante">sede vacante</a></i> ("vacant seat") of 1 year.</li>
</ul>
