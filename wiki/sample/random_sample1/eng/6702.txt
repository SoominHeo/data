<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Bucheon Philharmonic Orchestra</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <b>Bucheon Philharmonic Orchestra</b> (부천 필하모닉 오케스트라) is an orchestra based in <a href="/wiki/Bucheon" title="Bucheon">Bucheon</a>, a metropolitan city located between <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a> and <a href="/wiki/Incheon" title="Incheon">Incheon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Korea" title="Korea">Korea</a>. It was founded in 1988 as a part of Bucheon Civic Arts Company. In 1989, they engaged <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Heon-Jeong_Im&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Heon-Jeong Im (page does not exist)">Heon-Jeong Im</a> as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Music_Director" title="Music Director">Music Director</a> and Principal <a href="/wiki/Conducting" title="Conducting">Conductor</a>.</p>
<p>Since the 1990s, they have held many concert series such as <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Premiere" title="Premiere">premiere</a> of <a href="/wiki/Arnold_Schoenberg" title="Arnold Schoenberg">Arnold Schoenberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alban_Berg" title="Alban Berg">Alban Berg</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bart%C3%B3k" title="Bartók">Bartók</a>'s works and the complete cycle of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Beethoven" title="Beethoven">Beethoven</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Brahms" title="Brahms">Brahms</a>' symphonies. Between 1999 and 2003, they performed <a href="/wiki/Gustav_Mahler" title="Gustav Mahler">Gustav Mahler</a>'s complete symphonies (except Tenth Symphony's 5-movement performing version) for the first time in Korea.</p>
<p>In September 2002, they attended Asian Orchestra Week in <a href="/wiki/Tokyo" title="Tokyo">Tokyo</a> and received favourable criticism of their performance of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Berlioz" title="Berlioz">Berlioz</a>'s <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Symphonie_Fantastique" title="Symphonie Fantastique">Symphonie Fantastique</a>.</p>
<p>They are playing mostly in Bucheon Civic Center and Concert Hall of <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Arts_Center" title="Seoul Arts Center">Seoul Arts Center</a>.</p>
