<p><b>Bucheon Stadium Station</b> is a subway station on <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Subway_Line_7" title="Seoul Subway Line 7">Seoul Subway Line 7</a> with plans of it becoming a transfer station to <a href="/wiki/Sosa%E2%80%93Wonsi_Line" title="Sosa–Wonsi Line">Sosa–Wonsi Line</a> in 2018.</p>
