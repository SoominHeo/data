<p><i><b>All About My Mom</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">부탁해요, 엄마</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Butakhaeyo, eomma</span></i>; lit. "Please, Mom) is a 2015 <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Korean_drama" title="Korean drama">television series</a> starring <a href="/wiki/Eugene_(actress)" title="Eugene (actress)">Eugene</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lee_Sang-woo" title="Lee Sang-woo">Lee Sang-woo</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> It airs on <a href="/wiki/KBS2" title="KBS2">KBS2</a> every Saturday and Sunday.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Synopsis">Synopsis</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=All_About_My_Mom&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Synopsis">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Jin-ae (Eugene) works hard to make money only to end up spending it on her family, and her mother San-ok shows so much affection towards her first son only. Jin-ae then meets Kang Hoon-jae (Lee Sang-Woo), who coincidentally is the son of her boss who she admires, and marries him. While experiencing difficult times with her mother-in-law, Jin-ae begins to understand her own mother for the first time.</p>
