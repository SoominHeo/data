<p><b>Butambala District</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Districts_of_Uganda" title="Districts of Uganda">district</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Central_Region,_Uganda" title="Central Region, Uganda">Central Region</a> of <a href="/wiki/Uganda" title="Uganda">Uganda</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Location">Location</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Butambala_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Location">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This district is bordered by <a href="/wiki/Gomba_District" title="Gomba District">Gomba District</a> to the west and north-west, <a href="/wiki/Mityana_District" title="Mityana District">Mityana District</a> to the north-east, <a href="/wiki/Mpigi_District" title="Mpigi District">Mpigi District</a> to the east and south, and <a href="/wiki/Kalungu_District" title="Kalungu District">Kalungu District</a> to the south-west. The district headquarters at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gombe,_Butambala,_Uganda" title="Gombe, Butambala, Uganda">Gombe</a>, are located approximately 31 kilometres (19 mi), by road, west of the town of <a href="/wiki/Mpigi" title="Mpigi">Mpigi</a>, the largest metropolitan area in the sub-region.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> This is approximately 68 kilometres (42 mi), by road, south-west of <a href="/wiki/Kampala" title="Kampala">Kampala</a>, Uganda's capital and largest city.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Butambala_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Created by an act of parliament, the district became operational on 1 July 2010, having been split off of <a href="/wiki/Mpigi_District" title="Mpigi District">Mpigi District</a>, together with neighboring <a href="/wiki/Gomba_District" title="Gomba District">Gomba District</a>. Before becoming district, the area was Butambala County, one of the eighteen counties that constitute the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Buganda" title="Kingdom of Buganda">Kingdom of Buganda</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Population">Population</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Butambala_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Population">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The national population census in 1991 estimated the district population at about 74,100. The 2002 census put the population at about 86,800. In 2012, the population was estimated at about 99,400.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Economic_activity">Economic activity</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Butambala_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Economic activity">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Subsistence agriculture and small-scale animal husbandry are the backbone of Butambala District's economy. The main food crops are:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Beans" title="Beans">Beans</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Groundnuts" title="Groundnuts">Groundnuts</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sugar_cane" title="Sugar cane">Sugar cane</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bananas" title="Bananas">Bananas</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Matooke" title="Matooke">Matooke</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Maize" title="Maize">Maize</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mangoes" title="Mangoes">Mangoes</a></li>
</ul>
