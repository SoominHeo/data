<p>The <b>northern smooth-tailed treeshrew</b> (<i>Dendrogale murina</i>) is a species of <a href="/wiki/Treeshrew" title="Treeshrew">treeshrew</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Tupaiidae" title="Tupaiidae">Tupaiidae</a> found in <a href="/wiki/Cambodia" title="Cambodia">Cambodia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Thailand" title="Thailand">Thailand</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Vietnam" title="Vietnam">Vietnam</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-iucn_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-iucn-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
