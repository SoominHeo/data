<p>The <b>northern little yellow-eared bat</b>, <i><b>Vampyressa thyone</b></i>, is a <a href="/wiki/Bat" title="Bat">bat</a> <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> from <a href="/wiki/South_America" title="South America">South</a> and <a href="/wiki/Central_America" title="Central America">Central America</a>, where it is found from southern <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">Mexico</a> to <a href="/wiki/Bolivia" title="Bolivia">Bolivia</a>, <a href="/wiki/The_Guianas" title="The Guianas">the Guianas</a> and western <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-iucn_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-iucn-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Simmons_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Simmons-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
