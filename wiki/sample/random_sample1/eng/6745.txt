<p><b><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Index_Catalogue" title="Index Catalogue">IC</a> 405</b> (also known as the <b>Flaming Star Nebula</b>, <b><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sharpless_catalogue" title="Sharpless catalogue">SH</a> 2-229</b>, or <b><a href="/wiki/Caldwell_catalogue" title="Caldwell catalogue">Caldwell</a> 31</b>) is an <a href="/wiki/Emission_nebula" title="Emission nebula">emission</a>/<a href="/wiki/Reflection_nebula" title="Reflection nebula">reflection nebula</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-apod_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-apod-1">[1]</a></sup> in the constellation <a href="/wiki/Auriga_(constellation)" title="Auriga (constellation)">Auriga</a>, surrounding the bluish star <a href="/wiki/AE_Aurigae" title="AE Aurigae">AE Aurigae</a>. It shines at magnitude +6.0. Its celestial coordinates are RA 05<sup>h</sup> 16.2<sup>m</sup> dec +34° 28′.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-davidd_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-davidd-2">[2]</a></sup> It surrounds the irregular variable star <a href="/wiki/AE_Aurigae" title="AE Aurigae">AE Aurigae</a> and is located near the emission nebula IC 410, the open clusters <a href="/wiki/Messier_38" title="Messier 38">M38</a> and <a href="/wiki/Messier_36" title="Messier 36">M36</a>, and the K-class star <a href="/wiki/Iota_Aurigae" title="Iota Aurigae">Iota Aurigae</a>. The nebula measures approximately 37.0' x 19.0', and lies about 1,500 <a href="/wiki/Light-year" title="Light-year">light-years</a> away from <a href="/wiki/Earth" title="Earth">Earth</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-davidd_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-davidd-2">[2]</a></sup> It is believed that the proper motion of the central star can be traced back to the <a href="/wiki/Orion%27s_Belt" title="Orion's Belt">Orion's Belt</a> area.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-davidd_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-davidd-2">[2]</a></sup> The nebula is about 5 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Light-years" title="Light-years">light-years</a> across.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-apod_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-apod-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
