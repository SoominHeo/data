<p><b>Buram Station</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">불암역</span>; <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">Hanja</a>: <span lang="ko-Hani" xml:lang="ko-Hani">佛岩驛</span>) is a station of the <a href="/wiki/Busan%E2%80%93Gimhae_Light_Rail_Transit" title="Busan–Gimhae Light Rail Transit">BGLRT Line</a> of <a href="/wiki/Busan_Metro" title="Busan Metro">Busan Metro</a> in <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Buram-dong&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Buram-dong (page does not exist)">Buram-dong</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gimhae" title="Gimhae">Gimhae</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>.</p>
