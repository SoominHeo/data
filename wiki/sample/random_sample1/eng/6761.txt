<p><i><b>Carpilius</b></i> is a genus of <a href="/wiki/Crab" title="Crab">crabs</a> in the family <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Carpiliidae" title="Carpiliidae">Carpiliidae</a>, containing the following species:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Carpilius_convexus" title="Carpilius convexus">Carpilius convexus</a></i> <small>(Forskål, 1775)</small></li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Carpilius_corallinus" title="Carpilius corallinus">Carpilius corallinus</a></i> <small>(Herbst, 1783)</small></li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Carpilius_maculatus" title="Carpilius maculatus">Carpilius maculatus</a></i> <small>(Linnaeus, 1758)</small></li>
</ul>
