<p><b>Brassinolide</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Plant_hormone" title="Plant hormone">plant hormone</a>. The first isolated <a href="/wiki/Brassinosteroid" title="Brassinosteroid">brassinosteroid</a>, it was discovered when it was shown that pollen from <a href="/wiki/Rapeseed" title="Rapeseed">rapeseed</a> (<i>Brassica napus</i>) could promote stem elongation and cell division.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The biologically active component was isolated and named brassinolide.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
