<p><b>Brightlight Pictures Inc.</b> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Canadian" title="Canadian">Canadian</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Film_production" title="Film production">film production</a> and <a href="/wiki/Television" title="Television">television</a> <a href="/wiki/Production_company" title="Production company">production company</a> based in <a href="/wiki/Vancouver" title="Vancouver">Vancouver</a>, <a href="/wiki/British_Columbia" title="British Columbia">British Columbia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Brightlight_Pictures&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Brightlight Pictures is an award winning production company. Brightlight's Chairman, <a href="/wiki/Shawn_Williamson" title="Shawn Williamson">Shawn Williamson</a>, has produced more than 90 productions in the last 15 years and over 35 productions since launching Brightlight in 2001, including <i><a href="/wiki/White_Noise_(film)" title="White Noise (film)">White Noise</a></i> starring <a href="/wiki/Michael_Keaton" title="Michael Keaton">Michael Keaton</a>, which has grossed over $100 million since its world-wide theatrical release in 2005. Recently, Williamson has worked as a producer on the feature film <i><a href="/wiki/Horns_(film)" title="Horns (film)">Horns</a></i> starring <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Radcliffe" title="Daniel Radcliffe">Daniel Radcliffe</a>, for <a href="/wiki/Mandalay_Pictures" title="Mandalay Pictures">Mandalay Pictures</a> and <a href="/wiki/Red_Granite_Pictures" title="Red Granite Pictures">Red Granite Pictures</a>; <i><a href="/wiki/50/50_(2011_film)" title="50/50 (2011 film)">50/50</a></i> starring <a href="/wiki/Seth_Rogen" title="Seth Rogen">Seth Rogen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Joseph_Gordon-Levitt" title="Joseph Gordon-Levitt">Joseph Gordon-Levitt</a>; and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Company_You_Keep_(film)" title="The Company You Keep (film)">The Company You Keep</a></i>, a <a href="/wiki/Sony_Pictures_Classics" title="Sony Pictures Classics">Sony Pictures Classics</a> release for spring 2013 directed by <a href="/wiki/Robert_Redford" title="Robert Redford">Robert Redford</a> and starring Redford and <a href="/wiki/Shia_LaBeouf" title="Shia LaBeouf">Shia LaBeouf</a>.</p>
<p>Brightlight also developed, packaged and produced <i>Mr. Hockey, The <a href="/wiki/Gordie_Howe" title="Gordie Howe">Gordie Howe</a> Story</i> in partnership with Inferno Pictures and Balwin Entertainment for the <a href="/wiki/CBC_Television" title="CBC Television">CBC</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hallmark_Channel" title="Hallmark Channel">Hallmark Channel</a>. Other feature producing credits include <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Possession_(film)" title="The Possession (film)">The Possession</a></i> starring <a href="/wiki/Jeffrey_Dean_Morgan" title="Jeffrey Dean Morgan">Jeffrey Dean Morgan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kyra_Sedgwick" title="Kyra Sedgwick">Kyra Sedgwick</a> for <a href="/wiki/Mandate_Pictures" title="Mandate Pictures">Mandate Pictures</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lions_Gate_Films" title="Lions Gate Films">Lions Gate Films</a>; <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_Noise_2:_The_Light" title="White Noise 2: The Light">White Noise 2: The Light</a></i> starring <a href="/wiki/Nathan_Fillion" title="Nathan Fillion">Nathan Fillion</a> and <a href="/wiki/Katee_Sackhoff" title="Katee Sackhoff">Katee Sackhoff</a> with <a href="/wiki/Gold_Circle_Films" title="Gold Circle Films">Gold Circle Films</a>; <i><a href="/wiki/In_the_Name_of_the_King" title="In the Name of the King">In the Name of the King</a></i> starring <a href="/wiki/Jason_Statham" title="Jason Statham">Jason Statham</a>; <i><a href="/wiki/88_Minutes" title="88 Minutes">88 Minutes</a></i> starring <a href="/wiki/Al_Pacino" title="Al Pacino">Al Pacino</a>; <i><a href="/wiki/The_Wicker_Man_(2006_film)" title="The Wicker Man (2006 film)">The Wicker Man</a></i> starring <a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Cage" title="Nicolas Cage">Nicolas Cage</a> for <a href="/wiki/Nu_Image" title="Nu Image">Nu Image</a>/<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Millennium_Films" title="Millennium Films">Millennium Films</a> and <a href="/wiki/Emmett/Furla/Oasis_Films" title="Emmett/Furla/Oasis Films">Emmett/Furla Films</a>; <i><a href="/wiki/Slither_(2006_film)" title="Slither (2006 film)">Slither</a></i> with Gold Circle Films and <a href="/wiki/Universal_Studios" title="Universal Studios">Universal</a> and <i><a href="/wiki/Apollo_18_(film)" title="Apollo 18 (film)">Apollo 18</a></i> for <a href="/wiki/The_Weinstein_Company" title="The Weinstein Company">The Weinstein Company</a>. Brightlight also produced <i><a href="/wiki/Possession_(2009_film)" title="Possession (2009 film)">Possession</a></i> starring <a href="/wiki/Sarah_Michelle_Gellar" title="Sarah Michelle Gellar">Sarah Michelle Gellar</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Yari_Film_Group" title="Yari Film Group">Yari Film Group</a>; <i><a href="/wiki/Passengers_(2008_film)" title="Passengers (2008 film)">Passengers</a></i> starring <a href="/wiki/Anne_Hathaway" title="Anne Hathaway">Anne Hathaway</a> for Mandate Pictures and the feature film <i><a href="/wiki/Far_Cry_(film)" title="Far Cry (film)">Far Cry</a></i>, based on the video game of the same name.</p>
<p>Brightlight's television credits include the TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Saved_(TV_series)" title="Saved (TV series)">Saved</a></i> for <a href="/wiki/TNT_(TV_channel)" title="TNT (TV channel)">TNT</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fox_21_(production_company)" title="Fox 21 (production company)">Fox 21</a>; <i><a href="/wiki/About_a_Girl_(TV_series)" title="About a Girl (TV series)">About a Girl</a></i> for <a href="/wiki/MTV" title="MTV">MTV</a>'s <a href="/wiki/TeenNick" title="TeenNick">TeenNick</a> Network and <a href="/wiki/Global_Television_Network" title="Global Television Network">Global Television</a>; and <i><a href="/wiki/Search_and_Rescue_(TV_series)" title="Search and Rescue (TV series)">Search and Rescue</a></i> with Global TV and distributed by Lions Gate, Season One of which was the highest rated Canadian dramatic series in the spring of 2008. Brightlight also produced the <a href="/wiki/CTV_Television_Network" title="CTV Television Network">CTV</a>/<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Space_Channel" title="Space Channel">Space Channel</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Stormworld" title="Stormworld">Stormworld</a></i>, an Australian/Singaporean/Canadian Treaty co-production shot in Perth, Broome, Singapore and Vancouver.</p>
<p>Brightlight's UK/Canada feature co-production <i><a href="/wiki/Fifty_Dead_Men_Walking" title="Fifty Dead Men Walking">Fifty Dead Men Walking</a></i> starring <a href="/wiki/Ben_Kingsley" title="Ben Kingsley">Ben Kingsley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jim_Sturgess" title="Jim Sturgess">Jim Sturgess</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Zegers" title="Kevin Zegers">Kevin Zegers</a> had its world premiere as a Gala Presentation at the <a href="/wiki/Toronto_International_Film_Festival" title="Toronto International Film Festival">Toronto International Film Festival</a> in September 2008 and was released theatrically in the summer of 2009. Brightlight also produced <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Frankie_and_Alice" title="Frankie and Alice">Frankie &amp; Alice</a></i> starring <a href="/wiki/Halle_Berry" title="Halle Berry">Halle Berry</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Stellan_Skarsgard" title="Stellan Skarsgard">Stellan Skarsgard</a> and the feature film <i><a href="/wiki/Gunless" title="Gunless">Gunless</a></i>, a comedy western co-produced with <a href="/wiki/Niv_Fichman" title="Niv Fichman">Niv Fichman</a>'s Rhombus Media and distributed by <a href="/wiki/Alliance_Films" title="Alliance Films">Alliance Films</a> starring <a href="/wiki/Paul_Gross" title="Paul Gross">Paul Gross</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sienna_Guillory" title="Sienna Guillory">Sienna Guillory</a> and <a href="/wiki/Graham_Greene_(actor)" title="Graham Greene (actor)">Graham Greene</a>.</p>
