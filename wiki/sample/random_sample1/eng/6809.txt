<p><b>Brandy Lee Ledford</b> (born February 4, 1969) is an American actress, model and <i><a href="/wiki/Penthouse_(magazine)" title="Penthouse (magazine)">Penthouse</a></i> magazine's 1992 "Pet of the Year". She played the role of Desiree on <i><a href="/wiki/Modern_Family" title="Modern Family">Modern Family</a></i>, starred as Dawn Masterton on <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Baywatch_Hawaii" title="Baywatch Hawaii">Baywatch Hawaii</a></i>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Doyle_(Andromeda)" title="Doyle (Andromeda)">Doyle</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Science_fiction" title="Science fiction">science fiction</a> TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Andromeda_(TV_series)" title="Andromeda (TV series)">Andromeda</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Brandy_Ledford&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In addition to <i>Andromeda</i> and <i>Baywatch Hawaii</i>, Ledford also starred in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sci_Fi_Channel_(United_States)" title="Sci Fi Channel (United States)">SciFi Channel</a> series <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Invisible_Man_(2000_series)" title="The Invisible Man (2000 series)">The Invisible Man</a></i> and the critically acclaimed <i><a href="/wiki/Whistler_(TV_series)" title="Whistler (TV series)">Whistler</a></i>, for which she was nominated for two Best Actress awards, at the <a href="/wiki/Leo_Awards" title="Leo Awards">Leo Awards</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gemini_Awards" title="Gemini Awards">Gemini Awards</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Brandy_Ledford&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ledford was born in Denver, Colorado. In the early 1990s, she was married to snowboarder Damian Sanders, and appears in <i>Critical Condition</i> and <i>Snowboarders in Exile</i> as herself. She was also married to actor <a href="/wiki/Martin_Cummins" title="Martin Cummins">Martin Cummins</a>, with whom she shares a child.</p>
<p>Ledford is a devout, non-denominational <a href="/wiki/Christian" title="Christian">Christian</a> who believes in acceptance, tolerance and love for all.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
