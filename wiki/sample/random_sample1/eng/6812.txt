<p><b>Brunswick</b> (<span class="nowrap"><span class="IPA nopopups"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_English" title="Help:IPA for English">/<span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="/ˈ/ primary stress follows">ˈ</span><span title="'b' in 'buy'">b</span><span title="'r' in 'rye'">r</span><span title="/ʌ/ short 'u' in 'bud'">ʌ</span><span title="'n' in 'no'">n</span><span title="'z' in 'Zion'">z</span><span title="'w' in 'wind'">w</span><span title="/ɪ/ or /ə/ 'e' in 'roses'">ᵻ</span><span title="'k' in 'kind'">k</span></span>/</a></span></span> or <span class="nowrap"><span class="IPA nopopups"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_English" title="Help:IPA for English">/<span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="/ˈ/ primary stress follows">ˈ</span><span title="'b' in 'buy'">b</span><span title="'r' in 'rye'">r</span><span title="/ʌ/ short 'u' in 'bud'">ʌ</span><span title="'n' in 'no'">n</span><span title="'s' in 'sigh'">s</span><span title="'w' in 'wind'">w</span><span title="/ɪ/ or /ə/ 'e' in 'roses'">ᵻ</span><span title="'k' in 'kind'">k</span></span>/</a></span></span>) is the largest city in <a href="/wiki/Medina_County,_Ohio" title="Medina County, Ohio">Medina County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ohio" title="Ohio">Ohio</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>. The population was 34,255 at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/United_States_Census,_2010" title="United States Census, 2010">2010 census</a>. It is part of the Cleveland Metropolitan Area.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Brunswick,_Ohio&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Brunswick was laid out in 1815, and named randomly in a naming contest.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<p>On June 23, 2014 a tornado hit Brunswick, and the tornado was rated a high-end EF1 or EF2.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Brunswick,_Ohio&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Brunswick is located at <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Brunswick%2C_Ohio&amp;params=41_14_39_N_81_49_42_W_type:city"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">41°14′39″N</span> <span class="longitude">81°49′42″W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">41.24417°N 81.82833°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">41.24417; -81.82833</span></span></span></a></span> (41.244051, -81.828360).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR1_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-GR1-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p>According to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Census_Bureau" title="United States Census Bureau">United States Census Bureau</a>, the city has a total area of 12.96 square miles (33.57 km<sup>2</sup>), of which 12.92 square miles (33.46 km<sup>2</sup>) is land and 0.04 square miles (0.10 km<sup>2</sup>) is water.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Gazetteer_files_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-Gazetteer_files-4">[4]</a></sup></p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2010_census">2010 census</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Brunswick,_Ohio&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2010 census">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As of the <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FactFinder_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-FactFinder-6">[6]</a></sup> of 2010, there were 34,255 people, 12,967 households, and 9,565 families residing in the city. The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 2,651.3 inhabitants per square mile (1,023.7/km<sup>2</sup>). There were 13,600 housing units at an average density of 1,052.6 per square mile (406.4/km<sup>2</sup>). The racial makeup of the city was 95.5% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">White</a>, 1.2% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/African_American_(U.S._Census)" title="African American (U.S. Census)">African American</a>, 0.1% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Native_American_(U.S._Census)" title="Native American (U.S. Census)">Native American</a>, 1.2% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Asian_(U.S._Census)" title="Asian (U.S. Census)">Asian</a>, 0.6% from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Race_(U.S._Census)" title="Race (U.S. Census)">other races</a>, and 1.3% from two or more races. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)">Hispanic</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)">Latino</a> of any race were 2.3% of the population.</p>
<p>There were 12,967 households of which 36.2% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 58.3% were <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married couples</a> living together, 10.6% had a female householder with no husband present, 4.9% had a male householder with no wife present, and 26.2% were non-families. 21.9% of all households were made up of individuals and 7.5% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.63 and the average family size was 3.07.</p>
<p>The median age in the city was 39.1 years. 25.2% of residents were under the age of 18; 7.5% were between the ages of 18 and 24; 27% were from 25 to 44; 28.3% were from 45 to 64; and 11.9% were 65 years of age or older. The gender makeup of the city was 49.1% male and 50.9% female.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2000_census">2000 census</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Brunswick,_Ohio&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 2000 census">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As of the <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR2_8-2"><a href="#cite_note-GR2-8">[8]</a></sup> of 2000, there were 33,388 people, 11,883 households, and 9,280 families residing in the city. The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 2,662.3 people per square mile (1,028.0/km²). There were 12,251 housing units at an average density of 976.9/sq mi (377.2/km²). The racial makeup of the city was 97.09% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">White</a>, 0.74% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/African_American_(U.S._Census)" title="African American (U.S. Census)">African American</a>, 0.13% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Native_American_(U.S._Census)" title="Native American (U.S. Census)">Native American</a>, 0.86% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Asian_(U.S._Census)" title="Asian (U.S. Census)">Asian</a>, 0.02% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pacific_Islander_(U.S._Census)" title="Pacific Islander (U.S. Census)">Pacific Islander</a>, 0.38% from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Race_(United_States_Census)" title="Race (United States Census)">other races</a>, and 0.78% from two or more races. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)">Hispanic</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)">Latino</a> of any race were 1.36% of the population.</p>
<p>There were 11,883 households out of which 39.3% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 65.3% were <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married couples</a> living together, 9.3% had a female householder with no husband present, and 21.9% were non-families. 17.7% of all households were made up of individuals and 5.2% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.79 and the average family size was 3.18.</p>
<p>In the city the population was spread out with 27.7% under the age of 18, 8.1% from 18 to 24, 32.7% from 25 to 44, 23.2% from 45 to 64, and 8.2% who were 65 years of age or older. The median age was 35 years. For every 100 females there were 96.5 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 94.4 males.</p>
<p>The median income for a household in the city was $56,288, and the median income for a family was $62,080. Males had a median income of $42,675 versus $27,882 for females. The <a href="/wiki/Per_capita_income" title="Per capita income">per capita income</a> for the city was $21,937. About 3.2% of families and 4.6% of the population were below the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Poverty_line" title="Poverty line">poverty line</a>, including 5.7% of those under age 18 and 5.0% of those age 65 or over.</p>
