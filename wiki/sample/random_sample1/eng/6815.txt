<p><b>Brest</b> is a municipality in the <a href="/wiki/Stade_(district)" title="Stade (district)">district of Stade</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lower_Saxony" title="Lower Saxony">Lower Saxony</a>, <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>.</p>
<p>It belonged to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Archdiocese_of_Bremen" title="Archdiocese of Bremen">Prince-Archbishopric of Bremen</a>. In 1648 the Prince-Archbishopric was transformed into the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Bremen" title="Duchy of Bremen">Duchy of Bremen</a>, which was first ruled in <a href="/wiki/Personal_union" title="Personal union">personal union</a> by the Swedish and from 1715 on by the Hanoverian Crown. In 1823 the Duchy was abolished and its territory became part of the <a href="/wiki/Stade_(region)" title="Stade (region)">Stade Region</a>.</p>
