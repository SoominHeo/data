<p><b>Brendan Hines</b> (born December 28, 1976) is an American actor and singer-songwriter. He was raised in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Baltimore,_Maryland" title="Baltimore, Maryland">Baltimore, Maryland</a> where he graduated from <a href="/wiki/Loyola_Blakefield" title="Loyola Blakefield">Loyola Blakefield</a>. His mother was a former nun and his father a former priest who both left Brooklyn and the church to marry and teach philosophy. He has had a number of television roles, but is best known for his role as Eli Loker in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lie_to_Me_(TV_series)" title="Lie to Me (TV series)">Lie to Me</a></i>. He has also had leading roles in a handful of independent films.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Brendan_Hines&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Brendan Hines previously played <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_Terminator:_The_Sarah_Connor_Chronicles_minor_characters#Andy_Goode" title="List of Terminator: The Sarah Connor Chronicles minor characters">Andy Goode</a> in the <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Terminator_(series)" title="Terminator (series)">Terminator</a></i> television series, <i><a href="/wiki/Terminator:_The_Sarah_Connor_Chronicles" title="Terminator: The Sarah Connor Chronicles">Terminator: The Sarah Connor Chronicles</a></i>, Eli Loker on <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lie_to_Me_(TV_series)" title="Lie to Me (TV series)">Lie to Me</a></i> and Tyler Ford in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Middleman_(TV_series)" title="The Middleman (TV series)">The Middleman</a></i>. He starred in <i><a href="/wiki/Deep_in_the_Valley" title="Deep in the Valley">Deep in the Valley</a></i> in 2009. He was an early member of Bakerloo Theatre Project in upstate New York, playing Orsino in <i>Twelfth Night</i> in 2003. He currently plays the role of Logan Sanders in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Suits_(series)" title="Suits (series)">Suits</a></i>.</p>
<p>Hines writes and performs songs, solo and with a backing band, under the name "The Brendan Hines". In early 2008, he released an album of 10 original songs, "Good For You Know Who" and, in 2012, a 6-song EP entitled "Small Mistakes."</p>
