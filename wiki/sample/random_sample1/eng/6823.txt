<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Brett_Davern&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Brett Davern was born and raised in <a href="/wiki/Edmonds,_Washington" title="Edmonds, Washington">Edmonds, Washington</a>. His love of theatre and acting started at Madrona elementary school in Edmonds. He then attended <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Edmonds-Woodway_High_School" title="Edmonds-Woodway High School">Edmonds-Woodway High School</a>. During his Sophomore through Senior summers he would travel to <a href="/wiki/Stagedoor_Manor" title="Stagedoor Manor">Stagedoor Manor</a> Theatrical Training Center in upstate New York. After graduating high school in 2001 <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Davern enrolled at the <a href="/wiki/American_Musical_and_Dramatic_Academy" title="American Musical and Dramatic Academy">American Musical and Dramatic Academy</a> in New York City.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Brett_Davern&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>While living and auditioning in <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a> Brett landed the lead role in his first film as William in <i><a href="/wiki/Beautiful_Ohio" title="Beautiful Ohio">Beautiful Ohio</a></i>, starring alongside such accomplished actors as <a href="/wiki/William_Hurt" title="William Hurt">William Hurt</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rita_Wilson" title="Rita Wilson">Rita Wilson</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Julianna_Margulies" title="Julianna Margulies">Julianna Margulies</a> Brett moved to Los Angeles. He has guest starred on <i><a href="/wiki/CSI:_Miami" title="CSI: Miami">CSI: Miami</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Medium_(TV_series)" title="Medium (TV series)">Medium</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/NCIS_(TV_series)" title="NCIS (TV series)">NCIS</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/In_Plain_Sight" title="In Plain Sight">In Plain Sight</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Cold_Case" title="Cold Case">Cold Case</a></i>, among other shows.</p>
<p>Since 2011, he played <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Jake_Rosati&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Jake Rosati (page does not exist)">Jake Rosati</a> on <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Awkward." title="Awkward.">Awkward.</a></i>. The series has been praised as the voice of this generation and won a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/People%27s_Choice_Award" title="People's Choice Award">People's Choice Award</a> for favorite cable comedy in 2013.</p>
<p>Brett's has appeared in such films as <i><a href="/wiki/The_Pool_Boys" title="The Pool Boys">The Pool Boys</a></i>, <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Born_to_Race:_Fast_Track&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Born to Race: Fast Track (page does not exist)">Born to Race: Fast Track</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Love_and_Mercy" title="Love and Mercy">Love and Mercy</a></i> and <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Stanford_Prison_Experiment" title="Stanford Prison Experiment">Stanford Prison Experiment</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Brett_Davern&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In his free time Brett displays a real passion for racing cars and helping charities. In 2013 he competed in the <a href="/wiki/Toyota_Pro/Celebrity_Race" title="Toyota Pro/Celebrity Race">Toyota Pro/Celebrity Race</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Prix_of_Long_Beach" title="Grand Prix of Long Beach">Grand Prix of Long Beach</a>. The race raises funds for <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Racing_for_Kids&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Racing for Kids (page does not exist)">Racing for Kids</a>, an organization helping multiple children's hospitals in southern <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>. After qualifying in 5th position he managed to finish the race in 2nd place. In 2014 he competed again and won after qualifying on the pole with the fastest time.</p>
<p>Brett is passionate about helping charities including Racing for Kids, <a href="/wiki/Wounded_Warrior_Project" title="Wounded Warrior Project">Wounded Warrior Project</a>, <a href="/wiki/Boys_%26_Girls_Clubs_of_America" title="Boys &amp; Girls Clubs of America">Boys &amp; Girls Clubs of America</a>, Stars for Smiles, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ronald_McDonald_House" title="Ronald McDonald House">Ronald McDonald House</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
