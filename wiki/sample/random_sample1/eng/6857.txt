<p><i><b>Brida</b></i> is a novel by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Brazilian_people" title="Brazilian people">Brazilian</a> author <a href="/wiki/Paulo_Coelho" title="Paulo Coelho">Paulo Coelho</a>. It is the story of a beautiful young <a href="/wiki/Irish_people" title="Irish people">Irish</a> girl and her quest for knowledge. She finds a hermit <a href="/wiki/Magician_(paranormal)" title="Magician (paranormal)">mage</a> who teaches her to overcome fear and a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Witch" title="Witch">witch</a> who teaches her how to dance to the hidden music of the world. They see in her a gift, but must let her make her own voyage of discovery. As Brida seeks her destiny, she struggles to find a balance between her relationships and her desire to transform herself. The story is neatly woven around marrying the ancient art of witchcraft to contemporary life.</p>
