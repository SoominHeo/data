<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bluff&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Beach_ridge" title="Beach ridge">Beach ridge</a>, a high, steep <a href="/wiki/Bank_(geography)" title="Bank (geography)">bank</a>, as by a river or the sea, or beside a ravine or plain; a <a href="/wiki/Cliff" title="Cliff">cliff</a> with a broad face.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="United_States">United States</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bluff&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: United States">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Bluff_(Atlanta)" title="The Bluff (Atlanta)">The Bluff (Atlanta)</a>, a westside neighborhood of Atlanta, Georgia</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bluff,_Kentucky" title="Bluff, Kentucky">Bluff, Kentucky</a>, an unincorporated community</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bluff_(Pittsburgh)" title="Bluff (Pittsburgh)">Bluff (Pittsburgh)</a>, Pennsylvania, a neighborhood also known as Uptown</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bluff,_Texas" title="Bluff, Texas">Bluff, Texas</a>, an unincorporated community</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bluff,_Utah" title="Bluff, Utah">Bluff, Utah</a>, a census-designated place (CDP)</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Other_countries">Other countries</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bluff&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Other countries">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bluff,_Queensland" title="Bluff, Queensland">Bluff, Queensland</a>, a town in Queensland</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/The_Bluff_(Cayman_Islands)" title="The Bluff (Cayman Islands)">The Bluff (Cayman Islands)</a>, the highest point of the Cayman Islands, located on the island of Cayman Brac</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bluff,_New_Zealand" title="Bluff, New Zealand">Bluff, New Zealand</a>, a town and seaport in the South Island</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bluff,_KwaZulu-Natal" title="Bluff, KwaZulu-Natal">Bluff, KwaZulu-Natal</a>, a geographical region of Durban in KwaZulu-Natal</li>
<li>The informal name of <a href="/wiki/Rosetta_Head" title="Rosetta Head">Rosetta Head</a>, a headland adjoining Victor Harbor in South Australia.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Extra-planetary">Extra-planetary</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bluff&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Extra-planetary">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Bluff, a <a href="/wiki/List_of_craters_on_Mars" title="List of craters on Mars">crater on Mars</a> named after the New Zealand town.</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Game-oriented">Game-oriented</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bluff&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Game-oriented">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Fluid_mechanics">Fluid mechanics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bluff&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Fluid mechanics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Drag_coefficient#Bluff_and_streamlined_body_flows" title="Drag coefficient">Bluff body</a>: name given to an immersed body when the drag force created by the flow is dominated by pressure drag. When drag is dominated by its frictional component, the body is called a streamlined body.</li>
</ul>
