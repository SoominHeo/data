<p><b>Blady</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">블레이디</span>) is a five-member South Korean <a href="/wiki/Girl_group" title="Girl group">girl group</a> originally formed by SY6 Entertainment and currently produced by Star Planet. The group debuted on May 16, 2011 with a retro-inspired single "찌릿찌릿 (Spark Spark).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Current members consist of Dayeong, Giru, Gabin, Yeeun and Tina.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Blady&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2011:_Debut.2C_.22Spark_Spark.22.2C_and_.22Crazy_Day.22">2011: Debut, "Spark Spark", and "Crazy Day"</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Blady&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title='Edit section: 2011: Debut, "Spark Spark", and "Crazy Day"'>edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In 2011, Blady made its debuted originally with four members: Darae, Bunhong, Sunyeong and Kangyoon. The group released their debut song, the retro inspired<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> single "찌릿찌릿 (Spark Spark)", on May 16, 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> As early rookies they received praises from hit composer <a href="/wiki/Shinsadong_Tiger" title="Shinsadong Tiger">Shinsadong Tiger</a>, who's known for producing for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/B2ST" title="B2ST">B2ST</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/4minute" title="4minute">4minute</a>, as he saw in their training process that their "passion and skills" stood above many other girl groups debuting at the time. He went on to say, "In anticipation for their debut, the members have been sleeping three hours a night in order to devote everything else to their rehearsals." <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>A follow up single titled "미친 날 (Crazy Day)" was released on July 27, 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> The music video for "Crazy Day" was shot in <a href="/wiki/3D_film" title="3D film">3D</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Maldives" title="Maldives">Maldives</a> by famous director <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kang_Young_Man&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kang Young Man (page does not exist)">Kang Young Man</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-allkpop_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-allkpop-6">[6]</a></sup> The clip also featured dance moves choreographed by Main Spirit who has also worked with <a href="/wiki/Lee_Hyori" title="Lee Hyori">Lee Hyori</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bada_(singer)" title="Bada (singer)">Bada</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>At the end of their debuting year, the girls took home the 2011 19th Republic of Korea Culture Entertainment Awards Idol Music Rookie of the Year award.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-star.fnnews_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-star.fnnews-8">[8]</a></sup> Also, SY6 added a new member, Nahyun.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2013_-_2014:_Line-up_changes.2C_.22Blood_Type_B_Girl.22.2C_and_Blady_Soul">2013 - 2014: Line-up changes, "Blood Type B Girl", and <i>Blady Soul</i></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Blady&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title='Edit section: 2013 - 2014: Line-up changes, "Blood Type B Girl", and Blady Soul'>edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Despite rumors of disbandment in 2012 and early 2013, Blady released a single "B형 여자 (Blood Type B Girl)" on November 18, 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> The group returned with five members, Kangyoon being the only first generation member to return. Thus, she was promoted to leader status in the group. This comeback saw the addition of new members Coco, Tina, Yeji and Soojin.</p>
<p>The single release of "B형 여자 (Blood Type B Girl)" also saw a new remixed version of "미친 날 (Crazy Day)" titled "Bring Bring" with a stronger hip hop and rock flavor than its predecessor. "B형 여자 (Blood Type B Girl)" is a collaboration between renowned producer C-Luv (who has produced for <a href="/wiki/Rain_(entertainer)" title="Rain (entertainer)">Rain</a>, <a href="/wiki/MBLAQ" title="MBLAQ">MBLAQ</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kim_Hyun_Joong" title="Kim Hyun Joong">Kim Hyun Joong</a>) and songwriter Stay Tuned. The song features a funky urban electronic beat and draws inspiration from the fact that all the members of Blady have blood type B.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p><i>Blady Soul</i> is the first special unit to come from Blady, consisting of members Yeji and Soojin. The duo released a digital single, "떠나가지마 (Please Don't Leave Me)", to commemorate <a href="/wiki/White_Day" title="White Day">White Day</a>. The song garnered attention for its smooth R&amp;B style in contrast to Blady's normal bubblegum-pop style.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
<p>In late 2014, it was revealed that members Coco, Kangyoon, and Yeji had left the group.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2015_-_Present:_Line-up_changes.2C_Renovation.2C_and_.22Secret_Number.22">2015 - Present: Line-up changes, <i>Renovation</i>, and "Secret Number"</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Blady&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title='Edit section: 2015 - Present: Line-up changes, Renovation, and "Secret Number"'>edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In March 2015, it was revealed that member Soojin had also left Blady. Not long after, Star Planet announced that the group would be making another comeback as a five-member group. Along with Tina, there would be four new members, Giru, Dayeong, Yeeun, and Gabin, added to Blady's lineup.</p>
<p>A showcase on March 9, 2015 was held in Seoul to kick off promotions for their mini album 'Renovation'. On March 30, 2015 Blady released their music video for "Come to Me", the title track for Renovation, featuring the new lineup.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<p>On July 27, 2015, Blady released a special summer single entitled "Secret Number(비밀열쇠)". The first version of the music video was released on July 27, 2015 on both Blady's and 1THEK's official YouTube channels, and the second version was released on October 19 of the same year. Blady began promotions for "Secret Number(비밀열쇠)" the following week the song was released.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
