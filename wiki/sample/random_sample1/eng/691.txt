<p><b>July 4</b> is the 185th day of the year (186th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 180 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Monday, Wednesday or Saturday (58 in 400 years each) than on Thursday or Friday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Tuesday or Sunday (56). The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Aphelion" title="Aphelion">Aphelion</a>, the point in the year when the Earth is farthest from the Sun, occurs around this date.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=July_4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/362_BC" title="362 BC">362 BC</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Mantinea_(362_BC)" title="Battle of Mantinea (362 BC)">Battle of Mantinea</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Thebes,_Greece" title="Thebes, Greece">Thebans</a>, led by <a href="/wiki/Epaminondas" title="Epaminondas">Epaminondas</a>, defeated the <a href="/wiki/Sparta" title="Sparta">Spartans</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/414" title="414">414</a> – Emperor <a href="/wiki/Theodosius_II" title="Theodosius II">Theodosius II</a>, age 13, yields power to his older sister <a href="/wiki/Pulcheria" title="Pulcheria">Aelia Pulcheria</a>, who reigned as <a href="/wiki/Regent" title="Regent">regent</a> and proclaimed herself empress (<i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Augusta_(honorific)" title="Augusta (honorific)">Augusta</a></i>) of the <a href="/wiki/Byzantine_Empire" title="Byzantine Empire">Eastern Roman Empire</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/836" title="836">836</a> – <i><a href="/wiki/Pactum_Sicardi" title="Pactum Sicardi">Pactum Sicardi</a></i>, a peace treaty between the <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Benevento" title="Duchy of Benevento">Principality of Benevento</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Naples" title="Duchy of Naples">Duchy of Naples</a>, is signed.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/993" title="993">993</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ulrich_of_Augsburg" title="Ulrich of Augsburg">Ulrich of Augsburg</a> is <a href="/wiki/Canonization" title="Canonization">canonized</a> as a saint.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1054" title="1054">1054</a> – A supernova, called <a href="/wiki/SN_1054" title="SN 1054">SN 1054</a>, is seen by Chinese <a href="/wiki/Song_dynasty" title="Song dynasty">Song dynasty</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arab" title="Arab">Arab</a>, and possibly <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Indigenous_Peoples_of_the_Americas" title="Indigenous Peoples of the Americas">Amerindian</a> observers near the star <a href="/wiki/Zeta_Tauri" title="Zeta Tauri">Zeta Tauri</a>. For several months it remains bright enough to be seen during the day. Its remnants form the <a href="/wiki/Crab_Nebula" title="Crab Nebula">Crab Nebula</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1120" title="1120">1120</a> – <a href="/wiki/Jordan_II_of_Capua" title="Jordan II of Capua">Jordan II of Capua</a> is anointed as prince after his infant nephew's death.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1187" title="1187">1187</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Crusades" title="Crusades">Crusades</a>: <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Hattin" title="Battle of Hattin">Battle of Hattin</a>: <a href="/wiki/Saladin" title="Saladin">Saladin</a> defeats <a href="/wiki/Guy_of_Lusignan" title="Guy of Lusignan">Guy of Lusignan</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kings_of_Jerusalem" title="Kings of Jerusalem">King of Jerusalem</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1253" title="1253">1253</a> – Battle of West-Capelle: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/John_I_of_Avesnes" title="John I of Avesnes">John I of Avesnes</a> defeats <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Guy_of_Dampierre" title="Guy of Dampierre">Guy of Dampierre</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1359" title="1359">1359</a> – <a href="/wiki/Francesco_II_Ordelaffi" title="Francesco II Ordelaffi">Francesco II Ordelaffi</a> of <a href="/wiki/Forl%C3%AC" title="Forlì">Forlì</a> surrenders to the Papal commander <a href="/wiki/Gil_%C3%81lvarez_Carrillo_de_Albornoz" title="Gil Álvarez Carrillo de Albornoz">Gil de Albornoz</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1456" title="1456">1456</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_wars_in_Europe" title="Ottoman wars in Europe">Ottoman wars in Europe</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Belgrade_(1456)" title="Siege of Belgrade (1456)">Siege of Nándorfehérvár (Belgrade)</a> begins.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1534" title="1534">1534</a> – <a href="/wiki/Election_of_Christian_III" title="Election of Christian III">Christian III is elected King of Denmark and Norway</a> in the town of <a href="/wiki/Old_Rye" title="Old Rye">Rye</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1584" title="1584">1584</a> – <a href="/wiki/Philip_Amadas" title="Philip Amadas">Philip Amadas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Arthur_Barlowe" title="Arthur Barlowe">Arthur Barlowe</a> arrive at <a href="/wiki/Roanoke_Island" title="Roanoke Island">Roanoke Island</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1610" title="1610">1610</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Klushino" title="Battle of Klushino">Battle of Klushino</a> is fought between forces of the <a href="/wiki/Polish%E2%80%93Lithuanian_Commonwealth" title="Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth">Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tsardom_of_Russia" title="Tsardom of Russia">Russia</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Polish%E2%80%93Muscovite_War_(1605%E2%80%9318)" title="Polish–Muscovite War (1605–18)">Polish–Muscovite War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1634" title="1634">1634</a> – The city of <a href="/wiki/Trois-Rivi%C3%A8res" title="Trois-Rivières">Trois-Rivières</a> is founded in <a href="/wiki/New_France" title="New France">New France</a> (now Quebec, Canada).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1744" title="1744">1744</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Lancaster" title="Treaty of Lancaster">Treaty of Lancaster</a>, in which the <a href="/wiki/Iroquois" title="Iroquois">Iroquois</a> cedes lands between the <a href="/wiki/Allegheny_Mountains" title="Allegheny Mountains">Allegheny Mountains</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Ohio_River" title="Ohio River">Ohio River</a> to the British colonies, was signed in <a href="/wiki/Lancaster,_Pennsylvania" title="Lancaster, Pennsylvania">Lancaster, Pennsylvania</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1774" title="1774">1774</a> – <a href="/wiki/Orangetown_Resolutions" title="Orangetown Resolutions">Orangetown Resolutions</a> are adopted in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_New_York" title="Province of New York">Province of New York</a>, one of many protests against the <a href="/wiki/Parliament_of_Great_Britain" title="Parliament of Great Britain">British Parliament's</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Coercive_Acts" title="Coercive Acts">Coercive Acts</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1776" title="1776">1776</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Revolution" title="American Revolution">American Revolution</a>: The <a href="/wiki/United_States_Declaration_of_Independence" title="United States Declaration of Independence">United States Declaration of Independence</a> is adopted by the <a href="/wiki/Second_Continental_Congress" title="Second Continental Congress">Second Continental Congress</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1778" title="1778">1778</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Revolutionary_War" title="American Revolutionary War">American Revolutionary War</a>: American forces under <a href="/wiki/George_Rogers_Clark" title="George Rogers Clark">George Clark</a> capture <a href="/wiki/Kaskaskia,_Illinois" title="Kaskaskia, Illinois">Kaskaskia</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Illinois_campaign" title="Illinois campaign">Illinois campaign</a>.</li>
