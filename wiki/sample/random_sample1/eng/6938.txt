<p><b>Birkirkara Football Club</b> is a football club based in the town of <a href="/wiki/Birkirkara" title="Birkirkara">Birkirkara</a>, the largest town on the island of <a href="/wiki/Malta" title="Malta">Malta</a>. The club was formed in 1950, following the amalgamation of Birkirkara United and Birkirkara Celtic. Birkirkara currently play in the <a href="/wiki/Maltese_Premier_League" title="Maltese Premier League">Maltese Premier League</a>, which it has won on four occasions, most recently in the <a href="/wiki/2012%E2%80%9313_Maltese_Premier_League" title="2012–13 Maltese Premier League">2012–13 season</a>. Birkirkara also has a <a href="/wiki/Futsal" title="Futsal">Futsal</a> team under the same name.</p>
<p>Birkirkara is one of the founding members of the <a href="/wiki/European_Club_Association" title="European Club Association">European Club Association</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
