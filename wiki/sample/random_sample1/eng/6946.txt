<p><b>Hyunjung "Vivian" Cha</b> (born November 5, 1993) is a South Korean fashion model and television personality.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> In August 2015, Cha made her debut in The 18th Magazine CéCi Model Contest.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Vivian_Cha&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Cha's modeling career began when she was twenty one years old after she was chosen as No.1 of the 18th Model Contest by Magazine <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=C%C3%A9Ci&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="CéCi (page does not exist)">CéCi</a>. She started her first modeling career with Magazine CéCI,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> SURE,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Cosmopolitan,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> and LEON.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> she has become the face of Barrel in 2016 with her cousin <a href="/wiki/Girls%27_Generation" title="Girls' Generation">Girls' Generation</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kwon_Yuri" title="Kwon Yuri">Kwon Yuri</a>. She was also a model for commercial film of <a href="/wiki/BMW" title="BMW">BMW</a> <a href="/wiki/Mini_(marque)" title="Mini (marque)">MINI</a> Korea .<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> She has appeared on the talk show <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Get_it_Beauty&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Get it Beauty (page does not exist)">Get it Beauty</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> and the reality show <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Attraction_TV&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Attraction TV (page does not exist)">Attraction TV</a></i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> on Channel <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Onstyle&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Onstyle (page does not exist)">Onstyle</a>.</p>
