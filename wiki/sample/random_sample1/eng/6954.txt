<p>This is the discography of the South Korean idol boy group <a href="/wiki/Beast_(South_Korean_band)" title="Beast (South Korean band)">Beast</a>. Beast have been in the music business ever since making their live debut on KBS Music Bank with their debut track, "Bad Girl" on October 16, 2009. They have released 2 studio albums and 9 mini-albums and have also participated in singing OSTs of various Korean dramas.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
