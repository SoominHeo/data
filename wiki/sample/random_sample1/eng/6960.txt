<p><b>Bianca Melina Elisabeth Wahlgren Ingrosso</b>, (born 30 December 1994) is a <a href="/wiki/Swedes" title="Swedes">Swedish</a> singer. She participated in the Swedish national selection for the <a href="/wiki/Junior_Eurovision_Song_Contest_2006" title="Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2006">Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2006</a> along with her friend Malin Eriksson.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Their song was called <i>Kan det bli vi två</i> and finished in second place. She has also sung and acted in the musical <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sound_of_Music" title="Sound of Music">Sound of Music</a> at <a href="/wiki/G%C3%B6ta_Lejon" title="Göta Lejon">Göta Lejon</a> in 2007 as the role Lovisa von Trapp. In 2008 she participated in the musical <i>Hujeda mig så många sånger</i>, along with her brother <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Benjamin_Wahlgren" title="Benjamin Wahlgren">Benjamin Wahlgren</a> and uncle <a href="/wiki/Linus_Wahlgren" title="Linus Wahlgren">Linus Wahlgren</a> was the director. She participated in the celebrity dancing show <a href="/wiki/Let%27s_Dance_2016" title="Let's Dance 2016">Let's Dance 2016</a> which was broadcast on <a href="/wiki/TV4_(Sweden)" title="TV4 (Sweden)">TV4</a>, she placed second in the final against <a href="/wiki/Elisa_Lindstr%C3%B6m" title="Elisa Lindström">Elisa Lindström</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>She is the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Pernilla_Wahlgren" title="Pernilla Wahlgren">Pernilla Wahlgren</a> and <a href="/wiki/Emilio_Ingrosso" title="Emilio Ingrosso">Emilio Ingrosso</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
