<p><b>Bianca Alexa Santos</b> (born July 26, 1990)<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (October 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> is an American actress. Santos is best known for her role as Lexi Rivera in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ABC_Family" title="ABC Family">ABC Family</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/The_Fosters_(2013_TV_series)" title="The Fosters (2013 TV series)">The Fosters</a></i>, for her role as Lucy Velez in the <a href="/wiki/MTV" title="MTV">MTV</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Happyland_(TV_series)" title="Happyland (TV series)">Happyland</a></i>, and for starring in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Duff" title="The Duff">The Duff</a></i> as Casey Cordero.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cast_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-cast-2">[2]</a></sup> Most recently she starred in the film <a href="/wiki/Priceless_(2016_film)" title="Priceless (2016 film)">Priceless</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bianca_A._Santos&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Santos was born in Santa Monica, California.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> She is of Cuban and Brazilian descent.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Santos is also fluent in Spanish and Portuguese.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>Santos attended <a href="/wiki/California_Lutheran_University" title="California Lutheran University">California Lutheran University</a> majoring in psychology and minoring in sociology.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (February 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> Shortly after graduating, she moved back home with her parents and took acting classes.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bianca_A._Santos&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In May 2013, it was announced that Santos would join the cast of the new TV series <i><a href="/wiki/The_Fosters_(2013_TV_series)" title="The Fosters (2013 TV series)">The Fosters</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cast_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-cast-2">[2]</a></sup> Santos co-starred in the 2014 horror film <i><a href="/wiki/Ouija_(2014_film)" title="Ouija (2014 film)">Ouija</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> Santos was also cast in the role of Lucy Velez on MTV's original show <i>Happyland</i>. In 2016, she rejoined the cast of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Fosters_(2013_TV_series)" title="The Fosters (2013 TV series)">The Fosters</a></i>.</p>
