<p><b>Villaverde</b> is one of the 21 <a href="/wiki/Districts_of_Madrid" title="Districts of Madrid">districts</a> of the city of <a href="/wiki/Madrid" title="Madrid">Madrid</a>, <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography_and_history">Geography and history</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Villaverde_(Madrid)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography and history">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The municipality was absorbed by <a href="/wiki/Madrid" title="Madrid">Madrid</a> in the 1950s as a result of the plans that the <a href="/wiki/Francisco_Franco" title="Francisco Franco">Franco</a> government made to simplify the structure of big city administrations. Since then, is a <a href="/wiki/Districts_of_Madrid" title="Districts of Madrid">district</a>. It was in those years when it experimented a massive growth caused by the <a href="/wiki/Rural_flight" title="Rural flight">rural flight</a> in Spain. This is the reason that made Villaverde a typical <a href="/wiki/Working_class" title="Working class">working class</a> neighbourhood.</p>
<p>This condition leaves a heavy footprint in the district, because it has conditioned the current population composition, with many retired people (some of them returning to their towns in <a href="/wiki/Andalusia" title="Andalusia">Andalusia</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Castile-Le%C3%B3n" title="Castile-León">Castile-León</a>, ...) and immigrants attracted by the housing cheap prices.</p>
<p>The district is administratively divided into five wards (<i><span lang="es" xml:lang="es">barrios</span></i>):</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Butarque" title="Butarque">Butarque</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Los_%C3%81ngeles_(Madrid)" title="Los Ángeles (Madrid)">Los Ángeles</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Los_Rosales_(Madrid)" title="Los Rosales (Madrid)">Los Rosales</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/San_Crist%C3%B3bal_de_los_%C3%81ngeles" title="San Cristóbal de los Ángeles">San Cristóbal de los Ángeles</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Villaverde_Alto" title="Villaverde Alto">Villaverde Alto</a> (or San Andrés)</li>
</ul>
