<p><b>Villahermosa</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Municipality" title="Municipality">municipality</a> located in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Ciudad_Real" title="Province of Ciudad Real">Province of Ciudad Real</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Castile-La_Mancha" title="Castile-La Mancha">Castile-La Mancha</a>, <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>. It has a population of 2,559.</p>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Lagunas_de_Ruidera" title="Lagunas de Ruidera">Lagunas de Ruidera</a> are located within this town's municipal term.</p>
