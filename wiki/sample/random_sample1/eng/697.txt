<p>Year <b>812</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">DCCCXII</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Thursday" title="Leap year starting on Thursday">leap year starting on Thursday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=812&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=812&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Byzantine_Empire">Byzantine Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=812&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Byzantine Empire">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_11" title="January 11">January 11</a> – Ex-emperor <a href="/wiki/Staurakios" title="Staurakios">Staurakios</a>, a son of <a href="/wiki/Nikephoros_I" title="Nikephoros I">Nikephoros I</a>, dies of <a href="/wiki/Putrefaction" title="Putrefaction">putrefaction</a> in his wounds (see <a href="/wiki/811" title="811">811</a>) in a <a href="/wiki/Monastery" title="Monastery">monastery</a>. He has reigned only two months and eight days, before being <a href="/wiki/Exile" title="Exile">exiled</a> by senior officials in <a href="/wiki/Constantinople" title="Constantinople">Constantinople</a>.</li>
<li>Emperor <a href="/wiki/Michael_I_Rangabe" title="Michael I Rangabe">Michael I</a> re-opens <a href="/wiki/Pax_Nicephori#War_and_peace.2C_806.E2.80.93814" title="Pax Nicephori">peace negotiations</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Franks" title="Franks">Franks</a>, and recognises <a href="/wiki/Charlemagne" title="Charlemagne">Charlemagne</a> as emperor (<i><a href="/wiki/Basileus" title="Basileus">basileus</a></i>) of the <a href="/wiki/Carolingian_Empire" title="Carolingian Empire">Frankish Empire</a>. In exchange for this recognition, <a href="/wiki/Venice" title="Venice">Venice</a> is returned to the <a href="/wiki/Byzantine_Empire" title="Byzantine Empire">Byzantine Empire</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Byzantine%E2%80%93Bulgarian_wars#Conflict_with_Michael_I_Rangabe" title="Byzantine–Bulgarian wars">Byzantine–Bulgarian War</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Bulgars" title="Bulgars">Bulgars</a>, led by <a href="/wiki/Krum" title="Krum">Krum</a>, ruler (<i><a href="/wiki/Khan_(title)" title="Khan (title)">khan</a></i>) of the <a href="/wiki/First_Bulgarian_Empire" title="First Bulgarian Empire">Bulgarian Empire</a>, launch an invasion against the Byzantines. They capture the fortress cities of <a href="/wiki/Debelt" title="Debelt">Develt</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nesebar" title="Nesebar">Mesembria</a>, near the <a href="/wiki/Black_Sea" title="Black Sea">Black Sea</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=812&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>Charlemagne conquers <a href="/wiki/Catalonia" title="Catalonia">Catalonia</a>, as far south as the <a href="/wiki/Ebro" title="Ebro">River Ebro</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Balearic_Islands" title="Balearic Islands">Balearic Islands</a>. The counties come under the rule of <a href="/wiki/Bera,_Count_of_Barcelona" title="Bera, Count of Barcelona">Bera</a>, count of <a href="/wiki/County_of_Barcelona" title="County of Barcelona">Barcelona</a>. He signs a three-year <a href="/wiki/Peace_treaty" title="Peace treaty">peace treaty</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Caliphate_of_C%C3%B3rdoba" title="Caliphate of Córdoba">Caliphate of Córdoba</a>.</li>
<li>Charlemagne issues the <a href="/wiki/Capitulare_de_villis" title="Capitulare de villis">Capitulare de villis</a>, concerning the rights of a <a href="/wiki/Feudalism" title="Feudalism">feudal</a> landholder and the services owed by his <a href="/wiki/Dependant" title="Dependant">dependents</a>. It contains also the names of some 89 <a href="/wiki/Plant" title="Plant">plants</a>, of which the most are used <a href="/wiki/Medicinal_plants" title="Medicinal plants">medically</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Amalfi" title="Duchy of Amalfi">Republic of Amalfi</a> sends <a href="/wiki/Galley" title="Galley">galleys</a> to support the Byzantine general (<i><a href="/wiki/Strategos#Byzantine_use" title="Strategos">strategos</a></i>) of <a href="/wiki/Sicily" title="Sicily">Sicily</a>, Gregorio, against the <a href="/wiki/Aghlabids" title="Aghlabids">Aghlabid</a> invaders. It is one of the earliest evidences of the independence of the city.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Britain">Britain</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=812&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Britain">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>King <a href="/wiki/Sigered_of_Essex" title="Sigered of Essex">Sigered of Essex</a> is reduced to the rank of <a href="/wiki/Duke" title="Duke">duke</a>, by his <a href="/wiki/Mercia" title="Mercia">Mercian</a> overlords.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Abbasid_Caliphate">Abbasid Caliphate</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=812&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Abbasid Caliphate">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Fourth_Fitna" title="Fourth Fitna">Fourth Fitna</a>: Forces loyal to <a href="/wiki/Al-Ma%27mun" title="Al-Ma'mun">al-Ma'mun</a>, led by <a href="/wiki/Tahir_ibn_Husayn" title="Tahir ibn Husayn">Tahir ibn Husayn</a>, blockade <a href="/wiki/Baghdad" title="Baghdad">Baghdad</a>, which is loyal to al-Ma'mun's brother, Caliph <a href="/wiki/Al-Amin" title="Al-Amin">al-Amin</a>, and begin the year-long <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Baghdad_(812%E2%80%93813)" title="Siege of Baghdad (812–813)">Siege of Baghdad</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="China">China</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=812&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: China">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">Chinese</a> government takes over the issuing of paper <a href="/wiki/Cheque" title="Cheque">bank drafts</a>, the ancestor of <a href="/wiki/Banknote" title="Banknote">paper money</a>.</li>
</ul>
