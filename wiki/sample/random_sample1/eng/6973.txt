<p><b>Lee Byung-yoon</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko">이병윤</span>; born June 15, 1993), better known by the stage name <b>BewhY</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko">비와이</span>), is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korean" title="South Korean">South Korean</a> rapper and member of '$exy $treet &amp; Yello Music crew'. Initially he chose 'BY' as a stage name, but changed it to 'BewhY' to give a meaning to the name.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> He made a debut with a mixtape 'Be The Livest' in 2012 and won first place in 2016 <a href="/wiki/Show_Me_the_Money_5" title="Show Me the Money 5">Show Me the Money 5</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Rapper 'C Jamm' is his old friend from high school and BewhY is also well known for devout Christianity.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
