<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Björgvin Björgvinsson</h1>

<div class="hatnote" role="note">This is an <a href="/wiki/Icelandic_name" title="Icelandic name">Icelandic name</a>. The last name is a <a href="/wiki/Patronymic" title="Patronymic">patronymic</a>, not a <a href="/wiki/Surname" title="Surname">family name</a>; this person is referred to by the given name <i>Björgvin</i>.</div>
<p><b>Björgvin Björgvinsson</b> (born 11 January 1980 in <a href="/wiki/Dalv%C3%ADk" title="Dalvík">Dalvík</a>) is an Icelandic alpine skier who competed for <a href="/wiki/Iceland_at_the_2006_Winter_Olympics" title="Iceland at the 2006 Winter Olympics">Iceland at the 2006 Winter Olympics</a>. He speaks Icelandic and German.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> He also competed for <a href="/wiki/Iceland_at_the_2010_Winter_Olympics" title="Iceland at the 2010 Winter Olympics">Iceland at the 2010 Winter Olympics</a> and was selected as his <a href="/wiki/2010_Winter_Olympics_national_flag_bearers" title="2010 Winter Olympics national flag bearers">nation's flag bearer at the opening ceremony</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
