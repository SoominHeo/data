<p><b>Björn Hübner</b> (born 2 January 1986) is a German sabre <a href="/wiki/Fencing" title="Fencing">fencer</a>. He competed in the <a href="/wiki/Fencing_at_the_2012_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_team_sabre" title="Fencing at the 2012 Summer Olympics – Men's team sabre">men's team sabre</a> competition at the <a href="/wiki/2012_Summer_Olympics" title="2012 Summer Olympics">2012 Summer Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sports-reference_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-sports-reference-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
