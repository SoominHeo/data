<p>Year <b>821</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">DCCCXXI</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Tuesday" title="Common year starting on Tuesday">common year starting on Tuesday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=821&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=821&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Byzantine_Empire">Byzantine Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=821&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Byzantine Empire">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Thomas_the_Slav" title="Thomas the Slav">Thomas the Slav</a>, Byzantine general (<i><a href="/wiki/Turma#Byzantine_Empire" title="Turma">tourmarchos</a></i>), leads a revolt and secures control over most of the Byzantine <a href="/wiki/Theme_(Byzantine_district)" title="Theme (Byzantine district)">themes</a> (provinces) in <a href="/wiki/Anatolia" title="Anatolia">Anatolia</a>. He gets recognition from the <a href="/wiki/Abbasid_Caliphate" title="Abbasid Caliphate">Abbasid Caliphate</a>, and concludes a <a href="/wiki/Peace_treaty" title="Peace treaty">peace treaty</a> with Caliph <a href="/wiki/Al-Ma%27mun" title="Al-Ma'mun">Al-Ma'mun</a>. Emperor <a href="/wiki/Michael_II" title="Michael II">Michael II</a> is blockaded in <a href="/wiki/Constantinople" title="Constantinople">Constantinople</a>; Thomas assembles troops, gathers supplies, and builds <a href="/wiki/Siege_engine" title="Siege engine">siege machines</a>, but his first attack on the capital fails. He crosses with his fleet from <a href="/wiki/Abydos_(Hellespont)" title="Abydos (Hellespont)">Abydos</a> to <a href="/wiki/Thrace" title="Thrace">Thrace</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=821&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>February – Duke <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Borna_of_Croatia" title="Borna of Croatia">Borna of Croatia</a> dies after an 11-year reign, as <a href="/wiki/Vassal" title="Vassal">vassal</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Francia" title="Francia">Frankish Empire</a>. He is succeeded by his nephew, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vladislav_of_Croatia" title="Vladislav of Croatia">Vladislav</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Mladjov_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Mladjov-1">[1]</a></sup> Emperor <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Louis_I_the_Pious" title="Louis I the Pious">Louis I</a> recognizes him as prince of <a href="/wiki/Dalmatia" title="Dalmatia">Dalmatia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Liburnia" title="Liburnia">Liburnia</a>, at the <a href="/wiki/Council_of_Aachen" title="Council of Aachen">Council of Aachen</a>.</li>
<li>October – <a href="/wiki/Lothair_I" title="Lothair I">Lothair I</a>, co-emperor and eldest son of Louis I, marries <a href="/wiki/Ermengarde_of_Tours" title="Ermengarde of Tours">Ermengarde</a> in <a href="/wiki/Thionville" title="Thionville">Thionville</a> (north-eastern <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>). She is the daughter of Count <a href="/wiki/Hugh_of_Tours" title="Hugh of Tours">Hugh of Tours</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Britain">Britain</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=821&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Britain">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>King <a href="/wiki/Coenwulf_of_Mercia" title="Coenwulf of Mercia">Coenwulf of Mercia</a> dies in <a href="/wiki/Basingwerk_Abbey" title="Basingwerk Abbey">Basingwerk</a> near <a href="/wiki/Holywell" title="Holywell">Holywell</a> (<a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Wales</a>), while preparing for another assault on <a href="/wiki/Powys" title="Powys">Powys</a>, and is buried in <a href="/wiki/Winchcombe_Abbey" title="Winchcombe Abbey">Winchcombe Abbey</a>. He is briefly succeeded by his son <a href="/wiki/Saint_Kenelm" title="Saint Kenelm">Cynehelm</a>, but he is killed, probably fighting the Welsh, though supposedly through the treachery of his sister <a href="/wiki/Cwenthryth" title="Cwenthryth">Cwenthryth</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Mercia" title="Mercia">Mercian</a> throne passes to Coenwulf's brother, <a href="/wiki/Ceolwulf_I_of_Mercia" title="Ceolwulf I of Mercia">Ceolwulf I</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Abbasid_Caliphate">Abbasid Caliphate</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=821&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Abbasid Caliphate">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Tahir_ibn_Husayn" title="Tahir ibn Husayn">Tahir ibn Husayn</a>, an <a href="/wiki/Iranian_peoples" title="Iranian peoples">Iranian</a> general, is appointed to govern <a href="/wiki/Greater_Khorasan" title="Greater Khorasan">Khurasan</a>, as a reward for supporting the <a href="/wiki/Abbasid_Caliphate" title="Abbasid Caliphate">Abbasid</a> caliph <a href="/wiki/Al-Ma%27mun" title="Al-Ma'mun">al-Ma'mun</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Fourth_Fitna" title="Fourth Fitna">Fourth Fitna</a>. This begins the rule of the <a href="/wiki/Tahirid_dynasty" title="Tahirid dynasty">Tahirid dynasty</a> over Khurasan, which will last until <a href="/wiki/873" title="873">873</a>.</li>
</ul>
