<p><b>Viqueque</b> (<a href="/wiki/Tetum_language" title="Tetum language">Tetum</a>: <span lang="tet" xml:lang="tet"><i>Vikeke</i></span>) is a city in the south-east of <a href="/wiki/East_Timor" title="East Timor">East Timor</a>, 183 km from <a href="/wiki/Dili" title="Dili">Dili</a>, the national capital. Viqueque is the capital of <a href="/wiki/Viqueque_District" title="Viqueque District">Viqueque District</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Viqueque_Subdistrict&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Viqueque Subdistrict (page does not exist)">Viqueque Subdistrict</a>, in the eastern part of the country with five subdistricts under its control, they are: Uatu-Lari, Uatu-Carbau, Viqueque, Lacluta and <a href="/wiki/Ossu_(East_Timor)" title="Ossu (East Timor)">Ossu</a>. The city has a population of 6,859 (2015), the subdistrict has 20,640 (2004), the district 65,245 inhabitants (2004).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
