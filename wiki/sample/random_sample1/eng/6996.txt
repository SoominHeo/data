<p><b>Victoria</b> "<b>Vicky</b>" <b>Pattison</b> (born 16 November 1987) is an English television personality, presenter, columnist, writer and model. best known for appearing in the <a href="/wiki/MTV_(UK_and_Ireland)" title="MTV (UK and Ireland)">MTV</a> show <i><a href="/wiki/Geordie_Shore" title="Geordie Shore">Geordie Shore</a></i> from 2011 until 2014, <i><a href="/wiki/Ex_on_the_Beach" title="Ex on the Beach">Ex on the Beach</a></i> in 2014 and 2015 and her own show <i><a href="/wiki/Judge_Geordie" title="Judge Geordie">Judge Geordie</a></i> in 2015.</p>
<p>Pattison won the <a href="/wiki/I%27m_a_Celebrity...Get_Me_Out_of_Here!_(UK_series_15)" title="I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here! (UK series 15)">fifteenth series</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/I%27m_a_Celebrity...Get_Me_Out_of_Here!_(UK_TV_series)" title="I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here! (UK TV series)">I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here!</a></i> in December 2015 and became a permanent member on the panel of the <a href="/wiki/ITV_(TV_network)" title="ITV (TV network)">ITV</a> daytime chat show <i><a href="/wiki/Loose_Women" title="Loose Women">Loose Women</a></i> in January 2016 after a period of guest appearances, before quitting in September of the same year.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> She is also a team captain on the <a href="/wiki/Channel_5_(UK)" title="Channel 5 (UK)">Channel 5</a> panel show <i><a href="/wiki/It%27s_Not_Me,_It%27s_You_(game_show)" title="It's Not Me, It's You (game show)">It's Not Me, It's You</a></i>. In September 2016 it was announced that she would co-present <i>I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here! NOW!</i> replacing <a href="/wiki/Laura_Whitmore" title="Laura Whitmore">Laura Whitmore</a>, it was also later announced that the shows title would be retitled as <i><a href="/wiki/I%27m_a_Celebrity...Get_Me_Out_of_Here!_(UK_TV_series)" title="I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here! (UK TV series)">I'm a Celebrity: Extra Camp</a></i> and that Pattison would be presenting alongside <a href="/wiki/Joe_Swash" title="Joe Swash">Joe Swash</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chris_Ramsey_(comedian)" title="Chris Ramsey (comedian)">Chris Ramsey</a> and <a href="/wiki/Stacey_Solomon" title="Stacey Solomon">Stacey Solomon</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Vicky_Pattison&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Before <i>Geordie Shore</i>, Pattison knew all her life she wanted to be famous, she had a passion for drama and English based subjects in school. Vicky had various jobs, including working on the doors of some of Newcastle's top clubs and also in telesales.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-mtv_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-mtv-4">[4]</a></sup> Pattison stated that she was on her last warning in the call centre she worked at and she was glad to leave. She told how she disliked her job with the call centre and <i>Geordie Shore</i> was the perfect opportunity for her to leave.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-mtv_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-mtv-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Media_career">Media career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Vicky_Pattison&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Media career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Geordie_Shore">Geordie Shore</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Vicky_Pattison&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Geordie Shore">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Geordie_Shore" title="Geordie Shore">Geordie Shore</a></div>
<p>Pattison appeared in the popular <a href="/wiki/MTV" title="MTV">MTV</a> show <i><a href="/wiki/Geordie_Shore" title="Geordie Shore">Geordie Shore</a></i>, from 2011 to 2014 in the first series of the show, she had a short relationship with fellow co-star Jay Gardner.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> After her rise to fame on the show, she was featured on the front cover of the magazine <i><a href="/wiki/Loaded_(magazine)" title="Loaded (magazine)">Loaded</a></i> in October 2011 alongside <i><a href="/wiki/The_Only_Way_Is_Essex" title="The Only Way Is Essex">The Only Way Is Essex</a></i> star <a href="/wiki/Jess_Wright" title="Jess Wright">Jess Wright</a> and <i><a href="/wiki/Made_in_Chelsea" title="Made in Chelsea">Made in Chelsea</a></i> star <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Binky_Felstead" title="Binky Felstead">Binky Felstead</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> Pattison has also featured in the magazine <i><a href="/wiki/Nuts_(magazine)" title="Nuts (magazine)">Nuts</a></i> with fellow <i>Geordie Shore</i> star <a href="/wiki/Holly_Hagan" title="Holly Hagan">Holly Hagan</a>. In the second series of the show, Pattison began a turbulent on/off relationship with new cast member <a href="/wiki/Ricci_Guarnaccio" title="Ricci Guarnaccio">Ricci Guarnaccio</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> Pattison and former cast member Rebecca Walker were shown not to get on with each other, leading to constant conflict in the <i>Geordie Shore</i> house.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> Pattison found herself in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cancun" title="Cancun">Cancun</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">Mexico</a> during the third series where during filming, Guarnaccio proposed to her.</p>
<p>In the fifth series, Pattison calls her engagement off with Guarnaccio and ultimately ends the relationship. Guarnaccio later decided to leave the show following the break-up.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> On 4 July 2013, Pattison and fellow cast member <a href="/wiki/Holly_Hagan" title="Holly Hagan">Holly Hagan</a> were arrested on suspicion of assaulting a model, during filming for the seventh series. Filming for the series was suspended due to the incident arising.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> In December 2013, she has released her own fitness DVD entitled <i>Vicky's 7 Day Slim</i>. Before the beginning of the ninth series, she announced that it would be her last, making her final appearance during the series finale.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Television_career">Television career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Vicky_Pattison&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Television career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In 2014 and 2015, Pattison starred in the <a href="/wiki/MTV" title="MTV">MTV</a> reality television show <i><a href="/wiki/Ex_on_the_Beach" title="Ex on the Beach">Ex on the Beach</a></i>. Pattison has appeared and made guest appearances on <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Big_Brother%27s_Bit_on_the_Side" title="Big Brother's Bit on the Side">Big Brother's Bit on the Side</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/This_Morning_(TV_programme)" title="This Morning (TV programme)">This Morning</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Safeword" title="Safeword">Safeword</a></i>, <i>Celebrity Dinner Date</i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Murder_in_Successville" title="Murder in Successville">Murder in Successville</a></i>, <i>Most Annoying People 2011</i>, <i>TV's 50 Greatest Magic Tricks</i>, <i>Most Shocking Celebrity Moments 2011</i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Virtually_Famous" title="Virtually Famous">Virtually Famous</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ant_and_Dec%27s_Saturday_Night_Takeaway" title="Ant and Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway">Ant and Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Celebrity_Juice" title="Celebrity Juice">Celebrity Juice</a></i> live special.</p>
<p>Starting from June 2015, Pattison presented and starred in her own MTV show, <i><a href="/wiki/Judge_Geordie" title="Judge Geordie">Judge Geordie</a></i>, in which she tries to sort out feuds and problems between families, friends and relationships.</p>
<p>Since mid-2015, Pattison has had her name on a weekly column in the magazine <i><a href="/wiki/New!" title="New!">new!</a></i>. Pattison released her autobiography entitled <i>Nothing But The Truth</i> in August 2014 and updated it in March 2016. She signed a deal to write two novels which were published in July (<i>All That Glitters</i>) and October 2015 (<i>A Christmas Kiss</i>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 2 July 2015, Pattison appeared on BBC Radio 1's Innuendo Bingo.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 16 November 2015, it was confirmed that Pattison would be taking part in the <a href="/wiki/I%27m_a_Celebrity...Get_Me_Out_of_Here!_(UK_series_15)" title="I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here! (UK series 15)">fifteenth series</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/I%27m_a_Celebrity...Get_Me_Out_of_Here!_(UK_TV_series)" title="I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here! (UK TV series)">I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here!</a></i>. She entered the jungle on Day 3 as a late arrival alongside <a href="/wiki/Ferne_McCann" title="Ferne McCann">Ferne McCann</a> and <a href="/wiki/Spencer_Matthews" title="Spencer Matthews">Spencer Matthews</a>. On 6 December 2015, Pattison won the show with <a href="/wiki/Union_J" title="Union J">Union J</a> member <a href="/wiki/George_Shelley_(singer)" title="George Shelley (singer)">George Shelley</a> in second place.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
<p>Pattison was appearing as a regular panellist on <a href="/wiki/ITV_(TV_channel)" title="ITV (TV channel)">ITV</a>'s lunchtime discussion show <i><a href="/wiki/Loose_Women" title="Loose Women">Loose Women</a></i> from January 2016 before quitting in September after just nine months.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup> She was the youngest panellist on the show. In January she also appeared in a 2-part <i>Geordie Shore</i> special speaking about her time on the show.</p>
<p>In June 2016, she became a team captain on the <a href="/wiki/Channel_5_(UK)" title="Channel 5 (UK)">Channel 5</a> panel show <i><a href="/wiki/It%27s_Not_Me,_It%27s_You_(game_show)" title="It's Not Me, It's You (game show)">It's Not Me, It's You</a></i>, hosted by <a href="/wiki/Eamonn_Holmes" title="Eamonn Holmes">Eamonn Holmes</a>. Also starting from September 2016, she has appeared in a new segment on <i>This Morning</i> regularly along with <a href="/wiki/Ferne_McCann" title="Ferne McCann">Ferne McCann</a> where they go on a road trip together. In September 2016 it was announced that she would co-present <i>I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here! NOW!</i> from November 2016 alongside <a href="/wiki/Joe_Swash" title="Joe Swash">Joe Swash</a> replacing <a href="/wiki/Laura_Whitmore" title="Laura Whitmore">Laura Whitmore</a>, however it was also later announced that the show would be retitled as <i><a href="/wiki/I%27m_a_Celebrity...Get_Me_Out_of_Here!_(UK_TV_series)" title="I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here! (UK TV series)">I'm a Celebrity: Extra Camp</a></i> and that <a href="/wiki/Chris_Ramsey_(comedian)" title="Chris Ramsey (comedian)">Chris Ramsey</a> and <a href="/wiki/Stacey_Solomon" title="Stacey Solomon">Stacey Solomon</a> would be presenting alongside Pattison and Swash.</p>
