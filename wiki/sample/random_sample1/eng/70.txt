<p><br/>
Year <b>1336</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MCCCXXXVI</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Monday" title="Leap year starting on Monday">leap year starting on Monday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1336&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>February 25 – 4,000 defenders of <a href="/wiki/Pil%C4%97nai" title="Pilėnai">Pilėnai</a> commit a mass suicide rather than be taken captive by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Teutonic_Knights" title="Teutonic Knights">Teutonic Knights</a></li>
<li>February 25 – The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kemmu_restoration" title="Kemmu restoration">Kemmu restoration</a> ends and the <a href="/wiki/Muromachi_period" title="Muromachi period">Muromachi period</a> begins in <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</li>
<li>April 26 – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ascent_of_Mount_Ventoux" title="Ascent of Mount Ventoux">Ascent of Mount Ventoux</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Italians" title="Italians">Italian</a> poet <a href="/wiki/Petrarch" title="Petrarch">Petrarch</a>: he claims to be the first since <a href="/wiki/Classical_antiquity" title="Classical antiquity">classical antiquity</a> to climb a mountain for the view.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li>July 4 – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Minatogawa" title="Battle of Minatogawa">Battle of Minatogawa</a>: <a href="/wiki/Ashikaga_Takauji" title="Ashikaga Takauji">Ashikaga Takauji</a> defeats Japanese Imperial forces under <a href="/wiki/Kusunoki_Masashige" title="Kusunoki Masashige">Kusunoki Masashige</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nitta_Yoshisada" title="Nitta Yoshisada">Nitta Yoshisada</a></li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Date_unknown">Date unknown</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1336&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Date unknown">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>The reign of <a href="/wiki/Emperor_K%C5%8Dmy%C5%8D" title="Emperor Kōmyō">Emperor Kōmyō</a>, second of the Northern Ashikaga Pretenders, begins.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vijayanagara_empire" title="Vijayanagara empire">Vijayanagara</a> is founded on the southern part of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Deccan" title="Deccan">Deccan</a> plateau in <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a>, by the brothers Harihara and Bukka Raya.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Aberdeen,_Scotland" title="Aberdeen, Scotland">Aberdeen</a> is burned by the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">English</a>.</li>
</ul>
