<p>Year <b>825</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">DCCCXXV</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Sunday" title="Common year starting on Sunday">common year starting on Sunday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=825&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=825&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=825&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>Emperor <a href="/wiki/Louis_the_Pious" title="Louis the Pious">Louis the Pious</a> begins a military campaign against the <a href="/wiki/Wends" title="Wends">Wends</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sorbs" title="Sorbs">Sorbs</a>. Duke <a href="/wiki/Tunglo" title="Tunglo">Tunglo</a> surrenders his son as <a href="/wiki/Hostage" title="Hostage">hostage</a>, and submits to <a href="/wiki/Francia" title="Francia">Frankish</a> rule (approximate date).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gr%C3%ADmur_Kamban" title="Grímur Kamban">Grímur Kamban</a> becomes the first man to set foot in the <a href="/wiki/Faroe_Islands" title="Faroe Islands">Faroe Islands</a>, and settles down in <a href="/wiki/Funningur" title="Funningur">Funningur</a>, on the northwest coast of <a href="/wiki/Eysturoy" title="Eysturoy">Eysturoy</a> (beginning the <a href="/wiki/Norway" title="Norway">Norwegian</a> <a href="/wiki/Viking_Age" title="Viking Age">Viking era</a> on the islands).</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Britain">Britain</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=825&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Britain">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Ellandun" title="Battle of Ellandun">Battle of Ellandun</a>: King <a href="/wiki/Egbert_of_Wessex" title="Egbert of Wessex">Egbert of Wessex</a> defeats <a href="/wiki/Beornwulf_of_Mercia" title="Beornwulf of Mercia">Beornwulf of Mercia</a> near <a href="/wiki/Swindon" title="Swindon">Swindon</a>. The battle marks the end of the <a href="/wiki/Mercian_Supremacy" title="Mercian Supremacy">Mercian domination</a> of southern <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Stenton_231_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Stenton_231-1">[1]</a></sup> The kingdoms of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Kent" title="Kingdom of Kent">Kent</a>, <a href="/wiki/Surrey" title="Surrey">Surrey</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Sussex" title="Kingdom of Sussex">Sussex</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Essex" title="Kingdom of Essex">Essex</a> submit to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Wessex" title="Kingdom of Wessex">Wessex</a>, and <a href="/wiki/East_Anglia" title="East Anglia">East Anglia</a> acknowledges Egbert as overlord (<i><a href="/wiki/Bretwalda" title="Bretwalda">bretwalda</a></i>).</li>
<li>King <a href="/wiki/Hywel_ap_Rhodri_Molwynog" title="Hywel ap Rhodri Molwynog">Hywel ap Rhodri</a> of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Gwynedd" title="Kingdom of Gwynedd">Gwynedd</a> dies after an 11-year reign. The kingdom is seized by his grand-nephew, <a href="/wiki/Merfyn_Frych" title="Merfyn Frych">Merfyn Frych</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Isle_of_man" title="Isle of man">Man</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gafulford" title="Gafulford">Battle of Gafulford</a>: The men of <a href="/wiki/Dumnonia" title="Dumnonia">Cornish Dumnonia</a> clash with the <a href="/wiki/Wessex" title="Wessex">West Saxons</a> at modern-day <a href="/wiki/Camelford" title="Camelford">Camelford</a> (approximate date).</li>
</ul>
