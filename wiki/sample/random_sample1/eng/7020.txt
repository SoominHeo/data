<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Fiction">Fiction</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Big_Shot&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Fiction">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/The_Big_Shot_(1931_film)" title="The Big Shot (1931 film)"><i>The Big Shot</i> (1931 film)</a>, a pre-code comedy directed by Ralph Murphy, starring Eddie Quillan and Maureen O'Sullivan</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/The_Big_Shot_(1937_film)" title="The Big Shot (1937 film)"><i>The Big Shot</i> (1937 film)</a>, a comedy directed by Edward Killy, starring Guy Kibbee and Cora Witherspoon</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Big_Shot_Comics" title="Big Shot Comics">Big Shot Comics</a>, a 1940s comic book series, an anthology title published by Columbia Comics</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/The_Big_Shot" title="The Big Shot">The Big Shot</a></i>, a 1942 American film noir crime drama directed by Lewis Seiler, starring Humphrey Bogart and Irene Manning</li>
<li>Big Shot, an animated superhero character on the television show created by cartoonist Ben Edlund in 1986 <a href="/wiki/List_of_minor_characters_in_The_Tick#Superheroes" title="List of minor characters in The Tick"><i>The Tick</i></a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Big_Shots_(film)" title="Big Shots (film)"><i>Big Shots</i> (film)</a>, a 1987 American comedy adventure film directed by Robert Mandel, starring Ricky Busker and Darius McCrary</li>
<li>Big Shot, an <a href="/wiki/List_of_Autobots#Micromasters" title="List of Autobots">Autobot Micromaster</a> action figure from the <i>Transformers</i> series released in 1989</li>
<li>Big Shot, former mascot of the <a href="/wiki/Philadelphia_76ers#Mascot" title="Philadelphia 76ers">Philadelphia 76ers#Mascot</a> until 1996</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/List_of_Cowboy_Bebop_characters#Punch_and_Judy" title="List of Cowboy Bebop characters"><i>Big Shot</i></a>, a fictional TV show in the 1998 anime Cowboy Bebop</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Big_Shots_(TV_series)" title="Big Shots (TV series)"><i>Big Shots</i> (TV series)</a>, a 2007-2008 US television drama series "...the story of four friends (CEOs) at the top of their game..."</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Big_Shots_(CSI)" title="Big Shots (CSI)">"Big Shots" (CSI)</a>, 19th episode of season 7 (2007) <i>CSI: Crime Scene Investigation</i></li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>"Big Shot", a song by the Bonzo Dog Doo-Dah Band from their 1967 album <i><a href="/wiki/Gorilla_(Bonzo_Dog_Doo-Dah_Band_album)" title="Gorilla (Bonzo Dog Doo-Dah Band album)">Gorilla</a></i></li>
