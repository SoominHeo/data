<p><b>Victor Shaka</b> (born May 1, 1975) is a <a href="/wiki/Nigeria" title="Nigeria">Nigerian</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">footballer</a> who plays as a forward. His previous club is include <a href="/wiki/Trabzonspor" title="Trabzonspor">Trabzonspor</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Happy_Valley_AA" title="Happy Valley AA">Happy Valley AA</a> in <a href="/wiki/Hong_Kong" title="Hong Kong">Hong Kong</a>. He also played for <a href="/wiki/FC_Seoul" title="FC Seoul">FC Seoul</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ulsan_Hyundai_FC" title="Ulsan Hyundai FC">Ulsan Hyundai Horangi</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Busan_I%27Park" title="Busan I'Park">Busan I'Cons</a> of the <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/K_League" title="K League">K League</a></p>
