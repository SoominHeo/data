<p><b>Victor Simões</b>, full name <b>Victor Simões de Oliveira</b> (born March 23, 1981, in <a href="/wiki/Rio_de_Janeiro" title="Rio de Janeiro">Rio de Janeiro</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazilian</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">footballer</a> who last played for <a href="/wiki/FC_Goa" title="FC Goa">FC Goa</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Indian_Super_League" title="Indian Super League">Indian Super League</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Victor_Sim%C3%B5es&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Victor began his career in 2001 at Flamengo, one of the most important clubs in Brazil. then he transferred to <a href="/wiki/Tombense_Futebol_Clube" title="Tombense Futebol Clube">Tombense</a>. After two years in Brazil, he was transferred to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Germinal_Beerschot" title="Germinal Beerschot">Germinal Beerschot</a>, a Belgian club.</p>
<p>After just one season, he joined <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Club_Brugge_K.V." title="Club Brugge K.V.">Club Brugge</a> in January 2004. He secured his position in the starting XI in Bruges, participating in 23 league matches and 5 matches in the European Cup. He won the Belgian Supercup 2004 and a year later the title of Belgian champion. But in the summer of 2005, Victor became confined to the bench as he did not receive the confidence of new coach <a href="/wiki/Jan_Ceulemans" title="Jan Ceulemans">Jan Ceulemans</a>.</p>
<p>By the end of the year, he is relegated to the B team, and then was loaned in January 2006 to his former club <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Beerschot_AC" title="Beerschot AC">Germinal Beerschot</a>. He did not make any appearance until the end of the season, and then returned to Brazil, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Figueirense_Futebol_Clube" title="Figueirense Futebol Clube">Figueirense</a>.</p>
<p>Victor played for around a year at his new club, then joined the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chunnam_Dragons" title="Chunnam Dragons">Chunnam Dragons</a> South Korean club in summer 2007. After his spell at the South Korean club, he returned to Brazil again, joining <a href="/wiki/Botafogo_de_Futebol_e_Regatas" title="Botafogo de Futebol e Regatas">Botafogo</a>, and winning <a href="/wiki/Ta%C3%A7a_Guanabara" title="Taça Guanabara">Taça Guanabara</a>.</p>
<p>He was then loaned in January 2010 to Saudi club <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Al-Ahli_Jeddah" title="Al-Ahli Jeddah">Al-Ahli Jeddah</a>, winning <a href="/wiki/2011_King_Cup_of_Champions" title="2011 King Cup of Champions">2011 King Cup of Champions</a> in final against <a href="/wiki/Ittihad_FC" title="Ittihad FC">Ittihad</a> after drawing 0–0, Victor scored the last penalty-kick in a 4–2 win. After his loan spell, Victor was transferred to Al-Ahli permanently. Victor was the joint top scorer along with <a href="/wiki/Nasser_Al-Shamrani" title="Nasser Al-Shamrani">Nasser</a> in <a href="/wiki/2011%E2%80%9312_Saudi_Professional_League" title="2011–12 Saudi Professional League">2011–12 SPL</a>, helping Al-Ahli finish second. Victor also won the <a href="/wiki/2012_King_Cup_of_Champions" title="2012 King Cup of Champions">2012 King Cup of Champions</a>, against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Al-Nasr" title="Al-Nasr">Al-Nasr</a> in a final that ended 4–1.</p>
<p>He joined <a href="/wiki/Umm_Salal_SC" title="Umm Salal SC">Umm Salal</a> in January 2014 after leaving <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Al-Ahli_(Jeddah)" title="Al-Ahli (Jeddah)">Al-Ahli</a>. but he was released from his contract at the end of the season .</p>
<p>On 19 June 2015, <a href="/wiki/FC_Goa" title="FC Goa">FC Goa</a> announced that Victor will be part of the first team squad of the <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">Indian</a> club for the <a href="/wiki/2015_Indian_Super_League_season" title="2015 Indian Super League season">2015 Indian Super League season</a>. Victor left Goa at the end of October without playing a game for the club, due to injury.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Dudu_to_replace_injured_Duffy_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Dudu_to_replace_injured_Duffy-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
