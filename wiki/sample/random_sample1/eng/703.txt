<p>Year <b>891</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">DCCCXCI</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Friday" title="Common year starting on Friday">common year starting on Friday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=891&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=891&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=891&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_21" title="February 21">February 21</a> – <a href="/wiki/Guy_III_of_Spoleto" title="Guy III of Spoleto">Guy III</a>, duke of <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Spoleto" title="Duchy of Spoleto">Spoleto</a>, is crowned <a href="/wiki/Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Holy Roman Emperor">Holy Roman Emperor</a> by <a href="/wiki/Pope_Stephen_V" title="Pope Stephen V">Pope Stephen V</a>. His son <a href="/wiki/Lambert_of_Italy" title="Lambert of Italy">Lambert</a> is proclaimed king of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Italy_(Holy_Roman_Empire)" title="Kingdom of Italy (Holy Roman Empire)">Italy</a>, at the capital of <a href="/wiki/Pavia" title="Pavia">Pavia</a> in <a href="/wiki/Lombardy" title="Lombardy">Lombardy</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li>Summer – <a href="/wiki/Ursus_of_Benevento" title="Ursus of Benevento">Orso</a>, Lombard prince of <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Benevento" title="Duchy of Benevento">Benevento</a>, is deposed after the capture of <a href="/wiki/Benevento" title="Benevento">Benevento</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Byzantine_Empire" title="Byzantine Empire">Byzantines</a>. Benevento becomes the capital of the <i><a href="/wiki/Theme_(Byzantine_district)" title="Theme (Byzantine district)">thema</a></i> of <a href="/wiki/Longobardia" title="Longobardia">Longobardia</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FOOTNOTEKreutz199663.E2.80.9366_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-FOOTNOTEKreutz199663.E2.80.9366-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Leuven_(891)" title="Battle of Leuven (891)">Battle of Leuven</a>: Viking raiders on the <a href="/wiki/Dyle_(river)" title="Dyle (river)">Dyle River</a> (near <a href="/wiki/Leuven" title="Leuven">Leuven</a>), in modern-day <a href="/wiki/Flanders" title="Flanders">Flanders</a>, suffer a crushing defeat by <a href="/wiki/Francia" title="Francia">Frankish</a> forces, under King <a href="/wiki/Arnulf_of_Carinthia" title="Arnulf of Carinthia">Arnulf of Carinthia</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Arabian_Empire">Arabian Empire</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=891&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Arabian Empire">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>Muslim forces led by <a href="/wiki/Abdullah_ibn_Muhammad_al-Umawi" title="Abdullah ibn Muhammad al-Umawi">Abdullah ibn Muhammad al-Umawi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Umayyad_Caliphate" title="Umayyad Caliphate">Umayyad</a> emir of <a href="/wiki/Emirate_of_C%C3%B3rdoba" title="Emirate of Córdoba">Córdoba</a>, defeat the rebel leader <a href="/wiki/Umar_ibn_Hafsun" title="Umar ibn Hafsun">Umar ibn Hafsun</a> at <a href="/wiki/Aguilar_de_la_Frontera" title="Aguilar de la Frontera">Poley</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Al-Andalus" title="Al-Andalus">Al-Andalus</a> (modern <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_2" title="June 2">June 2</a> – <a href="/wiki/Al-Muwaffaq" title="Al-Muwaffaq">Al-Muwaffaq</a>, an Abbasid prince, dies at the capital of <a href="/wiki/Baghdad" title="Baghdad">Baghdad</a>. His son <a href="/wiki/Al-Mu%27tadid" title="Al-Mu'tadid">Al-Mu'tadid</a> is recognized as <a href="/wiki/Regent" title="Regent">regent</a>, and second heir of the <a href="/wiki/Abbasid_Caliphate" title="Abbasid Caliphate">Abbasid Caliphate</a>.</li>
</ul>
