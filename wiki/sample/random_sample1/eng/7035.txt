<p>The <b>Vienna International Film Festival</b>, or <b>Viennale</b>, is a <a href="/wiki/Film_festival" title="Film festival">film festival</a> taking place every October since 1960 in <a href="/wiki/Vienna" title="Vienna">Vienna</a>, <a href="/wiki/Austria" title="Austria">Austria</a>. The average number of visitors is about 75,000. Traditional cinema venues are <i>Gartenbaukino</i>, <i>Urania</i>, <i>Metro-Kino</i>, <i>Filmmuseum</i> and <i>Stadtkino</i>. At the end of the festival, the <i>Vienna Film Prize</i> is awarded.</p>

<div class="thumbcaption">

The <i>Stadtkino</i> during the Viennale 2004</div>

<p>The festival features a collection of new films from all over the world, as well as national and international <a href="/wiki/Premiere" title="Premiere">premieres</a>. Apart from new feature films in various <a href="/wiki/Film_genre" title="Film genre">film genres</a>, the festival focuses on <a href="/wiki/Documentary_film" title="Documentary film">documentary films</a>, <a href="/wiki/Short_film" title="Short film">short films</a>, <a href="/wiki/Experimental_film" title="Experimental film">experimental films</a> and crossover productions. Together with the <i>Austrian Film Museum</i>, a historical <a href="/wiki/Retrospective" title="Retrospective">retrospective</a> is organized every year, as well as special programs, tributes and homages to international institutions and individuals.</p>
<p>During the festival, the <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/FIPRESCI" title="FIPRESCI">Fipresci</a> Prize</i> is awarded by international <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Film_critic" title="Film critic">film critics</a>. Another prize is awarded by the readers of the Austrian <a href="/wiki/Newspaper" title="Newspaper">newspaper</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Der_Standard" title="Der Standard">Der Standard</a></i>.</p>
<p>The program of the festival includes galas, special events and celebrations, as well as discussions and meetings between international guests and local visitors.</p>
<p>The Vienna International Film Festival is different from the <i>Film Festival Rathausplatz</i> in central Vienna, which exclusively shows music films daily throughout August.</p>
