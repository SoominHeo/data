<p><b>Bingo Yawata Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">備後八幡駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Bingo-Yawata-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/West_Japan_Railway_Company" title="West Japan Railway Company">JR West</a> <a href="/wiki/Geibi_Line" title="Geibi Line">Geibi Line</a> <a href="/wiki/Train_station" title="Train station">station</a> located in Suga, Tōjō-chō, <a href="/wiki/Sh%C5%8Dbara,_Hiroshima" title="Shōbara, Hiroshima">Shōbara</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hiroshima_Prefecture" title="Hiroshima Prefecture">Hiroshima Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>. Tickets are available at the nearby Suga Area Post Office.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bingo-Yawata_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>1935-12-20: Bingo Yawata Station opens as a station of the Sanshin Line between <a href="/wiki/T%C5%8Dj%C5%8D_Station_(Hiroshima)" title="Tōjō Station (Hiroshima)">Tōjō Station</a> and <a href="/wiki/Onuka_Station" title="Onuka Station">Onuka Station</a></li>
<li>1987-04-01: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Japan_National_Railways" title="Japan National Railways">Japan National Railways</a> is privatized, and Bingo Yawata Station becomes a JR West station</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_building.2C_platform_and_environs">Station building, platform and environs</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bingo-Yawata_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Station building, platform and environs">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Bingo Yawata Station features two platforms which can handle two lines at once. Platform 1 handles trains bound for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bingo_Ochiai_Station" title="Bingo Ochiai Station">Bingo Ochiai Station</a> and <a href="/wiki/Niimi_Station" title="Niimi Station">Niimi Station</a>. Platform 2 is not used due to unrepaired dangerous tracks.</p>
<p>The Suga Area Post Office is located near the station, and passenger tickets may be purchased there.</p>
<p>Two Hiroshima Prefectural highways are accessible from the station:</p>
<ul>
<li>Route 237 (Bingo Yawata Teishajō Route)</li>
<li>Route 450 (Utsubori Bingo Yawata Teishajō Route)</li>
</ul>
