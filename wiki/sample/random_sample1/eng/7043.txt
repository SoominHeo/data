<p><i><b>Binbir Gece</b></i> (English: One Thousand and One Nights) is a <a href="/wiki/Soap_opera" title="Soap opera">soap opera</a> revolving around two Turkish characters, Sehrazat and Onur. The story is loosely based on the story <a href="/wiki/One_Thousand_and_One_Nights" title="One Thousand and One Nights">One Thousand and One Nights</a>, better known as <i><a href="/wiki/Arabian_Nights_(1974_film)" title="Arabian Nights (1974 film)">Arabian Nights</a></i>. It stars <a href="/wiki/Halit_Ergen%C3%A7" title="Halit Ergenç">Halit Ergenç</a> as Onur, his wife <a href="/wiki/Berg%C3%BCzar_Korel" title="Bergüzar Korel">Bergüzar Korel</a> as Şehrazat (they married after the finale), <a href="/wiki/Tardu_Flordun" title="Tardu Flordun">Tardu Flordun</a> as Kerem and Ceyda Düvenci as Bennu.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Title">Title</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Binbir_Gece&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Title">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The title is the name of the story better known to the English - speaking world as <i>Arabian Nights</i>. The film's storyline is loosely based on the story, containing references thereto.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Synopsis">Synopsis</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Binbir_Gece&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Synopsis">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Şehrazat is an aspiring architect who is in desperate need for money to pay for the treatment of her son, Kaan, who is suffering from <a href="/wiki/Leukemia" title="Leukemia">leukemia</a>. After pleading to borrow money from several sources, she is stuck with her boss, Onur, who agrees to give her the money she needs for Kaan's treatment on the condition that she spends a night with him.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Reception">Reception</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Binbir_Gece&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Reception">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Binbir Gece was a hit in Chile, where its success resulted in many Chileans naming their babies Onur (boys) &amp; Sheherzadae (girls). It played in the United States, Argentina and Brazil. It was a hit in Russia in the Turkish Drama Category.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-izleyiciplatformu.com_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-izleyiciplatformu.com-5">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>The series became a primetime hit in Croatia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Montenegro and Macedonia, as well as in Romania, Albania and Greece.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-obg_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-obg-10">[9]</a></sup> It increased the popularity of Istanbul as a tourist destination among <a href="/wiki/Croatia" title="Croatia">Croatians</a> and led to a greater interest in learning Turkish.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[10]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Broadcast">Broadcast</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Binbir_Gece&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Broadcast">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The series originally aired from October 7, 2006 to May 12, 2009 on <a href="/wiki/Kanal_D" title="Kanal D">Kanal D</a>. It aired in more than 56 countries.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[11]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-izleyiciplatformu.com_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-izleyiciplatformu.com-5">[4]</a></sup> The series aired in Chile on <a href="/wiki/Mega_(Chilean_television_channel)" title="Mega (Chilean television channel)">Mega</a> from March 3, 2014 until January 4, 2015<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[12]</a></sup> and in Brazil on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Band_(channel)" title="Band (channel)">Band</a> from March 9, 2015 until September 15, 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[13]</a></sup></p>
