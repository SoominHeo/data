<p><b>Villeneuve-la-Garenne</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Communes_of_France" title="Communes of France">commune</a> in the northern suburbs of <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>, <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>. It is located 9.2 km (5.7 mi) from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kilometre_Zero" title="Kilometre Zero">center of Paris</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Hauts-de-Seine" title="Hauts-de-Seine">Hauts-de-Seine</a> <a href="/wiki/Departments_of_France" title="Departments of France">department</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%C3%8Ele-de-France_(region)" title="Île-de-France (region)">Île-de-France</a> <a href="/wiki/Regions_of_France" title="Regions of France">region</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Villeneuve-la-Garenne&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The commune of Villeneuve-la-Garenne was created on 9 April 1929 by detaching its territory from the commune of <a href="/wiki/Gennevilliers" title="Gennevilliers">Gennevilliers</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transport">Transport</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Villeneuve-la-Garenne&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Transport">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Villeneuve-la-Garenne is served by no station of the <a href="/wiki/Paris_M%C3%A9tro" title="Paris Métro">Paris Métro</a>, <a href="/wiki/R%C3%A9seau_Express_R%C3%A9gional" title="Réseau Express Régional">RER</a>, or suburban rail network. The closest station to Villeneuve-la-Garenne is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saint-Denis_(SNCF)" title="Saint-Denis (SNCF)">Saint-Denis</a> station, which is an interchange station on Paris <a href="/wiki/RER_D" title="RER D">RER line D</a> and on the <a href="/wiki/Transilien_Paris_%E2%80%93_Nord" title="Transilien Paris – Nord">Transilien Paris – Nord</a> suburban rail line. This station is located in the commune of <a href="/wiki/Saint-Denis,_Seine-Saint-Denis" title="Saint-Denis, Seine-Saint-Denis">Saint-Denis</a>, 0.9 km (0.56 mi) from the town center of Villeneuve-la-Garenne.</p>
<p>The town is well-served by the <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a> <a href="/wiki/Metropolitan_area" title="Metropolitan area">metropolitan</a> <a href="/wiki/Bus" title="Bus">bus</a> system : several lines run through its territory. (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/R%C3%A9gie_Autonome_des_Transports_Parisiens" title="Régie Autonome des Transports Parisiens">RATP</a> <a href="/wiki/Bus" title="Bus">bus</a> lines 137, 177, 178, 261 and 378).</p>
<p>Since 15 November 2012, the city is being crossed by a tram (tram 1)</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Villeneuve-la-Garenne&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>As of January 1, 2011 there were 25,644 residents.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Numbers_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Numbers-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>As of 2016<sup class="plainlinks noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Villeneuve-la-Garenne&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup> almost 37% of the residents are under 25 years of age.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Education">Education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Villeneuve-la-Garenne&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>As of the 2015-2016 school year the commune had 1,280 preschool students, 1,973 elementary students, and 1,978 secondary students.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Numbers_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Numbers-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Junior high schools include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Collège <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Edouard_Manet" title="Edouard Manet">Edouard Manet</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li>Collège <a href="/wiki/Georges_Pompidou" title="Georges Pompidou">Georges Pompidou</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<p>Senior high schools:</p>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lyc%C3%A9e_polyvalent_Charles_Petiet" title="Lycée polyvalent Charles Petiet">Lycée polyvalent Charles Petiet</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Lyc%C3%A9e_Michel_Ange&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Lycée Michel Ange (page does not exist)">Lycée Michel Ange</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<p>The public library is the <i>Bibliothèque Aimé-Césaire</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
