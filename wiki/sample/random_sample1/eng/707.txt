<p>Year <b>910</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">CMX</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Monday" title="Common year starting on Monday">common year starting on Monday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=910&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=910&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Africa">Africa</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=910&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Africa">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The caliphate of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ubayd_Allah_al-Mahdi_Billah" title="Ubayd Allah al-Mahdi Billah">Ubayd Allah al-Mahdi Billah</a> (of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fatimid" title="Fatimid">Fatimid</a> dynasty) is established, after he enters the former <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Aghlabid" title="Aghlabid">Aghlabid</a> capital of Raqqada, in modern <a href="/wiki/Tunisia" title="Tunisia">Tunisia</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=910&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_12" title="June 12">June 12</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Lechfeld_(910)" title="Battle of Lechfeld (910)">Battle of Augsburg</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Hungarians" title="Hungarians">Hungarians</a> defeat the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Suabian" title="Suabian">Suabian</a> army, led by the <a href="/wiki/East_Francia" title="East Francia">Eastern Frankish</a> king <a href="/wiki/Louis_the_Child" title="Louis the Child">Louis IV</a> (the Child), using the famous <a href="/wiki/Feigned_retreat" title="Feigned retreat">feigned retreat</a> tactic of the nomadic warriors.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Cont Gausbert, the actual commander of the German army (because Louis IV is only 16 years old at this time), is killed in the battle.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_22" title="June 22">June 22</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Rednitz" title="Battle of Rednitz">Battle of Rednitz</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Hungarians" title="Hungarians">Hungarians</a> defeat the <a href="/wiki/Franks" title="Franks">Frankish</a> army, also killing its leader <a href="/wiki/Gebhard,_Duke_of_Lorraine" title="Gebhard, Duke of Lorraine">Gebhard, Duke of Lorraine</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_5" title="August 5">August 5</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Tettenhall" title="Battle of Tettenhall">Battle of Tettenhall</a>: The allied forces of <a href="/wiki/Mercia" title="Mercia">Mercia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wessex" title="Wessex">Wessex</a> defeat an army of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Northumbria" title="Northumbria">Northumbrian</a> <a href="/wiki/Vikings" title="Vikings">Vikings</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_topic">By topic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=910&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: By topic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Religion">Religion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=910&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Religion">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Order_of_Saint_Benedict" title="Order of Saint Benedict">Benedictine</a> monastery of <a href="/wiki/Cluny_Abbey" title="Cluny Abbey">Cluny</a> is founded.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Pope_Gabriel_I_of_Alexandria" title="Pope Gabriel I of Alexandria">Gabriel I of Alexandria</a> becomes Pope of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Coptic_Orthodox_Church" title="Coptic Orthodox Church">Coptic Orthodox Church</a>.</li>
</ul>
