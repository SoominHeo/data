<p><i><b>Lights and Shadows</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">빛과 그림자</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Bit-gwa Geurimja</span></i>) is a 2012 <a href="/wiki/South_Korean" title="South Korean">South Korean</a> retro-drama series, starring <a href="/wiki/Ahn_Jae-wook" title="Ahn Jae-wook">Ahn Jae-wook</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nam_Sang-mi" title="Nam Sang-mi">Nam Sang-mi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lee_Pil-mo" title="Lee Pil-mo">Lee Pil-mo</a> and <a href="/wiki/Son_Dam-bi" title="Son Dam-bi">Son Dam-bi</a>. It aired on <a href="/wiki/Munhwa_Broadcasting_Corporation" title="Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation">MBC</a> from November 28, 2011 to July 3, 2012 on Mondays and Tuesdays at 21:55 for 64 episodes.</p>
<p>The series was originally planned for 50 episodes, but it was extended to 64 due to high ratings.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
