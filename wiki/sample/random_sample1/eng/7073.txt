<p>"<b>Hikari no Signal</b>" <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">光のシグナル</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/Single_(music)" title="Single (music)">single</a> by Japanese <a href="/wiki/Boy_band" title="Boy band">boy band</a> <a href="/wiki/Kis-My-Ft2" title="Kis-My-Ft2">Kis-My-Ft2</a>. Hikari no Signal was used as a theme song for the film <i><a href="/wiki/Doraemon:_New_Nobita%27s_Great_Demon%E2%80%94Peko_and_the_Exploration_Party_of_Five" title="Doraemon: New Nobita's Great Demon—Peko and the Exploration Party of Five">Doraemon: New Nobita's Great Demon—Peko and the Exploration Party of Five</a></i>. It was released on March 5, 2014. It debuted in <a href="/wiki/List_of_Oricon_number-one_singles_of_2014" title="List of Oricon number-one singles of 2014">number one</a> on the weekly <a href="/wiki/Oricon_Singles_Chart" title="Oricon Singles Chart">Oricon Singles Chart</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and reached <a href="/wiki/List_of_Hot_100_number-one_singles_of_2014_(Japan)" title="List of Hot 100 number-one singles of 2014 (Japan)">number one</a> on the Billboard <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Japan_Hot_100" title="Japan Hot 100">Japan Hot 100</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> It was the 5th best-selling single in Japan in March 2014, with 237,696 copies.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> It was the 26th best-selling single of 2014 in Japan, with 244,808 copies.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
