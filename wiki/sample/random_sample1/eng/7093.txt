<p><b>Sanada Nobuyuki</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">真田 信之</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, 1566 – November 12, 1658)</span> was a <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> <a href="/wiki/Samurai" title="Samurai">samurai</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Sengoku_period" title="Sengoku period">Sengoku period</a>. He was the son of <i><a href="/wiki/Daimy%C5%8D" title="Daimyō">daimyō</a></i> <a href="/wiki/Sanada_Masayuki" title="Sanada Masayuki">Sanada Masayuki</a> and the older brother of <a href="/wiki/Sanada_Yukimura" title="Sanada Yukimura">Sanada Yukimura</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_Life">Early Life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sanada_Nobuyuki&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early Life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was the first son of <a href="/wiki/Sanada_Masayuki" title="Sanada Masayuki">Sanada Masayuki</a> (1544–1611). His younger brother was <a href="/wiki/Sanada_Yukimura" title="Sanada Yukimura">Sanada Yukimura</a>. He was married to <a href="/wiki/Komatsuhime" title="Komatsuhime">Komatsuhime</a> (Ina), a adopted-daughter of <a href="/wiki/Tokugawa_Ieyasu" title="Tokugawa Ieyasu">Tokugawa Ieyasu</a>.</p>
<p>At an early age, Nobuyuki's father served under the <i><a href="/wiki/Daimy%C5%8D" title="Daimyō">daimyō</a></i> <a href="/wiki/Takeda_Shingen" title="Takeda Shingen">Takeda Shingen</a> and sent Nobuyuki as a hostage to prove the Sanada clan's loyalty to the <a href="/wiki/Takeda_clan" title="Takeda clan">Takeda clan</a>. After the Takeda clan was destroyed by joint <a href="/wiki/Oda_clan" title="Oda clan">Oda</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tokugawa_clan" title="Tokugawa clan">Tokugawa</a> army, Nobuyuki fled to Ueda Castle, the stronghold of the Sanada Clan and where his family were. In AD 1585, <a href="/wiki/Tokugawa_Ieyasu" title="Tokugawa Ieyasu">Tokugawa Ieyasu</a> attacked <a href="/wiki/Ueda_Castle" title="Ueda Castle">Ueda Castle</a>; Nobuyuki fought alongside his father and was victorious. Later, as Masayuki served under Toyotomi Hideyoshi, Nobuyuki was sent to Tokugawa to be a retainer in order to preserve the clan if anything wrong happened to the Toyotomi clan.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Battle_of_Sekigahara">Battle of Sekigahara</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sanada_Nobuyuki&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Battle of Sekigahara">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Sekigaharascreen.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="1796" data-file-width="3816" height="104" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9f/Sekigaharascreen.jpg/220px-Sekigaharascreen.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9f/Sekigaharascreen.jpg/330px-Sekigaharascreen.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9f/Sekigaharascreen.jpg/440px-Sekigaharascreen.jpg 2x" width="220"/></a>
<div class="thumbcaption">
<div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Sekigaharascreen.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>
<a href="/wiki/Edo_period" title="Edo period">Edo period</a> screen depicting the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Sekigahara" title="Battle of Sekigahara">Battle of Sekigahara</a>.</div>
</div>
</div>
<p>During the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Sekigahara" title="Battle of Sekigahara">Battle of Sekigahara</a>, he fought on the side of Tokugawa Ieyasu, against whom Masayuki and his brother, Yukimura were fighting. After the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Sekigahara#Fall_of_the_western_army" title="Battle of Sekigahara">Western Army was defeated by Ieyasu</a>, Nobuyuki used this position to save his father's and brother's lives. After the battle Masayuki's territory was seized and he and Yukimura were exiled to Kudoyama in Mt. Koya in the Kii Province. Ueda was given to Nobuyuki.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Siege_of_Osaka">Siege of Osaka</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sanada_Nobuyuki&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Siege of Osaka">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 1614 the relationship between the Tokugawa and Toyotomi heating up again. Ieyasu ordered 10,000 army to surround the Osaka Castle. Yukimura escaped from Kudo and serve Toyotomi to take the fight against the Tokugawa, Accepting the truth, Nobuyuki can't spare Yukimura again and his brother is killed in battle.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="After_the_battle">After the battle</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sanada_Nobuyuki&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: After the battle">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Tokugawa generally have a high regard of Nobuyuki. In 1622, he became the first lord of the Matsushiro clan and lived to 92 years of age.</p>
