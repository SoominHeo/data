<p><i><b>Quest for Saddam</b></i> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/First_person_shooter" title="First person shooter">first person shooter</a> <a href="/wiki/Video_game" title="Video game">video game</a> released by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Petrilla_Entertainment" title="Petrilla Entertainment">Petrilla Entertainment</a> in May 14, 2003. Fitting its genre, the goal is to fight Iraqi soldiers and eventually to kill the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Boss_(video_games)" title="Boss (video games)">boss</a>, <a href="/wiki/Saddam_Hussein" title="Saddam Hussein">Saddam Hussein</a>.</p>
