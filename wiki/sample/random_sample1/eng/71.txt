<p><br/>
Year <b>1355</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MCCCLV</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Thursday" title="Common year starting on Thursday">common year starting on Thursday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1355&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93December">January–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1355&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_7" title="January 7">January 7</a> – <a href="/wiki/Portugal" title="Portugal">Portuguese</a> king <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Alphonso_IV_of_Portugal" title="Alphonso IV of Portugal">Afonso IV</a> sends three men to kill <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ines_de_Castro" title="Ines de Castro">Ines de Castro</a>, beloved of his son <a href="/wiki/Peter_I_of_Portugal" title="Peter I of Portugal">prince Pedro</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pedro" title="Pedro">Pedro</a> revolts and incites a civil war.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_10" title="February 10">February 10</a> – The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/St._Scholastica_riot" title="St. Scholastica riot">St. Scholastica's Day riot</a> broke out in <a href="/wiki/Oxford" title="Oxford">Oxford</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a>, leaving 63 scholars and perhaps 30 locals dead in two days.</li>
<li>April – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Philip_II_of_Taranto" title="Philip II of Taranto">Philip II of Taranto</a> marries <a href="/wiki/Maria_of_Calabria" title="Maria of Calabria">Maria of Calabria</a>, daughter of <a href="/wiki/Charles,_Duke_of_Calabria" title="Charles, Duke of Calabria">Charles, Duke of Calabria</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Marie_of_Valois,_Duchess_of_Calabria" title="Marie of Valois, Duchess of Calabria">Mary of Valois</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_5" title="April 5">April 5</a> – <a href="/wiki/Charles_IV,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Charles IV, Holy Roman Emperor">Charles IV</a> is crowned emperor in <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_18" title="April 18">April 18</a> – In <a href="/wiki/Venice" title="Venice">Venice</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Council_of_Ten" title="Council of Ten">Council of Ten</a> beheads <a href="/wiki/Doge_of_Venice" title="Doge of Venice">Doge</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Marin_Falier" title="Marin Falier">Marin Falier</a> for conspiring to kill them.</li>
<li>August – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Nesbit_Moor_(1355)" title="Battle of Nesbit Moor (1355)">Battle of Nesbit Moor</a>: Scottish army decisively defeats the English.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_1" title="September 1">September 1</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tvrtko_I" title="Tvrtko I">Tvrtko I</a> writes <i>in castro nostro Vizoka vocatum</i> from old town <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Visoki" title="Visoki">Visoki</a>.</li>
</ul>

<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ottoman_Turks" title="Ottoman Turks">Ottoman Turks</a> defeat <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_Empire" title="Bulgarian Empire">Bulgarian Empire</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Ihtiman" title="Battle of Ihtiman">Battle of Ihtiman</a>.</li>
