<p><b>Sara Loren</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Urdu_language" title="Urdu language">Urdu</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ur" xml:lang="ur"><span class="Nastaliq" style="font-size: 125%; font-family: 'Jameel Noori Nastaleeq', 'Urdu Typesetting', 'Noto Nastaliq Urdu', 'Noto Nastaliq Urdu Draft', 'Hussaini Nastaleeq', 'AlQalam Taj Nastaleeq', IranNastaliq, 'Awami Nastaliq', 'Awami Nastaliq Beta4', 'Awami Nastaliq Beta3', 'Awami Nastaliq Beta2', 'Awami Nastaliq Beta1', 'Nafees Nastaleeq', 'Nafees Nastaleeq v1.01', 'Pak Nastaleeq', 'PDMS_Jauhar', 'Alvi Lahori Nastaleeq';" title="Nastaliq">ساره لورين</span></span>‎; born as <b>Mona Lizza Hussain</b>), is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pakistani" title="Pakistani">Pakistani</a> actress and model.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sara_Loren&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Loren made her film debut in the 2010 <a href="/wiki/Pooja_Bhatt" title="Pooja Bhatt">Pooja Bhatt</a>'s romantic thriller film <i><a href="/wiki/Kajraare" title="Kajraare">Kajraare</a></i> opposite <a href="/wiki/Himesh_Reshammiya" title="Himesh Reshammiya">Himesh Reshammiya</a> and later played seductress Nisha in the 2013 film <i><a href="/wiki/Murder_3" title="Murder 3">Murder 3</a></i>.</p>
<div class="thumb tright">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:SaraLoren.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="236" data-file-width="306" height="170" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f9/SaraLoren.jpg/220px-SaraLoren.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/f/f9/SaraLoren.jpg 1.5x" width="220"/></a>
<div class="thumbcaption">
<div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:SaraLoren.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>
Loren at Launch of <a href="/wiki/Manish_Arora" title="Manish Arora">Manish Arora</a>'s store.</div>
</div>
</div>
<p>The following year she starred in <a href="/wiki/Yasir_Nawaz" title="Yasir Nawaz">Yasir Nawaz</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Anjuman_(2013_film)" title="Anjuman (2013 film)">Anjuman</a></i> for which she won the <a href="/wiki/Tarang" title="Tarang">Tarang Housefull Awards—Best Actress in a Leading Role</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>Loren made her acting debut in 2003 with the serial <i>Rabia Zinda Rahegi</i>. She has also appeared in <i>Mahnoor, Meharun Nisa, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Makan_TV_Series" title="Makan TV Series">Makan</a>,</i> <i><a href="/wiki/Mehar_Bano_aur_Shah_Bano" title="Mehar Bano aur Shah Bano">Mehar Bano aur Shah Bano</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sandal" title="Sandal">Sandal</a>, <a href="/wiki/Riyasat" title="Riyasat">Riyasat</a></i>, <i>Help of a Ghost</i>, <i>Dupatta, Umrao jan-e-adaa</i> and was nominated for Best Drama Actress for her performance in <i>Main Mar Gai Shaukat Ali</i> at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2nd_Pakistan_Media_Awards" title="2nd Pakistan Media Awards">2nd Pakistan Media Awards</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>Loren acted in the stage performances of <i><a href="/wiki/Anarkali" title="Anarkali">Anarkali</a></i>, <i>Shahyad Issi Ka Naam Mohabbat Hai Sheiftaa</i>, in <a href="/wiki/Karachi" title="Karachi">Karachi</a> and <a href="/wiki/Delhi" title="Delhi">Delhi</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (March 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<p>Loren made her screen debut with the 2010 <a href="/wiki/Pooja_Bhatt" title="Pooja Bhatt">Pooja Bhatt</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Bollywood" title="Bollywood">Bollywood</a> film <i><a href="/wiki/Kajraare" title="Kajraare">Kajraare</a></i>. The following year, she had a cameo appearance in song "Love Mein Ghum" in <a href="/wiki/Reema_Khan" title="Reema Khan">Reema Khan</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Love_Mein_Ghum" title="Love Mein Ghum">Love Mein Ghum</a></i>.</p>
<p>She played the role of seductress Nisha in the 2013 film <i><a href="/wiki/Murder_3" title="Murder 3">Murder 3</a></i> .<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> The following year, she made her <a href="/wiki/Lollywood" title="Lollywood">Lollywood</a> debut in <a href="/wiki/Yasir_Nawaz" title="Yasir Nawaz">Yasir Nawaz</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Anjuman_(2013_film)" title="Anjuman (2013 film)">Anjuman</a></i>. In 2014, She did an <a href="/wiki/Item_number" title="Item number">item number</a> "Saiyaan" in <a href="/wiki/Syed_Faisal_Bukhari" title="Syed Faisal Bukhari">Syed Faisal Bukhari</a>'s <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saltanat" title="Saltanat">Saltanat</a></i>. She then appeared in Shadaab Mirza's <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Barkhaa_(2015_film)" title="Barkhaa (2015 film)">Barkhaa</a></i>, where she played a small town girl turned bar dancer. The film ran for 50 days but was commercially declared a disaster.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Upcoming_projects">Upcoming projects</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sara_Loren&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Upcoming projects">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As of 2015, Loren has completed filming for Anil Balani's <i><a href="/wiki/Ishq_Click" title="Ishq Click">Ishq Click</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> She is currently filming for <a href="/wiki/Prakash_Jha" title="Prakash Jha">Prakash Jha</a>'s <i>Fraud Saiyyan</i> with <a href="/wiki/Arshad_Warsi" title="Arshad Warsi">Arshad Warsi</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
