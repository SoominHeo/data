<p><i><b>Son of Man</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">사람의 아들</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Sarameui adeul</span></i>) is an award-winning <a href="/wiki/1980_in_film" title="1980 in film">1980</a> <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> film directed by <a href="/wiki/Yu_Hyun-mok" title="Yu Hyun-mok">Yu Hyun-mok</a>. It is based on the same titled 1979 novel written by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yi_Munyol" title="Yi Munyol">Yi Munyol</a>.</p>
