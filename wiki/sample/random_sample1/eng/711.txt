<p>Year <b>968</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">CMLXVIII</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Wednesday" title="Leap year starting on Wednesday">leap year starting on Wednesday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=968&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=968&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Pechenegs" title="Pechenegs">Pechenegs</a> begin the <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Kiev_(968)" title="Siege of Kiev (968)">Siege of Kiev</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=968&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Battle_near_Silistra" title="Battle near Silistra">Battle near Silistra</a> occurs in the spring near the <a href="/wiki/First_Bulgarian_Empire" title="First Bulgarian Empire">Bulgarian</a> town of <a href="/wiki/Silistra" title="Silistra">Silistra</a>, but most probably on the modern territory of Romania.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_topic">By topic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=968&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: By topic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Religion">Religion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=968&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Religion">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Archbishopric_of_Magdeburg" title="Archbishopric of Magdeburg">Archbishopric of Magdeburg</a> is founded.</li>
<li>The first <a href="/wiki/Poland" title="Poland">Polish</a> bishopric is founded in <a href="/wiki/Pozna%C5%84" title="Poznań">Poznań</a>. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jordan_(bishop_of_Poland)" title="Jordan (bishop of Poland)">Jordan</a> becomes the first bishop.</li>
</ul>
