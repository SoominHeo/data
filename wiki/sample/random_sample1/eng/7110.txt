<p><i><b>Love Me Not</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">사랑따윈 필요없어</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Sarang ddawin piryo eopseo</span></i>; literally "Love and Such Is Not Necessary") is a 2006 South Korean romance film directed by <a href="/wiki/Lee_Cheol-ha" title="Lee Cheol-ha">Lee Cheol-ha</a> and starring <a href="/wiki/Moon_Geun-young" title="Moon Geun-young">Moon Geun-young</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kim_Joo-hyuk" title="Kim Joo-hyuk">Kim Joo-hyuk</a>.</p>
<p>It is based on the 2002 <a href="/wiki/Tokyo_Broadcasting_System" title="Tokyo Broadcasting System">TBS</a> <a href="/wiki/Japanese_television_drama" title="Japanese television drama">Japanese drama</a> <i>I Don't Need Love, Summer</i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">愛なんていらねえよ、夏</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Ai Nante Irane Yo, Natsu</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>, starring <a href="/wiki/Ry%C5%8Dko_Hirosue" title="Ryōko Hirosue">Ryōko Hirosue</a>. The 2013 <a href="/wiki/Korean_drama" title="Korean drama">South Korean television series</a> <i><a href="/wiki/That_Winter,_the_Wind_Blows" title="That Winter, the Wind Blows">That Winter, the Wind Blows</a></i> is also based on the same storyline.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Love_Me_Not&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Julian (<a href="/wiki/Kim_Joo-hyuk" title="Kim Joo-hyuk">Kim Joohyuk</a>) has lived off with the money he lures from his rich female customers. But now he faces usurious debts from a hasty expansion of his business, and he will be killed unless he clears the debt in one month. The only way to save himself is to pretend to be the long-lost brother of an heiress and kill her to get her huge fortune. Min (<a href="/wiki/Moon_Geun-young" title="Moon Geun-young">Moon Geun-young</a>), the blind heiress, cold-hearted like Julian, slowly opens herself to him, and he, too, falls for her. But Julian has to pay his creditor and what makes it worse for him is that the illness that took Min’s eyesight relapsed, threatening her life. This story takes a terrible turn when Julian becomes guilty, and pained with guilt.</p>
