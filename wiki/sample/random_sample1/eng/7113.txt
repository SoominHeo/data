<p><i><b>Love Lasts Three Years</b></i> (original title: <i><b>L'Amour dure trois ans</b></i>) is a 2011 French-Belgian comedy film written and directed by <a href="/wiki/Fr%C3%A9d%C3%A9ric_Beigbeder" title="Frédéric Beigbeder">Frédéric Beigbeder</a>.</p>
