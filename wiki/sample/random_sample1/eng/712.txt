<p>Year <b>970</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">CMLXX</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Saturday" title="Common year starting on Saturday">common year starting on Saturday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="By_place">By place</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=970&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: By place">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Africa">Africa</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=970&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Africa">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>Construction is completed on <a href="/wiki/Al-Azhar_Mosque" title="Al-Azhar Mosque">Al-Azhar Mosque</a> in <a href="/wiki/Cairo" title="Cairo">Cairo</a> (the world's oldest <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Islamic" title="Islamic">Islamic</a> <a href="/wiki/University" title="University">university</a>).</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=970&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>A major <a href="/wiki/Volcano" title="Volcano">volcano</a> erupts in <a href="/wiki/Mashu" title="Mashu">Mashu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=970&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>A devastating decade-long <a href="/wiki/Famine" title="Famine">famine</a> begins in <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>.</li>
<li>Oldest preserved document (by <a href="/wiki/Otto_I,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Otto I, Holy Roman Emperor">Otto I, Holy Roman Emperor</a>) mentioning <a href="/wiki/Leibnitz" title="Leibnitz">Leibnitz</a> in <a href="/wiki/Styria" title="Styria">Styria</a> (<a href="/wiki/Austria" title="Austria">Austria</a>)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Byzantine_Emperor_John_I" title="Byzantine Emperor John I">Byzantine Emperor John I</a> successfully defends the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Eastern_Roman_Empire" title="Eastern Roman Empire">Eastern Roman Empire</a> from a massive <a href="/wiki/Barbarian" title="Barbarian">barbarian</a> invasion.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Eric_the_Victorious" title="Eric the Victorious">Eric the Victorious</a> becomes the first king of Sweden.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Skagul_Toste" title="Skagul Toste">Skagul Toste</a> demands the English to pay the very first <a href="/wiki/Danegeld" title="Danegeld">danegeld</a>.</li>
</ul>
