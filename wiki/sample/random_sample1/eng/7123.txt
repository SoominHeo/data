<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Healthcare">Healthcare</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sariwon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Healthcare">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sariwŏn has the only pediatric hospital in the entire region; it serves 16 districts and 500,000 children and teens annually.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Industry">Industry</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sariwon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Industry">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sariwŏn has a Potassic/Potash Fertilizer Complex and a tractor factory.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Education">Education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sariwon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Educational institutions include the University of Agriculture, University of Geology, University of Medicine, University of Education nos. 1 &amp; 2 and the Sariwŏn Pharmaceutical College of Koryŏ.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Tourism">Tourism</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sariwon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Tourism">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The "Sariwŏn Folklore street" was constructed during Kim-Jong-il's rule. Built to display an ideal picture of ancient Korea, it includes buildings in the "historical style" and a collection of ancient Korean cannons. Although it is considered an inaccurate romanticized recreation of an ancient Korean street, it is frequently used as a destination for foreigners on official government tours. Many older style Korean buildings exist in the city.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transport">Transport</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sariwon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Transport">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sariwŏn is served by <a href="/wiki/Sariwon_Chongnyon_Station" title="Sariwon Chongnyon Station">Sariwŏn Ch'ŏngnyŏn</a> and several other stations on both the <a href="/wiki/Pyongbu_Line" title="Pyongbu Line">P'yŏngbu</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hwanghae_Ch%27%C5%8Fngny%C5%8Fn_Line" title="Hwanghae Ch'ŏngnyŏn Line">Hwanghae Ch'ŏngnyŏn</a> lines of the <a href="/wiki/Korean_State_Railway" title="Korean State Railway">Korean State Railway</a>.</p>
