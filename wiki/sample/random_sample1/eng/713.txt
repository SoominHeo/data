<p>Year <b>972</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">CMLXXII</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Monday" title="Leap year starting on Monday">leap year starting on Monday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
