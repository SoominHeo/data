<p><b>Samos Prefecture</b> (<a href="/wiki/Greek_language" title="Greek language">Greek</a>: <span lang="el" xml:lang="el">Νομός Σάμου</span>) was a <a href="/wiki/Prefectures_of_Greece" title="Prefectures of Greece">prefecture</a> in <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greece</a>, consisting of the islands of <a href="/wiki/Samos" title="Samos">Samos</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ikaria" title="Ikaria">Ikaria</a> and the smaller islands of <a href="/wiki/Fournoi_Korseon" title="Fournoi Korseon">Fournoi Korseon</a>. In 2011 the prefecture was abolished and the territory is now covered by the <a href="/wiki/Regional_units_of_Greece" title="Regional units of Greece">regional units</a> of <a href="/wiki/Samos" title="Samos">Samos</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ikaria_(regional_unit)" title="Ikaria (regional unit)">Ikaria</a>. Its capital was the town of <a href="/wiki/Vathy,_Samos" title="Vathy, Samos">Vathy</a>, on Samos.</p>
