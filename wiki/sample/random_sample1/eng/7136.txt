<p><i><b>Sabah Tanah Airku</b></i> (English: Sabah My Homeland) is the official state <a href="/wiki/Anthem" title="Anthem">anthem</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sabah" title="Sabah">Sabah</a>, <a href="/wiki/Malaysia" title="Malaysia">Malaysia</a> that was adopted in 1988.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Anthem_text">Anthem text</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sabah_Tanah_Airku&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Anthem text">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<dl>
<dd>Sabah tanah airku</dd>
<dd>Negeri kita yang tercinta</dd>
<dd>Pemuda pemudi</dd>
<dd>Semua marilah</dd>
<dd>Bangunlah bersatu semua</dd>
<dd>Marilah bersama serta maju jaya</dd>
<dd>Merdeka sepanjang masa</dd>
<dd>Bersatu segala bangsa sentosa</dd>
<dd>Sabah negeri merdeka</dd>
</dl>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Official_English_translation">Official English translation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sabah_Tanah_Airku&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Official English translation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<dl>
<dd>Sabah, my homeland</dd>
<dd>Our beloved state</dd>
<dd>Men and women, come</dd>
<dd>Arise all together</dd>
<dd>Come together to be successful</dd>
<dd>Always staying Independent</dd>
<dd>Unite all, nation prospers</dd>
<dd>Sabah, the Independent state</dd>
</dl>
