<p>The current <b>flag</b> of the state of <b><a href="/wiki/Sabah" title="Sabah">Sabah</a></b>, in <a href="/wiki/Malaysia" title="Malaysia">Malaysia</a>, was adopted on 16 September 1988. It is red, white and three different shades of blue. The mountain is in the canton as in the 1963 flag, but now in dark blue on a light blue background. The field is medium blue over white over red. The mountain shown on the flag (and the state's coat of arms) is <a href="/wiki/Mount_Kinabalu" title="Mount Kinabalu">Mount Kinabalu</a>.</p>
<p>The five different colours represent the five divisions in Sabah.</p>
<ul>
<li>A silhouette of <a href="/wiki/Mount_Kinabalu" title="Mount Kinabalu">Mount Kinabalu</a> represents the state of Sabah.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Zircon" title="Zircon">Zircon</a> blue represents peace and calmness.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Icicle" title="Icicle">Icicle</a> blue represents unity and prosperity.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Royal_blue" title="Royal blue">Royal blue</a> represents strength and harmony.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/White" title="White">White</a> represents purity and justice.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Chili_pepper" title="Chili pepper">Chilli</a> red represents courage and determination.</li>
</ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Flag_of_Sabah_1963-1982">Flag of Sabah 1963-1982</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Flag_of_Sabah&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Flag of Sabah 1963-1982">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:177px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Flag_of_Sabah_(1963-1982).svg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="271" data-file-width="543" height="87" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/2f/Flag_of_Sabah_%281963-1982%29.svg/175px-Flag_of_Sabah_%281963-1982%29.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/2f/Flag_of_Sabah_%281963-1982%29.svg/263px-Flag_of_Sabah_%281963-1982%29.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/2f/Flag_of_Sabah_%281963-1982%29.svg/350px-Flag_of_Sabah_%281963-1982%29.svg.png 2x" width="175"/></a>
<div class="thumbcaption">
<div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Flag_of_Sabah_(1963-1982).svg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>
Flag of Sabah from 1963-1982.</div>
</div>
</div>
<p>On 31 August 1963, Sabah adopted a four-striped flag (red over white over yellow over blue) with a green canton and a brown mountain.</p>
<p>The meaning of the flag created in 1963 is very similar to the current flag adopted in 1988.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Flag_of_Sabah_1982-1988">Flag of Sabah 1982-1988</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Flag_of_Sabah&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Flag of Sabah 1982-1988">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:177px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Flag_of_Sabah_(1982-1988).svg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="700" data-file-width="1400" height="88" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f8/Flag_of_Sabah_%281982-1988%29.svg/175px-Flag_of_Sabah_%281982-1988%29.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f8/Flag_of_Sabah_%281982-1988%29.svg/263px-Flag_of_Sabah_%281982-1988%29.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f8/Flag_of_Sabah_%281982-1988%29.svg/350px-Flag_of_Sabah_%281982-1988%29.svg.png 2x" width="175"/></a>
<div class="thumbcaption">
<div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Flag_of_Sabah_(1982-1988).svg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>
Flag of Sabah from 1982-1988.</div>
</div>
</div>
<p>The second flag design was created in 1981 by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/BERJAYA" title="BERJAYA">BERJAYA</a> (Sabah People's United Front Party) state government. It was formally adopted on 1 January 1982. It had a completely different design from the previous one: blue over white with a red triangle on the hoist. It is similar to the <i>Trisakti</i> flag used by the neighbouring state of <a href="/wiki/Sarawak" title="Sarawak">Sarawak</a> until 31 August 1988, which was red over white with a blue triangle on the hoist.</p>
<p>This flag should not be confused with the <a href="/wiki/Flag_of_the_Czech_Republic" title="Flag of the Czech Republic">flag of the Czech Republic</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Flag_of_the_Philippines" title="Flag of the Philippines">flag of the Philippines</a>.</p>
