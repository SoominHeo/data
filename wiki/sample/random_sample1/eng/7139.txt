<p><b>Savanne</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Districts_of_Mauritius" title="Districts of Mauritius">district</a> of <a href="/wiki/Mauritius" title="Mauritius">Mauritius</a>, situated in the south of the island. The district has an area of 244.8 km2 and the population estimate was at 68,585 as at 31 December 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-stats2015_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-stats2015-2">[2]</a></sup> The southern part of the island is one of the most scenic and unspoilt.</p>
