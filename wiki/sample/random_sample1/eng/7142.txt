<p><b>Savolax and Kymmenegård County</b> (<a href="/wiki/Swedish_language" title="Swedish language">Swedish</a>: <span lang="sv" xml:lang="sv"><i>Savolax och Kymmenegårds län</i></span>, <a href="/wiki/Finnish_language" title="Finnish language">Finnish</a>: <span lang="fi" xml:lang="fi"><i>Kymenkartanon ja Savon lääni</i></span>) was a <a href="/wiki/Counties_of_Sweden" title="Counties of Sweden">county</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Sweden_(1721%E2%80%931809)" title="Kingdom of Sweden (1721–1809)">Sweden</a> from 1747 to 1775.</p>
<p>In 1743 following a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Russo-Swedish_War_(1741%E2%80%931743)" title="Russo-Swedish War (1741–1743)">Russo-Swedish War (1741–1743)</a> part of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/County_of_Kymmeneg%C3%A5rd_and_Nyslott" title="County of Kymmenegård and Nyslott">County of Kymmenegård and Nyslott</a>, including residence city of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Villmanstrand" title="Villmanstrand">Villmanstrand</a>, was ceded to Russia in the <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_%C3%85bo" title="Treaty of Åbo">Treaty of Åbo</a>. Remaining part of the county was merged in 1747 with some territories from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/County_of_Nyland_and_Tavastehus" title="County of Nyland and Tavastehus">County of Nyland and Tavastehus</a> to a new County of Savolax and Kymmenegård. New residence city was <a href="/wiki/Lovisa" title="Lovisa">Lovisa</a>.</p>
<p>In 1775 the county was split into the <a href="/wiki/Savolax_and_Karelia_County" title="Savolax and Karelia County">Savolax and Karelia County</a> (<a href="/wiki/Swedish_language" title="Swedish language">Swedish</a>: <span lang="sv" xml:lang="sv"><i>Savolax och Karelens län</i></span>, <a href="/wiki/Finnish_language" title="Finnish language">Finnish</a>: <span lang="fi" xml:lang="fi"><i>Savon ja Karjalan lääni</i></span>) and the <a href="/wiki/Kymmeneg%C3%A5rd_County" title="Kymmenegård County">Kymmenegård County</a> (<a href="/wiki/Swedish_language" title="Swedish language">Swedish</a>: <span lang="sv" xml:lang="sv"><i>Kymmenegårds län</i></span>, <a href="/wiki/Finnish_language" title="Finnish language">Finnish</a>: <span lang="fi" xml:lang="fi"><i>Kymenkartanon lääni</i></span>).</p>
