<p><b>Sabina Mikina</b> (<a href="/wiki/Azerbaijani_language" title="Azerbaijani language">Azerbaijani</a>: <span lang="az" xml:lang="az"><i>Səbinə Mikina</i></span>; born October 24, 1987 in <a href="/wiki/Baku" title="Baku">Baku</a>) is an Azerbaijani sabre fencer.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-london-2012_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-london-2012-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Mikina represented <a href="/wiki/Azerbaijan" title="Azerbaijan">Azerbaijan</a> at the <a href="/wiki/2012_Summer_Olympics" title="2012 Summer Olympics">2012 Summer Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, where she competed only in the <a href="/wiki/Fencing_at_the_2012_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Women%27s_sabre" title="Fencing at the 2012 Summer Olympics – Women's sabre">women's individual sabre</a> event. She defeated Hungarian-born German fencer <a href="/wiki/Alexandra_Bujdoso" title="Alexandra Bujdoso">Alexandra Bujdoso</a> in the first preliminary round, before losing out her next match to Ukraine's <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Olga_Kharlan" title="Olga Kharlan">Olga Kharlan</a>, with a final score of 10–15.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
