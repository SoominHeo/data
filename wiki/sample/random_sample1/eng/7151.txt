<p><b>Sasha Grey</b> (born <b>Marina Ann Hantzis</b>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-latimes_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-latimes-4">[4]</a></sup> March 14, 1988) is an American actress, model, author, musician,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RegenMag_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-RegenMag-6">[6]</a></sup> and former <a href="/wiki/Pornographic_film_actor" title="Pornographic film actor">pornographic actress</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-AVNFormerPornStar_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-AVNFormerPornStar-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PhillyGensler_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-PhillyGensler-8">[8]</a></sup> She first made her name in mainstream media after appearing on several popular television programs and in pop culture magazines, examining her willingness to enter the world of <a href="/wiki/Hardcore_pornography" title="Hardcore pornography">hardcore porn</a> at a young age.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lamagazine_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-lamagazine-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> She has also been featured in movies, television shows, music videos and <a href="/wiki/Advertising_campaign" title="Advertising campaign">advertising campaigns</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-zeitgeist_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-zeitgeist-11">[11]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HypeBeast_12-0"><a href="#cite_note-HypeBeast-12">[12]</a></sup> She won numerous awards for her work in pornography between 2007 and 2010, including the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/AVN_Female_Performer_of_the_Year_Award" title="AVN Female Performer of the Year Award">Female Performer of the Year</a> at the <a href="/wiki/25th_AVN_Awards" title="25th AVN Awards">2008 AVN Awards</a>.</p>
<p>After her feature film debut as the lead in the <a href="/wiki/Academy_Awards" title="Academy Awards">Academy Award</a>-winning director <a href="/wiki/Steven_Soderbergh" title="Steven Soderbergh">Steven Soderbergh</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/The_Girlfriend_Experience" title="The Girlfriend Experience">The Girlfriend Experience</a></i> (2009), Grey shifted her focus to mainstream acting.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PhillyGensler_8-1"><a href="#cite_note-PhillyGensler-8">[8]</a></sup> She starred in the Canadian black comedy horror film <i><a href="/wiki/Smash_Cut" title="Smash Cut">Smash Cut</a></i> (2009)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-auto_13-0"><a href="#cite_note-auto-13">[13]</a></sup> and played a fictionalized version of herself in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Entourage_(season_7)" title="Entourage (season 7)">the seventh season</a> of the <a href="/wiki/HBO" title="HBO">HBO</a> comedy-drama series <i><a href="/wiki/Entourage_(U.S._TV_series)" title="Entourage (U.S. TV series)">Entourage</a></i>. She has also appeared in independent films such as <i><a href="/wiki/I_Melt_with_You_(film)" title="I Melt with You (film)">I Melt with You</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Open_Windows_(film)" title="Open Windows (film)">Open Windows</a></i>. She is a former member of <a href="/wiki/ATelecine" title="ATelecine">aTelecine</a>, an <a href="/wiki/Industrial_music" title="Industrial music">industrial music</a> band.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RegenMag_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-RegenMag-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-pendusound_14-0"><a href="#cite_note-pendusound-14">[14]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PlayGround_Mix_149:_aTelecine_15-0"><a href="#cite_note-PlayGround_Mix_149:_aTelecine-15">[15]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span></h2>
<p>Grey was born and raised in <a href="/wiki/North_Highlands,_California" title="North Highlands, California">North Highlands, California</a>, to a working-class family. Her mother worked for the state of <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>, while her father was a <a href="/wiki/Mechanic" title="Mechanic">mechanic</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lamagazine_9-1"><a href="#cite_note-lamagazine-9">[9]</a></sup> Her father was a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Greek-American" title="Greek-American">Greek-American</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup> while her mother is of <a href="/wiki/English_people" title="English people">English</a>, <a href="/wiki/Irish_people" title="Irish people">Irish</a> and <a href="/wiki/Poles" title="Poles">Polish</a> descent.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-xcriticinterview_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-xcriticinterview-3">[3]</a></sup> Her parents divorced when she was five years old, and she was subsequently raised by her mother, who remarried in 2000. Her father died in June 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup></p>
<p>Grey attended four high schools, among them Highlands High in California, having been unhappy in each one,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lamagazine_9-2"><a href="#cite_note-lamagazine-9">[9]</a></sup> though she graduated a year early at the age of 17.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-flavorwire_18-0"><a href="#cite_note-flavorwire-18">[18]</a></sup> In late 2005, she attended <a href="/wiki/Sacramento_City_College" title="Sacramento City College">Sacramento City College</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SGrey-NewsReview_19-0"><a href="#cite_note-SGrey-NewsReview-19">[19]</a></sup> and took classes in film, dance, and acting.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lamagazine_9-3"><a href="#cite_note-lamagazine-9">[9]</a></sup> She waited tables at a <a href="/wiki/Steakhouse" title="Steakhouse">steakhouse</a> through March 2006 and saved $7,000 for a move to Los Angeles.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lamagazine_9-4"><a href="#cite_note-lamagazine-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<p>Grey states that her parents were not happy with her involvement in the adult industry, but that they are on good terms nonetheless.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-20"><a href="#cite_note-20">[20]</a></sup></p>

<p>In May 2006, Grey moved to <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a> and began appearing in adult films just after turning 18.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lamagazine_9-5"><a href="#cite_note-lamagazine-9">[9]</a></sup> Originally she toyed with the name <a href="/wiki/Anna_Karina" title="Anna Karina">Anna Karina</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lamagazine_9-6"><a href="#cite_note-lamagazine-9">[9]</a></sup> taken after the <a href="/wiki/French_New_Wave" title="French New Wave">French New Wave</a> actress, before deciding on her present name. Grey quickly made a name for herself in the business after she asked Siffredi to punch her in the stomach; however, she later stated that she was <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Improvising" title="Improvising">improvising</a> and it was taken out of context.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-23"><a href="#cite_note-23">[23]</a></sup></p>
