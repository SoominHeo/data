<p><b>Sayantani Ghosh</b> (born September 6, 1985) is an <a href="/wiki/Indian_people" title="Indian people">Indian</a> television actress.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TOI_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-TOI-1">[1]</a></sup> She is best known for her portrayal of <i>Amrita</i> on The <a href="/wiki/Zee_TV" title="Zee TV">Zee TV</a>'s drama television series <i><a href="/wiki/Naaginn" title="Naaginn">Naaginn</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TOI_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-TOI-1">[1]</a></sup> She was also a contestant on <a href="/wiki/Bigg_Boss_6" title="Bigg Boss 6">Bigg Boss 6</a>.</p>
<p>She is also known for her roles of Pranali Gujral in <a href="/wiki/Adaalat" title="Adaalat">Adalat</a> and <a href="/wiki/Satyavati" title="Satyavati">Satyavati</a> in <a href="/wiki/Mahabharat_(2013_TV_series)" title="Mahabharat (2013 TV series)">Mahabharat (2013 TV series)</a> respectively.Recently she acted a special show for comedy nights with kapil aired on 31st January 2015.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sayantani_Ghosh&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sayantani Ghosh was born in Kolkata, West Bengal, on 6 September .<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BiggBoss_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-BiggBoss-2">[2]</a></sup> She won the Miss Calcutta beauty pageant and found some roles in Bengali films before landing her first television role in 2002, in the Indian soap opera <i><a href="/wiki/Kumkum_(TV_series)" title="Kumkum (TV series)">Kumkum</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BiggBoss_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-BiggBoss-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TOI_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-TOI-1">[1]</a></sup> She has also done cameo in <a href="/wiki/Zee_TV" title="Zee TV">Zee TV</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Mrs._Kaushik_Ki_Paanch_Bahuein" title="Mrs. Kaushik Ki Paanch Bahuein">Mrs. Kaushik Ki Paanch Bahuein</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>She also worked in <a href="/wiki/Naaginn" title="Naaginn">Naaginn</a> and <i><a href="/wiki/Adaalat" title="Adaalat">Adaalat</a></i> as Amrita and Prosecutor Pranali Gujral and gained; rave reviews from critics and popularity through these roles.</p>
<p>She has participated in the sixth season of <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bigg_Boss_(Season_2)" title="Bigg Boss (Season 2)">Bigg Boss</a></i>, the Indian version of original UK show <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Big_Brother_(TV_series)" title="Big Brother (TV series)">Big Brother</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BiggBoss_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-BiggBoss-2">[2]</a></sup> and got evicted from the show in the third week. She was a very humble person and she got along with every participant in Bigg Boss' House and didn't have any fights with anyone during the period she was at the house. In the house, she was a very good dancer and singer. But unfortunately she got evicted on day 20 from the Bigg Boss house.</p>
<p>Later in 2013, she appeared as <a href="/wiki/Satyavati" title="Satyavati">Satyavati</a> in the hit television series <a href="/wiki/Mahabharat_(2013_TV_series)" title="Mahabharat (2013 TV series)">Mahabharat (2013 TV series)</a>. Her role of <a href="/wiki/Satyavati" title="Satyavati">Satyavati</a> in the great epic was of a fisher-woman who later became; Emperor <a href="/wiki/Shantanu" title="Shantanu">Shantanu</a>'s second wife and step-mother to <a href="/wiki/Bhishma" title="Bhishma">Bhishma</a>. She had to exit from the show when her role of Satyavati decided to spend her last years in forest. In 2014, Ghosh portrayed Nividita Basu in <a href="/wiki/Ekta_Kapoor" title="Ekta Kapoor">Ekta Kapoor</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Itna_Karo_Na_Mujhe_Pyaar" title="Itna Karo Na Mujhe Pyaar">Itna Karo Na Mujhe Pyaar</a></i>. She also became a contestant on <i><a href="/wiki/Dare_2_Dance" title="Dare 2 Dance">Dare 2 Dance</a></i> which aired on <a href="/wiki/Life_OK" title="Life OK">Life OK</a>.</p>
<p>In 2015, Ghosh played Rajkumari Rajeshwari in <a href="/wiki/Sasural_Simar_Ka" title="Sasural Simar Ka">Sasural Simar Ka</a>. She then appeared in <a href="/wiki/Rishton_Ka_Mela" title="Rishton Ka Mela">Rishton Ka Mela</a>, <a href="/wiki/Comedy_Nights_with_Kapil" title="Comedy Nights with Kapil">Comedy Nights with Kapil</a> and <a href="/wiki/Comedy_Classes" title="Comedy Classes">Comedy Classes</a>. At then end of 2015, Ghosh appeared as Poulomi Maa in &amp;TV's <i><a href="/wiki/Santoshi_Maa_(TV_series)" title="Santoshi Maa (TV series)">Santoshi Maa</a></i>.</p>
