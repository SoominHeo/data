<p><b>Shawan (Sawen) County</b> (<a href="/wiki/Uyghur_language" title="Uyghur language">Uyghur</a>: Савән) is a county situated in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Xinjiang_Uyghur_Autonomous_Region" title="Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region">Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region</a> and is under the administration of the <a href="/wiki/Tacheng_Prefecture" title="Tacheng Prefecture">Tacheng Prefecture</a>. It has an area of 12,468 km² with a population of 200,000. The Postcode is 832100.</p>
