<p>The <b>Southeast Division</b> is one of the three divisions in the <a href="/wiki/Eastern_Conference_(NBA)" title="Eastern Conference (NBA)">Eastern Conference</a> of the <a href="/wiki/National_Basketball_Association" title="National Basketball Association">National Basketball Association</a> (NBA). The division consists of five teams, the <a href="/wiki/Atlanta_Hawks" title="Atlanta Hawks">Atlanta Hawks</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Charlotte_Hornets" title="Charlotte Hornets">Charlotte Hornets</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Miami_Heat" title="Miami Heat">Miami Heat</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Orlando_Magic" title="Orlando Magic">Orlando Magic</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Washington_Wizards" title="Washington Wizards">Washington Wizards</a>.</p>
<p>The division was created at the start of the <a href="/wiki/2004%E2%80%9305_NBA_season" title="2004–05 NBA season">2004–05 season</a>, when the league expanded from 29 to 30 teams with the addition of the Charlotte Hornets. The league realigned itself into three divisions in each conference. The Southeast Division began with five inaugural members, the Hawks, the Hornets, the Heat, the Magic and the Wizards.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The Hawks joined from the <a href="/wiki/Central_Division_(NBA)" title="Central Division (NBA)">Central Division</a>, while the Heat, the Magic and the Wizards joined from the <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Division_(NBA)" title="Atlantic Division (NBA)">Atlantic Division</a>. The Bobcats changed their name to the Hornets effective the 2014–15 season, after which it assumed the history of the original Hornets from 1988–2002. The Hornets name was previously used by the now-<a href="/wiki/New_Orleans_Pelicans" title="New Orleans Pelicans">New Orleans Pelicans</a> from 2002–2013.</p>
<p>The Heat has won the most Southeast Division titles, with eight, while the Magic have won three and the Hawks have won one. The Heat won the Southeast Division in four consecutive seasons from 2011 to 2014. Miami's three championships (<a href="/wiki/2005%E2%80%9306_NBA_season" title="2005–06 NBA season">2006</a>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=2011%E2%80%932012_NBA_season&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="2011–2012 NBA season (page does not exist)">2012</a>, and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=2012%E2%80%932013_NBA_season&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="2012–2013 NBA season (page does not exist)">2013</a>) each came after winning the Southeast Division. The Heat are also the most recent division champion, having won their eighth division championship in the <a href="/wiki/2015%E2%80%9316_NBA_season" title="2015–16 NBA season">2015–16 season</a>.</p>
