<p><b>Mitsuki Saiga</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">斎賀 みつき</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Saiga Mitsuki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born June 12, 1973)</span><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-oricon_profile_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-oricon_profile-3">[3]</a></sup> is a Japanese <a href="/wiki/Voice_acting_in_Japan" title="Voice acting in Japan">voice actress</a> who is affiliated with <a href="/wiki/Ken_Production" title="Ken Production">Ken Production</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ken_old_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-ken_old-1">[1]</a></sup> Her major roles include <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tsukasa_(.hack)" title="Tsukasa (.hack)">Tsukasa</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/.hack" title=".hack">.hack</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/List_of_Fullmetal_Alchemist_characters#Maria_Ross" title="List of Fullmetal Alchemist characters">Maria Ross</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Fullmetal_Alchemist" title="Fullmetal Alchemist">Fullmetal Alchemist</a></i>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/MakubeX" title="MakubeX">MakubeX</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/GetBackers" title="GetBackers">GetBackers</a></i>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wolfram_von_Bielefeld" title="Wolfram von Bielefeld">Wolfram von Bielefeld</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Kyo_Kara_Maoh!" title="Kyo Kara Maoh!">Kyo Kara Maoh!</a></i> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chie_Harada" title="Chie Harada">Chie</a> in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/My-Hime" title="My-Hime">My-Hime</a></i> / <i><a href="/wiki/My-Otome" title="My-Otome">My-Otome</a></i>, Teito Klein in <i><a href="/wiki/07-Ghost" title="07-Ghost">07-Ghost</a></i> and Jing in <i><a href="/wiki/Jing:_King_of_Bandits" title="Jing: King of Bandits">Jing: King of Bandits</a></i>. She is noted for having a deep voice that is used to portray male or masculine female characters. She won a Best Supporting Actress Award at the <a href="/wiki/2nd_Seiyu_Awards" title="2nd Seiyu Awards">2nd Seiyu Awards</a> for her work in <i><a href="/wiki/Moyasimon:_Tales_of_Agriculture" title="Moyasimon: Tales of Agriculture">Moyasimon: Tales of Agriculture</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Gurren_Lagann" title="Gurren Lagann">Gurren Lagann</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Toward_the_Terra" title="Toward the Terra">Toward the Terra</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2nd_seiyu_awards_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-2nd_seiyu_awards-4">[4]</a></sup> She also won the Overseas Fan Award at the <a href="/wiki/4th_Seiyu_Awards" title="4th Seiyu Awards">4th Seiyu Awards</a> for her portrayals of Wolfram in <i>Kyo Kara Maoh!</i> and Souji Okita in <i><a href="/wiki/Peacemaker_Kurogane" title="Peacemaker Kurogane">Peacemaker Kurogane</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4th_seiyu_awards_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-4th_seiyu_awards-5">[5]</a></sup> As the band <i><b>Mitsuki Saiga feat.JUST</b></i>, she released several albums and singles that have charted on Oricon.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-o-disco-albums_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-o-disco-albums-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-o-disco-singles_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-o-disco-singles-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
