<p><i><b>Cyrus</b></i>, longer title <i><b>Cyrus: Mind of a Serial Killer</b></i> is a 2010 thriller horror film based on real events, written and directed by Mark Vadik and starring <a href="/wiki/Brian_Krause" title="Brian Krause">Brian Krause</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lance_Henriksen" title="Lance Henriksen">Lance Henriksen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Danielle_Harris" title="Danielle Harris">Danielle Harris</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Synopsis">Synopsis</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Cyrus:_Mind_of_a_Serial_Killer&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Synopsis">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A thriller film based on real events,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> the film shows an independent television reporter and her cameraman, part of a small independent news crew interviewing a man in regards to a serial killer the man knew by the name of 'Cyrus' dubbed 'The County Line Cannibal'. The man traces back through the story of the serial killer and why he became the monster he is.</p>
