<p><b>September 12</b> is the 255th day of the year (256th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 110 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Monday, Wednesday or Saturday (58 in 400 years each) than on Thursday or Friday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Tuesday or Sunday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=September_12&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/490_BC" title="490 BC">490 BC</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Marathon" title="Battle of Marathon">Battle of Marathon</a>: The conventionally accepted date for the Battle of Marathon. The Athenians and their Plataean allies, defeat the first Persian invasion force of Greece.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/372" title="372">372</a> – <a href="/wiki/Sixteen_Kingdoms" title="Sixteen Kingdoms">Sixteen Kingdoms</a>: <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Xiaowu_of_Jin" title="Emperor Xiaowu of Jin">Jin Xiaowudi</a>, age 10, succeeds his father <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Jianwen_of_Jin" title="Emperor Jianwen of Jin">Jin Jianwendi</a> as Emperor of the <a href="/wiki/Jin_dynasty_(265%E2%80%93420)" title="Jin dynasty (265–420)">Eastern Jin dynasty</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1185" title="1185">1185</a> – Emperor <a href="/wiki/Andronikos_I_Komnenos" title="Andronikos I Komnenos">Andronikos I Komnenos</a> brutally put to death in <a href="/wiki/Constantinople" title="Constantinople">Constantinople</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1213" title="1213">1213</a> – <a href="/wiki/Albigensian_Crusade" title="Albigensian Crusade">Albigensian Crusade</a>: <a href="/wiki/Simon_de_Montfort,_5th_Earl_of_Leicester" title="Simon de Montfort, 5th Earl of Leicester">Simon de Montfort, 5th Earl of Leicester</a>, defeats <a href="/wiki/Peter_II_of_Aragon" title="Peter II of Aragon">Peter II</a> of <a href="/wiki/Aragon" title="Aragon">Aragon</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Muret" title="Battle of Muret">Battle of Muret</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1229" title="1229">1229</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Portop%C3%AD" title="Battle of Portopí">Battle of Portopí</a>: The Aragonese army under the command of <a href="/wiki/James_I_of_Aragon" title="James I of Aragon">James I of Aragon</a> disembarks at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Santa_Pon%C3%A7a" title="Santa Ponça">Santa Ponça</a>, <a href="/wiki/Majorca" title="Majorca">Majorca</a>, with the purpose of conquering the island.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1309" title="1309">1309</a> – The <a href="/wiki/First_Siege_of_Gibraltar" title="First Siege of Gibraltar">First Siege of Gibraltar</a> takes place in the context of the Spanish <a href="/wiki/Reconquista" title="Reconquista">Reconquista</a> pitting the forces of the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Castile" title="Kingdom of Castile">Kingdom of Castile</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Emirate_of_Granada" title="Emirate of Granada">Emirate of Granada</a> resulting in a Castilian victory.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1609" title="1609">1609</a> – <a href="/wiki/Henry_Hudson" title="Henry Hudson">Henry Hudson</a> begins his exploration of the <a href="/wiki/Hudson_River" title="Hudson River">Hudson River</a> while aboard the <i><a href="/wiki/Halve_Maen" title="Halve Maen">Halve Maen</a></i>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1683" title="1683">1683</a> – <a href="/wiki/Great_Turkish_War" title="Great Turkish War">Austro-Ottoman War</a>: <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Vienna" title="Battle of Vienna">Battle of Vienna</a>: Several European armies join forces to defeat the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1814" title="1814">1814</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_North_Point" title="Battle of North Point">Battle of North Point</a>: an American detachment halts the British land advance to <a href="/wiki/Baltimore" title="Baltimore">Baltimore</a> in the <a href="/wiki/War_of_1812" title="War of 1812">War of 1812</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1846" title="1846">1846</a> – <a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Barrett_Browning" title="Elizabeth Barrett Browning">Elizabeth Barrett</a> elopes with <a href="/wiki/Robert_Browning" title="Robert Browning">Robert Browning</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1847" title="1847">1847</a> – <a href="/wiki/Mexican%E2%80%93American_War" title="Mexican–American War">Mexican–American War</a>: the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Chapultepec" title="Battle of Chapultepec">Battle of Chapultepec</a> begins.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1848" title="1848">1848</a> – <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Switzerland</a> becomes a Federal state.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1857" title="1857">1857</a> – The <a href="/wiki/SS_Central_America" title="SS Central America">SS <i>Central America</i></a> sinks about 160 miles east of <a href="/wiki/Cape_Hatteras" title="Cape Hatteras">Cape Hatteras</a>, <a href="/wiki/North_Carolina" title="North Carolina">North Carolina</a>, drowning a total of 426 passengers and crew, including Captain <a href="/wiki/William_Lewis_Herndon" title="William Lewis Herndon">William Lewis Herndon</a>. The ship was carrying 13–15 tons of gold from the <a href="/wiki/California_Gold_Rush" title="California Gold Rush">California Gold Rush</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1885" title="1885">1885</a> – <a href="/wiki/Arbroath_36%E2%80%930_Bon_Accord" title="Arbroath 36–0 Bon Accord">Arbroath 36–0 Bon Accord</a>, a world record scoreline in professional Association football.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1890" title="1890">1890</a> – <a href="/wiki/Harare" title="Harare">Salisbury</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rhodesia" title="Rhodesia">Rhodesia</a>, is founded.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1897" title="1897">1897</a> – <a href="/wiki/Tirah_Campaign" title="Tirah Campaign">Tirah Campaign</a>: <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Saragarhi" title="Battle of Saragarhi">Battle of Saragarhi</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1906" title="1906">1906</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Newport_Transporter_Bridge" title="Newport Transporter Bridge">Newport Transporter Bridge</a> is opened in <a href="/wiki/Newport,_Wales" title="Newport, Wales">Newport, South Wales</a> by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Viscount_Tredegar" title="Viscount Tredegar">Viscount Tredegar</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1910" title="1910">1910</a> – Premiere performance of <a href="/wiki/Gustav_Mahler" title="Gustav Mahler">Gustav Mahler</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Symphony_No._8_(Mahler)" title="Symphony No. 8 (Mahler)">Symphony No. 8</a> in Munich (with a chorus of 852 singers and an orchestra of 171 players. Mahler's rehearsal assistant <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Conductor_(music)" title="Conductor (music)">conductor</a> was <a href="/wiki/Bruno_Walter" title="Bruno Walter">Bruno Walter</a>)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1919" title="1919">1919</a> – <a href="/wiki/Adolf_Hitler" title="Adolf Hitler">Adolf Hitler</a> joins the <a href="/wiki/German_Workers%27_Party" title="German Workers' Party">German Workers' Party</a> (later the <a href="/wiki/Nazi_Party" title="Nazi Party">Nazi Party</a>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1923" title="1923">1923</a> – <a href="/wiki/Southern_Rhodesia" title="Southern Rhodesia">Southern Rhodesia</a>, today called <a href="/wiki/Zimbabwe" title="Zimbabwe">Zimbabwe</a>, is annexed by the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>.</li>
