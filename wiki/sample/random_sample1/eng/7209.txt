<p><b>Silas Weir Mitchell</b> (born <b>Silas Weir Mitchell Neilson</b>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SPS_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-SPS-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Brown_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Brown-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> September 30, 1969) is an <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Character_actor" title="Character actor">character actor</a> known for starring as <a href="/wiki/List_of_Prison_Break_minor_characters#Prisoners" title="List of Prison Break minor characters">Charles "Haywire" Patoshik</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Fox_Broadcasting_Company" title="Fox Broadcasting Company">Fox</a> television series <i><a href="/wiki/Prison_Break" title="Prison Break">Prison Break</a></i>, and <a href="/wiki/List_of_Grimm_characters#Monroe" title="List of Grimm characters">Monroe</a> in the <a href="/wiki/NBC" title="NBC">NBC</a> television series <i><a href="/wiki/Grimm_(TV_series)" title="Grimm (TV series)">Grimm</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_and_education">Early life and education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Silas_Weir_Mitchell_(actor)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life and education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mitchell was born and raised in <a href="/wiki/Philadelphia" title="Philadelphia">Philadelphia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pennsylvania" title="Pennsylvania">Pennsylvania</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Turnquist_2012_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Turnquist_2012-4">[4]</a></sup> He is named after an ancestor, the 19th century physician and author <a href="/wiki/Silas_Weir_Mitchell" title="Silas Weir Mitchell">Silas Weir Mitchell</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Turnquist_2012_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-Turnquist_2012-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Gray_2012_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Gray_2012-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>He attended The Montgomery Country Day School, formerly in Gladwyne PA for his elementary years; is a graduate of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/St._Paul%27s_School_(Concord,_New_Hampshire)" title="St. Paul's School (Concord, New Hampshire)">St. Paul's School</a>, a college-preparatory boarding school in <a href="/wiki/Concord,_New_Hampshire" title="Concord, New Hampshire">Concord, New Hampshire</a>, (1987);<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SPS_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-SPS-1">[1]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Brown_University" title="Brown University">Brown University</a> (1991) in <a href="/wiki/Rhode_Island" title="Rhode Island">Rhode Island</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Brown_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-Brown-2">[2]</a></sup> where he majored in Theatre and Religion; and the <a href="/wiki/University_of_California,_San_Diego" title="University of California, San Diego">University of California, San Diego</a>, <a href="/wiki/Master_of_Fine_Arts" title="Master of Fine Arts">Master of Fine Arts</a> program (1995).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Turnquist_2012_4-2"><a href="#cite_note-Turnquist_2012-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Gray_2012_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-Gray_2012-5">[5]</a></sup> After graduating from Brown, he spent some time in <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a>, acting in minor theatre productions.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Turnquist_2012_4-3"><a href="#cite_note-Turnquist_2012-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Gray_2012_5-2"><a href="#cite_note-Gray_2012-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>

<p>Mitchell has had recurring guest roles in such television series as <i><a href="/wiki/24_(TV_series)" title="24 (TV series)">24</a></i> as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Minor_characters_in_24#Eli_Stram" title="Minor characters in 24">Eli Stram</a>; <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Numb3rs" title="Numb3rs">Numb3rs</a>; <i><a href="/wiki/My_Name_Is_Earl" title="My Name Is Earl">My Name Is Earl</a></i> as Earl's ex-con friend Donny Jones; and <i><a href="/wiki/Prison_Break" title="Prison Break">Prison Break</a></i> as escapee <a href="/wiki/List_of_Prison_Break_minor_characters#Prisoners" title="List of Prison Break minor characters">Charles "Haywire" Patoshik</a>. On the <a href="/wiki/DVD" title="DVD">DVD</a> commentary for <i>Prison Break</i>, he mentions he had previously auditioned for the roles of <a href="/wiki/Theodore_%22T-Bag%22_Bagwell" title='Theodore "T-Bag" Bagwell'>Theodore "T-Bag" Bagwell</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lincoln_Burrows" title="Lincoln Burrows">Lincoln Burrows</a>. Mitchell plays the role of an insane prison inmate in both <i>Prison Break</i> and <i>My Name Is Earl</i>. He also appears in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rat_Race_(2001_film)" title="Rat Race (2001 film)">Rat Race</a></i> (2001) as Lloyd (The Hardware Store Guy), and in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Whole_Ten_Yards" title="The Whole Ten Yards">The Whole Ten Yards</a></i> as Yermo.</p>
<p>One of his most notable roles is that of mentally unstable recurring character James Hogan on <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cold_Case_(TV_series)" title="Cold Case (TV series)">Cold Case</a></i>. Mitchell also made appearances on <i><a href="/wiki/CSI:_Miami" title="CSI: Miami">CSI: Miami</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/CSI:_NY" title="CSI: NY">CSI: NY</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Law_%26_Order:_SVU" title="Law &amp; Order: SVU">Law &amp; Order: SVU</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Burn_Notice" title="Burn Notice">Burn Notice</a></i> as charming but unstable arms dealer Seymour, <i><a href="/wiki/Dexter_(TV_series)" title="Dexter (TV series)">Dexter</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Monk_(TV_series)" title="Monk (TV series)">Monk</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Six_Feet_Under_(TV_series)" title="Six Feet Under (TV series)">Six Feet Under</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/X-Files" title="X-Files">X-Files</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Closer" title="The Closer">The Closer</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Boomtown_(2002_TV_series)" title="Boomtown (2002 TV series)">Boomtown</a></i>. He also appeared on the show <i><a href="/wiki/CSI:_Crime_Scene_Investigation" title="CSI: Crime Scene Investigation">CSI</a></i> in the Season 6 episode "Room Service".</p>
<p>In 2008, Silas made an appearance in seventh and final season of the U.S. cop show <i><a href="/wiki/The_Shield" title="The Shield">The Shield</a></i>.</p>
<p>In 2009, he appeared in <i>A Fork in the Road</i>, alongside <a href="/wiki/Jaime_King" title="Jaime King">Jaime King</a>, and in <i><a href="/wiki/Halloween_II_(2009_film)" title="Halloween II (2009 film)">Halloween 2</a></i>. Mitchell also appeared in <a href="/wiki/Fox_Broadcasting_Company" title="Fox Broadcasting Company">Fox</a>'s TV show, <i><a href="/wiki/Mental_(TV_series)" title="Mental (TV series)">Mental</a></i> with <a href="/wiki/Chris_Vance_(actor)" title="Chris Vance (actor)">Chris Vance</a>, also from <i><a href="/wiki/Prison_Break" title="Prison Break">Prison Break</a></i>.</p>
<p>In October 2011, Mitchell was cast as Monroe in the <a href="/wiki/NBC" title="NBC">NBC</a> TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Grimm_(TV_series)" title="Grimm (TV series)">Grimm</a></i>.</p>
