<p>The <b>Museum of Photography</b> (<a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a>: <span lang="de" xml:lang="de"><i>Museum für Fotografie</i></span>) in the <a href="/wiki/Charlottenburg" title="Charlottenburg">Charlottenburg</a> district of <a href="/wiki/Berlin" title="Berlin">Berlin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>, is one of the <a href="/wiki/Berlin_State_Museums" title="Berlin State Museums">Berlin State Museums</a> administered by the <a href="/wiki/Prussian_Cultural_Heritage_Foundation" title="Prussian Cultural Heritage Foundation">Prussian Cultural Heritage Foundation</a>.</p>
<p>It is located next to the <a href="/wiki/Berlin_Zoologischer_Garten_railway_station" title="Berlin Zoologischer Garten railway station">Zoologischer Garten railway station</a> in the building of a former <a href="/wiki/Landwehr" title="Landwehr">landwehr</a> officers' mess, erected in 1909 according to plans by <a href="/wiki/Heino_Schmieden" title="Heino Schmieden">Heino Schmieden</a>. The museum opened in 2004 and also houses the collection of the <a href="/wiki/Helmut_Newton" title="Helmut Newton">Helmut Newton</a> Foundation. In addition to the rotating special exhibits, the permanent exhibit "Helmut Newton's Private Property" displays some of the late photographers' personal articles.</p>
