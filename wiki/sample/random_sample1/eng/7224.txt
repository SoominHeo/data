<p><b>Wakana Sakai</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">酒井 若菜</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Sakai Wakana</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born 9 September 1980, in <a href="/wiki/Nogi,_Tochigi" title="Nogi, Tochigi">Nogi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shimotsuga_District,_Tochigi" title="Shimotsuga District, Tochigi">Shimotsuga District, Tochigi</a>, Japan)</span><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nikkan_gendai_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-nikkan_gendai-1">[1]</a></sup> is a Japanese actress, writer,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[5]</a></sup> and former gravure idol. Her real and former stage name is <b>Miyuki Sakai</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">酒井 美幸</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Sakai Miyuki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>.</p>
<p>Sakai is represented with Moon the Child, and later A-team. She is left-handed.</p>
