<p>The <a href="/wiki/Super-heavy_tank" title="Super-heavy tank">Super-heavy tank</a> <b>Panzerkampfwagen IX</b> and <b>Panzerkampfwagen X</b> were silhouette conceptual drawings in an edition of the German <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Signal_(magazine)" title="Signal (magazine)">Signal</a></i> military magazine. The drawings were not based on any actual designs, and were solely printed to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Counter-intelligence" title="Counter-intelligence">deceive Allied intelligence</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
