<p><b>Sakuramachi Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">桜町駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Sakuramachi-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a railway station on the <a href="/wiki/Iida_Line" title="Iida Line">Iida Line</a> in <a href="/wiki/Iida,_Nagano" title="Iida, Nagano">Iida</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nagano_Prefecture" title="Nagano Prefecture">Nagano Prefecture</a>, Japan, operated by <a href="/wiki/Central_Japan_Railway_Company" title="Central Japan Railway Company">Central Japan Railway Company</a> (JR Central).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lines">Lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sakuramachi_Station_(Nagano)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sakuramachi Station is served by the <a href="/wiki/Iida_Line" title="Iida Line">Iida Line</a> and is 130.1 kilometers from the starting point of the line at <a href="/wiki/Toyohashi_Station" title="Toyohashi Station">Toyohashi Station</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_layout">Station layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sakuramachi_Station_(Nagano)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Station layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The station consists of a single ground-level <a href="/wiki/Side_platform" title="Side platform">side platform</a> serving one bi-direcional track. There is no station building, but only a platform built on top of the platform. The station is unattended.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sakuramachi_Station_(Nagano)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sakuramachi Station opened on 3 August 1923. With the privatization of <a href="/wiki/Japanese_National_Railways" title="Japanese National Railways">Japanese National Railways</a> (JNR) on 1 April 1987, the station came under the control of JR Central.</p>
