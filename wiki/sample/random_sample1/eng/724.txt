<p><i><b>A's Doll House</b></i> is the second <a href="/wiki/Extended_play" title="Extended play">extended play</a> by South Korean singer <a href="/wiki/Ailee" title="Ailee">Ailee</a>. It was released on July 12, 2013, by YMC Entertainment and <a href="/wiki/Neowiz" title="Neowiz">Neowiz Internet</a>. The song "U&amp;I" was used to promote the EP.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Release">Release</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=A%27s_Doll_House&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Release">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On July 12, 2013, Ailee released the album, <i>A's Doll House</i>, as well as the music video for "U&amp;I".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The Japanese version of the song <i>U&amp;I</i> is also the second single by Ailee in Japan, which was released on March 19, 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Promotion">Promotion</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=A%27s_Doll_House&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Promotion">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Promotions for the album started on July 12, in <a href="/wiki/Korean_Broadcasting_System" title="Korean Broadcasting System">KBS</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Music_Bank_(TV_series)" title="Music Bank (TV series)">Music Bank</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Ailee also promoted on <a href="/wiki/Mnet_(TV_channel)" title="Mnet (TV channel)">Mnet</a>'s <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/M!_Countdown" title="M! Countdown">M! Countdown</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Munhwa_Broadcasting_Corporation" title="Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation">MBC</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Show!_Music_Core" title="Show! Music Core">Music Core</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Show_Champion" title="Show Champion">Show Champion</a>,</i> and <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Broadcasting_System" title="Seoul Broadcasting System">SBS</a>'s <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Music_Trend" title="The Music Trend">Inkigayo</a></i> in July. The single "No No No" was chosen to be part of her comeback with the second single "Rainy Day". On July 24 Ailee won her very first music show award on MBC <i>Show Champion</i> for "U&amp;I".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Ailee also won 2 <i><a href="/wiki/Music_Bank_(TV_series)" title="Music Bank (TV series)">Music Bank</a></i> awards.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Chart_performance">Chart performance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=A%27s_Doll_House&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Chart performance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"U&amp;I" was number one on Gaon Single Chart. "U&amp;I" achieved its highest debut on the <a href="/wiki/Korea_K-Pop_Hot_100" title="Korea K-Pop Hot 100">K-Pop Hot 100</a>, where it debuted at number one for two weeks.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> "Rainy Day" peaked at number 31 on the Korea K-Pop Hot 100 the week of September 4, 2013. "NoNoNo" peaked at number 21 on the Korea K-Pop Hot 100 the week of September 24, 2013.</p>
