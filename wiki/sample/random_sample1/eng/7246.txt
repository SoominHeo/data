<p>The <b>Sahamalaza sportive lemur</b> (<i>Lepilemur sahamalazensis</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Sportive_lemur" title="Sportive lemur">sportive lemur</a> endemic to <a href="/wiki/Madagascar" title="Madagascar">Madagascar</a>. It has a total length of about 51 centimetres (20 in) to 54 centimetres (21 in), of which about 26-27 centimeters are tail.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-garbutt_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-garbutt-4">[4]</a></sup> Sahamalaza's sportive lemur is found in northwestern Madagascar, living in subhumid forest and some secondary forests.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-garbutt_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-garbutt-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
