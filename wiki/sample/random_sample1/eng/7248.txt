<p><b>Saheonbu</b>(in <a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>:사헌부, in <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">Hanja</a>:司憲府) administered inspections during <a href="/wiki/Goryeo" title="Goryeo">Goryeo</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Joseon_dynasty" title="Joseon dynasty">Joseon dynasty</a> in <a href="/wiki/Korea" title="Korea">Korea</a>. The organ inspected Hanyang, the capital and periphery. It has several nicknames. It was also responsible for licensing officials, impeachment and legal inquiries, which also extends to the control of King's relatives. The strongest duty is to directly criticize king's order.</p>
<p>Since the organ took charge of judicial responsibility for officials and the subjects, the <a href="/wiki/Ethos" title="Ethos">ethos</a> was strict.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saheonbu&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The board of inspection actually began several centuries earlier. During Silla dynasty, the title was converted to Saganwon during the reign of <a href="/wiki/Gongmin_of_Goryeo" title="Gongmin of Goryeo">Gongmin of Goryeo</a>.</p>
<p>Taejo of Joseon established the organ right after the foundation of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Joseon_Dynasty" title="Joseon Dynasty">Joseon Dynasty</a> in 1392. The standard organization was 1 head officer and 12 officials with about 40 other bureaucrats.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Responsibility">Responsibility</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saheonbu&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Responsibility">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ol>
<li>Regular meeting of royal court</li>
<li>Payment of national debts</li>
<li>Tributes</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gwageo" title="Gwageo">Gwageo</a></li>
</ol>
<p>The system began in <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>, where the organ played a wider role at the royal court. In this sense, the organ and its responsibility was in line with Saganwon where the subjects remonstrate the order of King.</p>
<p>The issue of royal court was to control over the power between the king and the subjects, keeping abreast of the order of the government, which later produced severe side effects in some cases. Its work is quite similar to the board of audits and inspection in current time.</p>
