<p><b>Kobe-Sannomiya Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">神戸三宮駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kōbe-Sannomiya-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>, or simply <b>Sannomiya Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">三宮駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Sannomiya-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>, is located in the heart of <a href="/wiki/Kobe" title="Kobe">Kobe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>. This station is the main railway terminal of Kobe.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Layout">Layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kobe-Sannomiya_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>This station has a combination bay/island platform serving three tracks below ground level. Tracks 1 and 3 are for through-trains running between <a href="/wiki/Umeda_Station" title="Umeda Station">Umeda</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sanyo_Himeji_Station" title="Sanyo Himeji Station">Sanyo Himeji</a>, while track 2 is for trains headed to <a href="/wiki/%C5%8Csaka_Namba_Station" title="Ōsaka Namba Station">Osaka-Namba</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kintetsu_Nara_Station" title="Kintetsu Nara Station">Kintetsu Nara</a>.</p>
