<p><b>Sanni Mari Elina Kurkisuo</b> (born 26 May 1993), better known by her mononym <b>Sanni</b>, is a Finnish singer, songwriter and actress signed to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Warner_Music_Finland" title="Warner Music Finland">Warner Music Finland</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sanni_Kurkisuo&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2001 she won the Napero-Finlandia prize for a writing competition. In 2012 she graduated from Sibelius-lukio arts school. The same year, she appeared in a role in the Finnish film <i>Miss Farkku-Suomi</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>She has also pursued a musical career and was in a number of bands since the age of 13. She was featured in "Pumppaa", a single by <a href="/wiki/Aste_(rapper)" title="Aste (rapper)">Aste</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> She released her first solo single "<a href="/wiki/Prinsessoja_ja_astronautteja" title="Prinsessoja ja astronautteja">Prinsessoja ja astronautteja</a>" in April 2013. It peaked at number three on <a href="/wiki/The_Official_Finnish_Charts" title="The Official Finnish Charts">The Official Finnish Charts</a>. Her debut album <i><a href="/wiki/Sotke_mut" title="Sotke mut">Sotke mut</a></i> was released in 2013.</p>
<p>On February 13, 2015, she released a new single, "<a href="/wiki/2080-luvulla" title="2080-luvulla">2080-luvulla</a>".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Musiikkituottajat_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Musiikkituottajat-3">[3]</a></sup> Her second studio album <i><a href="/wiki/Lelu_(album)" title="Lelu (album)">Lelu</a></i>, was released on 24 April 2015. She participated in the fourth season of the Finnish music reality show <i><a href="/wiki/Vain_el%C3%A4m%C3%A4%C3%A4" title="Vain elämää">Vain elämää</a></i>.</p>
