<p><b>Shan Tao</b> (205–283, <a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">山濤</span>) is one of the <a href="/wiki/Seven_Sages_of_the_Bamboo_Grove" title="Seven Sages of the Bamboo Grove">Seven Sages of the Bamboo Grove</a>—a group of Chinese Taoist scholars, writers, and musicians who lived in the 3rd century CE. He is the nephew of <a href="/wiki/Zhang_Chunhua" title="Zhang Chunhua">Zhang Chunhua</a> and grandnephew of her mother, Lady Shan.</p>
