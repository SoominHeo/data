<p><b>Sandeepa Dhar</b> is an Indian actress.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Kashmiri_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Kashmiri-1">[1]</a></sup> Sandeepa made her acting debut in the 2010 <a href="/wiki/Bollywood" title="Bollywood">Hindi film</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Isi_Life_Mein" title="Isi Life Mein">Isi Life Mein</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> For her performance in the film, she was nominated for the <a href="/wiki/Filmfare_Award_for_Best_Female_Debut" title="Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut">Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Star_Screen_Award_for_Most_Promising_Newcomer_%E2%80%93_Female" title="Star Screen Award for Most Promising Newcomer – Female">Star Screen Award for Most Promising Newcomer</a> and <a href="/wiki/Stardust_Award_for_Superstar_of_Tomorrow_%E2%80%93_Female" title="Stardust Award for Superstar of Tomorrow – Female">Stardust Award for Superstar of Tomorrow</a>. Sandeepa did a cameo in <a href="/wiki/Dabangg_2" title="Dabangg 2">Dabangg 2</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-mid-day.com_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-mid-day.com-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-mid-day.com_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-mid-day.com-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> She received a very positive review by the famous film critic Komal Nahta for her performance in <a href="/wiki/Dabangg_2" title="Dabangg 2">Dabangg 2</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup>Dhar also received critical acclaim for Global Baba and her brief but very effective performance in Sajid Nadiadwala's <a href="/wiki/Heropanti" title="Heropanti">Heropanti</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>

<p>Sandeepa was born in <a href="/wiki/Srinagar" title="Srinagar">Srinagar</a>, India. She did her schooling from <a href="/wiki/National_Public_School,_Indiranagar" title="National Public School, Indiranagar">National Public School</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bengaluru" title="Bengaluru">Bengaluru</a> and completed her graduation in advertising from <a href="/wiki/St._Xavier%27s_College,_Mumbai" title="St. Xavier's College, Mumbai">St. Xavier's College, Mumbai</a>. She did her early schooling from St Michael's Convent, ITI Mankapur, a small town in Gonda District of U P, India. Sandeepa is a professionally trained dancer. Having won several awards for her dancing, she trained in <a href="/wiki/Bharatanatyam" title="Bharatanatyam">Bharatanatyam</a> for 8 years from the famous Vani Ganpati. She also trained in Jazz and Contemporary for 4 years from <a href="/wiki/Shiamak_Davar" title="Shiamak Davar">Shiamak Davar</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Terence_Lewis" title="Terence Lewis">Terence Lewis</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sandeepa_Dhar&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2010, Sandeepa Dhar made her cinematic debut in <a href="/wiki/Rajshri_Productions" title="Rajshri Productions">Rajshri Production's</a> Hindi film <i><a href="/wiki/Isi_Life_Mein" title="Isi Life Mein">Isi Life Mein</a></i> opposite actor <a href="/wiki/Akshay_Oberoi" title="Akshay Oberoi">Akshay Oberoi</a>. Her performance was well received, earning the actress a best newcomer nomination at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Star_Screen_Awards" title="Star Screen Awards">Star Screen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Filmfare_Awards" title="Filmfare Awards">Filmfare</a> and <a href="/wiki/Stardust_Awards" title="Stardust Awards">Stardust Awards</a>. <a href="/wiki/Taran_Adarsh" title="Taran Adarsh">Taran Adarsh</a> praised her performance in the film saying, "Sandeepa Dhar is another talent that catches your attention."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<p>Sandeepa's upcoming films include " Gollu aur Pappu" produced by Viacom 18 co starring Kunal Roy Kapur and Vir Das of Delhi Belly fame. She has been signed for a 3 film deal with Viacom 18.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<p>Sandeepa is currently playing the female lead in an international musical called "West Side Story" produced by the Australian Dance Theatre(ADT). Her character name is Maria who is a hopeless romantic and an innocent Puerto Rican young girl. She falls in love with Tony and finds herself at the centre of the violent conflict between the two gangs. The musical is inspired by Willian Shakespeare's play Romeo and Juliet. So far the musical has been staged in eight countries which include Japan, Philippines,Indonesia,Australia and New Zealand with over 100 shows.</p>
