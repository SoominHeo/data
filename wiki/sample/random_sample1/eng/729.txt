<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">A. Tryfiatis-Tripiaris</h1>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>A. Tryfiatis-Tripiaris</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greek</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Racing_cyclist" title="Racing cyclist">racing cyclist</a>. He competed at the <a href="/wiki/1896_Summer_Olympics" title="1896 Summer Olympics">1896 Summer Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Athens" title="Athens">Athens</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sports-reference_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-sports-reference-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Tryfiatis-Tripiaris competed in the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Cycling_at_the_1896_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_12-hour_race&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Cycling at the 1896 Summer Olympics – Men's 12-hour race (page does not exist)">12 hour race</a>, but did not finish.</p>
