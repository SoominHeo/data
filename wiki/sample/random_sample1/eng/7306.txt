<p><b>Sant'Arsenio</b> is a town and <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Salerno" title="Province of Salerno">Province of Salerno</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Campania" title="Campania">Campania</a> region of south-western <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>, located about 180 km southeast of <a href="/wiki/Naples" title="Naples">Naples</a> and about 76 km southeast of <a href="/wiki/Salerno" title="Salerno">Salerno</a>. As of 30 June 2006, it had a population of 2,714 (1,309 men and 1,405 women) and an area of 20 km².<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-istat_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-istat-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
