<p><b>Santino Fontana</b> (born March 21, 1982) is an American <a href="/wiki/Actor" title="Actor">actor</a>, singer, director, and composer, widely known for playing Greg on the television show <i><a href="/wiki/Crazy_Ex-Girlfriend_(TV_series)" title="Crazy Ex-Girlfriend (TV series)">Crazy Ex-Girlfriend</a></i>, and for voicing <a href="/wiki/Hans_(Disney)" title="Hans (Disney)">Prince Hans</a>, the primary antagonist of the 2013 <a href="/wiki/Walt_Disney_Animation_Studios" title="Walt Disney Animation Studios">Disney</a> animated film <i><a href="/wiki/Frozen_(2013_film)" title="Frozen (2013 film)">Frozen</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Santino_Fontana&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Fontana was born in <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-actorsequity1_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-actorsequity1-1">[1]</a></sup> He is of half Italian, one quarter Portuguese, and one quarter Spanish, descent.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Fontana graduated from <a href="/wiki/Richland_High_School_(Washington)" title="Richland High School (Washington)">Richland High School</a> in <a href="/wiki/Richland,_Washington" title="Richland, Washington">Richland, Washington</a> in 2000.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> He is a graduate of the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Minnesota" title="University of Minnesota">University of Minnesota</a>/<a href="/wiki/Guthrie_Theater" title="Guthrie Theater">Guthrie Theater</a> <a href="/wiki/Bachelor_of_Fine_Arts" title="Bachelor of Fine Arts">BFA</a> Actor Training Program, class of 2004.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Santino_Fontana&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2005, as a member of the Essentials, Fontana co-wrote the musical comedy <i><a href="/wiki/Perfect_Harmony_(musical)" title="Perfect Harmony (musical)">Perfect Harmony</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> and originated the role of Philip Fellowes V.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> In 2006 he starred as Matt in the Off-Broadway revival of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Fantasticks" title="The Fantasticks">The Fantasticks</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> His <a href="/wiki/Broadway_theatre" title="Broadway theatre">Broadway</a> debut was <i><a href="/wiki/Sunday_in_the_Park_with_George" title="Sunday in the Park with George">Sunday in the Park with George</a></i> in 2007. Fontana originated the role of Tony in the Broadway production of <i><a href="/wiki/Billy_Elliot_the_Musical" title="Billy Elliot the Musical">Billy Elliot</a></i> from October 1, 2008 to July 4, 2009. He was awarded the 2010 <a href="/wiki/Drama_Desk_Award" title="Drama Desk Award">Drama Desk Award</a> for Best Featured Actor in a Play for his work in <a href="/wiki/Brighton_Beach_Memoirs" title="Brighton Beach Memoirs">Brighton Beach Memoirs</a>. He starred as Prince Topher in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rodgers_%26_Hammerstein" title="Rodgers &amp; Hammerstein">Rodgers &amp; Hammerstein</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Cinderella_(2013_Broadway_production)" title="Cinderella (2013 Broadway production)">Cinderella</a></i> on Broadway, for which he was nominated for a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tony_Award_for_Best_Leading_Actor_in_a_Musical" title="Tony Award for Best Leading Actor in a Musical">Tony Award for Best Leading Actor in a Musical</a>.</p>
<p>In 2013, he provided the voice of <a href="/wiki/Hans_(Disney)" title="Hans (Disney)">Prince Hans</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Walt_Disney_Animation_Studios" title="Walt Disney Animation Studios">Walt Disney Animation Studios</a> film <i><a href="/wiki/Frozen_(2013_film)" title="Frozen (2013 film)">Frozen</a></i>.</p>
<p>In 2015, Fontana became the first guest artist to perform three times in the space of one year with the <a href="/wiki/Mormon_Tabernacle_Choir" title="Mormon Tabernacle Choir">Mormon Tabernacle Choir</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> Fontana also joined the cast of the CW romantic musical comedy-drama <i><a href="/wiki/Crazy_Ex-Girlfriend_(TV_series)" title="Crazy Ex-Girlfriend (TV series)">Crazy Ex-Girlfriend</a></i> that same year. On November 11th 2016, it was announced that Fontana was leaving the TV series after his one-year contract with the CW.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
