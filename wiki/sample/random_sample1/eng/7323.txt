<p><b>San José</b> (<small>Spanish pronunciation: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Spanish" title="Help:IPA for Spanish">[saŋ xoˈse]</a></span>) is a province of <a href="/wiki/Costa_Rica" title="Costa Rica">Costa Rica</a>. It is located in the central part of the country, and borders (clockwise beginning in the north) the provinces of <a href="/wiki/Alajuela_Province" title="Alajuela Province">Alajuela</a>, <a href="/wiki/Heredia_Province" title="Heredia Province">Heredia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lim%C3%B3n_Province" title="Limón Province">Limón</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cartago_Province" title="Cartago Province">Cartago</a> and <a href="/wiki/Puntarenas_Province" title="Puntarenas Province">Puntarenas</a>. The provincial capital is <a href="/wiki/San_Jos%C3%A9,_Costa_Rica" title="San José, Costa Rica">San José</a>, that is also the national capital. The province covers an area of 4,965.9 km².<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and has a population of 1,404,242.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
