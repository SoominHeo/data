<p><b>Salle</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/Town" title="Town">town</a> in the Province of <a href="/wiki/Pescara" title="Pescara">Pescara</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Abruzzo" title="Abruzzo">Abruzzo</a> region of <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>, well known for its castle and its history in the violin-string industry.</p>
