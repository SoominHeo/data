<p><b>Salih Uçan</b> (<small>Turkish pronunciation: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Turkic_dialects" title="Help:IPA for Turkic dialects">[saːlih ut͡ʃan]</a></span>, born 6 January 1994) is a Turkish professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Midfielder" title="Midfielder">midfielder</a> for Turkish club <a href="/wiki/Fenerbah%C3%A7e_S.K." title="Fenerbahçe S.K.">Fenerbahçe</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Salih_U%C3%A7an&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Fenerbah.C3.A7e">Fenerbahçe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Salih_U%C3%A7an&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Fenerbahçe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Fenerbah%C3%A7e_S.K._(football)" title="Fenerbahçe S.K. (football)">Fenerbahçe</a> signed Uçan for €1.4 million on 6 June 2012, for a five-year contract worth €220,000 per season. This transfer fee marked the highest fee paid to a Turkish youth player. The first half of the season, Uçan mostly sat in the bench and was substituted in a few games. After the winter break with the start of the second half, Salih started finding more and more chances to play. Impressively, he used the opportunities wisely and finally, on 14 March 2013, he scored his debut goal for the club in an <a href="/wiki/2012%E2%80%9313_UEFA_Europa_League" title="2012–13 UEFA Europa League">UEFA Europa League</a> match against <a href="/wiki/FC_Viktoria_Plze%C5%88" title="FC Viktoria Plzeň">Viktoria Plzeň</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2012%E2%80%9313_UEFA_Europa_League_knockout_phase#Second_leg_2" title="2012–13 UEFA Europa League knockout phase">round of 16</a>. His contribution helped Fenerbahçe secure qualification to the <a href="/wiki/2012%E2%80%9313_UEFA_Europa_League_knockout_phase#Quarter-finals" title="2012–13 UEFA Europa League knockout phase">quarter-finals</a> of the Europa League with a 1–1 draw in the <a href="/wiki/%C5%9E%C3%BCkr%C3%BC_Saraco%C4%9Flu_Stadium" title="Şükrü Saracoğlu Stadium">Şükrü Saracoğlu Stadium</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> He also scored two fantastic goals against <a href="/wiki/Orduspor" title="Orduspor">Orduspor</a> on 7 April 2013, and again his contribution helped Fenerbahçe to win four games in a row in the <a href="/wiki/2012%E2%80%9313_S%C3%BCper_Lig" title="2012–13 Süper Lig">Süper Lig</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Roma_.28loan.29">Roma (loan)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Salih_U%C3%A7an&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Roma (loan)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>On 7 July 2014, after weeks of protracted negotiations,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Uçan's move to <a href="/wiki/A.S._Roma" title="A.S. Roma">Roma</a> on a two-year loan deal for a fee of €4.75 million was completed. The agreement included an option for Roma to either extend the loan by another year or to sign Uçan outright for €11 million in 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> He made his official Roma debut in the 3–0 victory against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/A.C._Chievo_Verona" title="A.C. Chievo Verona">Chievo</a> on 18 October 2014. After two years with just 10 appearances, Roma chose to not make use of the option of a permanent signing.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-fi_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-fi-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Return_to_Fenerbah.C3.A7e">Return to Fenerbahçe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Salih_U%C3%A7an&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Return to Fenerbahçe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Uçan returned to <a href="/wiki/Fenerbah%C3%A7e_S.K._(football)" title="Fenerbahçe S.K. (football)">Fenerbahçe</a> at the end of his two-year loan spell at Roma.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-fi_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-fi-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Salih_U%C3%A7an&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Uçan played all the age categories for Turkish national team. He was also a part of <a href="/wiki/Turkey_national_under-20_football_team" title="Turkey national under-20 football team">Turkey national under-20 football team</a> during the <a href="/wiki/2013_FIFA_U-20_World_Cup" title="2013 FIFA U-20 World Cup">2013 FIFA U-20 World Cup</a>. On 15 November 2013, Salih played his first national match for <a href="/wiki/Turkey_national_football_team" title="Turkey national football team">Turkey</a> against <a href="/wiki/Northern_Ireland_national_football_team" title="Northern Ireland national football team">Northern Ireland</a> in <a href="/wiki/Adana" title="Adana">Adana</a>.</p>
