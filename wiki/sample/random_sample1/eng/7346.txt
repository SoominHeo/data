<p><i><b>The Living Reed</b></i> is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Historical_novel" title="Historical novel">historical novel</a> by <a href="/wiki/Pearl_S._Buck" title="Pearl S. Buck">Pearl S. Buck</a> in which life in <a href="/wiki/Korea" title="Korea">Korea</a>, from the latter part of the nineteenth century to the end of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Second_World_War" title="Second World War">Second World War</a>, is described through the viewpoints and lives of several members of four generations of a prominent <a href="/wiki/Aristocracy_(class)" title="Aristocracy (class)">aristocratic</a> family.</p>
