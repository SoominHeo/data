<p><b>Temple and Cemetery of Confucius and the Kong Family Mansion in Qufu</b>, Shandong Province of China, include <a href="/wiki/Temple_of_Confucius,_Qufu" title="Temple of Confucius, Qufu">Temple of Confucius</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cemetery_of_Confucius" title="Cemetery of Confucius">Cemetery of Confucius</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Kong_Family_Mansion" title="Kong Family Mansion">Kong Family Mansion</a>.</p>
<p>Since 1994, the Temple of Confucius(<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%AD%94" title="wikt:孔">孔</a><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%BA%99" title="wikt:庙">庙</a></span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/K%C7%92ng" title="wikt:Kǒng">Kǒng</a> <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Mi%C3%A0o" title="wikt:Miào">Miào</a></span></i>) has been part of the <a href="/wiki/UNESCO" title="UNESCO">UNESCO</a> <a href="/wiki/World_Heritage_Site" title="World Heritage Site">World Heritage Site</a> "Temple and Cemetery of Confucius and the Kong Family Mansion in Qufu". The two other parts of the site are the nearby <a href="/wiki/Kong_Family_Mansion" title="Kong Family Mansion">Kong Family Mansion</a> (Chinese: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%AD%94" title="wikt:孔">孔</a><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%BA%9C" title="wikt:府">府</a></span>; pinyin: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/K%C7%92ng" title="wikt:Kǒng">Kǒng</a> <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/F%C7%94" title="wikt:Fǔ">Fǔ</a></span></i>), where the main-line descendants of Confucius lived, and the <a href="/wiki/Cemetery_of_Confucius" title="Cemetery of Confucius">Cemetery of Confucius</a> (Chinese: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%AD%94" title="wikt:孔">孔</a><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%9E%97" title="wikt:林">林</a></span>; pinyin: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/K%C7%92ng" title="wikt:Kǒng">Kǒng</a> <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/L%C3%ADn" title="wikt:Lín">Lín</a></span></i>) a couple kilometers to the north, where Confucius and many of his descendants have been buried. Those three sites are collectively known in Qufu as <i>San Kong</i> (三孔), i.e. "The Three Confucian [sites]".</p>
