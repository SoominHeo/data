<p><b>Samseong-Dong</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Upper_class" title="Upper class">affluent</a> neighborhood or ward of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gangnam-gu" title="Gangnam-gu">Gangnam-gu</a> in <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-encyber_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-encyber-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>The area has a large concentration of upscale <a href="/wiki/Shopping_mall" title="Shopping mall">shopping malls</a>, popular restaurants and world-renowned hotels such as the <a href="/wiki/Ramada" title="Ramada">Ramada</a> Seoul Hotel and the <a href="/wiki/Hyatt" title="Hyatt">Hyatt</a> Park Hotel. Samseong-dong is also home to some of Asia's best nightclubs that are frequently patronized by wealthy residents and celebrities. Apart from its nightlife accolades, the <a href="/wiki/COEX_Convention_%26_Exhibition_Center" title="COEX Convention &amp; Exhibition Center">COEX mall</a>, the world's largest underground shopping mall, is located in Samseong-dong.</p>
