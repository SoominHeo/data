<p><b>Samoo Architects &amp; Engineers</b> (SAMOO), headquartered in Seoul, South Korea, is a multinational professional services firm which provides <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Architectural_design" title="Architectural design">architectural design</a>, <a href="/wiki/Engineering" title="Engineering">engineering</a>, <a href="/wiki/Interior_design" title="Interior design">interior design</a>, <a href="/wiki/Urban_design" title="Urban design">urban design</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sustainable_design" title="Sustainable design">sustainable design</a>, <a href="/wiki/Construction_management" title="Construction management">construction management</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Building_information_modeling" title="Building information modeling">building information modeling</a> services.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Currently the firm has over 760 employees based in offices located in Seoul, South Korea, New York, NY, U.S.A., Abu Dhabi, UAE, Doha, Qatar, Shanghai, China, Hanoi, Vietnam, Austin, TX, U.S.A.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Samoo_Architects_%26_Engineers&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>1976 Samoo Architectural Research Institute Founded <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li>1979 Converted to Korean Architecture Incorporation Company</li>
<li>1980 Acquired License for Overseas Construction Business</li>
<li>1985 Company Name Changed to Samoo Architects &amp; Engineers, Inc.</li>
<li>1993 Registered at the Korean Ministry of Works for Professional Overall Architecture Supervision Company</li>
<li>1993 Registered as a Korean Engineering Activity Company</li>
<li>1995 Established Branch Office in New York, USA</li>
<li>1997 Established Samoo Research Group</li>
<li>1998 Registered as a Korean Generalized Professional Supervision Company</li>
<li>2003 Entered into Construction Management Business</li>
<li>2004 Established a Branch Office in Shanghai, China</li>
<li>2006 30th Anniversary of Foundation</li>
<li>2010 Established a Branch Office in Vietnam</li>
<li>2010 Established a Branch Office in Austin, USA</li>
<li>2012 Established a Branch Office in Qatar</li>
