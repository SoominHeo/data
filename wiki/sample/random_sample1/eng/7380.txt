<p><b>Samch'ŏn County</b> is a county in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/South_Hwanghae" title="South Hwanghae">South Hwanghae</a> province, <a href="/wiki/North_Korea" title="North Korea">North Korea</a>.</p>
