<p><b>Sassa Narimasa</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">佐々 成政</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, February 6, 1536 – July 7, 1588)</span>, also known as <b>Kura-no-suke</b> (内蔵助), was a Japanese samurai of the <a href="/wiki/Sengoku_period" title="Sengoku period">Sengoku</a> through <a href="/wiki/Azuchi%E2%80%93Momoyama_period" title="Azuchi–Momoyama period">Azuchi–Momoyama period</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-.E4.BD.90.E3.80.85.E6.88.90.E6.94.BF.E8.B3.87.E6.96.99.E9.A4.A8_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-.E4.BD.90.E3.80.85.E6.88.90.E6.94.BF.E8.B3.87.E6.96.99.E9.A4.A8-1">[1]</a></sup> Narimasa was born in what is now <a href="/wiki/Nishi-ku,_Nagoya" title="Nishi-ku, Nagoya">Nishi-ku</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nagoya" title="Nagoya">Nagoya</a> (situated in contemporary <a href="/wiki/Aichi_District,_Aichi" title="Aichi District, Aichi">Aichi District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Owari_Province" title="Owari Province">Owari Province</a>), He became a retainer of <a href="/wiki/Oda_Nobunaga" title="Oda Nobunaga">Oda Nobunaga</a> in 1550<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-.E4.BD.90.E3.80.85.E6.88.90.E6.94.BF.E8.B3.87.E6.96.99.E9.A4.A8_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-.E4.BD.90.E3.80.85.E6.88.90.E6.94.BF.E8.B3.87.E6.96.99.E9.A4.A8-1">[1]</a></sup> and was granted <a href="/wiki/Etch%C5%AB_Province" title="Etchū Province">Etchū Province</a> as a reward for helping <a href="/wiki/Shibata_Katsuie" title="Shibata Katsuie">Shibata Katsuie</a> fight the <a href="/wiki/Uesugi_clan" title="Uesugi clan">Uesugi clan</a>. After Nobunaga's death, in 1584 he joined <a href="/wiki/Tokugawa_Ieyasu" title="Tokugawa Ieyasu">Tokugawa Ieyasu</a> in unsuccessfully challenging <a href="/wiki/Toyotomi_Hideyoshi" title="Toyotomi Hideyoshi">Toyotomi Hideyoshi</a>. He submitted to Hideyoshi and his life was spared. In 1587, he was given a fief in <a href="/wiki/Higo_Province" title="Higo Province">Higo Province</a> in Kyushu. However, due to difficulties in suppressing a local revolt, he committed suicide (<a href="/wiki/Seppuku" title="Seppuku">seppuku</a>) by Hideyoshi's instruction in 1588.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-.E4.BD.90.E3.80.85.E6.88.90.E6.94.BF.E8.B3.87.E6.96.99.E9.A4.A8_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-.E4.BD.90.E3.80.85.E6.88.90.E6.94.BF.E8.B3.87.E6.96.99.E9.A4.A8-1">[1]</a></sup> His daughter married <a href="/wiki/Kuge" title="Kuge">kuge</a> <a href="/wiki/Takatsukasa_Nobufusa" title="Takatsukasa Nobufusa">Takatsukasa Nobufusa</a> and they had a son <a href="/wiki/Takatsukasa_Nobuhisa" title="Takatsukasa Nobuhisa">Nobuhisa</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
