<p><b>Associação Desportiva Confiança</b>, or <b>Confiança</b> as they are usually called, is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Brazilian_Football_League_Teams" title="Brazilian Football League Teams">Brazilian football team</a> from <a href="/wiki/Aracaju" title="Aracaju">Aracaju</a> in <a href="/wiki/Sergipe" title="Sergipe">Sergipe</a>, founded on May 1, 1936.</p>
<p>Confiança's greatest rival is <a href="/wiki/Club_Sportivo_Sergipe" title="Club Sportivo Sergipe">Sergipe</a>.</p>
<p>Home stadium is the Proletário Sabino Ribeiro stadium, capacity 4,000.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Associa%C3%A7%C3%A3o_Desportiva_Confian%C3%A7a&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On May 1, 1936, was founded in <a href="/wiki/Aracaju" title="Aracaju">Aracaju</a> a club called Associação Desportiva Confiança, after Epaminondas Vital and Isnard Cantalice watched a <a href="/wiki/Volleyball" title="Volleyball">volleyball</a> match between Cotinguiba and Aracaju at Bairro Industrial (Industrial Neighborhood, in <a href="/wiki/English_language" title="English language">English</a>). Joaquim Sabino Ribeiro Chaves, Epaminondas Vital and Isnard Cantalice then founded a <a href="/wiki/Basketball" title="Basketball">basketball</a> and volleyball club at Bairro Industrial.</p>
<p>In 1949, the club disputed its first <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> competition, getting the nickname Dragão do Bairro Industrial, which means <i>Industrial Neighborhood Dragon</i>, and playing in blue and white colors.</p>
<p>On May 1, 1955, Confiança, through its paranymph, called Joaquim Sabino Ribeiro Chaves, build its stadium, named <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Est%C3%A1dio_Prolet%C3%A1rio_Sabino_Ribeiro&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Estádio Proletário Sabino Ribeiro (page does not exist)">Proletário Sabino Ribeiro</a>.</p>
<p>In 1976, the club played for the first time in the <a href="/wiki/Campeonato_Brasileiro_S%C3%A9rie_A" title="Campeonato Brasileiro Série A">Campeonato Brasileiro First Division</a>.</p>
<p>In 1989, the club disputed the first edition of <a href="/wiki/Copa_do_Brasil" title="Copa do Brasil">Copa do Brasil</a>, being eliminated in the first round by <a href="/wiki/Esporte_Clube_Bahia" title="Esporte Clube Bahia">Bahia</a> after two 0–1 defeats.</p>
<p>In 2004, Confiança disputed <a href="/wiki/Campeonato_Brasileiro_S%C3%A9rie_C" title="Campeonato Brasileiro Série C">Campeonato Brasileiro Série C</a>, being eliminated in the third stage by <a href="/wiki/Villa_Nova_Atl%C3%A9tico_Clube" title="Villa Nova Atlético Clube">Villa Nova</a>.</p>
