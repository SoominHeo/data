<p><b>Sangmu Baseball Team</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko" xml:lang="ko">상무 야구단</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">Korean</a> professional <a href="/wiki/Baseball" title="Baseball">baseball</a> team founded in 2005. This means the executive director of the <a href="/wiki/Korea_Armed_Forces_Athletic_Corps" title="Korea Armed Forces Athletic Corps">Korea Armed Forces Athletic Corps</a>. Their home stadium is Sangmu Baseball Stadium in <a href="/wiki/Seongnam" title="Seongnam">Seongnam</a>.</p>
