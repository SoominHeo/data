<p><b>Shangcai County</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">上蔡县</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">上蔡縣</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Shàngcài Xiàn</span></i>) is a county of <a href="/wiki/Henan" title="Henan">Henan</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. It is under the administration of <a href="/wiki/Zhumadian" title="Zhumadian">Zhumadian</a> city.</p>
