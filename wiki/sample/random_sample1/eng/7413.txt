<p><b>São Filipe</b> is a <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_Cabo_Verde" title="Administrative divisions of Cabo Verde">concelho</a></i> (municipality) of <a href="/wiki/Cape_Verde" title="Cape Verde">Cape Verde</a>. Situated in the western part of the island of <a href="/wiki/Fogo,_Cape_Verde" title="Fogo, Cape Verde">Fogo</a>, it covers 49% of the island area, and is home to 60% of its population. Its seat is the town <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A3o_Filipe,_Cape_Verde" title="São Filipe, Cape Verde">São Filipe</a>.</p>
<p>Agriculture is the main employment of the municipality followed by tourism and industries primarily dominating the municipal seat.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Subdivisions">Subdivisions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=S%C3%A3o_Filipe,_Cape_Verde_(municipality)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Subdivisions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The municipality consists of two <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_Cabo_Verde" title="Administrative divisions of Cabo Verde">freguesias</a></i> (civil parishes):</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/S%C3%A3o_Louren%C3%A7o_(S%C3%A3o_Filipe)" title="São Lourenço (São Filipe)">São Lourenço</a></li>

</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=S%C3%A3o_Filipe,_Cape_Verde_(municipality)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It was created in 1991, when the older Municipality of Fogo was split in two, the southwestern part becoming the Municipality of São Filipe and the northeastern part becoming the Municipality of Mosteiros. In 2005, an eastern parish of the municipality was split off to become the Municipality of Santa Catarina do Fogo.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Sports">Sports</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=S%C3%A3o_Filipe,_Cape_Verde_(municipality)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Sports">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The city has the stadium, it has the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Estadio_5_de_Julho" title="Estadio 5 de Julho">Estadio 5 de Julho</a> which is used for football (soccer), a multisport complex named Simão Mendes<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> and a pavilion named Luzia Nunes. Major clubs include <a href="/wiki/Acad%C3%A9mica_do_Fogo" title="Académica do Fogo">Académica do Fogo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Botafogo_FC_(Cape_Verde)" title="Botafogo FC (Cape Verde)">Botafogo FC</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vulc%C3%A2nicos" title="Vulcânicos">Vulcânicos</a>.</p>
<p>Each year, the São Filipe Municipal Cup, a football (soccer) competition takes place each year in the months of September or October. It features clubs from the municipality. The 2016 edition featured two clubs from outside the municipality.</p>
