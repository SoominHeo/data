<p>The <b>2012 AFC Futsal Club Championship</b> was the 3rd <a href="/wiki/AFC_Futsal_Club_Championship" title="AFC Futsal Club Championship">AFC Futsal Club Championship</a>. It was held in <a href="/wiki/Kuwait_City" title="Kuwait City">Kuwait City</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kuwait" title="Kuwait">Kuwait</a> between July 1 and July 6, 2012.</p>
<p>On 21 November 2011 the AFC Futsal Committee, under the chairmanship of Guam’s Richard Lai, proposed to award the hosting rights of the 2012 AFC Futsal Club Championship to Kuwait<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The draw for the tournament was held on 26 April 2012 in Movenpick Hotel, Kuwait City.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
