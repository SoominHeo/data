<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sam_Pinto&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Modelling_career">Modelling career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sam_Pinto&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Modelling career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sam started out as a print-ad and TV commercial model. At the age of 10, she got her first modelling job, appearing in the posters for <a href="/wiki/Coca-Cola" title="Coca-Cola">Coca-Cola</a>. Other TV and print-ad credits include Sunsilk, SMART, Voice combo sandwich, Colgate, Palmolive, and Mc Donald's.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-pep_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-pep-1">[1]</a></sup> She was also an endorser for a clothing brand, BAYO.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Pinoy_Big_Brother">Pinoy Big Brother</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sam_Pinto&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Pinoy Big Brother">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Pinoy_Big_Brother:_Double_Up" title="Pinoy Big Brother: Double Up">Pinoy Big Brother: Double Up</a></div>
<p>She became known to people after being a part of the reality series, <a href="/wiki/Pinoy_Big_Brother:_Double_Up" title="Pinoy Big Brother: Double Up">Pinoy Big Brother: Double Up</a>. Originally a House B housemate, she moved to House A after a week of her stay inside Big Brother's House. She has been saved by her fans through voting on several eviction nights before finally getting the boot on the night of January 9, 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_TV_appearances">Early TV appearances</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sam_Pinto&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Early TV appearances">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Before her stint on the reality show, she was seen acting with <a href="/wiki/Mark_Herras" title="Mark Herras">Mark Herras</a> on a <i>Maynila</i> episode in GMA Network. She was also a finalist in the <i><a href="/wiki/Myx_VJ_Search#MYX_VJ_Search_2008" title="Myx VJ Search">Myx VJ Search 2008</a></i>. Her first regular appearance was in a television show called <i><a href="/wiki/Midnight_DJ" title="Midnight DJ">Midnight DJ</a></i>, opposite <a href="/wiki/Oyo_Boy_Sotto" title="Oyo Boy Sotto">Oyo Boy Sotto</a>. She joined the show as a cast regular in the middle of season 4. She played Max, the Midnight DJ crew's resident psychic. Her character was first introduced in the "Oink! Oink!" episode.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Music_career">Music career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sam_Pinto&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Music career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Pinto was featured on <a href="/wiki/Shehyee" title="Shehyee">Shehyee</a>'s single <i><a href="/wiki/Trip_Lang" title="Trip Lang">Trip Lang</a></i> in 2013.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="2010.E2.80.93present">2010–present</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sam_Pinto&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: 2010–present">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="ABS-CBN">ABS-CBN</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sam_Pinto&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: ABS-CBN">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sam has been dubbed as the 'IT-girl of the moment'.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2011)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> She has appeared on the cover of various magazines such as Woman Today, Gadgets Magazine, and most notably the covergirl for the July issue of <a href="/wiki/FHM" title="FHM">FHM</a> magazine in 2010.</p>
<p>She played Amanda, a "mangkukulam" and Elias' (<a href="/wiki/Jake_Cuenca" title="Jake Cuenca">Jake Cuenca</a>) love interest in the weekly primetime series <i>Agimat Presents</i>: <i><a href="/wiki/Elias_Paniki" title="Elias Paniki">Elias Paniki</a></i> in ABS-CBN. Sam will soon be seen on the big screen via <a href="/wiki/Petrang_Kabayo" title="Petrang Kabayo">Petrang Kabayo</a>, a remake of the popular 80's film, with <a href="/wiki/Vice_Ganda" title="Vice Ganda">Vice Ganda</a> in the titular role.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="GMA">GMA</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sam_Pinto&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: GMA">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After endless rumours and speculation of her transfer to <a href="/wiki/ABS-CBN" title="ABS-CBN">ABS-CBN</a>'s rival network, Sam herself confirmed the news in her Twitter account: "Thank you KAPUSO family for welcoming me! I'm so excited. So finally I can answer, yes I'm a KAPUSO now :)."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> She signed a three-year exclusive contract with the Kapuso channel and starring in several upcoming shows of GMA Network.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>Sam played Samara, a warrior princess, in the 2010 Official Entry to the <a href="/wiki/Metro_Manila_Film_Festival" title="Metro Manila Film Festival">Metro Manila Film Festival</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Si_Agimat_at_Si_Enteng_Kabisote" title="Si Agimat at Si Enteng Kabisote">Si Agimat at Si Enteng Kabisote</a>. The movie is top-billed by Sen. <a href="/wiki/Bong_Revilla" title="Bong Revilla">Bong Revilla</a> and <a href="/wiki/Vic_Sotto" title="Vic Sotto">Vic Sotto</a>; Sam played the former's love interest.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="FHM">FHM</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sam_Pinto&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: FHM">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In 2011 and 2012 she was voted as FHM Philippines' Sexiest Woman in the World.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> In 2013 she was rejected by <a href="/wiki/Marian_Rivera" title="Marian Rivera">Marian Rivera</a> on her <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Threepeat" title="Threepeat">threepeat</a> attempt (which was similar to <a href="/wiki/Katrina_Halili" title="Katrina Halili">Katrina Halili</a>'s threepeat attempt in 2008), as she fell on the second spot until 2014. As Rivera vacates her crown, she faced <a href="/wiki/Ellen_Adarna" title="Ellen Adarna">Ellen Adarna</a> and fellow Bubble Shaker <a href="/wiki/Andrea_Torres" title="Andrea Torres">Andrea Torres</a> who become a heavy favourite. However she fell at the fourth spot in 2015 which was won by <a href="/wiki/Jennylyn_Mercado" title="Jennylyn Mercado">Jennylyn Mercado</a>.</p>
