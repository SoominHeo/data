<p><b>Saint-Denis</b> (<small>French pronunciation: ​</small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_French" title="Help:IPA for French">[sɛ̃.də.ni]</a></span>, or unofficially <b>Saint-Denis de la Réunion</b> for disambiguation) is the <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pr%C3%A9fecture" title="Préfecture">préfecture</a></i> (administrative capital) of the French <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/R%C3%A9gion_d%27outre-mer" title="Région d'outre-mer">overseas region</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/D%C3%A9partement_d%27outre-mer" title="Département d'outre-mer">department</a> of <a href="/wiki/R%C3%A9union" title="Réunion">Réunion</a>, in the Indian Ocean. It is located at the island's northernmost point, close to the mouth of the <a href="/wiki/Rivi%C3%A8re_Saint-Denis" title="Rivière Saint-Denis">Rivière Saint-Denis</a>.</p>
<p>Saint-Denis is the most populous commune in the French <a href="/wiki/Overseas_department" title="Overseas department">overseas departments</a>. At the 1999 census, there were 158,139 inhabitants in the urban area of Saint-Denis, 131,557 of whom lived in the city (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Commune_in_France" title="Commune in France">commune</a>) of Saint-Denis proper and the remainder in the neighbouring commune of <a href="/wiki/Sainte-Marie,_R%C3%A9union" title="Sainte-Marie, Réunion">Sainte-Marie</a>.</p>

<p>Saint-Denis was founded by <a href="/wiki/%C3%89tienne_Regnault" title="Étienne Regnault">Étienne Regnault</a> in 1669 and became the capital of Réunion island in 1738.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Neighbourhoods">Neighbourhoods</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint-Denis,_R%C3%A9union&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Neighbourhoods">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The town has many neighbourhoods : Le Barachois, Bellepierre, Bois-de-Nèfles, La Bretagne (Le Cerf), Le Brûlé, Les Camélias, Centre-ville, Champ-Fleuri, <a href="/wiki/La_Montagne,_R%C3%A9union" title="La Montagne, Réunion">La Montagne</a>(Le Colorado, Ruisseau Blanc, Saint-Bernard), Montgaillard, La Providence, La Rivière Saint-Denis (La Redoute), Ruisseau des Noirs, Saint-François, Saint-Jacques, Sainte-Clotilde (Le Butor, le Chaudron, Commune Prima, <a href="/wiki/Domenjod" title="Domenjod">Domenjod</a>, Le Moufia), La Source, La Trinité, Vauban</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transport_and_accommodation">Transport and accommodation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint-Denis,_R%C3%A9union&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Transport and accommodation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The closest airport is <a href="/wiki/Roland_Garros_Airport" title="Roland Garros Airport">Roland Garros Airport</a> which is also the main international airport of <a href="/wiki/R%C3%A9union" title="Réunion">Réunion</a>.</p>
