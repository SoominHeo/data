<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint-Laurent-du-Var&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The town was founded in the 11th century when a hospice was founded under <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saint_Lawrence" title="Saint Lawrence">Saint Lawrence</a>'s protection. The main activity was to help passengers to cross the <a href="/wiki/Var_(river)" title="Var (river)">Var</a>, which became a border between <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_France" title="Kingdom of France">Kingdom of France</a> and <a href="/wiki/County_of_Nice" title="County of Nice">County of Nice</a> in 1481.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint-Laurent-du-Var&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>St. Laurent is the second-largest <a href="/wiki/Suburb" title="Suburb">suburb</a> of the city of <a href="/wiki/Nice" title="Nice">Nice</a>, after <a href="/wiki/Cagnes-sur-Mer" title="Cagnes-sur-Mer">Cagnes-sur-Mer</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Urban_community_of_Nice_C%C3%B4te_d%27Azur" title="Urban community of Nice CÃ´te d'Azur">urban community of Nice CÃ´te d'Azur</a>. It lies adjacent to it on the west side on the other side of the river <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Var_River" title="Var River">Var</a>.</p>
<p>Nowadays, the town has developed much and its population has been multiplied by ten in the last century and it is now part of <a href="/wiki/Nice_metropolitan_area" title="Nice metropolitan area">Nice metropolitan area</a>.</p>
<p>The suburb's positioning, close to Nice and with an abundance of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Flatlands" title="Flatlands">flatlands</a> - which is a rare resource in this region - led to the building of CAP 3000, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/C%C3%B4te_d%27Azur" title="CÃ´te d'Azur">CÃ´te d'Azur</a>'s biggest mall, and an industrial zone with many dynamic activities.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transportation">Transportation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint-Laurent-du-Var&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Transportation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>St. Laurent is located close to <a href="/wiki/Nice_C%C3%B4te_d%27Azur_Airport" title="Nice CÃ´te d'Azur Airport">"Nice CÃ´te d'Azur"</a> Airport, in the eastern side of Var river, <a href="/wiki/Nice" title="Nice">Nice</a>. The town also has a harbour, an interchange on the <a href="/wiki/A8_autoroute" title="A8 autoroute">motorway A8</a> and a <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Saint-Laurent-du-Var_railway_station&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Saint-Laurent-du-Var railway station (page does not exist)">railway station</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Marseille%E2%80%93Ventimiglia_railway" title="MarseilleâVentimiglia railway">Marseille-Toulon-Nice-Ventimiglia line</a>.</p>
