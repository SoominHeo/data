<p>The French <i><a href="/wiki/Flag_of_France" title="Flag of France">tricolore</a></i> is the official <b><a href="/wiki/National_flag" title="National flag">national flag</a></b> used in the <b><a href="/wiki/Collectivity_of_Saint_Martin" title="Collectivity of Saint Martin">Collectivity of Saint Martin</a></b>.</p>
<p>Two unofficial flags have been reported, one depicts the island's coat of arms on a white field, the other is seen below.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources.
