<p><b>AMD</b> often refers to <a href="/wiki/Advanced_Micro_Devices" title="Advanced Micro Devices">Advanced Micro Devices</a>, a semiconductor manufacturer. It may also refer to:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/AMD_Academy_of_Fashion_and_Design" title="AMD Academy of Fashion and Design">AMD Academy of Fashion and Design</a>, a German fashion school</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Acid_mine_drainage" title="Acid mine drainage">Acid mine drainage</a>, an ecological problem concerning very acidic water resulting from mining</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Age-related_macular_degeneration" title="Age-related macular degeneration">Age-related macular degeneration</a>, a disease of the eye</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Aircraft_Manufacturing_and_Design" title="Aircraft Manufacturing and Design">Aircraft Manufacturing and Design</a>, a manufacturer of airplanes</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Algorithmic_mechanism_design" title="Algorithmic mechanism design">Algorithmic mechanism design</a>, a field of economics</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Alliance_for_a_Democratic_Mauritania" title="Alliance for a Democratic Mauritania">Alliance for a Democratic Mauritania</a>, or <i>Alliance pour une Mauritanie démocratique</i>, former Mauritanian opposition movement</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/AMD_65" title="AMD 65">AMD 65</a> Automata Módosított Deszantfegyver (Automatic Modified Descent), a variation of the AK-47 rifle from Hungary</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Certified_AM_Directional_Specialist" title="Certified AM Directional Specialist">AM Directional Specialist</a>, a professional title regulated by the Society of Broadcast Engineers</li>
<li>amd, the <a href="/wiki/Berkeley_Automounter" title="Berkeley Automounter">Berkeley Automounter</a>, a daemon on Unix-like operating systems</li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/A_Modest_Destiny" title="A Modest Destiny">A Modest Destiny</a></i>, the webcomic by Sean Howard</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Armenian_dram" title="Armenian dram">Armenian dram</a>, a monetary unit of Armenia, abbreviation code derived from ISO 4217</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Asynchronous_module_definition" title="Asynchronous module definition">Asynchronous module definition</a>, a JavaScript API</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Atomic_Minerals_Directorate_for_Exploration_and_Research" title="Atomic Minerals Directorate for Exploration and Research">Atomic Minerals Directorate for Exploration and Research</a>, Hyderabad, India</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sardar_Vallabhbhai_Patel_International_Airport" title="Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport">Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport</a>, Ahmedabad, India, from its IATA airport code</li>
</ul>
