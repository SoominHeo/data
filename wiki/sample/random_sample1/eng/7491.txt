<p><b>Šavnik</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Serbian_Cyrillic" title="Serbian Cyrillic">Serbian</a> Cyrillic: Шавник, <small>pronounced </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Serbo-Croatian" title="Help:IPA for Serbo-Croatian">[ʃǎv̞niːk]</a></span>) is a city in <a href="/wiki/Montenegro" title="Montenegro">Montenegro</a>, on the <a href="/wiki/%C5%A0avnik_(river)" title="Šavnik (river)">Šavnik river</a>. It is the central settlement of the <a href="/wiki/%C5%A0avnik_Municipality" title="Šavnik Municipality">Šavnik Municipality</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%C5%A0avnik&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Šavnik is a relatively new settlement, founded in 1861. It was populated by internal migrants from other parts of Montenegro, mostly craftsmen, which were needed by local farmers. The town quickly became a center for the <a href="/wiki/Drobnjaci" title="Drobnjaci">Drobnjaci</a> region, and incorporated a post office, school, primary court, and military post.</p>
<p>Industrialization during the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/SFR_Yugoslavia" title="SFR Yugoslavia">SFR Yugoslavia</a> era mostly bypassed Šavnik, so did any major transit road or rail links, so the economy of town went into stagnation. Population of Šavnik and entire municipality is since in slow but steady decrease. Most of the residents are migrating to <a href="/wiki/Nik%C5%A1i%C4%87" title="Nikšić">Nikšić</a> and southern Montenegro, and Šavnik is often a synonym for a poor and deteriorating town.</p>
<p>The future road <a href="/wiki/Risan" title="Risan">Risan</a> - Nikšić - Šavnik - <a href="/wiki/%C5%BDabljak" title="Žabljak">Žabljak</a>, and proximity of Žabljak as a main mountain tourist resort in Montenegro may boost Šavnik's economic prospects.</p>
<p><br/></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transport">Transport</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%C5%A0avnik&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Transport">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Šavnik is situated on a regional road halfway between <a href="/wiki/Nik%C5%A1i%C4%87" title="Nikšić">Nikšić</a> and <a href="/wiki/%C5%BDabljak" title="Žabljak">Žabljak</a>, and it is its only link with the rest of Montenegro.</p>
