<p><b>Xiaogan</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh">孝感</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Xiàogǎn</span></i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Prefecture-level_city" title="Prefecture-level city">prefecture-level city</a> in east-central <a href="/wiki/Hubei" title="Hubei">Hubei</a> province, People's Republic of China, some 60 kilometres (37 mi) northwest of the provincial capital of <a href="/wiki/Wuhan" title="Wuhan">Wuhan</a>. According to the 2010 census, its population totaled 4,814,542, of whom 908,266 lived in the built-up (<i>or metro</i>) area of Xiaonan District.</p>
