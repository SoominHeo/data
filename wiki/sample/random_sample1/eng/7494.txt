<p><b>Xiaonan District</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans">孝南区</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant">孝南區</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Xiàonán Qū</span></i>) is a district of the city of <a href="/wiki/Xiaogan" title="Xiaogan">Xiaogan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hubei" title="Hubei">Hubei</a>, People's Republic of China. It is under the administration of <a href="/wiki/Xiaogan" title="Xiaogan">Xiaogan</a> City.</p>
