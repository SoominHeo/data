<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Arabic_use">Arabic use</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sham&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Arabic use">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Al-Sham" title="Al-Sham">Al-Sham</a>, endonym of the region bordering the eastern Mediterranean Sea, usually known as the Levant or the region of Syria: Syria, Lebanon, Palestine, Israel, Jordan, Cyprus and the Turkish Hatay Province
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bilad_al-Sham" title="Bilad al-Sham">Bilad al-Sham</a> the Caliphate province of the same region</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/Jund_al-Sham" title="Jund al-Sham">Jund al-Sham</a>, militant group based in Afghanistan, meaning "Army of Syria"</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sham_el-Nessim" title="Sham el-Nessim">Sham el-Nessim</a>, Egyptian holiday marking the beginning of spring</li>
<li>Sham, or Alsahm, the Arabic name for the star <a href="/wiki/Alpha_Sagittae" title="Alpha Sagittae">Alpha Sagittae</a></li>
</ul>
