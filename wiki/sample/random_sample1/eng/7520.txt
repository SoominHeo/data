<p><b>Xiangyin County</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">湘阴县</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">湘陰縣</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Xiāngyīn xiàn</span></i>) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Counties_of_China" title="Counties of China">county</a> in <a href="/wiki/Hunan" title="Hunan">Hunan Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>, it is under the administration of <a href="/wiki/Yueyang" title="Yueyang">Yueyang City</a>. Located on the southeastern shores of <a href="/wiki/Dongting_Lake" title="Dongting Lake">Lake Dongting</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Xiang_River" title="Xiang River">Xiang River</a> runs south to north through the middle lands of the county, the <a href="/wiki/Zi_River" title="Zi River">Zi River</a> merges into Dongting in the westeastern margin. Xiangyin is bordered by <a href="/wiki/Yueyang_County" title="Yueyang County">Yueyang County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yuanjiang" title="Yuanjiang">Yuanjiang City</a> to the north, <a href="/wiki/Heshan_District,_Yiyang" title="Heshan District, Yiyang">Heshan District</a> of <a href="/wiki/Yiyang" title="Yiyang">Yiyang</a> to the west, <a href="/wiki/Wangcheng_District" title="Wangcheng District">Wangcheng District</a> of <a href="/wiki/Changsha" title="Changsha">Changsha</a> to the south, <a class="mw-disambig" href="/wiki/Miluo" title="Miluo">Miluo City</a> to the east. It has an area of 1,581.5 km<sup>2</sup> (610.6 sq mi) with rough 770,000 of population, the county is divided into 14 township-level divisions, the <a href="/wiki/County_seat" title="County seat">county seat</a> is <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Wenxing&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Wenxing (page does not exist)">Wenxing Town</a>.</p>
