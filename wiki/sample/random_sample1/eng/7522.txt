<p>The <b>Championnat de France de Futsal</b> "Division 1" or "D1" is the premier <a href="/wiki/Futsal" title="Futsal">futsal</a> league in <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>, organized by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Fran%C3%A7aise_de_Football" title="Fédération Française de Football">Fédération Française de Football</a>. There are 12 teams in the first level, the team ranked first wins the title of national Champion and qualifies for the <a href="/wiki/UEFA_Futsal_Cup" title="UEFA Futsal Cup">UEFA Futsal Cup</a>. The teams ranked 11th and 12th are relegated to <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Championnat_de_France_de_Futsal_Division_2&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Championnat de France de Futsal Division 2 (page does not exist)">Division 2</a>.<br/>
<br/>
"D2" is the second level, and has two groups with 10 teams each. The teams ranked first in each group are promoted to Division 1. The teams ranked 8th to 10th in each group are relegated. Below is the regional level.</p>
