<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shannon_Kook&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Shannon Kook-Chun was born in <a href="/wiki/Johannesburg" title="Johannesburg">Johannesburg</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Africa" title="South Africa">South Africa</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> He then moved to attend the <a href="/wiki/National_Theatre_School_of_Canada" title="National Theatre School of Canada">National Theatre School of Canada</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> For the majority of his acting roles, he is credited as Shannon Kook.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>Born to a Mauritian father of Chinese descent and a South African mother, his early appearances on stage after graduation was for The Forbidden Phoenix at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lorraine_Kimsa_Theatre_for_Young_People" title="Lorraine Kimsa Theatre for Young People">Lorraine Kimsa Theatre for Young People</a>, <a href="/wiki/Citadel_Theatre" title="Citadel Theatre">Citadel Theatre</a> in <a href="/wiki/Toronto" title="Toronto">Toronto</a>, and the world premiering of the Madness of the Square at the <a href="/wiki/Factory_Theatre" title="Factory Theatre">Factory Theatre</a>, <a href="/wiki/Toronto" title="Toronto">Toronto</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ontario" title="Ontario">Ontario</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>.</p>
<p>A time came when Shannon got a break in television and movies, working for <a href="/wiki/CBC_Television" title="CBC Television">CBC Television</a>, and <a href="/wiki/HBO" title="HBO">HBO</a>. It seemed the stage celebrity had a humanitarian side to his personality, when the year 2010 saw Shannon traveling to <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a> on behalf of an international charity trust called <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Free_the_Children" title="Free the Children">Free the Children</a> to build a school. A documentary film was made on the missionary visit. Shannon also really enjoys a good cuddle during his spare time.</p>
<p>Since 2015, Kook has starred in the <a href="/wiki/Lesbian" title="Lesbian">Lesbian</a> <a href="/wiki/Web_series" title="Web series">web series</a> <a href="/wiki/Carmilla_(series)" title="Carmilla (series)"><i>Carmilla</i></a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
