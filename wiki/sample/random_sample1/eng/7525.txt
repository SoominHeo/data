<p><i><b>Shadows in Paradise</b></i> is a 2010 American action film starring <a href="/wiki/Mark_Dacascos" title="Mark Dacascos">Mark Dacascos</a>, <a href="/wiki/Armand_Assante" title="Armand Assante">Armand Assante</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tom_Sizemore" title="Tom Sizemore">Tom Sizemore</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Sofya_Skya" title="Sofya Skya">Sofya Skya</a> and was directed and written by producer, director, and writer <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=J._Stephen_Maunder&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="J. Stephen Maunder (page does not exist)">J. Stephen Maunder</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shadows_in_Paradise_(2010_film)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During a rescue operation in Iraq, Special Forces member Max Forrester (<a href="/wiki/Mark_Dacascos" title="Mark Dacascos">Mark Dacascos</a>) is devastated when Sasha (<a href="/wiki/Sofya_Skya" title="Sofya Skya">Sofya Skya</a>), his squad mate and fiancée, goes missing. Two years later, Max, now out of the military, receives a call from his ex-commander, Captain Dyer (<a href="/wiki/Bruce_Boxleitner" title="Bruce Boxleitner">Bruce Boxleitner</a>), informing him that Sasha has been seen alive and moving freely about on a place called Paradise Island. He also informs Max the military is preparing to go in and extract her with possible charges of <a href="/wiki/Desertion" title="Desertion">desertion</a> or worse. Max sets off on his own to Paradise Island to find Sasha and learn the truth before Shadow Company gets to her first. Max eventually finds Sasha and learns she is working undercover as part of <a href="/wiki/Al-Qaeda" title="Al-Qaeda">Al-Qaeda</a> and is about to expose a weapons deal that will have a heavy impact on their organization. The story culminates in a battle wherein it is revealed that Shadow Company, led by their leader Ghost (<a href="/wiki/Armand_Assante" title="Armand Assante">Armand Assante</a>), are in fact the weapons dealers who were using Sasha as an alibi to come to the island. Max and Sasha foil the weapons deal and recover the weapons in a spectacular showdown.</p>
