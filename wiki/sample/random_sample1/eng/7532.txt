<p>The <b>Xinan Wang</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chinese_character" title="Chinese character">Chinese</a>: 西南王; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">Pinyin</a>: Xīnán Wáng), literally meaning King of the Southwest, is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Go_competitions" title="Go competitions">Go competition</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/People%27s_Republic_of_China" title="People's Republic of China">China</a> sponsored by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Zhongguo_Qiyuan" title="Zhongguo Qiyuan">Zhongguo Qiyuan</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Outline">Outline</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Xinan_Wang&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Outline">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Xinan Wang is a fast game tournament where players get 30 seconds for each move. The format is a single knockout, and the final is only one game. 16 players participate from 5 different Wei-qi national teams from southwestern <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. The winner's purse is 30,000 ($3,600).</p>
