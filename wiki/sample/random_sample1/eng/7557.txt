<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Western Siberia</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Western Siberia</b> or <b>West Siberia</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Западная Сибирь</span>) is a part of <a href="/wiki/Siberia" title="Siberia">Siberia</a>. The area of the region is 2.5 million square kilometres. Nearly 80% of the Western Siberia's area is located within the <a href="/wiki/West_Siberian_Plain" title="West Siberian Plain">West Siberian Plain</a>. The largest rivers of the region is the <a href="/wiki/Irtysh_River" title="Irtysh River">Irtysh</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Ob_River" title="Ob River">Obi</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
