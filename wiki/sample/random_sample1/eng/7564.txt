<p><b>Seo Young</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">서영</span>; born June 21, 1984) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korean" title="South Korean">South Korean</a> actress. Debuted in the 2006 <a href="/wiki/Munhwa_Broadcasting_Corporation" title="Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation">Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation</a> (MBC) drama <i>Fox, What are you doing</i> and rose to fame with her sexy image. In 2010, she started her own clothing store called Shine S.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-daum_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-daum-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
