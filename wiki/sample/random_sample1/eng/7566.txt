<p><b>Seo Young-hee</b> (born June 13, 1979) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> actress. She is best known for her supporting role in thriller <i><a href="/wiki/The_Chaser_(film)" title="The Chaser (film)">The Chaser</a></i> (2008) and her award-winning lead role in <a href="/wiki/Horror_film" title="Horror film">horror film</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Bedevilled_(2010_film)" title="Bedevilled (2010 film)">Bedevilled</a></i> (2010).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
