<p><b>Seo In-seok</b> (born February 22, 1949) is a South Korean actor. Seo made his acting debut in 1976, and among his notable <a href="/wiki/Korean_drama" title="Korean drama">television series</a> are the <a href="/wiki/Historical_period_drama" title="Historical period drama">historical period dramas</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Taejo_Wang_Geon_(TV_series)" title="Taejo Wang Geon (TV series)">Emperor Wang Gun</a></i> (2000) and <i><a href="/wiki/Jeong_Do-jeon_(TV_series)" title="Jeong Do-jeon (TV series)">Jeong Do-jeon</a></i> (2014).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
