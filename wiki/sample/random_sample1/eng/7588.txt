<p>The <b>West Frisian Wikipedia</b> (<a href="/wiki/West_Frisian_language" title="West Frisian language">Frisian</a>: <span lang="fy" xml:lang="fy"><i>Frysktalige Wikipedy</i></span>) is the <a href="/wiki/West_Frisian_language" title="West Frisian language">West Frisian-language</a> edition of the free online <a href="/wiki/Encyclopedia" title="Encyclopedia">encyclopedia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia" title="Wikipedia">Wikipedia</a>. There are also editions of Wikipedia in the other two <a href="/wiki/Frisian_languages" title="Frisian languages">Frisian languages</a>, one in <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=North_Frisian_Wikipedia&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="North Frisian Wikipedia (page does not exist)">North Frisian</a> and the other in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saterland_Frisian_Wikipedia" title="Saterland Frisian Wikipedia">Saterland Frisian</a>. It was started on September 2, 2002. On July 11 there were approximately 25,023 articles and 11,584 registered users.</p>
