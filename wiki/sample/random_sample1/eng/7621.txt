<p><b>Sulbing</b> (설빙), is a dessert cafe chain based in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. As of 2014, the chain had over 490 retail stores in South Korea. <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It serves mainly various flavors of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bingsu" title="Bingsu">bingsu</a> (Korean shaved ice).</p>
