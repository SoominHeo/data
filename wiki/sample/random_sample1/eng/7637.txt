<p><b>Echinoida</b> is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Order(biology)" title="Order(biology)">order</a> of sea urchins in the <a href="/wiki/Class_(biology)" title="Class (biology)">class</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Echinoidea" title="Echinoidea">Echinoidea</a>. They are distinguished from other sea urchins by simultaneously possessing both an un-sculpted test and a feeding lantern with large plates fused across the top of each pyramid.</p>
