<p><b>Sŏngsan Station</b> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Railway_station" title="Railway station">railway station</a> in Sŏngsal-li, <a href="/wiki/Sepo_County" title="Sepo County">Sep'o</a> county, <a href="/wiki/Kangwon_Province_(North_Korea)" title="Kangwon Province (North Korea)">Kangwŏn</a> province, <a href="/wiki/North_Korea" title="North Korea">North Korea</a>, on the <a href="/wiki/Kangwon_Line" title="Kangwon Line">Kangwŏn Line</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Korean_State_Railway" title="Korean State Railway">Korean State Railway</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Hayato_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Hayato-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The station, along with the rest of the former <a href="/wiki/Gyeongwon_Line" title="Gyeongwon Line">Kyŏngwŏn Line</a>, was opened by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Japanese_occupation_of_Korea" title="Japanese occupation of Korea">Japanese</a> on 16 August 1914.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lineop_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-lineop-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
