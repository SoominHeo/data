<p><i><b>Ssa2</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko" xml:lang="ko">싸2</span>) is the second album by South Korean <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Singer" title="Singer">singer</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/PSY" title="PSY">PSY</a>. The album was released on January 1, 2002. The album contains 16 songs. The album was also released worldwide through <a href="/wiki/ITunes_Store" title="ITunes Store">iTunes</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-itunes_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-itunes-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
