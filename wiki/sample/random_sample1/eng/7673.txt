<p><i><b>The Longest Most Meaningless Movie in the World</b></i> is an <a href="/wiki/Underground_film" title="Underground film">underground</a> movie made in the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">UK</a> that runs to 48 hours long. No actual footage was shot for the project, which instead consists entirely of outtakes, commercials, strips of undeveloped film, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Academy_leader" title="Academy leader">Academy leader</a>, and other filmic castoff material, creating a seemingly endless stream of newsreel and <a href="/wiki/Stock_footage" title="Stock footage">stock footage</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (March 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<p>It was the longest film ever made at the time of its release,<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (March 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> but has since been superseded by many other films.</p>
