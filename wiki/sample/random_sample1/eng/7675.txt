<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Segoshi Station</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Segoshi Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">瀬越駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Segoshi-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> was a railway station on the <a href="/wiki/Rumoi_Main_Line" title="Rumoi Main Line">Rumoi Main Line</a> in <a href="/wiki/Rumoi,_Hokkaido" title="Rumoi, Hokkaido">Rumoi, Hokkaido</a>, Japan, operated by <a href="/wiki/Hokkaido_Railway_Company" title="Hokkaido Railway Company">Hokkaido Railway Company</a> (JR Hokkaido). The station closed on 4 December 2016.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Segoshi_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On 10 August 2015, JR Hokkaido announced its plans to close the 16.7 km section of the line beyond Rumoi to Mashike in 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hobidas20150810_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-hobidas20150810-1">[1]</a></sup> In April 2016, it was officially announced that the section from Rumoi to Mashike would be closing in December 2016, with the last services operating on 4 December.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-doshin20160408_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-doshin20160408-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
