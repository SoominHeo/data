<p><b>Sarah Lee Bolger</b> (born 28 February 1991) is an Irish actress. She is best known for her roles in the films <i><a href="/wiki/In_America_(film)" title="In America (film)">In America</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Stormbreaker_(film)" title="Stormbreaker (film)">Stormbreaker</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Spiderwick_Chronicles_(film)" title="The Spiderwick Chronicles (film)">The Spiderwick Chronicles</a></i>, as well as her award winning role as <a href="/wiki/Mary_I_of_England" title="Mary I of England">Lady Mary Tudor</a> in the TV series <i><a href="/wiki/The_Tudors" title="The Tudors">The Tudors</a></i>, and for guest starring as <a href="/wiki/List_of_Once_Upon_a_Time_characters#Princess_Aurora" title="List of Once Upon a Time characters">Princess Aurora</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Once_Upon_a_Time_(TV_series)" title="Once Upon a Time (TV series)">Once Upon a Time</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sarah_Bolger&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Bolger was born in <a href="/wiki/Dublin" title="Dublin">Dublin</a>, the daughter of Monica and Derek Bolger, who is a butcher.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-daly_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-daly-1">[1]</a></sup> She has a younger sister, actress Emma Bolger. She attended The Young People's Theatre School in Dublin and Loreto High School, Beaufort in <a href="/wiki/Rathfarnham" title="Rathfarnham">Rathfarnham</a> from 2003 to 2009.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sarah_Bolger&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Bolger starred in <i><a href="/wiki/In_America_(film)" title="In America (film)">In America</a></i> with her sister Emma. From 2008 to 2010, she portrayed <a href="/wiki/Mary_I_of_England" title="Mary I of England">Princess Mary Tudor</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Tudors" title="The Tudors">The Tudors</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-daly_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-daly-1">[1]</a></sup> Bolger starred in <i><a href="/wiki/Stormbreaker_(film)" title="Stormbreaker (film)">Stormbreaker</a></i> alongside <a href="/wiki/Alex_Pettyfer" title="Alex Pettyfer">Alex Pettyfer</a>. She also starred in the <a href="/wiki/The_Spiderwick_Chronicles_(film)" title="The Spiderwick Chronicles (film)">film adaptation</a> of the children's novel <i><a href="/wiki/The_Spiderwick_Chronicles" title="The Spiderwick Chronicles">The Spiderwick Chronicles</a></i>. She filmed a pilot called <i><a href="/wiki/Locke_%26_Key" title="Locke &amp; Key">Locke &amp; Key</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> and will star in the movie <i>Starbright</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>Bolger guest starred as <a href="/wiki/List_of_Once_Upon_a_Time_characters#Princess_Aurora" title="List of Once Upon a Time characters">Princess Aurora</a> in the second, third, and fourth seasons of the fairy tale drama, <i><a href="/wiki/Once_Upon_a_Time_(TV_series)" title="Once Upon a Time (TV series)">Once Upon a Time</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>Bolger played the female lead role, Umi, in the English version of the 2011 Studio Ghibli film <i><a href="/wiki/From_Up_on_Poppy_Hill" title="From Up on Poppy Hill">From Up on Poppy Hill</a></i>, and starred as Lucy in <i><a href="/wiki/As_Cool_as_I_Am_(film)" title="As Cool as I Am (film)">As Cool as I Am</a></i> in 2013. In 2014, she made an appearance on TV series, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mixology_(TV_series)" title="Mixology (TV series)">Mixology</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> Bolger co-starred in David Gelb's thriller film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Lazarus_Effect_(2015_film)" title="The Lazarus Effect (2015 film)">The Lazarus Effect</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2015, she starred in the AMC martial arts show <i><a href="/wiki/Into_the_Badlands_(TV_series)" title="Into the Badlands (TV series)">Into the Badlands</a></i> and the Freddie Steinmark biopic <i><a href="/wiki/My_All_American" title="My All American">My All American</a></i>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Other_projects">Other projects</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sarah_Bolger&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Other projects">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In January 2011, Bolger was selected to be in photographer <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Abosch" title="Kevin Abosch">Kevin Abosch's</a> project "The Face of Ireland"<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> alongside other Irish celebrities including <a href="/wiki/Sin%C3%A9ad_O%27Connor" title="Sinéad O'Connor">Sinéad O'Connor</a>, <a href="/wiki/Neil_Jordan" title="Neil Jordan">Neil Jordan</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Pierce_Brosnan" title="Pierce Brosnan">Pierce Brosnan</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
