<p><b>Sarah Jeffery</b> (born April 3, 1996<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Hollywood.com_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Hollywood.com-1">[1]</a></sup>) is a Canadian actress, singer and dancer. She is known for her work in the <a href="/wiki/Fox_Broadcasting_Company" title="Fox Broadcasting Company">Fox television</a> sci-fi drama <i><a href="/wiki/Wayward_Pines" title="Wayward Pines">Wayward Pines</a></i>, the <a href="/wiki/Disney_Channel" title="Disney Channel">Disney Channel</a> original movie <i><a href="/wiki/Descendants_(2015_film)" title="Descendants (2015 film)">Descendants</a></i>, and the <a href="/wiki/NBC" title="NBC">NBC</a> crime drama <i><a href="/wiki/Shades_of_Blue_(TV_series)" title="Shades of Blue (TV series)">Shades of Blue</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life">Life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sarah_Jeffery&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sarah Jeffery was born in <a href="/wiki/Vancouver" title="Vancouver">Vancouver</a>, <a href="/wiki/British_Columbia" title="British Columbia">British Columbia</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Hollywood.com_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Hollywood.com-1">[1]</a></sup> She has been singing, dancing and acting in musicals and theatrical productions since she was three years old.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> In between acting projects, Jeffery performs on stage with the semi-professional dance crew, Body.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sarah_Jeffery&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Jeffery's first major acting role was in the Cartoon Network television pilot <i>Aliens in the House</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Hollywood.com_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-Hollywood.com-1">[1]</a></sup> Shortly thereafter landed a lead role in the <a href="/wiki/DirecTV" title="DirecTV">DirecTV</a> original series <i><a href="/wiki/Rogue_(TV_series)" title="Rogue (TV series)">Rogue</a></i>, working opposite <a href="/wiki/Thandie_Newton" title="Thandie Newton">Thandie Newton</a>, playing the role of her daughter, Evie Travis.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-rogue_casting_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-rogue_casting-3">[3]</a></sup> She subsequently joined the cast of <a href="/wiki/Fox_Broadcasting_Company" title="Fox Broadcasting Company">Fox</a>'s <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sci-fi" title="Sci-fi">sci-fi</a> drama series <i><a href="/wiki/Wayward_Pines" title="Wayward Pines">Wayward Pines</a></i> playing the recurring role of Amy,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-wayward_pines_casting_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-wayward_pines_casting-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> opposite <a href="/wiki/Matt_Dillon" title="Matt Dillon">Matt Dillon</a> and <a href="/wiki/Carla_Gugino" title="Carla Gugino">Carla Gugino</a>.</p>
<p>In 2015, Jeffrey played the role of Audrey, daughter of Princess Aurora, in Disney Channel Original Movie, <i><a href="/wiki/Descendants_(2015_film)" title="Descendants (2015 film)">Descendants</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> She followed this up by playing the role of Audrey in the animated spinoff to the film, <i><a href="/wiki/Descendants:_Wicked_World" title="Descendants: Wicked World">Descendants: Wicked World</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>In January 2016, she began a supporting role in <a href="/wiki/Adi_Hasak" title="Adi Hasak">Adi Hasak</a>'s American <a href="/wiki/Police_procedural" title="Police procedural">police procedural</a> crime series <i><a href="/wiki/Shades_of_Blue_(TV_series)" title="Shades of Blue (TV series)">Shades of Blue</a></i>, playing Cristina Santos, the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Jennifer_Lopez" title="Jennifer Lopez">Jennifer Lopez</a>'s character;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> the series was renewed for a second season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> The same year Jeffrey starred in the comedy film <i>Be Somebody</i> with Matthew Espinosa.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
