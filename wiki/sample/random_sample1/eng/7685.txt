<p><b>Sara Foster</b> (born February 5, 1981<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TNYT_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-TNYT-3">[3]</a></sup>) is an American actress, best known for her role as Jen Clark on <i><a href="/wiki/90210_(TV_series)" title="90210 (TV series)">90210</a></i>. She was a <a href="/wiki/Model_(person)" title="Model (person)">fashion model</a> before moving to the <a href="/wiki/Film_industry" title="Film industry">film industry</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sara_Foster&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Foster guest-starred as herself on the <a href="/wiki/HBO" title="HBO">HBO</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Entourage_(U.S._TV_series)" title="Entourage (U.S. TV series)">Entourage</a></i>, in which she had a romantic tryst with character <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Chase" title="Vincent Chase">Vincent Chase</a> (played by actor <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Grenier" title="Adrian Grenier">Adrian Grenier</a>) before appearing on <i><a href="/wiki/Jimmy_Kimmel_Live!" title="Jimmy Kimmel Live!">Jimmy Kimmel Live!</a></i> in the episode "Talk Show". She briefly hosted <i><a href="/wiki/Entertainment_Tonight" title="Entertainment Tonight">Entertainment Tonight</a></i> spin-off <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ET_on_MTV" title="ET on MTV">ET on MTV</a></i> in 2002. Based on that she was cast as the lead <a href="/wiki/Femme_fatale" title="Femme fatale">femme fatale</a> in<i><a href="/wiki/The_Big_Bounce_(2004_film)" title="The Big Bounce (2004 film)">The Big Bounce</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> a remake of a movie adaptation of the same novel by <a href="/wiki/Elmore_Leonard" title="Elmore Leonard">Elmore Leonard</a>. She also appeared in the <a href="/wiki/Backstreet_Boys" title="Backstreet Boys">Backstreet Boys</a> video "<a href="/wiki/Shape_of_My_Heart" title="Shape of My Heart">Shape of My Heart</a>".</p>
<p>In 2004, she played crime-fighting bisexual spy Amy in <a href="/wiki/Angela_Robinson" title="Angela Robinson">Angela Robinson</a>'s film <i><a href="/wiki/D.E.B.S._(2004_film)" title="D.E.B.S. (2004 film)">D.E.B.S.</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> In 2009, she signed on to play Jen Clark, the sister of Naomi Clark (<a href="/wiki/AnnaLynne_McCord" title="AnnaLynne McCord">AnnaLynne McCord</a>), on <a href="/wiki/The_CW" title="The CW">The CW</a> drama series <i><a href="/wiki/90210_(TV_series)" title="90210 (TV series)">90210</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> Additionally, Foster stars in the 2010 science-fiction film <i><a href="/wiki/Psych_9" title="Psych 9">Psych 9</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> which was directed by Andrew Shortell.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sara_Foster&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Foster is the daughter of music producer <a href="/wiki/David_Foster" title="David Foster">David Foster</a> and former model Rebecca Foster, and is married to tennis player <a href="/wiki/Tommy_Haas" title="Tommy Haas">Tommy Haas</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-intimate_wedding_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-intimate_wedding-9">[9]</a></sup> On November 15, 2010, Haas announced on his website that Foster had given birth to a baby girl. Foster gave birth to another daughter on November 11, 2015.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
