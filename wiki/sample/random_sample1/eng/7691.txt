<p><b>Sergey Nikolaevich Burdin</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Бурдин Сергей Николаевич</span>; born March 2, 1970) is a retired <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russian</a> football player. His previous club was including Russian club <a href="/wiki/PFC_CSKA_Moscow" title="PFC CSKA Moscow">CSKA Moscow</a>, <a href="/wiki/FC_Chernomorets_Novorossiysk" title="FC Chernomorets Novorossiysk">Chernomorets Novorossiysk</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/FC_Gazovik-Gazprom_Izhevsk" title="FC Gazovik-Gazprom Izhevsk">Gazovik-Gazprom Izhevsk</a> and <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> club <a href="/wiki/Jeju_United_FC" title="Jeju United FC">Bucheon SK</a> (currently <a href="/wiki/Jeju_United_FC" title="Jeju United FC">Jeju United FC</a>), <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seongnam_Ilhwa_Chunma" title="Seongnam Ilhwa Chunma">Seongnam Ilhwa Chunma</a>.</p>
