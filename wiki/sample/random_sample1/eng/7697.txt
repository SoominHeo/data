<p>This is a <b>list of political parties in <a href="/wiki/Serbia" title="Serbia">Serbia</a></b>. Serbia has a <a href="/wiki/Multi-party_system" title="Multi-party system">multi-party system</a> with numerous <a href="/wiki/Political_party" title="Political party">parties</a>, in which no one party often has a chance of gaining power alone. Parties must cooperate to form <a href="/wiki/Coalition_government" title="Coalition government">coalition governments</a>.</p>
