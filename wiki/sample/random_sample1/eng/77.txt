<p><br/>
Year <b>1391</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MCCCXCI</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Sunday" title="Common year starting on Sunday">common year starting on Sunday</a> (link will display full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1391&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93December">January–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1391&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_6" title="June 6">June 6</a> – <a href="/wiki/Antisemitism" title="Antisemitism">Anti-Jewish</a> pogroms erupt in <a href="/wiki/Seville#Castilian_rule" title="Seville">Seville</a>, Spain. Many thousands of Jews are massacred and the violence spreads throughout Spain and Portugal.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_18" title="July 18">July 18</a> – <a href="/wiki/Tokhtamysh%E2%80%93Timur_war" title="Tokhtamysh–Timur war">Tokhtamysh–Timur war</a>: <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Kondurcha_River" title="Battle of the Kondurcha River">Battle of the Kondurcha River</a> – <a href="/wiki/Timur" title="Timur">Timur</a> defeats <a href="/wiki/Tokhtamysh" title="Tokhtamysh">Tokhtamysh</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Golden_Horde" title="Golden Horde">Golden Horde</a> in present day southeast Russia.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_5" title="August 5">August 5</a> – <a href="/wiki/Antisemitism" title="Antisemitism">Anti-Jewish</a> riots spread to <a href="/wiki/Toledo,_Spain" title="Toledo, Spain">Toledo, Spain</a> and <a href="/wiki/Barcelona" title="Barcelona">Barcelona</a>. Many Jews leave Barcelona after the following massacres, though a large number remain in the city.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Date_unknown">Date unknown</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1391&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Date unknown">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Manuel_II_Palaiologos" title="Manuel II Palaiologos">Manuel II Palaiologos</a> becomes <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Byzantine_emperor" title="Byzantine emperor">Byzantine emperor</a> after his father, <a href="/wiki/John_V_Palaiologos" title="John V Palaiologos">John V Palaiologos</a>, dies of a nervous breakdown due to his continued humiliation by the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a>.</li>
<li>Yusuf II succeeds <a href="/wiki/Muhammed_V_of_Granada" title="Muhammed V of Granada">Muhammed V</a> as <a href="/wiki/Nasrid_dynasty" title="Nasrid dynasty">Nasrid</a> Sultan of <a href="/wiki/Granada" title="Granada">Granada</a> (now southern Spain).</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Stephen_Dabi%C5%A1a_of_Bosnia" title="Stephen Dabiša of Bosnia">Stephen Dabiša</a> succeeds <a href="/wiki/Tvrtko_I_of_Bosnia" title="Tvrtko I of Bosnia">Stephen Tvrtko I</a> as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/King_of_Bosnia" title="King of Bosnia">King of Bosnia</a>.</li>
<li>Shah Mansur becomes leader of the <a href="/wiki/Timurid_dynasty" title="Timurid dynasty">Timurid</a> occupied <a href="/wiki/Muzaffarids_(Iran)" title="Muzaffarids (Iran)">Muzaffarid</a> Empire in central <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Persia" title="Persia">Persia</a>.</li>
<li>A group of <a href="/wiki/Muzaffarids_(Gujarat)" title="Muzaffarids (Gujarat)">Muzaffarids</a> under Zafar Khan Muzaffar establish a new Sultanate at <a href="/wiki/Gujarat" title="Gujarat">Gujarat</a> in western India.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vytautas_the_Great" title="Vytautas the Great">Vytautas the Great</a>, claimant to the throne of <a href="/wiki/Lithuania" title="Lithuania">Lithuania</a>, forms an alliance with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Muscovy" title="Muscovy">Muscovy</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Roman_I_of_Moldavia" title="Roman I of Moldavia">Roman I</a> succeeds <a href="/wiki/Petru_I_of_Moldavia" title="Petru I of Moldavia">Petru I</a> as Prince of <a href="/wiki/Moldavia" title="Moldavia">Moldavia</a> (now <a href="/wiki/Moldova" title="Moldova">Moldova</a> and eastern <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Konrad_von_Wallenrode" title="Konrad von Wallenrode">Konrad von Wallenrode</a> succeeds <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Konrad_Z%C3%B6llner_von_Rotenstein&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Konrad Zöllner von Rotenstein (page does not exist)">Konrad Zöllner von Rotenstein</a> as Grand Master of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Teutonic_Knights" title="Teutonic Knights">Teutonic Knights</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bridget_of_Sweden" title="Bridget of Sweden">Bridget of Sweden</a> is canonized by <a href="/wiki/Pope_Boniface_IX" title="Pope Boniface IX">Pope Boniface IX</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ushkuiniks" title="Ushkuiniks">Ushkuinik</a> pirates from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Novgorod" title="Novgorod">Novgorod</a> sack the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Muscovy" title="Muscovy">Muscovy</a> towns of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Zhukotin" title="Zhukotin">Zhukotin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kazan" title="Kazan">Kazan</a>.</li>
<li>The Chinese invent <a href="/wiki/Toilet_paper" title="Toilet paper">toilet paper</a> for use by their emperors.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Henry_I_Sinclair,_Earl_of_Orkney" title="Henry I Sinclair, Earl of Orkney">Henry I Sinclair, Earl of Orkney</a>, takes control of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Shetland_Islands" title="Shetland Islands">Shetland Islands</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Faroe_Islands" title="Faroe Islands">Faroe Islands</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/University_of_Ferrara" title="University of Ferrara">University of Ferrara</a> is founded at the <a href="/wiki/Italian_Peninsula" title="Italian Peninsula">Italian Peninsula</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ming_dynasty" title="Ming dynasty">Ming</a> government orders 50 million trees planted in <a href="/wiki/Nanjing" title="Nanjing">Nanjing</a> area.</li>
</ul>
