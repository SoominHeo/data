<p><b>ATC code P02</b> <i>Anthelmintics</i> is a therapeutic subgroup of the <a href="/wiki/Anatomical_Therapeutic_Chemical_Classification_System" title="Anatomical Therapeutic Chemical Classification System">Anatomical Therapeutic Chemical Classification System</a>, a system of alphanumeric codes developed by the <a href="/wiki/World_Health_Organization" title="World Health Organization">WHO</a> for the classification of <a href="/wiki/Pharmaceutical_drug" title="Pharmaceutical drug">drugs</a> and other medical products. Subgroup P02 is part of the anatomical group <a href="/wiki/ATC_code_P" title="ATC code P">P <i>Antiparasitic products, insecticides and repellents</i></a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>National issues of the ATC classification may include additional codes not present in this list, which follows the WHO version.</p>
