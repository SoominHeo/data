<p><b>Sevran</b> (<small>French pronunciation: ​</small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_French" title="Help:IPA for French">[sə.vʁɑ̃]</a></span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Communes_of_France" title="Communes of France">commune</a> in the northeastern suburbs of <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>, <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>. It is located 16.2 km (10.1 mi) from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kilometre_Zero" title="Kilometre Zero">center of Paris</a>.</p>

<p>As of 2013 the origins of over half of its resident are from outside France. The largest ethnic backgrounds within the foreign origins are from sub-Saharan Africa, Algeria, and Morocco.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Forgotten_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Forgotten-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2013 <i><a href="/wiki/The_Economist" title="The Economist">The Economist</a></i> stated that Sevran was one of the poorest areas of the <a href="/wiki/Paris_Metropolitan_Area" title="Paris Metropolitan Area">Paris Metropolitan Area</a>. As of 2013 36% of the residents are considered to be below the poverty line. The national average is 12%. About 75% of Sevran's residents live in <a href="/wiki/Subsidized_housing" title="Subsidized housing">subsidized housing</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Forgotten_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-Forgotten-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Culture">Culture</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sevran&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Culture">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sevran is home to an important music and dance school, the Espace François Mauriac, also known as the conservatoire de Sevran. Many important French and international musicians have taught there, including <a href="/wiki/Claude_Ballif" title="Claude Ballif">Claude Ballif</a>, <a href="/wiki/Allain_Gaussin" title="Allain Gaussin">Allain Gaussin</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Vincent_Decleire&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Vincent Decleire (page does not exist)">Vincent Decleire</a>. The French famous rapper <a href="/wiki/Kaaris" title="Kaaris">Kaaris</a> born to <a href="/wiki/Ivory_Coast" title="Ivory Coast">Ivory Coast</a> parents also originates from Sevran. There is a <a href="/wiki/Madrasa" title="Madrasa">Qurʾānic school</a> in Sevran.</p>
