<p><b>Seinan Jo Gakuin University</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">西南女学院大学</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Seinan Jogakuin Daigaku</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/Private_university" title="Private university">private</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Women%27s_colleges" title="Women's colleges">women's college</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kitakyushu,_Fukuoka" title="Kitakyushu, Fukuoka">Kitakyushu, Fukuoka</a>, Japan. The predecessor of the school was founded in 1922, and it was chartered as a university in 1994.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Enkaku_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Enkaku-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The school motto is <i>Gratitude and Service</i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">感恩奉仕</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Seinan_Jo_Gakuin_University&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>1922 - Girls' School <i>Seinan Jo Gakuin</i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">西南女学院</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> was established by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Southern_Baptist" title="Southern Baptist">Southern Baptist</a> missionary<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Enkaku_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Enkaku-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li>1935 - <i>Seinan Kasei Gakuin</i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">西南家政学院</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> was established<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li>1946 - <i>Seinan Jo Gakuin Senmon Gakko</i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">西南女学院専門学校</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> was established<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></li>
<li>1950 - Seinan Jo Gakuin Junior College was established(renamed "Seinan Jo Gakuin University Junior College" in 2004<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Enkaku_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-Enkaku-1">[1]</a></sup>)</li>
<li>1993 - Seinan Jo Gakuin University was established</li>
</ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Annual_events">Annual events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Seinan_Jo_Gakuin_University&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Annual events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>Entrance ceremony: In April, the entrance ceremony is held in the form of a Christian worship ceremony.</li>
<li>The anniversary of the founding: In July, the day is a school holiday but first-year students and teachers attend a ceremony. Seinan Jo Gakuin University celebrated the ninetieth anniversary of the founding of the school in 2012.</li>
<li>Open campus: Open campus is an event to introduce Seinan Jo Gakuin University to high school students thinking about entering the University. University students serve as staff to guide the high school students around the campus.</li>
<li>College Festival: In October, the event is held at Seinan Jo Gakuin University. There are many refreshment booths run by students.</li>
<li>Graduation ceremony: In March</li>
</ul>
