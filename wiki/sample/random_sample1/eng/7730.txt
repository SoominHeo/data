<p>This is a <b>list of diplomatic missions of Saint Vincent and the Grenadines</b>. <a href="/wiki/Saint_Vincent_and_the_Grenadines" title="Saint Vincent and the Grenadines">Saint Vincent and the Grenadines</a>' diplomatic network does not extend to any neighbouring <a href="/wiki/Caribbean" title="Caribbean">Caribbean</a> countries. The country also has tourism representatives and honorary consulate in other countries (not listed below).</p>
