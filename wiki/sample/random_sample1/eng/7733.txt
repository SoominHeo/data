<p><b>Saint George</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Parish_(administrative_division)" title="Parish (administrative division)">parish</a> of <a href="/wiki/Antigua_and_Barbuda" title="Antigua and Barbuda">Antigua and Barbuda</a> on the island of Antigua. It includes <a href="/wiki/Long_Island_(Antigua_and_Barbuda)" title="Long Island (Antigua and Barbuda)">Long Island</a> and the localities of <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Barnes_Hill&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Barnes Hill (page does not exist)">Barnes Hill</a>, Marble Hill, Crosbies, Hodges Bay, Paradise View, Coolidge, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Carlisle,_Antigua_and_Barbuda&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Carlisle, Antigua and Barbuda (page does not exist)">Carlisle</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fitches_Creek" title="Fitches Creek">Fitches Creek</a>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Osbourn,_Antigua_and_Barbuda&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Osbourn, Antigua and Barbuda (page does not exist)">Osbourn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Piggotts" title="Piggotts">Piggotts</a>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=New_Winthorpes&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="New Winthorpes (page does not exist)">New Winthorpes</a>, Picadilly, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Gunthorpes&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Gunthorpes (page does not exist)">Gunthorpes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sea_View_Farm" title="Sea View Farm">Sea View Farm</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Paynters&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Paynters (page does not exist)">Paynters</a>.</p>
<p><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/V.C._Bird_International_Airport" title="V.C. Bird International Airport">V.C. Bird International Airport</a> is in Saint George Parish. The head office of <a href="/wiki/LIAT" title="LIAT">LIAT</a> is on the airport grounds.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Stanford_Cricket_Ground" title="Stanford Cricket Ground">Stanford Cricket Ground</a> is also located in the parish.</p>
