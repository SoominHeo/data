<p>The <a href="/wiki/Parishes_of_Barbados" title="Parishes of Barbados">parish</a> of <b>Saint Joseph</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Parishes_of_Barbados" title="Parishes of Barbados">parish</a> of <a href="/wiki/Barbados" title="Barbados">Barbados</a> on the eastern side of the island. The parish is home to two of the more notable botanic gardens in the country - <a href="/wiki/Flower_Forest" title="Flower Forest">Flower Forest</a> and <a href="/wiki/Andromeda_Gardens" title="Andromeda Gardens">Andromeda Gardens</a>. Parts of St. Joseph are also referred to as the Scotland District, a reference to the appearance of the landscape.</p>
<p>The main town in the parish is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bathsheba_(Barbados)" title="Bathsheba (Barbados)">Bathsheba</a>.</p>
<p>One of the highest points in the island is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chimborazo,_Saint_Joseph,_Barbados" title="Chimborazo, Saint Joseph, Barbados">Chimborazo</a> in Saint Joseph. Saint Joseph is also the home of the "Soup Bowl" where international surfing competitions are held.</p>
