<p><i><b>Sexy Voice and Robo</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">セクシーボイスアンドロボ</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seinen" title="Seinen">seinen</a> <a href="/wiki/Manga" title="Manga">manga</a> by <a href="/wiki/Iou_Kuroda" title="Iou Kuroda">Iou Kuroda</a>. It was originally serialized in the semi-alternative manga magazine <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ikki_(magazine)" title="Ikki (magazine)">Ikki</a></i> between 2000 and 2003, and has also been published worldwide, including in <a href="/wiki/North_America" title="North America">North America</a> by <a href="/wiki/Viz_Media" title="Viz Media">Viz Media</a> and <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a> by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ponent_Mon&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ponent Mon (page does not exist)">Ponent Mon</a>. In 2007, it was adapted into a live action <a href="/wiki/Japanese_television_drama" title="Japanese television drama">Japanese television drama</a>, which aired every Tuesday on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nippon_Television" title="Nippon Television">Nippon Television</a> between April and June 2007, spanning a total of 11 episodes.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The manga won an Grand Prize at the 2002 <a href="/wiki/Japan_Media_Arts_Festival" title="Japan Media Arts Festival">Japan Media Arts Festival</a>, and has received critical acclaim around the world.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p><i>Sexy Voice and Robo</i> is currently unfinished. On his blog, Kuroda states that he intends to write more some day, but is too busy. <a class="external autonumber" href="http://kurodaiou.blog57.fc2.com/blog-entry-31.html" rel="nofollow">[1]</a></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Story">Story</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sexy_Voice_and_Robo&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Story">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The story is about the adventures of a 14-year-old girl named Nico Hayashi <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">林二湖</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Hayashi Niko</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> who uses her talents of changing and altering her voice to manipulate men over the phone who want to participate in <a href="/wiki/Enjo_k%C5%8Dsai" title="Enjo kōsai">Enjo kōsai</a>. Through this she learns a lot about human nature, and gains a keen understanding of people through their voices. An aging gangster notices her talents and decides to hire her to solve various cases. While Nico is on her first case, she meets with a geeky man named Iichiro Sudo <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">須藤威一郎</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Sudō Iichirō</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>, who has an obsession with robot toy models. This obsession that leads Nico to call him "Robo", and the two become an unlikely team. After completing her first case Nico proclaims herself "Sexy Voice" and a variety of short loosely linked character-driven adventures ensue.</p>
