<p><b>Shenae Grimes-Beech</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> (<span class="nowrap"><span class="IPA nopopups"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_English" title="Help:IPA for English">/<span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="/ʃ/ 'sh' in 'shy'">ʃ</span><span title="/ə/ 'a' in 'about'">ə</span><span title="/ˈ/ primary stress follows">ˈ</span><span title="'n' in 'no'">n</span><span title="/eɪ/ long 'a' in 'base'">eɪ</span></span>/</a></span></span>; born October 24, 1989<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TVGuide_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-TVGuide-2">[2]</a></sup>), credited for most of her career as <b>Shenae Grimes</b>, is a Canadian actress. She portrayed <a href="/wiki/List_of_90210_characters" title="List of 90210 characters">Annie Wilson</a> on <i><a href="/wiki/90210_(TV_series)" title="90210 (TV series)">90210</a></i>, a <a href="/wiki/Spin-off_(media)" title="Spin-off (media)">spin-off</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Beverly_Hills,_90210" title="Beverly Hills, 90210">Beverly Hills, 90210</a></i>. Prior to that, she had a recurring role on the television series <i><a href="/wiki/Naturally,_Sadie" title="Naturally, Sadie">Naturally, Sadie</a></i>, and played <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Darcy_Edwards" title="Darcy Edwards">Darcy Edwards</a> on <i><a href="/wiki/Degrassi:_The_Next_Generation" title="Degrassi: The Next Generation">Degrassi: The Next Generation</a></i> for four seasons. Grimes played a cameo role in the horror film <i><a href="/wiki/Scream_4" title="Scream 4">Scream 4</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shenae_Grimes&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Grimes was born in <a href="/wiki/Toronto" title="Toronto">Toronto, Ontario</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TVGuide_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-TVGuide-2">[2]</a></sup> She has three siblings, Aiden, Liam and Maya. She attended <a href="/wiki/Forest_Hill,_Toronto" title="Forest Hill, Toronto">Forest Hill</a> <a href="/wiki/State_school" title="State school">Public School</a> for elementary school. She attended Forest Hill Collegiate Institute, along with her Degrassi co-star, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Aubrey_Graham" title="Aubrey Graham">Aubrey Graham</a> (Drake). She interned at Toronto's <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fashion_Television" title="Fashion Television">Fashion Television</a> as part of her high school education.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TVGuide-bio_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-TVGuide-bio-3">[3]</a></sup> Grimes attended City Academy, a private school for her last two years of high school.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shenae_Grimes&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Beginning with a recurring role in 2004, Grimes became a lead character on <i><a href="/wiki/Degrassi:_The_Next_Generation" title="Degrassi: The Next Generation">Degrassi: The Next Generation</a></i> in 2006, playing the role of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Darcy_Edwards" title="Darcy Edwards">Darcy Edwards</a>. In 2008, Grimes left the series and Canada after being cast as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Annie_Wilson_(90210_character)" title="Annie Wilson (90210 character)">Annie Wilson</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/90210_(TV_series)" title="90210 (TV series)"><i>90210</i></a></i>, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_CW_Television_Network" title="The CW Television Network">CW's</a> <a href="/wiki/Spin-off_(media)" title="Spin-off (media)">spin-off</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/Beverly_Hills,_90210" title="Beverly Hills, 90210">Beverly Hills, 90210</a></i>.</p>
<p>She also appeared in <i><a href="/wiki/Picture_This_(film)" title="Picture This (film)">Picture This</a></i> with <a href="/wiki/Ashley_Tisdale" title="Ashley Tisdale">Ashley Tisdale</a> and with her former <i>Degrassi: The Next Generation</i> co-star <a href="/wiki/Lauren_Collins" title="Lauren Collins">Lauren Collins</a>, and then in <i><a href="/wiki/True_Confessions_of_a_Hollywood_Starlet" title="True Confessions of a Hollywood Starlet">True Confessions of a Hollywood Starlet</a></i> alongside singer <a href="/wiki/JoJo_(singer)" title="JoJo (singer)">JoJo</a>.</p>
<p>In 2009, Grimes was named one of the world's most beautiful people, without makeup, by <i><a href="/wiki/People_(magazine)" title="People (magazine)">People</a></i> magazine.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> She also appeared in a <a href="/wiki/Music_video" title="Music video">music video</a> for rock band <a href="/wiki/Our_Lady_Peace" title="Our Lady Peace">Our Lady Peace</a>'s single "<a href="/wiki/Burn_Burn_(album)" title="Burn Burn (album)">All You Did Was Save My Life</a>".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>Grimes appears in the 2010 silent fashion short film <i>Unzipped</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> She wrote and directed a 2010 music video for her song "Myself and I", which features an appearance by her <i>90210</i> co-star <a href="/wiki/Ryan_Eggold" title="Ryan Eggold">Ryan Eggold</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> She also participated in <a href="/wiki/YouTube" title="YouTube">YouTuber</a> <a href="/wiki/Freddie_Wong" title="Freddie Wong">Freddie Wong</a>'s video "Gun Size Matters".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> She made a cameo in the horror film <i><a href="/wiki/Scream_4" title="Scream 4">Scream 4</a></i>, released in April 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2011, she traveled to Japan with her <i>90210</i> costars <a href="/wiki/AnnaLynne_McCord" title="AnnaLynne McCord">AnnaLynne McCord</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jessica_Stroup" title="Jessica Stroup">Jessica Stroup</a> to attend the Asia Girls Explosion (AGE) in Tokyo, after filming 90210's third season finale.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Zimbio_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-Zimbio-10">[10]</a></sup> Grimes was caught up in Japan due to the events of the tsunami and earthquakes. According to the actress, she was at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Buddha" title="Buddha">Buddha</a>'s temple at the time when the earthquakes first occurred.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TVREPLAY_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-TVREPLAY-11">[11]</a></sup> Consequently, she started a campaign called "Spread The Heart" with the aim of supporting Japan by victim relief after the events of the tsunami and earthquakes. Many celebrities joined the campaign which later became a big movement.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Shenae_Grimes:_Spread_The_Heart_12-0"><a href="#cite_note-Shenae_Grimes:_Spread_The_Heart-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<p>In May 2011, during her <i>90210</i> hiatus, she took a six-week internship at <i><a href="/wiki/Teen_Vogue" title="Teen Vogue">Teen Vogue</a></i> in New York City. She stated that working as an editor for a fashion magazine has always been her dream.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-.2790210.27_Star_Shenae_Grimes:_Grateful_for_.27Amazing.27_Teen_Vogue_Internship_13-0"><a href="#cite_note-.2790210.27_Star_Shenae_Grimes:_Grateful_for_.27Amazing.27_Teen_Vogue_Internship-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
<p>In the fall of 2011, she directed a music video for <a href="/wiki/Megan_and_Liz" title="Megan and Liz">Megan and Liz</a>'s original song "Are You Happy Now?" to raise awareness about anti-bullying, in partnership with DoSomething.org.</p>
<p>In 2013, Grimes starred in the film <i><a href="/wiki/Sugar_(2013_film)" title="Sugar (2013 film)">Sugar</a></i> about a runaway girl living on the streets of <a href="/wiki/Venice,_Los_Angeles" title="Venice, Los Angeles">Venice, Los Angeles</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shenae_Grimes&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Grimes began dating British model and musician <a href="/wiki/Josh_Beech" title="Josh Beech">Josh Beech</a> in March 2012. In December 2012, after nine months of dating, Grimes announced that she and Beech were engaged.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup> They were married on May 10, 2013 in <a href="/wiki/Ashford,_Kent" title="Ashford, Kent">Ashford, Kent</a>, England.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup></p>
