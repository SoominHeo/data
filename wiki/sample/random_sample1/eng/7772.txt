<p><b>Siobhan Margaret Finneran</b> (born 2 January 1966) is an English television and film actress.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> She is known for her regular roles in the <a href="/wiki/BBC" title="BBC">BBC</a> drama <i><a href="/wiki/Clocking_Off" title="Clocking Off">Clocking Off</a></i> (2000-2002), <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ITV_Network" title="ITV Network">ITV</a> comedy <i><a href="/wiki/Benidorm_(TV_series)" title="Benidorm (TV series)">Benidorm</a></i> (2007-2015), ITV drama <i><a href="/wiki/Downton_Abbey" title="Downton Abbey">Downton Abbey</a></i> (2010-2013) and BBC drama <i><a href="/wiki/Happy_Valley_(TV_series)" title="Happy Valley (TV series)">Happy Valley</a></i> (2014-). She also starred as Rita in the 1987 film, <i><a href="/wiki/Rita,_Sue_and_Bob_Too" title="Rita, Sue and Bob Too">Rita, Sue and Bob Too</a></i> and as Mrs Swift in the 2013 film, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Selfish_Giant_(2013_film)" title="The Selfish Giant (2013 film)">The Selfish Giant</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Siobhan_Finneran&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Finneran's first major role was as Rita in the 1986 film, <i><a href="/wiki/Rita,_Sue_and_Bob_Too" title="Rita, Sue and Bob Too">Rita, Sue and Bob Too</a></i> opposite <a href="/wiki/Michelle_Holmes" title="Michelle Holmes">Michelle Holmes</a> and <a href="/wiki/George_Costigan" title="George Costigan">George Costigan</a>. She went on to appear in the British soap operas <i><a href="/wiki/Coronation_Street" title="Coronation Street">Coronation Street</a></i> (1989) and <i><a href="/wiki/Emmerdale" title="Emmerdale">Emmerdale</a></i> (1998). Since 2007, she has played Janice in <a href="/wiki/ITV_(TV_network)" title="ITV (TV network)">ITV</a>'s comedy drama <i><a href="/wiki/Benidorm_(TV_series)" title="Benidorm (TV series)">Benidorm</a></i>, appearing in all 6 series and both specials. She appeared in the first 2 episodes of the seventh series, however the Garvey's family left, she is scheduled to return in Series 9 (2017) with the Garvey's. She was also a regular cast member of period drama series <i><a href="/wiki/Downton_Abbey" title="Downton Abbey">Downton Abbey</a></i>, appearing as lady's maid Sarah O'Brien from 2010-2013. Since leaving <i>Downton Abbey</i>, she has had regular roles in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Syndicate_(TV_series)" title="The Syndicate (TV series)">The Syndicate</a></i> (2013) and <i><a href="/wiki/Happy_Valley_(TV_series)" title="Happy Valley (TV series)">Happy Valley</a></i> (2014). For her role as Mrs Swift in the film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Selfish_Giant_(2013_film)" title="The Selfish Giant (2013 film)">The Selfish Giant</a></i> (2013), she was nominated for a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/British_Independent_Film_Award" title="British Independent Film Award">British Independent Film Award</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Siobhan_Finneran&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Finneran was married to former <i><a href="/wiki/Heartbeat_(UK_TV_series)" title="Heartbeat (UK TV series)">Heartbeat</a></i> star <a href="/wiki/Mark_Jordon" title="Mark Jordon">Mark Jordon</a> from 1997 to 2014. They have two children, Joseph and Poppy.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RTEten_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-RTEten-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
