<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Location">Location</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sheema_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Location">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sheema District is bordered by <a href="/wiki/Buhweju_District" title="Buhweju District">Buhweju District</a> to the north, <a href="/wiki/Mbarara_District" title="Mbarara District">Mbarara District</a> to the east, <a href="/wiki/Ntungamo_District" title="Ntungamo District">Ntungamo District</a> to the south, <a href="/wiki/Mitooma_District" title="Mitooma District">Mitooma District</a> to the southwest and <a href="/wiki/Bushenyi_District" title="Bushenyi District">Bushenyi District</a> to the west. <a href="/wiki/Kibingo,_Uganda" title="Kibingo, Uganda">Kibingo</a>, where the district headquarters are located,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> lies approximately 33 kilometres (21 mi), by road, west of <a href="/wiki/Mbarara" title="Mbarara">Mbarara</a>, the largest city in <a href="/wiki/Ankole_sub-region" title="Ankole sub-region">Ankole sub-region</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The coordinates of the district are:00 32S, 30 24E.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Overview">Overview</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sheema_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Overview">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sheema District was created by Act of the <a href="/wiki/Parliament_of_Uganda" title="Parliament of Uganda">Ugandan Parliament</a>, and it became operational on 1 July 2010. Prior to that the district was known as <i>Sheema County</i> and was part of <a href="/wiki/Bushenyi_District" title="Bushenyi District">Bushenyi District</a>. The district is part of <a href="/wiki/Ankole_sub-region" title="Ankole sub-region">Ankole sub-region</a>. The districts that compose Ankole sub-region include the following districts: 1. <a href="/wiki/Buhweju_District" title="Buhweju District">Buhweju District</a> 2. <a href="/wiki/Bushenyi_District" title="Bushenyi District">Bushenyi District</a> 3. <a href="/wiki/Ibanda_District" title="Ibanda District">Ibanda District</a> 4. <a href="/wiki/Isingiro_District" title="Isingiro District">Isingiro District</a> 5. <a href="/wiki/Kiruhura_District" title="Kiruhura District">Kiruhura District</a> 6. <a href="/wiki/Mitooma_District" title="Mitooma District">Mitooma District</a> 7. <a href="/wiki/Ntungamo_District" title="Ntungamo District">Ntungamo District</a> 8. <a href="/wiki/Rubirizi_District" title="Rubirizi District">Rubirizi District</a> and 9. Sheema District. The sub-region was home to an estimated 2.2 million people in 2002, according to national census conducted that year.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Population">Population</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sheema_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Population">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 1991, the national population census estimated the district population at about 153,000. The national census in 2002 estimated the population of the district at about 180,200. In 2012, the population of Sheema District was estimated at approximately 220,200.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
