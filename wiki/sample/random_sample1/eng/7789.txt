<p><b>Šentjur</b> (<small>pronounced </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Slovene" title="Help:IPA for Slovene">[ʃɛnˈtjuːɾ]</a></span> or <span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Slovene" title="Help:IPA for Slovene">[ʃənˈtjuːɾ]</a></span>; <a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a>: <span lang="de" xml:lang="de"><i>Sankt Georgen bei Cilli</i></span>) is a town in eastern <a href="/wiki/Slovenia" title="Slovenia">Slovenia</a>. It is the seat of the <a href="/wiki/Municipality_of_%C5%A0entjur" title="Municipality of Šentjur">Municipality of Šentjur</a>. The town lies on the <a href="/wiki/Voglajna" title="Voglajna">Voglajna River</a> east of <a href="/wiki/Celje" title="Celje">Celje</a>. The area was part of the traditional region of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lower_Styria" title="Lower Styria">Styria</a>. The municipality is now included in the <a href="/wiki/Savinja_Statistical_Region" title="Savinja Statistical Region">Savinja Statistical Region</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Name">Name</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%C5%A0entjur&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Name">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name of the settlement was changed from <i>Sveti Jurij pri Celju</i> (literally, 'Saint George near Celje') to <i>Šentjur pri Celju</i> in 1952.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The town was renamed again from <i>Šentjur pri Celju</i> to <i>Šentjur</i> in 1990.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Church">Church</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%C5%A0entjur&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Church">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Parish_church" title="Parish church">parish church</a> from which the settlement gets its name is dedicated to <a href="/wiki/Saint_George" title="Saint George">Saint George</a> (<a href="/wiki/Slovene_language" title="Slovene language">Slovene</a>: <span lang="sl" xml:lang="sl"><i>sveti Jurij</i></span>, colloquially <i>šent Jur(ij)</i>) and belongs to the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Diocese_of_Celje" title="Roman Catholic Diocese of Celje">Roman Catholic Diocese of Celje</a>. It was built between 1708 and 1721.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
