<p><i><b>Hello Baby</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">헬로 베이비</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> reality show where celebrities experience parenthood by raising children ages 5 and under. The main purpose is to see whether or not the celebrities make "good" parents.</p>
<p>The show already has 7 seasons, and has featured the groups, <a href="/wiki/Girls%27_Generation" title="Girls' Generation">Girls' Generation</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/SHINee" title="SHINee">SHINee</a>, <a href="/wiki/T-ara" title="T-ara">T-ara</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sistar" title="Sistar">Sistar</a>, <a href="/wiki/MBLAQ" title="MBLAQ">MBLAQ</a>, <a href="/wiki/B1A4" title="B1A4">B1A4</a> and <a href="/wiki/Boyfriend_(band)" title="Boyfriend (band)">Boyfriend</a>. With the exception of Girls' Generation's season which ran for 22 episodes, the seasons that followed all have 12 episodes each. It airs every Tuesday on KBS Joy.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-boyfriend1_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-boyfriend1-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Format">Format</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hello_Baby&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Format">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Each season, an idol group is given an infant or toddler to take care of, allowing them to experience parenthood to an extent.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> In the first season, <a href="/wiki/Girls%27_Generation" title="Girls' Generation">Girls' Generation</a> became 'mothers' to a nine-month-old boy. This was also the case for the boy band, <a href="/wiki/Shinee" title="Shinee">Shinee</a>, in the second season, though the child is four years old. In the third season, the girl group <a href="/wiki/T-ara" title="T-ara">T-ara</a> had to take care of three toddlers.</p>
<p>The past three seasons, each group had to take care of the babies on their own, with some male celebrities acting as guest 'dads' in the first season. In the fourth season, <a href="/wiki/Sistar" title="Sistar">Sistar</a> became mothers to a baby boy, with <a href="/wiki/Super_Junior" title="Super Junior">Super Junior</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Leeteuk" title="Leeteuk">Leeteuk</a> as the father.</p>
<p>While all of the show's seasons have different concepts, they are still similar in ways such as the 'parents' being given missions and activities to do for the day with the children.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Seasons">Seasons</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hello_Baby&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Seasons">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Season_1:_Girls.27_Generation.27s_Hello_Baby">Season 1: <i>Girls' Generation's Hello Baby</i></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hello_Baby&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Season 1: Girls' Generation's Hello Baby">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Hello Baby's first season premiered on June 23, 2009. With 9 members, <a href="/wiki/Girls%27_Generation" title="Girls' Generation">Girls' Generation</a> became mothers to a nine-month old boy named Cho Kyungsan.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> In the first episode, the members are given pointers about child care before they meet the baby. Each episode, guest fathers come on the show to help. At the end of the day, the dads pick 'best mom' who is given certain privileges and a 'worst mom' who is given some punishment, usually clean-up duties. They also occasionally play games and other activities. Girls' Generation's season was well-received, and its 5th episode ranked first among cable channels in its time slot.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> The show won 'Best Picture for Variety' at the 4th Korean Cable TV Awards in 2009.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> It ended with a total of 22 episodes on November 17, 2009.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Season_2:_Shinee.27s_Hello_Baby">Season 2: <i>Shinee's Hello Baby</i></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hello_Baby&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Season 2: Shinee's Hello Baby">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The second season started on January 19, 2010, and it features the boy band, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/SHINee" title="SHINee">SHINee</a>, who have to take care of a 4-year-old toddler named Jung Yoo-geun. Like its previous season, the members have to deal with daily problems with having a child, such as preparing his meals, calming him down when he cries and doing other activities such as playing with him or teaching him new words. Because the members are not always with the child, they frequently sent him videos when they are away. In the final episode, Yoo-geun is given a time capsule by his 'fathers' which he would open when he turns 21.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> The season ended on April 6, 2010, with 12 episodes and a special episode.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Season_3:_T-ara.27s_Hello_Baby">Season 3: <i>T-ara's Hello Baby</i></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hello_Baby&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Season 3: T-ara's Hello Baby">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The third season, featuring girl group <a href="/wiki/T-ara" title="T-ara">T-ara</a>, started on November 16, 2010. For several months, the group act as mothers to three young brothers named <a href="/wiki/Moon_Mason" title="Moon Mason">Moon Mason</a>, Moon Mavin and Moon Maden.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Season_4:_Sistar_and_Leeteuk.27s_Hello_Baby">Season 4: <i>Sistar and Leeteuk's Hello Baby</i></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hello_Baby&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Season 4: Sistar and Leeteuk's Hello Baby">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The fourth season started on September 2, 2011. It portrays the four <a href="/wiki/Sistar" title="Sistar">Sistar</a> members (<a href="/wiki/Hyolyn" title="Hyolyn">Hyolyn</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yoon_Bora" title="Yoon Bora">Bora</a>, <a href="/wiki/Soyou" title="Soyou">Soyou</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kim_Dasom" title="Kim Dasom">Dasom</a>) acting as mothers and <a href="/wiki/Super_Junior" title="Super Junior">Super Junior</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Leeteuk" title="Leeteuk">Leeteuk</a> as a father taking care of a baby named Kim Kyumin (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 김규민). The season finished broadcasting on November 18, 2011.</p>
<p>This season is similar to that of Leeteuk's <i>Find Mom</i> project. It shows the process of finding the favorite mom amongst the four Sistar members. Unlike the past seasons, it was the baby who was leading the shows, not the parents.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>

<p>The fifth season stars boy band <a href="/wiki/MBLAQ" title="MBLAQ">MBLAQ</a> as fathers of three multi-cultural children: Dayoung (<a href="/wiki/Vietnamese_people_in_Korea" title="Vietnamese people in Korea">Vietnamese-Korean</a>), Lauren (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Korean-Canadian" title="Korean-Canadian">Korean-Canadian</a>) and Leo (<a href="/wiki/French_people_in_Korea" title="French people in Korea">French-Korean</a>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> The season began airing on January 19, 2012 and ended on April 5, 2012 with a total of 12 episodes. Like other seasons of <i>Hello Baby</i>, MBLAQ had to fulfill the task as parents to meet the needs of their kids.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Season_6:_B1A4.27s_Hello_Baby">Season 6: <i>B1A4's Hello Baby</i></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hello_Baby&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Season 6: B1A4's Hello Baby">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The sixth season features boy band <a href="/wiki/B1A4" title="B1A4">B1A4</a> as fathers to a couple of countryside kids, Eunsol and Hyunwoo. The concept of this season is getting to know <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a>, since all the members of the band came from the countryside as well.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
<p>This season not only follows the established format of the care and growth of the children, but also unveils the members' journey into Seoul, since none of the members are from Seoul, as well as the children, who are not from Seoul either.</p>
