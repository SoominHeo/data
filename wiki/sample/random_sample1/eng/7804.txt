<p><b>Sonia Bohosiewicz</b> (born 9 December 1975) is a Polish actress. She has appeared in such films as <i><a href="/wiki/Rezerwat" title="Rezerwat">Rezerwat</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Ob%C5%82awa" title="Obława">Obława</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Polish-Russian_War_(film)" title="Polish-Russian War (film)">Polish-Russian War</a></i> and the television series <i><a href="/wiki/Czas_honoru" title="Czas honoru">Czas honoru</a></i>.</p>
