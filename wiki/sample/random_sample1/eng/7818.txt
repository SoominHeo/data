<p><b>Molgulidae</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Family_(biology)" title="Family (biology)">family</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tunicate" title="Tunicate">tunicates</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Class_(biology)" title="Class (biology)">class</a> <a href="/wiki/Ascidiacea" title="Ascidiacea">Ascidiacea</a>. Following a revision in 2007, the family <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hexacrobylidae" title="Hexacrobylidae">Hexacrobylidae</a> was synonymized with Molgulidae.</p>
