<p>The <b>Prime Minister of Somalia</b> (<a href="/wiki/Somali_language" title="Somali language">Somali</a>: <span lang="so" xml:lang="so"><i>Ra'iisul wasaaraha Soomaaliya</i></span>) is the <a href="/wiki/Head_of_government" title="Head of government">head of government</a> of <a href="/wiki/Somalia" title="Somalia">Somalia</a>. There have been 17 official prime ministers since the office was created in 1956. The first Prime Minister was <a href="/wiki/Abdullahi_Issa" title="Abdullahi Issa">Abdullahi Issa Mohamud</a>, who served prior to independence in the <a href="/wiki/Trust_Territory_of_Somaliland" title="Trust Territory of Somaliland">Trust Territory of Somaliland</a>. The incumbent Prime Minister of the <a href="/wiki/Federal_Government_of_Somalia" title="Federal Government of Somalia">Federal Republic of Somalia</a> is <a href="/wiki/Omar_Abdirashid_Ali_Sharmarke" title="Omar Abdirashid Ali Sharmarke">Omar Abdirashid Ali Sharmarke</a>.</p>
