<p><b>Saussure's shrew</b> (<i>Sorex saussurei</i>) is a species of <a href="/wiki/Mammal" title="Mammal">mammal</a> in the family <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Soricidae" title="Soricidae">Soricidae</a>. It is found in <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">Mexico</a> with a disjoint population in <a href="/wiki/Guatemala" title="Guatemala">Guatemala</a> that is provisionally assigned to this species, but may represent a distinct species.</p>
