<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Sokratis Lagoudakis</h1>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Sokratis Lagoudakis</b> (<a href="/wiki/Greek_language" title="Greek language">Greek</a>: <span lang="el" xml:lang="el">Σωκράτης Λαγουδάκης</span>) (born 1861 in <a href="/wiki/Crete" title="Crete">Crete</a> – died June 3, 1944 in <a href="/wiki/Alexandria" title="Alexandria">Alexandria</a>, Egypt) was a <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greek</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Long-distance_runner" title="Long-distance runner">long-distance runner</a> who competed at the <a href="/wiki/1896_Summer_Olympics" title="1896 Summer Olympics">1896 Summer Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Athens" title="Athens">Athens</a>.</p>
<p>Lagoudakis was one of 17 athletes to start the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Marathon_(sport)" title="Marathon (sport)">marathon</a> race. He finished last of the nine athletes to have completed the race.</p>
