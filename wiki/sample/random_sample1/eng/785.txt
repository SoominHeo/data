<p>"<b>Ambitious! Yashinteki de Ii Jan</b>" <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">Ambitious！野心的でいいじゃん</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Anbishasu! Yashinteki de Ii jan</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, "Ambitious! Ambition Is Good")</span> is the thirtieth single of <a href="/wiki/J-pop" title="J-pop">J-pop</a> idol group <a href="/wiki/Morning_Musume" title="Morning Musume">Morning Musume</a>. It was released on June 21, 2006. The <a href="/wiki/Single_V" title="Single V">Single V</a> was released on June 28, 2006.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Overview">Overview</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ambitious!_Yashinteki_de_Ii_Jan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Overview">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>There are regular and limited edition versions of this single. The regular version comes with a photocard in a poker card design. The limited edition comes with a DVD containing comments from the members and two performances from their <a href="/wiki/Rainbow_7" title="Rainbow 7">Rainbow 7</a> concert tour, Aozora ga Itsumademo Tsuzuku You na Mirai de Are! and <a href="/wiki/Sexy_Boy_(Soyokaze_ni_Yorisotte)" title="Sexy Boy (Soyokaze ni Yorisotte)">Sexy Boy (Soyokaze ni Yorisotte)</a>. It also came with 10 interchangeable covers, one featuring each member.</p>
<p>This is the last single to feature 5th generation graduated members <a href="/wiki/Asami_Konno" title="Asami Konno">Asami Konno</a> and <a href="/wiki/Makoto_Ogawa" title="Makoto Ogawa">Makoto Ogawa</a>, after which <a href="/wiki/Sayumi_Michishige" title="Sayumi Michishige">Sayumi Michishige</a> and <a href="/wiki/Risa_Niigaki" title="Risa Niigaki">Risa Niigaki</a> respectively took their solos in live performances of the song.</p>
