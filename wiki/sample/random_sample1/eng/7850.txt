<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transportation">Transportation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sotteville-l%C3%A8s-Rouen&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Transportation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tramway_de_Rouen" title="Tramway de Rouen"><i>métro</i></a> connects the commune with <a href="/wiki/Rouen" title="Rouen">Rouen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Saint-%C3%89tienne-du-Rouvray" title="Saint-Étienne-du-Rouvray">Saint-Étienne-du-Rouvray</a>.</p>
<p>The commune used to be a <a href="/wiki/Railway_town" title="Railway town">railway town</a> in the days of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Old_Rouen_tramway" title="Old Rouen tramway">old Rouen tramway</a>.</p>
