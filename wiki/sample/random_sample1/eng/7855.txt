<p><b>Sofia Marianne Ledarp</b> (born 8 April 1974)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is a <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Swedish</a> <a href="/wiki/Actor" title="Actor">actress</a>. Born in the <a href="/wiki/Stockholm" title="Stockholm">Stockholm</a> suburb <a href="/wiki/H%C3%A4gersten" title="Hägersten">Hägersten</a>, she studied at <a href="/wiki/Lule%C3%A5_Theatre_Academy" title="Luleå Theatre Academy">Luleå Theatre Academy</a> 1996–2000.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> In January 2008, she received the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Guldsolen&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Guldsolen (page does not exist)">Guldsolen</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Guldbagge_Award" title="Guldbagge Award">Guldbagge Awards</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> for her role as Lena in the 2007 film <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Den_man_%C3%A4lskar&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Den man älskar (page does not exist)">Den man älskar</a></i>. She is best known internationally for her role as Malin Erikson in the <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Millennium_series" title="Millennium series">Millennium</a></i> movies.</p>
