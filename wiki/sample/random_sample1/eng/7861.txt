<p><b>Queen Sohye</b> (7 October 1437 - 11 May 1504) (소혜왕후 한씨) was the only wife of <a href="/wiki/Crown_Prince_Uigyeong" title="Crown Prince Uigyeong">Crown Prince Uigyeong</a> of <a href="/wiki/Joseon" title="Joseon">Joseon</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Family">Family</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Queen_Insu&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Family">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>Father: <a href="/wiki/Han_Hwak" title="Han Hwak">Han Hwak</a> (1400 - 11 September 1456) (한확)</li>
<li>Mother: Lady Hong of the Namyang Hong clan (남양 홍씨)</li>
<li>Husband: <a href="/wiki/Crown_Prince_Uigyeong" title="Crown Prince Uigyeong">Yi Jang, Crown Prince Uigyeong</a> (1438 – 2 September 1457) (이장 의경세자)
<ul>
<li>Son: Yi Jeong, Grand Prince Wolsan (1454 - 21 December 1488) (이정 월산대군)
<ul>
<li>Daughter-in-law: Grand Princess Consort Seungpyeong of the Suncheon Park (1455 - 20 July 1506) (승평부대부인 박씨)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Son: <a href="/wiki/Seongjong_of_Joseon" title="Seongjong of Joseon">King Seongjong of Joseon</a> (19 August 1457 – 20 January 1494) (조선 성종)
<ul>
<li>Daughter-in-law: <a href="/wiki/Queen_Gonghye" title="Queen Gonghye">Queen Gonghye of the Cheongju Han clan</a> (8 November 1456 – 30 April 1474) (공혜왕후 한씨)</li>
<li>Daughter-in-law: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Deposed_Queen_Lady_Yun" title="Deposed Queen Lady Yun">Queen Jeheon of the Haman Yun clan</a> (15 July 1455 – 29 August 1482) (제헌왕후 윤씨)</li>
<li>Daughter-in-law: <a href="/wiki/Queen_Jeonghyeon" title="Queen Jeonghyeon">Queen Jeonghyeon of the Papyeong Yun clan</a> (21 July 1462 – 13 September 1530) (정현왕후 윤씨)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Daughter: Princess Myeongsuk (1455 - 4 October 1482) (명숙공주)
<ul>
<li>Son-in-law: Hong Sang (1457 - 1513) (홍상)</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
