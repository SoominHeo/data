<p><span id="article_start"></span>In <a href="/wiki/Astrology" title="Astrology">astrology</a>, a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Celestial_body" title="Celestial body">celestial body</a> is said to be in <b>detriment</b>, or exile, when it is positioned in the <a href="/wiki/Astrological_sign" title="Astrological sign">zodiac sign</a> opposite the sign it <a href="/wiki/Domicile_(astrology)" title="Domicile (astrology)">rules</a> (over which it has domicile). When a celestial body is in detriment it is said to be not comfortable in that sign and to tend to operate with the least strength.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
