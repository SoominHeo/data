<p><b>Son Seol-Min</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">손설민</span>; born 26 April 1990) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Midfielder_(association_football)" title="Midfielder (association football)">midfielder</a> for <a href="/wiki/Gangwon_FC" title="Gangwon FC">Gangwon FC</a> in the <a href="/wiki/K_League_Challenge" title="K League Challenge">K League Challenge</a>.</p>
