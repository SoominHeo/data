<p><b>Solène Rigot</b> (Solene Rigot) is a French actress and musician. She is mostly known for playing the lead role in the Belgian movie <a href="/wiki/Puppy_Love_(2013_film)" title="Puppy Love (2013 film)">Puppy Love</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Solene_Rigot&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Her first big role was in <a href="/wiki/17_Girls" title="17 Girls">17 Girls</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Her performance was praised in the French movie <a class="extiw" href="https://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/%27%27Les_R%C3%A9volt%C3%A9s%27%27_(film,_2014)" title="fr:''Les Révoltés'' (film, 2014)">fr:''Les Révoltés'' (film, 2014)</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> She is also a member of the French musical group known as Mr. Crock.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Solene_Rigot&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>French newspaper <a href="/wiki/L%27Express" title="L'Express">L'Express</a> reported that she grew up in Paris suburb <a href="/wiki/Rosny-sous-Bois" title="Rosny-sous-Bois">Rosny-sous-Bois</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> In her interview to French magazine <a href="/wiki/Les_Inrockuptibles" title="Les Inrockuptibles">Les Inrockuptibles</a>, she said that she took music lessons from childhood and then she auditioned for her first film <a class="extiw" href="https://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/La_Permission_de_minuit" title="fr:La Permission de minuit">fr:La Permission de minuit</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
