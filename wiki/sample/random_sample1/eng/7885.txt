<p><b>Solomon "Saba" Kvirkvelia</b> (<a href="/wiki/Georgian_language" title="Georgian language">Georgian</a>: <span lang="ka" xml:lang="ka">სოლომონ კვირკველია</span>; born 6 February 1992) is a <a href="/wiki/Georgia_(country)" title="Georgia (country)">Georgian</a> professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> player. Currently, he plays for <a href="/wiki/FC_Rubin_Kazan" title="FC Rubin Kazan">FC Rubin Kazan</a>. He made his <a href="/wiki/Russian_Premier_League" title="Russian Premier League">Russian Premier League</a> debut for <a href="/wiki/FC_Rubin_Kazan" title="FC Rubin Kazan">FC Rubin Kazan</a> on 22 July 2011 in a game against <a href="/wiki/FC_Terek_Grozny" title="FC Terek Grozny">FC Terek Grozny</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>On an international level, he debuted for <a href="/wiki/Georgia_national_football_team" title="Georgia national football team">Georgia</a> in a friendly match against <a href="/wiki/Liechtenstein_national_football_team" title="Liechtenstein national football team">Liechtenstein</a> on 5 March 2014.</p>
