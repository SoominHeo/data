<p><i><b>Solomon's Perjury</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">ソロモンの偽証</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> are two 2015 Japanese <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Suspense_film" title="Suspense film">suspense</a> <a href="/wiki/Mystery_film" title="Mystery film">mystery films</a> directed by <a href="/wiki/Izuru_Narushima" title="Izuru Narushima">Izuru Narushima</a>, based on the novel of the same title by <a href="/wiki/Miyuki_Miyabe" title="Miyuki Miyabe">Miyuki Miyabe</a>. The first, <i><b>Solomon's Perjury Part 1: Suspicion</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">ソロモンの偽証 前篇・事件</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>, was released on March 7 and the second, <i><b>Solomon's Perjury Part 2: Judgement</b></i> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">ソロモンの偽証 後篇・裁判</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>, was released on April 11, 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Reception">Reception</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Solomon%27s_Perjury&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Reception">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Part 1 earned <span style="white-space: nowrap">¥120.128 million</span> on its opening weekend in Japan.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>Derek Elley of <i><a href="/wiki/Film_Business_Asia" title="Film Business Asia">Film Business Asia</a></i> gave both films an 8 out of 10, saying that the first "fans out in involving ways" and that the second "wraps in a challenging and satisfying way."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> The films won the 2015 <a href="/wiki/Hochi_Film_Award" title="Hochi Film Award">Hochi Film Award</a> for Best Picture.</p>
