<p><br/>
<b>Song deok-gi</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">송덕기(宋德基)</span> ; 1893 ? -1987 ?), was an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ingan-munhwage" title="Ingan-munhwage">Ingan-munhwage</a> of <a href="/wiki/Taekkyeon" title="Taekkyeon">Taekkyeon</a>, which is the 76th <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Important_Intangible_Cultural_Properties_of_Korea" title="Important Intangible Cultural Properties of Korea">Important Intangible Cultural Properties of Korea</a>. He was known for the master as <a href="/wiki/Taekkyeon" title="Taekkyeon">Taekkyeon</a> and was designated for Korean traditional martial arts when Korean national team introduced <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Korean_culture" title="Korean culture">Korean culture</a> at the 17th <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rome_Olympics" title="Rome Olympics">Rome Olympics</a> in 1960. He used Hyeonam(<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">현암(玄庵)</span>; as a pseudonym.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Song_Deok-gi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b>Song deok-gi</b> was born in 1893 in Seoul. He met <a href="/wiki/Taekkyeon" title="Taekkyeon">Taekkyeon</a> master Lim Ho (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">임호(林虎)</span> ;) when he was 12 years old and began training with other boys about 10 years. At that time, <a href="/wiki/Taekkyeon" title="Taekkyeon">Taekkyeon</a> was known as a kind of folk game and many people around Seoul played it. He trained at empty lot, which was located under the top <a href="/wiki/Inwangsan" title="Inwangsan">Inwangsan</a> mountain hwanghakjeong (黄鹤亭), upper side of Sajik Park. People called Witdaepae (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">윗대패</span> ;) who lived inside of Seoul town walls and Ahratdaepae (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">아랫대패</span> ;) who lived outside of Seoul town walls. <b>Song deok-gi</b> was Witdaepae (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">윗대패</span> ;) so he had competition with Ahratdaepae (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">아랫대패</span> ;) people around Samcheong-dong, Aeohgae and the like. After compromise of national sovereignty of Joseon, <a href="/wiki/Taekkyeon" title="Taekkyeon">Taekkyeon</a> was prohibited by Japan's Ethnic extermination policy, he trained <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gungdo" title="Gungdo">Gungdo</a> near his house and he just practiced <a href="/wiki/Taekkyeon" title="Taekkyeon">Taekkyeon</a> skills, which he learned. He also was good at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gungdo" title="Gungdo">Gungdo</a> and Undertook the first official referee for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gungdo" title="Gungdo">Gungdo</a>. In 1958, he showed demonstration in front of the former president <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lee_Seung-man" title="Lee Seung-man">Lee Seung-man</a> at president's birthday with Kim Sung-hwan who was also a pupil of Lim Ho. The event caused him to start a research, systematic study and dissemination activities of <a href="/wiki/Taekkyeon" title="Taekkyeon">Taekkyeon</a> with other <a href="/wiki/Taekkyeon" title="Taekkyeon">Taekkyeon</a> masters. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lee_Seung-man" title="Lee Seung-man">Lee Seung-man</a> ordered to his Head of security to learn <a href="/wiki/Taekkyeon" title="Taekkyeon">Taekkyeon</a> by securities.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Song_Deok-gi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He taught horizontal bar in Artillery Unit, which was a military force at the end of the Joseon Dynasty and served as a soccer player in professional soccer team. In 1983, he was designated <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ingan-munhwage" title="Ingan-munhwage">Ingan-munhwage</a> as <a href="/wiki/Taekkyeon" title="Taekkyeon">Taekkyeon</a> was appointed to the 76th <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Important_Intangible_Cultural_Properties_of_Korea" title="Important Intangible Cultural Properties of Korea">Important Intangible Cultural Properties of Korea</a>. He opened <a href="/wiki/Taekkyeon" title="Taekkyeon">Taekkyeon</a> inheritance committee and the <a href="/wiki/Taekkyeon" title="Taekkyeon">Taekkyeon</a> class for students but it closed after one year for immature operation.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Awards">Awards</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Song_Deok-gi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Awards">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 1981,He won martial arts prize in the 1st Republic of Korea of the traditional martial arts contest.</p>
