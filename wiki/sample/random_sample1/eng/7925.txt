<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Shortland Islands</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">
					Jump to:					<a href="#mw-head">navigation</a>, 					<a href="#p-search">search</a>
</div>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <b>Shortland Islands</b> are group of islands belonging to the <a href="/wiki/Western_Province_(Solomon_Islands)" title="Western Province (Solomon Islands)">Western Province</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Solomon_Islands" title="Solomon Islands">Solomon Islands</a>, at <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Shortland_Islands&amp;params=6.92_S_155.88_E_"><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">6°55′S</span> <span class="longitude">155°53′E</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">6.92°S 155.88°E</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">-6.92; 155.88</span></span></span></a></span>. Named by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/John_Shortland_(naval_officer)" title="John Shortland (naval officer)">John Shortland</a>, they lie in the extreme northwest of the country's territory, close to the island of <a href="/wiki/Bougainville_Island" title="Bougainville Island">Bougainville</a>, <a href="/wiki/Papua_New_Guinea" title="Papua New Guinea">Papua New Guinea</a>. The largest island is <a href="/wiki/Shortland_Island" title="Shortland Island">Shortland Island</a>. Other islands include <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ovau_Island&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ovau Island (page does not exist)">Ovau Island</a>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Pirumeri_Island&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Pirumeri Island (page does not exist)">Pirumeri Island</a>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Magusaiai_Island&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Magusaiai Island (page does not exist)">Magusaiai Island</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fauro_Island" title="Fauro Island">Fauro Island</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ballale_Island" title="Ballale Island">Ballale Island</a> (which was occupied by the Japanese in <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a>, and is home to many abandoned World War II aircraft).</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a> claimed the islands until 1900.</p>
