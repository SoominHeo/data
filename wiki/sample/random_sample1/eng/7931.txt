<p><b>Șoldănești</b> is a district (<a href="/wiki/Moldovan_language" title="Moldovan language">Moldovan</a>: <span lang="mo"><a href="/wiki/Raion" title="Raion">raion</a></span>) in the north-east of <a href="/wiki/Moldova" title="Moldova">Moldova</a>, with the administrative center at <a href="/wiki/%C8%98old%C4%83ne%C8%99ti" title="Șoldănești">Șoldănești</a>. As of 1 January 2011, its population was 43,300.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%C8%98old%C4%83ne%C8%99ti_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Human_settlement" title="Human settlement">Localities</a> with the earliest documentary attestation are <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%C5%9Etef%C4%83ne%C5%9Fti,_V%C3%A2lcea" title="Ştefăneşti, Vâlcea">Dobrusa</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Oli%C5%9Fcani" title="Olişcani">Olişcani</a>, <a href="/wiki/R%C4%83spopeni" title="Răspopeni">Răspopeni</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Salcia,_%C5%9Eold%C4%83ne%C5%9Fti" title="Salcia, Şoldăneşti">Salcia</a> are certified in period 1437–1448. In the 16th–18th centuries, continued economic development (trade, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Crafts" title="Crafts">crafts</a>), cultural (build churches, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Monasteries" title="Monasteries">monasteries</a>) and has been a marked increase in the population. Since late 18th and early 19th century, the region's economic decay occurs as a result of the constant <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wars" title="Wars">wars</a> led by <a href="/wiki/Poland" title="Poland">Poland</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a> and <a href="/wiki/Russian_Empire" title="Russian Empire">Russian Empire</a> for <a href="/wiki/Sphere_of_influence" title="Sphere of influence">influence</a> in the region. In 1812 <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Bucharest_(1812)" title="Treaty of Bucharest (1812)">Treaty of Bucharest</a> divides <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Basarabia" title="Basarabia">Basarabia</a> from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Principality_of_Moldova" title="Principality of Moldova">Principality of Moldova</a>, the first being ceded to Russia. In 1918, after the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Russian_Revolution_(1917)" title="Russian Revolution (1917)">collapse of the Russian Empire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Union_of_Bessarabia_with_Romania" title="Union of Bessarabia with Romania">Basarabia decide union with the motherland Romania</a>, during this period (1918–1944) district, is part of <a href="/wiki/Soroca_County" title="Soroca County">Soroca County</a>. Basarabia is again busy this time of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/USSR" title="USSR">USSR</a> after the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Molotov-Ribbentrop_Treaty" title="Molotov-Ribbentrop Treaty">Molotov-Ribbentrop Treaty</a>. In 1991 as a result of the proclamation of <a href="/wiki/Independence_of_Moldova" title="Independence of Moldova">Independence of Moldova</a>, part of the Soroca County (1991–2003), and in 2003 became administrative unit of <a href="/wiki/Moldova" title="Moldova">Moldova</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%C8%98old%C4%83ne%C8%99ti_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Soldanesti district is located in north-eastern of <a href="/wiki/Moldova" title="Moldova">Moldova</a>, is the neighborhood north and west <a href="/wiki/Flore%C8%99ti_District" title="Florești District">Florești District</a>, in the north-east <a href="/wiki/Camenca_District" title="Camenca District">Camenca District</a>, in east <a href="/wiki/R%C3%AEbni%C8%9Ba_District" title="Rîbnița District">Rîbnița District</a>, south <a href="/wiki/Telene%C8%99ti_District" title="Telenești District">Telenești</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rezina_District" title="Rezina District">Rezina</a> districts. The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Territory_(country_subdivision)" title="Territory (country subdivision)">territory</a> is located within the Plateau of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nistru" title="Nistru">Nistru</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Relief" title="Relief">relief</a> is moderately, fragmented and represented by plateaus, low <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hills" title="Hills">hills</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dale_(origin)" title="Dale (origin)">dales</a> wide and hilly interfluvial shaped strings. Sometimes come across isolated dome-shaped hills. On the <a href="/wiki/Plateau" title="Plateau">plateau</a> are widespread <a href="/wiki/Erosion" title="Erosion">erosion</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Landslides" title="Landslides">landslides</a>. In the district high fertility <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Soils" title="Soils">soils</a> are characterized by particularly prevalent in typical <a href="/wiki/Chernozem" title="Chernozem">chernozem</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Leachates" title="Leachates">leachates</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Carbonated" title="Carbonated">carbonated</a> ordinary, gray and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Brown_soil" title="Brown soil">brown soil</a> of forest closed. Average reliability of the soil according to the Land Register of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Republic_of_Moldova" title="Republic of Moldova">Republic of Moldova</a> is 74-point average of districts of 63 points. Maximum altitude in district is the hill Zahorna, with 338 meters.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Climate">Climate</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%C8%98old%C4%83ne%C8%99ti_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Climate">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Climate" title="Climate">climate</a> is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Temperate" title="Temperate">temperate</a> continental, average temperature of January −4.50 °C, 21.00 °C in July, the average annual <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Precipitation_(meteorology)" title="Precipitation (meteorology)">precipitation</a> is 500–560 mm. Average wind speed 4–6 m\s.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Fauna">Fauna</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%C8%98old%C4%83ne%C8%99ti_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Fauna">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The fauna of the district is typical of Central Europe is characterized by: <a href="/wiki/Fox" title="Fox">foxes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hedgehog" title="Hedgehog">hedgehogs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hare" title="Hare">hares</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wild_boar" title="Wild boar">wild boars</a>, <a href="/wiki/Deer" title="Deer">deer</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wildcat" title="Wildcat">wildcats</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wolf" title="Wolf">wolves</a>. Of birds there are: <a href="/wiki/Quail" title="Quail">quail</a>, <a href="/wiki/Egret" title="Egret">egrets</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hawk" title="Hawk">hawks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Stork" title="Stork">storks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Crow" title="Crow">crows</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seagull" title="Seagull">seagulls</a> and others.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Flora">Flora</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%C8%98old%C4%83ne%C8%99ti_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Flora">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Forests" title="Forests">Forests</a> occupy 19.3% of the district and are complemented by <a href="/wiki/Oak" title="Oak">oak</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fraxinus_excelsior" title="Fraxinus excelsior">ash</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tilia" title="Tilia">lime</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hornbeam" title="Hornbeam">hornbeam</a>, <a href="/wiki/Acacia" title="Acacia">acacia</a> and others. Plants include: <a href="/wiki/Urtica" title="Urtica">nettle</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fescue" title="Fescue">fescue</a>, <a href="/wiki/Clover" title="Clover">clover</a>, <a href="/wiki/Artemisia_absinthium" title="Artemisia absinthium">wormwood</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cortusa_matthioli" title="Cortusa matthioli">bells</a> and others.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Natural_resources">Natural resources</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%C8%98old%C4%83ne%C8%99ti_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Natural resources">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The main mineral resources located in the district stands metallic resources, most of the stock is near the <a href="/wiki/Mihuleni" title="Mihuleni">Mihuleni</a> – <a href="/wiki/Limestone" title="Limestone">limestone</a>. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Oli%C5%9Fcani" title="Olişcani">Olişcani</a> deposits – <a href="/wiki/Gravel" title="Gravel">gravel</a>. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Soldanesti" title="Soldanesti">Soldanesti</a>, Olişcani, Mihuleni, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vadul_Ra%C5%9Fcov" title="Vadul Raşcov">Vadul Raşcov</a> – <a href="/wiki/Clay" title="Clay">clay</a>. <a href="/wiki/Alcedar" title="Alcedar">Alcedar</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dobru%C5%9Fa,_%C5%9Eold%C4%83ne%C5%9Fti" title="Dobruşa, Şoldăneşti">Dobruşa</a>, <a href="/wiki/R%C4%83spopeni" title="Răspopeni">Răspopeni</a> – <a href="/wiki/Sand" title="Sand">sand</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Rivers">Rivers</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%C8%98old%C4%83ne%C8%99ti_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Rivers">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The district is located in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nistru" title="Nistru">Nistru</a> River basin, the river <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ciorna" title="Ciorna">Ciorna</a> is the main river in the district. You meet many sources of <a href="/wiki/Drinking_water" title="Drinking water">drinking water</a>, <a href="/wiki/Groundwater" title="Groundwater">groundwater</a> is at depths between 0.5 m and 10–15 m.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Economy">Economy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%C8%98old%C4%83ne%C8%99ti_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Economy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Being a predominantly agricultural district, the main efforts are focused on supporting and <a href="/wiki/Research_and_development" title="Research and development">developing</a> priority directions of agriculture. <a href="/wiki/Arable_land" title="Arable land">Arable land</a> constitutes – 32 351 ha (54.4%), <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Perennial" title="Perennial">perennial</a> plantations – 3544 ha (5.9%), <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pastures" title="Pastures">pastures</a> – 4761 ha (8.0%). Industrial enterprises of all types of ownership manufactured in January–September 2010 production of 6.846 million <a href="/wiki/Moldovan_leu" title="Moldovan leu">lei</a> worth at current <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Prices" title="Prices">prices</a>. The volume index of industrial production from January–September 2009 was (in comparable prices) 96.6%.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Education">Education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%C8%98old%C4%83ne%C8%99ti_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In district 31 educational institutions working with a number of 5242 students. Currently in educational institutions operating in the district 509 teachers. Preschool – 28, children in <a href="/wiki/Kindergarten" title="Kindergarten">kindergarten</a> – 1458, teachers – 152.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Politics">Politics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%C8%98old%C4%83ne%C8%99ti_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Politics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Soldanesti district is a district with a preponderance of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Centre-right" title="Centre-right">right-wing parties</a>. <a href="/wiki/Alliance_for_European_Integration" title="Alliance for European Integration">AEI</a> get good results here, but <a href="/wiki/Party_of_Communists_of_the_Republic_of_Moldova" title="Party of Communists of the Republic of Moldova">PCRM</a> is in a constant percentage <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Decrease" title="Decrease">decrease</a>. During 2001–2009, the district was a predominantly communist (over 50%). District is one of the founding members of <a href="/wiki/Euroregion_Dniester" title="Euroregion Dniester">Euroregion Dniester</a>.</p>
