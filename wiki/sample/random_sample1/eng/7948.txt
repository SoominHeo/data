<p>The <b>Battle of Suriname</b> or <b>Battle of Surinam</b> was a battle between the <a href="/wiki/Batavian_Republic" title="Batavian Republic">Netherlands</a> and the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom_of_Great_Britain_and_Ireland" title="United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland">United Kingdom</a> for the control of the <a href="/wiki/Suriname" title="Suriname">Suriname</a> colony.</p>
<p>The colony, which was held by a Dutch garrison, was captured on May 5, 1804 by a British squadron under the command of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sir_Samuel_Hood" title="Sir Samuel Hood">Sir Samuel Hood</a>, together with troops under <a href="/wiki/Sir_Charles_Green,_1st_Baronet" title="Sir Charles Green, 1st Baronet">Sir Charles Green</a>.</p>
