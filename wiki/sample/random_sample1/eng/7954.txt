<p>The <b>Sumatran porcupine</b> (<i>Hystrix sumatrae</i>) is a species of <a href="/wiki/Rodent" title="Rodent">rodent</a> in the family <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hystricidae" title="Hystricidae">Hystricidae</a>. It is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Endemic" title="Endemic">endemic</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Indonesia" title="Indonesia">Indonesian</a> island of <a href="/wiki/Sumatra" title="Sumatra">Sumatra</a>, where it is hunted for food.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-iucn_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-iucn-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
