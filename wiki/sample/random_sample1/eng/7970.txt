<p>The <b>1996 Suwon Samsung Bluewings season</b> was <b><a href="/wiki/Suwon_Samsung_Bluewings" title="Suwon Samsung Bluewings">Suwon Samsung Bluewings</a>'</b>s first season in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/K-League" title="K-League">K-League</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Republic_of_Korea" title="Republic of Korea">Republic of Korea</a>. Suwon Samsung Bluewings is competing in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1996_K-League" title="1996 K-League">K-League</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1996_K-League_Cup" title="1996 K-League Cup">League Cup</a> and <a href="/wiki/1996_Korean_FA_Cup" title="1996 Korean FA Cup">Korean FA Cup</a>.</p>
