<p><b>Bennie K</b> (often stylized as <b>BENNIE K</b>) is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Urban_Contemporary" title="Urban Contemporary">Urban Contemporary</a> female duo formed in 1999 that consists of vocalist Yuki and rapper Cico. They met while Yuki was in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a> undergoing voice training with Roger Burnley. They made their debut in January 2001 with the single "Melody," released on the For Life Music label.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Bennie K's 8th single "Sunrise" reached the top 10 of the <a href="/wiki/Oricon" title="Oricon">Oricon</a> singles chart in 2004, and their 9th single "Dreamland" reached #2 in 2005. Their 4th album "Japana-rhythm" debuted at #1 on the <a href="/wiki/Oricon" title="Oricon">Oricon</a> albums chart.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Bennie K's songs usually combine Japanese and English. Their sound is primarily <a href="/wiki/Urban_contemporary" title="Urban contemporary">urban</a>, but they often use a variety of diverse musical styles, including <a href="/wiki/Latin_music_(genre)" title="Latin music (genre)">Latin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Country_music" title="Country music">country</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Members">Members</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bennie_K&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Members">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Yuki_.28.E3.83.A6.E3.82.AD.29">Yuki (ユキ)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bennie_K&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Yuki (ユキ)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Full Name: Yamamoto Yukiyo (山本ゆきよ)</li>
<li>Birthday: 2.23.84</li>
</ul>
<p>Yuki was born in <a href="/wiki/Osaka_Prefecture" title="Osaka Prefecture">Osaka Prefecture</a>, but grew up in <a href="/wiki/Kashima,_Saga" title="Kashima, Saga">Kashima</a> (鹿島市), <a href="/wiki/Saga_Prefecture" title="Saga Prefecture">Saga Prefecture</a>, located in Japan’s <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ky%C5%ABsh%C5%AB" title="Kyūshū">Kyūshū</a> region. She has a brother who is 4 years older than she is. Yuki is a graduate of Kashima City’s westside public junior high school. At the age of 14, Yuki successfully passed an audition as her graduation date approached. She subsequently did not appear at her junior high school graduation ceremony so that she could go over to America. While living in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>, <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>, she went back and forth between attending a local prep school, formal voice training, and dance lessons.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Cico_.28.E3.83.81.E3.82.B3.29">Cico (チコ)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bennie_K&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Cico (チコ)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Full Name: Yamamoto Chieko (山本知恵子)</li>
<li>Birthday: 1.7.78</li>
</ul>
<p>Cico grew up in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Suita" title="Suita">Suita</a> (吹田市), <a href="/wiki/Osaka_Prefecture" title="Osaka Prefecture">Osaka Prefecture</a>, located in Japan’s <a href="/wiki/Kansai_region" title="Kansai region">Kansai region</a>. She has an older brother and sister. Her alma mater was Osaka Kunei Senior High School. At one time, she was enrolled in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ritsumeikan" title="Ritsumeikan">Ritsumeikan University</a>’s college of international relations, but dropped out mid-semester. Cico eventually went over to <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a> to begin producing <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rap" title="Rap">rap</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop" title="Hip hop">hip-hop</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="DJ_Hi-Kick_.28.E3.83.8F.E3.82.A4.E3.82.AD.E3.83.83.E3.82.AF.29">DJ Hi-Kick (ハイキック)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bennie_K&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: DJ Hi-Kick (ハイキック)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Hi-Kick is Bennie K’s manager and DJ. His hometown is in <a href="/wiki/Nagano_Prefecture" title="Nagano Prefecture">Nagano Prefecture</a>, located in Japan’s <a href="/wiki/Ch%C5%ABbu_region" title="Chūbu region">Chūbu region</a>. He graduated from <a href="/wiki/Tokai_University" title="Tokai University">Tokai University</a> in 1950.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bennie_K&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Formation">Formation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bennie_K&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Formation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During the summer of 1999, Yuki and Cico had a chance encounter. Yuki was on a homestay in Los Angeles, and her residence was that of Cico’s aunt. While sharing the same quarters, the two became friends. Returning to Japan separately, they continued their musical endeavors. Not too long after, they had the opportunity to collaborate at a Christmas event, and decided afterward to unite and devote themselves to music.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Name_origins">Name origins</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bennie_K&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Name origins">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In the new year after deciding to work together, Yuki and Cico went to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ky%C5%8Dto" title="Kyōto">Kyōto</a> (京都) and prayed for future success at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fushimi_Inari_Taisha" title="Fushimi Inari Taisha">Fushimi Inari Taisha</a> (伏見稲荷大社), a shrine famous for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Inari_(mythology)" title="Inari (mythology)">Inari</a>, a god of business and harvest. On their way back, they came across the Gojō Bridge, recalling it as the site of a famous Japanese legend — the battle between <a href="/wiki/Benkei" title="Benkei">Benkei</a> (弁慶) and <a href="/wiki/Minamoto_no_Yoshitsune" title="Minamoto no Yoshitsune">Ushiwakamaru</a> (牛若丸). The powerful Japanese warrior monk Benkei is said to have posted himself at this bridge, fighting and winning consecutive duels with 999 passing swordsmen. His 1,000th duel was with Ushiwakamaru and did not end in Benkei’s favor. Benkei joined Ushiwakamaru as a retainer and they had many successful missions together.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Yuki and Cico noticed similarities between the legendary duo and themselves — despite an age difference between the two of them, they both seemed to have their sights aimed high. The duo considered "Benkei and Ushiwakamaru" as a name for their act, but later felt it was too unoriginal to just use the names of other people. When Yuki began learning guitar, she started out with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ben_E_King" title="Ben E King">Ben E. King</a>’s "<a href="/wiki/Stand_by_Me_(song)" title="Stand by Me (song)">Stand by Me</a>." As this was the first song the duo ever sang together, they decided to combine Benkei with Ben E. King — and the name Bennie K was born. To Yuki and Cico, Benkei is a symbol of power; they feel that this name choice reflects their commitment to putting out music that expresses themselves as powerful women.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="BK_Project">BK Project</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bennie_K&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: BK Project">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Following the release of "The "Bestest" Bennie K Show," Bennie K went off the radar for many months. On 8.19.09, Yuki and Cico updated the official Bennie K website, announcing that they would be starting a new venture called BK Project. The duo apologized for their delay in dispelling any rumors about their absence that may have stirred up anxiety in fans. Yuki has decided to put her singing on hold — and will shift to the role of producer — desiring to take on a greater goal of supporting other amateur and professional musicians. Yuki and Cico both recognize that they have received a great deal of help from other artists over the years — which greatly contributed to their successes — and feel that now is the time to show their gratitude by giving back to the music community. They will be working with American singer <a href="/wiki/Becca_(musician)" title="Becca (musician)">Becca</a> for their first production under the project — a single entitled "Dreamer" that will be released under the name Bennie Becca on 11.4.09.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
