<p><b>Suresnes</b> (<small>French pronunciation: ​</small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_French" title="Help:IPA for French">[sy.ʁɛn]</a></span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Communes_of_France" title="Communes of France">commune</a> in the western suburbs of <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>, <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>. It is located 9.3 km (5.8 mi) from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kilometre_Zero" title="Kilometre Zero">center of Paris</a>. The nearest communes are <a href="/wiki/Neuilly-sur-Seine" title="Neuilly-sur-Seine">Neuilly-sur-Seine</a>, <a href="/wiki/Puteaux" title="Puteaux">Puteaux</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rueil-Malmaison" title="Rueil-Malmaison">Rueil-Malmaison</a>, <a href="/wiki/Saint-Cloud" title="Saint-Cloud">Saint-Cloud</a> and <a href="/wiki/Boulogne-Billancourt" title="Boulogne-Billancourt">Boulogne-Billancourt</a>. It is also on a <a href="/wiki/Tram" title="Tram">tram</a> line giving access to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/La_Defense" title="La Defense">La Defense</a> and its rail services.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Suresnes&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Fort_Mont-Val%C3%A9rien" title="Fort Mont-Valérien">Fort Mont-Valérien</a> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/M%C3%A9morial_de_la_France_Combattante" title="Mémorial de la France Combattante">Mémorial de la France Combattante</a>) is situated in this commune, and has a beautiful view of Paris and the <a href="/wiki/Eiffel_Tower" title="Eiffel Tower">Eiffel Tower</a>.</p>
<p>In 1974 the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Spanish_Socialist_Workers_Party" title="Spanish Socialist Workers Party">Spanish Socialist Workers Party</a> held its 26th Congress in Suresnes (it was illegal in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Spain_under_Franco" title="Spain under Franco">Spain under Franco</a>). <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Gonz%C3%A1lez" title="Felipe González">Felipe González</a> was elected Secretary General, replacing <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rodolfo_Llopis_Ferr%C3%A1ndiz" title="Rodolfo Llopis Ferrándiz">Rodolfo Llopis Ferrándiz</a>. González was from the "reform" wing of the party, and his victory signaled a defeat for the historic and veteran wing of the Party. The direction of the party shifted from the exiles to the young people in Spain who had not fought in the <a href="/wiki/Spanish_Civil_War" title="Spanish Civil War">Spanish Civil War</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transport">Transport</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Suresnes&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Transport">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Suresnes is served by <a href="/wiki/Gare_de_Suresnes-Mont-Val%C3%A9rien" title="Gare de Suresnes-Mont-Valérien">Suresnes-Mont-Valérien</a> station on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Transilien_La_D%C3%A9fense" title="Transilien La Défense">Transilien La Défense</a> and <a href="/wiki/Transilien_Paris_%E2%80%93_Saint-Lazare" title="Transilien Paris – Saint-Lazare">Transilien Paris – Saint-Lazare</a> suburban rail lines.</p>
<p>The Pont de Suresnes carries the Allée de Longchamp (one of the main traffic arteries) from the <a href="/wiki/Bois_de_Boulogne" title="Bois de Boulogne">Bois de Boulogne</a> over the <a href="/wiki/Seine" title="Seine">Seine</a> into the western suburbs of Paris.</p>
