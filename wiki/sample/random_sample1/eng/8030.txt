<p><b>Shūgakuin Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">修学院駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Shūgakuin-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, station number: E05)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/Train_station" title="Train station">train station</a> located on the <a href="/wiki/Eizan_Electric_Railway" title="Eizan Electric Railway">Eizan Electric Railway</a> (Eiden) <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Eizan_Main_Line" title="Eizan Main Line">Eizan Main Line</a> in Yamabana-Itchodacho, <a href="/wiki/Saky%C5%8D-ku,_Kyoto" title="Sakyō-ku, Kyoto">Sakyō-ku</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kyoto" title="Kyoto">Kyoto</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kyoto_Prefecture" title="Kyoto Prefecture">Kyoto Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Layout">Layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sh%C5%ABgakuin_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The station is located along Kitayama-dōri and the northern end of Higashiōji-dōri. The station has 2 side platforms on the ground along the tracks. There is Shūgakuin Depot at the back of the building in the south-east of the station.</p>
