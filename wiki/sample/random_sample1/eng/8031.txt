<p><b>Shugo Chara Egg!</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">しゅごキャラエッグ!</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Shugo Kyara Eggu!</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> was a <a href="/wiki/Hello!_Project" title="Hello! Project">Hello! Project</a> unit, originally consisting of four <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hello_Pro_Kensh%C5%ABsei" title="Hello Pro Kenshūsei">Hello! Pro Egg</a> members: <a href="/wiki/Ayaka_Wada" title="Ayaka Wada">Ayaka Wada</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yuuka_Maeda" title="Yuuka Maeda">Yuuka Maeda</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kanon_Fukuda" title="Kanon Fukuda">Kanon Fukuda</a> and <a href="/wiki/Akari_Saho" title="Akari Saho">Akari Saho</a>. The group was announced on September 20, 2008 and debuted with a live performance.</p>
<p>Shugo Chara Egg's purpose was to sing the opening theme songs for the anime <i>Shugo Chara! Doki—</i>, the anime that the Hello! Project group <a href="/wiki/Buono!" title="Buono!">Buono!</a> had previously performed opening and ending songs for. Even though Shugo Chara Egg! performed the anime's opening songs, the ending songs were still sung by <a href="/wiki/Buono!" title="Buono!">Buono!</a>. Additionally, each of the four members wear costumes based on the "character transformations" (a key element of the series' plot) of Amu Hinamori, the main character of the series.</p>
<p>Shugo Chara Egg's debut single was titled "<a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Minna_no_Tamago&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Minna no Tamago (page does not exist)">Minna no Tamago</a>", and was used as the first opening theme for Shugo Chara!! Doki-. It was released on the Pony Canyon label on October 12, 2008. A new second generation Shugo Chara Egg! was created in August 2009, adding <a href="/wiki/Mizuki_Fukumura" title="Mizuki Fukumura">Mizuki Fukumura</a> and <a href="/wiki/Irori_Maeda" title="Irori Maeda">Irori Maeda</a> to replace Yuuka Maeda and Kanon Fukuda respectively. Nanami Tanabe was chosen as the new Amulet Diamond.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The girls are the hostesses for the live action segments of Shugo Chara! Party.</p>
<p>Wada and Fukumura are currently still members of <a href="/wiki/Hello!_Project" title="Hello! Project">Hello! Project</a>, as members of <a href="/wiki/Angerme" title="Angerme">Angerme</a> and <a href="/wiki/Morning_Musume" title="Morning Musume">Morning Musume</a> respectively.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shugo_Chara_Egg!&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2008:_Debut">2008: Debut</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shugo_Chara_Egg!&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 2008: Debut">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Shugo Chara Egg's debut single was titled "Minna no Tamago"</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2009.E2.80.932010:_Line-up_changes">2009–2010: Line-up changes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shugo_Chara_Egg!&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: 2009–2010: Line-up changes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In spring of 2009, the members of Shugo Chara! Egg participated in the musical adaptation of <i>Shugo Chara!</i>, starring Maeda in the leading role as Amu and Fukuda as Nadeshiko. Wada and Saho appeared in minor roles as unnamed students of Seiyō Academy.</p>
<p>In August 2009, Maeda, Fukuda, and Wada were withdrawn from the group to join <a href="/wiki/Angerme" title="Angerme">S/mileage</a>. To replace them, auditions were held. Mizuki Fukumura and Irori Maeda passed the auditions successfully as the new Amulet Heart and Amulet Clover, while Nanami Tanabe was finally chosen to replace Wada's role as Amulet Diamond in January 2010.</p>
<p>Later in 2010, Shugo Chara Egg disbanded, following the end of the anime series, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Shugo_Chara" title="Shugo Chara">Shugo Chara</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Activity_of_Members_after_Shugo_Chara.21_Egg">Activity of Members after Shugo Chara! Egg</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shugo_Chara_Egg!&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Activity of Members after Shugo Chara! Egg">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Maeda, Fukuda and Wada formed <a href="/wiki/Angerme" title="Angerme">S/mileage</a> with another Hello! Pro Egg member, <a href="/wiki/Saki_Ogawa" title="Saki Ogawa">Saki Ogawa</a>, and became full-time members of <a href="/wiki/Hello!_Project" title="Hello! Project">Hello! Project</a> in 2010. Wada is still an active member of S/mileage (now known as Angerme), whilst Maeda graduated in December 2011 and Fukuda graduated in November 2015. In early 2011 or late 2010, 2nd generation Amulet Clover, <a href="/wiki/Irori_Maeda" title="Irori Maeda">Irori Maeda</a>, left Hello! Pro Egg to join idol group AKBN0. In January 2011, 2nd generation Amulet Heart, <a href="/wiki/Mizuki_Fukumura" title="Mizuki Fukumura">Mizuki Fukumura</a>, was added to <a href="/wiki/Morning_Musume" title="Morning Musume">Morning Musume</a> as part of the 9th generation. Also in 2011, <a href="/wiki/Akari_Saho" title="Akari Saho">Akari Saho</a> finished her training in Hello! Pro Egg, and was later added to <a href="/wiki/Up_Up_Girls_(Kakko_Kari)" title="Up Up Girls (Kakko Kari)">Up-Front Girls</a> and <a href="/wiki/Up_Up_Girls_(Kakko_Kari)" title="Up Up Girls (Kakko Kari)">UFZS</a>. 2nd generation Amulet Dia, Nanami Tanabe, remained in Hello! Pro Egg until November 2014.</p>
