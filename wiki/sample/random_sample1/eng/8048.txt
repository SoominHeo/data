<p><b>Stuttgart</b> is one of the four administrative districts (<a href="/wiki/Regierungsbezirk" title="Regierungsbezirk">Regierungsbezirke</a>) of <a href="/wiki/Baden-W%C3%BCrttemberg" title="Baden-Württemberg">Baden-Württemberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>, located in the north-east of the state of <a href="/wiki/Baden-W%C3%BCrttemberg" title="Baden-Württemberg">Baden-Württemberg</a>, in the southwestern part of Germany.It is sub-divided into the three regions: <a href="/wiki/Heilbronn-Franken" title="Heilbronn-Franken">Heilbronn-Franken</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ostw%C3%BCrttemberg" title="Ostwürttemberg">Ostwürttemberg</a> and <a href="/wiki/Stuttgart_Region" title="Stuttgart Region">Stuttgart</a>.</p>

<p>The districts of Böblingen, Esslingen, Ludwigsburg, Rems-Murr and Göppingen form with the city of Stuttgart the <a href="/wiki/Stuttgart_Region" title="Stuttgart Region">Verband Region Stuttgart</a> with a directly elected regional assembly (Regionalversammlung).</p>
