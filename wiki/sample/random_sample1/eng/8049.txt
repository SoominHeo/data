<p><b>Shooting star</b> is a common name for the visible path of a <a href="/wiki/Meteoroid" title="Meteoroid">meteoroid</a> as it enters the atmosphere, becoming a meteor.</p>
<p><b>Shooting star</b> may also refer to:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Stellar_kinematics#High-velocity_stars" title="Stellar kinematics">Hypervelocity star</a>, a type of star that moves at unusually high velocities across the galaxy</li>
</ul>
