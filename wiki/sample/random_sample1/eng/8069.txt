<p><b>Sugamo Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">巣鴨駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>sugamo-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a railway station in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Toshima,_Tokyo" title="Toshima, Tokyo">Toshima, Tokyo</a>, Japan, operated by the <a href="/wiki/East_Japan_Railway_Company" title="East Japan Railway Company">East Japan Railway Company</a> (JR East) and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Toei_Subway" title="Toei Subway">Toei Subway</a>.</p>

<p>To the north of the station is a long <a href="/wiki/Sh%C5%8Dtengai" title="Shōtengai">shopping street</a>, Jizō-dōri <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">地蔵通り</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>, which is popular with older ladies, and the area is known as "<a href="/wiki/Harajuku" title="Harajuku">Harajuku</a> for Grannies".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cnngo_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-cnngo-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
