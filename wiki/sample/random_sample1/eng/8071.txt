<p><b>Sgurgola</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Frosinone" title="Province of Frosinone">Province of Frosinone</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Lazio" title="Lazio">Lazio</a>, located about 60 kilometres (37 mi) southeast of <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a> and about 15 kilometres (9 mi) west of <a href="/wiki/Frosinone" title="Frosinone">Frosinone</a>.</p>
<p>Sgurgola borders the following municipalities: <a href="/wiki/Anagni" title="Anagni">Anagni</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ferentino" title="Ferentino">Ferentino</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gorga,_Italy" title="Gorga, Italy">Gorga</a>, <a href="/wiki/Morolo" title="Morolo">Morolo</a>.</p>
