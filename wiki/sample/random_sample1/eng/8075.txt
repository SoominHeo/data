<p>A <b>snow angel</b> is a design, made in fresh <a href="/wiki/Snow" title="Snow">snow</a>, by lying on one's back and moving one's arms up and down, and one's legs from side to side, to form the shape of an <a href="/wiki/Angel" title="Angel">angel</a>. Making snow angels is a common childhood game.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Technique">Technique</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Snow_angel&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Technique">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The creation of the snow angel is a simple process. The first step is to find an undisturbed plane of fresh snow. The next step is to lie with arms and legs outstretched, on the snow. The limbs are then swept back and forth, creating a trough through the snow. When it is finished, the snow angel should have the appearance of a stylized angel, the movement of the arms having formed wings, and that of the legs having formed a gown.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Current_world_record">Current world record</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Snow_angel&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Current world record">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On March 28, 2007, Guinness World Records confirmed that <a href="/wiki/North_Dakota" title="North Dakota">North Dakota</a> holds the world record for the most snow angels made simultaneously in one place. The event occurred on February 17, 2007 when 8,962 snow angels were created by people on the <a href="/wiki/North_Dakota_State_Capitol" title="North Dakota State Capitol">state Capitol grounds</a> in <a href="/wiki/Bismarck,_North_Dakota" title="Bismarck, North Dakota">Bismarck</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Previously, the record was held by <a href="/wiki/Michigan_Technological_University" title="Michigan Technological University">Michigan Technological University</a> with 3,784 students, locals, and alumni making snow angels on the school football field.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
