<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">SriLankan Airlines destinations</h1>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b><a href="/wiki/SriLankan_Airlines" title="SriLankan Airlines">SriLankan Airlines</a></b>, is the flag carrier of Sri Lanka. Launched in 1979, the airline’s hub is located at <a href="/wiki/Bandaranaike_International_Airport" title="Bandaranaike International Airport">Bandaranaike International Airport</a> in <a href="/wiki/Colombo" title="Colombo">Colombo</a>, providing connections to its global route network of 96 destinations in 46 countries (including codeshare operations along with its partner <a href="/wiki/Oneworld" title="Oneworld">OneWorld</a> airlines). The following is a list of destinations served by <a href="/wiki/SriLankan_Airlines" title="SriLankan Airlines">SriLankan Airlines</a> and its subsidiary <a href="/wiki/Mihin_Lanka" title="Mihin Lanka">Mihin Lanka</a>, as of July 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Route_Map_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Route_Map-1">[1]</a></sup> The list includes the city and country name; the airport codes of the <a href="/wiki/International_Air_Transport_Association" title="International Air Transport Association">International Air Transport Association</a> (IATA airport code) and the <a href="/wiki/International_Civil_Aviation_Organization" title="International Civil Aviation Organization">International Civil Aviation Organization</a> (ICAO airport code); the airport name.</p>
