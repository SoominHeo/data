<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Svetlana_Khodchenkova&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Svetlana Khodchenkova was born in <a href="/wiki/Moscow" title="Moscow">Moscow</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russian_Soviet_Federative_Socialist_Republic" title="Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic">Russian SFSR</a>, <a href="/wiki/Soviet_Union" title="Soviet Union">Soviet Union</a>. Svetlana briefly worked for a modeling agency. In 2005 she graduated from the <a href="/wiki/Boris_Shchukin_Theatre_Institute" title="Boris Shchukin Theatre Institute">Boris Shchukin Theatre Institute</a>, the course M. Borisov.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Svetlana_Khodchenkova&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Khodchenkova’s debut role was in the film <i><a href="/wiki/Bless_the_Woman" title="Bless the Woman">Bless the Woman</a></i> (2003) directed by <a href="/wiki/Stanislav_Govorukhin" title="Stanislav Govorukhin">Stanislav Govorukhin</a>. For this role she was nominated for "<a href="/wiki/Nika_Award" title="Nika Award">Nika</a>" award as Best Actress. She continued her career in the role of a clairvoyant Cassandra historical television series <i>Talisman of Love</i> (2005). Svetlana played a ballerina in Pavel Sanaev’s film <i><a href="/wiki/Kilometer_Zero_(film)" title="Kilometer Zero (film)">Kilometer Zero</a></i> (2007).</p>
<p>In 2008, she won the award <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Polish_Film_Festival" title="Polish Film Festival">Polish Film Festival</a> for Best Actress in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Little_Moscow_(2008_film)" title="Little Moscow (2008 film)">Little Moscow</a></i>.</p>
<p>She is also known for her roles in <a href="/wiki/Box_office" title="Box office">box office</a> films <i><a class="extiw" href="https://ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%9B%D1%8E%D0%B1%D0%BE%D0%B2%D1%8C_%D0%B2_%D0%B1%D0%BE%D0%BB%D1%8C%D1%88%D0%BE%D0%BC_%D0%B3%D0%BE%D1%80%D0%BE%D0%B4%D0%B5" title="ru:Любовь в большом городе">Love in the Big City</a></i> (2009), its sequel, <i>Love in the Big City 2</i> (2010) and <i>Office Romance. Our Time</i> (2011).</p>
<p>She starred in the TV series <i><a class="extiw" href="https://ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%9C%D0%B5%D1%82%D0%BE%D0%B4_%D0%9B%D0%B0%D0%B2%D1%80%D0%BE%D0%B2%D0%BE%D0%B9" title="ru:Метод Лавровой">Lavrova's Method</a></i> (2011), and appeared as Irina in <i><a href="/wiki/Tinker_Tailor_Soldier_Spy_(film)" title="Tinker Tailor Soldier Spy (film)">Tinker Tailor Soldier Spy</a></i> (2011) Svetlana presented together with American colleagues at the <a href="/wiki/Venice_Film_Festival" title="Venice Film Festival">Venice Film Festival</a>. She co-starred with <a href="/wiki/Hugh_Jackman" title="Hugh Jackman">Hugh Jackman</a> in the 2013 superhero film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Wolverine_(film)" title="The Wolverine (film)">The Wolverine</a></i> as <a href="/wiki/Viper_(Madame_Hydra)" title="Viper (Madame Hydra)">Viper</a>, one of the film's antagonists.</p>
<p>Khodchenkova played a major role in the historical action film <i><a href="/wiki/Viking_(film)" title="Viking (film)">Viking</a></i> (2016), red carpet in December 2016.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Svetlana_Khodchenkova&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On December 13, 2005, she married her classmate <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Vladimir_Yaglych&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Vladimir Yaglych (page does not exist)">Vladimir Yaglych</a>, a Russian actor. In 2010 they divorced.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> In May 2015, she was engaged to Moscow businessman Georgiy Petrishin.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
