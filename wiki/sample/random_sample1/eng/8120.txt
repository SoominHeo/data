<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Swedish Recording Industry Association</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Grammofonleverantörernas förening</b> (<b>GLF</b>), or the <b>Swedish Recording Industry Association</b> in English, is an organization representing the music recording industry of <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Sweden</a>. It has compiled and published the official <a href="/wiki/Record_chart" title="Record chart">record charts</a> for the Swedish music recording industry since 1975, including the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Swedish_Albums_Chart" title="Swedish Albums Chart">Swedish Albums Chart</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Swedish_Singles_Chart" title="Swedish Singles Chart">Swedish Singles Chart</a>.</p>
<p>GLF has the following members: <a href="/wiki/Bonnier_Group" title="Bonnier Group">Bonnier</a> Amigo Group, <a href="/wiki/EMI" title="EMI">EMI</a> Svenska AB, Network Entertainment Group (MNW?), <a href="/wiki/Sony_BMG" title="Sony BMG">Sony BMG Sweden AB</a>, Sound Pollution AB, <a href="/wiki/Universal_Music_Group" title="Universal Music Group">Universal Music Sweden AB</a> , and <a href="/wiki/Warner_Music_Group" title="Warner Music Group">Warner Music Group Sweden AB</a>. Since 1986, GLF operates the Grammotex online catalog of recordings, currently holding 100,000 titles. The catalog service is open to music stores and distributors who pay a fee.</p>
