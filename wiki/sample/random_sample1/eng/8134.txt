<p><i><b>Super Swing Golf Season 2</b></i> (<i><b>Super Swing Golf</b></i> in Europe, and <i><b>Swing Golf PangYa 2nd Shot!!</b></i> in Japan) is a <a href="/wiki/Golf" title="Golf">golf</a> <a href="/wiki/Video_game" title="Video game">game</a> developed and published by <a href="/wiki/Tecmo" title="Tecmo">Tecmo</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Wii" title="Wii">Wii</a>. It was released in the <a href="/wiki/North_America" title="North America">North America</a> on December 11, 2007. It is the sequel to <i><a href="/wiki/Super_Swing_Golf" title="Super Swing Golf">Super Swing Golf</a></i>, and both are based on the <a href="/wiki/Korea" title="Korea">Korean</a> free <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Online" title="Online">online</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Multiplayer" title="Multiplayer">multiplayer</a> <a href="/wiki/Golf" title="Golf">golf</a> <a href="/wiki/Simulation" title="Simulation">simulation</a> game, <i><a href="/wiki/PangYa" title="PangYa">PangYa</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IGN_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-IGN-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Gameplay">Gameplay</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Super_Swing_Golf:_Season_2&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Gameplay">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Super Swing Golf: Season 2</i> includes an improved story mode, with an <a href="/wiki/Overworld" title="Overworld">overworld</a> map that does not necessarily follow events in a set order.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IGN_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-IGN-2">[2]</a></sup> On top of the multiplayer Stroke Play, Match Play and Balloon Pop games that came with the first game, two new multiplayer games were added to the party mode.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IGN_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-IGN-2">[2]</a></sup> The game has largely remained the same beyond these changes, the addition of new courses, and various cosmetic tweaks.</p>
<p>The game has two different control schemes; either using the Swing control scheme with the Wii Remote used as though it were a golf club, or using the Button control scheme<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GameSpy_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-GameSpy-3">[3]</a></sup> which is similar to the <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mario_Golf" title="Mario Golf">Mario Golf</a></i>-style of control scheme.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GameSpy_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-GameSpy-3">[3]</a></sup></p>

<p>The game was met with average reception upon release; <a href="/wiki/GameRankings" title="GameRankings">GameRankings</a> gave it a score of 71.69%,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-GR-4">[4]</a></sup> while <a href="/wiki/Metacritic" title="Metacritic">Metacritic</a> gave it 70 out of 100.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-MC_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-MC-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p><a href="/wiki/GameSpy" title="GameSpy">GameSpy</a> gave the game two-and-a-half stars out of five, criticizing the game for being more of an <a href="/wiki/Expansion_pack" title="Expansion pack">expansion pack</a> over the first game than a true sequel.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GameSpy_3-3"><a href="#cite_note-GameSpy-3">[3]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/1UP.com" title="1UP.com">1UP.com</a> gave the game a Bâ, reporting that the game has surprising depth that can be off-putting to those just looking to have a good time.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1UP_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-1UP-6">[6]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/IGN" title="IGN">IGN</a> handed out a score of 7.3 out of 10, and reported that despite just adding incremental improvements over the previous installments, "Super Swing Golf: Season 2 is still the best overall golf experience you can find on Wii."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IGN_2-4"><a href="#cite_note-IGN-2">[2]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/GameSpot" title="GameSpot">GameSpot</a> gave the game seven out of ten, calling it a good golf game, despite the minimal improvements over its predecessor.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GSpot_10-1"><a href="#cite_note-GSpot-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
