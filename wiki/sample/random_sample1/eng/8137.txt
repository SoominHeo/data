<p><b>Seiya Suzuki</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">鈴木 誠也</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Suzuki Seiya</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born August 18, 1994 in <a href="/wiki/Arakawa,_Tokyo" title="Arakawa, Tokyo">Arakawa</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tokyo" title="Tokyo">Tokyo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>)</span> is a professional <a href="/wiki/Japanese_people" title="Japanese people">Japanese</a> <a href="/wiki/Baseball" title="Baseball">baseball</a> player. He plays <a href="/wiki/Outfielder" title="Outfielder">outfielder</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Hiroshima_Toyo_Carp" title="Hiroshima Toyo Carp">Hiroshima Toyo Carp</a>.</p>
