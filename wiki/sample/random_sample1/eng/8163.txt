<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Sports">Sports</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Scoppito&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Sports">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="S.P.D._Amiternina">S.P.D. Amiternina</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Scoppito&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: S.P.D. Amiternina">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/S.P.D._Amiternina" title="S.P.D. Amiternina">S.P.D. Amiternina</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is an <a href="/wiki/Football_in_Italy" title="Football in Italy">Italian association football</a> club, based in this city.</p>
<p>In the season 2011–12 the team was promoted for the first time, from Eccellenza Abruzzo to <a href="/wiki/Serie_D" title="Serie D">Serie D</a> after.</p>
<p>Its colors are yellow and red.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transport">Transport</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Scoppito&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Transport">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Scoppito has a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Railway_stop" title="Railway stop">stop</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Terni%E2%80%93Sulmona_railway" title="Terni–Sulmona railway">Terni–Sulmona railway</a>, with trains to <a href="/wiki/Terni" title="Terni">Terni</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rieti" title="Rieti">Rieti</a> and <a href="/wiki/L%27Aquila" title="L'Aquila">L'Aquila</a>.</p>
