<p><b>Skhira</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar" xml:lang="ar">الصخيرة</span>‎‎) is a coastal town in Sfax Governate (Arabic: ولاية صفاقس), central-eastern <a href="/wiki/Tunisia" title="Tunisia">Tunisia</a>. It is located at around <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Skhira&amp;params=34_18_2_N_10_4_15_E_region:TN_type:city"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">34°18′2″N</span> <span class="longitude">10°4′15″E</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">34.30056°N 10.07083°E</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">34.30056; 10.07083</span></span></span></a></span>. It lies on the coast of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gulf_of_Gabes" title="Gulf of Gabes">Gulf of Gabes</a>. It has a large <a href="/wiki/Oil_terminal" title="Oil terminal">oil terminal</a> for pipelines coming from the Tunisian and <a href="/wiki/Algeria" title="Algeria">Algerian</a> oilfields. The old village grew in the late nineteenth century as the centre of the export trade in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Esparto_grass" title="Esparto grass">esparto grass</a>, used in the manufacture of <a href="/wiki/Paper" title="Paper">paper</a>.</p>
