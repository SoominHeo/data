<p><b>Style Icon Asia</b> (formerly known as <b>Style Icon Awards</b>; abbreviated as <b>SIA</b>) is an awards ceremony held annually in South Korea since 2008 to showcase Asian <a href="/wiki/Fashion_design" title="Fashion design">fashion designers</a> and to honor individuals who have made the most outstanding contributions in setting new paradigms and trends in <a href="/wiki/Fashion" title="Fashion">fashion</a>, broadcasting, <a href="/wiki/Culture" title="Culture">culture</a> and arts.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-billboard1_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-billboard1-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-rocky_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-rocky-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hosts_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-hosts-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-suzy_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-suzy-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-return_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-return-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
