Year <b>1414</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MCDXIV</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Monday" title="Common year starting on Monday">common year starting on Monday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1414&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93December">January–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1414&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_7" title="January 7">January 7</a> – <a href="/wiki/Michael_K%C3%BCchmeister_von_Sternberg" title="Michael Küchmeister von Sternberg">Michael Küchmeister von Sternberg</a> becomes the 28th <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grandmaster_(order)" title="Grandmaster (order)">Grand Master</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Teutonic_Order" title="Teutonic Order">Teutonic Order</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_28" title="May 28">May 28</a> – <a href="/wiki/Khizr_Khan" title="Khizr Khan">Khizr Khan</a> (<a href="/wiki/Timur" title="Timur">Timur</a>'s governor of <a href="/wiki/Multan" title="Multan">Multan</a>) takes the <a href="/wiki/Delhi_Sultanate" title="Delhi Sultanate">Delhi Sultanate</a> from <a href="/wiki/Daulat_Khan_Lodi" title="Daulat Khan Lodi">Daulat Khan Lodi</a>, founding the <a href="/wiki/Sayyid_dynasty" title="Sayyid dynasty">Sayyid dynasty</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_6" title="August 6">August 6</a> – <a href="/wiki/Joanna_II_of_Naples" title="Joanna II of Naples">Joanna II</a> succeeds her brother <a href="/wiki/Ladislaus_of_Naples" title="Ladislaus of Naples">Ladislaus</a> as <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Naples" title="Kingdom of Naples">Queen of Naples</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_16" title="November 16">November 16</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Council_of_Constance" title="Council of Constance">Council of Constance</a> begins.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Date_unknown">Date unknown</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1414&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Date unknown">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ernest,_Duke_of_Austria" title="Ernest, Duke of Austria">Ernest, Duke of Austria</a> (head of the <a href="/wiki/Leopoldian_line" title="Leopoldian line">Leopoldian line</a> of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Habsburg" title="House of Habsburg">House of Habsburg</a>) is the last duke to be enthroned in the <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Carinthia" title="Duchy of Carinthia">Duchy of Carinthia</a> according to the ancient <a href="/wiki/Carantania" title="Carantania">Carantanian</a> ritual of installing dukes at the <a href="/wiki/Prince%27s_Stone" title="Prince's Stone">Prince's Stone</a>; he adopts the title of <a href="/wiki/Archduke" title="Archduke">Archduke</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Alien_priory" title="Alien priory">Alien priory</a> cells are suppressed in <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Naming_conventions_(geographic_names)" title="Wikipedia:Naming conventions (geographic names)"><span title="The geographic scope near this tag is ambiguous. (February 2014)">where?</span></a></i>]</sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Tibet" title="Tibet">Tibetan</a> <a href="/wiki/Lama" title="Lama">lama</a> <a href="/wiki/Je_Tsongkhapa" title="Je Tsongkhapa">Je Tsongkhapa</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Gelug" title="Gelug">Gelug</a> school of <a href="/wiki/Buddhism" title="Buddhism">Buddhism</a> declines the offer of the <a href="/wiki/Yongle_Emperor" title="Yongle Emperor">Yongle Emperor</a> of China to appear in the capital at <a href="/wiki/Nanjing" title="Nanjing">Nanjing</a>, although he sends his disciple Chosrje Shākya Yeshes, who is given the title "State Teacher". The later <a href="/wiki/Xuande_Emperor" title="Xuande Emperor">Xuande Emperor</a> will grant Yeshes the title of a king upon a return visit to China (to the new capital at Beijing).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Durham_School" title="Durham School">Durham School</a> is founded as a <a href="/wiki/Grammar_school" title="Grammar school">grammar school</a> in the city of <a href="/wiki/Durham,_England" title="Durham, England">Durham</a> in England by <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Langley" title="Thomas Langley">Thomas Langley</a>, Prince-<a href="/wiki/Bishop_of_Durham" title="Bishop of Durham">Bishop of Durham</a>; it continues in existence as an independent school 600 years later.</li>
</ul>
