<p><b>Sterling Jerins</b> (born June 15, 2004) is an American <a href="/wiki/Child_actor" title="Child actor">child actress</a>, known for playing Lily Bowers on the <a href="/wiki/NBC" title="NBC">NBC</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Deception_(U.S._TV_series)" title="Deception (U.S. TV series)">Deception</a></i>, Constance Lane in <i><a href="/wiki/World_War_Z_(film)" title="World War Z (film)">World War Z</a></i>, Judy Warren in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Conjuring" title="The Conjuring">The Conjuring</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Conjuring_2" title="The Conjuring 2">The Conjuring 2</a></i>, and Lila DuFresne on <a href="/wiki/HBO" title="HBO">HBO</a>'s comedy series <i><a href="/wiki/Divorce_(TV_series)" title="Divorce (TV series)">Divorce</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sterling_Jerins&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2012, Jerins was added to the cast of the zombie apocalypse film <i><a href="/wiki/World_War_Z_(film)" title="World War Z (film)">World War Z</a></i>, directed by <a href="/wiki/Marc_Forster" title="Marc Forster">Marc Forster</a>, to play the role of Constance Lane, the younger daughter of Gerry (<a href="/wiki/Brad_Pitt" title="Brad Pitt">Brad Pitt</a>) and Karen Lane (<a href="/wiki/Mireille_Enos" title="Mireille Enos">Mireille Enos</a>). The film was released on June 21, 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Coup_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Coup-1">[1]</a></sup> <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> She then appeared in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Conjuring" title="The Conjuring">The Conjuring</a></i>, a supernatural horror film directed by <a href="/wiki/James_Wan" title="James Wan">James Wan</a>, which was released on July 19, 2013. Jerins played Judy Warren, the daughter of <a href="/wiki/Ed_and_Lorraine_Warren" title="Ed and Lorraine Warren">Ed and Lorraine Warren</a> (<a href="/wiki/Patrick_Wilson_(American_actor)" title="Patrick Wilson (American actor)">Patrick Wilson</a> and <a href="/wiki/Vera_Farmiga" title="Vera Farmiga">Vera Farmiga</a>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Coup_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Coup-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Jerins co-starred in the romantic comedy <i><a href="/wiki/And_So_It_Goes_(film)" title="And So It Goes (film)">And So It Goes</a></i>, directed by <a href="/wiki/Rob_Reiner" title="Rob Reiner">Rob Reiner</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> playing the supporting role of <a href="/wiki/Michael_Douglas" title="Michael Douglas">Michael Douglas</a>' character's granddaughter.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> In 2015, Jerins co-starred in the mystery thriller <i><a href="/wiki/Dark_Places_(2015_film)" title="Dark Places (2015 film)">Dark Places</a></i>, based on the <a href="/wiki/Dark_Places_(novel)" title="Dark Places (novel)">novel of same name</a> written by <a href="/wiki/Gillian_Flynn" title="Gillian Flynn">Gillian Flynn</a>. Jerins played a young version of <a href="/wiki/Charlize_Theron" title="Charlize Theron">Charlize Theron</a>'s lead character Libby Day.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> Also that same year, she co-starred with <a href="/wiki/Owen_Wilson" title="Owen Wilson">Owen Wilson</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lake_Bell" title="Lake Bell">Lake Bell</a> in the action thriller film <i><a href="/wiki/No_Escape_(2015_film)" title="No Escape (2015 film)">No Escape</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Coup_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-Coup-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2016, she reprised her role as Judy Warren in the supernatural horror film sequel <i><a href="/wiki/The_Conjuring_2" title="The Conjuring 2">The Conjuring 2</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> She currently co-stars in the <a href="/wiki/HBO" title="HBO">HBO</a> comedy series <i><a href="/wiki/Divorce_(TV_series)" title="Divorce (TV series)">Divorce</a></i>, with <a href="/wiki/Sarah_Jessica_Parker" title="Sarah Jessica Parker">Sarah Jessica Parker</a> and <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Haden_Church" title="Thomas Haden Church">Thomas Haden Church</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sterling_Jerins&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Jerins is the daughter of artist Edgar Jerins and actress Alana Jerins. Her elder sister, <a href="/wiki/Ruby_Jerins" title="Ruby Jerins">Ruby Jerins</a>, is also an actress who had a regular role in <i><a href="/wiki/Nurse_Jackie" title="Nurse Jackie">Nurse Jackie</a></i> and appeared in the 2010 films <i><a href="/wiki/Remember_Me_(2010_film)" title="Remember Me (2010 film)">Remember Me</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Shutter_Island_(film)" title="Shutter Island (film)">Shutter Island</a></i>.</p>
