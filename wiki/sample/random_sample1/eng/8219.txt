<p><b>Stéphane Roux</b> is a French comic book artist who is known mostly in America for his cover work for <a href="/wiki/Marvel_Comics" title="Marvel Comics">Marvel</a>, <a href="/wiki/DC_Comics" title="DC Comics">DC</a> and <a href="/wiki/Semic_Comics" title="Semic Comics">Semic</a> comics.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=St%C3%A9phane_Roux_(comics)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Stéphane Roux' has worked on such titles as <i><a href="/wiki/Witchblade" title="Witchblade">Witchblade</a>: Blood Oath</i>, scripted by <a href="/wiki/Jean-Marc_Lofficier" title="Jean-Marc Lofficier">Jean-Marc Lofficier</a>, as well as <i>Sibilla</i> (scripted by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Jean-Marc_Lain%C3%A9&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Jean-Marc Lainé (page does not exist)">Jean-Marc Lainé</a>) and <i><a href="/wiki/Strangers_(French_comic_book)" title="Strangers (French comic book)">Strangers</a></i> in the Semic pocket and comics publications.</p>
<p>His longest cover assignment was on <i><a href="/wiki/Birds_of_Prey_(comics)" title="Birds of Prey (comics)">Birds of Prey</a></i> (issues #104 to 127), with notable covers on various titles including <i><a href="/wiki/Countdown_to_Final_Crisis" title="Countdown to Final Crisis">Countdown to Final Crisis</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Savage_She-Hulk" title="Savage She-Hulk">Savage She-Hulk</a></i>, <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=X-Men:_Worlds_Apart&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="X-Men: Worlds Apart (page does not exist)">X-Men: Worlds Apart</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Amazing_Spider-Man" title="The Amazing Spider-Man">The Amazing Spider-Man</a> Extra</i> #2. He has been working on the DC comics series <i><a href="/wiki/Zatanna" title="Zatanna">Zatanna</a></i>, written by <a href="/wiki/Paul_Dini" title="Paul Dini">Paul Dini</a>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Wars:_Agent_of_the_Empire" title="Star Wars: Agent of the Empire">Star Wars: Agent of the Empire</a></i>, written by <a href="/wiki/John_Ostrander" title="John Ostrander">John Ostrander</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> He is doing covers, fill-ins and short stories for all the majors comic-book companies. He did the poster for <a href="/wiki/New_York_Comic_Con" title="New York Comic Con">New York Comic Con</a> 2013. He recently worked on a <i><a href="/wiki/Batman:_Legends_of_the_Dark_Knight" title="Batman: Legends of the Dark Knight">Batman: Legends of the Dark Knight</a></i> story (issue # 41) written by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ray_Fawkes&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ray Fawkes (page does not exist)">Ray Fawkes</a>. He also drew a <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Batman:_Black_and_White" title="Batman: Black and White">Batman: Black and White</a></i> story, written by <a href="/wiki/Paul_Dini" title="Paul Dini">Paul Dini</a> called "Role Models", in the Batman Black and White issue # 3, Jan 2014.</p>
<p>For French TV, he is one of the directors and the artistic director for the France 3 TV animated show <i><a class="extiw" href="https://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Foot_2_rue" title="fr:Foot 2 rue">Foot 2 rue</a></i>, and does storyboards, character designs and Artistic Direction for animated productions.</p>
