<p><b>Stepan Petrovich Schipachev</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Степа́н Петро́вич Щипачёв</span>; 1898 – 1980) was a Russian <a href="/wiki/Poet" title="Poet">poet</a>. He is best known for the poem <i>Lines of Love</i> and the collections <i>Musings</i> (1962), <i>A Man's Hand</i> (1964), and <i>Selected Works</i> (1965).</p>
