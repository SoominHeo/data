<p><b>Stella Maynes Maxwell</b> (born <span style="display:none">(<span class="bday">1990-05-15</span>)</span>15 May 1990) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Belgian" title="Belgian">Belgian</a>-<a class="mw-disambig" href="/wiki/Irish" title="Irish">Irish</a> fashion <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Model_(people)" title="Model (people)">model</a>. Since 2015, she has been one of <a href="/wiki/Victoria%27s_Secret" title="Victoria's Secret">Victoria's Secret</a>'s Angels.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Stella_Maxwell&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Maxwell was born in Belgium to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Northern_Irish" title="Northern Irish">Northern Irish</a> parents,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Stella Maynes and former Diplomat Maurice Maxwell.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> She has three older siblings: Patrick, Martin, and Helen. Maxwell was raised in Belgium until she was 13 years old,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-mirror_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-mirror-5">[5]</a></sup> where she attended the <a href="/wiki/European_School_of_Brussels_II" title="European School of Brussels II">European School, Woluwe</a>. She then moved with her family to <a href="/wiki/Canberra" title="Canberra">Canberra</a>, Australia,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-mirror_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-mirror-5">[5]</a></sup> where they stayed for one year<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> before moving to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wellington,_New_Zealand" title="Wellington, New Zealand">Wellington</a>, New Zealand when she was 14.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-mirror_5-2"><a href="#cite_note-mirror-5">[5]</a></sup> She attended <a href="/wiki/Queen_Margaret_College,_Wellington" title="Queen Margaret College, Wellington">Queen Margaret College</a> and the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Otago" title="University of Otago">University of Otago</a>, where she was discovered as a model.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-mirror_5-3"><a href="#cite_note-mirror-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Stella_Maxwell&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Her first fashion show was in the year 2009 with Ready to Wear – Autumn / Winter 2009 wherein she proudly walked the ramp for Charles Anastase, Mara Hoffman, Fashion East, Luella, and Osman Yousefzada.</p>
<p>She walked the <a href="/wiki/Victoria%27s_Secret_Fashion_Show_2014" title="Victoria's Secret Fashion Show 2014">Victoria's Secret Fashion Show 2014</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> where she was credited. In 2015, she became a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Victoria%27s_Secret_Angel" title="Victoria's Secret Angel">Victoria's Secret Angel</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p>Maxwell has appeared in editorials for Italian, French, British, Spanish, German, Russian, and Japanese <i><a href="/wiki/Vogue_(magazine)" title="Vogue (magazine)">Vogue</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Harper%27s_Bazaar" title="Harper's Bazaar">Harper's Bazaar</a></i>, American, Italian, French, British, Russian, and Brazilian <i><a href="/wiki/Elle_(magazine)" title="Elle (magazine)">Elle</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Madame_Figaro" title="Madame Figaro">Madame Figaro</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Glamour_(magazine)" title="Glamour (magazine)">Glamour</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/I-D" title="I-D">i-D</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/W_(magazine)" title="W (magazine)">W</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/V_(American_magazine)" title="V (American magazine)">V</a></i>.</p>
<p>She has walked the runways for <a href="/wiki/Marc_Jacobs" title="Marc Jacobs">Marc Jacobs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fendi" title="Fendi">Fendi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Moschino" title="Moschino">Moschino</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dolce_%26_Gabbana" title="Dolce &amp; Gabbana">Dolce &amp; Gabbana</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kenzo" title="Kenzo">Kenzo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alberta_Ferretti" title="Alberta Ferretti">Alberta Ferretti</a>, <a href="/wiki/DKNY" title="DKNY">DKNY</a>, <a href="/wiki/Moncler" title="Moncler">Moncler Gamme Rouge</a>, <a href="/wiki/Versace" title="Versace">Versace</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tory_Burch" title="Tory Burch">Tory Burch</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jeremy_Scott" title="Jeremy Scott">Jeremy Scott</a>, <a href="/wiki/Issey_Miyake" title="Issey Miyake">Issey Miyake</a>, <a href="/wiki/Topshop" title="Topshop">Topshop</a>, <a href="/wiki/Max_Mara" title="Max Mara">Max Mara</a>, <a href="/wiki/Balmain_(fashion_house)" title="Balmain (fashion house)">Balmain</a>, <a href="/wiki/Thierry_Mugler" title="Thierry Mugler">Thierry Mugler</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chanel" title="Chanel">Chanel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tommy_Hilfiger" title="Tommy Hilfiger">Tommy Hilfiger</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/DSquared2" title="DSquared2">DSquared2</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bottega_Veneta" title="Bottega Veneta">Bottega Veneta</a>, <a href="/wiki/Giles_Deacon" title="Giles Deacon">Giles Deacon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chlo%C3%A9" title="Chloé">Chloé</a>, <a href="/wiki/Giambattista_Valli" title="Giambattista Valli">Giambattista Valli</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Miu_Miu" title="Miu Miu">Miu Miu</a>.</p>
<p>Maxwell has appeared in advertising campaigns for <a href="/wiki/Versace" title="Versace">Versace</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alexander_McQueen" title="Alexander McQueen">Alexander McQueen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ermanno_Scervino" title="Ermanno Scervino">Ermanno Scervino</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mango_(clothing)" title="Mango (clothing)">Mango</a>, <a href="/wiki/Max_Factor" title="Max Factor">Max Factor</a>, <a href="/wiki/River_Island" title="River Island">River Island</a>, <a href="/wiki/Urban_Outfitters" title="Urban Outfitters">Urban Outfitters</a>, <a href="/wiki/Puma_SE" title="Puma SE">Puma</a>, Vianel, and <a href="/wiki/Victoria%27s_Secret" title="Victoria's Secret">Victoria's Secret</a>.</p>
<p>In 2016, Maxwell was voted No. 1 in 2016 Maxim's "Hot 100 List".</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Stella_Maxwell&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Maxwell splits her time between <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Los_Angeles,_California" title="Los Angeles, California">Los Angeles, California</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/New_York_City,_New_York" title="New York City, New York">New York City, New York</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
<p>Maxwell reportedly dated singer <a href="/wiki/Miley_Cyrus" title="Miley Cyrus">Miley Cyrus</a> in 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> Since late 2016, she has been dating actress <a href="/wiki/Kristen_Stewart" title="Kristen Stewart">Kristen Stewart</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup></p>
