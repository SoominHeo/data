<p><b>Stomiidae</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Family_(biology)" title="Family (biology)">family</a> of deep-sea <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ray-finned_fish" title="Ray-finned fish">ray-finned fish</a>, including the <b>barbeled dragonfishes</b>. They are quite small, usually around 15cm, up to 26cm.</p>
