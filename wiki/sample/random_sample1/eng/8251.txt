<p><b>Steve Cruz</b> (born on November 4, 1972<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>) is an <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">American</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_performers_in_gay_porn_films" title="List of performers in gay porn films">gay pornographic film actor</a> and director who appears in gay <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pornographic_films" title="Pornographic films">pornographic films</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gay_pornographic_magazines" title="Gay pornographic magazines">magazines</a>. Cruz is signed as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Raging_Stallion" title="Raging Stallion">Raging Stallion</a> exclusive. Cruz won the 2008 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/GayVN_Award" title="GayVN Award">GayVN Award</a> for Best Group Scene in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chi_Chi_Larue" title="Chi Chi Larue">Chi Chi Larue</a>'s <i>Link 5: The Evolution</i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> and the 2010 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/GayVN_Award" title="GayVN Award">GayVN Award</a> for Best Supporting Actor in Raging Stallion's <i>Focus/Refocus</i> with Gary Gibbon. He filmed <i>Link 5: The Evolution</i> prior to his contact with Raging Stallion. Cruz won the 2008 Grabby Award for Best Newcomer and Best Group Scene<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> in <a href="/wiki/Chi_Chi_LaRue" title="Chi Chi LaRue">Chi Chi LaRue</a>'s <i>Link 5: The Evolution</i>. He also was named Raging Stallion's man of the year in 2008.</p>
