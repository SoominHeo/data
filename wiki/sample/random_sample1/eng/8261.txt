<p><b>Stipe Lapić</b> (born 22 January 1983,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-summary_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-summary-1">[1]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Sinj" title="Sinj">Sinj</a>, <a href="/wiki/Croatia" title="Croatia">Croatia</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/Croatia" title="Croatia">Croatian</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> player who last played for <a href="/wiki/China_League_One" title="China League One">China League One</a> side <a href="/wiki/Chongqing_F.C." title="Chongqing F.C.">Chongqing F.C.</a>.</p>
<p>He played at <a href="/wiki/HNK_Hajduk_Split" title="HNK Hajduk Split">HNK Hajduk Split</a> and <a href="/wiki/PSV_Eindhoven" title="PSV Eindhoven">PSV Eindhoven</a>'s under-19 team. His first senior team was <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/NK_Dinamo_Zagreb" title="NK Dinamo Zagreb">NK Dinamo Zagreb</a>. From this, he played several team in some countries. He also capped <a href="/wiki/Croatia_national_under-21_football_team" title="Croatia national under-21 football team">Croatia national under-21 football team</a>.</p>
