<p><b>Stylianos "Stelios" Malezas</b> (<a href="/wiki/Greek_language" title="Greek language">Greek</a>: <span lang="el">Στέλιος Μαλεζάς</span>; born 11 March 1985) is a Greek professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">defender</a> for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/PAOK_F.C." title="PAOK F.C.">PAOK</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Greek_Super_League" title="Greek Super League">Greek Super League</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Stelios_Malezas&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="PAOK">PAOK</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Stelios_Malezas&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: PAOK">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>He started his career at AEP Katerini and moved to PAOK in 2003, thus signing his first professional contract. He plays as a centre back, having formerly played as a defensive midfielder. In the 2008–09 season, he gained his place in the starting eleven, making some regular first-team appearances. On 6 February 2009, he signed a new contract with <a href="/wiki/PAOK_FC" title="PAOK FC">PAOK FC</a>, which would keep him at the club until 2013. During all these years he played 164 games with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/PAOK" title="PAOK">PAOK</a> in all competitions.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Fortuna_D.C3.BCsseldorf">Fortuna Düsseldorf</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Stelios_Malezas&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Fortuna Düsseldorf">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Defender Malezas of Greek club <a href="/wiki/PAOK_FC" title="PAOK FC">PAOK FC</a>, is making the move to Germany for a reported fee of €577,000 (£450,000), with the money being paid in instalments over the next 12 months.Malezas was on Monday undergoing a medical and <a href="/wiki/Fortuna_D%C3%BCsseldorf" title="Fortuna Düsseldorf">Fortuna Düsseldorf</a>, who last season won promotion via the play-offs, are then expected to officially announce a three-year contract. General manager Wolf Werner told Express newspaper: <i>"We are about to sign Stelios Malezas from PAOK of Greece."</i> <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>In December 2013, Malezas was informed that is already named for returning to his former club <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/PAOK" title="PAOK">PAOK</a> and in an interview held from PAOK24 he said: <i>"As a mark of respect for the club, it must be clear that the given time there has been no communication with people of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/PAOK" title="PAOK">PAOK</a> nor the possibility of my return to it. I have a contract with Fortuna until summer. I do not know what will happen in January and after that I have the right to negotiate, but as we speak there is absolutely not any communication with PAOK."</i> Otherwise Malezas would not say no to the possibility of a return to PAOK, but this time there is nothing to confirm that he wants to leave Germany.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>The German dream seems to take a fee for Stelios Malezas, who the summer of 2014 will be past for the roster of <a href="/wiki/Fortuna_D%C3%BCsseldorf" title="Fortuna Düsseldorf">Fortuna Düsseldorf</a>. He failed to prove his worth to the people of the German club and specifically, the former PAOK defender has only made 10 appearances after half of the season passed. The coach of Fortuna, <a href="/wiki/Lorenz-G%C3%BCnther_K%C3%B6stner" title="Lorenz-Günther Köstner">Lorenz-Günther Köstner</a> believes that the former Thessalonian player can not cope with the demands of the team, so it can be assumed that is almost in exit.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Eventually on May 23, 2014 after two years with the club was informed that there was not in the staff's plan for next season. Malezas stated that <i>" Everybody does not like goodbyes, but I must do it. I have no choice"</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Panetolikos">Panetolikos</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Stelios_Malezas&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Panetolikos">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 11 July 2014, Malezas joined <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Panetolikos" title="Panetolikos">Panetolikos</a> on a two-year contract,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> after he completed his transfer from <a href="/wiki/Fortuna_D%C3%BCsseldorf" title="Fortuna Düsseldorf">Fortuna Düsseldorf</a> as a free agent.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Return_to_PAOK">Return to PAOK</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Stelios_Malezas&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Return to PAOK">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 10 August 2015, Malezas joined for a second spell <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/PAOK_F.C." title="PAOK F.C.">PAOK</a>, signing a two years' contract for an undisclosed fee. <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Stelios_Malezas&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On 28 February 2010, he was called up to represent Greece in a friendly against <a href="/wiki/Senegal_national_football_team" title="Senegal national football team">Senegal</a>. On 1 June it was announced that he would be in the 23-man squad for the <a href="/wiki/2010_FIFA_World_Cup" title="2010 FIFA World Cup">2010 FIFA World Cup</a>. He was also to the 23-man squad for the <a href="/wiki/UEFA_Euro_2012" title="UEFA Euro 2012">UEFA Euro 2012</a>.</p>
