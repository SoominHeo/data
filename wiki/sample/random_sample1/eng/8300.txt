<p><b>Spencer Locke</b> (born September 20, 1991) is an American actress. She is best known for her roles in <i><a href="/wiki/Cougar_Town" title="Cougar Town">Cougar Town</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Monster_House_(film)" title="Monster House (film)">Monster House</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Detention_(2011_film)" title="Detention (2011 film)">Detention</a></i>, and in the <i><a href="/wiki/Resident_Evil_(film_series)" title="Resident Evil (film series)">Resident Evil</a></i> film series.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Spencer_Locke&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Prior to appearing as Candida on <i><a href="/wiki/Phil_of_the_Future" title="Phil of the Future">Phil of the Future</a></i>, she had a recurring role as Bitsy on season one of the <a href="/wiki/Nickelodeon" title="Nickelodeon">Nickelodeon</a> <a href="/wiki/Television_program" title="Television program">series</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Ned%27s_Declassified_School_Survival_Guide" title="Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide">Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide</a></i>.</p>
<p>Locke has also guest starred as Brandee Case in <i><a href="/wiki/Without_a_Trace" title="Without a Trace">Without a Trace</a></i> in the episode "Wannabe", Kayla in <i><a href="/wiki/That%27s_So_Raven" title="That's So Raven">That's So Raven</a></i> in episode "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mad_Hot_Cotillion" title="Mad Hot Cotillion">Mad Hot Cotillion</a>", and as Jenny in the animated film <i><a href="/wiki/Monster_House_(film)" title="Monster House (film)">Monster House</a>.</i> She appeared in the <a href="/wiki/2007_in_film" title="2007 in film">2007 film</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Resident_Evil:_Extinction" title="Resident Evil: Extinction">Resident Evil: Extinction</a></i> as a girl found in the ruins of a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/K-Mart" title="K-Mart">K-Mart</a>, hoping to find a safe haven in Alaska.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HollywoodReporter_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-HollywoodReporter-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Spencer reprised her role as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Characters_in_the_Resident_Evil_films#K-Mart" title="Characters in the Resident Evil films">K-Mart</a> in the film <i><a href="/wiki/Resident_Evil:_Afterlife" title="Resident Evil: Afterlife">Resident Evil: Afterlife</a></i>, released September 10, 2010. She also has appeared as Amber Bradley on <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Vampire_Diaries_(TV_series)" title="The Vampire Diaries (TV series)">The Vampire Diaries</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-HollywoodReporter_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-HollywoodReporter-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Spencer guest starred in the <a href="/wiki/American_Broadcasting_Company" title="American Broadcasting Company">ABC</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Primetime" title="Primetime">primetime</a> comedy <i><a href="/wiki/Cougar_Town" title="Cougar Town">Cougar Town</a></i> as Kylie, Travis' girlfriend. She appeared regularly in season one and briefly during season two. On screen, the pair separated when Travis moved to college while Kylie was still in high school. Spencer made her last appearance as Kylie in a brief scene on the season two episode <i>Little Girl Blues</i> when Travis introduces his new girlfriend Kirsten (<a href="/wiki/Collette_Wolfe" title="Collette Wolfe">Collette Wolfe</a>) to the family. She has not appeared since.</p>
<p>Locke is the voice of <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Witch_and_Wizard:_The_Gift#Characters" title="Witch and Wizard: The Gift">Wisty Allgood</a></i> in the audiobooks of the <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Witch_and_Wizard" title="Witch and Wizard">Witch and Wizard</a></i> series by author <a href="/wiki/James_Patterson" title="James Patterson">James Patterson</a>. She was also the voice of Ashley in Forza Horizon 2 for Xbox.</p>
<p>She used to be on Nickelodeon's TV show <i><a href="/wiki/Big_Time_Rush" title="Big Time Rush">Big Time Rush</a></i> as one of the Jennifers.</p>
