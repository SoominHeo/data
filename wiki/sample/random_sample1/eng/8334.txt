<p>The <b>Slovenia national basketball team</b> (<a href="/wiki/Slovene_language" title="Slovene language">Slovene</a>: <span lang="sl"><i>Slovenska košarkarska reprezentanca</i></span>) is a team that is selected by the <a href="/wiki/Basketball_Federation_of_Slovenia" title="Basketball Federation of Slovenia">Basketball Federation of Slovenia</a> to compete in major international <a href="/wiki/Basketball" title="Basketball">basketball</a> events in representation of <a href="/wiki/Slovenia" title="Slovenia">Slovenia</a>.</p>
<p>Slovenia ended in the 6th place in the <a href="/wiki/EuroBasket_2005" title="EuroBasket 2005">EuroBasket 2005</a> and thus qualified for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Basketball_World_Championship_2006" title="Basketball World Championship 2006">Basketball World Championship 2006</a>. Since the independence of the country in 1991, the team has competed at every <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/FIBA_EuroBasket" title="FIBA EuroBasket">FIBA EuroBasket</a>, but failed to advance to the next group until 2005.</p>
