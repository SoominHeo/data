<p><b>Slussen</b> is a station of the <a href="/wiki/Stockholm_metro" title="Stockholm metro">Stockholm metro</a>, located in <a href="/wiki/Slussenomr%C3%A5det" title="Slussenområdet">Slussenområdet</a> in the district of <a href="/wiki/S%C3%B6dermalm" title="Södermalm">Södermalm</a>. The station is served by the red and green lines. Originally opened in 1933 as an underground tram stop, in 1950 it became the terminal of the first metro line running south to <a href="/wiki/H%C3%B6kar%C3%A4ngen_metro_station" title="Hökarängen metro station">Hökarängen</a>, it was again rebuilt in 1957 when the line north to <a href="/wiki/H%C3%B6torget_metro_station" title="Hötorget metro station">Hötorget</a> was opened.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Just outside the northern entrance to the metro station, there is a bus terminal for buses to the <a href="/wiki/Nacka_Municipality" title="Nacka Municipality">Nacka</a> and <a href="/wiki/V%C3%A4rmd%C3%B6_Municipality" title="Värmdö Municipality">Värmdö</a> municipalities. The terminus for the <a href="/wiki/Saltsj%C3%B6banan" title="Saltsjöbanan">Saltsjöbanan</a> railway was also located here, but it was moved to <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Henriksdal&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Henriksdal (page does not exist)">Henriksdal</a> in 2016 due to the reconstruction of Slussen. Traffic will resume when the reconstruction is complete, possibly in 2026.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
