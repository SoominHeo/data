<p><b>Shigino Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">鴫野駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Shigino-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Railway_station" title="Railway station">railway station</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Osaka_Municipal_Subway" title="Osaka Municipal Subway">Osaka Municipal Subway</a> <a href="/wiki/Imazatosuji_Line" title="Imazatosuji Line">Imazatosuji Line</a> and the <a href="/wiki/West_Japan_Railway_Company" title="West Japan Railway Company">West Japan Railway Company</a> (JR West) <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gakkentoshi_Line" title="Gakkentoshi Line">Katamachi Line (Gakkentoshi Line)</a> in <a href="/wiki/J%C5%8Dt%C5%8D-ku,_Osaka" title="Jōtō-ku, Osaka">Jōtō-ku</a>, <a href="/wiki/Osaka" title="Osaka">Osaka</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Layout">Layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shigino_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="JR_West">JR West</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shigino_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: JR West">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>There are two <a href="/wiki/Side_platform" title="Side platform">side platforms</a> with two elevated tracks. Construction on two additional tracks and one new platform on the south side of the station began in July 2011; when the Osaka-Higashi Line is extended to this station, it will use the existing tracks and platforms, with the Gakkentoshi Line moving to the two newly constructed tracks. The station will have two side platforms and one island platform in the middle, serving four tracks.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Osaka_Municipal_Subway">Osaka Municipal Subway</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shigino_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Osaka Municipal Subway">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
