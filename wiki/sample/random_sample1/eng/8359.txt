<p><b>Shinano-Kawashima Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">信濃川島駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Shinano-Kawashima-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Railway_station" title="Railway station">railway station</a> in Kawashima, <a href="/wiki/Tatsuno,_Nagano" title="Tatsuno, Nagano">Tatsuno Town</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kamiina_District,_Nagano" title="Kamiina District, Nagano">Kamiina District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nagano_Prefecture" title="Nagano Prefecture">Nagano Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>, operated by <a href="/wiki/East_Japan_Railway_Company" title="East Japan Railway Company">East Japan Railway Company</a> (JR East).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lines">Lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shinano-Kawashima_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Shinano-Kawashima Station is served by the old route of <a href="/wiki/Ch%C5%AB%C5%8D_Main_Line" title="Chūō Main Line">Chūō Main Line</a> (Okaya-Shiojiri branch) and is 13.8 kilometers from the branching point of the line at <a href="/wiki/Okaya_Station" title="Okaya Station">Okaya Station</a> and 224.2 from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Terminal_station" title="Terminal station">terminus</a> at <a href="/wiki/Tokyo_Station" title="Tokyo Station">Tokyo Station</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_layout">Station layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shinano-Kawashima_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Station layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The station consists of one ground-level <a href="/wiki/Side_platform" title="Side platform">side platform</a> serving a single bi-directional track. The station does not have station building, but only a shelter on the platform. The station is unattended.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shinano-Kawashima_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Shinano-Kawashima Station opened on 1 April 1955. With the privatization of <a href="/wiki/Japanese_National_Railways" title="Japanese National Railways">Japanese National Railways</a> (JNR) on 1 April 1987, the station came under the control of JR East.</p>
