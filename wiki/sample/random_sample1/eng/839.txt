<p>The <b>CAF Confederation Cup</b> is an international club <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">association football</a> competition run by the <a href="/wiki/Confederation_of_African_Football" title="Confederation of African Football">Confederation of African Football</a>. Select club sides from Africa's football leagues are invited to participate in this competition, which is the second club football competition in the continent behind the <a href="/wiki/CAF_Champions_League" title="CAF Champions League">CAF Champions League</a>.</p>
<p>The winner of the tournament faces the winner of the CAF Champions League in the following season's <a href="/wiki/CAF_Super_Cup" title="CAF Super Cup">CAF Super Cup</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=CAF_Confederation_Cup&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="floatright"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:CAF_Confederation_Cup.png"><img alt="CAF Confederation Cup.png" data-file-height="100" data-file-width="100" height="100" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/b/b9/CAF_Confederation_Cup.png" width="100"/></a></div>
<p>The competition was founded by the <a href="/wiki/Confederation_of_African_Football" title="Confederation of African Football">CAF</a> in 2004 by merging both the <a href="/wiki/African_Cup_Winners%27_Cup" title="African Cup Winners' Cup">African Cup Winners' Cup</a> and the <a href="/wiki/CAF_Cup" title="CAF Cup">CAF Cup</a> in a single competition.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualification">Qualification</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=CAF_Confederation_Cup&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Qualification">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>From the <a href="/wiki/CAF_5-Year_Ranking" title="CAF 5-Year Ranking">top twelve</a> placed <a href="/wiki/Confederation_of_African_Football" title="Confederation of African Football">CAF</a> member associations, the winner of the domestic cup and the third placed club in the domestic league of the considered associations, are eligible to participate in the CAF Confederation Cup, while only domestic cup winners from member associations ranked from 13 till 55 are eligible to participate in the competition.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Format">Format</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=CAF_Confederation_Cup&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Format">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The competition is played into two phases A and B.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Phase_A">Phase A</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=CAF_Confederation_Cup&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Phase A">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The matches of the Preliminary, the 1/16th, the 1/8th rounds are played according to the knock-out system with ties broken via the <a href="/wiki/Away_goals_rule" title="Away goals rule">Away goals rule</a>. The eight teams eliminated from the 1/8th finals of the <a href="/wiki/CAF_Champions_League" title="CAF Champions League">CAF Champions League</a> will automatically qualify to play the additional 1/8th finals round of the CAF Confederation Cup.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Phase_B">Phase B</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=CAF_Confederation_Cup&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Phase B">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>The eight teams which will qualify for the group matches from the additional 1/8th finals will be divided in two groups of four each. Each team shall play six matches against the other three opponents one match home and one match away and points granted upon the 3-1-0 system.</li>
<li>The two group winners shall meet in the final which shall be played in two matches, home and away. The team scoring the highest aggregate number of goals in the two matches will be declared winner.</li>
<li>In case of equality in the number of goals scored during the two matches, the team scoring the greatest number of away goals will be declared winner. If the number of goals scored on the away matches is equal, kicks from the penalty mark will be taken.</li>
</ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="The_Super_Cup">The Super Cup</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=CAF_Confederation_Cup&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: The Super Cup">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The winning team of the CAF Confederation Cup will take the engagement of playing the <a href="/wiki/CAF_Super_Cup" title="CAF Super Cup">CAF Super Cup</a> against the champion of the <a href="/wiki/CAF_Champions_League" title="CAF Champions League">CAF Champions League</a>. The match will be played in the following year to that of the concerned competition, in one match, on the CAF Champions League champion's venue.</p>
