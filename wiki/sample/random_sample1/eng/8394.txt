<p><b>Cyrille Thouvenin</b> (born 15 May 1976) is a French actor. Former pupil of <a href="/wiki/Cours_Florent" title="Cours Florent">Cours Florent</a> and a graduate of the <a href="/wiki/CNSAD" title="CNSAD">National Conservatory of Dramatic Art</a> (class of 2001).</p>
<p>Fascinated by <a href="/wiki/Moli%C3%A8re" title="Molière">Molière</a>, he started on television and then quickly made his classes at the cinema. He was appointed to Caesars in 2001 as "Most Promising Newcomer" for <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=La_Confusion_des_genres&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="La Confusion des genres (page does not exist)">La Confusion des genres</a></i>. He followed roles in film and television. He does not forget the theater, and also devoted himself to writing.</p>
<p>In 2011, he co-wrote and directed the show <i><a href="/wiki/Laurent_Lafitte" title="Laurent Lafitte">Laurent Lafitte</a>: comme son nom l'indique</i> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Palais_des_Glaces" title="Palais des Glaces">palais des Glaces</a> and <a href="/wiki/Th%C3%A9%C3%A2tre_des_Mathurins" title="Théâtre des Mathurins">théâtre des Mathurins</a>.</p>
