<p><b>Shimo-Igusa Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">下井草駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Shimo-Igusa-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a railway station on the <a href="/wiki/Seibu_Shinjuku_Line" title="Seibu Shinjuku Line">Seibu Shinjuku Line</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Suginami,_Tokyo" title="Suginami, Tokyo">Suginami, Tokyo</a>, Japan, operated by the private railway operator <a href="/wiki/Seibu_Railway" title="Seibu Railway">Seibu Railway</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lines">Lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shimo-Igusa_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Shimo-Igusa Station Station is served by the 47.5 km <a href="/wiki/Seibu_Shinjuku_Line" title="Seibu Shinjuku Line">Seibu Shinjuku Line</a> from <a href="/wiki/Seibu-Shinjuku_Station" title="Seibu-Shinjuku Station">Seibu-Shinjuku</a> in Tokyo to <a href="/wiki/Hon-Kawagoe_Station" title="Hon-Kawagoe Station">Hon-Kawagoe</a> in <a href="/wiki/Saitama_Prefecture" title="Saitama Prefecture">Saitama Prefecture</a>, with some through services to and from <a href="/wiki/Haijima_Station" title="Haijima Station">Haijima</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Seibu_Haijima_Line" title="Seibu Haijima Line">Seibu Haijima Line</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shimo-Igusa_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Shimo-Igusa Station opened on 16 April 1927.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-terada2013_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-terada2013-1">[1]</a></sup> Station numbering was introduced on all Seibu Railway lines during fiscal 2012, with Shimo-Igusa Station becoming "SS10".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Passenger_statistics">Passenger statistics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shimo-Igusa_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Passenger statistics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
