<p><b>Keita Shimizu</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">清水 圭太</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Shimizu Keita</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born June 24, 1961, in <a href="/wiki/Uji,_Kyoto" title="Uji, Kyoto">Uji, Kyoto</a>, Japan)</span>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> simply known as <b>Kei Shimizu</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">清水 圭</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Shimizu Kei</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>, is a Japanese comedian who is represented by the talent agency, <a href="/wiki/Yoshimoto_Kogyo" title="Yoshimoto Kogyo">Yoshimoto Kogyo</a>.</p>
