<p><b>Naoemon Shimizu</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">清水 直右衛門</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Naoemon Shimizu</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, ? – August 6, 1945)</span> was a <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> player. He played for <a href="/wiki/Japan_national_football_team" title="Japan national football team">Japan national team</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Playing_career">Playing career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Naoemon_Shimizu&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Playing career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Naoemon Shimizu played for <a href="/wiki/Rijo_Shukyu_FC" title="Rijo Shukyu FC">Rijo Shukyu FC</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="National_team">National team</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Naoemon_Shimizu&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: National team">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Japan_national_football_team" title="Japan national football team">Japan national team</a> for the <a href="/wiki/1923_Far_Eastern_Championship_Games" title="1923 Far Eastern Championship Games">1923 Far Eastern Championship Games</a> <a href="/wiki/Football_at_the_1923_Far_Eastern_Championship_Games" title="Football at the 1923 Far Eastern Championship Games">football competition</a> in <a href="/wiki/Osaka" title="Osaka">Osaka</a>.</p>
