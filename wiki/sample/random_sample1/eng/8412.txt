<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Buddhism_and_philosophy">Buddhism and philosophy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kanon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Buddhism and philosophy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>Kanon, a Japanese name for <a href="/wiki/Guanyin" title="Guanyin">Guanyin</a>, an East Asian bodhisattva and spiritual figure associated with mercy and compassion</li>

</ul>
