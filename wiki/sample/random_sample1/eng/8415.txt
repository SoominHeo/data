<p><b>Siberia Governorate</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">Сибирская губерния</span>) was an administrative division (a <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Guberniya" title="Guberniya">guberniya</a></i>) of the <a href="/wiki/Tsardom_of_Russia" title="Tsardom of Russia">Tsardom of Russia</a> and then the <a href="/wiki/Russian_Empire" title="Russian Empire">Russian Empire</a>, which existed from 1708 until 1782. Its seat was in <a href="/wiki/Tobolsk" title="Tobolsk">Tobolsk</a> (initially spelt as <i>Tobolesk</i>). The governorate was located in the east of Russian Empire and bordered <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a> in the south, <a href="/wiki/Kazan_Governorate" title="Kazan Governorate">Kazan Governorate</a> in the southwest, and <a href="/wiki/Archangelgorod_Governorate" title="Archangelgorod Governorate">Archangelgorod Governorate</a> in the northwest. In the north and the east, the governorate was limited by the seas of the Arctic and Pacific Oceans. In therms of the area, Siberia Governorate was by far the biggest among all the governorates, as it included areas in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Urals" title="Urals">Urals</a>, <a href="/wiki/Siberia" title="Siberia">Siberia</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Russian_Far_East" title="Russian Far East">Russian Far East</a> some of which were not yet even settled by Russians at the time.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Establishment">Establishment</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Siberia_Governorate&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Establishment">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Siberia Governorate, together with seven other governorates, was established on December 29 [<a href="/wiki/Adoption_of_the_Gregorian_calendar" title="Adoption of the Gregorian calendar">O.S.</a> December 18], 1708, by <a href="/wiki/Tsar" title="Tsar">Tsar</a> <a href="/wiki/Peter_the_Great" title="Peter the Great">Peter the Great</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Ukase" title="Ukase">edict</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1708List_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-1708List-1">[1]</a></sup> As with the rest of the governorates, neither the borders nor internal subdivisions of Siberia Governorate were defined; instead, the territory was defined as a set of cities and the lands adjacent to those cities.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Tarkhov_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Tarkhov-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transformations_and_disestablishment">Transformations and disestablishment</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Siberia_Governorate&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Transformations and disestablishment">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright">
<div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Capital_of_Siberia.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="1644" data-file-width="4240" height="85" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/68/Capital_of_Siberia.jpg/220px-Capital_of_Siberia.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/68/Capital_of_Siberia.jpg/330px-Capital_of_Siberia.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/68/Capital_of_Siberia.jpg/440px-Capital_of_Siberia.jpg 2x" width="220"/></a>
<div class="thumbcaption">
<div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Capital_of_Siberia.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>
Tobolsk in 1750</div>
</div>
</div>
<p>In 1719, the governorate was divided into three provinces: <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Vyatka_Province&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Vyatka Province (page does not exist)">Vyatka</a>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Solikamsk_Province&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Solikamsk Province (page does not exist)">Solikamsk</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tobolsk_Province" title="Tobolsk Province">Tobolsk</a>. Simultaneously, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Yarensky_Uyezd&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Yarensky Uyezd (page does not exist)">Yarensky Uyezd</a> with the administrative center of <a href="/wiki/Yarensk" title="Yarensk">Yarensk</a> was moved from Siberia Governorate to <a href="/wiki/Archangelgorod_Governorate" title="Archangelgorod Governorate">Archangelgorod Governorate</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 1724, Tobolsk Province was split into <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Yeniseysk_Province&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Yeniseysk Province (page does not exist)">Yeniseysk</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Irkutsk_Province" title="Irkutsk Province">Irkutsk</a>, and Tobolsk Provinces. In 1727, Vyatka and Solikamsk Provinces were transferred to Kazan Governorate.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sofronov_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-sofronov-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 1736, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Okhotsky_Uyezd&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Okhotsky Uyezd (page does not exist)">Okhotsky Uyezd</a> was split off from <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Yakutsky_Uyezd&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Yakutsky Uyezd (page does not exist)">Yakutsky Uyezd</a>. In the same year, Siberia Governorate was split into two independent areas: <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Siberia_Province&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Siberia Province (page does not exist)">Siberia Province</a>, which consisted of the former Tobolsk and Yeniseysk Provinces, under the authority of the governor (posted in Tobolsk), and Irkutsk Province. In 1737, the areas in the South Urals were organized into <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Iset_Province&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Iset Province (page does not exist)">Iset Province</a> with the center in the town of <a href="/wiki/Shadrinsk" title="Shadrinsk">Shadrinsk</a>, and the province was transferred into <a href="/wiki/Orenburg_Governorate" title="Orenburg Governorate">Orenburg Governorate</a>. In 1764, <a href="/wiki/Irkutsk_Governorate" title="Irkutsk Governorate">Irkutsk Governorate</a> was established in place of the former Irkutsk Province.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sofronov_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-sofronov-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 1782, Siberia Governorate was abolished, and its area split into <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Tobolsk_Viceroyalty&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Tobolsk Viceroyalty (page does not exist)">Tobolsk Viceroyalty</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kolyvan_Viceroyalty&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kolyvan Viceroyalty (page does not exist)">Kolyvan Viceroyalty</a>. Irkutsk Governorate was transformed into <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Irkutsk_Viceroyalty&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Irkutsk Viceroyalty (page does not exist)">Irkutsk Viceroyalty</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sofronov_4-2"><a href="#cite_note-sofronov-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Governors">Governors</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Siberia_Governorate&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Governors">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The administration of the governorate was performed by a governor. The governors of Siberia Governorate were<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li>1708-1714 <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Matvey_Gagarin&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Matvey Gagarin (page does not exist)">Matvey Petrovich Gagarin</a></li>
<li>1714-1716 Ivan Bibikov (acting governor)</li>
<li>1716-1719 Matvey Petrovich Gagarin, imprisoned in 1719 and executed by <a href="/wiki/Hanging" title="Hanging">hanging</a> in 1721 for corruption;</li>
<li>1719-1724 Alexey Mikhaylovich Cherkassky</li>
<li>1724-1726 Mikhail Vladimirovich Dolgorukov</li>
<li>1726-1727 Alexey Mikhaylovich Surov (acting governor)</li>
<li>1727-1728 Mikhail Vladimirovich Dolgorukov</li>
<li>1728-1731 Ivan Vasilyevich Boltin (vice-governor, acting governor)</li>
<li>1730 <a href="/wiki/Vasily_Lukich_Dolgorukov" title="Vasily Lukich Dolgorukov">Vasily Lukich Dolgorukov</a> (never arrived to Tobolsk, imprisoned and subsequently executed in 1739)</li>
<li>1731-1736 Alexey Lvovich Pleshcheyev</li>
<li>1736-1741 Pyotr Ivanovich Buturlin</li>
<li>1741-1742 Ivan Afanasyevich Shipov</li>
<li>1742-1752 Alexey Mikhaylovich Sukharev</li>
<li>1754-1757 Vasily Alexeyevich Myatlev</li>
<li>1757-1763 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fyodor_Soymonov" title="Fyodor Soymonov">Fyodor Ivanovich Soymonov</a></li>
<li>1763-1780 Denis Ivanovich Chicherin</li>
</ul>
