<p>The <b>Sears Centre</b> is an 11,000-seat multi-purpose arena in <a href="/wiki/Hoffman_Estates,_Illinois" title="Hoffman Estates, Illinois">Hoffman Estates, Illinois</a>, a northwest <a href="/wiki/Suburb" title="Suburb">suburb</a>, 25 miles from <a href="/wiki/Chicago" title="Chicago">Chicago</a>. The arena has 43 luxury suites on two separate levels. The venue opened on October 26, 2006, with performances by <a href="/wiki/Duran_Duran" title="Duran Duran">Duran Duran</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bob_Dylan" title="Bob Dylan">Bob Dylan</a>; it was estimated to attract over 750,000 visitors annually.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-searscentre.com_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-searscentre.com-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The arena is currently home to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chicago_Mustangs_(MASL)" title="Chicago Mustangs (MASL)">Chicago Mustangs</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Major_Arena_Soccer_League" title="Major Arena Soccer League">Major Arena Soccer League</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Windy_City_Bulls" title="Windy City Bulls">Windy City Bulls</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Chicago_Bulls" title="Chicago Bulls">Chicago Bulls</a>' affiliate in the <a href="/wiki/NBA_Development_League" title="NBA Development League">NBA Development League</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-rock150120_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-rock150120-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>The Sears Centre is located near the former site of the <a href="/wiki/Poplar_Creek_Music_Theater" title="Poplar Creek Music Theater">Poplar Creek Music Theater</a>, a venue that was also located in Hoffman Estates from 1980 to 1994. Food services are provided by <a href="/wiki/Levy_Restaurants" title="Levy Restaurants">Levy Restaurants</a>.</p>
<p>In 2011, the Village of Hoffman Estates took over ownership of the arena after <a href="/wiki/Ryan_Companies_US,_Inc." title="Ryan Companies US, Inc.">Ryan Companies US, Inc.</a> walked away from the arena due to the arena's lack of success.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> However, since the village took over the arena and hired Global Spectrum to manage it,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> the arena has shown improvements.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
