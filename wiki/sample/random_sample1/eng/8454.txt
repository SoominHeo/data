<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_overview">Station overview</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=City_Council_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Station overview">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The station is a two-level, underground station with an <a href="/wiki/Island_platform" title="Island platform">island platform</a> and four exits.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The station is 206 meters long and is located at the intersection of Jhongjheng 4th Rd. and Jhonghua 3rd Rd.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
