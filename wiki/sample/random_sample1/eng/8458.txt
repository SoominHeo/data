<p><b>Shizuoka Broadcasting System, Inc.</b> (<b>SBS</b>, <b>静岡放送</b>株式会社) is a <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> broadcaster in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Shizuoka_City" title="Shizuoka City">Shizuoka</a>. Its <a href="/wiki/Radio_station" title="Radio station">radio station</a> is affiliated with <a href="/wiki/Japan_Radio_Network" title="Japan Radio Network">Japan Radio Network (JRN)</a> and <a href="/wiki/National_Radio_Network_(Japan)" title="National Radio Network (Japan)">National Radio Network (NRN)</a>, and its <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/TV_station" title="TV station">TV station</a> is affiliated with <a href="/wiki/Japan_News_Network" title="Japan News Network">JNN (Japan News Network)</a>.</p>
<p></p>
