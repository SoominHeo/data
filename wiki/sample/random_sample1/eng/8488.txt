<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">List of tenants in Six World Trade Center</h1>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Six World Trade Center</b>, building 6 of the World Trade Center in <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a> was a U.S. Customhouse. completed in 1975 at a height of 105 ft (32 m). It was heavily damaged during the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/September_11,_2001_attacks" title="September 11, 2001 attacks">September 11, 2001 attacks</a> by the collapse of the North Tower at 10:28 a.m. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Eastern_Daylight_Time" title="Eastern Daylight Time">EDT</a> and later demolished in 2002. The following is a list of tenants of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/6_World_Trade_Center" title="6 World Trade Center">6 World Trade Center</a> prior to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/September_11,_2001_attacks" title="September 11, 2001 attacks">September 11, 2001 attacks</a>:</p>
