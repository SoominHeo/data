<p><b>Singyeongju Station</b> is a new station near the city of <a href="/wiki/Gyeongju" title="Gyeongju">Gyeongju</a>. It is on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gyeongbu_KTX_Line" title="Gyeongbu KTX Line">Gyeongbu KTX Line</a>. <i>Singyeongju Station</i> means "new Gyeongju Station." It should not be confused with <a href="/wiki/Gyeongju_Station" title="Gyeongju Station">Gyeongju Station</a>, which does not serve high speed trains.</p>
