<p><b>Sin Da-Woon</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 신다운) (born March 5, 1993) is a South Korean <a href="/wiki/Short_track_speed_skating" title="Short track speed skating">short track speed skater</a>. He won two distances and the overall classification at the <a href="/wiki/2013_World_Short_Track_Speed_Skating_Championships" title="2013 World Short Track Speed Skating Championships">2013 World Short Track Speed Skating Championships</a>.</p>
