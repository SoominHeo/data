<p>The <b>Sind bat</b> (<i>Eptesicus nasutus</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> of <a href="/wiki/Vesper_bat" title="Vesper bat">vesper bat</a>. It is found in <a href="/wiki/Afghanistan" title="Afghanistan">Afghanistan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iraq" title="Iraq">Iraq</a>, <a href="/wiki/Oman" title="Oman">Oman</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pakistan" title="Pakistan">Pakistan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Saudi_Arabia" title="Saudi Arabia">Saudi Arabia</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Yemen" title="Yemen">Yemen</a>.</p>
