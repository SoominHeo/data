<p><b>Xinbei District</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">新北区</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">新北區</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Xīnběi Qū</span></i>; literally: "new north"), <span class="citation-needed-content" style="padding-left:0.1em; padding-right:0.1em; color:#595959; border:1px solid #DDD;">alternatively called the New District or the High-tech Development Zone</span><sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="margin-left:0.1em; white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (June 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup>, is one of five district under the jurisdiction of <a href="/wiki/Changzhou" title="Changzhou">Changzhou</a> in <a href="/wiki/Jiangsu" title="Jiangsu">Jiangsu</a> province of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/People%27s_Republic_of_China" title="People's Republic of China">People's Republic of China</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The local language is the <a href="/wiki/Changzhou_dialect" title="Changzhou dialect">Changzhou dialect</a> of <a href="/wiki/Wu_Chinese" title="Wu Chinese">Wu Chinese</a>. It covers an area of 425 square kilometers in the northern part of <a href="/wiki/Changzhou" title="Changzhou">Changzhou</a> <a href="/wiki/Prefecture" title="Prefecture">prefecture</a>. In 2005 the total population was recorded at 480,000 people. The postal code for the district is 213161.</p>
<p>The district was established in August 1992 by the regional government as a high-tech manufacturing area. Businesses in the region also receive some preferential policies granted by the local government.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p><span class="citation-needed-content" style="padding-left:0.1em; padding-right:0.1em; color:#595959; border:1px solid #DDD;">It is also home to 7,636 domestic and overseas companies, 18 of which are on Fortune Global 500 list</span><sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="margin-left:0.1em; white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (June 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup>. As a result, a number of foreign styled restaurants and bars have been able to establish themselves, catering mostly to the <a href="/wiki/Expatriate" title="Expatriate">expatriate</a> community.</p>
<p>Xinbei is also home to the Changzhou campus of <a href="/wiki/Hohai_University" title="Hohai University">Hohai University</a>, <a href="/wiki/Changzhou_Trina_International_School" title="Changzhou Trina International School">Changzhou Trina International School</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Changzhou_International_School" title="Changzhou International School">Changzhou International School</a>.</p>
