<p><b>Shin Bong-sun</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">신봉선</span>, <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">hanja</a>: <span lang="ko" xml:lang="ko"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E7%94%B3" title="wiktionary:申">申</a><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%A5%89" title="wiktionary:奉">奉</a><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E4%BB%99" title="wiktionary:仙">仙</a></span> ; born October 6, 1980), is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korean" title="South Korean">South Korean</a> comedian. She took a six-year hiatus from comedy from 2009 to 2015, which ended when she joined <i><a href="/wiki/Comedy_Big_League" title="Comedy Big League">Comedy Big League</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
