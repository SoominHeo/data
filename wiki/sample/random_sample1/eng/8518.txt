<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Holy League (1684)</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><div class="hatnote" role="note">For other alliances with similar names, see <a class="mw-redirect mw-disambig" href="/wiki/Holy_League_(disambiguation)" title="Holy League (disambiguation)">Holy League</a>.</div>
<p>The <b>Holy League</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latin_Language" title="Latin Language">Latin</a>: <i>Sacra Ligua</i>) of 1684 was an alliance organized by <a href="/wiki/Pope_Innocent_XI" title="Pope Innocent XI">Pope Innocent XI</a> to oppose the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Turkish_War" title="Great Turkish War">Great Turkish War</a>. The League's initial members were the <a href="/wiki/Papal_States" title="Papal States">Papal States</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Holy_Roman_Empire" title="Holy Roman Empire">Holy Roman Empire</a> under Habsburg Emperor <a href="/wiki/Leopold_I,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Leopold I, Holy Roman Emperor">Leopold I</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Polish%E2%80%93Lithuanian_Commonwealth" title="Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth">Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth</a> of <a href="/wiki/John_III_Sobieski" title="John III Sobieski">John III Sobieski</a>, and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Venetian_Republic" title="Venetian Republic">Venetian Republic</a>; the <a href="/wiki/Tsardom_of_Russia" title="Tsardom of Russia">Tsardom of Russia</a> joined the League in 1686. The alliance lasted until the <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Karlowitz" title="Treaty of Karlowitz">Treaty of Karlowitz</a> brought an end to the war in 1699.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-History_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-History-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Roman_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Roman-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Pope Innocent was aided by Capuchin Friar <a href="/wiki/Marco_d%27Aviano" title="Marco d'Aviano">Marco d'Aviano</a> during the formation of the League.</p>
