<p><b>Shin A-young</b> (born February 18, 1987) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> television personality and announcer. She was a cast member in the reality show <i><a href="/wiki/The_Genius:_Black_Garnet" title="The Genius: Black Garnet">The Genius: Black Garnet</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> She is also an alumna of <a href="/wiki/Harvard_University" title="Harvard University">Harvard University</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
