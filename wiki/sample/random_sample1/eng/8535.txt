<p><i><b>The Divine Move</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">신의 한 수</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Sin-ui Hansu</span></i>) is a 2014 South Korean <a href="/wiki/Action_film" title="Action film">action</a>/<a href="/wiki/Neo-noir" title="Neo-noir">noir</a> film about a former baduk player's quest for revenge.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>The title refers to a particularly brilliant move, considered to be a "once in a lifetime" experience for only the best professionals, in the <a href="/wiki/Board_game" title="Board game">board game</a> baduk (called <a href="/wiki/Go_(game)" title="Go (game)">Go</a> in the West) which often turns what was a losing or close game into a winning effort at the most crucial moment.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Divine_Move&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Professional <a href="/wiki/Go_(game)" title="Go (game)">baduk</a> player Tae-seok loses a high-stakes game to infamous underground gambler Sal-soo, and ends up <a href="/wiki/Frameup" title="Frameup">framed</a> for the murder of his own brother and locked up in prison. He vows revenge and trains ferociously. After serving his seven-year sentence, he gets in touch with his brother's former associate "Tricks," hermit and blind master player "The Lord," and skillful junkyard owner Mok-su, "the Carpenter"; together, they begin formulating a plan to get back at Sal-soo and his men. Tae-seok slowly penetrates Sal-soo's inner circle and his gambling joint, and eliminates Sal-soo's men one by one. But Sal-soo discovers Tae-seok's true identity and engages him in one final game that will seal the fates of the two men involved.</p>
