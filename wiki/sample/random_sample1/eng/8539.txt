<p><b>Sin Il-soo</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">신일수</span>; born 4 September 1994) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Midfielder_(association_football)" title="Midfielder (association football)">midfielder</a> for <a href="/wiki/Seoul_E-Land_FC" title="Seoul E-Land FC">Seoul E-Land</a> in <a href="/wiki/K_League_Challenge" title="K League Challenge">K League Challenge</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
