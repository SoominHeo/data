<p>The <b>CD Shop Awards</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">CDショップ大賞</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Shīdī Shoppu Taishō</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is an annual set of music awards presented in Japan. It is sponsored by All-Japan CD Shop Clerks Union and awarded based on votes by CD shop clerks from all over Japan. It is referred to as a "music edition of <a href="/wiki/Japan_Booksellers%27_Award" title="Japan Booksellers' Award">Japan Booksellers' Award</a>".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ne10.3_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-ne10.3-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
