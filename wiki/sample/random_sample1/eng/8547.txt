<p><b>Xinzhuang District</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh">新莊區</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">Hanyu Pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Xīnzhuāng Qū</span></i>; <a href="/wiki/Tongyong_Pinyin" title="Tongyong Pinyin">Tongyong Pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn">Sinjhuang Cyu</span></i>) is an inner city <a href="/wiki/District_(Taiwan)" title="District (Taiwan)">district</a> in the western part of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/New_Taipei" title="New Taipei">New Taipei</a> in northern <a href="/wiki/Taiwan" title="Taiwan">Taiwan</a>. It has an area of 19.74 km<sup>2</sup> (7.62 sq mi) and a population of 413,443 people (2016).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Xinzhuang_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A former name of the area is <i>Pulauan</i> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh">武溜灣</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pe%CC%8Dh-%C5%8De-j%C4%AB" title="Pe̍h-ōe-jī">Pe̍h-ōe-jī</a>: <i><span lang="nan">Bú-liu-oan</span></i>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> On 15 January 1980, Xinzhuang was upgraded from an <a href="/wiki/Township_(Taiwan)" title="Township (Taiwan)">urban township</a> to be a <a href="/wiki/County-controlled_city" title="County-controlled city">county-controlled city</a> of <a href="/wiki/Taipei_County" title="Taipei County">Taipei County</a>. On 25 December 2010, Taipei County was upgraded to <a href="/wiki/New_Taipei_City" title="New Taipei City">New Taipei City</a>, and Xinzhuang City was upgraded to a <a href="/wiki/District_(Taiwan)" title="District (Taiwan)">district</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Overview">Overview</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Xinzhuang_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Overview">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The district is bordered by <a href="/wiki/Wugu_District" title="Wugu District">Wugu</a> and <a href="/wiki/Taishan_District,_New_Taipei" title="Taishan District, New Taipei">Taishan</a> to the north, <a href="/wiki/Sanchong_District" title="Sanchong District">Sanchong</a> to the east, <a href="/wiki/Banqiao_District" title="Banqiao District">Banqiao</a> and <a href="/wiki/Shulin_District" title="Shulin District">Shulin</a> to the south, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Taoyuan_City" title="Taoyuan City">Taoyuan City</a> to the west.</p>
