<p>The <b>Xinzhuang Baseball Stadium</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh">新莊棒球場</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Xīnzhuāng Bàngqiúchǎng</span></i>) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Baseball_stadium" title="Baseball stadium">baseball stadium</a> in <a href="/wiki/Xinzhuang_District" title="Xinzhuang District">Xinzhuang District</a>, <a href="/wiki/New_Taipei_City" title="New Taipei City">New Taipei City</a>, <a href="/wiki/Taiwan" title="Taiwan">Taiwan</a>. It is currently mostly used for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/CPBL" title="CPBL">CPBL</a> <a href="/wiki/Baseball" title="Baseball">baseball</a> games. The stadium can hold 12,500 people and was opened in 1997.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Ground_rules">Ground rules</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Xinzhuang_Baseball_Stadium&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Ground rules">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Part of the dugouts are covered by retractable roofs. The roofs must be retracted during games, and any fly ball that hits the retracted roof and bounces back is not considered a foul ball until it lands in foul terrority.</p>
