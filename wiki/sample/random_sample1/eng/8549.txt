<p><b>Xinji</b>(辛集，Xīnjí) is a <a href="/wiki/County-level_city" title="County-level city">county-level city</a> with <a href="/wiki/Sub-prefecture-level_city" title="Sub-prefecture-level city">sub-prefecture-level city</a> status, located under the administration of the <a href="/wiki/Prefecture-level_city" title="Prefecture-level city">prefecture-level city</a> of <a href="/wiki/Shijiazhuang" title="Shijiazhuang">Shijiazhuang</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hebei" title="Hebei">Hebei Province</a> in <a href="/wiki/North_China" title="North China">northern</a> <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. There are 8 towns (镇，zhèn) and 7 townships (乡，xiāng) under the administration of Xinji.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Xinji&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>Population: The population is 610,000 in the year 2003.</li>
<li>Area: 951 km2
<ul>
<li>Latitude: about 38° N</li>
<li>Longitude: about 115° E</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Area code: +86+311</li>
<li>Post Code: 052360</li>
<li>Driving Plate: 冀AF</li>
</ul>
<p>The city is located in North China Plain. The downtown area is about 7.7 km long, with a width 4.3 km.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Administrative_divisions">Administrative divisions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Xinji&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Administrative divisions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Xinji City is sub-divided into 8 towns and 7 townships within its jurisdiction.</p>
<p>Towns:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Xinji,_Xinji" title="Xinji, Xinji">Xinji</a> (辛集镇), <a href="/wiki/Zhangguzhuang" title="Zhangguzhuang">Zhangguzhuang</a> (张古庄镇), <a href="/wiki/Weibo_(town)" title="Weibo (town)">Weibo</a> (位伯镇), <a href="/wiki/Jiucheng,_Xinji" title="Jiucheng, Xinji">Jiucheng</a> (旧城镇), <a href="/wiki/Xinleitou" title="Xinleitou">Xinleitou</a> (新垒头镇), <a href="/wiki/Xincheng,_Xinji" title="Xincheng, Xinji">Xincheng</a> (新城镇), <a href="/wiki/Nanzhiqiong" title="Nanzhiqiong">Nanzhiqiong</a> (南智邱镇), <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wangkou,_Xinji" title="Wangkou, Xinji">Wangkou</a> (王口镇)</li>
</ul>
<p>Townships:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Xiaoxinzhuang_Township" title="Xiaoxinzhuang Township">Xiaoxinzhuang Township</a> (小辛庄乡), <a href="/wiki/Zhonglixiang_Township" title="Zhonglixiang Township">Zhonglixiang Township</a> (中里厢乡), <a href="/wiki/Tiangongying_Township" title="Tiangongying Township">Tiangongying Township</a> (天宫营乡), <a href="/wiki/Qianying_Township,_Hebei" title="Qianying Township, Hebei">Qianying Township</a> (前营乡), <a href="/wiki/Hemujing_Township" title="Hemujing Township">Hemujing Township</a> (和睦井乡), <a href="/wiki/Tianjiazhuang_Township" title="Tianjiazhuang Township">Tianjiazhuang Township</a> (田家庄乡), <a href="/wiki/Mazhuang_Township,_Xinji" title="Mazhuang Township, Xinji">Mazhuang Township</a> (马庄乡)</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Xinji&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Xinji was formerly named Shulu County (束鹿县，Shùlù Xian), and became a county-level city in 1986.</p>
