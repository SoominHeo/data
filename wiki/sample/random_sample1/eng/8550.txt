<p><b>Xinjin County</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans">新津县</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant">新津縣</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Xīnjīn Xiàn</span></i>; literally: "new ford") is under the administration of <a href="/wiki/Chengdu" title="Chengdu">Chengdu</a>, the capital of <a href="/wiki/Sichuan" title="Sichuan">Sichuan</a> province, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. It borders the <a href="/wiki/Prefecture-level_city" title="Prefecture-level city">prefecture-level city</a> of <a href="/wiki/Meishan" title="Meishan">Meishan</a> to the south.</p>
