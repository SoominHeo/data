<p><b>Shin Chang-moo</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">Hanja</a>:<span lang="ko" xml:lang="ko">申 昶武</span>, <a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">신창무</span>; born 17 September 1992) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Midfielder_(association_football)" title="Midfielder (association football)">midfielder</a> for <a href="/wiki/Daegu_FC" title="Daegu FC">Daegu FC</a> in <a href="/wiki/K_League_Challenge" title="K League Challenge">K League Challenge</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
