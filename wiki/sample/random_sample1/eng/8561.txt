<p><b>Shin-Takashima Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">新高島駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Shin-Takashima-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is an underground railway station on the <a href="/wiki/Minatomirai_Line" title="Minatomirai Line">Minatomirai Line</a> in <a href="/wiki/Nishi-ku,_Yokohama" title="Nishi-ku, Yokohama">Nishi-ku, Yokohama</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kanagawa_Prefecture" title="Kanagawa Prefecture">Kanagawa Prefecture</a>, Japan, operated by the <a href="/wiki/Public%E2%80%93private_partnership#Japan" title="Public–private partnership">third-sector</a> railway operating company <a href="/wiki/Yokohama_Minatomirai_Railway" title="Yokohama Minatomirai Railway">Yokohama Minatomirai Railway</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lines">Lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shin-Takashima_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Shin-Takashima Station is served by the 4.1 km underground <a href="/wiki/Minatomirai_Line" title="Minatomirai Line">Minatomirai Line</a> from <a href="/wiki/Yokohama_Station" title="Yokohama Station">Yokohama</a> to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Motomachi-Chukagai_Station" title="Motomachi-Chukagai Station">Motomachi-Chukagai</a>, and is located 0.8 km from the starting point of the line at <a href="/wiki/Yokohama_Station" title="Yokohama Station">Yokohama Station</a>. Trains through-run to and from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tokyu_Toyoko_Line" title="Tokyu Toyoko Line">Tokyu Toyoko Line</a> from <a href="/wiki/Shibuya_Station" title="Shibuya Station">Shibuya Station</a> and beyond on the <a href="/wiki/Tokyo_Metro_Fukutoshin_Line" title="Tokyo Metro Fukutoshin Line">Tokyo Metro Fukutoshin Line</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tobu_Tojo_Line" title="Tobu Tojo Line">Tobu Tojo Line</a> and <a href="/wiki/Seibu_Ikebukuro_Line" title="Seibu Ikebukuro Line">Seibu Ikebukuro Line</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_layout">Station layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shin-Takashima_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Station layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Shin-Takashima Station is an underground station with two <a href="/wiki/Side_platform" title="Side platform">side platforms</a> serving two tracks. It is the deepest underground of any station on the Minatomirai Line.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (August 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shin-Takashima_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
