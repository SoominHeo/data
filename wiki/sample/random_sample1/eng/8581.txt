<p><b>Sylwia Grzeszczak</b> (born 7 April 1989) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Polish_people" title="Polish people">Polish</a> <a href="/wiki/Singer-songwriter" title="Singer-songwriter">singer-songwriter</a> and pianist currently signed to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/EMI_Music" title="EMI Music">EMI Music Poland</a>.</p>
