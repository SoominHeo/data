<p><i><b>SimGolf</b></i> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Computer_game" title="Computer game">computer game</a> created by <a href="/wiki/Maxis" title="Maxis">Maxis</a> in 1996. The game allows players to design their own <a href="/wiki/Golf_course" title="Golf course">golf courses</a> and play them, much akin to how <i><a href="/wiki/SimCity_2000" title="SimCity 2000">SimCity 2000</a></i> can be used to create cities to play in <i><a href="/wiki/Streets_of_SimCity" title="Streets of SimCity">Streets of SimCity</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/SimCopter" title="SimCopter">SimCopter</a></i>.</p>
