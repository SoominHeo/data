<p><b>Somyung "Silver" Sim</b> (born September 27, 1984), known by the pseudonym <b>s.s.m~[SiLvEr]</b> is one of the most successful player of the <a href="/wiki/Real-time_strategy" title="Real-time strategy">real-time strategy</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Computer_game" title="Computer game">computer game</a> <i><a href="/wiki/StarCraft" title="StarCraft">StarCraft</a></i>. Also known to be a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Poker_player" title="Poker player">poker player</a>, he has made the final table of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/APPT" title="APPT">APPT</a> Macau 2008 (Asia Pacific Poker Tour). <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
