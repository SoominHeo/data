<p><b>Cameron John "CJ" Adams</b> (born April 6, 2000)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-bstaug_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-bstaug-1">[1]</a></sup> is an American teen actor. He is best known for his role as Timothy Green in the 2012 film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Odd_Life_of_Timothy_Green" title="The Odd Life of Timothy Green">The Odd Life of Timothy Green</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=CJ_Adams&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Adams was born and raised in <a href="/wiki/Rhode_Island" title="Rhode Island">Rhode Island</a>, the son of Donna and Matt Adams, a <a href="/wiki/The_New_York_Times" title="The New York Times"><i>New York Times</i></a> best-selling author, golf expert, and host of a show on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/SiriusXM" title="SiriusXM">SiriusXM</a> satellite radio.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-bstaug_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-bstaug-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=CJ_Adams&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Adams played the title character in the 2012 feature film <i><a href="/wiki/The_Odd_Life_of_Timothy_Green" title="The Odd Life of Timothy Green">The Odd Life of Timothy Green</a></i>, which earned him the <a href="/wiki/Young_Artist_Award" title="Young Artist Award">Young Artist Award</a> for <a href="/wiki/34th_Young_Artist_Awards#Best_Performance_in_a_Feature_Film_-_Young_Actor_Ten_and_Under" title="34th Young Artist Awards">Best Leading Young Actor Age Ten and Under</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Young_Artist_Awards_3_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Young_Artist_Awards_3-4">[4]</a></sup> He previously had a role in the 2007 feature film <i><a href="/wiki/Dan_in_Real_Life" title="Dan in Real Life">Dan in Real Life</a></i>.</p>
