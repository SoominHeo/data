<p><b>Xingren County</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">兴仁县</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">興仁縣</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Xīngrén Xiàn</span></i>) is a county of southwestern <a href="/wiki/Guizhou" title="Guizhou">Guizhou</a> province, China. It is under the administration of the <a href="/wiki/Qianxinan_Buyei_and_Miao_Autonomous_Prefecture" title="Qianxinan Buyei and Miao Autonomous Prefecture">Qianxinan Buyei and Miao Autonomous Prefecture</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography_and_climate">Geography and climate</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Xingren_County&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography and climate">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Due to its low latitude and elevation above 1,300 m (4,265.09 ft), Xingren County has a <a href="/wiki/Monsoon" title="Monsoon">monsoon</a>-influenced <a href="/wiki/Humid_subtropical_climate" title="Humid subtropical climate">humid subtropical climate</a> (<a href="/wiki/K%C3%B6ppen_climate_classification" title="Köppen climate classification">Köppen</a> <i>Cwa</i>), bordering on a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Subtropical_highland_climate" title="Subtropical highland climate">subtropical highland climate</a> (Köppen <i>Cwb</i>) with very warm, rainy summers and mild, damp winters. The monthly 24-hour average temperature ranges from 6.3 °C (43.3 °F) in January to 22.1 °C (71.8 °F) in July, while the annual mean is 15.25 °C (59.4 °F). Rainfall is very common year-round, occurring on 190 days of the year, but over 75% of the annual total (1,340 mm or 53 in) occurs from May to September. With monthly percent possible sunshine ranging from 25% in January to 42% in August, the county receives 1,532 hours of bright sunshine annually, with spring and summer sunnier than autumn and winter.</p>
