<p><b>Sandu <a href="/wiki/Sui_people" title="Sui people">Shui</a> Autonomous County</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">三都水族自治县</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Sāndū Shuǐzú Zìzhìxiàn</span></i>) is an <a href="/wiki/Autonomous_counties_of_the_People%27s_Republic_of_China" title="Autonomous counties of the People's Republic of China">autonomous county</a> of <a href="/wiki/Guizhou" title="Guizhou">Guizhou</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. It is under the administration of the <a href="/wiki/Qiannan_Buyei_and_Miao_Autonomous_Prefecture" title="Qiannan Buyei and Miao Autonomous Prefecture">Qiannan Buyei and Miao Autonomous Prefecture</a>.</p>
