<p><i><b>Before I Wake</b></i> (originally titled <i>Somnia</i>) is a 2016 American <a href="/wiki/Supernatural_fiction" title="Supernatural fiction">supernatural</a> <a href="/wiki/Fantasy_film" title="Fantasy film">fantasy</a> <a href="/wiki/Horror_film" title="Horror film">horror film</a> directed and edited by <a href="/wiki/Mike_Flanagan_(director)" title="Mike Flanagan (director)">Mike Flanagan</a> and co-written by Flanagan and Jeff Howard.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Mike_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Mike-3">[3]</a></sup> The film stars <a href="/wiki/Kate_Bosworth" title="Kate Bosworth">Kate Bosworth</a>, <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Jane" title="Thomas Jane">Thomas Jane</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Tremblay" title="Jacob Tremblay">Jacob Tremblay</a>, <a href="/wiki/Annabeth_Gish" title="Annabeth Gish">Annabeth Gish</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dash_Mihok" title="Dash Mihok">Dash Mihok</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Before_I_Wake_(2016_film)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The movie begins with a frightened man with a gun and a sleeping child. Trembling and perspiring, the man nervously enters the child's room . He looks around, as if searching for something that he expects to reveal itself in the shadows, then a sudden commotion makes him pull the trigger. The gunfire awakens the child and the man breaks down in tears, saying that he can't do it.</p>
<p>Later, Jessie (Kate Bosworth) and Mark (Thomas Jane) Hobson adopt a new foster child, eight-year-old Cody Morgan (Jacob Tremblay), after their young son Shawn dies suddenly. They soon find out that Cody's dreams become reality, but his nightmares are quite deadly.</p>
<p>On his first night, the couple are amazed as multicolored butterflies flutter across the living room, emitting a strong glow. Mark tries to capture one to show to Cody, as he likes butterflies. However, Cody wakes up and the butterflies disappear. The following day, he goes to school and finds a friend in a girl, and hostility from a mean kid. At home, Cody asks Mark who the kid in the picture was and Mark replies that he was their son, and that his name was Shawn. Cody asks about his whereabouts, to which Jessie replies that he is in heaven, and Cody tells her that that's where his mom is too. That night, the couple see their deceased child and try to hug him. Cody wakes up and Shawn disappears.</p>
<p>Upon discovering the amazing gift that Cody has, Jessie takes advantage of it. She lets Cody watch home videos of Shawn and then that night, the couple once again sees their beloved child. Days later, Mark notices the addiction that his wife has found and confronts her, telling her that she is just using Cody for his gift instead of loving him, which results in them arguing and Mark taking down the pictures of Shawn.</p>
<p>Cody falls asleep at school, and a nightmarish creature, who Cody calls "The Canker Man", appears before the mean kid, as the girl he's befriended watches in horror. Meanwhile Jessie goes to a doctor and expresses her complaints about her foster child not going to bed, to which the doctor prescribes medication. She mixes it with his drink, unbeknownst to her husband. That night Shawn once again appears before them, but turns into a nightmarish creature. Mark tries to wake Cody up but can't. Jessie confesses that she drugged the boy. The creature devours Mark as Jessie is thrown across the room and falls unconscious. She wakes up with Cody on the phone calling for help, as he found her lying on the floor. Suspecting it to be domestic violence, and knowing of the boy being drugged to sleep, social services takes Cody away.</p>
<p>Jessie steals Cody's social worker's files and talks to Cody's former foster parent, who took care of him for at least fifteen months. He reveals that, with butterflies appearing seemingly out of nowhere, Cody's dreams started to become reality. More and more of Cody's dreams came true, including that of the couple's deceased child. When the man's wife got sick, Cody became upset and that night, "The Canker Man" appeared and took her. Shocked and heartbroken, the former parent showed Cody all the couple's pictures in hopes him of recreating her in his dreams. However, what resulted was a poor copy of the wife, since Cody was too young to remember her face fully. The parent urges Jessie to kill Cody; not willing to give in, she digs more into Cody's background and finds more about his mother through several records. Finally, she goes to the orphanage where Cody was staying. In the orphanage, the staff notices that Cody has not been sleeping for the past 2 days. That night, the boy is given a tranquilizer to make him sleep, to which he helplessly protests. Jessie arrives to find the place dark and isolated. She witnesses nightmarish versions of people in every room, including that of Shawn, Cody and his foster family. She finally finds where Cody is, but before she can go near him, she is thrown across the hallway by the tall, slender nightmare creature. She pulls out a pillow shaped like a blue butterfly, hugs the creature, and as she holds it, it takes the form of Cody and disappears. Jessie places the butterfly shaped pillow on the sleeping child's chest and carries him out of the room. Along the way, she whispers to Cody and asks him to release all the people the creature has imprisoned. The nightmare then turns into a beautiful dream, and all the people taken hostage by "The Canker Man" are released. When the supervisor confronts her, she lets her take the still-unconscious boy home.</p>
<p>The following day, Jessie gives Cody the journal his birth mother Andrea had kept, which shows how much she had loved him and his gift of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lucid_dreaming" title="Lucid dreaming">lucid dreaming</a>. However, she had died of <a href="/wiki/Pancreatic_cancer" title="Pancreatic cancer">pancreatic cancer</a> when Cody was just six years old, which resulted in his anxiety, which occurs when people get sick. At bedtime, Cody asks Jessie to tell him a story. She tells him about a gifted boy who needed a mother, and a lonely mother who needed a child. Her story also tells about a mean kid who woke up, a couple which reunited, and the father who met his son. When Cody asks her if these will come true, she tells him that it is up to him to decide, since there is more to his dreams than meets the eye.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Production">Production</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Before_I_Wake_(2016_film)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Production">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On September 7, 2013, it was announced that <i><a href="/wiki/Oculus_(film)" title="Oculus (film)">Oculus</a></i> director Mike Flanagan was set to direct a horror film called <i>Somnia</i> he co-wrote with Jeff Howard for Intrepid Pictures, and that the producers would be Trevor Macy and William D. Johnson, with Demarest Films' Sam Englebardt co-producing and co-financing the film with MICA Entertainment headed by Dale Armin Johnson.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Mike_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-Mike-3">[3]</a></sup> Focus Features International initially handled international sales of the film.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Mike_3-2"><a href="#cite_note-Mike-3">[3]</a></sup> On November 7, 2013, it was announced that Sierra/Affinity would now handle all international rights which were previously held by FFI.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Kate-Thomas_4-2"><a href="#cite_note-Kate-Thomas-4">[4]</a></sup> On April 4, 2014, <a href="/wiki/Relativity_Media" title="Relativity Media">Relativity Media</a> acquired the US distribution rights to the film.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Fleming_Jr_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-Fleming_Jr-6">[6]</a></sup> In March 2015, the title was changed to ''<i>Before I Wake"</i>, apparently over Flanagan's objections.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Casting">Casting</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Before_I_Wake_(2016_film)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Casting">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On November 7, 2013, <a href="/wiki/Kate_Bosworth" title="Kate Bosworth">Kate Bosworth</a> and <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Jane" title="Thomas Jane">Thomas Jane</a> joined the lead cast of the film as the child's parents, and <a href="/wiki/Jacob_Tremblay" title="Jacob Tremblay">Jacob Tremblay</a> was set to play as Cody.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Kate-Thomas_4-3"><a href="#cite_note-Kate-Thomas-4">[4]</a></sup> On November 18, 2013, <a href="/wiki/Annabeth_Gish" title="Annabeth Gish">Annabeth Gish</a> joined the cast of the film to play Natalie, the case worker assigned to young Cody.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Annabeth_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-Annabeth-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Filming">Filming</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Before_I_Wake_(2016_film)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Filming">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Filming commenced on November 11, 2013, in <a href="/wiki/Fairhope,_Alabama" title="Fairhope, Alabama">Fairhope, Alabama</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-al_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-al-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gcnt_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-gcnt-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> On December 12, 2013, the crew filmed scenes at <a href="/wiki/Barton_Academy" title="Barton Academy">Barton Academy</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> Filming completed on December 16, 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-al_8-1"><a href="#cite_note-al-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gcnt_9-1"><a href="#cite_note-gcnt-9">[9]</a></sup> The music was composed by Danny Elfman and The Newton Brothers.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Release">Release</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Before_I_Wake_(2016_film)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Release">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On April 4, 2014, <a href="/wiki/Relativity_Media" title="Relativity Media">Relativity Media</a> acquired the US distribution rights to the film.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Fleming_Jr_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-Fleming_Jr-6">[6]</a></sup> The film was originally scheduled to be released on May 8, 2015, but was pushed back to September 25, 2015, and later pulled from the schedule due to the company's filing for bankruptcy.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup> The film was re-scheduled to April 8, 2016,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup> and then re-scheduled to September 9, 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RELEASE_15-0"><a href="#cite_note-RELEASE-15">[15]</a></sup> It was then pulled from the schedule.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup></p>
<p>In June 2016, it was announced that the film would screen at the Fantasia International Film Festival in July.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup> The film had its North American premiere there on July 31, 2016. <a class="external autonumber" href="http://www.fantasiafestival.com/2016/en/films-schedule/248/before-i-wake" rel="nofollow">[1]</a>. In U.S the film set to be released in 2017.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Reception">Reception</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Before_I_Wake_(2016_film)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Reception">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On <a href="/wiki/Rotten_Tomatoes" title="Rotten Tomatoes">Rotten Tomatoes</a>, the film has a rating of 36% based on 11 reviews; the average rating is 5.7/10.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RT_19-0"><a href="#cite_note-RT-19">[19]</a></sup></p>
