<p><b>Tsushima Airport</b> (<a href="/wiki/International_Air_Transport_Association_airport_code" title="International Air Transport Association airport code">IATA</a>: <b>TSJ</b>, <a href="/wiki/International_Civil_Aviation_Organization_airport_code" title="International Civil Aviation Organization airport code">ICAO</a>: <b>RJDT</b>) is an airport located 10 km (6.2 mi) northeast<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-AIP_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-AIP-1">[1]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Tsushima,_Nagasaki" title="Tsushima, Nagasaki">Tsushima</a>, a city in the <a href="/wiki/Nagasaki_Prefecture" title="Nagasaki Prefecture">Nagasaki Prefecture</a> of <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</p>
