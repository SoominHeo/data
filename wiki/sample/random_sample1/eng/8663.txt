It is administered as part of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Prefecture_(China)" title="Prefecture (China)">prefecture</a>-level <a href="/wiki/Prefecture-level_city" title="Prefecture-level city">city</a> of <a href="/wiki/Zhaoqing" title="Zhaoqing">Zhaoqing</a>. <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sihui&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Under the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Qing_Empire" title="Qing Empire">Qing</a>, <b>Sihui<span class="nowrap"> </span>County</b> made up part of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fu_(administrative_subdivision)" title="Fu (administrative subdivision)">commandery</a> of <a href="/wiki/Zhaoqing" title="Zhaoqing">Zhaoqing</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FOOTNOTEBolton_.26_al.1941.5Bhttps:.2F.2Fbooks.google.com.hk.2Fbooks.3Fid.3D6irEoGgDrm4C.26pg.3DPA262_262.5D_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-FOOTNOTEBolton_.26_al.1941.5Bhttps:.2F.2Fbooks.google.com.hk.2Fbooks.3Fid.3D6irEoGgDrm4C.26pg.3DPA262_262.5D-1">[1]</a></sup> As with much of the surrounding countryside, Sihui was a source for many members of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chinese_diaspora" title="Chinese diaspora">Chinese diaspora</a> during the late <a href="/wiki/Qing_dynasty" title="Qing dynasty">Qing</a>.</p>
