<p><b>Agustín Ignacio Orion</b> (<small>Spanish pronunciation: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Spanish" title="Help:IPA for Spanish">[aɣusˈtin oˈɾjon]</a></span>; born June 26, 1981) is an <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentine</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> goalkeeper who currently plays for <a href="/wiki/Racing_Club_de_Avellaneda" title="Racing Club de Avellaneda">Racing Club</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Agust%C3%ADn_Orion&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Orion was <a href="/wiki/San_Lorenzo_de_Almagro" title="San Lorenzo de Almagro">San Lorenzo</a>'s first choice goalkeeper in 2007, when he helped the club to win the <a href="/wiki/2006%E2%80%9307_Argentine_Primera_Divisi%C3%B3n#Torneo_Clausura" title="2006–07 Argentine Primera División">Clausura</a> tournament.</p>
<p>On 9 December 2009, the goalkeeper left San Lorenzo for 500000 euros and joined <a href="/wiki/Estudiantes_de_La_Plata" title="Estudiantes de La Plata">Estudiantes de La Plata</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>On July 2011 Orion signed with <a href="/wiki/Boca_Juniors" title="Boca Juniors">Boca Juniors</a>, where he won the <a href="/wiki/2011%E2%80%9312_Argentine_Primera_Divisi%C3%B3n_season#Torneo_Apertura" title="2011–12 Argentine Primera División season">2011 Apertura</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> In 2013, Agustín received offers from important European clubs,<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (January 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> but he decided to stay in <a href="/wiki/Boca_Juniors" title="Boca Juniors">Boca Juniors</a>, because he is an important piece in the team and he loves the club.</p>
<p>On August 2016 Orion signed with <a href="/wiki/Racing_Club_de_Avellaneda" title="Racing Club de Avellaneda">Racing Club</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Agust%C3%ADn_Orion&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Orion was called up to the <a href="/wiki/Argentina_national_football_team" title="Argentina national football team">Argentina national football team</a> as third goalkeeper behind <a href="/wiki/Roberto_Abbondanzieri" title="Roberto Abbondanzieri">Roberto Abbondanzieri</a> and <a href="/wiki/Juan_Pablo_Carrizo" title="Juan Pablo Carrizo">Juan Pablo Carrizo</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2007_Copa_Am%C3%A9rica" title="2007 Copa América">2007 Copa América</a> held in Venezuela.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-fifa_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-fifa-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>He made his debut for the <i>Albiceleste</i> against <a href="/wiki/Brazil_national_football_team" title="Brazil national football team">Brazil</a> in the <i><a href="/wiki/Supercl%C3%A1sico_de_las_Am%C3%A9ricas" title="Superclásico de las Américas">Superclásico de las Américas</a></i> in September 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-fifa_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-fifa-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>In June 2014, Orion was named in Argentina's squad for the <a href="/wiki/2014_FIFA_World_Cup" title="2014 FIFA World Cup">2014 FIFA World Cup</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
