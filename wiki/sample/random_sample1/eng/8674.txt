<p><b>Agnieszka Grochowska</b> (born 31 December 1979 in <a href="/wiki/Warsaw" title="Warsaw">Warsaw</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/Poland" title="Poland">Polish</a> actress. She has performed in more than twenty films since 2002. She is also famous for her work in <i><a href="/wiki/Shameless_(film)" title="Shameless (film)">Shameless</a></i> where she was in an incestuous relationship with her younger brother.</p>
