<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ana_Rujas&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>She was born in Madrid on 14 May 1989. At 16 she enrolled in a modeling agency.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ana_Rujas&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>She is currently dating actor <a class="extiw" href="https://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ricardo_G%C3%B3mez_(actor)" title="es:Ricardo Gómez (actor)">es:Ricardo Gómez (actor)</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> after her break-up with <a class="extiw" href="https://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joel_Bosqued" title="es:Joel Bosqued">es:Joel Bosqued</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ana_Rujas&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Her first big role was in <a class="extiw" href="https://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/90-60-90,_diario_secreto_de_una_adolescente" title="es:90-60-90, diario secreto de una adolescente">es:90-60-90, diario secreto de una adolescente</a>. She has acted in <a class="extiw" href="https://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/HKM" title="es:HKM">es:HKM</a>. She played the role of <a href="/wiki/Shaila_D%C3%BArcal" title="Shaila Dúrcal">Shaila Dúrcal</a> in the biopic of <a href="/wiki/Roc%C3%ADo_D%C3%BArcal" title="Rocío Dúrcal">Rocío Dúrcal</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> In 2016, she starred in the music video of <a href="/wiki/Zahara_(Spanish_musician)" title="Zahara (Spanish musician)">Zahara</a>, "Caída Libre".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> She had endorsed the jewelry brand "Aristocrazy".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> She recently acted in a <a href="/wiki/Play_(theatre)" title="Play (theatre)">Play</a>, <i>¿Qué sabes tú de mis tristezas?</i> (What do you know about my sorrows?).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ballesteros_2016_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-Ballesteros_2016-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
