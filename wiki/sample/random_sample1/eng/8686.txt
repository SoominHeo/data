<p><b>Anenii Noi</b> (<small>Romanian pronunciation: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Romanian" title="Help:IPA for Romanian">[aˈnenij ˈnoj]</a></span>) is a city in east-central <a href="/wiki/Moldova" title="Moldova">Moldova</a>, the seat of <a href="/wiki/Anenii_Noi_District" title="Anenii Noi District">Anenii Noi District</a>. It is located 36 km (22 mi) SE of the capital <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chi%C5%9Fin%C4%83u" title="Chişinău">Chişinău</a>.</p>
<p>According to the 2004 census, the city administers an area inhabited by 11,463 people. This area consists of the city itself, population 8,358, and five suburb villages: <i>Albiniţa</i>, population 370, <i>Beriozchi</i>, population 647, <i>Hîrbovăţul Nou</i>, population 484, <i>Ruseni</i>, population 1,090, and <i>Socoleni</i>, population 514. Of these, 6,324 are <a href="/wiki/Moldovans" title="Moldovans">Moldovans</a> (<a href="/wiki/Romanians" title="Romanians">Romanians</a>), 2,721 <a href="/wiki/Ukrainians" title="Ukrainians">Ukrainians</a>, 1,923 <a href="/wiki/Russians" title="Russians">Russians</a>, 68 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gagauzians" title="Gagauzians">Gagauzians</a>, 178 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bessarabia_Bulgarians" title="Bessarabia Bulgarians">Bulgarians</a>, 7 Jews, 11 <a href="/wiki/Poles" title="Poles">Poles</a>, 46 <a href="/wiki/Romani_people" title="Romani people">Gypsies</a>, and 185 others/undeclared. At the 1930 census, there were two localities: <i>Anenii Noi</i>, population 661 (558 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bessarabian_Germans" title="Bessarabian Germans">Bessarabian Germans</a>, 30 Russians, 19 Romanians, and 4 Poles), and <i>Anenii Vechi</i>, population 990 (891 Russians, 74 Romanians, 19 Germans, 4 Jews, and 2 Bulgarians) in <i>Plasa Bulboaca</i> of <a href="/wiki/Tighina_County" title="Tighina County">Tighina County</a>.</p>
<p>There are two restaurants and a few factories in Anenii Noi. Transportation is available to Anenii Noi every half-hour from Chişinău.</p>
