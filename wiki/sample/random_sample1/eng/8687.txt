<p><b>Annelies Törös</b> (born March 6, 1995) is a Belgian-Hungarian actress, model and beauty pageant titleholder who won <a href="/wiki/Miss_Belgium" title="Miss Belgium">Miss Belgium</a> in 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> She represented <a href="/wiki/Belgium" title="Belgium">Belgium</a> at <a href="/wiki/Miss_Universe_2015" title="Miss Universe 2015">Miss Universe 2015</a>. Törös, was born in <a href="/wiki/Antwerp" title="Antwerp">Antwerp</a> where she spent most of her youth. She has a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dual_nationality" title="Dual nationality">dual nationality</a>. Her father is <a href="/wiki/Hungarians" title="Hungarians">Hungarian</a> and her mother <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Belgian" title="Belgian">Belgian</a>. She is pursuing communication studies. At an early age Törös took up <a href="/wiki/Classical_ballet" title="Classical ballet">classical ballet</a>, her favorite hobby. She was only four years old. At the age of fifteen, she started her modeling career after she was discovered in the streets of <a href="/wiki/Antwerp" title="Antwerp">Antwerp</a> City.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Annelies_T%C3%B6r%C3%B6s&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Model">Model</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Annelies_T%C3%B6r%C3%B6s&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Model">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As a model Törös worked all over <a href="/wiki/Belgium" title="Belgium">Belgium</a>, also in <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tunisia" title="Tunisia">Tunisia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>, <a href="/wiki/Portugal" title="Portugal">Portugal</a> and the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>. She appeared in music video clips, commercials for <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull" title="Red Bull">Red Bull</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ice_Tea" title="Ice Tea">Ice Tea</a> and was also the face of Pontiac watches.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Miss_Belgium_2015">Miss Belgium 2015</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Annelies_T%C3%B6r%C3%B6s&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Miss Belgium 2015">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Törös was crowned as <i><a href="/wiki/Miss_Belgium_2015" title="Miss Belgium 2015">Miss Belgium 2015</a></i> on January 6, 2015 in <a href="/wiki/Plopsaland_De_Panne" title="Plopsaland De Panne">Plopsaland De Panne</a>. Törös represented the province of <a href="/wiki/Antwerp_(province)" title="Antwerp (province)">Antwerp</a>. She was also crowned as Miss Antwerp 2015 before (in 2014). As <a href="/wiki/Miss_Belgium_2015" title="Miss Belgium 2015">Miss Belgium 2015</a> titleholder She became one of the most successfully Misses in <a href="/wiki/Belgium" title="Belgium">Belgium</a>.</p>
<p>On December 20, 2015 Törös represented Belgium at <a href="/wiki/Miss_Universe_2015" title="Miss Universe 2015">Miss Universe 2015</a> in <a href="/wiki/Las_Vegas" title="Las Vegas">Las Vegas</a> and placed in the top fifteen out of 80 contestants from all over the world. She was eventually placed eleventh. Runner-up <a href="/wiki/Leylah_Alli%C3%ABt" title="Leylah Alliët">Leylah Alliët</a> competed in the <a href="/wiki/Miss_World_2015" title="Miss World 2015">Miss World 2015</a> competition.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
