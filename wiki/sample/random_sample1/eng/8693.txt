<p><b>Ada</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Serbian_Cyrillic_language" title="Serbian Cyrillic language">Serbian Cyrillic</a>: <span lang="sr"><span class="Unicode">Ада</span></span>; <a href="/wiki/Hungarian_language" title="Hungarian language">Hungarian</a>: <span lang="hu"><i>Ada</i></span>) is a town and municipality located in the <a href="/wiki/North_Banat_District" title="North Banat District">North Banat District</a> of the autonomous province of <a href="/wiki/Vojvodina" title="Vojvodina">Vojvodina</a>, Serbia. It is situated near the river <a href="/wiki/Tisza" title="Tisza">Tisa</a> in the geographical region of <a href="/wiki/Ba%C4%8Dka" title="Bačka">Bačka</a>. The town has a population of 9,564, while Ada municipality has 16,991 inhabitants (2011 census).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Municipality">Municipality</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ada,_Serbia&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Municipality">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <b>Ada municipality</b> includes the towns of Ada (the seat) and nearby <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mol,_Serbia" title="Mol, Serbia">Mol</a> (<i>Mohol</i>), and the villages of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Utrine,_Serbia" title="Utrine, Serbia">Utrine</a> (<i>Törökfalu</i>), <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Obornja%C4%8Da_(Ada)" title="Obornjača (Ada)">Obornjača</a> (<i>Völgypart-Nagyvölgy</i>) and <a href="/wiki/Sterijino" title="Sterijino">Sterijino</a> (<i>Valkaisor</i>).</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ada,_Serbia&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>According to the 2011 census, the total population of the Ada municipality was 16,991, including the following ethnic groups over 1%:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hungarians_in_Serbia" title="Hungarians in Serbia">Hungarians</a>: 12,750 (75.04%)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Serbs" title="Serbs">Serbs</a>: 2,956 (17.40%)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Romani_people_in_Serbia" title="Romani people in Serbia">Romani</a>: 323 (1.90%)</li>
<li>Others and undeclared: 962 (5.66%)</li>
</ul>
<p>All local communities in the municipality have a Hungarian majority.</p>
