<p><b>Admiralteysky Municipal Okrug</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Адмиралте́йский муниципа́льный о́круг</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_Saint_Petersburg" title="Administrative divisions of Saint Petersburg">municipal okrug</a> of <a href="/wiki/Admiralteysky_District" title="Admiralteysky District">Admiralteysky District</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Federal_cities_of_Russia" title="Federal cities of Russia">federal city</a> of <a href="/wiki/Saint_Petersburg" title="Saint Petersburg">St. Petersburg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>. Population: <span class="nowrap">22,634 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2010)" title="Russian Census (2010)">2010 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2010Census_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-2010Census-1">[1]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">30,533 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2002)" title="Russian Census (2002)">2002 Census</a>)</span>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PopCensus_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-PopCensus-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>It borders the <a href="/wiki/Neva_River" title="Neva River">Neva River</a> in the north, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=New_Admiralty_Canal&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="New Admiralty Canal (page does not exist)">New Admiralty Canal</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Kryukov_Canal&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Kryukov Canal (page does not exist)">Kryukov Canal</a> in the west, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fontanka" title="Fontanka">Fontanka</a> in the south, and <a href="/wiki/Voznesensky_Avenue" title="Voznesensky Avenue">Voznesensky Avenue</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Moyka_River" title="Moyka River">Moyka River</a> in the east.</p>
<p>Places of interest include the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Admiralty_building" title="Admiralty building">Admiralty building</a>, <a href="/wiki/Saint_Isaac%27s_Cathedral" title="Saint Isaac's Cathedral">Saint Isaac's Cathedral</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mariinsky_Palace" title="Mariinsky Palace">Mariinsky Palace</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Decembrists_Square" title="Decembrists Square">Decembrists Square</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Mariinsky_Theatre" title="Mariinsky Theatre">Mariinsky Theatre</a>.</p>
