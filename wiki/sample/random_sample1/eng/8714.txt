<p><b>Alxa Left Banner</b> (<a href="/wiki/Mongolian_language" title="Mongolian language">Mongolian</a>: <span style="display:inline-block; font-weight:normal; font-family:'Menk Hawang Tig', 'Menk Qagan Tig', 'Menk Garqag Tig', 'Menk Har_a Tig', 'Menk Scnin Tig', 'Oyun Gurban Ulus Tig', 'Oyun Qagan Tig', 'Oyun Garqag Tig', 'Oyun Har_a Tig', 'Oyun Scnin Tig', 'Mongolian BT', 'Mongolian Baiti', 'Noto Sans Mongolian', 'Mongol Usug', 'Mongolian White', 'MongolianScript', 'Code2000', 'Menksoft Qagan'; font-size: 1.5em; line-height: 1em; writing-mode: vertical-lr; -webkit-writing-mode: vertical-lr; -o-writing-mode: vertical-lr; -ms-writing-mode: tb-lr; writing-mode: tb-lr; vertical-align:text-top;">ᠠᠯᠠᠱᠠ</span> <span style="display:inline-block; font-weight:normal; font-family:'Menk Hawang Tig', 'Menk Qagan Tig', 'Menk Garqag Tig', 'Menk Har_a Tig', 'Menk Scnin Tig', 'Oyun Gurban Ulus Tig', 'Oyun Qagan Tig', 'Oyun Garqag Tig', 'Oyun Har_a Tig', 'Oyun Scnin Tig', 'Mongolian BT', 'Mongolian Baiti', 'Noto Sans Mongolian', 'Mongol Usug', 'Mongolian White', 'MongolianScript', 'Code2000', 'Menksoft Qagan'; font-size: 1.5em; line-height: 1em; writing-mode: vertical-lr; -webkit-writing-mode: vertical-lr; -o-writing-mode: vertical-lr; -ms-writing-mode: tb-lr; writing-mode: tb-lr; vertical-align:text-top;">ᠵᠡᠭᠦᠨ</span> <span style="display:inline-block; font-weight:normal; font-family:'Menk Hawang Tig', 'Menk Qagan Tig', 'Menk Garqag Tig', 'Menk Har_a Tig', 'Menk Scnin Tig', 'Oyun Gurban Ulus Tig', 'Oyun Qagan Tig', 'Oyun Garqag Tig', 'Oyun Har_a Tig', 'Oyun Scnin Tig', 'Mongolian BT', 'Mongolian Baiti', 'Noto Sans Mongolian', 'Mongol Usug', 'Mongolian White', 'MongolianScript', 'Code2000', 'Menksoft Qagan'; font-size: 1.5em; line-height: 1em; writing-mode: vertical-lr; -webkit-writing-mode: vertical-lr; -o-writing-mode: vertical-lr; -ms-writing-mode: tb-lr; writing-mode: tb-lr; vertical-align:text-top;">ᠬᠣᠰᠢᠭᠤ</span> Алаша Жегүн қосигу <i>Alaša Jegün qosiɣu</i>; <a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">阿拉善左旗</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Ālāshàn Zuǒ Qí</span></i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Banners_of_Inner_Mongolia" title="Banners of Inner Mongolia">banner</a> (administrative division) of <a href="/wiki/Inner_Mongolia" title="Inner Mongolia">Inner Mongolia</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. The town of Bayanhot, situated in the banner, is the seat of government of the greater <a href="/wiki/Alxa_League" title="Alxa League">Alxa League</a>, of which Alxa Left Banner is a part.</p>
<p>Alxa Left Banner is on the route of provincial highway S218, which is accessible via <a href="/wiki/China_National_Highway_110" title="China National Highway 110">China National Highway 110</a> via the city of <a href="/wiki/Wuhai" title="Wuhai">Wuhai</a>. It is also accessible by air via the <a href="/wiki/Alxa_Left_Banner_Bayanhot_Airport" title="Alxa Left Banner Bayanhot Airport">Alxa Left Banner Bayanhot Airport</a>.</p>
<p>Ethnic Mongols make up 27% of the banner population.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The average elevation is between 800 to 1500 meters above sea level. A large part of the banner is desert.</p>
<p>The banner is subdivided into four subdistricts, 9 towns, and 6 <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sumu_(administrative_division)" title="Sumu (administrative division)">sumus</a></i>.</p>
