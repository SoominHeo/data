<p><b>Araimachi Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">新居町駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Araimachi-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/Train_station" title="Train station">railway station</a> on the <a href="/wiki/T%C5%8Dkaid%C5%8D_Main_Line" title="Tōkaidō Main Line">Tōkaidō Main Line</a> of <a href="/wiki/Central_Japan_Railway_Company" title="Central Japan Railway Company">Central Japan Railway Company</a> in <a href="/wiki/Kosai,_Shizuoka" title="Kosai, Shizuoka">Kosai City</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shizuoka_Prefecture" title="Shizuoka Prefecture">Shizuoka Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>. The station is 272.9 rail kilometers from <a href="/wiki/Tokyo" title="Tokyo">Tokyo</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Araimachi_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On September 1, 1888 the section of the Tōkaidō Main Line connecting <a href="/wiki/Hamamatsu_Station" title="Hamamatsu Station">Hamamatsu Station</a> with <a href="/wiki/%C5%8Cbu_Station" title="Ōbu Station">Ōbu Station</a> was completed. Araimachi Station was established on January 10, 1915 for both passenger service and freight. Freight service was discontinued on April 26, 1971.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Layout">Layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Araimachi_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Araimachi Station has a <a href="/wiki/Side_platform" title="Side platform">side platform</a> serving Track 1, and an <a href="/wiki/Island_platform" title="Island platform">island platform</a> serving Track 2 and Track 3. The platforms are connected by an <a href="/wiki/Overpass" title="Overpass">overpass</a>. The station building has automated <a href="/wiki/Ticket_machine" title="Ticket machine">ticket machines</a>, <a href="/wiki/TOICA" title="TOICA">TOICA</a> automated <a href="/wiki/Turnstile" title="Turnstile">turnstiles</a> and a manned "Green Window" service counter. However, the station is not manned during early morning and late night hours.</p>
