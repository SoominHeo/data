<p><b>Altay</b> or <b>Aletai</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh">阿勒泰</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Ālètài</span></i>; <a href="/wiki/Uyghur_language" title="Uyghur language">Uyghur</a>: ئالتاي شەھىرى / Алтай Шəһири / Altay Shehiri / <i>Altay Xəⱨiri</i>; <a href="/wiki/Kazakh_language" title="Kazakh language">Kazakh</a>: <span lang="kk">التاي قالاسى / Алтай қаласы / Altay qalası / <i>Altay ⱪalase</i></span>; <a href="/wiki/Dungan_language" title="Dungan language">Dungan</a>: <span lang="dng">Аләтэ, <i>Aləte</i>, اَلْتَىْ</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/County-level_city" title="County-level city">county-level city</a> in <a href="/wiki/Ili_Kazakh_Autonomous_Prefecture" title="Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture">Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture</a>, in far northern <a href="/wiki/Xinjiang" title="Xinjiang">Xinjiang</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. It is the administrative centre of <a href="/wiki/Altay_Prefecture" title="Altay Prefecture">Altay Prefecture</a>, and has 142,000 inhabitants.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Altay_City&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Climate">Climate</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Altay_City&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Climate">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Altay has a cold <a href="/wiki/Semi-arid_climate" title="Semi-arid climate">semi-arid climate</a> (<a href="/wiki/K%C3%B6ppen_climate_classification" title="Köppen climate classification">Köppen</a> <i>BSk</i>), without the strong <a href="/wiki/Monsoon" title="Monsoon">monsoonal</a> influence on seasonal precipitation patterns that defines the climate across much of China and an annual precipitation total of only 191 millimetres (7.5 in). Winters are long, bitterly cold and dry, with a January 24-hour average of −15.5 °C (4.1 °F); however, the presence of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Altay_Mountains" title="Altay Mountains">Altay Mountains</a> to the north helps moderate the severity of winter cold as compared to locations further to the east. Spring and autumn are short but mild. Summers are very warm, but dry, with a July 24-hour average of 21.8 °C (71.2 °F). The annual mean is 4.53 °C (40.2 °F). With monthly percent possible sunshine ranging from 50% in December to 74% in August and September, sunshine is abundant and the city receives 2,993 hours of bright sunshine annually.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transport">Transport</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Altay_City&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Transport">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/China_National_Highway_216" title="China National Highway 216">China National Highway 216</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/China_National_Highway_217" title="China National Highway 217">China National Highway 217</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Altay_Airport" title="Altay Airport">Altay Airport</a></li>
<li>Train until <a href="/wiki/Beitun,_Xinjiang" title="Beitun, Xinjiang">Beitun city</a> which is about one hour's by car from Altay City.</li>
</ul>
<p>From Ürümqi (capital of Xinjiang Province) to Altay, it takes about one hour by plane, 9 hours by day bus, 12 hours by night bus and about 14 hours by train (train is until Beitun city).</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Culture">Culture</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Altay_City&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Culture">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Like other cities in <a href="/wiki/Xinjiang" title="Xinjiang">Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region</a>, there are a lot of minorities in Altay, especially <a href="/wiki/Uyghurs" title="Uyghurs">Uyghurs</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kazakhs" title="Kazakhs">Kazakhs</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Education">Education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Altay_City&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In Schools, students are taught mainly in Chinese (mandarin) and there are courses for Uyghur and Kazakh languages.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Tourism">Tourism</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Altay_City&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Tourism">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The most important tourism site is <a href="/wiki/Kanas_Lake" title="Kanas Lake">Kanas Lake</a> where is a legend of a large creature.</p>
