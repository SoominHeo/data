<p><b>Argun</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Аргу́н</span>; <a href="/wiki/Chechen_language" title="Chechen language">Chechen</a>: <span lang="ce" xml:lang="ce">Устрада</span>, <i>Ustrada</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">town</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chechen_Republic" title="Chechen Republic">Chechen Republic</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>, located on the <a href="/wiki/Argun_River_(Caucasus)" title="Argun River (Caucasus)">Argun River</a>. Population: <span class="nowrap">29,525 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2010)" title="Russian Census (2010)">2010 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2010Census_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-2010Census-3">[3]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">25,698 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2002)" title="Russian Census (2002)">2002 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PopCensus_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-PopCensus-5">[5]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">25,491 (<a href="/wiki/Soviet_Census_(1989)" title="Soviet Census (1989)">1989 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census1989_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-Census1989-6">[6]</a></sup> 22,000 (1968).</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Administrative_and_municipal_status">Administrative and municipal status</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Argun,_Chechen_Republic&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Administrative and municipal status">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Within the <a href="/wiki/Subdivisions_of_Russia#Administrative_divisions" title="Subdivisions of Russia">framework of administrative divisions</a>, it is incorporated as the <b><a href="/wiki/City_of_federal_subject_significance" title="City of federal subject significance">town of republic significance</a> of Argun</b>—an administrative unit with the status equal to that of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_the_Chechen_Republic" title="Administrative divisions of the Chechen Republic">districts</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ref162_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-Ref162-1">[1]</a></sup> As a <a href="/wiki/Subdivisions_of_Russia#Municipal_divisions" title="Subdivisions of Russia">municipal division</a>, the town of republic significance of Argun is incorporated as <b>Argun Urban Okrug</b>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ref624_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-Ref624-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
