<p>"<b>Crucify My Love</b>" is the fifteenth single by Japanese heavy metal band <a href="/wiki/X_Japan" title="X Japan">X Japan</a>, released on August 26, 1996.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Summary">Summary</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Crucify_My_Love&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Summary">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Its <a href="/wiki/A-side_and_B-side" title="A-side and B-side">B-side</a> is a live version of one of the band's earlier singles, "<a href="/wiki/Week_End_(X_Japan_song)" title="Week End (X Japan song)">Week End</a>", recorded on December 31, 1995 at the <a href="/wiki/Tokyo_Dome" title="Tokyo Dome">Tokyo Dome</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Commercial_performance">Commercial performance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Crucify_My_Love&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Commercial performance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The single reached number 2 on the <a href="/wiki/Oricon" title="Oricon">Oricon</a> charts, and charted for 9 weeks.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Oricon_singles_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Oricon_singles-1">[1]</a></sup> It is certified Gold by <a href="/wiki/Recording_Industry_Association_of_Japan" title="Recording Industry Association of Japan">RIAJ</a>.</p>
