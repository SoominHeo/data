<p><i><b>Darevskia armeniaca</b></i>, commonly known as the <b>Armenian lizard</b> or the <b>Armenian rock lizard</b>, is a <a href="/wiki/Parthenogenesis" title="Parthenogenesis">parthenogenetic</a> (unisexually breeding) <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> (or form) of <i><a href="/wiki/Darevskia" title="Darevskia">Darevskia</a></i>, a <a href="/wiki/Genus" title="Genus">genus</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lizards" title="Lizards">lizards</a> belonging to the family <a href="/wiki/Lacertidae" title="Lacertidae">Lacertidae</a>, the wall lizards.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> <i>Darevskia armeniaca</i> is native to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Armenian_Highland" title="Armenian Highland">Armenian Highland</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RDB_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-RDB-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
