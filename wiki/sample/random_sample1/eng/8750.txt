<p><b>Arzano</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a href="/wiki/Metropolitan_City_of_Naples" title="Metropolitan City of Naples">Metropolitan City of Naples</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Campania" title="Campania">Campania</a>, located about 9 km north of <a href="/wiki/Naples" title="Naples">Naples</a>.</p>
<p>Arzano borders the following municipalities: <a href="/wiki/Casandrino" title="Casandrino">Casandrino</a>, <a href="/wiki/Casavatore" title="Casavatore">Casavatore</a>, <a href="/wiki/Casoria" title="Casoria">Casoria</a>, <a href="/wiki/Frattamaggiore" title="Frattamaggiore">Frattamaggiore</a>, <a href="/wiki/Grumo_Nevano" title="Grumo Nevano">Grumo Nevano</a>, <a href="/wiki/Naples" title="Naples">Naples</a>.</p>
