<p><b>Arcinazzo Romano</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Metropolitan_City_of_Rome" title="Metropolitan City of Rome">Metropolitan City of Rome</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Latium" title="Latium">Latium</a>, located about 50 kilometres (31 mi) east of <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a>.</p>
<p>Arcinazzo Romano borders the following municipalities: <a href="/wiki/Affile" title="Affile">Affile</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jenne,_Italy" title="Jenne, Italy">Jenne</a>, <a href="/wiki/Piglio" title="Piglio">Piglio</a>, <a href="/wiki/Roiate" title="Roiate">Roiate</a>, <a href="/wiki/Serrone" title="Serrone">Serrone</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Subiaco,_Italy" title="Subiaco, Italy">Subiaco</a>, <a href="/wiki/Trevi_nel_Lazio" title="Trevi nel Lazio">Trevi nel Lazio</a>. It was called <b>Ponza</b> until 1891.</p>
<p>The area of Arcinazzo includes a wide plateau ("Altopiano" in <a href="/wiki/Italian_language" title="Italian language">Italian</a>), a popular holiday resort; the plateau is home to the remains of a Roman villa, the hunting residence of emperor <a href="/wiki/Trajan" title="Trajan">Trajan</a>.</p>
