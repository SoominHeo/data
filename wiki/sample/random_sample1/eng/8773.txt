<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">List of Catholic dioceses in Argentina</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <a href="/wiki/Catholic_Church" title="Catholic Church">Catholic Church</a> in <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentina</a> comprises fourteen <a href="/wiki/Ecclesiastical_province" title="Ecclesiastical province">ecclesiastical provinces</a> each headed by a Metropolitan <a href="/wiki/Archbishop" title="Archbishop">archbishop</a>. The provinces are in turn subdivided into 48 <a href="/wiki/Diocese" title="Diocese">dioceses</a> and 14 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Archdioceses" title="Archdioceses">archdioceses</a> each headed by a <a href="/wiki/Bishop" title="Bishop">bishop</a> or an archbishop.</p>
