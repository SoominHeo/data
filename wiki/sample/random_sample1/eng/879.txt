<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Science_and_technology">Science and technology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=CVT&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Science and technology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Capacitor_voltage_transformer" title="Capacitor voltage transformer">Capacitor voltage transformer</a>, an electrical transformer commonly used in high-voltage transmission line applications</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Centroidal_Voronoi_tessellation" title="Centroidal Voronoi tessellation">Centroidal Voronoi tessellation</a>, a geometric object</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chemical_vapor_transport" title="Chemical vapor transport">Chemical vapor transport</a>, a method for growing crystals</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Constant_voltage_transformer" title="Constant voltage transformer">Constant voltage transformer</a>, a type of saturating transformel as an AC voltage stabilizer</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Continuously_variable_transmission" title="Continuously variable transmission">Continuously variable transmission</a>, a vehicle transmission providing an infinite number of possible gear ratios</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Coordinated_Video_Timings" title="Coordinated Video Timings">Coordinated Video Timings</a>, specified by VESA</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Cycloidal_vibration_technology" title="Cycloidal vibration technology">Cycloidal vibration technology</a>, a form of small-amplitude, low-frequency 3-dimensional vibrational wave used for therapeutic applications</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Cytoplasm-to-vacuole_targeting" title="Cytoplasm-to-vacuole targeting">Cytoplasm-to-vacuole targeting</a>, a autophagy-related pathway in yeast</li>
<li>Compound Value Type, a type within <a href="/wiki/Freebase" title="Freebase">Freebase</a> which is used to represent data where each entry consists of multiple fields</li>
<li>Cardiovascular Thoracic surgery (see <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cardiothoracic_Surgery" title="Cardiothoracic Surgery">Cardiothoracic Surgery</a>), a field of medicine involved in surgical treatment of diseases affecting organs inside the thorax (the chest)—generally treatment of conditions of the heart (heart disease) and lungs (lung disease)</li>
</ul>
