<p><b>Amanda Fondell</b> (born 29 August 1994, <a href="/wiki/Linder%C3%B6d" title="Linderöd">Linderöd</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Sweden</a>)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Swedish_people" title="Swedish people">Swedish</a> singer and winner of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Idol_2011" title="Idol 2011">eighth series</a> of Swedish <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Idol_(Sweden)" title="Idol (Sweden)">Idol</a></i> which she won on 9 December 2011 with 52% of the votes.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>Fondell's first music single "<a href="/wiki/All_This_Way" title="All This Way">All This Way</a>" was released on 2 December 2011. The song was written and produced by <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Idol_2004" title="Idol 2004">Idol 2004</a></i> runner up <a href="/wiki/Darin_Zanyar" title="Darin Zanyar">Darin Zanyar</a> amongst others.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
