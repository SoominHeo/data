<p><br/>
Year <b>1471</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MCDLXXI</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Tuesday" title="Common year starting on Tuesday">common year starting on Tuesday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1471&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93December">January–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1471&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_1" title="March 1">March 1</a> – Emperor <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Le_Thanh_Tong" title="Le Thanh Tong">Le Thanh Tong</a> captures the Champa Capital, establishing new regions in middle Vietnam.</li>
<li>March – The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yorkist" title="Yorkist">Yorkist</a> King <a href="/wiki/Edward_IV_of_England" title="Edward IV of England">Edward IV</a> returns to England to reclaim his throne.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_14" title="April 14">April 14</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Barnet" title="Battle of Barnet">Battle of Barnet</a>: Edward defeats the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Lancaster" title="House of Lancaster">Lancastrian</a> army under <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Richard_Neville,_Earl_of_Warwick" title="Richard Neville, Earl of Warwick">Warwick</a>, who is killed.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_4" title="May 4">May 4</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Tewkesbury" title="Battle of Tewkesbury">Battle of Tewkesbury</a>: King Edward defeats a Lancastrian army under <a href="/wiki/Margaret_of_Anjou" title="Margaret of Anjou">Queen Margaret</a> and her son, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Edward_of_Westminster" title="Edward of Westminster">Edward of Westminster</a> the Prince of Wales, who is killed. Later in the month, King <a href="/wiki/Henry_VI_of_England" title="Henry VI of England">Henry VI of England</a> is murdered, eliminating all Lancastrian opposition.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_14" title="July 14">July 14</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Shelon" title="Battle of Shelon">Battle of Shelon</a>: The forces of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Muscovy" title="Muscovy">Muscovy</a> defeat the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Republic_of_Novgorod" title="Republic of Novgorod">Republic of Novgorod</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_9" title="August 9">August 9</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Sixtus_IV" title="Pope Sixtus IV">Pope Sixtus IV</a> succeeds <a href="/wiki/Pope_Paul_II" title="Pope Paul II">Pope Paul II</a>, to become the 212th <a href="/wiki/Pope" title="Pope">pope</a>.</li>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Date_unknown">Date unknown</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1471&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Date unknown">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Pachacuti" title="Pachacuti">Pachacuti Inca Yupanqui</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Inca_Empire" title="Inca Empire">Inca Empire</a> dies and is succeeded by his son <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tupac_Inca_Yupanqui" title="Tupac Inca Yupanqui">Tupac Inca Yupanqui</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Jo%C3%A3o_de_Santar%C3%A9m" title="João de Santarém">João de Santarém</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pedro_Escobar" title="Pedro Escobar">Pedro Escobar</a> cross the <a href="/wiki/Equator" title="Equator">Equator</a>. The southern hemisphere is discovered and sailors begin to be guided by a new constellation, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Southern_Cross" title="Southern Cross">Southern Cross</a>.</li>
<li>Portuguese sailors reach Mina de Ouro on the Gold Coast (present-day <a href="/wiki/Ghana" title="Ghana">Ghana</a>) and explore Cape St. Catherine, two degrees south of the equator. Mina de Ouro becomes the chief center for the gold trade and a major source of revenue for the crown.</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Moorish" title="Moorish">Moorish</a> exiles from Spain, led by Moulay Ali Ben Moussa Ben Rached El Alami, found the city of <a href="/wiki/Chefchaouen" title="Chefchaouen">Chefchaouen</a> in the north of Morocco.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Marsilio_Ficino" title="Marsilio Ficino">Marsilio Ficino</a>'s translations of the <a href="/wiki/Hermetica" title="Hermetica">Hermetica</a> into <a href="/wiki/Latin" title="Latin">Latin</a>, <i>De potestate et sapientia Dei</i>, are published.</li>
</ul>
