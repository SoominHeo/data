<p>"<b>Cake by the Ocean</b>" is the debut single by American band <a href="/wiki/DNCE" title="DNCE">DNCE</a>, released for sale on September 18, 2015 and to radio on September 29, 2015 through <a href="/wiki/Republic_Records" title="Republic Records">Republic Records</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-iTunes_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-iTunes-2">[2]</a></sup> It serves as the lead single from their debut EP, <i><a href="/wiki/Swaay" title="Swaay">Swaay</a></i> (2015)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> and is also included on their debut studio album, <i><a href="/wiki/DNCE_(album)" title="DNCE (album)">DNCE</a></i> (2016).</p>
<p>The song reached the top 10 on multiple national record charts including Australia, Canada, Germany, the United Kingdom, and the United States. It has also topped the US <i>Billboard</i> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Adult_Pop_Songs" title="Adult Pop Songs">Adult Pop Songs</a> airplay chart and been certified Gold or higher by a number of recording industry associations.</p>
<p><i><a href="/wiki/Billboard_(magazine)" title="Billboard (magazine)">Billboard</a></i> ranked "Cake by the Ocean" at number 44 on their "100 Best Pop Songs of 2016" list.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nielsen_Holdings_PLC" title="Nielsen Holdings PLC">Nielsen Music</a></i> named "Cake by the Ocean" the second most-played song in Canada for 2016, behind only <a href="/wiki/Justin_Bieber" title="Justin Bieber">Justin Bieber</a>'s "<a href="/wiki/Love_Yourself" title="Love Yourself">Love Yourself</a>".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Nielsen_Canada_2016_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Nielsen_Canada_2016-5">[5]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Composition">Composition</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Cake_by_the_Ocean&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Composition">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Cake by the Ocean" was co-written by <a href="/wiki/Joe_Jonas" title="Joe Jonas">Joe Jonas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Justin_Tranter" title="Justin Tranter">Justin Tranter</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Swedish</a> duo <a href="/wiki/Mattman_%26_Robin" title="Mattman &amp; Robin">Mattman &amp; Robin</a>, who also produced the song.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hey_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-hey-6">[6]</a></sup> Lyrically, the song is about <a href="/wiki/Sexual_intercourse" title="Sexual intercourse">sex</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-seven_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-seven-7">[7]</a></sup> but is also often interpreted to be referring to the <a href="/wiki/Cocktail" title="Cocktail">cocktail</a> <a href="/wiki/Sex_on_the_Beach" title="Sex on the Beach">Sex on the Beach</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> The song's title originated from the Swedish producers repeatedly confusing the phrase "sex on the beach" for "cake by the ocean."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hey_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-hey-6">[6]</a></sup> According to the sheet music published at musicnotes.com, the song is composed in <a href="/wiki/E_minor" title="E minor">E minor</a>, with a tempo of 120 beats per minute.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Music_video">Music video</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Cake_by_the_Ocean&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Music video">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A music video for "Cake by the Ocean", directed by Black Coffee and <a href="/wiki/Gigi_Hadid" title="Gigi Hadid">Gigi Hadid</a> (Joe Jonas' then girlfriend), was released on 16 October, 2015. Christina Garibaldi of <i><a href="/wiki/MTV" title="MTV">MTV</a></i> wrote "DNCE— Joe, Joo Lee, Cole Whittle and Jack Lawless— perform their song in front of a giant piece of cake on the beach. Soon, a bunch of women wearing bikinis join 'Team DNCE' lather up and get their cake fighting faces on as they meet their competition: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Fat_Jew" title="The Fat Jew">The Fat Jew</a>."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Live_performances">Live performances</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Cake_by_the_Ocean&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Live performances">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On January 31, 2016, DNCE performed a 1950s <a href="/wiki/Rock_and_roll" title="Rock and roll">rock and roll</a>-inspired version of "Cake by the Ocean" as part of Fox's television musical <i><a href="/wiki/Grease:_Live" title="Grease: Live">Grease: Live</a></i> as Johnny Casino and The Gamblers.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-rollingstone-dnce_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-rollingstone-dnce-11">[11]</a></sup> On February 12, 2016, the band performed the song live on <i><a href="/wiki/The_Ellen_DeGeneres_Show" title="The Ellen DeGeneres Show">The Ellen DeGeneres Show</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> On March 12, 2016, the band performed the song at the <a href="/wiki/2016_Kids%27_Choice_Awards" title="2016 Kids' Choice Awards">2016 Kids' Choice Awards</a>. On April 22, 2016, the band performed the song, in the UK, on <a href="/wiki/BBC_One" title="BBC One">BBC One</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/The_Graham_Norton_Show" title="The Graham Norton Show">The Graham Norton Show</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup> On April 30, 2016, the band performed the song at the <a href="/wiki/Radio_Disney_Music_Awards" title="Radio Disney Music Awards">Radio Disney Music Awards</a>. Cake by the Ocean was performed by DNCE at the <a href="/wiki/2016_Billboard_Music_Awards" title="2016 Billboard Music Awards">2016 Billboard Music Awards</a> that took place in Las Vegas, Nevada.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Usage_in_media">Usage in media</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Cake_by_the_Ocean&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Usage in media">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The song is featured in the films <i><a href="/wiki/Bad_Moms" title="Bad Moms">Bad Moms</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Middle_School:_The_Worst_Years_of_My_Life_(film)" title="Middle School: The Worst Years of My Life (film)">Middle School: The Worst Years of My Life</a>.</i> It's also featured in the television shows <i><a href="/wiki/Telenovela_(TV_series)" title="Telenovela (TV series)">Telenovela</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Park" title="South Park">South Park</a>, <a href="/wiki/No_Tomorrow_(TV_series)" title="No Tomorrow (TV series)">No Tomorrow</a>, <a href="/wiki/The_Real_O%27Neals" title="The Real O'Neals">The Real O'Neals</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Mary_%2B_Jane" title="Mary + Jane">Mary + Jane</a>.</i> The trailer for the film <i><a href="/wiki/Mike_and_Dave_Need_Wedding_Dates" title="Mike and Dave Need Wedding Dates">Mike and Dave Need Wedding Dates</a></i> also features the track.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup> The song is also featured in the video game <i><a href="/wiki/Just_Dance_2017" title="Just Dance 2017">Just Dance 2017</a>.</i> In August 2016, the track was featured in an Indonesian Mizone commercial. <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup></p>
