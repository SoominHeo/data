<p>The <b>Seal of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Territory_of_American_Samoa" title="Territory of American Samoa">Territory of American Samoa</a></b> is based in traditional local design. The fly switch (<i><a href="/wiki/Fue" title="Fue">fue</a></i>) represents wisdom and the staff (<a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=To%27oto%27o&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="To'oto'o (page does not exist)">To'oto'o</a>) represents authority. Both symbols are used by talking chiefs indicating their rank. The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tanoa" title="Tanoa">Tanoa</a> (kava bowl) represents service to the chief.</p>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Samoan_language" title="Samoan language">Samoan</a> motto <i>Samoa Muamua Le Atua</i> is translated “Samoa, God is First”.</p>
