<p>"<b>Daremo Shiranai</b>" (誰も知らない, "Nobody Knows") is a Japanese-language song and the 44th single released by Japanese boyband <a href="/wiki/Arashi" title="Arashi">Arashi</a>. "Daremo Shiranai" was used as the theme song for the drama <i><a href="/wiki/Shinigami-kun" title="Shinigami-kun">Shinigami-kun</a></i> starring Arashi member <a href="/wiki/Satoshi_Ohno" title="Satoshi Ohno">Satoshi Ohno</a>. It was the 12th best-selling single of the year in Japan, with 525,055 copies.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Single_information">Single information</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Daremo_Shiranai&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Single information">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The single was released in two editions: a limited edition including a bonus track and a bonus DVD with a music video for "Daremo Shiranai", and a regular CD only edition including two bonus tracks and karaoke tracks for all the songs. The limited edition also contains a 16-page booklet.</p>
