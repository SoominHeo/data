<p><b>Amira Ben Chaabane</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar" xml:lang="ar">أميرة بن شعبان</span>‎‎; born May 29, 1990 in <a href="/wiki/Tunis" title="Tunis">Tunis</a>) is a Tunisian <a href="/wiki/Sabre_(fencing)" title="Sabre (fencing)">sabre</a> fencer;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sports-reference1_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-sports-reference1-1">[1]</a></sup> gold medallist at the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=2010_African_Fencing_Championships&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="2010 African Fencing Championships (page does not exist)">2010 African Fencing Championships</a>. She <a href="/wiki/Fencing_at_the_2012_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Qualification" title="Fencing at the 2012 Summer Olympics – Qualification">qualified</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Fencing_at_the_2012_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Women%27s_sabre" title="Fencing at the 2012 Summer Olympics – Women's sabre">individual event</a> of the <a href="/wiki/2012_Summer_Olympics" title="2012 Summer Olympics">2012 Summer Olympics</a> as one of the best top-ranked fencers of the African zone. She was defeated 15-12 in the first round by <a href="/wiki/Chen_Xiaodong" title="Chen Xiaodong">Chen Xiaodong</a> of China.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sports-reference1_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-sports-reference1-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
