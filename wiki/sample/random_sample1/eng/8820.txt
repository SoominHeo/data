<p>The <b>Abakan</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Абака́н</span>), (from the <a href="/wiki/Khakas_language" title="Khakas language">Khakas</a> word for "bear's blood") is a river in the <a href="/wiki/Khakassia" title="Khakassia">Republic of Khakassia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>. It is formed by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Confluence_(geography)" title="Confluence (geography)">confluence</a> of the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Bolshoy_Abakan_River&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Bolshoy Abakan River (page does not exist)">Bolshoy Abakan</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Maly_Abakan_River&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Maly Abakan River (page does not exist)">Maly Abakan</a> rivers. It rises in the western <a href="/wiki/Sayan_Mountains" title="Sayan Mountains">Sayan Mountains</a> and flows northeast through the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Minusinsk_Depression" title="Minusinsk Depression">Minusinsk Depression</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Yenisei_River" title="Yenisei River">Yenisei River</a>. It is about 514 kilometres (319 mi) long (from the Bolshoy Abakan's <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Source_(river_or_stream)" title="Source (river or stream)">source</a>), and its <a href="/wiki/Drainage_basin" title="Drainage basin">drainage basin</a> covers 32,000 square kilometres (12,000 sq mi). The river is used for <a href="/wiki/Log_driving" title="Log driving">log driving</a> and irrigation.</p>
<p>The city of <a href="/wiki/Abakan" title="Abakan">Abakan</a> is located at the confluence of the Abakan and the Yenisei.</p>
