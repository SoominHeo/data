<p><b>Astrojildo Pereira</b> (8 November 1890 â 21 November 1965) was a <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazilian</a> politician, writer and journalist. He was a founding member of the <a href="/wiki/Brazilian_Communist_Party" title="Brazilian Communist Party">Brazilian Communist Party</a> in 1922.</p>
