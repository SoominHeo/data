<p>The <b>Asian Handball Nations Championship</b> is the official competition for senior national handball teams of <a href="/wiki/Asia" title="Asia">Asia</a> and takes place every two years. In addition to crowning the Asian champions, the tournament also serves as a qualifying tournament for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/World_Women%27s_Handball_Championship" title="World Women's Handball Championship">World Championship</a>.</p>
