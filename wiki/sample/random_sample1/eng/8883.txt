<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">List of sovereign states and dependent territories in Asia</h1>

<p>This is a <b>list of sovereign states and dependent territories in Asia</b>. It includes both <a href="/wiki/Sovereign_state" title="Sovereign state">fully recognized states</a>, states with limited recognition, and <a href="/wiki/Dependent_territory" title="Dependent territory">dependent territories</a> of both <a href="/wiki/Asia" title="Asia">Asian</a> and non-Asian states. It lists <a href="/wiki/List_of_sovereign_states_and_dependent_territories_in_Asia#Sovereign_states" title="List of sovereign states and dependent territories in Asia">48 sovereign states</a>, <a href="/wiki/List_of_sovereign_states_and_dependent_territories_in_Asia#Sovereign_states" title="List of sovereign states and dependent territories in Asia">6 states with limited international recognition</a>, and <a href="/wiki/List_of_sovereign_states_and_dependent_territories_in_Asia#Dependent_territories_and_other_territories" title="List of sovereign states and dependent territories in Asia">6 dependent territories and other territories</a>. While most of <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>'s area is in Asia, it is generalized as being in <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a> since its capital, <a href="/wiki/Moscow" title="Moscow">Moscow</a>, and most of its population are there.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geographical_boundaries_of_Asia">Geographical boundaries of Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_sovereign_states_and_dependent_territories_in_Asia&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geographical boundaries of Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The divisions between <a href="/wiki/Asia" title="Asia">Asia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a> occur at the <a href="/wiki/Ural_Mountains" title="Ural Mountains">Ural Mountains</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ural_River" title="Ural River">Ural River</a> and <a href="/wiki/Caspian_Sea" title="Caspian Sea">Caspian Sea</a> in the east, the <a href="/wiki/Caucasus_Mountains" title="Caucasus Mountains">Caucasus Mountains</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Black_Sea" title="Black Sea">Black Sea</a> with its outlets, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bosporus" title="Bosporus">Bosporus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dardanelles" title="Dardanelles">Dardanelles</a> in the south. <a href="/wiki/Azerbaijan" title="Azerbaijan">Azerbaijan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Georgia_(country)" title="Georgia (country)">Georgia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kazakhstan" title="Kazakhstan">Kazakhstan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a> all have territory in both Asia and Europe. <a href="/wiki/Armenia" title="Armenia">Armenia</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cyprus" title="Cyprus">Cyprus</a> are entirely in <a href="/wiki/Western_Asia" title="Western Asia">Western Asia</a> but are sociopolitically European countries.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (April 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<p>The division between Asia and <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">Africa</a> is usually considered to be the <a href="/wiki/Suez_Canal" title="Suez Canal">Suez Canal</a>, placing the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sinai_peninsula" title="Sinai peninsula">Sinai peninsula</a> in Asia, and therefore Egypt may be included as an Asian state.</p>
<p>The division between Asia and <a href="/wiki/Australasia" title="Australasia">Australasia</a> (<a href="/wiki/Oceania" title="Oceania">Oceania</a>) is disputed, and placed somewhere between <a href="/wiki/Java" title="Java">Java</a> and <a href="/wiki/New_Guinea" title="New Guinea">New Guinea</a>. <a href="/wiki/Indonesia" title="Indonesia">Indonesia</a> spans both areas, but is considered to be Asian. <a href="/wiki/East_Timor" title="East Timor">East Timor</a> is sometimes considered to be part of <a href="/wiki/Australasia" title="Australasia">Australasia</a>, but due to its being surrounded by Indonesia and its once being part of Indonesia, it is considered Asian. <a href="/wiki/Papua_New_Guinea" title="Papua New Guinea">Papua New Guinea</a> is occasionally considered Asian by virtue of neighbouring Indonesia, but this is rare, and it is generally accepted to be part of <a href="/wiki/Australasia" title="Australasia">Australasia</a>. The division between Asia and <a href="/wiki/North_America" title="North America">North America</a> is considered to be the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bering_strait" title="Bering strait">Bering strait</a>. Some of the <a href="/wiki/Aleutian_Islands" title="Aleutian Islands">Aleutian Islands</a> may be considered as Asian.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (November 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Sovereign_states">Sovereign states</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_sovereign_states_and_dependent_territories_in_Asia&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Sovereign states">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Further information: <a href="/wiki/Sovereign_state" title="Sovereign state">Sovereign state</a></div>
<p>A sovereign state is a political association with effective <a href="/wiki/Sovereignty" title="Sovereignty">sovereignty</a> over a population for whom it makes decisions in the national interest.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> According to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Montevideo_convention" title="Montevideo convention">Montevideo convention</a>, a state must have a permanent population, a defined territory, a government, and the capacity to enter into relations with other states.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="UN_member_states">UN member states</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_sovereign_states_and_dependent_territories_in_Asia&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: UN member states">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>There are 48 Asian states in this list. All are <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_United_Nations_member_states" title="List of United Nations member states">members</a> of the <a href="/wiki/United_Nations" title="United Nations">United Nations</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/State_of_Palestine" title="State of Palestine">Palestine</a> is an <a href="/wiki/United_Nations_General_Assembly_observers" title="United Nations General Assembly observers">observer state</a> and has never controlled the majority of its claimed territory.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>

<li id="cnote_d"><b><a href="#ref_d_1">^</a></b> Although Kuala Lumpur is the official capital of Malaysia, <a href="/wiki/Putrajaya" title="Putrajaya">Putrajaya</a> is the seat of government.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-37"><a href="#cite_note-37">[37]</a></sup></li>
<li id="cnote_e"><b><a href="#ref_e_1">^</a></b> Akrotiri and Dhekelia are British military bases, with the population including 8,000 United Kingdom personnel and dependents.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-38"><a href="#cite_note-38">[38]</a></sup></li>
<li id="cnote_f"><b><a href="#ref_f_1">^</a></b> Although the islands have no remaining indigenous inhabitants, a military base shared between the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a> and the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> is located on the island of <a href="/wiki/Diego_Garcia" title="Diego Garcia">Diego Garcia</a>, with a staff of 4,000 people.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BIOT_30-1"><a href="#cite_note-BIOT-30">[30]</a></sup></li>
