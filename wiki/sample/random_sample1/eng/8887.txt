<p>The <b>1967 Asian Club Championship</b> was the 1st edition of the annual Asian club football competition hosted by <a href="/wiki/Asian_Football_Confederation" title="Asian Football Confederation">Asian Football Confederation</a>. Six domestic league champions from six countries competed in the tournament.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Hapoel_Tel_Aviv_F.C." title="Hapoel Tel Aviv F.C.">Hapoel Tel Aviv F.C.</a> (ISR) became the first champions of Asia after defeating <a href="/wiki/Selangor_FA" title="Selangor FA">Selangor FA</a> (MLY) in the final.</p>
