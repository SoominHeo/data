<p>The <b>1987 Asian Club Championship</b> was the 7th edition of the annual Asian club football competition hosted by <a href="/wiki/Asian_Football_Confederation" title="Asian Football Confederation">Asian Football Confederation</a>. Several Asian clubs started the qualifying round in Fall of 1987.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Tokyo_Verdy" title="Tokyo Verdy">Yomiuri FC</a> (<a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>) became the second Japanese club to win the Asian Championship.</p>
