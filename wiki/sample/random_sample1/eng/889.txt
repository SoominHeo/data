<p><i><b>Colorful Sensibility Part 2</b></i> is the follow-up album of <i><a href="/wiki/Colorful_Sensibility" title="Colorful Sensibility">Colorful Sensibility</a></i> by South Korean band <a href="/wiki/F.T._Island" title="F.T. Island">F.T. Island</a>, released on October 17, 2008, two months after the main album.</p>
