<p>The <b>1992–93 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Asian_Club_Championship" title="Asian Club Championship">Asian Club Championship</a></b> was the 12th edition of the annual international club football competition held in the <a href="/wiki/Asian_Football_Confederation" title="Asian Football Confederation">AFC</a> region (Asia). It determined that year's club champion of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">association football</a> in <a href="/wiki/Asia" title="Asia">Asia</a>.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/PAS_Tehran_F.C." title="PAS Tehran F.C.">PAS Tehran</a> of <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a> won the final and become Asian champions for the first time.</p>
