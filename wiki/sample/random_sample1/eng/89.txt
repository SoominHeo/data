<p><br/>
Year <b>1498</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MCDXCVIII</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Monday" title="Common year starting on Monday">common year starting on Monday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1498&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93December">January–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1498&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_25" title="January 25">January 25</a> – <a href="/wiki/Portuguese_Empire" title="Portuguese Empire">Portuguese</a> explorer <a href="/wiki/Vasco_da_Gama" title="Vasco da Gama">Vasco da Gama</a> visits <a href="/wiki/Quelimane" title="Quelimane">Quelimane</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mozambique" title="Mozambique">Mozambique</a> in southeastern Africa.</li>
<li>February – Da Gama reaches <a href="/wiki/Malindi" title="Malindi">Malindi</a> in modern-day <a href="/wiki/Kenya" title="Kenya">Kenya</a>.</li>
<li>May
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/John_Cabot" title="John Cabot">John Cabot</a> leaves <a href="/wiki/Bristol" title="Bristol">Bristol</a> on an expedition, never to be seen again.</li>
<li>The English <a href="/wiki/Company_of_Merchant_Adventurers_of_London" title="Company of Merchant Adventurers of London">Merchant Adventurers</a> are granted a trade monopoly with the <a href="/wiki/Habsburg_Netherlands" title="Habsburg Netherlands">Netherlands</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_20" title="May 20">May 20</a> – Portuguese navigator <a href="/wiki/Vasco_da_Gama" title="Vasco da Gama">Vasco da Gama</a> arrives at <a href="/wiki/History_of_Kozhikode" title="History of Kozhikode">Calicut</a> (now <a href="/wiki/Kozhikode" title="Kozhikode">Kozhikode</a>), India, becoming the first European to get there by sailing around Africa, thus discovering the maritime route to India. He finds a local Arab merchant who is able to interpret for him.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_23" title="May 23">May 23</a> – <a href="/wiki/Girolamo_Savonarola" title="Girolamo Savonarola">Girolamo Savonarola</a>, ruler of <a href="/wiki/Florence" title="Florence">Florence</a>, is executed for criticizing the <a href="/wiki/Pope" title="Pope">Pope</a>.</li>
<li>June – <a href="/wiki/Niccol%C3%B2_Machiavelli" title="Niccolò Machiavelli">Niccolò Machiavelli</a> is elected by the Great Council as the second chancellor of the <a href="/wiki/History_of_Florence" title="History of Florence">Republic of Florence</a>.</li>
<li>Summer – The final Welsh revolt of the medieval era breaks out in <a href="/wiki/Meirionnydd" title="Meirionnydd">Meirionnydd</a>, <a href="/wiki/North_Wales" title="North Wales">North Wales</a>; <a href="/wiki/Harlech_Castle" title="Harlech Castle">Harlech Castle</a> is captured by the rebels before the revolt is suppressed.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_31" title="July 31">July 31</a> – On his third voyage to the <a href="/wiki/Western_Hemisphere" title="Western Hemisphere">Western Hemisphere</a>, <a href="/wiki/Christopher_Columbus" title="Christopher Columbus">Christopher Columbus</a> becomes the first European to discover the island of <a href="/wiki/Trinidad" title="Trinidad">Trinidad</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_1" title="August 1">August 1</a> – Columbus discovers the mouth of the <a href="/wiki/Orinoco" title="Orinoco">Orinoco</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_4" title="August 4">August 4</a>–<a href="/wiki/August_12" title="August 12">12</a> – Columbus explores the <a href="/wiki/Gulf_of_Paria" title="Gulf of Paria">Gulf of Paria</a>.</li>
<li>Voyage of <a href="/wiki/Jo%C3%A3o_Fernandes_Lavrador" title="João Fernandes Lavrador">João Fernandes Lavrador</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pedro_Barcelos" title="Pedro Barcelos">Pedro Barcelos</a> to <a href="/wiki/Greenland" title="Greenland">Greenland</a> during which they discover the land which they name <a href="/wiki/Labrador" title="Labrador">Labrador</a>.</li>
<li>Probable date at which <a href="/wiki/Leonardo_da_Vinci" title="Leonardo da Vinci">Leonardo da Vinci</a> completes painting <i><a href="/wiki/The_Last_Supper_(Leonardo_da_Vinci)" title="The Last Supper (Leonardo da Vinci)">The Last Supper</a></i> on the refectory wall of <a href="/wiki/Santa_Maria_delle_Grazie_(Milan)" title="Santa Maria delle Grazie (Milan)">Santa Maria delle Grazie (Milan)</a>.</li>
</ul>
