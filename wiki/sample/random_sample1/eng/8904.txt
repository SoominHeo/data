<p><b>Rin Aoki</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<a href="/wiki/Japanese_language" title="Japanese language">Japanese</a>: <span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">青木りん</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <a href="/wiki/Hepburn_romanization" title="Hepburn romanization">Hepburn</a>: <i>Aoki Rin</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>) born February 28, 1985</span> is a Japanese <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gravure_idol" title="Gravure idol">gravure model</a>, <a href="/wiki/AV_idol" title="AV idol">AV idol</a> and <a href="/wiki/Striptease" title="Striptease">erotic dancer</a>. She began as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gravure_idol" title="Gravure idol">gravure idol</a> and later moved to hardcore video work where she became an award-winning <a href="/wiki/AV_idol" title="AV idol">AV idol</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rin_Aoki&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Gravure_idol">Gravure idol</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rin_Aoki&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Gravure idol">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Aoki was born in <a href="/wiki/Yamagata_Prefecture" title="Yamagata Prefecture">Yamagata Prefecture</a> on February 28, 1985. She was first scouted in <a href="/wiki/Harajuku" title="Harajuku">Harajuku</a> as a gravure model in 2002 at the age of 17.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Her first gravure video, <i>Burst</i>, appeared in 2003 and she soon became a popular <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gravure_idol" title="Gravure idol">gravure idol</a>. After two years as a gravure model, Aoki decided to enter the adult video scene and signed with the major Japanese AV company <a href="/wiki/S1_No._1_Style" title="S1 No. 1 Style">S1 No. 1 Style</a>, one of the <a href="/wiki/Hokuto_Corporation" title="Hokuto Corporation">Hokuto Corporation</a> group of companies.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> It is reported that her contract guaranteed her an estimated 13,000,000 yen (about $166,000) per movie, a sum greatly above the average even for top AV idols.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="AV_actress">AV actress</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rin_Aoki&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: AV actress">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Her first AV production with S1, <i>K-cup Active Idol Risky Mosaic</i>, appeared in May 2006 and was directed by <a href="/wiki/Hideto_Aki" title="Hideto Aki">Hideto Aki</a> who also directed most of her subsequent videos with S1. She says she was comfortable with her first AV work but was embarrassed about showing her nipples because they were small and pale colored.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> After making ten original videos with S1 in almost a year, she left the studio along with <a href="/wiki/Maria_Ozawa" title="Maria Ozawa">Maria Ozawa</a> and other fellow S1 actresses to join the startup company <a href="/wiki/Das_(studio)" title="Das (studio)">DAS</a>. In contrast to S1 which specializes in the "softer" type of pornography with mostly straightforward boy-girl sex scenes,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> DAS videos ventured into more extreme areas such as simulated <a href="/wiki/Rape" title="Rape">rape</a>, <a href="/wiki/Creampie_(sexual_act)" title="Creampie (sexual act)">creampie</a> and <a href="/wiki/Urolagnia" title="Urolagnia">watersports</a>. Aoki starred in the first video of the DAS series DASD-001, <i>20 Shots Creampie!</i>, released on April 25, 2007. She has made several more movies for DAS including her first inter-racial role in the DAS "Black Gang Rape" series.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>By August 2008, Aoki was with a new start-up studio, OPPAI, which publicized her first video for them, <i>108 cm Kcup Rin</i>, with a signing and modeling event.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> Although continuing to make movies for OPPAI, she has also appeared in videos for <a href="/wiki/Attackers" title="Attackers">Attackers</a> and yet another new studio, Ranmaru.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Popularity_and_recognition">Popularity and recognition</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rin_Aoki&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Popularity and recognition">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Aoki is one of the latest examples of the very popular genre of "Big-Bust" models in the Japanese AV industry going back to <a href="/wiki/Kimiko_Matsuzaka" title="Kimiko Matsuzaka">Kimiko Matsuzaka</a>. As a measure of her popularity in Japan, the listing of the top 100 actresses by sales from the DMM website shows Aoki ranked #3 in 2006 and #9 in 2007.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p>Aoki had a small preview appearance in the S1 compilation video <i>Hyper – Barely There Mosaic</i> (ハイパーギリギリモザイク) with AV idols <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sora_Aoi" title="Sora Aoi">Sora Aoi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yua_Aida" title="Yua Aida">Yua Aida</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yuma_Asami" title="Yuma Asami">Yuma Asami</a>, <a href="/wiki/Maria_Ozawa" title="Maria Ozawa">Maria Ozawa</a> and <a href="/wiki/Honoka" title="Honoka">Honoka</a> which won the 2006 <a href="/wiki/AV_Open" title="AV Open">AV Open</a> competition among Japanese porn studios.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
<p>At the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Japanese_Adult_Video_Awards#7th_Takeshi_Kitano_Awards_.282006.29" title="Japanese Adult Video Awards">7th annual Takeshi Kitano Entertainment Awards</a> for 2006 sponsored by the Japanese tabloid newspaper <i><a href="/wiki/Tokyo_Sports" title="Tokyo Sports">Tokyo Sports</a></i> (東京スポーツ), Aoki was honored with the Star AV Actress Award (主演AV女優賞).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
