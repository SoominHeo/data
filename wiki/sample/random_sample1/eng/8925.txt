<p>"<b>Ai no Bakudan</b>" is the thirty-eighth single by <a href="/wiki/B%27z" title="B'z">B'z</a>, released on March 9, 2005. This song is one of B'z many number-one singles in <a href="/wiki/Oricon" title="Oricon">Oricon</a> charts.</p>
<p>The song was re-recorded in 2012 with English lyrics and released as "Love's Bomb" as part of the band's <a href="/wiki/ITunes" title="ITunes">iTunes</a>-exclusive English album.</p>
