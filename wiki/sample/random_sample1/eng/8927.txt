<p>The <b>2012 Idol Star Olympics Championship</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 아이돌 스타 올림픽) was held at <a href="/wiki/Mokdong_Stadium" title="Mokdong Stadium">Mokdong Stadium</a> in <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a> (on July 10, 2012) and <a href="/wiki/Yong_In_University" title="Yong In University">Yong In University</a> General Gymnasium in <a href="/wiki/Yongin" title="Yongin">Yongin</a>, South Korea and was broadcast on <a href="/wiki/Munhwa_Broadcasting_Corporation" title="Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation">MBC</a> from July 25 to 26, 2012. At the championships, a total number of 14 events (9 in athletics, 2 in archery, 2 in fencing and 1 in table tennis) were contested: 7 by men, 5 by women and 2 mixed. There were a total number of 150 participating <a href="/wiki/K-pop" title="K-pop">K-pop</a> singers and celebrities, divided into 9 teams.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (January 2017)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
