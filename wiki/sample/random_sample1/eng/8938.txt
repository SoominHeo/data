<p><b>Ichiro Aisawa</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">逢沢 一郎</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Aisawa Ichirō</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born June 10, 1954)</span> is a Japanese politician of the <a href="/wiki/Liberal_Democratic_Party_(Japan)" title="Liberal Democratic Party (Japan)">Liberal Democratic Party</a>, a member of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/House_of_Representatives_of_Japan" title="House of Representatives of Japan">House of Representatives</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Diet_of_Japan" title="Diet of Japan">Diet</a> (national legislature). A native of <a href="/wiki/Mitsu_District,_Okayama" title="Mitsu District, Okayama">Mitsu District, Okayama</a> and graduate of <a href="/wiki/Keio_University" title="Keio University">Keio University</a>, he was elected to the House of Representatives for the first time in 1986.</p>
<p>In 2003, he was appointed as Senior Vice-Minister for the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Foreign_Affairs_(Japan)" title="Ministry of Foreign Affairs (Japan)">Ministry of Foreign Affairs (Japan)</a>.</p>
<p>He was appointed as Chairmen of several Standing Committees of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/House_of_Representatives_of_Japan" title="House of Representatives of Japan">House of Representatives</a>; Committee on Foreign Affairs in 1997, Committee on Rules and Administration in 2006, Committee on Budget in 2007 and Committee on Fundamental National Policies in 2014.</p>
<p>He serves as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vice-President" title="Vice-President">Vice-President</a> of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Japan_Scout_Parliamentary_Association" title="Japan Scout Parliamentary Association">Japan Scout Parliamentary Association</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (November 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
