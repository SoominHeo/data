<p><i><b>Crazy</b></i> is the sixth <a href="/wiki/Extended_play" title="Extended play">mini-album</a> by South Korean <a href="/wiki/Girl_group" title="Girl group">girl group</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/4minute" title="4minute">4minute</a>, released on February 9, 2015. It features the singles "Cold Rain" and "Crazy".</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Crazy_(EP)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On January 26, Cube Entertainment announced that 4Minute would pre-release a track titled "Cold Rain", marking the first time the group has released a ballad as a single.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> On January 29, 4Minute announced a second single track "Crazy" and noted that it would be the title of their sixth EP.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Promotion_and_release">Promotion and release</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Crazy_(EP)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Promotion and release">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The music video for "Crazy" was released on February 9.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> The song impacted <a href="/wiki/Korean_Broadcasting_System" title="Korean Broadcasting System">Korean Broadcasting System</a>'s "K-Pop Connection" radio on February 10, 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Controversy">Controversy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Crazy_(EP)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Controversy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>According to KBS' music-review results on the February 4, 4minute's "Cut it Out" was banned for its use of slang. 4minute's agency Cube Entertainment rep told Star News, "Since 'Cut it Out' is a sub-track off their new album that they also plan to perform, we are in talks to request for a reconsideration." They decided to change the lyrics while performing on stage.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
