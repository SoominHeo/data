<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_Am.C3.A9rica">Club América</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isaac_Acu%C3%B1a&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Club América">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On April 9, 2010, Acuña made his debut for Club América against Jaguares de Chiapas coming on as a substitute in the 85th minute. On July 13, 2011, Acuna scored on a header against Atlas in the Reto II at The Home Depot Center in Carson, Ca from a center made by Oscar Adrian Rojas and the game was eventually won by Club America 2-1. <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<p>On January 1, 2011, Acuña joined Querétaro on loan.
