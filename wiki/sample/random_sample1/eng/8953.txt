<p><b>Isaac Scot Hayden</b> (born 22 March 1995) is an English professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Midfielder" title="Midfielder">midfielder</a> for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_League_Championship" title="Football League Championship">Championship</a> club <a href="/wiki/Newcastle_United_F.C." title="Newcastle United F.C.">Newcastle United</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isaac_Hayden&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Hayden was born in <a href="/wiki/Chelmsford" title="Chelmsford">Chelmsford</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isaac_Hayden&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Hayden made his full debut for <a href="/wiki/Arsenal_F.C." title="Arsenal F.C.">Arsenal</a> on 25 September 2013 playing in midfield in a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_League_Cup" title="Football League Cup">League Cup</a> third-round game against <a href="/wiki/West_Bromwich_Albion_F.C." title="West Bromwich Albion F.C.">West Bromwich Albion</a>. He started the match and was substituted after 84 minutes for <a href="/wiki/Kristoffer_Olsson" title="Kristoffer Olsson">Kristoffer Olsson</a>; Arsenal won on penalties.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> On 23 September 2014, Hayden made his second appearance for Arsenal, playing at centre back in another League Cup third-round game, a 1–2 home defeat against <a href="/wiki/Southampton_F.C." title="Southampton F.C.">Southampton</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 31 July 2015, Hayden joined <a href="/wiki/Hull_City_A.F.C." title="Hull City A.F.C.">Hull City</a> on a season-long loan to gain first team experience.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> Hayden made his debut for the club on the opening day of the 2015–16 season in a 2–0 home win to <a href="/wiki/Huddersfield_Town_A.F.C." title="Huddersfield Town A.F.C.">Huddersfield Town</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> Hayden opened his account when he scored the final goal for Hull in the 6–0 defeat of <a href="/wiki/Charlton_Athletic_F.C." title="Charlton Athletic F.C.">Charlton Athletic</a> on 16 January 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 11 July 2016, it was announced that Hayden has joined <a href="/wiki/Newcastle_United_F.C." title="Newcastle United F.C.">Newcastle United</a> on a five-year deal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-United_Complete_Hayden_Signing_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-United_Complete_Hayden_Signing-8">[8]</a></sup> Hayden scored his first goal for Newcastle in a 4-1 win over <a href="/wiki/Reading_F.C." title="Reading F.C.">Reading</a> on 17 August 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isaac_Hayden&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Hayden has represented England at <a href="/wiki/England_national_under-16_football_team" title="England national under-16 football team">U16</a>, <a href="/wiki/England_national_under-17_football_team" title="England national under-17 football team">U17</a>, <a href="/wiki/England_national_under-18_football_team" title="England national under-18 football team">U18</a>, <a href="/wiki/England_national_under-19_football_team" title="England national under-19 football team">U19</a>, <a href="/wiki/England_national_under-20_football_team" title="England national under-20 football team">U20</a> and <a href="/wiki/England_national_under-21_football_team" title="England national under-21 football team">U21</a> levels.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Style_of_play">Style of play</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Isaac_Hayden&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Style of play">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Ars%C3%A8ne_Wenger" title="Arsène Wenger">Arsène Wenger</a> has described Hayden as a player whose key assets are concentration, intelligence and strength. Equally comfortable at centre-back or in central-midfield, whilst having also previously featured at right-back and as a playmaker.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
