<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">List of Roman Catholic dioceses in Haiti</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic" title="Roman Catholic">Roman Catholic</a> Church in <a href="/wiki/Haiti" title="Haiti">Haiti</a> comprises two <a href="/wiki/Ecclesiastical_province" title="Ecclesiastical province">ecclesiastical provinces</a>, each headed by an <a href="/wiki/Archbishop" title="Archbishop">archbishop</a>. The province is in turn subdivided into 7 <a href="/wiki/Diocese" title="Diocese">dioceses</a> and 2 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Archdiocese" title="Archdiocese">archdioceses</a>, each headed by a <a href="/wiki/Bishop" title="Bishop">bishop</a> or an <a href="/wiki/Archbishop" title="Archbishop">archbishop</a>.</p>
