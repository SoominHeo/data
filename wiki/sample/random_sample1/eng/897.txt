<p>"<b>Dan Dan Kokoro Hikareteku</b>" <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">DAN DAN 心魅かれてく</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, "Bit By Bit I'm Falling Under Your Spell")</span> is the fourth single by <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> rock band <a href="/wiki/Field_of_View" title="Field of View">Field of View</a>. The single charted #4 rank in Oricon for first week. It charted for 11 weeks and sold a total of 52,800 copies. The song serves as the theme song to the <a href="/wiki/Anime" title="Anime">anime</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Dragon_Ball_GT" title="Dragon Ball GT">Dragon Ball GT</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
