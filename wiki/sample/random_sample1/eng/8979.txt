<p><b>Agerola</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a href="/wiki/Metropolitan_City_of_Naples" title="Metropolitan City of Naples">Metropolitan City of Naples</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Campania" title="Campania">Campania</a>, located about 35 km southeast of <a href="/wiki/Naples" title="Naples">Naples</a>. It is part of the <a href="/wiki/Amalfi_Coast" title="Amalfi Coast">Amalfi Coast</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Agerola&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The municipality of Agerola, situated close to the territory of the <a href="/wiki/Amalfi_Coast" title="Amalfi Coast">Amalfi Coast</a>, contains the <i><a href="/wiki/Frazione" title="Frazione">frazioni</a></i> (subdivisions, mainly villages and hamlets) of Bomerano, Campora, Pianillo (communal seat), Ponte, San Lazzaro, and Santa Maria.</p>
<p>Agerola borders on the following municipalities: <a href="/wiki/Amalfi" title="Amalfi">Amalfi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Furore" title="Furore">Furore</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gragnano" title="Gragnano">Gragnano</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pimonte" title="Pimonte">Pimonte</a>, <a href="/wiki/Positano" title="Positano">Positano</a>, <a href="/wiki/Praiano" title="Praiano">Praiano</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Scala_(SA)" title="Scala (SA)">Scala</a>.</p>
