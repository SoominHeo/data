<p><b>Achkhoy-Martan</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Ачхо́й-Марта́н</span>; <a href="/wiki/Chechen_language" title="Chechen language">Chechen</a>: <span lang="ce" xml:lang="ce">Тӏаьхьа-Марта</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">rural locality</a> (a <i><a href="/wiki/Village#Russia" title="Village">selo</a></i>) and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_center" title="Administrative center">administrative center</a> of <a href="/wiki/Achkhoy-Martanovsky_District" title="Achkhoy-Martanovsky District">Achkhoy-Martanovsky District</a> of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chechen_Republic" title="Chechen Republic">Chechen Republic</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>. Population: <span class="nowrap">20,172 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2010)" title="Russian Census (2010)">2010 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2010Census_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-2010Census-1">[1]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">16,742 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2002)" title="Russian Census (2002)">2002 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PopCensus_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-PopCensus-2">[2]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">14,680 (<a href="/wiki/Soviet_Census_(1989)" title="Soviet Census (1989)">1989 Census</a>)</span>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census1989_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Census1989-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
