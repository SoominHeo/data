<p>The <b>Academy Award for Best Story</b> was an <a href="/wiki/Academy_Awards" title="Academy Awards">Academy Award</a> given from the beginning of the Academy Awards until 1957, when it was eliminated in favor of the <a href="/wiki/Academy_Award_for_Best_Original_Screenplay" title="Academy Award for Best Original Screenplay">Academy Award for Best Original Screenplay</a>.</p>
