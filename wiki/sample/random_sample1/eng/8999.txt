<p><b>Akasaka-mitsuke Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">赤坂見附駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Akasaka-mitsuke-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a subway station in <a href="/wiki/Minato,_Tokyo" title="Minato, Tokyo">Minato, Tokyo</a>, Japan, operated by the Tokyo subway operator <a href="/wiki/Tokyo_Metro" title="Tokyo Metro">Tokyo Metro</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lines">Lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Akasaka-mitsuke_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Akasaka-mitsuke Station is served by the <a href="/wiki/Tokyo_Metro_Ginza_Line" title="Tokyo Metro Ginza Line">Tokyo Metro Ginza Line</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tokyo_Metro_Marunouchi_Line" title="Tokyo Metro Marunouchi Line">Tokyo Metro Marunouchi Line</a>, providing same-platform transfers between the two lines in the same direction (for example eastbound to eastbound). Reversing directions between the lines is also fairly easy as the eastbound lines are stacked above the westbound lines. It is also connected by underground passageways to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nagatacho_Station" title="Nagatacho Station">Nagatacho</a>, which is served by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tokyo_Metro_Yurakucho_Line" title="Tokyo Metro Yurakucho Line">Tokyo Metro Yurakucho Line</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tokyo_Metro_Hanzomon_Line" title="Tokyo Metro Hanzomon Line">Tokyo Metro Hanzomon Line</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tokyo_Metro_Namboku_Line" title="Tokyo Metro Namboku Line">Tokyo Metro Namboku Line</a>, and it is possible to transfer between the two stations without passing through the ticket gates.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_layout">Station layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Akasaka-mitsuke_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Station layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The station has two <a href="/wiki/Island_platform" title="Island platform">island platforms</a> serving four tracks. The Ginza Line and Marunouchi Line share platforms at this station.</p>
