<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93June">January–June</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1533&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–June">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_25" title="January 25">January 25</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Henry_VIII_of_England" title="Henry VIII of England">Henry VIII of England</a> marries <a href="/wiki/Anne_Boleyn" title="Anne Boleyn">Anne Boleyn</a>, who becomes his second queen consort.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_26" title="January 26">January 26</a> – <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Audley,_1st_Baron_Audley_of_Walden" title="Thomas Audley, 1st Baron Audley of Walden">Thomas Audley, 1st Baron Audley of Walden</a>, is appointed <a href="/wiki/Lord_Chancellor" title="Lord Chancellor">Lord Chancellor</a> of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_30" title="March 30">March 30</a> – <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Cranmer" title="Thomas Cranmer">Thomas Cranmer</a> becomes <a href="/wiki/Archbishop_of_Canterbury" title="Archbishop of Canterbury">Archbishop of Canterbury</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_23" title="May 23">May 23</a> – King Henry VIII of England's marriage with <a href="/wiki/Catherine_of_Aragon" title="Catherine of Aragon">Catherine of Aragon</a> is declared annulled by Archbishop Cranmer. Since <a href="/wiki/Pope_Clement_VII" title="Pope Clement VII">Pope Clement VII</a> had rejected Henry's petition for <a href="/wiki/Annulment" title="Annulment">annulment</a> in <a href="/wiki/1530" title="1530">1530</a>, Catherine continues to believe herself Henry's wife until her death.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_1" title="June 1">June 1</a> – Cranmer crowns Anne Boleyn as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Queen_consort_of_England" title="Queen consort of England">queen consort of England</a> in <a href="/wiki/Westminster_Abbey" title="Westminster Abbey">Westminster Abbey</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="July.E2.80.93December">July–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1533&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: July–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_11" title="July 11">July 11</a> – Henry VIII is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Excommunicated" title="Excommunicated">excommunicated</a> by <a href="/wiki/Pope_Clement_VII" title="Pope Clement VII">Pope Clement VII</a>, as is Archbishop Cranmer.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_22" title="July 22">July 22</a> – <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Constantinople_(1533)" title="Treaty of Constantinople (1533)">Treaty of Constantinople</a> between the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Archduchy_of_Austria" title="Archduchy of Austria">Archduchy of Austria</a>: <a href="/wiki/Ferdinand_I,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Ferdinand I, Holy Roman Emperor">Ferdinand I, King of the Romans</a>, withdraws his claims to most of <a href="/wiki/Hungary" title="Hungary">Hungary</a> and <a href="/wiki/John_Z%C3%A1polya" title="John Zápolya">János Szapolyai</a>, <a href="/wiki/Voivode" title="Voivode">voivode</a> of <a href="/wiki/Transylvania" title="Transylvania">Transylvania</a>, becomes <a href="/wiki/King_of_Hungary" title="King of Hungary">King of Hungary</a> under the <a href="/wiki/Suzerainty" title="Suzerainty">suzerainty</a> of <a href="/wiki/Suleiman_the_Magnificent" title="Suleiman the Magnificent">Suleiman the Magnificent</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sultan" title="Sultan">Sultan</a> of the Ottoman Empire.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_26" title="July 26">July 26</a> – <a href="/wiki/Sapa_Inca" title="Sapa Inca">Sapa Inca</a> <a href="/wiki/Atahualpa" title="Atahualpa">Atahualpa</a> is executed by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Garotte" title="Garotte">garotte</a> at the orders of <a href="/wiki/Francisco_Pizarro" title="Francisco Pizarro">Francisco Pizarro</a> in <a href="/wiki/Cajamarca" title="Cajamarca">Cajamarca</a>. The Spanish arrange for his younger brother <a href="/wiki/T%C3%BApac_Huallpa" title="Túpac Huallpa">Túpac Huallpa</a> to be crowned as a successor, but he dies of <a href="/wiki/Smallpox" title="Smallpox">smallpox</a> soon afterwards</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_7" title="September 7">September 7</a> – <a href="/wiki/Anne_Boleyn" title="Anne Boleyn">Anne Boleyn</a> gives birth to Princess Elizabeth, the future <a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_I_of_England" title="Elizabeth I of England">Elizabeth I of England</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_15" title="November 15">November 15</a> – <a href="/wiki/Francisco_Pizarro" title="Francisco Pizarro">Francisco Pizarro</a> arrives in <a href="/wiki/Cusco" title="Cusco">Cusco</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peru" title="Peru">Peru</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/December_3" title="December 3">December 3</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ivan_the_Terrible" title="Ivan the Terrible">Ivan IV</a> succeeds his father <a href="/wiki/Vasili_III_of_Russia" title="Vasili III of Russia">Vasili III</a> as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grand_Prince_of_Moscow" title="Grand Prince of Moscow">Grand Prince of Muscovy</a> at the age of three years.</li>
<li>December – <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Hernando_de_Grijalva&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Hernando de Grijalva (page does not exist)">Hernando de Grijalva</a> and his crew discover the uninhabited <a href="/wiki/Revillagigedo_Islands" title="Revillagigedo Islands">Revillagigedo Islands</a> off the <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Ocean" title="Pacific Ocean">Pacific</a> coast of <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">Mexico</a>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/December_21" title="December 21">December 21</a> (Feast of St <a href="/wiki/Thomas_the_Apostle" title="Thomas the Apostle">Thomas the Apostle</a>) – They discover <i>Isla Santo Tomé</i>, probably <a href="/wiki/Socorro_Island" title="Socorro Island">Socorro Island</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/December_28" title="December 28">December 28</a> – They discover <i>Isla de los Inocentes</i>, probably <a href="/wiki/San_Benedicto_Island" title="San Benedicto Island">San Benedicto Island</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Date_unknown">Date unknown</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1533&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Date unknown">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Statute_in_Restraint_of_Appeals" title="Statute in Restraint of Appeals">Statute in Restraint of Appeals</a> declares the king to be the supreme sovereign in <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a> and forbids judicial appeals to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Papacy" title="Papacy">papacy</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Paracelsus" title="Paracelsus">Paracelsus</a> interprets the <a href="/wiki/Bible" title="Bible">Bible</a> in <a href="/wiki/Appenzell" title="Appenzell">Appenzell</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Pechenga_Monastery" title="Pechenga Monastery">Pechenga Monastery</a> is founded in the far north of the <a href="/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Moscow" title="Grand Duchy of Moscow">Grand Duchy of Moscow</a>.</li>
<li>1533–<a href="/wiki/1534" title="1534">1534</a> – Sultan <a href="/wiki/Suleiman_the_Magnificent" title="Suleiman the Magnificent">Suleiman the Magnificent</a> makes the <a href="/wiki/Ruthenians" title="Ruthenians">Ruthenian</a> <a href="/wiki/Harem" title="Harem">harem</a> girl <a href="/wiki/Roxelana" title="Roxelana">Roxelana</a> his legal wife.</li>
</ul>
