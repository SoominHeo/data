<p>The <b>1993 DFB-Supercup</b>, known as the <b>Panasonic DFB-Supercup</b> for sponsorship purposes, was the 7th <a href="/wiki/DFL-Supercup" title="DFL-Supercup">DFB-Supercup</a>, an annual <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> match contested by the winners of the previous season's <a href="/wiki/Bundesliga" title="Bundesliga">Bundesliga</a> and <a href="/wiki/DFB-Pokal" title="DFB-Pokal">DFB-Pokal</a> competitions.</p>
<p>The match was played at the <a href="/wiki/BayArena" title="BayArena">Ulrich-Haberland-Stadion</a> in <a href="/wiki/Leverkusen" title="Leverkusen">Leverkusen</a>, and contested by <a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%9393_Bundesliga" title="1992–93 Bundesliga">league</a> champions <a href="/wiki/SV_Werder_Bremen" title="SV Werder Bremen">Werder Bremen</a> and <a href="/wiki/1992%E2%80%9393_DFB-Pokal" title="1992–93 DFB-Pokal">cup</a> winners <a href="/wiki/Bayer_04_Leverkusen" title="Bayer 04 Leverkusen">Bayer Leverkusen</a>. Bremen won the match 7–6 on penalties, after a 2–2 draw, claiming their second title.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
