<p><b>Kazue Akita</b> (穐田和恵; born February 23, 1985 in <a href="/wiki/Osaka" title="Osaka">Osaka</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>) is a Japanese singer. She graduated from <a href="/wiki/SDN48" title="SDN48">SDN48</a> as a 1st generation member.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Trivia">Trivia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Akita_Kazue&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Trivia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>Ranked 11th in the fan election for SDN48's first single's senbatsu.</li>
<li>Had a solo while in SDN called "Haruiro no Tsubasa" that she performs till today.</li>
<li>Was one of the 8 members that are on the group's front line (Kami8).</li>
<li>Graduated on March 31, 2012.</li>
</ul>
