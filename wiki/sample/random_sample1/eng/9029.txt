<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Athens Lawn Tennis Club</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <b>Athens Lawn Tennis Club</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Tennis" title="Tennis">tennis</a> club founded in <a href="/wiki/Athens" title="Athens">Athens</a>, Greece, in 1895. The club hosted the <a href="/wiki/Tennis_at_the_1896_Summer_Olympics" title="Tennis at the 1896 Summer Olympics">tennis</a> events for the <a href="/wiki/1896_Summer_Olympics" title="1896 Summer Olympics">1896 Summer Olympics</a>.</p>
<p>The club now has courts for both <a href="/wiki/Squash_(sport)" title="Squash (sport)">squash</a> and tennis.</p>
