<p><b>Daigaku Honyaku Center</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">大学翻訳センター</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Daigaku Honyaku Senta</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> (corporate name:<b>DHC Corporation</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">株式会社DHC</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i><a href="/wiki/Kabushiki_gaisha" title="Kabushiki gaisha">Kabushiki gaisha</a> DHC</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> manufacturer dealing in <a href="/wiki/Cosmetics" title="Cosmetics">cosmetics</a> and <a href="/wiki/Health_food" title="Health food">health food</a> <a href="/wiki/Dietary_supplement" title="Dietary supplement">supplements</a> headquartered in <a href="/wiki/Tokyo" title="Tokyo">Tokyo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>. It was established in 1972. Their flagship brand name is "Olive Virgin Oil".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Originally started in Japan as a translation center, DHC Corporation ventured into other enterprises and is now a leading <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Manufacturer" title="Manufacturer">manufacturer</a> of cosmetics, vitamins, healthy foods, and lingerie. In addition to these ventures, DHC Corporation has an educational and publishing department, hotel and spa, and two <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Aesthetic" title="Aesthetic">aesthetic</a> salons. It is also a minor shareholder in the music chart operator <a href="/wiki/Oricon" title="Oricon">Oricon Inc.</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Parent_company" title="Parent company">parent company</a> to <a href="/wiki/Japan_FM_League" title="Japan FM League">JFL</a> affiliate station <a href="/wiki/Cross_FM" title="Cross FM">Cross FM</a>.</p>
