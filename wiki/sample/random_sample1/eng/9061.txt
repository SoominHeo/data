<p><b>Irvingiaceae</b> is a family of <a href="/wiki/Flowering_plant" title="Flowering plant">flowering plants</a>, consisting of 13 species in the 3 genera<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Christenhusz-Byng2016_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Christenhusz-Byng2016-2">[2]</a></sup> <i><a href="/wiki/Allantospermum" title="Allantospermum">Allantospermum</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Irvingia" title="Irvingia">Irvingia</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Klainedoxa" title="Klainedoxa">Klainedoxa</a></i>. <i><a href="/wiki/Desbordesia" title="Desbordesia">Desbordesia</a></i>, formerly accepted is now included in <i>Irvingia</i>.</p>
<p>The family is named for the Scottish <a href="/wiki/Ship%27s_doctor" title="Ship's doctor">naval surgeon</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Edward_George_Irving" title="Edward George Irving">Edward George Irving</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
