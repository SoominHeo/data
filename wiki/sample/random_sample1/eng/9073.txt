<p><b>Ahmed Hegazy</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar" xml:lang="ar">أحمد حجازي</span>‎‎) (born January 25, 1991) is an <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">association football</a> player who currently plays for <a href="/wiki/Al_Ahly_SC" title="Al Ahly SC">Al Ahly</a>. He plays as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">Central Defender</a>. He has been capped to play for <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>'s under-20 national team, under-23 national team, and recently, <a href="/wiki/Egypt_national_football_team" title="Egypt national football team">the first team</a> as well<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club">Club</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ahmed_Hegazy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Ismaily">Ismaily</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ahmed_Hegazy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Ismaily">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>His professional playing career began in November 24, 2009, when he made his debut with Egyptian club, <a href="/wiki/Ismaily_SC" title="Ismaily SC">Ismaily</a> playing as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">Full Back</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Fiorentina">Fiorentina</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ahmed_Hegazy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Fiorentina">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 22 December 2011, the Egyptian club announced that he has made a deal with <a href="/wiki/ACF_Fiorentina" title="ACF Fiorentina">Fiorentina</a>, an Italian football club, in exchange for 1.5 million euros. The Egyptian company will receive 15% sell on fee.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> He was given the number 3 shirt. Hegazy made his unofficial debut for Fiorentina this week, playing 30 minutes in a friendly against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Viareggio_Calcio" title="Viareggio Calcio">Viareggio Calcio</a>, He played 15 min in his Official debut against <a href="/wiki/Atalanta_B.C." title="Atalanta B.C.">Atalanta B.C.</a> on 18 Nov 2012, and scored his first goal for La Viola later that month in the second half of a <a href="/wiki/Coppa_Italia" title="Coppa Italia">Coppa Italia</a> match against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Juve_Stabia" title="Juve Stabia">Juve Stabia</a> on November 28. On 12 December 2012 Fiorentina announced that Hegazy had completed a surgery after sustaining a severe leg injury in training. The club also announced that Ahmed Hegazy had undergone reconstructive surgery on his anterior cruciate ligament. The expected recovery period before the player could return to sports activity is 5–6 months.<br/>
On 8 December 2014, Fiorentina announced that the player will be released in January 2015, along with <a href="/wiki/Oct%C3%A1vio" title="Octávio">Octávio</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mounir_El_Hamdaoui" title="Mounir El Hamdaoui">Mounir El Hamdaoui</a>, <a href="/wiki/Oleksandr_Pavlovych_Yakovenko" title="Oleksandr Pavlovych Yakovenko">Oleksandr Iakovenko</a> and <a href="/wiki/Joshua_Brillante" title="Joshua Brillante">Joshua Brillante</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Perugia">Perugia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ahmed_Hegazy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Perugia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 2 February 2015, Hegazy joined <a href="/wiki/Serie_B" title="Serie B">Serie B</a> side <a href="/wiki/A.C._Perugia_Calcio" title="A.C. Perugia Calcio">Perugia</a> on loan for the rest of <a href="/wiki/2014%E2%80%9315_Serie_B" title="2014–15 Serie B">2014–15 Serie B</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> He made his debut for the club in a 3-1 loss to <a href="/wiki/Vicenza_Calcio" title="Vicenza Calcio">Vicenza</a>.He played the whole 90 minutes, and that was his first complete match in Italy.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Al_Ahly">Al Ahly</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ahmed_Hegazy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Al Ahly">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 1 September 2015, Hegazy joined <a href="/wiki/Egyptian_Premier_League" title="Egyptian Premier League">Egyptian Premier League</a> side <a href="/wiki/Al_Ahly_SC" title="Al Ahly SC">Al Ahly</a> for 5 years.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Style_of_play">Style of play</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ahmed_Hegazy&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Style of play">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ahmed Hegazy is widely known as the 'Nesta of the Pyramids'. He was given this nickname as his playing style somewhat resembles the playing style of his Italian counterpart, <a href="/wiki/Alessandro_Nesta" title="Alessandro Nesta">Alessandro Nesta</a>.</p>
