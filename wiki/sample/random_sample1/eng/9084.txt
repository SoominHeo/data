<p><b>Anna Dello Russo</b> is an Italian fashion journalist. She is a <a href="/wiki/Creative_consultant" title="Creative consultant">creative consultant</a> and <a href="/wiki/Editor-at-large" title="Editor-at-large">editor-at-large</a> for <i><a href="/wiki/Vogue_(magazine)#Other_editions" title="Vogue (magazine)">Vogue Japan</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nyt_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-nyt-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-david_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-david-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference nowrap">:141</sup></p>
<p>In 2010, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/YOOX" title="YOOX">YOOX</a> T-shirts printed with ten different images of Dello Russo sold out on the first day of sale.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-amy_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-amy-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2013 Dello Russo designed <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fashion_accessories" title="Fashion accessories">accessories</a> for the Swedish <a href="/wiki/H%26M" title="H&amp;M">H&amp;M</a> chain.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-david_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-david-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference nowrap">:141</sup></p>
