<p><b>Ahn Nae-sang</b> (born December 25, 1964) is a South Korean actor. He began his career on the stage,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-dandelion_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-dandelion-1">[1]</a></sup> and in 1994 made his film debut in the <a href="/wiki/Bong_Joon-ho" title="Bong Joon-ho">Bong Joon-ho</a> <a href="/wiki/Short_film" title="Short film">short film</a> <i>Baeksekin</i> ("White Man" or "<a href="/wiki/White-collar_worker" title="White-collar worker">White-collar worker</a>"), followed by <a href="/wiki/Jang_Sun-woo" title="Jang Sun-woo">Jang Sun-woo</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Bad_Movie" title="Bad Movie">Bad Movie</a></i> in 1997. He has since starred in numerous films, with supporting roles in <a href="/wiki/Lee_Chang-dong" title="Lee Chang-dong">Lee Chang-dong</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Oasis_(2002_film)" title="Oasis (2002 film)">Oasis</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Poetry_(film)" title="Poetry (film)">Poetry</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> and a leading role in <i>Hoichori</i> ("Cane").<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> He also appears in television series, notably <i><a href="/wiki/Conspiracy_in_the_Court" title="Conspiracy in the Court">Conspiracy in the Court</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> <i><a href="/wiki/First_Wives%27_Club" title="First Wives' Club">First Wives' Club</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Three_Brothers_(TV_series)" title="Three Brothers (TV series)">Three Brothers</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> and <i><a href="/wiki/High_Kick:_Revenge_of_the_Short_Legged" title="High Kick: Revenge of the Short Legged">High Kick: Revenge of the Short Legged</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
