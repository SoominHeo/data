<p><b>DJ Shine</b> (born June 11, 1974) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Korean_people" title="Korean people">Korean</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rapper" title="Rapper">Rapper</a>, <a href="/wiki/Record_producer" title="Record producer">Producer</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/DJ" title="DJ">DJ</a> formerly associated with popular <a href="/wiki/Koreans" title="Koreans">Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop" title="Hip hop">Hip hop</a> group, <a href="/wiki/Drunken_Tiger" title="Drunken Tiger">Drunken Tiger</a>.</p>
<p>Before he met <a href="/wiki/Tiger_JK" title="Tiger JK">Tiger JK</a> he performed as a DJ, in small clubs in <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a> and <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>. Shine met JK in a rap event, and they found similar interests and formed a Hip hop group in Los Angeles. After a successful <a href="/wiki/Concert" title="Concert">concert</a> the duo attracted a South Korean Music company, and got signed and debuted as <a href="/wiki/Drunken_Tiger" title="Drunken Tiger">Drunken Tiger</a>, with their debut album in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>.</p>
<p>Shine was a member of Drunken Tiger until mid-2005, when Shine left the group due to personal reasons. After Shine left Drunken Tiger he has focused more on mainstream and poppish than <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Old_School_Rap" title="Old School Rap">Old School</a> style. Shine also stated in an interview in 2006, that he will release a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Solo_album" title="Solo album">solo album</a> it has yet to be confirmed. Shine is also the <a href="/wiki/Executive_producer" title="Executive producer">executive producer</a> of music company, K &amp; Music.</p>
<p>On 2006, he produced and wrote a song for the <a href="/wiki/Soundtrack" title="Soundtrack">soundtrack</a> for the movie <i>Cinderella</i> titled, <i>Plastic World</i>. The lyrics are all written in English. Shine also made a Korean version of <a href="/wiki/Flo_Rida" title="Flo Rida">Flo Rida</a>'s hit single, <a href="/wiki/Right_Round" title="Right Round">Right Round</a>. Although it cannot be confirmed but Shine has given hints in releasing his solo album very soon.</p>
