<p><br/>
Year <b>1560</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MDLX</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Monday" title="Leap year starting on Monday">leap year starting on Monday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1560&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93June">January–June</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1560&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–June">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_27" title="February 27">February 27</a> – <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Berwick_(1560)" title="Treaty of Berwick (1560)">Treaty of Berwick</a>: Terms agreed with the <a href="/wiki/Lords_of_the_Congregation" title="Lords of the Congregation">Lords of the Congregation</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Scotland" title="Kingdom of Scotland">Kingdom of Scotland</a> for forces of the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">Kingdom of England</a> to enter Scotland to expel <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_France" title="Kingdom of France">French</a> troops defending the Regency of <a href="/wiki/Mary_of_Guise" title="Mary of Guise">Mary of Guise</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_7" title="March 7">March 7</a> – A Spanish-led expedition, commanded by <a href="/wiki/Juan_de_la_Cerda,_4th_Duke_of_Medinaceli" title="Juan de la Cerda, 4th Duke of Medinaceli">Juan de la Cerda, 4th Duke of Medinaceli</a>, overruns the Tunisian island of <a href="/wiki/Djerba" title="Djerba">Djerba</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_17" title="March 17">March 17</a> – Leaders of the <a href="/wiki/Amboise_conspiracy" title="Amboise conspiracy">Amboise conspiracy</a>, including Godefroy de Barry, seigneur de La Renaudie, make an unsuccessful attempt to storm the château of <a href="/wiki/Amboise" title="Amboise">Amboise</a>, where the young French king and queen are residing. La Renaudie is subsequently caught and executed, along with over a thousand of his followers.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_15" title="April 15">April 15</a> – <a href="/wiki/Denmark%E2%80%93Norway" title="Denmark–Norway">Denmark</a> buys the <a href="/wiki/Terra_Mariana" title="Terra Mariana">Estonian</a> island of <a href="/wiki/Saaremaa" title="Saaremaa">Saaremaa</a> from its last prince bishop.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_11" title="May 11">May 11</a> – At the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Djerba" title="Battle of Djerba">Battle of Djerba</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman</a> fleet, commanded by <a href="/wiki/Piali_Pasha" title="Piali Pasha">Piali Pasha</a>, overwhelms a large joint European (mainly Spanish) fleet, sinking about half its ships.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_12" title="June 12">June 12</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Okehazama" title="Battle of Okehazama">Battle of Okehazama</a>: <a href="/wiki/Oda_Nobunaga" title="Oda Nobunaga">Oda Nobunaga</a> defeats <a href="/wiki/Imagawa_Yoshimoto" title="Imagawa Yoshimoto">Imagawa Yoshimoto</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="July.E2.80.93December">July–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1560&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: July–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_6" title="July 6">July 6</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Edinburgh" title="Treaty of Edinburgh">Treaty of Edinburgh</a> is signed between England, France and Scotland. The French withdraw from Scotland. This largely ends the <i><a href="/wiki/Auld_Alliance" title="Auld Alliance">Auld Alliance</a></i> between France and Scotland, and ends the wars between England and its northern neighbour.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_2" title="August 2">August 2</a> – <a href="/wiki/Livonian_War" title="Livonian War">Livonian War</a>: <a href="/wiki/Tsardom_of_Russia" title="Tsardom of Russia">Russians</a> defeat the <a href="/wiki/Livonian_Brothers_of_the_Sword" title="Livonian Brothers of the Sword">Livonian Brothers of the Sword</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Ergeme" title="Battle of Ergeme">Battle of Ergeme</a>, precipitating the dissolution of the order.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_17" title="August 17">August 17</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Reformation_Parliament" title="Scottish Reformation Parliament">Scottish Reformation Parliament</a> adopts a <a href="/wiki/Protestantism" title="Protestantism">Protestant</a> confession of faith and <a href="/wiki/Papal_Jurisdiction_Act_1560" title="Papal Jurisdiction Act 1560">rejects papal authority</a>, beginning the <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Reformation" title="Scottish Reformation">Scottish Reformation</a> and disestablishing <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Roman_Catholicism_in_Scotland" title="Roman Catholicism in Scotland">Roman Catholicism in Scotland</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_21" title="August 21">August 21</a> – A <a href="/wiki/Solar_eclipse_of_August_21,_1560" title="Solar eclipse of August 21, 1560">total eclipse of the sun</a> is observable in <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_29" title="September 29">September 29</a> – <a href="/wiki/Eric_XIV_of_Sweden" title="Eric XIV of Sweden">Eric XIV</a> becomes <a href="/wiki/Monarchy_of_Sweden" title="Monarchy of Sweden">king</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Sweden</a> upon the death of his father <a href="/wiki/Gustav_I_of_Sweden" title="Gustav I of Sweden">Gustav Vasa</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/December_5" title="December 5">December 5</a> – Seventeen-year-old <a href="/wiki/Mary,_Queen_of_Scots" title="Mary, Queen of Scots">Mary, Queen of Scots</a>, is widowed by the death of her first husband, King <a href="/wiki/Francis_II_of_France" title="Francis II of France">Francis II of France</a>. Her mother-in-law, <a href="/wiki/Catherine_de%27_Medici" title="Catherine de' Medici">Catherine de' Medici</a>, becomes regent of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_France" title="Kingdom of France">France</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/December_6" title="December 6">December 6</a> – <a href="/wiki/Charles_IX_of_France" title="Charles IX of France">Charles IX of France</a> succeeds his elder brother, <a href="/wiki/Francis_II_of_France" title="Francis II of France">Francis II</a>, at the age of ten.</li>
</ul>
