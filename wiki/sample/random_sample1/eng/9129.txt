<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Ahn Sang-soo (born May 1946)</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><div class="hatnote" role="note">For the former Grand National Party chairman, see <a href="/wiki/Ahn_Sang-soo_(born_February_1946)" title="Ahn Sang-soo (born February 1946)">Ahn Sang-soo (born February 1946)</a>.</div>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">This is a <a href="/wiki/Korean_name" title="Korean name">Korean name</a>; the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Korean_family_names" title="List of Korean family names">family name</a> is <a href="/wiki/Ahn_(Korean_surname)" title="Ahn (Korean surname)"><i>Ahn</i></a>.</div>
<p><b>Ahn Sang-soo</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">안상수</span>; <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">Hanja</a>: <span lang="ko-Hani" xml:lang="ko-Hani">安相洙</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">An Sang-su</span></i>; born 28 May 1946) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Politician" title="Politician">politician</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Saenuri_Party" title="Saenuri Party">Saenuri Party</a> who served as two-time mayor of <a href="/wiki/Incheon" title="Incheon">Incheon</a> from 2002 to 2010. In the <a href="/wiki/South_Korean_local_elections,_2010" title="South Korean local elections, 2010">2010 local elections</a> he lost his position to <a href="/wiki/Song_Young-gil" title="Song Young-gil">Song Young-gil</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Democratic_Party_(South_Korea,_2008)" title="Democratic Party (South Korea, 2008)">Democratic Party</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> He was a candidate for the Saenuri nomination in the <a href="/wiki/South_Korean_presidential_election,_2012" title="South Korean presidential election, 2012">2012 presidential elections</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> As mayor, Ahn was responsible for initiating a range of expensive construction projects which have been implicated in causing financial difficulties.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
