<p><b>Ahn Sun-ju</b>, or <b>Sun-ju Ahn</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko" xml:lang="ko">안선주</span>, born 31 August 1987) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Professional_golfer" title="Professional golfer">professional golfer</a> currently playing on the <a href="/wiki/LPGA_of_Japan_Tour" title="LPGA of Japan Tour">LPGA of Japan Tour</a>. She led the LPGA of Japan Tour money list in 2010, 2011, and 2014.</p>
