<p><b>An Se-hyeon</b> is a South Korean swimmer.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lessports_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-lessports-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>She competed at the <a href="/wiki/2015_World_Aquatics_Championships" title="2015 World Aquatics Championships">2015 World Aquatics Championships</a> and at the <a href="/wiki/2016_Summer_Olympics" title="2016 Summer Olympics">2016 Summer Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Rio_de_Janeiro" title="Rio de Janeiro">Rio de Janeiro</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lessports_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-lessports-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
