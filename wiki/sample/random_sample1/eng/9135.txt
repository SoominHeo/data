<p><b>Ansim Station</b> is a station of <a href="/wiki/Daegu_Metro_Line_1" title="Daegu Metro Line 1">Daegu Metro Line 1</a> at <a href="/wiki/Dong_District,_Daegu" title="Dong District, Daegu">Dong-gu</a> in <a href="/wiki/Daegu" title="Daegu">Daegu</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. This is the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Terminal_train_station" title="Terminal train station">terminus</a> of Daegu Metro Line 1. The name of the station comes from the King <a href="/wiki/Taejo_of_Goryeo" title="Taejo of Goryeo">Taejo of Goryeo</a>, who was victorious in battles with <a href="/wiki/Later_Baekje" title="Later Baekje">Later Baekje</a>, and felt relieved when he went to the location "Ansim." The literal translation of "Ansim" is "relieved" and "peace of mind."</p>
