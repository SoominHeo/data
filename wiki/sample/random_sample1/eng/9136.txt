<p>The <b>Anyang Korea Ginseng Corporation</b>, or <b>Anyang KGC</b>, is a professional <a href="/wiki/Basketball" title="Basketball">basketball</a> club in the <a href="/wiki/Korean_Basketball_League" title="Korean Basketball League">Korean Basketball League</a>. They are based at Anyang Arena in the city of <a href="/wiki/Anyang" title="Anyang">Anyang</a> which is located 20 kilometers south of <a href="/wiki/Seoul" title="Seoul">Seoul</a>. Anyang KGC won the first KBL championship in the 2011–12 season.</p>
