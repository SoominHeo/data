<p><b>Anyang 1st Street</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">안양일번가</span>; <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">Hanja</a>: <span lang="ko-Hani" xml:lang="ko-Hani">安養一番街</span>) is the commercial hub in <a href="/wiki/Manan-gu" title="Manan-gu">Manan-gu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Anyang,_Gyeonggi" title="Anyang, Gyeonggi">Anyang</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. Anyang 1st Street is also the most crowded area in Anyang. Anyang 1st Street has underground shopping malls, a department store, many shopping stores, bars, restaurants, movie theaters, banks, bookstores, clinics, etc.</p>
