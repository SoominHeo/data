<p><b>Ahn Jae-hwan</b> (April 25, 1972 (some sources said that he was born on June 8, 1972 or 1974) – officially September 8, 2008) was a <a href="/wiki/South_Korean" title="South Korean">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Actor" title="Actor">actor</a>.</p>
<p>His <a href="/wiki/Wife" title="Wife">wife</a> was <a href="/wiki/Comedian" title="Comedian">comedian</a> Jung Sun-hee.</p>
<p>Ahn was found dead in his car on September 8, but the exact time of his death has not been revealed; police confirmed that he had died several days prior as his body was already decaying.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It is considered to be a case of <a href="/wiki/Suicide" title="Suicide">suicide</a> by <a href="/wiki/Carbon_monoxide_poisoning" title="Carbon monoxide poisoning">carbon monoxide poisoning</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> He was 36 years old.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
