<p><b>An Han-bong</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: 안한봉, <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">Hanja</a>: 安漢奉; born October 15, 1968 in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Haenam" title="Haenam">Haenam</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jeollanam-do" title="Jeollanam-do">Jeollanam-do</a>) is a retired <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Greco-Roman_wrestling" title="Greco-Roman wrestling">Greco-Roman wrestler</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>He won the gold medal at the <a href="/wiki/1992_Summer_Olympics" title="1992 Summer Olympics">1992 Summer Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Barcelona" title="Barcelona">Barcelona</a>.</p>
