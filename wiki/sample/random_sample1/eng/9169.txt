<p><b>Algar</b> is a city in the province of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/C%C3%A1diz_(province)" title="Cádiz (province)">Cádiz</a>, Spain. According to the 2005 <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a>, the city has 1,644 inhabitants. Algar is in the <a href="/wiki/White_Towns_of_Andalusia" title="White Towns of Andalusia">White Towns of Andalusia</a> to an altitude of 212 meters.</p>
