<p><b>Al Dhafra SCC</b> is a United Arab Emirati <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> club from <a href="/wiki/Madinat_Zayed" title="Madinat Zayed">Madinat Zayed</a>. As of the 2013/14 season they play in the <a href="/wiki/UAE_Arabian_Gulf_League" title="UAE Arabian Gulf League">UAE Arabian Gulf League</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
