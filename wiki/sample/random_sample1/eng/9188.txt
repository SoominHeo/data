<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allan_(footballer,_born_1997)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Internacional">Internacional</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allan_(footballer,_born_1997)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Internacional">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Porto_Alegre" title="Porto Alegre">Porto Alegre</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rio_Grande_do_Sul" title="Rio Grande do Sul">Rio Grande do Sul</a>, Allan rose slowly through the ranks as a youth product of his hometown club <a href="/wiki/Sport_Club_Internacional" title="Sport Club Internacional">Internacional</a>. Goal Brazil’s deputy editor Rodrigo Calvozzo claimed Allan "was always treated by Internacional as one of the most important players in the team."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TIA_Bio_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-TIA_Bio-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Liverpool">Liverpool</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allan_(footballer,_born_1997)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Liverpool">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In the summer of 2015, he joined English <a href="/wiki/Premier_League" title="Premier League">Premier League</a> club <a href="/wiki/Liverpool_F.C." title="Liverpool F.C.">Liverpool</a> for a fee of £500,000 after impressing the club's coaches at the Frenz International Cup.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="SJK_.28loan.29">SJK (loan)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allan_(footballer,_born_1997)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: SJK (loan)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>On 2 September 2015, he was loaned to Finnish club <a href="/wiki/Sein%C3%A4joen_Jalkapallokerho" title="Seinäjoen Jalkapallokerho">Seinäjoen Jalkapallokerho</a> on a short term deal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Allan scored on his debut eight days later, coming on in the 56th minute for <a href="/wiki/Jussi_Vasara" title="Jussi Vasara">Jussi Vasara</a> and opening the scoring sixteen minutes later in a 1–1 draw away to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/KuPS" title="KuPS">KuPS</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> He finished the season with two goals in eight appearances, in the process helping the club win the <a href="/wiki/Veikkausliiga" title="Veikkausliiga">Veikkausliiga</a> title.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-title_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-title-6">[6]</a></sup> On 29 October 2015, Allan returned from his loan spell to Liverpool, greeting the new manager of the club, <a href="/wiki/J%C3%BCrgen_Klopp" title="Jürgen Klopp">Jürgen Klopp</a>, for the first time.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Sint-Truiden_.28loan.29">Sint-Truiden (loan)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allan_(footballer,_born_1997)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Sint-Truiden (loan)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>On 18 January 2016, Liverpool announced that Allan had been loaned to Belgian club <a href="/wiki/Sint-Truidense_V.V." title="Sint-Truidense V.V.">Sint-Truiden</a> for the remainder of the season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-fourfourtwo_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-fourfourtwo-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Return_to_Liverpool">Return to Liverpool</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allan_(footballer,_born_1997)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Return to Liverpool">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>At the beginning of the 2016/17 season, Allan returned to Liverpool in expectation of receiving a work permit to play in the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>, however he was not granted a permit, meaning he must have another season out on loan in order to attain one.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Loan_to_Hertha_BSC">Loan to Hertha BSC</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allan_(footballer,_born_1997)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Loan to Hertha BSC">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Allan signed for <a href="/wiki/Hertha_BSC" title="Hertha BSC">Hertha BSC</a> on the 5th August 2016 on a season-long loan deal. Hertha head coach <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pal_Dardai" title="Pal Dardai">Pal Dardai</a> signed the Brazilian youngster after a recommendation from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jurgen_Klopp" title="Jurgen Klopp">Jurgen Klopp</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Style_of_play">Style of play</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allan_(footballer,_born_1997)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Style of play">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A central midfielder by trade, Allan is primarily left-footed and employs a strong passing range, honed with time spent as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Deep-lying_playmaker" title="Deep-lying playmaker">deep-lying playmaker</a> in Brazil. Allan has proven himself a useful <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Box-to-box_midfielder" title="Box-to-box midfielder">box-to-box midfielder</a> with promising attacking instincts.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TIA_Bio_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-TIA_Bio-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
