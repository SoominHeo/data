<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alexander_B%C3%BCttner&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Vitesse">Vitesse</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alexander_B%C3%BCttner&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Vitesse">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Doetinchem" title="Doetinchem">Doetinchem</a>, Büttner began his career with local club VV Doetinchem, but spent his formative years with <a href="/wiki/Jong_Ajax" title="Jong Ajax">Ajax</a> before joining <a href="/wiki/SBV_Vitesse" title="SBV Vitesse">Vitesse</a> at the age of 16 in 2005. He made his professional debut for Vitesse in 2008 and scored his first goal for the club in a 3–1 home win over <a href="/wiki/FC_Volendam" title="FC Volendam">FC Volendam</a>.</p>
<p>On 6 July 2012, Vitesse agreed a fee with <a href="/wiki/Southampton_F.C." title="Southampton F.C.">Southampton</a> over the transfer of Büttner to the English club, subject to a medical.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> The proposed move to Southampton was subsequently halted "due to an unspecified 'demand' from an unnamed third party"; this was believed to be a claim for a percentage of the transfer fee.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Manchester_United">Manchester United</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alexander_B%C3%BCttner&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Manchester United">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 21 August 2012, Büttner signed a five-year contract with <a href="/wiki/Manchester_United_F.C." title="Manchester United F.C.">Manchester United</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> where he was given the number 28 shirt.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> Büttner made his league debut on 15 September, setting up the second goal and scoring the third, in a 4–0 home win against <a href="/wiki/Wigan_Athletic_F.C." title="Wigan Athletic F.C.">Wigan Athletic</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> His performance gained him the <a href="/wiki/Man_of_the_match" title="Man of the match">man of the match</a> award.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> On 23 October, he made his UEFA club competition debut in a 3–2 home victory over <a href="/wiki/S.C._Braga" title="S.C. Braga">Braga</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2012%E2%80%9313_UEFA_Champions_League_group_stage" title="2012–13 UEFA Champions League group stage">group stage</a> of the <a href="/wiki/2012%E2%80%9313_UEFA_Champions_League" title="2012–13 UEFA Champions League">2012–13 UEFA Champions League</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> On 5 January, Büttner made his <a href="/wiki/FA_Cup" title="FA Cup">FA Cup</a> debut in a 2–2 draw against <a href="/wiki/West_Ham_United_F.C." title="West Ham United F.C.">West Ham United</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> United went on to win the <a href="/wiki/2012%E2%80%9313_Premier_League" title="2012–13 Premier League">Premier League</a> and Büttner received a winner's medal by a special dispensation given by the manager.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> He scored his second goal for Manchester United on the final day of the season in a dramatic 5–5 draw against <a href="/wiki/West_Bromwich_Albion_F.C." title="West Bromwich Albion F.C.">West Bromwich Albion</a>. After collecting a pass from <a href="/wiki/Tom_Cleverley" title="Tom Cleverley">Tom Cleverley</a> in the inside left channel, he drove into the penalty area and fired a low shot into the far corner.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Dynamo_Moscow">Dynamo Moscow</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alexander_B%C3%BCttner&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Dynamo Moscow">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 24 June 2014, Büttner announced that he was leaving Manchester United for <a href="/wiki/FC_Dynamo_Moscow" title="FC Dynamo Moscow">Dynamo Moscow</a>, on a three-year deal for £4.4 million potentially rising to £5.6 million. He cited a lack of playing time and a personal rift between himself and future United coach <a href="/wiki/Louis_van_Gaal" title="Louis van Gaal">Louis van Gaal</a> as the reason of his departure.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup></p>
<p>He scored his first goal for Dynamo Moscow in a 2-2 draw against Cypriot club <a href="/wiki/AC_Omonia" title="AC Omonia">Omonia</a> in the Europa League.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup></p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Anderlecht_.28loan.29">Anderlecht (loan)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alexander_B%C3%BCttner&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Anderlecht (loan)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>On 1 February 2016, he agreed to join <a href="/wiki/R.S.C._Anderlecht" title="R.S.C. Anderlecht">Anderlecht</a> on loan until the end of 2015/16 season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup> On 5 February 2016, Büttner made his Anderlecht debut in a 2–2 draw with <a href="/wiki/KV_Mechelen" title="KV Mechelen">KV Mechelen</a>, featuring for the entire 90 minutes.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup> A week later, Büttner went onto score his first goal for Anderlecht, netting the second in the Belgian side's 3–0 victory over <a href="/wiki/S.V._Zulte_Waregem" title="S.V. Zulte Waregem">Zulte Waregem</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Return_to_Vitesse">Return to Vitesse</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alexander_B%C3%BCttner&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Return to Vitesse">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 16 January 2017, he returned to <a href="/wiki/SBV_Vitesse" title="SBV Vitesse">Vitesse</a>, signing a 2.5-year contract with the club.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-20"><a href="#cite_note-20">[20]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alexander_B%C3%BCttner&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Büttner has represented the Netherlands at under-20 and <a href="/wiki/Netherlands_national_under-21_football_team" title="Netherlands national under-21 football team">under-21</a> levels.</p>
<p>On 7 May 2012, Büttner was named in the provisional list of 36 players for the <a href="/wiki/UEFA_Euro_2012" title="UEFA Euro 2012">UEFA Euro 2012</a> tournament, one of nine uncapped players to be chosen by <a href="/wiki/Netherlands_national_football_team" title="Netherlands national football team">Netherlands</a> manager <a href="/wiki/Bert_van_Marwijk" title="Bert van Marwijk">Bert van Marwijk</a> as part of the preliminary squad.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
