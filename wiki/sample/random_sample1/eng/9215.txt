<p><b>Alexandrovsk</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Алекса́ндровск</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">town</a> in <a href="/wiki/Perm_Krai" title="Perm Krai">Perm Krai</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>, located on the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Lytva_River&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Lytva River (page does not exist)">Lytva River</a> (<a href="/wiki/Kama_River" title="Kama River">Kama</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Tributary" title="Tributary">tributary</a>), 185 kilometers (115 mi) northeast of <a href="/wiki/Perm" title="Perm">Perm</a>, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_center" title="Administrative center">administrative center</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Krai" title="Krai">krai</a>. Population: <span class="nowrap">14,495 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2010)" title="Russian Census (2010)">2010 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2010Census_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-2010Census-3">[3]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">16,231 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2002)" title="Russian Census (2002)">2002 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PopCensus_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-PopCensus-7">[7]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">20,073 (<a href="/wiki/Soviet_Census_(1989)" title="Soviet Census (1989)">1989 Census</a>)</span>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census1989_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-Census1989-8">[8]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Administrative_and_municipal_status">Administrative and municipal status</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alexandrovsk,_Perm_Krai&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Administrative and municipal status">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Within the <a href="/wiki/Subdivisions_of_Russia#Administrative_divisions" title="Subdivisions of Russia">framework of administrative divisions</a>, it is, together with two <a href="/wiki/Urban-type_settlement" title="Urban-type settlement">work settlements</a> (<a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Vsevolodo-Vilva&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Vsevolodo-Vilva (page does not exist)">Vsevolodo-Vilva</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Yayva&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Yayva (page does not exist)">Yayva</a>) and thirty-five <a href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">rural localities</a>, incorporated as the <b><a href="/wiki/City_of_federal_subject_significance" title="City of federal subject significance">town of krai significance</a> of Alexandrovsk</b>—an administrative unit with the status equal to that of the <a href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_Perm_Krai" title="Administrative divisions of Perm Krai">districts</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ref127_1-4"><a href="#cite_note-Ref127-1">[1]</a></sup> As a <a href="/wiki/Subdivisions_of_Russia#Municipal_divisions" title="Subdivisions of Russia">municipal division</a>, the town of Alexandrovsk, together with six rural localities, is incorporated as <a href="/wiki/Alexandrovskoye_Urban_Settlement" title="Alexandrovskoye Urban Settlement">Alexandrovskoye Urban Settlement</a> within Alexandrovsky Municipal District and serves as the municipal district's <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_center" title="Administrative center">administrative center</a>, while the two work settlements and twenty-nine rural localities are grouped into two urban settlements and one rural settlement within Alexandrovsky Municipal District.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ref315_2-4"><a href="#cite_note-Ref315-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

Опубликован: "Бюллетень Законодательного Собрания и администрации Пермской области", №1, II часть, 27 января 2005 г.</span> (Legislative Assembly of Perm Oblast. Law #1888-412 of December 1, 2004 <i>On Establishing the Borders and on Granting the Status to the Municipal Formations of the Administrative Territory of the Town of Alexandrovsk of Perm Krai</i>, as amended by the Law #175-PK of March 7, 2013 <i>On Amending Various Laws of Perm Oblast, Komi-Permyak Autonomous Okrug on Establishing the Borders and on Granting the Status to the Municipal Formations of Perm Krai</i>. Effective as of after ten days following the day of the official publication.).</li>
</ul>
