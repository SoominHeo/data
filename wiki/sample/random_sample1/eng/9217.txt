<p><b>Aleksandr Nikiforovich Voronin</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Александр Никифорович Воронин</span>, May 23, 1951 – September 26, 1992) was a <a href="/wiki/Russian_Soviet_Federative_Socialist_Republic" title="Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic">Russian</a> <a href="/wiki/Powerlifting" title="Powerlifting">weightlifter</a> and Olympic champion who competed for the <a href="/wiki/Soviet_Union" title="Soviet Union">Soviet Union</a>.</p>
<p>He was born in <a href="/wiki/Chelyabinsk" title="Chelyabinsk">Chelyabinsk</a>.</p>
<p>Voronin won a gold medal at the <a href="/wiki/1976_Summer_Olympics" title="1976 Summer Olympics">1976 Summer Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Montreal" title="Montreal">Montreal</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-db-ol1976-weightlifting_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-db-ol1976-weightlifting-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
