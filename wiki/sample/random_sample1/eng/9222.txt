<p><b>Alexandria University</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar" xml:lang="ar">جامعة الإسكندرية</span>‎‎) is a public research university in <a href="/wiki/Alexandria" title="Alexandria">Alexandria</a>, <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>. It was established in 1938 as a satellite of <a href="/wiki/Cairo_University" title="Cairo University">Fuad University</a> (the name of which was later changed to Cairo University), becoming an independent entity in 1942. It was known as Farouk University until the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Egyptian_Revolution_of_1952" title="Egyptian Revolution of 1952">Egyptian Revolution of 1952</a> when its name was changed to the University of Alexandria. <a href="/wiki/Taha_Hussein" title="Taha Hussein">Taha Hussein</a> was the founding <a href="/wiki/Rector_(academia)" title="Rector (academia)">rector</a> of Alexandria University. It is now the second largest university in Egypt and has many affiliations to various universities for ongoing <a href="/wiki/Research" title="Research">research</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Faculties">Faculties</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alexandria_University&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Faculties">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<p>At the time it became an independent institution in 1942, it had the following <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Faculty_(university)" title="Faculty (university)">faculties</a>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Agriculture" title="Agriculture">Agriculture</a></li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Alexandria_Faculty_of_Medicine&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Alexandria Faculty of Medicine (page does not exist)">Faculty of Medicine</a> (1942)</li>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/The_arts" title="The arts">Arts</a> (1938)</li>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Commerce" title="Commerce">Commerce</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Faculty_of_Engineering,_Alexandria_University" title="Faculty of Engineering, Alexandria University">Faculty of Engineering</a> (1941)</li>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Law" title="Law">Law</a> (1938)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Faculty_of_Science,_Alexandria_University" title="Faculty of Science, Alexandria University">Faculty of Science</a> (1942)</li>
</ul>
<p>A number of institutional entities were established as follows:</p>
<ul>
<li>Faculty of Sports Education (1955)</li>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Nursing" title="Nursing">Nursing</a> (formerly the High Institute of Nursing) (1954)</li>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Pharmacy" title="Pharmacy">Pharmacy</a> (1956)</li>
<li>High Institute of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Public_Health" title="Public Health">Public Health</a> (1963)</li>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Education" title="Education">Education</a> (1969)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Alexandria_Faculty_of_Dentistry" title="Alexandria Faculty of Dentistry">Faculty of Dentistry</a>: founded in 1971 and serves the city of Alexandria. It is located in El Azareta near the famous <a href="/wiki/Bibliotheca_Alexandrina" title="Bibliotheca Alexandrina">Alexandria Library</a>.</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Medical_Research_Institute&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Medical Research Institute (page does not exist)">Medical Research Institute</a> (1971)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Faculty_of_Veterinary_Medicine_of_Alexandria_University&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Faculty of Veterinary Medicine of Alexandria University (page does not exist)">Faculty of Veterinary Medicine</a> (1974)</li>
<li>Institute of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Graduate_Studies" title="Graduate Studies">Graduate Studies</a> &amp; Research (1983)</li>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Tourism" title="Tourism">Tourism</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hotel" title="Hotel">Hotels</a> (1983)</li>
<li>Faculty of Finearts</li>
<li>Faculty of Kindergarten</li>
</ul>
<p>In 1989, four faculties, located in Alexandria and administered by <a href="/wiki/Helwan_University" title="Helwan University">Helwan University</a>, were annexed to Alexandria University. These are the faculties of <a href="/wiki/Agriculture" title="Agriculture">Agriculture</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fine_Arts" title="Fine Arts">Fine Arts</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Physical_Education" title="Physical Education">Physical Education</a> for Boys and Physical Education for Girls.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alexandria_University&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Prof. Dr. Osama Ibrahim Al-Sayed Ahmad Al Zengery was elected President of Alexandria University in December 2011. This was the first time in Egypt to have presidential elections.</p>
<p>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Damanhour" title="Damanhour">Damanhour</a> branch was separated from the university by <a href="/wiki/President_of_Egypt" title="President of Egypt">Presidential</a> decree in 2010, and is now the independent <a href="/wiki/Damanhour_University" title="Damanhour University">Damanhour University</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Ranking">Ranking</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alexandria_University&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Ranking">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Alexandria University was ranked 147th worldwide based on Times Higher Education's World University Rankings 2010–2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> The 2010 rankings were controversial, as a single <a href="/wiki/Mohamed_El_Naschie" title="Mohamed El Naschie">professor</a>'s practice of publishing a great number of articles in a journal of which he himself was the editor was identified as a crucial contributing factor for the high rating of Alexandra University.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NYTimes1_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-NYTimes1-6">[6]</a></sup> Alexandria University is ranked 301+ worldwide based on Times Higher Education's World University Rankings 2011–2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> Alexandria University is ranked 601+ worldwide based on QS World University Rankings 2011/12.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> It was ranked 701+ in the QS World University Rankings 2014/2015.</p>
<p>According to Webometrics' ranking of world universities, it is 1223rd worldwide and 4th in Egypt.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></p>
