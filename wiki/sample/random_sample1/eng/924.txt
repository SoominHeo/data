<p>"<b>Dear Angel</b>" is <a href="/wiki/Olivia_Lufkin" title="Olivia Lufkin">Olivia's</a> third solo single released on October 10, 1999. It was also released as the first <a href="/wiki/Olivia_Lufkin" title="Olivia Lufkin">Olivia</a> single on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vinyl_record" title="Vinyl record">vinyl record</a>, included the original version "Dear Angel", its English version and two exclusives remixes. This was released on 1999-12-17 only at <a class="external text" href="http://www.shinseido.co.jp/cgi-bin/WebObjects/Catalog.woa/wa/detail?r=RR12-88144" rel="nofollow">Shinseido</a> with catalog NÂº RR12-88144. "Dear Angel" and its B-side "Remember Me?" were both composed by Olivia, the first songs in her career to be written by herself. The English version of "Dear Angel" also features lyrics written by Olivia.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PV3_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-PV3-1">[1]</a></sup> The song, like her previous single "<a href="/wiki/Re-act" title="Re-act">Re-act</a>," was used in <a class="extiw" href="https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E3%82%AB%E3%83%8D%E3%83%9C%E3%82%A6%E5%8C%96%E7%B2%A7%E5%93%81" title="ja:ã«ããã¦åç²§å">Kanebo Cosmetics</a>' <i>Kate</i> commercials, and featured Olivia personally.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PV3_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-PV3-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The music video was shot on September 8, 1999 aboard the <a href="/wiki/Hikawa_Maru" title="Hikawa Maru">Hikawa Maru</a> cruise liner in Yamashita Park, <a href="/wiki/Yokohama" title="Yokohama">Yokohama</a>. Shots were filmed in a parlour and the wedding hall.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PV3_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-PV3-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
