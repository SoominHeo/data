<p>This is a <b>list of <a href="/wiki/Head_of_state" title="Head of state">heads of state</a> of <a href="/wiki/Algeria" title="Algeria">Algeria</a></b> since the formation of the <a href="/wiki/Provisional_Government_of_the_Algerian_Republic" title="Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic">Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic</a> (GPRA) in exile in <a href="/wiki/Cairo" title="Cairo">Cairo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a> in 1958 during the <a href="/wiki/Algerian_War" title="Algerian War">Algerian War</a>, through independence in 1962, to the present day.</p>
<p>A total of five people have served as <a href="/wiki/President_of_Algeria" title="President of Algeria">President of Algeria</a> (not counting two Presidents of the GPRA and four interim heads of state). Additionally, two persons, <a href="/wiki/Houari_Boumediene" title="Houari Boumediene">Houari Boumediene</a> and <a href="/wiki/Liamine_Z%C3%A9roual" title="Liamine Zéroual">Liamine Zéroual</a>, have served both as interim head of state and as President of Algeria.</p>
