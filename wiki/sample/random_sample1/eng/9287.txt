<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Arrondissements of the Alpes-Maritimes department</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The 2 <b><a href="/wiki/Arrondissements_of_France" title="Arrondissements of France">arrondissements</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Alpes-Maritimes" title="Alpes-Maritimes">Alpes-Maritimes</a> <a href="/wiki/Departments_of_France" title="Departments of France">department</a></b> are:</p>
<ol>
<li><a href="/wiki/Arrondissement_of_Grasse" title="Arrondissement of Grasse">Arrondissement of Grasse</a>, (<a href="/wiki/Subprefectures_in_France" title="Subprefectures in France">subprefecture</a>: <a href="/wiki/Grasse" title="Grasse">Grasse</a>) with 62 <a href="/wiki/Communes_of_France" title="Communes of France">communes</a>. The population of the arrondissement was 560,338 in 2013.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Arrondissement_of_Nice" title="Arrondissement of Nice">Arrondissement of Nice</a>, (<a href="/wiki/Prefectures_in_France" title="Prefectures in France">prefecture</a> of the Alpes-Maritimes department: <a href="/wiki/Nice" title="Nice">Nice</a>) with 101 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 520,433 in 2013.</li>
</ol>
