<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Taxonomy">Taxonomy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Amphiboloidea&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Taxonomy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2005_taxonomy">2005 taxonomy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Amphiboloidea&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 2005 taxonomy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>According to the <a href="/wiki/Taxonomy_of_the_Gastropoda_(Bouchet_%26_Rocroi,_2005)" title="Taxonomy of the Gastropoda (Bouchet &amp; Rocroi, 2005)">taxonomy of the Gastropoda (Bouchet &amp; Rocroi, 2005)</a>, it is a superfamily in the informal group <a href="/wiki/Basommatophora" title="Basommatophora">Basommatophora</a>, within the <a href="/wiki/Pulmonata" title="Pulmonata">Pulmonata</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Bouchet_2005_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Bouchet_2005-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>This superfamily has contained only one family, the <a href="/wiki/Amphibolidae" title="Amphibolidae">Amphibolidae</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Bouchet_2005_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-Bouchet_2005-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2007_taxonomy">2007 taxonomy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Amphiboloidea&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 2007 taxonomy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Golding et al. (2007)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Golding_2007_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-Golding_2007-2">[2]</a></sup> have established new families:</p>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Maningrididae" title="Maningrididae">Maningrididae</a> Golding, Ponder &amp; Byrne, 2007<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Golding_2007_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-Golding_2007-2">[2]</a></sup> - with the only species <i><a href="/wiki/Maningrida_arnhemensis" title="Maningrida arnhemensis">Maningrida arnhemensis</a></i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Golding_2007_2-3"><a href="#cite_note-Golding_2007-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Phallomedusidae" title="Phallomedusidae">Phallomedusidae</a> Golding, Ponder &amp; Byrne, 2007<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Golding_2007_2-4"><a href="#cite_note-Golding_2007-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2010_taxonomy">2010 taxonomy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Amphiboloidea&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2010 taxonomy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Basommatophora (<a href="/wiki/Siphonarioidea" title="Siphonarioidea">Siphonarioidea</a> and Amphiboloidea and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hygrophila_(Gastropoda)" title="Hygrophila (Gastropoda)">Hygrophila</a>) have been found <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Polyphyletic" title="Polyphyletic">polyphyletic</a> and so Jörger et al. (2010)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-J.C3.B6rger_2010_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-J.C3.B6rger_2010-1">[1]</a></sup> have moved Amphiboloidea to <a href="/wiki/Panpulmonata" title="Panpulmonata">Panpulmonata</a>.</p>
