<p><b>Abdul Ghani Abd Rahman</b> (born 12 December 1985) is a <a href="/wiki/Malaysia" title="Malaysia">Malaysian</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who formerly played for <a href="/wiki/Negeri_Sembilan_FA" title="Negeri Sembilan FA">Negeri Sembilan FA</a>. He has also played for <a href="/wiki/Selangor_FA" title="Selangor FA">Selangor FA</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/MK_Land_FC" title="MK Land FC">MK Land FC</a>. After MK Land FC folded and ended its participation in the Malaysia league, he was signed by Felda United.</p>
