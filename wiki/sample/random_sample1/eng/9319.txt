<p><b>Apsheronsk</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Апшеро́нск</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">town</a> and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_center" title="Administrative center">administrative center</a> of <a href="/wiki/Apsheronsky_District" title="Apsheronsky District">Apsheronsky District</a> of <a href="/wiki/Krasnodar_Krai" title="Krasnodar Krai">Krasnodar Krai</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>, located on the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Pshekha_River&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Pshekha River (page does not exist)">Pshekha River</a> (in the <a href="/wiki/Kuban_River" title="Kuban River">Kuban</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Drainage_basin" title="Drainage basin">basin</a>). Population: <span class="nowrap">40,225 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2010)" title="Russian Census (2010)">2010 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2010Census_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-2010Census-3">[3]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">39,608 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2002)" title="Russian Census (2002)">2002 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PopCensus_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-PopCensus-6">[6]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">34,505 (<a href="/wiki/Soviet_Census_(1989)" title="Soviet Census (1989)">1989 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census1989_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-Census1989-7">[7]</a></sup> 33,400 (1968).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Apsheronsk&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name comes from <a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a> <i>āb šur</i> (salty water) and the suffix <i>-ān</i>, which marks the plural. This also gave its name to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Absheron_peninsula" title="Absheron peninsula">Absheron peninsula</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Republic_of_Azerbaijan" title="Republic of Azerbaijan">Azerbaijan</a>, where the capital Baku is located.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Apsheronsk&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It was founded in 1863 as the <i><a href="/wiki/Stanitsa" title="Stanitsa">stanitsa</a></i> of <b>Apsheronskaya</b> (<span lang="ru" xml:lang="ru">Апшеронская</span>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gr_5-2"><a href="#cite_note-gr-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Apsheronsk&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Climate">Climate</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Apsheronsk&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Climate">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Under the <a href="/wiki/K%C3%B6ppen_climate_classification" title="Köppen climate classification">Köppen climate classification</a> Apsheronsk has a <a href="/wiki/Humid_subtropical_climate" title="Humid subtropical climate">humid subtropical climate</a> climate (<i>Cfa</i>).</p>
<p>There is significant rainfall throughout the year in Apsheronsk. The average annual temperature in Apsheronsk is 12.0 °C. The warmest month of the year is July with an average temperature of 22.5 °C. In January, the average temperature is 1.8 °C. It is the lowest average temperature of the whole year.</p>
<p>The driest month is February with 62 mm. Most precipitation falls in November and December, with an average of 94 mm. About 941 mm of precipitation falls annually.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Administrative_and_municipal_status">Administrative and municipal status</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Apsheronsk&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Administrative and municipal status">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Within the <a href="/wiki/Subdivisions_of_Russia#Administrative_divisions" title="Subdivisions of Russia">framework of administrative divisions</a>, Apsheronsk serves as the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_center" title="Administrative center">administrative center</a> of <a href="/wiki/Apsheronsky_District" title="Apsheronsky District">Apsheronsky District</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-KrasnodarK_admlist_1-6"><a href="#cite_note-KrasnodarK_admlist-1">[1]</a></sup> As an administrative division, it is, together with three <a href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">rural localities</a>, incorporated within Apsheronsky District as the <b><a href="/wiki/Town_of_district_significance" title="Town of district significance">Town</a> of Apsheronsk</b>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-KrasnodarK_admlist_1-7"><a href="#cite_note-KrasnodarK_admlist-1">[1]</a></sup> As a <a href="/wiki/Subdivisions_of_Russia#Municipal_divisions" title="Subdivisions of Russia">municipal division</a>, the Town of Apsheronsk is incorporated within Apsheronsky Municipal District as <b>Apsheronskoye Urban Settlement</b>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-KrasnodarKApsheronskyD_mun_2-4"><a href="#cite_note-KrasnodarKApsheronskyD_mun-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
