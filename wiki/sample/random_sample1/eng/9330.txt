<p><b>Andre Tricoteux</b> is an actor and stuntman, known for playing <a href="/wiki/Colossus_(comics)" title="Colossus (comics)">Colossus</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Deadpool_(film)" title="Deadpool (film)">Deadpool</a></i> and Orc War Captain in <i><a href="/wiki/Warcraft_(film)" title="Warcraft (film)">Warcraft</a></i>. He has done stunt work on many TV and movies such as <i><a href="/wiki/Once_Upon_a_Time_(TV_series)" title="Once Upon a Time (TV series)">Once Upon a Time</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Seventh_Son_(film)" title="Seventh Son (film)">Seventh Son</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/See_No_Evil_2" title="See No Evil 2">See No Evil 2</a></i>.</p>
<p>In the 2016 <a href="/wiki/List_of_films_based_on_Marvel_Comics" title="List of films based on Marvel Comics">Marvel film</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Deadpool_(film)" title="Deadpool (film)">Deadpool</a></i>, he portrayed the <a href="/wiki/Colossus_(comics)" title="Colossus (comics)">Colossus</a> character in a restricted on-set <a href="/wiki/Motion_capture" title="Motion capture">motion capture</a> performance and stunt work, a role he shared with fellow stuntman T. J. Storm. Serbian actor <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Stefan_Kapicic" title="Stefan Kapicic">Stefan Kapicic</a>, who was cast at the last moment, provided the character's voice, and the film's motion capture supervisor Greg LaSalle provided the final facial performance.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Tricoteux's performance in the film has been blended by <a href="/wiki/Computer-generated_imagery" title="Computer-generated imagery">CGI</a> with that of four other actors: the three aforementioned and stuntman Glenn Ennis for initial facial shapes.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
