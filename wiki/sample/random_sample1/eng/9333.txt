<p><b>Henri Gance</b> (17 March 1888 – 29 November 1953) was a <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">French</a> <a href="/wiki/Olympic_weightlifting" title="Olympic weightlifting">weightlifter</a> who won a gold medal at the <a href="/wiki/1920_Summer_Olympics" title="1920 Summer Olympics">1920 Summer Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Antwerp" title="Antwerp">Antwerp</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sr_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-sr-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Gance took up weightlifting in 1908 and was a French champion in 1920.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sr_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-sr-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
