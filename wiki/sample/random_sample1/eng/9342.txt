<p><b>Angers Sporting Club de l'Ouest</b> (commonly referred to as <b>Angers SCO</b> or simply <b>Angers</b>) is a <a href="/wiki/Football_in_France" title="Football in France">French association football</a> club based in <a href="/wiki/Angers" title="Angers">Angers</a>. The club was founded in 1919 and currently play in <a href="/wiki/Ligue_1" title="Ligue 1">Ligue 1</a>, the first division of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/French_football" title="French football">French football</a>, having achieved promotion to the league in 2015 after 21 years. It plays its home matches at the <a href="/wiki/Stade_Jean-Bouin_(Angers)" title="Stade Jean-Bouin (Angers)">Stade Jean-Bouin</a>. The club has played 23 seasons in the top division, <a href="/wiki/Ligue_1" title="Ligue 1">Ligue 1</a>, and have also participated in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UEFA_Cup" title="UEFA Cup">UEFA Cup</a> after finishing 4th in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1971-72_Division_1" title="1971-72 Division 1">1971–72 season</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Angers_SCO&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This team was founded in 1919, which was the same year that the <a href="/wiki/French_Football_Federation" title="French Football Federation">FFF</a> was founded.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>During this team's history, this team had bounced between the top two tiers on multiple occasions. However, this team did spent time in the third tier on several occasions; the 2006–07 season was their last season in the third tier.</p>
<p>The first season that Angers debuted in the second division in France was in 1945. In this season, Angers SCO was placed in the North group, at that time, the second division was split into two group, the North and South. Angers finished third, being seven points short to <a href="/wiki/Stade_Fran%C3%A7ais_Paris_(football)" title="Stade Français Paris (football)">Stade Fran</a>, who was promoted to the first division.</p>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/2014%E2%80%9315_Ligue_2" title="2014–15 Ligue 2">2014–15 Ligue 2 season,</a> this team achieved promotion after a long time, being third placed.</p>
<p>As of now, in the 2015–16 Ligue 1 season, they are currently placed in fifth. In their opening league match, they were to visit <a href="/wiki/Montpellier_HSC" title="Montpellier HSC">Montpellier HSC</a>. In this game, Angers won the match 0–2. In their second fixture, the team were playing their first home game of the season, against <a href="/wiki/FC_Nantes" title="FC Nantes">FC Nantes</a>, which was a goalless draw. In their third game, Angers visited newly promoted team <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gazelec_Ajaccio" title="Gazelec Ajaccio">Gazelec Ajaccio</a>, which was a successful 0–2 win. In the fourth fixture of the league, Angers received Nice, which ended as a 1–1 draw. As that time, Angers was undefeated, being placed in 4th. However, they lost their next fixture, their fifth to <a href="/wiki/FC_Lorient" title="FC Lorient">Lorient</a> 3–1. Then Angers defeated Troyes 1–0, making it their first home game win of the season. The next fixture, being the seventh, Angers drew 0–0 against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Stade_Reims" title="Stade Reims">Stade Reims</a>. Then, a three-game streak of winning occurred, winning an impressive away victory against Olympique Marseille, winning 1–0 against <a href="/wiki/SC_Bastia" title="SC Bastia">SC Bastia</a>, and winning <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/FC_Toulouse" title="FC Toulouse">Toulouse</a> 1–2. Then Angers drew against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/En_Avant_Guingamp" title="En Avant Guingamp">Guingamp</a>, which was scoreless. Then two back to back losses occurred, losing 1–0 to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/AS_Monaco" title="AS Monaco">AS Monaco</a> and 0–2 to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Stade_Rennes" title="Stade Rennes">Stade Rennes</a>. Then a 0—0 draw against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/SM_Caen" title="SM Caen">SM Caen</a> came. Then Angers won again, against <a href="/wiki/Lille_OSC" title="Lille OSC">Lille</a>, with the score 2–0. Then a scoreless draw with Paris Saint-Germain, who is currently in first place in the league. After that game, Angers won an impressive 0–2 away win against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Olympique_Lyon" title="Olympique Lyon">Olympique Lyon</a>. Then a 1–1 draw against <a href="/wiki/FC_Girondins_de_Bordeaux" title="FC Girondins de Bordeaux">Bordeaux</a> happened, then a defeat of 1–0 to <a href="/wiki/AS_Saint-%C3%89tienne" title="AS Saint-Étienne">Saint-Étienne</a>, and later a win against SM Caen. However, in the next fixture, Nice defeated Angers 2–1. Their last fixture was against <a href="/wiki/Paris_Saint-Germain_F.C." title="Paris Saint-Germain F.C.">Paris Saint-Germain</a>, which Angers lost 5–1 in the <a href="/wiki/Parc_des_Princes" title="Parc des Princes">Parc des Princes</a>.</p>
