<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>There have been several groups called the <b>Patriot Party</b>, the <b>Patriotic Party</b>, or similar:</p>
<ul>
<li>American Patriot Party</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Aruban_Patriotic_Party" title="Aruban Patriotic Party">Aruban Patriotic Party</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Belarusian_Patriotic_Party" title="Belarusian Patriotic Party">Belarusian Patriotic Party</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/British_Columbia_Patriot_Party" title="British Columbia Patriot Party">British Columbia Patriot Party</a>, in Canada</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Parti_patriote" title="Parti patriote">Parti patriote</a>, in Canada</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/New_Patriotic_Party" title="New Patriotic Party">New Patriotic Party</a>, in Ghana</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Patriotic_Party_(Guatemala)" title="Patriotic Party (Guatemala)">Patriotic Party (Guatemala)</a>, in Guatemala</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Patriotic_Renovation_Party" title="Patriotic Renovation Party">Patriotic Renovation Party</a>, in Honduras</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Patriot_Party_(Indonesia)" title="Patriot Party (Indonesia)">Patriot Party (Indonesia)</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Irish_Patriot_Party" title="Irish Patriot Party">Irish Patriot Party</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Patriotic_Party" title="National Patriotic Party">National Patriotic Party</a>, in Liberia</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Patriots_(faction)" title="Patriots (faction)">Patriots (faction)</a>, in the Netherlands</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/New_Zealand_Patriot_Party" title="New Zealand Patriot Party">New Zealand Patriot Party</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Patriotic_Party" title="Patriotic Party">Patriotic Party</a>, in the Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth (1788-1792)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Patriotic_Party_of_Pridnestrovie" title="Patriotic Party of Pridnestrovie">Patriotic Party of Pridnestrovie</a>, in Transnistria</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Patriot_Whigs" title="Patriot Whigs">Patriot Whigs</a>, in the United Kingdom (1725)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Patriotic_Party_(UK)" title="Patriotic Party (UK)">Patriotic Party (UK)</a> (1964)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Patriotic_Party_(Turkey)" title="Patriotic Party (Turkey)">Patriotic Party (Turkey)</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Patriot_Party_(1960s%E2%80%931980s)" title="Patriot Party (1960s–1980s)">Patriot Party (1960s–1980s)</a>, in the United States</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/White_Patriot_Party" title="White Patriot Party">White Patriot Party</a>, in the United States (1987)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/United_Citizens_Party" title="United Citizens Party">United Citizens Party</a> of South Carolina, known as the Patriot Party for part of its history</li>
<li>various parties formed as a result of the <a href="/wiki/Ross_Perot_presidential_campaign,_1992" title="Ross Perot presidential campaign, 1992">1992 independent presidential campaign of Ross Perot</a>, many of which were later incorporated into the <a href="/wiki/Reform_Party_of_the_United_States_of_America" title="Reform Party of the United States of America">Reform Party of the United States of America</a></li>
</ul>
