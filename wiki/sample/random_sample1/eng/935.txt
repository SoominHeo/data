<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Electrical_and_computer_engineering" title="Electrical and computer engineering">Electrical and computer engineering</a>, a discipline that combines electrical engineering and computer science and engineering</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Electronics_and_communication_engineering" title="Electronics and communication engineering">Electronics and communication engineering</a>, a discipline that combines electronics engineering and Computer, Information and communication technology</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/United_Nations_Economic_Commission_for_Europe" title="United Nations Economic Commission for Europe">United Nations Economic Commission for Europe</a>, a United Nations agency promulgating automotive technical regulations based on international consensus</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Early_childhood_education" title="Early childhood education">Early childhood education</a>, an approach to the education of small children</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/External_combustion_engine" title="External combustion engine">External combustion engine</a>, a form of heat engine</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Electron-Cloud_Effect" title="Electron-Cloud Effect">Electron-Cloud Effect</a>, a phenomenon associated with particle accelerators</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Early_College_Experience&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Early College Experience (page does not exist)">Early College Experience</a>, a program allowing college credit for advanced high school classes</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%C3%89cole_Centrale_d%27%C3%89lectronique&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="École Centrale d'Électronique (page does not exist)">École Centrale d'Électronique</a>, a French Graduate School of Engineering located in Paris</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Encyclopedia_of_Chess_Endings&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Encyclopedia of Chess Endings (page does not exist)">Encyclopedia of Chess Endings</a>, One of <a href="/wiki/Chess_endgame_literature" title="Chess endgame literature">Chess endgame literature</a> books</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/East_Coast_Expressway" title="East Coast Expressway">East Coast Expressway (ECE)</a>, a Malaysian expressway</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Explicit_Congestion_Notification" title="Explicit Congestion Notification">Explicit Congestion Notification</a> Echo, an extension to TCP and IP computer networking</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Edinburgh_Corn_Exchange" title="Edinburgh Corn Exchange">Edinburgh Corn Exchange</a>, a concert venue and conference centre located in the Chesser area of Edinburgh, Scotland</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Equipement_et_Construction_Electrique&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Equipement et Construction Electrique (page does not exist)">Equipement et Construction Electrique</a>, ECE - Zodiac Aerospace, a French company, within <a href="/wiki/Zodiac_Aerospace" title="Zodiac Aerospace">Zodiac Aerospace</a> group</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Excelsior_College_Examinations" title="Excelsior College Examinations">Excelsior College Examinations</a>, a set of exams provided by Excelsior College; passing one can earn credit at other colleges besides Excelsior</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Einstein-Cartan-Evans_theory" title="Einstein-Cartan-Evans theory">Einstein-Cartan-Evans theory</a>, a proposed unified theory of physics</li>
</ul>
