<p>The <b>Amarillo Civic Center</b> is a multi-purpose convention center in <a href="/wiki/Amarillo,_Texas" title="Amarillo, Texas">Amarillo, Texas</a>. It consists of multiple facilities including:</p>
<ul>
<li>A 2,848-seat auditorium with 2,324 permanent seats and used for concerts, Broadway shows and other events.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Cal_Farley" title="Cal Farley">Cal Farley</a> Coliseum, a 4,987-seat multi-purpose <a href="/wiki/Arena" title="Arena">arena</a> serving as home to the <a href="/wiki/Amarillo_Bulls" title="Amarillo Bulls">Amarillo Bulls</a> of the <a href="/wiki/North_American_Hockey_League" title="North American Hockey League">North American Hockey League</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Amarillo_Venom" title="Amarillo Venom">Amarillo Venom</a> of <a href="/wiki/Champions_Indoor_Football" title="Champions Indoor Football">Champions Indoor Football</a>. The arena, which has 4,879 permanent seats, is also used for concerts, banquets, conventions, ice shows, <a href="/wiki/Wrestling" title="Wrestling">wrestling</a> and trade shows (the arena features 17,100 square feet (1,590 m<sup>2</sup>) of floor space). The arena measures 38' 10 from floor to rafters, 50'10 from floor to ceiling.</li>
<li>A 16,000-square-foot (1,500 m<sup>2</sup>) Grand Plaza, designed as a tribute to Texas and seating up to 1,100 for smaller concerts, banquets, and other special events. The Grand Plaza contains a Texas-accented floor, skylight ceiling, and fountains and planters.</li>
<li>Two exhibit halls, the North which has 24,565 square feet (2,282 m<sup>2</sup>) of edxhibit space and used for trade shows, conventions, meetings and banquets (capacity is up to 2,200) and with a 25-foot (8 m) ceiling height; and a 26,000-square-foot (2,400 m<sup>2</sup>), 14-foot (4 m) South Exhibit Hall, also used for similar events.</li>
<li>Three meeting rooms—the Heritage, Hospitality and Regency rooms.</li>
</ul>
<p>There is a memorial statue of the late <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Space_shuttle" title="Space shuttle">space shuttle</a> commander <a href="/wiki/Rick_Husband" title="Rick Husband">Rick Husband</a>, one of the city's most famous sons, in front of the building.</p>
