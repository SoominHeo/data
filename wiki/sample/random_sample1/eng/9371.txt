<p><b>Ashleigh May Brewer</b> (born 9 December 1990) is an Australian actress. She had a recurring role in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Sleepover_Club_(TV_series)" title="The Sleepover Club (TV series)">The Sleepover Club</a></i>, before she joined the cast of <i><a href="/wiki/H2O:_Just_Add_Water" title="H2O: Just Add Water">H<sub>2</sub>O: Just Add Water</a></i>. Brewer played the role of <a href="/wiki/Kate_Ramsay" title="Kate Ramsay">Kate Ramsay</a> in the long-running Australian soap opera <i><a href="/wiki/Neighbours" title="Neighbours">Neighbours</a></i> from 2009 until 2014. She currently portrays the role of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ivy_Forrester" title="Ivy Forrester">Ivy Forrester</a> on the <a href="/wiki/CBS_Daytime" title="CBS Daytime">CBS Daytime</a> <a href="/wiki/Soap_opera" title="Soap opera">soap opera</a> <i><a href="/wiki/The_Bold_and_the_Beautiful" title="The Bold and the Beautiful">The Bold and the Beautiful</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_and_personal_life">Early and personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ashleigh_Brewer&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early and personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born and raised in <a href="/wiki/Brisbane" title="Brisbane">Brisbane</a>, Brewer graduated from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Forest_Lake_College" title="Forest Lake College">Forest Lake College</a> in 2008.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Courier_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Courier-3">[3]</a></sup> After moving to Melbourne, Brewer shared a flat with fellow <i>Neighbours</i> co-star <a href="/wiki/Margot_Robbie" title="Margot Robbie">Margot Robbie</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Courier_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-Courier-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ashleigh_Brewer&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Brewer appeared in the recurring role of Alana in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Sleepover_Club_(TV_series)" title="The Sleepover Club (TV series)">The Sleepover Club</a></i> when she was twelve years old.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> She went on to make minor appearances in <i><a href="/wiki/Blue_Heelers" title="Blue Heelers">Blue Heelers</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/H2O:_Just_Add_Water" title="H2O: Just Add Water">H<sub>2</sub>O: Just Add Water</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Courier_3-2"><a href="#cite_note-Courier-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>A week after graduating from her school, Brewer arrived in Melbourne to look for acting work.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Courier_3-3"><a href="#cite_note-Courier-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-AB_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-AB-5">[5]</a></sup> While she was in the city, Brewer successfully auditioned for the ongoing role of <a href="/wiki/Kate_Ramsay" title="Kate Ramsay">Kate Ramsay</a> in the long-running soap opera <i><a href="/wiki/Neighbours" title="Neighbours">Neighbours</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Courier_3-4"><a href="#cite_note-Courier-3">[3]</a></sup> The actress relocated to Melbourne for filming and she made her debut as Kate on 15 May 2009.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Courier_3-5"><a href="#cite_note-Courier-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-AB_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-AB-5">[5]</a></sup> The following year, Brewer was nominated for the <a href="/wiki/Logie_Award_for_Most_Popular_New_Female_Talent" title="Logie Award for Most Popular New Female Talent">Most Popular New Female Talent</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Logie_Award" title="Logie Award">Logie Award</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> In July 2013, Brewer celebrated filming 1000 episodes.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<p>After five years of playing Kate, Brewer confirmed her departure from <i>Neighbours</i> in November 2013. Brewer decided to quit the show to pursue new acting projects.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Quits_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-Quits-8">[8]</a></sup> She filmed her final scenes the following month.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> Brewer's character was killed off and she admitted that she was delighted with Kate's exit storyline.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 22 May 2014, it was announced that Brewer had been cast in the newly created role of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ivy_Forrester" title="Ivy Forrester">Ivy Forrester</a> in the American soap opera <i><a href="/wiki/The_Bold_and_the_Beautiful" title="The Bold and the Beautiful">The Bold and the Beautiful</a></i>. Brewer's character is the niece of established character <a href="/wiki/Eric_Forrester" title="Eric Forrester">Eric Forrester</a> (<a href="/wiki/John_McCook" title="John McCook">John McCook</a>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BandB_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-BandB-11">[11]</a></sup> Brewer began filming on 24 May.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Geoff_12-0"><a href="#cite_note-Geoff-12">[12]</a></sup> She has a three-year contract with the show and made her debut as Ivy in July 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BandB_11-1"><a href="#cite_note-BandB-11">[11]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Geoff_12-1"><a href="#cite_note-Geoff-12">[12]</a></sup> Brewer appeared in a documentary special celebrating <i>Neighbours</i><span class="nowrap" style="padding-left:0.1em;">'</span> 30th anniversary titled <i>Neighbours 30th: The Stars Reunite</i>, which aired in Australia and the UK in March 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup></p>
