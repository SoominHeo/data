<p><b>Maria Ashley Eckstein</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Married_and_maiden_names" title="Married and maiden names">née</a> <b>Drane</b>; born September 22, 1981), credited as <b>Ashley Eckstein</b> or <b>Ashley Drane</b>, is an American actress and fashion designer, known to audiences of <i><a href="/wiki/Blue_Collar_TV" title="Blue Collar TV">Blue Collar TV</a></i> and as the voice of <a href="/wiki/Ahsoka_Tano" title="Ahsoka Tano">Ahsoka Tano</a> on <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars_(2008_TV_series)" title="Star Wars: The Clone Wars (2008 TV series)">Star Wars: The Clone Wars</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Wars_Rebels" title="Star Wars Rebels">Star Wars Rebels</a></i>. She also played a small role as Alicia in the film <i><a href="/wiki/Sydney_White" title="Sydney White">Sydney White</a></i> and as Ms. Cole in the film <i><a href="/wiki/Alice_Upside_Down" title="Alice Upside Down">Alice Upside Down</a></i>, with both films being released in 2007. Eckstein also had a recurring role as Muffy on the <a href="/wiki/Disney_Channel" title="Disney Channel">Disney Channel</a> sitcom <i><a href="/wiki/That%27s_So_Raven" title="That's So Raven">That's So Raven</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ashley_Eckstein&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Eckstein was born in <a href="/wiki/Louisville,_Kentucky" title="Louisville, Kentucky">Louisville, Kentucky</a>, and raised in <a href="/wiki/Orlando,_Florida" title="Orlando, Florida">Orlando, Florida</a>. Her first job was as a cast member at <a href="/wiki/Disney%27s_Hollywood_Studios" title="Disney's Hollywood Studios">Disney's MGM Studios</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-OBJ_Her_Universe_Article_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-OBJ_Her_Universe_Article-1">[1]</a></sup> She married former <a href="/wiki/Major_League_Baseball" title="Major League Baseball">Major League Baseball</a> player and <a href="/wiki/2006_World_Series" title="2006 World Series">2006 World Series</a> MVP <a href="/wiki/David_Eckstein" title="David Eckstein">David Eckstein</a> on November 26, 2005, in his hometown of <a href="/wiki/Sanford,_Florida" title="Sanford, Florida">Sanford, Florida</a>, followed by a reception at <a href="/wiki/Walt_Disney_World" title="Walt Disney World">Walt Disney World</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-phil2006_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-phil2006-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>In addition to doing film and voice work, Eckstein also started Her Universe, a fashion brand that produces sci-fi themed clothing targeted specifically for girls and women.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
