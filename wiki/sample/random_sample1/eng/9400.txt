<p><b>Andrew Stetson</b> (born August 24, 1979) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Canadian" title="Canadian">Canadian</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Male_model" title="Male model">model</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Andrew_Stetson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Andrew Stetson worked as a carpenter before becoming a fashion model at the age of 23. He was discovered by Norwayne Anderson of the NAM agency.</p>
<p>After a year of modeling in New York and Europe, he returned to renovating homes and started a home renovation business, although his friends and family would later encourage him to return to modeling. He has since walked for many designers and has appeared in numerous ad campaigns for <a href="/wiki/Armani" title="Armani">Armani</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chanel" title="Chanel">Chanel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dolce_%26_Gabbana" title="Dolce &amp; Gabbana">Dolce &amp; Gabbana</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Zara_(clothing)" title="Zara (clothing)">Zara</a>, <a href="/wiki/Calvin_Klein" title="Calvin Klein">Calvin Klein</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Levi%27s" title="Levi's">Levi's</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bloomingdales" title="Bloomingdales">Bloomingdales</a>, and <a href="/wiki/H%26M" title="H&amp;M">H&amp;M</a>. He also made the cover of gay French magazine <a href="/wiki/T%C3%AAtu" title="Têtu">Têtu</a> in November 2005 and again in May 2010, the latter issue devoting six full pages to him.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> On August 2006, Stetson had signed a three-year contract for <a href="/wiki/Calvin_Klein" title="Calvin Klein">Calvin Klein</a>'s fragrance, Euphoria.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> He was succeeded by Carson Parker as the face of Euphoria and <a href="/wiki/Garrett_Neff" title="Garrett Neff">Garrett Neff</a> as the spokesmodel for the company.</p>
<p>In 2011 on March 19, Andrew Stetson married his girlfriend Stine in Denmark.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
