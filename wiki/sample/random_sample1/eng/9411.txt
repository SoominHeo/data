<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Anthony_Ingruber&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Anthony Ingruber is an actor born in 1990 in the Philippines, as the son of a diplomat. The Dutch-Australian<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> grew up in <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>, <a href="/wiki/New_Zealand" title="New Zealand">New Zealand</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cyprus" title="Cyprus">Cyprus</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Netherlands</a> and <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>. In Canada, Ingruber attended a <a href="/wiki/Drama_school" title="Drama school">drama school</a> and used internet platforms like <a href="/wiki/YouTube" title="YouTube">YouTube</a> to publish his acting performances.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Anthony_Ingruber&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Since 2006, Ingruber played supporting roles in different TV series. In 2008, he uploaded videos on YouTube which included a <a href="/wiki/Han_Solo" title="Han Solo">Han Solo</a> performance from <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Wars_(film)" title="Star Wars (film)">Star Wars</a></i>. Ingruber became known on the Internet for his physical similarity to a young <a href="/wiki/Harrison_Ford" title="Harrison Ford">Harrison Ford</a> and his impressions.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2008, Ingruber had a very small <a href="/wiki/Extra_(acting)" title="Extra (acting)">extra</a> part in <a href="/wiki/James_Cameron" title="James Cameron">James Cameron</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Avatar_(2009_film)" title="Avatar (2009 film)">Avatar</a></i> as a technician. His first movie role was in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Disney" title="Disney">Disney</a> TV movie <i><a href="/wiki/Avalon_High_(film)" title="Avalon High (film)">Avalon High</a></i> in 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> He also worked for <a href="/wiki/Weta_Workshop" title="Weta Workshop">Weta Workshop</a> while he lived in New Zealand.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2015, Anthony Ingruber portrayed the role of "Young William"; the young version of Harrison Ford's character "William" in the drama <i><a href="/wiki/The_Age_of_Adaline" title="The Age of Adaline">The Age of Adaline</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> Ingruber is currently active in the film industry doing voice-over and acting in industrial, independent and feature films.</p>
