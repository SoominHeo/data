<p><b><a href="/wiki/Antigua_and_Barbuda" title="Antigua and Barbuda">Antigua and Barbuda</a></b> is an island nation made up of <a href="/wiki/Antigua" title="Antigua">Antigua</a>, which, along with its satellite islets and rocks, is divided into six <b>parishes</b>; and two <b>dependencies</b>, the islands <a href="/wiki/Barbuda" title="Barbuda">Barbuda</a> and <a href="/wiki/Redonda" title="Redonda">Redonda</a>.</p>
