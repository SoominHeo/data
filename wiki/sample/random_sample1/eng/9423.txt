<p><b>Alan Ruscoe</b> is a British actor who is best known for his work as various aliens, monsters and androids in the <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Wars" title="Star Wars">Star Wars</a></i> films and the television series <i><a href="/wiki/Doctor_Who" title="Doctor Who">Doctor Who</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alan_Ruscoe&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On television, he has played Baraquel, Sariel and Araquiel in Sky One's <i><a href="/wiki/Hex_(TV_series)" title="Hex (TV series)">Hex</a></i> season 2, as well as a number of characters in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Doctor_Who_serials#Series_1_.282005.29" title="List of Doctor Who serials">2005 series</a> of <i>Doctor Who</i>: <a href="/wiki/List_of_Doctor_Who_villains#Blon_Fel-Fotch_Pasameer-Day_Slitheen" title="List of Doctor Who villains">Blon Fel-Fotch Passameer-Day Slitheen</a> in "<a href="/wiki/Aliens_of_London" title="Aliens of London">Aliens of London</a>" and "<a href="/wiki/World_War_Three_(Doctor_Who)" title="World War Three (Doctor Who)">World War Three</a>" (and briefly in "<a href="/wiki/Boom_Town_(Doctor_Who)" title="Boom Town (Doctor Who)">Boom Town</a>"), lead <a href="/wiki/Auton" title="Auton">Auton</a> in "<a href="/wiki/Rose_(Doctor_Who)" title="Rose (Doctor Who)">Rose</a>", the Anne Droid in "<a href="/wiki/Bad_Wolf" title="Bad Wolf">Bad Wolf</a>" and "<a href="/wiki/The_Parting_of_the_Ways" title="The Parting of the Ways">The Parting of the Ways</a>", Trine-E in "<a href="/wiki/Bad_Wolf" title="Bad Wolf">Bad Wolf</a>", and Lute of the Forest of Cheem in "<a href="/wiki/The_End_of_the_World_(Doctor_Who)" title="The End of the World (Doctor Who)">The End of the World</a>". Ruscoe has also appeared in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Doctor_Who_audio_plays_by_Big_Finish" title="List of Doctor Who audio plays by Big Finish"><i>Doctor Who</i> audio dramas</a> <i><a href="/wiki/The_Veiled_Leopard" title="The Veiled Leopard">The Veiled Leopard</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Settling" title="The Settling">The Settling</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gallifrey:_Annihilation" title="Gallifrey: Annihilation">Gallifrey: Annihilation</a></i> and <i>Bernice Summerfield: Paradise Frost</i> for <a href="/wiki/Big_Finish_Productions" title="Big Finish Productions">Big Finish Productions</a>. He also played Andrew Stone, a Mars colonist later taken over by an alien lifeform in the November 2009 <i><a href="/wiki/Doctor_Who" title="Doctor Who">Doctor Who</a></i> special "<a href="/wiki/The_Waters_of_Mars" title="The Waters of Mars">The Waters of Mars</a>".</p>
<p>In films, Ruscoe has played <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_Star_Wars_Old_Republic_characters#Dofine.2C_Daultay" title="List of Star Wars Old Republic characters">Daultay Dofine</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lott_Dod" title="Lott Dod">Lott Dod</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Plo_Koon" title="Plo Koon">Plo Koon</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Wars:_Episode_I_%E2%80%93_The_Phantom_Menace" title="Star Wars: Episode I – The Phantom Menace">Star Wars: Episode I – The Phantom Menace</a></i>, and the Mangalore Kino in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Fifth_Element" title="The Fifth Element">The Fifth Element</a></i>. He also played "the Poulterer" in a 2004 television adaptation of <i><a href="/wiki/A_Christmas_Carol" title="A Christmas Carol">A Christmas Carol</a></i>. He appears alongside Terence Stamp, Vanessa Redgrave and Gemma Arterton in "Song For Marion" released this year.</p>
<p>In theatre, he has played Deputy and Snake Preacher in <i><a href="/wiki/Whistle_Down_the_Wind_(musical)" title="Whistle Down the Wind (musical)">Whistle Down the Wind</a></i>, Riff Raff in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Rocky_Horror_Show" title="The Rocky Horror Show">The Rocky Horror Show</a></i> in the English Theatre Frankfurt, Bob Cratchit in the 2004 touring version of <i><a href="/wiki/Scrooge_(musical)" title="Scrooge (musical)">Scrooge</a></i> and Gussie Fink-Nottle in the 2007 UK tour of <i>By Jeeves</i>. In Germany, he played "Bob" the pizza-boy-eating alien for the <a href="/wiki/T-Online" title="T-Online">T-Online</a> television and poster campaign.</p>
