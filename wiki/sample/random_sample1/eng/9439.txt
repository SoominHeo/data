<p><b>Allisyn Ashley Arm</b> (born April 25, 1996)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aaarResume_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-aaarResume-1">[1]</a></sup> is an American actress and sketch <a href="/wiki/Comedian" title="Comedian">comedian</a>. Arm is most known for her main role as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Zora_Lancaster" title="Zora Lancaster">Zora Lancaster</a> on <i><a href="/wiki/Sonny_with_a_Chance" title="Sonny with a Chance">Sonny with a Chance</a></i> and in <a href="/wiki/Disney_Channel" title="Disney Channel">Disney Channel</a>'s sketch comedy series <i><a href="/wiki/So_Random!" title="So Random!">So Random!</a></i></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allisyn_Ashley_Arm&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Allisyn Ashley Arm was born on April 25, 1996 in <a href="/wiki/Glendale,_California" title="Glendale, California">Glendale, California</a> to Steve and Anji Arm. After seeing a number of plays in the <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a> area, her parents saw her act when she was half a year old and decided to put her into an acting school in Arizona.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-aaaBiography_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-aaaBiography-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allisyn_Ashley_Arm&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The first acting job Arm booked was for a <a href="/wiki/Bounty_(brand)" title="Bounty (brand)">Bounty</a> commercial that never aired.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-First_Acting_Job_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-First_Acting_Job-5">[5]</a></sup> Despite the fact that her first attempt at commercial acting was done in vain, she eventually starred in many televised advertisements that did air;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Commercials_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-Commercials-6">[6]</a></sup> and in May 2002, she was cast in her very first television appearance on the medical drama series: <i><a href="/wiki/Strong_Medicine" title="Strong Medicine">Strong Medicine</a></i>. She would go on to make appearances in episodes of <i><a href="/wiki/Friends" title="Friends">Friends</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/10-8" title="10-8">10-8</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Miracles_(TV_series)" title="Miracles (TV series)">Miracles</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Judging_Amy" title="Judging Amy">Judging Amy</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Early_Television_Appearances_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-Early_Television_Appearances-7">[7]</a></sup> Arm also had roles on films such as <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Man_of_the_House_(2005_drama_film)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Man of the House (2005 drama film) (page does not exist)">Man of the House</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/King_of_California" title="King of California">King of California</a></i> with <i><a href="/wiki/The_Twilight_Saga_(film_series)" title="The Twilight Saga (film series)">Twilight</a></i>’s <a href="/wiki/Ashley_Greene" title="Ashley Greene">Ashley Greene</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Early_Film_Appearances_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-Early_Film_Appearances-8">[8]</a></sup> In 2009, Arm appeared as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Zora_Lancaster" title="Zora Lancaster">Zora</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Disney_Channel" title="Disney Channel">Disney Channel</a> original series <i><a href="/wiki/Sonny_with_a_Chance" title="Sonny with a Chance">Sonny with a Chance</a></i>. After the show's cancellation, she appeared in the run of what had originally been a show within a show, the <a href="/wiki/Sketch_comedy" title="Sketch comedy">sketch comedy</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/So_Random!" title="So Random!">So Random!</a></i> in 2011. She also appeared in Amber Lily's music video "Overloadin" with her <i>So Random!</i> co-star <a href="/wiki/Audrey_Whitby" title="Audrey Whitby">Audrey Whitby</a> in 2011. Arm recently voiced the character Stormy in the <a href="/wiki/Disney_Junior" title="Disney Junior">Disney Junior</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/Jake_and_the_Never_Land_Pirates" title="Jake and the Never Land Pirates">Jake and the Never Land Pirates</a></i>.</p>
<p>Allisyn Ashley Arm later developed a <a href="/wiki/YouTube" title="YouTube">YouTube</a> <a href="/wiki/List_of_YouTubers" title="List of YouTubers">personality</a> named <a class="external text" href="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCNO4ervBHCw5EkjwBOnCN-Q" rel="nofollow">Astrid Clover</a> and is assisted by Caleb Pierce as Gilly, <a href="/wiki/Audrey_Whitby" title="Audrey Whitby">Audrey Whitby</a> as Rain, Jonathon McClendon as Kale Murry, Autumn Wendel as Autumn, Jacey Tomlinson as Jacey, <a href="/wiki/Dylan_Riley_Snyder" title="Dylan Riley Snyder">Dylan Riley Snyder</a> as Boom Guy, Tess Baines as Luna, <a href="/wiki/Joey_Luthman" title="Joey Luthman">Joey Luthman</a> as Beckett, Matthew Scott Montgomery as Stacy-John, Austin James as Wesley, Cooper Green as Cooper, Elizabeth Johnson as Harmony, Emily Cavalluzzi as Olive, Sam Lant as Colton, Keely Marshall as Finley, <a href="/wiki/Kira_Kosarin" title="Kira Kosarin">Kira Kosarin</a> as Stella, Shauna Case as Tessa, Gatlin Green as Poppie, and Josie Arm as Astrid's sister Bliss Clover.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allisyn_Ashley_Arm&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>As a supporter of <a href="/wiki/People_for_the_Ethical_Treatment_of_Animals" title="People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals">PETA</a>, Allisyn Ashley Arm has been a vegetarian since she was 5 years old as a part of a vegetarian family. At age 12, she became <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vegan" title="Vegan">vegan</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> Arm later attended the grand opening of the Bob Barker Building that was established by <a href="/wiki/Bob_Barker" title="Bob Barker">Bob Barker</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Influences">Influences</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Allisyn_Ashley_Arm&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Influences">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In an interview with <a href="/wiki/People_(magazine)" title="People (magazine)">People</a>; Arm said, "I watch a lot of <a href="/wiki/Saturday_Night_Live" title="Saturday Night Live">SNL</a> skits online. I love <a href="/wiki/Chris_Farley" title="Chris Farley">Chris Farley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jim_Carrey" title="Jim Carrey">Jim Carrey</a>. I always think, 'What would Jim Carrey do in this scene?' He's a lot of my inspiration for my character (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Zora_Lancaster" title="Zora Lancaster">Zora</a>)."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Allisyn_Ashley_Arm.27s_influences_12-0"><a href="#cite_note-Allisyn_Ashley_Arm.27s_influences-12">[12]</a></sup></p>
