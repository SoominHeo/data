<p><b>Amber Dawn Montana</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-twitter_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-twitter-1">[1]</a></sup> also known as <b>Amber Frank</b> (born December 2, 1998<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-resume_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-resume-4">[4]</a></sup>) is an American actress. She is known for her role as Taylor Hathaway in the <a href="/wiki/Nickelodeon" title="Nickelodeon">Nickelodeon</a> series <i><a href="/wiki/The_Haunted_Hathaways" title="The Haunted Hathaways">The Haunted Hathaways</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Amber_Montana&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Amber Montana started acting at the age of 7 in Florida before she moved to California.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> While in Florida she also attended Westchase Elementary School in Westchase, Tampa, Florida. After moving to California she got her first minor role in the 2008 drama movie <i>She Could Be You</i>. Later on, she appeared in different TV shows. In 2012, she was cast for the lead role as Taylor in the Nickelodeon sitcom <i><a href="/wiki/The_Haunted_Hathaways" title="The Haunted Hathaways">The Haunted Hathaways</a></i> after she had auditioned eight times for that role. She currently resides in <a href="/wiki/Santa_Clarita,_California" title="Santa Clarita, California">Santa Clarita, California</a>. Montana is of <a href="/wiki/Cubans" title="Cubans">Cuban</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Spanish_people" title="Spanish people">Spanish</a> descent.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
