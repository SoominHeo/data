<p>The <b>Ynys Môn football team</b> represents the island of <a href="/wiki/Anglesey" title="Anglesey">Anglesey</a> (<a href="/wiki/Welsh_language" title="Welsh language">Welsh</a>: <span lang="cy" xml:lang="cy"> <i>Ynys</i> <i>Môn</i></span>) at the biannual <a href="/wiki/Island_Games" title="Island Games">Island Games</a>. Ynys Môn is not a member of <a href="/wiki/FIFA" title="FIFA">FIFA</a> or <a href="/wiki/UEFA" title="UEFA">UEFA</a>, it is an <a href="/wiki/Island" title="Island">island</a> county within <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Wales</a> and plays under the auspices of the <a href="/wiki/Football_Association_of_Wales" title="Football Association of Wales">Football Association of Wales</a>, the governing body for football in Wales.</p>
