<p><i><b>Primula sieboldii</b></i> (Japanese primrose) is a species of <a href="/wiki/Primula" title="Primula">primrose</a> that is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Endemic_(ecology)" title="Endemic (ecology)">endemic</a> to <a href="/wiki/East_Asia" title="East Asia">East Asia</a>. The common name <b>Japanese primrose</b> also applies to the related species <i><a href="/wiki/Primula_japonica" title="Primula japonica">Primula japonica</a></i>.</p>
