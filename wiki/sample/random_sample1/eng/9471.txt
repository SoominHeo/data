<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Yamagata Broadcasting Company</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Yamagata Broadcasting Co., Ltd.</b> (<b>YBC</b>, <b>山形放送</b>株式会社) is a Japanese broadcaster in <a href="/wiki/Yamagata,_Yamagata" title="Yamagata, Yamagata">Yamagata</a>. Its <a href="/wiki/Radio_station" title="Radio station">radio station</a> is affiliated with <a href="/wiki/Japan_Radio_Network" title="Japan Radio Network">Japan Radio Network (JRN)</a> and <a href="/wiki/National_Radio_Network_(Japan)" title="National Radio Network (Japan)">National Radio Network (NRN)</a>, and its <a href="/wiki/Television_station" title="Television station">television station</a> is affiliated with <a href="/wiki/Nippon_News_Network" title="Nippon News Network">Nippon News Network (NNN)</a> and Nippon TV Network System (NNS).</p>
