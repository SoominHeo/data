<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yamada_Futaro" title="Yamada Futaro">Futaro Yamada</a> (1922–2001), author</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hidetaka_Yamada" title="Hidetaka Yamada">Hidetaka Yamada</a> (born 1976), badminton player</li>
