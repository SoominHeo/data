<p><b>Takashi Yamazaki</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">山崎 貴</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Yamazaki Takashi</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born June 12, 1964 in <a href="/wiki/Matsumoto,_Nagano" title="Matsumoto, Nagano">Matsumoto, Nagano</a>)</span> is a Japanese <a href="/wiki/Film_director" title="Film director">film director</a>, <a href="/wiki/Screenwriter" title="Screenwriter">screenwriter</a> and <a href="/wiki/Visual_effects" title="Visual effects">visual effects</a> director. He won the Best Director and Best Screenplay prizes at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Japanese_Academy_Awards" title="Japanese Academy Awards">Japanese Academy Awards</a> in 2006 for <i><a href="/wiki/Always_Sanch%C5%8Dme_no_Y%C5%ABhi" title="Always Sanchōme no Yūhi">Always: Sunset on Third Street</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> He is a member of the animation and film visual effects studio <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Shirogumi&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Shirogumi (page does not exist)">Shirogumi</a>.</p>
