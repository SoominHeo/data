<p><b>Kota Yabu</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">薮 宏太</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Yabu, Kota</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, born 31 January 1990)</span> is a Japanese idol as well as a member and leader of <a href="/wiki/Hey!_Say!_JUMP" title="Hey! Say! JUMP">Hey! Say! JUMP</a>. He is under the management of <a href="/wiki/Johnny_%26_Associates" title="Johnny &amp; Associates">Johnny &amp; Associates</a>. He was born in <a href="/wiki/Tokyo" title="Tokyo">Tokyo</a>, and attends <a href="/wiki/Waseda_University" title="Waseda University">Waseda University</a>, studying human science.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>On September 23, 2001, he entered <a href="/wiki/Johnny_%26_Associates" title="Johnny &amp; Associates">Johnny &amp; Associates</a> as a trainee. <a href="/wiki/Kei_Inoo" title="Kei Inoo">Kei Inoo</a>, a member of <a href="/wiki/Hey!_Say!_JUMP" title="Hey! Say! JUMP">Hey! Say! JUMP</a>, was also there during that time. From the time he joined the agency (2001) to the year 2004, he had the most high pitched voice in the history of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Johnny%27s_Entertainment" title="Johnny's Entertainment">Johnny's Entertainment</a>, which eventually led him up to become the leader of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Johnny%27s_Jr" title="Johnny's Jr">Johnny's Jr</a>. Two months later, he was chosen to be the leader of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ya-Ya-yah" title="Ya-Ya-yah">Ya-Ya-yah</a>. They released a CD which was very unusual because they were still members of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Johnny%27s_Jr" title="Johnny's Jr">Johnny's Jr</a>.</p>
<p>On September 21, 2007, he began performing as a member of <a href="/wiki/Hey!_Say!_JUMP" title="Hey! Say! JUMP">Hey! Say! JUMP</a>. He is the oldest member of the group.</p>
