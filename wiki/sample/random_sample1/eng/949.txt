<p><i><b>Epilogue</b></i> is a special release album from Korean hip-hop group <a href="/wiki/Epik_High" title="Epik High">Epik High</a>. The album is a collection of unreleased tracks from the group's discography spanning 7 years and 11 albums.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It is also the first album released without <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/DJ_Tukutz" title="DJ Tukutz">DJ Tukutz</a>, due to his departure for the Korean military in October 2009.</p>
<p>Epilogue debuted #1 on the U.S. iTunes Music Store digital hip-hop/rap charts in addition to charting at #1 in New Zealand, #2 in Australia, #3 in Canada, #9 in Japan, #22 in France, #40 in Germany, and #60 in the United Kingdom.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>The music video for the lead single "Run," premiered on March 8th, 2010, featuring L from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Infinite_(South_Korean_band)" title="Infinite (South Korean band)">Infinite</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
