<p><b>Yaese</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">八重瀬町</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Yaese-chō</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a <a href="/wiki/List_of_towns_in_Japan" title="List of towns in Japan">town</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Shimajiri_District,_Okinawa" title="Shimajiri District, Okinawa">Shimajiri District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Okinawa_Prefecture" title="Okinawa Prefecture">Okinawa Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</p>
<p>Yaese was formed on January 1, 2006 by a merger between the town of <a href="/wiki/Kochinda,_Okinawa" title="Kochinda, Okinawa">Kochinda</a> and the village of <a href="/wiki/Gushikami,_Okinawa" title="Gushikami, Okinawa">Gushikami</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>As of February 2008, Yaese has a population of 26,758;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> with a population density of 994.72 persons per km². The total area of the town of Yaese is 26.90 square kilometres (10.39 sq mi).</p>
