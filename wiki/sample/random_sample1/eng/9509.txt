<p><i><b>Rustic Period</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">야인시대</span>) is a 2002 <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Korean_drama" title="Korean drama">television series</a>. It aired on <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Broadcasting_System" title="Seoul Broadcasting System">SBS</a> on Mondays to Tuesdays at 22:00 for 124 episodes beginning July 29, 2002.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
