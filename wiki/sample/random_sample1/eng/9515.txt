<p><b>Yakovlevsky District</b> is the name of several administrative and municipal districts in <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Yakovlevsky_District,_Belgorod_Oblast" title="Yakovlevsky District, Belgorod Oblast">Yakovlevsky District, Belgorod Oblast</a>, an administrative<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BelgorodO_adm_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-BelgorodO_adm-1">[1]</a></sup> and municipal<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BelgorodO_mun_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-BelgorodO_mun-2">[2]</a></sup> district of <a href="/wiki/Belgorod_Oblast" title="Belgorod Oblast">Belgorod Oblast</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Yakovlevsky_District,_Primorsky_Krai" title="Yakovlevsky District, Primorsky Krai">Yakovlevsky District, Primorsky Krai</a>, an administrative<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PrimorskyK_adm_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-PrimorskyK_adm-3">[3]</a></sup> and municipal<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PrimorskyKYakovlevskyD_mun_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-PrimorskyKYakovlevskyD_mun-4">[4]</a></sup> district of <a href="/wiki/Primorsky_Krai" title="Primorsky Krai">Primorsky Krai</a></li>
</ul>
