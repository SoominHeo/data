<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Education">Education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jacques_Zoon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Following a <a href="/wiki/Gymnasium_(school)" title="Gymnasium (school)">gymnasium</a> education in <a href="/wiki/Alkmaar" title="Alkmaar">Alkmaar</a>, Zoon studied flute at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sweelinck_Conservatorium" title="Sweelinck Conservatorium">Sweelinck Conservatorium</a> in <a href="/wiki/Amsterdam" title="Amsterdam">Amsterdam</a> with <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Koos_Verheul&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Koos Verheul (page does not exist)">Koos Verheul</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Harrie_Starreveld&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Harrie Starreveld (page does not exist)">Harrie Starreveld</a>, graduating with honors. He continued his studies at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Banff_Center_for_the_Arts" title="Banff Center for the Arts">Banff Center for the Arts</a> in Canada, where he took <a href="/wiki/Master_class" title="Master class">master classes</a> with <a href="/wiki/Geoffrey_Gilbert" title="Geoffrey Gilbert">Geoffrey Gilbert</a> and <a href="/wiki/Andr%C3%A1s_Adorj%C3%A1n" title="András Adorján">András Adorján</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Orchestras">Orchestras</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jacques_Zoon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Orchestras">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>As a teenager and during his studies, Zoon played in the Dutch National Youth Orchestra and the <a href="/wiki/European_Union_Youth_Orchestra" title="European Union Youth Orchestra">European Union Youth Orchestra</a>, among others under direction of <a href="/wiki/Claudio_Abbado" title="Claudio Abbado">Claudio Abbado</a> and <a href="/wiki/Leonard_Bernstein" title="Leonard Bernstein">Leonard Bernstein</a>. From 1988 to 1994, Zoon was principal flutist of the <a href="/wiki/Royal_Concertgebouw_Orchestra" title="Royal Concertgebouw Orchestra">Royal Concertgebouw Orchestra</a>, his arrival coinciding with that of conductor <a href="/wiki/Riccardo_Chailly" title="Riccardo Chailly">Riccardo Chailly</a>. With this orchestra he performed flute concertos by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mozart" title="Mozart">Mozart</a>, <a href="/wiki/Andr%C3%A9_Jolivet" title="André Jolivet">André Jolivet</a>, <a href="/wiki/Frank_Martin_(composer)" title="Frank Martin (composer)">Frank Martin</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Sofia_Gubaidulina" title="Sofia Gubaidulina">Sofia Gubaidulina</a>. Until 1997, he also was principal flutist of and frequent soloist with the <a href="/wiki/Chamber_Orchestra_of_Europe" title="Chamber Orchestra of Europe">Chamber Orchestra of Europe</a>, conducted by Claudio Abbado, <a href="/wiki/Bernard_Haitink" title="Bernard Haitink">Bernard Haitink</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nicolaus_Harnoncourt" title="Nicolaus Harnoncourt">Nicolaus Harnoncourt</a>. He played as principal flute with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Berlin_Philharmonic_Orchestra" title="Berlin Philharmonic Orchestra">Berlin Philharmonic Orchestra</a> under the baton of Claudio Abbado. From 1997 to 2001, he was principal flutist of the <a href="/wiki/Boston_Symphony_Orchestra" title="Boston Symphony Orchestra">Boston Symphony Orchestra</a> under <a href="/wiki/Seiji_Ozawa" title="Seiji Ozawa">Seiji Ozawa</a>. In 1998, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Boston_Globe" title="Boston Globe">Boston Globe</a> elected him as "musician of the year". Zoon is a regular principal flutist of the <a href="/wiki/Lucerne_Festival_Orchestra" title="Lucerne Festival Orchestra">Lucerne Festival Orchestra</a>. He has also been principal flutist of the <a href="/wiki/Orchestra_Mozart" title="Orchestra Mozart">Orchestra Mozart</a> since 2004 conducted by Claudio Abbado. He has played as a soloist with orchestras and at festivals in Europe, Japan and the United States. He has made many recordings for multiple labels such as <a href="/wiki/Deutsche_Grammophon" title="Deutsche Grammophon">Deutsche Grammophon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Philips" title="Philips">Philips</a>, <a href="/wiki/Decca_Records" title="Decca Records">Decca</a> etc.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Teaching">Teaching</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jacques_Zoon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Teaching">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Zoon has been professor of flute at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rotterdam_Conservatory" title="Rotterdam Conservatory">Rotterdam Conservatory</a> (1988–1992), <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Indiana_University_(Bloomington)" title="Indiana University (Bloomington)">Indiana University</a> (1994–1997), and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/New_England_Conservatory" title="New England Conservatory">New England Conservatory</a> and <a href="/wiki/Boston_University" title="Boston University">Boston University</a> (1997–2001). Since 2002, he has taught at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Conservatoire_Sup%C3%A9rieur_de_Musique_de_Gen%C3%A8ve" title="Conservatoire Supérieur de Musique de Genève">Conservatoire Supérieur de Musique de Genève</a> and since 2008 at <a href="/wiki/Madrid" title="Madrid">Madrid</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Queen_Sof%C3%ADa_College_of_Music" title="Queen Sofía College of Music">Queen Sofía College of Music</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Instituto_Internacional_de_M%C3%BAsica_de_C%C3%A1mara&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Instituto Internacional de Música de Cámara (page does not exist)">Instituto Internacional de Música de Cámara</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Instrument_maker">Instrument maker</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jacques_Zoon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Instrument maker">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Zoon plays an old wooden French flute using headjoints he manufactures himself. He designed and created a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Traverso" title="Traverso">traverso</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Boehm_system" title="Boehm system">Boehm system</a>, with which he recorded <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bach" title="Bach">Bach</a>'s <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Partita_in_A_minor_for_solo_flute" title="Partita in A minor for solo flute">Partita in A minor for solo flute</a>. He published a better key design for the C-sharp. With the Alkmaar based flute builder Alfred Verhoef he constructed a "push flute", the <i>flautus tremendus</i>, with which he performed the Dutch premiere of Gubaidulina's flute concerto. With the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/William_S._Haynes_Flute_Company" title="William S. Haynes Flute Company">William S. Haynes Flute Company</a> of Boston he developed a new wooden flute (named "the Zoon model").<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
