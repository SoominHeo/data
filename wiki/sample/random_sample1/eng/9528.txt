<p><b>Yahiro Station</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja">八広駅</span><span class="t_nihongo_comma" style="display:none">,</span> <i>Yahiro-eki</i><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>)</span> is a railway station on the <a href="/wiki/Keisei_Oshiage_Line" title="Keisei Oshiage Line">Keisei Oshiage Line</a> in <a href="/wiki/Sumida,_Tokyo" title="Sumida, Tokyo">Sumida, Tokyo</a>, Japan, operated by the private railway operator <a href="/wiki/Keisei_Electric_Railway" title="Keisei Electric Railway">Keisei Electric Railway</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lines">Lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yahiro_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Yahiro Station is served by the 5.7 km <a href="/wiki/Keisei_Oshiage_Line" title="Keisei Oshiage Line">Keisei Oshiage Line</a>, and is located 2.4 km from the starting point of the line at <a href="/wiki/Oshiage_Station" title="Oshiage Station">Oshiage</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-terada2013_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-terada2013-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Station_layout">Station layout</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yahiro_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Station layout">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This station has one <a href="/wiki/Island_platform" title="Island platform">island platform</a> and one <a href="/wiki/Side_platform" title="Side platform">side platform</a> serving three tracks.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yahiro_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
