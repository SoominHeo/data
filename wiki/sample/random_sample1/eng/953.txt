<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="US_Navy_designations">US Navy designations</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=F4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: US Navy designations">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The fourth fighter bought by the US Navy from a company was assigned the designation F4, following by the letter that company had been assigned. The designation system was later changed and the F4H was redesignated F-4.</p>

<li><a href="/wiki/Douglas_F4D_Skyray" title="Douglas F4D Skyray">Douglas F4D Skyray</a>, a 1960s United States Navy fighter aircraft used in the</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Grumman_F4F_Wildcat" title="Grumman F4F Wildcat">Grumman F4F Wildcat</a>, a United States Navy fighter aircraft in World War II</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/McDonnell-Douglas_F-4_Phantom_II" title="McDonnell-Douglas F-4 Phantom II">McDonnell-Douglas F4H/F-4 Phantom II</a>, an American supersonic fighter-bomber</li>
