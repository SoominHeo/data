<p><i><b>The Promise</b></i> was the second studio album released by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/R%26B" title="R&amp;B">R&amp;B</a> duo <a href="/wiki/Fly_to_the_Sky" title="Fly to the Sky">Fly to the Sky</a>. Like the debut album, the album included poppy ballads and upbeat dance tracks. Hits from this album includes "The Promise" and "Maybe God Knows." As a bonus track, the remix of "The Promise" is also included in the album. The album peaked at #2, selling more than 200,000 copies in its first month of release.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> However, it quickly slipped off the charts, falling less than 4,000 copies short of surpassing the chart performance of the duo's debut album. The album features vocal collaboration by <a href="/wiki/Kangta" title="Kangta">Kangta</a> and <a href="/wiki/BoA" title="BoA">BoA</a> and songs written by R&amp;B singer <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kim_Jo-Han" title="Kim Jo-Han">Kim Jo-Han</a>.</p>
