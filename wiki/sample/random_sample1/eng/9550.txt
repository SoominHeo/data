<p><b>Yangdong District</b> is a district of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Guangdong_Province" title="Guangdong Province">Guangdong Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. It is under the administration of <a href="/wiki/Yangjiang" title="Yangjiang">Yangjiang</a> city.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yangdong_District&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Yangdong was the site of <b>Xin Prefecture</b> (<span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%96%B0" title="wikt:新">新</a><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%B7%9E" title="wikt:州">州</a></span>, <i>Xinzhou</i>) under the <a href="/wiki/Song_dynasty" title="Song dynasty">Song</a>.</p>
