<p><b>Yangjae Citizen's Forest (Maeheon) Station</b> is a subway station in Seoul, South Korea, on <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Metropolitan_Subway" title="Seoul Metropolitan Subway">Seoul Metropolitan Subway</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Shinbundang_Line" title="Shinbundang Line">Shinbundang Line</a>. It opened on October 28, 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Naming">Naming</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yangjae_Citizen%27s_Forest_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Naming">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It is named for the nearby <a href="/wiki/Yangjae_Citizens%27_Forest" title="Yangjae Citizens' Forest">Yangjae Citizens' Forest</a> which is located near Yangjae Tollgate on the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Gyeongbu_Highway&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Gyeongbu Highway (page does not exist)">Gyeongbu Highway</a>, the entrance to Seoul City.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Yangjae_Stream">Yangjae Stream</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yangjae_Citizen%27s_Forest_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Yangjae Stream">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Yangjae Stream, or <a href="/wiki/Yangjaecheon" title="Yangjaecheon">Yangjaecheon</a> in Korean, is a 5.5 kilometres (3.4 mi)—long body of water that stretches from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gwanak-san" title="Gwanak-san">Gwanak-san</a> through the southern area of <a href="/wiki/Gangnam_Station" title="Gangnam Station">Gangnam Station</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gangnam-gu" title="Gangnam-gu">Gangnam-gu</a>. There are two swimming areas for kids, a number of stepping stone bridges to cross, and two sites for an ecosystem watch.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-stream_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-stream-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
