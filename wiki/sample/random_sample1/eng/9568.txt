<div class="hatnote" role="note">This is a <a href="/wiki/Chinese_name" title="Chinese name">Chinese name</a>; the <a href="/wiki/Chinese_surname" title="Chinese surname">family name</a> is <a href="/wiki/Yang_(surname)" title="Yang (surname)">Yang</a>.</div>
<p><b>Yang Zuo</b> was a military general serving under the warlord <a href="/wiki/Gongsun_Yuan" title="Gongsun Yuan">Gongsun Yuan</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Three_Kingdoms" title="Three Kingdoms">Three Kingdoms</a> period of <a href="/wiki/History_of_China" title="History of China">Chinese history</a>. In 238, he led a front of troops along with <a href="/wiki/Bei_Yan" title="Bei Yan">Bei Yan</a> during <a href="/wiki/Sima_Yi" title="Sima Yi">Sima Yi</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sima_Yi%27s_Liaodong_campaign" title="Sima Yi's Liaodong campaign">Liaodong campaign</a> against Gongsun. Yang then engaged Sima during a protracted battle, but ended up being lured out by Sima and defeated. He then escaped into Xiangping (襄平), Gongsun's headquarters, and surrendered to Sima when the city was under siege.</p>
