<p><b>Lee Eun-Jin</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko">이은진</span>, born March 17, 1979), better known by her stage name <b>Yangpa</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko">양파</span>, meaning "onion"), is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korean" title="South Korean">South Korean</a> singer.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> She studied at <a href="/wiki/Berklee_College_of_Music" title="Berklee College of Music">Berklee College of Music</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Boston,_Massachusetts" title="Boston, Massachusetts">Boston, Massachusetts</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yangpa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="1990s">1990s</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yangpa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1990s">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Yangpa debuted in 1996 with the album <i>Novice's Love</i> which had the greatest success in her career. Her second <i>I Want to Know</i> album was moderate successful, as was her third album, <i>Addio</i>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2000s">2000s</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yangpa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 2000s">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Although active at the beginning of the decade, Yangpa began to experience health issues, causing her to go on hiatus. She released an album in 2007 titled <i>The Windows Of My Soul</i> and collaborated with the singer Miho,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> but her activities were short lived and she once again went on a break.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2010s">2010s</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yangpa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2010s">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In early 2011, it was announced that Yangpa had signed onto Core Contents Media and would be making her comeback after her four-year absence. Her EP, <i>Elegy Nouveau</i>, was released on March 28, 2011, which contained the self-composed tracks "Bon Appetit" and "그때 그 사람".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> After the concept photos for the album were released, controversy aroused over whether or not the singer had gotten cosmetic surgery. Her agency initially denied the claims, but Yangpa stated in a later interview that she had some work done.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>Yangpa released the song "Sarangeun da Geureongeoraeyo" 사랑은 다 그런거래요" (Love is All the Same) featuring <a href="/wiki/Davichi" title="Davichi">Davichi</a>'s Lee Haeri and <a href="/wiki/F-ve_Dolls" title="F-ve Dolls">F-ve Dolls</a> member Han Na-Yeon (HANNA) on April 4, 2012, and announced that she would be releasing a new album entitled <i>Together</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> Shortly after the songs release, an alternative version of the song titled "Ibyeoreun da Geureongeoraeyo" (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko">이별은 다 그런거래요</span>, "<i>Parting is all the Same</i>") was introduced and featured <a href="/wiki/Speed_(South_Korean_band)" title="Speed (South Korean band)">Speed</a>'s Shin Jong Kook.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>On 22 October 2016, Yangpa particpated in <i>Immortal Song 2</i> and won for this broadcast with 435 points.</p>
