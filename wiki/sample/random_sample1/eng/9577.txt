<p><b>Yang Hyo-Jin</b> (Hangul: 양효진, Hanja: 梁孝眞; born 14 December 1989, Busan) is a South Korean female professional volleyball player. She currently plays for Korean club <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Suwon_Hyundai_Engineering_%26_Construction_Hillstate" title="Suwon Hyundai Engineering &amp; Construction Hillstate">Hyundai Engineering &amp; Construction Hillstate</a>. In the 2012 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Summer_Olympics" title="Summer Olympics">Summer Olympics</a> in London she appeared in 3 matches, including a quarter final victory.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><br/></p>
