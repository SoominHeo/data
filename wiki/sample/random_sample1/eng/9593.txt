<p><b>Amara Miller</b> (born May 4, 2000)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-DOB_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-DOB-1">[1]</a></sup> is an American child actress who earned a Phoenix Film Critics Society Award nomination for "Best Performance by a Youth in a Supporting Role" for her debut role as Scottie King in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Academy_Award" title="Academy Award">Academy Award</a>-winning film <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Descendants_(film)" title="The Descendants (film)">The Descendants</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Miller lives in <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Grove,_California" title="Pacific Grove, California">Pacific Grove, California</a>, with her parents, Ahnalisa and Michael Miller.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-DOB_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-DOB-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
