<p><b>Amanda Fuller</b> (born August 27, 1984) is an American television and film actress.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> She currently portrays Kristin Baxter on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ABC_Television_Network" title="ABC Television Network">ABC</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Last_Man_Standing_(U.S._TV_series)" title="Last Man Standing (U.S. TV series)">Last Man Standing</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Amanda_Fuller&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_career">Early career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Amanda_Fuller&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In '<i><a href="/wiki/%27Til_There_Was_You" title="'Til There Was You">'Til There Was You</a></i>, she played the role of a 13-year-old girl whose mature role was played by <a href="/wiki/Jennifer_Aniston" title="Jennifer Aniston">Jennifer Aniston</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> In <i><a href="/wiki/Askari_(film)" title="Askari (film)">Askari</a></i>, she played opposite <a href="/wiki/Academy_Awards" title="Academy Awards">Oscar</a>-winning actress <a href="/wiki/Marlee_Matlin" title="Marlee Matlin">Marlee Matlin</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Present_career">Present career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Amanda_Fuller&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Present career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Fuller's credits include cult indie film <i><a href="/wiki/Red,_White_%26_Blue_(film)" title="Red, White &amp; Blue (film)">Red, White and Blue</a></i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> and <i><a href="/wiki/Creature_(2011_film)" title="Creature (2011 film)">Creature</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> with appearances in <i><a href="/wiki/Law_%26_Order:_Special_Victims_Unit" title="Law &amp; Order: Special Victims Unit">Law &amp; Order: Special Victims Unit</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/CSI:_Crime_Scene_Investigation" title="CSI: Crime Scene Investigation">CSI: Crime Scene Investigation</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/NCIS_(TV_series)" title="NCIS (TV series)">NCIS</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/7th_Heaven_(TV_series)" title="7th Heaven (TV series)">7th Heaven</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Judging_Amy" title="Judging Amy">Judging Amy</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Boston_Public" title="Boston Public">Boston Public</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Buffy_the_Vampire_Slayer_(TV_series)" title="Buffy the Vampire Slayer (TV series)">Buffy the Vampire Slayer</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/8_Simple_Rules" title="8 Simple Rules">8 Simple Rules</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Division" title="The Division">The Division</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Touched_by_an_Angel" title="Touched by an Angel">Touched by an Angel</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Malcolm_in_the_Middle" title="Malcolm in the Middle">Malcolm in the Middle</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Practice" title="The Practice">The Practice</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/L.A._Doctors" title="L.A. Doctors">L.A. Doctors</a></i>, and <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/That_70s_Show" title="That 70s Show">That 70s Show</a></i>, and a role in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Graphic_adventure_game" title="Graphic adventure game">graphic adventure game</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Frankenstein:_Through_the_Eyes_of_the_Monster" title="Frankenstein: Through the Eyes of the Monster">Frankenstein: Through the Eyes of the Monster</a></i>.</p>
<p>Fuller directed the Los Angeles production of the theatrical play <i>This is our Youth</i> written by <a href="/wiki/Kenneth_Lonergan" title="Kenneth Lonergan">Kenneth Lonergan</a> starring <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tyler_Williams" title="Tyler Williams">Tyler Williams</a> (of television’s <i><a href="/wiki/Everybody_Hates_Chris" title="Everybody Hates Chris">Everybody Hates Chris</a></i>).</p>
<p>Fuller was a recurring guest star on the 2012 season of <i><a href="/wiki/Grey%27s_Anatomy" title="Grey's Anatomy">Grey's Anatomy</a></i> on ABC.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2012, Fuller joined the cast of the ABC sitcom <i><a href="/wiki/Last_Man_Standing_(U.S._TV_series)" title="Last Man Standing (U.S. TV series)">Last Man Standing</a></i> starring <a href="/wiki/Tim_Allen" title="Tim Allen">Tim Allen</a>. She currently portrays the role of eldest daughter Kristin Baxter, replacing <a href="/wiki/Alexandra_Krosney" title="Alexandra Krosney">Alexandra Krosney</a>, who played the role in the show's first season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Amanda_Fuller&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Amanda Fuller graduated from <a href="/wiki/Notre_Dame_High_School_(Sherman_Oaks,_California)" title="Notre Dame High School (Sherman Oaks, California)">Notre Dame High School</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sherman_Oaks,_California" title="Sherman Oaks, California">Sherman Oaks, California</a> in 2002. Fuller currently resides in Los Angeles, California.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p>
