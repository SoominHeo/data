<p><b>1627 (<a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MDCXXVII</a>)</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year" title="Common year">common year</a> <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Friday" title="Common year starting on Friday">starting on Friday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> C) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Monday" title="Common year starting on Monday">common year starting on Monday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> G) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>, the 1627th year of the <a href="/wiki/Common_Era" title="Common Era">Common Era</a> (CE) and <i><a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a></i> (AD) designations, the 627th year of the <a href="/wiki/2nd_millennium" title="2nd millennium">2nd millennium</a>, the 27th year of the <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th century</a>, and the 8th year of the <a href="/wiki/1620s" title="1620s">1620s</a> decade. As of the start of 1627, the Gregorian calendar was 10 days ahead of the Julian calendar, which remained in localized use until 1923.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1627&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="July.E2.80.93December">July–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1627&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: July–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>January – The <a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Dutch</a> ship <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%27t_Gulden_Zeepaert_(schip,_1626)" title="'t Gulden Zeepaert (schip, 1626)">'t Gulden Zeepaert</a></i>, skippered by <a href="/wiki/Fran%C3%A7ois_Thijssen" title="François Thijssen">François Thijssen</a>, makes the first recorded sighting of the coast of <a href="/wiki/South_Australia" title="South Australia">South Australia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_17" title="February 17">February 17</a> – <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> lands the first <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">European</a> settlers on <a href="/wiki/Barbados" title="Barbados">Barbados</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_3" title="March 3">March 3</a> – After the <a href="/wiki/First_Manchu_invasion_of_Korea" title="First Manchu invasion of Korea">First Manchu invasion of Korea</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Joseon" title="Joseon">Joseon</a> dynasty of <a href="/wiki/Korea" title="Korea">Korea</a> becomes a <a href="/wiki/Tributary_state" title="Tributary state">tributary state</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Manchu_people" title="Manchu people">Manchus</a>, but still pays respects to the <a href="/wiki/Ming_dynasty" title="Ming dynasty">Ming dynasty</a> of <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. After rejecting a Manchu alteration to the original diplomatic terms in 1636, the Manchus invade again in <a href="/wiki/1637" title="1637">1637</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_20" title="June 20">June 20</a> – <a href="/wiki/Hinchingbrooke_House" title="Hinchingbrooke House">Hinchingbrooke House</a> is sold by <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Cromwell_(died_1655)" title="Oliver Cromwell (died 1655)">Oliver Cromwell</a> to <a href="/wiki/Sidney_Montagu" title="Sidney Montagu">Sidney Montagu</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_4" title="July 4">July 4</a>–<a href="/wiki/July_19" title="July 19">July 19</a> – <a href="/wiki/Turkish_Abductions" title="Turkish Abductions">Turkish Abductions</a>: <a href="/wiki/Barbary_pirates" title="Barbary pirates">Barbary pirates</a> raid <a href="/wiki/Iceland" title="Iceland">Iceland</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_20" title="July 20">July 20</a>–<a href="/wiki/August_19" title="August 19">August 19</a> – <a href="/wiki/Eighty_Years%27_War" title="Eighty Years' War">Eighty Years' War</a>: <a href="/wiki/Frederick_Henry,_Prince_of_Orange" title="Frederick Henry, Prince of Orange">Frederick Henry, Prince of Orange</a>, lays <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Groenlo_(1627)" title="Siege of Groenlo (1627)">siege to Grol</a>, the last <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spanish</a> stronghold in the eastern <a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Netherlands</a>, and captures it after a 1-month siege.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_22" title="July 22">July 22</a> – The English, under the <a href="/wiki/Duke_of_Buckingham" title="Duke of Buckingham">Duke of Buckingham</a>, invade <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/R%C3%A9_Island" title="Ré Island">Ré Island</a> in support of the <a href="/wiki/Huguenot" title="Huguenot">Huguenots</a> in <a href="/wiki/La_Rochelle" title="La Rochelle">La Rochelle</a>. The invasion does not go well.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_27" title="July 27">July 27</a> – An <a href="/wiki/Earthquake" title="Earthquake">earthquake</a> destroys the cities of <a href="/wiki/San_Severo" title="San Severo">San Severo</a> and <a href="/wiki/Torremaggiore" title="Torremaggiore">Torremaggiore</a> in southern <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>.</li>
<li>September – The <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_La_Rochelle" title="Siege of La Rochelle">Siege of La Rochelle</a> begins.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_20" title="November 20">November 20</a> – <a href="/wiki/Thirty_Years%27_War" title="Thirty Years' War">Thirty Years' War</a>: <a href="/wiki/Bogislaw_XIV,_Duke_of_Pomerania" title="Bogislaw XIV, Duke of Pomerania">Bogislaw XIV, Duke of Pomerania</a>, signs the <a href="/wiki/Capitulation_of_Franzburg" title="Capitulation of Franzburg">Capitulation of Franzburg</a>, in which <a href="/wiki/Pomerania" title="Pomerania">Pomerania</a> is forced to pay for the Imperial army that <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wallenstein" title="Wallenstein">Wallenstein</a> sent to occupy it. Nonetheless, despite the treaty, Pomerania is devastated by the Imperial troops.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_21" title="November 21">November 21</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ferdinand_III,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Ferdinand III, Holy Roman Emperor">Archduke Ferdinand III of Austria</a>, <a href="/wiki/Heir_apparent" title="Heir apparent">heir apparent</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Habsburg_Monarchy" title="Habsburg Monarchy">Habsburg Monarchy</a> and a future Holy Roman Emperor, already <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Hungary" title="Kingdom of Hungary">King of Hungary</a> ascends to be <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Bohemia" title="Kingdom of Bohemia">king</a> of the religiously troubled <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Bohemia" title="Kingdom of Bohemia">Bohemia</a> where his (still living) father's repression of Protestantism has triggered the ongoing <a href="/wiki/Thirty_Years%27_War" title="Thirty Years' War">Thirty Years' War</a> in <a href="/wiki/1618" title="1618">1618</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_28" title="November 28">November 28</a> – <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Polish-Swedish_War_(1625%E2%80%931629)" title="Polish-Swedish War (1625–1629)">Polish-Swedish War</a>: A <a href="/wiki/Polish%E2%80%93Lithuanian_Commonwealth" title="Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth">Polish-Lithuanian fleet</a> defeats a <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Swedish</a> <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Swedish_fleet&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Swedish fleet (page does not exist)">fleet</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Oliwa" title="Battle of Oliwa">Battle of Oliwa</a>.</li>
</ul>
