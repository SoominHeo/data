<p><b>Enping</b>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Postal_map_romanization" title="Postal map romanization">formerly</a> <a href="/wiki/Romanization_of_Chinese" title="Romanization of Chinese">romanized</a> as <b>Yanping</b>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[a]</a></sup> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/County_(China)" title="County (China)">county</a>-level <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=County-level&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="County-level (page does not exist)">city</a> in <a href="/wiki/Guangdong" title="Guangdong">Guangdong</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>, administered as part of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Prefecture_(China)" title="Prefecture (China)">prefecture</a>-level <a href="/wiki/Prefecture-level_city" title="Prefecture-level city">city</a> of <a href="/wiki/Jiangmen" title="Jiangmen">Jiangmen</a>.</p>
<p>Enping administers an area of 1,698 km<sup>2</sup> (656 sq mi) and had an estimated population of 460,000 in 2005. Its <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chinese_diaspora" title="Chinese diaspora">diaspora</a> accounts for around 420,000 <a href="/wiki/Overseas_Chinese" title="Overseas Chinese">overseas Chinese</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (October 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> The area around Enping is known for its many <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hot_springs" title="Hot springs">hot springs</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Enping&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Enping is located in southwest <a href="/wiki/Guangdong" title="Guangdong">Guangdong</a>, at the edge of the <a href="/wiki/Pearl_River_Delta" title="Pearl River Delta">Pearl River Delta</a> and beside the <a href="/wiki/South_China_Sea" title="South China Sea">South China Sea</a>. Enping borders <a href="/wiki/Kaiping" title="Kaiping">Kaiping</a> to the northeast and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Yangjing&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Yangjing (page does not exist)">Yangjing</a> to the southwest.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Enping&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b>Enping<span class="nowrap"> </span>County</b> was established in <span class="smallcaps"><span style="font-variant: small-caps; text-transform: lowercase;">AD</span></span><span class="nowrap"> </span>220. Under the <a href="/wiki/Qing_dynasty" title="Qing dynasty">Qing</a>, it made up part of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fu_(administrative_subdivision)" title="Fu (administrative subdivision)">commandery</a> of <a href="/wiki/Zhaoqing" title="Zhaoqing">Zhaoqing</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FOOTNOTEBolton_.26_al.1941.5Bhttps:.2F.2Fbooks.google.com.hk.2Fbooks.3Fid.3D6irEoGgDrm4C.26pg.3DPA262_262.5D_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-FOOTNOTEBolton_.26_al.1941.5Bhttps:.2F.2Fbooks.google.com.hk.2Fbooks.3Fid.3D6irEoGgDrm4C.26pg.3DPA262_262.5D-1">[1]</a></sup> and was one of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Four_Counties" title="Four Counties">Four Counties</a> responsible for much of the early <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chinese_diaspora" title="Chinese diaspora">Chinese diaspora</a> from Guangdong in the 19th century. Many <a href="/wiki/Overseas_Chinese" title="Overseas Chinese">overseas Chinese</a> trace their ancestry to Enping, particularly among the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chinese_in_Venezuela" title="Chinese in Venezuela">Chinese in Venezuela</a>. Migrants from Enping and their families make up about 200,000 of the country's estimated 400,000 Chinese. Emigration to Venezuela occurred primarily in the decades including and following the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/World_Wars" title="World Wars">World Wars</a>, with the largest batch leaving at the end of the <a href="/wiki/Cultural_Revolution" title="Cultural Revolution">Cultural Revolution</a> in the late 1970s. Enping was made a <a href="/wiki/County-level_city" title="County-level city">county-level city</a> in 1994.</p>
