<p><i><b>Mother's Garden</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">엄마의 정원</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Eomma-ui Jeong-won</span></i>) is a 2014 South Korean daily television <a href="/wiki/Korean_drama" title="Korean drama">drama</a> starring <a href="/wiki/Jeong_Yu-mi_(actress_born_1984)" title="Jeong Yu-mi (actress born 1984)">Jeong Yu-mi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Choi_Tae-joon" title="Choi Tae-joon">Choi Tae-joon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Uhm_Hyun-kyung" title="Uhm Hyun-kyung">Uhm Hyun-kyung</a>, <a href="/wiki/Go_Se-won" title="Go Se-won">Go Se-won</a> and <a href="/wiki/Go_Doo-shim" title="Go Doo-shim">Go Doo-shim</a>. It aired on <a href="/wiki/Munhwa_Broadcasting_Corporation" title="Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation">MBC</a> from March 17 to September 18, 2014 on Mondays to Fridays at 20:50 for 126 episodes.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mother%27s_Garden&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Seo Yoon-joo, the daughter of a family who owns a <a href="/wiki/Conglomerate_(company)" title="Conglomerate (company)">conglomerate</a>, is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Veterinarian" title="Veterinarian">veterinarian</a> who works specifically with horses. Yoon-joo has a cheerful and straightforward personality, and she is in love with Cha Ki-joon, the son of another rich family. Though Ki-joon is a second-generation <a href="/wiki/Chaebol" title="Chaebol">chaebol</a>, he secretly dreams of becoming a chef.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
