<p><b>Ennis Esmer</b> (born December 29, 1978) is a Canadian actor and comedian.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ennis_Esmer&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Esmer was born in <a href="/wiki/Ankara" title="Ankara">Ankara</a>, <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>, before moving to <a href="/wiki/Toronto" title="Toronto">Toronto, Ontario</a>, Canada at the age of 3.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-CTV_Biography_of_Ennis_Esmer_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-CTV_Biography_of_Ennis_Esmer-1">[1]</a></sup> He is a graduate of the fine arts program at <a href="/wiki/Earl_Haig_Secondary_School" title="Earl Haig Secondary School">Earl Haig Secondary School</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-school_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-school-2">[2]</a></sup> and studied theatre and acting at <a href="/wiki/York_University" title="York University">York University</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-school_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-school-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ennis_Esmer&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Esmer first came to prominence as the host of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/CKXT-TV" title="CKXT-TV">Toronto 1</a>'s short-lived variety series <i><a href="/wiki/The_Toronto_Show" title="The Toronto Show">The Toronto Show</a></i> in 2003.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-school_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-school-2">[2]</a></sup> He has had roles in television series such as <i><a href="/wiki/The_Listener_(TV_series)" title="The Listener (TV series)">The Listener</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-school_2-3"><a href="#cite_note-school-2">[2]</a></sup> <i><a href="/wiki/Wipeout_Canada" title="Wipeout Canada">Wipeout Canada</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-school_2-4"><a href="#cite_note-school-2">[2]</a></sup> <i><a href="/wiki/Covert_Affairs" title="Covert Affairs">Covert Affairs</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-la_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-la-3">[3]</a></sup> <i><a href="/wiki/Billable_Hours" title="Billable Hours">Billable Hours</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-school_2-5"><a href="#cite_note-school-2">[2]</a></sup> <i><a href="/wiki/The_L.A._Complex" title="The L.A. Complex">The L.A. Complex</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-la_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-la-3">[3]</a></sup> <i><a href="/wiki/Blindspot_(TV_series)" title="Blindspot (TV series)">Blindspot</a></i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-blind_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-blind-4">[4]</a></sup> and <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Red_Oaks_(TV_series)" title="Red Oaks (TV series)">Red Oaks</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-blind_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-blind-4">[4]</a></sup> as well as films such as <i><a href="/wiki/Young_People_Fucking" title="Young People Fucking">Young People Fucking</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-school_2-6"><a href="#cite_note-school-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
