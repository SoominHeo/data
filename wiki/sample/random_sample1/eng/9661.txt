<p><b>Paraíba</b> (born 12 January 1992<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>) is a <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazilian</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> forward who currently plays for <a href="/wiki/Bucheon_FC_1995" title="Bucheon FC 1995">Bucheon FC</a> in the <a href="/wiki/K_League_Challenge" title="K League Challenge">K League Challenge</a>.</p>
