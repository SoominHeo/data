<p><b>Edward August Hennig</b> (October 13, 1879 – August 28, 1960) was an <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Artistic_gymnastics" title="Artistic gymnastics">gymnast</a> who competed in the <a href="/wiki/1904_Summer_Olympics" title="1904 Summer Olympics">1904 Summer Olympics</a>.</p>
<p>He died in <a href="/wiki/Summit_County,_Ohio" title="Summit County, Ohio">Summit County, Ohio</a>.</p>
<p>Hennig won two gold medals, one of them at <a href="/wiki/Gymnastics_at_the_1904_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_club_swinging" title="Gymnastics at the 1904 Summer Olympics – Men's club swinging">club swinging</a>. In the horizontal bar event he and his countryman <a href="/wiki/Anton_Heida" title="Anton Heida">Anton Heida</a> had the same score and the gold medal was shared between them.</p>
<p>He also competed in the <a href="/wiki/Gymnastics_at_the_1904_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_pommel_horse" title="Gymnastics at the 1904 Summer Olympics – Men's pommel horse">pommel horse event</a> without winning a medal. In the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gymnastics_at_the_1904_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_all-around" title="Gymnastics at the 1904 Summer Olympics – Men's all-around">all-around event</a> he finished 50th, in the <a href="/wiki/Gymnastics_at_the_1904_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_team" title="Gymnastics at the 1904 Summer Olympics – Men's team">team competition</a> he was a member of the <i>Turnverein Vorwärts (Cleveland)</i> which finished 13th. In the <a href="/wiki/Gymnastics_at_the_1904_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_triathlon" title="Gymnastics at the 1904 Summer Olympics – Men's triathlon">gymnastics triathlon</a> he finished 59th and in the <a href="/wiki/Athletics_at_the_1904_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_triathlon" title="Athletics at the 1904 Summer Olympics – Men's triathlon">athletics triathlon</a> he finished 36th.</p>
