<p>The name Edwin means "rich friend". It comes from the <a href="/wiki/Old_English" title="Old English">Old English</a> elements "ead" (rich, blessed) and "wine" (friend).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The original <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Anglo-Saxon" title="Anglo-Saxon">Anglo-Saxon</a> form is <b>Eadwine</b>, which is also found for Anglo-Saxon figures.</p>
