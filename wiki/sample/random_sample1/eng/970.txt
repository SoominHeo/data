<p><b>FC Villa Clara</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Cuba" title="Cuba">Cuban</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> club based in <a href="/wiki/Santa_Clara,_Cuba" title="Santa Clara, Cuba">Santa Clara</a>, <a href="/wiki/Villa_Clara_Province" title="Villa Clara Province">Villa Clara Province</a>, which currently play Cuban first division.</p>
