<p>The <b>Emirates Towers</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar" xml:lang="ar">أبراج الإمارات</span>‎‎) is a building complex in Dubai that contains the <a href="/wiki/Emirates_Office_Tower" title="Emirates Office Tower">Emirates Office Tower</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jumeirah_Emirates_Towers_Hotel" title="Jumeirah Emirates Towers Hotel">Jumeirah Emirates Towers Hotel</a>. The two towers, which rise to 355 m (1,165 ft) and 309 m (1,014 ft), respectively, stand as the 42nd <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-History_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-History-4">[4]</a></sup> and 100th <a href="/wiki/List_of_tallest_buildings" title="List of tallest buildings">tallest buildings in the world</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> The two towers are connected by a 9,000 m² (96,875 ft²) two-storey retail complex known as "The Boulevard". The Emirates Towers complex is located on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sheikh_Zayed_Road" title="Sheikh Zayed Road">Sheikh Zayed Road</a> in <a href="/wiki/Dubai" title="Dubai">Dubai</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_Arab_Emirates" title="United Arab Emirates">United Arab Emirates</a>, and is a symbol of the city of <a href="/wiki/Dubai" title="Dubai">Dubai</a>. The Emirates Towers was constructed by the construction wing of <a href="/wiki/Al_Ghurair_Investment" title="Al Ghurair Investment">Al Ghurair Investment</a> group.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p>
<p>A curiosity of the design is that the towers have a similar number of floors; the taller office tower actually contains 54 floors, while the hotel tower contains 56 floors. This is because the individual floor heights of the office tower are greater than that of the hotel.</p>
<p>The Emirates Towers complex is set in over 570,000 m² (42 <a href="/wiki/Acre" title="Acre">acres</a>) of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gardens" title="Gardens">gardens</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Lake" title="Lake">lakes</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Waterfalls" title="Waterfalls">waterfalls</a> and public seating areas. There is <a href="/wiki/Parking" title="Parking">parking</a> space for up to 1,800 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Automobile" title="Automobile">cars</a>.</p>
