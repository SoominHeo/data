<p><b>Washington Emilio MacEachen Vásquez</b> (born 4 May 1992) is a professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">association football</a> player who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">defender</a> for <a href="/wiki/Uruguayan_Primera_Divisi%C3%B3n" title="Uruguayan Primera División">Uruguayan Primera División</a> club <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/CA_Pe%C3%B1arol" title="CA Peñarol">Peñarol</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Emilio_MacEachen&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Emilio came through the <a href="/wiki/Youth_system" title="Youth system">youth system</a> at Uruguay’s most successful club, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/C.A._Pe%C3%B1arol" title="C.A. Peñarol">C.A. Peñarol</a>, and was promoted to the first team in the <a href="/wiki/2010%E2%80%9311_Uruguayan_Primera_Divisi%C3%B3n_season" title="2010–11 Uruguayan Primera División season">2010-11 Primera División season</a>, for which he was assigned squad number 2. He made seven appearances in this season, one of which was as a substitute. His debut came in a 2-0 win against <a href="/wiki/C.A._Cerro" title="C.A. Cerro">Cerro</a> on 13 March 2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Emilio was also a squad member for Peñarol’s 2011 <a href="/wiki/Copa_Libertadores" title="Copa Libertadores">Copa Libertadores</a> campaign, in which the club reached the final. He was an unused substitute in six 2011 Libertadores matches, including the first leg of the final against <a href="/wiki/Santos_FC" title="Santos FC">Santos</a> of <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
