<p><b>Everland Resort</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">에버랜드 리조트</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Ebeoraendeu Rijoteu</span></i>) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Theme_park" title="Theme park">theme park</a> and vacation resort located in <a href="/wiki/Yongin" title="Yongin">Yongin</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gyeonggi-do" title="Gyeonggi-do">Gyeonggi-do</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. It is owned and operated by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Samsung_Everland" title="Samsung Everland">Samsung Everland</a>. The resort opened on April, 1976, as a single theme park (<a href="/wiki/Everland" title="Everland">Everland</a>), but developed into a resort with water parks, hostels, speedways, golf course and museums.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Attractions">Attractions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Everland_Resort&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Attractions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Everland Resort has two main entertainment sections: <a href="/wiki/Everland" title="Everland">Everland</a> and <a href="/wiki/Caribbean_Bay" title="Caribbean Bay">Caribbean Bay</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Units">Units</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Everland_Resort&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Units">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Everland">Everland</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Everland_Resort&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Everland">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Everland" title="Everland">Everland</a></div>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Caribbean_Bay">Caribbean Bay</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Everland_Resort&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Caribbean Bay">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Caribbean_Bay" title="Caribbean Bay">Caribbean Bay</a></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Caribbean_Bay" title="Caribbean Bay">Caribbean Bay</a> is <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>'s largest (2010) <a href="/wiki/Water_park" title="Water park">water park</a>. The outdoor zone is not open all year due to weather conditions (Winter and Rainy Seasons). The aquatic center(inside) opens all year.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-visitseoul_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-visitseoul-1">[1]</a></sup> It is divided into 5 distinct zones.</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Wild_River">Wild River</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Everland_Resort&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Wild River">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Wild River (opened in summer 2008) is a zone for more advanced water play. It has 6 water tube slides including the Tower Bumerango.</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Sea_Wave">Sea Wave</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Everland_Resort&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Sea Wave">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Main attraction in the Sea Wave zone is the Wave Pool. It generates 2.4 metres (7 ft 10 in) artificial waves. It also has a sandy rest zone.</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Aquatic_Center">Aquatic Center</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Everland_Resort&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Aquatic Center">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Aquatic Center is an indoor zone in Caribbean Bay. It has several normal pools, 3 water slides, indoor wave pool, spas, and saunas. It is open all year.</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Fortress">Fortress</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Everland_Resort&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Fortress">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Fortress is a water-themed ride. It has streaming pool (called "Lazy Pool"), a Surfing Ride, an Adventure pool and spas.</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Bay_Slide">Bay Slide</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Everland_Resort&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Bay Slide">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Bay Slide is a water ride located within the park. It has 6 tube rides and 3 water bobsleighs.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Everland_Speedway">Everland Speedway</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Everland_Resort&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Everland Speedway">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Everland Speedway is South Korea's first racing track. The speedway has held racing events of various scales. It also gives opportunities to experience and enjoy race car driving for ordinary people with no previous racing experience.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Home_Bridge">Home Bridge</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Everland_Resort&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Home Bridge">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Home Bridge is a hostel at Everland Resort.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Glen_Rose">Glen Rose</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Everland_Resort&amp;action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Glen Rose">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Glen Rose is a golf course at Everland Resort.</p>
