<p><b>Ebbe Schwartz</b> (3 May 1901 – 19 October 1964) was a <a href="/wiki/Denmark" title="Denmark">Danish</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football</a> administrator.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ebbe_Schwartz&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>From 1950 until his death in 1964 he was president of the <a href="/wiki/Danish_Football_Association" title="Danish Football Association">Danish Football Association</a> (the DBU).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-dbu-past-pres_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-dbu-past-pres-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li>He served as <a href="/wiki/UEFA" title="UEFA">UEFA</a> President (1954-1962).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-uefa_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-uefa-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-manzenreiter_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-manzenreiter-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li>From 1962 until 1964 he was a member of the executive committee and vice-president of the world football federation <a href="/wiki/FIFA" title="FIFA">FIFA</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-uefa_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-uefa-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
