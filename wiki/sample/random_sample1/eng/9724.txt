<p><b>Evelyne Leu</b> (born 7 July 1976) is a former <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Swiss</a> <a href="/wiki/Freestyle_skiing" title="Freestyle skiing">freestyle skier</a>.</p>
<p>She won a Gold medal in Aerial skiing at the <a href="/wiki/Freestyle_skiing_at_the_2006_Winter_Olympics#Women.27s_Aerials" title="Freestyle skiing at the 2006 Winter Olympics">2006 Winter Olympics</a>. However, she failed to reach the Finals at the <a href="/wiki/Freestyle_skiing_at_the_2010_Winter_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Women%27s_aerials" title="Freestyle skiing at the 2010 Winter Olympics – Women's aerials">2010 Winter Olympics</a> when she crashed in her second jump during qualifying.</p>
<p>She retired in April 2010.</p>
