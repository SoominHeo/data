<div class="hatnote" role="note">The native form of this <a href="/wiki/Personal_name" title="Personal name">personal name</a> is <i>Eszenyi Enikő</i>. This article uses <a href="/wiki/Personal_name#Western_name_order" title="Personal name">Western name order</a> when mentioning individuals.</div>
<p><b>Enikő Eszenyi</b> (born <a href="/wiki/Csenger" title="Csenger">Csenger</a>, January 11, 1961) is a <a href="/wiki/Hungary" title="Hungary">Hungarian</a> actress. She appeared in 1991's <i><a href="/wiki/Paths_of_Death_and_Angels" title="Paths of Death and Angels">Paths of Death and Angels</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-festival-cannes.com_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-festival-cannes.com-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
