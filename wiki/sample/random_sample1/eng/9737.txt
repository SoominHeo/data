<p>The <b>Estadio Pedro Bidegain</b> (<small>Spanish pronunciation: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Spanish" title="Help:IPA for Spanish">[ˈpeðɾo βiðeˈɣain]</a></span>), more often known as <b>El Nuevo Gasómetro</b>, is the home stadium of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Club_Atl%C3%A9tico_San_Lorenzo_de_Almagro" title="Club Atlético San Lorenzo de Almagro">San Lorenzo</a> football club in <a href="/wiki/Buenos_Aires" title="Buenos Aires">Buenos Aires</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
<p>San Lorenzo's old ground, the historic <i><a href="/wiki/Estadio_Gas%C3%B3metro" title="Estadio Gasómetro">Viejo Gasometro</a></i> in nearby <a href="/wiki/Boedo" title="Boedo">Boedo</a> was expropriated by the military government in 1979.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (March 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<p>The stadium is located in <a href="/wiki/Bajo_Flores" title="Bajo Flores">Bajo Flores</a> but San Lorenzo fans wish to return to their original home (Viejo Gasómetro), and won a court action saying that <a href="/wiki/Carrefour" title="Carrefour">Carrefour</a> should sell back the land that their supermarket is now built on.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
