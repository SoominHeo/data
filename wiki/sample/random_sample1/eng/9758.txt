<p><b>Société Airlinair</b> is a French <a href="/wiki/Regional_airline" title="Regional airline">regional airline</a> based in <a href="/wiki/Rungis" title="Rungis">Rungis</a>, <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> operating scheduled regional flights (some of which are on behalf of <a href="/wiki/Air_France" title="Air France">Air France</a>), and <a href="/wiki/Aircraft_lease" title="Aircraft lease">aircraft lease</a> services.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FI_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-FI-2">[2]</a></sup> The airline has set up bases at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Paris-Orly_Airport" title="Paris-Orly Airport">Paris-Orly Airport</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lyon-Saint_Exup%C3%A9ry_Airport" title="Lyon-Saint Exupéry Airport">Lyon-Saint Exupéry Airport</a>. As an Air France regional partner, it also offers flights from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Paris-Charles_de_Gaulle_Airport" title="Paris-Charles de Gaulle Airport">Paris-Charles de Gaulle Airport</a></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Airlinair&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The airline was established in 1998 and started operations in May 1999. It was founded by four principal shareholders including <a href="/wiki/Lionel_Gu%C3%A9rin" title="Lionel Guérin">Lionel Guérin</a> and is owned by private investors (80.5%) and <a href="/wiki/Brit_Air" title="Brit Air">Brit Air</a> (19.5%).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FI_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-FI-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Since 31 March 2013, all Airlinair flights are operated under the name <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/HOP!_(airline)" title="HOP! (airline)">HOP!</a>, the new Air France's regional brand.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
