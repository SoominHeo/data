<p><b>Aimee Garcia</b> (born November 28, 1978) is an <a href="/wiki/Americans" title="Americans">American</a> actress. She is known for her television roles as <a href="/wiki/List_of_George_Lopez_characters#Veronica_Palmero" title="List of George Lopez characters">Veronica Palmero</a> on the ABC sitcom <i><a href="/wiki/George_Lopez_(TV_series)" title="George Lopez (TV series)">George Lopez</a></i>, Yvonne Sanchez on the CBS <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Period_drama" title="Period drama">period drama</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Vegas_(TV_series)" title="Vegas (TV series)">Vegas</a></i>, and <a href="/wiki/List_of_Dexter_characters#Jamie_Batista" title="List of Dexter characters">Jamie Batista</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Showtime_(TV_network)" title="Showtime (TV network)">Showtime</a> drama <i><a href="/wiki/Dexter_(TV_series)" title="Dexter (TV series)">Dexter</a></i>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Aimee_Garcia&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Garcia was born in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chicago,_Illinois" title="Chicago, Illinois">Chicago, Illinois</a>, to a <a href="/wiki/Mexicans" title="Mexicans">Mexican</a> mother from <a href="/wiki/Pachuca" title="Pachuca">Pachuca</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Ricans" title="Puerto Ricans">Puerto Rican</a> father from <a href="/wiki/San_Juan,_Puerto_Rico" title="San Juan, Puerto Rico">San Juan</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>She was cast as Maria on <a href="/wiki/The_WB" title="The WB">The WB</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Greetings_from_Tucson" title="Greetings from Tucson">Greetings from Tucson</a></i> and appeared in the <i><a href="/wiki/Global_Frequency" title="Global Frequency">Global Frequency</a></i> television pilot as Aleph. She had a role in the <a href="/wiki/Disney_Channel" title="Disney Channel">Disney Channel</a> original movie <i><a href="/wiki/Cadet_Kelly" title="Cadet Kelly">Cadet Kelly</a></i> (2002), and co-starred, as Lydia, alongside <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Anderson" title="Anthony Anderson">Anthony Anderson</a> in <a href="/wiki/The_WB" title="The WB">The WB</a> sitcom <i><a href="/wiki/All_About_the_Andersons" title="All About the Andersons">All About the Andersons</a></i>. In 2006, she began appearing on the series <i><a href="/wiki/George_Lopez_(TV_series)" title="George Lopez (TV series)">George Lopez</a></i>, playing his niece.</p>
<p>Garcia starred alongside <a href="/wiki/Jessica_Simpson" title="Jessica Simpson">Jessica Simpson</a> in the comedy-drama <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Major_Movie_Star" title="Major Movie Star">Major Movie Star</a></i> (2008). She also played a brief part in the movie <i><a href="/wiki/D-War" title="D-War">D-War</a></i> (2007) and narrated the <a href="/wiki/Adam_Sandler" title="Adam Sandler">Adam Sandler</a> film <i><a href="/wiki/Spanglish_(film)" title="Spanglish (film)">Spanglish</a></i> (2004).</p>
<p>From 2009 to 2010, she was in the cast of the <a href="/wiki/Peter_Berg" title="Peter Berg">Peter Berg</a> NBC medical drama <i><a href="/wiki/Trauma_(TV_series)" title="Trauma (TV series)">Trauma</a></i>, as EMT helicopter pilot Marisa Benez.</p>
<p>Garcia spent three years on <a href="/wiki/Showtime_(TV_network)" title="Showtime (TV network)">Showtime</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Dexter_(TV_series)" title="Dexter (TV series)">Dexter</a></i> as Jamie Batista, for which she was nominated for a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/SAG_Award" title="SAG Award">SAG Award</a> for Best Ensemble in Dramatic Television Series.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
<p>She played Dr. Jae Kim in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/MGM" title="MGM">MGM</a>'s reboot film, <i><a href="/wiki/RoboCop_(2014_film)" title="RoboCop (2014 film)">RoboCop</a></i> (2014).</p>
<p>In 2016, Garcia was added to the main cast for Season 2 of the <a href="/wiki/Fox_Broadcasting_Company" title="Fox Broadcasting Company">Fox</a> TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Lucifer_(TV_series)" title="Lucifer (TV series)">Lucifer</a></i>, playing LAPD forensic scientist Ella Lopez.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
