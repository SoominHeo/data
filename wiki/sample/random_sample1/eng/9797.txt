<p><b>Egídio de Araújo Pereira Júnior</b>, or simply <b>Egídio</b> (born June 16, 1986 in <a href="/wiki/Rio_de_Janeiro" title="Rio de Janeiro">Rio de Janeiro</a>), is a <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazilian</a> <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)#Full-back" title="Defender (association football)">left back</a>. He currently plays for <a href="/wiki/Sociedade_Esportiva_Palmeiras" title="Sociedade Esportiva Palmeiras">Palmeiras</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eg%C3%ADdio_Pereira_J%C3%BAnior&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Paran.C3.A1">Paraná</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eg%C3%ADdio_Pereira_J%C3%BAnior&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Paraná">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Egídio had a brief loan spell with <a href="/wiki/Paran%C3%A1_Clube" title="Paraná Clube">Paraná</a> in early 2007 and another loan to <a href="/wiki/Esporte_Clube_Juventude" title="Esporte Clube Juventude">Juventude</a> in 2008, this time playing in the <a href="/wiki/Campeonato_Brasileiro_S%C3%A9rie_B" title="Campeonato Brasileiro Série B">Brazilian Série B</a>.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Figueirense">Figueirense</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eg%C3%ADdio_Pereira_J%C3%BAnior&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Figueirense">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On May 26, 2009 <a href="/wiki/Clube_de_Regatas_do_Flamengo" title="Clube de Regatas do Flamengo">Flamengo</a> loaned Egídio to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Figueirense_Futebol_Clube" title="Figueirense Futebol Clube">Figueirense</a>, to play in the <a href="/wiki/Campeonato_Brasileiro_S%C3%A9rie_B" title="Campeonato Brasileiro Série B">Brazilian Série B</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Cruzeiro">Cruzeiro</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eg%C3%ADdio_Pereira_J%C3%BAnior&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Cruzeiro">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Egídio played for Cruzeiro from 2013 to 2014, winning the Brazilian league title twice during this period.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Dnipro">Dnipro</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eg%C3%ADdio_Pereira_J%C3%BAnior&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Dnipro">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 6 January 2015 Egídio joined Ukrainian club <a href="/wiki/FC_Dnipro_Dnipropetrovsk" title="FC Dnipro Dnipropetrovsk">Dnipro</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
