<p><b>1655 (<a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MDCLV</a>)</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year" title="Common year">common year</a> <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Friday" title="Common year starting on Friday">starting on Friday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> C) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Monday" title="Common year starting on Monday">common year starting on Monday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> G) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>, the 1655th year of the <a href="/wiki/Common_Era" title="Common Era">Common Era</a> (CE) and <i><a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a></i> (AD) designations, the 655th year of the <a href="/wiki/2nd_millennium" title="2nd millennium">2nd millennium</a>, the 55th year of the <a href="/wiki/17th_century" title="17th century">17th century</a>, and the 6th year of the <a href="/wiki/1650s" title="1650s">1650s</a> decade. As of the start of 1655, the Gregorian calendar was 10 days ahead of the Julian calendar, which remained in localized use until 1923.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93June">January–June</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1655&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–June">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_5" title="January 5">January 5</a> – <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Go-Sai" title="Emperor Go-Sai">Emperor Go-Sai</a> ascends the throne of <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_16" title="February 16">February 16</a> – Dutch Grand Pensionary advisor <a href="/wiki/Johan_de_Witt" title="Johan de Witt">Johan de Witt</a> marries Wendela Bicker.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_8" title="March 8">March 8</a> – <a href="/wiki/John_Casor" title="John Casor">John Casor</a> became the first legally recognized slave as a result of a civil case in what was to be the United States.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_25" title="March 25">March 25</a> – <a href="/wiki/Saturn" title="Saturn">Saturn</a>'s largest moon, <a href="/wiki/Titan_(moon)" title="Titan (moon)">Titan</a>, is discovered by <a href="/wiki/Christiaan_Huygens" title="Christiaan Huygens">Christiaan Huygens</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_4" title="April 4">April 4</a> – Battle of <a href="/wiki/Ghar_al_Milh" title="Ghar al Milh">Porto Farina</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tunis" title="Tunis">Tunis</a>: English admiral <a href="/wiki/Robert_Blake_(admiral)" title="Robert Blake (admiral)">Robert Blake</a>'s fleet defeats the <a href="/wiki/Barbary_Coast" title="Barbary Coast">Barbary</a> pirates.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_7" title="April 7">April 7</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Alexander_VII" title="Pope Alexander VII">Pope Alexander VII</a>, born Fabio Chigi, succeeds <a href="/wiki/Pope_Innocent_X" title="Pope Innocent X">Pope Innocent X</a> as the 237th <a href="/wiki/Pope" title="Pope">pope</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_24" title="April 24">April 24</a> – The Easter Massacre of the <a href="/wiki/Waldensians" title="Waldensians">Waldensians</a> by <a href="/wiki/Charles_Emmanuel_II,_Duke_of_Savoy" title="Charles Emmanuel II, Duke of Savoy">Charles Emmanuel II, Duke of Savoy</a> slaughters 1,500 men, women, and children, as memorialized in a <a href="/wiki/On_the_Late_Massacre_in_Piedmont" title="On the Late Massacre in Piedmont">poem</a>; <a href="/wiki/Pope_Francis" title="Pope Francis">Pope Francis</a> apologizes in 2015.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_26" title="April 26">April 26</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Dutch_West_India_Company" title="Dutch West India Company">Dutch West India Company</a> denies <a href="/wiki/Peter_Stuyvesant" title="Peter Stuyvesant">Peter Stuyvesant</a>'s request to exclude Jews from <a href="/wiki/New_Amsterdam" title="New Amsterdam">New Amsterdam</a> (<a href="/wiki/Manhattan" title="Manhattan">Manhattan</a>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_28" title="April 28">April 28</a> – <a href="/wiki/Robert_Blake_(admiral)" title="Robert Blake (admiral)">Admiral Blake</a> severely damages the arsenal of the <a href="/wiki/Bey" title="Bey">Bey</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tunis" title="Tunis">Tunis</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_10" title="May 10">May 10</a> – English troops land on <a href="/wiki/Jamaica" title="Jamaica">Jamaica</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_13" title="June 13">June 13</a> – Adriana Nooseman-van de Bergh becomes the first actress in Amsterdam theater.</li>
</ul>
