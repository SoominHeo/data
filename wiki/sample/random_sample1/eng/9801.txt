<p><b>Équateur Province</b> is one of the 26 <a href="/wiki/Provinces_of_the_Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo" title="Provinces of the Democratic Republic of the Congo">provinces of the Democratic Republic of the Congo</a>. Specified under Article 2 of the country's <a href="/wiki/Constitution_of_the_Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo" title="Constitution of the Democratic Republic of the Congo">2006 Constitution</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>The new province was finally created in 2015 from <a href="/wiki/%C3%89quateur_District" title="Équateur District">Équateur District</a> and the independently administered city of <a href="/wiki/Mbandaka" title="Mbandaka">Mbandaka</a>. Both were part of the larger pre-2015 <a href="/wiki/%C3%89quateur_(former_province)" title="Équateur (former province)">former Équateur province</a>.</p>
