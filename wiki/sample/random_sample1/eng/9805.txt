<p>The <b>President of the Republic of Ecuador</b> (<a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a>: <span lang="es"><i>Presidente de la República del Ecuador</i></span>) serves as both the <a href="/wiki/Head_of_state" title="Head of state">head of state</a> and <a href="/wiki/Head_of_government" title="Head of government">head of government</a> of <a href="/wiki/Ecuador" title="Ecuador">Ecuador</a>, is the highest political office in the country as the head of the executive branch of government. As per the <a href="/wiki/2008_Constitution_of_Ecuador" title="2008 Constitution of Ecuador">current Constitution</a>, the President can serve two four-year terms. Prior to that, the president could only serve one four-year term. The current President of Ecuador is <a href="/wiki/Rafael_Correa" title="Rafael Correa">Rafael Correa</a>, first elected in <a href="/wiki/Ecuadorian_general_election,_2006" title="Ecuadorian general election, 2006">2006</a>. Correa was re-elected to a second term in <a href="/wiki/Ecuadorian_general_election,_2009" title="Ecuadorian general election, 2009">2009</a>, and to a third term in <a href="/wiki/Ecuadorian_general_election,_2013" title="Ecuadorian general election, 2013">2013</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=President_of_Ecuador&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Presidency of Ecuador has been marked by periods of instability, causing the office to change presidents frequently throughout the <a href="/wiki/History_of_Ecuador" title="History of Ecuador">history of the country</a>. At least five times, the duties of the president have been charged to a provisional government or a military junta. Often, the office has been left to an interim or acting president, many of whom would go on to become president. The President who has served the most terms in office is <a href="/wiki/Jos%C3%A9_Mar%C3%ADa_Velasco_Ibarra" title="José María Velasco Ibarra">José María Velasco Ibarra</a>, who served five. Before current re-elected president <a href="/wiki/Rafael_Correa" title="Rafael Correa">Rafael Correa</a>, the last president to serve out his complete term in office was <a href="/wiki/Sixto_Dur%C3%A1n_Ball%C3%A9n" title="Sixto Durán Ballén">Sixto Durán Ballén</a>, who served from 1992 to 1996.</p>
