<p><b>El Sayed Mohammed Nosseir</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar" xml:lang="ar">السيد نصير</span>‎‎, August 31, 1905 – October 11, 1977) was an <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egyptian</a> <a href="/wiki/Olympic_weightlifting" title="Olympic weightlifting">weightlifter</a>.</p>
<p>He was born in <a href="/wiki/Tanta" title="Tanta">Tanta</a>.</p>
<p>Nosseir won the gold medal in the light heavyweight class at the <a href="/wiki/1928_Summer_Olympics" title="1928 Summer Olympics">1928 Summer Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Amsterdam" title="Amsterdam">Amsterdam</a>. He lifted 355.0 kg to book a new world record, winning <a href="/wiki/Egypt_at_the_Olympics" title="Egypt at the Olympics">Egypt</a>'s first Olympic gold medal.</p>
