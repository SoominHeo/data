<p><b>Elâzığ Province</b> (<a href="/wiki/Turkish_language" title="Turkish language">Turkish</a>: <span lang="tr" xml:lang="tr"><i><span style="padding-right:0.1em;">Elâzığ ili</span></i></span>) is a province of Turkey with its seat in the city of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/El%C3%A2z%C4%B1%C4%9F" title="Elâzığ">Elâzığ</a>. The source of the <a href="/wiki/Euphrates" title="Euphrates">Euphrates</a> river is located in this province.</p>
<p>The province had a population of 568,753 in 2014. The population of the province was 569,616 in 2000 and 498,225 in 1990. The total area of the province is 8,455 square kilometres (3,264 sq mi), 826 km<sup>2</sup> (319 sq mi) of which is covered by reservoirs and natural lakes.</p>
<p>The current governor of the province is Muammer Erol (since 2006). <a href="/wiki/Turkish_people" title="Turkish people">Turks</a> and <a href="/wiki/Zazas" title="Zazas">Zazas</a> are majority in the province.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Elaz%C4%B1%C4%9F_Province&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Elâzığ stretches in a general west-southwest to east-northeast direction, along the line of equal geographic latitude and longitude.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Economy">Economy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Elaz%C4%B1%C4%9F_Province&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Economy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Historically, Elazığ Province produced <a href="/wiki/Silver" title="Silver">silver</a>, which ceased being mined in 1885. The Turkish government attempted to modernize the mines; however, the cost of fuel and energy caused the shut down. There was a <a href="/wiki/Silver" title="Silver">silver</a> mine still producing in 1903 in <a href="/wiki/Palu,_Elaz%C4%B1%C4%9F" title="Palu, Elazığ">Palu</a>, as with <a href="/wiki/Coal" title="Coal">coal</a>. Coal is also found in other areas of the province. <a href="/wiki/Gold" title="Gold">Gold</a> and <a href="/wiki/Salt" title="Salt">salt</a> was also produced.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Prothero74_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Prothero74-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
