<p><b>Hélène Fillières</b> (born 1 May 1972) is a French actress, film director and screenwriter. She is the sister of filmmaker <a href="/wiki/Sophie_Filli%C3%A8res" title="Sophie Fillières">Sophie Fillières</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
