<div class="hatnote" role="note">This is a <a href="/wiki/Chinese_name" title="Chinese name">Chinese name</a>; the <a href="/wiki/Chinese_surname" title="Chinese surname">family name</a> is <a href="/wiki/L%C3%BC_(surname)" title="Lü (surname)">Lü</a>.</div>
<p><b>Lü Kai</b> (birth and death dates unknown), <a href="/wiki/Courtesy_name" title="Courtesy name">courtesy name</a> <b>Jiping</b>, was an official of the state of <a href="/wiki/Shu_Han" title="Shu Han">Shu Han</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Three_Kingdoms" title="Three Kingdoms">Three Kingdoms</a> period. He served as the Administrator of Yongchang <a href="/wiki/Commandery_(China)" title="Commandery (China)">commandery</a> (永昌郡). He defended it from <a href="/wiki/Yong_Kai" title="Yong Kai">Yong Kai</a>'s rebel forces at one time. During <a href="/wiki/Zhuge_Liang%27s_Southern_Campaign" title="Zhuge Liang's Southern Campaign">Zhuge Liang's Southern Campaign</a>, he presented <a href="/wiki/Zhuge_Liang" title="Zhuge Liang">Zhuge Liang</a> with a map of the <a href="/wiki/Nanzhong" title="Nanzhong">Nanzhong</a> region and served as a guide for the army.</p>
