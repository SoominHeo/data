<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">FIFA Club of the Century</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p>The <b>FIFA Club of the Century</b> was an award presented by <a href="/wiki/FIFA" title="FIFA">FIFA</a> to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Real_Madrid" title="Real Madrid">Real Madrid</a> on 13 December 2000. Voting for the award was restricted to subscribers of the bi-monthly <i>FIFA World Magazine</i> (<a href="/wiki/FIFA" title="FIFA">FIFA</a>'s official magazine).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gala_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-gala-1">[1]</a></sup> A trophy was presented to Real Madrid in December 2000.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-bernabeu_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-bernabeu-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-bbc_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-bbc-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-rte_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-rte-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2006 a crest was added to Real Madrid's playing shirts recognising their status as FIFA Club of the Century.</p>
