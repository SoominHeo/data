<p><b>Yeo Sung-Hye</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">여성해</span>; born 6 August 1987) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">defender</a> for <a href="/wiki/Gyeongnam_FC" title="Gyeongnam FC">Gyeongnam FC</a> in the <a href="/wiki/K_League_Challenge" title="K League Challenge">K League Challenge</a>.</p>
