<p><b>"Onna ni Sachi Are"</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span class="t_nihongo_kanji" lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">å¥³ã«å¹¸ãã</span><sup class="t_nihongo_help noprint"><a href="/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets" title="Help:Installing Japanese character sets"><span class="t_nihongo_icon" style="color: #00e; font: bold 80% sans-serif; text-decoration: none; padding: 0 .1em;">?</span></a></sup>, "Good Luck, Girls")</span> is the thirty-fourth single of <a href="/wiki/J-pop" title="J-pop">J-pop</a> idol group <a href="/wiki/Morning_Musume" title="Morning Musume">Morning Musume</a> and the first to feature eighth generation members <a href="/wiki/Junjun_(singer)" title="Junjun (singer)">Junjun</a> and <a href="/wiki/Linlin" title="Linlin">Linlin</a>. It was released on July 25, 2007<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-entry_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-entry-1">[1]</a></sup> and its accompanying <a href="/wiki/Single_V" title="Single V">Single V</a> on August 1.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>Like its predecessor, the single was released in three editions. Limited Edition A contained a bonus DVD, while a 40-page photo booklet came with limited edition B, enclosed in a special package. The catalog numbers of each limited edition are EPCE-5483-4 and EPCE-5485, respectively. The regular edition has the catalog number EPCE-5486. The first press of all editions contained a serial-number card for a draw to win tickets to the release event.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-entry_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-entry-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
