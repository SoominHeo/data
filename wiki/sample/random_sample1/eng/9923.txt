<p><b>Yŏhaejin Station</b> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Railway_station" title="Railway station">railway station</a> in greater <a href="/wiki/Tanchon" title="Tanchon">Tanch'ŏn</a> city, <a href="/wiki/South_Hamgyong_Province" title="South Hamgyong Province">South Hamgyŏng</a> province, <a href="/wiki/North_Korea" title="North Korea">North Korea</a>. Located on the <a href="/wiki/Pyongra_Line" title="Pyongra Line">P'yŏngra Line</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Korean_State_Railway" title="Korean State Railway">Korean State Railway</a>, it is also the starting point of the <a href="/wiki/Kumgol_Line" title="Kumgol Line">Kŭmgol</a> line. The station was opened in 1924.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Hayato_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Hayato-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
