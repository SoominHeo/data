<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Hexagram_1">Hexagram 1</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_hexagrams_of_the_I_Ching&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Hexagram 1">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="floatright"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Iching-hexagram-01.svg"><img alt="Iching-hexagram-01.svg" data-file-height="70" data-file-width="70" height="70" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9a/Iching-hexagram-01.svg/70px-Iching-hexagram-01.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9a/Iching-hexagram-01.svg/105px-Iching-hexagram-01.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9a/Iching-hexagram-01.svg/140px-Iching-hexagram-01.svg.png 2x" width="70"/></a></div>
<p><i>Hexagram 1</i> is named 乾 (qián), "Force". Other variations include "the creative", "strong action", "the key", and "god". Its inner (lower) <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ba_gua" title="Ba gua">trigram</a> is ☰ (乾 qián) force = (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%E5%A4%A9" title="天">天</a>) heaven, and its outer (upper) trigram is the same.</p>

<p><i>Hexagram 21</i> is named 噬嗑 (shì kè), "Gnawing Bite". Other variations include "biting through" and "biting and chewing". Its inner trigram is ☳ (震 zhèn) shake = (<a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%9B%B7" title="wiktionary:雷">雷</a>) thunder, and its outer trigram is ☲ (離 lí) radiance = (<a href="/wiki/Fire_(Wu_Xing)" title="Fire (Wu Xing)">火</a>) fire.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Hexagram_30">Hexagram 30</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_hexagrams_of_the_I_Ching&amp;action=edit&amp;section=30" title="Edit section: Hexagram 30">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="floatright"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Iching-hexagram-30.svg"><img alt="Iching-hexagram-30.svg" data-file-height="70" data-file-width="70" height="70" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/ba/Iching-hexagram-30.svg/70px-Iching-hexagram-30.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/ba/Iching-hexagram-30.svg/105px-Iching-hexagram-30.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/ba/Iching-hexagram-30.svg/140px-Iching-hexagram-30.svg.png 2x" width="70"/></a></div>
<p><i>Hexagram 30</i> is named 離 (lí), "Radiance". Other variations include "the clinging, fire" and "the net". Its inner trigram is ☲ (離 lí) radiance = (火) fire, and its outer trigram is identical. The origin of the character has its roots in symbols of long-tailed birds such as the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Peacock" title="Peacock">peacock</a> or the legendary <a href="/wiki/Phoenix_(mythology)" title="Phoenix (mythology)">phoenix</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Hexagram_61">Hexagram 61</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_hexagrams_of_the_I_Ching&amp;action=edit&amp;section=61" title="Edit section: Hexagram 61">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="floatright"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Iching-hexagram-61.svg"><img alt="Iching-hexagram-61.svg" data-file-height="70" data-file-width="70" height="70" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1e/Iching-hexagram-61.svg/70px-Iching-hexagram-61.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1e/Iching-hexagram-61.svg/105px-Iching-hexagram-61.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1e/Iching-hexagram-61.svg/140px-Iching-hexagram-61.svg.png 2x" width="70"/></a></div>
<p><i>Hexagram 61</i> is named 中孚 (zhōng fú), "Center Returning". Other variations include "inner truth" and "central return". Its inner trigram is ☱ (兌 duì) open = (<a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%BE%A4" title="wiktionary:澤">澤</a>) swamp, and its outer trigram is ☴ (巽 xùn) ground = (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%E9%A2%A8" title="風">風</a>) wind.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Hexagram_62">Hexagram 62</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_hexagrams_of_the_I_Ching&amp;action=edit&amp;section=62" title="Edit section: Hexagram 62">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="floatright"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Iching-hexagram-62.svg"><img alt="Iching-hexagram-62.svg" data-file-height="70" data-file-width="70" height="70" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/54/Iching-hexagram-62.svg/70px-Iching-hexagram-62.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/54/Iching-hexagram-62.svg/105px-Iching-hexagram-62.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/54/Iching-hexagram-62.svg/140px-Iching-hexagram-62.svg.png 2x" width="70"/></a></div>
<p><i>Hexagram 62</i> is named 小過 (xiǎo guò), "Small Exceeding". Other variations include "preponderance of the small" and "small surpassing". Its inner trigram is ☶ (艮 gèn) bound = (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%E5%B1%B1" title="山">山</a>) mountain, and its outer trigram is ☳ (震 zhèn) shake = (<a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%9B%B7" title="wiktionary:雷">雷</a>) thunder.</p>
