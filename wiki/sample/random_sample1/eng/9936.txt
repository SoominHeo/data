<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>Korean School in Yantai</b> (Korean: 연대한국학교, <a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">烟台韩国学校</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">煙臺韓國學校</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Yāntái Hánguó Xuéxiào</span></i>) is a Korean international school in <a href="/wiki/Yantai" title="Yantai">Yantai</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shandong" title="Shandong">Shandong</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. It was established on March 5, 2001.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It serves levels kindergarten through senior high school.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
