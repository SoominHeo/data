<p><i><b>Dating Agency: Cyrano</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">연애조작단; 시라노</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Yeonaejojakdan; Sirano</span></i>) is a 2013 South Korean television series starring <a href="/wiki/Lee_Jong-hyuk" title="Lee Jong-hyuk">Lee Jong-hyuk</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Choi_Sooyoung" title="Choi Sooyoung">Choi Sooyoung</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lee_Chun-hee" title="Lee Chun-hee">Lee Chun-hee</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hong_Jong-hyun" title="Hong Jong-hyun">Hong Jong-hyun</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cho_Yoon-woo" title="Cho Yoon-woo">Cho Yoon-woo</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Based on the 2010 <a href="/wiki/Romantic_comedy_film" title="Romantic comedy film">romantic comedy film</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Cyrano_Agency" title="Cyrano Agency">Cyrano Agency</a></i>, the series is about a dating agency that orchestrates romantic scenarios for paying clients, all in an effort to raise enough money to save an old theater.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<p>The early <a href="/wiki/Working_title" title="Working title">working title</a> was <i>Flower Boy Dating Agency</i> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang" xml:lang="ko-Hang">꽃미남 연애조작단</span>; <a href="/wiki/Revised_Romanization_of_Korean" title="Revised Romanization of Korean">RR</a>: <i><span class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none" title="Korean transliteration">Ggotminam Yeonaejojakdan</span></i>). It is the fourth in cable channel <a href="/wiki/TVN_(South_Korea)" title="TVN (South Korea)">tvN</a>'s "Oh Boy" project targeted at the teenage <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Demographic" title="Demographic">demographic</a>, following <i><a href="/wiki/Flower_Boy_Ramen_Shop" title="Flower Boy Ramen Shop">Flower Boy Ramen Shop</a></i> (2011), <i><a href="/wiki/Shut_Up_Flower_Boy_Band" title="Shut Up Flower Boy Band">Shut Up Flower Boy Band</a></i> (2012), and <i><a href="/wiki/Flower_Boys_Next_Door" title="Flower Boys Next Door">Flower Boys Next Door</a></i> (2013).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Cast">Cast</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dating_Agency:_Cyrano&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Cast">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Lee_Jong-hyuk" title="Lee Jong-hyuk">Lee Jong-hyuk</a> as Seo Byung-hoon</li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dd>After he loses his best friend in an accident, the once-renowned theater director suffers from guilt and decides to save his late friend's theatre troupe. Using his masterful stage skills, he forms the Cyrano Dating Agency.</dd>
</dl>
<ul>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Choi_Sooyoung" title="Choi Sooyoung">Choi Sooyoung</a> as Gong Min-young</li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dd>A romantic, and one of the members of the dating agency. She believes the true feelings of her clients are more important than profit for the agency and is attracted to Byung-hoon.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></dd>
</dl>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Lee_Chun-hee" title="Lee Chun-hee">Lee Chun-hee</a> as Cha "Master" Seung-pyo</li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dd>The chef of the restaurant that's next door to the theater where the dating agency is housed. He is the loan shark who originally lent the money to Do-il for the Cyrano theater, and is later revealed to be Do-il's younger brother. He is attracted to Min-young.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup></dd>
</dl>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hong_Jong-hyun" title="Hong Jong-hyun">Hong Jong-hyun</a> as Moo-jin</li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dd>Recruited into the dating agency because of his genius engineering skills, he is the brains behind all hi-tech gadgets used by the agency in the field. He becomes Hye Ri's boyfriend in episode 14.</dd>
</dl>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Cho_Yoon-woo" title="Cho Yoon-woo">Cho Yoon-woo</a> as Do Arang</li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dd>A 19-year-old high schooler who dreams of becoming a theater actor.</dd>
</dl>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bae_Seong-woo" title="Bae Seong-woo">Bae Seong-woo</a> as Lee Min-shik</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kim_Min-kyo" title="Kim Min-kyo">Kim Min-kyo</a> as Go Young-dal</li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dd>Min-shik and Young-dal are Seung-pyo's loan shark goons, who intimidate Byung-hoon into paying his debt, and occasionally join in the agency's missions. Both of them eventually become Cyrano's prominent actors.</dd>
</dl>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ha_Yeon-joo" title="Ha Yeon-joo">Ha Yeon-joo</a> as Hye-ri</li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dd>A waitress at Cha Seung-pyo's restaurant. She is attracted to Moo-jin and later starts dating him.</dd>
</dl>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kim_Jung-hwa" title="Kim Jung-hwa">Kim Jung-hwa</a> as Yoon Yi-seol</li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dd>Byung-hoon's first love from high school, who ended up marrying Do-il. She was the one driving during Do-il's accident, which causes numbness to her right hand at times.</dd>
</dl>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Lee_Min-woo_(actor)" title="Lee Min-woo (actor)">Lee Min-woo</a> as Go Do-il</li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dd>Do-il, Yi-seol and Byung-hoon were best friends in high school and founders of the Cyrano Theater Troupe. He later married Yi-seol, and died in a car accident.</dd>
</dl>
