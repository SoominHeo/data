<p><b>FK Mladá Boleslav</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Czech_Republic" title="Czech Republic">Czech</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> club based in the city of <a href="/wiki/Mlad%C3%A1_Boleslav" title="Mladá Boleslav">Mladá Boleslav</a> <span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA_for_Czech" title="Help:IPA for Czech">[ˈmladaː ˈbolɛslaf]</a></span>. The club currently plays in the <a href="/wiki/Czech_First_League" title="Czech First League">Czech First League</a>.</p>
<p>Mladá Boleslav were runners up in the <a href="/wiki/2005%E2%80%9306_Czech_First_League" title="2005–06 Czech First League">2005–06 Czech First League</a> and went on to play in the <a href="/wiki/2006%E2%80%9307_UEFA_Champions_League" title="2006–07 UEFA Champions League">2006–07 UEFA Champions League</a>, winning their opening tie against <a href="/wiki/V%C3%A5lerenga_Fotball" title="Vålerenga Fotball">Vålerenga</a> although they were eliminated in the third qualifying round by <a href="/wiki/Galatasaray_S.K._(football)" title="Galatasaray S.K. (football)">Galatasaray</a>. The club won the <a href="/wiki/Czech_Cup" title="Czech Cup">Czech Cup</a> in 2011 and qualified for the <a href="/wiki/2011%E2%80%9312_UEFA_Europa_League" title="2011–12 UEFA Europa League">2011–12 UEFA Europa League</a>, although they were defeated over two legs by <a href="/wiki/AEK_Larnaca_F.C." title="AEK Larnaca F.C.">AEK Larnaca</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=FK_Mlad%C3%A1_Boleslav&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Recent_times">Recent times</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=FK_Mlad%C3%A1_Boleslav&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Recent times">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The team was promoted to <a href="/wiki/Czech_First_League" title="Czech First League">Czech First League</a> for the first time in its history in 2004 and in their <a href="/wiki/2004%E2%80%9305_Czech_First_League" title="2004–05 Czech First League">first top-flight season</a> fought against relegation, eventually finishing in 14th place.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The club's greatest success was achieved in the <a href="/wiki/2005%E2%80%9306_Czech_First_League" title="2005–06 Czech First League">2005–06 season</a>, as they finished runners-up in the <a href="/wiki/Czech_First_League" title="Czech First League">Czech First League</a>, earning a place in the <a href="/wiki/2006%E2%80%9307_UEFA_Champions_League_qualifying_rounds" title="2006–07 UEFA Champions League qualifying rounds">qualifying rounds</a> of the <a href="/wiki/UEFA_Champions_League" title="UEFA Champions League">UEFA Champions League</a>. They came through their first tie, defeating <a href="/wiki/V%C3%A5lerenga_Fotball" title="Vålerenga Fotball">Vålerenga</a> (3–1 and 2–2), then lost against <a href="/wiki/Galatasaray_S.K._(football)" title="Galatasaray S.K. (football)">Galatasaray</a> (2–5 away, 1–1 home), dropping into the <a href="/wiki/2006%E2%80%9307_UEFA_Cup#First_round" title="2006–07 UEFA Cup">UEFA Cup first round</a>. The club went on to achieve a surprising 4–3 aggregate victory over <a href="/wiki/Olympique_de_Marseille" title="Olympique de Marseille">Marseille</a> (1st leg: 0–1, 2nd leg 4–2). However, the team was eliminated after reaching the group stage, taking just 3 points from 4 matches (<a href="/wiki/Panathinaikos_F.C." title="Panathinaikos F.C.">Panathinaikos</a> 0–1, <a href="/wiki/Hapoel_Tel_Aviv_F.C." title="Hapoel Tel Aviv F.C.">Hapoel Tel Aviv</a> 1–1, <a href="/wiki/Paris_Saint-Germain_F.C." title="Paris Saint-Germain F.C.">Paris Saint-Germain</a> 0–0, <a href="/wiki/FC_Rapid_Bucure%C8%99ti" title="FC Rapid București">Rapid București</a> 1–1).</p>
<p>The following season, the club qualified directly for the <a href="/wiki/2007%E2%80%9308_UEFA_Cup_first_round" title="2007–08 UEFA Cup first round">first round</a> of the UEFA Cup after <a href="/wiki/2006%E2%80%9307_Czech_First_League" title="2006–07 Czech First League">finishing 3rd</a> in the league. (<a href="/wiki/Lubo%C5%A1_Pecka" title="Luboš Pecka">Luboš Pecka</a> was the top goalscorer in the league that year.) Qualification for the <a href="/wiki/2007%E2%80%9308_UEFA_Cup_group_stage" title="2007–08 UEFA Cup group stage">group stage</a> was only narrowly secured by beating <a href="/wiki/U.S._Citt%C3%A0_di_Palermo" title="U.S. Città di Palermo">Palermo</a> 4–2 on penalties after a nail biting 1–1 aggregate scoreline. On the verge of being eliminated with the score reading 1–0 Palermo, (with their goal in the first leg still standing) in the 2nd leg, Tomáš Sedlacek scored the winner in the 2nd leg with only seconds to spare. In their group Mladá Boleslav defeated <a href="/wiki/IF_Elfsborg" title="IF Elfsborg">IF Elfsborg</a> 3–1, but again failed to reach the knockout stages of the competition after losing matches against <a href="/wiki/Villarreal_CF" title="Villarreal CF">Villarreal</a> 1–2, <a href="/wiki/AEK_Athens_F.C." title="AEK Athens F.C.">AEK Athens</a> 0–1 and <a href="/wiki/ACF_Fiorentina" title="ACF Fiorentina">Fiorentina</a> 1–2. The club subsequently achieved a 7th place league finish in the <a href="/wiki/2007%E2%80%9308_Czech_First_League#League_standings" title="2007–08 Czech First League">2007–08</a> season, missing out on European qualification.</p>
<p>During its short first league history four of the club's players have been capped internationally: <a href="/wiki/Marek_Mat%C4%9Bjovsk%C3%BD" title="Marek Matějovský">Marek Matějovský</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marek_Kuli%C4%8D" title="Marek Kulič">Marek Kulič</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jan_Rajnoch" title="Jan Rajnoch">Jan Rajnoch</a> and <a href="/wiki/Michal_Papadopulos" title="Michal Papadopulos">Michal Papadopulos</a>.</p>
<p>The major sponsor of the club is <a href="/wiki/%C5%A0koda_Auto" title="Škoda Auto">Škoda Auto</a>.</p>
