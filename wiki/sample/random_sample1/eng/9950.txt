<p><b>The Lovers (VI)</b> is the sixth <a href="/wiki/Trump_(card_games)" title="Trump (card games)">trump</a> or <a href="/wiki/Major_Arcana" title="Major Arcana">Major Arcana</a> card in most traditional <a href="/wiki/Tarot" title="Tarot">Tarot</a> decks. It is used in <a href="/wiki/Tarot_card_games" title="Tarot card games">game playing</a> as well as in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tarot_reading" title="Tarot reading">divination</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Interpretation">Interpretation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Lovers&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Interpretation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In some traditions, the Lovers represent relationships and choices. Its appearance in a spread indicates some decision about an existing relationship, a <a href="/wiki/Temptation" title="Temptation">temptation</a> of the heart, or a choice of potential partners. Often an aspect of the <a href="/wiki/Querent" title="Querent">Querent</a>'s life will have to be sacrificed; a <a href="/wiki/Bachelor" title="Bachelor">bachelor</a>(ette)'s lifestyle may be sacrificed and a relationship gained (or vice versa), or one potential partner may be chosen while another is turned down. Whatever the choice, it should not be made lightly, as the ramifications will be lasting.</p>
<p>The Lovers is associated with the <a href="/wiki/Zodiac" title="Zodiac">star sign</a> <a href="/wiki/Gemini_(astrology)" title="Gemini (astrology)">Gemini</a>, and indeed is also known as <b>The Twins</b> in some decks. Other associations are with <a href="/wiki/Air_(classical_element)" title="Air (classical element)">Air</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mercury_(planet)" title="Mercury (planet)">Mercury</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Hebrew_alphabet" title="Hebrew alphabet">Hebrew letter</a> <b>ז</b> (<a href="/wiki/Zayin" title="Zayin">Zayin</a>).</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="In_pop_culture">In pop culture</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Lovers&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: In pop culture">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>The Lovers is the name of a boss in <i><a href="/wiki/The_House_of_the_Dead_4" title="The House of the Dead 4">The House of the Dead 4</a></i> videogame by <a href="/wiki/Sega" title="Sega">Sega</a>, where it is depicted as two giant tarantulas mating. All of the bosses in the <a href="/wiki/The_House_of_the_Dead_(series)" title="The House of the Dead (series)"><i>House of the Dead</i> series</a> are named after the Major Arcana.</li>
<li>The Lovers card, along with the other Tarot cards of the Major Arcana, appears in the 2011 video game, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Binding_of_Isaac_(video_game)" title="The Binding of Isaac (video game)">The Binding of Isaac</a></i>, and its 2014 remake, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Binding_of_Isaac:_Rebirth" title="The Binding of Isaac: Rebirth">The Binding of Isaac: Rebirth</a></i>, where they act as consumable items with varying effects. The Lovers creates healing items for the player.</li>
<li>It also appears on a video game, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Persona_4" title="Persona 4">Persona 4</a></i> as an arcana card, where the character Rise Kujikawa is the player's Social Link to The Lovers arcana, displaying friendship.</li>
<li>It also appears in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Persona_3" title="Persona 3">Persona 3</a></i> as an arcana card, where the character Yukari Takeba is the player's Social Link to the Lovers arcana, displaying friendship.</li>
<li>The Lovers card was chosen by Ethan Chandler in Season One, Episode One of <i><a href="/wiki/Penny_Dreadful_(TV_series)" title="Penny Dreadful (TV series)">Penny Dreadful (TV series)</a></i>.</li>
<li>The Lovers is the name of the Stand used by the character Steely Dan in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jojo%27s_Bizarre_Adventure" title="Jojo's Bizarre Adventure">Jojo's Bizarre Adventure</a>: Stardust Crusaders.</li>
<li>The Lovers also appear in the book series the <a href="/wiki/The_Arcana_Chronicles" title="The Arcana Chronicles">Arcana Chronicles</a> by Kresley Cole</li>
</ul>
