<p><b>Yeon Je-woon</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hangul" title="Hangul">Hangul</a>: <span lang="ko-Hang">연제운</span>; born August 28, 1994) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korean</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as <a href="/wiki/Forward_(association_football)" title="Forward (association football)">forward</a> for <a href="/wiki/Seongnam_FC" title="Seongnam FC">Seongnam FC</a> in <a href="/wiki/K_League_Classic" title="K League Classic">K League Classic</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yeon_Je-woon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Jo Ju-young joined <a href="/wiki/K_League_Classic" title="K League Classic">K League Classic</a> side <a href="/wiki/Seongnam_FC" title="Seongnam FC">Seongnam FC</a> in January 2016. He made his first appearance for Seongnam in the league game against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jeonbuk_Hyundai_Motors" title="Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors">Jeonbuk</a> on June 12. He scored his debut goal in the league game against <a href="/wiki/Sangju_Sangmu_FC" title="Sangju Sangmu FC">Sangju</a> on 10 July.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
