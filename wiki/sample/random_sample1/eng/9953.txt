<p><b>Yanzhou</b> or <b>Yan Province</b>（兗州／兖州, or in historical records often written as 沇州) was one of the <a href="/wiki/Nine_Provinces_(China)" title="Nine Provinces (China)">Nine Provinces</a> of ancient China.</p>
