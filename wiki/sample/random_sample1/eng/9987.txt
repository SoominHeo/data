<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Flag of the British Antarctic Territory</h1>

<p>The <b>flag of the British Antarctic Territory</b> was granted on 1 August 1963, a year after the <a href="/wiki/British_Antarctic_Territory" title="British Antarctic Territory">British Antarctic Territory</a>, a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/British_Overseas_Territory" title="British Overseas Territory">British Overseas Territory</a>, was created. Previously, the Territory was a part of the <a href="/wiki/Falkland_Islands_Dependencies" title="Falkland Islands Dependencies">Falkland Islands Dependencies</a> and used the same <a href="/wiki/Flag_of_the_Falkland_Islands" title="Flag of the Falkland Islands">flag</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Flag_of_the_British_Antarctic_Territory&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Description">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The flag is a <a href="/wiki/British_ensign" title="British ensign">white ensign</a> with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Union_Flag" title="Union Flag">Union Flag</a> in the canton, defaced with the <a href="/wiki/Coat_of_arms_of_the_British_Antarctic_Territory" title="Coat of arms of the British Antarctic Territory">coat of arms of the British Antarctic Territory</a>, which was introduced in 1952. The coat of arms features a lion, representing the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>, and a penguin, representing the native wildlife. The crest of the coat of arms is the <a href="/wiki/RRS_Discovery" title="RRS Discovery">RRS Discovery</a>, which first took <a href="/wiki/Robert_Falcon_Scott" title="Robert Falcon Scott">Robert Falcon Scott</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ernest_Shackleton" title="Ernest Shackleton">Ernest Shackleton</a> to <a href="/wiki/Antarctica" title="Antarctica">Antarctica</a> as part of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/British_National_Antarctic_Expedition" title="British National Antarctic Expedition">British National Antarctic Expedition</a>. She is currently a <a href="/wiki/Museum_ship" title="Museum ship">museum ship</a> in <a href="/wiki/Dundee" title="Dundee">Dundee</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The motto on the coat of arms is <i>Research and Discovery</i>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Use_and_related_flags">Use and related flags</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Flag_of_the_British_Antarctic_Territory&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Use and related flags">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The flag flies over the British research stations in the Territory and at the headquarters of the <a href="/wiki/British_Antarctic_Survey" title="British Antarctic Survey">British Antarctic Survey</a> in <a href="/wiki/Cambridge" title="Cambridge">Cambridge</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Vessels of the British Antarctic Survey use a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Blue_ensign" title="Blue ensign">blue ensign</a> defaced with the shield from the coat of arms, which can be seen below. This ensign was introduced by Royal Warrant on 30 May 1969.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> The flag of the <a href="/wiki/Commissioner_for_the_British_Antarctic_Territory" title="Commissioner for the British Antarctic Territory">Commissioner for the British Antarctic Territory</a>, a position currently held by <a href="/wiki/Peter_Hayes_(diplomat)" title="Peter Hayes (diplomat)">Peter Hayes</a>, is also seen below.</p>
<p>The flag is the only British Overseas Territory flag to use the white ensign.</p>
