<p><b>Yeungnam University Hospital Station</b> is a station of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Daegu_Subway_Line_1" title="Daegu Subway Line 1">Daegu Subway Line 1</a> in <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Daemyeong-dong&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Daemyeong-dong (page does not exist)">Daemyeong-dong</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nam_District,_Daegu" title="Nam District, Daegu">Nam District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Daegu" title="Daegu">Daegu</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. It is named since there is the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Yeungnam_University_Hospital&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Yeungnam University Hospital (page does not exist)">Yeungnam University Hospital</a> (Yeongdae-byeongwon) around.</p>
