<p><b>Yeongdeok County</b> (<i>Yeongdeok-gun</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_South_Korea" title="Administrative divisions of South Korea">county</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/North_Gyeongsang" title="North Gyeongsang">North Gyeongsang</a> Province, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>. It is well known for crabs.</p>
