href="/wiki/%EC%9C%84%ED%82%A4%EB%B0%B1%EA%B3%BC:%EC%A4%91%EA%B5%AD%EC%96%B4%EC%9D%98_%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80_%ED%91%9C%EA%B8%B0" title="위키백과:중국어의 한글 표기">*</a>]</sup></small>, <span style="font-size: smaller;"><a href="/wiki/%EA%B4%91%EB%91%A5%EC%96%B4" title="광둥어">광둥어</a>:</span> <span lang="yue" xml:lang="yue">gu<sup>2</sup> tin<sup>1</sup> lok<sup>6</sup></span> <small>구틴록</small>, <span href="/wiki/%EC%9C%84%ED%82%A4%EB%B0%B1%EA%B3%BC:%EC%98%81%EC%96%B4%EC%9D%98_%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80_%ED%91%9C%EA%B8%B0" title="위키백과:영어의 한글 표기">*</a>]</sup></small>, <a href="/wiki/1970%EB%85%84"
