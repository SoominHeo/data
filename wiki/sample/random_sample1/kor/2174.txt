<h2><span class="mw-headline" id=".EC.83.9D.ED.83.9C">생태</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%EA%B3%A8%EB%8B%B4%EC%B4%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 생태">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
