<p><b>다랑어속</b>(<i>Thunnus</i>)은 <a href="/wiki/%EA%B3%A0%EB%93%B1%EC%96%B4%EA%B3%BC" title="고등어과">고등어과</a>에 속하는 물고기의 부류이다. 모두 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%EC%B0%B8%EC%B9%98" title="참치">다랑어</a> 또는 참치라 부른다.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id=".ED.95.98.EC.9C.84_.EC.A2.85">하위 종</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%EB%8B%A4%EB%9E%91%EC%96%B4%EC%86%8D&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 하위 종">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>다랑어속에는 다음 8종이 속한다.</p>
<ul>
<li>다랑어아속(<i>Thunnus</i>)
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/%EB%82%A0%EA%B0%9C%EB%8B%A4%EB%9E%91%EC%96%B4" title="날개다랑어">날개다랑어</a>, <i>Thunnus alalunga</i> <small>(Bonnaterre, 1788)</small>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/%EB%82%A8%EB%B0%A9%EC%B0%B8%EB%8B%A4%EB%9E%91%EC%96%B4" title="남방참다랑어">남방참다랑어</a>, <i>Thunnus maccoyii</i> <small>(Castelnau, 1872)</small>.</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%EB%88%88%EB%8B%A4%EB%9E%91%EC%96%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="눈다랑어 (없는 문서)">눈다랑어</a>, <i>Thunnus obesus</i> <small>(Lowe, 1839)</small>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/%EC%B0%B8%EB%8B%A4%EB%9E%91%EC%96%B4" title="참다랑어">참다랑어</a>, <i>Thunnus orientalis</i> <small>(Temminck &amp; Schlegel, 1844)</small>.</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%EB%8C%80%EC%84%9C%EC%96%91%EC%B0%B8%EB%8B%A4%EB%9E%91%EC%96%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="대서양참다랑어 (없는 문서)">대서양참다랑어</a>, <i>Thunnus thynnus</i> <small>(<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%EC%B9%BC_%EB%A6%B0%EB%84%A4" title="칼 린네">Linnaeus</a>, 1758)</small>.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>황다랑어아속(<i>Neothunnus</i>)
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/%ED%99%A9%EB%8B%A4%EB%9E%91%EC%96%B4" title="황다랑어">황다랑어</a>, <i>Thunnus albacares</i> <small>(Bonnaterre, 1788)</small>.</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%EA%B2%80%EC%A0%95%EC%A7%80%EB%8A%90%EB%9F%AC%EB%AF%B8%EB%8B%A4%EB%9E%91%EC%96%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="검정지느러미다랑어 (없는 문서)">검정지느러미다랑어</a>, <i>Thunnus atlanticus</i> <small>(Lesson, 1831)</small>.</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%EB%B0%B1%EB%8B%A4%EB%9E%91%EC%96%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="백다랑어 (없는 문서)">백다랑어</a>, <i>Thunnus tonggol</i> <small>(Bleeker, 1851)</small>.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
