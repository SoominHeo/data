<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="ko"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/10%EC%A2%85_%EA%B2%BD%EA%B8%B0" title="10종 경기">10종 경기</a> 세계 기록</b>이 <a href="/wiki/%EA%B5%AD%EC%A0%9C_%EC%9C%A1%EC%83%81_%EA%B2%BD%EA%B8%B0_%EC%97%B0%EB%A7%B9" title="국제 육상 경기 연맹">국제 육상 경기 연맹</a>으로부터 공인받기 시작한 것은 1922년부터이다. <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-iaaf_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-iaaf-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>As of June 21, 2009, 35 men's world records have been ratified by the IAAF in the event.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-iaaf_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-iaaf-1">[1]</a></sup> The first score in the following table indicates the score using the tables in use at the time, the second score is based on tables currently in use.</p>
<p>The first world record in the women's decathlon was recognized by the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=International_Association_of_Athletics_Federations&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="International Association of Athletics Federations (없는 문서)">International Association of Athletics Federations</a> in 2004. As of June 21, 2009, 2 world records have been ratified by the IAAF in the event.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-iaaf_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-iaaf-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
