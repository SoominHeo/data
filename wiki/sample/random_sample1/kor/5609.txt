<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="ko">모리셔스의 국가</h1>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="ko"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>어머니의 나라</b>(Motherland)는 <b><a href="/wiki/%EB%AA%A8%EB%A6%AC%EC%85%94%EC%8A%A4" title="모리셔스">모리셔스</a>의 <a href="/wiki/%EA%B5%AD%EA%B0%80_(%EB%85%B8%EB%9E%98)" title="국가 (노래)">국가</a></b>이다. Jean Georges Prosper가 작사, Philippe Gentil M.B.E.가 작곡하였다.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id=".EA.B0.80.EC.82.AC">가사</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%EB%AA%A8%EB%A6%AC%EC%85%94%EC%8A%A4%EC%9D%98_%EA%B5%AD%EA%B0%80&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 가사">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<dl>
<dd>Glory to thee,</dd>
<dd>Motherland, oh motherland of mine,</dd>
<dd>Sweet is thy beauty,</dd>
<dd>Sweet is thy fragrance,</dd>
<dd>around thee we gather,</dd>
<dd>as one people,</dd>
<dd>as one nation,</dd>
<dd>In peace, justice and liberty,</dd>
<dd>Beloved country may God bless thee,</dd>
<dd>for ever and ever.</dd>
</dl>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id=".ED.95.B4.EC.84.9D">해석</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%EB%AA%A8%EB%A6%AC%EC%85%94%EC%8A%A4%EC%9D%98_%EA%B5%AD%EA%B0%80&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 해석">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<dl>
<dd>영광스러운 그대여</dd>
<dd>오, 어머니의 나라는 우리의 것이라.</dd>
<dd>달콤한 아름다움과</dd>
<dd>달콤한 향수는</dd>
<dd>우리 주위를 끌어 모으네.</dd>
<dd>하나의 국민과</dd>
<dd>하나의 조국은</dd>
<dd>평화롭고 정의롭고 자유로운 곳이라.</dd>
<dd>사랑하는 주께서 지켜 주시네.</dd>
<dd>이제 변함이 없으리라.</dd>
</dl>
