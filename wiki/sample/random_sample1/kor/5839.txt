<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="ko">미국령 버진아일랜드의 국가</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">위키백과, 우리 모두의 백과사전.</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="ko"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>버진아일랜드의 행진</b>(Virgin Islands March)은 <a href="/wiki/%EB%AF%B8%EA%B5%AD%EB%A0%B9_%EB%B2%84%EC%A7%84%EC%95%84%EC%9D%BC%EB%9E%9C%EB%93%9C" title="미국령 버진아일랜드">미국령 버진아일랜드</a>의 <a href="/wiki/%EA%B5%AD%EA%B0%80_(%EB%85%B8%EB%9E%98)" title="국가 (노래)">국가</a>로, <a href="/wiki/1963%EB%85%84" title="1963년">1963년</a>에 채택되었다. <a href="/wiki/1920%EB%85%84%EB%8C%80" title="1920년대">1920년대</a>에 Alton Adams가 작사하였고 Sam Williams가 작곡했다.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id=".EA.B0.80.EC.82.AC">가사</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%EB%AF%B8%EA%B5%AD%EB%A0%B9_%EB%B2%84%EC%A7%84%EC%95%84%EC%9D%BC%EB%9E%9C%EB%93%9C%EC%9D%98_%EA%B5%AD%EA%B0%80&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 가사">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<dl>
<dd>All hail our Virgin Islands.</dd>
<dd>Em'ralds of the sea,</dd>
<dd>Where beaches bright with coral sand</dd>
<dd>And trade winds bless our native land.</dd>
<dd>All hail our Virgin Islands,</dd>
<dd>Bathed in waters blue,</dd>
<dd>We give our loyalty,</dd>
<dd>Full to thee,</dd>
<dd>And pledge allegiance forever true.</dd>
<dd>To thee our Virgin Islands,</dd>
<dd>Loving voices raise</dd>
<dd>A song in praise of brotherhood,</dd>
<dd>Where right makes might to fight for good.</dd>
<dd>To thee our Virgin Islands,</dd>
<dd>Haven of the free,</dd>
<dd>We sing our love to thee,</dd>
<dd>Joyously,</dd>
<dd>Our own fair islands of liberty.</dd>
<dd>March on oh Virgin Islands,</dd>
<dd>In the joyful throng,</dd>
<dd>Uphold the right and right the wrong</dd>
<dd>Where only peace and love belong.</dd>
<dd>March on oh Virgin Islands,</dd>
<dd>Democratic land.</dd>
<dd>Together hand in hand,</dd>
<dd>Take your stand,</dd>
<dd>Forever soldiers in freedom's band.</dd>
<dd>God bless our Virgin Islands,</dd>
<dd>Humbly now we pray,</dd>
<dd>Where all mankind can join today</dd>
<dd>In friendly warmth of work and play.</dd>
<dd>God bless our Virgin Islands,</dd>
<dd>Beautiful and tall.</dd>
<dd>Beneath a sunny sky,</dd>
<dd>Hilltops high</dd>
<dd>Hold out a welcome for one and all.</dd>
</dl>
