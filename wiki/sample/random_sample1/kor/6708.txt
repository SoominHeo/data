<h2><span class="mw-headline" id=".EB.B6.80.ED.83.84.EC.9D.98_.EC.99.95.EB.93.A4.281907.EB.85.84.E2.80.93.ED.98.84.EC.9E.AC.29">부탄의 왕들(1907년–현재)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%EB%B6%80%ED%83%84%EC%9D%98_%EC%97%AD%EB%8C%80_%EC%99%95%EB%93%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 부탄의 왕들(1907년–현재)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
