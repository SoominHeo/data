<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="ko">브르타뉴의 국가</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
<div id="siteSub">위키백과, 우리 모두의 백과사전.</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">
					이동:					<a href="#mw-head">둘러보기</a>, 					<a href="#p-search">검색</a>
</div>
<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="ko"><script>function mfTempOpenSection(id){var block=document.getElementById("mf-section-"+id);block.className+=" open-block";block.previousSibling.className+=" open-block";}</script><p><b>내 아버지의 옛 땅</b>(Bro Gozh ma Zadoù)은 <a href="/wiki/%EB%B8%8C%EB%A5%B4%ED%83%80%EB%89%B4" title="브르타뉴">브르타뉴</a>의 <a href="/wiki/%EA%B5%AD%EA%B0%80_(%EB%85%B8%EB%9E%98)" title="국가 (노래)">국가</a>로, <a href="/wiki/1903%EB%85%84" title="1903년">1903년</a>에 채택되었다. 프랑수아 자프렌누가 <a href="/wiki/1897%EB%85%84" title="1897년">1897년</a>에 작사했으며, 제임스 제임스가 <a href="/wiki/1856%EB%85%84" title="1856년">1856년</a>에 작곡했다.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id=".EA.B0.80.EC.82.AC">가사</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%EB%B8%8C%EB%A5%B4%ED%83%80%EB%89%B4%EC%9D%98_%EA%B5%AD%EA%B0%80&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 가사">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ni, Breizhiz a galon, karomp hon gwir Vro !<br/>
Brudet eo an Arvor dre ar bed tro-do.<br/>
Dispont kreiz ar brezel, hon tadoù ken mat,<br/>
A skuilhas eviti o gwad.</p>
<p><b>후렴</b></p>
<dl>
<dd>O Breizh ! ma Bro ! Me 'gar ma Bro. (Oh Brittany, my country! I love my crountry.)</dd>
<dd>Tra ma vo 'r mor 'vel mur 'n he zro. (As long as the sea will be a wall around her.)</dd>
<dd>Ra vezo digabestr ma Bro ! (Be free my country!)</dd>
</dl>
<p>Breizh, douar ar sent kozh, douar ar varzhed,<br/>
N'eus Bro all a garan kement 'barzh ar bed.<br/>
Pep menez, pep traoñienn, d'am c'halon zo kaer,<br/>
Enno 'kousk meur a Vreizhad taer !</p>
<p><b>후렴</b></p>
<p>Ar Vretoned zo tud kalet ha kreñv;<br/>
N'eus pobl ken kalonek a-zindan an neñv,<br/>
Gwerz trist, son dudius a ziwan eno,<br/>
O ! pegen kaer ec'h out, ma Bro !</p>
<p><b>후렴</b></p>
<p>Mar d'eo bet trec'het Breizh er brezelioù bras,<br/>
He Yezh a zo bepred ken beo ha bizkoazh.<br/>
He c'halon birvidik a lamm c'hoazh 'n he c'hreiz,<br/>
Dihunet out bremañ, ma Breizh !</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id=".EC.98.81.EB.AC.B8.ED.95.B4.EC.84.9D">영문해석</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%EB%B8%8C%EB%A5%B4%ED%83%80%EB%89%B4%EC%9D%98_%EA%B5%AD%EA%B0%80&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 영문해석">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<dl>
<dt>English</dt>
</dl>
<p>Us, Bretons by heart, love our true country!<br/>
Armorica, famous throughout the world.<br/>
Without any fear in battle, our so good fathers,<br/>
Shed their blood for you.<br/></p>
<p>Brittany, my country that I love,<br/>
As long as the sea, like a wall surrounds you,<br/>
Shall my country be free!</p>
<p>Brittany, land of the Old Saints, land of the bards,<br/>
There is no other country that I love as much.<br/>
Every mountain, every glen is the dearest to my heart,<br/>
Many an heroic Breton are resting there.<br/></p>
<p>The Bretons are a strong and tough people.<br/>
No people under the skies is as brave as them,<br/>
Whether they may sing a sad gwerz or a nice song.<br/>
Oh, my so beautiful country!<br/></p>
<p>If in the past Brittany may have been defeated in battle,<br/>
Her language will always remain well alive,<br/>
Her flaming heart is still beating in her chest :<br/>
You are now awakened, my dear Brittany!</p>
