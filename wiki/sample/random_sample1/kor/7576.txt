<h2><span class="mw-headline" id=".EC.A3.BC.EC.9A.94.EB.A6.AC">주요리</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%EC%84%9C%EC%9A%B8%ED%8A%B9%EB%B3%84%EC%8B%9C_%EC%9A%94%EB%A6%AC&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 주요리">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
