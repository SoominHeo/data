The auction closed on 11 January with a winning bid of $38,100 that brought the final tally to $1,037,100 in gross income.
During the January 2006 auction, the website was subject to a distributed denial-of-service attack  and ransom demand, which left it inaccessible to visitors for a week while its security system was upgraded.
The Federal Bureau of Investigation and Wiltshire Constabulary investigated the attack and extortion attempt.
